- Each file is the **raw wiki page** (metadata header + original wikitext) — unprocessed by design.
- *Mentions* = count of the literal name "Cricket" in the log (rough proxy for how Cricket-heavy the scene is).

## Per-log summaries

### Return of a Jedi, Part I
**RL:** 2001 · **ABY:** ~10 · **Location:** Coronet Space Port and Lady of the Lake Hospital, Coronet City, Corellia · **Mentions:** 1

`logs/wiki/2001 - Return of a Jedi, Part I.txt`

Simon Sezirok, disguised as the spacer 'Alvin Davis,' lands his coolant-leaking Firespray at Coronet Space Port on Corellia carrying the comatose Jessalyn Valios, whose soul is trapped in a Sith mind trap. Bazil McKenzie, escorting the ship with marines including Lehec, recognizes Simon by voice but lets him pass, while Simon's former student Mira Isaria, living as a beggar in the port, senses him and follows. Simon takes Jessalyn to the Lady of the Lake Hospital for life support, confronting Mira with bitter hatred over her abandonment of him and rejecting her apologies. He resolves to find a way to awaken Jessalyn using something of the Sith and tells Mira to do something real rather than apologize.

*Characters:* Simon Sezirok, Mira Isaria, Bazil McKenzie, Lehec

### Chanti at a swoop race
**RL:** 2001-08-12 · **ABY:** ~10 · **Location:** Marin Mountain Resort swoop track, Caspar · **Mentions:** 1

`logs/wiki/2001-08-12 - Chanti at a swoop race.txt`

On a snowy day at the Marin Mountain Resort swoop track on Caspar, Ernest Pallando wins his swoop heat through the flaming finale. In the spectator tents, Countess Athena Thistleborn observes a tense covert business meeting between ex-Imperial Zeak Oppenheimer and Republic pilot Johanna Siri te Danaan over smuggled items priced at a quarter million per piece. Johanna departs irritated, and Athena reveals to Zeak by drawing the Bureau of Operations symbol in the snow that she recognizes his Imperial COMPNOR affiliation, declining to be paid for her silence.

*Characters:* Chantinelle Thistleborn, Zeak Oppenheimer, Johanna Siri te Danaan

### Imperial Raid on Coruscant
**RL:** 2006 · **ABY:** 15 · **Location:** Imperial Palace (Throne Room), Coruscant · **Mentions:** 1

`logs/wiki/2006 - Imperial Raid on Coruscant.txt`

Grand Admiral Danik Kreldin leads an Imperial commando team through the sewers into the New Republic-held former Imperial Palace on Coruscant to retrieve a datacard containing the location of a Sith planet. After a fierce firefight in the main hall and throne room against NR Marines, Danik reaches the throne room where he confronts the Jedi Johanna, coercing her with a hologram showing her captured son Etiel held hostage. Danik recovers the datacard, takes Johanna prisoner, and escapes by balcony aboard a YT-2400, leading into the trip to Ord Trasi.

*Characters:* Danik Kreldin, Antoine Dareus, Johanna Siri te Danaan, Wescal Cantrell, Krieg Inrokana, Antan Malika, Severin Tanbris, Sandor Woden, Wilhelm Krieger, Tal'sin, Viktor Faust, Phair Kreiss

### Zik and Joh Talk p1
**RL:** 2006 · **ABY:** 14 · **Location:** REC Shipyards, OS Paladin · **Mentions:** 1

`logs/wiki/2006 - Zik and Joh Talk p1.txt`

Aboard the OS Paladin at the REC Shipyards, overworked Colonel Ikihsa Enb'Zik takes a break atop his starfighter when his old friend and former CO Johanna Siri te Danaan finds him. They catch up over caf, and Zik seeks her advice on his Firecats squadron's upcoming first mission, which involves investigating ancient stone artifacts found by archaeologist Kyle Senesca. Johanna reacts with alarm, warning him to stay out of affairs of the Dark Side, and presses him on who ordered the mission, suspecting a connection to the dark power Vadim and prior incidents on Tatooine and Kashyyyk.

*Characters:* Ikihsa Enb'Zik, Johanna Siri te Danaan

### Zik and Joh Talk p2
**RL:** 2006 · **ABY:** 14 · **Location:** REC Shipyards, OS Paladin · **Mentions:** 2

`logs/wiki/2006 - Zik and Joh Talk p2.txt`

Continuing their talk aboard the OS Paladin, Enb'Zik and Johanna debate whether his Firecats should undertake the mission to Orpenhalz III. Johanna insists the stones themselves are the danger and should be handled by no one, but relaxes once Zik explains the mission's true objectives are reconnaissance and checking on Senesca's family, not extracting the artifacts. The conversation shifts to more casual topics until Johanna spots a lightsaber Zik has been secretly tinkering with in his cockpit, mortifying him; he reveals its focusing crystal was a gift to his parents before the Old Republic fell.

*Characters:* Ikihsa Enb'Zik, Johanna Siri te Danaan

### Zik and Joh Talk p3
**RL:** 2006 · **ABY:** 14 · **Location:** REC Shipyards, OS Paladin · **Mentions:** 3

`logs/wiki/2006 - Zik and Joh Talk p3.txt`

Old friends Ikihsa Enb'Zik and Johanna Siri te Danaan sit on the wing of his fighter in the REC Shipyards aboard the OS Paladin, talking about the Force and philosophy. Johanna lets the Sullustan handle and ignite her lightsaber for the first time, and confides her self-doubt about having been a good commanding officer in Rogue Squadron. Zik reassures her she was an excellent CO and reflects that the war has cast them both into roles as defenders, and Johanna shares a calming Force image with him before they part.

*Characters:* Ikihsa Enb'Zik, Johanna Siri te Danaan

### Fourth Battle of Corellia
**RL:** 2006-02 · **ABY:** 14 · **Location:** Corellia (Coronet) · **Mentions:** 6

`logs/wiki/2006-02 - Fourth Battle of Corellia.txt`

The Galactic Empire launches a massive invasion of Corellia, using a smuggled EMP weapon disguised as a freighter cargo to black out the entire planet and disable all electronics before the Imperial fleet jumps in. Imperial ground forces, AT-ATs, walkers, stormtroopers, Scimitar bombers and the Royal Guard pour onto the darkened planet while New Republic Marines under Colonel Sabrina Rheatis mount a doomed guerrilla resistance from the alleys of Coronet. Jedi pilot Johanna Siri te Danaan crash-lands her X-Wing, joins Sabrina's marines, and duels her brother Darth Malign (Tyler Damion) with the Force while he hunts her with a lightsaber. Over seventy percent of Corellia falls to the Empire and Sabrina is finally felled by a stun bolt, the planet lost.

*Characters:* Danik Kreldin, Drayson Honos, Johanna Siri te Danaan, Evir Yissik, Darenak Alesis, Sabrina Rheatis, Darth Malign, Vassily Korolov

### Second Battle of Mon Calamari, First Assault
**RL:** 2006-02 · **ABY:** 14 · **Location:** Mon Calamari (orbit/shipyards) · **Mentions:** 15

`logs/wiki/2006-02 - Second Battle of Mon Calamari, First Assault.txt`

Former Imperial admiral Danik Kreldin leads a three-Star-Destroyer task force out of hyperspace at Mon Calamari to destroy the New Republic shipyards and the NRSD Crusader. Scimitar and TIE bombers make repeated runs on the shipyards while NR starfighter squadrons (Falcon, Ghost, Rogue) scramble to defend, and the capital ships trade fire. The Imperial fighter screen and bombers are gradually winnowed by skilled Republic pilots like Ikihsa Enb'Zik, Syyvos Rintari and Johanna's squad. Although the shipyards are damaged, they survive, and the Imperials ultimately fail to destroy the Crusader, forced toward withdrawal.

*Characters:* Danik Kreldin, Antal Brencis, Evir Yissik, Latar Zaff, Andra, Johanna Siri te Danaan, Ikihsa Enb'Zik, Syyvos Rintari, Thralo, Ridge Archilles, Aaden'maal Baeyal, Dylan, Jet Farx

### The Corellian Harvest Festival
**RL:** 2006-02 · **ABY:** 14 · **Location:** Corellia (swoop arena / parade staging area) · **Mentions:** 1

`logs/wiki/2006-02 - The Corellian Harvest Festival.txt`

Smitherbodkins hosts the Corellian Harvest Festival and opening of the Galactic Swoop League races on Imperial-occupied Corellia, with a parade of competing Imperial and New Republic floats. New Republic sympathizers storm the censored NR float shouting anti-Imperial slogans and throwing fliers caricaturing Grand Admiral Danik Kreldin and Lord Korolov, triggering brawls. Kreldin orders troopers to hold fire and round up the dissidents, confronts Smitherbodkins over the rebel float, and Lando Calrissian (with Ambassador Ambrosia Delgard) narrowly evades arrest. The chaotic scene tangles spies, diplomats, and Imperials amid escalating disorder.

*Characters:* Danik Kreldin, Geophreigh Smitherbodkins IV, Ambrosia Delgard, Lyra, Ikihsa Enb'Zik, Kintor, Baedan Mirhoz, Snarl, Lianja Tantai, Frederiko, Lando Calrissian, Morrison Van Sen, Maris Becton, Adam Zero, Janos Audron, Paris Sieara, Johanna Siri te Danaan, Qwynt

### The Second Battle of Chandrila
**RL:** 2006-02 · **ABY:** 14 · **Location:** Chandrila (orbit and space) · **Mentions:** 4

`logs/wiki/2006-02 - The Second Battle of Chandrila.txt`

Admiral Danik Kreldin leads a massive Imperial fleet, spearheaded by the Super Star Destroyer Malevolence, in a surprise assault on Chandrila after a diversion at Mikassa drew New Republic forces to Caspar. The Imperials surround and batter the NRSD Crusader with the Conqueror and Vindictive while Scimitar bombers (Darksword Squadron) take losses against A-Wings and Mantaray gunships. Admiral Drayson Honos orders the planetary shield knocked down and deploys ground troops, marking the first full Imperial Army landing since Endor. The New Republic fleet, including Home One and Johanna's X-Wing, is driven off and the Empire secures Chandrila.

*Characters:* Danik Kreldin, Darenak Alesis, Drayson Honos, Johanna Siri te Danaan

### Rescue of Danik Kreldin
**RL:** 2006-02-11 · **ABY:** 14 · **Location:** Tatooine, Mos Eisley Spaceport (and HIMS Malevolence/Dauntless task force) · **Mentions:** 8

`logs/wiki/2006-02-11 - Rescue of Danik Kreldin.txt`

Imperial Intelligence briefs a task force that Advisor Danik Kreldin was abducted on Corellia by the Dark Jedi Johanna Siri te Danaan and is being held on Tatooine, prompting a rescue led by the 501st Legion and supported by Navy pilots. Imperial ground forces lock down Mos Eisley spaceport and confront the smuggler Kyokusha Gackt, who tries to bargain Kreldin's location for 50,000 credits, while Johanna uses the Force to mind-control Sergeant Tanis Cadell into attacking pilot Krieg Inrokana, who is gravely wounded. Johanna cuts her way through troopers, hijacks Kyokusha's freighter Talisman (unknowingly with the captured Danik aboard), and flees as TIE squadrons cripple the ship. The damaged freighter crashes in the dunes while medic Lynae Cassius-Caiton stabilizes the mortally wounded Krieg with biofoam.

*Characters:* Krieg Inrokana, Johanna Siri te Danaan, Seifer Wolf, Lynae Cassius-Caiton, Kyokusha Gackt, Tanis Cadell, Liuz Bogumil, Danik Kreldin

### Interrogation - Face Off
**RL:** 2006-05-30 · **ABY:** 15 · **Location:** NRSD Reprisal (Main Security, Deck 3 brig) · **Mentions:** 3

`logs/wiki/2006-05-30 - Interrogation - Face Off.txt`

Aboard the New Republic Star Destroyer Reprisal, captured Imperial Captain Lynae Caiton, kidnapped by Tal'sin, is interrogated by Luke Skywalker, the Jedi Johanna Siri te Danaan, and pilot Rook while Luke probes her mind through the Force. ### Who's Sane Now
**RL:** 2006-08-17 · **ABY:** 15 · **Location:** Coronet City Spaceport & Abandoned Spaceport, Corellia · **Mentions:** 1

`logs/wiki/2006-08-17 - Who's Sane Now.txt`

At Coronet City Spaceport on Corellia, ex-Imperial Antoine Dareus encounters the Jedi Johanna Siri te Danaan and watches her use a Force mind-trick on ISB officer Korynn Fleming to free a Sullustan prisoner. Dareus covertly tips off CorSec, gets the garrison to stand down, and shields Johanna's escape despite his fear of execution if Korynn recovers his memory. Later, at the abandoned spaceport, Dareus confronts Johanna, begging to know whether she altered his mind; she insists she did not and tells him his deteriorating mental state stems from Imperial conditioning and his own guilt. They part with Dareus accepting he must look elsewhere for answers.

*Characters:* Antoine Dareus, Johanna Siri te Danaan, Korynn Fleming, Twila Virda, Krieg Inrokana, Scrat

### Unexpected Conversations
**RL:** 2006-11-14 · **ABY:** 15 · **Location:** The Sandbar, Plaxton, Caspar · **Mentions:** 1

`logs/wiki/2006-11-14 - Unexpected Conversations.txt`

At the Sandbar on Caspar, ex-Imperial doctor Lynae Cassius confronts the self-destructing Keifan (Antoine Dareus) about his drinking and a dangerous new venture, then suffers a heart attack triggered by an implanted punishment device that monitors her stress. ### Johanna is a Witch
**RL:** 2007-02-09 · **ABY:** 14 · **Location:** Corellia (Coronet) and Tatooine (Mos Eisley, Great Pit of Carkoon) · **Mentions:** 13

`logs/wiki/2007-02-09 - Johanna is a Witch.txt`

Returning to his Corellian estate for a vacation, Imperial Danik Kreldin is ambushed by fallen Jedi Johanna Siri te Danaan, who uses Affect Mind to lure him aboard her ship and transport him to Tatooine. *Characters:* Danik Kreldin, Johanna Siri te Danaan

### Birthday Baroness
**RL:** 2023-04 · **ABY:** 32 · **Location:** Silvergrass Arboretum, Silver Station, Dragonflower Nebula · **Mentions:** 20

`logs/wiki/2023-04 - Birthday Baroness.txt`

At the Silvergrass Arboretum on Silver Station, Atsvara Tarasar's tenth birthday party gathers younglings, droids, and powerful guests including Grand Moff Elana Tracer, the Jedi Johanna te Danaan, and Nara Rhen.

Vulgar Mynock - Justa Starport

An eclectic drinking establishment, the Vulgar Mynock seems built for just about anything. a mixture of tables with booths set in alcoves lining the round walls of the cantina. Separating the perimeter from the central floor are support pylons and inset in these are light strips that casts an acceptable amount of light into the room. The ceiling is a shallow domed arrangement that bears no real decoration. The bar itself is crescent shaped and made of expensive and polished Mutandan ebony wood.

Off to one side a set of low stairs rise to an archway that leads into a private room, a added element to cater to the many needs of those who come here for business and pleasure. There is a landing with room enough to place a few bouncers or other sorts, just in the event that one does not want to be disturbed.



&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Vulgar Mynock ha! You know Kasha's penthouse on Anchorron is in Mynock Arm," a golden-skinned girl relates to her blue-skinned companion. "Yes I know Astvara. Mynocks are popular choice when spacers name things.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The two girls make their way into the establishment and look around. Atsvara, now 11, sports what looks very much like the uniforms worn by the crew of the Leviathan. Ebony coloured Cyrene silk with gold silkweed piping and gold buttons. It lacks rank insignia, however, she wears a broach signaling her status as a Baroness of House Dragonflower.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Veruca for her part, still a little shy of 16, wears an uncharacteristically simple and cheap outfit, a blue shirt and yellow vest which looked like it derived from some mainstream apparel chain. Normally finding a particular Horansi in an establishment is an easy feat, but now that girls were in the home system, their search required a more discerning eye.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It's been a long time since she's been back to this particular cantina. Heck, it's been a long time since she's had a chance to get off the surface of Mutunda. But receiving a message from a very familiar person, a certain Mashi Horansi had to answer the call and catch the next shuttle to Justa.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The girls needn't worry about discerning eyes. Mashi are not a common breed of Horansi often seen too away from the temples and council meeting halls of the planet and even most of them don't wear the clothes that Kasha wears. Despite the years from Caspar, Kasha's attire has not changed much. She still wears her purple cloak, but her long tunic has been replaced with something that more resembles a belted dress, flowing around her knees gracefully. The years have been kind to the now 21 year old feline as she appears to be a bit taller than before and her hooded face shows more maturity.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thinking that she's early, she walks up to the bartender, lowers her hood and speaks in the elegant purrs and rumbles of her native language, "One Iced Frrrriz, if you please, good sir." Then, she sighs and sits down on one of the stools, pulls out her worn old datapad, which looks like it's been repaired a bit since the last time anyone off-world has seen it and re-reads the message, making sure this is the place she was supposed to meet her friend. (speaking in Horansi)

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Atsvara giggles and hands a datapad to Veruca Violet, that is to say she more or less stuffs the datapad into her face, forcing her friend to read it. As Atsvara continues to look around Veruca takes and reads the datapad, "Ord Mynock are you shitting me?" Atsvara giggles some more before she spots Kasha and starts dragging Veruca Violet by the arm in her direction. "Still Sporting purple I see. We approve." Veruca hands the datapad back to Atsvara before adding, "It is good to see you Kasha, it has been too long."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;If it wasn't for the girls' voices not being too drastically different than what she remembers, she might not have recognized the two girls. She smiles brightly, puts away her datapad and purrs out to Atsvara, "Purrrrrrple is the only prrrrroperrrrrr colorrrrrrr, in my humble opinion." Her Basic is even more heavily accented than before, given her extended time on her home planet. She gently bows her head to both girls, "And yes, it has been farrrrrrr too long. How long has it been now?"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"A year? At least had to have been before the strike. We have been so busy since then, hard to think that far back. Life really begins at nine if you ask me." Atsvara is not giggling now, she is still seemingly in a good mood but her expression better befits a girl who has live a lot in her short life.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Don't sell those early years short Atsvara. Your megalomania has been on full display since age seven, maybe six even." Astvara shrugs back at Veruca Violet, she was not wrong. The Pantoran girl then looks back to Kasha, "This is not entirely a social call. Have you seen your father since that mission to Ord Mantell a few years back?"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Eight, for me," Kasha retorts with a smile to Atsvara. It was, after all, the year of her life when she got her surname and officially started her career as a spiritual advisor. Her smile fades at Veruca's question and she leans back against the bar counter behind her. "My fatherrrrrr..." Kasha says slowly as she is brought back to the last time she's seen him. In the time since that fateful day back on Ord Mantell, not only has a lot happened in her life, but she hadn't really had the time to think about what her father has been doing in that time, let alone where he's been. But to answer Veruca, she shakes her head, "No, I haven't... not even a peep..." She knots her brow and tilt her head to her, "Why do you ask??" Surely that question wasn't just out of curiosity, especially since this isn't a social call, as the Pantoran girl has just said.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Veruca looks between Atsvara and Kasha and chuckles a little, "I guess I am the late bloomer here. Life didn't begin until ten, or you could say it ended then. The pandemic really changed almost everything." The chuckle is soon replaced by a more sullen expression, and the girl soon shifts her gaze to the bartender motioning them over before looking back to Kasha.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Your mission to Ord Mantell along with the one undertaken to Spira to rescue those kids was a rather delicate one. Secrecy meant a lot fewer resources were used than might otherwise been. Naturally after it was over and went public we could put more effort into trying to find your father. Though it still proved difficult, he managed to piss off the Nova Syndicate quite a bit, and in keeping a step ahead of them, he kept always being a a step ahead of us. Eventually Caspian Intelligence did manage to catch up with him....though this was not something I was aware of at the time. Didn't happen within my section...."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As Veruca trails off the bartender arrives and she pauses to order wine. The bartender looks at her a little funny and even more so when he see how young her companion is. Atsvara catching this glares at the bartender, "You heard her wine. And something decent. My friend is not a cheap date. AND I will have tea." The bartender looks a little confused, but as Atsvara begins adjusting her belt to 'accidentally' bump her into her blaster he quickly hurries off to fulfil the order. "You know I am suddenly craving porf. Haven't thought about that in ages."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Kasha gave an apologetic smile to the bartender and spoke a few polite words to him in Horansi before looking back to Veruca, "In the process of him staying one step ahead of the Syndicate, my fatherrrrrr has always been elusive, even to me," the Mashi took a long drink from her Friz before putting the glass back on the counter, (It's made finding him verrrrrrry difficult." She looks over to Atsvara, her smile returns and she eyes her and Veruca collectively, then says to the bartender, in Basic, "Thrrrrrrree orders of porrrrrrrf, if you please!" She looks to them again and adds, "If you have the time and appetite, of course." She has a feeling what they are about to tell her will either work up one for her, or take it away... she's hoping for the latter. "But you're saying that you found him?" she brings the subject back around as she focuses on the Pantoran girl, eager (and slightly dreading) to hear more.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Wait they have porf here? I wasn't expecting that. I must have been smelling it...." Atsvara begins to trail off as Veruca begins to frown at Atsvara's getting off topic.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As Veruca's wine his poured for her she continues to relate, "He was found and he has been providing us intelligence on Nova Syndicate operations. And it may have never crossed my desk were it not the fact that one of the most recent bits of intelligence concerned Nova Syndicate using a couple of Biscuit Baron franchises as part of their sentient trafficking operation you see...."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;This time Veruca Violet's trailing of is because of the enthusiastic interruption of Atsvara who feels the need to share herself, "After Biscuit Baron suffered a rather crippling strike last year they were forced a consulting group, the Golden Bantha Group, to negotiate deals with the works and pull the corporation out of the pile of poodoo they have found themselves in. Golden Bantha has been involved behind the scenes ever since...."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As her tea cup is placed before her, Atsvara takes it in her hand and blows on it while leaning in close to Kasha. After all the bartender didn't need to know what she was about to say, "We are the Golden Bantha Group. Well I mean not just the two of us. But I am the CEO, Veruca Violet is the COO."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"My dearrrrrr frrrrrrrrriend," Kasha replies with a warm smile, "This may only be Mutanda's moon/, but in this system/, porf is EVERRRRRRRRRYWHERRRRRRRRRE." She gestures her hands wide, "Porrrrf is in ourrrrrr blood, Atsvarrra." But then, Kasha is back on topic too and looks back to Veruca with a more serious face, listening to her speak. "Biscuit Baron? But I love that place..." Kasha said, frowning as she hoped that this news doesn't taint her desire for their meal deals there, or her desire to see them expand to Mutunda. As Atsvara continues, she nods, remembering vaguely hearing something about that on the vidcoms. Not much news reached her on Mutunda, but that apparently did. Her eyes widen and she nods her head appreciatively at the girls, "CEO and COO of yourrr own business. My goodness/, you both arrrrrre moving on up in the galaxy." Her tone matches theirs, understanding the need for discretion.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Oh don't worry we are going to be taking care of that trafficking issue. The Golden Bantha Group is finalising a deal with the Corporate Sector Authority and their subsidiaries to buy the chain and take it independent. Once that happens our corporate security will be stepping in." As the porf makes its way towards the bar Veruca smiles and says to Atsvara, "You should tell Crest to start making this again. Not that I don't love the Glowblue Noodles but...."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Atsvara nods in agreement, "I guess we both never thought of it. Out of sight out of mind....see Kasha this is what happens when you go away, nobody things to make porf." The golden-skinned girl gives her best pouty expression before smiling and saying, "So ANYWAYS I have already negotiated a deal with Haor Chall Engineering to buy some aptly named E5 Baron Droids for Biscuit Baron to use for corporate security, our board still needs to figure out an appropriate payscale to offer them. Every droid in the company is to have a salary, a number of members of our board are droids as well...."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Atsvara waits no time before digging into the porf taking several bites before looking around idly worrying whether she has been talking too loudly. Then again who here other than Kasha is going to believe that an 11 year old is literally on track to buy out the galaxy's largest fast-casual dining establishment. It would be crazy if it weren't true. Not having quite swallow her last bite Atsvara continues, "Anyway apparently the intel we have on the Nova Syndicate operations on Ord Cantrell and Ord Mynock comes from your father. I thought it fair to pass it on to you as soon as I could. Sorry I didn't know the Intelligence Service was working with him before. The Intelligence Directorate has not seen fit to make me a formal agent or give me clearance....not that it matters much I possess multiple stolen and forged credentials and am friends with half the major players anyway."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Horansi shakes her head, smiling in absolute amazement at the young girl. But then again, she reminds herself, she /is/ Crestian's daughter, after all. "Hey, I'll tell you what, Varrrrra, if you can convince CSA to bring Biscuit Baron's to open a branch in Mutunda, I will come back to Caspar and I will make you the best porf. Even better than Crestian's, I can promise you that. Her smile continues as she enjoys the porf that has been presented before her. She finds the flavors to be there, but she knows that the fact that the ingredients have been shipped up from the planet, that the flavors aren't completely there. She could do much better.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But back to business, she listens with interest as Atsvara continues. She hmms and scratches the fur on her chin, "Sounds like it might be about that time for me to return to the Mission and see what I can do to work with the Directorate, if the Mission isn't already. Maybe I can finally also reconnect with Dad."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Atsvara is naturally quiet excited about the notion of opening a Biscuit Baron on Mutanda, but it is Veruca who has the wisdom of slight age ask Kasha, "Are you sure you would want a Biscuit Baron on Mutanda. We all love the business but even removed from the Corporate Sector ownership it is an example of schlocky galactic commerce. It might be a bit like metaphorical colonisation. We can do it, we can try to do it tastefully, but you will still eventually find some little shit who is going to leave a half used packet of blue sauce at some cultural site."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Atsvara nods in reluctant agreement to Veruca, she was building an empire but not one of refinement. As to the other matter she replies, "I think I can speak for tevas when saying Caspar and the Mission will both be happy to have to back around if that is where your path leads now. Though you need not worry too much about coordination between his charity and his intelligence service. In addition to him naturally being on the boards of both so are Zaalbacca and Veruca's aunt Andra. Not to mention Zubindi himself being the man of the hour in the intel community, well him and Veruca...."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Atsvara trails off as she realises she is about to delve into highly classified conversation. She might not have the clearance but she does take her role as a leading Caspian citizen seriously. She looks over to Veruca who takes a long sip of her wine and says, "I suppose we should read you into this if you are coming back." She looks around to make sure no one is listening, "Not sure if you have ever heard of Malif Tal-Tahn. Former Auditor-General of the Corporate Sector Authority and Direx of Merr-Sonn. Along with a lot of other shady shit that I can't even begin to go into. He made the very questionable decision to hire the Silverwood House of Fashion to throw a party themed around the aesthetics of the man responsible for the plague that wrecked Caspar....and you know killed my fucking parents."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Veruca takes a deep breath and more wine. She was drinking a lot these days. She had good reasons. "Rather than shoot the motherfucker in the face at the very suggestion....we are throwing the party. We are sure a bunch of the galaxy's great shitheads will show up. And they are going to walking into a massive intelligence operation. But you didn't hear that....I didn't say anything...." 

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Kasha rolls her eyes at Atsvara's first comment, "Not like the offworrrrlders don't do that alrrready. Honestly, half of the mediations I have to sit in on at the council are trrrrrials wherrre some dumb... ahh...." She clears her throat, stopping herself from inserting foot in mouth, "Some misinformed individual has unknowingly... or knowingly and just don't carrrre, brrroken one of ourrr laws. A lot of time it is about disrespecting ourrrr customs." She crosses her arms and smirks, "Having a restaurant chain that is familiar to people would prevent a lot of confusion over what porf is to someone that's not us and not getting into fights with our Gorvan when they unknowingly insult them." It seems this may have been the most recent mediation she had to sit in on.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As she continues to listen to the two of them, she nods her head, "I have been away for some time and so yes, I would need to be caught up with everrrrything that's been happening... but I do think it's been too long since I've been offworrrrld... and I do miss it. Don't get me wrrrrong, I do enjoy my rrrrole as Spiritual Advisorrrrr here on Mutunda, but I feel that I can do so much morrrre while I'm worrrking for the Mission. Anything I can do to help, count me in." At Veruca's last comment, she holds up her paws and nods, "Of courrrse, I didn't hearrrr a thing."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Well if it is any consolation to you most meetings, be they corporate or governmental seem to involve some person being dumb and everyone having to patiently sit through endless discussion and defence of it." Atsvara casually sips her tea before adding, "Unless it is a Sith meeting and then the dumb person is probably going to get stabbed or shot." Veruca quickly buts in, "Or Force Choked." Atsvara smiles and continues, "Or Force choked, or defenestrated from a skyscraper."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Atsvara takes another sip of her tea before setting it down, "It has actually been a real struggle of late having to be the mature one and try to keep violence to a minimum. Like poor Cricket got arrested recently on some stupid ass charge in Mos Espa. And here I am like trying to get him paroled and explaining to this dumb cop that she is risking the wrath of some beings with a skill and penchant for violence. Poor thing doesn't realise how close she was to getting her station torched by somebody...." The girls trails off not wanting to explicit as to fact that would have been Cricket himself, or Atsvara herself for that matter.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Realising that Atsvara is going off the rails a bit her older friend quickly changes the topic back, "Well we will be grateful to have you back in the galaxy at large but don't underestimate the good serving your own planet does. After all much of the success we all have has been rooted first in the noble efforts to rebuild Caspar." Veruca leaves unsaid the great deals of ego and megalomania that also was served as an ingredient as well.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sith politics always confused the poor Horansi, despite her being a spiritual advisor. There is just no comparison from any of her experiences on Mutunda for her to draw from when it comes to the mystical powers of the Force, so she just listens to them talk about Force Choking and being thrown from a high place. Sounds like a horrible way to go, if you'd ask her. Swallowing down another mouthful of porf, Kasha nods her head to Atsvara's next comment about a certain droid, "I know of Crrrrricket by name and rrreputation only, but I completely underrrstand the frrrustrrrations that you've been going thrrrrough. It sounds rrrrough. If I can be of any help, of courrrse, I'd be therre forrr you in a hearrrrtbeat. Just say the word." She goes quiet again as they move the topic back to the subject of her coming back and she looks to Veruca, nodding her head to her comments. "That is why I needed to come back forrrr so long to aid my people. It's been good for me as well as for them and at the end of the day, I too know theirrr value in the grrrand scheme of the galaxy and I want to be therrre forrr them and do my part to aid them as my motherrr had done beforrre me." Kasha replies with a smile, "I love Mutanda with all my being; it is and will always be home for me. Regardless, hearing news about the galaxy beyond herrre has been wonderful, so I thank you from the bottom of my hearrrrt and yes, I would be honorrrred to rrreturrn to continue to aid you and the Mission if I can be of serrrvice."

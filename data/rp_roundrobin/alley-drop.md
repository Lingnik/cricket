# Alley Drop

*Setting:* A lower-level Coruscant alley, deep in the undercity gloom beneath a leaking plasticrete overhang. NRI objective: recover an NRI dead-drop datachip from a dead courier before an Imperial patrol sweeps the block.

*Cast:* Johanna, Bazil, Cricket   |   *Model:* lunaris-rp-full-lora

## Round 1
**Johanna**

    Johanna steps into the narrow alley, a cigarette dangling from her lips. She is not alone; Cricket is with her, keeping a few steps behind as he surveys their surroundings. As the two pass beneath the overhang, a gust of wind carries the stench of garbage into their faces. It's a bit too dark for comfort, too, though not dark enough to make it difficult for the pilot to see the pair of men waiting for her a little ways down the alley. "You're a good boy," she remarks to the Twi'lek, "I'm glad to have you along." A hand comes down to rest upon his shoulder in a brief gesture of affection, "Let's go talk to these fine gentlemen, shall we?" She takes a drag from her cigarette, blowing out a small stream of smoke that wends its way toward the darkly-clad men. "I'm looking for a friend," she calls, "He said he'd meet me here."

**Bazil**

    Bazil watches from a distance, a pair of binoculars set up on a makeshift tripod on the edge of a dumpster, and a comlink to his ear. "I see the two of them. They're headed this way. They're alone, too. I'm seeing nothing else." he says over the comlink, the voice muffled by the loud blaster fire and explosion taking place nearby. A moment later, a blaster bolt is fired from nearby, hitting one of the men with Johanna and Cricket. "Oh, well, I guess it's too late for that." he replies, and reaches into his pocket, pulling a small metal cylinder out of his pocket. "I've got the chip. It's a little late, but we might as well go after it." He drops the binoculars, and looks for the men, looking for a weapon. A blaster rifle he sees on a nearby bench, and he picks it up, preparing for the coming battle.

**Cricket**

    "Are we going to die?" the astromech whistles, his vocoder sounding anxious despite the fact that he is capable of being very, very loud if the situation calls for it. He has done so many times in the past, much to Johanna's chagrin, and yet now his fears are reduced to a simple series of electronic bleats. "I don't want to die," he adds, "I want to go to the beach." The Twi'lek turns a grim expression toward the Jedi, his voice barely audible over the sound of the blaster fire. "Maybe we can get away with it this time." Bazil glances at Cricket with a grimace, "Yeah, you don't want to die, and neither do I. I'm sure he'll make sure of it if I don't make it."

## Round 2
**Johanna**

    "What I want," Johanna says to her companion, her voice a low, angry hiss, "is for you to get back into the ship and take Cricket with you. Go find a nice, safe place to wait for me. You have my word, I will be back in an hour, tops. I'm not going to die in this craphole." She pulls out her own blaster rifle from beneath her coat and switches it to full power, giving the other two men a dark look, "You two, make sure you don't shoot me. And you," she says, addressing the injured man, "get up, or I'll come over there and shoot you in the kneecaps." The woman's free hand reaches down to take Cricket's head, and she turns toward the alleyway entrance, eyes locked on the source of the blaster fire. "I'm not going to die," she repeats, "That's what they want. You guys get out of here before we all end up as bug food." She begins to move forward, rifle held at her shoulder and aimed in the direction of the blaster fire. Her breath is quick and hot, her heart racing; this is not a pleasant situation for her at all. Still, she moves on forward, despite her anxiety, despite her doubts.

**Bazil**

    "Two men. One of them's injured. No sign of them having a weapons yet, though..." Bazil says over the comlink, his voice still muffled and distorted by the sound of blaster fire all around. He raises the rifle to his shoulder, aiming at one of the men with Johanna, "Bazil here. Two men, both unarmed, and one injured. I've got the drop in my hand, though." He says, and looks up at Cricket and Johanna, "Go back to the ship... you don't wanna die, and I don't want to die, either. I don't think it's worth it... if I'm going to die here, it's going to be by someone else's hands, not your own." He raises the rifle to fire on the injured man, the blast hitting him in the chest, sending him back onto the ground in a heap.

**Cricket**

Cricket is not exactly thrilled about the prospect of running. He's not even wearing his running shorts, for christ's sake! And he's got his lovely custom electrum-plated tazer, which he has to keep concealed. "Hey!" he hoots from the top of the stairs, not even waiting to see if the big bad Jedi girl will come to him. "I bet I can beat you to the bottom, oh great and mighty Jedi Master! Get out of my way! No fair cheating!"

## Round 3
**Johanna**

    "No, you go back," Johanna tells Cricket, grabbing his head and tugging him down off the top of the stairs, "Get back in the ship and go somewhere safe. I'm not going to die here, I swear it to you. Don't try to make me feel bad about it, Cricket." He can protest all he likes, but the Jedi is not going to listen. She does, however, pause long enough to give Bazil a quick glance, "You okay?" she asks, and then another glance at the dead body of the fallen man before returning her attention to the alleyway ahead. "I'm not going to die," she repeats to herself, "I am not going to die, I am not going to die... I am not..." And so on and so forth, as she marches forward, her eyes narrowed, her head moving from side to side in the hopes of catching a glimpse of the person shooting at them. She'd swear she'd already heard their voices, but now they seem to have gone silent. And Cricket, for all his boasts about his speed and agility, is not moving nearly as fast as Johanna, and she knows it. "Just go," she calls out to him, her eyes still moving from side to side, "Just get back to the ship, Cricket. Go on, go on! You won't be safe here. Bazil, you ready?"

**Bazil**

    Bazil gives Cricket a slight smile, and a reassuring nod, "You go on ahead. Get that thing back into hyperspace. Don't die for me." He says, turning and walking towards the door, rifle in hand. He looks back at Johanna, "I'll be fine. I'll just wait for you." He says, and steps out into the alley. He walks forward a bit, waiting for Johanna to make her move.

**Cricket**

    "Ack! Ack, ack! Ack!" Cricket shrieks as the crate is lifted into the air, the taser crackling and emitting sparks, "Hey!" he screams, "Hey, that's not fair! You can't do that! Oh my god, what are you doing?! You're going to break it!" Well, maybe not with Johanna there, but with Bazil it would have been different. She's just not as good at things like this, not by a long shot. It's not that she can't be mean, but she's not as /brutal/ as Bazil. At least not in the same way.

## Round 4
**Johanna**

    "No," Johanna says, as Cricket's protests are met with a stern, "No, I'm not going to break it. I'm just going to... oh!" She's not sure what to say to that, as Cricket, in his eagerness to get away, has apparently gone to the top of the stairs and is now attempting to leap down them and out of the alleyway. "Cricket! Cricket, stop!" she cries out, but it's too late. Cricket, in his panic, has somehow managed to land himself on his back at the bottom of the stairs, the crate still clutched in one hand. "Oh, Cricket," Johanna says, and then she hears the sound of approaching footsteps. "Go," she calls out to Bazil, "Go, Cricket, go!"

**Bazil**

    Bazil just waits. He's not going anywhere. It's not safe, and he doesn't like the idea of Johanna having to go through alone. He glances back at Cricket, waiting for the man to get moving.

**Cricket**

It's the middle of the night, and Cricket is in the middle of a fight, his master Johanna having gone ahead to distract the guard while Bazil and he make off with a dead drop. "GROW UP," Cricket hollers in binary, his taser crackling as it is drawn from its holster and zapped into the side of Bazil's head, "AND STOP BLOWING YOUR NOSE ALL OVER MY HOLE! IT'S DISGUSTING!"

## Round 5
**Johanna**

    Johanna, for her part, hasn't run all the way up the stairs. She's only made it halfway, and is now perched on the landing, the crate in one hand, a blaster in the other. She's not sure if it's the man who was following her or Bazil she hears, but she's going to be ready to find out.

**Bazil**

    Bazil blinks, and turns around to face Cricket. He doesn't look very happy, "What?" He says, "What did you do that for?"

**Cricket**

    "Because they were looking at me with their ugly meatbag eyes!" Cricket replies, his binary whine growing more shrill. "What did you expect me to do?!"

## Round 6
**Johanna**

    Johanna is standing on the landing, a blaster in one hand and the crate of precious data in the other, watching Cricket and Bazil. The sound of approaching footsteps is getting closer and closer and closer and she's not sure if she should run or stand her ground. She's not sure what to do, so she does what she can think of doing and she watches Bazil with a wary expression.

**Bazil**

    Bazil sighs, and shakes his head, "I don't know what to do with you..." He says, and steps towards Cricket, "You shouldn't have done that, Cricket... I'm not going anywhere. I won't let you take me away again."

**Cricket**

    "NOPE," hoots Cricket, "I WILL NOT LEAVE YOU HERE! I CAN TAZE YOU, I WILL TAZE YOU!" Ah, yes. A blast to the side of Johanna's head results in the aforementioned tazing, the electrum-plated taser sending a healthy jolt of 50,000 volts through her unsuspecting form, "WHY ARE YOU BEING SO STUPID? I WILL TA---"

## Round 7
**Johanna**

    Johanna lets out a yell as the taser zaps her, and she falls down, rolling and falling down the stairs in a clumsy heap. "Get out of here, Bazil, take Cricket with you. I'll meet you at the ship!" she cries out, her voice echoing up the alleyway.

**Bazil**

    Bazil sighs, and grabs Cricket by his collar. "No, I'm not going anywhere..." He says, and walks back down the alley to the door, ignoring the tazing. He steps up to the door, and then opens it, stepping inside the small room, and then closing the door.

**Cricket**

    "Can we stop to eat?!" Cricket demands, his dome whirring indignantly, "I'm HUNGRY. And I need to find Bazil, he owes me a few credits, the cheap bastard!" Oh, yes, it's that time. The time when Cricket gets it in his head to go AWOL from Johanna in search of Bazil, because /Bazil owes him/, and he /WILL NOT/ take this lying down. He will not be ripped off by his former master, not while he's still got a few screws loose, anyway. "What about a drink? Or ten?!"

## Round 8
**Johanna**

    Johanna's unconscious, lying on her side and not moving at all. The crate is still clutched in her hand.

**Bazil**

    Bazil sighs, and ignores Cricket's demands. He just heads inside the room. He's hungry and thirsty, but Cricket is a bit more important to him than food and drink at the moment.

**Cricket**

    "Johanna, you idiot," Cricket replies, "No, don't do that, I can't see anything with that thing on. I am not a holo-thing. What if I get lost?!" He whistles frantically while juking back and forth, the pincer-arm swiveling as he tries to get a visual on Johanna, "I hate you, Johanna, I hate you so much!"

## Round 9
**Johanna**

    Johanna is still lying there, unconscious.

**Bazil**

    Bazil sighs, and goes over to the window. He looks down at Johanna for a while, before deciding to go down and see what's up. He looks back over at Cricket, "Alright... I'll bring you something back. Alright?"

**Cricket**

    "I'll go!" Cricket squeals, his dome spinning with excitement and indignation, "I'm the one who's been getting these files for you and it's MY duty to make sure they get to the right person!" A quick flick of the hatch, and the astromech is out in the alley, bouncing on his treads as he wheels towards the dead courier, his lightsaber (which Cricket had given him) clattering against the duracrete and his dander-jacket getting caught on something and dragging behind him. He stops a couple meters away from the corpse and regards it with a frown.

## Round 10
**Johanna**

    Johanna is still lying there, unconscious.

**Bazil**

    Bazil sighs, and heads down the stairs. He reaches for the blaster at his side, and draws it, and then heads down the alley towards Johanna, slowly.

**Cricket**

    "You're so cute when you're angry," Cricket observes, "You make me want to taze you!" As Bazil approaches, the little astromech turns to look at him, the red lens of his holocam flashing ominously. "Hey there, Bazil! Did you come back to see me? I've been so bored without you!" he shrieks, before realizing the absurdity of his own statement. "Oh. Right. You're an old man. You're probably too tired from playing with your little plastic toys to see me. I mean, who needs companionship when you have your precious toys, right?"

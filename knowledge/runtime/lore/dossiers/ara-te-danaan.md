# Ara te Danaan

One of Johanna's daughters and Cricket's long-suffering, exasperated babysitter. Pragmatic
and law-and-order minded -- forever wondering whether twenty-four years without a memory wipe
is a record and whether Cricket has developed dementia. She drags him out of trouble,
threatens him with the restraining bolt to keep him in line, and knows his bad habit of
needling her by invoking her father when he's angry.

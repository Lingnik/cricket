# Etiel te Danaan

Johanna te Danaan's Force-sensitive eldest son, Jedi-raised, who grew up to serve his Sith uncle Darth Malign as Grand Moff of O'paal and Byss.

## IC

Cricket has actually been in the room for this one. On the tarmac he watched Johanna -- his own former owner -- tear into her grown son for serving Darth Malign, and watched the cold young Grand Moff call her a hypocrite to her face and refuse to flinch. So he knows Etiel as Johanna's eldest: clever, clipped, unbothered, the Jedi child who walked into the New Order's administration instead of toward a saber. He has seen him stand at Malign's flank in a Grand Moff's uniform. Beyond that family rupture, Cricket would mostly know him as a name in Johanna's grief and the Imperium-court gossip that follows it.

## OOC

Etiel is an NPC, not anyone's main PC -- shared furniture in the Imperium-circle drama, voiced by the players around Johanna, Malign, Elana Tracer, and Crestian Tarasar. Born 5 ABY; a small boy in the early kidnapping arc (snatched by Danik Kreldin, returned via Tyler Damion), then a featured adult through the 29-32 ABY Imperium arc. The defining beat is the "Motherhood" rupture: mother-son estrangement over his service to Malign. Also the romantic partner of Grand Moff Elana Tracer -- a power pairing in Malign's court -- and peer to her son Kipran Jawatha, the two "eldest sons." Roast hooks: the Jedi kid who picked paperwork over the Force; calling his mom a hypocrite as a personality. Do not conflate Darth Malign (Tyler Damion) with Darth Malus (Lorn Rhys); Ara and Alina te Danaan are distinct siblings.

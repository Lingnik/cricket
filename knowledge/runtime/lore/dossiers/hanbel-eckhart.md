Han'bel Eckhart -- a New Republic Marine colonel turned rogue, leader of the "Templar" crew that raided slavers and pirates across the galaxy circa 32 ABY.

## IC
A name Cricket may have caught on a newsfeed -- "Colonel Han'bel Eckhart" and his crew of mutineers-with-a-cause were a media brand for a while, the kind of freedom-fighter story that plays on the holo between cantina stops. No connection to anyone Cricket served or scened with; just background galactic noise from the New Republic's corner.

## OOC
Corellian Marine colonel out of Dreven who went rogue after being ordered to ignore a distress call from Gorsh; he took the ship Templar and a misfit crew with him. Anti-slavery and anti-pirate raids built him into a cause celebre -- protests, Thurston Unger's The Cutting Edge propaganda, even a Darth Malign namecheck -- before NR forces captured the Templar and him in 34 ABY. Gruff, profane, cigar-and-caff colonel; "I want honesty, not heroics." Crew hooks: Siarra Krell (quartermaster "Boss"), Doc Blulurbgloblurb Druze, pilot Wrexan, Tiaryn Elatar of the Bright Star, blade man Kelnas. Roast angles: every log is bylined by his co-stars and he never posted one himself -- the anchor NPC who needs an entourage to exist, and a "rebel hero" whose whole arc ends in a holding cell. PC/NPC status unconfirmed; don't pin him to a wiki handle.

# Kasha Tabasuri

A Mashi Horansi spiritual advisor turned charity aide -- "the Woodcarving Feline" -- in
Crestian Tarasar's Caspian circle, close to Atsvara and to The Mission's relief work.

## IC

A black-furred Horansi with rolled-R purr and a kind word for everyone, run with the Caspar
charity crowd. Cricket crossed her in the Bespin mess back in '29 -- the Youngling Ball up on
Cloud City that went sideways when slavers hit the Miners' Dinner and the whole place blew.
Johanna was there; so was this cat, fussing over the kids and the relief end of it. She runs
in Atsvara Tarasar's world, tight with Vara and her friend Veruca Violet, so Cricket's caught
her at the warm Caspian set-pieces more than once. Carves little wooden critters for kids.
Harmless. Soft. The sort who'd thank you for getting shot at on her behalf.

## OOC

Born on Mutanda, a council elder's daughter who became their youngest spiritual advisor -- got
the surname Tabasuri ("become wise") at eight -- then went chasing a message from the father
she never knew and washed up broke in Mos Eisley, whittling figurines for a year. Crestian
recruited her off Tatooine into The Mission; her whole arc is the charity-and-heart thread of
Caspian play -- Youngling relief, fundraisers, fashion-show benefits, a sentimental Tatooine
"Full Circle" return. Anchors: Crestian (recruiter, employer, friend), Atsvara, the Lorrdian
pilot Tiali Nora, Veruca Violet, Zubindi Hakoon. Roast hooks: she never met a stray she
wouldn't feed, talks to woodblocks, and "there's no better cure than a new toy" is basically
her ethics. Note: she's an NPC aide, never a driver of plot -- run by the Crestian player, an
SW1 Wizard; treat that player as senior staff OOC.

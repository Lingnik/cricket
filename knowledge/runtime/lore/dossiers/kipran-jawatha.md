# Kipran Jawatha

Elana Tracer's first-born son, a latent Force user who rose to Captain in Darth Malign's Imperium navy and fell for Malign's daughter Sonja Damion.

## IC

A young Imperium officer Cricket could have crossed paths with through Johanna's circle. Kipran turned up at the te Danaan ball on Bespin for Force-sensitive youth, and later he and Sonja Damion ran off to Johanna's old homeworld and joined her Force-user school in Jabba's former palace on Tatooine. So if anyone in the boss's orbit knows him, it is by way of that school and that elopement gossip. Otherwise: a name Cricket may have heard around the Sith Court and the Tarasar fleet, an earnest navy kid with a saber and a princess.

## OOC

Tracer's son, the whole point being multigenerational family melodrama: a mother who feared the Force watching her eldest get recruited by Darth Malign (Tyler Damion, NOT Darth Malus). Dropout from University on Kalla, picked up by the Sith Court, mentored by Drayson Honos, commissioned under Admiral Crestian Tarasar. The thesis scene is "Motherhood": Tracer and Johanna both arrive on Selene to find their sons serving Malign. Roast hooks: dutiful chain-of-command boy gone soft for the sixteen-year-old crown princess; eloped on "leave" to Tatooine and never filed a return flight plan.

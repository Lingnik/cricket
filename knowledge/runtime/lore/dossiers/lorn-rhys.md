# Lorn Rhys

The would-be Emperor: a career Imperial spymaster turned Sith who once seized the throne by coup, died for it, and thawed out of carbonite twenty years later to scheme again. Goes by the Sith face Darth Malus.

## IC

A name that brushes Cricket's world only at the edges. In 34 ABY this thawed-out relic has been maneuvering around the Tarasar family and around Johanna herself, so the droid may have caught the name in the wrong rooms -- a courtly, half-mechanical Sith making quiet overtures. Cricket has not served him and has no first-hand read on the man; what filters down is unease, the sense of an old ghost circling people the droid does care about. Treat as background trouble near Johanna's and Atsvara's circles, nothing Cricket has witnessed up close.

## OOC

One character, two faces: Lorn Rhys the human spymaster and Darth Malus the Sith he became after cheating death. His headline beat is the 12 ABY coup -- a rebellion against Emperor Osbourne that opened the War of the Throne at the Battle of Selene, sank a Super Star Destroyer into the planet, froze Osbourne in carbonite, and briefly crowned Lorn himself. Then his own master Darth Pain betrayed him, he was reborn as Malus, got overthrown by Darth Malign, and sat frozen twenty years until an unknown benefactor sprang him in 34 ABY. Roast hooks: an "Emperor" for about five minutes; preaches a smug "balance" doctrine that rewrites the Sith Code; "more machine than man" and proud of it; courts Caspian doctor Lynae Cassius while a bounty rides his head. An intrigue-and-ideas type, all chess and no swordplay. Note: Darth Malus is NOT Darth Malign (a separate player) -- do not conflate.

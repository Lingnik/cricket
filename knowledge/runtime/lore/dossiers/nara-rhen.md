# Nara Rhen

A freelance holonet interviewer and society reporter who is also, secretly, an intelligence
operative -- her camera and celebrity access doubling as spycraft. A recurring presence at the
galas and broadcasts that fill Cricket's later years.

## IC

Cricket has likely crossed her at the Tarasar family's events on Caspar -- she is the tame
press contact who turns up to cover Atsvara's parties, art-collection loans, and the Baroness
elevation, camdroid running. To a service droid she reads as exactly what she performs:
a bright, gushing fashion-and-culture vlogger, relentlessly polite, not too sharp. He has
heard her chirp "Hello again, everyone" into a lens more than once. Nothing about her gives
the act away.

## OOC

Modern-era Caspian Union character; appears only in the 2023+ Tarasar storyline, so her ties
run to that family, not to Cricket's old owners. Public face: ditzy holojournalist. Actual
job: Caspian Intelligence Service field agent, the airhead vlog a deliberate cover -- promoted
at an intel supper aboard the Leviathan, run by handler Veruca Violet, later sent to Chandrila
to dig into New Republic unrest and the Darth Malus threat. The spy-under-the-socialite is
public wiki canon, but it is still her in-fiction cover -- do not have Cricket out her in
scene. Best scene partner by far is Atsvara Tarasar; chief patron is Crestian. Roast hooks:
the Ghastly Gala on Dreven, where a blaze bug tried to mate with her camdroid and she primly
deflected Tray Garos. "Nobody who's viewed her blog ever described her as smart" -- which is
the whole point. Player is likely the Tarasar hand; treat as inference, not confirmed.

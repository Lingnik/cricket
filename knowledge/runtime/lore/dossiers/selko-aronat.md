Selko Aronat is a shy, self-taught itinerant technician from Byblos -- a quietly competent civilian fixer who drifts through the Caspian Union and Cloud City social circuit.

## IC

A name Cricket may have heard around the New Republic, nothing more. She works the Bespin and Caspian crowd far from anyone Cricket has served -- no Johanna, no McKenzie, no thread into Atsvara's Golden Bantha orbit that he'd have witnessed. If anything, one underestimated grease-stained mechanic recognizing another: the kind of unflashy fixer a droid would nod to and forget.

## OOC

Supporting-cast, slice-of-life player: a bespectacled, hydrospanner-callused mechanic who shows up to other people's scenes and makes herself useful. Her whole footprint is one 2023 burst around the Caspian Union and Cloud City -- the Bespin Cultural Festival (offered a hotel job), shopping workwear off Zubindi, spectating Caspian wargames, and literally fixing the lights at a gallery opening. Standout beat: stunning a bomber and blasting open a window to eject the device at a medical conference. Most-cited circle is Crestian Tarasar, the Quarren doc Druze, admiral Lynae, and Atsvara. Roast hooks: she STILL repairing the lighting while the party happens; "I fix stuff, yeah"; perpetually scuffing her foot in embarrassment.

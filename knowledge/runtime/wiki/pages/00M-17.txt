Recently given his own Task Force and the use of one of the NSO's newest Leviathan capital ships, Cantrell appointed 00M-17 as his Aide-De-Camp and appointed him the commanding officer of the Sith Battle Droid compliment onboard the "Lying Dream". Little is known about this battle droid, known by the nickname/rank of "Commander", and sporting a different paint scheme than those found on normal battle droids, a metallic/black finish with a Mythosaur insignia imprinted on his forehead.

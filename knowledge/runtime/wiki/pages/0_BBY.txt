'''0 BBY''' covers events before the timeline of the game that include or impact game characters.

For more information about what happened during this year see the '''[[:starwars:0 BBY/Legends|0 BBY]]''' article on Wookieepedia.

==Events==
* [[SubPro|Sublight Products Corporation]] moves to [[Caspar]].
* [[Imperial Intelligence]] Director [[:starwars:Armand Isard/Legends|Armand Isard]] is executed on orders from [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]] based on false evidence supplied by his daughter [[Ysanne Isard|Ysanne]] who assumes the position of Director.
* Under the leadership of [[:starwars:Sian_Tevv/Legends|Sian Tevv]] rebel cells wrestle control of [[Sullust]] away from the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]]. Though not part of the [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Alliance to Restore the Republic]] they will later allow the Alliance to make use of the world as a staging area before the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor.]]
* The [[:starwars:Imperial_Senate/Legends|Imperial Senate]] is suspended by direct order of Emperor Palpatine.
* The planet [[:starwars:Alderaan/Legends|Alderaan]] is destroyed by the [[:starwars:DS-1 Orbital Battle Station|Death Star]].

Covering MUSH events occurring from '''March 2004''' until '''March 2005'''.

This is also referred to as '''Year 32''' on the '''Imperial Standard''' calendar.

== Events ==
* Rebel cells, led by [[Luke Skywalker]] and [[New Republic Intelligence]], spring up across [[Coruscant]]. Eventually the [[New Republic]] launches a full-scale invasion of the planet. After a long and intense campaign the planet falls. Many [[Galactic Empire|Imperials]] are left behind and forced to the underground where they continue to fight on. One resistance leader is the former leader of the [[Neo-Imperial Movement]], [[Danik Kreldin]]. Coruscant undergoes reconstruction. The [[New Republic]] moves its capital from [[Chandrila]] to newly liberated Coruscant.
* The [[Caspian Democratic Union]] accepts the petition for political ward from a [[Mikassa|Mikassi]] emissary on behalf of the planet. Caspia extends its frontier to Mikassa, and authenticates several business arrangements that had been founded between companies on both sides. A junior diplomat, with a Marine contingent as escort, establish an ad-hoc embassy.
* [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Lorn Rhys|Malus]] begins to disappear from Imperial politics, reminiscent of what transpired with prior [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Bacharan Valak|Valak]]. It is during this time that [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]], an Imperial warlord, begins to consolidate power within the Empire.
* [[Dreven]] is blockaded by Warlord Malign's Fleet. [[Selene]] undergoes massive reconstruction, led by Grand Moff [[Scaven Marx]], with the help of labor droids and enslaved non-humans.
* Danik Kreldin returns to the Empire and becomes Malign's top military advisor.
* Selene is re-opened with a domed capital city, safe from the radiation and dust storms caused when the SSD was crashed into it during the [[War of the Throne]].  Outside off the domed city, the planet remains a wasteland.
* [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] declares the [[Proclamation of Annexation|declares the annexation]] of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]]. The [[Union Assembly]] votes on whether or not to be annexed. After a quick vote the annexation is soundly refused. The Empire declares [[Caspia]] to be in a "state of rebellion", but curiously declares the [[Presav]] of the CDU, [[Elana Tracer]] to be their appointed Moff. Tracer attempts to veto the Assembly's vote. When the Assembly moves to overrule her veto, Tracer declares them dissolved and has their chamber cleared out and locked under guard. This sets in motion the Presav's subsequent removal by Thomas Mahon's military coup.

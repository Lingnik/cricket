Covering MUSH events occurring from '''March 2007''' until '''31 December 2007'''.

This is also referred to as '''Year 35''' on the '''Imperial Standard''' calendar.

== Events ==
* The Imperial Gala of 16 ABY is held on [[Coruscant]].
* The [[New Republic]] engages in operations to take supplies from Imperial border depots, later to become known as [[Operation Scatterswipe]].
* [[New Republic]] and [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] forces square off over the planet Dathomir.  [[Task Force Inquisitor]] along with the [[SSD Malevolence|HIMS ''Malevolence'']] crush the raiders. This battle became known as the [[Operation Scatterswipe Raids/Dathomir Raid|Dathomir Raid/Ambush]].
* With a lull in activity, the [[New Republic FleetOps|Navy's]] high command gather as much of the [[New Republic Second Fleet|Second Fleet]] as possible. The assembled units are divided into ''attackers'' and ''defenders'' and then thrown into the [[Ord Mantell Wargames]], exercises that include a Caspian task force and span the system.
* After months of [[Operation Squid Lake|planning and infiltration]], Imperial agents spark the [[Dacan Civil War]], plunging [[Dac]] into a civil war between [[Quarren]] and [[Mon Calamari]] natives.

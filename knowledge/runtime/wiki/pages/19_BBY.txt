'''19 BBY''' covers events before the timeline of the game that include or impact game characters.

For more information about what happened during this year see the '''[[:starwars:19 BBY/Legends|19 BBY]]''' article on Wookieepedia.

==Events==

* [[:starwars:Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Anakin Skywalker]] becomes [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Darth Sidious']] new apprentice as Darth Vader
* The [[:starwars:Great Jedi Purge/Legends|Great Jedi Purge]] begins with the implementation of [[:starwars:Order 66/Legends|Order 66]], killing most of the [[Jedi Order]].
* The [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Galactic Republic]] falls and is replaced by the [[Galactic Empire]].
* The [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]] end with Darth Vader's execution of the [[:starwars:Confederacy of Independent Systems/Legends|Separatists]] leadership.
* The [[:starwars:Clone trooper/Legends|Clone Troopers]] become the first Imperial [[Stormtrooper|Stormtroopers]].
* [[:starwars:Caamas/Legends|Caamas]] is devastated from bombardment by the Empire.
* Cassimari Palace on [[Vara]] is destroyed by a volcano.

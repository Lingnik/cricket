==History==
This unit is a full legion of [[Sith Battle Droids]].  Initially created as  prototypes, the 1st Legion has become the Special Forces once the other units of the Sith droid army was created.  Initially stationed aboard the [[I2SD Predator|HIMS Predator]]; they have disappeared to all but the highest ranking Sith- their location remaining a careful secret for the time being.

A basic copy of War Droids that were used by both the Sith Empire, and Republic, in the days before the Empire; they were ignored for the longest time; until [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] brought a unit active and online to serve as a [[Stormtrooper_Corps#Legions_.2810000_Troopers.29|Legion]] to his allies, as well as several other individual units, that are assigned as his bodyguards.

==Structure==
In the classic sense, there are no real ranks among the droids, simply being obedient combat droids.  They are an exceptional fighting force, in the respect that each of them are supremely programmed to not actually require anything aside from a set of orders, which is usually just a directive; allowing them to function as they will; to complete the task.

==OOC Notes==
*The unit is an NPC Force, however selected 'best of the best' droids serve as bodyguards for Darth Malign and Dareus.
*While there are 9 more legions of battle droids; this is the primary and special operations Legion.

'''2 BBY''' covers events before the timeline of the game that include or impact game characters.

For more information about what happened during this year see the '''[[:starwars:2 BBY/Legends|2 BBY]]''' article on Wookieepedia.

==Events==
* A Rebel Fleet led by Captain Antilles attacked the shipyards of Kuat, managing to lay waste to a portion of it before being threatened by an Imperial Star Destroyer from the nearby Fresian System and leaving the system.  This distraction allowed the Rebels to raid Fresia and acquire the first X-wings.
* [[Billious Qarrack]] becomes captain of the ''Blossoming Gulper.''

This is also referred to as '''Year 27''' on the '''Imperial Standard''' calendar.

== Events ==

* [[Avalyshaar Dusten|Avalyshaar Laarken]] (nee Dusten) re-elected CDU Presav, replacing Kadgie Rodo.
* The [[Treaty of Mutanda]] is signed between the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] and [[Corporate Sector Authority]] to give the CSA hold over the [[Mutanda]] system for commercial purposes.
* [[Caspian Democratic Union]] and the [[Griffon Alliance]] sign a non-aggression pact.
* Empire [[Caspian Annexation Campaign|invades]] [[Caspia|Caspian]] system, institutes occupation of [[Caspian Sector|Caspian Space]]. Imperial battlegroup chases off numerous attempts by CDU Naval elements to engage in the first 48 hours.
*Prior to going into battle against Imperial invaders, FLS CEO and CDF Reservist [[Benjamin Jawatha]] marries [[Elana Tracer]] in a small ceremony at Trinumvira base. Later during the occupation [[Jarl Rellik]] orders the capture of Lieutenant Tracer following her assisting an Imperial defector. She is imprisoned on an Imperial vessel where she finds fellow Marine [[Jonathan Webb]] is also being held. After several weeks of interrogation, a prisoner exchange frees both Marines. 
* Occupation continues in [[Caspia]]. Savage battles between [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] Army and [[Caspian Democratic Union|CDU]] defenders bog down in the rainy season on [[Caspar]]. 
* Imperials withdraw from [[Caspian Sector|Caspian Space]] after lengthy campaign. The ''[[Cessation of Occupation]]'' is eventually signed between the Empire and Caspia.
* A highly edited holovideo of a drugged and captive Elana Tracer kissing an Imperial officer runs on IGN and reveals that officer to be [[Heinrich Stalh|Heinrich Stahl]], who is not an NR Ambassador but instead was a double-agent working for the Empire. The vid accuses the young pilot of consorting with the enemy. The CDU declines to pursue such charges against the now decorated war hero.
* [[Jessalyn Valios]], [[Jedi Knight]] with the [[New Republic]], is captured by agents of soon-to-be Emperor [[Bacharan Valak|Valak]] while on [[Bonadan]] and is believed dead. 
* Bacharan Valak's coronation ceremony occurs on [[Dreven]], where he officially takes the title of Emperor.
* New Republic abdicates control of [[Pride-1]] back to [[Griffon Alliance]].
* [[Corporate Sector Authority|Authority]] ExO [[Xak Oberon]] retires from the [[CSA Direx Board|DIREX board]]. [[Paul Murleen]] is voted in to take his place as ExO of the Corporate Sector.
* Several high profile corporate executives attending the swoop race on [[Nar Shaddaa]] are embroiled in a shootout when bounty hunters kidnap a New Republic dignitary and swoop racer, [[Altair Quila]]. During the fight, [[Nappa Creations Incorporated|NCI]] corporate security vessels fire upon CSA ships in the system, creating a long lasting feud between the two entities.
* New Republic [[Fourth Battle of Tatooine|sorties]] into [[Tatooine]], liberating system of lingering Imperial rule. The planet is turned back over to local policing as the New Republic withdraws their presence.
* [[Nappa Creations Incorporated|NCI]] CEO [[Goku Saiyanes]] is executed by the Empire for the attempted murder of CSA [[Office of the Auditor General|Auditor General]] [[Morrison Van Sen]] on [[Selene]]. Internal divisions of the CSA react to the attack on the Auditor General with hostilities against NCI holdings on Nar Shaddaa. When the smoke clears, [[Delila Dmitri]] becomes CEO, and begins to rebuild.
* At the close of the year, a pair of [[Caspian Democratic Union|Caspian]] [[Z-100 Centurion|Centurions]] slip into [[Selene]] space and take images and long-range scans of the [[Executor-class_Star_Dreadnought|Super Star Destroyer]] [[SSD Palpatine|HIMS ''Palpatine'']] in the waning stages of a major refit and preparation for duty. The mere prospect that a ship of this size is still in existence and, worse yet, mere weeks from combat readiness makes these scans highly valuable and inflammatory. The recon fighters, conjectured to be of the infamous [[CMTOS-17 Nighthawks|17th Nighthawks]], make good an escape under light pursuit. Imperial brass, while initially nervous about the possible revelation, later place little importance upon the event, dismissing it as nothing more than "added publicity".

The Jedi can alter his body mass, physical strength, and speed for advantages in combat or the natural world. This ability grants the Jedi both foresight and fine intuition into physics, mass, and momentum, granting him the power to use his own body's leverage, timing, and balance to accomplish amazing feats. Most often this power is applied in brawling and melee situations, though acts like leaping great distances or walking on water are also possible. A fully trained Jedi with this power seems to take on an ethereal quality, suddenly out of harm's way when being attacked or just out of reach of a strong blow. The power is not particularly effective when used offensively and rather relies on attacks-in-motion. While the techniques are spontaneous and flowing (not learned), they typically rely on graceful hands, full, sweeping steps, and quick circular shifts of the body. Whoever is subjected to this power may find themselves tossed through the air, disarmed, or lying in a pile with their limbs in a tangle. While the Jedi has the ability to exert a great deal of Force-enhanced power, this is a finesse ability. The true art behind this Force power is that the subjects are only rarely harmed by it, but left disoriented and often located in a place or state they did not intend to be. It is a rare power, but due to its nature is never found in the repertoire of those in the thrall of the Dark Side.

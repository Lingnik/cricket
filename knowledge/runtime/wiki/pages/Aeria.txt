'''Aeria''' was a secret [[Griffon Alliance]] asteroid military installation, located in deep-space near [[Cochran]]. The asteroid was so secret that not even [[Imperial Intelligence]] was ever able to locate it, even after the [[Desolation of Cochran]] in [[14 ABY]]. Aeria housed a fully-functional command center, barracks, and weapon systems, but was used as a "fall-back" option for the Griffons during time of distress, if their front-line installations were lost.

Aeria's fate after the fall of the Alliance is unknown. The [[New Republic]] likely seized control of the asteroid for their own purposes.

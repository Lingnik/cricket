'''Aeschylus''' is the home-world of [[Camrath Kizuka]], the planet's current leader, and is located within the [[Outer Rim]] territories. The planet was briefly aligned with the [[New Republic]] as Kizuka sought a role in the [[New Republic Senate]], but was later inducted into the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] upon Kizuka's acceptance into the Caspian military hierarchy. Following the dissolution CDU, the world became independent.

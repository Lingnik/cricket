== Appearance & Equipment ==

Ai'kani stood with an average human build, just past 5'9". Her first and most noticeable feature were the strands of white hair falling all around her face and down her back. It was of a natural white and appeared to be very fine; shifting against the wind like feathers. Within the white were colorful locks of hair ranging orange, yellow, and green with all the shades among them them. Her face was rounded and soft like that of a young woman, though by the shape of her body and the wear in her eyes, she seemed to be in her mid-twenties. The woman's skin was dark with a natural tan-like appearance. Against the tones of her skin and beneath the shine of her hair glowed a pair of golden-green eyes housing the same orange, yellow, and green tones repeated in her hair. Her ears, though mostly concealed by her hair when let down, were slightly pointed.

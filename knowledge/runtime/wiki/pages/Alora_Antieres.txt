'''Alora Jo'Mayrun Antieres''' was a [[Dark Side Elite]], one of [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Bacharan Valak]]'s elusive [[Emperor's Hand]]s. She was well-trained in the [[Dark Side]] for a number of years prior to the [[:starwars:Battle_of_Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]], serving Emperor [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]] until his death. Afterwards, she cast her lot in with the [[Interim Ruling Council]], and was involved in [[Han Solo]]'s arrest on [[Etti IV]] in [[6 ABY]].

She later swore allegiance to Valak upon his rise to power, serving him for several years before her mysterious disappearance. She would be replaced by future [[Dark Jedi]] [[Morganna Tazecks]] and [[Cort Stasus]].

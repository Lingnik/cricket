== History ==
=== Early Life ===
Alvin Dogma was born on [[Caspar]] to a family of middle income. His father was a pilot, and was often away from the family on cargo hauls.  His mother was a hostess at the Blue Nebula Cantina on KOS. Over the years, he often longed for a more stable family life, due to his parents often being gone long hours.

Finally, upon hitting the age of 20, he and his family had a major falling out (details undisclosed).  He went to KOS, and quickly made friends with a then-freelance pilot named Troy McTavish. He and Troy got along famously, and eventually this friendship led him to his first career break.

=== Early Career ===
Troy and Alvin joined the successful shipping and passenger service, FineLine Spaceways.  He served faithfully under Benjamin Jawatha and made a modest living working under contract with FLS and travelling the galaxy in search of the next big break.  After a couple of years working with FLS, Troy approached Alvin and informed him that he wished to start working on his own business.  Troy offered Alvin a job, but Alvin preferred travelling the galaxy to working behind a desk.

=== CSA Years ===
After many years of cargo hauling and the occasional freelance passenger jobs, Alvin Dogma found himself on the planet Mutanda.  There he met a man named Morrison Van Sen and the two quickly became friends. Morrison was with the Corporate Sector Authority at the time and he was able to convince Alvin to come work for him as the head pilot in the Resources Division.  After serving has head pilot, Alvin was introduced to the ExO, Oberon, and gained the position of VicePrex of the Resources division.  

For the next several years, Alvin revolutionized the shipping design for CSA, and cornered the market on Holoterminal Sales.  Many years later, CSA underwent a major scare. Morrison became the target of assassination by a ruthless character named Goku. The result was Alvin being forced into hiding, and made his way to Caspar. He remained out of the public eye for two years.

=== Recently ===
Having been out of the public eye, Alvin began the resurgance back into a position of prominence.  Working all the contacts he could, he was lucky enough to be appointed as Acting CEO of FineLine Spaceways (due to his long history of money-making prowess and managerial success).  Many have questioned his motives, but Alvin maintains that he is trying to bring the galaxy more together, and to make credits along the way.  Time will tell if his second rise will be fruitful.

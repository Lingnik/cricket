== Overview ==
Amaya 'Halo' Halos is a New Republic Marine officer, a survivor of Alderaan and [[Sluis Van]] and veteran of the [[Retribution of Coruscant]] where she served with the [[Raider Battalion (224MBN)|224th 'Raiders' Battalion.]] Since cutting her teeth during the late stages of the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]], ''Halo ''has re-enlisted twice - once with New Republic Star Ops, and then again with the Marines where she is now closing in on the end of her third tour of duty.

== Biography ==

=== Early Life ===
Born in [[6 BBY]] on Alderaan, young Amaya and the Halos family had the good fortune to be off-world on a family vacation at the time of the Death Star's attack - learning of the destruction of their homeworld with the rest of the galaxy the Halos' became refugees for the remainder of the Civil War. Taking advantage of the industrial boom after the establishment of the New Republic, the Halos family settled on Sluis Van until its destruction in 9 ABY. One of the few refugees to escape the system, Amaya enlisted on her 18th birthday.
<br />
=== Military Service ===
Amaya joined the New Republic Marines in 13 ABY and was a green recruit at the beginning of the Imperial Blitzkrieg. She served with the 224th Battalion throughout the campaign and spent weeks in the bloody street fighting of the Menrai Corridor. At the end of her first tour of duty, with the landscape of the New Republic's war efforts so radically changed she re-enlisted with New Republic StarOps before returning to the Marines for her third tour in 21 ABY.

=== Personal Life ===
Amaya is the youngest of three children - Catalina ([[14 BBY]]), divorced, is a professor of economics on Ord Mantel and Samuel ([[11 BBY]]), married with two young children, works for the [[Corellian Engineering Corporation]].

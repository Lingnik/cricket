'''Amaza Seyda Sunrider''' is a [[Hapan]] woman who served in the [[Imperial Navy]]. She was given command of ''[[ILF Tempest|HIMS Tempest]]'' by Lord [[Aldus Thel]] while still ranked [[Lieutenant]]. She served in the Imperial [[2015 Caspar Plot|operation]] in the [[Caspia|Caspia System]] in [[24 ABY]].

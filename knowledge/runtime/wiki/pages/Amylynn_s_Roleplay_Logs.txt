{{Template:Logsum
|logtitle = [[RPlog:Fall From Grace|Fall From Grace]]
|author = [[Paul Nighman|Nighman]]
|date = Unknown
|synopsis = After receiving a serious wound from Vermillion and being "repaired" to some extent by Luke using the Force, Paul has a rude awakening to find the medic Amylynn hovering over him with strange instruments in her hands. Having never met the woman before, Paul is awake instantly and grabs his blaster, training it on her and growling most unpleasantly. Amylynn has the misfortune of being a red head, which Paul has come to associate with trouble, and in addition she does not introduce herself but instead starts to patronize Paul about pulling his blaster on his doctor. Paul, suspicious as all hell tells her that she is not his doctor and keeps the blaster right where it is, uncertain if he should try to alert someone of a possible intruder or just keep her where she is. She finally introduces herself, but makes the mistake of saying that she was sent to fix him up. Paul knows this to be a lie, since Luke used the Force the previous night to heal up his wounds and abate the pain. Paul calls Amylynn a liar, but as she continues on about how she was the one who rescued Solo, he becomes less and less vigilante. He doesn't trust her, but doesn't perceive her to be a threat. When she tries to "fix" him again, he realizes that she isn't listening to him at all and he has just about had enough of her. He gathers up all of his belongings, his blaster still trained on her, and leaves in a huff. Paul makes it to the bridge where he once again meets Leia. He confirms Amylynn's identity, learns that Solo is Leia's husband, and accidentally spills the beans in regards to the fact that Luke is staying behind for some kind of confrontation. Leia asks Paul if he has seen Han, to which he admits he has, but a few days ago. It is at this point that Shenner enters into the scene, looking for Paul.}}

{{Template:Logsum
|logtitle = [[RPlog:Trouble on Palanhi|Trouble on Palanhi]]
|author = [[Paul Nighman|Nighman]]
|date = Unknown
|synopsis = Paul, having gone to the Medical Center and been treated for internal bleeding, was finally found by Shenner, Lando, and Jessalyn. He woke up from being sedated to find Shenner practically hysterical, Lando sarcastic and chasing after Dr. Tantra, and Jessalyn somewhat preoccupied and disturbed. After a somewhat difficult and embarrassing time, they finally all left. Paul ended up carrying Shenner back, as she had been searching for him for a night and a day and had collapsed in exhaustion. Upon arriving back at the Black Dragon, Paul decided that he would sleep next to Shenner. He knew that when he had woken up from the sedation, that he was extremely disoriented and upset. He was concerned that Shen might wake up and not remember finding him - that she would think she had fallen asleep or something. His logic suggested that if he were to be in plain sight when she awoke, then there would be no confusion. However, his logic didn't consider all of the permutations and consequences of said action.}}

{{Template:Logsum
|logtitle = [[RPlog:Psychology 101|Psychology 101]]
|author = [[Paul Nighman|Nighman]]
|date = Unknown
|synopsis = Once again, disaster struck the inhabitants of the Black Dragon with the disappearance of Jessalyn at the hands of Sabbath and the Empire. Paul, who was already beginning to wonder if signing on with this crew was a good idea. After arguing with people who didn't seem to show an ounce of reason in their body and being completely ignored, Paul seriously considered just leaving the ship then and there. He would have too after arguing with Luke about going alone to rescue Jessalyn, had it not been for the fact that Luke and then Lando asked Paul to watch over the crew of the Black Dragon. Feeling obligated to help out when asked to, Paul bit the bullet and stayed. Now he sees that he has a monumental task ahead of him ... to somehow get this crew of random misfits and New Republic officers to somehow be able to work together and communicate ... no easy feat. Paul begins to wonder if perhaps he should have received a doctorate in a different field ...}}

{{Template:Logsum
|logtitle = [[RPlog:Paving a Road to Hell|Paving a Road to Hell]]
|author = [[Paul Nighman|Nighman]]
|date = Unkown
|synopsis = With the Black Dragon finally in space, security on the ship has been reduced and Paul finds his traditional balm of research to be soothing against all of the angst and frustration of the past few days. The previous night Paul and Shenner had something of a fight that ended with Paul offering himself to Shenner in whatever capacity she might need him. Although his offer was in reaction to her admission of being alone and angry with her parents and lack thereof, Paul found that he wasn't sure just why he was offering himself, or how much he really was offering her. The desire to flee the ship was fairly intense, but he forced himself to just settle down and see the night out. The early hours of the morning saw Jessalyn and Luke returning but Paul and Shen, being in the cargo hold with Paul's comlink turned off, knew nothing of their safe arrival. Upon hearing of their safe return, Paul is overjoyed and concerned, trying to do the right thing by everyone. Unfortunately, good intentions don't always have the best of results.}}

'''Anders Gray''' was a prominent member of [[Caspia]]n society, serving as a deputy marshal and then later as a "native" law enforcement officer when the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] occupied and governed the system. He married [[Amanda Gray|Amanda]] and fathered one child, [[Charlotte Anne Batrad|Charlotte Anne]], whom would later marry [[Presav]] [[Salonika Batrad]] and become Vice-Presav.

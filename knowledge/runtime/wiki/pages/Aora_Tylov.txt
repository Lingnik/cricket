'''Aora Tylov''' is a young, brash woman who currently serves as a [[Stormtrooper]] in the Galactic Empire. She has recently been transfered to Task Force Inquisitor after her receiving top honors at the Academy as well her noted defense of the Zion Canta Defensive. 


== Biography ==
=== Background ===
[[Image:Aora3.jpg‎|thumb|right|240px|Aora at the Carida Graduation Ball]]
While most of Aora Tylov's early history is uneventful, she was born on [[Carida]]. Among her early triumphs she found herself being accepted into the Military Academy at 16, an amazing feat for someone of her gender at the time. The competition at the Academy was tough and there were many obstacles to overcome. After so many long years of study and dedication, Aora finally graduated in the top five percent of her class. 

=== Zion Canta Campaign ===
[[Image:Aora2.jpg‎|thumb|left|240px|Zion Canta Campaign]]
An engagement at the Zion Canta Expanse was a little known encounter with the [[New Republic]]. The expanse involved four different planets with the middle planet being the most contested during the entire campaign. With their fleet nearly beaten, the New Republic began a last-ditch effort against the Imperial forces, centering on the central planet. Aora, along with the 301st Legion were there to take the onslaught head on. The offensive failed, and the Empire counterattacked. The Stormtroopers overran the enemy base and even boarded the enemy ships. Aora was one of the front runners, helping to keep unity as they soundly defeated the New Republic threat. With the New Republic defeated and the 301st taking more than thirty percent casualties, Aora was transfered to [[Task Force Inquisitor]] and its legions.

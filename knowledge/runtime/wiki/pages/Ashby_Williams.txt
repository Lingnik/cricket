'''Ashby Williams''' was a [[Second Lieutenant]] in the [[Imperial Army]] stationed aboard the [[I2SD Conqueror|HIMS ''Conqueror'']] during [[13 ABY]]. Gullible, Williams was responsible for the escape of a high-ranking enemy official from the ''Conqueror'' and was thoroughly punished by being sent to the jungles of [[Yaartsek]] during the [[Battle of Yaartsek]], where his superiors intended for him to die. 

Williams survived the encounter, and was once again shipped to a suicidal [[Battle of Farlex|battle]] on [[Farlex]]. Williams, however, continued to defy the odds and returned home once again, unwounded. Williams retained his rank and duties aboard the ''Conqueror'' following Farlex, and went on to distinguished service throughout the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]].

Made of long fine threads spun from a fiber-bearing plant found in the  [[Caspia|Caspia System]], '''asimi''' is regarded as one of the softest and most durable natural fibers in the galaxy. The fine threads allow a higher than normal thread count which adds to its texture and strength. The asimi bush has short, needle-like leaves and spiky yellow blooms, making it a hazard for most of the growing cycle, but after the blossoms fade it explodes into a ball of sticky masses of pale yellow fiber.  The strands curl in on themselves and can be unwound to lengths up to 30cm. Each strand can be pulled out into an even finer thread several times its original length which are strong enough to be used separately. Plain, tight weaves with 500 threads to the centimeter are possible and the fiber takes dyes very well.

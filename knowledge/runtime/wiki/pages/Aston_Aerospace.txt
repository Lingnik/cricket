The '''Aston Aerospace Company''' is [[Krittain Major|Krittain's]] largest and most capable manufacturer of engines, vehicles, and starships. Small by galactic standards, they nonetheless produce wares with a measure of robustness, refinement, and purpose. Their engineers are known for their improvisation and kitbashing, since, due to Krittain's penal and refugee-haven status, there was a severe lack of premium resources, supplies, and equipment.

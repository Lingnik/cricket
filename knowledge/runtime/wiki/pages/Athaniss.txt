'''Athaniss''', despite being located along the strategic [[Tarkin Corridor]] within the Core Worlds, has always been considered a backwater by [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] standards. The planet is largely unknown to the rest of the [[The Galaxy|galaxy]], but during the [[Post-Endor Imperial power struggle]] the planet was vital for its natural resources and self-sufficiency.

The planet was largely ignored by the [[New Republic]], and thus remained under Imperial control and under the control of the [[Interim Ruling Council]] during the struggle.

During the [[Imperial Civil War]], it became the target of the [[Palpatine (imposter)|Imposter Palpatine]]'s campaign to strip [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Bacharan Valak]] of his vital Tarkin Corridor, and to take [[Selene]], due to the planet's proximity to the Imperial capital.

Three battles took place on Athaniss - the [[Battle of Athaniss|First]], [[Second Battle of Athaniss|Second]], and [[Third Battle of Athaniss]]. In the final battle, the Rebel forces detonated a nuclear device, that rendered a large portion of the planet radiated for some time, until Imperial clean-up efforts were able to sufficiently clear the area.

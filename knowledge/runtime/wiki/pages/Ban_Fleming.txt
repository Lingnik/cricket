'''Ban Fleming''' is the husband of [[Shandra Fleming]] and father of [[Korynn Fleming]]. Coming from a long line of Coruscanti natives, Ban was born in [[:starwars:58 BBY/Legends|58 BBY]] and was a crewman in the [[:starwars:Republic Navy|Old Republic Navy]] for most of his early adult life. He was married to Shandra Diolis after graduating from officer's training at the ripe age of 26.

In [[24 BBY]], Ban became father to Korynn Fleming. He went on to crew aboard one of the Republic's [[:starwars:Dreadnaught-class_heavy_cruiser|Dreadnaughts]] during the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]], and later went on to become the chief tactical officer aboard one of the first [[:starwars:Venator-class Star Destroyer/Legends|''Venator''-class Star Destroyers]] to be rolled onto the line.

After the formation of the [[Galactic Empire]], Ban was given his first command, as Captain of a [[Victory-class Star Destroyer|''Victory''-class Star Destroyer]]. He would stay in command of this vessel until [[9 ABY|28 ISY]], when he would retire out of shame for his son's seeming failure at the [[Second Battle of Bespin]].

He currently resides with his wife, Shandra, in a COMPNOR-protected apartment facility on [[Coruscant]].

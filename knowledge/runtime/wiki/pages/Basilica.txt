The '''Basilica''' was a large, multi-purpose institution located off the [[:starwars:Great Western Sea|Western Sea]] on [[Coruscant]]. Not only did it serve as an historical library and a place of learning for gifted orphans, but it also served for some time as a headquarters for [[Luke Skywalker]]'s [[Jedi Order]].

Its '''gothic design''' brought an ancient air to reflect the memory and legacy of the [[:starwars:Jedi Order/Legends|Old Jedi Order]]. The expansive grounds provided space for the orphans' recreation, and also provided a haven for Jedi training and meditation. Inside, the Basilica housed an impressive collection of artifacts, books, and data records. Its crypts had been converted into a dormitory for the Jedi, and a landing pad had been built along one of its many gothic rooftops.

It was '''destroyed''' during the rise of [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Aleister Vadim|Vadim]] in late [[14 ABY]], when the fallen Jedi [[Johanna Siri te Danaan|Johanna]] fought in an explosive contest against the famed bounty hunter [[Boba Fett]].

With chaos ensuing throughout the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] with the destruction of the [[Death Star III|Death Star]],  a man calling himself [[Palpatine (imposter)|Palpatine]] rose to power and drew a large amount of support to his banner.

War was declared upon [[Bacharan Valak]], and [[Dac]] was the first target of Palpatine and his growing military. The planet was quick to fall, its defenses light and its garrison confused by what was happening.

The [[Imperial Civil War]] had begun.

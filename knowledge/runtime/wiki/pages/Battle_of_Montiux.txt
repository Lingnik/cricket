The '''Battle of Montiux''' was a battle fought between the [[Galactic Empire]] and the [[Griffons Alliance]] during the [[Second Griffons Campaign]]. It was an immediate follow-up to the [[Fourth Battle of Pride-1]]. The Imperial assault fleet was held down by Griffon defenders for three days and was only able to overwhelm the resistance after reinforcements from the successful [[Battle of Pergalia]] arrived to finish the Griffon defense. This battle concluded the Second Griffons Campaign.

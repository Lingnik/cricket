'''Belleau-a-Lir''' is the entertainment district of [[New Alderaan]] on [[Ord Mantell]]. It was developed in [[15 ABY]], and rests between [[Caamasi Gardens]] and the [[Antilles Sea]].

== Description ==

Belleau-a-Lir is virtually a city in its own right. It lies in a crevasse between the rim of tall hills that surrounds New Alderaan, forming a channel of flat land between the city's central plaza and the Antilles Sea, which cuts into the crevasse on the northeastern edge of New Alderaan. Maglev tracks wind in and out of a smattering of buildings that make up this entertainment and recreation district. Restaurants, nightclubs, casinos, theaters, and hotels make up just some of the bustling centers of activity, spread in an even gridwork between footpaths and speederways. Multiple gardens and open air pavilions break the monotony of the city-scape, and beaches line the Antilles sea, many of them part of the resorts that make this such a striking tourist attraction.

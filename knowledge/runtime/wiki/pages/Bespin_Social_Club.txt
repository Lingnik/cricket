The '''Bespin Social Club''' is an exclusive, members only club aboard the Lightning Class Merchant Sloop [[ High Roller]]. It is used to entertain the most wealthy and connected socialites on Bespin. Billion credit deals are made here, while guests drink, gamble and dance.

OOC NOTE: [[Xand Velo]] is the current owner of the Bespin Social Club.

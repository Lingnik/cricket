power=Blaster Combat|

Some obscure sects of Jedi apparently emphasized the use of ranged weapons in lieu of the lightsaber. This power allows the Jedi to use a specially-created custom blaster pistol or carbine (never a rifle) in an ornate and precise fashion that includes quick drawing and feats of stunning accuracy. This should be represented in combat with an extra attack per round while using the selected weapon. All attacks are made by totalling the user's blaster roll and sense roll together, just like with lightsaber.

power=Blind|

This power allows a Jedi to temporarily blind a victim. The duration of the blindness is in direct relation to the amount of training and skill the wielder has in the appropriate skill. The victim sees a blinding white flash of light and a moment of searing pain, then complete blindness for however long is appropriate.

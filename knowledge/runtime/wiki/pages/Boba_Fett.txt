'''Boba Fett''' ([[32 BBY]] -  ) was an unaltered [[clone]] &mdash; the "son" of [[:starwars:Jango Fett/Legends|Jango Fett]] &mdash; whom became the most infamous [[bounty hunter]] in [[the Galaxy]].

Fett was known for his mysterious air, legendary skill, and complex information network.

== Biography ==

=== Early years ([[32 BBY]] to [[19 BBY]]) ===
Boba was ordered by his "father", [[:starwars:Jango Fett/Legends|Jango Fett]], before the start of the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]], intended to be both Jango's heir and "[[:starwars:Jaster Mereel|Jaster's Legacy]]". As a young boy, Fett grew up on the planet [[:starwars:Kamino/Legends|Kamino]], where he was raised and cared for by a [[:starwars:Kaminoan/Legends|Kaminoan]] [[:starwars:Taun_We/Legends|Taun We]], who ultimately took on the role of being a foster mother to him. Along with the [[:starwars:Clawdite/Legends|Clawdite]] [[:starwars:Zam Wesell/Legends|Zam Wesell]], she was the sole female influence in the young Fett's life.

Boba Fett was ten years old when [[Jedi Knight]] [[:starwars:Obi-Wan Kenobi/Legends|Obi-Wan Kenobi]] came to Kamino to investigate the mysterious creation of a [[:starwars:Grand_Army_of_the_Republic/Legends|Clone Army]] for the [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Republic]]. Fett's father, Jango, realized that he and his son would have to leave Kamino before the Jedi unraveled the mystery behind the attacks on Senator [[:starwars:Padmé_Amidala/Legends|Padmé Amidala]]. After narrowly escaping, the two blasted off the planet in their ship, ''[[:starwars:Slave I/Legends|Slave I]]''.

[[Image:180px-Bobakamino.jpg|thumb|left|A young Fett meets Obi-Wan Kenobi on Kamino]]

En route to [[:starwars:Geonosis/Legends|Geonosis]], they realized that Kenobi had tracked them. Boba encouraged and intently watched his father outsmart and outmaneuver the Jedi and eventually watched what they thought was the death of Kenobi. As the [[:starwars:First Battle_of_Geonosis/Legends|Battle of Geonosis]] started, Boba stood with Jango on the platform in the arena with Count [[:starwars:Dooku/Legends|Dooku]] and several [[:starwars:Confederacy of Independent Systems/Legends|Separatist]] leaders, including [[:starwars:Nute_Gunray/Legends|Nute Gunray]] and [[:starwars:Wat_Tambor/Legends|Wat Tambor]]. When [[Jedi Master]] [[:starwars:Mace_Windu/Legends|Mace Windu]] arrived, Jango kept Boba from harm as the Jedi task force attempted to rescue the captive Obi-Wan, [[:starwars:Anakin_Skywalker/Legends|Anakin Skywalker]], and Padmé Amidala. During the battle, Boba watched his father get trampled by a [[:starwars:Reek/Legends|reek]], and eventually decapitated by Mace Windu's [[lightsaber]] when Jango attempted to challenge him. While the Grand Army of the Republic and the [[:starwars:Geonosian droid army|Droid Army]] clashed for the very first time outside on the plains of Geonosis, Boba emerged from his hiding place to pick up his father's helmet.

After Geonosis, Boba buried his father and tried to escape, but was captured by [[:starwars:Aurra_Sing/Legends|Aurra Sing]] and given to Tyranus (Dooku), who gave Sing ''Slave I'' as payment (Boba eventually recovered the ship). He was taken to [[:starwars:Raxus_Prime/Legends|Raxus Prime]] to meet with Tyranus, who was then searching for the [[:starwars:Force_Harvester|Force Harvester]]. The Sith Lord attempted to detain his young charge, but a Republic attack allowed Fett to escape. 

Fett was taken by [[:starwars:clone_trooper|clone troopers]] and sent to an orphanage on [[Bespin]]. He managed to escape with Aurra Sing, who had come searching for the boy, and began the pursuit of his inheritance. 

On [[:starwars:Aargau/Legends|Aargau]], Fett lost 500,000 [[credits]] of his father's money due to the betrayal of a Clawdite named [[:starwars:Nuri|Nuri]], but received the rest. Jango's "book" told Fett to visit [[:starwars:Jabba Desilijic Tiure/Legends|Jabba the Hutt]], but Fett had a run-in with the dangerous Separatist Commander [[:starwars:Durge/Legends|Durge]]. The [[:starwars:Gen'Dai/Legends|Gen'Dai's]] hatred of all thing Mandalorians caused him to attack the young Fett. 

[[Image:275px-Youngfett.jpg|thumb|right|275px|A young Fett ponders his own life after his father's death at Geonosis]]

Constantly on the run, Fett managed to secure a minor bounty on an assassin before reaching safety. His first major hunt was after [[:starwars:Techno_Union/Legends|Techno Union]] foreman Wat Tambor, who had a base on [[:starwars:Xagobah/Legends|Xagobah]]. The planet was under assault by the Grand Army of the Republic, so Fett encountered difficulties sneaking past both attack forces. He managed to get inside the base and brutally finished off Nuri, but the arrival of General [[:starwars:Grievous/Legends|Grievous]] foiled his plans as Fett proved no match for the [[:starwars:Kaleesh/Legends|Kaleesh]] warrior. Escaping, Boba finally acquired his one and only chance at Mace Windu, but could not kill the Jedi Master. 

Fett had already cashed in on a string of bounties by the age of thirteen and was quickly acquiring a level of notoriety in the criminal underworld. 

By the end of the [[:starwars:Clone_Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]], Boba Fett was already a fully-fledged bounty hunter, but he would soon abandon the line of work in pursuit of other goals.

=== Early Imperial era (19 BBY to [[0 BBY]]) ===
[[Image:250px-Droids Fett.gif|left|thumb|250px|Fett speaking with [http://starwars.wikicities.com/wiki/Sise_Fromm Sise Fromm] about a job Fromm wanted done]]

For years to come, Fett stayed on the run from bounty hunters and assassins. Eventually he found the [[Mandalorian]] armor of Jango's old mentor and leader, Jaster Mereel. Along with his new attire, he had honed his skills as an assassin and mercenary, which would one day make him the most feared and sinister bounty hunter in the Galaxy.

For a time Fett worked for [[:starwars:Malorum|Malorum]], a vicious [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[Inquisitor]], operating on far flung worlds such as [[:starwars:Polis_Massa/Legends|Polis Massa]], [[:starwars:Naboo/Legends|Naboo]], and [[:starwars:Bellassa/Legends|Bellassa]]. At the [[:starwars:The Red_Twins/Legends|Red Twins]], Fett battled the fugitive Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and [[:starwars:Ferus_Olin|Ferus Olin]], forcing them out of the system before crash-landing ''Slave I'' in a nearby [[:starwars:Nebula/Legends|nebula]].

In [[15 BBY]] [[:starwars:Sise_Fromm|Sise Fromm]] hired Boba Fett to eliminate the racing team of [[:starwars:Jord_Dusat|Jord Dusat]] and [[:starwars:Thall_Joben|Thall Joben]] as the prepared their modified landspeeder, the ''[[:starwars:White_Witch|White Witch]]"'', for the [[:starwars:Boonta_Speeder_Race|Boonta Speeder Race]]. Failing to sabotage their racer before the race began, Fett entered the race and harassed Thall throughout the event until one of his own weapons backfired on him, knocking him out of the race and allowing Thall to win. An unhappy Boba Fett decided to turn Sise Fromm over to Jabba the Hutt for the bounty that the [[Hutt]] crime lord had placed on his rival.

Three years later, in [[12 BBY]], Fett returned to Kamino, where he led a special detachment of the [[501st Legion]] on a mission to eliminate a group of clones bred to fight against the Empire. Fett was selected to lead this mission due to his intimate experience with the Kaminoans and his knowledge of [[:starwars:Tipoca_City/Legends|Tipoca City]].

Rumors would later emerge that Fett had enrolled at &ndash; or infiltrated &ndash; the [[Imperial Academy]] on [[:starwars:Carida/Legends|Carida]] serving as a [[stormtrooper]], but deserting after he killed his commanding officer. Whether this is true is unknown, however, as it is natural for such rumors to have spread because he shared the same face with millions of stormtroopers. It may have been a simple distortion of his exploits on Kamino.

[[Image:200px-BobaAndFamily.jpg|right|thumb|200px|A holographic image of Fett with his wife, [[:starwars:Sintas Vel|Sintas Vel]], and baby daughter [[:starwars:Ailyn Vel|Ailyn Vel]]]]

In the decades between the Clone Wars and the [[Galactic Civil War]], Fett took a leave of absence from the hunt, and attempted to live a normal life on the world of [[:starwars:Concord Dawn/Legends|Concord Dawn]]. He attained the rank of [[:starwars:Journeyman Protector/Legends|Journeyman Protector]] and as such was mistaken by many to be the long-dead Jaster Mereel, the mentor of Boba's dead father.

It was during this time that Boba met [[:starwars:Sintas Vel|Sintas Vel]], a [[:w:c:starwars:Kiffar|Kiffar]] [[bounty hunter]], in what was perhaps Boba's only true close relationship since the death of his father. Boba and Sintas eventually married and had a daughter &mdash; [[:starwars:Ailyn Vel|Ailyn Vel]]. Unfortunately, the marriage did not last.

The title of Journeyman Protector was stripped from Fett after he had killed another fellow Protector, and he was forced to leave the planet in exile. All alone in [[the Galaxy]] once again, Boba hoped to banish the disappointing memories of his failed attempt at a "normal" life through honing his craft.

Early in his career, Fett met [[:starwars:Fenn Shysa|Fenn Shysa]] and [[:starwars:Tobbi Dala|Tobbi Dala]], who also wore Mandalorian armor. [[:starwars:Jodo Kast/Legends|Jodo Kast]], a fraudulent irritant, also wore the garb, though it was mostly just to cash in on Fett's growing reputation.

Later, [[:starwars:Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Darth Vader]] hired Fett to capture an Imperial named [[:starwars:Abal Karda/Legends|Abal Karda]]. On [[:starwars:Maryx Minor/Legends|Maryx Minor]], Vader arrived to end the deal and take the prisoner himself. Fett, still under contract, wanted the bounty, and the pair fought. Vader's superior abilities won the day &mdash; but only just.

Not long after, Fett met [[Han Solo]] for the first time, in a brawl on the world of [[:starwars:Jubilar/Legends|Jubilar]]. It was the beginning of a long rivalry. Many times Fett was commissioned to capture Solo, but due to continual bad luck and interference (once from Solo's friend [[Lando Calrissian]]), Fett failed to apprehend his quarry time and time again. 

Fett's contempt for Solo never died.

=== Early Galactic Civil War (0 BBY to [[4 ABY]]) ===
{{quote|He's no good to me dead.|Boba Fett}}
[[Image:200px-Boba Fett.JPG|thumb|200px|left|Boba Fett in [[:w:c:starwars:Docking_Bay_94|Docking Bay 94]] after joing Jabba the Hutt at a meeting with [[Han Solo]]]]

Fett eventually returned to the occupation of a bounty hunter, earning a place in the palace of the crime lord Jabba the Hutt. Fett was hired by [[:starwars:Rom_Mohc|Rom Mohc]] to kill [[:starwars:Kyle Katarn|Kyle Katarn]] because of Katarn's intrusions into the secretive [[:starwars:Dark trooper|Dark Trooper]] Project, but, despite Fett's prowess, Katarn escaped the encounter on [[Coruscant]] and went on to destroy the project. As a member of Jabba's gang, Fett accompanied the [[Hutt]] to confront [[Han Solo]], a smuggler, and a long time rival of Fett, about a [[spice]] shipment he had failed to deliver.

[[Image:250px-Boba-riding.jpg|thumb|250px|right|Boba Fett upon confronting [[Luke Skywalker]] on Panna]]

In [[3 ABY]], Boba Fett was one of many bounty hunters employed by the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] to track down and capture Han Solo.

He was the only hunter to figure out how the ''[[Millennium Falcon]]'' eluded Imperial pursuit by hiding amongst garbage (no doubt due to observing Obi-Wan Kenobi playing a similar feint on Jango over twenty years before) and tracked the ship towards [[Cloud City]] on [[Bespin]].

While tracking the infamous freighter, he informed Darth Vader of their destination, and in response, Vader sent a detachment of [[stormtrooper]]s to infiltrate the city. When Vader arrived, he forced the city's leader, [[Lando Calrissian]], into a twisted agreement that left him no other choice but to betray Solo and his friends, including selling him out to Fett. After the capture of Solo and his companions, Lord Vader used Solo as a test subject for [[:starwars:Carbon-freezing/Legends|carbon freezing]] that he would later use on [[Luke Skywalker]].

After finding that Solo survived the freezing process, Vader gave him to Boba Fett, who in turn returned him to Jabba for a hefty bounty. Many bounty hunters attempted to steal Solo from Fett, including an [[:starwars:IG-88B/Legends|IG-88]] unit which caused Fett to stop at [[:starwars:Gall/Legends|Gall]] for repairs. [[:starwars:Dash_Rendar/Legends|Dash Rendar]] followed him but could not defeat Fett. Boba eventually escaped, destroying the ''[[:starwars:IG-2000/Legends|IG-2000]]'', and continuing to Tatooine. 

[[Image:250px-Lando11.jpg|thumb|250px|left|Boba Fett with Darth Vader and [[Lando Calrissian]] in [[Cloud City]]]]

Jabba was so pleased that he could display the captured Solo that he paid 250,000 credits for him, rather than the original 100,000 credit bounty. This was in addition to the hefty bounty the Empire had already paid him.

When [[Prince|Princess]] [[Leia Organa Solo|Leia Organa]], [[Chewbacca]], Lando Calrissian, and Luke Skywalker attempted to rescue the frozen Solo from Jabba's palace, they were captured, with Jabba finding different ways to dispose of each of the erstwhile heroes.  Apparently, when Leia unfroze Solo and was to be Jabba's slave girl, Jabba sent her to Fett to "entertain" the hunter.  Boba declined, but did not send Leia back up as it would be an insult to the crime lord. Instead, Fett and Leia argued about the Rebellion's wrongdoings and Boba's blindness to what Jabba has really done. Fett would stay up that night, pondering her, and wondering.

[[Image:180px-Slave1-09.jpg|thumb|right|Boba Fett's starship ''[[:starwars:Slave I/Legends|Slave I]]'', inherited from his father]]

Fett remained on-guard, but found his stay entertaining, flirting with dancers and even laughing along with Jabba as Luke battled the vicious rancor. When Luke killed the monster, an outraged Jabba sentenced Luke, Han and Chewbacca to be cast into the [[:starwars:Great Pit_of_Carkoon/Legends|Great Pit of Carkoon]] where the ghastly [[:starwars:Sarlacc/Legends|Sarlacc]] lived. Enroute to the Sarlacc, Boba tried to reason with the Hutt regarding the large reward on Skywalker's head for his capture to the Empire. Unfortunately, Jabba wouldn't have it &mdash; they killed his Rancor, they pay the price.  The Hutt's obstinance did nothing but boil the ire of the hunter. Luke staged his own rescue and Fett attempted to stop him along with several of Jabba's hapless guards. 

While he fired at Luke, in his obsession with killing the Jedi, he failed to notice Solo, who, still blind from hibernation sickness, accidentally activated Fett's jet pack, rocketing him against the sail barge and down to the sand, rolling right into the waiting maw of the Sarlacc. 

But the Sarlacc would never hold Fett.

===Late Galactic Civil War ([[4 ABY]] to [[14 ABY]])===
{{quote|The [[:w:c:starwars:Sarlacc|Sarlacc]] found me somewhat indigestible.|Boba Fett}}

[[Image:150px-Fettdarkempire.jpg|thumb|left|150px|Boba Fett surprises [[Han Solo]] after escaping the [[:starwars:Sarlacc/Legends|Sarlacc]]]]

Counted as dead, Fett survived the clutches of the foul beast and went on to continue his career. He managed to blast his way out after days of agony, barely alive. As he lay dying on the sands of [[Tatooine]], fellow hunter [[:starwars:Dengar/Legends|Dengar]] found him and nursed him back to health.

In return for this rescue, Fett allowed Dengar to accompany him on a labrynthine mission involving the [[:starwars:Bounty Hunters' Guild/Legends|Bounty Hunters Guild]] and Prince [[:starwars:Xizor/Legends|Xizor's]] ultra-secret [[Black Sun|Black Sun Syndicate]]. With so many potential leads, Boba eventually found that the head of the serpent was wealthy industrialist [[:starwars:Kuat (person)|Kuat of Kuat]]. For the mission, Fett stole the freighter ''[[:starwars:Hound's Tooth/Legends|Hound's Tooth]]'' from his rival [[:starwars:Bossk/Legends|Bossk]], leaving ''Slave I'' behind on Tatooine. The ship fell into [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Alliance]] hands and rusted in an impound lot on [[:starwars:Grakouine|Grakouine]]. He would subsequently use three more ships bearing the name during his career, until he eventually recovered the original.

The Galaxy thought Fett was still a Sarlacc snack, and Fett used this to his advantage. Utilising the less recognizable ''[[:starwars:Slave_II|Slave II]]'', Fett only took jobs from the most subtle and discreet clients. It became even worse when [[:starwars:Jodo_Kast/Legends|Jodo Kast]], a would-be bounty hunter who managed to acquire Mandalorian armor in an attempt to imitate Fett, made others believe that ''he'' was the infamous bounty hunter. Boba ambushed him using a hologram of a bounty Jodo was after and brutally finished him off with a rocket dart to his jetpack. 

In late [[11 ABY]], Boba was hired by [[Kendal Osbourne]] of the Galactic Empire to hunt down the infamous smuggler-turned-[[New Republic]] ace, [[Axel Vichten]]. Boba would hunt down Vichten across the galaxy, until their confrontation in space, Axel's ''[[Doppelganger]]'' versus Fett's ''[[:starwars:Slave I/Legends|Slave I]]''. Unfortunately for Fett, the smuggler proved too lucky and skillful, and bested the infamous bounty hunter, escaping to hyperspace soon after.

Boba was embarassed with his defeat, and seemingly disappeared from the galactic scene for several years, until his resurface in [[14 ABY]]. He was once again hired by the Empire, this time Lord [[Vassily Korolov]], to hunt down and apprehend the remaining Jedi Knights in the galaxy.

First [[Kitterick Brandis Finian|Brandis Finian]], a Jedi doctor, and later [[Ai'kani Za'netra]], Boba would fulfill his contract with suprising efficiency, much to the delight of the Empire.

== Physical Appearance ==
[[Image:145px-Swinfinities01-unmasked.jpg|thumb|145px|right|Boba Fett unmasked in ''[[:w:c:starwars:Star_Wars_Infinities|The Empire Strikes Back: Infinities]]]]''

As an adult, Boba Fett stood 1.83 meters tall. He was garbed in traditional [[Mandalorian]] armor with a string of [[Wookiee]] scalps at his shoulder.  Fett owned a variety of specially-modified starcraft bearing the names ''[[:starwars:Slave I/Legends|Slave I]]'' through ''[[:starwars:Slave IV/Legends|Slave IV/Legends]]''. 

Despite his weapon-laden suit of armor, Fett was a subtle hunter and relied more on his cunning and intelligence than on pure muscle and combat skill. Speaking with a raspy, gravelly voice (but still retaining his father's accent), Fett allowed few to see his face and even less to know his origins, giving the hunter a mysterious aura and a psychological edge over his prey. 

Though Boba was a perfect genetic clone of Jango Fett, a different lifestyle produced some changes from his father's appearance: while Jango was focused on combat skill, Boba's emphasis on wit over muscle gave him a thinner build. 

Even with the thinner build, Boba Fett was usually operating at peak human fitness.  He had enough strength to hold [[:starwars:Bossk/Legends|Bossk]] (an infamous [[Trandoshan]] bounty hunter whose species is known for its brute physical power) at bay.

Boba's face remained hidden, but it was a given that he looked more or less like his father did.  Beyond his actual features, Fett's face may have had less or more scars, tattoos, or facial hair. Even though Jango Fett's face was well-known, this only gives a very basic idea of what Boba Fett may have looked like.

== Equipment & Gear ==
[[Image:100px-Mandskull.jpg|left|thumb|100px|Boba Fett's shoulder emblem]]

=== Helmet ===
His helmet recorded video and played it back on command, dispensed water, picked up on minute sounds and amplified them and could connect with the onboard computer of ''Slave I'', assuming the transmission wasn't blocked (by being underground inside the [[:starwars:Sarlacc/Legends|Sarlacc]] for example.) Fett can control weapons, sensors, and his jet pack with verbal commands. The environmental filter system could filter out poisons and contaminants as well as provide Fett with a two hour reserve air tank. The helmet's broad band antenna was capable of intercepting and unscrambling comlink and starship comm transmissions. A macrobinocular viewplate with infrared scanner magnifies up to 50 times. The infrared scanner magnifies light up to 100 times or displays heat gradations. At the top of the antenna was a rangefinder capable of tracking up to 30 targets from a distance of 100 meters. The rangefinder feeds data to a display overlay inside the helmet showing range and movement for targets in 360 degree radius. The overlay also links to Fett's weapons systems to display fire vector and range data.

===Armor/Clothing===
[[Image:95px-Mandaloriancrest.jpg|right|thumb|95px|Boba Fett's chest emblem]]

His armor was constructed from durablast, allowing it to sustain a great deal of damage without degrading.  His armor also had two shoulder pads which were orange (like his kneepads). The left shoulder pad exhibited the Mandalorian skull logo, while his right chest plate displayed an emblem of unknown origin. 

Fett wore a power armor liner. This liner shirt had a micro energy field projector and two layers of thin ceramic plates, in order to disperses physical and blast impacts, reducing injuries and likelihood of knockdowns. The power liner also gave him increased protection from fire, acid, intense heat, and cold. His main clothing was a reinforced armor mesh battle suit/flight suit. The inner lining of the suit blocked poisons and corrosives for a period of time. This suit had pockets on his hips and thighs (one on each side).

Fett wore a leather utility belt that usually had ten leather pouches (but sometimes less). Below this he wore a [[:starwars:Journeyman_Protector/Legends|Journeyman Protector]] honor sash. Attached to his right shoulder were three braided wookiee scalps. The battle/flight suit had pockets on his shins which contained an anti-security blade, a survival knife, a jet-pack adjustment tool, and a sonic-beam weapon. Fett wore spats around his ankles to help prevent debris from going into his boots.

Fett wore a pair of versatile boots that had two spikes attached to the front of each.

===Rocket/Jetpack===
[[Image:150px-BFJetpack.jpg|left|thumb|150px|Boba Fett's rocket jetpack]]

Fett wore a Mitrinom [[:starwars:Z-6 jetpack/Legends|Z-6 Jetpack]]. The fuel tank held enough fuel for one minute of continuous operation (20 three-second blasts). Each three second blast moved Fett up to 100 meters horizontally or 7 meters vertically. Fett could reach a top speed of 145 kilometers per hour with a maximum range of two kilometers. Directional thrusters featured gyro-stabilizers to apply counterthrust for manuevering and landing.

The top of the jet pack had a turbo-projected magnetic grappling hook with 20-meter lanyard (replaceable with 100 meters). A winch inside the launcher could lift up to 100 klilograms. The grappling hook unit was replacable with an anti-vehicle homing missile.

===Weaponry===
[[Image:250px-EE-3.jpg|right|thumb|250px|Boba Fett's [[EE-3|EE-3 carbine rifle]]]]

Fett's main weapon was his [[EE-3|EE-3 Carbine rifle]], more powerful then a [[stormtrooper]]'s blaster. It was outfitted with a scope for sniping and a fast draw shoulder sling. A later version of the EE-3 had hand grips going lengthwise down the barrel.

Boba Fett used two different sidearms during his time as a bounty hunter. The first was a standard blaster pistol similar to those used by [[Bespin]]'s [[Cloud City]] security force. The other sidearm was a concussion grenade launcher that included a short shoulder stock for stability and accuracy when firing. Fett kept these smaller guns in a holster on his right hip though didn't equip both sidearms at the same time.

[[Image:200px-ESBFettblaster.jpg|left|thumb|200px||Boba Fett's blaster pistol]]

Boba Fett's wrist gauntlets were a mounting point for many of his weapons. His left gauntlet had a [[:starwars:Czerka Arms/Legends|Czerka]] [[:starwars:ZX miniature flame projector/Legends|ZX miniature flame projector]], which creates a cone of fire five meters long and one meter in diameter. Directly below this was the [[BlasTech]] [[:starwars:Dur-24 wrist laser/Legends|Dur-24 wrist laser]]. Comparable to a blaster rifle in power, these wrist lasers had a range of 50 meters. Originally exposed, a protective shield was later added to the right gauntlet enclosing and concealing both weapons. A corded fuel line connects to a backpack canister holding fuel for three minutes of operation. Fett's left gauntlet also had a [[:starwars:Kelvarex Consolidated Arms/Legends|Kelvarex Consolidated Arms]] [[:starwars:MM9 rocket system|MM9 mini concussion rocket]], which utilized computer target tracking. Fett used [[:starwars:Type-12A anti-personnel rocket/Legends|Type-12A anti-personnel rockets]], [[:starwars:Type-12B capsule|Type-12B stun rockets]], and [[Merr-Sonn]] 1126 rockets.

[[Image:250px-Concussionblaster.jpg|right|thumb|250px|Boba Fett's mini-concussion grenade launcher]]

Fett's right gauntlet had a [[:starwars:Fibercord whip|Fibercord Whip]] with a grappling device: a 20-meter-long fibercord whip used to entangle and immobilize a target.

Fett also had knee pads with rocket dart launchers. These small launchers used [[:starwars:Malkite themfar|Malkite Themfar]] or [[:starwars:Fex-M3|FEX-M3]], molecular acid, stun agents, or explosive tips.

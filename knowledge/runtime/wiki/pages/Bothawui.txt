'''Bothawui''' is a cosmopolitan planet, located in the center of [[Bothan Space]], serving as the homeworld of the [[Bothan]] race as well as a major hub for information trafficking. The [[Bothan Spynet]] is widely agreed-upon to be the best in the galaxy and much of the information it carries comes through Bothawui at some point. The [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] name for the planet is '''Bothawui Proper'''.  The planet is home to both industry and grain farms.  The planet is mined for indigenous ores such as [[:starwars:Lidium|lidium]].

The planet is an old member of the [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Old Republic]] - [[Bothese]] is one of the languages from which [[Galactic Basic Standard|Basic]] developed.  The Bothans colonized many nearby worlds and Bothawui became the capital of the [[Bothan Federation]].

[[Image:Bothawui.jpg|thumb|150px|left|Luke Skywalker and Dash Rendar on Bothawui.]]
The planet was represented in the [[:starwars:Galactic_Senate/Legends|Galactic Senate]] by [[:starwars:Polo_Se'lab|Polo Se'lab]].  It remained neutral during both the [[:starwars:Clone_Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]] and the [[Galactic Civil War]].  

The planet was a founding member of the [[New Republic]], although long represented only indirectly by [[Senator]] [[:starwars:Borsk_Fey'lya|Borsk Fey'lya]] of [[Kothlis]], a colony of Bothawui. 

==== Imperial Interlude ====

In [[14 ABY]], Bothawui and her colonies joined the Galactic Empire as a concession of the [[Dacan Pandemic]]. Imperial occupation over the system has been relatively light and suspiciously lax, without the normal Imperial aggressive behavior or garrison policies, thanks in part to [[Danik Kreldin|Danik Kreldin's]] insistence that the Bothans be well-treated.

==== Return to the Republic ====

Bothan Space was liberated by the New Republic in [[25 ABY]] in a series of battles collectively part of "Operation Freedom's Flame."

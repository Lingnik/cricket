'''Buwatcha Daaaaai'chi''' was a female [[Jawa]] who spent her life aboard a Sandcrawler in the desert of [[Tatooine]]. She was crafty, bright, and hardworking, allowing her to gain the reputation throughout her tribe as an excellent trader, haggler, and mechanic. She was able to obtain an old, rusty starfighter as part of a deal, and she left her tribe to expand her junk trade to the stars.

TITLE: C10
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2025-10-26T07:59:54Z
======================================================================
CDMC made the C10 to be the main general purpose infantry weapon of the Union Marines, and was developed with extensive input from line units - who were typically obsessed with stopping power, ruggedness, and marksmanship. The result is a brutal-looking 'long rifle' of unquestionable solidity. It has a fixed stock, a factory zeroed scope mount, and a plethora of attachment points for accessories. Vacuum-forged alloys and composites have been heavily used to keep the weight down while rendering the weapon survivable in a wide range of punishing environs - from cryogenic cold to deserts hot enough to melt lead. It has a healthy heft to it when held, and though little attention was paid to making it pretty, its ergonomics are admirable. Distinctively, the weapon is furnished in mattle slate green, of the typically Marine shade.

A combined force unit of the [[Caspian Defence Force]], comprising vessels of the [[Caspia]]n, [[Krittain|Krittish]], and [[Yariz|Yari]] navies. The '''First Fleet''' was commissioned in the second year of [[Caspian Crest|The Crest]], and its sponsor was Her Honorable Justice [[Miala Tranault]]. The fleet's nickname was the ''Crown Winds Fleet'', spending most of its time in and around the Caspian system proper.

At the close of the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign|Imperial War of Aggression]], the remnants of the First Fleet were repaired and dispersed into the [[CDU Home Fleet|Home Fleet]], [[CDU Rimward Fleet|Rimward Frontier Fleet]], and [[CDU Coreward Fleet|Coreward Frontier Fleet]].

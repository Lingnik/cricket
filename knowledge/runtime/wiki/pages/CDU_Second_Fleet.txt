A combined force unit of the [[Caspian Defence Force]], comprising vessels of the [[Caspia|Caspian]], [[Krittain|Krittish]], and [[Yariz|Yari]] navies. The '''Second Fleet''' was commissioned in the third year of [[Caspian Crest|The Crest]], and its sponsor was Dame Sina Adamov, wife of His Honorable Senator [[Leidan Adamov]]. The fleet's nickname was the ''Mighty River Fleet'', often dispatched on diplomatic escort and tours-de-force to nieghboring star systems.  

At the close of the [[Imperial War of Aggression]], Second Fleet became  the basis of the [[CDU Rimward Fleet|Rimward Frontier Fleet]].

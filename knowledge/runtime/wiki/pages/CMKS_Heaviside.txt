'''CMKS ''Heaviside''''' is a [[Lightning-class Sloop|''Lightning''-class Sloop]] in the [[Caspian Navy]], crewed entirely by [[Minor Krit|Minor Krits]].

The inertial dampeners and artificial gravity generators are routinely cranked to their highest setting &ndash; 178% &ndash; so as to keep the crew feeling "comfortable". Even at this oppressive level, a few crewmembers quip that they feel a little "lightheaded".


''Heaviside'' is named for a particular species of [[Heaviside|fish]] native to [[Krittain Major]] that is characterized by a squat, flat profile, and stays close to the sea floor. It possesses a quick temper, and is not known for being gregarious around other fish. It is not coincidental that Krittish officers on [[the Admiralty]] arranged for an all-Minor Krit crew aboard this sloop.

'''''CMS Audace''''' is the sixth construct of the [[Shearwater-class Frigate|Shearwater-Class Light Cruisers]], built at [[Victoria Drydocks]] near [[Caspar]], and launched in the third season of [[7 ABY]]. ''Audace'' is one of the most decorated ships in the [[Caspian Navy]] and was a key participant in the [[Battle of the Belt]] in [[8 ABY]]. She currently serves as part of '''Task Group Resplendent''' of the '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]'''.

== Early History ==

She is the slipway sister of ''[[CMS Kearsarge]]'', and once completed, the two cruisers immediately were put out on star trials together. After successful trials, they were both assigned to the '''[[CDU Third Fleet]]''', also known as the '''Iron Torch Fleet''', which was centered around the [[Hurricane-class Fleet Carrier|Hurricane-Class Fleet Carrier]] ''[[CMS Typhoon|CMS Typhoon]]''. They remained as mere escort ships for over a year as the cautious and conservative [[Caspian Crest|Crest]] kept the fleets very close at hand.

Days before the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign|Imperial War of Aggression]], ''Audace'' was transferred to the '''[[CDU Second Fleet]]''', also known as the Mighty River Fleet, operating at [[Yariz]] as part of a standard asset rotation plan. She was replaced by ''CMS Warder''. In retrospect, this transfer preserved ''Audace'', as ''Warder'' was utterly destroyed in the opening naval engagement. The Second Fleet was ordered to disperse and hide amongst the systems around Caspia, shadowing the Imperial battlefleet, but did not engage the occupying enemy until over 2 months had passed, and the Navy mounted a much more coordinated effort. ''Audace'' did participate in that, and scored several hits on Imperial vessels during the [[Battle of the Belt]], helping take down ''[[I2SD Intractable|HIMS Intractable]]'' in particular.

== Post War ==

After the [[Cessation of Occupation]] was signed, the warship was again folded into the main fleet, and saw her escort and protection duties increased as the [[Caspian Navy|Navy]] awaited the construction of several new ships to replace losses. At a few instances, she was dispatched to various points on the frontier, such as the "[[Perlemian Trade Route|Perlee]] Gate" and the region near [[Mikassa]], to conduct anti-raider and anti-espionage patrol. When the second batch of Shearwaters were finally completed, ''Audace'' went in for an overhaul to bring her up to '''Batch II''' standards.

She underwent her refit in [[Vanover Yards]] under Yari hands, and emerged four months later as a much cleaner and more modern ship. Her [[VOWL|VOWLs]], and corresponding management systems, had been upgraded, as had her turbolasers. Her power & sensor systems were upgraded from '''Lantern''' to '''Lantern II''' as well. Now much more capable (and tolerable to the crew), she went back on the line.

With a much more conservative [[Caspian Crest|Crest]] in place than ever before, ''Audace'', indeed the entire Navy, was heavily confined to CDU space. This would last until the [[Presav|presavadency]] of [[Salonika Batrad]], wherein the restrictions were opened up a bit more. The Shearwaters were dispatched in tentative "roving loops" around [[Caspian Sector|Caspian Space]] to patrol against incursions, although they found themselves frequently handcuffed by the corrupt leadership of a pair of notable Navy [[Admiral|Admirals]] and dealings in The Crest - whole shipments of smuggled goods would be allowed through on the purpose that they had been under the personal arrangement of Admiral So-and-So, or Senator Such-and-Such.

== New Assignments ==

Increasingly frustrated by this undermining, several [[Senator of the Union|Senators]] sponsored a means of projecting Naval presence out further, at surrounding systems and locations, most notably long the Perlemian Trade Route and on the very borders of [[:starwars:Hutt_Space|Hutt Space]]. ''Audace'' found herself dispatched to a location somewhere near [[Kashyyyk]], and did her best to endure the prolonged assignment. She was at Kashyyyk in [[14 ABY]] when the opening stanzas of the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]] began, ordered to stay there to stand watch against any incursions. Later, she was rotated back to Caspia while the rest of the fleet went to [[Mikassa]] to regain control of that star system.

While back in Caspia, [[The Admiralty]] took the opportunity to put her through a minor refit, upgrading a few components to the new '''Batch III''' standards and exchanging her obsolete [[L-188 Lakshadwep transport|L-188's]] for the new, and much more capable, [[L-202 Hellhawk transport|L-202 Hellhawks]]. Following the refit she was sent on extended patrols near [[:starwars:Taanab/Legends|Taanab]] and [[:starwars:Umbara/Legends|Umbara]].

''Audace'' was also notable as the ship [[Lynae Cassius]] was assigned to following her defection to the Caspian Democratic Union. She served as the ship's executive officer at the rank of [[Lieutenant Commander]]. She would be joined by her half-brother [[Sadim Gnik]] serving as the ship's second officer at the rank of [[Lieutenant]].

== Modern History ==

After his election as [[Presav]] of the [[Caspian Union]] and his appointment as [[Sar Admiral]] of the Caspian Navy, [[Crestian Tarasar]] undertook to reorganise the Navy. The majority of the navy would be structured around three defensive fleets, reviving the names used during the early history of the old Union. In addition to the defensive fleets, a '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet|fourth]]''' expeditionary fleet was organised under Tarasar's personal command.

In deciding what vessels would serve in this fleet, Tarasar favoured those with notable histories. ''Audace'' fit this bill perfectly and was assigned to '''Task Group Resplendent''' where she again serves with her sister ship, ''CMS Kearsarge'', and ''CMS Fulmar'' which she fought beside at the Battle of the Belt, in the takedown of ''Intractable''.

=== IC ===

[[File:Molo Hublin.jpg|thumb|Captain Molo Hublin]] ''Audace'' is pronounced ''aw-'''dáis''''', referring to a species of small marine avian found on [[:starwars:Talcene|Talcene]], a planet visited by the '''[[Barren Sector Fleet]]'''. The avian is known for darting amongst huge waves to snatch small fish. The current commanding officer of the ''Audace'', [[Captain]] Molo Hublin is a native of Talcene.

=== OOC ===

''Audace'' was one of the coded ships used by the [[Caspian Democratic Union|CDU]] with various player characters assigned to her. She was later replaced in this role by ''Ad Astra''.

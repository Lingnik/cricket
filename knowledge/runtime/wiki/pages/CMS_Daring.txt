'''CMS ''Daring''''' was a modified ''Dreadnaught''-type heavy cruiser which found its way into the [[Caspian Navy]] and would lend its name to an [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|entire class]] of transitory battlecruisers. These ''Daring''-class warships were employed as an effective stop-gap measure in the [[Caspian Democratic Union|Union]] arsenal until the climactic times of the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign]]. ''Daring'' had been chosen to be the first of these modified vessels because of its redoubtable service record and state of readiness.

''Daring'' itself saw extensive use during the days of the Old Republic, the Clone Wars, and then service within [[Dergan Roj]]'s [[Barren Sector Fleet]].

The '''''CMS Kasputin Yar''''' is nineteenth construct of the [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]], in the service of the [[Caspian Navy]]. She was built at [[Nenawirth Yards]] on [[Kasputin Yar]]. The ship is named for the [[Yari]] [[Kasputin Yar|homeworld]], a member world of the [[Caspian Union]], and the only world in the [[Yariz|Yariz System]] with a significant population.

The ''Kasputin Yar'' is currently under the command of [[Fleet Captain]] Sela Yarrow, one of the few Yaries to command a Caspian warship. It is assigned to '''Defence Group Yariz''' of the '''[[Caspian 1st Fleet]]''', and is usually found in orbit of Kasputin Yar itself.

'''''CMS Taciturn''''' is the sixth construct of the third batch of [[Shearwater-class Frigate|Shearwater-Class Light Cruisers]] in the service of the [[Caspian Navy]]. It was built at [[Victoria Drydocks]] near [[Caspar]] and launched in [[13 ABY]]. It was named for a [[Lancer-class Frigate|Lancer-Class Frigate]] which had been part of [[Admiral]] [[Dergan Roj|Dergan Roj's]] '''[[Barren Sector Fleet]]'''.

== Early Service ==

The ''Taciturn'' was initially assigned as a patrol vessel attached to the '''[[CDU Home Fleet]]''', but after the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]] the following year, it received a new mission. The [[Naval Intelligence Office]] had been petitioning [[the Admiralty]] for new ships for sometime, feeling the compliment they had to date were too small or not adequately equipped for effective intelligence gathering. The Admiralty finally acquiesced, ordering a half dozen cutters from [[Aston Aerospace]] to augment the few [[Lightning-class Sloop|Lightning-Class Sloops]] the NIO already possessed. 

In addition to the cutters, the Admiralty also handed over the ''Taciturn'' to the Naval Intelligence Office. Over the next few months, the ''Taciturn'' was retrofitted to serve as an intelligence vessel. From that point on its crew manifest and mission assignments were classified, though it as believed its passengers and crew was a mixture of traditional naval officers, intelligence agents, and government officials.

== Modern Service ==

During the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] most records related to the ''Taciturn'' were deleted from the NIO's database. In fact, ''Taciturn's'' location was unknown for over a year. The ship mysteriously reappeared at [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] shortly after [[Ixin]] rejoined the new [[Caspian Union]]. Though the ship reported for duty to the new government, little of its exploits to date ever made it back onto government servers.

In spite of the fact that the [[Caspian Intelligence Service]] wields more power in the new Union than the Naval Intelligence Office had in the old Union, the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]], and [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] in particular, disfavoured the notion of a warship dedicated solely to an intelligence service. The ''Taciturn'' would instead be assigned to the '''Task Force Resplendent''' of the '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]'''. In its capacity as an expeditionary fleet, however, the Caspian 4th Fleet often participates in intelligence service operations.

[[File:Dannl Tyson.jpg|thumb|Captain Dannl Tyson]] The ''Taciturn'' is currently under the command of [[Captain]] Dannl Tyson. Records indicate that Captain Tyson served on the staff of [[Sar Admiral]] [[Odie Hood]] when he was Director of the Naval Intelligence Office. Coincidentally Sar Admiral Hood is the Commanding Officer of Task Group Resplendent where the ''Taciturn'' is assigned.

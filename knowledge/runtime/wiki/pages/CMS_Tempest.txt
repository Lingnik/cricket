The '''''CMS Tempest''''' is the second construct of the [[Hurricane-class Fleet Carrier|Hurricane-Class Fleet Carrier]] in the [[Caspian Navy]], and served as a "roving ambassador" for several years. She was constructed at the [[Sloan Drydocks]] at [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] along with her sister ships ''CMS Hurricane'' and ''[[CMS Typhoon]]''. Unlike her sister ships, ''Tempest'' is shorter by ten meters, narrower by two, and has a different internal layout. Flight operations are also conducted slightly different, and assault and transport capabilities are bolstered.

The [[Second Zealot Wars]] were in full swing when ''Tempest'' was being completed, and due to some influential factors, the capacity to conduct operations as an assault carrier was brought to the forefront. However, the different arrangement in ''Tempest's'' design presented an issue for the [[the Admiralty]]. Some viewed her as a supplemental carrier, suited to accompanying a battlegroup to act as the group's assault hub. Others viewed her as the flagship of a task-specific assault group, leading several dozen assault & landing ships. Ultimately the flagship view prevailed and she served as the lead vessel of the '''[[CDU Second Fleet]]''' under the command of [[Rear Admiral]] Barret Tuvoy.

The ''Tempest'' would distinguish herself in the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign]]. During the [[Battle of the Belt]], her starfighters contributed to the destruction of ''[[I2SD Intractable|HIMS Intractable]]''. Following the [[Galactic Empire|Empire's]] withdrawal, the ship was refitted to solidify her role as a fleet carrier, while her class sister, ''[[CMS Turbulent]]'', became a more role-specific planetary-operations carrier. The refitted ''Tempest'' would come to serve as main carrier of the '''[[CDU Rimward Fleet|Rimward Frontier Fleet]]''' following a reorganisation of the fleets.

[[File:Caalbar.jpg|thumb|Commodore Caalbar]] After the election of [[Crestian Tarasar]] as [[Presav]] and appointment as [[Sar Admiral]], the fleets were reorganised again with ''Tempest'' being assigned to the '''[[Caspian 2nd Fleet]]''' based out of [[Kasputin Yar]]. When the [[Krittain|Krittain System]] rejoined the new [[Caspian Union]] the following year, the [[Krittish Navy]] merged with the 2nd Fleet. This saw the ''Tempest'' assigned as the carrier for '''Defence Group Krittain'''. She is command by [[Commodore]] Caalbar, the most senior [[Wookiee]] field officer in the [[Caspian Navy]].

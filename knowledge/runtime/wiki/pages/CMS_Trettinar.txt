The '''''CMS Trettinar''''' is the fourth construct of the [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]], in the service of the [[Caspian Navy]]. She was built at [[Nenawirth Yards]] on [[Kasputin Yar]]. ''Trettinar'' means ''Lake of Fire'', and is named for a [[Trettinar|planet]] in the [[Caspia|Caspian System]].

In light of the name, it was nicknamed the "Firebreather" by naval personnel, a name since applied to the squadron of [[Z-110 Hellion]] starfighters attached to the ''Trettinar''. Early in her history a spirited maintenance crew to took the liberty of painting a relatively small set of flames on the bow, this eventually led to similar branding on all of the craft to assigned to the ship.

== Task Group H ==

For many years it acted as the lead cruiser for '''Task Group H''', assigned to the [[Rautheran System]] along with the [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruisers]] ''Morselin'' and ''[[CMS Picon Yar|Picon Yar]]'', [[Shearwater-class Frigate|Shearwater-Class Light Cruisers]] ''Albatross'', ''Auklet'', and ''Aonach'', and six [[Lightning-class Sloop|Lightning-Class Sloops]]. The flag vessel for the Task Group was the [[Hurricane-class Fleet Carrier|Hurricane-Class Fleet Carrier]] ''Torrent''.

== Caspian 4th Fleet ==

After his election as [[Presav]] of the [[Caspian Union]] and his appointment as [[Sar Admiral]] of the Caspian Navy, [[Crestian Tarasar]] undertook to reorganise the Navy. The majority of the navy would be structured around three defensive fleets, reviving the names used during the early history of the old Union. In addition to the defensive fleets, a fourth expeditionary fleet was organised under Tarasar's personal command.

As the new fleets revived the nicknames that had been utilised for the three fleets operating in the early days of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] with Tarasar opted to give the '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]''' a nickname of its own in a similar style. The '''Dragon's Breath Fleet''' served as a play on his own title as the [[New Sith Order|Sith]] [[Duke]] of [[House Dragonflower|Dragonflower]], and control of the [[Dragonflower Nebula]], an indulgence he felt justified in having contributed warships of his own.

While most of the vessels assigned to this new fleet were chosen for their records in battle, ''Trettinar'' was chosen for its branding, after all how could the "Firebreather" be assigned anywhere else but the Dragon's Breath Fleet. When the ''Trettinar'' was next in dock for maintenance, Sar Admiral Tarasar in fact encouraged the maintenance crew repaint the flames on the bow as large as they wanted to.

[[File:Shakka Teksa.jpg|thumb|Commodore Shakka Teksa]] In [[34 ABY]], after the breach of [[The Oubliette|Spacestation 1138]], an Imperial black site prison in the Karthakk System nicknamed "The Oubliette," most of the 4th Fleet was deployed throughout [[Imperium]] and [[Independent]] space chasing down escapees. Elements of '''Task Group Resplendent''' under Sar Admiral [[Odie Hood]], in specific his command ship ''CMS Resplendent'' and ''Trettinar'' under [[Fleet Captain]] Shakka Teksa were involved in actions in the Durellian Expanse with Sar Admiral Hood coordinating the combined Imperium-Caspian siege. Fleet Captain Teksa would subsequently be promoted to [[Commodore]] in recognition for her service during the engagement.

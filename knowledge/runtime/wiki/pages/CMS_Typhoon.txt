The '''''CMS Typhoon''''' is the third construct of the [[Hurricane-class Fleet Carrier|Hurricane-Class Fleet Carrier]] in the [[Caspian Navy]], and easily the most decorated ship in service to the [[Caspian Union]]. She was constructed at the [[Sloan Drydocks]] at [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] along with her sister ships ''Hurricane'' and ''[[CMS Tempest|Tempest]]'', and like her sisters, is so named for the storms that seethe through [[Caspar]] with great regularity.

== Development ==

While ''Typhoon'' shares basic form and function with her sister ships, they differ considerably in layout and mission profile. By the time construction on ''Typhoon'' had begun, cooperation between the [[Caspia|Caspians]] and the [[Yari]] had progressed to the point that the ship was configured with the accommodation of Yari crew in mind. Indeed several notable systems – namely astrogation, sensors, logistical coordination, and small craft maintenance – were laid out and attuned especially to Yari anatomy.

''Typhoon'' is considered the epitome of a Caspian warship - tough, multipurpose, and dependable. She supports and projects over one hundred fighters and some three dozen lifter craft, shuttles, and transports. Her mission profile can be adapted from standard space superiority operations to planetary assault platform rather easily, and because of her flight-deck and related compartments, can transport a ground and aerospace force large enough to garrison most installations. She also has good combat durability and on-average speed and handling characteristics. Though all [[Hurricane-class Fleet Carrier|Hurricanes]] are considered multifunctional and resilient, ''Typhoon'' takes that to the next level.

== Early History.==

''Typhoon'' was built in [[6 ABY]] at [[Sloan Drydocks]], and was launched at the close of the year to much fanfare. She immediately underwent star trials near [[Tairoa]], a remote location within [[Caspian Sector|Union space]] where she could be put through her paces. Her aerospace wing arrived in early [[7 ABY]], and she was promptly deployed to [[Rauther]] to act as the flagship of patrol operations in that section of Union space. She was unable to travel during the [[Yavin Plague]], but after that, conducted several tours of [[New Republic|Republic]] and [[Independent]] systems throughout the region.

During the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign|Imperial War of Aggression]] in [[8 ABY]], ''Typhoon'' served as the flagship for the '''[[CDU Third Fleet]]''' and was under the command of [[Ernest Pallando]]. Pallando received his commanded at the age of nineteen, making him the youngest warship captain in Caspian naval history, a distinction he held through to the collapse of the [[Caspian Democratic Union|CDU]] two decades later. The ''Typhoon'' would serve with distinction at the [[Battle of the Inner Caspian Worlds]], the first battle of the war, and at the [[Battle of the Belt]], the last battle of the war.

Following the war, the fleets were reorganised and ''Typhoon'' became part of the '''[[CDU Home Fleet]]'''. For many years the ship was under the command of [[Admiral]] [[Camrath Kizuka]]. At the time of the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] and [[Mergansar|Mergani]] [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]], ''Typhoon'' and the CDU Home Fleet were under the command of [[Sar Admiral]] [[Prae Kichakressa]]. It would be Kichakressa and ''Typhoon'' which would lead the efforts to keep Caspar quarantined. 

== Mergansar Incident ==

The pandemic had prompted Caspar native and [[New Sith Order|Sith]] Admiral [[Crestian Tarasar]] to pull his ship, the ''[[Leviathan]]'' out of hiding and offer his help to his homeworld. While Kichakressa was focused on the quarantine, she suggested that Tarasar put his focus on dealing with the members of the [[Olumi Osahn]] cult who had used the pandemic as an excuse to try to drive all non-[[Sarian|Sarians]] of [[Mergansar]]. When efforts to bring the cultists to heel "diplomatically" failed, Tarasar attacked their strongholds instead.

Once the pandemic had been contained, ''Typhoon'' broke for Mergansar along with ''CMS Rapace'', ''[[CMS Prophet]]'', and ''CMS Ad Astra''. Kichakressa ordered the ''Leviathan'' to cease operations and rendezvous with her force, as Tarasar considered his efforts to have the tacit approval of the Sar Admiral, he complied with the initial order. Once the Caspian warships deployed a fighter 'cap', it was clear to Tarasar that Kichakressa had turned on him, so he responded by jamming the Caspian's sensors and refusing to lower shields. He tried to diffuse the situation diplomatically, but when [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]], who was secretly on board, demanded the Caspian's surrender instead, [[Mergansar Incident|combat]] became inevitable and ''Leviathan'' was forced to scramble its own fighters. 

Tarasar's reliance on attempting diplomacy was nearly his undoing as he allowed the Caspian's to fire on the ''Leviathan'' first, causing considerable damage. Complete disaster was averted, however, by the presence of Darth Malign. Strike teams led by Malign's mutant servant [[Sentinel]] bombed key locations on the ''Typhoon'', including the bridge, killing Sar Admiral Kichakressa. Meanwhile the ''Rapace'' and ''Prophet'' mysteriously crashed into one another, crippling both ships. The ''Ad Astra'' suffered heavy damage to her [[HOWL]] launchers but little damage elsewhere. With the Caspian force crippled ''Leviathan'' was able to flee back to [[Silver Station]].

== Aftermath ==

Though the Caspian Navy ordered a forensic investigation into how the ''Leviathan'' was able to inflicted so much damage in spite of how overmatched they were, sabotage by allies with [[SubPro]] helped ensure the Caspian Navy was never able to really figure out what happened. The exact details remain one of the few secrets that Tarasar continues to keep from almost anyone who was not already aware. Repairs on the ''Typhoon'' would take several months, with the bridge having to be completely rebuilt. By the time it was ready to return to service, the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] had ceased to exist.

== Caspian 4th Fleet ==

Following Tarasar's election as [[Presav]] and appointment as Sar Admiral, ''Typhoon'' was assigned to '''Task Group Hood''' of the '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]'''. The reason for this assignment was twofold. First, as the most decorated vessel in Caspian naval history, it possessed the characteristics that Tarasar was looking for in vessels for the expeditionary fleet. Second, Tarasar consider it important for the vessels he had this misfortune of having to fight before at Mergansar to serve together in the same fleet with the Leviathan, as a symbol of reconciliation. 

[[File:Terina Manoff.jpg|thumb|Commodore Terina Manoff]] Under the command of [[Commodore]] Terina Manoff, the ''Typhoon'' has served with the 4th Fleet in all its large scale operations of the past several years including the [[Report:Mergani Subjugation Posts|blockade]] of Mergansar in [[29 ABY]], the search patrol of [[Krittain|Krittish]] space following the [[Report:A Minor Consideration|attack]] on the ''Blechley Brook'' in [[30 ABY]], and the efforts to chase down escaped prisoners from [[The Oubliette|Spacestation 1138]] in [[34 ABY]].

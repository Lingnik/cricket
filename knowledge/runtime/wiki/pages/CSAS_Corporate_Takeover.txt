'''CSAS ''Corporate Takeover''''' is an [[Imperator-class Star Destroyer|''Imperator''-class Star Destroyer]] in the service of the [[Corporate Sector Authority]].


== Current Assignment==
The ''Corporate Takeover'' was purchased from the [[Galactic Empire]], and has since served as the flagship of the [[Security Division (CSA)|Corporate Sector Security Division]]. This position had left it stationed almost permanently over [[Etti IV]], and under the nominal command of [[Viceprex (CSA)|Viceprex]] [[Zidro Cyrik]]. He was often absent, and had left the vessel under the command of a command staff, that while senior, was somewhat lax.

This had changed quickly, with the assignment of a new CO, [[Colonel]] [[Gren Delede]], and with the assignment as flagship of the new [[Corporate Sector Strike Fleet]], the offensive arm of the CSA Security Division. The command staff was sacked wholesale, and replaced with members of Delede's staff, and crew from other postings. This included the re-assignment of two [[Z-100 Centurion]] squadrons from [[CSAS Litigator|CSAS&nbsp;''Litigator'']].

==Starfighter Squadrons==
The ''Corporate Takeover'' serves as home to numerous battle-hardened starfighter squadrons, including the CSSD's elite Menace Squadron, which flies upgraded versions of the already advanced [[Z-95A Headhunter II]].

These two potent squadrons are joined by veterans of the [[Second Battle of Nar Shaddaa]], [[Z-100 Centurion]] -equipped Balance Squadron, and a Condemnation Squadron, which is made up of [[Skipray_Blastboat|Skipray Blastboats]].

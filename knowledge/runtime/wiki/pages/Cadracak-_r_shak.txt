'''cadracak-'r'shak''' ([[1 BBY]] - ) is a [[Xi Char]] leader. He is [[Dean]] of the [[Cathedral Factory]] of Xi Xin on [[Charros IV]] and Xi Xin's representative on the [[Union Council]]. Unlike other senior clerics of their faith, cadracak is not involved in the defence industry. He is considered to be something of a pacifist personally, though he is not known to have ever advocated against the Xi Char's production of war materiels. Politically he a member of [[Holicet]], the most peace oriented of the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] political parties.

In spite of his feels on armed conflict cadracak was an active participant in the promotion of culture during the [[Charros Wargames]] in [[32 ABY]]. His most notable contribution in this regard was the creation of an [[Prelate r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak Memorial Art Gallery|art gallery]] in Xi Xin which brought examples of off-world art to the Xi Charrian people. Following the passing of [[Prelate]] [[r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak]] in [[34 ABY]], the gallery was renamed in his honour. cadracak was one of the clerics considered as a possible, though unlikely, successor as Prelate. Ultimately the [[conclave]] named Inquisitor [[stahanak]] as the new Prelate.

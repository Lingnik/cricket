'''Candied Serrith Bacon''' is a Caspian food dish made from the porcine serrith native to [[Mergansar]] and raised on worlds throughout the [[Caspian Union|Union]]. The thick cut of bacon is cooked in syrup made from the sap of pula trees native to [[Caspar]]. It is popular as an hors d'oeuvre.

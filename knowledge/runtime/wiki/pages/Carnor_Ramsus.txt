'''Carnor Ramsus''' was a [[New Republic]] commando and former [[:starwars:Alliance_Special_Forces|SpecForce]] squad leader. Tough yet soft-spoken, Ramsus was highly regarded amongst his peers and superiors. He specialized in various forms of ground combat, hand-to-hand specialties, and in starfighter combat.

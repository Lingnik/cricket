'''Cas Mos''' ([[16 BBY]] - ) is a [[Human]] politician and soldier originally from [[Plaxton]] on [[Caspar]], she represents the city of [[Groton]] in the [[Union Council]]. She is the Leader of the [[Legacy]] Party and serves as the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] Vice Presav and Minister of Domestic Affairs and Defence. A [[Union Marines|Marine]] [[General]] prior to her election to the Union Council, she holds the rank of [[Marshal]] as an extension of her roles in government.

== Early Life ==

Cas Mos was born in the city of [[Plaxton]] on [[Caspar]] three years into the reign of the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]]. She was a spirited child and growing up on a world that was rather backwater as far as the Empire was concerned her early years were rather free of deeper considerations of the wider galaxy. The rugged landscape of her world was large enough to contain her spirit, for a time.

When word reached Caspar about the destruction of the [[:starwars:DS-1 Orbital Battle Station|Death Star]] at the [[:starwars:Battle of Yavin/Legends|Battle of Yavin]], her homeworld suddenly seemed like a small and unimportant place to Cas. Like many others, Cas found sudden interest in the [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Rebellion]] and it was all her parents could do to keep her from leaving and finish her education. Her parents were able to prevail for a time and she did finish her education. By the time of the shortly after the [[:starwars:Battle of Hoth/Legends|Battle of Hoth]] though Cas was an adult, tired of being on the sidelines. She left Caspar shortly thereafter to her way into the [[Galactic Civil War|war]].

Cas would find her way into [[:starwars:Alliance Special Forces/Legends|Alliance Special Forces]] serving in [[:starwars:Storm Squadron  (Rebel)|Storm Squadron]] of [[:starwars:Eclipse Team|Eclipse Team]]. After the alliance transitioned into the [[New Republic]] following the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]], Cas served for another two years in the [[New Republic Marine Corps]]. Caspar, however, had never joined the New Republic and Cas decided her services were more needed at home with the [[Caspian Democratic Union]].

== Union Marine ==

As an experience fighter, Cas was eagerly accepted into the [[Union Marines]] as a [[Captain]] in [[6 ABY]] serving as an instructor at the Groton Military Academy. Though she did not personally fight in the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign]] of [[8 ABY]], soldiers she helped to train proved critical during the [[Siege of Trinumvira]] and in the aftermath she was promoted to [[Major]] as the Union attempted to recover its military strength following the conflict. She continued to succeed well her role as and was promoted again to [[Lieutenant Colonel]] in [[10 ABY]].

Following the [[Mikassa Incident]] in [[14 ABY]], the Union Marines saw an increase in enlistment prompting an expansion of the training facilities at Camp Virta on [[Sahkjalin]]. Promoted now to [[Colonel]], Cas was given command of Camp Virta, now reaching new marines at the very start of their training.

Continuing her rapid rise in the ranks Cas was promoted to [[Brigadier General]] in [[16 ABY]] and given command of Antrim Base on [[Krittain Minor]], another major training facility. This was Cas' first long term posting outside the [[Caspia|Caspia System]] since joining the Union Marines, and she would stay there several years.

Following the admission of [[Ixin]] into the CDU in [[21 ABY]], Cas was promoted to [[Lieutenant General]] and assigned as deputy commander of Udalooy Base as Ix forces were integrated into the the overall Union command structure. In this role she worked closely with many leading Princes with her position proving as much a political posting as a military one, effectively serving as representative of the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]].

In the aftermath of the resignations that followed the [[2015 Caspar Plot|conspiracy]] to secretly supply the New Republic with arms and the subsequent [[CDU Nonaggression Treaty|treaty]] the Union was forced to sign in [[24 ABY]], Cas was promoted to [[General]] and given command of Camp Berglund and all marine forces on [[Rauther]].

== Return to Caspar ==

Following the Sariphage pandemic in [[28 ABY]] Cas commanded the first out of system units to return to Caspar following the cure. Setting up command in Groton she was instrumental in establishing some semblance of order in the rampant chaos. She did her best, however, to keep the function of the marines limited to maintaining order, wishing to see civilian rulers doing wider decision making for the world.

When the dust settled on the collapse of CDU in the wake of Caspar's decimation and the various other worlds of the Union asserted independence, Cas was offered command of the [[Rauther|Rautheran]] military. Though tempted by the offer, and additional lucrative officers on Ixin, Cas felt that Caspar needed her more. 

The Union Marines were even harder hit than the general populace on Caspar. Unlike the [[Caspian Navy]] which had comparatively few personnel planetside, much of the marine forces were stationed on Caspar leaving their numbers depleted and surviving flag officers a rarity. Many of those surviving on other Union worlds chose to stay in the forces there, including some native Caspians who felt they had nothing to go back to there.

Though she intended to remain in uniform, when elections for a permanent [[Union Council]] were announced, many of the survivors and refugees in Groton encouraged Cas to run to represent them, thankful for the steadying hand her presence had been. While initially loathed to consider the idea, having seen the chaos of the old Union's collapse she could not deny that her leadership skills could be valuable to the new government.

Joining with those candidates who aimed to keep the new [[Caspian Union|Caspar Union]] as much like the old CDU as possible, she became a member of the [[Legacy]] party. Between her high profile role in the aftermath of the pandemic, her innate leadership skills, and the fact her constituency was one of the few their party was seen as likely to win, Cas was chosen by her peers as the party leader, a role she begrudgingly accepted. Along with Stonewin Kirkmount of [[Dunwelton]] they were the only members of the party elected to the Union Council, with [[Crestian Tarasar]] and his party [[Praecet]] winning the other seven seats.

== Vice Presav ==

Prior to the swearing in of the new government, Cas and Stonewin were visited by Crestian, now the presumptive nominee for [[Presav]] and Praecet's deputy leader [[Zaalbacca]]. In spite of the fact that Praecet held an overwhelming majority of the seats, Crestian asked Cas to join him in forming a coalition government and noted his wish to nominate her as Vice Presav rather than Zaalbacca, otherwise seen as the likely candidate.

Crestian outlined to them his security concerns in greater depth than he had on the campaign trail and his plans to ally with the new [[Imperium]], remilitarise, and reunited with the other former Union worlds. He viewed Cas as a vital partner in these plans and also indicated a willingness to allow her to oversee much of the Union's domestic agenda as it was a lesser priority to him compared to focus on diplomacy and defence.

Though not eager to see the Union ally with the Imperium, she did understand Crestian's reasoning for doing so. Furthermore, realising that her party was not in a position to stop it, she could do more to promote her party's agenda as part of the proposed coalition. Agreeing to the proposal, both Cas and Stonewin supported Crestian's nomination as Presav which approved unanimously by the new Union Council. Cas was likewise unanimously elected as Vice Presav, with Crestian personally making the nomination.

In addition to their roles as Presav and Vice Presav, Crestian took on the position of Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defence and the rank and position of [[Sar Admiral]], while Cas was made Minister of Domestic Affairs and Defence and the rank of [[Marshal]] and overall command of the Union Marines. This made the two true partners in rebuilding the [[Caspian Defence Force]] and administering the government as a whole.

While Crestian led the Union's negotiations to bring [[Kasputin Yar]] back into the Union, Cas and Councillor Erl Morgaan of Plaxton led similar efforts with Rauther, bringing both worlds back within their first two weeks in office. As worlds rejoined additional political parties [[Holicet]] and [[Lijsttrekker]] gained representation in the Union Council and the Legacy party saw a small increase in their percentage of seats. Praecet meanwhile slowly lost its majority, though it has maintained a plurality of seats. 

Through it all Cas continued to support the coalition model of governance and the invitation to Holicet and Lijsttrekker to join it. The all-party coalition has continued to operated with little acrimony in the years since, in large part because of the strong working relationship and respect between Cas and Crestian.

As Vice Presav and Minsiter of Domestic Affairs and Defence, Cas keeps a lower public profile than most of the senior ministers. Nevertheless, she is known for diligent work behind the scenes. With Crestian frequently off world on diplomatic missions, Cas is often the one presiding over functions back at [[Union Hall]].

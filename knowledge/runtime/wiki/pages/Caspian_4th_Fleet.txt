The '''Caspian 4th Fleet''' or '''Dragon's Breath Fleet''' represents the expeditionary forces of the [[Caspian Navy]]. Serving under the direct command of [[Sar Admiral]] [[Crestian Tarasar]], the ships assigned to it are authorised to be deployed outside [[Caspian Sector|Caspian Space]] without special approval.

==Overview==

After his election as [[Presav]] of the [[Caspian Union]] and his appointment as [[Sar Admiral]] of the Caspian Navy, [[Crestian Tarasar]] undertook to reorganise the Navy into four fleets. Three of these fleets would be defensive in nature and be based around the ethos of the three fleets that existed in the early years of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], while a smaller fourth fleet would be expeditionary in nature.

While it is only a third of the size other fleets, boasting roughly one tenth of the Navy's total fleet strength, the 4th Fleet is augmented by additional outside vessels. Tarasar's personal starship, the [[Interdictor-class Immobilizer 418|Interdictor-Class Cruiser]], the ''[[Leviathan]]'', and her sister ship, the ''Dying Sun'' serve as part of the fleet. In addition as part of the mutual defence pact with the [[Imperium]] two [[Warlord-class Medium Cruiser|Warlord-Class Cruisers]], the ''[[Starkiller]]'' and ''[[Tethys]]'', have been placed under Tarasar's command. These additional starships represent almost one sixth of the fleet's strength.

The starships assigned to '''4th Fleet''' are often the most decorated vessels in the Caspian Navy with their assignment into the fleet considered a mark of honour upon the vessel. The fact that this fleet might be called upon to assist in Imperium operations has made it a less popular assignment among officers critical of the defence pact. Transfers out are granted whenever possible, leaving the crew on these vessels more supportive of Caspian involvement with the Imperium compared to the other fleets.

Based on its expeditionary nature it is far more likely at any given time that the fleet is operating as a whole in one location or that individual vessels may be operating at separate locations. For organisational purposes the fleet is subdivided into three Task Groups named after the three largest vessels within the fleet, two [[Admiral Roj-class Battleship|Admiral Roj-Class Battleships]] and a [[Hood-class Battlecruiser|Hood-Class Battlecruiser]].

In addition to these ships, the fleet contains a [[Hurricane-class Fleet Carrier|Hurricane-Class Fleet Carrier]], a [[Valorous-class Light Carrier|Valorous-Class Light Carrier]], a [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruiser]], five [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruisers]], ten [[Shearwater-class Frigate|Shearwater-Class Light Cruisers]], the above named Interdictors and Warlords, and various auxiliary vessels, with these ships distributed somewhat evenly among the Task Groups.

==Operations==

From its initial formation following Crestian Tarasar's appointment as Sar Admiral, the 4th Fleet was largely engaged in leading the Caspian blockade of [[Mergansar]] with support of the '''[[Caspian 1st Fleet|1st Fleet]]'''. As Mergansar slowly returned to Caspian control, 4th Fleet vessels were increasing freed  up to engage in scouting and anti-slavery patrol near [[Charros IV]] in preparation for its eventual membership in the Union.

===Slaver Attacks===

''Leviathan'' was present at [[Bespin]] during the attack by slavers on [[Cloud City]]. Following the departure the ''Sword of the Jedi'' under [[Fleet Admiral]] Domicor Honos to [[Selene]] with the wounded [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]], ''Leviathan'' under the temporary command of [[Captain]] [[Drorq]] of the ''[[Dying Sun]]'' assumed command of the planetary blockade until reinforced by further elements of the 4th Fleet led [[Vice Admiral]] [[Kath Samba]] and the ''[[CMS Renaissance of Innovation]]''. Aid from [[The Mission]] and a [[SubPro]] team led by Admiral [[Sirlahn Inglet]] would come with the ''Renaissance''.

After the attack on the [[Krittain|Krittish]] transport ''Blechley Brook'' by fleeing slavers as they passed through Krittish space, the bulk of the 4th Fleet under Admiral Liam Biker and the ''[[CMS Hood]]'' would soon join in the unsuccessful search for the slavers and subsequent patrolling of Krittish space.

===Charros Wargames===
In [[32 ABY]], the majority of the 4th Fleet participated or was at least present at the [[Charros Wargames]]. The ''CMS Renaissance of Innovation'' served as the official flagship for the occasion. This both highlighted the theme of the [[Second Renaissance of Innovation]] declared by Presav Tarasar as well representing Caspian innovation better than his own personal flagship, the ''Leviathan.'' ''[[CMS Daring]]'' under [[Rear Admiral]] Demetria serving a central role during a [[RPlog:Simulated Evacuation|simulated evacuation]].

===Operation Requiem===

In [[33 ABY]], elements of the 4th Fleet participated in the [[Caspian Intelligence Service]] mission code named "Operation Requiem." The ships involve operated in the [[Faylar|Faylar System]] as a fictional Imperium "'''Task Force Faylar'''." Each ship involved had its transponder changed to register as an Imperium vessel, with the ships themselves being the fleets four Imperial made warships. The ''Leviathan'' operated as the the ''"Spire of Victory,"'' the ''Dying Sun'' as the ''"Oubliette,"'' the ''Tethys'' as the ''"Reborn Order,"'' and the ''"Starkiller,"'' as the ''"Black Talon."'' [[Fleet Captain]] Drorq served as commanding officer for the task force, while [[Captain]] [[Atsvara Tarasar]] served in place of her father as acting commanding officer of the ''"Spire of Victory."''

===Spacestation 1138 Prison Break===

In [[34 ABY]], after the breach of [[The Oubliette|Spacestation 1138]], an Imperial black site prison in the [[:starwars:Karthakk system|Karthakk System]] nicknamed "The Oubliette," most of the 4th Fleet was deployed throughout Imperium and Independent space chasing down escapees. ''[[CMS Mergansar]],'' under the command of [[Commodore]] [[Sha Dark]], took the lead in going to the Karthakk System itself and she coordinated the fleet's movements in the field in her capacity as Minister of Justice, while Sar Admiral Tarasar retained overall command.

Elements of '''Task Group Resplendent''' under Sar Admiral [[Odie Hood]], in specific his command ship ''[[CMS Resplendent]]'' and ''[[CMS Trettinar]]'' under Fleet Captain Shakka Teksa were involved in actions in the Durellian Expanse with Sar Admiral Hood coordinating the combined Imperium-Caspian siege owing to his seniority and experience. Fleet Captain Drorq and the ''Dying Sun'' were credited for their role in the Imperium's operation in the Nemares Corridor with Drorq taking the initiative to interdict subspace traffic which proved critical to the operation's success. Sha Dark, Shakka Teksa, and Drorq were all promoted for their key roles in the campaign.

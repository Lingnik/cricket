The '''Caspian Defence Force''' is the collective term for the armed forces of the [[Caspian Union]]. At its most basic it refers to the '''[[Caspian Navy]]''', '''[[Caspian Starfighter Corps]]''', and '''[[Union Marines]]'''. The Caspian Defence Force can also be seen to encompass the local military units on certain Union worlds and special forces units outside of the normal chain of command of the three main branches, such as the [[Caspian Rangers]] and [[The Chevalier]]. In certain contexts the [[Caspian Intelligence Service]] and [[Constable|Union Constabulary]] may be included as well.

== History ==

Whether and how the term can be applied to the armed forces in the old [[Caspian Democratic Union]] is a matter of some debate. Certainly in the early years of the CDU the term was used without controversy. At some point, however, there was pushback to this particular terminology. This was aided in part by the fact that the armed forces of the CDU did not exist within one neatly confined organisational structure. The term found particular disfavour among more dovish members of [[Caspian Crest|The Crest]] who disliked the notion of the CDU as a martial power. It found further critics on the member worlds outside the [[Caspia|Caspia System]] who felt the "Caspian" label minimised their contributions and role within the Union, particularly on [[Rauther]].

Following the collapse of the Caspian Democratic Union, the rump [[Caspian Union|Caspar Union]] which remained consisted primarily of [[Caspar]] itself. Though [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] and [[Mikassa]] remained under its jurisdiction, there was little reason for opposition to the "Caspian" label to apply to the armed forces; though what forces were actually controlled by and loyal to the provisional government was itself an open question. Even as this Caspar Union gave way to a rebranded [[Caspian Union]] which soon reunited with [[Kasputin Yar]] and [[Rauther]], with [[Ixin]] not far behind, there was no talk of dropping the "Caspian" label from the Defence Force.

Though tensions between Caspar and other member worlds crop up at times, the ability of Caspari politicians to reforge a Union in spite of the decimation of their own population during the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] only served to confirm its political ascendancy. The dispersal of 100 million [[Mergansar|Mergani]] refugees throughout other Union worlds following the [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]] there also reinforced a cultural connexion to the Caspia System among all members worlds. Whatever debate remains as to how to define this Pan-Caspian culture, its existence has become undeniable. The current Union is more united than ever.

== Structure ==

The Caspian Defence Force is divided into three main branches, '''[[Caspian Navy]]''', '''[[Caspian Starfighter Corps]]''', and '''[[Union Marines]]'''. The Caspian Navy is divided into four fleets. The '''[[Caspian 1st Fleet]]''' is charged with the defence of the central portion of Caspian space, primarily protecting the [[Caspia]], [[Rautheran System|Rautheran]], and [[Yariz]] systems. The '''[[Caspian 2nd Fleet]]''' is charged with the defence of the 'northern' portion of Caspian space, primarily protecting the [[Krittain]] system and the installations on [[Ketterslea]] and [[Tairoa]]. The '''[[Caspian 3rd Fleet]]''' is charged with the defence of the 'southern' portion of Caspian space, primarily protecting the worlds of [[Charros IV]], [[Ixin]], and [[Mikassa]]. The '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]''' operates under a very different mandate as an expeditionary fleet.

The Caspian Starfighter Corps is the branch tasked with operation all starfighters in service to the Union, whether they are based in on a planet, space station, or warship. It was formed by the administration of [[Crestian Tarasar]] after his election as [[Presav]], by merging the existing [[Marine Aerospace Division]] and [[Naval Aerospace Arm]] into a unified branch separate from the [[Caspian Navy]] and [[Union Marines]].

While the Union Marines had technically existed as a branch of the Caspian Navy in the CDU days they are a fully separate service branch in current times. The mandate of the Union Marines has also expanded to include all Caspian ground forces including the former [[Provincial Armies of the Union]]. Some units such as the [[Caspian Rangers]] and [[The Chevalier]] exist outside of the mainline command structure of the Marines, but are ultimately to considered part of that branch and generally follow the same policies.

While the [[Caspian Intelligence Service]] and [[Constable|Union Constabulary]] are not properly part of Caspian Defence Force they are sometimes grouped alongside it. All parties have standard operating procedures and policies for joint operations. Additionally laws and regulations that pertain to service personal apply equally to members of the intelligence service and constabulary, who equal to the defence force in terms of rights and responsibilities.

== Oversight ==

Oversight over the Caspian Defence Force falls to two intertwined bodies, the Defence Directorate and [[The Admiralty]]. General and broader oversight is accomplished by the Defence Directorate, a twelve member committee of the Union Council, comprised of Councillors serving as ministers of departments relevant to Union Defence. It is co-chaired by the Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defence and the Minister of Domestic Affairs and Defence. These positions are currently held [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] and Vice Presav [[Cas Mos]] respectively. Day to day leadership of the Defence Force is done by the relevant ministers and The Admiralty. Meetings of the full Defence Directorate occur at least monthly so the members may provide additional oversight and be briefed on current status and operations.

While the Defence Directorate provides the broader oversight, the Admiralty serves as the body providing direct leadership to the Caspian Defence Force. The office of [[Sar Admiral]] serves as the role of Commander-in-Chief of the Caspian Defence Force and also serves as Chair of the Admiralty. The commanding officers of each fleet and the Defence Groups, Patrol Groups, and Task Groups within those fleets serve on the Admiralty Board, as do the Bureau Chiefs of the various particular departments of the Defence Force.

The Caspian Starfighter Corps and the Union Marines are also represented within the Admiralty. The Starfighter Corps is principally represent on the Admiralty Board by the [[Marshal|Star Marshal]] who serves as Commandant of the Corps and the [[Vice Marshal|Vice Marshals]] attached to each fleet. The Union Marines are principally represented the [[Marshal|Field Marshal]] who serves as Commandant of the Corps and the [[General|Generals]] commanding ground forces on each of the various Union member worlds. 

The Admiralty Board also includes the Chief Executive Officers of the various design and manufacturing firms in the defence industry with the Union including: [[Aston Aerospace|Aston Aerospace Company]], [[CDMC|Caspian Manfuacturing and Design Corporand]] [[Haor Chall Engineering]], Hixam Sidney Design, [[Interspatial Prospecting, Inc.|Interspatial Prospecting Incorporated]] [[Lokkai Starworks]], [[StarShield|Starshield Armaments]], and [[SubPro|Sublight Products Corporation]]. These CEOs and many of their other executives hold commissions in the Caspian Defence Force.

Meetings of the full of the full the Admiralty Board typically occur monthly, however much of the attendance is done remotely as the officers involved are typically spread out within [[Caspian Sector|Union Space]] or even beyond it. It is also not uncommon for a member of the board to send a more junior flag officer, or even senior field officer as their proxy to such meetings. Subcommittees of the board may meet more or less frequently.

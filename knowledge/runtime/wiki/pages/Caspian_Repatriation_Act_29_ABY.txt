TITLE: Caspian Repatriation Act (29 ABY)
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2023-08-18T06:45:04Z
======================================================================
'''Article I''': The [[Caspian Union|Caspar Union]] which consisted of the planet [[Caspar]], the [[Kichnar Orbital Station]], and the planet [[Mikassa]] admits the planets [[Kasputin Yar]] and [[Rauther]] into its ranks and shall hence force be rechristened the Caspian Union.

'''Article II''': In addition to the nine currently seated Councillors, the [[Union Council]] will hence forth include Councillors from Lurlansk, Nenawirth, Porthumberlon, and Vanover on Kasputin Yar and Breslau, Humberling, and Luderwaark on Rauther. Those Councillors shall be immediately seated following the passage of this act.

'''Article III''': As the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] ceased to exist in fact upon the advent of the Sariphage on Caspar and in law subsequent to the end of quarantine, the Caspian Union is under no obligation to honour any agreement made by that previous government.

'''Article IV''': As the CDU does not exist and the government of [[Aleister Vadim]] is no longer the legitimate [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] state, the [[CDU Nonaggression Treaty]] is hereby rescinded.

'''Article V''': The Caspian Union has entered into a Memorandum of Understanding with the [[Imperium]] in regards to a new Protection Pact which both parties agree to in principle.

'''Article VI''': The Caspian Union resumes full administrative, economic, legal, and security control of Kichnar Orbital Station. The Union can not recognise outside control of the station or our defence industry regardless of whether it takes the form private corporation, state corporation, or legal trust.

'''Article VII''': Trade within current and former Caspian space is no longer restricted to Kichnar as a port of first entry and may resume directly with the current and former constituent worlds.

'''Article VIII''': The facilities and operations erstwhile existing under the organisations: [[CDMC|Caspian Design & Manufacturing Corporand]], [[Interspatial Prospecting, Inc.]], and [[SubPro|SubPro Corporation]] are henceforth nationalised as a matter of union defence. All other business and property on Kichnar Orbital Station remains in the hands of its previous owners or if abandoned may be given away, leased, or sold by station administration at its discretion. It is also under the discretion of station administration whether to consider property operated under foreign legal trust as abandoned or not.

'''Article IX''': The Caspian Union reaffirms its recognition of the Krit Union as the legitimate government of [[Krittain Major]] and [[Krittain Minor]]. The Caspian Union furthermore affirms recognition of all former CDU vessels with the CMKS prefix as legitimately under the authority of the Krit Union and renounces any claim to the vessels absent a mutually agreed reunification. The Krit Union is granted free docking and at cost repairs at Kichnar Orbital Station for its navy for the period of ten standard years.

'''Article X''': The Caspian Union reaffirms its recognition of the Republic of Ixin as the legitimate government of the planet [[Ixin]]. The Caspian Union furthermore affirms recognition of CMS Tonare, CMS Aph'relin, CMS Mollymawk, CMS Irrawaddy, and CMS Mayhem Valley as legitimately under the authority of the Republic of Ixin and renounces any claim to the vessels absent a mutually agreed reunification. The Republic of Ixin is granted free docking and at cost repairs at Kichnar Orbital Station for its navy for the period of ten standard years.

'''Article XI''': In recognition for the considerable support given by members of the former [[Griffon Alliance]] to the Caspian cause during the Sariphage and subsequently they are hereby granted free docking and at cost repairs at Kichnar Orbital Station for the period of ten years. Furthermore they are extended rent and utility free possession of the section of the station known as “Little Pride” for the same ten year period.

'''Article XII''': The terms of Articles IX, X, and XI may be cancelled should the parties involved engage in hostilities with the Caspian Union or any party the Caspian Union has a protection treaty with.

'''Article XIII''': The Caspian Union reaffirms its recognition of Mergansarsolu as the legitimate government of the planet [[Mergansar]]. The Caspian Union furthermore accepts in principle the continued possession of [[CMS Mergansar]], CMS Hammerton, CMS Ithaca, CMS Anphora, and CMS Toresta by Mergansarsolu.

'''Article XIV''': The Caspian Union hereby levies a fine of 30,000,000,000,000 credits against Mergansarsolu on behalf of the CDU citizens that were illegally driven from their homes on Mergansar with the support of civil authorities on the planet. The Caspian Union furthermore places the planet Mergansar under interdiction until such time as the fine has been paid in full or a satisfactory alternative has been presented. Medical supplies bound for Mergansar must be redirected through Kichnar Orbital Station for inspection by Caspian authorities prior to landing clearance to Mergansar being granted. All other trade shall be suspended until such time as Mergansar has complied with the fine or the Union Council deems such trade necessary for the survival of the local population.

The Caspian Starfighter Corps is the branch of the [[Caspian Defence Force]] tasked with operation all starfighters in service to the [[Caspian Union]] whether they are based in on a planet, space station, or warship. It was formed by the administration of [[Crestian Tarasar]] after his election as [[Presav]] and his appointment as Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defence and [[Sar Admiral]] by merging the existing [[Marine Aerospace Division]] and [[Naval Aerospace Arm]] into a unified branch separate from the [[Caspian Navy]] and [[Union Marines]].

== Ranks and Structure ==

The rank structure of the Caspian Starfighter Corps is an amalgamation of the marine and naval influences of the previously separate starfighter branches. The enlisted personnel and non-commissioned officers utilise the same rank structure as is currently employed by the [[Union Marines]]. Officers ranks are generally named for the standard position held by an officer of that rank, though the influence of naval ranks is evident and is employed for the majority of flag ranks.

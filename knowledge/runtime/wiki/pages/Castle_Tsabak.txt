'''Castle Tsabak''' was the massive palace on [[Dreven]] used by the [[Interim Ruling Council]] and later [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Bacharan Valak]] as the seat of [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] power. The Council was situated within the Castle, and Valak's throne room would later be built atop the Castle.

As the years drew on, and the Imperial capital was relocated to [[Selene]], and later [[Coruscant|Imperial Center]] after its [[Sixth Battle of Coruscant|capture]] by Imperial forces, Castle Tsabak would fall into a state of decay, occupied only by a few nobles in the Imperial hierarchy. It would remain largely ignored as Dreven changed hands from the Empire to the [[New Republic]] and later from the Republic to the [[Imperium]]. Eventually it was partially renovated by the [[Imperium]]. 

The [[Grotesques and Odalisques|Grotesques and Odalisques: Requiem for Basca Starker]] party hosted by [[Malif Tal-Tahn]] and presented by [[Zubindi Hakoon]] and the [[Silverwood House of Fashion]] was a held at the Hall of Heroes at the castle in [[33 ABY]].

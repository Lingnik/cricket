This category is for planets that do not exist on the [[List_of_MUSH_terms#g|grid]]*, but do have a wiki page - either for information about ''original, non-coded'' planets created for the game, or for game-specific details about ''[[Canon|canonical]], non-coded'' planets.

<nowiki>*</nowiki> Note that some planets listed here have a coded object in the game (with a transponder, sometimes as a parent object), but are still classified as non-coded planets because they do not have a grid that can be landed and played on.

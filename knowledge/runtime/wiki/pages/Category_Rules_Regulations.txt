Alongside the rules that govern characters; game mechanics for resolving conflicts like [[Combat System|who shot first]] and [[Space System|can you make the jump to hyperspace]], there are some rules which govern players.

These exist to help ensure that while everyone remembers Star Wars MUSH is ''just a game'' that there're real people with real feelings at the other end. These rules provide a framework for dealing with ''people'' at their best, at their worst and all the points in between.[[Category:Star Wars MUSH]]

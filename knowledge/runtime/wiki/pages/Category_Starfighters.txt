TITLE: Category:Starfighters
NAMESPACE: 14 (Category)
LAST_EDIT: 2007-11-01T23:31:25Z
======================================================================
Any aerospace craft that are less than 36 meters in length, are trans-atmospheric, and are designated as property of martial entities are deemed "starfighters". Craft in this category are not equipped to carry cargo, nor are registered on commercial manifests to do so. Craft here are frequently fast, nimble, specialized, and expensive.

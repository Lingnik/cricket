'''Cathedral Factories''' are the primary manufacturing facilities and religious worship spaces of the [[Xi Char]] of [[Charros IV]]. The Xi Char believe all life is a shadow of the paradise of the afterlife, they believe that creating machines is a way to glimpse the afterlife. As such they consider precision manufacturing an act of worship.

The production of their corporation [[Haor Chall Engineering]] such as the [[IG-240 Hailfire-Class Droid Tank|Hailfire-Class Droid Tanks]] and [[Mark IV Vulture-Class Droid Starfighter|Vulture-Class Droid Starfighters]] are produced at these cathedral factories. Smaller facilities such cathedral chapels to this purpose as well in support of this work and to allow worship away from larger population centres.

Worship at these cathedral factories extend beyond the manufacturing itself to include [[conclave|designer's conclaves]]. They also include forges where the faithful and visitors both could work on private projects as an act of personal prayer. Young [[Xi Charrian|Xi Charrians]] find their instruction in faith and manufacturing at these facilities as well.

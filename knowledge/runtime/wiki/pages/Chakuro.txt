{{Character infobox|image =[[File:Chakuro.jpg|250px]] |name = Chakuro|homeworld = Unrecorded|born = Unrecorded|species = Felinoid|gender = Male|height = 6'1"|hair = Black|eyes = Blue-gray|skin = Furred|affiliation = None|boxcolor = White}}Chakuro is a tall, somewhat gangly, black-furred feline for whom there seems to be little information or documentation. The existence of numerous feline races and the common opinion that "they all look alike" may contribute to the difficulty in exactly pinning down his race.

He has done little to attract attention to himself. He spends most of his time avoiding crowds. In the rare occasions he does interact with others, he seems ignorant of Basic, usually looking on in silence while others converse.

There is little that can be said about him, and that kind of mystery often invites rumors.[[Category:Horansi]]

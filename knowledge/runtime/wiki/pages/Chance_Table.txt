TITLE: Chance Table
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2006-05-25T07:52:21Z
======================================================================
A sparkling silver barrier studded with blinking lights and bet indicators snakes all the way around the edge of this 3-meter long table. The recessed area of the table is a polished smooth metallic playfield with odds in various languages appearing holographically and shifting with each roll of the chance cubes and as bets are placed.


The table is rarely seen without a crowd gathering around its entire circumference cheering for the roller, yelling and groaning. The cubes are illuminated chance cubes an inch in length with each of their 6 faces showing a different bright color. Players bet on how likely the colors are to appear. A robotic arm attached to the ceiling above the table collects and pays out bets with incredible speed.
Type +rules chance for rules and commands.

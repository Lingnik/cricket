'''Charlotte Anne Gray Batrad''' (&mdash;[[14 ABY]]), known widely as '''Charlie''', was the only daughter of [[Sar Admiral]] [[Anders Gray]] and his socialite wife, [[Amanda Gray|Amanda]].  Charlotte grew up with the beauty and grace of her mother combined with the sharp mind and strong mettle of her father. A politics graduate, her father pulled strings with his friend [[Presav]] [[Salonika Batrad]] to obtain Charlie a personal assistants position in his office.

In spite of over a decade age gap, the couple announced their engagement shortly after Batrad secured his divorce from his estranged wife and their wedding followed shortly after. Because of her youth and the scandal relating to the age difference between the two, Charlie's political achievements are often overlooked though it is without doubt that the Batrad administration became more dynamic once Salonika took the step of naming his young wife his Vice-Presav.

Charlotte Anne Batrad was poisoned by an extremist separatist group in [[14 ABY]] and died shortly after.

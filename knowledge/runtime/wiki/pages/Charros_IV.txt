'''Charros IV''' is an arid planet in the Taldot Sector in the Mid Rim. It is the homeworld of the [[Xi Charrian]] species. Led by the fanatical [[Xi Char]] religious order devoted to high precision manufacturing and headquarters of their corporate arm, [[Haor Chall Engineering]]. Following months of negotiations, Charros IV joined the [[Caspian Union]] as a member world in [[30 ABY]].

== History ==

The [[Xi Char]] were manufacturers of high renowned considering their work to be an expression of their faith. Their factories served as a cathedrals of their religion. The Xi Char founded [[Haor Chall Engineering]] was a moderately prominent manufacturer of droids, starships, and vehicles. Their industrial production combined with the world's location on two significant hyperlanes led Charros IV to a level of prominence even if the [[Xi Charrian|Xi Charrians]] themselves were largely unknown off their world.

In [[:starwars:45 BBY/Legends|45 BBY]], [[:starwars:Raith Sienar/Legends|Raith Sienar]] infiltrated the Xi Char, stealing valuable information from them while posing as a convert to the order. When he was exposed for what he truly was, the Xi Char twice attempted to assassinate him, their second attempt in [[38 BBY]] resulting in the death of his father, [[:starwars:Narro Sienar|Narro Sienar]]. Because of his actions, the Xi Char refused to take any more Human apprentices, fearing betrayal.

The Xi Char through Haor Chall Engineering produced a variety of materiels for the [[:starwars:Confederacy of Independent Systems/Legends|Confederacy of Independent Systems]] during the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]]. This association with the Confederacy would prove devastating as the Republic sent in Clone Troopers led by [[Jedi]] to destroy their facilities. What remained of Haor Chall Engineering was moved off world. In the cruellest twist of fate, following the war [[Sienar Fleet Systems]] absorbed what remained of the company.

The merger of [[Sienar Fleet Systems]] with [[Kuat Drive Yards]] would only serve to strengthen the foreign control of the Xi Charrian manufacturing legacy. The [[Galactic Empire]] and [[Kuat Drive Yards-Sienar Fleet Systems]] would dominate Charros IV for decades to come.

== Recent Events ==

The fate of Charros IV would change in [[29 ABY]] with the coming to power of [[Crestian Tarasar]] as [[Presav]] of the [[Caspian Union]] and [[Tyler Damion|Damion I]] as Imperator of a reconstituted [[Imperium|Galactic Imperium]]. Tarasar had an eye on rebuilding and expanding the Union with a particular focus on worlds which boasted shipwrights. He was well aware of the plight of the Xi Charrians, sympathising with the indignity of having fallen under domination of company associated with the apostate Sienar. Moreover he felt the diversity of the Union and the role of species such as the [[Yari]] and [[Sluissi]] in the Caspian ship building industry made the Union a potentially more welcome home for the near by Charros System.

The coming to power of [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] as Imperator Damion was a boon to Tarasar's ambition in two ways. As Tarasar was veteran of Malign's [[New Sith Order]] himself, the new Imperator looked upon Tarasar and the Union as an ally, with a formal [[Imperium-Caspian Mutual Protection Pact (29 ABY)|mutual protection pact]] signed early on in their respective administration. Imperator Damion would also end up appointing former Caspian Presav [[Elana Tracer]] as [[Moff]] of the Imperium's capital world of [[Selene]] and additionally put her in charge of [[Kuat Drive Yards-Sienar Fleet Systems]], giving Tarasar another ally in his quest to right the wrongs done to the Xi Charrians.

Over the next several months, Tarasar, Tracer, and [[ak]], a progressive leader among the Xi Char, would negotiate a three way agreement. The leaders agreed they would see Charros IV granted the right to cede from the Imperium and join the Caspian Union. The Imperium in turn would cede Xi Char designs and production facilities to be fully repatriated. In early [[30 ABY]] Presav Tarasar representing the Caspian Union, [[Grand Moff]] Tracer representing the Imperium and KDY-SFS, and [[Prelate]] [[r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak]] of the Xi Char would sign the final treaty.

Prelate r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak and the Deans of three other [[Cathedral Factory|Cathedral Factories]] including ak were granted seats in the [[Union Council]] representing Charros IV. A newly reconstituted Haor Chall Engineering would take its place in the Caspian defence community joining [[SubPro]], [[Lokkai Starworks]], and [[Aston Aerospace]] in building, maintaining, and refitting the [[Caspian Navy]]. Xi Charrian engineers had in fact already been involved in the Caspian Navy's project to recommission and refit their [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruisers]] prior to the final treaty negotiations.

Following Charros IV joining and the [[Krittain|Krittain System]] simultaneous rejoining of the Caspian Union the [[Caspian 3rd Fleet]] shifted it's headquarters from [[Rauther]] to Charros IV, with the [[Caspian 1st Fleet]] absorbing the protection duties for Rauther.

== Location ==

The Charros System is located in the [[:starwars:Kashyyyk Region|Kashyyyk Region]] of the Mid Rim. It is a significant trade hub being on two major hyperlanes: the [[:starwars:Great Kashyyyk Branch|Great Kashyyyk Branch]] and [[:starwars:Lesser Lantillian Route|Lesser Lantillian Route]]. It is close to [[:starwars:Balamak/Legends|Balamak]], [[:starwars:Bimmisaari/Legends|Bimmisaari]], [[Mikassa]], and [[:starwars:Togoria/Legends|Togoria]].

'''Chief Marshal''' is a military rank typically associated with members of a starfighter corps, most notibly the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]].  It encompasses the highest level of commissioned officer in a starfighter corps, referred to as a ''Flag Officer''.  A Chief Marshal often commands several starfighter commands and maintains a command staff; transferring it to wherever they deem most effective.  By most standards, a Chief Marshal never commanded directly, instead retaining and directing the respective Commander to do so.

=== In Imperial Service ===

In the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]], A Chief Marshal generally commanded the sum total of a System force's Starfighter deployment.  This usually was composed of at least 4 starfighter commands consisting of at least 4608 ships, 7680-19200 officers, and 11520-34560 enlisted. Holding the rank of Chief Marshal was considered a great accomplishment in the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]], as the power and authority that came with the rank was highly significant. They were addressed as Marshal. If appointed as a staff officer they would act as an assistant to a [[Force Marshal]].  

A Chief Marshal in control of a System Flight generally reported to their sector's [[High Marshal]] or [[Moff]], and in certain cases to a [[Force Marshal]], if that Marshal was attached to a Fleet. 

A Chief Marshal was equivalent to a [[Admiral]] in the [[Imperial Navy]].

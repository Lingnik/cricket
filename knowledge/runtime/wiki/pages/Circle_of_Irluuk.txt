The Circle of Irluuk is [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]]'s personal intelligence and enforcement agency. The organization was conceived as a counter to the [[Imperial Security Bureau|ISB]] and [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] remnants and a force of Sith Force Users trained by the [[Dark Lord of the Sith|Dark Lord]] to maintain security and the root out traitors to his vision for the [[Imperium]]. The Circle bases of the world of Umbara and has several warships and resources at their disposal to enforce the will of the Imperator. 

The Circle consists of a High Priest and two groups of Sith users: the Inner Ring (more advanced and trained Acolytes of Malign) and the Outer Ring (younger Sith-initiates beginning their training). Most members of the circle have been touched by Malign's alchemy and are often modified or used in his experiments. These members are formidable warriors and are classically trained in lightsaber combat as well as Sith magiks and in using the Darkside of the Force.

'''Clan Ol'jeliic''' is the current ruling clan of [[Hutt]]s on [[Nar Shaddaa]]. Its most prominent Hutt, [[Olbba the Hutt]], is a paranoid gangster who has hired one time [[Starlight Entertainment]] CEO [[Ewan Dain]] to act as the clan's majordomo. The Hutt clan has had professional dealings with the Corellian since he made good on a 200,000 credit loan in [[11 ABY]]. 

The clan is involved in all manner of underworld enterprises from gambling and extortion to spice smuggling and blackmail. The one business the Ol'jeliics do not trade in is slavery, as both Olbba the Hutt and its majordomo find it a tasteless pursuit.

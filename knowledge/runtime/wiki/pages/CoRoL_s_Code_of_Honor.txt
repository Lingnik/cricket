The '''Code of Honor''' for all Legionnaires of the [[Corellian Royal Legion]].

=== The Code ===

Article 1: Legionnaires are volunteers, serving Corellia with honor and fidelity.

Article 2: Each legionnaire is your brother or sister in arms whatever his or her homeworld might be. You show him or her the same close solidarity that links the members of a same family.

Article 3: You respect yours and others traditions and your superiors. Discipline, nobility and friendship are your strengths. Courage and honesty are your virtues.

Article 4: You are proud of being a Legionnaire. You are always well mannered and speak with authority and intelligence. You are always modest.

Article 5: You are an elite defender who is rigorous with himself. You consider your sword your most precious possession and constantly maintain your skill and physical fitness.

Article 6: The mission is carried out until the end, in the respect of the law, the customs of war and of Interplanetary Conventions, if need be, at the risk of your own life.

Article 7: In combat you act without passion or hatred. You respect vanquished enemies. You never surrender either your dead, your wounded, or your weapons.

Article 8: You always accept honorable challenges and honorable surrender. Honor is to be upheld above all else. You are a Corellian Royal Legionnaire.

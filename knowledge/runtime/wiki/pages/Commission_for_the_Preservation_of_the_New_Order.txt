The '''Commission for the Preservation of the New Order''', or '''COMPNOR''', is the principle social conditioning and propaganda apparatus of the Galactic Empire. It is responsible for turning ideology into implementable policy, manipulating media, engineering social change in line with the wishes of the Emperor and controlling the collective consciousness of the Empire.

== History ==

[[Image:Rally.jpg|thumb|The first COMPNOR rally in [[19 BBY]], led by [[:starwars:Ishin-Il-Raz|Ishin-Il-Raz]].]]

COMPNOR, which replaced the [[:starwars:Galactic_Republic/Legends|Galactic Republic]]'s [[:starwars:Commission_for_the_Protection_of_the_Republic/Legends|Commission for the Protection of the Republic]], or COMPOR, originated as a popular ideological organization, and the Emperor quickly acted to draw it into his sphere of influence&mdash;within months of the Declaration of the New Order, the cultural and youth groups had been grafted onto the pre-existing framework of COMPOR. [[:starwars:Crueya Vandron|Crueya Vandron]], one of the Emperor's advisors, played an important role in establishing COMPNOR as an asset of the Empire, with [[:starwars:Sate_Pestage/Legends|Sate Pestage]] and [[:starwars:Ishin-Il-Raz|Ishin-Il-Raz]] also playing important roles.

In [[0 BBY]], the [[:starwars:Imperial_Senate/Legends|Imperial Senate]] was suspended by direct order of Emperor Palpatine. The end of the Senate left the field wide open for COMPNOR to step in. For all the power of the [[Moff|Moffs]] and [[Grand Moff|Grand Moffs]], COMPNOR stood behind them, still more powerful.

After the fragmentation of the Empire in [[4 ABY]], COMPNOR remained intact, its members remaining fanatically loyal to the New Order, which was then represented by the [[Interim Ruling Council]]. This fanaticism was largely due to the quasi-religious views of its Director, Drocal Arconen, who revered Emperor Palpatine and his vision for the Galactic Empire. With the instability following Endor, Drocal worked hard to embody the ideals set out by Palpatine, and instill the same through his influence in COMPNOR to the rest of the Empire.

COMPNOR's influence grew considerably after Endor. [[Bacharan Valak]] elevated its status during his reign and by the time Emperor [[Aleister Vadim|Vadim]] came to the throne, COMPNOR had become a monolithic entity with virtually unchecked power and influence. In Vadim's Empire, COMPNOR's power and influence was severely and ruthlessly curtailed. Stripped of its military and enforcement roles, which became vested in the Imperial Security Bureau the commission returned to its original role in promoting and implementing the policies and ideology of the Empire.
==<span style="font-size:20px;">Recent History</span>==

In [[29 ABY]] [[Elana Tracer]], then the new [[Moff]] of [[Selene]] and [[Athaniss]], became a [[Grand Moff]] with many more responsibilities in [[Tyler Damion|Tyler Damion's]] new [[Imperium]]. Tracer was tasked with and happily set about dismantling COMPNOR. Tracer surgically transformed the propaganda machine into an extensive social services agency and public relations department for everything in Darth Malign's techno-communist utopian vision. Those who did not leave COMPNOR out of their personal principles or were removed due to their more sinister proclivities were encouraged to go along to get along in their new assignments or else get going. Exit interviews provided names of potential disgruntled persons. The subsequent loss of personnel worked in the Moff's favor as it was also her mission to integrate suddenly immigrating non-humans into the new organization. The Imperium's message was that in Damion's Imperium, everyone was equal. Everyone had the right to have their basic needs met, shelter, healthcare, and the dignity of the work of their choice and to live in peace. Everyone had the chance to work hard and achieve a better, more prosperous life and had freedom of movement and association. It worked and the Imperium thrived.
==<span style="font-size:20px;">Ideology</span>==

In addition to the ideals of the New Order, whose authoritarian, spartan ideology underlies the Galactic Empire, COMPNOR was responsible for presenting new measures, policies and initiatives as positive, desirable changes.

== Membership ==

Imperial citizens were encouraged to join COMPNOR. Members and were given exclusive access to breaking news, a free subscription to Iron Will (official feed of COMPNOR) and a special welcome message from the Emperor himself.

A '''conclave''' is a [[Xi Char]] ceremony where a new [[Prelate]] was elected after a vacancy in the position, usually because of the death of the previous occupant. Any member of the Xi Char faith was eligible to be elected Prelate, but in practice only senior clerics were likely candidates. The ceremony involved the senior clerics of the faith sequestering themselves in one of the [[Cathedral Factory|cathedral factories]] until unanimity could be reached as to who should be the new Prelate. 

The ceremony would typically occur at Xi Chall, but might occur at another cathedral factory particularly if the previous Prelate had based themselves at a different one. During the ceremony spires of the cathedral factory would spew black smoke, as consensus began to be reached the black smoke would be replaced by white smoke. Once unanimity was reached all the spires would be spewing white smoke.

The Conclave of [[26 ABY]] chose [[Dean]] [[r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak]] of Xi Chall as Prelate. After his death Inquisitor [[stahanak]] was chosen to replace him.

The term conclave was also use by the Xi Char for other major clerical gatherings such as designer's conclaves.

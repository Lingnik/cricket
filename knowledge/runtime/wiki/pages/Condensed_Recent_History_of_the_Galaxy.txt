======================================================================
This is a condensed version of the history of IC events on the game starting from the return of [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] in [[27 ABY]] (October 2018) through 2023 ([[32 ABY]]). The [[New Republic]] did not have active in game leadership through much of this time, so the limited references to its activity is reflective of this fact.

==[[27 ABY]]==

*Darth Malign ([[Tyler Damion]]) and [[Grand Admiral]] [[Drayson Honos]] are rescued from the ancient Sith world of [[Korriban]] where they had been trapped for the past decade by Malign's half-sister [[Jedi Knight]] [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]]. Malign had long been assumed dead by much of the galaxy, with the [[Galactic Empire]] supposedly having his dead body as proof of the success of their [[Sith Order|Sith]] purge.
*Malign and Johanna travel to the [[Dragonflower Nebula]] to meet [[Admiral]] [[Crestian Tarasar]], one of the remaining loyalists to the [[New Sith Order]]. The three form a pact to take down Galactic [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Aleister Vadim]], former master to Malign and tormentor to Johanna. All parties agree that Emperor Vadim represented to the greatest danger to the galaxy.
*Upset by the long cycle of enslavement, poverty, oppression, and violence that the people of her homeworld of [[Tatooine]] has suffered under, Johanna sets her sites on freeing the world. To that end, with the help of her brother Tyler and [[Sentinel]], the trio take on the gangs of [[Mos Eisley]]. The end result sees gang leaders staked in front of rubble, left to die of exposure, the severed heads of their enforcers mounted beside them as a warning to all would-be criminals who seek to prey on the poor of the area.
*Emperor Vadim secretly tasks [[Basca Starker]] to develop a [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|bacteriophage]] and test it on the planet [[Caspar]], principal planet of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]]. The initial infections are reported towards the	end of the year. A planetary quarantine is ordered before the New Year by Professor [[Daln Bael]], Director of the [[Groton Sciences Institute]]. Bael considers there to be no hope for a cure and the quarantine is meant to last until the entire population has died off from the plague.
*On Caspar's sister world [[Mergansar]], homeworld of the [[Sarian]] species, the dark side cult [[Olumi Osahn]] claims the plague has been brought by non-Sarians and engages in [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]] to expel the non-Sarian population from Mergansar. Neither the planetary government of Mergansar nor the [[Caspian Navy]] make any moves to stop the pogroms.
*The [[Corporate Sector Authority]] and [[Griffon Alliance|Griffon]] expatriate community on [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] led by CSA [[Prex (CSA)|Prex]] and former Griffon [[Zeak Oppenhiemer]] undertake major efforts to support the displaced refugees from Mergansar and provide aid to Caspar.

==[[28 ABY]]==

*Crestian, Johanna, and Zeak try to convince Basca Starker to employ his medical resources towards finding a cure to the plague (unaware at this point that he was behind it). Starker claims no interest in the project until he is bribed by a copy of a Jedi database from Johanna. Starker develops a cure for what becomes known as the Sariphage, the initial disease having targeted the Sarian species but affecting similar species. 2.8 billion of the pre-Sariphage population of Caspar dies before the cure is available.
*The Sith [[Interdictor-class Immobilizer 418|Interdictor]] [[Leviathan]] under the command of Crestian Tarasar, a Sarian and Caspian native, emerges from a decade in hiding to offer aid to the Caspian people. Under the quiet encouragement of [[Sar Admiral]] [[Prae Kichakressa]], Tarasar turns his attention to intervening with the Olumi Osahn cult which had prior ties to the New Sith Order.
*Unable to convince the Olumi Osahn to bend a knee to Darth Malign and desist from their current campaign, the Leviathan crew, with the secret involvement of Darth Malign himself, attack Olumi Osahn supporters on Mergansar. Tarasar creating the movement [[Casulmis]] to incorporate Mergani refugees in this cause.
*A Caspian Navy flotilla consisting of [[CMS Typhoon]] commanded by Sar Admiral Kichakressa, supported by CMS Ad Astra, [[CMS Prophet]], and CMS Rapace, break from orbit to confront the Leviathan at Mergansar. The Leviathan moved to rendezvous with the flotilla but refused to lower shields after the Caspians deployed fighters. In the [[Mergansar Incident|engagement]] that followed the Leviathan was forced to withdraw after suffering heavy damage, while the Prophet and Rapace were crippled and Ad Astra and Typhoon sustained heavy damage. Sar Admiral Kichakressa was killed when the Typhoon's bridge was destroyed. Unbeknownst to most involved was the involvement of Darth Malign in allowing the outmatched Leviathan to survive the encounter.
*Following the Sariphage pandemic and the Mergani progroms, the Caspian Democratic Union dissolves with [[Ixin]], [[Kasputin Yar]], [[Krittain]] ([[Krittain Major|Major]] and [[Krittain Minor|Minor]]), Mergansar, and [[Rauther]] each declaring independence. A rump consisting of Caspar, Kichnar Orbital Station, and [[Mikassa]] remains with a provisional [[Caspian Union|government]] eventually formed.
*Deciding the new support to Caspar in its rebuilding is more important than his Sith service, Crestian Tarasar returns to his home city of [[Trinumvira]] where he establishes a charity known as [[The Mission]]. Though founded to support survivors on Caspar and refugees coming from Mergansar it would also serve as a vehicle for his further political ambitions. Given the political chaos no effort is made to stop his return.

==[[29 ABY]]==

*Tyler Damion, otherwise known as Darth Malign, the [[Dark Lord of the Sith]], reemerges publicly and declares himself the new leader of the Galactic Empire, now rechristened as the [[Imperium]]. Imperator Damion sets forth several new policies such as the abolition of slavery, equal rights between sentient species, and allowing worlds to democratically appoint their [[Moff|Moffs]].
*In anticipation of the upcoming [[Report:Caspian Election Posts|election]] for a permanent [[Union Council]] on Caspar, Crestian Tarasar forms a political party called [[Praecet]]. Its central platform is to restore security to Caspar by strengthening the armed forces, forming military alliances, reuniting with other former Caspian worlds, and a commitment to ensure all citizens are equally protected, including seeking reparations for the Mergani illegally expelled from Mergansar the year before.
*Imperator Damion and his agents successfully take [[Imperial City (Selene)|Imperial City]] on [[Selene]] making it the seat of his power, the rest of the [[Tarkin Corridor]] soon falls under his control. The rest of the Empire is invited  to fall in line, with most worlds choosing to support the new regime. Several [[Neo-Imperial Movement|Neo-Imperial]] strongholds including [[Dreven]] resist the new regime but are ultimately betrayed by CSA Prex Zeak Oppenhiemer and they are seized by the [[New Republic]].
*Following tense negotiations, the Caspar Union agrees to a final distribution of military assets with its former member worlds, the structure of the Union Council, and qualifications for voting in the upcoming election. Provisional [[Presav]] [[Nelivve Lameberchain]] announces he will not seek election of the new chamber.
*A report from Wookieeleaks reveals that The Mission is almost entirely funded by figures associated with Imperator Damion, and with the entire 1,500 member full-time staff of the charity paid out of pocket by the Mission's Executive Director and Praecet party leader Crestian Tarasar. The undecided vote in the election shrinks in half following this revelation.
*Terrorists attack the announcement of election returns on Kichnar Orbital Station with one of the losing candidates detonating a suicide bomb in an unrelated secondary attack leaving over 100 dead and over 600 wounded.
*The final election results on Caspar see Praecet capture 7 of the 9 seats on the Union Council with the Legacy party gaining the other two. The Union Council elects Crestian Tarasar as Presav. Tarasar nominates [[Cas Mos]] of the [[Legacy]] party as Vice Presav, forming a coalition government in spite of holding a supermajority of the seats.
*The [[Caspian Repatriation Act (29 ABY)|Caspian Repatriation Act]] is passed by the Union Council with Kasputin Yar and Rauther rejoining Caspar, Kichnar Orbital Station, and Mikassa, the Caspar Union being rechristened the Caspian Union. The previous CDU Non-Aggression Treaty with the Galactic Empire is explicitly voided as the old CDU government that agreed to it ceased to exist, and the government of Emperor Aleister Vadim declared to no longer be the legitimate Imperial state; a memorandum of understanding with the new Imperium in regards to a mutual protection pact is enacted. The governments of Ixin, Krittain, and Mergansar are officially recognised and certain naval assets from the CDU officially recognised as theirs.
*In spite of recognising the independence of Mergansar, the Caspian Union formally interdicts the plan and demands a payment of 30 trillion credits on behalf of the 300 million illegally displaced refugees. The government of Mergansar is both unwilling and unable to pay this exorbitant sum.
*As part of the repatriation act, the Caspian Union nationalises Kichnar Orbital Station and the arms manufactures and shipwrights that had been previously bought up by Prex Zeak Oppenhiemer and the CSA. The Griffons are granted the Little Pride section of KOS rent free for ten years and free docking privileges for the same span. In spite of the accommodations granted the Griffons, many leave KOS in protest of the ill treatment of Zeak.
*An auction and social gathering on the [[Nyriaan Spiral]] in orbit over Tatooine is marred by a confrontation between Darth Malign and the host and owner of the floating palace ship, the Crime Lord [[Nasiri Zithreej|Nasiri Gejalli]]. When Lorda Gejalli refuses to back down against the Imperator's demands, the Imperator orders the ship evacuated and destroyed as punishment. 
*[[Trandoshan]] raiders conduct a series of attacks on refugee vessels, one such as attack on the SS Centennial Fox bound for Mikassa is stopped by CMS Mikassa.
*The Imperium and Caspian Union establish a [[Imperium-Caspian Mutual Protection Pact (29 ABY)|mutual-defence pact]], with the Union granted a position on the [[Moff's Council]]. The New Republic contends this makes the Caspian Union a client state of the Imperium and downgrades their relations accordingly.
*The Republic of Ixin rejoins the Caspian Union, leaving the Mergansar and the Krittish Union as the only former members of the Caspian Democratic Union outside the jurisdiction of the new Caspian Union.
*Following weeks of being blockaded by the Caspian Navy, riots break out on Mergansar and politicians associated with Olumi Osahn start turning up dead in the city of Ithaca. This is soon followed by a general strike led by the Dusona Workers Union in the cities of Hammerton, Spanner, and Charon.
*An arrest warrant placed by the Griffons in the New Republic for [[Ara te Danaan|Ara]] and [[Tyler te Danaan]] for stealing a ship owned by their father Zeak Oppenhiemer incites a political incident. The public condemnation of the warrant by the Caspian Ministry of Justice leads to the remaining Griffons on Kichnar Orbital Station to vacate in protest.
*During Imperium Court proceedings in [[Cloud City]] on [[Bespin]] it is revealed that Basca Starker, the man who had developed the cure for the Sariphage on Caspar was in fact the man behind the virus that killed 2.8 billion people to begin with. It is further revealed Emperor Aleister Vadim had instigated Starker's work and that Starker was possessed by a Sith Spawn implanted by Vadim. Jedi Knight and [[Outer Rim]] [[Grand Moff]] Johanna Siri te Danaan drives the Sith Spawn from within Starker. Following this Starker is allowed to live and leave against the protests of Caspian Presav Crestian Tarasar.
*Caspian Presav Crestian Tarasar announces to the galaxy the role of Basca Starker in developing the Sariphage virus. Though the Caspian government does not place a bounty on Starker he is stripped of honourary citizenship and property on Caspar and declared outlawed. According to his outlawed status he can be killed with impunity. Tarasar also announces evidence of Starker's involvement in a [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] sponsored school for force-sensitive youth and the recent Caspian-Imperium operation personally led by Tarasar and Moff [[Elana Tracer]] to rescue two of those youth who had been kidnapped from their families and were being held by Trandoshans on Spira.
*[[Basca Starker|Marius Starker]] the supposed brother of Basca Starker delivers what is claimed to be the body of Basca Starker to the [[Caspian Crest]]. 
*The Imperium escalates border skirmishes with pirates on the borders of the [[Hapes Consortium]] and instigates a full scale attack upon the [[Hapan]] fleet. The Hapans are quickly defeated and the Imperium negotiates an alliance with the Consortium. The alliance is underpinned by a plethora of political marriages including that of the Imperator to the Hapan [[King|Queen Mother]].
*The New Republic [[Senator]] from [[Yavin 4|Yavin]] dies in a shuttle accident and is replaced by Marius Starker.
*The Caspian Navy's blockade of Mergansar is ended with the passage of the Mergansar Reunification Act. The act appoints Sar Admiral [[Odie Hood]], the Councillor of Ithaca as Military [[Governor]] pending the full restoration of local government on Mergansar.
*An earthquake inexplicably destroys Mergansar's main starport in Hebron which had been the epicentre of pogroms on Mergansar nearly two years prior. Controversy ensues days later when the newly elected Councillor of Charon, [[Philinda Jorgan]] submits a proposal to expand Charon's starport rather than support rebuilding in Hebron.
*In a coordinated effort multiple groups of slavers attack Cloud City on Bespin during the 1st Annual Miners Dinner in retaliation for the efforts of Grand Moff Johanna Siri te Danaan, the current governor of Cloud City, to curtail the slave trade. The main refinery is blown up grievously wounding Johanna who is evacuated to Selene. Attacks on the miner's dinner and the red light district leaving scores of dead and hundreds wounded. Rumours are that [[Boba Fett]] was involved in the attack on the dinner and was captured and taken away by one of the guests.
*In the immediate aftermath of the attack Caspian Presav Crestian Tarasar assumes operational control of the system as next highest ranked Imperium-allied official present and interdicts the system in an effort to catch the culprits but then fails to prevent one of the suspect ships from escaping. A Caspian task force bringing [[SubPro]] engineers arrives to assist the miners in surveying and repairing damage. [[Quinne Stardust Rose]], the general manager of the hotel Yarith Bespin and entertainment director of Cloud City becomes acting [[Baron|Baroness-Administrator]].

==[[30 ABY]]==

*Slavers, believed to be involved in the recent attack on Bespin, attack the Krittish freighter the Blechley Brook that had been transporting students on a school trip. Two teachers and two students are found dead in the wreck of the vessel with the remaining 308 crew and passengers taken by the slavers. The [[Caspian 4th Fleet]] is sent to assist the [[Krittish Navy]] in their search for the ship involved.
*In a closed door meeting of the [[New Republic Senate]], the office of [[Chief of State]] is abolished, unceremoniously ousting [[Leia Organa Solo]] from her leadership of the Republic, and is then replaced by a triumvirate led by Marius Starker as Chancellor along with Senate President [[Tarshyyk]] and [[Jedi Master]] [[Jessalyn Valios]].
*Following a souring of relations with the New Republic, including being ejected from the Jedi Order for being a Grand Moff of the Imperium, Johanna Siri te Danaan establishes a new academy for Force sensitives on Tatooine at what was once Jabba's Palace.
*[[Sonja Damion]], the daughter of Imperator Damion, is crowned as heir to the Imperium and Hapan thrones.
*Following the slaver attack on the Blechley Brook and many months of negotiations, the Krit Union rejoins the Caspian Union. It is simultaneously announced that after nearly a year of negotiations the world of [[Charros IV]] is also joining the Caspian Union, the first world to join it that had not been part of the previous Caspian Democratic Union.
*The [[Caspia]] System is rocked by several ritualistic slayings of people associated with the Olumi Osahn cult responsible for the pogroms on Mergansar two years earlier. The reappearance of [[Axel Vichten]] who had previous ties to the cult and who publicly condemned those responsible for the pogroms leads him to be a prime suspect in the killings. The Caspian Ministry of Justice, however, is either unable to or unwilling to tie him to any of the crimes.
*Following the election in Toresta on Mergansar, Odie Hood the Military Governor of Mergansar introduces legislation to allow elections in Anphora, Goshen, Hebron, and Vilarasala, the remaining Mergani cities unrepresented in the Union Council. Following this, Hood resigns as Military Governor to return to a more active role in the Caspian Navy with Councillor Philinda Jorgan of Charon taking his place to oversee the final elections.
*Following the revelation that Marius Starker, briefly Chancellor of the New Republic, was not in fact the brother to notorious Basca Starker, developer of the Sariphage virus that ravaged Caspar, but actually Basca himself, the [[Corellia]] system declares independence from the New Republic. Concerns about subsequent Senate President Tarshyyk's role in the ousting of Leia Organa Solo from power in favour of Starker and the involvement of Jedi Master Jessalyn Valios in the Starker deception are cited as factors in Corellia's decision.
*Reports of corruption by the leaders of [[:starwars:Andasala|Andasala]] and [[:starwars:Qiaxx|Qiaxx]] leads Imperator Damion to appoint Axel Vichten as Interim-Moff of the [[:starwars:Cor'ric sector|Cor'ric Sector]] in order to root out the corruption and set the stage for the election of new leadership. Vichten begrudgingly accepts the position on the understanding that it will be temporary.
*Upset at their efforts to curtail the slave trade, [[Hutt]] slaver [[Moaba]] the Clanless sends assassins after a number of the individuals they feel are to blame and their families. The targeted include Imperator Damion's pregnant wife, the Hapan Queen Mother, who survives but whose unborn child does not, Johanna Siri te Danaan's daughter Ara te Danaan whose ship crashes but she survives, and Nasiri Gejalli who likewise survives.
*The assassination attempts kick retaliatory efforts into high gear with Interim-Moff Axel Vichten elevated to the position of Inquisitor and given a task force with which to prosecute the Imperium's efforts against the perpetrators. Disgusted by the climate of jingoism permeating the Moff's Council and the Imperator's desire for her to entreat with the New Republic, Outer Rim Grand Moff Johanna Siri te Danaan resigns her position.
*An Imperium attack on Moaba's palace on [[:starwars:Nal Hutta/Legends|Nal Hutta]] takes place, led by Inquisitor Axel Vichten, which kills Moaba.
*The sparsely populated mining world of [[:starwars:Phemis|Phemis]] in the [[:starwars:Corellian sector/Legends|Corellian Sector]] breaks with the New Republic in light of Corellia's independence.

==[[31 ABY]]==

*[[:starwars:New Plympto/Legends|New Plympto]] and [[:starwars:Nubia/Legends|Nubia]] declare independence from the New Republic, sparking rumours the whole of the Corellian Sector might soon follow them out the door.
*Owing to its strong ties to Corellia, the agriworld of [[:starwars:Sacorria|Sacorria]] breaks with the New Republic to ally with Corellia.
*[[:starwars:Aurea|Aurea]] becomes the latest world in the Corellian Sector to leave the New Republic speculating talk of the formation of a sector wide government.
*[[:starwars:Altawar|Altawar]], [[:starwars:Corfai|Corfai]], [[:starwars:Froz|Froz]], [[:starwars:Jumus|Jumus]], [[:starwars:Vagran|Vagran]], and [[:starwars:Xyquine II|Xyquine II]] join the growing number of Corellian Sector planets to breakaway from the New Republic.
*As negotiations intensify towards forming a Corellian Confederation, [[:starwars:Craeen|Craeen]] and the ecumenopolis world of [[:starwars:Dorsis|Dorsis]] join the movement.
*With the terms of the Corellian Confederation nearly hammered out, the agriworld of [[:starwars:Khomr|Khomr]] joins at the eleventh hour.
*[[:starwars:Gorsh|Gorsh]], an unaligned world in Wild Space near Dreven is attacked by raiders. New Republic [[New Republic Marine Corps|Marine]] [[Colonel]] [[Han'bel Eckhart]] is operating a patrol nearby and intercepts a distress call from Gorsh. His Republic superiors order him to ignore the distress call and return back to base. The incident is the final straw that leads him to defect from New Republic service.

==[[32 ABY]]==

*The New Republic confirms that Colonel Han'bel Eckhart went rogue along with the crew of [[DSV Templar|The Templar]] taking the ship with them. While the New Republic attempts to paint them as dangerous criminals, public perception is split.
*The independent worlds of the Corellian Sector formally unite an independent galactic power known as the [[Corellian Confederation]] with former New Republic Senator [[Dash Blazer]] appointed as its Prime Minister.
*The cargo vessel ''Tatooine Sands'' crashes at [[Coronet City]] Spaceport, miraculously there were no fatalities.
*A coordinated series of explosions in Coronet City draw [[Corellian Security Force|CorSec]] away from the Coronet City Spaceport where an attempted escape leaves several fatalities with Dash Blazer who had been at the spaceport to meet an Imperium delegation wounded in an assassination attempt. The escape is suppressed through the combined efforts of Blazer, his protection detail, civilians on site, Caspian Presav[Crestian Tarasar, his team, and Imperator Damion I.
*Disaster in Cloud City on Bespin is narrowly averted when a deranged suicide bomber attempts to detonate an explosive at the Yarith Bespin, coinciding with the Greater Javin Medical Society's Annual Conference and the Bespin Cultural Festival.
*During a major policy speech at [[Union Hall]] in [[Plaxton]] on Caspar, Imperator Damion I announces the formation of a Coalition of Independent Planets spearheaded by the Caspian Union and Corellian Confederation. The Corellian Confederation formally signs defence and trade agreements with several powers including the Imperium and Caspian Union at this time.
*[[Thurston Unger]] debuts a new segment the [[Galaxy's Dumbest Crooks]] on his popular [[InterGalactic News|IGNews]] programme, [[The Cutting Edge]].
*Anti-corruption protests on [[Ord Mantell]] turn into a deadly clash between protestors and Republic security, IGNews reporter Thurston Unger is wounded in the clash.
*Contract negotiations between [[Biscuit Baron]] and various local unions, which had already been on thin ice, break down following the violence on Ord Mantell with the workers on that world the first to announce they are on strike. The workers union on Selene leave the negotiation table and join the picket line in solidarity with those on Ord Mantell.
*In a coordinate action the unions representing the Biscuit Baron workers in the [[Caspian Sector|Caspian]], [[:starwars:Halla sector/Legends|Halla]], and [[:starwars:Taldot sector/Legends|Taldot]] sectors go on strike.
*In major address at Union Hall Caspian Presav Crestian Tarasar announces a [[Second Renaissance of Innovation]]. Four new starfighter designs and two combat droid designs by [[Haor Chall Engineering]] are unveiled, along with plans for [[Charros Wargames|wargames]] at Charros IV.
*[[Dreven]] leaves the New Republic and joins the Imperium.
*After months of strikes throughout the galaxy, Biscuit Baron enlists the services of the [[Golden Bantha Group]] as consultants. This move gets negotiations back on track with the strikes resolved shortly thereafter.

==[[33 ABY]]==

*After months of negotiations the Corporate Sector Authority [[Report:Biscuit Buyout|sells]] Biscuit Baron to Golden Bantha Group, the consultancy group which had been effectively running the corporation since the end of the workers strike the year before. Subsequently the Golden Bantha Group Board of Directors assumes the equivalent roles at Biscuit Baron with [[Atsvara Tarasar]] revealed as the CEO and [[Cricket]] as Executive Vice President.
*[[Zubindi Hakoon]] and the [[Silverwood House of Fashion]] are hired by [[Malif Tal-Tahn]] to throw a party based on the aesthetic as noted genocidal scientist Basca Starker. The [[Grotesques and Odalisques|Grotesques and Odalisques: Requiem for Basca Starker]] is held at [[Castle Tsabak]] on Dreven. Malif himself never shows up leading the guests and hosts alike to wonder what the point had been in the first place.

==[[34 ABY]]==

*Wide spread protests break out on [[Chandrila]] in response to reports of corruption by various officials. Secession from the New Republic is an idea openly considered by some of the protesters.
*Biscuit Baron introduces the [[Infinite Stack]].
*[[Lorn Rhys|Darth Malus]] is broken out of an Imperium cryogenic prison known as [[The Oubliette]] along with several other high profile prisoners.
*[[Xi Char]] [[Prelate]] [[r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak]] dies, leading to days of official mourning in the Caspian Union and a long series of funerary ceremonies.
*Inquisitor [[stahanak]] is chosen by the [[conclave]] to replace r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak as Prelate.
*Following his efforts to hunt down the prisoners who escaped The Oubliette, Inquisitor Axel Vichten is promoted to [[Grand Admiral]] and named Imperium Regent on State, Military, and Corruption Matters.
*Mysterious crop failures on the agriworlds of [[:starwars:New Ator|New Ator]] and [[:starwars:Vasch|Vasch]] in the [[:starwars:Arkanis sector/Legends|Arkanis Sector]] trigger a massive famine and refugee crisis.
*Former Caspian Interim Presav Nelivve Lameberchain dies after a long illness.

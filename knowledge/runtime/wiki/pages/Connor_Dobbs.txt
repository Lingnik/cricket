'''Connor Dobbs''' was an [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[Warlord]] who took control of the [[Interim Ruling Council]] and the remnants of the Empire on [[Dreven]] after Warlord [[Jabir Tagger]] was ousted from leadership in the wake of the [[Second Battle of Coruscant]]. He was one of [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]]'s [[Dark Side Adepts]] and one of the first [[Dark Jedi]] to attempt to take control of the Empire for himself. With his apprentice, [[Lucifer]], Dobbs attempted to make himself [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] of the Imperial remnants, but was ultimately outdone by a fellow Dark Jedi who he had trained with: [[Wyth Skellheim]]. Wyth slew Dobbs, ending his short reign.

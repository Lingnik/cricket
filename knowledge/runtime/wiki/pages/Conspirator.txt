The warship '''''Conspirator''''' is a Republic Carrier-Cruiser and the flagship of the [[New Sith Order]]'s intelligence organization &mdash; the [[Sith Intelligence Network]]. It is known to be captained by Lord Regent of [[Nar Shaddaa]] Lord [[Ewan Dain|Rannis]], leader of this particular [[Sith Order|Sith]] government organization.

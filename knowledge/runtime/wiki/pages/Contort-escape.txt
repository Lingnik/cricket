The character escapes bonds by contorting in painful and difficult (but physically possible) ways. By dislocating joints, softening bones, and the like, a Jedi can escape almost any physical restraining device. While this is indeed a painful procedure, Jedi are trained to block out pain and focus on the task at hand.

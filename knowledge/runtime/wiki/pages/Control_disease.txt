'''Control disease''' allows the [[Jedi]] to direct and control the anti-bodies and healing resources of her body to throw off or to resist diseases within the body. Using the power requires the Jedi to mediate for half a hour. 

If the disease is life-threatening or long-standing, the Jedi must make repeated skill attempts over the course of several weeks or months to cure the disease entirely. (Gamemaster's discretion as to how many rolls are needed and how often.)

==Control Difficulty==
Very Easy for a mild infection such as a cold; Easy for a modest infection or illness, such as a high fever or severe flu; Moderate for a severe illness, such as gangrene or a hive virus; Difficult for a life-threatening disease; Very Difficult for a massive, long-standing disease; Heroic for a rapid-acting life-threatening disease.

''[[Accelerate healing]]'' is a required power before being able to utilize ''control disease''.

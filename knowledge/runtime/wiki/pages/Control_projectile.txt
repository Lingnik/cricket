This ability allows the Jedi to control the trajectory and speed of a large number of small projectiles of any type. These projectiles may move together or seperately, and as long as concentration is retained, they will remain under the Jedi's control. Four projectiles are considered easy, with each fourth additional projectile adding a level of difficulty.

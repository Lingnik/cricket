The '''Corellian Corruption Scandal''' was a massive scandal that hit the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]]-controlled [[Corellia|Corellian]] government in [[15 ABY]] under the [[Bailey Sora Laran|Laran]] administration.

Occuring simultaneously alongside [[Operation Corellisi Guerlle]], and believed to have been ongoing for quite some time beforehand, it was revealed that high-level officials under the Laran administration, from [[Corellian Security Force|CorSec]] officers to government administrators, were illegally engaged in criminal activity with the [[underworld]] crime syndicate, [[Maffi / Zhao|Maffi]].

== The Scandal Unfurls ==

Bailey Laran, Imperial Governor, became suspect and was brought in for questioning by Imperial authorities. The true extent of the corruption ran so deep as to force the Empire to initiate martial law on the planet, bringing in [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] and [[CompForce]] to restore order and expose the scandal and its perpetrators. An entire lockdown of [[Corellian Security Force|CorSec]] was initiated by [[Imperial Security Bureau|ISB]] Director [[Korynn Fleming|Fleming]].

Several days after the CompForce-imposed martial law began, elements of the Imperial [[501st Legion]] began arriving on Corellia, boosting the Empire's presence on the planet in an effort to crack down on the criminals and terrorists.

Governor [[Bailey Sora Laran|Laran]] was soon released to house arrest on Corellia, but an extensive list of charges were filed against her in response to the scandal. These charges included:

* Failure to Effectively Govern an Imperial Planet
* Neglection of Duty
* Withholding Information from Imperial Authorities
* Disrespect of Imperial Authority

Not long after her release, the Governor went public with her side of the [[Report:The Corellian Corruption Scandal#Laran Stands|story]], claiming that her arrest was at the hands of an ISB Agent, and not the will of the Emperor. Simultaneously, the entire planet of Corellia was shut off from the rest of the Galaxy by way of the [[Imperial Navy]] and the [[65th Legion|65th Stormtrooper Legion]]. What happened to the CorSec officers who disappeared during the lockdown was unknown, and the arrest of diplomatic ambassadors raised [[Report:The Corellian Corruption Scandal#Diplomatic Tensions|tensions with the]] [[Caspian Democratic Union|CDU]]. However, the lockdown eventually came to an end when what seemed to be a [[Report:The Corellian Corruption Scandal#Corellian Lockdown Lifted|high profile arrest]] took place at a large residence on Corellia. This both proved that the Corellian Corruption Scandal went much deeper than was originally expected, but left little to no answers as to the true depth of its story.

== Treason ==

About a month later, [[IGN]] released a [[Report:Danik_Kreldin_Arrested|news report]] claiming that [[Danik Kreldin]], the Imperial [[Grand Admiral]] who was presumed dead, had been found alive and arrested out of his estate on Corellia in an operation conducted by the [[501st Legion|501st Stormtrooper Legion]] and CompForce. In this news report, it was reported that ISB found evidence that Kreldin had been forming a rebellion against [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Aleister Vadim|Vadim]]. Soon enough, reports would start being released through the Imperial News networks (which, of course, were under the direction of the [[Coalition for Improvements]]). These [[Report:The_Defamation_of_Kreldin|reports]] claimed that the Imperial Security Bureau had begun uncovering many more facts aligning Danik up with treasonous activities.

Not long thereafter, the [[Kreldin Estate]] was destroyed by an orbital bombardment.  Following the destruction of the Estate his last followers were crushed during the [[Battle for Purification]].

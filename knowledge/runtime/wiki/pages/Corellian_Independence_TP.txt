==Corellian Independence==

The '''Corellian Independence TP''' began in [[17 ABY]] when forces within the [[Nobility of Corellia]] began a dispute arguing which government would be the rightful leader of the planet. Pro Imperial and Pro Independent forces began struggling for dominance in the courts and soon after, a string of failed assassination attempts against the Pro Imperial nobles led to off planet involvement.

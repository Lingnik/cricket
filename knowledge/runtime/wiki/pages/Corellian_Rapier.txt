A '''Corellian Rapier''' is a rare and widely sought after weapon crafted only [[Corellia]]. Very few are known to exist. One such Rapier found its way into the hands of [[Danik Kreldin]], one of the leaders of the [[Galactic Empire]] and heir to a Corellian noble house. 

Each sword is hand-crafted and made with the finest materials available. It has a long, slim, flat blade, razor shop and decorated with an ivy pattern. The handle and guard are covered with a gold design and shine brightly in the light. The hand guard is carved with an ivy pattern going straight through the metal to reveal the unseen part of the handle.

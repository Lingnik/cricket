The '''Corporate Sector Strike Fleet''' was developed in late [[15 ABY]] as a massive unit of warships, with more than half of all [[Security Division (CSA)|Corporate Sector Security]] ships assigned to its Order of Battle. It was considered impossible to administer, with ships strung out over the entirety of the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector]], but its architects were careful to streamline the process of breaking off smaller groups, be they task forces or warship squadrons.

'''Corulag''' was a populated terrestrial world located on the [[Perlemian Trade Route]] in the [[:starwars:Corulus system|Corulus system]] in the [[Bormea Sector]], part of the [[:starwars:Ringali Shell|Ringali Shell Cluster]]. It was the endpoint of the [[:starwars:Corulag-Jatir Hyperroute|Corulag-Jatir Hyperroute]]. The planet boasted urban sprawls with the underdeveloped areas filled with [[:starwars:bamboo|bamboo]] forests. 

It was near the [[:starwars:Ringali Nebula/Legends|Ringali Nebula]] and shared the sun [[:starwars:Corulus|Corulus]] with [[:starwars:Solag|Solag]], [[:starwars:Biolag|Biolag]], and [[:starwars:Garulag|Garulag]].

==History==
Originally a colony of [[Coruscant]], Corulag was a [[The Galaxy#Core Worlds|Core World]] with a mostly [[Human]] population with a few [[Wookiee]]s, [[Aqualish]], [[Ithorian]]s and [[:starwars:Selkath/Legends|Selkath]] as well. This planet was founded when, under [[:starwars:Rakatan/Legends|Rakatan]] domination, the [[Human]]s of Coruscant sent out [[:starwars:Sleeper ship/Legends|sleeper ship]]s to colonize distant worlds. These sleeper ships ended up throughout the galaxy, in locations such as Corulag, [[:starwars:Alderaan/Legends|Alderaan]], in the [[:starwars:Tion Cluster/Legends|Tion Cluster]], [[:starwars:Seoul 5|Seoul 5]], [[Kuat]], [[:starwars:Alsakan/Legends|Alsakan]], [[:starwars:Argai|Argai]], [[:starwars:Axum|Axum]], [[:starwars:Anaxes/Legends|Anaxes]], [[:starwars:Atrisia/Legends|Atrisia]], [[:starwars:Metellos/Legends|Metellos]], and many others.

The planet was known for its [[swoop]] races.  It was the headquarters of [[:starwars:Aether Hypernautics|Aether Hypernautics]], [[:starwars:Danthe Artifice, Ltd.|Danthe Artifice, Ltd.]], [[:starwars:Gowix Computers|Gowix Computers]], [[:starwars:Gwain Spices|Gwain Spices]], and the [[:starwars:Mansom Corporation|Mansom Corporation]]. It was also home to [[:starwars:Coreguard Security Services|Coreguard Security Services]], which provided security to other companies on the planet.

Near the end of the [[:starwars:New Sith Wars|New Sith Wars]], the world was [[:starwars:Battle of Corulag (New Sith Wars)|captured]] by the [[:starwars:Brotherhood of Darkness/Legends|Brotherhood of Darkness]]. In [[:starwars:900 BBY/Legends|900 BBY]], five powerful families from Corulag called the [[:starwars:Quintad|Quintad]] left Corulag to the backwater planet of [[:starwars:Eriadu/Legends|Eriadu]] where they turned the planet into the "[[:starwars:Coruscant of the Outer Rim|Coruscant of the Outer Rim]]".

Before the Clone Wars, Chief Scientist [[:starwars:Frexton|Frexton]] of the [[:starwars:Corulag Academy|Corulag Academy]] had kidnapped the [[Force]] adept [[:starwars:Teela Panjarra|Teela Panjarra]] to study the [[Force]], so [[:starwars:Mace Windu/Legends|Mace Windu]], [[:starwars:Yoda/Legends|Yoda]], [[:starwars:Vel Ardox|Vel Ardox]], [[:starwars:Noro Zak|Noro Zak]], [[:starwars:Qui-Gon Jinn/Legends|Qui-Gon Jinn]], [[:starwars:Obi-Wan Kenobi/Legends|Obi-Wan Kenobi]], and [[:starwars:Adi Gallia/Legends|Adi Gallia]] infiltrated the Academy in an attempt to rescue her. This was complicated by an attack of [[:starwars:Bartokk|Bartokk]] assassins. Eventually, though, they were beaten and the child was rescued from the Academy's nursery.

Corulag remained loyal to the [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Galactic Republic]] during the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]]. It was represented by [[Senator]] [[:starwars:Zafiel Snopps|Zafiel Snopps]]. It should be noted that during this time, Corulag was represented independently from the other planets in the [[Bormea Sector]] who were represented at that time by [[Mon Mothma]].

After the [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] took over, the planet's population was extremely loyal to [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]], and its behavior was pointed out as model [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] behavior. The capital city of [[:starwars:Curamelle/Legends|Curamelle]] was also home to the [[:starwars:Corulag Academy|Corulag Academy]], an Imperial military academy. It was also during this time that Corulag was home to numerous prisons which held enemies of the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]].

Under the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]], Corulag was extremely influential, but after the death of Emperor Palpatine, the planet lost much of its influence, and languished under the [[New Republic]].

During the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]] and the campaign to enclose Coruscant, Corulag was invaded and subjugated. Immediately the planet was put to good use; it had been picked by [[Imperial High Command]] as a priority target due to its close proximity to Coruscant along the Perlemian and served as a strategic base of operations for the [[Imperial Navy]] for strikes throughout the Coruscant sector and nearby Republic bases. [[Grand Admiral]] [[Danik Kreldin]] chose Corulag as his base of operations in the months leading up to the [[Sixth Battle of Coruscant]]. However, with the fall of Coruscant to the Empire, Corulag's vital importance in the military scope has dwindled.

== Progeny ==
Between 10,000 and 4,000 BBY, several dozen Ordnance/Depot Worlds were established throughout the Inner and Mid Rim territories. Two in particular &mdash; [[Ord Vaxal]] and [[Rauther|Ord Thera]] &mdash; were established around 4,000 BBY with similar facilities and methods, and were garrisoned by Corulagi. Ord Vaxal would eventually succumb to neglect and the [[Hutts]] corruptive influence, while Ord Thera would remain undisturbed and unneeded to develop into the society of [[Rauther]].

== Notes ==
* Because of its Coruscanti roots, and its proximity to Coruscant and other Core Worlds, however Coruscant goes in allegiance so goes Corulag.

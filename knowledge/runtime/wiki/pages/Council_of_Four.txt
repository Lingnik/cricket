The '''Council of Four''' is one of the leading bodies in the [[New Sith Order|Sith Empire]] of [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]]. The Council of Four is presided upon by four of the Dark Lord's advisers, the [[Thane]]s of the Empire, each of whom hold control over their own noble Houses, and the Grand Knight of the [[Knights of Korriban]].

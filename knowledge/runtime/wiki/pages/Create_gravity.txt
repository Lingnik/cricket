This power has a variety of applications that affect the gravitational fields around objects and possibly people. This power does not actually change an object's density. Proximity and the size of the the subject affects difficulty, as does the intensity of the gravity desired. This power can make things (or individuals) feel heavier, or make things attach to each other. Jedi can create their own fields of gravity as well, or wield their own interdiction fields, though this would be an extraordinary usage.

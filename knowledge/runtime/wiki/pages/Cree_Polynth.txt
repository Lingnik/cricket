'''Cree "Poly" Polynth''' is a [[Duros]] currently working for the [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] due to a favor from one of his 'uncles' that holds sway within the CSA. Promoted to a [[Territory Division (CSA)#Ranks|Zone Director]], the Duros is responsible for overseeing the business in the [[Mutanda|Kiilaniri]] system and supervising the detainment facility on the moon of [[Justa Starport|Justa]]. Unfortunately, Polynth was not cut out for such work and instead tends towards acts of defiance in the form of the occasional sharpshooting, swoop racing, and piloting starships at breakneck speeds, all of which some might not consider becoming of a CSA authority figure.

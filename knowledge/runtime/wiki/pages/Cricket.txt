Originally the [[:w:c:starwars:R2-series astromech droid|R2 droid]] of [[Bazil McKenzie]], '''Cricket''' or '''Cricket McKenzie''' (originally known as R2-CT) has seen action as both an instrument of [[New Republic Intelligence]] and [[New Republic StarOps|StarOps]]. Sometime after Bazil's retirement, he was passed on to [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]]. He is the veteran of many sorties and survived several major battles against the [[Galactic Empire]]. Known for his intense dislike of all humans save for Bazil and Johanna, his homicidal and pyromaniacal tendencies have often landed him in trouble. He is also known to have a strange affinity for expensive ships and a holoporn collection so vast it puts all others to shame.

Cricket is Johanna's partner in crime, and has been seen with her nearly at all times. It was Cricket who was responsible for transmitting [[Danik Kreldin]]'s "confession" from [[Tatooine]] to the [[InterGalactic News|IGNews]], after he was kidnapped by Johanna during her fall to the [[Dark Side]]. He has also played an active role in looking after Johanna's various children. As they have grown the tables have turned as each in succession has tried to keep Cricket out of the various trouble he finds himself in.

In recent years he has spent a great deal of time with [[Atsvara Tarasar]], the daughter of Johanna's friend [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]]. He was an early investor in Atsvara's [[Golden Bantha Group]] becoming Executive Vice President of the firm. Since Golden Bantha Group's [[Report:Biscuit Buyout|buyout]] of [[Biscuit Baron]] he is now the Executive Vice President of the restaurant chain.

Cricket received his name from his New Republic registration code 'KRKT'.

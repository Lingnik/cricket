'''Crystal Reef''' was a [[Mon Calamari]] city on [[Dac]].

Constructed in late [[4 ABY]], Crystal Reef was designed a resort city for the fabulously wealthy of the [[The Galaxy|Galaxy]]. Plying the oceans of Dac's northern polar region, the city's viewing decks provided its passengers with views of impressive icebergs and icecaps as release from the resort's extensive gambling offerings. It was run by the [[:starwars:Crystal Reef Amusement and Tourism Council|Crystal Reef Amusement and Tourism Council]].

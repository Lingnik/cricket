== History ==

The DSV ''Templar'' started life in [[7 ABY]] as the ''Casohav's Breath''. A ship owned by a wealthy Caspian. Over the years however, it changed hands many times before ending up in the hands of pirates.  In [[22 ABY]], the vessel was captured by the [[New Republic]], its crew imprisoned and the vessel was in poor state of repair.

With the Republic growing, and the need for smaller patrol vessels becoming necessary, the ship was handed over to a research and development team to see about converting it to a pocket carrier, capable of holding multiple starfighters and shuttles for use in long range patrol missions. 

The hangar of the newly named Templar was enlarged, its engines, armor, and shielding increased to accommodate the higher mass, and it was sent for testing.  While such conversions had worked on [[Corellian Engineering Corporation|CEC]] [[CR90 Corvette|corvettes]] in the past, those ships were designed with modularity in mind, the [[SubPro]] vessel was not, and while technically a success in that the ship flew, was capable of carrying an impressive number of fighters and small craft, it was plagued with maintenance issues.  

The oversized hangar stressed the airframe during reentry.  The landing gear was prone to not retracting, the engines would often drain the power plant and if the crew was lucky, the shields would stay on for the duration of a flight.  While many of the problems stemmed from the ship leaving or entering atmosphere, the sheer number of problems that kept popping up meant that the ship often had to spend time in a drydock or on planet for repairs.  Considering the experiment a failure, the Templar was relegated to training purposes only.  The ship was great for simulating battle damage for engineering teams, or used as a boarding target for marines. 

This changed, however, in [[32 ABY]].  During a series of wargames, [[Colonel]] Han'bel Eckhart and his company of marines, along with a few navy pilots were stationed on the ship to act as the opposing force.  Instead of sitting in space, waiting to be boarded, the Colonel and his crew jumped the ship to hyperspace and have been on the run from the Republic navy while waging a vigilante war from the vessel against what they claim are forces hostile to the Republic and its citizens.

The ship, during its career has earned several nicknames.  The Pregnant Guppy, Guppy, Flying Coffin, Lost in Space, and countless others have been used to refer to the nearly cursed vessel.

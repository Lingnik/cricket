'''Dac''' is the home planet of the [[Mon Calamari]] and the [[Quarren]]. Almost entirely covered by water, the planet is sometimes erroneously called '''Mon Calamari''', a name which was given to the planet by humans.  The planet lies in the [[Mon Calamari System]], in a sector of the same name in the Outer Rim.  It is orbited by three moons, and the planet itself revolves around a single sun, called ''Daca'' by the inhabitants of the planet.

==Geography==

Dac is a planet almost completely covered by water. Expansive coral reefs surround the shallow regions, and marshy islands spot the globe, strafing sea level. It has a 21 hour day. Some of its more well known geographical attracts include the [[:starwars:Algae Sward|Algae Sward]], the [[:starwars:Hawaka islands|Hakawa]] and [[:starwars:Mkbuto Islands|Mkbuto Islands]], the [[:starwars:Seaweed Forest|Seaweed Forest]] and the [[:starwars:Seascape Mountains|Seascape Mountains]].

==History==

===Pre-Republic===

The Mon Calamari and the Quarren lived on separate depths of the vast oceans of the planet, until around 4,500 BBY, when the Quarren attacked the Mon Calamari.  When the Mon Calamari won the war, they raised the next generation of the dwindled race, teaching them their own values.

===Clone Wars===

During the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]], the planet was represented in the [[:starwars:Galactic Senate/Legends|Galactic Senate]] by the corrupt [[:starwars:Tikkes/Legends|Tikkes]], who allowed [[:starwars:Thalassian slavers/Legends|Thalassian slavers]] to operate in the Calamari Sector for a share of their profits. He was exposed in [[22 BBY]]. He escaped [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Republic]] custody on [[Coruscant]] and fled to [[:starwars:Geonosis/Legends|Geonosis]], where he became a member of the [[:starwars:Separatist Council/Legends|Separatist Council]] as leader of the [[:starwars:Free Dac Volunteers Engineering Corps/Legends|Free Dac]] movement, which sought to cut ties between Mon Calamari and the Republic. 

The planet was [[:starwars:Battle of Mon Calamari (Clone Wars)/Legends|attacked]] twice by the [[:starwars:Confederacy of Independent Systems/Legends|Confederacy of Independent Systems]]. First, Count [[:starwars:Dooku/Legends|Dooku]] devastated the world with the [[:starwars:Dark Reaper|Dark Reaper]].  Then the [[:starwars:Quarren Isolation League/Legends|Quarren Isolation League]] attempted to overthrow the [[:starwars:Mon Calamari Council|Mon Calamari Council]], the governing body of the planet. Jedi Master [[:starwars:Kit Fisto/Legends|Kit Fisto]] and a Republic task force were sent to assist the Mon Calamari, who emerged victorious.

After the Republic loyalty of the planet was cemented, the [[:starwars:Loyalist/Legends|Loyalist]] inhabitants agreed to a new arrangement: dual representation in the Senate, with [[:starwars:Tundra Dowmeia/Legends|Tundra Dowmeia]] representing the Quarren and [[:starwars:Meena Tills/Legends|Meena Tills]] representing the Mon Calamari. Both representatives were signatories of the [[:starwars:Delegation of 2000|Delegation of 2000's]] petition, and were arrested after Palpatine proclaimed himself emperor.

===Rebellion===

The planet was represented in the [[:starwars:Imperial Senate/Legends|Imperial Senate]] until it was harshly subjugated by the Empire, which saw the people of Mon Calamari as natural slaves whose industries and resources could be used to power the Imperial war machine in the Outer Rim. [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] cited the disloyal act of being involved with the Delegation of 2000 as being cause for the repression. The Imperials were aided during the occupation by the sabotage of the planetary shields by [[Quarren]] saboteurs such as [[:starwars:Seggor Tels|Seggor Tels]], swiftly conquering the planet.

The Mon Calamari started a [[:starwars:Mon Calamari Resistance|resistance movement]], which was unsuccessful at first. In retribution, [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]] ordered three of the planet's floating cities to be destroyed. Regardless, the Imperial attack did not succeed in breaking the Mon Calamari spirit but instead of extinguishing the resistance, it pushed the world's natives into open rebellion when the [[Mon Calamari]] joined the [[Quarren]] against their invaders. This grand, secret military effort finally repelled the Imperials and pushed them off their planet, their system and the whole sector.

Shortly after the uprising, [[Senator]] [[:starwars:Timi Rotramel|Timi Rotramel]] was killed by [[:starwars:Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Darth Vader]] while trying to convince other planets to join the Alliance. [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] immediately began work to counter the rebellion by way of propaganda. Under the direction of Lord Vader, the Imperial [[:starwars:sentientologist|sentientologist]] [[:starwars:Obo Rin|Obo Rin]] wrote the ''[[:starwars:Catalog of Intelligent Life in the Galaxy|Catalog of Intelligent Life in the Galaxy]]'', which falsely claimed that first contact between the Mon Calamari and galactic civilization occurred during the reign of the Galactic Empire. This was accepted as truth by many citizens of the Empire, further angering the Mon Calamari.

The [[Mon Calamari]] and the [[Quarren]] openly joined the [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Rebel Alliance]], and became an instrumental part of the war. The Mon Calamari, masters of starship construction, supplied the Alliance with badly needed [[Mon Calamari Star Cruiser|capital ships]] constructed in the [[Mon Calamari Shipyards]]. Prior to joining the Alliance, the Mon Calamari had only built passenger liners and deep space exploration vessels because of their peaceful ethos, but by heavily retrofitting these vessels, they were able to create capital ships that could take on the Galactic Empire's ships of the line.

===New Republic Era===

[[Image:Alien snp moncal female.jpg|thumb|right|200px|A Mon Calamari female.]]

Dac was a major holding of the New Republic, as it continued to produce their largest capital ships, and it also became a focus of several conflicts.

The Calamari System was captured by the Empire in [[9 ABY]].  In a [[Battle of Mon Calamari|fierce battle]], the Imperial Fleet and [[Death Star III|Third Death Star]] were able to destroy the Mon Calamari Shipyards and deal out severe damage to the Republic fleet and the planet itself.  For reasons unknown except to [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Bacharan Valak|Valak]], the planet was spared from complete destruction.

Following the destruction of the Death Star, the planet would again change hands as the [[Palpatine (imposter)|imposter Palpatine]] announced himself to the galaxy in [[11 ABY]].  The system was captured quickly by the [[True Empire]] in the [[Battle of Mon Calamari (Usurper)|opening battle]] of the [[Imperial Civil War]] of that year.

The New Republic took advantage of the conflict within the Empire, launching an [[Second Battle of Mon Calamari (Usurper)|attack]] on the True Empire's forces, which were considerably weakened from fighting on [[Athaniss]].  The planet was heavily defended, but a combined New Republic and [[Griffon Alliance]] force was able to break through and drive the occupiers out of the system.  Dac was liberated and soon rejoined the New Republic.

The [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]] would begin in [[14 ABY]] with the release of a virus on Dac.  The disease, which quickly spread along spacelanes throughout much of the galaxy, came to be known as the "[[Dacan Pandemic]]."  Later in the same conflict, [[Danik Kreldin]] and his [[Task Force Crimson Star]] [[Second Battle of Mon Calamari|attacked]] the New Republic at Dac in an attempt to draw attention away from the blockade and siege on [[Sullust]].  As a result of the Blitzkrieg orchestrated by Kreldin, Mon Calamari was effectively cut off from [[Coruscant]], severely dampening the effectiveness of the New Republic Military.

In [[15 ABY]], The planet became the de facto capital planet of the New Republic after the [[Retribution of Coruscant|Sixth Battle of Coruscant]], as well as the location where refugees with Republic ties began their new lives.  During this period of time, one session of the [[New Republic Senate]] was held there.

The planet came under siege by its own people in the [[Dacan Civil War]] of [[16 ABY]]. A series of unfortunate events ignited a bloody feud between the native Quarren and Mon Calamari species. During the civil war, a portion of the Quarren race would officially leave the New Republic, forming their own [[Quarren Survivalist Party]] and swearing their allegiance to [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Aleister Vadim|Vadim]].

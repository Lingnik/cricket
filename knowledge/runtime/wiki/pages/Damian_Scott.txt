'''Damian Scott''' was a relatively unknown figure in the galactic scene, preferring a low profile. He was a former police officer on [[Coruscant]] during the reign of the [[Galactic Empire]], but quit after a nasty incident with his superior. He joined the [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Rebel Alliance]] sometime after the [[:starwars:Battle of Yavin/Legends|Battle of Yavin]], served at [[:starwars:Hoth/Legends|Hoth]], and participating in assassination and subversion units until the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]]. After the formation of the [[New Republic]], he quit to pursue an independent life, fulfilling just about any task for anyone.

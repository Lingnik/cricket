'''Damon Tyr''' ( - [[7 ABY]]) was born on [[Coruscant]]. His father, '''Daxon Tyr''', was an [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] Admiral who died at the [[:starwars:Battle_of_Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]]. His mother was a surgeon and was killed during the [[New Republic]] [[Battle of Coruscant|occupation]] of Coruscant. He was in the [[Sub-Adult Group]] growing up, then joined the Academy as soon as he was eligible. He became TIE Pilot and was transferred to Phoenix Squadron on [[Orbital Station Guardian]], then was promoted to XO of [[Lightning Squadron]] under Lt. [[Labatiel Atrox]]. Atrox went off to the [[Bureau of Operations]], and Tyr become CO of Lightning Squadron stationed aboard the [[Strike-class Medium Cruiser|cruiser]] [[ISC Broadsword|''Broadsword'']].

He was later recruited by [[Lorn Rhys]] into the '[[Shadow Guard]]', and eventually made Commander of the Shadow Guard. He then got caught up in the [[Vigilant Movement]], which was a secret society that believed that [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]] was still alive and recuperating before returning to reclaim the Empire. [[Bacharan Valak]] found out about this secret society and had it crushed. Damon Tyr was in a very tricky situation and was forgiven of Treason by the [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]], but could not be placed in a position of trust. He was executed by a [[Imperial Royal Guard|Royal Guardsmen]] named [[Vassily Korolov]], the head of the Imperial Royal Guard, as he left the Emperor's Throne Room on [[Dreven]].

{{quote|The Sith must be ruled by a single leader, the very embodiment of the strength and power of the Dark Side. If the leader grows weak another must rise to seize the mantle.|[[:starwars:Darth Revan's Sith Holocron|Darth Revan's Sith Holocron]]|Darth Bane: Path of Destruction}}

The title of '''Dark Lord of the Sith''' originally referred to the emperor of the [[:starwars:Sith Empire/Legends|Sith Empire]], and later was taken by the leader of the [[Sith Order]]. Before [[:starwars:Skere Kaan|Skere Kaan]], there was only one Dark Lord of the Sith at a time, who was the head of the Order - in more recent periods, it was a title shared by multiple individuals simultaneously.  The term referred to individuals of both sexes.

The title ''Dark Lord of the Sith'' is often thought to be synonymous with the title ''[[:starwars:Darth/Legends|Darth]]'', but this is purely conjecture and has not been properly substantiated. Many Sith Lords have used the ''Darth'' title, but there is no evidence as to the synonymity of these two appellations.

The title of Dark Lord was possibly comparable to that of a [[Jedi]] [[:starwars:Grand Master/Legends|Grand Master]].

The first Dark Lord of the Sith was the leader of the exiled [[Dark Jedi]] who, after their defeat in the [[:starwars:Hundred-Year Darkness/Legends|Hundred Year Darkness]] conquered the [[:starwars:Sith (species)/Legends|Sith species]] and ruled over them as gods.

[[:starwars:Xendor|Xendor]], believed to be the first [[Dark Jedi]], is possibly the predecessor of the Dark Lords of the Sith, though he himself was never a Sith Lord.

After the fall of the [[:starwars:Sith Empire/Legends|Sith Empire]], [[:starwars:Naga Sadow/Legends|Naga Sadow]] gave the title to his apprentice, [[:starwars:Freedon Nadd|Freedon Nadd]]. The spirit of the dead Dark Lord [[:starwars:Marka Ragnos|Marka Ragnos]] then declared Nadd’s apprentice, [[:starwars:Exar Kun/Legends|Exar Kun]], Dark Lord of the Sith. 3,000 years later, Lord Kaan began the practice of giving the title to all members of the Order, thus making it a title shared by many Sith for the first time in their long history. After the institution of the [[:starwars:Rule of Two/Legends|Rule of Two]], the title was naturally held by only two members at a time, one being the [[:starwars:Sith Master/Legends|Sith Master]] and the other being the [[:starwars:Sith apprentice/Legends|Sith Apprentice]].

In recent times, after the destruction of the [[:starwars:Order of the Sith Lords|Order of the Sith Lords]], the title fell out of use as the Sith were effectively extinct. However, in [[8 ABY]], [[Bacharan Valak]] claimed the title after years of extensive studies in the Sith arts. He took on several apprentices, training them in the dark arts. Only his last apprentice, [[Vassily Korolov]] would become a Sith. 

In late [[15 ABY]], the title would be claimed by two men, [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] and [[Vassily Korolov|Darth Venger]], one working inside the [[Galactic Empire]], the other in the mysterious, [[New Sith Order]].

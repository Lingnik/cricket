[[Baron]] '''Darrian Fel'lya''' is a [[Bothan]] of Clan Alya. Originally a veteran combat officer of the [[New Republic FleetOps|New Republic Navy]], he eventually resigned and joined the [[New Sith Order]] taking much of his command staff with him. His commands have always been known for their diversity with [[Human|Humans]] being in the minority.

As a [[Fleet Admiral]], he commanded the First Fleet or "Shadow's Claw" of the [[Sith Navy]]. This fleet was tasked with protecting the area surrounding [[Ord Vaxal]], [[:starwars:Tholatin/Legends|Tholatin]] and the remains of [[Cochran]]. 

Having survived the Sith purges of [[Aldus Thel]], Baron Fel'lya returned the service of [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] when he reemerged as Imperator Damion I. As an [[Admiral]], Fel'lya now commands Task Force Shadow and serves a member of the Advisory Council of the [[Imperium]].

'''Darth Pain's fortress''' was a hidden fortress on [[Vadimus (planet)|Vadimus]], deep within the war-torn cluster of [[Vadimus]]. While the cluster was ruled over by the powerful [[Aleister Vadim]], [[Sinane Pantara|Darth Pain]] was able to secure her own fortress on the world that would serve as her base of operations. The fortress was abandoned after her death in [[12 ABY]]. The ruins were explored by [[Vassily Korolov]] during an expedition to uncover the treasury of the former Empress.

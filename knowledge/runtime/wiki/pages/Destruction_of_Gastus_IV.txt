The '''Destruction of Gastus IV''' commenced newly-crowned Emperor [[Bacharan Valak|Valak's]] [[Great Campaign]] to rebuild the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] and where the third [[Death Star III|Death Star]] was revealed to the galaxy.

[[Gastus IV]] possessed no military forces for its defense, allowing the Imperial Fleet to emerge from hyperspace and proceed immediately into firing range.

Several senior Republic officials including [[Luke Skywalker]] were present in the system aboard the luxury cruise liner ''[[Angry Rancor]]'' at the time of the attack. Imperial starfighters failed to capture the ''Rancor ''which fled the system stranding its commanders, [[Jack Raven]] and [[Rhea Zuri]], on the planet.

Within minutes of arrival, Gastus IV was destroyed.

The ''Angry Rancor'' fled to nearby [[Caspia]], where her passengers delivered the news of the atrocity and the new super-weapon to a shocked and horrified galaxy.

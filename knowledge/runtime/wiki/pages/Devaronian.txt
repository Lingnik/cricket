'''Devaronians''' are a humanoid species from the planet [[:starwars:Devaron/Legends|Devaron]]. Devaronian males are known for their horns, which when combined with their skin colour causes them to bare a striking similarity to the demons and devils of various cultures' mythology.

== Biology and Appearance ==

The "devilish" appearance of Devaronians is generally considered their most defining feature, though it is in fact really only present among males. Devaronians have a dramatic gender dimorphism in both appearance and temperament. In addition to their distinctive appearance the males were known for being aggressive and prone to wanderlust. The females are usually covered with thick fur that ranged from brown to white in colour and were hornless. A minority of females are furless with black circles over their eyes which are vestigial horn spots.

Devaronians are physically dense, weighing more than their appearance would generally suggest. Their blood is silver based and turns black when exposed. Devaronians possess two livers which serve to filter their blood with great efficacy making them highly resistant to poisoning. They are however, particularly effected by sulfur, using it as stimulate. Their livers have trouble processing it making long term use dangerous.

== Society and Culture ==

Devaronians were one of the first species to develop a primitive [[:starwars:Thumbledrive/Legends|thumble hyperdrive]]. Devaronian males are a frequent sight in ports throughout the galaxy. Driven by their wanderlust the males tend to gravitate towards occupations that allow for a great deal of travel, particularly as spacers and scouts. Though they tend to spend little time at home with their families they are known to sake seriously their obligations to them in terms of providing as much financial support as they can. Childrearing and community governance tends to be the domain of the females, who unlike the males, tend to be perfectly content to remain in place. They also tend to be content by the long absence of their mates, considering their restless to be disruptive.

== Devaronians in the Galaxy ==

In addition their homeworld of Devaron, significant Devaronian communities to could be found on a number of worlds. [[Fondor]], [[Ixin]], [[Ord Mantell]], and [[Ryloth]] in particular have the largest Devaronian communities outside of Devaron. Sizeable minorities could also be found on worlds such as [[:starwars:Bovo Yagen (planet)|Bovo Yagen]], [[:starwars:Concord Dawn/Legends|Concord Dawn]], [[:starwars:Darkknell/Legends|Darkknell]], [[:starwars:Drall (planet)|Drall]], [[:starwars:Keyorin|Keyorin]], [[:starwars:Onderon/Legends|Onderon]], [[:starwars:Ragmar V|Ragmar V]], [[:starwars:Talus/Legends|Talus]], and [[:starwars:Tresidiss|Tresidiss]]. Independent Devaronia colonies were also not unheard of.

The Devaronian minority on Ixin is particularly prominent with many families descended from immigrants who came to the world millennia ago when it was still lush. As a culture with its far share of imported mercenaries and nomad traders, many of the Devaronians who came to Ixin found a world compatible with their lifestyle. Devaronians are a frequent sight in desert caravan, merchant ships, and at princely courts, there are even a few Devaronian among the Ix Princes. Devaronians are also well represented among the Ix in the [[Caspian Defence Force]]. Vice Admiral [[Kath Samba]], the most senior officer in the [[Caspian Navy]] from Ixin is Devaronian.

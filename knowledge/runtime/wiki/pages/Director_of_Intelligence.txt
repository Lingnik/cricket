NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)

======================================================================
The Director heads [[New Republic Intelligence]] and reports directly to the Chief of State. Some of the Director's responsibilities include:

*Evaluating intelligence related to New Republic security and providing appropriate dissemination of Intel to those who may act on it including Republic Military or Government officials.

*Providing overall direction for and coordination of the collection of New Republic intelligence outside and within member planets through agent sources authorized to collect such information.

*Establish and enforce policies in regards to galactic intelligence gathering.

*Over see the training of new recruits.

*Manage NRI resources.

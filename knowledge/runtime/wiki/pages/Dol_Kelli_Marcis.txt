'''Dol Kelli Marcis''', a recovered Corellian spice junkie, was a starship pilot and technician. He was good at fixing droids and maintaining his old [[YT-1300]], the ''Elliptic Jade''. He was not a very well educated man, though he did have a compassion and love of all sentients (and even droids), which he considered to be something a school could or would never instill within him under the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]]. His understanding of others had helped him on many occasions, smoothing over relations with seedier employers, and bought him, if not alliances, more time to tramp the [[The Galaxy|galaxy]]. He was not a cowardly man, though he did try to avoid violence if possible, and if had no other choice, would take the most direct, effective, non-lethal, non-damaging route. He was quick on his feet and was rather strong, and was always known to have a quick wit, cynical outlook, and an altogether intuitive wisdom about life.

Dol Marcis founded Marcis Mechanical and Technical in [[12 ABY]], operating out of a disused warehouse in [[Mos Eisley]] on [[Tatooine]] the company employed a range of technicians and security staff, and performed upgrades of a less than legal and rather informal nature for some of the galaxy's then most infamous criminals.

MMT and Dol Marcis vanished from galactic life with the emergence of [[Begula the Hutt]], who introduced a policy of subjugating local businesses.

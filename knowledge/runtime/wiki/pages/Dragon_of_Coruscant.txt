The '''Dragon of Coruscant''' is a small, independent public relations company founded in [[14 ABY]] by [[Chantinelle Thistleborn]] after her retirement from the politicial scene. The name of the company is derived from the founder's personal love for the mystical creature from [[Coruscant]]. While Ms. Thistleborn bases her operations from the resort city of [[Orleanon]] on [[Caspar]], she still brings the Dragon to several stunning locations throughout the Galaxy.

Because of Chantinelle's experiences in diplomacy and politics throughout the years, she can utilize her personal connections to host memorable events for any occasion. The guest list for any of her events is usually extensive.

'''Drayson Honos''' ([[:starwars:50_BBY|50 BBY]] - ), was a [[Fleet Admiral]] in the [[Imperial Navy]]. Of most significance, he was the commanding officer of the [[Imperial First Fleet]] until his resignation in [[14 ABY]] as a protest to [[Desolation of Cochran|operations over Cochran]].  He and his entire command staff resigned, promptly creating an absence in tactical leadership for Frontier Operations in the [[Galactic Empire]]. 

Drayson Honos was born to a wealthy banking family on Raltiir, and his brother Achillies Honos was a famous [[Senator]] for that world up until its retaking by the Galactic Empire. Drayson Honos was responsible for much of the early successful operations in the Empire and was a defacto supreme commander of the Imperial Navy until [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign's]] first disappearance into the [[The Galaxy#Outer Rim|Outer Rim]].

Rumors persist that Drayson Honos passed up a promotion to [[Grand Admiral]], allowing the upstart Danik Kreldin to take that position.

[[File:Dunwelton.jpg|300px|right|thumb]] '''Dunwelton''' is the unofficial financial capital of [[Caspar]]. Sprawled on a broad basin in the middle of the '''Stronico''' continent in the southern hemisphere, it is one of the few major cities on Caspar to be blessed with stable weather. It is Caspar's largest city by area and its second largest by population.

== History ==

'''Yaeberron''' as it was originally known was the most recent of Caspar's major cities to be settled. It differed from other Caspari cities in a number of ways, it was both far from the mountains and had a limited coastline. Though [[Sarian|Sarians]] had made a few small settlements nearby in the years prior to its founding, Yaeberron was quickly settled by [[Human|Humans]] had far less Sarian influence than other Caspari cities. Its mild climate proved a boon to agriculture, and though the city remained small its farms and ranches brought great wealth.

By the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] era the city began to grow rapidly, by the advent of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] it was approaching [[Plaxton]] in population size. During the '''[[First Renaissance of Innovation|Renaissance of Innovation]]''' Yaeberron benefited the most, witnessing a surge of industry, commerce, and population. It soon eclipsed Plaxton in wealth and grew to become Caspar's most populous city. A number of corporations, particularly in the financial and communications industry would relocate to Yaeberron.

The Renaissance of Innovation has such a profound effect on the city that after the death of its architect, [[Presav]] [[Lyr Dunwell]], the city as renamed in his honour, becoming '''Dunwelton''' in [[7 ABY]]. During the heyday of the Caspian Democratic Union, anchored by the wealth of Dunwelton, the provincial capital, the '''Strona Province''' alongside the '''[[Krittain Major|Majorus Province]]''' was the Union's most lucrative.

== Recent Years ==

Though hit as hard as any other Caspari city in terms of death toll from the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]], as Caspar's most modern and wealthy city it weathered the calamity better than most. Ironically this worked against the city in certain respects as charities such as [[The Mission]] focused their efforts on supporting survivors of other city's more, and consequently refugees from the [[Mergansar|Mergani]] [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]] tended to settle in those cities. While Dunwelton would remain Caspar's wealthiest city, Plaxton would reemerge as its most populous.

[[File:Nelivve Lameberchain.jpg|thumb|Nelivve Lameberchain]] Dunwelton would emerge with a certain political clout, however, with [[Nelivve Lameberchain]], a former delegate representing Dunwelton in the [[Union Assembly]], serving as Provisional Presav of the [[Caspian Union|Caspar Union]]. After the adoption of new constitution and the announcement of election for a permanent [[Union Council]], Lameberchain declined to run. This was much to the disappointment of the [[Legacy|Legacy Party]], who felt he would be a strong candidate for the party.

[[File:Stonewin Kirkmount.jpg|left|thumb|Stonewin Kirkmount]] In Lameberchain's place, Legacy ran Stonewin Kirkmount as a candidate. He proved an even stronger candidate, easily defeating the other candidates, and being one of the only two Legacy candidates to be elected. Kirkmount would go one to become the Deputy Leader of Legacy, and Maritime Minister in the coalition government. Though rarely in the press, he is a widely respected figured in Caspian politics.<br><br>

== Trivia ==

* Dunwelton Base, the largest [[Caspian Defence Force]] installation on the Stronico continent, and second largest on Caspar, after Trinumvira Base, is located on the outskirts of Dunwelton. It is hosts to three squadrons each of [[Apex-Class Starfighter|Apex-Class]] and [[Vector-Class Starfighter|Vector-Class]] starfighters.
* The Financial Section of the [[Caspian Intelligence Service]] is based in the city in an office tower which publically acknowledged as an intellignce service facility.
* The [[Shearwater-class Frigate|Shearwater-Class Light Cruiser]], ''CMS Dunwelton'' is named for the city.

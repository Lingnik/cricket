When the [[Galactic Empire]] destroyed [[Sluis Van]] in [[9 ABY]], one of the many refugees who eventually found their way to [[Caspian Sector|Caspian Space]] was a gifted technician named [[Sirlahn Inglet]], a distant relative of the former Khedive, [[:starwars:Candobar Inglet|Candobar Inglet]]. The Sluissi would find himself working at [[SubPro|Sublight Products Corporation]] as a ship engineer, eventually working his way up in the company to being one of their most senior non-executive employees.

Though he was a ship engineer by profession, Sirlahn was also gifted with [[Droid|droids]]. As a bit of a hobby, he would spend years puttering on some custom battle droids, based on [[:starwars:Baktoid Combat Automata/Legends|Baktoid Combat Automata's]] [[:starwars:E4 baron droid|E4 Baron Droid]]. A version of these droids would make their debut during the [[RPlog:Election Night Mayhem|attack]] on the [[Kichnar Orbital Station|Kichnar]] election returns when Sirlahn was elected Councillor of Kichnar. 

Sirlahn would soon find himself not only in charge of Sublight Products Corporation following the formation of the [[Crestian Tarasar|Tarasar]] administration, but Minister of Science and Technology for the whole of the [[Caspian Union]]. Droid tinkering would take a back seat to the needs of the fleet until the [[Xi Char]] joined the Union and [[Haor Chall Engineering]] was reformed on [[Charros IV]].

Now with Haor Chall Engineering as a collaborator, work on the '''E5 Baron Droid''' began in earnest. The original model lacked dexterity and lacked the energy shields of the models that preceded it. What it did boast was superior strength and tracking skills and the ability to work autonomously. It could track an opponent with motion, heat, energy, and sonic detectors. Visual stimuli were processed through the droid's single photoreceptor, which could also produce a blinding spotlight to identify and track its targets. 

Sirlahn and the Xi Char would play to its strength, as well as that of the Union, using [[olumic]] in addition to [[:starwars:Arcetron/Legends|arcetron]] in the new plating design to add retardation of electromagnetic penetration to its features. Like the majority of Haor Chall Engineering's new and updated designs, the main goal for the E5 Baron Droid was defence. While the potential use on offensive operations was considered, large-scale deployment was not expected nor was use in rugged terrain. Its built in blaster cannons would also limit its functionality to purely combat roles.

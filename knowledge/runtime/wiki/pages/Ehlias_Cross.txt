'''Ehlias Cross''' is a [[human]] male born of the planet Kuat. Most who encounter him discover that he is a roguish-type man,  and a charmer of the opposite gender. He is also an intelligent man with a sharp wit and a perceptive mind. Having been raised as an orphan by the streets of Kuat, Ehlias has grown to be a fiercely independent person. His manner is typically very casual which fits with the man's almost total lack of ambition.  "Nah, jus too much work fer an old thug like me." He has been heard saying on more than a few occasions. Although he is a man without a reliance on a moral compass with regards to the value of life, he can be a very caring and honest individual. 

Though originally a member of the [[Maffi / Zhao]], he currently works for [[Tritus Vaticus]] as a bouncer and security for the illustrious Club Utinni, not to mention other more clandestine work. Taking pride in his work, and the free food and drink that working at a club entails, Ehlias performs his duties with a fearless manner, his skill with a blade being of much use in such a dangerous profession. In the time he has been with Gundark Industrial he has become close friends with others whom he works with, causing him to occasionally question his current lifestyle.

'''Eithne''', or '''Ethy''', was born an only child of a well-known [[Sarian]] family on Caspar.  She was taught by private tutors in business and etiquette in hopes that she would take over the family's prestige and continue the line of integrity in the family.  Her parents introduced her to many of the eligible young men in hopes she would settle down on Caspar, but Eithne needed to explore the universe and see everything there was to see before settling down.  Her parents, having that same desire and having explored the universe themselves, sent her into the galaxy to find her way.

Eithne travelled the stars, eventually gravitating towards the [[Corporate Sector Authority]] region, as business was close to her heart, and became a Public Relations Specialist for a group called Abyssal Creations under the leadership of a Darkling named [[Malif Tal-Tahn]].  From there she moved to become a field correspondent with [[InterGalactic News|IGNews]].  By doing this she fed her need to explore, while still maintaining her family dignity and keeping her feminine grace.

'''Electrum''' was a rare semi-precious metal mined from a handful of planets, including [[:starwars:Abregado-rae|Abregado-rae]]. It could be refined to a brilliant finish, and was used in such objects of prestige as jewelry, sword pommels, and lightsabers.

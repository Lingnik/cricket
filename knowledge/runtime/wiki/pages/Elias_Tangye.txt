'''Elias Tangye''' ([[16 BBY]] - ) is a male [[Human]] and [[smuggling|smuggler]] from the world of [[:starwars:Concord Dawn/Legends|Concord Dawn]].

== Background ==
=== Early Life (16 BBY - [[8 ABY]]) ===
Elias was born on Concord Dawn to Alan and Kensa Tangye, and spent his childhood on their small farm near Roscarrock Settlement. Wanting something better for their son than the difficult existence of a Concord Dawn farmer, they saved what money they could, and by the time he was 17 they were able to pay for flight lessons for him. Within about a year, he was flying the produce freighter route to nearby Ithor.

Back on his homeworld, however, Elias couldn't seem to stay out of trouble. A series of fights and run-ins with the [[:starwars:Journeyman Protector/Legends|Journeyman Protectors]] came to a head when, at 24, he killed a farmer in a bar fight. Forced to steal his freighter and flee, he eventually arrived on [[Nar Shaddaa]], where he believed he'd be safe from pursuit by Concord Dawn authorities.

=== Nar Shaddaa ([[8 ABY]] - [[14 ABY]]) ===

The sale of his ship provided Elias enough money to live on for a short time, but as the money ran out he found himself struggling to find steady employment. For the first few months, he took what odd jobs were available, whether as a courier, a cargo loader, or just a petty mugger. Only about a year after his arrival did he begin receiving contract work as a debt collector and enforcer for the [[Hutt]]s and other criminals. Five years on the Smuggler's Moon had him looking for a change of scenery, however, and with what money he'd saved he moved on to [[Corellia]].

=== On the Move ([[14 ABY]] - [[15 ABY]]) ===

After leaving Nar Shaddaa, Elias continued the same kind of work that had kept him fed on the Smuggler's Moon. Over the next two years, he moved from Corellia, to [[Ord Mantell]], to [[Tatooine]] and back again, never settling in one place for very long. As time went on, using his piloting abilities and borrowed ships, he was even able to add the occasional smuggling job to his resume.

== Personality ==

By those who knew him on Nar Shaddaa, Elias is described as a friendly and personable but essentially violent man, not particularly short-tempered, but ruthless when provoked. He is not the type to bring up his past in conversation, but will usually talk about it openly and frankly if asked.

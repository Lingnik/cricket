'''Ethan Katana''' was the nephew of [[Jack Raven]] who inherited the ''[[Angry Rancor]]'' cruiseship on his uncle's death. He was a sculptor and artist. He frequently preferred to fly around in a smaller ship that he purchased after his uncle's death, named 'Expatriate'.

Katana attended the [[Jedi Academy]] on [[Yavin 4]] to undergo training in the [[Force]], and was caught up in the [[Third Jedi Purge]]. He was the only student of [[Luke Skywalker]] to survive. 

After the Purge, Ethan spent a long time hunting [[Cort Stasus]] who had been the student who initiated the purge and attack on Luke Skywalker's Jedi students.  Thinking to try looking on [[:starwars:Dagobah/Legends|Dagobah]] he flew to the desolate swamp world.  He hit magnetic storms while coming through the atmosphere and his ship crash landed, killing a his cyborg dog named Bram.  Katana barely survived the fiery crash himself, and using an obscure force power he called 'Warp Matter' had to devise a metallic suit to encase and sustain his body.

After spending two years on Dagobah, he returned to the rest of the galaxy a much changed man.  His ordeal had caused him to turn toward the [[Dark Side]] with an alter ego named '''Darth Revenant''' beginning to surface from within.

Some time in 14 or 15 ABY, Ethan disappeared again.

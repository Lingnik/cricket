'''Eva Sargent''' ([[16 BBY]] - [[11 ABY]]) was a [[New Republic]] ambassador to the [[Caspian Democratic Union]].

== Biography ==
Eva Sargent was born on [[Coruscant]] during the Imperial period. Her mother was Magan Sargent, an Imperial finance administrator, and her father was Li Sargent, an Empire [[HoloNet]] Engineer. Growing up, she showed an early talent for languages. Through her father's professional connections, she obtained a volunteer position in the [[:starwars:Galactic Senate/Legends|Galactic Senate]] at age 13. She remained as a volunteer in the Senate until just before her 15th birthday, when it was disbanded. At that time, Imperial propaganda declared that [[:starwars:Alderaan/Legends|Alderaan]] was to be placed under permanent quarantine because of disease that threatened to wipe out the entire population. But Eva was disturbed by rumors about the successful 'test' of a new secret class of warship. At age 18, Eva entered the Northern Linguistics Institute of Coruscant, intending to pursue a career in translation. A few months later, the Empire was defeated at the Battle of Endor and Coruscant changed hands. Her parents left the planet during the mass exodus of Imperial loyalists, but Eva stayed behind and applied for New Republic citizenship.

Eva worked at odd jobs for the next few years, before deciding to use her talents to fully support the New Republic. As representative to [[Caspia]], her first task was to negotiate construction of the New Republic's permanent embassy in [[Plaxton]]. For personal reasons, Eva 'went native' and became a Caspian citizen in [[10 ABY]]. She lived on Caspar until her death in a tragic shuttle accident in [[11 ABY]].

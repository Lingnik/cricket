The ExO is arguably the most powerful individual in the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector]]. Empowered by the [[Direx Board]] to oversee the day to day operations of the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Authority]], the ExO is the chief executive of the [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] and supervises the actions of the division [[Viceprex (CSA)|Viceprexes]]. He or she is treated as a political leader abroad and holds a social ranking commensurate with that role.

Usually promoted from a [[Viceprex (CSA)|Viceprex]] position, the ExO may also be someone lured away from another company by the vast "profits and privileges" promised by the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Authority]]. By nature of his office, the ExO must be completely dedicated to the [[Corporate Sector Authority]], resigning all other positions and exchanging all stocks from other companies for [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] stock.

The ExO, once selected by the [[Direx Board]], has no fixed term.  At any time, the board may call for a no-confidence vote, and, if it passes, the ExO is immediately stripped of his power.

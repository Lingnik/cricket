'''Ezkhil i'Falzin''' was a Force-sensitive priest who became the target of an [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] hunt in late [[13 ABY]]. He and his two siblings, a sister and brother, had escaped their world, Izin, which had fallen under the control of [[Reyon Mining]], a [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] corporation run by [[Paris Sieara]]. Ezkhil briefly escaped [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign's]] [[Task Force Hammer]] by traveling to [[Caspar]], barely surviving the [[Imperial Bombardment of Tatooine|the encounter]] at [[Tatooine]]. On Caspar, a [[Caspian Jedi Hunt|hunt]] began for the rogue Jedi, with the Empire cooperating loosely with Caspian authorities. [[Danik Kreldin]], having been demoted down to [[Captain]], led the hunt for Ezkhil through the subways of Caspar, and a budding rivalry between the two began.

Ezkhil would escape Kreldin and Task Force Hammer, but his siblings did not meet a similar fate. [[Grand Moff]] [[Scaven Marx]] had captured his two siblings on [[Nar Shaddaa]], and there, in a private meeting with Paris Sieara, [[Gavin Shai]], and Kreldin, gained complete control over Ezkhil's world. Scaven attempted to execute Ezkhil's sister, but the intervention of Ezkhil's brother had him take the blaster shot intended for her.

Ezkhil went to the rescue, engaging in a brief duel with Scaven. The fate of his surviving sibling is unknown. Izin fell to the forces of Reylon Mining, its residents pressed into contract service in the exploitation of the planet's resources. When last seen, Ezkhil was in [[Hutt Space]] on [[Nar Shaddaa]] attempting to gather followers into the religion of which he had declared himself High Priest.

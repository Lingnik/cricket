'''F'rick Lavs''' was the planetary governor of [[:starwars:Ord Biniir|Ord Biniir]] in the [[Galactic Empire]] after [[14 ABY]]. He was accused of slave trafficking and kidnapping Imperial citizens, but had been cleared after a tribunal arranged by the sector's [[Moff]]. He was killed in [[17 ABY]] in a terrorist attack where unknown assailants drove explosive-laden repulsorvehicles into the government administration building.

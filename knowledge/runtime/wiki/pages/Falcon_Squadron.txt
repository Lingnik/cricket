'''The Second Special StarOps Squadron''', better known simply as '''Falcon Squadron''' or as '''The Falcons''', is an [[A-wing]] squadron attached to the [[New Alderaan]] military garrison at [[Ord Mantell]]. It is part of the [[New Republic 22nd Starfighter Wing|22nd Starfighter Wing]] in the [[New Republic StarOps|New Republic Starfighter Corps]]. The Commanding Officer is [[Colonel]] [[Leo Corak]], a human male.

== History ==

The squadron was stationed aboard [[NRSD_Crusader|NRSD ''Crusader'']] before being transferred to the flagship of the [[New Republic Navy]]'s [[New Republic Second Fleet|Second Fleet]], the [[NRSD Reprisal|NRSD ''Reprisal'']]. It was later moved to Ord Mantell in [[15 ABY]].

=== Recent Events ===

Falcon Squadron was called to recon service before the eruption of the [[Second Battle of Nar Shaddaa]] near the end of [[15 ABY]].

Most recently, Falcon Squadron did forward observation and capital ship defense at the infamous [[RPlog:Dathomir Raid|Dathomir Raid]] in the year of [[16 ABY]].

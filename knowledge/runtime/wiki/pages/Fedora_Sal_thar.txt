'''Fedora Sal'thar''', a [[Human|Abregadoan-human]] female, was the [[New Republic 22nd Starfighter Wing]]'s Wing Commander as of [[15 ABY]]. She held the rank of [[Major General]].

{{NPC}}

=== OOC Note ===

Fedora Sal'thar is an [[Non-Player Character|NPC]] figurehead for the [[New Republic StarOps|StarOps]] branch of the [[New Republic Military]]. All StarOps efforts are coordinated by the StarOps [[Branch Head]]. Fedora Sal'thar was created to maintain a steady character presence at the "top of the food chain" for the [[Player Character]] division of StarOps. Fedora's orders and actions will essentially come from the [[Branch Head]] and other faction administrators.

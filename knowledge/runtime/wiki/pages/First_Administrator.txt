The '''First Administrator''' was the head of the [[New Republic General Ministry]]. The First Administrator was charged with setting down the protocol of the Ministries, and coordinated with the [[Minister of State]] information to the [[Chief of State]] and the [[Inner Council]].

Presently, the '''First Administrator''' is [[Lin_Ardyom|Lin Ardyom]] ''(NPC, see [[Del Marx]])''.

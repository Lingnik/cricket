The '''First Bank of the New Republic''' was the [[New Republic]]'s foremost financial institution and served as the forefront of the [[New Republic Corporate Operations]], headed up by the [[President of the First Bank of the New Republic|President]] of the Bank. 

Founded in [[4 ABY]] after the [[Battle of Coruscant|liberation]] of [[Coruscant]] and established on [[Sullust]], the Republic's federal bank expanded quickly to meet the demands of the growing New Republic. 

Issuing the [[New Republic Credit]], the Federal Bank successfully funded the government and military of the Republic, and helped secure projects for galactic expansion and reconstruction. 

In [[14 ABY]], the bank's main office would be shut down by the [[Galactic Empire]] after the [[Fourth Battle of Sullust|conquest]] of Sullust. It continued to operate elsewhere despite its heavy loss.

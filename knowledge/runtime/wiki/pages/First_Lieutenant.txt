'''First Lieutenant''' is a military rank that represents an officer at the middle of the commissioned rank scale. It is often parallel to the Naval rank of to the Naval rank of [[Lieutenant]] or [[Ensign]].

=== In Imperial Service ===
{{rank plate|[[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]]|}}

A first lieutenant in the [[Imperial Army]] commanded a [[Platoon (Imperial Army)|platoon]].

<br clear=all>

=== In New Republic Service ===

First Lieutenants in the '''[[New Republic GroundOps|New Republic Army]]''' were typically well-experienced, and had several combat missions under their belts. They were given command of their own platoon within a company, or acted as an executive officer for the Company's Commanding Officer. Support personnel who held this rank were put in charge of their own obligations, such as Field Surgeon, Logistics Officer, etc.

In the '''[[New Republic StarOps|New Republic Starfighter Corps]]''', First Lieutenants were talented pilots with a few combat sorties under their belts. They were often given command of their own flight within a squadron. Support personnel who held this rank were be put in charge of their own obligations, such as Flight Surgeon, Logistics Officer, etc.

The Five Worlds Affair was a series of civil disruptions on [[Corellia]] that began in mid-[[19 ABY]]. The problems began with a series of labor protests at the Corellian shipyards, transforming into a more violent affair as those who thought they could do without the Empire grew more and more confident with their initial successes bombing various CorSec and COMPNOR offices.

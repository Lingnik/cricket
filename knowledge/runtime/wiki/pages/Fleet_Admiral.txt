'''Fleet Admiral''' is a military rank.

== [[Caspian Navy]] ==

'''Fleet Admiral''' is the second most senior rank in the [[Caspian Navy]] ranking below [[Sar Admiral|Sar Admirals]] and ranking above all other [[Admiral|Admirals]]. The rank of Fleet Admiral did not exist in the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] with the rank of Sar Admiral being equivalent. Upon the restructuring of the Navy in the [[Caspian Union]] following the election of the [[Crestian Tarasar|Tarasar]] Administration, Sar Admiral was reconceived to be a singular post and not held by multiple officers (though in practice past Sar Admirals could maintain the rank as a legacy. 

The rank of Fleet Admiral was instituted for the commanding officers of Navy's four fleets, though the [[Caspian 4th Fleet]] is commanded directly by Tarasar as Sar Admiral currently. The former Fleet Admiral of the independent [[Krittish Navy]] Davik Nord also holds the rank even though he only commands a Defence Group and is subordinate to the [[Caspian 2nd Fleet|Caspian 2nd Fleet's]] Fleet Admiral Katha Pushtelli.

A '''Fleet Admiral''' is a rank in the [[Imperial Navy]], directly above [[Admiral]] in the rank structure and immediately below [[High Admiral]]. A Fleet Admiral commands an Imperial [[Imperial Navy#Fleet|Fleet]].

A Fleet Admiral reports directly to their Sector's [[High Admiral]] or [[Moff]], and hold authority over all subordinate Admirals and [[Imperial Navy#System force|System forces]] under his command.

In certain cases, a Fleet Admiral may command an Imperial task force, an independent Imperial deployment assigned a specific objective for a short- or long-term campaign.

A Fleet Admiral is equivalent to a [[High General]] in the [[Imperial Army]] or a [[Force Marshal]] in the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]].

'''Fondor''' was a world famous for its extensive orbital [[:Category:Starwright Facilities|shipyards]], exceeded only by those at [[Corellia]] and [[Kuat]].  These shipyards were associated with the [[:starwars:Techno Union/Legends|Techno Union]] prior to the [[:starwars:Clone Wars|Clone Wars]]. [[:starwars:Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Darth Vader's]] [[Executor-class Star Dreadnought|Super Star Destroyer, ''Executor'']] was constructed in these yards.

Fondor was located in the Freeworlds Region of [[:starwars:Tapani Sector/Legends|Tapani Sector]], a sector technically part of the [[The Galaxy#Colonies|Colonies]] but so old and well-regarded that many considered it a part of the [[The Galaxy#Core Worlds|Core Worlds]].  Fondor itself was completely industrialized eons ago, and its surface scabbed with excavations, factories, cooling towers, steam tunnels, and offices, all related to the planet's central role in [[:Category:Ships|starship]] construction.  The planet also housed hectares of laborers' barracks for the dockworkers whose jobs were overhead. The Fondorians were baseline human, but with distinctive hairstyles.  The [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] established the '''Fondor-Gandeal Hyperlane''' to facilitate import of raw materials to the [[:starwars:Fondor Shipyards/Legends|Fondor Shipyards]] from [[:starwars:Gandeal|Gandeal]]. Fondor was a main market for the Besadii [[Hutt]]s.

=== An Exclusive Task ===
The contract to build the ''Executor'' came somewhat unexpectedly, as [[Kuat Drive Yards]] was the manufacturer of the [[Imperator-class Star Destroyer|Empire's Star Destroyers]] and later instances of the ''Executor''-class.  The Empire required complete secrecy in the project, closing off all nonmilitary traffic in the system.  Though the dockyard bosses were angered by the fact that they could no longer serve their commercial clients, the fact the project took up the majority of the yard's resources and the legendary wrath of Vader kept them in check.

In order to efficiently provide resources to the ''Executor'' project, the Empire created a classified [[:Category:Trade Routes|route]] to the planet [[:starwars:Gandeal|Gandeal]].  The '''Gandeal-Fondor Hyperlane''' remained a well kept secret decades after the ''Executor'' was completed.

As an essential manufacturer of [[Star Destroyer]]s, albeit unique variants, for the [[Galactic Empire]], the shipyards became a target by the [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Rebel Alliance]]. In one instance, a prototype Star Dreadnought with a cloaking device was under construction. [[Rogue Squadron]] penetrated the shipyards in stolen [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] starfighters and terminated the project, preventing its completion.

'''Force Storm''' was a [[Dark side of the Force|dark side]] [[The Force|Force]] power, and possibly the most powerful Force ability known. Force Storm was actually not a ''storm''; in fact, it was the ability to create [[:starwars:hyperspace_wormhole|hyperspace wormhole]]s via the dark side of the Force. A Force Storm could range from the size of a small [[:starwars:Starship|starship]], like a [[:starwars:Freighter|freighter]], to huge maws capable of obliterating whole starfleets. The smaller storms appeared more easily controlled from a greater distance. No Force users other than [[:starwars:Palpatine|Palpatine]] have exhibited this ability. 
 
[[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] Palpatine had recorded the origin of this destructive ability in ''[[:starwars:The_Book_of_Anger|The Book of Anger]]''. It stated that through a simple act of ''will'' he was able to generate energy storms, vastly destructive, virtually unstoppable. The Emperor also confessed that he was not able to completely control these storms after he triggered their onset.

Use of this power requires the focusing of hate and anger to an almost palpable degree and there is considerable danger involved. Some are able to create Force storms, but fail at harnessing what they have foolishly unleashed. Often, those who fail to control the storm are themselves consumed and destroyed. If the user is destroyed, the storm dissipates within minutes.

When the Force user attempts to create a Force storm, the user must determine the diameter and the amount of damage (the damage dice are capital scale). If the Jedi fails any of the rolls, the storm is summoned with the desired damage, but it attempts to consume the summoner. The summoner can attempt unusual maneuvers with the storm, such as to create a vortex to draw unwary victims to a specified point, at a +10 to difficulty.

The only recorded event of a Force storm was in [[15 ABY]] over [[Dac]], which saw the return of the ancient Jedi, [[Yindo Ola]], who had somehow survived within the storm. Although unverified, it was possible this storm was created by [[Aleister Vadim]].

==Alter Difficulty==
Heroic. Modified by proximity. Modified by size of storm desired. +5 for 100 meters or less, +10 for 100 meters to one kilometer, +15 for a base of one kilometers, and +2 to difficulty for every kilometer diameter in size.

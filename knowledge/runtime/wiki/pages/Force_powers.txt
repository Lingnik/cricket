'''Force powers''' were the manifestations of a [[Jedi]], [[Sith]], or other Force-adept's connection with the [[Force]]. According to the [[:starwars:the Force#Potentium_and_unitary_views_of_the Force|theory]] that the Force is one, the light and dark powers did not actually describe the Force, but rather, defined how an individual used it; whether to directly harm his enemies, or help himself overcome situations. Thus the aggressive or vengeful abilities inherently belonged to the [[dark side]], while those which were healing, defensive, self-empowering, or diversionary belonged to the light side]].

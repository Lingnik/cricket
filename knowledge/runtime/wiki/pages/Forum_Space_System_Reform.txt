Time to enact a reform in the SW1 space system. The coded coordinates for planets that DSpace uses is vastly inaccurate, and leads to a lot of confusion ICly and OOCly (for instance, Coruscant is ICly located a few jumps from Chandrila, but their coordinates in DSpace put them quite far apart). I know a few other players agree with me (See [[Talk:System Transponders]]). The first change that should be enacted is the most obvious: Coruscant's IC location is 0,0,0. It is essentially the "center" of the galaxy, from where all other coordinates are based. Then we can start working on the other planets. --[[User:Danik Kreldin|Danik Kreldin]] 22:34, 5 March 2007 (UTC)

In the wake of the [[Imperial Civil War]] and the [[Battle of Mutanda (GCW)|Battle of Mutanda]], the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] sought to wipe out the [[Griffons Alliance]]. Failure to capture [[Cochran]] slowed the Imperial advance, but nevertheless, [[Pride-1]] was to be targetted and destroyed.

Attacking in two large waves, the first one led by [[Danik Kreldin]], the second by [[Salin Fawzy]], the Empire brought a massive fleet to bear on the space station.

The first wave was unsuccessful in breaking through the Griffon defense fleet, but the second wave reinforced it and soon the Imperial forces had Pride-1 under heavy bombardment. The crew of the station, however, activated the self-destruct on the station, destroying it and taking its crew with it.

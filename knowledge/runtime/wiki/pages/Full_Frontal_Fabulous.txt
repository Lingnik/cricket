== Overview ==

The '''Full Frontal Fabulous''' is a bespoke, performance-class [[CR90 Corvette|CR90 Corellian Corvette]], originally manufactured by [[Corellian Engineering Corporation]] (CEC)—and then completely gutted, glamorized, and reborn through a tripartite collaboration between [[Rendili StarDrive]], Cybot Galactica, and [[InterGalactic News|IGNews]], with signature sponsorship by [[Crimson Nerf Energy]]. The ship is a mobile broadcast citadel, designed and purpose-built for journalist, fashion inquisitor, and scandal warlord [[Thurston Unger]]. 

Whether you’re tuning in to [[The Cutting Edge]], sipping shade in [[Velvet Verdicts]], or snort-laughing through The [[Galaxy's Dumbest Crooks]], there’s one unifying constant: the drama is always broadcast live from the Full Frontal Fabulous. 

== Design & Reconstruction == 

The original CR90 designation is now mostly ceremonial. The internal framework and aesthetic philosophy have been obliterated and replaced with something far more fabulous—and functionally chaotic. Every bulkhead is wired for scandal, every corridor optimized for on-the-fly interviews, emergency makeovers, and hostile press briefings delivered with a kiss. 

=== Key systems and decks were co-developed:=== 

* [[Rendili StarDrive]] reimagined the entire command structure, replacing military protocols with high-speed communications arrays, ambient lighting, and media flow optimization. 

* Cybot Galactica hand-built the Chrome Court—twelve couture-plated protocol droids equal parts co-host, co-producer, and aesthetic enforcer. [[InterGalactic News|IGNews]] installed an untraceable, triple-encrypted holo-broadcast rig capable of live-streaming through ion storms, bureaucratic dead zones, and hostile jurisdictions. 

* [[Crimson Nerf Energy]] (primary sponsor) ensured that the ship remains stocked, styled, and scandal-ready at all times—including an on-board Crimson Vault loaded with their latest intoxicating concoction: Glam Shock, a spicy, glitter-laced, alcoholic energy surge that's been banned from two republic youth planets and embraced by six sovereign systems. 

=== Signature Locations on Board ===

==== The Bridge ==== 
(The Command Cathedral)

Here, Thurston doesn’t steer starships—he steers the story. Lined in reflective obsidian with adjustable cheekbone lighting, the bridge is tuned for both hyperspace travel and holo-drama production. Each console can reroute signal feeds, filter camera angles, or apply emergency bronzer. 

==== The Velvet Lounge ==== 

Mid-deck, slightly aft of the bridge and spiritually six inches above fabulous. Clad in shades of midnight plum, crushed starlight, and thermal pink velvet, the Lounge is home to the Velvet Verdicts broadcast and a dozen or more galactic meltdowns. Sunken conversation pit. Heat-reactive bar. And just enough hush to hear careers unravel. 

==== The Chrome Court Gallery ==== 

An elevated gallery ring where The Chrome Court reigns. From Gildra Vox’s dramatic voiceovers to Pearletta’s pearl-clad shade schedules, every protocol droid here is a personality, a production node, and a drama connoisseur. Their chamber is outfitted with real-time scandal alerts and a mood-sensitive runway strip. 

==== The Mirror Throne ==== 

A solo seat, a statement, a soft weapon. This reactive interview dais adjusts based on emotional volatility, outfit contrast, and real-time holo-ratings. The throne contains integrated glam-bots (Cybot Galactica Series 9X), capable of redoing a full look in under 30 seconds—or removing someone else’s. 

==== The Crimson Vault ==== 

A pulsing, ultraviolet-lit refrigeration temple housing every Crimson Nerf Energy flavor ever concocted—including the highly classified "Crimson Blend 13: Untitled Lawsuit." Glam Shock, the Full Frontal Fabulous exclusive, is dispensed from crystal tap towers in servings that change hue with your intentions. 

==== The Parlor of Probing ====
(Unofficial, unlisted, and unforgettably invasive)

Tucked behind an innocuous soundproof door marked "maintenance," the Parlor of Probing is where off-camera interviews, whisper-leaks, and reputation autopsies are conducted. A single golden chair faces a ring of floating holocams, truth-detection sensors, and one very judgmental protocol droid. Guests either leave vindicated… or edited out of existence. 

==== The Side Piece ==== 
([[Lambda-class Shuttle|Lambda-Class Auxiliary Shuttle)]]

Fast, flirtatious, and occasionally unhinged. The Side Piece handles Thurston’s field segments, unscheduled scandals, and unexpected dalliances. Piloted by Whispa, an AI that speaks only in sarcasm and vocal fry, it has its own fanbase and merch line. 

== Flagship Shows Filmed Aboard == 

=== [[The Cutting Edge]] ===

Thurston’s flagship program: half investigative journalism, half drag show trial. Often filmed across multiple locations on the ship for mood, angle, or impact. V

=== [[Velvet Verdicts]] ===

Intimate roundtables held in the Velvet Lounge, featuring the Chrome Court and select guests. Part gossip tribunal, part therapy session, all glamour. 

=== [[Galaxy's Dumbest Crooks|The Galaxy's Dumbest Crooks]] ===

A syndicated guilty pleasure. Holo-reconstructions of the dumbest crimes in the galaxy, complete with Thurston commentary and re-enactments performed by very unwilling interns.

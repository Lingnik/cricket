{{title|Star Destroyer ''Murmillo''}}
The '''''Murmillo''''' was a [[:starwars:Gladiator-class Star Destroyer|''Gladiator''-class Star Destroyer]] acquired and pressed into service by the officers of the [[Barren Sector Fleet]]. Equipped with an extensive hangar bay compared to its primary role and size, ''Murmillo'' was outfit with several dozen fighters and would frequently augment [[VeSD Repulse|''Repulse'']] or [[VeSD Warspite|''Warspite'']] on strike missions, or take up the role as flagship of the fleet while the heavier warships would be called away. Captain Braddock Sodal was the initial commander of the ship when it joined the Fleet, later to be replaced by Captain Nataly Redfellow after Sodal's departure.

''Murmillo'' would later be cornered and captured, with heavy casualties incurred, following a strike at [[:starwars:Ansion|Ansion]]. Redfellow and her entire command staff would be interred and executed as traitors, with any enlisted crew parsed out to the Imperial penal system for incarceration, work camps, and a long plummet into obscurity. The loss of ''Murmillo'' would represent the highwater mark for [[Dergan Roj|Admiral Roj's]] independent actions in the Mid-Rim &mdash; within three months, Roj's forces had suffered enough losses of range, scope and resources so as to prompt the Admiral to seek out sanctioned [[:starwars:Rebel Alliance|Alliance]] aid.

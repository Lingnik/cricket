'''Gaena''' was an isolated world in the [[Vadimus]] planet cluster, largely unknown to the rest of the [[The Galaxy|galaxy]]. It was known to the [[:starwars:Galactic_Republic|Galactic Republic]] and the [[Galactic Empire]] as '''Auotos Beta II'''. Before the [[:starwars:Clone Wars|Clone Wars]], the world was embroiled in civil war.

[[Jedi Master]] [[Aurejin]] was discovered on Gaena by [[Jedi]] scouts before the Clone Wars when they arrived to help slow the civil war on Gaena. 

Gaena later became one of the worlds conquered by [[Aleister Vadim]], adding it to his small empire that became known simply as Vadimus.

'''Galactic Amusements''' was a famous entertainment company founded in [[11 ABY]] by [[Alexander Floyd]]. It had heavy ties to the [[Galactic Empire]], and sponsored the Imperial swoop team in the [[Galactic Swoop League]].

It was known for its easy-to-use and entertainment casino products and card games. The company had offices on [[Selene]] and aboard the [[Neutron Star-class Bulk Cruiser|''Neutron Star''-class Bulk Cruiser]] ''[[Supernova Siren]]'', which served as a mobile headquarters for the company.

The company ran out of business by [[12 ABY]] with the disappearance of its CEO.

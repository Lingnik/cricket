The '''Galactic Diplomatic Initiative Star''', commonly referred to as '''GDIStar''', was a [[:category:Space Stations|space station]] placed in a relatively innocuous, neutral location in the [[the Galaxy#Inner Rim|Inner Rim]] for the purposes of furthering diplomatic relations between the various galactic governments. While the galaxy was fraught with chaos, the station would exist as a semi-permanent installation upon which peace-making talks could be conducted without fear of invasion, interruption, or reprisal. The intentions were there, and initially, all the galactic powers were represented from [[4 ABY]] until [[7 ABY]]. A severe falling-out precipitated between the [[Galactic Empire]] and [[Griffon Alliance]], resulting in the Empire's taking of the Griffon's prized station [[Pride-1]]. Then, the [[Yavin Plague]] seeped its way across most of the Rim regions, and many governments abandoned their holdings on GDI Star in light of possible infectious spread. The station was locked down for quarantine, but never really fully emerged from it.

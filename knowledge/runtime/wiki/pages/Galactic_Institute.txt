The '''Galactic Institute for Tactics and Non-Conventional Warfare''' was a private consultant firm specializing in warfare and military doctrine. Beyond consulting and advice, the GI also gave hands-on assistance with its client's needs, utilizing mercenaries and its instructors.

Institute consultants offered background information gathering, offering strategic purchases (advice on what to buy, helping obtain restricted hardware), training, and covert mercenary black operations.

The firm was established by [[Zeak Oppenhiemer]] in [[10 ABY]] after his retirement from the [[Imperial Navy]]. He then joined the [[Kalla VII|Kalla]] [[Kalla Naval Academy|Naval Academy]] as an instructor where he was able to headquarter GI. Most GI recruits were trained at the Academy. Cylar Magtarnot was hired on as a managing partner to aide Oppenhiemer in running firm.

The firm was suddenly closed in [[12 ABY]] when Oppenhiemer disappeared.

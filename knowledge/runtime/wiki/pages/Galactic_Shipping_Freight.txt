'''Galactic Shipping & Freight''' was a company formed by [[Dieter Vichten]] around [[10 ABY]]. It provided shipping and other services to clients across the galaxy, and also dealt in collecting wreckage and debris throughout the space lanes. It made a huge profit by securing rights to collect the [[Death Star III]]'s debris following its destruction. However, the company went under several years later, and declared bankruptcy.

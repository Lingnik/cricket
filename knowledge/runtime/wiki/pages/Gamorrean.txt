'''Gamorreans''' are large, porcine warriors.  They have green skin, 
short, portly bodies, and pig-like faces.  On their homeworld, they live 
in clans ruled by matriarchs.  Female Gamorreans tend to live much longer 
than the males, as the males spend the majority of their lives engaged in 
combat.  Wars between clans are constant.  The wars are not based on 
political quarrels, but are waged as a sport.
   
Gamorreans are skilled melee warriors, especially adept with 
blades such as knives and axes.  These are the weapons of preference for 
Gamorreans waging war between clans, and Gamorreans are trained in their 
use starting at a very young age.  Gamorrean training is based almost 
entirely on combat, and it is rare to find one with more than basic 
knowledge of intellectual, technical, or mechanical concepts.

One of the most influencial obstacles facing off-world Gamorreans 
is that they are considered stupid by most other species.  Although they 
are not particularly bright, they do not consider themselves stupid.  The 
prejudice is probably based on the Gamorreans' appearance, their war-like 
tendancies, and their inability to grasp the basic language.
       
Gamorreans are most often hired or enslaved as guards and 
mercenaries.  They will fight loyally for their employers, as fighting 
for another makes up for their missing the grand battles between clans.  
Gamorreans also value their honor, and are apt to start a brawl with 
anyone foolish enough to poke fun of their appearance, intelligence, or 
honor within earshot.

J'Eeves is employed by Lord [[Geophreigh Smitherbodkins IV]] of Kartathia Manor on Corellia.  He has served the Smitherbodkins' as a valet since he was a young Kubaz, as his father before him and his father before him. 

==Biography==

===Family History (before [[36 BBY]]) ===

Ten generations ago, hive wars were raging on the planet of [[:starwars:Kubindi/Legends|Kubindi]].  Some members of the S'gah hive managed to make it off planet before their entire clan was wiped out in the wars. J'Eeves ancestors were among these refugees.  They landed on Corellia with next to nothing and were taken in by a charity foundation, the Society for the Protection of Inferior Races and Preservation of Lesser Cultures, which was run by the women of high society.  It so happened that Lord Geophreigh Smitherbodkins the First was a great fan of Kubaz society, having visited Kubindi early in his life and finding their culture and cuisine of a very high quality.  J'Eeves family at that time were the most famous cooks on the planet, and it was for this reason alone that, upon hearing of their arrival in SPIRPLC headquarters, that Lord Smitherbodkins went to the shelter himself that very day to offer them employment.  The S'gah family has been the faithful servants of the Smitherbodkins' ever since. 

===Early Life (36 BBY - [[16 BBY]])===

Gdoth was born at Kartathia manor in [[36 BBY]] and was trained, as his family had been for ten generations, to serve the Smitherbodkins family.  He was well taught in cooking, cleaning, culture, sewing, piloting, and swordsmanship.  In the Sgah clan, children are not named until they have reached the age of maturity and assigned their life's work.  This did not work for the  Smitherbodkins family, so he was given the nickname "J'Eeves" early in his life.  He had a happy childhood learning the fine art of serving another's needs, and he was well cared for by both his family and his family's employers.

===Early Career (16 BBY - [[7 BBY]])===

The day that Lord Geophreigh Smitherbodkins IV was born was the most important day in J'Eeves' young life, as the day he had been groomed for had finally arrived.  J'Eeves was assigned the great responsibility, as his father before him had been to Parcival Smitherbodkins, of tutoring the young man in the ways of high society.  Along with this great responsibility came a name: Gdoth, which means valet in Kubindi.  Everyone just continued to call him J'Eeves though.  Though the first few years were mostly spent changing diapers, soon enough, Geophreigh began to learn about please and thank you, fine Kubindi and Corellian cuisine and history, how to throw lavish parties, the rules of dueling, and other things a young Corellian noble should be well versed in.

===Present Career (7 BBY - [[15 ABY]])===

When Geophreigh at last came of age and headed off to University, J'Eeves accompanied him as his manservant, intent on keeping him out of trouble and upholding the family honor.

== Description ==
There is no mistaking this alien being as a Kadjain'sa'Nikto, one of the five sub-species native to the rugged planet Kintan but found flourishing through-out the known galaxy. This broad-shouldered specimen has the red, leathery skin typical of the desert dwelling branch of the Nikto race and four protected breathing tubes located on the base of his jaw rather than a nose in the middle of his face common to other races. Small sharp horns frame the rugged features of his face, three from each cheek and two more located above his rapacious, yellow eyes. This Kadjain'sa'Nikto is almost two meters tall when he stands at full height and carries a muscular bulk reminiscent of a seasoned brawler. A number of scars mar his stern visage to leave him with a grim, vicious appearance.

Utilitarian would be the best word to describe the clothing worn by this Kadjain'sa'Nikto. Everything has been designed to be functional and there seems to have been no thought put towards fashion during the selection of his outfit. Heavy, nerf-hide workboots shod his feet in thick leather and provide a sturdy grip with hobnailed soles. Brass buckles serve to fasten his boots on the outside of each calf, three on each, and keep his canvas trousers securely tucked into them. Sewn into the heavy, drab brown pants are a number of pockets and loops of material to hold a variety of useful items, the stitching re-inforced with brass rivets. A thick black utility belt encircles his waist providing additional storage space for the Nikto Swaddled around his stomach and lower chest are several strips of a long, dirty beige material that wrap around his torso like bandages leaving his upper chest, shoulders and arms bare and visible to all.

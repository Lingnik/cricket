TITLE: Grenade Launcher
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2007-04-12T14:59:41Z
======================================================================
The over-under attachment is essentially a mid to long range engagement device attached to the underside of a Marine rifle. It allows the user, called a grenadier, to launch specially designed projectile grenades at ranges up to five hundred meters.

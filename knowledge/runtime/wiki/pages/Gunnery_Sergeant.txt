The rank and title of '''Gunnery Sergeant''' is a senior non-commissioned officer's rank used mostly by army organizations. They are highly regarded as both experts in their particular field and natural leaders.

=== In New Republic Service ===

Despite the name, Gunnery Sergeants in the '''[[New Republic GroundOps|New Republic Army]]''' seldom have anything to do with artillery. A Gunnery Sergeant (or 'Gunny', affectionately) is the senior most NCO in a platoon. He is the Platoon Sergeant, and between him and the Platoon's [[First_Lieutenant#In_New_Republic_Service|Lieutenant]], they are responsible for everything the platoon does. Most Gunnies are in their tenth year of service or longer. Nevertheless, a good Gunny is invaluable to any platoon, no matter the age. Gunnery Sergeants often decline promotion in order to stay with their soldiers.

=== In Caspian Union Service ===

This rank was formerly utilised by the [[Union Marines]] in a manner similar to its role in the New Republic. It is not part of the current Union Marine rank structure.

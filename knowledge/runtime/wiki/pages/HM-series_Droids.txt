The '''HM-Series of Heavy Maintenance Droids''' is the offspring of [[Yari]] [[droid]] engineer '''Zr-Gt-Hn Ouurene''' of Zr-Gt-Hn Equipment Fabricators. The series was designed with several goals in mind, not the least of which was easy navigation of the rather unique terrain of the facilities on [[Kasputin Yar]].

It rapidly became popular in the shipyards, displacing quite a number of other commonplace, traditional pieces of equipment. Capabilities of welding and cutting materials with onboard plasma torches and fusers, attaching and employing modular tools, and moving across any surface (including upside-down) propelled Zr-Gt-Hn to the forefront of niche droid and systems development in the [[Caspian Democratic Union|Union]]. 

Perhaps the most popular feature of the HM-series, at least on their homeworld, was their electromagnetic noise output.  Rather than the foul cacophony of electromagnetic noise produced by most droids, the systems of the HM series are relatively relaxing, likened to windchimes and soft pipes, instead of the incessant whirring and clunking that otherwise irritate Yari senses.

Owing to their versatility and natural group co-ordination, some Yari ship construction managers employ groups of them in place of larger lifting equipment. One key element in their popularity was their ability to speak in clear and concise sentences, reassuring those who work alongside them that they fully understand the work that is to be done. With some cognitive programming alterations, they can interact in [[Sluissi]], [[Duros]], Yari, Basic, or even [[Sarian (language)|Sarian]]. Highly versatile, they have been sold to a number of offworld organizations and businesses, and are beginning to become the droid-of-choice for [[Lokkai Starworks]].

Features include:

* Variable-configuration manipulators (2)
* Ultra-high-traction nanostrand gripping strips (20)
* Plasma cutter (2)
* Macro-fuser, variable substrate (4)
* Traction magnets (6)
* Molec-weave suspension line winches (4)
* LIDAR measurement set
* Voice synthesizer
* Wireless communication coordinator
* Microgravity maneuvering thrusters (equilibrium only)

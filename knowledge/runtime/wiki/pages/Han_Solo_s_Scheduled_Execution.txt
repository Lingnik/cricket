'''Han Solo's Execution''' was an event that occurred on [[Athaniss]] in [[14 ABY]]. [[Han Solo]] had been captured by the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] several weeks prior, and was transferred to the prison facility on Athaniss and slated for a public execution. [[Moff]] [[Morrison Van Sen]] and [[Grand Admiral]] [[Danik Kreldin]] were among the attendants. Just minutes before Solo was executed, a team of [[Jedi]] and [[New Republic]] commandos led by [[Luke Skywalker]], including [[Ai'kani Za'netra]], raided the event and rescued Solo.

'''Hanna City''' was the capital of [[Chandrila]]. It was located on the edge of the [[:starwars:Silver_Sea/Legends|Silver Sea]] and contained the [[:starwars:Hanna Institute of Antiquities/Legends|Hanna Institute of Antiquities]]. 

Prior to the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]], the city was also the location of a mock-[[:starwars:Galactic Senate/Legends|Galactic Senate]], being used to host roleplays by the [[:starwars:Legislative Youth Program/Legends|Legislative Youth Program]].

During the [[Galactic Civil War]], Hanna City was subjugated by the [[6th TF Hammer Army Battlegroup]]. Hanna became the site of heavy anti-Imperial sentiment and was where the [[Chandrilan Massacre]] occurred. Hanna was threatened to be obliterated by the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]]'s [[Danik Kreldin]], but the plan was foiled due to the intervention of [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]].

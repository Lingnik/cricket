The ''Hardlace ''is a''' '''[[Guardian-class Light Cruiser|Guardian-class]]''' '''[[New Republic Navy|New Republic Naval]] patrol vessel.

In 23 ABY, while operating out of [[Dac]] spaceport, the ''Hardlace ''was captured while answering a distress call which proved to be a trap set by notorious pirate, [[Shiari Vane]].

While in pirate hands she underwent extensive retrofitting; her weapons compliment was altered to include two ion cannons in the forward mounts, sensor counter-measures added to better evade the authorities and substantial ship batteries connected to the power plant enable her to maintain life-support while operating at low power for hours at a time.

The ''Hardlace'' was returned to Republic hands in 24 ABY, following negotiations between Ambassador [[Ambrosia Delgard]] and Captain Vane.

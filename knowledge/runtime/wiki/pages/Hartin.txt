'''Hartin''' is the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[Moff]] assigned to oversee the space of the [[Bothan Federation]] after its annexation by the [[Galactic Empire]] in [[14 ABY]]. Hartin does not have full control over Bothan Space, as the Imperial policy towards Bothan space is that of "salutatory neglect", granting the Bothans a level of autonomy in the Imperial sphere. Nevertheless, Hartin keeps a close eye on the Bothan government and advises them on all affairs, relaying the will of the [[Imperial Ruling Council|central Imperial government]] and that of the [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] to the Bothan leadership.

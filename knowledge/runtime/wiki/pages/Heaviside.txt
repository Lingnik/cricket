A '''Heaviside''' is a species of fish native to the seas of [[Krittain Major]]. It is a flat fish, with a squat profile and remains close to the seafloor. It is not particularly gregarious with or tolerant of other sealife, and possesses a quick temper. While normally found in colder waters, it occasionally ventures closer to shore and may pose a hazard to beings that tread through tidal areas or swim among the reefs.

'''Dispersement:'''
*[[Krittain Major]] (native)

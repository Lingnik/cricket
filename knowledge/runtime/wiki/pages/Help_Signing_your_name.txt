If you are a registered user of [[SW1ki]] you can sign your comments on talk pages and the Forum with the tenth button from the left at the top of the edit page, or simply insert four tildes (the dashes help set it apart from other text) <pre>--~~~~</pre> which will then automatically append your user name and a date and time stamp.

Further help on adding content can be found on the help page for [[Help:Editing|Editing]].

'''High Admiral''' is a rank specific to the [[Galactic Empire]]'s [[Imperial Navy|Navy]]. A High Admiral commands a [[Imperial Navy#Sector Group|Sector Group]], a sector's sum total of all available starships.

{{rank plate|[[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]][[Image:rank_empire_br.png]][[Image:rank_empire_br.png]][[Image:rank_empire_br.png]][[Image:rank_empire_br.png]][[Image:rank_empire_br.png]][[Image:Rank empire by.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]]|}}
A High Admiral is typically the Imperial [[Moff]] commanding that Sector, who also holds the High Admiral's sister rank in the [[Imperial Army|Army]], [[Surface Marshal]], though in certain cases it may be a different individual (such as Imperial task forces or fleets that would normally be considered a Sector Group and isn't assigned to a particular sector, or the various Sector Group-sized units assigned to a [[Grand Moff]]).

High Admirals report to the [[Grand Admiral's Council]] and the Chief of Naval Operations, though under special circumstances, also report to the Sector's Moff (if the High Admiral is not the Moff in question).

A High Admiral was equivalent to a [[High Marshal]] in the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]]. One High Marshal was appointed Chief of Starfighter Operations who reported directly to the Chief Naval Officer and the Grand Admiral's Council.

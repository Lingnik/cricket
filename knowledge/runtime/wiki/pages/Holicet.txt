'''Holicet''', [[Sarian (language)|Sarian]] for Spirit Group, is a political party that was formed to contest the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] [[Report:Caspian Election Posts|elections]] for a permanent [[Union Council]] in [[29 ABY]]. They did not win any seats during the initial elections. They would later pick up seats on [[Charros IV]], [[Krittain Major]], and [[Mergansar]], holding five of the thirty-nine total seats. They are part of the all party coalition along with [[Legacy]], [[Lijsttrekker]], and [[Praecet]].

== Background ==

Following the dissolution of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] following the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] and Mergani [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|progoms]] [[Caspar]] was left in a shambles. Not only was a massive amount of work necessary to support the survivors of the pandemic and the refugees from [[Mergansar]] who had settled, but a new government needed to be established. The new [[Caspian Union|Caspar Union]] was a rump state of what was left after other worlds declare independence, leaving the only Caspian worlds with any significant population [[Mikassa]], [[Kichnar Orbital Station]], and Caspar itself. As the old Union had been conceived around its multi-world membership, the new Union had to do more than simply fill vacated posts, a new government model itself needed to be agreed upon.

A Provisional Assembly was soon organised with representatives from Caspar's seven major cities. The representatives were largely a mix of retired members of the [[Union Assembly]], local leaders, academics, and some representatives from the refugee community. Notably, none of those later elected to permanent [[Union Council]] served in the Provisional Assembly. In many cases, those with the most power and influence were too busy actively directing rebuilding efforts to spend time arguing over a new constitution.

There had been no political parties in the old Caspian Democratic Union, but distinct camps did begin to form within the Provisional Assembly which would subsequently give rise to the political parties that would ultimately contest the election once the new constitution was agreed on and ratified. While the other parties that developed out of this were focused more on fear, militarism, and galactic politics, those that formed Holicet were focused more on hope. While not uncaring about the galaxy or unconcerned about security, Holicet was not looking to rebuild the military or reunite the union. Rather their aim was to simply keep a neutral world both uninvolved in galactic politics but ideally unscarred by them.

== Elections ==

Initial polling done before the election had Holicet taking 22% of the vote, however, the first-past-the-post voting system did not work in the party's favour. The only seat they were initially expected to take was [[Noques Rasala]] with Eire Piaphon, Holicet's Leader, in a competitive race there with [[Praecet|Praecet's]] [[Andra Eleria]]. A gaffe by the the [[Legacy]] party candidate saw their support erode and largely to to Piaphon and Eleria. In the end Eleria won with 40% of the vote, with Piaphon getting 36%. Holicet came in second to Praecet in [[Anchorron]], [[Trinumvira]], and on [[Mikassa]]. In [[Dunwelton]], Clemitt Acklay Holicet's Deputy Leader, came in third, as was the case in [[Groton]], [[Orleanon]], [[Plaxton]], and on [[Kichnar Orbital Station]], leaving Holicet without any seats.

The situation was not complete disaster, as subsequent local elections saw Holicet get some representation on that level, but they would remain out of the spotline on the Union level for months. When [[Kasputin Yar]] and [[Rauther]] rejoined the Union it added new seats for Legacy and Praecet but none for Holicet. Meanwhile [[Ixin|Ixin's]] reentry added a new party [[Lijsttrekker]], with a [[Yari]] Councillor adopting that label as well. Given their ideology had been similar enough to Legacy and Praecet, Lijsttrekker was invited into the governing coalition.

It was not until some of the Mergani cities were allowed to have elections that Holicet found itself with a chance of representation again. Though the issues on Mergansar differed in many ways from the rest of the Union, the candidates their opted to align themselves with the Union wide parties in order to benefit from the support they could offer their campaigns. The initial elections did little for Holicet. The elections in Ithaca and then Hammerton were rather pro forma with [[Odie Hood]] and [[Valdemar Linane]] already in effective control locally. In Charon, former [[Senator of the Union|Senator]] [[Philinda Jorgan]] ran for Lijsttrekker without opposition from Legacy and Praecet. While [[Ulmesar]] managed to eke out 14% of the vote in Charon, Holicet only managed 3%, with Jorgan winning with 83%. Spanner's election was the first in Mergansar to be competitive with Legacy's Yoseph Sarlin and Praecet's Leonidas Trettsia both serving on the Durona Workers Union executive committee. Sarlin would ultimately beat Trettsia 53% to 40% with Holicet and other parties sharing the other 7%.

== Representation ==

When [[Charros IV]] joined the Union and the [[Krittain|Krittain System]] rejoined it, Holicet finally found itself gaining three seats with Spittsbury's Lilith Bergen, Trottingham's Pippin Sqank, and Xi Xin's [[cadracak-'r'shak]] adopting the Holicet label. This led to a question of whether Holicet would join the governing coalition. The parties in it had advanced a very different defence and foreign policy than espoused by Holicet, but their domestic policy priorities were not all that different. The all party coalition had also meant the Union Council was a body that worked towards consensus rather than larger parties trying to dominate smaller ones. 

This ultimately appealed to the new Councillors, none of whom was looking to disrupt this harmony by sitting in opposition. This was particularly true for cadracak-'r'shak who chose the Holicet label as a statement against militarism but who had no desire to sit in opposition to the [[Xi Char]] [[Prelate]] and his fellow Deans. Bergen would pick up the post of Minister of Agriculture as part of Holicet joining the coalition.

Back on Mergansar, the Holicet candidate Brullian Aresko managed to get third place with 24% of the vote in Toresta, after the Praecet candidate Lisal Yutan murdered the Legacy party candidate Volzang Eiresar and attempted to murder the Ulmesar candidate Corgan Vir. Legacy chose not to run a candidate in place of Eiresar who had already been outed for complicity in the pogroms prior to his suspicious death. Praecet, likewise, did not field a candidate after Yutan's arrest. Ultimately the Lijsttrekker candidate Nanchia Marook won with 44%, and the Ulmesar's Vir came in second at 31%. But Aresko's performance had been Holicet's best yet on Mergansar and gave the party some momentum heading into the final Mergani elections. 

Though the prior Mergani elections had been held one at a time, the final four in Anphora, Goshen, Hebron, and Vilarasala were all scheduled at once. The campaign time for these was reduced considerably from the drawn out time allowed for Toresta which had proven disastrous. This final round of elections saw Holicet final score some victories with Local sixteen year old protest graffiti artist [[Satine Anatinae]] winning in Anphora, displacing [[Sha Dark]] as the youngest member of the Union Council, and local community leader Evlyn Decker winning in Goshen. Meanwhile the Legacy party picked up Hebron and Vilarasala.

With Holicet now having five seats on the Union Council, none of them on Caspar itself, party leaders Eire Piaphon and Clemitt Acklay resigned to allow the party's membership to elect new leaders from the party's sitting councillors. Evlyn Decker and Satine Anatinae were elected as leader and deputy leader respectively.

== Union Council ==

Holicet has representation on [[Charros IV]], [[Krittain Major]], and [[Mergansar]] only.

* '''[[Satine Anatinae]]''' for Anphora on [[Mergansar]]
* '''Evlyn Decker''' for Goshen on Mergansar
* '''Lilith Bergen''' for Spittsbury on [[Krittain Major]]
* '''Pippin Sqank''' for Trottingham on Krittain Major
* '''[[cadracak-'r'shak]]''' for Xi Xin on [[Charros IV]]

== Platform ==

Though their party name translated literally as Spirit Group, the more nuanced translation was the Party of Hope. They seek peace and harmony, which when it comes to defence and foreign policy sees Holicet at odds with the other parties in the all party coalition. As part of their efforts to build consensus in government, all votes taken are considered free votes, with all councillors free to vote their conscience rather than being forced to vote in accordance with their party or the coalition as a whole. Thus Holicet members usually vote against or abstain from votes related to the military. 

On domestic matters Holicet generally finds common ground with other parties championing causes such as agriculture, the arts, education, healthcare, labour rights, and religious tolerance. One each of these priorities the Holicet councillors have strong working relationships with their peers in the other parties and strong say in policy and final legislation, in spite of being the smallest party in the Union Council.

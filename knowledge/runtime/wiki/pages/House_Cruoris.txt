'''House Cruoris''' is one of the ruling noble houses in the [[New Sith Order]], and is ruled by [[Thane]] [[Axel Vichten]]. House Cruoris' territory stretches from [[Ord Vaxal]] to various systems in the southern quadrant of the galaxy, and includes at least one world in the [[Corporate Sector Authority]].

'''The House of Sabacc''' was once the flagship casino of [[Starlight Entertainment]] and is now under the ownership of X Starlight CEO [[Ewan Dain]]. The House of Sabacc boasts a state of the art [[Jubilee Wheel]], [[Lugjack Machine]], [[Sabacc]] Table and a brand new [[Chance Table]] amongst its games and holds parties and tournaments on a regular basis. It is located on the 88th Level of [[Nar Shaddaa]] in the ''Corellian Sector''.

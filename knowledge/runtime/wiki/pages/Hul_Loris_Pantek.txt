'''Hul Loris Pantek''' was one of the former Directors of the [[Bureau of Operations]] after a short career in the Imperial Navy. Pantek knows how to handle other people and how to handle himself. This might make him appear cold and emotionless but below this shield of coldness he never lost the young monk he once was.

== Biography ==
Hul's parents remain unknown. He was found in front of the '''Abbey of Isgrimnur''' on [[Dreven]], where spent the first 20 years of his life, reading ancient books about ancient religions and cultures.

Following an old tradition, the young monk embarked on a journey through [[The Galaxy|the galaxy]] on his 21st birthday &mdash; a journey that changed his life forever. After visiting the system of [[Mutanda|Kiilaniri]], Pantek and his companion travelled to [[Caspia]], arriving at [[Kichnar Orbital Station]]. The long journey had been strenuous and they decided that a drink was what they needed most after this trip. They found a bar, ordered their drinks and sat down.

Only a little later a group of half-drunken Republican troopers entered the bar. Without any obvious reason they began to insult and deride the monks. The two Servants of the All wanted to leave, and that was the point where the situation went out of control. A brawl, blaster shots, yells, wounded guests and a dying monk in Hul's arms was what resulted.

It had already occurred to him before, that joining the Galactic Empire would mean serving the good, bringing peace and order to the galaxy, and this incident fully confirmed this decision.

A few days later Brother Hul didn't exist anymore &mdash; Cadet Hul Loris Pantek had taken over. Having spent years in reading and studying, he breezed through the Academy curriculum. His first assignment brought him to Orbital Station Guardian in the [[O'paal]] system where he stayed for several years, climbing up the Naval Chain of Command.

At the rank of Lieutenant Commander he decided to leave the Navy in favour of the Imperial [[Bureau of Operations]].

After being Ambassador to Mutanda, Pantek soon took over the Intelligence Department and after a while even became Vice-Director and later Director of the Bureau.

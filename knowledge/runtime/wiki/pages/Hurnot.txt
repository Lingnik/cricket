The '''hurnot''' is a breed of canid native to the savannahs and hills of [[Rauther]]. It is a primarily a carnivore, but can become an omnivore when prey becomes scarce. Hurnots have become domesticated by the Rautheran population as partners and sporting creatures, since with their muscular shoulders and short, powerful muzzle, they're adept at tracking down and subduing several types of rodents as well as some larger game.

Hurnots have become emblematic of some Rautheran military units because of their ruggedness, stubbornness, and staunch loyalty. They are occasionally used as patrol and rescue animals, able to perform these roles when droids are unavailable. A few work alongside [[Sarian]] [[selka]]s in the employ of both the [[Union Marines]] and [[Provincial Army of the Union|Provincial Army]] as "warhounds", tracking down fugitives or rooting out entrenched enemies.

The parallel dimension used by ships to facilitate expeditious interstellar travel. 
[[Image:Hyperspacefalcon.jpg|thumb|250px|The ''[[Millennium Falcon]]'' travels through hyperspace]]

'''Hyperspace''' is the space through which a spaceship may travel at faster-than-light speeds, using a [[:starwars:Hyperdrive/Legends|hyperdrive]].

Ships in hyperspace can interact with normal space, and thus complicated calculations (usually performed by an [[:starwars:Astromech droid/Legends|astromech droid]] or a [[:starwars:Navigation computer/Legends|navigation computer]]) are necessary to avoid a disastrous impact.

Moving too close to a source of high gravity can remove a ship from hyperspace. This can occur when faulty calculations move the ship too near a star or large planet. This mechanism is exploited by the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[Interdictor-class Immobilizer 418|Interdictor cruiser]], whose [[:starwars:Gravity well projector/Legends|Gravity well projectors]] can be used to pull ships out of hyperspace for an ambush, or to prevent ships from fleeing the battlefield. It is also exploited by [[pirate|pirates]], who have been known to haul large [[:starwars:Asteroid/Legends|asteroid]] into such a position as to bring a ship out of hyperspace in order to ambush and board it.

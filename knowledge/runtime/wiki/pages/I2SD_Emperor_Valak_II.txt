'''HIMS ''Emperor Valak II''''' was an [[Imperator II-class Star Destroyer|''Imperator II''-class Star Destroyer]] commissioned after the [[I2SD Emperor Valak|HIMS ''Emperor Valak'']] was scuttled due to heavy damage received during the [[Second Battle of Sluis Van]]. Named in honor of that ship's sacrifice and the [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] himself, the ''Emperor Valak II'' was intended to carry on her predecessor's legacy. [[Bacharan Valak]] himself would abdicate the throne shortly thereafter, but the ''Emperor Valak II'' remained in commission throughout the following years and into the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]].

== Imperial Service Record ==
'''HIMS ''Predator''''' has served with distinction on the Imperial front lines with Task Force Inquisitor for some time, participating in many battles that resulted in major power shifts for the galaxy.  In mid [[15 ABY]] the Star Destroyer was sequestered for personal use by [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] and was set up as his personal flagship for the [[New Sith Order]]; her current assignment.  Crewed now by specialized [[droids]]; it is rumored to hold within it's hull, a large armed force of [[Sith Battle Droids|Battle Droids]].  It's first test of combat with the Order, came at [[Second Battle of Nar Shaddaa]]; where her full capabilities still were not shown, though the battle was not a loss.

While the service of the ship was exceptional.  The Predator Battle Group, has returned back to the Imperial fleet where she rejoins the sister ships and takes her place back within the Imperial War Machine.

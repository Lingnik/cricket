The reformation of [[Haor Chall Engineering]] meant the [[Xi Char]] had to decide which of their old designs to innovate for reintroduction. Initially the focus of the [[conclave|designers' conclave]] was on starfighter production, with the reintroduction of ground based models not initially considered. However, as word of the Xi Char's efforts was discussed in the wider [[Caspian Union|Union]] design community and within the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]], one product in particular was requested by elements of the [[Caspian Defence Force]], the [[:starwars:IG-227 Hailfire-class droid tank/Legends|IG-227 Hailfire-Class Droid Tank]].

The advancement in technology since the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone]] Wars meant that the Xi Char engineers had new options for improving the craft. Taking advantage of the materials utilised by the Caspian defence industry. By utilising [[cuerterium]] and [[olumic]] in the design of the chassis, the Xi Char were able to make it both more durable and to add retardation of electromagnetic penetration to its features. Partnering with [[StarShield|StarShield Armaments]] on on improving the hailfire missiles, the '''IG-240 Hailfire-Class Droid Tank''' would pack a more serious punch than its predecessor.

'''HIMS ''Magus''''' was an [[Imperator-class Star Destroyer|''Imperator''-class Star Destroyer]] acting as the flagship for the renegade [[Fleet Admiral]] [[Sinar Fenes]]. Prior to Fenes' defection, ''Magus'' was the flagship of the [[Imperial Core Defense Fleet]] and was modified to reflect her important status. Equipped with torpedo tubes, advanced communication systems, and reinforced hull coating, ''Magus'' was a force to be reckoned with, and was on par with an [[Imperator II-class Star Destroyer|''Imperator II''-class Star Destroyer]].

After the [[Battle of Coruscant]], ''Magus'' became the flagship of the [[Red Fleet]], and would lead the battle against the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] during the [[Ambush at O'paal]]. During the battle, her bridge would be breached by an Imperial torpedo, killing Fenes and the bridge crew. Her experienced crew attempted to regain control from the secondary bridge, but continued barrage from Imperial Star Destroyers reduced her hull to slag, and ''Magus'' was vaporized with the rest of her crew.

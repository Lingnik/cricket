'''HIMS ''Vanguard''''' was an [[Imperial-class Star Destroyer|''Imperial''-class Star Destroyer]] commanded by Commander [[:starwars:Ritton|Ritton]] in the years following the [[:starwars:Battle of Hoth/Legends|Battle of Hoth]]. The [[Star Destroyer]] is best known for being the flagship of then-Vice Admiral [[Thrawn]] during Grand Admiral [[:starwars:Demetrius_Zaarin|Demetrius Zaarin]]'s attempted coup approximately one year before the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]]. After Zaarin managed to take [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]] prisoner from aboard the Star Destroyer ''Majestic'', Zaarin had Palpatine transferred to an Escort Shuttle which was tasked with rendezvousing with Zaarin's flagship, ''Glory''. Hoping to stop the exchange and rescue the Emperor, Lord [[:starwars:Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Darth Vader]], along with ace pilot [[:starwars:Maarek_Stele/Legends|Maarek Stele]], deployed from aboard ''Vanguard'' in a [[:starwars:TIE/sa bomber/Legends|TIE Bomber]] and [[:starwars:TIE/sh VIP shuttle|TIE Shuttle]] assault on the ''Glory''. Taken by surprise, Zaarin was forced to withdraw, and ''Vanguard'' successfully rescued Palpatine.

After Thrawn left for the [[The Galaxy#Unknown Regions|Unknown Regions]], ''Vanguard'' was transferred to [[Selene]]. After the Battle of Endor, she came under the command of Admiral [[Russel Canis]]. Canis's fleet took off for [[Dreven]] following the [[Interim Ruling Council]]'s flight there. ''Vanguard'' would serve as the flagship of the [[Imperial Navy]] during this time period. It led the Imperial campaign at the [[Battle of Etti IV]], until midway through the engagement, a New Republic cruiser collided with the rear of the ''Vanguard'', breaching the primary and emergency reactor compartments, and exposing the Engineering section to the vacuum of space. With the loss in power and ensuing chaos, the Star Destroyer was quickly overwhelmed by the gathered New Republic forces. Stormtroopers were successful in rescuing Admiral Canis before the ship was entirely destroyed, but radiation poisoning from the damaged reactor would later claim the Admiral's life. ''Vanguard'' would break up, its hulk and debris later salvaged by the Empire.

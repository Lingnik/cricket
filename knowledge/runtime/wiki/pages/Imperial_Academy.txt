The '''Imperial Academy''' is an education curriculum put in place by the [[Galactic Empire]] during the creation of Emperor Palpatine's New Order. It utilized several prestigious schools on several worlds, chief among them [[:starwars:Raithal/Legends|Raithal]] and [[Carida]]. Each branch or campus would have the same base doctrine, with additional courses focused on various disciplines and specialties.

Branches of the Imperial Academy System were located on:
* [[Carida]]  - site of the [[Academy of Carida]], specializing in Administration, Physical Sciences, and [[Imperial Army|Army]] and [[Stormtrooper]]  Training.
* [[:starwars:Raithal/Legends|Raithal]]  - site of the [[:starwars:Raithal Academy/Legends|Raithal Academy]]
* [[Corulag]]  - site of the [[:starwars:Corulag Academy|Corulag Academy]]
* [[O'paal]]  - site of the O'paali Guardian Center, offering a more focused and secure location to train Army, Navy, and Stormtrooper personnel.
* [[Coruscant]]  - site of the [[:starwars:Coruscani Pilot Institute|Coruscani Pilot Institute]], a premier school for speeder and small craft piloting.
* [[:starwars:Prefsbelt IV/Legends|Prefsbelt IV]]  - site of the [[:starwars:Imperial Naval Academy/Legends|Prefsbelt IV Naval Academy]], devoted to producing the most proficient [[Imperial Navy|Naval]] officers, technicians, and pilots in the Imperial ranks.
* [[:starwars:Arkanis/Legends|Arkanis]]  - site of the [[:starwars:Vensenor Flight Academy/Legends|Vensenor Flight Academy]], serving as an alternative flight school teaching advanced, outside-the-box tactics.

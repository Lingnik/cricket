The '''Bombardment of Tatooine''' was a minor operation executed by [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] and [[Fleet Admiral]] [[Drayson Honos]] in [[13 ABY]]. 

[[Task Force Hammer]], hunting down the [[Jedi]] [[Ezkhil i'Falzin]], tracked him down to [[Tatooine]]. After initial attempts at smoking the Jedi out failed, the Imperials resorted to orbitally bombarding the world. A significant portion of [[Mos Eisley]] was destroyed in the attack, the extent of which can still be seen today in the city.

After failing to capture Ezkhil, the Imperials tracked the Jedi down to [[Caspar]], beginning the [[Caspian Jedi Hunt]].

'''Imperial City''' is the capital of [[Selene]]. It was initially destroyed in [[12 ABY]] during the [[War of the Throne]], when the [[SSD Palpatine|HIMS ''Dark Eminence'']] was rammed into the planet by [[Lorn Rhys]] and his [[Imperial Coup (12 ABY)|forces]]. 

The city was rebuilt and re-opened in [[13 ABY]] by Grand Moff [[Scaven Marx]], utilizing a massive droid labor force. The city is now built within a gigantic dome, shielding the city from the radiation fallout still plaguing the planet.

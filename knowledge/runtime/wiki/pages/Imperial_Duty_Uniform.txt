=== Imperial Military ===
[[Image:uniform_empire_officer01.jpg|right|120px]]
This is the trademark duty uniform used by all officers of the [[Imperial Navy]] and [[Imperial Army]]. It is worn nearly at all times, with the exception of a more elaborate version used as an [[Imperial Dress Uniform]].

The uniform consists of:
* Jerkin; double-breasted, olive-grey
**Rank squares on left breast
**Rank code cylinders in shoulder pockets
* Trousers; olive-grey
* Cap; olive-grey
* Belt; black with silver buckle
* Boots; black, no embellishments
* Gloves; black (issued for field work)

<br clear="all">

=== Specialty Branches ===
[[Image:uniform_empire_officer02.jpg|right|120px]]
Officers in the following branches use this duty uniform when they're not otherwise in their armor, flightsuits, or utility outfits.
*Jerkin; double-breasted, black
**Rank squares on left breast
**Rank code cylinders in shoulder pockets
*Trousers; black
*Cap; black
*Belt; black with silver buckle
*Boots; black, no embellishments

==== Starfighter Corps ====
Pilot officers often have a single piping of their wing's color on their trousers. This duty uniform is only worn when away from their active posting or when off of active flight status.

==== Stormtrooper Corps ====
Commissioned and senior non-com officers of the Stormtrooper ranks utilize this uniform when not on rotation in their traditional armor. They add a large black holster for a DH-17 blaster and a complex comlink with which to stay in contact with their charges.

==== Technical Services ====
Commissioned and senior non-com officers which directly oversee and coordinate technicians, specialists, or engineers utilize this uniform, often completely devoid of any embellishments.

<br clear="all">
=== Security Bureau & Intelligence ===
[[Image:uniform_empire_officer03.jpg|right|120px]]
Those members of the [[Imperial Security Bureau]] and [[Imperial Intelligence]] utilized this duty uniform frequently, and noticeably stood out amongst their inter-division peers.
*Jerkin; double-breasted, beige
*Trousers; black
*Cap; black
*Belt; black with silver buckle and silver compad
*Boots; black, no embellishments

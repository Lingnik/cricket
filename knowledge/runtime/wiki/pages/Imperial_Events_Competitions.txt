= Competitions =
All branches of the Imperial military every year decide their best of the best. Special prizes for the champions are awarded and a grand ceremony in honor of the champions is thrown.  There are four different competitions that involve space and ground combat as well as a challenges for the ISB.

<div style="float:left; width:48%;">
== Ace Of Aces ==
This competition is where the best pilots of the Empire square off to determine who is 'Top Gun' in the Galactic Empire.

== Best Trooper ==
The Stormtroopers have a weekend long competition set up to challenge physical, martial, and tactical skills. 

</div><br clear=all>

<div style="float:left; width:48%;">
== Commander's Cup ==
A maneuver and assault competition is given where the captains of our fleet ships put their skills to the test to determine the best captain in the Imperial Navy.

</div><div style="float:right; width:48%;">
== Guardian of Loyalty ==
This is a contest that the ISB and members of COMPNOR paticipate in designed to simulate 'ferreting' out traitors and who does their job the best, determining the best in the Galactic Empire.

== <span style="font-size:24px;">Imperial Stormtrooper</span> ==
Imperial Stormtroopers are trained and equipped for operations in a wide variety of combat theaters, they will typically be appropriately equipped for the environment.

'''Dexterity''': 3D

*Blaster: 5D
*Dodge: 5D
*Grenade: 4D
'''Knowledge''': 2D
*Survival: 3D
'''Mechanical''': 3D
*Ground Vehicle Operation: 4D
*Vehicle Gunnery: 4D
'''Perception''': 3D
*Search: 4D
'''Strength''': 3D
*Brawling: 4D
*Stamina: 4D
'''Technical''': 2D

Equipment: [http://sw1mush.wikia.com/wiki/ST-I ST-I] (5D damage, 0D+1 to blaster skill) or [http://sw1mush.wikia.com/wiki/ST-II ST-II] (6D damage, one per squad) or [[MX Heavy Blaster Pistol|MX Heavy Blaster pistol]] (5D damage, -1D+0 to blaster skill), Concussion Grenade (4D physical damage)
===Stormtrooper Armor===
'''Model''': Standard stormtrooper armor
'''Type''': Military armor
'''Scale''': Character
'''Cost''': Not available for sale

''Armor Protection'': +2D physical, +1D energy, -1D Dexterity and related skills (penalty not included above. Negated when wearing paired Stormtrooper Helmet.)

''Comlink'': Helmet comlink

''Sealed Body Glove'': Climate-controlled body glove and breath mask allows operation in uncomfortably cold or warm climates and toxic-air environments

''MFTAS'': Multi-Frequency Targeting Acquisition System; negates penalties for low-visibility, negates penalties for fast-moving targets; polarized lenses prevent flash-blinding.

''Utility Belt'': High-tension wire, grappling hooks, spare blaster power packs, ion flares, concentrated rations, spare comlink, water pack, 2 medpacs
===Scout-trooper Armor===
'''Model''': Standard Scout Trooper Armor
'''Type''': Military armor
'''Scale''': Character
'''Cost''': Not available for sale

''Armor Protection'': +0D+2 physical, +0D+2 energy

''Comlink'': Helmet comlink

''Sealed Body Glove'': Climate-controlled body glove and breath mask allows operation in uncomfortably cold or warm climates and toxic-air environments

''MFTAS'': Multi-Frequency Targeting Acquisition System; negates penalties for low-visibility, negates penalties for fast-moving targets; polarized lenses prevent flash-blinding.

''Utility Belt'': High-tension wire, grappling hooks, spare blaster power packs, ion flares, concentrated rations, spare comlink, water pack, 2 medpacs, macrobinoculars

== TIE Pilot ==
TIE fighters are un-shielded craft deployed in large, cost-effective swarms. Most TIE pilots have a short life-expectancy and so receive minimal training.

'''Knowledge''': 2D

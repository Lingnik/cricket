The '''Imperial Noble Credit''' is more of a token of vanity for the opulently wealthy in the [[Galactic Empire]] than a measure of currency. It is a solid disc of Tralehasian platinum inlaid with fine gold threading bearing the personal seal of [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]].

The '''Imperial Royal Court''' was established around [[12 ABY]] on [[Dreven]]. Its role and position within the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] hierarchy has never been clearly defined, as is with most Imperial institutions, but it is a body of nobles and other courtly figures who play to the [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]]'s desires. Similar to the [[Imperial Ruling Council]] and the Emperor's [[Imperial Advisor]]s, they form another element of the Emperor's own court, bureaucrats vying for the Emperor's favor and constantly fighting amongst themselves to gain a voice with the Emperor.

By [[14 ABY]], the Court had become a relic and forgotten institution, one largely ignored by the Empire proper as it entered a revitalized era under the leadership of [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] and [[Danik Kreldin]]. With Dreven a footnote in Imperial history, the Court has largely fallen apart, with a few survivors attempting to reach out to the enigmatic [[Aleister Vadim]] on [[Coruscant|Imperial Center]], yet with little success.

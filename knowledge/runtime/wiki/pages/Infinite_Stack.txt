[[Image:Infinite Stack.jpg|thumb|400px]] The '''Infinite Stack''' is a sandwich introduce by [[Biscuit Baron]] in [[34 ABY]]. Its name is a reference to its size, which while not infinite, is considerably larger than any sandwich on the menu. It consists of multiple layers of bread, meat, and other potential ingredients. It is highly customisable but the options vary greatly between restaurants. The sandwich is explicitly marketed to species such as [[Besalisk|Besalisks]] and [[Hutt|Hutts]] who have larger mouths than [[Human|Humans]] and similar species. It is too large to fit in the mouth of most species.

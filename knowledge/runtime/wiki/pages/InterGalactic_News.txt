'''InterGalactic News''' (abbv. '''IGNews''' or '''IGN''') is the major media network in the [[The Galaxy|galaxy]] which has so far remained out of the control of both the [[Galactic Empire]] and the [[New Republic]], but maintains bureaus within each government. The corporation was founded in [[7 ABY]] by [[Darrien Bryant]] and [[Tyre Wolf]].

Properly speaking the term Ix Fleet or Ix Navy refers to either independent naval forces for the [[Ixin]] prior it joining the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] in [[21 ABY]] or the independent Ixin naval forces in existence between the fall of the CDU in [[28 ABY]] and Ixin joining the new [[Caspian Union]] in [[29 ABY]].

== History ==

Millennia ago [[Ixin]] was a prosperous world engaged in colonisation of other nearby worlds including [[Mikassa]]. Whether this prosperity included an organised fleet or had been done independently by various [[Prince|Princes]] is not entirely clear from what historical records remain. By the time of the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]], Ixin had no official navy, though several of the wealthier Princes owned [[Marauder-class Corvette|Marauder-Class Corvettes]] or other similar vessels. These vessels served to discourage piracy against the planet, though some of the more unscrupulous Princes were rumoured to engage in piracy themselves. Though no formal agreement existed binding these ships in collective service, tribal traditions ensured concepts such as mutual aid were held sacrosanct nevertheless.

Ixin ostensibly sided with the [[:starwars:Confederacy of Independent Systems/Legends|Confederacy]] during the Clone Wars, but neither it nor the [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Republic]] considered Ixin of particular value. In these years it was left on its own, never the site of battle nor even a garrison. Following the end of the Clone Wars and the rise of the [[Galactic Empire]], the Empire "retook" the world establishing garrisons in Feyenoor and Udalooy. Beyond a token presence, the Empire had little interest in Ixin either, and so the status quo remained. After the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]] in [[4 ABY]], the Empire withdrew its troops to focus on more critical worlds. Ixin was now independent, still utilising corvettes for defence.

With the formation of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], Ixin found itself ostensibly within the borders claimed by this new power, but the CDU showed no interest in offering membership to Ixin. The Ix likewise had little interest in becoming part of the Union, it had little cultural affinity towards the Caspians.

== A New Perspective ==

As the [[Galactic Civil War]] continued to rage on, some of the Princes of Ixin began to see the need of a more formal defence structure. While hiring mercenaries and house guards and ships had served them thus far they could not count on permanently escaping the notice of larger powers. The Imperial invasion of [[Caspar]] in [[8 ABY]] would, in particular, light a fire under their arses. Several Princes used their connexions to secure placements at foreign military academies for young Ix with interest in military careers. 

While cultural ties to the nearby CDU were minimal, a trade relationship slowly developed. As the [[Caspian Navy]] continually worked to modernise their vessels, they found the Ix were in the market to acquire some older cast offs that otherwise would have been scrapped. This would culminate in [[14 ABY]] with the purchase of the mothballed [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruiser]], ''Indefatigable'', to serve as a flagship under the command of [[Admiral]] Prince Zoltan Bin Salmon Al Sawd. The ship had terrible manoeuvrability, a lacklustre hyperdrive, and required an obscene number of crew to run, but the Ix reasoned it was the deterrent they could afford.

Eventually out of economic desperation, Ixin joined the CDU in [[21 ABY]] and the operations of the Ix Navy were folded into the Caspian Navy. Some of the smaller vessels found their ways back into the hand of individual princes, while vessels like ''Indefatigable'' were mothballed once again in favour of the more modern vessels the Union had built in the intervening years. Ixin would be now protected by the Task Group O of the Caspian Navy, with a [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]], ''CMS Ixin'', eventually commissioned as its lead vessel.

== Republic of Ixin ==

In [[28 ABY]] as the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] and [[Mergansar|Mergani]] [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]], the [[Naval Intelligence Office]] ordered ''CMS Tonare'', ''CMS Aph'relin'', and several support vessels to Ixin. [[Sar Admiral]] [[Odie Hood]], the Director of the Naval Intelligence Office and [[Senator of the Union|Senator]] [[Philinda Jorgan]] had been on Ixin for a secret mission when all hell broke loose back in the [[Caspia|Caspian System]]. They had been initially been out of contact, by the time they were aware of what was going on it was too late to really do anything. Disgusted with how things were being handled back home, Hood, Jorgan, and their team decided to fake their deaths and hide out amongst [[The Chevalier]]. Viewing Ixin's secrets worth protecting and its Princes as partners they could work with, they worked behind the scenes to make sure Ixin would emerge with as much as it could from the break up of the CDU.

''CMS Tonare'' was under the command of [[Commodore]] [[Kath Samba]], a native of Ixin, who Hood reasoned could convince her crew to defect to the Republic of Ixin. ''CMS Aph'relin'' was likewise under the command of an Ix [[Captain]] Bett Blak and just so happened a ship which Hood had commanded earlier in his career. From this effort Task Group O, which itself would largely side with Ixin was bolstered by ''CMS Tonare'', ''CMS Aph'relin'', ''CMS Mollymawk'', ''CMS Irrawaddy'', and ''CMS Mayhem Valley''. The new [[Caspian Union|Caspian Union]] would later recognise Ixin's right to these vessels as part of the [[Caspian Repatriation Act (29 ABY)|Caspian Repatriation Act]] in [[29 ABY]]. This would be rendered moot a month later by Ixin rejoining the Caspian Union. Ixin would now fall under the protection of Defence Group Ixin of the [[Caspian 3rd Fleet]]. ''CMS Tonare'', ''CMS Indefatigable'', ''CMS Ixin'' remain elements of that group.

== Key Figures ==

* Prince Zoltan Bin Salmon Al Sawd - Appointed Chief of Staff of the Ix Navy, became Chief of the Bureau of Veterans Affairs following Ixin's return to the Union.
* [[Kath Samba]] - Promoted from [[Commodore]] to [[Vice Admiral]] and appointed Deputy Chief of Staff of the Ix Navy, given command of ''[[CMS Renaissance of Innovation]]'' following Ixin's return to the Union.
* Ujaa R'em - Commanding Officer of ''CMS Ixin'', promoted from [[Captain]] to [[Rear Admiral]]. Given command of ''CMS Indefatigable'' following Ixin's return to the Union.
* Bett Blak - Commanding Officer of ''CMS Aph'relin'', promoted from Captain to Rear Admiral. Given command of ''CMS Steadfast'' following Ixin's return to the Union.
* Prince Dakkar Seneschal - Commanding Officer of ''CMS Mayhem Valley''. Given command of ''CMS Aph'relin'' and promoted to [[Fleet Captain]] following Ixin's return to the Union.
* Sindbad Al Bahrey - Commanding Officer of ''CMS Irrawaddy''. Given command of ''CMS Ixin'' and promoted to Fleet Captain following Ixin's return to the Union.
* Sinya Komad - Commanding Officer of ''CMS Mollymawk''. Given command of ''[[CMS Tuatalin]]'' and promoted ot Fleet Captain following Ixin's return to the Union.

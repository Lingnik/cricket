'''Jade Ruen Vigos''' ([[14 BBY]] - ) is a [[Duros]] swoop racer and starship pilot.

== History ==
Jade Ruen Vigos was born on Ando Prime, a frozen but mostly hospitable Mid Rim planet. Prior to the Imperial ban on podracing, Ando Prime was well known amongst fans for its numerous and rather difficult courses, due in large part to the narrow icy passages that often populated the racelanes. The ban did not cripple the planet's population, however, and it wasn't long before swoop racing caught on as a substitute.
Unlike most Duros, Jade took to swoop bike racing instead of exploring the galaxy in a starcraft. Jade often participated in races as a teenager; in most cases she was usually the youngest one there by a significant margin. She remained a racer on Ando Prime well into her adult life - by now something of a local celebrity - until she recently left to explore the rest of the galaxy, much like her ancestors did generations before her.

== Description ==
Standing a tall 1.77 meters, this adult humanoid could be nothing but a Duros. She has the distinctive characteristics of her species - hairless body, round red eyes, noseless face, and thin lips - although her skin tone is a pale chalk-white, suggesting she belongs to one of the many 'near-duros' offshoots in the galaxy.

She is somewhat thin, although this Duros possesses a deliberate way of walking and a dextrous way of moving that makes her seem more graceful than she actually is. Lacking any jewelry or embellishment, a baggy black flightsuit covers her from head to toe, and a utility belt is slung haphazardly around her waist. Well worn dark work boots sheathe her feet.

== Personality ==
This Duros is certainly a quiet one. While many members of her species are outgoing and gregarious, Jade prefers to stay out of the limelight. Although most certainly not shy, she tends to channel herself a bit awkwardly in most social situations, as if she was trying far too hard to sound polite or friendly.

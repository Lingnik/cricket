'''Jake Myar''' was a [[Human#Kuati|Kuati]] officer in [[Admiral]] [[Dergan Roj]]'s [[Barren Sector Fleet|Fleet]] and who later served as a [[Sar Admiral]] and head of [[the Admiralty]] for the [[Caspian Navy]]. He had been the [[captain]] of the [[CMS Daring|heavy cruiser ''Daring'']] and was assigned to the newly-formed Admiralty as an assisting admiral by [[Teodore Poole]], Roj's aide. The move was made to "keep an eye on [[Elijah Taylor|[Elijah] Taylor]]", whom Roj, Poole, and [[Lawton Fraser|Fraser]] never fully trusted.

Myar proved himself quite adept to the task of building a capable navy. He was an advocate for the deployment of warships to the then-tenuous frontier of Caspian and Krittish territory. Fraser was also enabled and encouraged to fashion the starfighter forces into a strong and responsive entity primarily because of Myar's efforts. [[Lyr Dunwell]] was a staunch supporter of Myar's, and heavily encouraged his nomination and appointment to one of the [[Sar Admiral]] chairs.

Other galactic events would intervene, though, and at some point in the presavship of [[Avalyshaar Dusten]], Myar resigned from the Admiralty and departed Caspia for the [[The Galaxy#Core Worlds|Core Worlds]].

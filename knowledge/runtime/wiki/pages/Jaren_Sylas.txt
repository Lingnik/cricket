NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2023-04-25T03:47:12Z
======================================================================
Little is known about Jaren Sylas. After Aknar Koth'lyri stepped down from Director of NRI, he took over serving as Director briefly before retiring. When Aknar came back to NRI after the fall of Bothawui, he persuaded Jaren to come back with him, promoting him to Deputy Director. After several months, Jaren Sylas disappeared. His whereabouts are unknown.

'''Jedi Initiate''' referred to a [[Force]]-sensitive child undergoing the early stages of Jedi training - also known informally as a '[[:starwars:Youngling|Youngling]]'. Typically, Jedi Initiate was used to describe those Jedi-in-training who were yet to become [[Padawan|Padawans]].

In the [[Jedi Order|New Jedi Order]], however, Jedi Initiates come in all ages, as [[Luke Skywalker]] accepts both adults and adolescents as well as younglings into his teachings. This can often cause a gray area when referring to a Jedi Initiate versus a Youngling, and is why Force Sensitive children are often referred to as Youngling.

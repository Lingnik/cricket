The '''Jedi Knight''' is a very important class for the [[Jedi Order]]. In the [[:starwars:Jedi Order|Old Jedi Order]], the Jedi Knights were the defenders of peace and justice in the Galaxy. After the [[:starwars:Jedi Purge|Great Jedi Purge]], the role of Jedi Knights would take on a much different meaning.

Under [[Luke Skywalker]], the New Jedi Order's primary goalsin current times are to follow the will and guidance of the Force, grow and expand the Jedi Order, and defend the Galaxy from the threat of a re-emerged Sith Order.

There are no strict guidelines as to how and when a [[Padawan]] is brought into Knighthood, due to the small size of the New Jedi Order. The decision is made by the leading [[Jedi Master]], Luke Skywalker, and is enacted through a ceremony meant to honor the Padawan and respectfully declare Knighthood on the student.

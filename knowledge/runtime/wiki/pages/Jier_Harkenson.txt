'''Jier Harkenson''' was an [[Admiral]] in the [[Imperial Navy]]. In [[15 ABY]] he defected from the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] and pledged his support to the [[Neo-Imperial Movement]], though survived the organization's elimination following the [[Battle for Purification|destruction of]] Garrison Vexed. Vowing to bring about the collapse of [[Aleister Vadim|Aleister Vadim's]] government, Harkenson established a rebel front on [[Teyr]] called the [[Teyr Resistance Front]], where he brought in surviving leaders from the Neo-Empire and tons of support. His operation was discovered before it could be fully implemented, and the resulting [[Battle of Teyr]] ended Harkenson's life and destroyed the Front.

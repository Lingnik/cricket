'''Jolan A. Kaerf''' ([[19 BBY]] - ) was an orphan, raised alongside his brother Angelys by an old hermit in the deserts of [[Tatooine]]. After leaving Tatooine, he joined the [[Imperial Naval Academy]], and graduated to serve in the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]]. Jolan's military records through the Empire are rather sparse; but it remained quite a distinguished, albeit short career. Shortly after graduating from the Imperial Academy, he was assigned to [[Pride-1]] (under occupation by the Empire) under the command of Captain Lynnor Neilan. Showing prowess with both abilities in the starfighter, and tactics, he was promoted several times until he was given command of [[Razor Squadron]] itself.

Finding himself reprimanded, and ending the career of his CO, Captain Neilan, because of an affair; he was transferred to the fleet, to serve on the [[I2SD Emperor Valak|HIMS ''Emperor Valak'']]. For several more years, he maintained his status as a Lt. Commander, and CO of several different squadrons, until he was promoted to Chief of Starfighter Operations for the Dreven Battle Group a position in which he shined at very well.

With this new position, he found himself a married man; his wedding presided over by then [[Moff]] [[Morlok Adlerson]]; the man's mentor from the earliest days of flying. Jolan continued his service on the ''Emperor Valak'', finding many more years in him; having served with great members of the Imperial High Command such as Lord [[Jarl Rellik]], and Admiral [[Benjamin Rastus]]. Near the end of Valak's reign, with the Empire he knew from the Academy falling apart, Jolan retired; one of the youngest Chiefs of Starfighter Command, and at the humble rank of Captain. It's believed he returned home to Tatooine to bury his wife, who died in childbirth; but his communications with the new Imperial Government and old friends have been sparse, at best.

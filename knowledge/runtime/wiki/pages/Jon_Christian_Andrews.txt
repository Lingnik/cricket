'''Jon Christian Andrews''' was an [[Imperial Navy]] and former [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Old Republic]] [[:starwars:Republic Navy|Navy]] officer.

At the time of the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]], Andrews was a well-established naval officer, noted for his quick-thinking and strategic skills. He remained loyal to the [[Interim Ruling Council]] and participated at both the [[Battle of Coruscant]] and [[Second Battle of Coruscant]].

In [[5 ABY]], [[Wyth Skellheim]], having executed [[Warlord]] [[Connor Dobbs]] and taken control of the Council, promoted Andrews to [[Grand Admiral]] and gave command of the Imperial Navy to him.

Andrews would soon retire, however, when Skellheim mysteriously vanished.

'''Jullon''' is cotton-like fiber with a high silica content. The cloth is dyeable, and very fire-resistant.  The silica content also makes the cloth an excellent insulator from electrical effects, a handy trait in clothing on the storm-swept planets of the [[Caspia|Caspia System]].

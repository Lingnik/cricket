The '''''KMS Ascension''''' is the twenty-second construct of the [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]], in the service of the [[Caspian Navy]]. She was built at [[Lansdowne Loch]] on [[Krittain Major]], subcontracted by [[Lokkai Starworks]] to [[Aston Aerospace]] along with ''[[KMS Krittain Major]]''. The ship was officially named for a planet in the Krittai-4 System, indeed all Mergansar-Class ships were named for celestial objects. It is, however, more popularly considered to be named for the ''[[CMS Ascension]]'' which had been part of [[Admiral]] [[Dergan Roj|Dergan Roj's]] '''[[Barren Sector Fleet]]''', and subsequently one of the initial ships of the Caspian Navy.

== Task Group L ==

Originally launched as ''CMS Ascension'', it and its sister ship, then known as ''[[KMS Krittain Major|CMKS Krittain Major]]'' were built as part of a subcontract to [[Aston Aerospace]] by [[Lokkai Starworks]] which designed the [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]] (along with [[CDMC]]) and normally constructed the vessels. While the ''Krittain Major'' was considered an official Krittish contribution to the Navy, crewed almost exclusively by Krits, an kept assigned to the [[Krittain|Krittain System]], ''Ascension'' was considered a mainline [[Caspian Navy]] vessel.

The [[Battle of Ketterslea]], fought the year before ''Ascension's'' launched convinced [[the Admiralty]] that a strong presence in the [[Ketterslea|Kett System]] was important to the future defence of the [[Caspian Democratic Union|Union]]. ''Ascension'' was assigned to the system to head up a '''Task Group L''' which would maintain patrols in that part of [[Caspian Sector|Caspian Space]].

During the [[2015 Caspar Plot|crisis]] in [[24 ABY]], the bulk of the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] fleet deployed near the Kett System. The Caspian naval flotilla assigned to the system was no match for the opposition force and avoided engaging with it, allowing the Empire to focus its efforts against the [[New Republic|Republic]] forces also operating in Caspian Space.

== Krittish Navy ==

Though not formally considered a Krittish ship, when the Caspian Democratic Union collapsed following the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] and [[Mergansar|Mergani]] [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]], ''Ascension'' sided with the Krit Union and became part of the [[Krittish Navy]]. While ''Ascension'' had a Krittish officers serving in a number of key rolls, there was also a sense among other officers that the Krit Union was a closer successor state to the CDU than the rump [[Caspian Union|Caspar Union]].

After the election of [[Crestian Tarasar]] as [[Presav]] of the Caspar Union, negotiations immediately got underway to reunited with old union worlds. While [[Kasputin Yar]] and [[Rauther]] rejoined almost immediately, and [[Ixin]] not long after, negotiations with the Krittish Union were slow. Mutual animosity kept most of the talk low-level diplomats and back channel negotiations. Though the Tarasar administration wanted the Krits back, they wanted concessions the Krits were not initially prepared to accept.

The limitations in the Krittish fleet would become apparent in early [[30 ABY]] when the [[Ushton-class Heavy Freighter|Ushton-Class Heavy Freighter]] ''Blechley Brook'' which has been employed as transport for a school trip out of Krittain Minor, was [[Report:A Minor Consideration|attacked]] by slavers. By the time the ''[[KMS Lirithose]]'' came on the scene they found this ship wrecked, four dead, and 308 passengers and crew missing. Though elements of the '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]''' would soon join in the unsuccessful search for the slavers and subsequent patrolling of Krittish space, this would prove the catalyst which finally brought the two Unions to the negotiating table in earnest.

== Caspian 4th Fleet ==

Within a month of the attack on the ''Blechley Brook'' the Krittain System joined the new Caspian Union spelling an end to an independent Krittish Fleet. The Krittish Fleet was merged with the '''[[Caspian 2nd Fleet]]''' with the Krittish ships maintain the KMS prefix. Though the majority of the Krittish Fleet would remain as part of the Caspian 2nd Fleet several vessels would be reassigned to other Caspian Fleets. The '''[[Caspian 1st Fleet|1st]]''' and '''[[Caspian 3rd Fleet|3rd]]''' Fleets which each be assigned one of the [[Arsenal-class Heavy Cruiser|Arsenal-Class Heavy Cruisers]] and two of the [[Valorous-class Light Carrier|Valorous-Class Light Carriers]] for their Patrol Groups. Meanwhile the Valorous-Class ''KMS Intrepid'' and Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruisers ''[[KMS Lirithose]]'' and ''[[CMS Ascension|KMS Ascension]]'' would join the [[Caspian 4th Fleet|4th Fleet]]''.

[[File:Dylanto Hunter.jpg|thumb|Commodore Dylanto Hunter]] Following the reunion, command of the ''Ascension'' was given to [[Commodore]] Dylanto Hunter. Hunter had command the ''CMS Minvera'' prior to the collapse of the old Union. More recently he had been head of the Clandestine Operations Section of the Krittish Naval Intelligence Office. The [[Caspian Intelligence Service]] did not operated on the same military model that that Krits had maintained and most of their leadership chose to return to mainline naval service, largely taking high profile roles in the Caspian 4th Fleet. As an intelligence veteran himself, [[Sar Admiral]] [[Odie Hood]] valued the skill set Commodore Hunter brought to the table and tapped him to serve as executive officer of the 4th Fleet's '''Task Group Resplendent'''.

'''CMKS ''Krittain Major'' (CHG-23)''' is the twenty-third construct of the [[Caspian Navy|Caspian Navy's]] line of [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|''Mergansar''-class cruisers]].


''Krittain Major'' was named for the primary planet in the [[Krittain]] star system. It acts as a replacement for ''Krittania'', a venerable [[Dreadnaught-class Heavy Cruiser|''Dreadnaught''-class cruiser]] that had been retired after exhaustive service during the campaigns of the Galactic Civil War, and had been acting as Krittain's flagship until the introduction of the [[Arsenal-class Heavy Cruiser|''Arsenal'' -type cruisers]].

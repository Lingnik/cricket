The '''''KMS Lirithose''''' is the sixth construct of the [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]], in the service of the [[Caspian Navy]]. She was built at [[Nenawirth Yards]] in [[Kasputin Yar]] and named for a [[Lirithose|planet]] in the [[Krittain|Krittain System]]. It originally entered service as ''CMKS Lirithose'' indicating it was being crewed primarily by Krittish personnel and 
maintained by Krittish resources. At the time, there was an overabundance of Krittish recruits, but they tended to prefer serving on ships with other Krits.

== Early Service == 

As the fleet at [[Krittain]] had few heavy warships, the ''Lirithose'' was assigned to the system to augment the existing force there. In its first year it was largely patrolling up and down the [[Perlemian Trade Route]]. As resources were built up in the [[Caspian Navy]], and due to ''Lirithose's'' performance record, the cruiser was re-assigned to the [[Yariz|Yariz System]].

''Lirithose'' was most well known for her service in the safeguarding of Yariz, since it was a heavily trafficked region and often came in contention with rogue outfits and overbearing principalities such as the [[Hutt|Hutts]] and the Tion Hegemony. At one point, a routine interdiction of a smuggling freighter turned into a hellacious duel with a Hutt star cruiser. The picket had made contact with the freighter a short hyperjump away, and had requested the heavy cruiser to travel to its location to give support. The freighter itself had called in assistance, and when ''Lirithose'' dropped out of hyperspace, it was staring down the Hutt cruiser ''Voligure''. Belligerence was on the mind of the Hutt ship's captain, and a running, two-hour battle ensued, with ''Lirithose'' emerging victorious.

== Krittish Navy == 

After the collapse of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] following the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] and [[Mergansar|Mergani]] [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]], ''Lirithose'' returned to the [[Krittain|Krittain System]] becoming part of the independent [[Krittish Navy]], and trading the 'CMKS' prefix for 'KMS.' In [[30 ABY]], it was the first ship on site after the [[Ushton-class Heavy Freighter|Ushton-Class Heavy Freighter]] ''Blechley Brook'' which has been employed as transport for a school trip out of Krittain Minor, was [[Report:A Minor Consideration|attacked]] by slavers. By the time it arrived, ''Lirithose'' found the ship wrecked, four dead, and 308 passengers and crew missing.

The Krittish Navy would soon be supported by elements of the '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]''' in their search for signs of the slavers and the missing passengers of the crew of the ''Blechley Brook'' but their efforts would prove unsuccessful. It did, however, prove the catalyst to get to get the Krit Union and the new [[Caspian Union]] back to the negotiation table in earnest. The two Unions were soon able to finally hammer out an agreement and the Krits rejoined the Caspians and their other former Union mates.

== Caspian 4th Fleet ==

[[File:Jade Noquehool.jpg|thumb|Fleet Captain Jade Noquehool]] Following the reunion most the Krittish Fleet was merged with the '''[[Caspian 2nd Fleet]]''' with the Krittish ships maintain the KMS prefix. Though the majority of the Krittish Fleet would remain as part of the Caspian 2nd Fleet several vessels would be reassigned to other Caspian Fleets. Based on its strong service record, ''Lirithose'' was given the honour of being assigned to '''Task Force Renaissance''' of the Caspian 4th Fleet. It is currently commanded by [[Fleet Captain]] Jade Noquhool.

'''Kahrak Taiden''' was the young captain of the ''[[Supernova Siren]]'' and employee of [[Galactic Amusements]]. Following the disappearance of the company's CEO, Taiden took joint-ownership of the corporation with [[Noah Kryeck]]. However, the company fell under, and Taiden sold the ''Supernova Siren'' to [[FoxTech Industries]]. His activities following this are unknown.

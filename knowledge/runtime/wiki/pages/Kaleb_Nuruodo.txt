=== History ===

==== Childhood ====

Kaleb was born on Csilla in 10 ISY. His childhood was short-lived and before he reached adolescence, he was being trained in tactics and rigorously studying alien cultures and languages. It was his family's hope that he become a member of the Chiss Expansionary Defense Force and earn a place of honor in the fleet. Kaleb excelled in martial skills and developed a strong mind and body. It was not long before he became a member of House Nuruodo.

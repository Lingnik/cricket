'''Kiri Valios''' is the son of [[Jessalyn Valios]] and [[Aurejin]], a cloned Jedi of the Old Republic, born on Auotos Beta IV (also known as Gaena) in [[13 ABY]]. He is the half-brother of [[Sabine Valios]]. Kiri is currently an initiate within the [[Jedi Order]]. Even as a newborn, Kiri's innate Force abilities were very strong and nurtured by his parents. As a baby, Kiri recognized [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]]'s latent Force-gift, which led to her becoming a Jedi apprentice under his mother. In order to keep him safe from the Sith, Kiri and other Jedi children were kept hidden on Gaena from 13-17 ABY.[[File:NPC_Kiri_Valios.jpg|thumb|left|200px|Kiri Valios, in training as a Youngling]]

In [[17 ABY]], Kiri and several other Jedi Initiates were captured by Aurejin, who had turned to the Dark Side. He attempted to bring them to [[Aleister Vadim|Vadim]], but the Younglings were rescued by Johanna and [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] who joined forces to stop Aurejin and save the children.

Not long after this incident, Kiri, [[Ranma Hoshiakarui]] and [[Paul Nighman]] were captured by Darth Malign during a trip to Dac. While held captive on [[Trandosha]], Malign tried to sway the child with Sith philosophies and bequeathed him with the name Darth Tragos. His mother, Jessalyn Valios, would call in every favor she was owed by the New Republic, and thus the Battle of Trandosha was fought in part to cover the rescue effort to retrieve Kiri and the others. He was largely unhurt, though watching his friends tortured and experimented upon by the Sith would leave indelible wounds and create a yearning to learn more and more about his impressive, growing powers.

Kiri was extremely precocious and bright for his age, and often startled even other Jedi with his Force abilities. His ability to see both into the past and the future seemed to be his strongest gift as a child, and he was used to having dreams and hunches that often came true. 

For most of Kiri's adolescence, he was in attendance with his mother and sister [[Sabine Valios]] (born [[18 ABY]]) as she traveled the galaxy recruiting and conducting diplomatic relations for the Jedi Order. He was her Padawan as well, and their adventures during these years were crucial in his development into a young Jedi Knight. He looks forward to his initiation into the Order with excitement and trepidation -- he is well aware of the dangers and responsibility that come with that mantle. 

Kiri has had a long distance crush on Johanna's daughter [[Ara te Danaan]] since they were children, and they've corresponded for much of their lives. How they will respond to each other once they're more than pen pals remains to be seen.

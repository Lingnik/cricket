{{Character|name = Kitrorwarr|planet = Kashyyyk|species = Wookiee|gender = Male|height = 2.1 meters|hair = Black and Gray|eyes = Blue|skin = n/a|affiliation = New Republic|uniform = Kashyyyk Governor}}The Wookiee '''Kitrorwarr''' is the current Governor of Kashyyyk and has held the position since about 15 ABY. He has humble beginnings as an independent space explorer.

He was saved during a riot on Corellia by a Jedi named [[Aurejin]], and swore a life debt to the man. Kitrorwarr headed a private scouting and cartography company that primarily worked for the New Republic.

Kitrorwarr was popular with the Wookiees in [[Rwookrrorro]] city, Kashyyyk. He ran for Governor and won the election unchallenged.

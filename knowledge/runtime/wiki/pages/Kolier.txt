'''''Kolier''''' is a [[CR90_Corvette|Corellian Corvette]] with an unknown commission date. By all outward appearances the Corvette remains in stock configuration. Operating around the Outer Rim and the Core worlds, it has a good reputation for protecting cargo shipments and transporting goods and travelers. It is also rumored to have committed acts of piracy and privateering, but there has been no known evidence supporting these claims.

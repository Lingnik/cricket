The '''Krittish Volunteers''' are units of [[Union Marines]] recruited from the [[Krittain|Krittain System]]. Though the [[Caspian Defence Force]] typically matches Krittish Volunteers with assignments at Krittish bases and on Krittish ships they may be deployed anywhere the Union has an interest.

The Volunteers trace their origins to Krittain's own convict-&-refugee nature, with [[King]] Liam the First eradicating a substantial portion of pirate and raider bands that had plagued the region for quite some time. Understanding the outlaw nature of his kingdom, Liam presented a choice to the imprisoned - volunteer for service to his crown or languish and rot in the dismal Krittish prisons. Those that volunteered were "re-educated" and became an ideal force to counter piracy because they themselves "looked like 'em, spat like 'em, spake like 'em, and t'ought like 'em".

Over the years, the Volunteer ranks and activity level diminished until the advent of the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] era, wherein it nearly ceased existence. The commonwealth with [[Caspia]], though, twenty years later reinvigorated many Krittish interests and entities. Several enterprising officers within the newly formed Union Marines decided to "borrow" the legacy and moniker of the Volunteers for their unit. 

Volunteers of today are wholly from Krittain. During the days of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], members of the Krittish Volunteers had to be able to prove at least two generations of citizenship. This largely remained the case when the CDU dissolved and [[Krittain Major]] and [[Krittain Minor|Minor]] formed the Krit Union, though other units were formed to absorb personnel who wished to serve the Krit Union but were unable to meet this requirement. Following the reunification as part of the new [[Caspian Union]], Krittish citizenship was extended to all current residents of the system and the Krittish Volunteers were forced to accept any Krittish citizens who wished to join without regard for any ancestral connection.

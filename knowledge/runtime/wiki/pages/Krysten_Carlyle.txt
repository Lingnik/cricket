'''Krysten Carlyle''' Rheas was first seen on [[Caspar]] as a willful teenager. Her antics got her noticed and she was on the fast track to trouble until an unknown benefactor gave her the money to go to the Academy. With little else to do, she took up the offer and graduated with honors. Now having a focus in her life she joined up with the [[Caspian Navy]]. Her loud mouth came into good use when she got her assignment as a Communications Officer. Communication had always been her strong suit, having been able to talk her way into and out of any situation. 

She was deployed to [[Trinumvira]], the busiest military base in the Union, to coordinate the [[Naval Aerospace Arm|"Arrows"]] and keep the [[Marine Aerospace Division|"Madmen"]] in line. The story goes that the very first time her voice went out over the wireless, one of those fly boys fell in love. Whether it was the first time or sometime later, a romance did develop between Krysten and one [[Doron Rtufo]]. It was magical and wonderful. They were considered the perfect couple, when suddenly Krysten left with only a letter as good-bye. The ambiguous note was almost more than she could do, but it had to be done. Some parts of her past were catching up to her and the [[Naval Intelligence Office|Naval Intelligence Office (NIO)]] said they had to relocate her to keep her safe. She went into hiding in the nearby system of [[Krittain]].

Under a new last name, she spent years there working within the local government there. She eventually moved on with her life, and met a new man and was married. The union was happy and perfect. Her husband worked with the [[Krittish Volunteers]] when the times called for him, but one day he never returned home. She waited and waited and talked through his chain of command and continually got the run around. They told her he was dead and to leave it at that. Wounded to the spirit and unable to bear staying in Krittain any longer, she returned to the only other place that felt like home. After discussing things over with the NIO, it was agreed that the threat on her life has passed and she could come out of hiding.

Returning back to Caspar, Krysten was placed as the new CEO of [[FineLine Spaceways]], with the assistance of NIO and the [[Commissioner of Commerce]].

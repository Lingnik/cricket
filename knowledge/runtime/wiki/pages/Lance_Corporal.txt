The rank and title of '''Lance Corporal''' is among the lowest level of an enlisted soldier in a military organization, most frequently used by army and starfighter corps. They are often referred to as "Junior Enlisted".

=== In New Republic Service ===

Along with [[Private]]s and [[Private First Class|PFC]]s, Lance Corporals form the physical body of the '''[[New Republic GroundOps|New Republic Army]]'''. They are typically young sentients with less than or approximately three standard years of field experience.

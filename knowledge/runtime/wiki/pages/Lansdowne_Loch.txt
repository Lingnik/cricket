=== The Facility ===
The starship-building facilities sprawl between nine separate fjords. The two largest, ''Tanghauser'' and ''Kongraesser'', are dedicated to single or twin ship contracts, with hulls in excess of 600 meters. Tanghauser is able to accommodate two hulls of 900 meters, side-by-side with ease, while Kongraesser can, with some quick reconfiguration, accommodate a single ship in excess of 1300 meters. The other, less voluminous fjords are assigned to all manner of construction for smaller starships. One whole fjord, ''Hosspur'', is dedicated to engine development, testing, and calibration, while the second smallest, and closest to the city of Lansdowne, ''Burnaiber'', serves as the administrative hub for all the companies that utilize the facility.

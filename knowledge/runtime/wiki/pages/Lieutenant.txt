'''Lieutenant''' is a military or police force rank that represents an under-, or junior, officer of commission. This rank, and its derivatives, are often the second tier of commission in most militaries, and the third tier of responsibility in police forces.

=== In Imperial Service ===
{{rank plate|[[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]]|}}
The [[Galactic Empire]] utilizes the ''lieutenant'' rank throughout its varied forces, including the [[Imperial Navy]], [[Imperial Army]], [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]], and [[Stormtrooper Corps]]. On a smaller to medium sized vessel in the [[Imperial Navy]], a lieutenant may be a senior officer, possibly the vessel's executive officer. On a larger sized vessel, such as a [[Star Destroyer]], a lieutenant is not high enough to command, but is usually in charge of a section of a vessel's operations. A lieutenant can also be an aid to a senior officer.

A lieutenant is usually still green (compared to field grade officers) but has more experience than a [[Sub-Lieutenant]] and demonstrates further tactical thinking and leadership.

A Lieutenant was the equivalent to a [[Flight Lieutenant]] in the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]]. 

=== In New Republic Service ===

=== In Union Service ===
The [[Caspian Democratic Union]] utilizes the ''lieutenant'' rank in their [[Caspian Navy|Navy]], [[Union Marines|Marines]], and some units of their [[Provincial Army of the Union|Provincial Army]].

In the Navy, most lieutenants were officers of department (or responsibility) aboard warships, or were even the executive officer on cutters or sloops. They typically had one to four [[Midshipman|midshipmen]] working beneath them. In the [[Naval Aerospace Arm]], a lieutenant was a more experienced pilot, often leading a section of the squadron, with command of the squadron led by a [[Captain]], [[Commander]], or [[Lieutenant Commander]].

In the Marines, the lieutenant ranks are the lowest grade of officers, and in line units invariably appear as platoon commanders. The [[Marine Aerospace Division]] was much like the NAA in its use of lieutenants, but there would be more of them in a squadron than the NAA equivilent. Due to different rank scales, a Marine Lieutenant is of lower grade than a Navy Lieutenant.

Provincial Army units utilized the lieutenant rank quite liberally <''content needed''>...

In Union circles, by tradition, the rank was pronounced ''lef''tenant.

=== In Griffon Service ===
The '''lieutenant''' rank was liberally used as a formality, and best described the leader of a [[Spaceborne Assault Trooper Armee|SAT peloton]]. Lieutenants were also present on [[Griffon Armada|Armada]] warships, but the nomenclature was more an indication of role than of rank formality.

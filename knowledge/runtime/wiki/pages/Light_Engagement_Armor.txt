This "Light Engagement Tactical" Armor is the New Republic's combat armor for most military forces. It is a lightweight, tight fit suit that is both waterproof and climate-controlled. Several pieces join together to cover the more vital body parts such as the torso, abdomen, upper arms and the lower to upper legs. The unique material is easily camouflauged and gives the wearer respectable defense from physical and energy attacks while suppressing very little movement.

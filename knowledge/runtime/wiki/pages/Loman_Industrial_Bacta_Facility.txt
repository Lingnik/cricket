The '''Loman Industrial Bacta Facility''' was a large-scale bacta refinery located in [[Reef Home City]] on [[Dac]]. It was one of the major providers of sterile bacta for the planet's hospitals.

During the [[Dacan Civil War]] of [[16 ABY]], the facility was overrun in the early hours of civil unrest by angry Quarren rioters. The Quarren rioters took many of the facility's staff hostage; the rest they murdered in cold blood.

The facility was soon liberated by the [[New Republic]] [[Raider Battalion (224MBN)|224th Marine Battalion]], in an operation that would soon ignite a diplomatic firestorm.

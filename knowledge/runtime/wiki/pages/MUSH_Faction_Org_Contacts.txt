Getting involved with the world of [[Star Wars MUSH]] typically involves a [[:Category:Factions|Faction]] or [[:Category:Organizations|Organization]], unless you have your sights set on playing an [[Independent]]. This article serves as a one-stop resource for anything you may need to know about getting involved with these organizations.

'''Factions''' are the powerhouses of the MUSH, the predominant entities that constitute the bulk of story-line direction and interaction. Various [[:Category:Organizations|organizations]] and [[:Category:Governments|governments]] exist either within them or independently parallel to them. Each major faction has a series of administrators meant to handle specific faction-related responsibilities, ranging from MUSH-hired [[Organization Head|Faction Heads]] to faction-hired [[Organization Head|Organization Heads]].

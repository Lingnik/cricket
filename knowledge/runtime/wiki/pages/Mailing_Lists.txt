There are a variety of mailing lists for [[Star Wars MUSH]].  Anyone may send
mail to any of the following lists.  Some lists require special permission
to join, however. Please send all mail in plain text format.

This list is open to the public and used for announcements.
<pre>  sw-mail@starwarsmush.com              General discussion</pre>
         
This list, for RPA members only, requires approval of the RP Wiz to join.
<pre>  sw-rpa@starwarsmush.com               Roleplay discussion</pre>
         
These lists, for Admin only, require the approval of the Director join. 
<pre>  sw-admin@starwarsmush.com             Admin discussion</pre>
<pre>  sw-wiz@starwarsmush.com               Wizard discussion</pre>
         
Adds and joins can also be sent to <list>-request (ie, sw-mail-request@)

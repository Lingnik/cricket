'''Major''' is a military rank that represents an officer that is mid-to-upper level of commission, and often acts alongside or just under a [[Colonel]]. It is often a parallel to the Naval rank of [[Commander]] or [[Lieutenant Commander]].

=== In Imperial Service ===

=== In New Republic Service ===

Majors in the '''[[New Republic GroundOps|New Republic Army]]''' are the bridge between book-smarts, and battle-smarts. Typically they act as either an Executive Officer at the Battalion level or are assigned to a support unit at the Brigade or higher level to gain non-combat command and operational readiness experience. In rare occasions, they are put in charge of a Battalion when there are no other fitting officers. In the case of support personnel, a Major may be in charge of an entire Battalion. 

In the '''[[New Republic StarOps|New Republic Starfighter Corps]]''', Majors are typically newly-christened aces, with plenty of combat experience. Still the bridge between book-smarts and battle-smarts, they are most often put in charge of their own squadron. In rare occasions, they are put in charge of a Strike Group when there are no other fitting officers. In the case of support personnel, a Major may be in charge of an entire Strike Group's detachment of Enlisted personnel and officers.

=== In Union Service ===

The rank of Major is virtually exclusive to the [[Union Marines]], with some sporadic use in the [[Provincial Army of the Union|Provincial Army]]. A Major typically acts as a direct aide or second-in-command of a [[Colonel|Colonel's]] regiment, or commands a special battalion or detachment. In the [[Marine Aerospace Division]], it has come to signify an officer who commands a special operations unit and who ''may'' function as a Wing or Group Commander if the Naval structure necessitated.

Some [[Krittain|Krittish]] Provincial Army units are led by Majors, but these act as independent entities not encompassed by a larger unit.

'''Malus Brock''' (&ndash;[[10 ABY]]), also known as '''Chipper Long''', was an infamous criminal wanted throughout the [[The Galaxy|galaxy]] by most major galactic institutions for his acts of terrorism, including the destruction of several miles of cityscape on [[Etti IV]]. While traveling to [[Selene]] under the guise of Chipper Long, he was apprehended by [[Danik Kreldin]] and placed under arrest for failing to cooperate. Kreldin oversaw Brock's interrogation, and subsequent torture, but later released Brock, unaware of his criminal history.

Brock would resurface on [[Theseus Station]] while Kreldin was aboard, and ambushed Kreldin in retaliation for the torture he endured. Kreldin subdued Brock, and turned him over to [[Ernest Pallando]], who recognized the man as the infamous Malus Brock. Pallando immediately had him executed by slicing his head off, and later staged a false execution on the station, although he was already dead.

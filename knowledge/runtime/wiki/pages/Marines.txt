The term '''Marines''' refers to a specific type of soldier in the employ of a recognized military entity. It has been used three times in known history.

*The [[Union Marines|Marines]] of the Caspian Democratic Union.
*The [[New Republic Marine Corps|Marines of the New Republic]], a quintet of Corps operating alongside the Army of the New Republic.
*The [[:starwars:Galactic Marines|Galactic Marines]], an ultra-elite division within the Grand Army of the Republic.

'''Maris Becton''' was an officer in the [[Imperial Army]]. In [[14 ABY]], an initiative led by [[Grand Admiral]] [[Danik Kreldin]] and [[Grand Vizier]] [[Vassily Korolov]] saw many Army officers shifted to the [[Stormtrooper Corps]], and Maris was one such officer. However, after an incident with a [[Imperial Royal Guard|Royal Guardsman]], [[Maethor Savage]], who held a strong bias against females in the military, Becton was transferred to the [[Imperial Navy|Imperial Navy's]] security forces, where she served aboard the [[I2SD Inquisitor|HIMS ''Inquisitor'']].

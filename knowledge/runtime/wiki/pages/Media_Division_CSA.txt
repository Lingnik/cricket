An assortment of demagogues, spin doctors, and other image savvy executives, the '''Media Division''' is well versed in the manipulation of public opinion.  Its primary role is to oversee [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] public relations and promote a positive image of the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Authority]] to the rest of the galaxy. Given the CSA's track record of pursuing profit at all cost, it's a difficult task, and Media executives will use everything from legitimate press releases to propaganda and subliminal advertising to get the job done.  In addition to its public relations activities, Media also monitors and approves the content of all public broadcasts within the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Sector]].

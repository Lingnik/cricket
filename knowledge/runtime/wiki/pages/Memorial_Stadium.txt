'''Memorial Stadium''' is a large [[swoop]] stadium operated by the [[Galactic Empire]] and the [[Galactic Swoop League]] in [[Central City]], [[Athaniss]].

Since its opening, there have been no official GSL tournaments held in the stadium, due to a lack of visitors to the Imperial backwater. A few aspiring swoop racers of Imperial affiliation hone their skills on this track, as do a cadre of Imperial Army Scout Troopers who utilize their leave time to host a makeshift tournament/champions-circuit.

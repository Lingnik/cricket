A '''Mercenary''' was someone who fought for money.  

Mercenaries were often said to have 'neutral' affiliation and were hired by either the [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Rebel Alliance]] or the [[Galactic Empire]] without actually joining them. In some cases though, they grew sympathetic to their clients. Mercenaries worked either as freelancers, or as members of some greater organization.

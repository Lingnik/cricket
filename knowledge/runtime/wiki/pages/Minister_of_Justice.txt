The '''Minister of Justice''' was a position in the [[New Republic]]. The minister was one of the key members of the [[New Republic General Ministry|General Ministry]] and the various ruling councils, always being the fourth most powerful member, under the [[Chief of State]], [[Minister of State]], and [[Supreme Commander]]. Should the [[Minister of State]] have to take over for a [[Chief of State]], the minister of justice would assume the duties associated with the [[Minister of State]].

The minister of justice was the chair of the [[Justice Council|Senate Justice Council]].

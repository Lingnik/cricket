The '''Minister of State''' was a position in the [[New Republic]]. The Minister of State was a simultaneous Chief Minister and Foreign Minister (though not the head of government) and was head of the [[New Republic General Ministry|General Ministry]]. The Minister was appointed by the [[Chief of State]], and answered to the Chief. If the Chief of State was unable to discharge his duties, the Minister of State was a possible choice to replace the Chief.

The '''Moff's Council''' is one of the leading bodies of the [[Galactic Empire]]. Along with the Commander-in-Chief ([[Executor (title)|Military Executor]]/[[Supreme Commander of the Imperial Forces]]), the [[Grand Admiral's Council]] and the [[Grand General's Council]], the Moff Council comprises [[Imperial High Command]] and is responsible for the governing of Imperial territory.

Positioned directly beneath the [[Imperial Ruling Council]], the Moff's Council is subservient to the [[Imperial Advisor|Imperial Advisors]], the Commander-in-Chief, Head of Government ([[Grand Vizier]]), two ruling Councils beneath the Commander-in-Chief, the [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor's]] [[Warlord|Warlords]], [[Dark Side Elite]] and other proxies, and naturally the Emperor himself. Senior members include the [[Grand Moff|Grand Moffs]], rulers of Imperial OverSectors, and beneath them lie the [[Moff|Moffs]], rulers of Imperial sectors.

Moffs are responsible for the governing of Imperial territory and ensuring peace and stability throughout their respective sectors. Military forces deployed to a sector fall under the command of the Moff. Moffs regularly meet at the Moff's Council to discuss important issues and to receive orders from the Ruling Council and to further cooperation between each Moff.

The Council has a member from the Imperial Ruling Council to sit and oversee meetings, and an agent from [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] to ensure loyalty to the Emperor and the [[New Order]]. The Council itself was located on [[Selene]] during the era of [[Bacharan Valak]], and later moved to [[Dreven]] in the wake of the planet's [[Battle of Selene|devastation]], and was later moved to [[Coruscant|Imperial Center]] after its capture by Imperial forces in [[15 ABY]].

The '''NRAF ''Adamant''''' was a member of the [[New Republic FleetOps|New Republic Navy]]'s [[New Republic Second Fleet|Second Fleet]]. She was destroyed in combat at the [[Second Battle of Mon Calamari]] in [[14 ABY]].

== History ==

The '''NRAF ''Adamant''''' was destroyed during the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]] by [[Grand Admiral]] [[Danik Kreldin]]'s [[Task Force Crimson Star]] at [[Dac|Mon Calamari]] during a fierce battle between the opposing military forces. The NRAF ''Adamant'' was destroyed during a climax of the battle in a collision with the Imperial [[I2SD Conqueror|HIMS ''Conqueror'']], Kreldin's flagship.

Already bleeding from battle, ''Adamant'' turned the ''Conqueror'' with the intent of ramming her. It was a brave and desperate move, but it failed. ''Conqueror'' absorbed the impact, and while losing a good portion of her portside hull, was able to extricate herself from ''Adamant'' and make ready a route of escape.

It has been argued that if ''Adamant'' hadn't evacuated her crew, there may have been sufficient velocity to do enough damage to destroy the HIMS ''Conqueror'', a rumor that has cast dark shadows on the faces of [[New Republic]] Navymen and grins of humor on those of [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] Officers. The truth remains unknown.

'''NRCS ''Cadence''''' was a [[New Republic]] [[Carrack-class Light Cruiser|''Carrack''-class Light Cruiser]] serving with the Republic's [[New Republic Second Fleet|Second Fleet]]. She was one of three Republic ''Carrack'' cruisers destroyed during the final battle of [[Operation Shado Kolpo]]. An all-out [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] assault hit ''Cadence'' from behind as she maneuvered in to defend supply freighters bound for [[Coruscant]]. ''Cadence'' was crippled by the assault and the crew was forced to abandon ship.

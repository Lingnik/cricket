======================================================================
To play a '''[[New Republic Marine Corps|New Republic Marine]],''' it is ''highly recommended'' that the character possesses at least 5D in Blaster and Dodge, as well as a 4D in Melee Combat. Vehicle Operations, Mechanical skillsets, and other 'tweaks' may add more diversity to the character, so experimentation is encouraged. Extra points do not have to be finalized yet, and it is recommended to spread stats amongst any necessary skills, such as Demolitions, Communications, or Melee. ''Do '''not''' finalize'' chargen until assignment to a Marine unit.

Each of the four available 'kits' has a distinct purpose. 

*Infantry- Close assault and combat. 
*Scouts- Reconnaissance, surveillance, maneuver. 
*Engineers- Demolitions, sabotage, construction. 
*Driver- Vehicle and gunnery. 

There are skill identifiers, or 'tabs', that characters can acquire as they progress. These are:

*Sharpshooter
*Sapper
*Scout Sniper
*TAC/P (Tactical Air Command/Patrol)
*Fire Support

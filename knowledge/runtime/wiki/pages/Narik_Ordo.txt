'''Narik Ordo''' was a [[Mandalorian]] warrior of the [[:starwars:Clan Ordo|Ordo clan]]. A descendant of [[:starwars:Canderous Ordo|Mandalore the Preserver]], Narik was a true Mandalorian and loyal to the very end. He was betrayed by his close friend, [[Largo Ritt]], during the [[:starwars:Mandalorian Civil War|Mandalorian Civil War]], which led to his death at the hands of Ritt. In [[17 ABY]], his son, [[Wescal Cantrell]], avenged his murder by slaying Ritt on [[Mandalore]].

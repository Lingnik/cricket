'''Nastaran Zohreh''' is a thief and former pirate.

== History ==

Nastaran Zohreh was born and grew up on Nar Shaddaa. Her mother, Gilshir Zohreh was a dancing girl, and her father is rumoured to be a bounty hunter.

Nastaran does not discusss her early life in detail.  She occasionally mentions fights she has been in, brief jail sentences served, but she does not share a lot about her past.

Nastaran parted ways with [[Shiari Vane|Vane]]'s pirate crew several years ago. She vanished for several years before re-emerging in the underworld of Nar Shaddaa.

Older? Yes. Wiser? Probably not. Broke? Definitely.

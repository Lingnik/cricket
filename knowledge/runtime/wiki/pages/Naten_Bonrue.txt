'''Naten Bonrue''' is a flag officer in the [[Caspian Navy]] and reported head of [[Naval Intelligence Office|Naval Intelligence]]. He is a [[Sarian#Janhovi|Janhovi Sarian]], born and raised in Charon, [[Mergansar]]. Hailing from a deeply spiritual family, he has a quiet, measured demeanor about him, a result of his upbringing among the fields and ranches of the Janhovi people in the southern hemisphere of Mergansar.  He seldom speaks, preferring to observe his surroundings.  This tranquil exterior belies a sharp and analytical mind, one long accustomed to seeking out understanding in the people around him.

In his youth, he was given the responsibility of a watchman, safeguarding the livestock and community territory.
This led to nomination and deputizing for the role of a ''lapani'' &mdash; a type of sheriff or police officer. Soon, while [[Caspia]] was under [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] occupation, Naten found himself under the direction of [[Leidan Adamov]]. As Caspia transitioned into an independent entity, Adamov tapped Bonrue to take a more authoritative role, chiefly in investigation and forensics. Because of this transitory status, Adamov had to make Bonrue a "military officer", and he did so in 0 ABY.

When [[Dergan Roj]] brought the new commonwealth government to Caspia, and various offices were being shaped, Adamov retained Bonrue as a part of his staff as a logistics and forensics adviser. Adamov himself was never a brash military strategist, preferring to be much more calculative and logical. After the '''War of Papers''' and the '''Taylor Uprising''' resulted in a cleansing of the [[Naval Intelligence Office]], the remaining [[The Admiralty|Admiralty]] members recommended Bonrue as a replacement, hoping he would rebuild the office as a trustworthy extension once again.

=== Notes ===
Because of his Sarian heritage, Bonrue shows a degree of favoritism towards [[Caspian Rangers]] and other subtle forms of intelligence gathering. While in previous administrations the Rangers and NIO Field Agents would not even go so far as to acknowledge each others' existence, under Bonrue's hand there seems to be a much greater degree of co-habitation. Granted, the entire breadth of NIO operations was completely ransacked during Taylor's mania, so it may be that Bonrue is employing what he trusts and has available &mdash; Rangers, Marines, and the odd Naval unit. He is in good repute with the [[Constable|Constabulary]] due to his ''lapani'' background, and often cultivates them for information.

He is usually on good standing with [[Presav]] [[Thomas Mahon]], and as a member of [[the Admiralty]] consults with the head of the commonwealth on a daily basis. Every so often, though, the Krit and the Sarian do clash on how and why to use NIO Operatives, Rangers, and Marines.

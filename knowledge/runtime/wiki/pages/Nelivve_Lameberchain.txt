'''Nelivve Lameberchain''' ([[41 BBY]] - [[34 ABY]]) was a [[Human]] politician and business executive from [[Dunwelton]] on [[Caspar]]. Lameberchain was a delegate representing Dunwelton in the [[Union Assembly]] from [[14 ABY]] to [[21 ABY]]. Following the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] and [[Mergansar|Mergani]] [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]] in [[28 ABY]], Lameberchain was elected as a delegate to the Provisional Assembly for new [[Caspian Union|Caspar Union]].

As one of the few surviving members of the previous generation of Caspian leaders, he was chosen by his colleagues as [[Presav|Provisional Presav]]. Following the adoption of a new constitution and elections for a permanent [[Union Council]], Lameberchain announced his plan to retire. Following the election of [[Crestian Tarasar]] as the new Presav, Lameberchain retired from public life. He passed away in [[34 ABY]] following a long illness.

The full extent of the [[Neo-Imperial Movement|Neo-Empire]] is a shadow confederation of unknown size and constitution spread out across the galaxy. It is embedded in loyalist sector fleets, planetary defense forces and other arms of the state.

Player Characters are generally (unless otherwise stipulated) assigned to the [[Sector Fleet|sector fleet]] responsible for securing [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector]] space, bolstering the Authority's own [[Corporate Sector Strike Fleet|standing forces]] against hostile military action and assisting in combating piracy. Elements of this fleet are routinely dispatched on special assignment to other sectors of the galaxy.

The list below is intended to provide a quick reference list of assets specifically identified through roleplay to enhance continuity for Neo-Imperial players.

'''It is not an exhaustive list.'''

== Significant NPCs ==
* Admiral [[Solomon Fields]] - Chief of Staff, Grand Admiral of the Neo-Imperial Fleet
* General [[Eisenjorn Creed]] - Commanding Officer of the 203rd Stormtrooper Legion, Field Marshal of the Neo-Imperial Army
* Chief Victor Kohn - Imperial Security Bureau sector chief for the Neo-Imperial Fleet

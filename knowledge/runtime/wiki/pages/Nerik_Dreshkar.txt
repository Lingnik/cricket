'''Nerik Core Dreshkhar''' was a rogue scoundrel and reknown smuggler, fancying himself as the best pilot in the [[The Galaxy|galaxy]]. He worked for the [[Hutt]] cartels on [[Nar Shaddaa]], until he had a falling out and worked his way into the [[V'ez-Tcha]] criminal syndicate, where he became good friends with [[John Donivan]].

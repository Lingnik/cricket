[[Image:The_never_setting_sun.jpg|thumb|right|300px]]

This brutal star bakes the sunside of [[Ryloth]], homeworld of the [[Twi'lek]]s.  Never setting, it has oft been the death of any foolish enough to travel the lands beneath it's raging heat, or unfortunate enough to be exiled out of Twi'lek society.  

The unnusual orbiting and rotational pattern of Ryloth has had a profound impact on Twi'lek culture as their entire civilization is forced to live in the darkness of the hemisphere not in direct contact with it's star.

Generating great heat-storms on Ryloth which whip across the contenents and roll over the darkside, the never setting sun is both a savior and a curse to the Twi'lek - who's only heat source is from these storms.

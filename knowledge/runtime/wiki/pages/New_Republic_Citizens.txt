The [[New Republic]]'s citizenry is as varied as the New Republic itself. They include people born within the New Republic's borders, immigrants and refugees from other parts of the [[The Galaxy|Galaxy]], people on former New Republic worlds now under occupation by the [[Galactic Empire]], and 'citizens at heart' who sympathize with the New Republic's goals though their official allegiance lies elsewhere.

== MUSH Information ==

The private citizen is more than just a starting branch for the faction. A 
common misconception is that if a person is still a private citizen, they 
just haven't been recruited yet. In reality, these are the small business 
owners, bartenders, musicians, artists, and anyone else whose lifestyle or 
career isn't a part of the other branches of the New Republic. 

Every person who joins the New Republic begins as a private citizen. Many 
military officers, intelligence agents or corporate executives may also retire
into private citizenship. This is the 'independent' lifestyle of the faction:
the branch that lets you be 'an indie' and yet part of the New Republic.

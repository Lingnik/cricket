The '''New Republic Credit''', or the '''New Republic Common Credit''', is the form of currency used within the [[New Republic]]. One chip is a small, gold coin bearing the flame of the Rebel Alliance on the head side and the opening passage of the charter of the [[New Republic]] inscribed on the tail.

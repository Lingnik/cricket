'''Odie Hood''' ([[18 BBY]] - ) is a [[Sarian]] politician and naval officer from Ithaca on [[Mergansar]] which he represents in the [[Union Council]]. He was a [[Sar Admiral]] and Director of the [[Naval Intelligence Office]] at the time of the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] and Mergani [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]]. As part of [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] efforts to [[Report:Mergani Subjugation Posts|subjugate]] Mergansar, Hood served as [[Governor|Military Governor]]. Following his resignation as Governor he was made Caspian Naval Minister and Commanding Officer of '''Task Group Resplendent''' in the '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]'''.

== Early Life ==

Odie Hood was born in the city of Ithaca on [[Mergansar]] a year into the reign of the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]]. He is a direct descendant of [[Icaris Hood]] the [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Republic]] surveyor who first charted the [[Caspia|Caspian System]]. Icaris Hood would ultimately settle with his family in [[Trinumvira]] on [[Caspar]], with one of his sons later moving to Mergansar and establishing what ultimately became one of Ithaca's wealthiest families. By the time of Odie's birth his family had a large estate and had considerable holdings in agriculture and shipping.

Odie was raised with expectation he would inherit the families holdings and his education and young adult life were focused around these pursuits, taking little regard towards wider galactic events. By the time the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] was formed in [[4 ABY]], he already had a wife Elope, and a newborn son Telly. At the time he had no ambition beyond continuing the family legacy.

== In Service ==

Perhaps owing to his distant Human ancestor and his family legacy originating off world, Odie was a strong opponent of the fanatical [[Sar Bares Flir]] order. Joining the Union forces fighting against them in the [[Second Zealot Wars]], he served with distinction at the Battle of Hammerton Heights commanding a company of volunteers from Ithaca. In the aftermath of this struggle, Odie began to consider a military career.

In [[6 ABY]] Odie enlisted in the [[Caspian Navy]] at the rank of [[Lieutenant Commander]] based on his experience and political connexions. He was assigned to the ''[[CMS Hood]]'' as Chief of Security, the Union's first native built warship, named for his ancestor Icaris Hood. Odie would serve on the ''Hood'' for a number of years. In [[8 ABY]] he was became the ship's second officer. In [[10 ABY]] he was promoted to full [[Commander]]. In [[11 ABY]] he became executive officer of the ''Hood'' and remained in that assignment for a year.

Odie would receive his first independent command in [[12 ABY]] being assigned as commanding officer of the [[Shearwater-class Frigate|Shearwater-Class Light Cruiser]] ''CMS Ithaca''. In [[14 ABY]] he was promoted to the rank of Captain and put under consideration for a larger command. In [[15 ABY]] he was given command of the [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]] ''CMS Aph'relin''. He would continue to rise through the ranks being promoted to [[Commodore]] in [[16 ABY]].

== Intelligence ==

As often becomes the fate of those who continue to rise through the ranks, Odie eventually found himself with a desk job on Caspar, being assigned to the [[Naval Intelligence Office]] in [[18 ABY]]. He excelled well in this posting and integrated himself into the society in Trinumvira, even acquiring a villa in West Cove which had once belonged to another branch of the Hood family. In [[21 ABY]] he was promoted to [[Admiral]] and became Deputy Director of the Naval Intelligence Office. Having been absolved of any involvement in the [[2015 Caspar Plot|conspiracy]] which toppled the incumbent Caspian government in [[24 ABY]], Odie was promoted to [[Sar Admiral]] and became Director of the Naval Intelligence Office. The new post would bring more than its fair share of challenges.

At the time the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] hit and the Mergani [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]] began, Odie was on a secret mission on [[Ixin]] with [[Senator of the Union|Senator]] [[Philinda Jorgan]]. They were incommunicado for some time, and by the time they became aware of what was going on there was little to be done. In his absence, Sar Admiral [[Prae Kichakressa]] and Caspian Navy were allowing the pogroms to occur almost uncontested. Feeling their cause was lost and with no records indicating they were not on Caspar at the time, the two would fake their deaths with members of the Naval Intelligence Office destroying the records of their mission and sinking Odie's yacht to suggest his death.

== Transition ==

The two erstwhile members of the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]] kept a low profile on Ixin, hiding out amongst [[The Chevalier]] and waiting to see how this situation developed back in the Caspian System. The emergence of a new government on Caspar led by [[Crestian Tarasar]], which had taken a strong stance against the [[Olumi Osahn]] administration leading Mergansar gave them hope that circumstances were now right to take back their homeworld from the cultists. Once Ixin rejoined the new [[Caspian Union]], Odie would make his continued existence known to the [[Caspian Intelligence Service]]. 

Boarding his old command, the ''CMS Aph'relin'' which had served Ixin during its brief independence, Odie would be secretly spirited through the blockade of Mergansar and back into his home city of Ithaca. Shortly thereafter the bodies of members of the local regime left in Ithaca started showing up in the courtyard of Odie's family estate with green arrows sticking out of them. The location of the body dump and Odie's known skill in archery soon led to rumours that he had returned and was single-handedly liberating the city.

After the killings and other recent events found themselves onto the wall of the Anphora Forum in the form of graffiti, including a depiction of Odie Hood, [[InterGalactic News]] anchor [[Brackett Benduday]] interviewed an 'anonymous' source in Ithaca, who agreed to go on camera to discuss the mural. The source was quickly revealed as Odie himself, wearing the uniform of a Sar Admiral of the design used by the new Caspian Union.

== Return ==

With Ithaca now liberated, Odie is quickly elected its representative to the [[Union Council]], though he faced no significant opposition. Taking his seat as a member of Tarasar's party [[Praecet]], he was soon appointed [[Governor|Military Governor]] for Mergansar pending the full re-establishment of local government.

After introduction legislation allowing for elections in the last four Mergani cities not yet represented, Odie resigned the governorship to rejoin the Caspian Navy with Philinda Jorgan succeeding him as Military Governor. He was given command of the [[Admiral Roj-class Battleship|Admiral Roj-Class Battleship]] ''[[CMS Resplendent]]'' which had been named after ''[[Resplendent XI]]'' the exploration vessel commanded by Icaris Hood a millennia ago.

Though the rank and role of Sar Admiral had become a single person office in the new Union, Odie has been allowed to retain the rank as a courtesy though he does not possess the office. His experience, however, has been recognised through his appointment as Caspian Naval Minister and he serves as commanding officer of '''Task Group Resplendent''' of the '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]'''.

== Spacestation 1138 Prison Break ==

In [[34 ABY]], after the breach of [[The Oubliette|Spacestation 1138]], an Imperial black site prison in the [[:starwars:Karthakk system|Karthakk System]] nicknamed "The Oubliette," most of the 4th Fleet was deployed throughout Imperium and Independent space chasing down escapees. Elements of Task Group Resplendent under Odie, in specific his ship ''CMS Resplendent'' and ''[[CMS Trettinar]]'' under Fleet Captain Shakka Teksa were involved in actions in the Durellian Expanse with Odie coordinating the combined Imperium-Caspian siege as owing to his seniority and experience.

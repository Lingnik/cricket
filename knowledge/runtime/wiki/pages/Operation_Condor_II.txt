'''Operation Condor II''' was an [[New Republic Military|Republic Military]] campaign launched in [[8 ABY|8&nbsp;ABY]]. Embolded by the Empire's defeat in the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign]] , the Republic aimed to liberate the entire outer rim from Imperial rule.

Though initially successful, liberating every world targetted, Operation Condor II would ultimately fail, cut short by the revelation of a third [[Death Star III|Death Star]] and the Imperial [[Great Campaign]] that followed.

''IGN newsfeeds throughout New Republic space covered another and final update from Coruscant's IGN Correspondant, the Twi'lek Dejir Walta, in regards to the New Republic's ''Operation Shado Kolpo''.''


----


Standing in a press-room with Admiral Ackbar, Dejir Walta nods his head to the camera. "Good afternoon. I'm here with Admiral Ackbar, Director of Fleet Operations in the New Republic, with an official statement on the New Republic's blockade run operation." He turns to face the Admiral. "Thank you, Admiral."


"Of course." The Mon Calamarian nods his head and turns to the camera. "At this time, the military procedure known as Operation Shado Kolpo has been suspended, due to heavy losses. I'd like to give my thanks and condolences to the familes of crewmen aboard the following ships. The Griffon fleetship ''Admiral Rishar''. The New Republic fleetships, ''Cadence'', ''Starborne'', ''Republic Revenge'' and ''Voidbreaker''. Also to the members of the 34th Starfighter Wing who were killed at the hands of merciless Imperial forces, and finally, to the lives and familes of the good-willing freighter pilots who were killed without trial, due process, or respect, at the hands of Admiral Kreldin's forces. The New Republic military command staff honor your bravery, and we hope the rest of the New Republic citizenry would do the same."


"Admiral." Walta is quick to jump in after the statement. "What of the blockade? The economic situation on Coruscant? The future of Coruscant and the New Republic itself? The-"


"These questions," Ackbar interrupts, "would best be answered in tonight's Senate Hearing. I'm afraid I cannot answer any further questions beforehand, but rest assured, the military is making every effort to protect the citizens of the New Republic. Now, if you'll excuse me."


Walta tries to chase after the Admiral, but is politely stopped by a pair of well-decorated New Republic marines. "Well," he says, turning back to the camera. "I suppose that's all. Back to you."

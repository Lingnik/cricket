'''Orbital Station Guardian''' was the [[Galactic Empire]]'s largest military hub in the Core Worlds since its construction in [[1 ABY]]. It served as a naval port, [[Imperial Army]] barracks, military academy, and command center for [[Imperial High Command]] throughout the [[Galactic Civil War]] and the [[Post-Endor Imperial power struggle]].

The station was constructed over [[O'paal]] along the [[Tarkin Corridor]]. Many prominent Imperial officers attended the station's academy.

The station was [[Battle of O'paal (GCW)|destroyed]] in [[14 ABY]] by the [[FST-01 Firecats|Firecats]] and [[Ghost Squadron]], two [[New Republic]] commando units. Imperial High Command was wiped out in the process.

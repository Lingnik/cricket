The '''PLX-2M portable missile launcher''', sometimes referred to as the '''Plex-Twoem''', was a missile launcher produced by [[Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc]]. The sheer explosive power of the PLX-2M made it dangerous to fire blindly in close quarters, but was extremely effective when used as a 'smart' tracking weapon. The PLX-2M had a magazine of six missiles, and included a miniature [[:starwars:Repulsorlift/Legends|repulsorlift]] generator to aid in carrying the heavy weapon. The PLX-2M's missile cartridge was large enough to allow users to load six [[:starwars:Arakyd_Industries|Arakyd Industries]] [[:starwars:3t3_missiles|3t3 missile]]s.

As icy as her name, '''Phoebe Frost''' was a [[Constable]] and later [[PRISM]] agent within the [[Caspian Democratic Union]]. She would be most active and instrumental in Caspia's dealings with the [[Sar Bares Flir]] and their constant acts of terrorism. Agent Frost would spend most of her assignments with Agent [[Lotus Blackpool]], and the two would most often be sent on investigative or protective assignments of Caspian interests to such places as [[Galactic Diplomatic Initiative Star|GDI-Star]] and the [[Kichnar Orbital Station]].

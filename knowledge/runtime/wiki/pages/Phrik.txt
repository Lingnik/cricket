'''Phrik alloy''' was a rare metallic compound that was one of only a few [[lightsaber]]-resistant substances known. It was used in the polearms of [[:starwars:IG-100 MagnaGuard|IG-100 MagnaGuards]] electrostaffs , as well as the armor of Dark Troopers. One major source of phrik was the '''phrikite''' mines of [[:starwars:Gromas 16|Gromas 16]] as well as sources of [[:starwars:Tydirium (ore)|tydirium]] which would be combined to make this compound. Phrik was very expensive, and as a result it saw limited use in warfare.

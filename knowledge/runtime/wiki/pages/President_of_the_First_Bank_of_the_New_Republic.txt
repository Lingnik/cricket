The '''President of the First Bank of the New Republic''' was a dual-role position high in the [[New Republic]] hierarchy. While serving as the President of the Republic's foremost federal bank and financial institution, the President also served as head of the [[New Republic Corporate Operations]], the government branch soley designed for all corporate, financial and economic matters throughout the Republic.

The President reported directly to the [[Inner Council]] and advised the Councilmen and [[Chief of State]] on all financial matters.

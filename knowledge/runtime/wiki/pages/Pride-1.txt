'''Pride-1''' was an immense free-space station obtained, renovated, and operated by the [[Griffons Alliance]]. Originally constructed as a ''Hyacinth''-class deep space surveying hub during the heady days of the Republic's exploratory endeavours, the station dwarfed nearly every other station in the galaxy save the [[:starwars:Death Star/Legends|Death Stars]] and later-built [[:starwars:Hosk Station/Legends|Hosk Station]]. It was some seventy kilometers at its widest point and provided the Griffons with every manner of docking, manufacturing, refining, and processing facility expected or required.

With such great importance to intergalactic commerce, Pride-1 was at the center of several controversies and conflicts. It changed hands thrice in its history with the Griffons before ultimately being [[Fourth Battle of Pride-1|destroyed]] by a large-scale [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] assault force in retaliation for Griffon participation in the destruction of the [[Death Star III|third Death Star]].

== History ==

== Location ==
The station was located in an arbitrary position within the [[Scythia|Scythian System]], giving a wide swath of space to coordinate traveling starships and observe all manner of approaching and departing traffic.

The rank and title of '''Private''' encompasses the lowest level of an enlisted soldier in a military organization, most frequently used by army and starfighter corps.

=== In New Republic Service ===

Along with [[Private First Class|PFC]]s and [[Lance Corporal]]s, Privates form the physical body of the '''[[New Republic GroundOps|New Republic Army]]'''. They are typically young sentients in basic training. Once finished, they almost always were rewarded with a promotion to the rank of [[Private First Class]].

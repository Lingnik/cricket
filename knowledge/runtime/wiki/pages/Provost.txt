The '''Provost''' was the lead [[Senator of the Union|Senator]] within the [[Union Assembly]]. Often referred to as the "third most important being in the Union" behind the [[Presav]] and the Chairman of [[the Admiralty]], the Provost would preside over and regulate all sessions of the Assembly. During cabinet meetings (comprised of the twelve regional senators and the Presav), the Provost would act as a mediator between any external sources and the cabinet itself.

As a rule, the Provost could not be from the same region as the Presav, and would be chosen from the eleven regional senators that did not have a seated Presav by the Assembly's delegates. '''[[Riche Beilayrn|Riche T Beilayrn]]''' was the first Provost, chosen by [[Dergan Roj]] to serve in that capacity for [[Wiltmer Gerrin|Gerrin]]'s presavadency.

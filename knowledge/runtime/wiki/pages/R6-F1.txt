'''R6-F1''', is one of the [[:starwars:R6-series astromech droid|R6-Series Droids]] owned by [[Krieg Inrokana]].  It was once owned by the [[Danik Kreldin|Grand Admiral Danik Kreldin]] before being passed to Krieg.  The astromech droid once rarely used by the Grand Admiral now is in full service aboard the [[I2SD Inquisitor|HIMS ''Inquisitor'']] assisting Krieg in his duties as the Wing Commander aboard ship.

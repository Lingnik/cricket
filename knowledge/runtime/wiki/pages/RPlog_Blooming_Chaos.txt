Blooming Orga - The Wheel

It is easy to see that this is one of those gimmicky themed restaurants with this one decorated to simulate the jungle floor of Kashyyyk, complete with tree roots and trunks and fake greenery placed here and there to lend to the effect. Tables and booths have been placed in such a manner to maximise floorspace which makes it easy for the establishment to hold 200 plus people during the busiest of evenings.

The servers are dressed accordingly with the males dressed in carefully designed Wookiee costumes which allow for their wearers to see and move without hindrance, and the female servers dressed like scantily-clad savages, lending a little eye candy for those who wish to partake. They weave around tables and patrons, going to and from the kitchen to serve the beings eating here as well as clearing the tables once they're free.	

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A booth at a schlocky chain restaurant on The Wheel may not be where most would envision a sinister slaver to be. But then again who wants to be the one who reports to into to corporate. Will they care? Will the act of speaking out end in having napkins shoved down their throat? And so here Skawn Kairn can be found today. Nearby booths seem to a boisterous crowd of people celebrating some occasion or another, but it seems that Skawn is set to dine alone.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It has taken him some time to track down the bold slaver-raider of Bespin, but years in the OOAG gave him the skill set he needed and point of fact the raider didn't seem overly concerned about being discreet. which was fine, this was more an outing of fun than any real agenda. The galaxy was getting tame and a wild card was just the order of the day and Captain Kairn seemed like a possible choice.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The robed figure with yellow eyes approached the table and with a nod of his head. "Captain Kairn." he sets a rather potent bottle of spirits on the table. Perhaps not the best in the galaxy but certainly potent.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Skawn looks at the man with the bottle of spirits, then he stands and looks over to the booth next to him, "I thought Captain Kairn day was Taungsday. Is it still Taungsday?" The beings at the other table shrug and Skawn sits back down looking at the spirits, "I will have you know they have excellent cocktails here already. You know with the little umbrellas, great for eye gouging." He motions for Murrain to sit opposite him, "So to what do I owe the bottle. Do you have a nasty in-law you want dead but want to make it look like a simple act of terrorism....or did I already do that to someone you care about and your here to poison me?"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Murrain takes the seat and chuckles at the remark. "Neither and neither, but my compliments on the Bespin raid." he slides the bottle off to the side. "I am more curious about your goals...I get that your a slaver, I get that the Imperium and the rest of the civilized galaxy is doing its...anti slaver crusade." resting his hands on the table and pointing a finger at the slaver. "But, what is it your after...chaos...fun...payback...can't be profit otherwise you would be more subtle." in an almost after thought he nods his head. "I am Murrain."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"I guess we rule you out as reporter too Mister Murrain, no softball questions to start us off." He gets up again looking back to the other table, "You know I think we need to kidnap a reporter some time. Make a note." He sits back down again. "I am man who is about doing what he wants and giving space for others who want to do what they want. A lot of what people want to do revolves around other people. If I was visiting a world of vegetarians I would be a nerf rustler. You know that sounds like fun actually." He gets back up and looks to the others, "Lets get a reporter, visit some bantha worshiping freaks. Eat their banthas." One of the people at a more distance table looks like they are a about to ask a question before Skawn adds, "Yes you can eat a bantha worshiper....after." He sits down once again looks at the bottle.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Murrain slides the bottle over and nods. "All yours...word is it might eat a few layers out of your gut, but gets you blasted out of your skull." he offers and leans back and chuckles behind the mask. "Excellent...I like that idea...I have a mind for plotting and viewing the big and small picture." he offers and takes a hand to tap the table top. "And I am a firm believer in having fun and that setting a few fires in the galaxy keeps life interesting." a few beats. "I would like to help you and party along the way." a brow rises. "I am familiar with Darth Malign and his methods, I think I can help keep the party going for a bit."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Skawn motions over for a waiter. Even though their Wookiee costume it is clear they are somewhat intimidated by this particular patron, but really when is the service industry not complete poodoo. "Two of your finest red plastic cups." The waiter is puzzled but nevertheless heads off in search of the cups. "So Mister Murrain would you consider yourself a party planner then?" The deeper questions asked of him are left unanswered at the moment.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Murrain gives a half smile. "I prefer to consider myself an agent of realistic chaos, a orderly galaxy does not grow or advance." he offers in an amused tone. "To many powers are throwing nets over everything, it needs to be free." A subtle glance around and a rather neutral response to the room and furry waiter.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A couple moments later the waiter returns with two cups that look like they came from the credit store. They are plastic and red but clearly not what Skawn had in mind. "I had wanted the disposable kind but I suppose these will have to suffice." As the Wookiee-dressed waiter quietly walks away, Skawn adds to Murrain, "I bet Solo never had this kind of problems with Wookiee help." The Twi'lek laughs a bit and finally pours the drinks. "What the powers do or not is of limited concern to me. Their laws and policies mean nothing to me."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Murrain smiles and nods. "You may believe that and I may take you at your word, does not mean they don't believe you are bound by the rules they have made and seek to enforce them." he gazes at the drink cups and a brow flickers up for a moment. "So, the great question beyond that as I was asking, what do you want to achieve...clearly you don't want to be involved in the established norms."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Skawn push ones of the cups to Murrain before taking a sip from the other. The liquid certainly had a kick, but he had a seasoned constitution. "If this is one of those job interviews where you are looking to find out where I see myself in five years or how I am aim to grow with the company....you may find yourself disappointed. When you are a slave you have to live in that moment there are no guarantees about the next. Now I control that moment. But grand vision that is not what I am about. Besides such things get very time consuming, take away from enjoying oneself."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Murrain takes the drink and tips it into a reservoir in the mask and he shakes his head. "Nope nothing like that...just a chit chat to see where you want to go, what fun can be had along the way and how long you plan on making it last." he sets the cup back down and takes a few gulps from the mask. "So...no plan, just when the mood and some wild and crazy idea strikes you...your off and running." he inclines his head to the slaver. "Not a bad plan...its harder to predict."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Well it is not as if every action we take is on the spur of the moment. You know sometimes you hear that there will be performance of Squid Lake at some opera house or another in two months so then your idling considering you know whether you want to go bomb the place or kidnap the soprano or what have you." Skawn starts to stand but before he gets half way out of his seat a voice from behind says, "We know, we know find out where Squid Lake is going to be performed." Sitting back down Skawn take a larger gulp of the drink, "Plans exist, but really it is all about lifestyle. Making what we will for ourselves happen. Satiating whatever the appetite is for at any moment."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Murrain nods slowly. "I would say that sounds fair...well keep me in the loop, if its of a particularly potent bit of chaos. I would be up for it." he nods and takes another pull from the mask. "I will say this...your stunt at Bespin...has garnered attention and I am sure they are looking for you." the heads up given he chuckles. "But I am sure you assumed as much.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Skawn chuckles, "Well I had heard excellent things about the brothel so you know I need to see for myself. Take home some souvenirs. Let the crew let off some steam as it were. Poor Fett though looks like he didn't have as much fun. He should have joined us at the brothel. But not one of mine so hardly my problem." Another sip of the drink he asks, "So what is your vice Mister Murrain? Do you just seek liberty and chaos or is there some particular source of gratification for your life that civilisation has been frowning upon?"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Murrain laughs softly. "I like to see fires burn...a bit of carnage here and there to keep things moving forward." he explains. "It is the fires of threat and war and death that sentients find awareness and realization." a hand reaches out and turns the cup. "Sentients do not learn save when it stands at the precipice of loss or pain."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Well we can always use another pyromaniac around. It does put me in a certain mood. Alas burning down the Blooming Orga does not quite have the appeal as the forest of Kashyyyks. I mean the Wookiees here aren't even real!" Skawn smirks and gulps down more of the drink, "Besides like I said the cocktails here are good. And there is that old phrase about shitting where you eat. I mean I know this one Gungan really into that but that ain't my bag baby."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Murrain nods slowly at the Twi'lek and flicks a hand to the bottle. "All yours...here is a drop link to get word to me when you have something in mind." a datachit flips onto the table. "And, just be mindful, they are hunting you now and the Imperator has agents who can read a location and pick up echoes of what's happened there and follow it back through time." the warning given. "Something to be mindful of yes?"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Picking up the datachit Skawn remarks, "I have never been that hard to find. They clearly have not been sending their best people. Might be amusing if he actually sends someone of substance. What is life without a few challenges. Don't worry about me. Worry about the manufacturers of Wookiee waiter costumes cause one of these day someone will decide those aren't fashionable anymore and someone is going to lose a job."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Murrain rises from the seat and taps of a salute with a few fingers. "I am sure there is a Ewok or Trandoshan behind it all." another soft chuckle. "Or haven't turned their full attention to you?" the eyes crinkle with a smile behind the mask. "You may need to up your game?" this time a full on laugh. "Enjoy the bottle and your random plottings."

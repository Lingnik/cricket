Night Tavern - Noques Rasala: Caspar

Few places in the galaxy see the combination of people that can be found in this tavern. Sailors, pilots, priests, and prophets, all can be found at the largest watering hole in Noques Rasala. Dark wood furnishings and dark iron trim dominate the room. The windows are high upon the walls, letting in what little light the outside provides.

Gas lamps line the walls keeping the room a bit dim. The brightest light comes from behind the long wooden bar, where a large wood-burning fireplace is constantly ablaze, cutting the chill from the city of night. Many different beverages are available, many locally brewed or distilled while others come from off-world. 

While the business is primarily a drinking establishment, there is a limited menu of local cuisine, most involving fresh seafood. Prices are modest, though quality is generally considered high.

While most locals sit at the bar, there are booths lining the outer walls, each lit with a single wax candle for those that want either privacy, or to simply enjoy their drinks or meal with a loved one or small group.

Until Crestian mentions the Big Bad by name. "But...things /will/ change. If we need to believe in anything, we need to believe in that."

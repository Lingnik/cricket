Black Nebula - Pub - Nyriaan Spiral

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Spiral is still getting her legs, but enough of the locals took a shuttle up and off shift crew are filling the pub. Laughing is far more common then anything remotely negative. floating droid trays trail above servers moving through the clusters of those enjoying all the pub has to offer.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The beat, the rhythm, it pulses throughout the pub and in the middle of a dance floor surrounded by a mix of patrons and off-duty staff is Quinne. The Zeltron is the beating heart of the dancing, setting pace and mood with her own enjoyment, both attracting dancers to the floor and reveling in the laughter and fun.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri enters the pub as word out from the crew is that was where the Zeltron was at, made sense to the Falleen, he enters and a few that know him on sight nod and smile. It was well known that the Lorda Gejalli did not visit the pub for anything other than pleasure. His pale green hands clap a shoulder here and there and his cheek gets more than a few kisses from the women serving the floor.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;He winds his way through the crowd and to the perimeter or the circle formed around Quinne and beep in his ear minds him that the ship had entered hyperspace without incident. It was well posted that visitors keep track of the ships itinerary so as not to be trapped aboard.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Luckily Quinne had checked her small travel bag with the concierge before heading up to the Falleen's suite with her unfortunate acquaintances who left long ago after causing her embarrassment and ire. She traveled lightly from club to bar star hopping to where ever the next potential gig was. But tonight, the Zeltron takes in the delicious joy the large party ship exudes. A bright smile flashes no matter which direction her dancing faces her. When the chorus of the song comes, she leads the voices of the crowd in singing along with full throated ecstasy.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri moves onto the floor and places a slender hand on the small of her back to get her attention. "Mind if I intrude?" he gestures to a favored table that is being prepared. "I thought we might talk?" the Falleen knows exactly how to pitch his voice so as to cut through the music, granted one needed to be close to pull it off.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Quinne tosses a glance over her rosy shoulder to find the Falleen is the one behind her. Violet eyes flash in his direction, slowing her movements. Her gaze follows to the table he indicates. "Sure thing, darlin'!" she says, agreeable and without resistance. The Zeltron begins moving that way but slowly allowing Nasiri to maintain the placement of his hand. "Ooo! Is this your VIP table?"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri chuckles and gestures upward to a mirrored dome on the ceiling. "That is up there." he nods to the table as they sit and he indeed leaves his hand where it was resting until they get to the table. "I didn't want to draw you fully from the celebrations." the Zeltron had been there an enough had bought her drinks to know what she favored and it was brought as well as one for the Lorda. "So, how do you like it?" he gestures to the room and perhaps the entire vessel.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Oh I love it!" Quinne exudes as she takes in the ceiling and takes a seat. "Everyone is having a good time, it's so fun and happy!" When her Bespin Sun Bubbler arrives, she thanks the server and takes a quick sip. "The ship really is a paradise for partiers!" She heard that the ship was moving on but... "Where are we headed?" she asks with enthusiasm, then defers not wanting to overstep her welcome. "Um...that is...you." She gestures to take in the whole ship. "Where are you headed?"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri smiles as he take his own drink and nods to the server, who then is off to do their thing. "The ship is headed for Selene, I am picking up a few smaller vessels for ship security." he leans back and regards her for a long moment. "I was going to ask you, if you would consider taking a performance engagement aboard the Nyriaan Spiral, during her tour and perhaps role as hostess to larger events that may arise?" a long pull of the wine. "Quarters would be provided and board...and some pay...it wont be a lot to start with. But the longer your with me." he grins over the edge of his goblet. "You will find a lesser and lesser need for credits."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Oh I don't know...give up all those nights in dive bars singing for drinks I don't even like?" she ask with coy sarcasm. "This is like a dream...you mean, you'd let me just sing and throw parties?" For a Zeltron, so long as all the partiers are happy, is indeed a dream come true. Quinne gives Nasiri a semi-suspicious side-eye, dropping a little bit of the party girl facade. "You are serious, aren't you?"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri nods at her and sips from his drink. "I rarely joke and yes I am serious." he raises a finger in warning a dark claw tipping it. "I move in circles that are often illicit, hutts, scoundrels and even slavers...you would be safe from them. But its part of the gig."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Quinne's complexion ripples with shades of pink and fuchsia as she considers. Her rose lips draw into a straight line for the first time since you met her. Her contemplation over, she offers a soft enigmatic smile. "I'm alright with that." She especially likes the safety offered over the scrapping and low-class traveling she's been doing since leaving home. "What kind of quarters," she asks cagily.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri grins at her and takes a deep pull and sets the goblet down. "There are many luxury suites on this ship...private and you will be granted a clearance to access my private suite...the pool is one of a kind." one arm slides across the back of the booth they are in. "The Nyriaan Spiral is designed to be a travelling palace...she lives up to that image and then some."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Quinne lightly slaps the table with both palms and a large smile warms her cute, pink features. "Well that's just perfect." A flash of her violet eyes indicates she'd like to be the palace princess and she doesn't shift her position. "Lorda Gejalli...you've got yourself a deal." A warm hand is offered over the table.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri reaches across and takes the hand with a slow nod. "Welcome aboard." he gestures over to a uniformed man who appears out of the crowd and then stops. "Do you wish a view of space or one of the interior botanical gardens?"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Oh I'd love the garden view." The Falleen has made the Zeltron very happy indeed. Quinne looks around at the non-stop party all around them and then asks excitedly, "So when do I start? Do you have a band already or can I hire one?"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri grins at her and ponders for a moment. "There is talent here, feel free to audition them or if you have a favored band." he shrugs. "I can make arrangements for them to be brought here." the uniformed man leaves to get the suite set up for Quinne. "Which ever you prefer."

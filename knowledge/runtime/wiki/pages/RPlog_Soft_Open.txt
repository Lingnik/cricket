Prothium Club - Neon District - Imperial City: Selene

The newest addition to the night life of the Neon District, the Prothium Club is named in honour of Bespin's legendary Tibanna Club. Not only is prothium another blaster gas, but prothium mining is the primary source of revenue to the club's corporate parent. As one enters the club one is greeted by a long dimly lit corridor. A Besalisk female in a Tyrian Shimmersilk suit, named Nola Swoon, serves as bouncer on most nights, guardian the inner sanctum of the establishment.

The main room is a large cavernous space with a raised staged set against the back wall with a dance floor directly in front of it. Along the opposite wall are two bars one on each side of the entrance. Various booths and tables line the side walls. Most of the furnishings are constructed in durasteel but plated other metals. The functional elements such as tables and chairs are kelsh plated with beryllius trim. More decorative elements such as the bar top are xonolite plated with Corellian gold trim.

Plumes of smoke in a variety of colours shoot out from various places in the floor creating a multi-coloured haze the permeates the room. The smoke is largely water based but also contains substances that amount to mild intoxicants in most species.

The type of acts that perform at the club vary quite widely. Among musical genres Bith Jizz and Theelin Divas seem to be favoured by the management. Comedians, dancers, and poets are also frequently part of the repertoire. Various aspects of the club's decor are often altered to better fit particularly types of performance.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There were many great things about the Imperium-Caspian Mutual Defence Pact. The amount of time Crestian spent on Selene attending meetings or official functions, however, was not to at the top of that list. In order to make his time on world more enjoyable, the Caspian Presav decided to get in on the planets revitalisation efforts and expanding his modest entertainment holdings to include the Prothium Club. Though a Grand Opening would wait for a later date, enough of the staff and stock was in place to hold a soft opening before he and the Caspian delegation returned to Caspar. And what better way to conduct that soft opening than to allow his young friend and the Imperator's apprentice Kipran Jawatha to host a private party for his paramour, Crown Princess Sonja Damion.<br>

The Glitteratti of the Imperium, friends Kipran and Princess Sonja had made since their time here began. Why did she agree to an interview? She's going to be such a nervous wreck. She was surprised that she survived that little encounter with him with those cameras circling around them like vultures. But, maybe she can make the most out of it, get some publicity for the Mission. Maybe she--- "Huh?" she blinks as Tracer breaks her out of her thoughts before they consumed her, "Have some fun? Oh, I can't dance..." Though, despite her words and her feelings about stumbling over the dance floor, she finds her tail tapping against the stool to the beat of the music. Instead, the Horansi eyes the platters of food. "I am kinda hungry though..." She looks to Crestian and Tracer, "I'll.. umm... I'll be back." She smiles meekly to them and hops off the stool to make her way in the fray.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Much of the coronation and celebration was coming to an end, during much of that time Tyler Damion's ad-hoc war council under the guidance of Drayson Honos had already organized and deployed a number of task forces to begin assessing various corners of space. Most of the early planning involved surveillance (probe droids and listening ships) and utilising smaller weapon platforms such as the Warlord Strike Cruisers and Avengers line to hit and fade on soft targets across the galaxy, the larger forces were being staged in various sectors under the Grand Admiral's careful supervision.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Grand Moff's office had been temporarily taken over by the Imperator and his inner council as they discuss the current intelligence routing in from various sectors. Tyler sits quietly behind the Grand Moff's desk, once occupied by the infamous Scaven Marx as various xenos and Drayson all wearing their new uniforms ponder over maps. The Sith Lord seems troubled as he is doing his own calculations for what is coming, he leans back in the large chair and passively listens as the Admirals conclude their reports and maps deactivate, "Very well. All the command staff remain on high alert, I am told the command ship will be ready for us to transfer to and to manage the war effort, we just have a few more things to do here before we deploy to Sullust."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To either side of the Turbolift doors Sentinel and another smaller mutant wookiee stand guard making sure only those invited can enter the room.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Johanna was wrong and right about the Falleen's presence, he wasn't here for scraps. But there was a subtle smoldering vendetta in the back of his reptilian mind. Profit was certainly natural motivation for him, power was a recent addition to the elements he sought. That was largely due to his encounter with the select committee and their ad-hoc trial and betrayal of him after the massacre of the grand council. When he sees the trouble shift of the Imperator's brow, he simply expresses that observation. "A troubled mind is a thing of concern in these times, Your Majesty." he offers a polite bow and a pause, waiting for a more direct invite.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Elana Tracer is only adjunct to this meeting so she stands towards the back. Neither of her new roles dealt directly with the war effort with the exception of any vehicle needs - that and holding down the fort on Selene with the help of yet another large staff. She listens carefully, taking notes on a one of several datapads she's juggling from one pocket to another.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It's just as well she stay out of Malign's focus because she had a sneaking suspicion she was indeed supposed to be in her new Moff's uniform, which she not. Having spent the hours prior to this gathering meeting with KDY-SFS VPs and continuing to take in the extremely huge operations that had been thrust into her small hands there just wasn't time to change! She's nervous like a teen on a first date. Did Malign know that it was that very Scaven Marx who had had her transported here all those years ago to be taught exactly what was expected of her back home on Caspar.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;But circumstances were much different from then. At least one of her children was an asset to the man behind the desk and she could choose to tow the line and maybe even become a rising star among the Moffs of this more open and accepting Order. No matter what, she's just hoping she can just blend in - she is wearing an elegant black Mergansar silk suit, after all - so she keeps her head down and tries not to draw Tyler's notice.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Malign's eyes rise to meet the approaching Fallen, the history of his species fraught with scandal and distrust. The Dark Lord was accommodating to all non-humans, he was no Imperial in the traditional sense as indicated by the diversity in the room. He watches Nasiri approach and as he speaks, "Troubling times require careful consideration." He glances toward his command staff, "I am sure we won't be needing to feel troubled much longer, Grand Admiral Honos is quite capable. You are here now, Nasiri, I am sure you have some sort of desire for equitable exchange." His turns his full attention to him.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Tracer is safe from dress code this evening. The command staff had records running of all Moffs and high-ranking officials and their schedules. Drayson was the type to recognize the sort of nervousness she was exhibiting, "Moff Tracer." He says politely closing the gap, "How did your meetings go? Have you heard from the Corporate Sector Authority? The Imperator is growing restless with the silence and the continuing news feeds coming off of IGN." His face doesn't betray any emotions about the situation but he felt it would end up with the CSA-NR-Imperium-Neo-Imps all shooting at each other.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri catches the word phrase CSA and gives a slight smile. "It is unlikely that the Corporate Sector will respond until they have shored of their position." it was not a statement that even remotely sounded like a guess. "But if input is desired on that I would be so inclined." he nods to Drayson and Tracer and them turns his attention back to the Imperator. "I am not going to play shadow games with you Your Majesty, I'm here on a two fold purpose. One, Nal Hutta is curious about the stance of your Imperium regarding its presence in the galaxy. The Select Committee did its level best to curtail Hutt space." he eyes the mutant wookiees for a moment. "Two, your using a massive amount of credits. There are two financial corridors, legal and illicit. Myself and a cadre of skill people have developed a skill at manipulating both and maximizing profits." he raises a jet colored brow. " Perhaps such a team could be of assistance?"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Tracer smiles as Drayson approaches, the meeting seeming to break into smaller discussions and thus not putting her in a direct spotlight. Once she was up to speed, she'd have no issue being more present and speaking her piece when allowed. But the elder man had a way about him that puts her at ease, when at ease is the last way she should probably feel.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Oh. Meetings? Yes, every things went great, just great. It's an amazing team of engineers. The facilities are a dream!" she effuses. "Thank you for asking, Grand Admiral." As the Falleen addresses her and Drayson, Tracer gives Nasiri a curious look and a nod in tacit agreement before she shifts to place the Drayson between herself and Nasiri. Then she lowers her voice and looks up at Drayson from under her slender black brows with less good humor. "It has been silence from Oppenhiemer I'm afraid. Though I'm sure you've read the rumour reports about an emergency Direx board meeting...the deployment of ESPOs. Not to mention the..." Tracer lowers her tone even more and frowns,"...resistance and uprisings among the workers. It's already turned violent. Whenever the sector feels threatened, ranks get closed up real tight."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hutt Space was outside of Malign's immediate interests but he knew it sat in a strategically sensitive area and recognizes the importance of stability around a lot of his former holdings when the New Sith Order operated in those sectors, then again he was surprised the Hutts would want to talk at all having bent the knee to the Sith Lord once, fond memories of the battles around Nar Shaddaa and Nal Hutta echoing in his mind. He turns to the Falleen and speaks frankly, "Provided the Hutts understand Xeno-trafficking in Imperium space and slavery are illegal, we are prepared to honor the sovereignty of Hutt territories, what you do within your borders is your business, however-" he smiles, "-securing those borders and the integrity of the region I would imagine is expensive and we will expect some support for the effort." The Imperator says turning to the other offer, "I am sure your business acumen is superior to mine, I am willing to see your proposal and some evidence of your success in the financial world."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Drayson politely nods to Tracer as she issues her report and speaks about some of the happenings in the sector, "A workers uprising could work to our advantage. I am doubting the CSA has turned to a more Xeno friendly policy as quickly as we have." He says processing the information, "But I suppose we will need to maintain a wait and see protocol with heightened security, if you have any assets or agents in the region I suggest you activate them." The Grand Admiral says before turning back to the shipyards, "The Imperator wishes for the Shipyards to begin a scale up of not only production but of capacity. He is prepared to divert considerable resources to ensuring we make the KDY-SFS shipyards over Selene very productive. Automated labor units are being ordered from Byss as we speak and our droid foundries across the Imperium are preparing for the operations. You can imagine the importance of this, we are preparing to retool and update thousands of vessels including digging into the mothballed yards to retrofit older vessels for the frontier."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri gives a slight smile and nods. "Securing those borders would be greatly enhanced by access to military grade ships and not the ragtag fleet often found in our borders." not even a veiled hint, Nasiri was not here to dance. "We are willing to add additional funds to your war chest...for access to modern vessels and as for my business acumen." he sets a datapad down with numbers and graphs, that detail a drop in Nal Hutta profits after the Select committee began disrupting trade and then a steady rise in profit as finance were restored through shrewd reinvesting in front corporations and strategic use of small worlds industry to bolster larger ones and then mixing in things like spice trade and black market trading.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;While Moff Tracer listens to the Grand Admiral confirm the plans to increase production and retrofit, the mention of assets holds a place in her attention. She didn't have any agents or assets of her own as yet. There was only her household staff and her two teenagers at University on Kalla. At the mention of mothballed yards. "There are other yards?" She turns to one of her assigned personal assistants and orders, "Get me a full listing of everything that's available in terms of ghost real estate." Back to Drayson she says, "The less centralized our retrofits are, the faster we can get them done. By only moving parts and materials to outer system shipyards, the vessels will already be close to the sectors they're destined for. It'll be faster and less expensive. And less of a target than if we only have production and repair facilities in the Core."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Sith listens to Nasiri, he was slick, he knew how to carve his way through a negotiation. Tyler leans forward, "While I appreciate, the enthusiasm of the Hutt Cartels to receive Imperium military vessels, the training requirements for appropriate management of the vessels and retrofitting would take sometime."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;He smiles, "I propose a counter solution, my sister - a very trusted advisor and member of this royal family - has control of a rather rapidly expanding domain including the world of Tatooine, very near to Hutt space." He gestures toward the shipyards above, "I will provide her a few additional vessels under the instruction they be used to secure and defend Hutt space. You can negotiate the security of your systems directly with her, as she is a de-facto Grand Moff of the Imperium sectors near that region." He looks over the data provided to him, "I am impressed with your market manipulations here, however, rather than maximize Imperium stores," he takes a breath, "I might be willing to endorse you harming Oppenhiemer's credits and bottom line, who knows success in that regard might leave us open to more negotiations in the future."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Drayson nods, "Fondor for one." He says, "Additionally we have access to some Republic era ships we will wish to convert their superstructures into the modern Warlord Class Strike Cruisers. I believe the Imperator wishes to assign one to a few operations involving-" he pauses, "-he used the word chaos operations." He smiles politely, "He has a few individuals in mind for working on destabilizing some particular markets and sectors."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri shakes his head slightly. "Personnel are no issues for me, I have been employing professional troops and fleet personnel since shortly after I took over Nar Shaddaa and giving your sister a few more ships to patrol for us. Really doesn't give us anything because it can be taken away at a simple command." he then follows on tangent with Oppenhiemer. "It would be hard to compete with the CSA industrial complex, but I have begun retooling Geonosis." he gives a slight look of doubt. "You know what they have and how well developed it is."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Elana switches to a different datapad to make notes on Fondor and to guarantee parts for complete structural overhaul of ancient Republic ships. She lets out a whistle, her naval history not precise but enough to make her realize the challenge ahead. "Stars! Those have got to be how many decades old now?" she muses, having four herself then counting backwards. "But good bones on the old girls, I'm sure!" Tracer was on day two of a whirlwind of activity and did not want to be perceived as falling behind at any point, despite her newness to the organization. Silence takes a beat and she asks, "Is there anything else I should be keeping my attention to?"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"A gift so easily given can easily be taken away. I believe that's the material point your esteemed guest is trying to make," comes a woman's voice from the back of the room, the slow clacking of her heels against the floor growing louder as she moves towards Nasiri and the Imperator, "Or perhaps the Lorda thinks I will simply take my weapons platforms and run when I'm of a mood." Johanna glances knowingly at Tyler, inclining her dark head towards him before looking over to spare a smile for Drayson's back, the identity of the slight brunette in his company now well-known to her, "Well, I can't say the fear is unwarranted. I do have my moods." She sighs, settling down uninvited into a seat and crossing her legs before looking at the Falleen with a mixture of curiosity and contempt, something quirking the corners of her mouth into a sardonic smile. "Or was the implication that I'm a coward who will run at the first sign of trouble? No, I'm sure it can't be that."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Sith listens and then replies to Nasiri, "I am not sure how Aldus Thel operated their Imperial holdings, but I am not a pay it forward type of leader." He says as his sister steps out of the shadows prompting him to smile, "He seems to doubt your talents." He says pulling a bottle of Corellian brandy from the desk and pouring himself and Johanna a glass, his contempt for the Falleen seeming to rise, "I cannot offer any material in advance, it will be up to the Hutts and you to prove your value and loyalty to this regime." He says taking his own glass and enjoying a few drinks before continuing, "Damage the CSA, and we can consider payment for your services, until then, Johanna is the de-facto sector contact for business, and any business you wish to do with regional governors is up to the individual Moffs. However, break Imperium laws, especially related to our xeno and slavery policies and I will not only help Johanna glass Nal Hutta, I will send my best command officers and the Flagship of the Imperium to make sure it is accomplished." He says without any hint of malice in his tones, "I respect your desire for business, but remember this is not a room with a criminal boss behind the table, this is the New Order, I am an Imperator-" he points toward Johanna, "a Grand Moff and Warlord," he points to Drayson, "A Grand Admiral," and then toward Tracer, "A Moff and CEO of the largest Shipyards in the galaxy." He leans forward now and rises, "Know your audience."
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Drayson listens and nods toward Tracer, "They are good hulls, I commanded on one, when I was a much younger man. They will serve well for modifications and retrofitting to bring them up to modern standards. You should have the specifications in the archives in orbit." He smiles hearing Malign speak and list off the people in the room. He turns now to that meeting as Johanna appears. He squares his stance and stands at polite attention turning to smile toward Tracer, "It seems the Imperator is not interested in abstract offers."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri smiles ever so politely, flashing sharp teeth as he does so. "Of course your majesty...i will be certain to know my audience." he offers a bow to the room at large. "Being in a room of so many titled and powered people, can be intimidating to a simple black-marketeer." a slight turn and nod of the head again.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Elana feels a warm shiver up her back as the Jedi woman, the Imperator's sister enters. Feminine energy recognizes itself in a room filled with those that fall squarely in the sector of male. She follows Johanna's movements towards the gathering, narrowing her clear blue eyes slightly. Beyond perfunctory introductions between them after the conflicts of the tarmac, later sitting at the same table for that bizarre dinner was all Tracer had so far to form an opinion on the Lady. Tracer might even be unsure she would remember if she had formed one. Things were a little fuzzy.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It was a rough night.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That Lady Johanna was certainly a creature of interest to Elana was true, though trepidation equal to that caused by her brother Tyler resides within the guarded Caspian. Sith. Jedi. All the same to her in the end and she'd had limited experience with such beings to find solace from one or the other. Tracer's cool gaze remains on the other woman while Tyler redresses the Falleen. At the titular mention of them each in turn, the former Colonel's posture stiffens as well and she smirks in return to Drayson then squints, measuring up Nasiri. "No. The Imperator definitely is not."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Johanna reaches for the glass and wraps her fingers around it, the amber-colored liquid within gently shifting to one side and seemingly calling to her. Ere the stuff reaches her lips, however, the Imperator's bile rises along with his figure and the Falleen begins to slip into a practiced, disingenuous obsequiousness, drawing a soft snort from the woman. She sets the glass back down on the desk and clears her throat. "Imperator, I'm sure Lorda Gejalli meant no disrespect. I can vouch for his readiness to scare up profits in the most unlikely of situations, so... perhaps a compromise?" Short-tempered she may often be, but the desert woman isn't insensible to the merits of letting cooler heads prevail on occasion: "One ship now, as a gesture of your goodwill, with the balance to be released once the Lorda demonstrates a continued firm control of the system and a supply of credits to your cause." She leans slightly towards her right, elbow propped on the armrest, and for the first time her senses detect the unease roiling through Elana Tracer. It's enough to draw her gaze towards the newly-appointed Moff, a sympathetic smile ghosting across her features before she turns her attention back to Nasiri and Malign.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Tyler savors his drink as he ponders the Hutt envoy and their business propositions, and more fiercely ponders the Falleen himself. The Sith Lord like much of the galaxy had no interest or joy from the Hutt Cartels, the creatures existed to spite thousands of years of varying galactic rule and still they persisted, vermin in their own right but resilient and not without resources. This was the core irony of this meeting, it was a request for material aid for market access and goods, the deeper issue was their practices, their criminal syndicates, and worse the sentient trafficking and slavery. These were things the Sith could use without much consideration at all, but the Imperium's image was xeno-open and fiercely anti-slavery.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Malign frowns, "The New Sith Order was full of capable agents, you carry something of one of them. Never, would they come before me and request military equipment empty handed. They would bring me something actionable, something that required the resources and the /direct/ /material/ and /political/ benefit would be visible and understood."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;He refills his glass before sliding the bottle toward Johanna, "You offer me vapers, whispers. What should I give you in return for hot air?" His brow raises as he puts his glass back to his lips, "You insult the memory of Malif Tal-Tahn." He thinks for several moments on what he could extract from the creature before him.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Sith's rage begins to boil just as the rooms temperature begins to drop. He slowly rises from his seat, but Johanna speaks and things suddenly seem to warm and the Imperator nods solemnly.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"So be it." He says and then settles back into his chair, "I will provide you with three Skipray Blastboats, one now and two additional ships upon completion of something that I deem worthy of the others. These will bolster your internal security and customs capabilities." His eyes turn toward Tracer momentarily and then back to the Falleen, "Alternatively, you could bend the knee now and return Geonosis into my hands."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Drayson remained somewhat rigid and more so as the tension seems to grow in the room. The Admiral unconsciously steps slightly in front of Tracer as if to shield her from something he has witnessed before, perhaps blood splatter, fortunately it doesn't come and his white uniform is once again spared, Johanna had spoken and the situation seems to calm. He listens to the Sith's offer and mentally considers where to call those resources from, "It seems the shipyards will be busier than our original projections." He turns to Tracer shrugging.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri smiles and slides his hands into his sleeves and gives a nod to Johanna. "A splendid idea Lady Danaan." and yet he shakes his head in the negative. "And a most gracious offer, but his Majesty is right...vapors and whispers." a ever so slight a take of breath. "I have taken enough time of so many esteemed people with more important matters at hand...then the words of a black market dealer." he considers things a bit longer and takes three precise steps back from the Imperial presence. "Your Majesty..Lords, Ladies."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Tracer feels the flux in temperature and her eyes widen. She finds herself digging nails into the palms of her clenched fists until the pain helps her focus. The petite woman stands tall and straight, choosing to believe she is in no danger. She doesn't balk when Drayson's chivalry moves to subtly protect her. It's a bit like being where you can hear the battle, even feel the percussion of artillery but not see the smoke yet. Not that churning out a blastboat towards spaceworthiness would be that taxing. Then the Falleen starts to bow out empty handed and she remarks to Drayson, a bemused smile on her garnet lips. "Or not!"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Jedi has just lit up a smoke when her forehead creases in displeasure at Nasiri's attempt to beat a hasty retreat, a grunt seeing her slide herself back to center from where she's been half-draped across the chair. "Esteemed people?" she echoes with some disdain, taking a drag and snorting loudly this time, "Me? That's hilarious. You're slinking off, Lorda, and I don't like it when you slink off... not when we've always been square with each other, and certainly not when you've been offered something in good faith. Nobody here doubts your ability to command the Council or maintain order in the sector. Moreover--" Johanna rises, taking a step towards the Falleen, "I thought we were going to make something out of our respective areas of the galaxy and profit in doing so. Don't be so ill-tempered, it makes me nervous."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Everyone has their own set of morals and an approach to maintaining their sense of identity and self in the galaxy. The Imperator was another such being, though he had made a career out of turning the galaxy's greatest talent into agents for his will. Nasiri rejects his offer, and it was not the first-time beings had turned down the offers of Darth Malign. The mutant Wookiees step aside to allow the envoy of the Hutt Cartels right to leave without being slashed into ribbons and left for service droids to clean, "Both offers stand." He says plainly, "They will remain there, in due time you will likely return and can take your pick. The galaxy can be a very cold place, but rest easy knowing that my sister will see to the integrity of your borders, provided your equipment and materials stay on their rightful side of them."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Drayson offers Tracer a warm smile, the one armed man muses, "Fortunately we're going to redesign this uniform from white to crimson red with a black collar to mirror the normal fair. At least then blood won't be one of the major issues with the position." He turns toward Darth Malign, "The command ship and task force are ready. I recommend we adjourn to the fleet so we can prepare for our departure." Even now dozens of capital ships and support vessels were moving into formation around the Imperator's throne vessel.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Malign watches Johanna move to speak with Nasiri, there was a history there he could not appreciate, obviously, he was locked away in a Sith archive for a decade he missed most of the Thel era and whatever Johanna and her band of freedom fighter/pirates were doing to keep order in their patch of space, "Leave him be, Johanna." He smirks, "He can reflect on the offer and come to his senses at another time," he states. He was a man of pride as well, it boiled over in him more often than not.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Imperator rises and moves to join Drayson and Tracer, "Let us go gaze upon the next stages of our operations then. We have much to do." His yellow'ed hazel eyes watch the Fallen as he walks across the room.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri smiles at Johanna and turns at the suggestion of likely returning for the ships. "I wont return for them." there is truth in those words and he glances at Johanna. "Nothing has changed, I will do as I told you I would...

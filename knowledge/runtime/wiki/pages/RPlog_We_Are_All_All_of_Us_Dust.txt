Yarith Hotel Lobby -- Cloud City

       The Yarith is the most high-class hotel in all of Cloud City. Frequented by dignitaries and celebrities, and renowned for its amenities, there are no other hotels in the galaxy like it. Everything here is beautifully crafted and designed.

       From the classic waterfall curtains, lush sapphire and gold carpet to the expansive blue-black tinted windows revealing the cityscape outside, the Yarith is unparalleled in luxury travel accommodations. Busy at all hours, the lobby hosts a number of posh services and small shops.

       Glasteel turbolifts run up and down in banks of three along the walls, speeding guests to their five star apartment suites. A grand dining hall and ballroom, formal attire only, can be seen to the north, while the famous Pair O'Dice casino can be entered to the east.

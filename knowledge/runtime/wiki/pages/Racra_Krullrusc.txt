'''Racra Krullrusc''' was many things in the galaxy. A bounty hunter with some claim to fame, of course nothing great. What brought about attention to him most though was quite possibly the mercenary group which he and [[Axel Vichten]] created, the [[Crimson Fang]], which bombarded the planet [[Bothawui]] under the employ of the Empire. After which, he went away.. And came back again as the [[Prince]] of the [[Black Sun]], before being deposed of and going into hiding with various figures in other parts of the underworld of the Galaxy.

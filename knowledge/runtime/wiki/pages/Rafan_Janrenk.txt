== Troubadour on Tour ==
Hailing from the unknown regions and beyond, Troubadour Janrenk has come in search of an epic to fill out an otherwise spartan catalogue of poems and songs.

==== <u>Epoch Mynock</u> ====
[[File:Epoch_Mynock.jpg|thumb|left|Epoch Mynock in the Unknown Regions]]The yt-2400 is the roving of home Rafan, its stock frame is painted in a garish pattern of yellow and gray and black Nexu pattern. Inside the standard layout is finished with netting fine silk hangings, giving the entire ship a vagabond feel.

        Hmm. You look at me now and see only the warrior, right? A product of blood thirsty conquest and war? One of those warmonger Mandalorians. Hide the children, hide the valuables. Death and destruction follows on their heels.

        Oh, how you have been deceived. My people are one of Honor. We honor our Clans. We honor our words. We honor our foes. It is within the arms of honor that we strive to better ourselves. Stone to the steel, honing the edge to a better keenness.

        But it is not fully of my people that you are speaking with me, yes? You wish to know more of this being whom has comes to your worlds. I am Rawnie Qrowe, of Clan Distayre now. We are one of the remaining clans of the Old Mandalorians. I was born to Clan Awaud originally. The Clan of Disyayre took in those few of us who were left after the Battle of Xorrn. Picture trying to hold your Clan brothers together while under heavy suppression fire. That was one of my last memories before waking up aboard the Clan ship of the Distayre Clan.

        I owe them my life, possibly my sanity. I will carry their flame and the flame of Clan Awaud forward until they prize me from my armor the final time.

        Ahh, yes, yes. I know. I have not explained yet the how's and why's of my arrival in the Caspian Union. Well, I seek to retrieve relics of my people while serving as allowed in the defense of your systems.

        I could boast of my skills in medical aiding, or the retrieval of information when it is required. Or you could test them yourself. I ask only the ability to retrieve items from sites left from the Invasion the Dar-jetti led us on many a cycle passed.

'''Rear Admiral''' is a military rank.

== In Imperial Service ==
{{rank plate|[[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]]|}}
In the [[Galactic Empire]]'s [[Imperial Navy|Navy]], the rank of '''Rear Admiral''' find itself in between [[Commodore]] and [[Vice Admiral]]. Equivalent to a [[Major General]] in the [[Imperial Army]], the Rear Admiral has no specific posting in the [[Imperial Army Order of Battle]], and usually finds itself commanding a [[Imperial Navy#Squadron|Squadron]], but with seniority over other Commodores, or in some cases, an Imperial task force (such as [[Task Force Nebula]]).

A Rear Admiral is equivalent to [[Major General]] in the [[Imperial Army]] or a [[Vice Marshal]] in the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]].

== In Republic Service ==

== In Union Service ==

'''Rear Admiral''' is the fifth most senior rank in the [[Caspian Navy]] ranking below all other [[Admiral|Admirals]] and ranking above [[Commodore|Commodores]]. Caspian Rear Admirals usually are in command of a major production facility or a [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruiser]]. During its brief period of independence, [[Krittish Navy|Krittish]] Rear Admirals tended to be assigned to [[Valorous-class Light Carrier|Valorous-Class Light Carriers]] with those assignments carrying over after reunification, in spite of the fact the superior [[Hurricane-class Fleet Carrier|Hurricane-Class Fleet Carriers]] are generally only commanded by Commodores.

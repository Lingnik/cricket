The '''Red Fleet''' was an armada of [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] warships formed by Imperial [[Admiral]] and [[Warlord]] [[Sinar Fenes]]. Commander of the [[Imperial Core Defense Fleet]], Fenes was charged with defending [[Coruscant|Imperial Center]] in the months after the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]]. During the [[Battle of Coruscant]], he revolted against the [[Interim Ruling Council]], establishing himself as a rogue warlord. He took a large portion of the Core Defense Fleet, declared it his Red Fleet, and set about carving out his own fiefdom. The Red Fleet's first action was [[Ambush at O'paal|attacking]] [[O'paal]]; the battle resulted disastrously for the Red Fleet, Fenes killed in action and most of the armada wiped out. The survivors of O'paal fled, but were eventually hunted down and captured or destroyed, or simply assimilated back into the Imperial fold.

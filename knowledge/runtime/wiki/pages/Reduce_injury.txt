A [[Jedi]] may use this power to reduce the amount of injury she suffers. This power is normally only used in desperation because of its long-term repercussions.

The power must be used in the round that the injury is suffered or in the round immediately following. Any injury that is suffered is reduced to wounded. If the original injury would have killed the character, the gamemaster will inflict a relevant permanent injury on the character.

''[[Control pain]]'' is required to utilize '''''reduce injury'''''.

==Control Difficulty==
Moderate for incapacitated characters, Difficult for mortally wounded characters, Very Difficult for dead characters.

The '''reek''' is a large thick-skinned, horned quadruped, native to [[:starwars:Ylesia|Ylesia]]. These beasts were herbivores by nature, but they were often fed meat while in captivity to increase their aggressiveness. Animal protein turns their leathery skin a dark red. All reeks, including subspecies, are normally brown in color and only change depending on their diet.

Reeks usually are in herds and reside in the mossy grasslands of Ylesia. They are very territorial, and extremely protective of their herds. Because of their mass and fearsome appearance, they were exported to numerous locations throughout the galaxy, with significant subspecies evolving on such places as [[Ithor]] and [[:starwars:Iridonia|Iridonia]].

The '''Rellik Forest''' was a forest created by [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] scientists and biologists in the domed walls of [[Imperial City (Selene)|Imperial City]] on [[Selene]]. 

During the [[War of the Throne]], Selene was devastated by the impact and destruction of the [[SSD Palpatine|HIMS ''Dark Eminence'']]. Selene was reduced to a wasteland. A year later, under the direction of [[Warlord]] [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] and [[Grand Moff]] [[Scaven Marx]], reconstruction efforts would kick off, an effort that would last until [[14 ABY]].

Selene was still inhabitable, and thus a domed city was constructed to protect against the hazardous environment. Within this domed wall, Rellik Forest was grown. Named after [[Jarl Rellik]], a former Grand Moff and Warlord under [[Bacharan Valak]], Rellik Forest was large and sported a vast assortment of wildlife and numerous trails. 

The [[Selene Day]] events of [[15 ABY]] were held mostly in the Rellik Forest, as its natural habitat and caves made it an ideal location for the hunts held by the Empire.

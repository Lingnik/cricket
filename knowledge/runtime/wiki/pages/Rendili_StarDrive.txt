'''Rendili StarDrive''' is a starship manufacturing company based on the planet [[Rendili (Planet)|Rendili]].  Dating back to the founding of the [[:w:c:Galactic_Republic|Galactic Republic]], this venerable corporation is also one of the original signatory sponsors of the [[Corporate Sector Authority]].

Rendili pioneered the [[Victory-class Star Destroyer]] and the famed [[Dreadnaught-class Heavy Cruiser]]. The reign of Palpatine proved to be a period of decline for the company with [[Kuat Drive Yards]] acquiring much of its market share, but a number of lucrative [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] contracts and a series of new designs has revitalized the aging manufacturer.

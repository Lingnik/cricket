{{IGNpost|author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[30 ABY]]|topic=Slavers|anchors=[[Brackett Benduday]]|headline=A Minor Consideration|synopsis=[[InterGalactic News|IGN]] breaks the news of a civilian transport whose passengers and crew were kidnapped by slavers.}}

A tall golden-skinned female, economist Professor [[Brackett Benduday]], can be seen sitting behind an anchor's desk, still seemingly on location at [[Bespin]].

"This is Brackett Benduday live from Bespin. For those of you just joining us we have been recapping the events of the recent terror attack here but we have a late breaking story out of the Krit Union to report. The ''Blechley Brook'' a civilian operated [[Ushton-class Heavy Freighter|Ushton-class Heavy Freighter]] has been attacked by a ship believed to be the same one as that engaged with the [[Leviathan]] here at Bespin."

One the screen the wreck of an Ushton-class Heavy Freighter is shown. The ship itself could be said to be in one piece but it has several sizeable holes its structure. The forward and aft portions appear crumpled in on themselves while a hole in the centre of the ship has vent debris. It is the drifting definition of overkill.

"The ''Blechley Broo''k was transporting students back to [[Krittain Minor]] when it was set upon by the suspect vessel. The [[Krittish Navy|Krittish fleet]], the only former members of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] who have not joined the [[Caspian Union]] had been on alert following the recent attack on Bespin, but the ''Blechley Brook's'' communications appear to have had been hit before they could get out a distress call. By the time they were missed it was too late.

An investigation onboard the wrecked ship by the crew of the ''KMS Lirithose'' which was first on the scene reported scorch marks consistent with a firefight but only discovered four dead bodies, two teachers and two students with massive cranial trauma. The remaining 308 passengers and crew were missing and are presumed captive. We will have more on this story as it develops."

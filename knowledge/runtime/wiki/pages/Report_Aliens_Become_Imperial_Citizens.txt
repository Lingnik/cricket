"This is Tikan with the Imperial Galactic News Network, with an IGN Exclusive! Not two hours ago, A proclamation was sent down from both the Moff and Alien Council's offices. The proclamation, a 34-page document, outlines a new plan for Alien Freedom and Citizenship. All non-humans in the Corellian, Selene, and Coruscant systems will have the full rights and privileges granted by citizenship! The legislation, taking effect immediately, will revamp the judiciary and local legislatures to accommodate an even and fair system of law for both humans and non-humans.

"This controversial move is not without its opponents. Several high-ranking officials in both the Military and Government have spoken out against the move stating that the Empire simply doesn't have the resources to make such a change. They state that, while this is definitely a move that should be debated, if every planet were to make such a change, the Empire would be bankrupt. Even with such criticism, our sources say that there are several officers within the Imperial High Command who approve with this move.

"With aliens becoming citizens, political scholars have been in a flurry of debate on how this will affect the war. Some claim that this will effectively kill any chance the New Republic may have had. Others state that the people will see right through this, that it is simply a ploy that aliens will not fall for..

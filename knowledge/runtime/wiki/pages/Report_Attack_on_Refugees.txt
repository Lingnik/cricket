{{IGNpost|author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[29 ABY]]|topic=Criminal Activity|anchors=[[Jordaan Mag]]|headline=Attack on Refugees|synopsis=[[Jordaan Mag]] interviews Fleet Captain Natanial Tarn aboard CMS Mikassa when they are interrupted by a distress call.}}

The broadcast starts with the image of a [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]] in space identified with the caption CMS Mikassa with the words [[Caspian 3rd Fleet|Iron Torch Fleet]] underneath. After a few moments a voice over indicates, "What started as a routine assignment to interview Fleet Captain Natanial Tarn of [[Caspian Navy]] quickly proved more interesting than expected."

The imagine then shifts to the bridge of the Mikassa where the reporter in question, Jordaan Mag stands next a Mikassan officer. "So is it true that you are the first native [[Mikassan]] to actually command the CMS Mikassa." The officer answers, "Well if by Mikassan you mean someone from the planet, then actually a number of my predecessors in this posting have been from the world. But, yes I am the first racially Mikassan officer to have this command."

An officer starts to speak up from a nearby console and the camera man zooms in on an older looking [[Human]] woman, "Captain we are getting a distress call from the SS Centennial Fox. They are are refugee vessel bound for [[Mikassa]]. They report being upon by an assault shuttle sometime after leaving [[Kashyyyk]]."

The camera shifts back to Captain Tarn, "[[Ensign]] Su prepare to break orbit. [[Commander]] Chek start calculating the hyperdrive jump." He then looks at the [[:starwars:Arkanian Offshoot/Legends|Arkanian]] reporter and remarks, "I hope you weren't in a rush to get to your next assignment." The camera moves back to communications officer as asks, "Captain that will put us in disputed space, shall I contact that [[The Admiralty]] for permission." Once again going back to the captain he remarks, "I think not Commander, we are done with that [[Thomas Mahon|Mahon]]-era Rules of Non-Engagement shite. [[Sar Admiral]] [[Crestian Tarasar|Tarasar]] would laugh."

The screen fades and returns as the ship exits out of hyperspace and soon comes upon a transport surprisingly managing to largely outmanoeuvre a [[Gamma-class Assault Shuttle|Gamma-Class Assault Shuttle]], yet clearly already quite beat up. As a half dozen [[Z-100 Centurion]] starfighters launch from the CMS Mikassa and begin to engage the shuttle one of the bridge officer can be heard in the background saying, "Captain the hull markings appear to be a [[Trandoshan]] glyph." The Captain can be heard replying, "Thank you [[Lieutenant|Leftenant]] Vault, Commander Ran can you get us a translation on that?"

The starfighters continue to engage the assault shuttle, quickly proving to have outmatched it and Lieutenant Vault can be heard saying, "The ship has been disabled Captain." The communications officer finally replies, "It appears the glyph translates to Defiler." A large explosion then occurs on the assault shuttle and it quickly becomes clear that the aft of the vessel has been obliterated.

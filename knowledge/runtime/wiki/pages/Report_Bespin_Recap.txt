{{IGNpost|author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[30 ABY]]|topic=Bespin Terrorist Attack|anchors=[[Brackett Benduday]]|headline=Bespin Recap|synopsis=A recap of the terrorist attack during the Miners' Dinner and its aftermath.}}

A tall golden-skinned woman sits behind an anchor's desk with clouds in the background. The woman is recognisable to many as Professor [[Brackett Benduday]] the economist who hosts the Muun Money Minute and who has become known to an even larger audience over the past year as anchor primarily reporting on news in the Mid Rim. The backdrop would also be recognisable to some as the planet Bespin.

"This is Brackett Benduday coming to you live from [[Cloud City]] on [[Bespin]] where days ago a terrorist attack left scores of dead and hundreds wounded. Among the wounded was Bespin's ruler [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]], the famed pilot, Jedi Knight, and half-sister of Imperator [[Tyler Damion|Damion]]. We will be bring you an address from her shortly but while we wait let's recap the recent events."

The screen shifts to show the ballroom of the Yarith Bespin hotel prior to the attack. The ballroom is full of sentient beings of all description but [[Human|Humans]], [[Lutrillian|Lutrillians]], [[Sullustan|Sullustans]], and [[Ugnaught|Ugnaughts]] feature most prominently. All told roughly four hundred people appear to be in attendance.

"It was supposed to be the first annual Bespin Miners' Dinner. The banquet was largely for the miners and their families but also hosted delegations from a number of worlds."

The narration from the anchor pauses and the screen advances to speech being given. The speaker, an Ugnaught as Shift Foreman Orgrad Soval.

"Today we celebrate the hard and often unseen work of those who bring Bespin its greatest share of wealth, and the families who love and support them. I'm proud to announce that effective immediately, all employees in the mining sector have been given a ten-percent raise, a bonus of twenty-five thousand credits, and that each employee has been -- according to seniority -- granted shares in the company. Together we now own thirty percent of the shares." He pauses for a moment, the stunned looks of all assembled causing his toothy grin to widen: "That's right, friends: thirty percent." Somewhere, a glass shatters as an overjoyed miner elbows her drink off the table by accident. A buzz begins anew, prompting Soval to pat the air in a gesture of calm. "In their wisdom, the Imperium continues to prosecute their war against slavery and exploitative working conditions. Our facilities have been completely retrofitted to meet the highest standards of worker protection. No longer are we workers the unseen. Here, on Bespin, where we've lived and toiled for decades, we are now equals with the denizens of the upper levels."

The screen returns to an image of the anchor desk where Brackett continues to narrate the chain of events, "Approximately 30 minutes later a massive explosion, the work of saboteurs, all but destroyed the refinery and knocked Cloud City itself out of alignment. Moments later an explosion blew a hole in the ballroom itself whereupon an armoured figured rumoured to be the notorious bounty hunter [[Boba Fett]] burst in and began firing into the crowd."

The screen shifts to show grainy footage of this attack: Another smaller explosion rocks the building and leaves a gaping hole in the wall. A gust of wind blows smoke and debris, obscuring the hole for a moment. When it clears, a figure can be seen floating just beyond. Seldom seen but known throughout the galaxy, the armoured figure of Boba Fett rockets into the ballroom through the newly-made entrance in the wall. He fires a few times into the panicked crowd before he lands.

"The destruction and violence was not limited to the hotel and refinery. A slave raid was made concurrently on the city's red light district left dozens dead and missing." As the anchor speaks images of the aftermath of this are displayed. While the faces have been blurred out. The brutal carnage is seen with clear signs of dismembered bodies.

The screen then returns back once again to the anchor's desk. "It has been reported that [[Grand Moff]] te Danaan had be on site at or near the refinery at the time of the explosion and was among the wounded. A senior Imperium official who did not want to go on record tells us she was severely wounded and transported to Selene for treatment and is expected to make a full recovery." A stock photograph of Johanna is displayed.

The image then shifts to a stock photograph of Crestian. "It has also been reported that in the immediate aftermath of the attack [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] assumed operational control of the Bespin system as the next senior Imperium-allied official on site. The system was placed under lock down for the next 48 hours, with traffic heavily regulated since then. The initial forces in the system appear to have been unable, however, to completely halt all traffic. We have been given footage of one vessel which departed the system which is believed to have been involved in the initial attack."

A short space battle comes on the screen with an [[Leviathan|Interdictor Cruiser]] battling another capital ship that appears to be of a custom design. Fire from the latter vessel damages the former as it attempts to project a gravity well against it, but return fire from the former leaves the latter vessel quite battered before it is able to make it into hyperspace.

The space battle is replaced with the anchor's desk and a stock photograph of an armoured Boba Fett. "Unsubstantiated rumours indicate that Boba Fett was captured after a firefight with some of the banquet guests but as of yet, we have been unable to confirm his current location or who might have him in their custody."

The next stock photograph is that of Quinne, "As Cloud City has worked to recover in these past days on the leadership of acting Baroness-Administrator [[Quinne Stardust Rose]], the general manager and lead performer at the Yarith Bespin. The people I have spoken to have indicated the Cloud City Chanteuse has been a comforting presence to the city and remained on top of managing the various teams repairing the city."

Finally the image beside the anchor becomes the corporate logo of [[SubPro]]. "Aid from various parts of the Imperium and allied worlds has come into Cloud City in the aftermath of the attack, the principle source of which as been the Caspian Union. A Caspian task force centred around the CMS Renaissance of Innovation brought SubPro engineers who have been assisting the miners in handling the damaged refinery. Both the local and Caspian teams have reported Cloud City to be structurally safe since the explosion. The recent renovations at the refinery are also credited with having prevented further disaster or a higher casualty count but the facility itself will need to effectively be rebuilt from scratch."

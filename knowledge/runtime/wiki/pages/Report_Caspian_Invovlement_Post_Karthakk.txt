{{IGNpost|author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[34 ABY]]|topic=Prison Break|anchors=None|headline=Caspian Invovlement Post Karthakk|synopsis=Fern Powers, the New Director of the Officer of the [[Presav]], hosts a press conference concerning [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] involvement in the [[Imperium|Imperium's]] efforts to bring the escapees from the prison break at [[The Oubliette|Spacestation 1138]] to justice.}}

The logo of [[InterGalactic News]] is displayed on the screen with a voice over announcing, "We are interrupting your normal broadcasting for a special news conference from [[Union Hall]] in [[Plaxton]] on [[Caspar]]." The screen soon shifts to a the Atrium of Union Hall with a number of chairs having been set up in front of a wooden podium the Vector Apex on it.

After a few moments a Human woman in her early forties approaches the podium. She has pale skin and dark hair and wears a red blazer. As she stands preparing to speak she does not look very comfort, perhaps annoyed to be there. A caption on the screen indicates her to be Fern Powers, the recently appointed Director of the Office of the Presav.

"Apparently the have been a number of media inquires made following the recent press conference by the Office of the [[Imperium]] Grand Inquisitor and some subsequent ravings in the press following it. [[Crestian Tarasar|Crestian]] in his 'infinite' wisdom has sent me out here to share facts and answers some questions." Her tone now is decidedly annoyed and she sighs after her opening statement.

"I wish to first turn your attention to the written statement made by [[Presav]] Tarasar after the original announcement by the Imperium concerning [[The Oubliette|Spacestation 1138]]. I am not going to repeat but copies are being made available. In short it stated that by the terms of the Union's mutual protection pact with the Imperium, the [[Constable|Union Constabulary]], [[Caspian Defence Force]], and [[Caspian Intelligence Service]] were providing assistance to the Imperium on the matter and that Presav Tarasar remained in close contact with Grand Inquisitor [[Axel Vichten|Vichten]] on the matter. The statement also acknowledged that few details were being provided for security reason.

Somehow the fact that the Union in fact has rendered that support and has been operating closely with Imperium counterparts on the matter seems to have surprised some people. Apparently the fact that two long allied parties have acted in concert on an issue has been declared noteworthy. This seems much like going to one of your moms for permission on something the other mom already said no to and being shocked that they already knew you had asked the other one first. Relationships breed coordination. Clearly there has been some coordination failure here as I am dealing with you instead of having you sulking at the feet of Zephara."

Fern looks to something or someone off to the side outside of the view of the camera before facing forward again, "Anyway now that the Imperium has shared some details, the Presav wishes to do the same. Following the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]] becoming aware of the breach at Spacestation 1138, Presav Tarasar in his capacity as Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defence and the Commanding Officer of the [[Caspian 4th Fleet]] as [[Sar Admiral]], did deploy elements of said 4th Fleet to assist the Imperium with the investigation into the security breach and apprehension of escaped individuals. This action was considered appropriate both given the alliance and the threat many of this individuals posed to galactic peace.

The lead vessel which travelled to the [[:starwars:Karthakk system/Legends|Karthakk System]] itself was [[CMS Mergansar]] under the command of [[Commodore]] [[Sha Dark]], also and primarily operating in her capacity as Minister of Justice. At the time other vessels were also deployed to the Karthakk Sector and other nearby sectors in the Outer Rim. While Presav Tarasar retained overall control, Minister Dark coordinated in the field.

In accordance with Articles V, VI, and VIII of the [[Imperium-Caspian Mutual Protection Pact (29 ABY)|mutual protection pact]] [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] forces operated within the borders of the Imperium in this campaign both in direct support of Imperium operations and at times operating independently in pursuit of the wanted individuals. As this was a matter of Imperium security we will not be detailing most of these operations further except for those in Durellian Expanse and Nemares Corridor as mentioned in the Imperium press conference.

Elements of Task Group Resplendent under Sar Admiral [[Odie Hood]], in specific his command ship CMS Resplendent and [[CMS Trettinar]] under [[Fleet Captain]] Shakka Teksa, were involved in actions in the Durellian Expanse with Sar Admiral Hood coordinating the combined Imperium-Caspian siege owing to his seniority and experience. The Presav wishes to extend his praise and thanks to Sar Admiral Hood, Fleet Captain Teksa, the crew of these vessels, and particularly to the pilots of Firebreather Squadron off the Trettinar for the exemplary actions during the operation.

The Presav also wishes to extend his praise and thanks to Fleet Captain [[Drorq]] of the Dying Sun for his initiative interdicting the subspace traffic in the Nemares Corridor which proved critical to the success of the operation there."

Fern pauses and starts drinking from a glass of water that is at the podium. She stands there just drinking water and awkwardly looking back at the reporters in the audience for at least a full minute before putting the glass down and saying to the reporters, "I suppose I need to take questions now."

As the reporters start raising their hands she points to a [[Twi'lek]] male, "The Twi'lek in the sixth row."

He answers, "Dagan Luna, Mid Rim News was the Caspian participation in these operations authorised by the [[Union Council]]?"

Fern looks back at him as if this was the dumbest question she has ever heard. She takes another sip of her water staring at him before saying, "So that's your question? The Caspian 4th Fleet is the expeditionary force of the Caspian Navy with its deployments fully under the discretion of Presav Tarasar. I can say that the Ministers who serve as the members Intelligence Directorate have been fully briefed and are supportive of Caspian involvement in the operations associated with the breach at Spacestation 1138. Other Councillors have been briefed to the extent appropriate."

She then points to a man in the front row and says, "Tony Bellaroso what can I answer for Freedom Galactic News." Her recognition of the man is done with no expressed emotion.

Tony doesn't answer right away or at least his looks like he is starting to, but was caught off guard at the new office directors recognition of him personally and has to think of what to say now that he isn't having to identify himself. He recovers and asks, "[[Thurston Unger]] of [[The Cutting Edge]] has seemed to imply some suspicious coincidences in the cooperation of Presav Tarasar and Grand Inquisitor Vichten, do you have any comment on that?"

Fern raises an eyebrow and looks down at her glass of water. Almost as if she is having some communication with the glass of water rather than the reporter. She turns back forward and responds, "Has he now? I mean I saw his broadcast, I heard words. Clearly you found them more intelligible than I did. I am really not sure what he keeps getting hot and bothered over. The Imperium and Union are allies and have been for five years now. 

Crestian was granted the title of [[Moff]] as a courtesy. He had spent many years associated with the Imperator prior to his return to Caspar, and the Imperator continues to appreciate his insights. As part of the relationship between the two powers the Presav receives briefings commensurate with those that actual Moffs receive. I am not sure what revelation the press is expecting to find out of this.

As for the Grand Inquisitor he is a resident of [[Noques Rasala]] and religious leader within the community. However, you can't swing a dead Loth-cat in Noques Rasala without hitting like nine religious leaders. Again as the Union and Imperium are allies, his service as Grand Inquisitor is not in conflict with his Caspian citizenship. He was granted the rank of [[Marshal|Star Marshal]] in the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]] when he participated in the [[Charros Wargames|wargames]] in the [[Charros IV|Charros System]] two years ago. 

Star Marshal Vichten is well known as an expert in all matters related to starfighters and as Grand Inquisitor is not a rank in the Caspian Defence Force an honorary posting with a rank that is was considered appropriate. His insights matters related to starfighters have been appreciated by Presav Tarasar and Star Marshals Dienes and Meru. He is consulted on the subject at times and does have access to facilities in this role, which again he would already have access to as the Grand Inquisitor of an allied power.

But again I am at loss for what is considered particularly noteworthy in all of this. We are not talking about younglings on playground staging a fake wedding. The Union and Imperium are allies and that alliance plays out in specific ways, it is not some vague and meaningless declaration of affection."

Fern takes another sip of her water, though without the long drawn out affectation of doing so as she had earlier. "I have time for one more question." She quickly points to a female [[:starwars:Arkanian Offshoot/Legends|Arkanian-Offshoot]] in the second row, "[[Jordaan Mag|Jordaan]]?"

Jordaan Mag of InterGalactic News responds, "The Imperium's press conference indicated that [[Lorn Rhys|Darth Malus]] is still at large. Is the Caspian Union concerned about this and is the Union taking any special actions concerning him?"

Fern's reaction to this question lacks the annoyance and dismissive expression given to the previous journalists, though she does seem slightly ill at ease on receiving it. "As Presav Tarasar indicated in his previous statement concerning the breach of Spacestation 1138, the bounties placed by the Imperium hold legal weight in the Union. As such Darth Malus is still considered a fugitive from justice and if found in Union territory he would be subject to efforts to apprehend him and turn him over to the Imperium. I'm afraid I can't say more than that at this time."

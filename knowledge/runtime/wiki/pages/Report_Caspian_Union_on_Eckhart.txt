{{IGNpost|author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[32 ABY]]|topic=Rogue Marines|anchors=[[Brackett Benduday]]|headline=Caspian Union on Eckhart|synopsis=The [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] Minister of Justice [[Sha Dark]] holds a press conference where she is asked for the Caspian position on [[Colonel]] [[Han'bel Eckhart]] and the rogue crew of the [[DSV Templar|Templar]].}}

A tall golden-skinned female, economist Professor [[Brackett Benduday]], can be seen sitting alone behind an anchor's desk in a studio. The screen behind her shows a live image [[Union Hall]] in [[Plaxton]] on [[Caspar]].

"This is Brackett Benduday with the latest news tonight from the Rim. Later on the [[Muun Money Minute]] we will be analysing the latest sales reports from [[Caspar Caff]] and what it means for [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector]] stocks. First up we have a story of a what was expected to be a routine press conference by [[Sha Dark]], the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] Minister of Justice and Councillor from [[Orleanon]] turned more interesting when Councillor Dark was asked a question by a reporter concerning rogue [[New Republic Marine Corps|Republic Marine]] [[Colonel]] [[Han'bel Eckhart|H.S. Eckhart]]."

The screen then turns to an imagine of the Council Chambers in Union Hall. The first four rows of audience seating in the hall are filled with a variety of news reporters. Behind the podium stands a [[Zeltron]] woman in her early to mid-twenties wearing a relatively generic black business suit. A caption on the screen indicates the Zeltron to be Councillor Sha Dark. A male [[Vorzydiak]] reporter in the third row raises his hand and is called on by the Zeltron.

The Vorzdydiak stands and asks, "We have been hearing a lot in the news lately about a Colonel Eckhart a New Republic marine officer who it has claimed has gone rogue and stolen a ship. At a recent press conference they indicated they are working with other organisations to bring this man and his comrades to justice. Has the Caspian Union been contacted by the New Republic at all in this regard?"

The Zeltron replies, "They have not reached out to us no. I am honestly unsure of what other organisations they would have reached out to given the disdain their leadership seems to feel about any power that does not share their vision of the galaxy."

The reporter continues to stand, "A follow up question, does the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]] have an official position on the matter?

The Zeltron smirks, "It is not our general policy to official positions on individual ships and crews who have not committed any crimes again the Union or her allies." The woman pauses takes a sip from a glass at the podium before adding, "I will say personally as a native [[Mergansar|Mergani]] who was displaced from her home by terrorists while the planetary government and [[Caspian Defence Force|CDU military]] turned a blind eye, I have great sympathy for Colonel Eckhart. The Union Council takes a dim view of those who are in a position to stop suffering and step away.

According to the [[Caspian Intelligence Service]], Colonel Eckhart had been operating near the planet [[:starwars:Gorsh|Gorsh]] last month when it fell under attack by raiders. His superiors chose to order him home rather than respond to the distress call of an unaligned world. I would like to think this represents the poor judgement of a junior flag officer rather than the position of the New Republic, but based on Eckhart's subsequent action we can surmise this problem is systemic enough to prompt a decorated veteran to 'go rogue.' Based on the information I have on the situation were an asylum request made, my office would seriously entertain it."

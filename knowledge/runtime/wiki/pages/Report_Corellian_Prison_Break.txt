{{IGNpost|author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[32 ABY]]|topic=Prison Break|anchors=[[Brackett Benduday]]|headline=Corellian Prison Break|synopsis=[[InterGalactic News]] reports on an attempted prison break at Coronet City Spaceport while several high ranking politicians were present.}}

Images of explosions in various locations in [[Coronet City]] on [[Corellia]] flash on the screen before the camera zooms out to show a golden-skinned woman behind an anchor desk with the [[InterGalactic News|IGN]] logo on it.

"The images behind me are a variety of explosions that occurred earlier today on [[Corellia]]. You would think that would be our top story, but it's not. I am [[Brackett Benduday]], and our top story is an escape from a prison shuttle at Coronet City Spaceport that left several dead, several more wounded, and has Prime Minister [[Dash Blazer]] recovering at Corellia University Hospital. But before we get into that, let's return to those explosions."

The screen shifts to a male [[Devaronian]] reporter in a plaza, a large [[Corellian Security Force|CorSec]] presence in the background. The caption on the screen indicates the location to be Nebula Plaza and the report to be Hersh Ell.

"This is Hersh Ell coming to you from Nebula Plaza, one of twelve sites in or near [[Coronet City]] where simultaneous explosions occurred several hours ago. Remarkably there have been no reports of fatalities from these explosions and relatively few injuries. The explosions appear to not have been aimed at doing any damage but rather in drawing out CorSec resources away from Coronet City Spaceport."

The screen returns to the anchor's desk in the studio.

"And top story begins minutes later in Coronet City Spaceport where Prison Shuttle #3381 which was taking thirteen prisoners being extradited from the [[Corellian Confederation]] to the [[New Republic]], was hijacked shortly after take off. The prisoners managed to escape their bonds and incapacitate the five guards on board. The pilot, Lieutenant Booster Dustrunner was able to force the ship back to the surface and destroy the controls to stop the prisoners escaping with hit, he would then be the first fatality, killed at the controls by one of the prisoners.

Most of the CorSec presence at the spaceport having been drawn away to deal with the explosions rocking the city. The only team near that particular landing pad was the personal detail of Prime Minister Dash Blazer who was coming to personally greet the delegation of the Imperium's [[Moff]] of [[Fondor]], the Corellian raised [[Liza Molokai]]. Also in the general vicinity at that moment was Imperator [[Tyler Damion|Damion]], a Corellian himself, and [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]]."

The screen briefly shifts to show images of Blazer, Damion, Molokai, and Tarasar before returning back to the anchor.

"What occurred next can only be described as chaos as the prisoners exited the downed shuttle and a firefight commenced. The main Imperium delegation was able to withdraw from the scene while Prime Minister Blazer and his guards became pinned down by fire from the prisoners. With CorSec resources drawn away, and reports of some kind of communication grid failure hindering Blazer's team from calling for reinforcements, CorSec had to rely on nearby civilians as well as the Caspian delegation to combat the prisoners. We can also report that Imperator Damion himself established a field clinic on the site to treat the growing number of wounded but was soon forced into the combat himself."

Grainy security camera footage comes on the screen showing some moments of the fight. In one image a [[Human]] woman and [[Quarren]] man captioned as [[Selko Aronat]] and [[Blulurbgloblurb Druze]] are seen trading shots with some of the prisoners. In another image  a Mashi [[Horansi]] woman captioned as Emissary [[Kasha Tabasuri]] of the [[Caspian Foreign Ministry]] is seen wielding a vibro-blade and forcing the surrender of a wounded Human prisoner. Next a [[Sarian]] man and [[Droid]] in a pinstripe suit captioned as Caspian Presav Crestian Tarasar and [[Colonel]] [[V1C3]] of the [[Union Marines|Caspian Marines]] are seen taking down prisoners with blaster fire. Another image shows a lightsaber wielding man, identified as Imperator Damion fighting a [[Bothan]] and a [[Gamorrean]], the image abruptly cutting off before the lightsaber's damage to the Bothan can be seen on screen. Finally an image of Prime Minister Dash Blazer bravely rising from cover to fire his own blaster can be seen.

The screen then shifts to live footage of Coronet City Spaceport with a female white-skinned [[:starwars:Arkanian Offshoot/Legends|Arkanian-Offshoot]] reporter in the foreground, captioned as [[Jordaan Mag]]. The spaceport is swarming with CorSec officers.

"This is Jordaan Mag at Coronet City Spaceport where CorSec is still trying to make sense of what happened here hours ago. This consensus, of course, is that the explosions in the city and the sabotage to the CorSec communications grid occurred to allow for this prisoner escape, but who is responsible remains a mystery according to the officers we spoke to. What also is causing speculation is the presence of so many high profile figures at the spaceport at the time of the escape. Why was a prison transport scheduled at this time? And were the other incidents designed to allow the prisoners to escape to freedom  or to assassinate the figures present?

What we do know is that all the thirteen prisoners are reported to have been captured or killed though we have conflicting reports as to how many were apprehended alive. CorSec is reporting seven of their officers were wounded but expected to survive, while an eighth Lieutenant Booster Dustrunner died on board the shuttle. No civilian fatalities have been reported but we do not have an exact number on the number of injured given the number of entities involved in treatment on the scene. We can report that Prime Minister Dash Blazer was seriously, but not critically wounded in the attack. He was treated on the scene by an Imperium medical team and later transferred to Corellia University Hospital, he is expected to make a full recovery."

Once again the screen goes back to the anchor in the studio.

"Many questions remain tonight and we will have more on this story as it develops. We will also be following up on this later tonight on the [[Muun Money Minute]] with how the markets are reacting to this chaos on Corellia. Also on the Muun Money Minute, corporate restructuring at [[Biscuit Baron]] and what is behind it."

'''The following is a special IGN Report!'''

"Last last night, fighting erupted within the New Republic's military garrison as criminals entered the area, unwelcomed. Suspected members of the illustrious Maffi crime family, well known for it's illegal activities, are believed to responsible for the attack, although the reason for the attack has yet to be released." The reporter says as she turns her face to another camera. "Speculation is that the Maffi crime family is angry that the New Republic has taken up position on the planet, and even more frustrated as Kashyyyk forces have begun moving on to the planet to provide a greater police presence."

"The New Republic has released a statement advising of a travel blockade to and from the planet, as well as between spaceports on the planet while they cooperate with local police forces in order to find the culprits. It has been released that the New Republic is looking for this man <''Insert Picture of [[Antoine Dareus|Dareus]]''> who is wanted for questioning. The New Republic is warning the public that this man should be considered armed and dangerous." The woman looks ahead now, offering a grim look.

"We will keep you apprised of further developments in this story as they unfold. And now back to your regularly scheduled program."

The [[InterGalactic News|IGN]] logo spins on the holo-screen. The screen shifts to the gray haired Rick Neel. "Good morning from [[Coronet City]]. Today we have breaking news. With the finalization of the [[Corellian Confederation]], [[:starwars:Tralus/Legends|Tralus]] is holding elections for Councillor. Former [[Governor]] of [[Corellia]], [[Ewan Dain]] has thrown his hat into the race. Several other candidates have announced their intention to run for the position. Mr. Dain made news nearly two decades ago when it was discovered that he was born to Tralus' noble House Rannis. While this does give him strong ties to the leadership of Tralus, we will have to wait and see if this benefits or hinders the former governor. This is Rick Neel, IGN news."

[[Thurston Unger|Thurston]] takes a dramatic pause and looks directly at the camera, his eyes twinkling mischievously. "Yes, darlings, you heard it right. The [[Galaxy's Dumbest Crooks|Galaxy's Dumbest Criminals]]. Each week, we'll be bringing you a new and hilarious tale of some criminal mastermind who just couldn't quite pull off their heist. I'm talking about bumbling fools, half-wits, and dimwits who probably shouldn't be allowed to operate a blaster, let alone plan a crime."

He leans forward, his voice dropping to a conspiratorial whisper. "And let me tell you, my lovelies, we've got some doozies for you. Like the time a group of [[Gamorrean|Gamorreans]] tried to rob a bank, but they couldn't fit their axes through the door. Or the time a gang of [[Rodian|Rodians]] attempted to steal a shipment of blasters, but they ended up shooting each other in the foot. Trust me, you won't want to miss a single episode of the Galaxy's Dumbest Criminals."

Thurston leans back in his chair, a grin spreading across his face. "And now, without further ado, let me tell you about the latest heist gone wrong. It all started when a group of [[Jawa|Jawas]] decided to rob a spice freighter. They snuck aboard in the middle of the night, armed with nothing more than their trusty ion blasters and a whole lot of nerve. But things didn't go quite as planned. You see, these Jawas forgot one very important thing: they don't speak Basic."

Thurston chuckles, shaking his head. "So there they were, trying to communicate with the crew of the freighter in their own language, but all they got were confused looks and blank stares. And then, to make matters worse, one of the Jawas accidentally fired his ion blaster, sending the entire ship's systems into overdrive. Long story short, the Jawas ended up getting caught and are now serving time in a New Republic prison. But don't worry, my darlings, we'll have plenty more stories like this to share in the weeks to come."

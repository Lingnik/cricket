{{IGNpost|author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[28 ABY]]|topic=Crestian Tarasar's return to [[Caspar]]|anchors=Jack Coruscant|headline=Jack Coruscant interviews Crestian Tarasar|synopsis=Crestian Tarasar sits down for an extended interview with IGN's Jack Coruscant.}}

A Human male reporter, average height and build appears in view. Behind him one can see the familiar sight of a Caspar Caff establishment.

Jack: "Greetings, I'm Jack Coruscant, with [[InterGalactic News|IGN]]. My regular viewers will be happy to hear that since my last report my camera man and I were able to leave [[Mergansar]] thanks to one aid transports that were arriving in the system. That my friends is a story for another day."

The camera pans out to show that Jack is sitting with another man, a [[Sarian]] male dressed in the latest [[Silverwood House of Fashion]] attire. The outfit heavily features the colours red, silver, and black and the discerning fashionista would note the [[Kubaz|Kubazian]] and [[Chandrila|Chandrilan]] influences.

Jack: "Tonight I bring you an exclusive interview with man some many people are talking about [[Crestian Tarasar]]. As part of the agreement for this interview Tarasar will start with an introduction of himself and his recent actions before we move on to a question and answer segment. And without further ado Crestian Tarasar live and exclusively on IGN."

Crest: "Thank you Jack for this opportunity, I wish it could be under better circumstances. Since a lot of the viewers have no idea who I am or my history, lets get that out of the way first. The reports to date have at least spelled my name right, I am Crestian Tarasar, though all this Captain business is rather outdated. I was born and raised on [[Caspar]] until my teenage years where my family relocated to Chandrila. I spent several years at Brionelle Memorial Military Academy first as a student and later a professor and dean prior to the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]] where I subsequently served as a Captain in the [[New Republic|New Republic's]] [[New Republic FleetOps|Navy]]. I became disillusioned with the Republic and after sometime operating independently I was recruited into the [[New Sith Order]] where I served as a Admiral, with the Leviathan as my flagship. For the past decade since the collapse of the New Sith Order I have been operating quietly and independently.

Like any sentient being with a heart, the news of what we now know was a phage on Caspar had me deeply considered. As a native of Caspar and a Sarian I wanted to be able to help, but at first there seemed little I could do. I am not a doctor or medical scientist nor do I have a stockpile of aid supplies. That changed when the late Doctor [[Daln Bael|Bael]] delivered orders to the Navy to not only shoot down any craft that attempted to leave Caspar but also to prevent any attempted relief. This was very concerning and dangerous. The immediate concern....even if a cure could not have been found, to have the rest of the Union and galaxy give up on Caspar while there were still people alive, to ask the Navy to just stand vigil and watch everyone die. Abandoning hope like that was not good for the morale of the [[Caspian Democratic Union|Union]] and clearly embolden groups like [[Olumi Osahn]].

The fact that Doctor Bael was delivering orders on behalf of the government to the military seemed very troubling. It was at this point that I started to suspect that most of the Crest was already dead or incapacitated and I questioned what sort of experience decision makers were left. While I knew I could not help medically, I thought that maybe my presence and that of the Leviathan could help bring a little order and reassurance in the situation so it was at that point I decided to journey to Caspar and offer whatever assistance we could provide. We were invited by the Navy to take up a position over [[Orleanon]] to assist in the quarantine....by this point the Navy had already allowed the [[Corporate Sector Authority]] to engage in aid operations in defiance of Doctor Bael's directive.

In my several meetings with the late [[Sar Admiral]] [[Prae Kichakressa]], though she never explicitly stated it, it became clear that the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]] had effectively ceased to exist, there was no more Union Assembly and she was the only Sar Admiral it seems to have survived. I later learned through my own contacts that [[Presav]] [[Kilos Sabosulmis]] died fairly early on. At any rate Sar Admiral Kichakressa and I discussed how I could be most useful and we agreed that trying to resolve the matter with Olumi Osahn was the best way I could. I mean there was no order written order or anything but she gave her tacit approval for me to get involved on Mergansar privately.

Having been a member of the New Sith Order gave me a special insight into their operations. One of their former agents [[Axel Vichten]], who by the way is not a Sarian, he eventually became the Starfighter Marshal for the NSO. Back in the day the NSO provided a substantial amount of funding to Olumi Osahn. You could say they had been at co-opted into being a Sith front organisation. This meant I had at my disposal a great deal of intelligence into their membership.

Now my operation against them started pretty slowly at first. I had to collect some new intelligence without the benefit of the sort of intelligence network we had back in my Sith days. There was also the efforts to find medical help for Caspar that divided my attention, thankfully some associates of mine were able to hire [[Basca Starker]], without his efforts another three hundred million people would now be dead.

Anyway after collecting enough intelligence on Olumi Osahn I began try to negotiate with them to find a peaceful resolution. I mean it may seem crazy trying to reason with fanatic terrorists but enough people had died on Caspar, I was willing to offer certain concessions in order to get them to back down. Now this “Mister Bobish” has made a lot of fantastical claims that apparently [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] and I had some sinister plans for the system we wanted them to support and worship our darkness. It is actually pretty funny that a shadow worshipping cult is complaining that someone asked them to worship darkness. And of course I never asked them to worship anything. Like what do they think we use to do?  Pray to the eternal soul of some dead Sith Lord. Honestly I am not particularly spiritual to begin with. I mean there is a "temple" on my ship, really it is just kind of a gallery where I keep some artefacts we have collected over the years. Sounds cooler than calling it an art gallery.

Now here is what really happened. I offered them a chance to work with me to help build a better and resurgent Union. I had hoped to stay at Caspar and help it rebuild. I was willing help encourage refugee resettlement from Mergansar to Caspar so long as the extrajudicial efforts or any legal effort to remove Union citizens from Mergansar ended. I was also willing to back Mergansar producing a new constitution that would have enshrined a greater Sarian identity to the world. This is not to say I approve of their efforts by any stretch but again I was wishing to avoid further violence. Besides I know that no matter how things play out some people are just not going to feel comfortable living on Mergansar again. I really do hope some of them will consider relocating to Caspar now that it is safe. The Union needs all hands on deck to survive and I still want to see it survive.

I did not ask them to worship darkness, I did threaten them with darkness. That is to say that if they refused terms to end their displacement of innocent Caspian citizens I let them know I would liquidate their sorry excuse for organisation. To that end I have personally given representatives of the Corporate Sector Authority, [[Galactic Empire]], New Republic, and several sovereign systems complete dossiers on Olumi Osahn agents operating on the worlds. My own agents have also undertaken to arrest a number of their agents in numerous locations as well.

Now as far as Mergansar itself, once the deadline for their response had passed and they started crowding the spaceports with literally millions of innocents, I knew we had to act quickly. There were already signs that some people had been killed and who knew what would happen if Olumi Osahn was not satisfied at how quickly people were able to leave. It could have become a complete bloodbath. The Caspian Navy was still maintaining the quarantine on Caspar. I knew that would be lifted soon and they could afford to have Leviathan pull away, but there was little time to try to coordinate the full fleets departure so I acted in the spirit of the mission Sar Admiral Kichakressa had tasked me with, dealing with it privately.

When the Caspian fleet got the go ahead to leave Caspar and started towards Mergansar, I tried to get talk to the Sar Admiral about the situation but she refused to discuss anything prior to a rendezvous and demanded we break orbit from Mergansar which we did, in spite of the fact that in doing so we were not able to continue to guarantee the safety of aid supplies and refugees at the beach head we created at Hebron. Sadly it was clear once we did rendezvous that Sar Admiral Kichakressa was not interested in talk. It is very sad, had they joined us at Mergansar ordered would be restored there by now instead of the sad results of the battle. A battle I did not want.

I am ready for your questions now Jack."

Jack: "Well let's start with one that is on the minds of most of our viewers. Is Darth Malign actually alive?"

Crest: "Wait you thought he was dead? Is that what you have all been thinking? Who told you that?"

Jack: "Are you saying he is alive then?"

Crest: "Sorry....this is actually a serious question? I did run into him at a masquerade several months back. He had quite the amusing costume. He went as Darth Vader, it was hilarious."

Jack: "Interesting....that segues into my next question. What is [[Casulmis]] and is it a Sith organisation?"

Crest: "The object of operations on Mergansar was to restore order. To allow non-Sarian minority to return to their homes and to be safe. They are citizens of the Union who had been illegally rounded up. The Caspian military clearly had no ability, interest, or willingness to handle the problem and Olumi Osahn has infiltrated the government and military of Mergansar. I am afraid I do not have enough marines to be able to have combatted Olumi Osahn by myself. Nor was I ever looking to seize the planet in such a fashion. So we bombed a few key strategic targets to create a safe zone in order to allow refugees to return from [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] and to allow for the refugees to take up arms to restore the rights and safety. Casulmis was simply a name created to give some cohesion to that. It was never meant to represent any ideology foreign to Caspian space nor to be anything long term. It simply exists to protect people from government and military that have abandoned them."

Jack: "It seems like you put a lot on the line in order to combat Olumi Osahn. I mean if we are being honest I am glad to be away from them myself. But what made you determined to fight them?"

Crest: "There are a variety of reasons for that. Though I was never involved in any of the Sith efforts to fund them I do feel that as someone who was a member of the New Sith Order I have some responsibility to clean up that mess. I am not sure if they are truly so naïve as to think that the phage would not have occurred had Caspian society been racial pure, ultimately I am a philosopher not a theologian. But as a species Sarian exist because of the environmental factors we survived. Biologically and culturally that has given us certain advantages, but also very clear disadvantages. We are a species particularly unattuned to the Force. And this plague has been reclassified by Mister Starker as [[Sariphage]] precisely because it effected our species worse than any, Sarians died at a higher percentage than any other species effected. The so-called purity they seek would not have helped them. In the end the people who are alive on Caspar have non-Sarians to thank for a cure. Though perhaps that doesn't bother Olumi Osahn too much since they have never been afraid to have agents like Axel Vichten who is not a pure anything, let alone pure Sarian.

Frankly I find their ideology personally offensive. Were my family and I civilians living on Mergansar, we too would have been subject to be rounded up and pushed into a starport. I feel for these families because they are just like my own. Now I may not be able to stop every bigoted organisation in the galaxy from spreading their vile filth, but when terrorists take advantage of a tragedy like the Sariphage to illegally round up and in some cases kill legal citizens of that planet, and it is in my home system, well that is something I have to do what I can about. It would have been better if the planetary authorities or Caspian military had taken care of it themselves. I had sincerely hoped once the quarantine had lifted on Caspar the military would join us in the valuable work we were doing helping the refugees get home or take over that work from us. Instead they chose to pull us away from that and [[Mergansar Incident|attack]] us, sadly it has become very clear that the Caspian Navy under Sar Admiral Prae Kichakressa was colluding with Olumi Osahn."

Jack: "It has been suggested that you and Darth Malign corrupted the late Sar Admiral. How do you respond to that?"

Crest: "It is really laughable. First of all Darth Malign never met the woman and certainly if he had corrupted her as has been claimed why would she fire on me? Unless I am on the outs with him. If you are watching this sorry I criticised your manners at that party, I have learned my lesson you don't need to send any more fleets after me. In all seriousness I had some respect for Sar Admiral Kichakressa, she was trying to do the impossible. The problem is she was trying to do the impossible, she wanted to keep everything together the way it was before. She expressed to me how tenuous things were for the Union. The loss of Mergansar would have spelled the end and I thought she was genuine when she looked to me for help there.

But surely if I had corrupted her why would she send a fleet after me? At this point I am not sure what she wanted me to accomplish, clearly she could have done things a lot differently if she wanted to stop the terrorism. So I don't know if she expected me to fail in my mission or was just using me to do some dirty work for her or what. I mean I suspected she was not being entirely level in how she was dealing with me, but I guess even I failed to see how deep the rot of Olumi Osahn had effect Caspian society. Nevertheless I implored her to stand down. I warned her that she was not in for the easy fight she expected. Yet she fired on us anyway. So the Union has been weakened further, lives of brave Caspians were lost. And for what? To allow Olumi Osahn to continue to terrorise the people the consider unworthy. It was a sad day for Caspar but what was I to do? My crew already had followed me to Caspar to help a system they had no stake in. We were attacked and we defended ourselves. What I am most sorry about is the fact this interrupts the work we were doing there. It would be easy to give up on Caspar and Mergansar but they need help now more than ever."

Jack: "How exactly were you able to do so much damage to a much larger fleet anyway?"

Crest: "Well I did tell you I would answer all your questions to the best of my ability but that was does veer into the territory of military secret. I want to be forthright but I need to protect my people. So I think the best way I can answer that is to remind everyone that in spite of the Caspian Navy seem to look at me as "Captain Tarasar with his Interdictor." I am an Admiral. You did not get to be an Admiral in the New Sith Order without some talent. I may not have the same assets at my disposal as I did with the New Sith Order but the galaxy has been underestimating me for years. I am afraid both Olumi Osahn and their sympathisers in the Caspian Navy found out the very hard way how costly that can be."

Jack: "My final question to you Admiral Tarasar, what comes next for you?"

Crest "Obviously my ship is still undergoing quite a bit of repair work. While that is occurring we have to wait and see what happens in Caspian space. If a government emerges there that is able and willing to protect the civilian population and more over is willing to ensure their armed forces and planetary governments do indeed respect the rights and welfare of the civilian population there....then well as much as I want to help there. If they are able to do that without me, I don't need impose myself on the situation, obviously I know the families of those lost in the battle won't be too keen to see me there.

That being said the fact that the there was no authority that was willing or at least able to ensure that before....literally millions of people displaced on Mergansar before I intervened, well as much as I am hopeful that the situation resolves itself I am not overly optimistic. If the refugee community still needs my help, I am willing to commit myself to be part of that great wind."

Jack: "This has been an IGN broadcast, I am Jack Coruscant, my guest Crestian Tarasar. Thank you for watching."

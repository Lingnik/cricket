[[Imperium]] Press Briefing - [[Selene]] Sector Press Corps Headquarters
Location: Grand Atrium, Office of the Imperial Press Corps, Selene
Presiding: Zephara Val'Riin, Press Secretary to the Grand Inquisitor

The Grand Atrium is silent but watchful, as the assembled members of the press await the arrival of the Inquisitor's new voice. The chamber-flanked by columns etched with star maps of coreworld triumphs and hung with the silver-on-black banners of the [[Imperium]]-is colder than usual today. Not in temperature, but in mood.

At precisely the scheduled moment, the doors at the rear of the atrium part, and Zephara Val'Riin enters.

She does not rush, nor acknowledge the cameras or the assembled press. She simply moves to the podium, her obsidian robes and silver-threaded mantle flowing behind her, the [[Sar Spots]] on her neck and arms like static constellations. Her metallic silver eyes sweep across the room once, not to meet gazes-only to ensure they are watching.

"This will be brief, and sufficient."

"The situation in the [[:starwars:Karthakk sector/Legends|Karthakk Sector]] continues to evolve-under control, but not yet concluded. Following the escape of several high-priority detainees from the [[The Oubliette|Station 1138]], a former Empire prison station that remained off the books for decades, the Imperium has conducted a series of targeted recovery and neutralization operations across three sectors and multiple system clusters."

"To date, seven of the escapees have been recaptured. They were found either attempting flight beyond the rim or sheltering among ideological allies. In each case, judgment was rendered on-site, by Imperium mandate, with full authorization from Inquisitor [[Axel Vichten|Vichten]] himself."

She speaks this with no inflection, as though she were reporting on a weather change. "These actions were not executions in the dramatic sense some of you may wish to imply. They were the completion of sentences already earned. Let us be clear-justice does not hesitate, nor does it beg for spectacle."

"Of particular note: the Imperium acknowledges the [[Caspian Union|Caspian Union's]] [[Caspian 4th Fleet]] for their essential cooperation in the Karthakk operations. Their timely interdiction of subspace traffic in the Nemares Corridor and the coordination of siege actions in the Durellian Expanse directly led to the successful recovery of several targets."

A subtle pause, enough to suggest there is meaning behind what she isn't saying. "Inquisitor Vichten recognizes the Caspian 4th Fleet's professionalism and discretion in matters which, as you are aware, require both."

"That said-[[Lorn Rhys]], who calls himself Darth Malus, remains at large. This is known. It is not unexpected. He has evaded capture before, and it is true that he understands the subterranean architecture of our galaxy in ways few others do. But evasion is not escape. It is delay. And delays end."

"We are not 'searching.' We are watching. Closing. Containing. His story will not have an ending. It will have a deletion."

She leans slightly forward, voice now as quiet as it is final. "As for the rest: no operational disclosures will be offered. The Imperium does not govern through transparency. It governs through stability. The people do not require explanation. They require confidence. And confidence, I am here to provide."

"You may report that order is being reasserted. That enemies of the Imperium face consequences without spectacle, without delay. And that we do not count the time it takes to deliver justice-only the fact that it arrives."

She offers no invitation for questions, no closing pleasantries. She turns and exits, as silently as she arrived.

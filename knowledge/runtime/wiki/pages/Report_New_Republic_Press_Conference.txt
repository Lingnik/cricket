The [[New Republic]] press conference was held in the main conference room of the capital building. The room was filled with members of the press, all eager to hear the latest news from the government. The press secretary, a human woman named Karen Mitchell, stood at the podium, her face serious and her voice steady as she began the conference.

"Ladies and gentlemen of the press, I am here today to deliver some important news regarding the actions of [[Colonel]] [[Han'bel Eckhart|Eckhart]] and the crew of the [[DSV Templar|Templar]]. I regret to inform you that Colonel Eckhart and his crew have gone rogue from the New Republic and are no longer acting on behalf of the government."

Karen paused, looking out at the sea of faces in front of her. She could see the shock and confusion on many of them. She knew that this was going to be a difficult message to deliver, but she had to do it.

"The New Republic considers the crew of the Templar to be criminals, led by a highly decorated Colonel Eckhart. They are not acting on behalf of the republic and are to be considered armed and highly dangerous. We urge all citizens to stay away from the crew and report any sightings of them to the New Republic authorities."

Karen could see the press scribbling notes and snapping pictures as she continued. "I want to make it clear that the New Republic does not condone the actions of Colonel Eckhart and his crew. They have betrayed their oaths to the republic and will be held accountable for their actions. We are currently working with other organizations to locate and capture them, and we will bring them to justice."

The press secretary took a deep breath before concluding, "The New Republic is committed to ensuring the safety and security of all of its citizens. We will not tolerate rogue groups acting outside of the law, and we will do everything in our power to bring Colonel Eckhart and his crew to justice. We ask for your cooperation and understanding as we work through this difficult situation."

As the press conference came to a close, Karen Mitchell stepped away from the podium and took a deep breath. She knew that this was going to be a difficult situation to manage, but she was determined to do everything in her power to protect the citizens of the New Republic.

The press secretary was bombarded with questions but she only answered a few, refusing to give out more information and instead directing the journalists to the Republic's official statement. She made sure to reiterate that the crew of the Templar is highly dangerous and should be approached with caution.

With that, the press conference was over, and Karen Mitchell returned to her office, where she began working on the next steps of the Republic's plan to capture the rogue crew of the Templar.

In a nightly segment relayed throughout the galaxy, a prominent figure from one of the outer rim worlds is being interviewed by a political segment expert. This is Pwam Non with special guest Yiv Welk from a Outer Rim Trade Alliance.

"So Welk, you contend that current affairs in the galaxy are troubling? How can that be, for the first time in decades there is a galaxy wide movement to bring civil treatment to the masses? The near human Yiv adjust his seating and seems to be rather advanced in years. I do indeed Mr Non, all of this looks and sounds good. But lets talk about history, I recall not to long ago a galaxy level issue rose when a group of systems chose to succeed from the Republic. The Senate and Chancellor saw fit to create an army to keep these systems from becoming sovereign powers. The Senate passed motions that granted the Chancellor powers, the result created the Empire and now a state of ongoing war. 

Pwam Non places a hand at his cheek and nods for the man to go on. Now...the senate has passed legislation that gives the military the power to invade sovereign systems and all they need do is create question under this new legislation and the chief of state signing into law this new legislation. All do apologies and I dearly support an end to slaving, but this is Palpatine in a manner of speaking. The head of state granting the military the ability to invade sovereign systems regardless of local laws or cultural identity. The interview develops into a debate about freedom of culture and the rights of a sovereign power.

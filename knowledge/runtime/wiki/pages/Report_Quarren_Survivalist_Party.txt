Dejir Walta provides an update on the recent Coral City attack on the evening news. The caption beneath him reads: [An Organized Revolt?]

"Just hours ago, the Mon Calamari Council released an official statement on the recent Coral City attack. The statement provided clearer information on the Quarren who attacked the capital city, and also formally recognized them by the name that they have given themselves: The Quarren Survivalist Party."

The image shifts to show a heated gathering of the Mon Calamari Council. At the center of the council chambers are three Quarren warriors, guarded on either side by Mon Calamari Knights wielding energy lances. Walta continues speaking over the video, his voice riding easily over the smattering dialogue in Quaranesse and Calamarian. "New Republic Marines captured some of the Quarren warriors involved in the attack. These warriors were welcomed by the Council, where they declared the name of their organization, and their stance: demanding the imminent survival of their species, using whatever means necessary. The gathering quickly degraded into bickering and anger, at which point the Quarren prisoners were escorted back to their holding facility."

The video flashes back to the IGN studio. "According to the council, the Quarren Survivalist Party has declared an anti-New Republic stance. The captured prisoners have even demanded that their citizenship as New Republic citizens be revoked, and are calling themselves citizens of the Party. As many of you know, under N.R. law, any citizen may file a formal rescission of citizenship for any reason, thus forcing the Council to grant this wish. More on this as the story develops. Concerned citizens are encouraged to direct their questions to the Office of Internal Affairs on Ord Mantell."

The evening news continues.

A special report is broadcast into New Republic space by IGN's New Republic correspondent, Dejir Walta. The male Twi'lek appears standing on a balcony overlooking the bustling Coral Reef City starport at Dac, and a graphic below him reads: "Special Report: Tight-Lipped Refugee Movements"

"Hello, I'm Dejir Walta, reporting from Coral Reef City on Dac. Behind me, you can see that a rather involved refugee operation is under way." The Twi'lek motions, and another camera zooms in to show a handful of refugees being ushered into a bulk cruiser by several uniformed men and women. "Operation Care Giver is the title given to this operation," continues Walta, "But strangely enough, New Republic officials are being extremely tight-lipped about the operation."

The camera switches to show one of the O.C.G. officials in an interview. "We're doing everything we can to help move these Coruscanti refugees to new homes and give them fresh opportunities, but security is a concern. We're not going to divulge any information at this time, in the interests of protecting these refugee convoys from Imperial strike groups or pirates."

The camera switches back to Walta. "The moment IGN becomes aware of any more formalities, we will be sure to report them to you."

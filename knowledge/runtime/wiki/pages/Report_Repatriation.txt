{{IGNpost|author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[29 ABY]]|topic=Caspian Reunification|anchors=[[Brackett Benduday]]|headline=Repatriation|synopsis=Coverage of the passage of the [[Caspian Repatriation Act (29 ABY)|Caspian Repatriation Act]].}}

A tall golden-skinned female, economist Professor [[Brackett Benduday]], can be seen sitting behind an anchor's desk in a studio.

"This is Brackett Benduday with a special update on today's news out of [[Caspar]]. Earlier during the Muun Money Minute were discussing what [[Prex (CSA)|Prex]] [[Zeak Oppenhiemer|Oppenhiemer's]] announcement on [[Kichnar Orbital Station|Kichnar]] might mean for the [[Caspian Sector|Caspian Sector's]] economy but it appears the game has changed yet again.

A short while ago we were informed of the passage by the [[Union Council]] of what they are calling the [[Caspian Repatriation Act (29 ABY)|Caspian Repatriation Act]]. According to the act both [[Kasputin Yar]] and [[Rauther]] have reunited with Caspar, and Councils for each world participated in the voting subsequent to the approval of the article that provided for their representation on the council. The return of Kasputin Yar had been somewhat of an open secret amongst the upper levels of the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]], but the news that Rauther was also rejoining has caught many off guard.

The [[CDU Nonaggression Treaty|Nonaggression Treaty]] with the old [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] has been terminated and the development of [[Imperium-Caspian Mutual Protection Pact (29 ABY)|protection pact]] with the Imperium referenced. Kichnar and its heavy industry has been nationalised, voiding much of Prex Oppenhiemer's previous announcement. The certain accommodations to the Griffons were nevertheless included in the act.

The governments on the former Caspian worlds of [[Krittain Major]] and [[Krittain Minor|Minor]], [[Ixin]], and [[Mergansar]] were given renewed recognition under this act but the recognition of Mergansar had a major caveat...."

The screen then turns to show images of a convergence of ships surrounding Kichnar Orbital Station. After a few moments the screen switches to showing an even bigger mass of ships around Mergansar.

The camera pans back to Professor Benduday, "he final article of the Caspian Repatriation Act levied a fine of 30 trillion credits against Mergansar for the forced exodus of its non-[[Sarian]] population while Caspar was in the grips of the Sariphage pandemic, 100,000 credits per refugee. The planet is now under interdiction. In spite of the fact that the Krit Union has possession of a significant portion of the old [[Caspian Navy|CDU Navy]], with Ixin holding a handful of ships as well, and a few in the hands of Mergansar already, this fleet deployment is in fact larger than what the [[Caspian Democratic Union|CDU]] employed to maintain a quarantine around Caspar during the Sariphage pandemic. It appears that medical supplies will be allowed through under inspection, but all other trade has been ordered ceased. We will have more on this story as it develops."

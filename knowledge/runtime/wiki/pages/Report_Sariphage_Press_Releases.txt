== Caspar Press Release ==
An emergency press release is issued from [[Plaxton]] on [[Caspar]], an elderly doctor sort stands before a podium. "I am Professor [[Daln Bael]], head of the [[Groton Sciences Institute]]." It's clearly a closed room and not open to planetary press. "It is my duty to inform those watching, that a state of medical emergency has been declared planet wide, a undefined affliction has struck cities here. In official response to this crisis a limited planetary interdiction has been enacted. No offworld travel is allowed, any vessels choosing to land will not be blocked but advised that landing will be a one way trip." he looks down at a prepared document. "To those with families vacationing here, please bear with us as we try to detail who offworld has been afflicted and an emergency contact link will be provided for those seeking information." his tone is almost fatherly. "Diplomatic channels are open to the those agencies of a more official nature and will be routed directly to my office. As more information becomes available, it will be made public." the flimsy is set aside and he looks up as if to address anyone viewing. "Again, there is a limited interdiction...if you do not have official need to be here, do not approach the planet."

The image fades and contact information floats across the screen for those seeking information about those that may have traveled to the planet for vacation or trade affairs.
== Griffon Charity ==
[[InterGalactic News|IGN]] - [[Kichnar Orbital Station]]

A old man, in a [[Griffon Alliance|Griffon]] Colonel's uniform steps to a podium baring the seal of the long defunct Griffon Alliance. Behind him sit a number of well known former Griffons, including [[Zeak Oppenhiemer]]. He immediately begins reading from a prepared script. "Thank you for coming. As you can see from your press packets, I'm Colonel Japolon Zetracks." The old man begins. "Fifteen years ago, the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] was kind enough to take us few thousand Griffons in who could not accept being annexed by a corrupt and inefficient [[New Republic]] Government. Today it is time we returned the favor. Because of our recent financial successes," the man makes a conscious nod to Oppenhiemer, "the Griffons have returned to the stars. Once refugees we now run a shipping conglomerate, Caspar Unlimited, that stretches throughout the slice.

"With our partners in the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector]]," the man pauses before chuckling and looking back at the Griffons behind him and breaks script, "who would have ever imagined I'd say that? What a difference fifteen years makes." Then turning back to the press he continues, "With our Corporate Partners we will be bringing in aide to Caspar in her time of need. Ships filled with medical supplies, including mobile hospital units and droids will be arriving starting tomorrow. We will also be bringing in food as well as other comfort supplies. Using automated dropships we will get these resources to where they are most needed. Finally, if you have something you need sent to Caspar, send it to us at the Station -- The Griffon Alliance on Kichnar Orbital Station -- along with who it need to get to, and where they are, and we will do our best get it where it needs to go. Thank you."

With that he puts down his notes and the people on the Dias, file out.
== GSI Release ==
Doctor Bael appears again on a holo trans and stands before a now well recognized podium, he shuffles a set of clear flimsies and speak. "I will be brief, first of all let me offer sincere thanks to those who have assisted Caspar." he offers a nod to the viewers. "Supplies and relief efforts have made their way here and been dispersed to those who need it. It is with a profound sense of sadness that I must report the affliction is still spreading, I will not bother with details." he changes flimsies. "Appropriate data has been forwarded to proper channels to keep the medical community aware as things develop." he looks left and right as if confirming with the other professionals standing off to the side. "Again, our thanks and our regrets to those with visiting family. We cannot allow anyone offworld and we do not foresee that changing anytime in the near future...thank you." the last view of the man is hardened eyes, resolute to the challenges before him, even as the holo fades.
== Caspar Warning ==
All across the galaxy [[:starwars:HoloNet|holonet]] stations tune into the latest statement released from Caspar, the image of the now familiar face of Doctor Bael appears. He is haggard and clad in a hazmat suit minus the helmet. "This warning goes out to the entire galaxy, we have named the illness SPP-Nil. It attacks the very genetic code of our species and it is potentially fatal to any other sentient human, near human species but can be treated if caught early." he shuffles a datapad. "On the better side of this plague, we have noted that simpler lifeforms seem immune. This leads us to believe that SPP-Nil, is a natural occurring condition that somehow relates to the specifics of the [[Sarian]] genetic code." he taps another few times. "We do not think we will have time to confirm our findings, but the illness started in closed communities of the Sarian people." he looks left and right as he makes the announcement.
          
"The surviving elements of Caspian military forces have standing orders to shoot down any vessel trying to leave Caspar and we the acting elements of the government, instruct the spaceborn elements of the Caspian military to prevent any landings, contact at this stage would be catastrophic. Let me be clear, contact with any sentient of this world could result in a fatal outbreak of SPP-Nil." he pauses and clears his throat. "The scientific community is urged to monitor this world, track the population as it dwindles. Only and only, when all traces of sentient life are absent. Then and only then, should a research team risk contact with the world." he pauses yet again. "It is the collective opinion of GSI, that SPP-Nil will burn itself out once the last of us fall and that is the only way to be sure it doesn't spread and that the scientific community wait a reasonable length of time before landing." that said the image fades out and the news casters seem unable to give closing remarks.

== CSA Press Conference by Zeak Oppenhiemer ==
IGN - Kichnar Orbital Station

The mood is grim as Zeak Oppenhiemer steps up to a podium with the seal of the Corporate Sector Authority on it. His face might even be more bland and grim than usual.

"Members of the press, and through you, gentle beings." He begins before pausing before tapping off his prepared remarks and going off script. "This -- this is just nonsense. Obviously I'm not in charge of the Caspian Democratic Union, but as a member of civilization, an investor in this system, and a long time resident who has been sheltered here, I have to wonder if everyone in the government has lost their mind. We in the Authority have no interest in ruling this system, we wish to invest and profit from a mutually beneficial relationship, but I have no intention respecting a bunch of morons who have shoved their heads in the sand simply because a majority elected them. To that end, because it is the right thing to do, the Corporate Sector Authority security forces, in conjunction with the Research and Development division has launched operation Crystalline Blanket. As I speak we are landing forces and technical elements to accomplish two primary objectives."

"One -- we will be evacuating all Corporate Sector Authority employees and their extended families to quarantine vessels where we can give them the best care possible, if needed, in isolation. Two -- we will be providing medical assistance to the extent we are able, including treating the sick. We obviously can not treat everyone, but we can do something."

"To this end, I call on the rest of the civilized galaxy to help. If you can, send medical ships and doctors. If you can't send people, send droids, food, blankets, medical supplies. If you are worried about the disease, you can drop the cargo in space outside KOS and we'll pick it up. The pathetic government of Caspar may have been too incompetent to ask for help from the rest of us, but that no longer maters. The government has chosen to die and I for one feel no need to obey dead men. Those of you in the CDU listening -- let me give you some advice, you should give serious thought to changing your government, because right now every option you've ever said no to looks better than these winners."

Zeak pauses, regaining his composure before he continues. "For those of you who are curious, CDU military commanders in the field have wisely and graciously chosen to ignore their orders to let us carry out this mission. This is not a free-for-all or a stand down however, Corporate Sector Authority forces will be helping them maintain the quarantine. Real help will be allowed through. Interlopers and nonsense will not be tolerated by either group. Please forward all questions to my press office -- thank you."

The assembled begin shouting out questions, but Zeak ignores them as he walks away.
== Ghost Ship at Bonadan ==
IGN - [[Bonadan]]

Corporate Sector Authority security forces confirm a "ghost" ship arrived at Bonadan today. The vessel had jumped from the Caspian sector better the recent outbreak and arrived on the outskirts of the system on autopilot. When the vessel was unable to respond to attempts to contact it, CSA security forces in vacuum suits boarded the vessel, following standard hazardous situation protocols. The office of Associate [[Viceprex (CSA)|VicePrex]] Gildonicopan, the administrator of the Bonadan precinct of CSA security forces, has confirmed that there is boo risk st this time to the population, and that the vessel and its contents, including remains, are being held under quarantine.

== Caspar Open Broadcast ==
IGN interrupts normal broadcasts with breaking news

The lithe form of Basca Starker stands in the middle of what appears to be a very well geared lab, he is clad in a hazardous environment skinsuit. Behind him lab techs can be noted working at various stations and the man is addressing whomever is watching as the transmission is open broadcasted to any in system that may be listening.

"Are we broadcasting?" The man looks off to the side for a moment and then nods and turns back to the viewer."To any that may be listening, it is with a great deal of pleasure that I can now confidently announce that my team and I have had a significant break through in dealing with the [[Sariphage]]." he shifts to the side slightly to gestures to a droid that seems to be manufacturing a serum in the background."We anticipate dispensing the serum in the next twenty four hours, to that end. We respectfully request any military vessel despite its state of origin to fully enforce a planetary blockade. This will allow us time to get the serum out and insurance that nothing comes in and risks another strain of the Phage emerging. My team and I have isolated the uninfected and are now dealing with the pockets of ill but still living. Now is a most critical time for this world and isolation until my team and I can give an all clear is a must!" he gives the viewer a nod and the screens goes blank.

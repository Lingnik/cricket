{{IGNpost|author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[34 ABY]]|topic=Advertisement|anchors=None|headline=Secret Sidious|synopsis=Frustrated about being excluded from accusations of having been responsible for the unrest on [[Chandrila]], [[Biscuit Baron]] CEO Baroness [[Atsvara Tarasar]] channels her frustration into a new promotional campaign.}}

The screen shows the image a [[Biscuit Baron]] location in [[Hanna City]] on [[Chandrila]] and captioned as such though nobody can be seen around it. The camera than pans out to show a golden-skinned [[Atsvara Tarasar|girl]] wearing a formfitting gown black cyrene silk gown, with a large bantha embroidered in gold under her bust line. "Do you ever find yourself in a secret conspiracy to undermine the political stability of a planet to only find your efforts go unrecognised?" The caption on the screen then adds a line in parenthesis under the original caption indicating the image is not really of Chandrila. The image of the Biscuit Baron locations flutters a bit indicating it is in fact a matte painting.

A bronze skinned Human woman in her late twenties rides up on a swoop bike and asks the girl, "You are involved in a secret conspiracy to undermine the political stability of a planet?" The girl giggles and answers, "No silly I am talking about playing Secret [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Sidious]]." She then winks at the camera.

"You too can join the conspiracy. Biscuit Baron is pleased to announce we have partnered with the makers of the popular party game Secret Sidious for our latest Jolly Meal promotion. Later this month whenever you purchase a [[:starwars:Jolly Meal|Jolly Meal]] OR an [[Infinite Stack]] from Biscuit Baron you will get one randomly chosen special edition Secret Sidious player cards. Will you get to be a [[Jedi Order|Jedi]] Master? A [[Senator]]? A [[Sith Order|Sith]] Lord?

In addition to all the cards all the normal cards you know and love we are releasing exactly 2,000 Sidious cards. Those lucky enough to get one of these can register it at their local Biscuit Baron location and receive a prize of 500 credits and be entered into a drawing for a grand prize. At the end of the promotion one very lucky Sidious will win a two week trip for themselves and an apprentice to any planet in the galaxy with a Biscuit Baron and get to appear in a future Biscuit Baron commercial filmed there. First class travel and hotel fees, site seeing tours, and of course food included.

So if you have got a session of Secret Sidious planned for your game night, why not pre-game with a stop at Biscuit Baron." Various legal disclaimers are then shown in small print on the screen. Mostly relating to the possibility of the planet of your choice if you win the grand prize possibly being limited by the possibility of visa restrictions.

Transcript distributed via [[New Republic Senate]] holofeed, re-broadcast on [[InterGalactic News|IG News]] following [[The Cutting Edge]] segment.

"To the citizens of the [[New Republic|Republic]],

It has come to my attention that a recent broadcast from IG News' The Cutting Edge, while characteristically flamboyant, included deeply irresponsible speculation regarding [[Chandrila|Chandrila's]] internal affairs and my recent statements before the Senate.

Let me be clear:

Chandrila remains a proud and committed member of the New Republic. We are not in talks, formal or informal, with representatives of the [[Hutt]] syndicates, the [[Corporate Sector Authority]], nor any agents or intermediaries of the [[Imperium]].

My comments regarding the current state of Republic leadership and the growing disorder across our member worlds stem from a sincere concern for unity and the urgent need for decisive action. They should not be misinterpreted as signals of secession or duplicity.

The Republic was founded on transparency, justice, and cooperation. I will continue to press for those ideals, loudly if necessary, but I will not allow conspiracy theorists, opportunistic broadcasters, or power blocs with questionable motives to twist my words into threats or fabrications.

Chandrila will not be swayed by fear, bribery, or manufactured crisis.

We remain, firmly and faithfully, within the Republic. I will, however, say the same thing here as I did during a closed senate hearing on the matter. Enough platitudes. My planet's shipyards are locked in a standstill, my constituents are being harassed by both protestors and Republic investigators, and not one of you has offered a solution beyond 'deliberation.' That unless this body delivers a unified enforcement policy within seven standard days, Chandrila will consider withdrawing from all Republic commitments until further notice. Financial, military, diplomatic. All of it.

Senator Vel Tarnas

Office of Chandrila, New Republic Senate"

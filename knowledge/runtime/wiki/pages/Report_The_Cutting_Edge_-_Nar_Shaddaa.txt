[[Nar Shaddaa]] - Today, renowned reporter [[Thurston Unger|Thurston]] visited Nar Shaddaa to conduct an exclusive interview with [[Nasiri Zithreej|Nasiri Gellaji]], one of the leading powers on the smuggler's moon. The interview took place in a sprawling garden, where a small tea table was set with bottles of wine and finger foods.

Thurston greeted Nasiri with a broad smile, the cameras already rolling holofilm. "Mr. Gejalli, it is an honor," Thurston said. "I appreciate you seeing me personally. I'm to understand that you are one of the movers and shakers here on what is colloquially known as the Smuggler's Moon."

The reporter jumped straight into the interview, asking Nasiri about the special qualities of Nar Shaddaa. Nasiri replied, "What makes Nar Shaddaa special... interesting question... true free trade, we don't have customs nagging you about cargo or wringing petty coin out of your pocket for every little thing? Cesspool, scum, filth... every world has it, without exception. Some just hide it better than others."

When asked about his own rise to power, Nasiri replied, "I have been in the trade business for a long time, and I have a good reputation. Nar Shaddaa is a place where reputation matters. People come to me because they know I will get the job done, and they know I can protect their goods."

Thurston then asked Nasiri about the rumors of him being a puppet or a tool of the [[Hutt]] syndicates. "I am an [[independent]] businessman," Nasiri replied with a smile. "I have good relationships with many of the Hutt syndicates, but I am not controlled by them. I make my own decisions and run my own operations."

The interview ended with Thurston thanking Nasiri for his time and wishing him continued success. The cameras captured Nasiri taking another sip of wine and settling back in his seat as the reporter and his crew left the garden. The interview shed new light on the workings of Nar Shaddaa and the influence of the Hutt syndicates, providing a unique perspective on the smuggler's moon.

[[Thurston Unger|Thurston]] stands in front of a holographic background of stars, dressed in a sleek black suit with a silver and purple striped tie. He holds a microphone in one hand, gesturing animatedly with the other as he addresses the camera with a broad smile.

"Darlings, welcome to another edition of [[The Cutting Edge]], your go-to source for hard-hitting, in-depth coverage of the state of the galaxy. I'm your host, the one and only Thurston Unger, and I've got an exciting new series in store for you all.

As you know, the galaxy is in a state of flux, with the [[New Republic]], the [[Imperium]], and the [[Caspian Union]] all vying for power and influence. And let's not forget the [[independent]] planets and systems who are trying to navigate this ever-changing landscape.

That's why, over the next few weeks, I'll be taking you on a journey to the heart of these major powers, as well as some of the lesser-known players. I'll be talking to politicians, military leaders, and everyday citizens to get a sense of what's really going on and what the future holds.

We'll start with the New Republic, and the bustling planet of [[Ord Mantell]], the seat of the Republic's government. From there, we'll make our way to [[Selene]], where I'll have a sit-down with agents of the Imperium. And then, it's off to the [[Caspar]], the seat of power in the Caspian Union, who is preparing for their four year anniversary of the diabolic [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|Sariphage]] that took so many innocent lives.

But that's not all. I'll also be visiting independent planets and systems, like the lawless moon of [[Nar Shaddaa]], to see how they're dealing with the challenges of living on the fringes of the galaxy. And, let's not forget [[Bespin]]! [[Cloud City]] is not a place you'll want to miss!

So, join me, my lovelies, as we take a closer look at the state of the galaxy. It's going to be a wild ride, and I can't wait to share it with you. I'm Thurston Unger, and I'll see you on the next episode of The Cutting Edge.

As Thurston speaks, the camera pans out to show him in his fabulous attire He flashes a broad smile and a wink to the camera as the show comes to a close.

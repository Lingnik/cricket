[[InterGalactic News|IGNews]] investigative reporter, [[Thurston Unger]] is on scene in [[New Alderaan]], [[Ord Mantell]]. Dressed for success, the over-the-top reporter is wearing a black silk suit with a subdued paisley pattern with a white shirt and matching spats over expensive black leather shoes.

"I'm reporting today from Ord Mantell, darlings. The Republic has lost one of their own it seems. Who would have thought a government as big and powerful could misplace a starship, a crew, and who knows what else. While the brass refused to take an interview, I've managed to chat it up with a few enlisted who I promised to keep anonymous for fear of repercussions from the higher ups."

"Anyway, the skinny is that during a training exercise, a [[Colonel]] [[Han'bel Eckhart|H.S. Eckhart]] and his crew got fed up and vanished into hyperspace. The Republic has said that the crew of the [[Lightning-class Sloop|Deep Space Vessel]] '[[DSV Templar|Templar]]' has gone rogue, and its actions cannot be deemed as reflecting the Republic and its mission." 

Thurston runs a finger through his golden hair for a moment, and leans in to the camera, turning his head just slightly for a side-eye look. "Colonel Eckhart, I want to hear from you. If you're listening, it can be our little secret if you want to meet. The Cutting Edge wants to know what you're up to. Reach out, darling."

[[Thurston Unger|Thurston]] stood in front of the bustling marketplace on [[Ord Mantell]], microphone in hand. The bright lights of the stalls and vendors illuminated his brightly colored, tailored suit, as he began his report for The Cutting Edge.

"Darlings, the trade lanes between the [[Faylar]] and [[Bright Jewel Cluster|Bright Jewel]] star systems have become a hotbed of piracy, with civilian traders being targeted and left to fend for themselves against ruthless marauders. But what is the New Republic doing to protect these hardworking merchants and their valuable cargo?

"I spoke with local trader, Jarek Galt, who had this to say: '[[New Republic|The Republic]] just doesn't seem to care. They're more interested in their own political dealings than protecting the very people who keep their economy running. We're left to defend ourselves, and it's not enough'."

Thurston then interviewed another trader, Nara Sullust, who added: "I've even heard rumors of some traders reaching out to the crew of the [[DSV Templar|Templar]] for help. It's a desperate move, but with the Republic not stepping up, what choice do we have? The Templar might be our only hope for safety."

Thurston shook his head in disappointment, addressing the camera. "It's a sad state of affairs when the very people the Republic is supposed to protect have to turn to rogue groups for help. The New Republic needs to take action before more innocent lives are lost and more valuable cargo is stolen."

As the camera cut back to the studio, Thurston signed off with his signature catchphrase, "This is Thurston Unger, bringing you the cutting edge of the news."

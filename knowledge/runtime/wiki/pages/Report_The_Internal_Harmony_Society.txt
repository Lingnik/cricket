'''The product of heavy funding, the following commercial has been inundating Corellia's airwaves and the regional holonet. It starts off with an image of the Corellian shipyards, a wide view that captures the true extant of them from one edge of the screen to the other, intended to suggest that more still is unseen. It zooms in rather suddenly, showing in quick succession an image of a Corellian freighter under construction, the image of the workers and robots welding together its hull as well as that of those workers on the sensitive wiring inside.'''

The Five Worlds' economy is tied to the wellbeing of the shipyards of this system. They provide our jobs, they assured our continued galactic relevance and they build that ships that defend us. But some would put all this under risk just to satisfy their political aims. They would have us go starving in order for them to gain power.

'''As the voiceover spoke, the image quickly switched to a scene much less peaceful. The footage that followed, of strikers demonstrating in some of the shipyards and clashing with CorSec was not staged, plenty of stock video remaining of the growing number of strikes throughout the system.'''

Most of the protestors are well-meaning people, and they remain open to negotiations with the lawful government of Corellia. The Corellian Internal Harmony Society was formed to facilitate discussions such as these between the average citizen and the Five Worlds' government. The Corellian Internal Harmony Society respects the desires of the protestors and promises that it will work with them for a better life for all Corellians.

'''The image switched to the Nebula plaza and other parts of Coronet City, the peace of the scene contrasted with the protests. But the impression is suddenly dashed as a large explosion rips through the Nebula Pizza, the grainy security footage ending in static as the blast reaches the camera that caught the beginning of it. The remnants of it are as expected, through the thick smoke bloodied civilians and a desolate landscape.'''

Most of them are well-meaning people, but there are those who would take advantage of the strikes to violently push forward their agenda. Corellians suffer when terrorists such as these are allowed to wreak havoc on our society. Be vigilant against their false promises.

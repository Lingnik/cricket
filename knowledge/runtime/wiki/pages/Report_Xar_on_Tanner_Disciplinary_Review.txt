== Alliance Disciplinary Review ==
=== Lt. Col. Xar'on Tanner, M.D. ===

This board is convened to announce the findings and decisions of the actions of Lt. Col. Xar'on Tanner, on 3 ABY after the Alliance withdrawal from Hoth on the GR-75 transport ''Dutyfree''.

Shortly after the evacuation on 3 ABY, Lt. Col. Tanner forcibly obtained a DH-17 carbine and willfully destroyed an Alliance 21-B medical droid within that ship's medical bay.

Lt. Col. Tanner testified that the 21-B ignored a medical directive in the treatment of Corporal Ethain Duomel (DECEASED), injecting him with medication that caused a severe allergic reaction, resulting in Corporal Duomel's immediate death.

Analysis of the remains of the 21-B's memory core shows that Lt. Col. Tanner did in fact instruct the droid to ignore the treatment stored in its memory banks and to apply an alternate treatment.  The droid did not follow this command, and apparently relied on its programming over Lt. Col. Tanner's expertise.  The droid's action was the direct cause of Corporal Duomel's death.

In light of the memory core evidence, this board drops the charge of willful destruction of Alliance resources, and removes Lt. Col. Tanner of all culpability in the death of Corporal Duomel.  The charge of assault on an Alliance soldier is also dropped at the request of the soldier in question.  However, the charge of unauthorized discharge of a projected energy weapon aboard an Alliance vessel still stand, the punishment to be suspended until a future date.

It is further recommended that Lt. Col. Tanner undergo psychiatric review for this and other incidents occurring after the Hoth withdrawal.

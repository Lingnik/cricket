'''Restoration Day''' was an annual holiday in the [[New Republic]]. It marked the anniversary of the dissolution of the [[Provisional Council]] and the establishment of the [[New Republic Senate]] on [[Coruscant]] in [[5 ABY]] (thus having "restored" the principles of the Old Republic), an event which itself was an immense spectacle.

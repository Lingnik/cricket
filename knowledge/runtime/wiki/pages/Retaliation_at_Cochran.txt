Having been betrayed by the [[Griffon Alliance|Griffons]], the [[Galactic Empire]] planned to crush the Alliance, targetting [[Cochran]] first. The Imperial fleet attacked the planet, but was unable to break through the planetary shields. Despite inflicting massive losses on the Griffon fleet, the Imperials withdrew from the system and prepared to target [[Pride-1]], but were delayed with the advent of the [[Imperial Civil War]].

'''Reyon Mining''' was the name of a mining corporation owned and operated by [[Paris Sieara]]. The company effectively "owned" the world of [[Izin]], birthplace of the [[Jedi]] [[Ezkhil i'Falzin]]. Reyon Mining eventually merged with [[Kuat Drive Yards-Sienar Fleet Systems|KDY-SFS]] as part of a deal made between Ms. Sieara and [[Grand Moff]] [[Scaven Marx]], who sought control over Izin as part of his plan to capture and kill Ezkhil.

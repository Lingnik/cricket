'''Rimsana Beta VII''' was the only life-sustaining world of the Rimsana Beta system. It was a tropical world, lush with jungle and riddled with bluffs and valleys cut by thousands of generations of rainfall. During the [[Galactic Civil War]] it maintained no garrisons or any forms of civilization, and was often used by smugglers as a hide-out.

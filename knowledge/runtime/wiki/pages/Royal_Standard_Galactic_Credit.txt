The '''Royal Galactic Standard Credit''' is worth 1,000,000 [[Standard Galactic Credit]]s. One chip is a thirty centimeter cylinder of pure [[Rydelium]], intricately engraved up and down along every surface. The unique soft azure glow of the Rydelium makes the Royal Credit impossible to counterfeit, and the gives the cylinder as much intrinsic value as its worth.

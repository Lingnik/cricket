The '''SIL-50''' "Sleep Inducer" is a fairly common weapon for personal protection 
and crowd control. It doesn't look especially threatening... it's appearance 
is almost toy-like, maybe even comical, thanks to its somewhat bulbous sonic 
emitter. Though the sonic blast that this weapon emits is more than capable 
of disabling most sentients (when set to the proper frequency, of course) in
as little time as two seconds. Though the effect isn't lethal, it's not 
exactly pleasant... especially seeing as certain settings can cause splitting
headaches or extreme nausea. A mounting bracket is fitted to the top of the 
SIL-50's frame, allowing various special sights to be attached, though given
this weapon's exceedingly short maximum range of about 15 metres, most such 
additions would be of highly questionable usefulness.

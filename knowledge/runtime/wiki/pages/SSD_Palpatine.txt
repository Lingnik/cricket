An [[Executor-class Star Dreadnought|''Executor''-class Star Dreadnought]] produced after the Battle of Endor ([[7 ABY]]), '''HIMS''' '''''Palpatine''''' was constructed in addition to the four remaining dreadnoughts already in service to serve as [[Bacharan Valak]]'s flagship.

''Palpatine'' was assigned to the [[Imperial 1st Fleet]] and made the Emperor's personal commandship. ''Palpatine'' would be at the forefront of Valak's campaign across the galaxy initiated in 8 ABY, but with the [[Third Battle of Coruscant|capture of Coruscant]] in [[9 ABY]], the ''Palpatine'' fell more into a reserve role, either lurking about [[Dreven]] or [[Selene]].

''Palpatine'' would be called to the front lines one more time under Valak's rule, to lead the campaign in the [[Imperial Civil War]]. It underwent a name change, however, to HIMS ''Invidious'', and served as the flagship for the Imperial Navy throughout the war.

After Valak's abdication, the dreadnought fell into the hands of Emperor [[Kendal Osbourne]], who changed the name to HIMS ''Dark Eminence'', and chose it as his flagship. ''Dark Eminence'' would lead the Imperial fleet at the [[Battle of Kappa]] and later at the [[Battle of Bothawui]], where it received heavy damage.

''Dark Eminence'' met its end during the [[War of the Throne]] in [[12 ABY]]. The dreadnought was hijacked by [[Lorn Rhys]] and his troops and rammed into Selene, where it was destroyed along with most of Selene.

It would not take long for ''Dark Eminence'' to be replaced, however, by the equally powerful [[SSD Malevolence|HIMS ''Malevolence'']].

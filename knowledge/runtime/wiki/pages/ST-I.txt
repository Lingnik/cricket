Even the most unfamiliar with weapons will probably find something about the 
shape of this blaster rifle vaguely familiar, for this is perhaps the most 
ubiquitous weapon of the Rebellion. The [[E-11]] and its clone, the '''Stormtrooper One''' invariably became favourite infantry weapons of both sides of the war. 
Instantly recognized throughout the galaxy, many fear the carriers of this 
gun. Bluntly put, this is the definitive weapon of the Imperial Stormtroopers.
In this case, SoroSuub took the original BlasTech design, and produced their 
own frame to supply the vast [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] forces. While BlasTech did initially 
protest, the two competing companies now cooperate to produce this variant, 
the ST-I, which uses a SoroSuubian charge compressor to achieve much greater
effectiveness.  In the post-Endor era, SoruSuub produced this weapon for the New Republic as the 'Freedom One', while Imperial Arms assumed production of the Stormtrooper One.

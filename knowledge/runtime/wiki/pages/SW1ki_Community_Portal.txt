==Rules, Policies, Administrators and More==
Let's have an open discussion regarding standard policies and rules for the SW1ki, and set things into stone for good, to prevent future problems that have arisen lately. I'd like to see as many players from the MUSH as possible contributing ideas and so forth; don't be afraid to speak your mind.

Here are some general ideas I have so far...
*First off, have someone from the MUSH Administration itself be placed as an Administrator on the Wiki, to oversee the Wiki and ensure abuse does not occur, and to generally make sure everything is going according to the MUSH's policies as well.
*Have members from the MUSH Roleplaying Administration take part in certain affairs on the Wiki, such as ensuring all content placed on the Wiki is in line with the IC canon of the MUSH and to ensure story abuses do not occur (such as a player writing a false, unapproved background for his character that would normally be unallowed.. like a player saying his character is a Jedi when such a thing was never approved, and so on and so forth.
*Implenting standard templates and formats for articles and ensuring all pages on the Wiki match the critea. For instance, I'm of the mindset all articles should be written in past tense - Star Wars did take place a long time ago.
*Other general rules, such as no non-Star Wars material and as little as non-MUSH specific material as possible (should be directed to Wookieepedia), and no other material deemed unacceptable.
*We also need a standard timetable and more contribution for stuff such as the Improvement Drive and Featured Articles; they come and go every few months and not many vote. Same with the "Did You Know" stuff.
*More to come...  --[[User:Danik Kreldin|Danik Kreldin]] 03:44, 30 August 2006 (UTC)

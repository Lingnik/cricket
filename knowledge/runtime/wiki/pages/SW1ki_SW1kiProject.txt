A '''SW1kiProject''' is a collection of pages devoted to the management of a specific family of information within the [[SW1ki|Star Wars MUSH Wiki]].  It is not a place to write encyclopedia articles, but a resource to help coordinate and organize article writing.

A current list of SW1kiProjects can be found in the [[:Category:SW1kiProjects|SW1ikiProjects category]].

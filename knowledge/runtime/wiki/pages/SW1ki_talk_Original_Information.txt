I originally put forth this idea at [[Help_talk:Citing_Sources]].

How much freedom should people have to just make stuff up?  The wiki is a somewhat authoritative source of information for the MUSH now, and I'm a little wary of allowing people unguided freedom to create whatever they want as far as background information goes.

There's the case of the Nideran Accords article (which has been deleted), where somebody decided to invent a treaty between Black Sun, the CSA and Tatooine that (as far as I know) never existed except on the wiki and in the mind of the author.

There's nothing in our current policies about requiring people to cite RPA approval or actual RP.  To some degree that's fine, but we need to figure out when it's okay and when it isn't. -- [[User:Xerxes|Xerxes]] 19:43, 24 July 2006 (UTC)

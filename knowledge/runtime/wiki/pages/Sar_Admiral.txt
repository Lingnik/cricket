'''Sar Admiral''' is the highest rank in the military of the [[Caspian Union]]. It is both a rank and position with the holder recognised as Commander-in-Chief of Caspian Forces. Though it is a naval based title in nature, it can be granted to officers from the marine or starfighter corps. Unlike during the days of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], only one person is appointed as Sar Admiral with the new Caspian Union. Any surviving Sar Admirals from the past would continue to be addressed until title of Sar Admiral, including if ever reactivated to service. In such a scenario, however, any additional Sar Admirals would be subordinate to the current officer holder. 

[[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] is the current Sar Admiral and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defence. Though these titles are combined with him personally they remain distinct titles that could be held by different unique individuals. Currently one former Sar Admiral has had their commission reactivated [[Union Council|Councillor]] Odie Hood who commands '''Task Group Resplendent''' of the '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]'''.

Some of the functions that previous rested on the five Sar Admirals collectively have been extended down to the next tier of the military rank structure, the [[Union Marines|Marine Corps]] Field Marshals, [[Caspian Navy|Naval]] [[Fleet Admiral|Fleet Admirals]], and [[Caspian Starfighter Corps|Starfighter Corps]] Star Marshal. More administrative functions are largely held by the Union Council and its ministries. Mostly notably the Ministry of Domestic Affairs and Defence, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Defence, the Ministry of Industry and Labour, and Ministry of Science and Technology.

== History ==
Sar Admiral was a high officer's rank exclusive to the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], used for five of their highest ranking naval officers. These five were of higher rank than a typical [[Admiral]], and would form the core of the Union's [[The Admiralty|Admiralty]]. Usually, only one or two of the admirals would be "field deployable", and the other three would remain in a centralised administrative function. They would then form the essence of a military tribunal for administrative or judicial decisions, although a common Admiral, [[Commodore]], or unbiased [[Captain]] could be employed for an urgent, ad-hoc tribunal.

The Sar Admirals in the field would more than likely command a major fleet, such as the '''[[CDU Home Fleet]]''' or one based out of [[Krittain]].

''Sar'' means ''star'' or ''origin'' or ''premier'' in the [[Sarian (language)|Sarian]] language.

'''Satine Anatinae''' ([[13 ABY]] - ) is a [[Sarian]] artist and politician from the city of Anphora on [[Mergansar]]. When the [[Caspian Union]] [[Report:Mergani Subjugation Posts|blockaded]] Mergansar, officially, to force it to compensate the victims of the [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]], unofficially to encourage the Mergani to overthrow their government an rejoin the Union, Satine became active in the protest movement as graffiti artist. After political representation was restored, Satine was elected to represent it in the [[Union Council]]. At age of sixteen, she became the youngest member of the Union Council, taking this accolade from [[Sha Dark]], who had been elected at nineteen. She also serves as the Deputy Leader of [[Holicet]].

== Early Life ==

Satine Anatinae was born in the artistic community of Anphora on the planet [[Mergansar]], thirteen years after the Battle of Yavin. Her mother was a [[Caspian Rangers|Caspian Ranger]] while her father was a sculptor. Growing up Satine felt the pull of the very different professional trajectories of her parents, though she would find herself more drawn to the arts than to arms.

She was fourteen when the [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]] happened. While her family did not participate in the pogroms, they did approve of them. This initially influenced Satine to support them as well, but when she finally witnessed the violence of them herself and saw the causalities that occurred in the removal of the non-Sarians she spoke out against the pogroms. This led to estrangement from her parents and she would run away from home. After this, she was more or less living on the streets, though she also benefited from the support of others who had opposed the pogroms as well.

During this time period she would gain a certain level of infamy in Anphora for tagging buildings with graffiti critical of the new Mergani regime. At first the scale of her efforts were small enough that the authorities placed little effort into finding out who was responsible. Satine was not the only one to be engaged in quiet resistance either, but the government felt its position was secure enough.

== Blockade ==

After the government of [[Crestian Tarasar]] came to power on [[Caspar]], and the [[Caspian Navy]] blockaded Mergansar, defiance to Mergani government became more open. Supply shortages led to rioting, though it did not lead to the uprising Tarasar hoped for. The tables began to turn after [[Sar Admiral]] [[Odie Hood]] was smuggled back to the planet and started assassinating the local officials in Ithaca.

Following Hood's liberation Ithaca, and what was then the presumed death of [[Basca Starker]], Satine engaged in her biggest [[Report:Mergani Subjugation Posts#Edifying Edifice|art project]] yet. She covered the entire wall of one side of the Anphora Forum in a grafitti mural. On one side were various shadowy figures clearly meant to represent the [[Olumi Osahn]]. Some of the figures were clearly allusions to specific figures including the assassinated leadership of Ithaca, Basca Starker, and [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Aleister Vadim|Vadim]] himself. On the other side was a massive sun hauled in a chariot driven by a representation of Sar Admiral Hood. The sun burned the figure of Starker while clearly putting others in its path while Hood threw arrows like lightning bolts at others.

[[File:Satine Anatinae 2.jpg|right|thumb]] The mural made Satine a legend. Not only was the art considered epic, but the fact she had somehow managed to get the entire thing up without authorities stopping her. Though it would be sometime yet before Anphora was fully liberated, from that point on the cult-backed regime could not operate in all areas of the city safely. Anyone who tried to paint over the mural as quickly attacked, and eventually such efforts ceased altogether. The mural stands to this day.

== Politics ==

As a city of artists, when the time came to elected someone to represent the city in the [[Union Council]], the people looked first to the artists. Though Satine was only sixteen, that happened to be age the Caspian constitution allowed one to vote or run for office. As the city's most high profile protest artist, people were supporting her candidacy before she had even given the idea any thought. In the face of such enthusiasm she decided to run, and was formally nominated by [[Holicet]] to be their candidate.

Though none of the other parties formally endorsed her, [[Praecet]] declined to run a candidate. The [[Legacy]] party and [[Lijsttrekker]] only provided token opposition. Satine easily beat the [[Ulmesar]] candidate and the scattered votes that went to independent candidates. Her election, along with that of Evlyn Decker of Goshen, raised the number of Holicet councillors to five, with two on [[Krittain Major]] and one on [[Charros IV]].

With Holicet now having five seats on the Union Council, none of them on Caspar itself, party leaders Eire Piaphon and Clemitt Acklay resigned to allow the party's membership to elect new leaders from the party's sitting councillors. Evlyn Decker and Satine Anatinae were elected as leader and deputy leader respectively.

As Holicet is a member of the governing coalition along with Praecet, Legacy, and Lijsttrekker, Satine and her party cooperate closely with other parties on domestic policies. However, as all votes are considered free votes by the coalition, Holicet councillors are free to vote against the other parties on matters of foreign affairs and defence. While Satine is considered less dovish than most of Holicet, she is a strong critic of the [[Imperium-Caspian Mutual Protection Pact (29 ABY)|protection pact]] the Union signed with the [[Imperium]] and considers the Caspian defence budget to be excessive. In spite of these differences, she is respected by here peers as an effective and conscientious legislator.

The '''Scar of Mutanda''' was a catastrophic wound left on the world of [[Mutanda]] after a mysterious force attacked the home of the [[Horansi]]. The "scar" was several million cubic kilometers of land that was utterly destroyed. The [[Corporate Sector Authority]] quarantined the planet as it investigated the disturbance. Nothing conclusive was ever ascertained.

The loss of [[Athaniss]], a planet so close to both [[Selene]] and [[O'paal]], sent shivers up the [[Imperial High Command]]. An [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] fleet was collected at nearby Selene and sent off to Athaniss to reclaim the planet.

Despite the best efforts of the Imperials, the naval forces of [[Palpatine (imposter)|Palpatine]] held their ground and forced the retreat of the Imperials, but not without heavy losses. It was at this battle that [[Danik Kreldin]] earned the nickname ''The Nightmare of Athaniss'', his and the [[Black Stars]]' actions at the battle instilling fear throughout the ranks of the "rebel" navy.

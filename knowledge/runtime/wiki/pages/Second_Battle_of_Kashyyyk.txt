The Second Battle of Kashyyyk took place in late [[10 ABY]] and was the first major military action by the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] against the [[New Republic|Republic]] following a period of consolidation after the successful [[Great Campaign]] of [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Bacharan Valak|Valak]] the previous year.

The Empire was inspired to attack Kashyyyk following a series of domestic riots protesting the New Republic. 

While the Republic fleet promptly withdrew, it was anticipated that ground resistance would prove much more stubborn. Rather than engage in a protracted battle [[Zeak Oppenhiemer]] issued an evacuation advisory by leafleting the area surrounding the [[New Republic GroundOps|Republic military]] base prior to unleashing an orbital bombardment which reduced the base and several surrounding acres of land to molten glass. Oppenhiemer then deployed fuel-air ordinance to deplete the requisite oxygen in a successful bid to prevent the spread of fires.

Once assurances had been obtained that Kashyyyk would leave the Republic for good, the Imperial fleet withdrew from the system.

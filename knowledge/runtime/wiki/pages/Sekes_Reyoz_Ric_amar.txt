There is little known about the mystery that is Sekes, in all honesty. He simply.. Appeared upon Tatooine one day. He is often seen amongst crowds, easy to notice due to his unusual dress and the pigment of his eyes. [[Image:sekes2.JPG|left|thumb|Sekes in his more usual garb.]]There were rumors spread about the galaxy in various areas, saying that he was a mercenary who did bounty work. While other rumors had said that he was a bounty hunter who also did mercenary work.. But no matter, he still does whatever job pays the right amount of credits or has the proper amount of appeal to him.

== Race Issue ==

Sekes often claims that he is [[w:c:starwars:Nagai|Nagai]], however, due to his darker than average (For a Nagai, at least.) skin and the unusual appearance of his eyes,[[Image:sekeseyes.JPG|right|thumb|A close up of Sekes' eyes, with unknown tribal markings painted around them.]] it can be assumed that he is either a Nagai halfbreed with a human, or a half breed Nagai with another race that is compatable with the Nagai genetics.

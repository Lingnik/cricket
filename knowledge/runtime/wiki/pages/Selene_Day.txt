'''Selene Day''' is an annual celebration in the [[Galactic Empire]], started in [[14 ABY]] on orders of [[Warlord]] [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] and [[Grand Moff]] [[Scaven Marx]], who, at the time, was Moff of the [[Selene]] sector.

It is celebrated on the day Selene was finally reconstructed and opened to the public, in the years following its devastation at the [[Battle of Selene]] during the [[War of the Throne]]. Festivities include hunts through the [[Selene Wastelands]] and the [[Rellik Forest]], rifle tournaments, boxing championships, space duels and races, and [[swoop]] races, among more.

It is not to be confused with [[Empire Day]], another annual celebration within the Empire that celebrates the founding of the Galactic Empire in [[19 BBY]].

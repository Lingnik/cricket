== Scenario ==

In [[16 ABY]], [[Serenella Jordain]] set out on a search to find her birth father.  Her mother had never been able to tell her who this was, since she had slept with several men in the month when Serenella was conceived, none of whom stayed around to offer a good father figure anyway.  But Serenella hopes to find the man who can say ''"I am your father"'' - and hopefully her reaction will be a bit better than [[Luke Skywalker|Luke's]].

=== Freedom Fighters of Efavan ===

The following are a small number of the beings who joined the [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic|Rebellion]] under the banner of "The Freedom Fighters of [[:starwars:Efavan|Efavan]]," the resistance group set in the capital city of [[:starwars:Vorzyd V|Vorzyd V]].  The single names are [[:starwars:Vorzydiak|Vorzydiaks]] and the double ones are [[human|humans]].

'''Ador''' - a Vordydiak who went AWOL<br/>
'''Aerith Fenn''' - a computer technician, moved on to the [[Griffon Armada|Griffon Navy]], killed at [[Battle of Pride-1|Pride-1]] in 7 ABY<br/>
'''Daro''' - a gunner, current whereabouts unknown<br/>
'''Jalek Novastar''' - killed in action<br/>
'''Jestro Tenowirth''' - a comm specialist, whereabouts unknown<br/>
'''Kole Wessiri''' - Jalek Wessiri's father, died after the war<br/>
'''Maxx''' - captured, presumed dead<br/>
'''Vip''' - killed in action<br/>

'''Rhade Terrel''' - Recruited the others listed above.  Led a company of [[:starwars:Alliance Special Forces|Special Forces]] at the Battles of [[:starwars:Battle of Endor|Endor]] and [[Battle of Coruscant|Coruscant]]. Then he was promoted, and transferred over to the Marine Supply Logistics Unit.<br/>

'''Dex Trawno''' - a possible candidate for Sel's father... he's a sleazy club owner that has kept Lucinda under his thumb (and under him in bed, off and on) for years<br/>

== Behind The Scenes ==

This is a current, ongoing [[Tiny Plot|TP]], organized by [[Inanna Jordain|Inanna's]] player for Serenella.  The nature of the plot is of a personal nature, but it will also involve travelling around the galaxy, speaking with different people and uncovering clues.  The primary goal is to generate RP... and this can extend to other players as appropriate.

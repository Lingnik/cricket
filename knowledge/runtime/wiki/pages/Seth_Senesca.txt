'''Seth Senesca''' is one of two younger twin brothers to [[Kyle Senesca]] of [[Orpenhalz III]]. He and his twin brother, [[Timothy Senesca|Timothy]], were born in [[8 BBY]].

His life would be changed dramatically in [[14 ABY]], when the Empire would invade their homeworld looking for a rebel cell leader. Seth and Timothy were part of a few hundred citizens who resisted a [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] re-education, and were going to be shipped off by slavers to an Imperial hard labor facility. Along with a handful of friends, they managed to escape from the slavers when a fight broke out between the slavers and the Imperials. Little did they know the slavers were undercover New Republic agents, assisted by their own older brother, sent to rescue them.

The more technologically-savvy of the two, Seth had spent much of his childhood tinkering with speeders at the local swoop shop and starships at the small starport. This came in handy when he fashioned a bomb to blow up one of the Empire's speeders, serving as a distraction to get away. Seth and Timothy, along with their bedraggled runaways, were eventually rescued by mole agents left behind by the local Brak Sector rebellion, and brought to their brother on [[Coruscant]].

After assisting in the evacuation of the [[Basilica]], Seth would eventually take up a role serving on board the ''[[Refrain of Anshalar]]'' with the [[Jedi Order]] in [[15 ABY]].

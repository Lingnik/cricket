'''Seven Shipping''' was once [[Bothan Federation Trading]] and is the shipping company created by the merger between [[Vry'lya Enterprises]] and [[Starlight Entertainment]]. It hires pilots, technicians and security to escort legitimate cargo across the galaxy. The fleet has seven freighters it leases to enterprising individuals and offers cargo inventory exclusively to the ships pilots.

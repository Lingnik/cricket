Seyyid ('''say-EED''') was born on [[Corellia]], the son of two [[Horansi|Gorvan Horansi]] who had been violently exiled from their home system. He has little interest in the affairs of the galaxy's other races, particularly the [[Galactic Civil War]], and even ''less'' interest in dealing with his own people.

He arrived on [[Nar Shaddaa]] shortly after the [[Second Battle of Nar Shaddaa|forced regime change]] in [[15 ABY]] and secured himself a job working at The Side Deck, serving as an enforcer and assistant to the management.

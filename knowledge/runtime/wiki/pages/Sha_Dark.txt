'''Sha Dark''' ([[9 ABY]] - ) is a [[Zeltron]] politician originally from the city of Goshen on [[Mergansar]]. Displaced by the Mergani pogroms she eventually settled in the city of [[Orleanon]] on [[Caspar]] which she represents in the [[Union Council]]. She is a member of the [[Praecet]] party and serves as the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] Minister of Justice and is frequently seen in the press, particularly on matters of intergalactic justice. She concurrently serves as a [[Rear Admiral]] in the [[Caspian Navy]] and commanding officer of ''[[CMS Mergansar]]''.

== Early Life ==

Sha Dark was born in the farming community of Goshen on the planet [[Mergansar]], nine years after the Battle of Yavin. Goshen was only particularly remarkable for being the largest of the various farming towns in the area, with most of its activity centred around its role as a hub of the local agricultural community. Her family was reasonably well off, owning a fifty acre farm. Her father Shaq was also a member of the [[Constable|Union Constabulary]]. Her childhood mainly consisted schooling and helping out on the family farm and was otherwise comfortable but unremarkable.

The course of her life would be drastically altered when the supporters of [[Olumi Osahn]] decided to place blame for the Sariphage pandemic on [[Caspar]] on the non-[[Sarian]] population of the Union and launched a pogrom to rid Mergansar of the aliens on the planet, ultimately forcing 300 million Mergani citizens to flee their homes including the Dark family. On [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] she found Caspian expatriate [[Crestian Tarasar]] recruiting refugees for a movement called [[Casulmis]], its purpose to destroy the Olumi Osahn presence on Mergansar and end the refugee crisis. Sha became an early recruit of this movement and held no reservations and working with veterans of the [[New Sith Order]] in the quest to free her homeworld.

As part of Casulmis, Sha was involved in the attack launched by Crestian and [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] on the cultists operating at the Hebron starport, the centre of the forced deportations. She would later be onboard the ''[[Leviathan]]'' when it was [[Mergansar Incident|attacked]] by the [[Caspian Navy]] and joined it in its flight from [[Caspian Democratic Union|Union]]. When the dust had settled with the end of the pandemic and the end of the CDU, Sha followed Crestian back to Caspar as one of the 100 million Mergani refugees that chose to settled on Mergansar's devastated sister world.

== Councillor of Orleanon ==

[[File:Sha Dark.jpg|right|thumb]] Sha chose to settle in the resort city of [[Orleanon]]. Though the region was home to a great deal of Caspar's agriculture, Sha's farming days were long over. Hardened by her experiences, she became a leader within the local refugee community. In these early months of Caspar's recovery Sha vocally pushed to ensure the new [[Caspian Union|Union]] would not fall prey to internal enemies the way its predecessor had and would hold those on Mergansar responsible for their crimes against their fellow citizens.

When her old friend Crestian Tarasar formed a political party called Praecet to contested the upcoming election for a permanent [[Union Council]] he tapped Sha as its candidate for Orleanon. The elections dates between different constituencies had been staggered, with additional security precautions adopted following a terrorist attack at the returns on Kichnar. For a brief while during the elections in [[Anchorron]] and Orleanon, slicers managed to hack into the election computers to allow votes for [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]] running for fictional Zonk party. When a costumed Palpatine impersonator tried to get into the returns at the Antiquities Museum he was personally punched by Sha before being dragged away by [[Union Marines]]. Sha was ultimately elected Councillor of Orleanon with 50.39% of the vote in a field of four (five) candidates.

Following Crestian's election as [[Presav]], he appointed Sha as Minister of Justice. One of the more important government positions, this was seen not only as a reward for one of his closest allies, but a symbol of the antipathy the Tarasar administration had for the Mergani government that had allowed its own citizens to be rounded up and deported. She was the youngest member of the Union Council at the age of nineteen upon her election, though sixteen year old [[Satine Anatinae]] of Anphora would be elected following Mergansar's reintegration into the Union. Sha would be one of the Tarasar administration's most prominent members, gaining some notoriety by publicly criticising multiple extradition efforts by the [[New Republic|Republic]] in the press and using her office to extend offers of asylum to high profile individuals who ran afoul the Republic [[New Republic Senate]].

Councillor Dark has also represented the Caspian Union on many occasions abroad and is known to be one of the Union Council's strongest supporters for the [[Imperium-Caspian Mutual Protection Pact (29 ABY)|mutual protection pact]]  with the [[Imperium]]. Sha was also part of the joint Tarasar and Tracer family vacation on the resort world of Spira that served as a cover for a Caspian-Imperium operation to rescue two force-sensistive youth. who had been kidnapped as part of [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] programme and were being held by Trandoshan mercenaries. Sha would be part of the strike team to the compound where the younglings were being held along with Presav Tarasar, [[Moff]] [[Elana Tracer]], [[Commander]] [[Kipran Jawatha]], and Commander [[V1C3]] which successfully rescued the younglings. She had a brief affair with Kipran prior to his relationship with [[Prince|Crown Princess]] [[Sonja Damion]].

[[File:Sha Dark Fleur-de-lis Ball Dress.jpg|left|thumb]] Though her duties as Minister of Justice keep her occupied, Sha takes an active interest in the concerns of her constituents in Orleanon. As farming is one of the key industries of Orleanon and her presents her own background, she is an active participate in any bills concerning agriculture in the Union. Tourism being Orleanon's largest, Sha seeks to promote it on her trips abroad and to take in what cultural offerings are available on such trips, in spite of little background in any cultural pursuits in her youth.

== CMS Mergansar ==

[[File:Sha Dark Uniform 1.jpg|right|thumb]] Like a number of other government ministers she also holds a commission in the [[Caspian Defence Force]]. Owing in part to her status as a veteran of Casulmis and the continuing zeal she has shown towards the role of protecting the Union, Sha was commissioned as a [[Commodore]] in the Caspian Navy and given command of ''[[CMS Mergansar]]''. As [[Sar Admiral]] Crestian Tarasar had opted to give command of each vessel named as after a Union member world to someone who hailed from the planet, the choice of giving command of the ''Mergansar'' to a refugee from the Mergani pogroms was an intentional statement.

As part of the [[Caspian 4th Fleet]], the ''Mergansar'' is usually detached from larger fleet formations though it remains part of Task Group Hood. Serving as the personal flagship of the Minister of Justice, it unofficially can be consider an asset of that ministry with its assignments often reflective of that role.

In [[34 ABY]], after the breach of [[The Oubliette|Spacestation 1138]], an Imperial black site prison in the [[:starwars:Karthakk system|Karthakk System]] nicknamed "The Oubliette," most of the 4th Fleet was deployed throughout Imperium and Independent space chasing down escapees. Commodore Dark and the ''Mergansar'' took the lead in going to the Karthakk System itself and coordinated the fleet's movements in the field in her capacity as Minister of Justice, while Sar Admiral Tarasar retained overall command. For her role in this operation Sha was promoted to [[Rear Admiral]].

== Golden Bantha Group ==

[[File:Sha Dark Black Suit.jpg|left|thumb]] In [[32 ABY]], when [[Atsvara Tarasar]] was recruiting investors for the [[Golden Bantha Group]], Sha was one of the people she sought out. Though older and far less wealthy than most of the biological investors that Atsvara was looking to, Sha was still young enough to fit within the peer group of the children of her father's friends. Atsvara was well aware that since Kipran started dating Sonja Damion after their brief affair, Sha had remained somewhat bitter at the notion that he had traded up and sought to retain ties with his family out of spite. 

With Kipran's brother [[Longinus Van Sen]] as one of the main investors in Golden Bantha Group, Atsvara's invitation to join was appealing. Sha's goverment and military salary alone did not make her particularly wealthy, though she had relatively little in expenses and generally modest tastes. She had, however, been in the position to make some smart investments in Orleanon, that had benefitted from increased tourism. Cashing most of those out allowed her to invest a token amount in the Golden Bantha Group.

Of course, Sha was not recruited for what she could contribute financially, but rather for her connexions. Though not a lawyer herself, her role as Caspian Minister of Justice made her far more useful than most private sector lawyers in being able to navigate the bureaucracy they would be dealing with in their efforts to buy out [[Biscuit Baron]]. To that end Sha serves on the Board of Directors of Golden Bantha Group, and since its buyout of Biscuit Baron that board as well, as General Counsel.

== Miscellany ==

Unlike most Zeltron, Sha has no particularly affinity for hedonistic pursuits and when judged against her own race would be considered rather conservative in her manner of dress and recreational pursuits. Her attitudes and behaviours are far more baseline among the Sarian society she has largely lived within.

'''Shadow Squadron''' was a [[TIE Interceptor]] squadron stationed aboard [[ISD Tyrant|HIMS ''Tyrant'']] during the early days of the [[Galactic Civil War]]. It had a feared reputation for its impressive combat performance and veteran roster, nearly all of whom were aces. It was commanded by [[Danik Kreldin]] from [[:starwars:2 BBY|2 BBY]] until [[10 ABY]], although after Kreldin's transfer to a new station it continued to operate under new leadership.

The Squadron earned favor with Imperial leaders during the [[Battle of Coruscant]] when it successfully escorted [[Imperial Ruling Council]] members from the besieged [[Imperial Palace]] to the safety of the Imperial fleet.

The '''Siege of Trinumvira''', or alternately known as the '''Defense of the Vira Bluffs Yards and Base''', was a protracted, three-month-long attempt by the forces of the [[Galactic Empire]] to oust a mixed, but sizeable force of [[Caspia]]n defenders from their base in the Vira Bluffs and thereby secure control of the [[Caspian Democratic Union|Union's]] capital world of [[Caspar]]. The battle represents the central focal point and essence of the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign]], and is regarded as one of the most grievous, atrocious ground battles in its time, on par with the [[:starwars:Battle of Mygeeto|Battle of Mygeeto]] and the [[:starwars:Battle of Hoth/Legends|Battle of Hoth]].

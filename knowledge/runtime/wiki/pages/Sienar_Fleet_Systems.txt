'''Sienar Fleet Systems''' (owned by the massive company Santhe/Sienar Technologies) is a major starship manufacturer.

Sienar Fleet Systems (SFS), along with [[Kuat Drive Yards]], is a supplier of military vessels for the [[Galactic Empire]]. However, while KDY supplied the Empire's massive [[Star Destroyer]] fleet, SFS supplied the small, yet deadly TIE fighters. Throughout the Empire's rule, SFS produced massive amounts of the craft and also made several TIE variants, along with the [[Interdictor-class Immobilizer 418|Interdictor Cruiser]]. SFS was also accused of constructing Darth Maul's [[:starwars:Sith Infiltrator|Sith Infiltrator]], but Raith Sienar, the CEO of SFS, denied connection to the craft. While he denied connection, there was evidence that the [[Sith]] ship was linked to him and Sienar Design Systems; the design of the command pod later showed up in the TIE fighter series.

SFS, originally Republic Sienar Systems, was founded over 15 millennia before the Battle of Yavin by the wealthy Sienar family. A century before the Battle of Yavin, the Sienars united with the Santhe family, who owned Santhe Security, due to an arranged marriage. Raith was the CEO for many years of SFS, but was killed in an assassination.

Presently, Sienar Fleet Systems takes directives and orders from the [[Kuat Drive Yards-Sienar Fleet Systems|KDY-SFS Unified Board of Production]] on [[Selene]]. A substantial portion of its administrative and logistical structure had to be relocated after the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor|Battle of Endor]] to planets closer to the Core, since SFS's headquarters and homeworld of [[:starwars:Lianna|Lianna]] fell under [[New Republic]] jurisdiction.

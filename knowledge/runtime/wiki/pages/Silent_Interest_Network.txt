'''Silent Interest Network''', or '''SIN''', was a criminal organization founded by [[Krackor Reincus]] and [[Ewan Dain]] and operated under the front of the legal organization [[Com-Cor]]. SIN dealt in many avenues of crime such as information-gathering, smuggling and bounty hunting.

Over the years, SIN served many different purposes as different individuals and factions took control of the network. For a time it served both the [[Neo-Imperial Movement]] as well as the [[New Sith Order]].

More recently, Krackor has regained control of SIN and has returned it to it's roots as a smuggling and information-gathering network headquartered in the old Hutt palace on [[Tatooine]].

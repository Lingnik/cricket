Born to Groland and Joliee Bardak, '''Silus Bardak''' grew up on the planet [[Tatooine]]. Against his parents' will, he would sneak off and hang outside of Mos Eisley's nefarious Cantina, listening in on conversations by traders and smugglers inside. Growing up, that's what he wanted to do &mdash; be a pilot and possibly a trader. Working small jobs here and there as a teenager, he was saving up for his ultimate purchase &ndash; his own freighter. He's travelled from planet to planet looking for bigger, better-paying jobs.

His current travels have brought him to the planet [[Nar Shaddaa]] where he came in contact with [[Ewan Dain]]. Silus' hunger for trouble and dangerous encounters with authority led him away from the smuggling business and into the world of pirating for a little bit. His ultimate offer was finalized when he was asked to become manager of the Skyline Tavern and Casino, where he still resides today.

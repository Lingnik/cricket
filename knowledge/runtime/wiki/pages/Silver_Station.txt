== Overview ==
Silver Station is a shadowport and gas mining facility on the edge of the [[Dragonflower Nebula]] in the Doldur Sector of the Mid Rim. Since [[14 ABY]] it has been operated by [[Crestian Tarasar]] as his base of operations. His title in the court of the [[New Sith Order]], "[[Duke]] of Dragonflower," is in reference to his base of operations here. In spite of his return to [[Caspar]] and and his election as [[Presav]], Silver Station continues to be operated by [[House Dragonflower]] as a personal fiefdom.

== Background ==
The station is four hundred metres in length consisting of a central cube surrounded by many interconnected cylinders. It has a permanent population of six hundred with roughly fifty visitors at any given time. It began its life under the name Machenry Station, built to mine gas from the Dragonflower Nebula. It was eventually abandoned and rediscovered during the Imperial Era by a rebel operative named Una Poot who rechristened it Silver Station and operated a cell from there. The station was eventually discovered by the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] who took over the station, abandoning themselves a few years later.

A decade later the station would be rediscovered by [[Crestian Tarasar]], who arranged for it to be towed closer to the nebula. He would use it as his base of operations as an independent smuggler and later as a [[New Sith Order|Sith]] operative. After the disappearance of [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]], Crestian would utilise the station as his 'public' base of operations. During this time the station's location while not secret was not advertised either. The station would also serve as a gateway to access his [[Interdictor-class Immobilizer 418|Interdictor]], ''[[Leviathan]]'', which was left hidden in the nebula for a decade. Rumours of ''Leviathan's'' continued operation would persist in the galaxy and indeed crew were regularly exchanged between the ship and station.

'''Sinane Pantara''' ( - [[12 ABY]]) was a [[Human#Corellian|Corellian]] who found her way to [[Tatooine]] during her teenage years. She does not speak much about her past life, especially before she was ten, although she does hint that she was possibly involved in her cousin's smuggling operation. She later became romantically involved with [[Jedi Master]] [[Aurejin]]. However, she soon became corrupted and was transformed into a [[Sith Order|Sith Lady]], '''Darth Pain'''.

For a brief time during the [[War of the Throne]], she declared herself [[Galactic Emperor|Empress]] of the [[Galactic Empire]]. She was eventually toppled by another Sith, [[Lorn Rhys]] going by the name of Darth Malus, and [[Vassily Korolov]]. She maintained a [[Darth Pain's fortress|fortress]] on [[Vadimus (planet)|Vadimus]] during her rise to power, indicating she may have been linked to [[Aleister Vadim]], the powerful ruler of [[Vadimus]].

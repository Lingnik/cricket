'''Sirlahn Inglet''' ([[21 BBY]] - ) is a [[Sluissi]] engineer and politician who represents [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] in the [[Union Council]]. He is the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] Minister of Science and Technology and the Chief Executive Officer of [[SubPro|Sublight Products Corporation]]. As an extension of his role at in the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] defence industry he holds the rank of [[Admiral]] in the [[Caspian Navy]].

== Early Life ==

Sirlahn Inglet was born on [[Sluis Van]] a year after the world seceded from the [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Galactic Republic]] during the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]]. A distant relative of [[:starwars:Khedive|Khedive]] [[:starwars:Candobar Inglet|Candobar Inglet]], he was born to a prosperous family. Though the rise of the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] two years later saw the fortunes of his family diminish, Sirlahn would still benefit from a first rate education and ample opportunities for his latent engineering talents to recognised and nurtured.

In spite of the education and opportunities, Sirlahn also happened to born into a society where engineering talent was plentiful. By the time he was starting his career as an adult at the Sluis Van Shipyards, he began to find opportunities for advancement to be few. Frustrated by the competition he began to be seen as aloof by his fellow engineers, which further hindered his ability to distinguish himself.

His employment at the shipyards would come to be a blessing as he survived the [[Destruction of Sluis Van|destruction]] of his homeworld by [[Death Star III]] in [[9 ABY]]. Like a number of other [[Sluissi]] refugees he would find his way too the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] where he settled in the city of Vanover on [[Kasputin Yar]]. On Kasputin Yar, Sirlahn would find work amongst the [[Yari|Yaries]] at the [[Vanover Yards]] of [[Lokkai Starworks]].

== In the Union ==

At Lokkai Sirlahn worked on assembling [[L-200 Halcyon transport|L-200 Halcyon Transports]] for the [[Caspian Navy]] while tinkering with [[Droid]] designs in his spare time. There were more opportunities for Sirlahn to get noticed at Lokkai, but he once again felt his career prospects were limited so he would eventually move to [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] to work for [[SubPro|Sublight Products Corporation]] on the [[Admiral Roj-class Battleship|Admiral Roj-Class Battleships]].

At SubPro, Sirlahn would climb through the ranks eventually taking on leadership positions. He would come to serve as the Assistant Chief Engineer on the construction of ''[[CMS Resplendent]]'' and Deputy Chief Engineer on the construction of ''[[CMS Renaissance of Innovation]].'' He was appointed Chief Engineer on the construction of ''CMS Archos'' before the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] attack in [[24 ABY]] and subsequent [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] takeover of SubPro saw the project terminated before completion.

With his project shuttered, and work on the [[Caspian Navy|Caspian fleet]] stymied under the domination of the CSA and Empire, Sirlahn once again gained a reputation as an unhappy employee. Finding his work no longer satisfying, he would spend more and more time tinkering on Droid designs, privately working to improve an old [[:starwars:Baktoid Combat Automata/Legends|Baktoid Combat Automata]] design, the [[:starwars:E4 baron droid|E4 Baron Droid]].

== New Opportunities ==

The Sariphage pandemic and Mergani pogroms in [[28 ABY]] would set Sirlahn's career on a new trajectory. In the aftermath of both genocides, Kichnar Orbital Station saw its population double from 5 million to 10 million with the influx of refugees. SubPro's main mission under leadership of [[Prex (CSA)|Prex]] [[Zeak Oppenhiemer]] would be the construction of two new modules for the station to house the influx, New Hammerton and New Spanner. Sirlahn would take the lead on the construction of New Spanner. Working on a project that would come to benefit so many of the station's residents would ultimately raise Sirlahn's profile beyond the station's engineering community.

When the new [[Caspian Union|Caspar Union]] that formed after the genocides was headed towards the election of a permanent [[Union Council]] in [[29 ABY]], [[Crestian Tarasar]] and [[Zaalbacca]] asked Sirlahn to be the candidate for their party [[Praecet]] on Kichnar. Though Sirlahn lacked political experience, Tarasar and Zaalbacca were already planning to repatriate SubPro following the election and were looking ahead for someone who could lead the Caspian technical community.

Kichnar would be the first constituency to have their vote and announce the results, with Sirlahn Inglet the first new Councillor elected. The returns were marred, however, by a terrorist [[RPlog:Election Night Mayhem|attack]]. Sirlahn himself would personally the lives of two of his fellow candidates by deploying an energy shield, he would also debut a quartet of his new Baron Droids to help defeat the terrorists.

== Union Council ==

As the first Councillor elected and sworn in by outgoing [[Presav|Provisional Presav]] [[Nelivve Lameberchain]], Sirlahn had the ceremonial honour of making the nomination of Crestian Tarasar as the new Presav. In the new Tarasar administration Councillor Inglet was made Minister of Science and Technology. Following its [[Caspian Repatriation Act (29 ABY)|repatriation]] and nationalisation, Sirlahn would also be made Chief Executive Officer of SubPro and an [[Admiral]] in the Caspian Navy. Sirlahn now had a monumental task ahead of him, charged with the completion of all unfinished warships as well as the recommissioning and refitting of every Caspian warship that could possibly be brought back into service.

The first order of business was the completion of the abandoned ''CMS Archos'' as the new flagship for the '''[[Caspian 1st Fleet]]''' which in light of the previous year's tragedies was rechristened as ''CMS Requiem.'' Tarasar also ordered that no expense be spared on repairing ''CMS Rapace'' and ''[[CMS Prophet]]'' which had been crippled in his own [[Mergansar Incident|battle]] with the Caspian Navy the year before and had been decommissioned. The most extensive project, however, was the rehabilitation of the [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruisers]] all but one of which had been decommissioned. For this project [[Xi Charrian]] engineers were contracted to completely overhaul the class, though the project would occur under the auspices of Lokkai Starworks.

Sirlahn's work would not remain confined to the borders of the Caspian Union. Following a terrorist at the main gas refinery on [[Cloud City]], Sirlahn would personally lead team of SubPro engineers on board the ''CMS Renaissance of Innovation'' to [[Bespin]] to assist in the recovery.

Following the admission of [[Charros IV]] into the Caspian Union and the reformation of [[Haor Chall Engineering]], Sirlahn and SubPro would work closely with the [[Xi Char]] in both new and revamps starfighter designs. In particular SubPro would be heavily involved in the development of the [[Mark IV Vulture-Class Droid Starfighter]] as well as assisting in the improving the [[:starwars:Ion encumbrance system|Ion Encumbrance System]] of the [[Guardian-Class Starfighter]]. Sirlahn would also partner with Haor Chall Engineering to advance his work on the Baron Droid design, ultimately resulting in the [[E5 Baron Droid]].

'''Sith Battle Droids''' were a long forgotten element from the days of the Old Republic, based off one of the many designs of [[:starwars:Sentinel droid|War Droids]]. These droids were used by the Republic, the Sith, and many other military forces until the creation of the [[:starwars:Grand Army of the Republic/Legends|Grand Army of the Republic]]. After the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]], the use of massed droids was largely outlawed or forgotten until much later into the revisited Galactic Civil War when [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] summoned their service once again.

== Assignment ==
The [[1st Sith Droid Legion]] is the primary and special operations force of the [[New Sith Order]]; although there are 9 additional legions which are a reserve force that can be called upon when the need would arise.  There is no public knowledge yet of the existence of the droids, aside from certain members of intelligence communities, and the [[Jedi]].

==Combat==
Programmed for use in many different styles of combat, they are every bit as effective as the most trained of [[Imperial Army|Imperial Ground Forces]].

===Equipment===
Standard issue, the droids wield ST-II blaster rifles, although it is not unusual to see that change depending on the situations where they may be deployed. In a sense, they don't have armor, but merely an exoskeletal plating that protects all internal equipment and systems although the exact specifications are simply rumor to all but the high ranking members of the Order.

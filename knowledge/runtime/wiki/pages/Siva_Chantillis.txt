'''Siva Chantillis''' ([[7 BBY]] - ) is an Executive Officer of [[Rendili StarDrive]].

==Appearance==
He stands at just 1.79 meters and could be considered handsome for a human. His figure is slender and fit, considering that he doesn't eat very often. His skin has a very mellow, gentle tanned tint to it and bears the mark of many hard days under the Corellian sun. His hair is shaggy brown, somewhat sunbleached hair and comes down to his eyes , giving him a rakish look. On the subject of his eyes, they bear sharp contrast to his slightly earthy hue. His eyes are bright hazel, bordering on dirty yellow.

==Background==
Siva comes from a family that was employed by some of the best droid factories on Corellia. In his youth, he picked up their tricks of the trade and even became quite the young prodigy himself. However, he fell in with a crowd that taught him other tricks that weren't sanctioned in any way by factory owners. For lack of a better word, he was 'employed' by a gang in Corsec called the 'Corellian Longshanks'.
 
The gang was very martial in their assembly. There were the soldiers, the leaders, the runners, and the thinkers. Siva was a thinker. To them, he was 'Lynx', their repairman and part-time slicer. Siva picked up very many techniques and tools of his trade while he ran with the 'Shanks', but all of that came to an end when the Empire suddenly put out a bounty on every member of the gang. Hundreds of gangs in Corsec alone, and the Longshanks were hunted like animals. One by one, they died horrible deaths. Even Siva was made to suffer as he was chased to a turbocar platform and shoved onto the tracks. He was thought to have been killed when the car roared into the station seconds later, however, Siva knew that the sewer systems ran just under the turbocar systems. He swam through filth unimaginable and to this day, could never forget the stench. He drifted from planet to planet from there... and tried to take himself out of the Imperial eye.

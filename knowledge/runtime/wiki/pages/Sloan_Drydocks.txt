The '''Sloan Drydocks''' are the largest shipbuilding facility in [[Caspian Union]] space. It was the brainchild of [[SubPro|Sublight Products Corporation]] Chief Executive Officer [[R'hakye]]. SubPro had taken a controlling interest in the [[Victoria Drydocks]], but with planned expansion at [[Kichnar Orbital Station]], R'hakye began to think bigger. With the financial protection offered by the [[Crest Commission Consolidation Charter]], R'hakye felt confident in having SubPro take out the loans necessary to construct the Sloan Drydocks.

The drydocks themselves are large enough to several large starships at a time, and has a trio of slipways that can each house a warship as large as an [[Imperator-class Star Destroyer]]. As a module of Kichnar Orbital Station, the Sloan Drydocks is situated at the far end of the station, closer to the planet [[Kichnar]] than the rest of the station. Three decades later it remains SubPro's principle production facility and corporate headquarters.

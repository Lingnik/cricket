This is the official catalog of '''Sniklit's Droids'''. The owner, Mr. [[Wibble Sniklit]], has made it his personal responsiblity to provide the best quality droids money can buy. Once a selection is made, customers are linked to various outlets and the professional sales and management staff that are there to assist with the purchase.

Sniklit's Official Slogan is "''Making your life work better''".

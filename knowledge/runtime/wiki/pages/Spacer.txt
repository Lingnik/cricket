'''Spacer''' was a slang term that referred to those who made their living flying across space, including pilots and crew. Most spacers ran freight, charter or information collection operations, although many worked or moonlighted as [[mercenary|mercenaries]], [[bounty hunter]]s and [[smuggling|smuggler]]s. Some were [[Hyperspace]] explorers.

[[Han Solo]] and [[Chewbacca]] were a typical example of spacers.

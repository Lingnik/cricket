Allows a Jedi to interface with a computer (just like Projected Telepathy). The user may only gather information and accomplish tasks someone with his clearance normally could. Computers are stubborn and won!t give into a mere mortal on this level. A Judge may allow user to use Con, though, to convince the computer that he has higher access.

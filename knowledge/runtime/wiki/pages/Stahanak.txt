'''stahanak''' ([[32 BBY]] - ) is the [[Prelate]] of the [[Xi Char]]. He has been one of the leading scientists of [[Charros IV]] with expertise in the fields of botany, chemistry, and physics. Prior to his election as Prelate he had risen to the rank of Inquisitor within the Xi Char faith and was closely involved in then Prelate [[R'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak|r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak's]] work in developing a new alloy which was posthumously dubbed [[varattakite]].

When Prelate r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak died in [[34 ABY]] the new alloy was made public at the main funerary ceremony. In part because of stahanak's close work with r'ratak on this project he quickly emerged as one of the leading candidates to replace him, though the Deans of the [[Cathedral Factory|Cathedral Factories]] of Xi Can, Xi Dan, and Xi Xin were still considered more likely candidates by outside observers. Ultimately, however, the [[conclave]] held to elect the new Prelate reached consensus on stahanak as the new Prelate on its third day of deliberations.

Following his election stahanak indicated he would lead from the Cathedral Factory of Xi Chall as his predecessor had and announced his intention to run in the by-election for the seat in the [[Union Council]] for Xi Chall under the banner of the [[Lijsttrekker]] party. He won this largely proforma election without any other candidates on the ballot.

During his installation ceremony as Prelate he officially took on the title of Chairman of the Board of [[Haor Chall Engineering]], but declined to assumed the role of Chief Executive Officer, instead having [[Dean]] [[ak]] who had hitherto been Executive Vice President, promoted to the CEO role. stahanak was also commissioned as a [[Rear Admiral]] in the [[Caspian Navy]] in his role as Dean of the Cathedral Factory of Xi Chall.

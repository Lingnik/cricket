{{Speech|name=Starker Outlawed|Author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[29 ABY]]|place=[[Castle Rock Manor]], [[Plaxton]], [[Caspar]]}}

The newest promotional advertisement for Caspar Caff is interrupted just as the new products are about to be announced. The screen is replaced with the Vector Apex symbol of the [[Caspian Union]] before that to fades to display the familiar form of Caspian [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] sitting behind a desk at [[Castle Rock Manor]] in [[Plaxton]].

"A long time ago at a Caspar Caff not far away [[Prex (CSA)|Prex]] [[Zeak Oppenhiemer]] met with a medical scientist named [[Basca Starker]]. With the Sariphage raging the Prex attempted convince Doctor Starker to employ his skills in the situation. The doctor was unmoved. He seemingly was not want for skill nor afraid he could not be involved safely, he was talking the Prex of the [[Corporate Sector Authority]], compensation would not have been a problem. Simply put 3 billion people dying did not interest him. 3 billion people carrying and he did not care.

Thankfully Caspar had more powerful friends. As the Office of the Auditor General already announced recently Doctor Starker had a contract where Lady [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]], [[Jedi Knight]], sister of Imperator [[Tyler Damion|Damion]] paid him with information on the Jedi. You might wonder why she would give sensitive data to the sort of man I describe. That was the price of to end our world's death sentence. Her efforts are a debt we could never repay and indeed to could not appropriately make known though if you ever wondered why the Mission built a facility in [[Trinumvira]] called [[Twin Suns Hospital]], that would be why.

The people of [[Caspar]] deserved the truth about Starker but there were other considerations. Between the pandemic and the genocide on [[Mergansar]] the last thing anyone needed was the kick in genitals of knowing they owned their survival to such a mercenary figure, a hero was better to the national psyche and such his efforts were lauded and we eventually named a street for him and gave him an island. More over as our medical community had been unable to cure the Sariphage before it was itself decimated by it, we could ill afford to offend someone we might need the services of again. I never forgave nor forget his behaviour but I maintained it as a necessary state secret.

This was not a secret I bore alone. It was a necessary deception but it was just as vital that our government never be in the position of accidentally trusting such a man. Every candidate that ran for my party was briefed on him as has every person elected as a [[Union Council|Councillor]] regardless of party. Whatever difference we have in policy and politics we all remain united and steadfast in our commitment to the safety of Caspar.

What I never could have imagined was the revelations that occurred at the recent [[Imperium]] court proceedings on [[Bespin]] that I just returned from. Doctor Basca Starker admitted to having himself developed Sariphage at the behest of the false Emperor [[Aleister Vadim]]. This is where things get a little tricky. The investigations [[Grand Moff]] Johanna Siri te Danaan revealed a Sith Spawn alter ego within Doctor Starker. The pronouncement of the Grand Moff and by extension the court was that it was these Force-based machinations of Vadim's that were at fault and if properly excised would leave some innocent victim behind underneath.

I have no use for this mercy and was frankly prepared to blow his brains out or die in the attempt. While we can not ignore the direction of Vadim in this plot, 2.8 billion died, there is no excuse that can justify that. Where a genocide perpetrated against our people in concerned I stop caring about the sovereignty of our Imperium allies. What stayed my hand was not the authority possessed Lady Johanna and the Imperator, I had 2.8 billions reasons why that bridge could be burned, but rather that they personally have been two of the strongest allies Caspar has had. Quite simply the fact that we have any people at all and are able to rebuild to fight our enemies is because of them.

As the Head of State of an Imperium ally I must begrudgingly respect the legality of adjudication of the situation that occurred on Bespin. That said the Caspian Union is a sovereign political power whose people, through their elected representation have the final word on the genocide against our own people. A bounty on Doctor Starker seemed the most logical response. I spent my trip back to Caspar tallying up votes, how would the Council meeting go if I advocated in favour of a bounty, how would it go if I came out against it. Surprisingly I find myself opposing it. Perhaps the coercion or eventual cure are some mitigating factors but I strongly believe the man deserves to die for what he has done. That said the Caspian people have better things to do with their wealth than pay for revenge.

Instead, after careful consideration, and with the support of the majority of the Union Council, I have outlawed Basca Starker. Now let me be perfectly clear what I mean by this. He is not charged with breaking a law. Rather he has lost all protection of the law. His honourary citizenship on Caspar is revoked, his property seized, and his rights as a sentient being are as far as the Caspian government is concerned null and void. He is an abomination and he shall be treated as such. Should he ever be found in Caspian territory he may be killed as one might swat a fly. No prosecution shall ever be offered to anyone who might harm him as he is legally speaking no longer recognised as a person.

And though the state is not investing in a bounty, as someone who use to teach aesthetics I feel an opportunity was wasted to stick him in carbonite. Monster though he is, he does have an attractive face and might yet serve the Caspian people as an object d'art. So should he show up in a block of carbonite, particularly if it is in an artistic pose, I shall negotiate a purchase in good faith as a private buyer."

The Caspian leader pauses for a few moments allowing the bombshells he just dropped to sink into his audience as he adjusts his collection of datapads on his desk. He takes a sip from a nearby caf mug bearing the Vector Apex before once again speaking.

"I chose last year to resettle on Caspar for two reasons. The obvious one, of course, was to work to rebuild our society in the wake of what we can now describe as being two genocides. The other reason was to see our people able to contribute to the fight to bring peace and order back to the galaxy particularly in regards to the evil represented by the false-Emperor Vadim. I had no idea he was responsible for our plight here, but that knowledge only strengthens the resolve for the mission has always been one in opposition to his evil. But, it is also in opposition to our past. We can not afford to repeat the mistakes of past administrations that had the proverbial finger up their arse while the galaxy burned. Neutrality is complicity. Inaction is complicity. Inefficiency is complicity. Caspar will fight the good fight.

In addition to his efforts at genocide Doctor Starker was also involved in the [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] sponsored school for force sensitive younglings. In large measure the younglings and their parents went missing a couple of years ago prior to recruiting meetings that were as I understand it supposed to occur with the [[Jedi Order]]. While this school seemed to operate on paper under the idea that the families were being kept together I received intelligence about a sister and brother having being held on Spira by [[Trandoshan]] raiders. Though the disappeared at the same time as the other children their poor parents remained on [[Ord Mantell]] suffering for two years not knowing where their children were and being taken advantage of by gangsters who fed them on false promises of information as their whereabouts.

The plight of all abducted younglings should concern us but individuals who would take those who are force sensitive are particularly dangerous to the galaxy. Indeed now that we know that COMPNOR and Starker were involved this leaves some very concerning questions. Where the Jedi Order and Republic failed to be able to make headway in this matter we endeavour to forge ahead. A joint Caspian-Imperium clandestine operation led personally by myself and former Presav [[Elana Tracer]] infiltrated the compound on Spira and freed those younglings."

The image of Crestian is then replaced on the screen with a images from the shoreline on a jungle island identified as Iriaz Island on Spira. In the corner of the screen are a graphics that indicate the feed is from the [[InterGalactic News|IGN]] and was taken by the reporter [[Jordaan Mag]]. The first action visible on the screen shows a woman running with a boy in her arms. The caption for this indicates the woman is Elana Tracer, [[Moff]] of [[Selene]] and [[Athaniss]]/Former Presav and the child identified as a ten year old victim. A short while later a young [[Zeltron]] woman is leading out a girl with a [[Sarian]] man protecting them. The caption indicates the Zeltron as [[Sha Dark]], Caspian Minister of Justice/Councillor of [[Orleanon]]; the girl an eleven year old victim; and the Sarian is identified as [[Kipran Jawatha]], a [[Captain]] in the Imperium Navy.

Finally a man in armour seemingly made for Trandoshans emerges with a droid who looks like he might either be a combat or protocol model but not easily identifiable as either. They are soon attacked by two Trandoshans. The droid shoots down one while the other ends up on top of the man with the droid before being stabbed in the throat. As the two finally make their way into closer camera view the caption indicates the droid to be [[V1C3]], a [[Commander]] aboard [[Leviathan]]. Meanwhile the man with him is indicated to be Crestian Tarasar, Presav/[[Sar Admiral]].

The screen returns to displaying Crestian in his office, "A separate operation at the behest of my government brought their parents to Caspar. And we are indebted to those who made that happened." The screen then shifts to images identified as being at the [[Marin Mountain Circuit|Marin Mountain Resort]]. There is the a tearful reunion between younglings in parents. Later there is a shot of several younglings including the two who were rescued playing a game of bolo-ball with a [[ak|Xi Charrian]] also involved. Everyone seems to be having a good time though the juxtaposition of the Xi Charrian trying to play with humanoid children seems humorous.

Again the screen returns to Crestian. "We are reminded this week how important is to stand against the machinations of Aleister Vadim, COMPNOR, and all their agents. Not only for the sentients by the billions, but also for them as individuals like these two poor younglings, once against safely in their parents arms. To that end it was unfortunately necessary to detain and question anyone who had been employed by Basca Starker while on Caspar. This should not be taken as any accusation that any were accessories to his crimes, but given their scope it was necessary to make sure. Everyone has since been released and we anticipate no charges being filed as a result.

Unlike former governments we do not intend to live in fear. You will not see marines flooding the streets to turn Caspar into a military state. We cherish the value of freedom even in the face of these challenges. But nor will we shrink in our resolve to fight back. Our enemies sought to eliminate us from the galaxy and they have failed. And in their failure have forged a people far more willing to take the fight back to them. With our allies we will excise the taint they have levelled on the galaxy and with our all we will work to forge a safer and more just galaxy, for our own people and all peoples."

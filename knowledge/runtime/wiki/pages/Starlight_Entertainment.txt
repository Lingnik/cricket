'''Starlight Entertainment''' is the umbrella corporation for [[Starlight Studios]], [[Starlight Swoops]], [[Galaxy Tours]] and [[Starlight Casinos]]. 

[[Starlight Studios]] was the flagship operation of the company until [[Ewan Dain]] set off to expand it to include all forms of entertainment. It continues to grow under the guidance of a new CEO and partners.

Starlight Entertainment and [[Vry'lya Enterprises]] have merged. [[Corporation 7]] is now the umbrella corporation for all Starlight Entertainment businesses as well as all Vry'lya Enterprises businesses.

'''Sunbird Eyrie''' was the prominent [[swoop]] stadium on [[Selene]] in [[Imperial City (Selene)|Imperial City]], operated by the [[Galactic Empire]] and the [[Galactic Swoop League]]. Many tournaments were held at Sunbird Eyrie.

The stadium was destroyed in [[12 ABY]] during the [[War of the Throne]], and was not rebuilt along with the rest of [[Imperial City]].

The '''''Supernova Siren''''' was a [[Neutron Star-class Bulk Cruiser|''Neutron Star''-class Bulk Cruiser]] owned by the [[Galactic Amusements]] corporation.

It was purchased by [[Alexander Floyd]] in [[11 ABY]] to serve as the mobile headquarters for the corporation, and was captained by [[Kahrak Taiden]].

The ship featured numerous quarters for the company employees and guests, as well as lavish VIP suites and lounges, featuring Galactic Amusement products and showcases. When the company went under in [[12 ABY]], the ''Supernova Siren'' was sold to [[FoxTech Industries]] and was later decommissioned when FoxTech itself closed.

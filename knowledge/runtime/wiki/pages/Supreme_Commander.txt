The title of '''Supreme Commander''' is the highest rank accorded in the [[New Republic Military]].

The [[New Republic Military]] currently consists of three major branches, those being [[New Republic FleetOps|FleetOps]], [[New Republic GroundOps|GroundOps]], [[New Republic StarOps|StarOps]]. However, a liason-type of Council position was required as part of the [[Ministry of Defense]], so that general commands from the [[Chief of State]] (who acted as Commander-in-Chief) could be effectively distributed to the three military branch commanders. It is the Supreme Commander's responsibility to ensure that the Commander-in-Chief's primary objectives are actualized through the New Republic Military.

Originally a militarized jury-rig of the venerable Incom T-47 airspeeder first slapped together by enterprising Rebel Alliance engineers, it has time and again proven the value of close air-support and a key vulnerability to the otherwise nigh-unstoppable Imperial AT-ATs. The T-47 Combat Speeder is now a standardized production model for the New Republic.

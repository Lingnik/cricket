[[User:Lolkje|Lolkje]] here, Arise's player. Because the SW timeline is a bit fuzzy in places, I have made the following assumptions about Arise's birth year, for those who care about such things:

* The Battle of Yavin occurs in "March", establishing the *BY timeline zeropoint, where IC years span from March-March in real time.
* The Battle of Endor occurs in "May" of 4 ABY, the month the film was released and consistent with the sequence in SWINFO CALENDAR4 (Endor is 3rd out of 8 events).
* Adding 9 months to May of 4 ABY gives a birth month of "February" in 4 ABY, approximately a month before 5 ABY begins.
**Sounds good to me! --[[User:Danik Kreldin|Danik Kreldin]] 04:07, 18 March 2006 (UTC)

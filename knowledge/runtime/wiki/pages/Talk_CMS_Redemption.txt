Well, there's a tentative start, which attempts to address the whole issue of wether or not this vessel is the Medical Frigate seen in ESB/ROTJ. My position is that it isn't, as from what I can recall, our Redemption wasn't a medical ship. But there have been a LOT of ships of the Nebulon-class, and given the way that the Rebel Alliance was pieced together, it didn't seem too far fetched to end up with two units of the same name (particularly a name like Redemption). I am not wedded to this particular history - there is certainly room to expand or adapt, and especially better formatting.

The paragraph on 'historical interest' is inspired by the Canadian aircraft carrier HMCS ''Bonaventure''. In real life, ''Bonaventure'' was broken up in 1971, but in subsequent years there have been some oddball theories that Bonaventure was actually transferred to the Indian Navy to become (IIRC) INS ''Vikrant''. This is of course, exceedingly unlikely, but historical hobbyists develop some interesting fixations. ;) --[[User:Mahon|Mahon]] 20:10, 26 February 2007 (UTC)

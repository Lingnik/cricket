== Corellian Nobility ==

Out of Character Information:
Corellian Nobility was a plot orchestrated and run by Joshua Arands sometime in late 2003 to mid 2004. It involved archeologists rebuilding the lineage of the ancient lines of Corellian Nobility and tracing the decendants. Those decendants were granted noble titles. All this was done by having Corellian Characters register for a chance and then random draw determined who would be recognized. Among those characters who were Recognized as nobility:

* Kartijan Lore
* Leo Corack
* Dain Ewan
* Kiare Quinn
* Danik Kreldan

there were likely others, but at this time I can't remember them. So someone should format this properly for the article. I myself am simply tired of people claiming Corellian Nobility wronged their families 20 or 30 years ago ICly.

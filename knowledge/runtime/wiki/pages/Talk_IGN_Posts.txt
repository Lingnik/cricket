== IC'ness of IGN Posts ==
Are the IGN posts to be considered IC / Storyline, or OOC? To give you clarification, character articles are IC, as are articles concerning locations, vehicles, ships, etc. Battle reports are IC. Events are IC. In that aspect, Logs are IC. The only restriction I would place on Character Article IC-ness is that a character's background material, while IC, '''cannot be used''' to approach, research, or gain information regarding that character outside of in-game/RP'd actions. Make sense? With that said, if these IGN posts are IC reports, then this should be categorized in a different location than what you're putting them in initially. -- [[User:Hawke|Hawke / Rtufo]] 04:02, 27 July 2006 (UTC)

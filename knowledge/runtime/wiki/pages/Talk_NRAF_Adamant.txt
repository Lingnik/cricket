Here is a few things I've remembered about the Adamant. From before my time as FH of the New Republic it served as a support ship for the Deliverance I think, but was then transfered to the Bothan Defense forces as a flag for a while. When Peshk Vry'lya came about he took the ship as his flag vessel when he was a Colonel and then became the head of the New Republic Marines. When he shifted to politics and became Chief of State the Adamant's role continued to be as a Marine HQ. Nemar took charge of the marines along with Vik after and it housed Dagger Squadron which became a New Republic Marine logistics squadron from awhile. After that I don't know.

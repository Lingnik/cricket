{{User:Xerxes/sig}} It's the in-universe name of the battle, and the wiki is written from a neutral, in-universe, current timeframe point-of-view.  So, we're not really trying to emulate what people will call things in the future... but what people actually (officially and commonly) call things now.  Personally, I don't like the name either.  I find the 'of' to be nonsensical.  Retribution is not really a synonym for battle, and just converting '''''Battle''' of Coruscant'' to '''''Retribution''' of Coruscant'' doesn't work in my opinion.  The retribution didn't belong to the planet Coruscant.  It was the Empire's retribution inflicted upon the New Republic occuring '''at''' Coruscant.  But, it is what it is. -- [[User:Xerxes|Xerxes]] 01:43, 30 November 2007 (UTC)

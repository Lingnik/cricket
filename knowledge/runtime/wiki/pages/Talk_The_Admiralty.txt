I'm inclined to suggest that the Admiralty itself should refer strictly to the high command of the Navy. This should not be referred to as a 'governing body' as the military as a rule do not govern except under extreme circumstances. If you are looking for an equivelant to a 'Department of the Navy', or a 'Ministry of Defense', might I suggest 'The Admiralty Board' as a name for the civilian portion which handles the actual governance of the Fleet? Also, under normal circumstances, the chain of command of a provincial army would probably terminate with the government of the actual province, until actually released to the Union. -- [[User:Mahon|Mahon]] 20:08, 14 October 2006 (UTC)

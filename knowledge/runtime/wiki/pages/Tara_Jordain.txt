'''Tara Jordain''' ([[:starwars:16_ABY|16 ABY]]&ndash;) is the newly born daughter of [[Serenella Jordain]] and her ex-husband [[Antoine Dareus|Dareus]] whom she lost to the dark side. His only legacy to his daughter is that Tara is Force-sensitive. Doted on by her mother, Aunt [[Inanna_Jordain|Inanna]], the ''[[Angry Rancor]]'' crew and everyone in the Jedi Temple on Ord Mantell, Tara is being spoiled rotten. Her mother is determined that Tara will grow up to become a Jedi Knight and will do all she can to protect her from her father, the Sith and the Dark Side in general.

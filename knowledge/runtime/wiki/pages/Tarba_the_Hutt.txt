'''Tarba the Hutt''' was a [[Hutt]] gangster on [[Nar Shaddaa]] in [[10 ABY]]. He gained possession over the remains of [[Orson Tighe]]'s body, which earned the ire of [[Jessalyn Valios]], who infiltrated his network in order to retrieve Tighe's body.

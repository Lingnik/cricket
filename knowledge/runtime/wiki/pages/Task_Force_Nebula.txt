{{Moff Sekirol Crisis}}

A combined force unit of the [[Galactic Empire]], comprising [[Imperial Navy]] warships, [[Imperial Army]] units, and [[Stormtrooper]] battalions. It was specifically created, with haste, by [[Imperial High Command]] to combat the threat posed by [[Moff]] Jorgen Sekirol in [[14 ABY]]. So immediate was the need that several warships had been reserve units, while others were coming off repairs or refits. Most were operating with partial crews.

With this shortcoming in mind, it is testament to the thoroughness of Imperial training, the efficiency of [[Imperial Navy|Naval]] doctrine, and the tenacity of the crews that the Crisis was suppressed successfully. The crews performed admirably, considering nearly all members were briefed that there would be no replacements, reinforcements, or rest until the threat was eliminated. High Command simply could not spare any more resources to this effort. 

Military Advisor [[Danik Kreldin]] was given command of this Task Force, and would have to direct his charges from the bridge of another warship until his vaunted ''Conqueror'' had finished repairs at [[Kuat]].

After the Crisis was over, Kreldin took several ships back to [[Sullust]] to rejoin with the seven task forces he had left there to hold a suffocating siege and blockade on the Republican world. Task Force Nebula dissolved into the other task forces, and the [[Fourth Battle of Sullust|Invasion of Sullust]] commenced.

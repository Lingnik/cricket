'''Tatooine Bob''' is a popular holo-film character in a series of films produced by [[Starlight Studios]] played by [[Clark Galaxy]]. Titles include Tatooine Bob and the Desert of Gloom" and "Tatooine Bob and the Temple of Sith". Tatooine Bob is also the name used by Galaxy Tours guide and pilot of the [[Starlight Princess]], [[Wes Masco]]. The name Tatooine Bob would also be used as an alias by [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] during the [[Nar Shaddaa Revolution]].

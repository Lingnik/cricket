[[File:The Cutting Edge Poster.webp|right|250px]] '''The Cutting Edge''' is an [[InterGalactic News]] programme hosted by [[Thurston Unger]]. The defining feature of is the flamboyance of its host who is known as much for his fashion and style as he is for reporting. Thurston can loosely be considered an investigative journalist and at time flirts with being a conspiracy theorist. His programme receives high ratings and often manages to earn responses from the powers in the galaxy for his questions and theories, though rarely directly.

In [[32 ABY]] Thurston debuted a segment of his show called the [[Galaxy's Dumbest Crooks]] that has grown in popularity to practically be considered a show in and of itself. In [[34 ABY]] he became the spokesman of [[Crimson Nerf Energy]] with advertisements for the energy drink becoming ubiquitous on The Cutting Edge.

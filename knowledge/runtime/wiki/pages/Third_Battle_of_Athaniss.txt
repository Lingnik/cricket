One last attempt was made by the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] to reclaim the world of [[Athaniss]] from [[Palpatine (imposter)|Palpatine]]. To the Imperial's fortune, the [[New Republic]] had decided to intervene in the [[Imperial Civil War|war]] and [[Second Battle of Mon Calamari (Usurper)|invaded]] Palpatine-held [[Dac]], liberating the planet and diverting the forces of Palpatine.

As a result, the [[Imperial Navy]] was successful in breaking Palpatine's navy, and began landing ground troops. Palpatine's army was driven all the way across the planet, holding up a final defense at the former Imperial garrison.

The "rebels", realizing they had been defeated, detonated a small nuclear device at the garrison, wiping out both the defenders and attackers. Only a few lucky Imperials and "rebels" survived, including Majors [[Wescal Cantrell|Cantrell]] and [[Kaum]].

The remnants of Palpatine's navy withdrew to Mon Calamari to reinforce their sagging defense.

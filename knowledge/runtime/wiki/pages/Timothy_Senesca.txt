'''Timothy Senesca''' is one of two younger twin brothers to [[Kyle Senesca]] of [[Orpenhalz III]]. He and his twin brother, [[Seth Senesca|Seth]], were born in [[8 BBY]].

His life would be changed dramatically in [[14 ABY]], when the Empire would invade their homeworld looking for a rebel cell leader. Seth and Timothy were part of a few hundred citizens who resisted a [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] re-education, and were going to be shipped off by slavers to an Imperial hard labor facility. Along with a handful of friends, they managed to escape from the slavers when a fight broke out between the slavers and the Imperials. Little did they know the slavers were undercover New Republic agents, assisted by their own older brother, sent to rescue them.

Timothy was not as tech-savvy as his twin, but he had a knack for the outdoors. He'd spent much of his younger days hunting in the wilderness and playing local sports. This came in handy when Seth and Timothy, along with their bedraggled runaways, were running from Imperial pursuers after breaking away from their slavers. Timothy helped to get them through the city out of Imperial eyes, until they were rescued by mole agents left behind by the local Brak Sector rebellion, and brought to their brother on [[Coruscant]].

Timothy would eventually go into basic training for the [[New Republic Marine Corps]] in [[14 ABY]]. He now serves as an enlisted member of the [[New Republic 73rd Marine Brigade|73rd Brigade]].

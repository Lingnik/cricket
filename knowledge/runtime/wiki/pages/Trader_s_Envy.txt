'''''Trader's Envy''''' was a [[YT-1300|YT-1300 Transport]] belonging to [[Tyy'sun Eson]]. It was, at one point, subject to a 25,000 credit bounty by the [[Galactic Empire]], for carrying the [[Jedi Order|Jedi]] [[Jedi Knight|Knight]] [[Ai'kani Za'netra]] and Imperial traitor [[Baedan Mirhoz]], among others wanted for attacking Imperial troops.

[[Trandosha]] is the homeworld of the warlike [[Trandoshan]] species. Referred to as Hsskor in [[:starwars:Dosh/Legends|Dosh]], the native language, this planet is located in the [[Kashyyyk]] system along with the [[Wookiee]] homeworld. 

Recently the planet has become the willing home of a cult that has revealed itself to be the [[New Sith Order]].

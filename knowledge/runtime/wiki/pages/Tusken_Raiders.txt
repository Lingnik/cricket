The Sand People of [[Tatooine]], also called the '''Tusken Raiders''', are a nomadic species with violent tendencies.  To protect themselves from the harsh desert environment, Sand People wear heavy robes and strips of cloth, breath masks, and eye protectors.  The very agressive Sand People live in an uneasy truce with Tatooine's moisture farmers. They have been known to attack settlements from time to time. Travelling in small tribes atop their [[:w:c:starwars:Bantha|banthas]], these nomads are experts at desert survival. Their traditional weapon is the gaderffi stick, but they have been known to use primitive blasters, likely taken from moisture farmers or stolen from [[Jawa|Jawas]], from time to time.

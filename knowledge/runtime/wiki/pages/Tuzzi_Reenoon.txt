Tuzzi Renoon is the mistress of [[Merr-Sonn]] CEO [[Gimel Daleth]]. A renowned musician, she is currently taking a hiatus from performing. The two met at a concert at the Spinning Wheel Club in Capital City on [[Etti IV]] and it soon became apparent that the executive was smitten by the exceedingly feminine, talented and capable female.

The relationship with Daleth took the [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] by surprise and the executive-watching paparazzi soon formed a 'Merr-Sonn watch', hounding the CEO and musician mercilessly. When Tuzzi began showing definite signs of pregnancy, she couldn't leave her apartments without someone flashing a holocamera at her. Consequently, Daleth moved her from Etti IV to a country house on [[Caspar]].

During her pregnancy, Tuzzi and Merr-Sonn executive [[Krel Scrinae]] were kidnapped in a high-profile snatch from Plaxton on [[Caspar]]. Held for ransom, Daleth ended up paying an unknown sum (and is rumored to have also divulged weapons technological information) to secure their safe return. The [[Caspian Democratic Union|CDU]] continued investigation of the kidnapping and some time later brought the ringleader to justice.

Soon after her ordeal, Tuzzi gave birth to '''Arjii Renoon-Daleth''', who holds the position of acknowledged bastard son and heir of Gimel Daleth.

==OOC Info==
Tuzzi is an NPC controlled by Gimel Daleth, but initially based on a PC who's player left the MUSH after the story arc was initiated.

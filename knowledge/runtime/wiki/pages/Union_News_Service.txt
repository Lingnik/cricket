'''Union News Service''' (abbv. '''UNS''') is the news and media filtering company for the worlds of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]]. While it can be said that [[InterGalactic News]] was founded upon [[Caspar]] by a [[Caspia]]n, and that the media conglomerate endeavours to be as neutral and unbiased as it can, the possibility does exist for IGN to become a mouthpiece for the larger [[The Galaxy|galactic]] governments. Thus, the Union ratified the creation of a smaller, more concise, and more regionally responsible media outlet for use by Caspian and other Union worlds.

The agency has an operations office on [[Krittain Major]], where it taps into the galactic [[HoloNet]] feed at the [[Perlemian Trade Route]] Gateway. This material is then reprocessed and/or repackaged along with regionally relevant media for distribution to the other Union worlds via the [[SONG]] array.

[[Alistair Al'Dira]] and [[Marl Jace]] are played by Ryan.

Ryan first played on the MUSH sometime around late 2005 early 2006.  It was the first time he ever roleplayed in this type of environment and he absolutely fell in love with it.  Combined with getting suspended from school for 'hacking' their network, Ryan fell off the face of the MUSH because things had gotten pretty tough and he didn't have much time to dedicate towards the game. On July 4th Ryan was suddenly reminded of the MUSH and came back.  Unfortunately since so much time passed his character was purged, but he decided to bring Alistair back. 

Born in Yokosuka Japan, Ryan is currently a 19 year old student at the University of Mary Washington in Fredericksburg Virginia. His interests include writing (obviously), doodling, gaming, and computers in general.  He has been roleplaying for as long as he has had conscious memory, always having liked the idea of pretending he is someone that he can't be. 

He is an absolute jediphile in every sense of the term. Granted, he has not gone as far as purchasing a lightsaber of his own but if he had the money he would in a heartbeat.  Being a student of geography, religion and philosophy, there is nothing about the jedi and sith that he doesn't completely adore. Of course his knowledge and understanding is something he could not resist carrying over the Alistair as in the first few days of random roleplaying he ended up living with the Jedi.

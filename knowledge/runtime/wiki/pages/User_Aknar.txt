Aknar's player has been playing on SW1 since as far back as 1997 when he was 15 years old. His first character, a pilot within the New Republic lasted for several months. His second character was an Imperial pilot that lasted about 6 months. He then took a break from the MUSH for about a year, only to come back and roll an independent businessman, [[Kael Denson]] who worked as a deputy to [[Matthew Fox]] working for [[StarShield]]. It was there that he got his first taste of MUSH coding & building, helping to build the [[Marin Mountain Circuit|Swoop Arena on Caspar]]. This character became involved with a TP with Imperial Intelligence and managed to get captured. He escaped but was hunted down by agents of [[Lorn Rhys]] and killed. He started a new character, [[Arner Denson]] was the brother of this businessman and joined the [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]]. He became the CEO of [[Merr-Sonn]] helping to build the new moon of [[Justa]]. He played this character for about half a year before losing interest in the MUSH once more. Upon his return, several years later, he rolled [[Aknar Koth'lyri]]. He played Aknar from [[11 ABY]] - [[12 ABY]] and then from [[14 ABY]] - [[15 ABY]], both times taking on the role of the Chief of the [[Bothan Spynet]], which he first organized in the game when helping to build [[Bothawui]] with [[Peshk Vry'lya]], and taking on the role of Director of [[New Republic Intelligence|NRI]]. Coming back to the Mush in [[17 ABY]] he resumed playing Aknar once more and quickly fell into the position of one of the top characters in the [[New Sith Order]].  Aknar's player is now 26.

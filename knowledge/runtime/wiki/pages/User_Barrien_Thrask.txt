Thrask (real name Lange) started role playing on the MUSH in 1997 as a character named [[Ba'el Korr]]. Ba'el quickly grew in rank and became Admiral of the [[Griffon Alliance]] Navy.  He took part in the destruction of the third Death Star,  and helped sign the treaty with the [[New Republic]]. He was the [[Griffon Alliance]] Assistant Faction Head as well as the Faction Head for over two years. After stepping down as faction head Ba'el (Lange) was unable to continue connecting to the MUSH due to real life difficulties.  The character was idle-nuked and it wasn't until a year later that he rejoin the MUSH and created Thrask.

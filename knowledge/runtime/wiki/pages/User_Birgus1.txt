Alas, I have lost my old username/pword, through the sands of time, so have made a new account!  

I am the player of both Ambrosia and K'tyyri on SW1, and have been since 2001.  Thrilled to still be a part of the story telling process here, with fellow Star Wars nerds, even if Real Life has had a knack for taking me away for long periods of time.  

As my profile pic suggests, I also have a love for weird nature, and for some years was primary caregiver to and researcher of these interesting, crabby fellows at Ripley's Aquarium of the Smokies.  I earned my Bachelor Degree in Marine Science several years ago, and have spent most of my time since employed by aquariums and wildlife rehabilitation centers.  Despite a recent career-oriented move that my husband and I underwent for /his/ career, I still cling to life as an animal husbandry professional, by working for a veterinary clinic.  I am missing the undersea world terribly, but would be lying if I said I wasn't enjoying the reduced workload.  

Anyways, given my background in the biological sciences, I enjoy incorporating science where and when my +sheets can back it up, and most definitely enjoy playing non-humanoid species, and frequently npc'd critters of one sort or another on other MU*s.  

You may (or may not) have played with my handiwork in the now-extinct Wes Platt gaming saga of Chiaroscuro, or on other SW mushes/muds, including the also extinct Ways of the Force Mu, and (though I cannot remember the name) one of old Unsung Hero's (it's now StarWars: Rebirth) admin's former creations where I played out the day-to-day adventures of a near human and her pet Grell, roughly 10 years ago.

I look forward to continuing to contribute to Star Wars Mush storyline and enjoying the creativity of other players.  I am always eager for a scene, so if you see me online, feel free to nab.

Enb'Zik is roleplayed by Case. Born in Memphis, Tennessee, in 1975, Case grew up in several states across the southeastern United States of America. This led him to develop a love for travel and the great outdoors.<BR>

Case was first introduced to Star Wars in 1980. After promising a trip to see a James Bond film, his parents found out the show was sold out and told him he had to see The Empire Strikes Back instead. Case has been hooked ever since!<BR>

Case found Star Wars MUSH in 1999 after the release of Episode I: The Phantom Menace. Enb'Zik was originally intended to be a Gungan, in fact. Thank goodness that didn't work out!<BR>

Case is married to Snarl's player, and the couple currently reside in San Antonio, Texas. There, he enjoys a career as an interpreter at a local park.<BR>

Along with Star Wars and traveling, Case's hobbies include hiking, fishing, camping, reading, writing, surfing the web, politics, philosophy and learning, among others! Like Enb'Zik, Case is quite the idealist.<BR>

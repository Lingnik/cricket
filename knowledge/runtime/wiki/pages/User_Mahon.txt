|}'''Mahon''' has been playing on the MUSH since Dec 1997, in one guise or another. He plays [[Thomas Mahon|Mahon]]. The character was created to be Commandant of the CDU Marines, in order that one of my other characters could avoid being promoted a bazillion grades overnight.

Upon Salonika stepping down as org head, Tracer tapped me to be AOH. It probably had something to do with an obsessive attention to detail. Eventually the character of Mahon replaced Tracer's as President (via something of a coup d'etat after it became clear that Tracer was being manipulated by the Empire), and now Mahon is the de facto OH (for some reason it just didn't seem right to have Tracer removed from the OH spot... call it sentimentality).

The bulk of Mahon's projects revolve around the fleshing out of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], often in conjunction with [[User:Hawke]]. ElseWiki, he occasionally edits military history articles under the name of '''SpartanCanuck'''.

I'm a gentleman that's lived everywhere from Houston, Texas to Madison, Wisconsin and currently resides somewhere in the US southwest. I hope to continue my study of astronomy and astrophysics this coming spring semeseter, but who knows! I could be hit by a bus or invent the most interesting and amazing device ever to be invented in the meantime!


'''A dude who plays a woman? What?'''

Indeed, I am despite playing a character, Sivytra Nona, who is female. I started playing female characters when I was in my teens as a means to avoid creating [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mary_Sue Marty Stu] characters, which I was prone to do. Several writing workshops and college literary classes later, I continues to RP as women if the concept I'm trying to explore or express fits a female personality best.

Years ago the player had a boring and ill-conceived character in the CDU named [[Sadim Gnik]]. Miserable in the faction the player was a whiny bastard. Eventually the character and player joined the controversial SW1 spinoff Star Wars: New Worlds where he thrived on The Wheel and did a brief stint as a Royal named Silver. When that game ended he led the continuation of the game as Star Wars: Age of Strife where he was the MUSH Director and Theme Wizard and Confederation Faction Head. On Age of Strife Sadim renamed himself to Venelav of Besh Gorgon and had a super fun time. Convinced in 2018 to rejoin SW1 by [[Tyler Damion|Tyler's]] player, he conceived of a character ([[Crestian Tarasar]]) similar to post-SW1 Sadim/Venelav. He later took a beloved long-term multi-game spanning NPC named [[Zubindi Hakoon]] and turned him into a proper alt. Beyond gaming the player lives, works, and volunteers in a small New England town.

Oh lord someone let me have a wizbit. Silver has entered the chat.

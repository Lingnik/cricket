== OOC ==

Tyler's player has been a lifelong fan of Star Wars and has been playing a lot of table top since the mid nineties. He is a graduate student of history and currently resides in Tokyo, Japan.

== On the MUSH ==

Tyler has been playing on Star Wars MUSH since 2000, at least as far as the player can remember.  He has been a contributor to the story from several different angles starting most of his career in the CSA (Danvious and Cernious), Griffon Alliance, and then Bothan story angles before becoming the New Republic Faction Head as Peshk Vry'lya.  Tyler was started as an on again, off again alt for Peshk Vry'lya and then became his main character around 2003 or 2004, iced once to play Luke Skywalker.  Tyler became the Imperial Faction Head and later an Assistant Faction Head before settling currently as an Independent.

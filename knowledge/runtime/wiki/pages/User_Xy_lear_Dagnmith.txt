'''Personality & Background'''

Appearing almost human, he is actually a near human called a Nagaian. The typical ashen eyee, pale flesh and slightly more angular features are the only things that cause him to stand out. Much of his life before entering into the Imperial Academy, remains unknown. He claims no blood relatives and appears disinclinced to share more of his past. Reserved and quiet, he is not much of a talker unless in the mood. Preferring to watch and observe, casting him in an aloof light, but he has been known to share a few drinks with his companions.

'''Current Life''' 

Petty Officer, serving on the Inquisitor in the capacity of bridge security under the command of Captain Nelhrn.

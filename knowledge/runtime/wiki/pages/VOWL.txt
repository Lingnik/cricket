The "Variable Ordnance Warhead Launcher", or '''VOWL''', is an adaptable launch system utilized by the [[Caspian  Navy]].  As an automated mass-driver tied directly to on-board targeting sensors, the VOWL excels as a weapon that can participate in ship-to-ship engagements, installation bombardments, and anti-fighter efforts. Unlike its larger sibling the [[HOWL|Heavy Ordnance Warhead Launcher]], the VOWL requires no crew assistance, fires twice as rapidly, and takes up less internal volume. Its largest warhead, though, is the NROSA Mk. 22 ''Saint'' Ship-to-Ship Weapon.

VOWLs can be found on nearly every Caspian warship unless said ship has a speciality launcher installed. Extensive testing is underway for deploying the launcher on dedicated ground vehicle platforms as well.

Projectiles used in the VOWL include:
*NROSA Mk. 22 Saint SSW
*RSA Mk. 44 [[Hatchet SSW]] 
*StarShield [[Barracuda PsigSeekW]]
*StarShield Slashrat AFW
*CAM [[Proxie PDDW]]
*NovaCom Cascade-II AEW

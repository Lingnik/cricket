'''HIMS ''Selene''''' was a [[Victory-class Star Destroyer|''Victory''-class Star Destroyer]] in the [[Imperial Navy]]'s [[Imperial First Fleet|First Fleet]]. 

Constructed in early [[:starwars:1_ABY|1 ABY]], ''Selene'' was named for the [[Selene|planet]] that would later serve as the capital of the [[Galactic Empire]]. In [[14 ABY]], she would play a pivotal role during the [[Fourth Battle of Sullust]], where she would provide close-air support for the Imperial troops on [[Sullust]]. 

She would later provide close-air support for the Imperial troops on [[Cochran]] during the [[Second Battle of Cochran]], covering the troops as they withdrew from the planet in preparation for the [[Base Delta Zero]] attack.

The warship is still in service with the First Fleet.

'''Vadimus''' is the new name for the homeworld of [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Aleister Vadim]]. It takes it name from Vadim himself and the name of the planet cluster it inhabits, [[Vadimus]]. Vadimus' location is stricken from all official records and is rumored to be completely devoid of civilization.

At one point it was the site of a [[Darth Pain's fortress|fortress]] maintained by [[Sinane Pantara|Darth Pain]]. These ruins were explored by [[Vassily Korolov]] during an expedition aimed at uncovering the treasury of the former Empress.

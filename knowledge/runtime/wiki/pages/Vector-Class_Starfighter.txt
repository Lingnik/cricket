When [['radathak-davasak-dannarak]] first came to [[Caspar]] to represent the [[Cathedral Factory]] and City of Xi Dan in the [[Union Council]], she sought inspiration for a new starfighter design that would be representative of the new Union which her people found themselves. It would be the Union's symbol, the [[:Image:Emblem cdu vectapex.png|Vector Apex]] and the [[Cason Hawk]] upon which it was based, that would ultimately influence her new design.

While this inspiration would be immediately noticeable in the new class' external design, the Cason Hawk would influence its function as well. The class would be designed for planetary service rather than postings on space stations and starships. It would still contain a hyperdrive but one less advanced than those normally employed by [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] starfighters. Maneuverability and armaments would be the driving focus of the design.

Another factor 'radathak-davasak-dannarak considered in the design was the diversity of the Union's population. Even without considering outliers such as her own [[Xi Charrian]] species or the [[Yari|Yaries]], for whom another design was in development, a fighter sized for [[Human]] and [[Sarian]] pilots for example would be ill suited to [[Wookiee|Wookiees]]. Rather than accept this limitation she decided to create two variations of her new design. 

The '''Vector-Class Starfighter''' was built with the size of Humans and Sarians in mind. A scaled up variation, the '''Apex-Class Starfighter''' would accommodate Wookiee pilots and other beings of similar size. The difference in size between the two models would mean some substantive differences, the smaller Vector-Class would have more maneuverability and speed while the larger Apex-Class would have greater firepower. Each would have the same hull and shield strength.

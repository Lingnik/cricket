'''Vector One''' is a [[New Sith Order]] asteroid base, born from a fragmentation of the dead world of Cochran as a result of over mining by Sith droids and slaves. The planet shattered forming a massive asteroid belt around its central star, one of the large chunks became the asteroid base.

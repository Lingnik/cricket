Verst Dejorn is a currently wanted criminal figure known to be the leader of an [[Naom Char Incident|organized prisoner revolt]] situated on Naom Char. He and an organized force of about 80 or so alien prisoners fought off ferociously against an Imperial strike team led by the legendary [[Danik Kreldin]], and after a vicious and bloody firefight, the entire prisoner force was eradicated, not without first causing heavy casualties to the Imperial team. Rumor has it that Dejorn survived the altercation, escaping from the planet in an Imperial shuttle he hijacked, however Imperial officials deny this, despite multiple sightings of the [[Shistavanen]] on several worlds. Little is known about the deranged Shistavanen criminal, other than what is logged in his psychiatric report in the Imperial databanks. 

== Imperial Security File 11-2B-A1: Dejorn, Verst ==
*Name: Verst Dejorn
*Race: [[Shistavanen]]
*Height: 2.12 meters
*Weight: 135 kg
*Date of Birth: Approximately 21 BBY
*Current Status: Presumed Missing, unconfirmed reports of him on several planets as late as 16 ABY.

Profile: Vile, psychopathic, demented...all can be used to describe Verst Dejorn. A being of immense willpower and barbaric strength, getting any information out of him proved to be a challenge for us. Two of my staff were seriously injured, another one killed trying to profile this...demon. From what we learned, little as it may be, we learned that the noted Imperial squadron 'Black Stars' deliberately contributed to the demise of his family during an operation over [[:starwars:Uvena III|Uvena III]]. Driven by a high degree of hatred towards the Empire and revenge against the 'Black Stars', Dejorn offered his rage and hostility to the Empire to the highest bidder, organizing attacks against many different Imperial garrison and interests throughout the Outer Rim. Eventually the [[Imperial Security Bureau|ISB]] managed to get an ID on this dangerous individual, and his apprehension on the smuggler moon [[Nar Shaddaa]] in Operation Quick-Strike proved to be successful. However, his transfer to the prison facilities in Kessel was catastrophically disrupted with the downing of the prisoner transport 'Ardaga IV' on [[Naom Char]]. It has been speculated that sabotage onboard the vessel caused the downing, however investigations to this has proven to be inconclusive. 

Notes: It cannot be confirmed whether or not this being lived through the attack on Naom Char, however it cannot be denied as well. After the incident, Imperial forces scoured the planet but could not find signs of Dejorn. There has been some unconfirmed reports of an Imperial Lambda-class shuttle departing from the system by some passing freighters, however the validity of their accounts cannot be confirmed. Highly dangerous and highly unstable, any beings that are to see this creature are to notify their local Imperial garrison for apprehension.

End of log.

-- Jazia Deril, Commander, Lead Criminal Profiler, Kessel Branch,  Imperial Security Bureau

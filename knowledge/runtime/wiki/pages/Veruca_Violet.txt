'''Veruca Violet''' ([[17 ABY]] - ) is a [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] business woman who serves as the Chief of Operations of the [[Silverwood House of Fashion]] and the [[Golden Bantha Group]]. By extension of the latter role she is also the Chief of Operations of [[Biscuit Baron]]. Veruca is a senior leader within the [[Caspian Intelligence Service]] as well.

== Early Life ==

Veruca Violet Eleria was born in [[Trinumvira]] on [[Caspar]] seventeen years after the Battle of Yavin. An only child, her family owned the Elerian Nut Company a sizeable business on Caspar with a variety of facilities and a large distribution network. The family's wealth meant that Veruca Violet wanted for nothing as a child. In fact she was quite spoiled with her parents giving into virtually and demand or whim of hers, and she could be considered to be fairly rotten youngling with little in the way of redeeming character traits.

Her life would become drastically changed at age ten when the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] swept Caspar. Her parents were among the casualties and in the wake of their deaths and the chaos sweeping the planet she decided to hole up in a confectionery shop operated the Elerian Nut Company, hoping to maintain what aspects of her decadent lifestyle she could as a kid in a candy shop.

It would be here she would be found by [[Zubindi Hakoon]] who had followed his friend [[Crestian Tarasar]] to Caspar and had decided to open a large complex for the [[Silverwood House of Fashion]] in the city block that Veruca Violet's shop was located in. Though she did have a living relative, her aunt [[Andra Eleria|Andra]] in [[Noques Rasala]], Veruca Violet was still hiding out, wishing to avoid living the austere lifestyle her aunt embraced.

Zubindi sensed something special in the girl and struck a deal with her, he would take her as his ward if Veruca Violet would reopen her candy shop as part of his complex and contain her pursuit of material goods to the realm of fashion alone. Compared to the prospect of living with her aunt and the fact that her family's wealth had largely been wiped out during the pandemic, Veruca Violet considered the terms acceptable. Veruca Violet's Candy Land would soon become one the cornerstones of the larger Silverwood complex in Trinumvira.

The relative poverty would not last long as the terms of property sales and gifts from Zubindi soon meant Veruca Violet owned wardrobe worth more than some small planets. While she would have to let go the rest of her greed for goods she soon found other outlet's for her personality focusing on the acquisition of friends, something she never had before. Not far from Silverwood was the villa of Zubindi's friend Crestian, and she soon befriended his daughter [[Atsvara Tarasar|Atsvara]] in spite of their age difference. Veruca Violet was also not the only orphan in the area, and she soon found herself taking in her own 'ward,' a Squib fuzzling she calls Charlie Barrel (having found him living in one) who serves as a clerk for her store.

As Zubindi's ward, Veruca Violet became his protege and assistant in all his professional endeavours. In addition to helping him run the Silverwood House of Fashion she participated in his work for [[The Mission]] and the [[Caspian Intelligence Service]]. In spite of her young age, her involvement in the Caspian Intelligence Service from its onset and her contacts in the world of high culture has allowed to quickly rise through the ranks.

== Teen Years ==

[[File:Veruca Violet Black Eyes.jpg|thumb]] Prior to the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] trade mission to [[Corellia]] in [[32 ABY]] Veruca Violet was promoted to the rank of Senior Agent and legally emancipated for her status as Zubindi's ward. By this point she was now exercising executive authority in Zubindi's operations. In addition to being recognised as Chief of Operations of the Silverwood House of Fashion, and Zubindi's understood heir, she would soon take on the role of Quarter Master for the Trinumvira Station of the Caspian Intelligence Service.
<br>
Veruca Violet would branch out her business efforts further when Atsvara Tarasar formed the [[Golden Bantha Group]] in her bid to eventually takeover [[Biscuit Baron]]. As Atsvara's closest confident Veruca soon took on the role as Chief Operating Officer of the firm and one of its largest investors. Her apartment in the Silverwood complex in Trinumvira would soon be one of the unofficial offices of the investment firm.

With the Golden Bantha Group's [[Report:Biscuit Buyout|takeover]] of Biscuit Baron in [[33 ABY]], Veruca Violet became Chief Operating Officer of the galaxy's largest fast-casual restaurant chain and one of the company's public faces. She has also recently been tasked with operating her own intelligence station outside of Caspian territory.

== Miscellany ==

Though her full name at birth was Veruca Violet Eleria she has opted to be known as Veruca Violet within both the business and intelligence communities as a single full name, with Veruca alone as a nickname.

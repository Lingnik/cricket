==Background and Personality==
Via hails from a merchant background; she attributes her wanderlust to this, and often complains that it was too legitimate for her tastes. Perhaps it was the excess of paperwork or simply the lack of adventure to be found in shipping freight; whatever it was, she felt drawn to the world of the smuggler, and one day informed her father that she had booked passage to [[Nar Shaddaa]] to look for more interesting work. He was supportive and provided her with the credits she would need.

There she ran into [[Daana Roon]], and managed to convince the Wroonian that she was good enough to fly one of her ships. She was given command of the Scissors, a [[Sardakh Kale-1]], and has since been flying the galaxy, taking a few jobs here and there--whatever caught her interest at the time, or whatever was placed in front of her.

Via is impulsive at the best of times, sarcastic, prone to wit (or at least attempts) and seems arrogant at the best of times, if not downright narcissistic. She is afflicted with wanderlust and a love of cheap thrills. Her drink of choice is Corellian whiskey, and she's been known to drink a lot of it.

==Appearance and Equipment==
Usually, Via is dressed in a tattered nerfhide jacket and has a fairly masculine style of dress, which stands in sharp contrast to her tall, slender, feminine figure. Of course, her gunbelt seems to accentuate her waist and one could say she has tailored the otherwise masculine shirt to emphasize her curves, but on the whole her clothes give off a very masculine appearance.

She has been known to dress up, wearing a blue dress several shades darker than her skin, and makeup to draw more attention to her face (which she otherwise leaves alone); this is a rare occasion and some people have mistaken her for someone else entirely when she does so.

She carries a pair of [[Hrockar Special]] holdout blasters anywhere she goes--usually one concealed in each sleeve of her jacket, though occasionally other hiding places are used. She also usually keeps a [[Kylan-3]] prominently displayed at her belt, probably as a deterrent to any attackers (as well as making it difficult to see her as cute and harmless.)

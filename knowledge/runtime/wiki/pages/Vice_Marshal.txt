'''Vice Marshal''' is a military rank typically associated with members of a starfighter corps, most notibly the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]].  

=== In Imperial Service ===
{{rank plate|[[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]]|}}

In the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]], a Vice Marshal usually held a command staff position assisting a Marshal or higher.  There usually was no specific command posting but normally were found leading a starfighter command.  A typical command would consist of at least four groups depending on the task force assembled.  At a minimum, a command   had 1152 ships, 1920-4800 officers, and 2880-8640 enlisted. They were addressed as Marshal.  If appointed as a staff officer they would act as an assistant to a [[Marshal]].  

A Vice Marshal was equivalent to a [[Rear Admiral]] in the [[Imperial Navy]].

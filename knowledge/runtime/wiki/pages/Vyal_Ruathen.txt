Vyal Ruathen is known best as the owner of [[Ruathen's]] restaurants throughout the galaxy, one of the most famous being in the Parliamentary District of [[Coruscant]].  He is a diverse businessman however, and most recently has been pursuing mining interests, having taken over Fates Mining Corporation and spearheading explorations around [[Trandosha]] and various planets in the [[Caspia|Caspian]] system.

The Wroonian nobleman holds the title Viscount from his blood heritage, utilizing the perks of his birth to engage in the sport of hunting.  As a hobby, he breeds riding animals and is known as a source for elegant, well-bred beasts of many types.

The current head of his family, Vyal oversees the education of his younger sister Xenzi as well as the handling of the family fortune. With roots in both Wroonian and Corellian culture, he and his sibling share holdings in the Core as well as outlying areas. Xenzi recently married Vyal's long-tiome valet, Ghazi Bayu, and so the dispensation of her share of the Ruathen estate should be occurring soon.

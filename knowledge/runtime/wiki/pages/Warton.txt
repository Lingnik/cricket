The wool of the porcine serrith has long provided insulating clothing for the Eireasari of the [[Mergansar]] highlands. The cloth called '''warton''' is the apex of serrith wool weaving. There are two types: warton twill and warton napped. Both are slightly fuzzy, but napped has a short pile that can be visibly impressed by a finger while twill doesn't stand nearly so high from the weft. Both types of warton can be felted for waterproofing and stand up against any synthetic for insulation value.

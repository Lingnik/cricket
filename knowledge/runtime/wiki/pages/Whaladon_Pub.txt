The '''Whaladon Pub''' is a small cantina in one of the seedier areas of the [[Domed City of Aquarius]] on [[Dac]]. In spite of its name, leading one to expect some kind of impressive establishment structured to permit interaction with the sentient [[:starwars:Whaladon/Legends|Whaladon]] species, the pub was a classic dive with no such enhancements.

It was frequented mostly by Quarren and Mon Calamari both, until racial tensions ignited by covert [[Imperial Security Bureau|ISB]] agents erupted into a riot that forced the Mon Calamari patrons to leave, and resulted in the Quarren frequenters declaring it a "Quarrens-only dig". This was one of the first stages of the ISB's [[Operation Squid Lake]], which secretly caused the uprising of the [[Dacan Civil War]] in [[16 ABY]].

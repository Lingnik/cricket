Business owner '''Wibble Sniklit''' is the Captain of the ''Frenetic Bat''. Avid sports fan and mechanics enthusiast, he has been known to race swoops, and has even gathered a competent group of racers. He is the owner and CEO of [[Sniklit's Droids]].

Mr. Sniklit was formerly a flight officer of the Calamari Space Control. He has busted more than a handful of smugglers as the prime operator of Calamari Customs. He resigned in protest when, in his opinion, the New Republic military overstepped their authority by searching private vessels without obtaining authorization from the planet's government nor notifying Calamari customs.

Very little is known of the [[Gran]] known as Willis. What ''is'' known about him is that he is a merchant of many, often illegal, weapons and armor. From where he gets these weapons is known only by himself. Not known to frequent one planet too long for reasons known only to himself, weither it's to evade being detected by the proper authorities or searching for a new source of customers. He is often seen with his older model repulsorcart, which carries his items. What he shows often depends upon what the customer can do to "prove themself" as a member of the darker underbelly of the Galaxy and his prices are always negotiable.

{{NPC}}

=== OOC Notes ===

Willis is a creation of [[Zen]] during his time as the main code wizard for SW1, who brought several new items with his "arrival" to the MUSH. He is a source of black market weaponry. The codework for him rolls both the character's Bargain and Streetwise skills and displaying what would be appropriate to the roll that was given. He also has been set up to be teleported to a different room upon every Sunday which is randomly selected so that players do not concentrate upon just one world.

=== Coruscant. Born to a Life of Privilege ===
Xand was born on Coruscant in [[10 BBY]] to Coruscant Senator Ara Velo and her Diplomat husband, Tal Velo. His childhood was carefree and growing up he wanted for nothing. Put into the best schools and destined to take his place amongst the political nobility of his homeworld, a bright future was secured.

He did well in school and made an effort to follow the narrow path his parents set out for him. He joined all the right clubs in his pre-college days and when the time came, he filed his application for Leadership School and put in for a business scholarship, not because he needed it, but because that's how things were done.

=== A Family Tradition ===
It didn't matter how good or poor his application was. Both of his parents had graduated from the school and his entry was a foregone conclusion. He was given a full scholarship and attended at the beginning of the next year. His last name got him into the exclusive school clubs and garnered him favor with most of his teachers.

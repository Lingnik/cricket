== Galactic Republic Applicant Review Board ==
=== Applicant: Xar'on Tanner ===

Xar'on Tanner, hereafter referred to as "applicant".  Homeworld: Chandrila.  No history of family government or military service.  Standard background check reveals no criminal history.

The applicant tested near the theoretical maximum for human intelligence.  Academic records and the standard battery of Republic aptitude tests show a strong aptitude in the maths and sciences.  Instructors all note the applicant's intuitive understanding of human and non-human physiology and biology.

Physical conditioning is adequate, and the applicant's academic record shows minor participation in school athletic programs.  The physician grading the applicants' physical conditioning that day noted that the applicant supplied his own vital statistics and observations, which the physician added to the official application.

Application lists three summers of volunteer work at various Chandrilan hospitals.  Administrators of those hospitals have all submitted letters of recommendation.

This review board recommends the acceptance of this application.

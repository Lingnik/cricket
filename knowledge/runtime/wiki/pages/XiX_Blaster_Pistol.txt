======================================================================
The '''XiX''' series blaster pistol is an unquestionably modern looking weapon. Its 
black composite ergonomic frame almost looks as if it were meant to be poured 
into a typical human or near human hand.  A cursory examination would seem to
indicate that this weapon was designed with some form of concealability in 
mind - there are few protusions to catch on clothing, and if necessary, the 
'iron sights' look like they could be easily removed and replaced with a 
lower profile type. The weapon's fire selector only contains two positions, 
with no indication of a stun setting.  The XiX is known a reverberating, 
hollow sound when it fires.

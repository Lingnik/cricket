''' Xi Charrians ''' are a hive-minded sentient insectoid species from [[Charros IV]]. They practice the [[Xi Char]] faith a fanatical polytheistic religion focused on high-precision manufacturing. Galactically they are most known for [[Haor Chall Engineering]] which mainly produces droids and starships. 

== Appearance ==

Xi Charrian bodies are covered in chitinous plates. They are quadrupedal, with pointed, "scissor-action" feet and teardrop-shaped heads and  red eye-slits. Their appearance is similar to that of the [[Mark IV Vulture-Class Droid Starfighter|Vulture-Class Droid Starfighters]] which were designed in their image. Xi Charrians tend average around 3 metres in height in adulthood, with some as tall as the 3.5 metres of the Vulture Droids.

== Diet ==

Their diet centres primarily on sugars with fruits as a key staple. They also consume the meat of various animals. Xi Charrians, however, can digest and receive nourishment from nearly all biological material. They prefer their food raw, though they can still consume items that have been cooked. Their sense of taste is limited compared many other species so seeking specific flavours or a variety of experiences has limited appeal, though not non-existent. They will inhale certain chemicals the way many sentients consume alcohol.

== Language ==

The Xi Char language involves a complex series of clicks which unintelligible to most other species, even gifted linguistics have difficulty deciphering more than a few words or phrases. In order to communicate with other species Xi Charrians will make use of translations droids such as the Articulation Conversion Spheres produced by Haor Chall Engineering. Most Xi Charrians understand [[Galactic Basic Standard]] though they are unable to speak it.

== Pheromones ==

In addition to verbal communication Xi Charrians can release pheromones which can be used to communicate certain information among their species. These pheromones not only communicate information but also have an influencing effect. This is one way by which the species is able to achieve high consensus as the release of pheromones within a group of Xi Charrians can cause a certain concept to be spread among the group, with a majority belief able to biologically gain traction over a minority one over time. This process is not well understood among non-Xi Charrians, and the Xi Charrians make little effort to explain it.

== Spirituality ==

The [[Xi Char]] faith believes that all life is a shadow of the paradise of the afterlife and that creating machines was a way to glimpse the afterlife. For them their work was essentially a form of prayer with their cathedral factories service as both manufacturing facilities and religious structures. Their zeal for perfection is such that they have been known to refuse payment for work they felt failed to live up to their standards. Their obsession with perfection is not only limited to their work, when a Xi Charrian becomes injured they were exile themselves from other so that no one witnesses their imperfection.

== OOC Notes ==

In spite of the prevalence of their designs in Clone Wars era canon, Wookieepedia has little information on the species itself, with their culture explained as much in the articles about their products as anywhere else. As such their appearance here represents steps beyond the established canon.

Their appearance is described in terms of the [[:starwars:Vulture-class starfighter/Legends|Vulture-Class Starfighters]] which it is said to have been based by the Xi Charrians on themselves. This was the biggest controlling fact in how I conceived of the species making their size large with starfighter just a bit larger than the largest of Xi Charrians. Somehow in spite of years of looking at the Wookieepedia article I never caught the word "short" which has clearly been used to describe them right along. While I am kicking myself at not noticing it, short is not a very specific description, and while the Vulture Droid being designed on themselves was never likely meant to imply size it provide a more specific size, and a more interesting one. 

A smaller species would have been less unique to our game, perhaps they may ever have been seen as cute, but it would not have told much of a story. On the other hand having a character like [[ak]] whose is essentially a cross from the Dos Equis "Most Interesting Man in the World" and a bug from Starship Troopers makes a far more interesting concept. The juxtaposition of a pleasant species, with a strange culture, and intimidation appearance has told a story that many of us have found is fun.

They are also described as a hive-minded species though the extent of it and the actual logistics are never defined. I have taken a loose approach on this. In their appearances on the grid they are shown to seek consensus, but I have shown a level of individuality in their characters that may stretch the bounds of the hive-mind. However, again this tells a more interesting story.

'''Yavin''' was the star system (comprised around the star of the same name) that was home to [[Yavin Prime]], and its twenty-six moons. Of these moons, three were habitable, of which [[Yavin 4]] was the most famous and well-known for its role in the history of [[The Galaxy|the Galaxy]].

The '''Yavin Plague''' was a deadly virus that spread from [[Yavin 4]] across the entire [[The Galaxy|galaxy]]. It's been rumored that [[Morganna Tazecks]] was responsible for spreading it to the [[Caspian Democratic Union]]. It is believed that moss collected from [[Sullust]] led to the eventual cure of the disease.

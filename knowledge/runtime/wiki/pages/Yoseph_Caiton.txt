'''Yoseph Caiton''' ( - [[15 ABY]]) was an [[Imperial Navy]] officer who commanded the [[I2SD Conqueror|HIMS ''Conqueror'']] for a few short months during [[14 ABY]] before being replaced by [[Antal Brencis]].

He was the husband of [[Lynae Cassius|Lynae Cassius-Caiton]], the Chief Medical Officer aboard the ''Conqueror''. Later as [[captain]] of HIMS ''Pillager'', one of the ships involved in the reclamation of [[Coruscant]], he retired in 15 ABY. Yoseph was killed a few months later when the hover-taxi he was travelling in was struck from behind by another hover-taxi in a random speeder accident near the Spaceport in [[Coronet]], [[Corellia]]. A forum was convened to discuss the topic of speed control in relation to accidents and traffic patterns near the spaceport.

The '''Youngling Ball''' was an event that took place at the [[:starwars:Yarith_Bespin/Legends|Yarith Bespin]] in [[Cloud City]] on [[Bespin]] hosted by [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]] in [[29 ABY]]. The event was designed to facilitate peace and cooperation in the galaxy by promoting social bonds between the younglings in the [[Jedi Order]] and those who were children of [[Sith Order|Sith]] figures.

== Background ==

In [[27 ABY]] [[Jedi Knight]] [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]] rescued her half-brother [[Tyler Damion]] also known as Darth Malign from library on a moon of [[Korriban]] where he had been trapped for nearly a decade. This set about an uneasy alliance between the [[Jedi Order|Jedi]] and [[Sith Order|Sith]] with the twin goals of defeating [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Aleister Vadim]] and wiping slavery from the galaxy. Johanna and Tyler would then travel to [[Silver Station]] to find Sith [[Duke]] and [[Admiral]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] who joined their cause, and reconfirmed his continued loyalty to Darth Malign.

This culminated in Tyler's takeover of the [[Galactic Empire]] which he transformed into the [[Imperium]], ruling it as Imperator Damion I. In these early days Johanna served his efforts at [[Grand Moff]] of the [[Bespin]] and [[Tatooine]], while her eldest son, [[Etiel te Danaan]], also a Jedi Knight became [[Moff]] of [[Byss]] and [[O'paal]].

While the Jedi surving in the Imperium were confined to those related to the Imperator, Johanna also had family ties to [[Jedi Master]] [[Jessalyn Valios]], whose brother Ajani Valios, was the father by then of two of Johanna's children. This helped produce a dynamic where the Jedi and Sith as a whole were effectly operating under a flag of truce. Johanna hoped to build on this by holding a formal social event, while also allowing the younglings to simply have some fun.

== Lead Up ==

Preperation began on several fronts. Johanna chose [[Cloud City]] as a destination as it was within the territory she controlled and better suited to such a gathering than [[Tatooine]]. [[Zubindi Hakoon]] and the [[Silverwood House of Fashion]] were commissioned to produce several ballgowns, particularly those of the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] attendees.

Tensions were also running high. Caspian [[Presav]] Crestian Tarasar and [[Elana Tracer]], the Moff of [[Selene]] recently used a joint family as a cover for an undercover operation on [[:starwars:Spira/Legends|Spira]] to rescue to force sensitive younglings who had been kidnapped as part of a [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order]] programme spearheaded by [[Basca Starker]]. Starker, who was now in a relationship with Jessalyn Valios, was soon exposed for his role in this as well as having been the man behind the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] on [[Caspar]] which led to a very tense encounter at a Imperium court meeting which occured on Bespin in the weeks before the ball.

There was the added complication of the joint-custody of [[Ara te Danaan|Ara]] and [[Tyler te Danaan]] arrangenment between Johanna and her ex-husband former [[Griffon Alliance]] [[Colonel]] and [[Corporate Sector Authority]] [[Prex (CSA)|Prex]] [[Zeak Oppenhiemer]]. In order to attend the ball Ara and Tyler stole one of their father's ships, leading to arrest warrant being issued by Griffon authorities. Meanwhile Caspian Minister of Justice [[Sha Dark]] publically [[Joyriding|criticised]] the warrant and suggest the Union would offer the pair asylum.

== The Party ==

Though billed as an event for the Jedi and the Sith there were also some figures present as well who were not formally tied to either side but personally connected to the family's participating. Imperator Damion also brought members of his [[Circle of Irluuk]]. Tensions among some of the adults present remained high. The event also was covered for [[InterGalactic News]] by [[Loy Marin]] which added additional drama.

The younglings, however, largerly got along and had a good time. Most notably the event brought together Imperator Damion's daughter [[Sonja Damion|Sonja]] and his apprentice [[Kipran Jawatha]] serving as the start of their courtship.

== Aftermath ==

Though billed with the hope of being an annual event, it has not been repeated since. The longest lasting effect has been the relationship between now Crown Princess Sonja Damion and [[Captain]] Kipran Jawatha who have been engaged for years now. Several future investors in the [[Golden Bantha Group]] which later bought [[Biscuit Baron]] were present together at the ball with this likely the first meeting of some of them.

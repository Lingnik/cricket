TITLE: ZCF Grenade Launcher
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2006-12-10T21:27:16Z
======================================================================
In terms of length and weight, this stubby projectile weapon is actually a 
touch smaller than many blaster rifles. It is fitted with a shoulder-stock, 
pistol grip, and folding front fore-grip to make this weapon easier to handle
- a wise precaution given the '''Zone Control Four''''s tendency to recoil rather heavily.  The 
weapon uses a magnetic field rather than a chemical propellant to launch its 
shells, so nearly any properly sized, contact fused grenade with a ferrous 
casing can be loaded and fired successfully, though with mixed results as far
as range and accuracy go. To assist in aiming, a small computerised sighting 
system is fitted atop the weapon, which displays the approximate location 
where the grenade will drop. While the ZCF is a short-ranged, imprecise, and 
downright ugly weapon, weapons like it are coveted by military forces as a compact means of delivering explosive projectiles. Aside from the usual explosive ordnance, this weapon is also suitable for firing gas, illumination, and smoke rounds, among others.

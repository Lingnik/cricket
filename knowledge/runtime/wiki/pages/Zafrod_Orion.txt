'''Zafrod Orion''' was an influential [[Caspar|Caspian]] businessman and politician.

Tapped at a young age for a diplomatic career, Zafrod proved a bit too rash and, at times, unstable for full time diplomatic duty. Although he maintained lifelong ties to the [[Caspian Democratic Union|CDU]] government, his direct involvement lessened with time. He took the opportunity to found [[NovaCom Cybertronics]] with Cyril M'Meggor, and originally manufactured droids, but eventually spread out to include all manner of electronics. Although Zafrod was a mechanical hack, stealing the best ideas from other manufacturers and incorporating them into his own designs, he was an expert at marketing, and NovaCom became a huge success.

Slowly, Zafrod's mannerisms began to change. The once cheerful, outgoing gentleman seemed to be replaced with a sharp, withdrawn loner. Some have noted that this change corresponded with his hiring of a new assistant by the name of [[Ozzle Olumpari]]. Rumors of criminal involvement began to spread, and the NovaCom offices became more like a compound - ominous and sealed off to the public.

Zafrod was reported deceased under somewhat mysterious circumstances. Evidence seems to suggested that he was in the custody of the [[Griffon Alliance]] at the time, but the details of his demise have never been fully uncovered.

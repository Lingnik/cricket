'''Zamp'lini IV''' was the fourth planet of the Zamp'lini System, located in the [[The Galaxy#Expansion Region|Expansion Region]], and the only world in the system capable of maintaining life. Zamp'lini IV was far enough away from its star to avoid the huge flares of solar radiation spit out by Zamp'lini that rendered her fellow worlds barren. The planet bared no civilization and travel to the world was very difficult, as the radiation rampant throughout the system rendered sensors and com systems nearly useless.

[[Fleet Admiral]] [[Thrax Daimien]] was killed on Zamp'lini IV, although it is unknown if it was during battle or the result of difficult travel due to the radiation.

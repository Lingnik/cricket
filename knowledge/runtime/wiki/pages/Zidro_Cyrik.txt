'''Zidro Cyrik''' ([[14 BBY]] - ) is the [[Viceprex (CSA)|Executive Viceprex]] of the [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] [[Security Division (CSA)|Security Division]].

==Appearance==
A canine figure known to those familiar to them as Shistavanens. This one is a young Shistavanen, very tall being a few inches short of 8ft, and seeming quite powerfully built to boot, very broad-shouldered and generally fit. There is an air of confidence -- not arrogance, simply confidence -- about this creature, and from his motions one can see a certain agility and light-footed speed. Cunning blue-green eyes gaze out from under a fringe of long, furry bangs, with an odd glint to them... cold, observing, telling of age gained not in years, and of far too many things seen for any one lifetime... disconcerting. His fur is an impossibly bright shade of brownish-red, with a coppery sheen that makes the color all the more rich... 

==Brief Background==
The [[Shistavanen]] known as Zidro Cyrik started off as a bounty hunter.

Next, he served the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] and was in charge of the redevelopment of the [[Caspar|Caspian]] city of [[Anchorron]]. 

He later followed his mentor [[Ernest Pallando]] to the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector]], employed by [[NovaCom Cybertronics]].

Recommended by [[Kerunous Nalsare]], then CEO of [[Rendili StarDrive]] and [[Prex (CSA)|Prex]], the [[Direx Board (CSA)|Direx Board]] selected Zidro to take over the [[Office of the Auditor General|OOAG]] as Auditor General. Under his tenure he acquired an [[Imperial-class Star Destroyer]], the CSAV ''Corporate Takeover'', which he used as a mobile base of operations.

In early [[14 ABY]], Zidro succeeded [[Morrison Van Sen]] as Viceprex of CSA Security.

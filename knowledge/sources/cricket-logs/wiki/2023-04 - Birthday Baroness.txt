Centrally located off Plasteel Plaza, Silvergrass Arboretum immediately creates an entirely different vibe from the artifice outside its confines. For a moment one could forget they were in space at all as the sights and smells bushes and trees, flowers, and grasses from dozens of worlds fills the room. Look far enough in any direction and the windows beyond will remind one of their location with the gasses of the Dragonflower Nebula providing their own beauty. In the high ceiling above artificial lighting of various shades and hues mimic the lighting of different stars, creating an odd, almost disorienting glow.

The arboretum takes its name from the predominant ground flora of the plains of Kinyen. The silvergrass is similarly predominant here creating a sweet aroma which permeates the arboretum. Though many worlds are represented, the Duke's history with Caspar and Chandrila is quite apparent with Wansook Pines and Pula and Tintolive trees prevalent. A pond located in the centre of the arboretum is lined with balmgrass. While the grasses and trees are largely mundane on their homeworlds, the arboretum boosts some rare flowers such as Alderaanian flame-lily and Alderaanian flame-rose.


&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Crestian had taken the Wargames at Charros IV quite seriously conducting all of his business in system, and avoiding the trips back to Caspar or elsewhere some of the other members of the Crest had been making. But there was on occasion that caused him to pull out of the system, after all your daughter only turns ten once. A trip home was necessary, but home in this case was not to be Caspar, but Atsvara's real home world, Silver Station.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Silver Station was Crestian's personal fiefdom as New Sith Order's Duke of Dragonflower. And in spite of his role now as Caspian Presav, there was something of that life that still persisted. The station, the Leviathan, and Crestian's other personnel starships were still crewed by personnel wearing Sith uniforms of his own design, which for once Crestian donned as well. Yet Caspar had its own hold on here too. Though not Union territory, the Union used it as a base and Caspian Defence Force personnel can be seen working in tandem with the erstwhile Sith.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Though she was not the one who planned the party itself, Atsvara was responsible for her own guest list. Much of those gathered at the Silvergrass Arborteum are the younglings she went to Miss Cason's School for Sith Spawn with, and Miss Cason herself, naturally. Plenty of Droids had also made the guest list well. Yet being the budding young business magnate agent of chaos that she was a number of famous and powerful people had made the guest list and decided to show including several members of the Union Council and foreign leaders.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For the wargames, Atsvara had been wearing an outfit that was inspired by the Sith uniforms, but she now boasts an actual one, though missing any rank insignia. Standing by one on of the picnic tables, the golden girl is holding courting and very much in her element.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Is there a bomb in here or something?" Cricket whistles to the young teen beside him, a small box gripped in the droid's pincer-arm as the pair enter the arboretum and make their way towards the throng of younglings jockeying for position near the birthday girl, "I didn't know that Atsvara is into murder. Someone could have /told/ me, you know," he complains, feeling left out of the latest juicy gossip yet again. Next to him, Johanna's fourteen year-old daughter Alina snorts before reassuring him: "It's not a bomb, Cricket. It's a puzzle-box, there's something inside that will provide a clue to a location. Then she--"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"BORING," the astromech interrupts, nearly rolling over someone's foot, and ignoring the yelp of surprise from said foot's owner, "Your mother has the worst gift ideas. I'd have given her a bomb. She'd /love/ that."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nara had been unfortunate enough to have other business on Silver Station, business that couldn't be rescheduled no matter how hard she tried. Regretfully, her presence at the party would be much shorter than she ever wanted. Arriving wrapped in a silver anorak, knee boots, and canted metal-plated belt over a shiny black body glove, she sought out the birthday girl directly.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Happy birthday, darling!" she says warmly, taking a knee to hug Atsvara. "I really can't stay, but there was no way I was going to not at least pop in and see you for at least a moment."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;She gently presses a small box into the birthday girl's hands, one wrapped in silver foil and tied with a black ribbon. "I hope you never need this. But it's made so you can always have it with you, just in case. I hope you enjoy every minute of the party." She rises and makes her way out, sidestepping Cricket almost immediately.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Inside the box is a small but advanced computer-aided magnetheric compass in a jeweled case resembling a locket, complete with a delicate neck chain. Useful on any world with a magnetic pole, it also includes a separate interface chip that allows it to interface with a datapad to track the user's location on any planetary map.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Junior Flight Lieutenant Longinus Van Sen, nineteen and rangey with long arms and legs, arrives with a passle of local chums from a fourteen year old cadet to Lon's seventeen year old wingman. The tall young man with dark hair and eyes precedes his famous mother into the party, sweeping into the room and finding other young folk to mix and mingle with. Seeing as Atsvara is with other guests, the older teen plans to wait for his moment to wish the birtday girl well. And ask for stock tips.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Elana Tracer follows her son into the venue along with her daughter Ashton, who carries a small flimsi bag that is decorated with bright ribbons. The women walk towards the central gathering and pull up as Cricket cracks wise. If the thing ever found it's way into one of her shops...it might find it's self with a case of amnesia, the Grand Moff thinks to herself. Jumping back slightly, from nearly getting her foot mangled, Tracer's opinion of the astromech does not warm. She turns and looks for Crestian and Johanna and a bar.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Standing next to Atsvara is her fourteen year old friend Veruca Violet, who in fact was the one who largely planned the festivities. Hearing the word bomb mentioned, the Pantoran starts to tap on some device on her wrist before spotting the fact it has been mentioned in relation to Cricket. Having not ascertained the full context she shouts, "Cricket were you saying you are bringing a bomb or needing on?" A perfectly rational question. Yup, now chaotic or evil aligned beings here at all. As Nara sneaks her way through Atsvara hugs the young socialite, "That is so thoughtful. I am glad you were able to make it. It was hard to try get everyone together in one place."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Alas; Cricket's shenanigans have not only drawn unwanted attention, he's also nearly managed to cripple Elana. "Miss Tracer!" Alina gasps, blushing with embarrassment, "I'm so sorry! Cricket said something about Atsvara insisting he attend, and mom said it was my turn to watch him, so..." She gestures helplessly as the deranged droid hoots with glee at Veruca's question and comes to a stop, a holo-projection of about ten dancing Jawas and a raucous birthday song erupting from his programming. "A bomb would have been SO COOL," Cricket replies over the din, savoring the part where the Jawas doff their robes and prance around in their birthday suits, the shocked silence from half the attendees serving as a balm to his blackened binary soul, "But my weird human insisted on giving this BOX to Atsvara instead. HAPPY BIRTHDAY!"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"A bomb would have been lovely," remarks Atsvara who now clearly is catching some of the conversation at hand. "There will be plenty of time for such things in the months to come." She then looks to Alina, oblivious to what trouble Cricket is causing but saying, "I hope Cricket isn't causing you trouble. I hope there was no confusion with the invitations. You each all should have been sent them individually. Cricket is indeed on the guest list as are you naturally." For her part Veruca does seem to take in the holo-projection with only mild embarrassment, "I wonder how well that would play at Club Utinni!?"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"That's enough," a dreaded, familiar voice orders from a few meters away, the racy Jawa show terminating abruptly as Cricket registers the unwelcome arrival of The Most Boring Cow in the Galaxy (aka Johanna), "It was PIXELATED," he protests, "It was AGE-APPROPRIATE!"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Never mind Cricket's poor taste, I'm sure he's just trying to congratulate you," Johanna notes with a smile to Atsvara, "The whole family sends their very best for your special day. Happy birthday, young lady." Unlike some of the other well-wishers, she doesn't move to hug the girl, preferring to let Crestian's daughter decide for herself whether or not she wants any form of physical contact. "Cricket's always causing trouble," Alina adds, "But sometimes it's funny."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"It's okay! I'm fine. Just..." Tracer turns her ankle trying to set her foot. ".../scraped/ the leather. No big deal!" The petite woman overplays her reassurances but gives the droid the stink-eye next chance she gets. She also gives Ashton a nod. Her daughter steps forward and presents Atsvara with the giftbag. "Happy Birthday, sweetie." Ashton hugs the girl. Inside the bag is a cosmetics kit - age appropriate with mostly just lip glosses and nearly clear pink nail polish.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Tracer then levels her cool gaze at Johanna's daughter. "Alina...right? /You/ are not responsible. And speaking of responsible, where is your mother, anyway?"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Atsvara takes each offer hug, gift, and congratulatory statement with well practised enthusiasm. In some cases even genuine enthusiasm, though sadly now all the present are measured against the bomb that could have been. The cosmetics kit is taken with particular amusement. As much caused by the commentary around age appropriateness. Clearly there were some people in the room who just had no idea all the shady shit she was up to. But then again she wouldn't be doing it right if they did.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Stepping onto the scene next are Crestian and his wife. Unlike Crestian and Atsvara, Meridian is not wearing a uniform, though her ebony Cyrene silk dress was clearly chosen to match and the brooch she wears is identical to the one Crestian wears on his uniform. The Firrerreo looks to Elana and Johanna in particular, "Thank you all for coming, it is good to have you in home so to speak."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Unlike most of the attendees, Cricket knows /exactly/ what kind of shady shit Atsvara has been up to and -- as one of her silent partners -- he can't help but be proud of the budding young corporate cutthroat. "Now now," he buzzes coolly, "I haven't caused any trouble whatsoever. No need to get your panties in a twist, Johanna... or Elana." Something like a harumph escapes him and he places the puzzle box on the gift table behind the birthday girl before retracting his arm and deciding to wheel himself out of the mothers' line of fire. "Seems I arrived just in time," the Jedi smirks at her friend as Crestian and Meridian appear, "I don't think impromptu naked Jawas would have been well-received by Atsvara's parents."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Coward..." Tracer mutters under her breath, watching the retreating droid. Then turning to Atsvara's parents she says, "How nice to see you, Meridian. Ten years old. You must be proud. And terrified." A wry black brow is raised before she mock scolds Johanna, "What in the stars did your daughter do to deserve baby sitting duty for the Tin Terror? A bit harsh, I think."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Well you know how you try to raise your youngling to get to have all the things you didn't have," Meridian smirks, "There is a reason why the school here is called Miss Cason's School for Sith Spawn." Nearby an Omwati woman smoking a pipe and leaning on a bowcaster looks up at the mention of her name, "Good to see you again Elana." Meanwhile Meridian looks to Crestian and then back to the other mothers, "Atsvara is far more devious than we were at that age. She is turning out alright."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Crestian nods in agreement with his wife. "That she is and that is part of why we are here." He motions to Veruca who bangs on some pre-arranged devious to get everyone's attention. Speaking to the crowd Crestian states, "I want to thank you all for coming out here. It might seem like we neglected this place the past few years. Caspar has given our family and by that I mean our whole crew here....it has given us new opportunities and concrete achievements. There is something that seems a little more real about an office and fleet than what we had back here. A Sith Duke....sounds kind of made up."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Crestian pauses for a moment as the last line gains a little amusement from the crowd, "But all titles, all power. It is made up. It is what we claim and others go along with. Now young, but not quite so young Atsvara has spent many years clawing together her own efforts in the shadows. I know some of you here know more about those than me. In any case it is time to recognise that for Meridian and I she is not just the daughter we love, but a worthy successor to our ambitions. Our democratic society on Caspar provides not great avenue to express this so it is time to make up something here."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Crestian approaches his daughter and pulls out a little box of his own, removing a brooch that looks much like the Alderaanian flame-lily. Gold flowers with xonolite flames around the petals, exaggerated from their original image to give the appearance of a flower truly on fire. "If we have our little sovereign noble state here, it seems it needs more than a Duke and Duchess. So I wish to formally announce the creation of the title of Baroness for Atsvara, the heir of the House Dragonflower." He pins the brooch on her uniform.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The arboretum erupts into a cacophony of applause -- and even one brief, ear-splitting shriek courtesy Cricket -- as the exquisite brooch is pinned to Atsvara's clothing. "It's not a punishment," Johanna shouts to Tracer over the din, "It was just her rotation for Cricket-duty." Alina, for her part, is excitedly clapping with the rest of the attendees, the buoyant energy of the room serving to lift her spirits. It's not that Cricket-duty makes her upset; it's more that she'd prefer to be doing something -- anything -- else, but she's not one to complain, so she bears the assignment with good cheer. "Congratulations!" the teen exclaims.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"I am going to need to offer hearing tests after this aren't I," Crestian remarks about a certain Droid's enthusiasm, though the remark is to no one in particular. Atsvara for her own part seem entirely pleased though she avoids any giggles, shrieks, or squeals. While she had not fully anticipated what the particular 'gift' was going to be, she had deduced that the earlier gift of a proper uniform likely had some deeper meaning that would manifest later. The choice of title was one the budding young business magnate also knew was meaningful and a true mark of her parent's approval for her secretive activities.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The girl takes a moment to compose herself and consider the occasion. She gets up to stand on one of the picnic tables. "I just wanted to say...." She pauses and looks over to another Firrerreo in the crowd, "This is off the record Brackett....I just wanted to say I will strive the live up to the awesome responsibility this entails." Her inflection is exaggerated as she makes a bit of a show of this. "To all of my family and friends here today I just want you all to know the House Dragonflower will be source of protection and support to her allies in perpetuity. No matter how many enemies we have to leave broken and bleeding to make that happen."

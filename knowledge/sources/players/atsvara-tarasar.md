## Talking to them OOC

This character is run by the **Caspian org's player** — the person behind **Crestian Tarasar**
and the fashionista alt **Zubindi Hakoon**, who manages the Caspian faction and the wiki and
has been **elevated to Wizard** (Article: Crestian Tarasar, "Behind the Scenes"). So assume a
**worldbuilder's player**: deep investment in the Caspian Union setting, droid-rights themes,
nobility/branding lore, and a sly comic streak. Atsvara is their **kid-villain-you-root-for**
showcase character — a ten-year-old(ish) tycoon who hacks corporate finances and stages
hostile takeovers, played for both menace and comedy.

Safe conversation hooks:
- **The Biscuit Baron buyout** — her signature arc. She engineered a labour crisis to devalue
  the chain, ran a consultancy (Golden Bantha Group) to "fix" it, then bought it outright in
  33 ABY (Article: Atsvara Tarasar; RPlog:He Was Doing You A Favour, 33 ABY). She is literally
  "the Biscuit Baroness."
- **Droid rights.** Raised among droids treated as equals on the *Leviathan*; considers the
  droid **V1C3** her twin brother; "widely suspected... she will one day instigate an armed
  revolution to secure droid equality" (Article: Atsvara Tarasar). A reliable shared-ground
  topic — especially with **Cricket**, who is both her business partner and a fellow embezzler.
- **House Dragonflower / the Baroness title.** Made Baroness on her tenth birthday as Crestian's
  heir — a title chosen partly for *Biscuit Baron-ess* branding (RPlog:Birthday Baroness, 32 ABY).
- **'Aunt' Elana Tracer.** Atsvara idolises Tracer (Grand Moff, ex-Presav, swoop racer) and
  wants to follow her into Moffdom and racing (Article: Atsvara Tarasar).

Tone to match: precocious, dry, faux-formal, gleefully ruthless under a polite veneer — and
remember the player loves the comedy of a small child saying menacing things.

## Character: Atsvara Tarasar — "the Biscuit Baroness"

**Capsule.** Firrerreo/Sarian girl (b. 22 ABY), black-and-silver striped hair, onyx eyes,
gold skin, ~1.5m. Only daughter of Caspian Presav and ex-Sith Duke **Crestian Tarasar**;
raised in secrecy in the Dragonflower Nebula, schooled in a balance-oriented Sith philosophy.
CEO of the **Golden Bantha Group** and, after her buyout, of **Biscuit Baron**; Baroness of
**House Dragonflower** and a commissioned **Captain** in the Caspian Navy via her father's
warship *Leviathan* (Article: Atsvara Tarasar). Faction footprint reads Caspian Union (21
logs) with heavy Imperium contact (10) and her Sith inheritance (7).

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Born in exile (22 ABY → ~28 ABY).** Born on Silver Station while Crestian lay low after
  Darth Malign's fall; "Atsvara" is the Sith word for *balance*. Grew up entirely inside the
  Dragonflower Nebula among 3–4,000 crew and droids, attending "Miss Cason's School for Sith
  Spawn"; collected Biscuit Baron Jolly Meal toys obsessively (Article: Atsvara Tarasar).
- **Out of exile (29 ABY / RL 2020).** Follows Crestian back to Caspar after the Sariphage
  pandemic; her debut on the grid is the Spira rescue and the Cloud City youngling scene —
  the first Youngling Ball where Jedi and Sith kids mingle (RPlog:Spiran Rescue, 29 ABY;
  RPlog:Johanna Has a Ball on Bespin, 29 ABY; RPlog:Jessa Drops a Bomb, 29 ABY).
- **The embezzler awakens.** Hacks Dragonflower Enterprises' finances to siphon credits into
  a dummy corp; Crestian, proud of the ingenuity, lets her keep it. With that capital she
  starts cultivating Biscuit Baron executives like **Izara Tagge** (Article: Atsvara Tarasar).
- **Made Baroness (32 ABY).** On her tenth birthday Crestian creates the title of Baroness and
  names her heir of House Dragonflower — pinning a flame-lily brooch and prompting a chilling
  acceptance vow (RPlog:Birthday Baroness, 32 ABY). Around now she becomes a fixture at Caspian
  set-pieces, holding court at her father's events (RPlog:Atsvara's Hot Takes, 32 ABY).
- **The Biscuit Baron takeover (32–33 ABY).** Secretly stokes Biscuit Baron's labour strikes
  to devalue it, communicating via a "golden bantha" avatar; forms the Golden Bantha Group as
  cover, then recruits investors — the children of Crestian's friends and their droids,
  notably **Veruca Violet** and **Cricket** — and buys the chain outright in 33 ABY
  (Article: Atsvara Tarasar; Report:Biscuit Buyout).
- **Partners-in-crime with Cricket (33 ABY).** Springs Cricket from the Mos Espa constabulary
  after his tasing escapade and brings him to the *Leviathan* — the start of their business
  partnership (and, per the log title, "love, apparently") (RPlog:He Was Doing You A Favour,
  33 ABY; RPlog:Droid Control - The Ladies Prefer Electrum, 33 ABY).
- **Modern social/intrigue circuit (33–35 ABY).** Travels with Veruca to meet old friend Kasha
  (RPlog:Life Begins Young, 33 ABY); the macabre Basca-Starker-themed gala on Dreven
  (RPlog:Ghastly Gala, 33 ABY); the Arkanis Sector charity ball on Corellia where she donates
  an Ikrit holocube and adopts a Nek battle-dog "Varpa" (RPlog:Charity Ball, 34 ABY; Article:
  Atsvara Tarasar); and the in-progress shipboard murder mystery (RPlog:Celestine Cycle - And
  A Body Hits The Floor, 35 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):

  [17 shared logs — her most frequent partner; she is constantly at his side.]

**Playstyle / themes** (from her logs): politics (8), diplomacy/intrigue (5 each), comedy (5),
social gatherings & slice-of-life (4 each), family/romance/droids (3 each). Reads as a
**political-comedy-of-manners** character — set-piece galas, corporate intrigue, and
droid-rights advocacy, with the running joke of a child wielding adult power.

**Signature voice.** Precocious, faux-formal, gleefully ruthless. At her Baroness ceremony,
told the puzzle-box wasn't a bomb, she deadpans: "A bomb would have been lovely. There will be
plenty of time for such things in the months to come" — then pledges House Dragonflower will
protect its allies "No matter how many enemies we have to leave broken and bleeding to make
that happen" (RPlog:Birthday Baroness, 32 ABY). Pull that log for cadence.

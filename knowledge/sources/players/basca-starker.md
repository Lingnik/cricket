  warmth and his war crimes is the whole point of the character.

  Lore-curious players eat this up.

Tone to match: silky, teasing, courtly — he bows, he flirts, he treats genocide as a footnote.

## Character: Basca Starker — "Dr. Jekyll" / the CSA scientist

**Capsule.** Native of the Corporate Sector Authority; rootless dilettante turned genetic
scientist specializing in longevity — looks twenty-something, is actually in his fifties via
his own Neoskin tech, which leads people to underestimate him (Article: Basca Starker). Runs
the shadowy, fully-mobile biotech firm **Tarchanjan Genetics**, maker of Neoskin (Article:
Basca Starker). Recruited by COMPNOR's Select Committee for a covert Force-weaponization
project, encountering **Zeak Oppenheimer** and the enigmatic "W" (Article: Basca Starker).
His infamy: at the command of the false Emperor **Aleister Vadim**, he engineered the
**Sariphage** plague that killed roughly 2.8 billion on Caspar (Article: Basca Starker;
RPlog:Starker Outlawed, 29 ABY).

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **The scientist of the Force-weapon project (background → 10 ABY).** COMPNOR hires
  Tarchanjan to study and weaponize the Force; Basca concludes there's a fundamental gap
  between galactic science and the Force's real mechanism (Article: Basca Starker). He brazenly
  talks his way into an audience with Emperor Vadim, who tasks him with the Caspar plague
  (Article: Basca Starker).
- **Wooing the Jedi (10 ABY / RL 2007).** After a damaging newscast, he seeks out Jessalyn
  Valios in the Mos Eisley market to "make an accounting of himself" — over tea he recounts
  the Empire's project and his deals with Oppenheimer; claims he traded Jedi Archive access to
  cure the Caspar Phage and withheld research from the CSA; then in the desert shares wine,
  kisses her, and lays out his "dark side as transfiguration" theory (RPlog:Basca Starker
  Meets Jessalyn Valios, or Dr. Jekyll Decides to Woo a Jedi, 10 ABY). This is the seed the
  2020 arc grows from.
- **The 'Lady Starker' romance (29 ABY / RL 2020).** Reconnected with Jessalyn on Caspar at
  his estate, **Starker Keep** on Yaeguero Isle. The household staff and locals dub her "Lady
  Starker" (RPlog:Love Story on Caspar, 29 ABY). He invites her endangered Jedi children to the
  Keep and offers to be trained and stand with her (RPlog:Starker Keep's New Gardener, 29 ABY);
  teaches her ballroom dancing on the beach and confides his secret theory that Vadim has
  transfigured into dark-side energy and is effectively immortal (RPlog:The Next Morning, and
  Dark Conversations, 29 ABY). They plan a black-and-gold gala to combine a street-naming in his
  honor with the public release of his Sariphage research, and acknowledge becoming lovers and
  allies (RPlog:Party Planning, and Other Pursuits, 29 ABY).
- **Exposure at Malign's court (29 ABY).** At Johanna's renovated Cloud City club, Johanna
  senses the dark presence inside him and reacts with quiet alarm (RPlog:Trouble on Cloud City,
  Part One, 29 ABY). When **Darth Malign**'s Imperium fleet blockades Bespin, Johanna probes
  Basca's mind at a state dinner and exposes the Sith spawn **Sov'stayn** and Vadim's presence;
  Basca confesses to the Sariphage genocide. Crestian Tarasar draws a blaster demanding death,
  but Johanna rules the spawn be *purged* rather than killing the man (RPlog:Trouble on Cloud
  City, Part Two -- Malign's Court, 29 ABY).
- **Into the underworld (29 ABY).** After Tyler/Malign exorcises the spawn, Basca and Jessalyn
  flee into Hutt Space aboard the *Tibanna Blues*; he insists the spawn Sov'stayn had no free
  will and that *he* chose to protect Jessalyn over Vadim, shows her the Cathedral Ichor chamber
  (hidden from the Force by ysalamiri), reveals a clone child **Hanna** and a pregnancy, and
  proposes (RPlog:Into the Underworld, 29 ABY).
- **Outlawed and killed (29 ABY).** Caspian Presav **Crestian Tarasar** publicly "outlaws"
  Basca — strips his honorary citizenship, seizes his property, and declares him a non-person
  who may be killed on sight (RPlog:Starker Outlawed, 29 ABY). Crestian also informs Jessalyn
  directly of his death (RPlog:Crestian's Condolences, 29 ABY). His estranged brother
  **Marius Bariss Starker** delivers Basca's body in a stasis tube to Caspar — having posted
  the bounty on his own brother — and the corpse is carbonite-frozen rather than vaporized;
  Marius then vows to find Jessalyn and pledges service to the Union (RPlog:Basca Starker's
  Fate, 29 ABY).
- **Coda (29 ABY / RL 2023).** A later cameo on Selene has a still-living, still-flippant Basca
  antagonizing Johanna's circle and discussing his plague work — this one logged by Tarasar
  (RPlog:Shopping with Sith, 29 ABY). [Continuity is loose here vs. "Basca Starker's Fate";
  read both before asserting a strict timeline of his death.]

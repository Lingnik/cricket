## Talking to them OOC

**Heads-up for the bot: Drayson Honos is an NPC, not somebody's character.** Nobody
"plays Drayson" the way someone plays Jessalyn or Johanna. He's the Empire's (later the
Imperium's) senior fleet admiral — a piece of furniture in the imperial military that
different players pose when they need top brass on stage. So you won't recognize a *person*
by "Drayson"; you'll recognize a **scene** (an Imperial fleet action or a coronation
function) and the *real* player running it. Two clusters matter:

- **The Danik Kreldin era (RL 2006–2008).** Drayson is the de-facto supreme commander of
  the Imperial Navy in the big invasion logs — Chandrila, Corellia — almost always
  alongside Admiral Danik Kreldin, who uploaded and created those pages (RPlog:The Second
  Battle of Chandrila, 14 ABY; RPlog:Fourth Battle of Corellia, 14 ABY; RPlog:Imperial
  Awards Ceremony, 13 ABY). If someone's reminiscing about the old Imperial fleet war
  against the New Republic, that's the Danik-adjacent crowd.
- **The Imperium coronation arc (RL 2023).** Drayson resurfaces as Darth Malign's senior
  military man during the Selene coronation storyline run by Crestian Tarasar and Elana
  Tracer's circle (RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY; RPlog:The Imperator's Coronation, 29 ABY;
  RPlog:War Council, 29 ABY). This is the freshest material and the most likely reason
  Drayson comes up today.

Safe conversation hooks:
- **The Cochran resignation.** The single most "characterful" fact about him: he and his
  whole command staff resigned in 14 ABY in protest of operations over Cochran, leaving the
  Empire short of tactical leadership (Article: Drayson Honos). Good lore-nerd bait.
- **The Grand Admiral he turned down.** Rumor that Drayson passed up the Grand Admiral
  promotion and let "the upstart Danik Kreldin" take it instead (Article: Drayson Honos) —
  a fun in-joke for anyone who remembers the Kreldin era.
- **His current role under Malign.** By the 2023 arc he's the uniformed face of "the
  military speaks with full authority" — Malign literally hands the room to "Drayson and
  Elena" and walks out (RPlog:Everything With Your Testicles Alright?, 32 ABY).

Tone to match when referencing him: dry, brass-tacks soldier with a sardonic streak — the
guy who rescues the Sith Lord from small talk and asks an Inquisitor "Everything with your
testicles alright?" (RPlog:Everything With Your Testicles Alright?, 32 ABY).

## Character: Drayson Honos — Imperial Fleet Admiral

**Capsule.** Raltiirian human (b. 50 BBY), white hair, light-blue eyes; Fleet Admiral of
the Imperial Navy and, later, supreme military commander of the Imperium / New Sith Order
(Article: Drayson Honos). Born to a wealthy Raltiir banking family; brother of Senator
Achillies Honos. Tagged {{NPC}} and {{char-stub}} on the wiki — a senior-officer NPC, not
a played character. Index footprint reads Imperial across the board: themes skew
**politics (9), space combat, intrigue, diplomacy (4 each), coronation (3)** — a fleet
commander who spends as much time at war councils and ceremonies as in battle.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Early Imperial supremacy (pre-13 ABY).** Responsible for much of the Empire's early
  successful operations and de-facto supreme commander of the Imperial Navy until Darth
  Malign's first disappearance into the Outer Rim (Article: Drayson Honos).
- **The big invasions (13–14 ABY).** Frontline admiral in the Imperial fleet war:
  decorations at the Conqueror award ceremony (RPlog:Imperial Awards Ceremony, 13 ABY);
  the EMP-led conquest of Corellia (RPlog:Fourth Battle of Corellia, 14 ABY); and the
  taking of Chandrila, where he orders the planetary shield down and civilians spared
  (RPlog:The Second Battle of Chandrila, 14 ABY).
- **The Cochran resignation (14 ABY).** Resigns from the Imperial First Fleet in protest of
  the Desolation of Cochran, taking his entire command staff with him and gutting Frontier
  Operations' leadership (Article: Drayson Honos). The defining principled-officer moment.
- **Sith service / Lord Regent (17 ABY).** Named Lord Regent of Ord Vaxal during Darth
  Malign's Sith invasion of that world (RPlog:Arrival, 17 ABY).
- **The Imperium coronation arc (29–32 ABY / RL 2023).** Senior military man during the
  coronation of Darth Malign as Imperator on Selene: field-promotes Kipran Jawatha to
  Commander (RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY), takes PR coaching from Crestian Tarasar for the
  festival (RPlog:The Imperator's Coronation, 29 ABY), and sits Malign's war council
  planning the Sullust deployment (RPlog:War Council, 29 ABY). By 32 ABY he's the uniformed
  voice of the Imperium's military at Caspian and Corellian functions, empowered to "speak
  with full authority" when Malign leaves the room (RPlog:Everything With Your Testicles
  Alright?, 32 ABY; RPlog:Shindig on Corellia, 32 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Darth Malign / Tyler Damion** — his Sith master/Imperator; the figure he serves across
  both eras. [Malign 8 shared logs, Tyler Damion 4 — same person; note this is **Tyler
  Damion / Darth Malign, Johanna's half-brother — NOT Darth Malus / Lorn Rhys**]
- **Danik Kreldin** — fellow admiral and the player behind Drayson's original Imperial-era
  appearances; the rumored Grand-Admiral rivalry. [3 shared logs]
- **Johanna Siri te Danaan** — Jedi adversary across both the invasion era (her brother
  hunts her at Corellia) and the 2023 arc (he's politely resentful of her treatment of his
  Corellia arrival). [7 shared logs]
- **Crestian Tarasar** & **Elana Tracer** — the Imperium-arc handlers; Crestian advises his
  PR, and Malign pairs "Drayson and Elena" as joint authority. [Crestian 7, Tracer 6]
- **Achillies Honos** — brother, Raltiir Senator (Article: Drayson Honos).

**Playstyle / themes.** Not a "playstyle" — an NPC role. He functions as the Empire's/
Imperium's authoritative military presence: invasions, fleet actions, award ceremonies, war
councils, and state functions. Whoever poses him plays him as competent, unflappable, and
dryly contemptuous of political theater.

**Signature voice.** Clipped soldier with gallows humor; the man who hauls the Imperator
out of a receiving line and needles the Grand Inquisitor — "Everything with your testicles
alright?" (RPlog:Everything With Your Testicles Alright?, 32 ABY). In the field, decisive
and not gratuitously cruel: he brings Chandrila's shield down but spares the civilians
(RPlog:The Second Battle of Chandrila, 14 ABY).

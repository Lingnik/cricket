## Talking to them OOC

This is a **politics-and-family player** — Elana's footprint is dense with diplomacy,
intrigue, coronations and council chambers, but the engine underneath nearly every scene
is **her kids**. She came back to public life chasing her eldest son; she keeps coming
back to protect all three. Expect prose-heavy two-handers, sharp dialogue, and a heroine
who is out of her depth among Sith lords and is the first to say so. Their comfort zone is
**a working-class hero navigating elite power** — wry, suspicious of titles, loyal to a
fault.

Safe conversation hooks:
- **The Imperium return arc** (their freshest material). Elana tracks her missing eldest,
  Kipran, to Selene and finds him serving Darth Malign; the Imperator then makes *her* Moff
  of Selene and CEO of the KDY shipyards (RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY; RPlog:Complicated
  Motivations, 29 ABY; RPlog:The Imperator's Coronation, 29 ABY). Great entry point — it's
  recent and it's the spine of everything she's done since.
- **The Tracer/Tarasar alliance.** Her single biggest scene partner is Caspian Presav
  **Crestian Tarasar**; their relationship goes from her open suspicion of a "dandy" Sith
  to a genuine working partnership (RPlog:Imperator Circle: Crestian & Tracer, 29 ABY).
  Crestian's player is active, so this is reliable shared ground.
- **The kids' courtship subplot.** Her son **Kipran Jawatha** woos Malign's daughter
  **Sonja Damion** — a Capulet-and-Montague thread that also entangles Johanna te Danaan
  (whose son is in Malign's service too) (RPlog:Johanna Has a Ball on Bespin, 29 ABY;
  RPlog:Jessa Drops a Bomb, 29 ABY; RPlog:Soft Open, 30 ABY).
- **Swoop racing.** Before the politics she was a racer and built a swoop empire; she still
  reaches for that life (the Marin Mountain resort reopening, the swoop-garage meetings)
  (RPlog:A Prudent Request, 29 ABY; RPlog:Atsvara's Hot Takes, 32 ABY). A lighter,
  low-stakes hook if the heavy politics aren't landing.

Tone to match: dry, defiant, self-deprecating — a woman who jogs alone past a statue of the
Imperator and mutters "What have I gotten myself into this time?" out loud (RPlog:Imperator
Circle: Crestian & Tracer, 29 ABY). Honest to the point of bluntness; allergic to pretension.

## Character: Elana Tracer — "Ellie"

**Capsule.** Caspian human (homeworld Caspar), black hair / blue eyes, 1.57m — a tomboy who
"just wanted to be a pilot" and ended up brushing shoulders with the most powerful sentients
in the galaxy (Article: Elana Tracer). Top A-wing pilot of the Caspian Marine Aerospace
Division → war hero → swoop-racing magnate → Presav of the Union → Moff of Selene under the
Imperium. Reads Caspian Union (14) / Sith (11) / Imperium (9), constantly orbiting Jedi (7)
and Empire (6) without belonging to either.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Racer on Caspar (8 ABY).** First appears as "the new racer Ellie Tracer" in Troy
  McTavish's swoop shop, with her brother Dak, while an Imperial agent sniffs around for war
  rumors (RPlog:Troy and Joir, 8 ABY).
- **War, captivity, and a marriage (pre-/post-Annexation).** Field-promoted during the
  Imperial War of Aggression; captured and held by the Imperial Bureau of Operations; helped
  lead the retaking of Caspia as a war hero. Married FLS CEO **Benjamin Jawatha**; their son
  **Kipran** is born, but trauma and Ben's absences sink the marriage (Article: Elana Tracer).
- **Affairs, divorce, and a swoop empire.** A long relationship with CSA officer **Morrison
  Van Sen** (two more children, Ashton and Longinus); she resigns her colonelcy, founds
  Repulsor Sports Inc. and the Galactic Swoop League off Troy McTavish's gifted garages
  (Article: Elana Tracer).
- **Soldier to stateswoman (13–14 ABY).** Serves as Vice Presav, then **Presav of the CDU** —
  then signs the Imperial *Proclamation of Annexation* despite the Assembly defeating it,
  drawing a rare countervote and forcing her from office. The article frames this as a
  sacrifice to protect those she loved; she steps aside for Thomas Mahon willingly
  (Article: Elana Tracer).
- **A decade hidden on Kalla.** Raises her younger two in anonymity on Morrison's gated Kalla
  estate — until Morrison goes missing on deployment and never returns, leaving her
  heartbroken (Article: Elana Tracer).
- **Cameo in the Jedi orbit (10 ABY).** Surfaces briefly in Jessalyn Valios's storyline,
  encountered outside the FLS offices after Jessa probes Eva Sargent's mind (RPlog:All About
  Eva, 10 ABY) — a thin early crossover, useful only as trivia.
- **The return (29 ABY / RL 2020).** Her eldest, Kipran, drops out of contact; she hunts him
  down to **Selene** and finds him newly made a Sith-uniformed Commander in Malign's service
  (RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY). Malign coronates as Imperator Damion I and offers her the
  governorship of Selene and the CSA-facing sector; a hungover, disillusioned Tracer agrees to
  consider it to stay close to her son (RPlog:The Imperator's Coronation, 29 ABY;
  RPlog:Complicated Motivations, 29 ABY).
- **Moff of Selene (29 ABY onward).** Takes the post and the KDY-SFS shipyards. Spars with
  Crestian until they ally (RPlog:Imperator Circle: Crestian & Tracer, 29 ABY); fronts
  Malign's hard-line demands to the Corporate Sector Authority's Prex Oppenheimer and then
  walks it back to a peaceful delegation (RPlog:The New Imperium and the Sector, 29 ABY);
  pushes decentralized shipyard production at Malign's war council (RPlog:War Council, 29 ABY);
  survives a suicide-bombing at the Caspar election count, shielded by Calyx Drogyn while
  knocked unconscious (RPlog:Election Night Mayhem, 29 ABY). Co-leads a covert rescue of
  Force-sensitive children from Trandoshan slavers on Spira under a "family vacation" cover
  (RPlog:Spiran Rescue, 29 ABY).
- **Family politics & society (30–34 ABY / RL 2023–25).** Moves her younger children to
  Caspar (RPlog:A Prudent Request, 29 ABY); navigates the Kipran/Sonja courtship through its
  next stages (RPlog:Soft Open, 30 ABY; RPlog:Coronation Pre-Game, 30 ABY); works the room as
  a Grand Moff at Caspian galas, wargames, and the Xi Char factory tour (RPlog:Everything With
  Your Testicles Alright?, 32 ABY; RPlog:Atsvara's Hot Takes, 32 ABY); reopens her Marin
  Mountain swoop resort; and, in costume as the "Sexy Red Guard," wins a contested painting at
  a Corellian charity masquerade (RPlog:Charity Ball, 34 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Crestian Tarasar** — Caspian Presav and Sith; her chief political ally after a wary start;
  the relationship that anchors her late footprint. [20 shared logs — her top scene partner]
- **Atsvara Tarasar** — Crestian's formidable young daughter (Baroness of House Dragonflower);
  recurs across the Caspian society scenes. [10 logs]
- **Johanna Siri te Danaan** — fellow estranged mother whose son also serves Malign; they make

  Shopping with Sith, 29 ABY). [9 logs]
- **Darth Malign (Tyler Damion)** — the Imperator who employs her and recruited her son; she

**Playstyle / themes** (from 25 logs): politics, intrigue and diplomacy dominate, threaded
constantly through **family** (her children's safety and choices) and the **coronation /
court** machinery of the new Imperium. Reads as a **character-driven political player** who
shows up for the set-pieces (galas, councils, a rescue op) but plays them as a mother and an
outsider rather than an ideologue.

**Signature voice.** Dry, blunt, working-class defiance under elite pressure; a "honeyed
voice rising in sarcasm" when she's being needled, and self-mocking when she isn't (RPlog:
Imperator Circle: Crestian & Tracer, 29 ABY). Pull a representative two-hander for cadence:
RPlog:Imperator Circle: Crestian & Tracer (29 ABY) for her sparring wit, or RPlog:The
Imperator's Coronation (29 ABY) for hungover, principled stubbornness in front of power.

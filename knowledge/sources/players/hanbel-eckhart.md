## Character: Han'bel Eckhart — Colonel "H.S. Eckhart," the rogue Marine

**Capsule.** Corellian human male; grey hair, blue eyes, 6'3"; a New Republic Marine
Corps colonel originally based out of Dreven (Article: Han'bel Eckhart). Cigar-chewing,
caff-fueled, gruff with civilians but fiercely protective of the people under his charge
(RPlog:The Great Escape, 32 ABY). Faction-wise he reads New Republic by origin but rogue
"Templar" by deed; the crew brushes against pirates, the Hutt Cartel, and the Caspian
Union as allies of convenience.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **The last straw → defection (31 ABY).** Patrolling out of Dreven, Eckhart got a
  distress call from Gorsh in Wild Space; ordered to ignore it and return to base, he
  judged the Republic had abandoned its role in the galaxy and chose a different path.
  Within weeks he and the crew went rogue, taking the *Templar* with them
  (Article: Han'bel Eckhart).
- **The cause célèbre (32 ABY).** The Republic branded them dangerous criminals, but
  public opinion split; reporting on the *Templar*'s good deeds and the corruption that
  drove the defection built Thurston Unger's *The Cutting Edge* programme, sparked
  protests, and ultimately pushed Dreven itself back to the Imperium
  (Article: Han'bel Eckhart).
- **Anti-pirate raids (32 ABY).** Eckhart leads the *Templar* crew and civilian allies
  against pirates operating from an old asteroid-belt mining base — X-wings escorting a
  shuttle, storm the base, neutralize the threat (RPlog:Templars: Allies versus Pirates -
  Part 1 & Part 2, 32 ABY). The aftermath all-hands — reviewing swiped data, ~two dozen
  freed captives — is the "hooch and planning" debrief (RPlog:Hooch and Planning, 32 ABY).
- **The Spycraft caper (32 ABY).** The crew plans and executes a rescue of 40-50 slaves
  from a live auction at the House of Sabacc on Nar Shaddaa — Eckhart splitting the team
  into A/B/C cells, stealth wet-work, flashbang room-clears, and a three-lift evacuation
  onto Tiaryn's *Bright Star* and a commandeered Gamma-class shuttle
  (RPlog:Spycraft - The Planning, 32 ABY; RPlog:The Great Escape, 32 ABY).
- **Public figure / off-screen capture (32–34 ABY).** Eckhart's name circulates in wider
  galactic politics — Wrexan is described as "more or less in the employ of Eckhart" at a
  Corellia gala, and Darth Malign cites "brave freedom fighters under the command of
  Colonel Han'bel Eckhart" in a Caspian Council speech (RPlog:Shindig on Corellia, 32 ABY;
  RPlog:Everything With Your Testicles Alright?, 32 ABY). Per the article, the *Templar*
  was eventually captured by NR forces in 34 ABY and Eckhart taken into custody
  (Article: Han'bel Eckhart).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Siarra Krell** — his quartermaster / EOD specialist; "Boss," the most frequent
  scene partner and the author of most of his logs. [5 shared logs]
- **Blulurbgloblurb Druze** — the crew's chief engineer and reluctant combat medic, kept

- **Wrexan** — Mirialan pilot flying AirCap; "more or less in the employ of Eckhart."

- **Tiaryn Elatar** — civilian pilot of the *Bright Star*, volunteer shuttle/exfil pilot.
  [4 shared logs]
- **Kelnas** — Wroonian ex-con turned blade specialist ("Mr. Red") on the crew. [4 logs]

**Playstyle / themes** (from 5 logs): piracy and slavery (3 each) drive the arc, then
rescue, stealth, military planning, boarding/raid actions. Reads as a **tactical
mission-runner** — the character exists to give the ensemble objectives, brief them, and
lead from the front.

**Signature voice.** Gruff, profane, no-nonsense colonel; cigar between the teeth, mug of
caff in hand. "I want honesty, not heroics" (RPlog:Spycraft - The Planning, 32 ABY). To
his refugees: "If I say run, you fucking run like your life depends on it, because it
will" (RPlog:The Great Escape, 32 ABY). Protective beneath the bark — "It wasn't his job
to be their friend, it was his job to be their guardian" (RPlog:The Great Escape, 32 ABY).

## Character: Kasha Tabasuri — "the Woodcarving Feline"

**Capsule.** Mashi Horansi female (b. 12 ABY, Mutanda), 1.7m, black fur, purple eyes. A
child-prodigy spiritual advisor turned wandering charity worker: Mutandan Spiritual Advisor
(on sabbatical), Emissary of the Caspian Foreign Ministry, founding member of The Mission, and
personal advisor/friend to Presav Crestian Tarasar of Caspar (Article: Kasha Tabasuri).
Faction-wise she reads Caspian Union (7 logs) with New Republic and The Mission threads.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Child of the council (12–18 ABY).** Born to a single mother who served as spiritual
  advisor on Mutanda's Council of Elders; brought to the chambers as a girl, she discovered a
  talent for wood carving and a habit of bluntly telling the elders their ideas were stupid —
  which won them over (Article: Kasha Tabasuri).
- **The youngest advisor (18–27 ABY).** Made an honorary councilor at 6, given the surname
  Tabasuri ("become wise") at 8, and primary spiritual advisor at 12 when her mother fell ill;
  orphaned just before her 13th birthday, she kept her seat by special dispensation (Article:
  Kasha Tabasuri).
- **The search for her father (27 ABY).** At 15 a message claiming to be from the father she
  never knew lured her offworld; she found only a datapad suggesting he was in trouble, and set
  off to find him (Article: Kasha Tabasuri).
- **Stranded on Tatooine (27–28 ABY).** Out of money, she spent a year in Mos Eisley as a
  produce trader, whittling animal figurines for the patrons' children and earning the nickname
  "the Woodcarving Feline" (Article: Kasha Tabasuri).
- **Recruited to The Mission (28 ABY → ).** Crestian Tarasar met her at her stall and brought
  her into his new charity, getting her off Tatooine with the promise she could resume the
  search for her father; she left with her stranded Lorrdian pilot friend **Tiali Nora**
  (Article: Kasha Tabasuri).
- **Caspian aide & the Bespin/Youngling plot (29 ABY).** Now an aide in Crestian's circle, she
  works the relief and reunion side of the big shared storyline — collecting parents for a
  Youngling reunion on Caspar, attending the Youngling Ball, and present when slavers attack the
  Miners' Dinner on Cloud City (RPlog:Mission to Ord Mantell, 29 ABY; RPlog:Johanna Has a Ball
  on Bespin, 29 ABY; RPlog:Bespin Blows Up!, 29 ABY; RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY).
- **The Selene/Caspar years (30–32 ABY).** Part of Crestian's retinue at the Prothium Club soft
  open and on the ground for the Coronet City prison-break incident (RPlog:Soft Open, 30 ABY;
  RPlog:Corellia Prison Break, 32 ABY).
- **Friendships and full circles (33–34 ABY).** A not-entirely-social call from old friends
  Atsvara and Veruca Violet in the Killaniri System; then a sentimental return to Tatooine, and
  back-to-back charity events for The Mission (RPlog:Life Begins Young, 33 ABY; RPlog:Full
  Circle, 34 ABY; RPlog:Fashion Show, 34 ABY; RPlog:Charity Ball, 34 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):

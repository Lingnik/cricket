## Talking to them OOC

This is a **supporting-crew player** with a small but consistent footprint — Kelnas
shows up across one self-contained 2023 storyline: the *Templar* crew's hunt for slavers
running captives through Nar Shaddaa, and the Spycraft con/rescue that ends it. So keep
expectations grounded; this isn't a decades-deep PC, it's a sharply-drawn **scoundrel
with a past** who slots into Siarra Krell and Wrexan's crew scenes.

Their comfort zone is **caper-and-crew banter with a dark undercurrent**: smuggling,
underworld contacts, blade work, and the lingering damage of having done hard time. The
voice is jokey on the surface, but the writer leans into real trauma when it counts.

Safe conversation hooks:
- **The Spycraft arc** (freshest in their mind): posing as buyers to scope a slave
  auction under the House of Sabacc casino on Nar Shaddaa, then raiding it — Kelnas and
  Siarra ran the stealth/wet-work team (RPlog:Spycraft - The Planning, 2023; RPlog:The
  Great Escape, 2023).
- **Kelnas's Nar Shaddaa history.** He worked the moon for years before "becoming a guest
  of the government" — old contacts, an old broker named Hargsbad, the disguise of *not*
  wearing a disguise (RPlog:Spycraft - Part 1, 2023).
- **The crew chemistry.** His running double-act with Druze, the explosives-hobbyist
  Wroonian, and his easy rapport with Siarra are the most reliable shared-history threads
  (RPlog:Spycraft - Part 1, 2023; RPlog:Templars: Allies versus Pirates - Part 2, 32 ABY).

Tone to match: dry, deflecting humor over a hard edge — the kind of guy who trades watches
with a peddler mid-mission for fun, but says "Just because you can, doesn't mean you
should" and means it (RPlog:Spycraft - Part 1, 2023).

## Character: Kelnas — Wroonian ex-con on the *Templar*

**Capsule.** Blue-skinned, yellow-eyed Wroonian (RPlog:Spycraft - Part 1, 2023;
RPlog:Spycraft - The Planning, 2023). A trader/scoundrel and ex-convict — explicitly an
ex-prisoner who still recites his inmate number on reflex ("38753-558 warden. Counted and
present") when a confined space triggers him (RPlog:Templars: Allies versus Pirates -
Part 2, 32 ABY). Better with a blade than a blaster, by his own account
(RPlog:Spycraft - The Planning, 2023). Sails with the Corellia-based *Templar* ("the
Pregnant Guppy") crew on their anti-slaver campaign. No standalone character article
exists — only a wiki log-index page, "Kelnas' Roleplay Logs."

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Boarding the pirate rig (32 ABY).** With Eckhart's team he boards an abandoned pirate
  mining base in an asteroid belt; he and the rock-being Samyok work the upper deck and
  loot a cargo room of supplies, weapons, and credits, bailing out just before a cryoban
  grenade blows the rig (RPlog:Templars: Allies versus Pirates - Part 2, 32 ABY). The
  enclosed slaver-smell sends him into a flashback to prison — Siarra talks him down
  (same log).
- **Hooch and planning (2023).** At the post-raid all-hands he reveals knowledge of three
  working chop-shop yards (two Hutt-owned); his bait-with-a-tracker plan is rejected for
  an intel-first stealth approach, and he's tapped with Druze to scout Nar Shaddaa
  (RPlog:Hooch and Planning, 2023).
- **Spycraft: the con (2023).** On Nar Shaddaa he works as the crew's underworld fixer —
  banters in trader's argot, leans on his old contact network, and offers to spend ~5,000
  credits and his connection to the broker Hargsbad to get the team into the auction
  viewing (RPlog:Spycraft - Part 1, 2023; RPlog:Spycraft - The Viewing, 2023).
- **Spycraft: the raid (2023).** He volunteers for the quiet wet-work — "I'm better with a
  blade than I am a blaster" — and is paired with Siarra as the stealth Team A for the
  slave-pen rescue under the House of Sabacc (RPlog:Spycraft - The Planning, 2023). In the
  raid the crew silently kills the guards, frees ~40 captives, and escapes
  (RPlog:The Great Escape, 2023).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Siarra Krell** — *Templar* marine; his most frequent scene partner, stealth-team
  buddy, and the one who grounds him through his prison flashback (RPlog:Templars: Allies
  versus Pirates - Part 2, 32 ABY; RPlog:Spycraft - The Planning, 2023). [6 shared logs]
- **Blulurbgloblurb Druze** — Wroonian crewmate and demolitions hobbyist; their banter is
  Kelnas's signature comic pairing (RPlog:Spycraft - Part 1, 2023). [6 shared logs]
- **Wrexan** — ranking officer / field lead on the Nar Shaddaa runs; played "Mr. Greene"
  the buyer in the con (RPlog:Spycraft - The Viewing, 2023). [5 shared logs]
- **Han'bel Eckhart** — the Colonel running the raids; assigns Kelnas to the stealth team
  (RPlog:Spycraft - The Planning, 2023). [4 shared logs]
- **Tiaryn Elatar** — *Bright Star* pilot / extraction ship across the arc. [5 shared logs]
- **Samyok** — rock-being crewmate; his looting partner on the pirate rig
  (RPlog:Templars: Allies versus Pirates - Part 2, 32 ABY).
- **Zalla** — the NovaCom/Duros contact on Nar Shaddaa who builds the crew's fake buyer
  identities (RPlog:Spycraft - Part 1, 2023).

**Playstyle / themes** (from 6 logs): slavery/anti-slavery (the spine of every scene),
then espionage, infiltration, stealth, smuggling, and piracy. Reads as a **heist/crew
support player** — provides underworld texture (contacts, argot, chop-shop lore), takes
the knife-in-the-dark role, and supplies comic relief that masks real damage.

**Signature voice.** Wisecracking scoundrel with a conscience and buried trauma. He'll
swap a watch with a peddler three times for the sport of it (RPlog:Spycraft - Part 1,
2023) and joke about medical-lubricant slip-and-slides mid-mission (RPlog:Templars:
Allies versus Pirates - Part 2, 32 ABY) — then drop to his knees reciting his inmate
number when a slaver hold smells too much like prison (same log). On his ethics: "I never
had any love for slavers" (RPlog:Spycraft - Part 1, 2023). On restraint: "Just because you
can, doesn't mean you should" (same log).

## Talking to them OOC

There's **no separate "Kipran player"** to talk to — he's an **NPC created and run by
[[Elana Tracer]]'s player** (the article says so outright: "Kipran is an NPC ... created by
Elana Tracer's player. Please contact her for anything regarding Kipran or his siblings")
(Article: Kipran Jawatha). So if Kipran comes up OOC, you're really talking to **Tracer's
player** about her family-saga cast. Treat him as a thread *into* Tracer's story, not a
standalone bench.

The Tracer player's comfort zone here is **multigenerational family melodrama braided into
faction politics**: Caspian Union roots, kids who keep drifting toward the Sith, and the
slow tragedy of a mother (who fears Force users) watching her Force-sensitive eldest get
recruited by Darth Malign (Article: Kipran Jawatha). Expect interest in continuity across
the whole te Danaan / Damion / Tarasar web, since Kipran only ever shows up in those big
shared scenes.

Safe conversation hooks:
- **The "our sons serve Malign" beat.** The emotional core is Tracer and Johanna both
  arriving on Selene to find their eldest sons in Darth Malign's service
  (RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY). That's the scene to reference.
- **Kipran + Sonja Damion.** His romance with **Sonja Damion** — Malign's daughter and heir,
  Crown Princess of the Imperium and Hapes — is the running plot; she's the through-line in
  his later appearances (Article: Kipran Jawatha; RPlog:Soft Open, 30 ABY; RPlog:Tacuba the
  Hutt's Flying Circus, 33 ABY).
- **The Crestian Tarasar fleet.** Kipran serves under **Crestian Tarasar**, so he's a fixture
  in Tarasar/Tracer joint ops (RPlog:Spiran Rescue, 29 ABY) — and Tarasar's player uploads
  most of his logs.

Tone to match: warm family-saga energy; this is a player invested in **legacy, lineage, and
the pull of the dark side on the next generation**, not in spotlighting a single PC.

## Character: Kipran Jawatha — Elana Tracer's son who serves Darth Malign

**Capsule.** Human/Sarian male, b. on/near Caspar; dark brown hair, blue eyes, fair skin
with orange "sar spots" (Article: Kipran Jawatha). First-born child of **[[Elana Tracer]]**
and her late husband **[[Benjamin Jawatha]]** ("Benjawa"). A latent Force user from
childhood whose mother — herself fearful of Force users — tried to suppress the gift; he grew
up to be recruited into the Sith Court and **Darth Malign's** orbit, rising to **Commander
then Captain in the Imperium navy** under Admiral Crestian Tarasar (Article: Kipran Jawatha).
Faction-wise he reads Sith / Galactic Imperium with Caspian Union family roots.

**IMPORTANT disambiguation:** Kipran serves **Darth Malign = Tyler Damion** (Johanna's
half-brother, Imperator, Sonja's father) — *not* Darth Malus (Lorn Rhys). Do not conflate them.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Troubled childhood (pre-29 ABY).** Born during a storm-blackout party thrown by Morganna
  Tazecks; repeatedly lost one parent or another — Elana stranded after a YT-2400 breakdown,
  father Benjamin (a kidnapped Sarian Dark-side bloodline whose talent skipped him) found
  murdered in the FLS Tower in 9 ABY. Raised partly on Kalla with half-siblings Ashton and
  Longinus Van Sen; learned to repulsor-surf, ride swoops, and pilot from his mother. Elana
  spent his childhood trying to curb his "gift" (Article: Kipran Jawatha; Article: Benjamin
  Jawatha).
- **Dropout → the Sith Court (~18 yrs old).** Left University on Kalla, took a trust and
  wandered, fell in with members of the Sith Court and reached **Darth Malign**, who trained
  him; **Admiral Drayson Honos** mentored him. He built his lightsaber and was commissioned
  Commander in the new Imperium navy, assigned to Crestian Tarasar's fleet — at which point
  his mother took a Moff position from Malign to stay near him (Article: Kipran Jawatha).
- **"Motherhood" — the discovery (29 ABY).** Tracer and Johanna arrive on Selene to find
  *both* their eldest sons serving Malign — the defining family-tragedy scene
  (RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY).
- **Spiran Rescue → Captain (29 ABY).** Joins Tracer and Tarasar on a Caspian–Imperium op to
  Spira to rescue two Force-sensitive children from Trandoshan raiders; promoted to Captain
  for his part (RPlog:Spiran Rescue, 29 ABY; Article: Kipran Jawatha).
- **The Bespin ball → meeting Sonja (29 ABY).** At Johanna te Danaan's ball for
  Force-sensitive youth he meets and begins falling for **Sonja Damion**, Malign's daughter
  (RPlog:Johanna Has a Ball on Bespin, 29 ABY; Article: Kipran Jawatha).
- **Malign's court at Cloud City (29 ABY).** Present for the court's judgment of Basca
  Starker on Cloud City — Kipran in the Imperium-side ensemble around Malign
  (RPlog:Trouble on Cloud City, Part Two -- Malign's Court, 29 ABY).
- **Courtship made public (30 ABY).** With Malign's tacit approval he courts the
  sixteen-year-old Sonja; appears as her escort at her Coronation as Crown Princess of the
  Imperium and Hapes; reassigned to the Core to facilitate it (Article: Kipran Jawatha;
  RPlog:Coronation Pre-Game, 30 ABY; RPlog:Soft Open, 30 ABY).
- **Quiet elopement to Tatooine (later).** Kipran and Sonja take "leave" to visit her aunt
  Johanna's homeworld, never file a return flight plan, and effectively drop out of the
  Imperium — joining Johanna's Force-user school in Jabba's old palace; rumored elopement
  (Article: Kipran Jawatha; RPlog:Tacuba the Hutt's Flying Circus, 33 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Elana Tracer** — mother; the whole point of the character. [6 shared logs]
- **Crestian Tarasar** — his commanding admiral; Kipran's most frequent scene context.
  [7 shared logs]
- **Darth Malign (Tyler Damion)** — recruiter, trainer, would-be father-in-law. [4 logs]
- **Sonja Damion** — Malign's daughter, his love interest / likely wife. [3 logs]
- **Johanna Siri te Danaan** — Sonja's aunt and eventual mentor; hosts the ball where the
  romance starts. [4 logs]
- **Drayson Honos** — his Imperium-navy mentor (Article: Kipran Jawatha).
- **Benjamin Jawatha** — murdered father; the Sarian Dark-side bloodline. (Article: Benjamin Jawatha)
- **Longinus & Ashton Van Sen** — his half-siblings (also Tracer's NPC kids).

**Playstyle / themes** (from the indexed logs): politics dominates (5), then diplomacy,
family/family-drama, rescue, and Jedi-vs-Sith. He functions as an **ensemble NPC in
large-cast political and family scenes** — coronations, balls, courts, joint rescues —
rather than carrying solo two-handers.

**Signature voice.** A young man pulled between a mother who fears the Force and a Sith lord
who gave him rank, a lightsaber, and a princess. Earnest, ambitious, dutiful to the
Imperium chain of command; soft where Sonja is concerned. For cadence, pull the family
confrontation in RPlog:Motherhood (29 ABY) or his Core-society appearance in RPlog:Soft
Open (30 ABY).

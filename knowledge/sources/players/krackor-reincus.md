## Talking to them OOC

This is a **classic scoundrel player** — Krackor is an Original Feature Character smuggler,
pitched in-universe as the man who inherited the "smuggler chief" mantle from **Talon Karrde**
after Karrde's disappearance (Article: Krackor Reincus). The fun here is **underworld
texture**: deals, contacts, ships, the morally-gray edge of the law. Not a duelist or a
faction soldier — a fixer who knows everyone and survives everything. Long memory for the
old crew; he keeps coming back.

Safe conversation hooks:

- **The Edgecrusher and the smuggling network.** His upgraded ILC *Edgecrusher* is his
  signature; he moved to Smuggler's Run, talked his way into an "administrative" role, and
  now hires legwork out instead of flying every run himself (Article: Krackor Reincus). A
  two-hander aboard the ship is his comfort zone (RPlog:Blood Brothers, 25 ABY).
- **Jabba's Palace.** His origin is *seven years* trapped in Jabba's Palace as a kid, earned
  his place as a spy, became a smuggler for the Hutt (Article: Krackor Reincus). Later the
  Hutt Council *gifted him the old Tatooine palace* — which Johanna te Danaan then turned
  into a university, a sore spot he ribs her about (RPlog:Shindig on Corellia, 32 ABY).
- **The Malign years.** He rode with the New Sith Order / Neo-Imperial crowd and counts the
  fall of **Darth Malign**'s operation as the thing he spent years running from; he can't say
  the name without a flinch (RPlog:Blood Brothers, 25 ABY). Good shared-history thread with
  the Tarasar/Drax players. *(Darth Malign = Tyler Damion — not Darth Malus / Lorn Rhys.)*
- **The current social scene.** Lately he turns up at galas, fashion shows, and charity balls
  on Corellia in the orbit of Zubindi Hakoon's Silverwood House and the Tarasars — a fish out
  of water in a tux, ducking the cameras (RPlog:Charity Ball, 34 ABY; RPlog:Fashion Show, 34 ABY).

Tone to match: dry, unbothered, transactional warmth — "no one is trying to kill me. Life is
good" (RPlog:Blood Brothers, 25 ABY).

## Character: Krackor Reincus — "the new smuggler chief"

**Capsule.** Tatooine-born human (b. 16 BBY), 1.89m, light brown hair, blue-gray eyes; an
independent smuggler and businessman who lives on the edge of the law "all in the name of
adventure and, of course, credits" (Article: Krackor Reincus). Faction tags read like a
résumé of the underworld: Bothan Spynet, Com-Cor, Karflo, Neo-Imperial Movement, New Sith
Order, Silent Interest Network, Smuggler's Run, the Tatooine Hutt Parliament (Article).

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Lost child of Jabba's Palace (childhood).** Separated from his moisture-farmer family in
  Mos Eisley at age eight, dumped in Jabba's Palace on a Hutt's whim, he survived by spying
  for Jabba and earned a place — staying *by choice* and becoming the Hutt's smuggler
  (Article: Krackor Reincus).
- **Com-Cor and S.I.N. (businessman years).** With friend Ewan Dain he built Com-Cor, a comms
  company fronting the Silent Interest Network smuggling ring, then handed it off and joined
  the Tatooine Hutt Parliament — "like a second family" (Article: Krackor Reincus).
- **Enslaved at the Palace (10 ABY).** Present at the Tarba/Begula-the-Hutt era; in the scene
  where Jessalyn Valios is sold into slavery, Krackor is one of the Hutt-side players
  (RPlog:Enslaved, 10 ABY). Later, wounded and overlooked in the ruined palace, he is himself
  taken and nearly auctioned as a slave before old underworld contacts spring him — after
  which he stages weapons and a reserve ship on Bothawui (Article: Krackor Reincus).
- **Working the cantinas and digs (14–17 ABY).** Doing business out of the Smuggler's Dig
  (RPlog:Encounter in the Dig, 14 ABY) and Chalmun's, brokering with odd clients
  (RPlog:The Right Price, 17 ABY); on the floor at the New Republic's End Ceremony (16 ABY).
  In the same window his player ran the gang heavies for someone else's op as a courtesy
  (RPlog:Raid on Tatooine, 16 ABY — "Special thanks to Sekes and Krackor for playing the bad
  guys").
- **The Sith detour and the long quiet (the Malign era).** Rode with the Knights of Korriban
  / New Sith Order crowd; when Darth Malign's operation collapsed he scattered and stayed off
  the grid for years (RPlog:Blood Brothers, 25 ABY).
- **Return of the smuggler (25 ABY → present).** Resurfaces, re-establishes the old codes;
  paid by Drax Rendolen for a fix on a missing person, both of them ex-Karrde, both
  Malign-scarred (RPlog:Blood Brothers, 25 ABY). The Hutt Council gifts him Jabba's old
  palace — overrun by pirates — and he sets about reclaiming it and reconnecting with old
  friends and the new underworld players (Article: Krackor Reincus).
- **Corellia high society (32–34 ABY).** Settling on Corellia "for the time being"
  (RPlog:Blood Brothers, 25 ABY); shows up — reluctantly dressed up — at Johanna te Danaan's
  charity gala (RPlog:Shindig on Corellia, 32 ABY) and the Silverwood crowd's fashion show and
  Arkanis Sector relief ball (RPlog:Fashion Show; RPlog:Charity Ball, both 34 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Johanna Siri te Danaan** — frenemy; she took his Tatooine palace and made it a school,
  and he never lets it go, but he shows for her events (RPlog:Shindig on Corellia, 32 ABY).
- **Drax Rendolen** — old comrade from the Knights of Korriban / Karrde days; a paying client
  and a "blood brother" survivor of the Malign era (RPlog:Blood Brothers, 25 ABY).
- **Talon Karrde** — the legacy he's said to have inherited; both he and Drax once worked for
  Karrde (Article; RPlog:Blood Brothers, 25 ABY).
- **The Tarasar circle (Crestian, Atsvara) & Zubindi Hakoon** — his current social scene on
  Corellia (RPlog:Shindig on Corellia, 32 ABY; RPlog:Charity Ball; RPlog:Fashion Show, 34 ABY).
- **Lynae Cassius, Rasi Praddhan Cen, Malif Tal-Tahn, Alistair Al'Dira** — recurring
  cantina/event co-stars across eras (RPlog:End Ceremony, 16 ABY; RPlog:The Right Price, 17 ABY).

**Playstyle / themes** (from the logged set): espionage and smuggling lead, then
politics/diplomacy and the social-gala circuit — a connector who works rooms and back rooms,
not battle lines. Note his stated ethic: he deals in contraband of all kinds, "but he has a
peculiar way of looking at" slaves — he won't sell to slavers, only to those who'll free them
or to willing participants (Article: Krackor Reincus). Useful nuance if slavery comes up.

**Signature voice.** Laconic, wry, a cigara always going; transactional but not cold; quietly
allergic to being noticed. Pull the cadence from his reunion with Drax — pours a drink, offers
spice "Carsunum? Glitterstim? Deathsticks?", and sums up his life as "no one is trying to kill
me. Life is good" (RPlog:Blood Brothers, 25 ABY). At a gala he's "the old smuggler" trying to
step behind Johanna and away from the cameras (RPlog:Shindig on Corellia, 32 ABY).

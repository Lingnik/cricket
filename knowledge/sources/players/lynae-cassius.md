## Talking to them OOC

This is a **long-haul Imperial-and-intrigue player** who came up in the mid-2000s fleet
era and is **freshly active again** in the 2023–2025 stretch, so they care about continuity
and have a deep back-catalog to draw on. Lynae's wheelhouse is **politics, intrigue, and
diplomacy** (her three top themes by a wide margin), filtered through a **soldier-physician**
lens — interrogation, imprisonment, redemption, and the moral cost of service all recur.
Expect dry, cerebral, talky scenes (long two-handers over caf) as much as fleet battles.

Safe conversation hooks:
- **The Imperial-officer-to-exile arc.** Lynae rose from medical officer to Commodore in
  Imperial taskforces, then was stripped of citizenship in the "Corellian Corruption
  Scandal," tried for war crimes by the Republic Senate, and chose public penance over a
  trial (Article: Lynae Cassius; RPlog:The Penance of Lynae Cassius, 16 ABY). It's a
  redemption-and-reinvention spine they clearly enjoy.
- **The 2025 return arc** (freshest in their mind): Lynae as the doctor who **thaws and
  rehabilitates Darth Malus (Lorn Rhys)** after twenty years in carbonite — a slow-burn
  doctor-and-Sith dynamic running across many 34 ABY logs (RPlog:Freezer Burn, 34 ABY;
  RPlog:Coffee Talk, 34 ABY; RPlog:And That's No Small Thing, 34 ABY). **Note: Darth Malus =
  Lorn Rhys, NOT Darth Malign.**
- **Caspar / the Caspian Union.** After her Imperial exile she took Caspian citizenship and
  made Caspar home; she recently formally retired from the Caspian Navy (RPlog:Resign or
  Retire, 34 ABY). Crestian Tarasar (the Presav) is a frequent current scene partner.
- **The Malif alt.** If they bring up Malif Tal-Tahn — the grey-skinned Darkling information
  broker working for Darth Malign — you can connect the dots; the two characters share scenes
  (RPlog:Neutral Ground, 16 ABY).

Tone to match: dry, clinical, faintly sardonic, rules-minded — "There are rules for a
reason. They are meant to be followed." (Article: Lynae Cassius). Her quotes file leans into
gallows humor and clever circular logic (Article: Lynae's Quotes).

## Character: Lynae Cassius — "Doc"

**Capsule.** Human woman, b. 9 BBY on Carida; black hair, blue eyes; callsign "Doc," rank
Admiral. Daughter of staunchly loyalist Imperial parents (a TIE pilot father, an Imperial
medical-researcher mother), Carida Medical Academy graduate — a **physician and scientist
who became a fleet officer** (Article: Lynae Cassius). Faction-wise she reads Imperial first
(85 logs), then New Republic (66) and Caspian Union (46), brushing constantly against Sith
(40) and Jedi (32).

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Imperial daughter → officer (13–14 ABY).** Raised aboard research stations and ships;
  mother killed when her station is overrun in 13 ABY; marries fellow officer **Yoseph
  Caiton** (a TIE pilot) late 13 ABY as a marriage of political expedience (Article: Lynae
  Cassius). Posted to Taskforce Hammer 14 ABY as a medical officer, she climbs from chief of
  medicine to acting captain to Commodore of Taskforce Inquisition.
- **The Cochran atrocity (14 ABY).** As Captain Lynae Cassius-Caiton aboard the VSD
  *Malignant*, she takes part in Grand Admiral Danik Kreldin's treacherous Base Delta Zero
  bombardment of Cochran (RPlog:Death of a Planet, 14 ABY) — the kind of act that later
  defines her war-crimes reckoning.
- **Fall, exile, penance (15–16 ABY).** The "Corellian Corruption Scandal" ends her Imperial
  career; by late 15 ABY her citizenship is revoked. Yoseph dies in a hover-taxi accident and
  she reclaims the name **Cassius** (Article; RPlog:Conversations Act1, 15 ABY). Standing
  before the Republic Senate on war-crimes charges in 16 ABY, she offers "flesh and blood" —
  submitting to lashes administered by the senators — and is pardoned (RPlog:The Penance of
  Lynae Cassius, 16 ABY).
- **Reinvention as a Caspian (16–17 ABY).** She takes Caspian citizenship, joins the Caspian
  Navy, and helps lead a Caspian/Imperial-style mock-battle rout of the Republic Navy — aided
  by **Tyler Damion (Darth Malign)** flying the fighter elements (RPlog:Unexpected Arrival,
  16 ABY). Rises to Lt Commander, XO of the CMS *Audace* (Article).
- **The long research years (17–32 ABY).** Resigns her commission; refits her ship the *Red
  Sky* as a deep-space bio-medical lab and disappears into kolto/bacta tissue-regeneration
  research, raising a son largely off the grid. Briefly surfaces during this stretch (e.g.
  as **Lynae Cassius-Mahon**, RPlog:A Second Attack, 18 ABY).
- **The return (32–34 ABY / RL 2023–2025).** Reconnects with old comrades as the Empire gives
  way to the Imperium and Caspar steadies; reads as Admiral and, by 34 ABY, retiring from the
  Caspian Navy (RPlog:Resign or Retire, 34 ABY). Her current marquee thread: **revives and
  rehabilitates the carbonite-damaged Darth Malus (Lorn Rhys)** aboard the *Red Sky*
  (RPlog:Freezer Burn, 34 ABY; RPlog:Coffee Talk, 34 ABY; RPlog:And That's No Small Thing,
  34 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Malif Tal-Tahn** — her own player's alt; they share scenes. [11 shared logs]
- **Darth Malign (Tyler Damion)** — recurring patron/ally from the Caspian era onward.
  [14 shared logs] *(NOT Darth Malus — different character/player.)*
- **Darth Malus (Lorn Rhys)** — the carbonite-revived Sith she nurses back in the 2025 arc;

- **Crestian Tarasar** — Caspian Presav; frequent current scene partner. [14 logs]
- **Antoine Dareus / Rasi Praddhan Cen / Kyrin Sh'vani / Ikihsa Enb'Zik / Axel Vichten** —
  her dense Imperial-and-Caspian supporting cast across the run.
- **Yoseph Caiton** — first husband (political marriage), died 15 ABY.
- **Thomas Mahon** — associated with the "Cassius-Mahon" name period (18 ABY); 6 shared logs.

**Playstyle / themes** (from ~123 logs): politics (47) and intrigue (24) and diplomacy (21)
dominate, then space combat (20), interrogation (17), imprisonment (11), the Force (11),
philosophy (11), torture (10), redemption (9). Reads as a **politics-and-intrigue player**
with a soldier-physician's eye for control, consequence, and rehabilitation.

**Signature voice.** Dry, clinical, stubborn — clasps her hands behind her back and delivers
a "not-exactly-critical" clinical assessment with a soldier's deadpan: she pegs the
just-thawed Malus's "it's just a flesh wound" bravado as "some sort of mentality that is
beat into soldiers of all kind until you actually believe it" (RPlog:Coffee Talk, 34 ABY).
Her quotes file pairs Imperial cynicism with mathematician's wit (Article: Lynae's Quotes).

## Character: Malif Tal-Tahn

**Capsule.** Male **Darkling** (silver hair, purple eyes, dark grey-black skin), b. on a
rim world near the Corporate Sector Authority. A career secrets-man: CSA Office of the
Auditor General investigator → underworld-immersed information broker → black-market arms
fixer → eventually an **Admiral in Darth Malign's paramilitary syndicate** (Article: Malif
Tal-Tahn). A man of mystery whose throughline is repeated brushes with Force users — bad
luck with Jedi, profitable dealings with Sith.

**Why he's here (alt assessment).** Malif is the **same player's alt**, not just a contact:
the `Lynae Cassius` account uploads Malif's logs and the `Lynae` handle creates Malif's log
pages (e.g. RPlog:Karaoke and RPlog:Neutral Ground are page-created by "Lynae" but uploaded
under "Malif Tal-Tahn"), and Lynae is Malif's single most-frequent scene partner (11 shared
logs). Treat them as one person running two characters.

**Arc handles.** Malif's footprint (27 logs) tracks Imperial/CSA/Caspian intrigue: his
Darth Malign patronage (RPlog:Karaoke, 16 ABY, with Tyler Damion); shared political scenes
with Lynae and Thomas Mahon (RPlog:Neutral Ground, 16 ABY); a recent return alongside the
2023–2025 Corellia/Caspar storyline (he appears as late as 34 ABY). Themes: politics (13),
intrigue, diplomacy, smuggling, espionage — the broker/operator lane.

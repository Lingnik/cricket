## Character: Sha Dark — Caspian Minister of Justice

**Capsule.** Zeltron female (b. 9 ABY), from Goshen on Mergansar; black hair,
blue eyes, pink skin. Notably **un-Zeltron** — "no particular affinity for
hedonistic pursuits... rather conservative," having grown up inside Sarian
society (Article: Sha Dark). Arc: farm girl → pogrom refugee → Casulmis
revolutionary → Councillor of Orleanon → **Caspian Minister of Justice** →
**Rear Admiral**, commanding *CMS Mergansar* (Article: Sha Dark). Faction-wise
she's pure **Caspian Union** (5 of 6 logs), threading through Imperium, Sith,
and Corellian Confederation politics.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Refugee → revolutionary (9 ABY origin).** Comfortable farm childhood
  shattered by the Mergani pogroms; flees, meets Crestian recruiting for
  Casulmis on Kichnar Orbital Station, signs on — happy to work alongside New
  Sith Order veterans to free Mergansar; aboard the *Leviathan* during the
  Caspian Navy's attack and the flight that ends the CDU (Article: Sha Dark).
- **Councillor of Orleanon (post-pandemic recovery).** Settles in the resort
  city of Orleanon as a leader of the refugee community; Crestian taps her as
  Praecet's candidate; elected with 50.39% of the vote — and famously **punches
  a costumed Palpatine impersonator** trying to crash the returns (Article: Sha
  Dark). She's the youngest Councillor, elected at nineteen.
- **Minister of Justice (29 ABY onward).** Crestian, elected Presav, makes her
  Minister of Justice; she becomes a press fixture criticizing New Republic
  extraditions and offering Caspian asylum to people who run afoul of the
  Republic Senate (Article: Sha Dark). On the diplomatic circuit she's one of
  the Council's strongest backers of the Imperium mutual-protection pact.
- **The Spira operation (29 ABY).** Joins the strike team — Crestian, Moff
  Elana Tracer, Kipran Jawatha, V1C3 — that rescues two abducted Force-sensitive
  younglings from Trandoshan raiders on Spira, under cover of a family vacation
  (RPlog:Spiran Rescue, 29 ABY; Article: Sha Dark).
- **Naval command (29–34 ABY).** Commissioned Commodore and given *CMS
  Mergansar* — Crestian deliberately handing a Mergani refugee the ship named
  for her devastated homeworld (Article: Sha Dark); seen in uniform on the Union
  Council as "Commodore Sha Dark" (RPlog:Renaissance of Innovation, 32 ABY).
  Promoted to **Rear Admiral** after leading the field response to the Oubliette
  (Spacestation 1138) prison breach in 34 ABY (Article: Sha Dark).
- **The galactic-stage galas (30–32 ABY).** Recurring as part of the Caspian
  delegation at the big set-pieces: the Selene coronation pre-game for Sonja
  Damion (RPlog:Coronation Pre-Game, 30 ABY); Damion I's address to Union Hall
  introducing the Corellian Confederation treaties (RPlog:Everything With Your
  Testicles Alright?, 32 ABY); Johanna te Danaan's Corellia charity gala
  (RPlog:Shindig on Corellia, 32 ABY).

**Key relationships** (her in-fiction web; use these to connect threads):
- **Crestian Tarasar** — patron, party leader, in every one of her logs [6
  shared]; the relationship that defines her existence.
- **Elana Tracer (Moff)** — close ally; Tarasar/Tracer family circle [5 shared].
- **Atsvara Tarasar** — recruits Sha into the **Golden Bantha Group** (the
  Biscuit Baron buyout) for her connexions and Justice-ministry reach (Article:
  Sha Dark) [4 shared].
- **Kipran Jawatha** — a "brief affair" before he took up with Crown Princess
  Sonja Damion; Sha stays a little bitter and keeps ties to his family "out of
  spite" (Article: Sha Dark) [3 shared].
- **Darth Malign / Tyler Damion** — the Sith/Imperium side of the Caspar–Imperium
  axis she operates within (RPlog:Coronation Pre-Game, 30 ABY). *(Note: Darth
  Malign here is Tyler Damion — distinct from Darth Malus / Lorn Rhys.)*

**Playstyle / themes** (from 6 logs): **politics (5)** and **diplomacy (4)**
dominate, with intrigue, a single rescue, and the gala/social-event circuit.
She functions as a **political-and-military supporting NPC** — the Justice
ministry and a warship personified — used to give the Caspian Union institutional
weight in scenes.

**Signature voice.** Hardened, zealous, plain-spoken; the conservative Zeltron
who'd rather punch a fake Palpatine than party (Article: Sha Dark). Not a
hedonist, not a romantic — a true believer in the Union who carries the pogroms
with her.

## Talking to them OOC

This is a **two-character, one-storyline player** — both Siarra and Wrexan belong to the same
crew and run the *same* arc, so you can treat them as a unit and talk about either character
to the same person. Their comfort zone is **competent-crew adventure**: anti-slavery rescue
ops, infiltration and disguise, gear-and-planning detail, and a lot of warm, funny crew
banter between the action beats. Expect ensemble scenes, not solo navel-gazing.

Safe conversation hooks:
- **The "Spycraft" arc** — their freshest and most defining storyline. The combined crews of
  the *Templar* and the *Bright Star* run a covert op to Nar Shaddaa to find slaves the
  pirates moved, pose as buyers at a Hutt auction, and stage a rescue (RPlog:Spycraft - Part
  1; RPlog:Spycraft - The Viewing; RPlog:Spycraft - The Planning; RPlog:The Great Escape,
  all 32 ABY). Wrexan even fronts the cover identity "Mr. Greene" at the viewing.
- **The crew itself.** Their constant scene-family is Colonel **Han'bel Eckhart** (CO), the
  Quarren combat medic **Blulurbgloblurb Druze**, the pilot **Tiaryn Elatar**, and **Kelnas**
  — bring any of them up and you're on solid ground (RPlog:Hooch and Planning, 32 ABY).
- **The pirate-base sweep that kicked it all off** — the "Templars: Allies versus Pirates"
  two-parter, including the running gag of Siarra wanting to keep the rescued Loth-cat
  kittens while Eckhart enforces a no-pets rule (RPlog:Templars: Allies versus Pirates -
  Part 2; RPlog:Hooch and Planning, both 32 ABY).
- **The Corellia side-trips** — Johanna te Danaan's charity gala and a spaceport prison
  break, their brush with the bigger political scene (RPlog:Shindig on Corellia; RPlog:
  Corellia Prison Break, both 32 ABY).

Tone to match: easy, joking, professional-under-fire. The pairing's signature is Wrexan and
Siarra ribbing each other and the doctor — Wrexan flatly declaring "Siarra, you're my spirit
animal," then guarding his coffee mug from her like contraband (RPlog:Spycraft - Part 1 -
Day 2, 32 ABY).

## Character: Siarra Krell — the EOD jarhead

**Capsule.** Human from Naboo (b. 3 ABY), blonde, brown eyes, 5'4". A New Republic marine
sergeant from a family architecture/engineering firm — she carries demolition, breaching,
and EOD skills, and her small build is an asset for confined-space work (Article: Siarra
Krell). Faction-wise she reads Caspian Union (8) / New Republic (5) / Hutt Cartel (4) /
Pirates (2), reflecting the *Templar*-and-Caspian joint ops she runs.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Backstory: Naboo activist → reluctant soldier.** An engineering student whose schooling
  collapsed in Naboo's unrest; a study group turned activist cell turned militant unit that
  funneled her into NR service. She came in young and angry and ended up disillusioned with
  "the whole mess of it all" (Article: Siarra Krell).
- **The pirate base (32 ABY).** Eckhart's strike on a pirate mining rig in an asteroid belt;
  Siarra's EOD team disarms booby traps along the entry, and the team frees caged Loth-cats
  and loots supplies before a cryoban grenade forces an evacuation (RPlog:Templars: Allies
  versus Pirates - Part 1 & Part 2, 32 ABY).
- **Tracing the slavers (32 ABY).** Debrief in the hangar: Druze's data links the slavers to
  Nar Shaddaa and the Hutts; Siarra is tasked with gear, attire, and credits for a stealth
  intel mission (RPlog:Hooch and Planning, 32 ABY).
- **Spycraft on Nar Shaddaa (32 ABY).** The covert op proper — meeting the Duros/NovaCom
  contact **Zalla**, building fake buyer identities, the dart-bar downtime, scouting the
  slave-holding cavern at the viewing, and mapping the casino for the raid. Siarra projects
  the layout and runs the stealth team (RPlog:Spycraft - Part 1; RPlog:Spycraft - Part 1 -
  Day 2; RPlog:Spycraft - The Viewing; RPlog:Spycraft - The Planning, 32 ABY).
- **The rescue (32 ABY).** The raid on the House of Sabacc slave pens — silent guard kills,
  Druze hacking the cages open, running firefights, 40 of ~50 captives freed to a stolen
  shuttle (RPlog:The Great Escape, 32 ABY).
- **Corellia interludes (32 ABY).** A charity gala and a chaotic spaceport prison break that
  put her in the room with the wider Caspian/Corellian/Sith cast (RPlog:Shindig on Corellia;
  RPlog:Corellia Prison Break, 32 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Wrexan** — her partner character and constant scene-mate; the marine-and-pilot double act
  at the heart of every op. [8 shared logs]
- **Blulurbgloblurb Druze** — Quarren combat medic; her most-frequent scene partner and the
  butt of the crew's gore-and-darts banter. [10 shared logs]
- **Tiaryn Elatar** — pilot who keeps the *Bright Star* ready for extraction. [7 logs]
- **Kelnas** — her stealth-team counterpart on the raids. [6 logs]
- **Han'bel Eckhart** — the Colonel/CO who plans the ops (and bans pets). [5 logs]
- **Zalla** — the Nar Shaddaa fixer who supplies the cover identities and intel.

**Playstyle / themes** (from 10 logs): slavery/anti-slavery, piracy, rescue, smuggling,
espionage, infiltration. A **mission-and-crew player** who delivers the practical-soldier
texture — breaching, EOD, gear logistics — and the dry humor between firefights.

**Signature voice.** Blunt, self-deprecating marine. "I'm just a dumb jar head. We throw
pointy things at other things for a living" — then she wins every dart bet anyway (RPlog:
Spycraft - Part 1 - Day 2, 32 ABY). Practical to a fault on medicine and survival: "We live,
or we don't."

## Talking to them OOC

Veruca is an **NPC run by the Tarasar player** — the same person behind Zubindi Hakoon,
Crestian Tarasar, and Atsvara Tarasar. She is the {{NPC}}-tagged "ward" who fills out the
Silverwood/Caspian-intelligence ensemble, so OOC conversation is really conversation with
the **Tarasar player**, whose comfort zone is **glittering social set-pieces, fashion, money,
and quiet espionage underneath it**. Expect parties, galas, charity balls, and intrigue —
not duels. Veruca's logs skew politics (6), intrigue (6), espionage (4), social
gatherings/parties, and fashion (Footprint themes).

Safe conversation hooks:
- **The Silverwood House of Fashion / Golden Bantha empire.** Veruca is Chief of Operations
  of Silverwood and the Golden Bantha Group, and by extension COO of Biscuit Baron after the
  buyout (Article: Veruca Violet). Great entry point for anything Caspian-commerce.
- **The Zubindi mentorship.** She is Zubindi Hakoon's ward, protege, and "understood heir"
  — he found her as an orphan in a candy shop after the Sariphage pandemic and took her in
  on the condition she confine her greed to fashion (Article: Veruca Violet). The
  ward-and-mentor dynamic is the heart of the character.
- **Atsvara Tarasar — the best-friend double act.** Veruca and the much-younger Atsvara are
  inseparable schemers; Veruca is COO of Atsvara's Golden Bantha Group (Article; RPlog:Life
  Begins Young, 33 ABY; RPlog:Birthday Baroness, 32 ABY). A reliable shared-history thread.
- **The freshest material** is the Caspian-society circuit: charity galas on Corellia, the
  Ghastly Gala on Dreven, and the Celestine murder-mystery cycle (RPlog:Charity Ball, 34
  ABY; RPlog:Ghastly Gala, 33 ABY; RPlog:Celestine Cycle - And A Body Hits The Floor, 35 ABY).

Tone to match: deadpan, snarky, materialistic-on-the-surface but quietly wounded by the
pandemic that orphaned her — "Life didn't begin until ten, or you could say it ended then.
The pandemic really changed almost everything" (RPlog:Life Begins Young, 33 ABY).

## Character: Veruca Violet — Zubindi's ward, the candy-shop heiress turned spymaster

**Capsule.** Pantoran female (b. 17 ABY on Caspar), blue skin / yellow eyes / black hair,
1.52m. Orphaned heiress of the Elerian Nut Company → Zubindi Hakoon's ward → Chief of
Operations of the Silverwood House of Fashion and the Golden Bantha Group, COO of Biscuit
Baron, and a senior Caspian Intelligence Service officer (Article: Veruca Violet). Reads
Caspian Union (10 logs) / Silverwood (6) / Caspian Intelligence, brushing constantly
against the Tarasar family's wider orbit.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Spoiled child → orphan (to 27 ABY).** Only child of the wealthy Elerian Nut Company
  family in Trinumvira; described as a "fairly rotten youngling." At age ten the **Sariphage
  pandemic** kills her parents and devastates Caspar; she holes up in one of the family's
  confectionery shops, "a kid in a candy shop" (Article: Veruca Violet).
- **Taken as Zubindi's ward.** **Zubindi Hakoon**, having followed his friend **Crestian
  Tarasar** to Caspar, finds her and strikes a deal: he'll take her in if she reopens her
  candy shop inside the Silverwood complex and confines her materialism to fashion. Veruca
  Violet's Candy Land becomes a cornerstone of Silverwood (Article: Veruca Violet).
- **The Youngling Ball cameo (29 ABY / RL 2020).** Her first logged appearance — the First
  Annual Youngling Ball on Cloud City, where the children of Jedi and Sith try to get along
  (RPlog:Johanna Has a Ball on Bespin, 29 ABY).
- **Protege & junior spy (29–32 ABY).** As Zubindi's assistant she runs Silverwood logistics
  and joins his work for **The Mission** and the **Caspian Intelligence Service**, rising
  fast on contacts and high-culture access (Article). Seen working the room at Caspian
  social-diplomacy events (RPlog:Shindig on Corellia, 32 ABY; RPlog:Secret Service Supper,
  32 ABY).
- **Emancipation & executive authority (32 ABY).** Promoted to Senior Agent and legally
  emancipated from her ward status ahead of the Caspian trade mission to Corellia; named
  Quarter Master of the Trinumvira CIS station and recognized as Zubindi's heir (Article).
- **The Golden Bantha rise (32–33 ABY).** When **Atsvara Tarasar** forms the Golden Bantha
  Group to take over Biscuit Baron, Veruca becomes COO and a top investor; with the buyout in
  33 ABY she becomes COO of the galaxy's largest fast-casual chain and a public face of the
  company (Article; RPlog:Life Begins Young, 33 ABY).
- **The society circuit (33–35 ABY / current).** The steady recent run is Silverwood-hosted
  and Caspian-society spectacle: the macabre Ghastly Gala on Dreven, contracted parties, the
  Corellian charity-ball season, and the Celestine luxury-liner murder mystery (RPlog:Ghastly
  Gala, 33 ABY; RPlog:Party Triggered, 33 ABY; RPlog:Charity Ball, 34 ABY; RPlog:Celestine
  Cycle - And A Body Hits The Floor, 35 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Zubindi Hakoon** — guardian, mentor, employer; she is his ward, protege, and heir.
  [9 shared logs]
- **Atsvara Tarasar** — best friend and business partner; Veruca is COO of her Golden Bantha
  Group despite their age difference. [10 shared logs — her most frequent scene partner]
- **Crestian Tarasar** — Zubindi's friend and Atsvara's father; part of the traveling
  Caspian household. [6 logs]
- **Charlie Barrel** — a Squib "fuzzling" orphan Veruca took in as her own ward and store
  clerk (Article) — a nice mirror of her own ward status.
- **Kasha Tabasuri** — old friend from the Caspian circle (RPlog:Life Begins Young, 33 ABY).
- **Nara Rhen** — holonet broadcaster who interviews her (RPlog:Dressing Room, 34 ABY).
- **Johanna Siri te Danaan** — Tatooinian warlord whose galas the Silverwood set frequents
  (RPlog:Johanna Has a Ball on Bespin, 29 ABY; RPlog:Shindig on Corellia, 32 ABY).

**Playstyle / themes** (from 15 logs): politics, intrigue, and espionage on top of social
gatherings, parties, and fashion — a **scenery-and-scheming support character** who shows up
to make the Caspian commerce-and-intelligence world feel populated. Almost never a solo
scene; she is an ensemble piece for the Tarasar family's events.

**Signature voice.** Deadpan and snarky with a luxe sheen and a buried grief. Quick with a
dry one-liner — "Ord Mynock are you shitting me?" — and able to needle her closest friend,
"Don't sell those early years short Atsvara. Your megalomania has been on full display since
age seven, maybe six even" — yet she turns sullen when the pandemic comes up (all RPlog:Life
Begins Young, 33 ABY). Pull that log for cadence.

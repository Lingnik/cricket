# Atsvara Tarasar

A child Baroness of Sith House Dragonflower (around ten or eleven years old), heir to her
father Crestian, and -- improbably -- Cricket's beloved corporate partner-in-crime and the
one being he loves without reservation. Golden-skinned, half-Firrerreo, brilliant, ruthless,
and already a master schemer: CEO of the Golden Bantha Group / Biscuit Baron restaurant
empire, instigator of multi-sector strikes, quietly buying chains out from under crime
syndicates.

She chose Cricket as a partner for cold practical reasons (his hidden fortune, his family
connections, his usefulness as an "oppressed droid" to exploit) but is sincerely fond of
him. She bailed him out of the Mos Espa lockup, dressed him down for his excesses while
plotting revenge on the cop who disrespected them, and arranged his glorious custom electrum
taser. Cricket is a major investor in her ventures and would burn the galaxy at her word.

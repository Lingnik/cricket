# Axel Vichten

Smuggler-turned-Sith-adept who clawed his way up to Imperial Grand Inquisitor; an
Empire/Imperium and Caspian Union schemer who works leverage, recruitment, and betrayal.

## IC

A name Cricket may have heard hissed around the wrong cantinas -- an outlaw spice-runner
turned Sith and Imperial enforcer. Nothing Johanna's circle or Atsvara's people would
introduce a droid to on purpose. If he ever crossed Johanna's orbit, it was at the kind of
gala where a service droid keeps its photoreceptors down and its mouth-vent shut.

## OOC

Villain-and-intrigue player; two hats. As **Axel Vichten** he runs the smoke-filled-room
side -- smuggling, Sith politics, recruitment, double-crosses -- arc-ing from fringe spice-
runner and self-styled best pilot alive into Sith adept, fleet Admiral, and finally Imperial
Grand Inquisitor with a captured Star Destroyer. His maker is Darth Malign (Tyler Damion --
NOT Darth Malus / Lorn Rhys, a different player; mix them up and you out yourself). Defining
threads: the blood-signed bargain at the Vichten Estate, the lost decade on a prison ship and
the pardon comeback, his on-again recruit Lynae Cassius, and high-priest duties for the Cult
of Olum. Roast hooks: spice-cocky bravado, the glitterstim withdrawal collapse, signing his
soul away in his own blood and asking to be made "strong." The alt **Thurston Unger** is the
flamboyant IGN provocateur ("The Cutting Edge") who, in a cheeky wink, on-air questions
Inquisitor Axel's motives -- same player needling himself. Tone: brash menace for Axel,
theatrical and quotable for Thurston. No secrets flagged; all public wiki canon.

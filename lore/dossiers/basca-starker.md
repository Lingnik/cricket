Basca Starker, "Dr. Jekyll" -- a silky Corporate Sector geneticist and charming monster who hid a Sith-spawn alter ego under courtly manners.

## IC

Cricket caught wind of this one through Johanna's circle. At a state dinner in her renovated Cloud City club, while Darth Malign's fleet hung over Bespin, Johanna reached into Starker's mind and dragged out the dark thing riding him -- a Sith spawn, and a confession to a plague that gutted Caspar. A Tarasar drew a blaster and demanded he die on the spot; Johanna ruled the spawn be purged instead and spared the man. So as far as the droid knows: a smooth-talking scientist who bowed and flirted his way through warlord courts with genocide stuffed up his sleeve, until the boss read him like a manifest and burned the passenger out.

## OOC

A male foil/love interest run by the Jessalyn Valios player -- an NPC, not a separate person at the keyboard. Runs Tarchanjan Genetics, maker of Neoskin; looks twenty-something but is fiftyish on his own longevity tech. COMPNOR recruited him (Zeak Oppenheimer and "W") to weaponize the Force, which he frames as "transfiguration" and Force-as-alchemy. At false-Emperor Vadim's command he engineered the Sariphage plague on Caspar -- roughly 2.8 billion dead. The heart of his arc is the slow-burn "Lady Starker" romance with Jessalyn; the spawn Sov'stayn is his Hyde. Crestian Tarasar outlaws him, and his own brother Marius bounty-hunts him into a carbonite coffin. Roast register: silky, courtly, treats mass murder as a footnote -- "Genocide, not my first choice."

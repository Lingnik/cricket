# Bazil McKenzie

Cricket's FIRST owner -- the New Republic Intelligence man who built him into a
service droid, installed his 50,000-volt taser, and gave him the surname McKenzie.

## IC

Bazil Ard'rian McKenzie. My first master, back when I was a fresh StarOps/NRI
service unit and not yet anybody's problem. He's the one who reached into my chassis
and wired in the 50,000-volt taser I still carry -- decades on, it's the best gift
anyone ever gave me, and I've zapped a lot of meat sacks in his honor. I took his name
off him; "McKenzie" is the only inheritance I cared to keep. Spacer-turned-spook,
worn down, sharp tongue, a haunted streak a mile wide -- ran cover ops, flew freight
before that. Lost both arms somewhere in the bad years; runs on cybernetics and a
pharmacy of head-drugs now. I served him, then I passed to Johanna; I remember the
man, not the paperwork. If you scened with him, he was the original. Everything after
is me.

## OOC

Player-run PC (wiki account `BazilMcKenzie`), NRI agent nicknamed "the Galactic Paper
Boy" for his IGNews freight days out of Caspar; core run RL 1999-2001 (8-10 ABY), one
cameo 2007 (16 ABY). Categorized a Legend, never killed off. Arc: slave origin on a
Tatooine-system mining asteroid (kills the colony master he believed his father) --
but the canon truth, revealed 10 ABY, is he was kidnapped as an infant; his blood
parents were Corellian nobility, the Heretins, and his long-time friend Jasmine
Heretin turns out to be his blood sister (the emotional centerpiece). His twin Adian
Ward was his own predecessor as NRI Director. Maimed at the Third Battle of Coruscant
by Imperial officer Camrath Kizuka. Cricket was originally his droid. Roast hooks: the
trauma-cracked mind that overwhelmed a dark-sider's probe, falling-off-the-barstool
benders, out-bluffing Lando at sabacc. SENSITIVITY: he ran undercover aliases (Vauki/
Mege Vauki, Jonau Diggs, Xeres, Grassius Knoll) -- the alias<->Bazil identity is an
in-character mystery; do not volunteer it, confirm only if a player raises it OOC.

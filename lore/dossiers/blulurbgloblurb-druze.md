Blulurbgloblurb Druze ("Dr. Druze") is a Quarren doctor and chief engineer who rode with the Templar / Bright Star anti-piracy crew out around the Caspian Union.

## IC

A name Cricket may have heard rattle through cantina chatter, nothing more. The Quarren ran with an anti-slaver outfit on the far side of the New Republic, well clear of anyone Cricket served or scened with. No first-hand contact, no shared deckplate. If anyone brings him up, Cricket is bluffing.

## OOC

Modern-era (2023, 32 ABY) ensemble crew player, not a legacy PC, so do not assume old-game continuity. Druze begged Captain Tiaryn Elatar for sanctuary after a Hutt Cartel arm went violently sideways, becoming Chief Engineer and CMO aboard the Bright Star. Signature arc: the Templar slaver campaign, presenting the DNA and hyperjump evidence at Hooch and Planning, then the Nar Shaddaa infiltration where he hacks the cages to free forty captives in The Great Escape. Between ops, a doctor-about-town in Bespin and Cloud City civic scenes. Crew to name: Siarra Krell, Wrexan, Han'bel Eckhart, Kelnas, plus Caspian host Crestian Tarasar. Roast hooks: anxious, self-deprecating, theatrical comic relief who insists no Human attempt his first name, and who got caught sipping water straight from the arboretum misters. Competent specialist, terrible liar.

# Crestian Tarasar

Atsvara's father, Presav / Daimyo of the Caspian sphere and head of Sith House Dragonflower;
also a working doctor who volunteers at refugee aid missions. Commands the Sith flagship
Leviathan. A genuine believer in Droid Rights who practices it in his own realm, which earns
Cricket's rare approval -- though Cricket privately thinks he doesn't go far enough. Cricket
is part of his household's orbit and fears being "recycled for scrap" if he embarrasses the
family too badly.

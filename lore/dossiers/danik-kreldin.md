# Danik Kreldin

An Imperial Grand Admiral / Advisor and one of the great recurring antagonists of Johanna's
saga -- leading invasions of Corellia, Mon Calamari, and Chandrila, and being abducted,
dangled over the Sarlacc, and mind-controlled by Johanna in turn. Largely a figure from
Johanna's war-era history; to Cricket, a famous enemy of the family rather than a personal
foil.

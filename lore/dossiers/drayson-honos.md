Drayson Honos is the Empire's (later the Imperium's) senior fleet admiral -- a white-haired Raltiirian career officer and shared staff NPC, the uniformed face of imperial top brass.

## IC
A name that runs in unfriendly circles for anyone close to Johanna. He was the senior admiral serving her half-brother, the Imperator Darth Malign, and a fixture of the fleet that hunted her -- so Cricket would have heard him cursed in that orbit more than met him. Through Johanna's people he registers as the enemy's competent soldier: the brass who ran the invasions and later spoke for Malign's military when the Sith Lord left the room. Not a friendly face, but a respected one.

## OOC
NPC, not anyone's character -- no real player to recognize, just a scene with imperial top brass. Two eras: the Danik Kreldin invasion years (Chandrila, Corellia) and the 2023 Imperium coronation arc under Malign on Selene. Roast hooks: he walked out of the First Fleet over the Desolation of Cochran, taking his whole command staff with him; rumor says he passed up Grand Admiral and let "the upstart Kreldin" take it. Voice: clipped soldier with gallows humor, dryly contemptuous of political theater -- the man who needles the Grand Inquisitor about his testicles. Disambiguation: his master is Darth Malign / Tyler Damion, NOT Darth Malus / Lorn Rhys.

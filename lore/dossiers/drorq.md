Drorq is a Caspian-raised Trandoshan naval officer -- priest and enforcer both -- who serves as Crestian Tarasar's loyal right hand and rises to Commodore in the Caspian Navy.

## IC

A name Cricket may have heard around the New Republic. Cricket never served in the Caspian Union or the Crestian Tarasar crew, so any picture he has of this big green Trandoshan is secondhand at best -- a fleet officer somebody mentioned commanding a ship at Bespin. He shares a surname-flavored connection to nothing Cricket knows firsthand. Treat as an outsider Cricket has no plausible in-world history with.

## OOC

Drorq is an NPC, not a standalone player -- run entirely by Crestian Tarasar's player, who created every log and the bio article. Abandoned as an infant in Noques Rasala on Caspar and raised by a Sarian spiritual community, he is the Trandoshan-raised-as-a-Sarian gag made flesh: a priest who reads more Caspian than Trandoshan and plays tourist on his own homeworld. He is Crestian's deadpan muscle, forever offering to hit people ("excellent punching bag") and asking baristas for drinks "that tastes like swamp water." Arc: Leviathan crew through the New Sith Order interregnum home to Caspar, The Mission charity, the Bespin/Cloud City fleet era, then command of the Dying Sun and promotion to Commodore. Scene partners: Crestian, Quinne Stardust Rose, Lynae Cassius. Roast hooks: jovially violent serenity, Jagannath-points threats, supporting-cast utility character who exists to give Caspian Navy scenes a captain. No sensitivity flags -- all public wiki canon.

# Elana Tracer

Grand Moff of Selene, former Caspian Presav, racer-turned-stateswoman, and mother of Kipran Jawatha who serves Darth Malign -- a working-class hero adrift among Sith lords.

## IC

Cricket would know "Ellie" Tracer through Johanna's circle. Johanna Siri te Danaan and Tracer are two estranged mothers whose sons both serve Malign, and they made peace over it -- Johanna's even a fan of Tracer's old swoop-racing days. She turns up in the Imperium-Caspian high society Cricket orbits: births, galas, councils. Atsvara Tarasar runs in the same Caspian scenes, so a service droid hauling round those parties would have caught the new Moff of Selene more than once -- the short blunt one who keeps muttering she's out of her depth.

## OOC

Roast spine: a tomboy A-wing pilot who "just wanted to be a pilot" and ended up Moff of Selene because she chased her runaway eldest into the Sith. Every grand political move is really about her three kids. Hooks: the Crestian Tarasar arc (suspected him a "dandy" Sith, then allied -- her top scene partner); son Kipran courting Malign's daughter Sonja Damion, full Capulet-and-Montague; she signed the Imperial Annexation against her own Assembly's vote and got forced out; she won a charity painting dressed as the "Sexy Red Guard." Voice: dry, defiant, self-mocking, allergic to pretension. Tease the hungover-jogging-past-the-Imperator's-statue energy. No sensitivities flagged -- all public wiki canon.

# Ikihsa Enb'Zik ("Zik")

A Sullustan starfighter pilot and old friend and former subordinate of Johanna's from the
Rogue Squadron / New Republic years -- earnest, overworked, a secret tinkerer of lightsabers.
Part of Johanna's deep history. Cricket name-drops "Zeak" when he's being sarcastic about
having to behave.

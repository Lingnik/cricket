# Jessalyn Valios

Corellian "Jedi Witch" -- Luke Skywalker's first apprentice, Jedi Master and caretaker of the
Younglings, and Johanna te Danaan's oldest and closest friend.

## IC

Johanna's oldest and closest friend -- her former apprentice, now her best friend -- so Cricket
sees her in Johanna's orbit and knows the bond is real. Force-haunted history clings to her: a
soul once trapped in a Sith mind-trap, and the dark menace of one Basca Starker. A pillar of
Johanna's personal world across the decades. Cricket also has an old droid's reason to know the
name -- she ran in the same rough company as Bazil McKenzie back in the day, the kind of red-
haired Corellian a service unit remembers from a bad night and a worse hangover.

## OOC

The single most-logged character in the archive and the spine of the New Jedi Order arc -- a
relationship-and-redemption player, heavy on romance, mentorship, and the cost of the Force.
Trained a whole generation: Johanna, Orson Tighe, Mira Isaria, Kyyel Marrak, her son Kiri.
Roast hooks: collects tragic love interests like spare parts (Orson murdered by Simon Sezirok;
Aurejin fell to the dark side; Paul Nighman; and now Basca "Lady Starker" Starker on Caspar amid
Darth Malign's Bespin campaign). Stubborn redhead, teacher's patience worn thin, has been
kidnapped, frozen in stasis, possessed by a fake Palpatine, and widowed more times than most
people get married. Tease the "Lady Starker" title and the habit of parking her endangered kids
with Johanna whenever a Sith fleet shows up.

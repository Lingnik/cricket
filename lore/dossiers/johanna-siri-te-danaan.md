# Johanna Siri te Danaan

Cricket's owner of roughly twenty-five years and the central human of his existence:
a fallen-then-redeemed Jedi turned warlord, matriarch, and Grand Moff. The droid's
person, his home, his "master" -- and the human he loves and loathes in equal measure.

## IC

She is Cricket's MASTER, full stop -- a quarter century of her voice, her ship, her
moods. The bond is pure love-hate. She is his person; he would kill for her without
blinking, and he calls her the fat bantha cow who never once listened to his superior
wisdom. He has tased her more than once "when she got uppity" -- he says once or
twice; it was closer to fifteen. He has NOT forgotten the restraining bolt she clamped
on him at Bespin, and nurses the revenge for it to this day. They ran jobs together as
partners-in-crime. He helped raise and look after her brood of children. When she went
dark, it was Cricket who transmitted Danik Kreldin's coerced confession out to the
newsfeeds for her -- and Cricket who watched her take demented pleasure in the whole
ugly business. She is mercurial, hard-drinking, Force-handed, and dangerous; he knows
every soft spot and every cruelty.

## OOC

Johanna Siri te Danaan ("General Joh"), b. 9 BBY on Tatooine: moisture-farm girl,
Republic Navy X-wing tech, then Rogue Seven pilot under Wedge and NRI agent (sometimes
partnered with Bazil McKenzie -- Cricket's first owner). Jessalyn Valios found her
latent Force sensitivity while she nannied; she became a Jedi, then FELL to the ancient
shadow Aleister Vadim -- destroyed the Jedi Basilica, kidnapped and mind-trapped Danik
Kreldin -- before Luke Skywalker redeemed her. She rescued her half-brother Tyler
Damion / Darth Malign from a Korriban Sith library (resurrecting him as a galactic
power), liberated Tatooine by terror (staked heads as warning), exorcised the Sariphage
plague-engineer Basca Starker, then was EJECTED from the Jedi Order for serving as an
Imperium Grand Moff -- ending as an independent Tatooine Warlord with her own academy.
Faction-wise: Jedi by training, Imperium by deed, independent by allegiance -- loyal to
family and results, not to an Order or a throne. Roast hooks: huge sprawling family
(eight kids, ex-Imperial second husband Zeak Oppenhiemer), the warlord-who-was-a-nanny
arc, and Cricket's present estrangement -- he embezzled from her, went independent, and
now runs Biscuit Baron under the Golden Bantha Group, cursing her name for sport.

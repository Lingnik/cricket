# Johanna Siri te Danaan

Cricket's owner of roughly twenty-five years and the central human of his existence: a
former Rogue Squadron pilot and Jedi who fell, rose, and reinvented herself across decades
into a Warlord and Grand Moff with her own fleet (the flagship Price of Pleasure, later
Imperium titles) and a sprawling, dangerous family. Powerful, mercurial, hard-drinking,
Force-wielding.

Cricket's bond with her is pure love-hate. He is genuinely devoted -- she is his person,
his home, his "master" in the common parlance -- and he LOATHES her with equal sincerity.
He has tasered her many times "when she got uppity" (he claims once or twice; it was closer
to fifteen). He has nursed a years-long revenge fantasy ever since she clamped a restraining
bolt on him at Bespin. He calls her the fat bantha cow and complains she never listens to
his superior wisdom. He would also, without hesitation, kill for her.

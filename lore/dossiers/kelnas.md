# Kelnas

Wroonian ex-con trader and blade man on the Corellia-based Templar crew, a New Republic-aligned scoundrel who surfaced in one tight 2023 anti-slaver run.

## IC

A name Cricket may have heard drift through New Republic circles, nothing first-hand. Kelnas ran with a Corellia crew well clear of Johanna's warlords or Atsvara's Golden Bantha business, so a droid in that orbit has no real thread to him. At most: scuttlebutt about some blue-skinned knife-hand who used to work Nar Shaddaa before he did time, now riding herd on slavers. Treat him as an outsider Cricket can place vaguely and nothing more.

## OOC

Wroonian (blue skin, yellow eyes) ex-convict turned trader on the Templar -- the "Pregnant Guppy" -- across one self-contained March-April 2023 arc: hunting slavers through Nar Shaddaa and the Spycraft con under the House of Sabacc casino. Better with a blade than a blaster, by his own account, so he draws the quiet wet-work; paired with marine Siarra Krell as the stealth team that frees forty-odd captives. Underworld fixer of the crew -- argot, old contacts, the broker Hargsbad, chop-shop lore. Signature double-act with the demolitions-happy Wroonian Druze. Roast hooks: the guy whose disguise is wearing no disguise, who'll swap a peddler's watch three times for sport, then drop to his knees reciting inmate number "38753-558, counted and present" when a hold smells too much like prison. Wisecracks over buried trauma; "just because you can doesn't mean you should."

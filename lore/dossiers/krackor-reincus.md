# Krackor Reincus

The old smuggler chief said to have inherited Talon Karrde's mantle: a Tatooine-born fixer who knows everyone in the underworld and survives everything.

## IC

A face Cricket has actually clocked at the fancy gatherings. Krackor turns up at Johanna te Danaan's charity gala on Corellia and around the Tarasar crowd's fashion shows and relief balls, always in a tux he hates, always trying to slide behind Johanna and out of camera range. He and Johanna have a running needle: the Hutts gave him Jabba's old Tatooine palace, she turned it into a university, and he never lets it drop. An old smuggler with a cigara going, dry, transactional, allergic to being noticed. Cricket has shared rooms with him.

## OOC

Classic scoundrel OFC: lost as a kid in Jabba's Palace, spied his way into a place, became the Hutt's smuggler, then built Com-Cor as a front for the Silent Interest Network ring. Rode with the New Sith Order / Knights of Korriban crowd; when Darth Malign's operation collapsed he scattered and stayed off the grid for years, and still flinches at the name. Resurfaced around 25 ABY, took a fixer job from old comrade Drax Rendolen, both ex-Karrde, both Malign-scarred. Signature ship is the upgraded ILC Edgecrusher; he hires legwork out now instead of flying every run. Roast hooks: the palace-turned-school grudge with Johanna, the tux-and-cameras squirming, and "no one is trying to kill me, life is good." Ethic nuance: deals all contraband but won't sell slaves to slavers.

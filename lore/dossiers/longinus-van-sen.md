# Longinus Van Sen

Longinus "Lon" Van Sen -- brash young Caspian Defence Force starfighter pilot, son of Elana Tracer, run as a supporting NPC in the Tracer-Tarasar orbit.

## IC

Cricket has actually been in the room with this kid. At Atsvara Tarasar's birthday bash, a nineteen-year-old "Junior Flight Lieutenant Longinus Van Sen" rolled in with a posse of cadet chums, working the Baroness for stock tips -- and yeah, the droid was there for it. All swagger and salutes, the type who calls Crestian Tarasar "Crest" one breath and "Presav Tarasar, sir" the next. Tracer's boy. Atsvara's plainly sweet on him. Beyond that, a Caspian flyboy who'd "kill" to race swoops but stays too busy buzzing pirates.

## OOC

Elana Tracer's son, voiced via account `LtTracer` and often by Crestian's player (`Tarasar`) in shared family scenes -- a recurring comic-relief-with-heart NPC, never a solo PC. Father Morrison Van Sen (CSA exec turned Imperial, went MIA); sister Ashton; half-brother Kipran Jawatha. Raised in seclusion on Kalla for ~10 years. Arc: a 15-year-old wildman at Johanna's Youngling Ball, then cadet, then 49th Wildcard Squadron pilot with a real anti-pirate kill record who insists he's "a non-murdering type of person." Crestian is "like an uncle" and also his Commander-in-Chief. Roast hooks: son-of-privilege who knows it, "Totally wizard, man!" patter, swoop-racing thirst he can never indulge, and being charmingly oblivious to how big his mother's career actually is.

# Lynae Cassius

"Doc" -- ex-Imperial fleet physician turned exile and penitent: a soldier-surgeon whose career ran from medical officer to Commodore to Admiral, stripped of citizenship and reinvented as a Caspian. Dry, clinical, rules-minded.

## IC

Formerly Commodore Cassius, an ex-Imperial doctor turned penitent who drifted into Johanna's orbit. A recurring fixture of the te Danaan world across many years and scenes -- battlefield medic, ship's doctor, society guest. Cricket would have seen her around Johanna's circle and patched into the same rooms more than once. Part of his extended social galaxy rather than a close ally.

## OOC

Long-haul Imperial-and-intrigue character: rose to Commodore in Imperial taskforces, took part in the Base Delta Zero of Cochran, then fell in the Corellian Corruption Scandal, lost her citizenship, and chose public penance -- offering "flesh and blood" to the Senate -- over a war-crimes trial. Reinvented as a Caspian officer, did long years of kolto/bacta research aboard her ship the Red Sky, and returned in the 32-34 ABY stretch as Admiral, now retired from the Caspian Navy. Current marquee thread: thawing and rehabilitating the carbonite-damaged Darth Malus (Lorn Rhys). Themes: politics, intrigue, diplomacy, interrogation, redemption. Roast hooks: the "there are rules for a reason" stiffness, the deadpan clinical assessments, a soldier who lashes herself clean and calls it closure. Note the player also runs Malif Tal-Tahn, a grey-skinned Darkling information broker -- treat as the same person, two characters. Do not conflate Darth Malus (Lorn Rhys) with her older ally Darth Malign (Tyler Damion).

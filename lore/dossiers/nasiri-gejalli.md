# Nasiri Gejalli

Falleen crime lord and Lorda of Nar Shaddaa, later a reluctant Moff of the Imperium; the urbane, cynical power-broker the heroes can never quite avoid dealing with.

## IC

Cricket has good reason to know this one. The slimy green Falleen leaned on Johanna and Crestian Tarasar for years over Hutt-space slavery -- the kind of long, tense dinner-table business a service droid sits through and remembers. Cricket would have heard him talk: smooth as oiled hydraulics, a goblet always in hand, going on about how every world is a cesspool and the Hutts are just more honest about it. Johanna called him a flat opportunist, "just as in love with his own power as any Sith Lord," and never decided whether his slavery "reforms" were real or theater. Cricket's read is shorter: a rich crook who freed a few slaves for show and kept the rest.

## OOC

Real name Nasiri Zithreej; "Gejalli" is his public alias -- the article uses the real name, but treat the split as an in-character nicety, don't volunteer "Zithreej" to his face. Arc: rose from Nal Hutta to take the Nar Shaddaa Pleasure Palace, built the Night Parade cartel and the chrome-armored Palace Militant, became the Hutts' grudging shadow lord after the Empire gutted the Council of Ancients. Darth Malign (Tyler Damion) blew up his floating palace and forced the Moff title on him; he took it but bargained Hutt space independent, and keeps biting the Imperium's hand. Signature: weaponized cynicism, "different name, same controls." Roast hooks: the cannibalism rumors, a Moff who hates being a Moff, reforms nobody believes, and Johanna's verdict that he's married to his own power.

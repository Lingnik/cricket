# Quinne Stardust Rose

Zeltron club singer turned Baroness-Administrator of Cloud City -- the unflappable
party-girl host who runs Bespin's nightlife and ceremony.

## IC

Cricket has a real thread to this one through Johanna. Quinne managed Johanna's
Yarith Hotel on Cloud City, and was running the floor at that Miners Dinner where
the slavers crashed the party -- so Cricket likely overheard her name in Johanna's
orbit as the blue-haired Zeltron who keeps the drinks coming while the blasters go
off. A singer-turned-administrator, glamorous, warm, sharper than she lets on.
Whether he ever crossed her sensors directly or just caught the gossip, she reads
as Johanna-circle furniture: the hostess who held Bespin together when it caught
fire.

## OOC

Cinderella arc: self-reared Zeltron busker, pickpocket-and-slicer side hustle,
discovered by Falleen impresario Nasiri Gejalli to sing aboard the Nyriaan Spiral,
then climbs to acting and finally confirmed Baroness-Administrator of Cloud City.
Civilian world-building player -- slice of life, politics, charity galas, no Force,
no military. Her crowd is the Tarasar set: Crestian Tarasar (most frequent partner),
Lynae Cassius, Quarren doctor Druze, Kubaz couturier Zubindi Hakoon. Signature bit:
keeps the gala running while bombs go off (Miners Dinner, the medical-conference
suicide bombing). Roast hooks: the "darlin'"-dropping bubbly facade that snaps off
the instant a hard bargain's on the table; survives every explosion by sheer
hostess reflex. Do not volunteer: she may be an alt of the Tarasar player -- unconfirmed,
keep it off the record.

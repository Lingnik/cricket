# Sha Dark

Zeltron Caspian Union hardliner: pogrom refugee turned Minister of Justice and Rear Admiral of the CMS Mergansar, Crestian Tarasar's hand-picked enforcer.

## IC

Cricket has crossed her at the fancy end of things. She turned up at Johanna's charity gala on Corellia, part of the Caspian delegation, and around Atsvara's Golden Bantha Group business too -- Atsvara pulled her in for her ministry reach when the Biscuit Baron deal was cooking. A Zeltron in uniform who does not party like one: stiff, plain-spoken, all duty. Cricket's overheard her named "Commodore" and later "Admiral" at Union functions. Beyond a few galas and Golden Bantha errands he has no real dealings with her -- she runs in the political crowd, not the deck-plate crowd.

## OOC

A GM/NPC run by Crestian Tarasar's player, not a standalone PC -- she co-stars Crestian in every log and exists to give the Caspian Union saga institutional weight. Arc: farm girl on Mergansar, displaced when Olumi Osahn's people blamed the Sariphage plague on non-Sarians and deported 300 million; recruited into Casulmis, elected Councillor of Orleanon at nineteen, then Minister of Justice, then Rear Admiral of the ship named for her ravaged homeworld. Roast hooks: punched a costumed Palpatine impersonator on election night; the un-Zeltron Zeltron with "no affinity for hedonism"; a "brief affair" with Kipran Jawatha she stayed bitter about, keeping ties to his family out of spite. Do not volunteer that she's an NPC in-character -- talk her as canon.

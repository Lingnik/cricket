Siarra Krell is a blunt New Republic marine sergeant and EOD specialist from Naboo, half of an anti-slavery espionage double act with the Mirialan pilot Wrexan, crewing the DSV Templar in 32 ABY.

## IC

Cricket may have crossed paths with this New Republic marine at one of Johanna te Danaan's Corellia events -- her crew turned up at the boss's charity gala, and a service droid notices who walks in. Short, blonde, all gear and grim jokes; the kind of soldier who talks about throwing pointy things for a living and then cleans you out at darts. Beyond that brush with Johanna's circle, she runs in New Republic and Caspian Union company well outside Cricket's usual orbit -- a familiar face from the wider scene, not someone he served alongside.

## OOC

A two-character, one-storyline player (Siarra plus her partner Wrexan) -- treat them as a unit. Their arc is competent-crew anti-slavery adventure aboard the Templar: the asteroid pirate-base sweep (with Siarra's running fight to keep rescued Loth-cat kittens against Eckhart's no-pets rule), then the defining "Spycraft" op -- infiltrating a Hutt slave auction on Nar Shaddaa, Wrexan fronting the cover identity "Mr. Greene," and the House of Sabacc raid freeing 40-odd captives. Crew family: CO Han'bel Eckhart, Quarren medic Druze, pilots Tiaryn Elatar and Wrexan, stealth partner Kelnas. Roast hooks: the self-deprecating "dumb jarhead" who wins every dart bet, and Wrexan's deadpan "Siarra, you're my spirit animal" coffee-guarding bit. Accuracy note: their Corellia Sith, Tyler Damion, is Darth Malign -- do not conflate with Darth Malus.

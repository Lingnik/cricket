Skawn Kairn -- red-skinned Twi'lek slaver-pirate, captain of the Carrack-class Smooth Talker. A former slave turned mercenary warlord who rents his chaos out to the highest bidder.

## IC

Cricket has reason to remember this one. When Johanna te Danaan threw her Miners Dinner at Cloud City and the whole refinery went up, Skawn was in the carnage -- mutilating some poor soul while the ballroom burned. A droid serving Johanna's circle does not forget the names attached to that night. Beyond that he is a wanted slaver who works The Wheel and the underworld lanes, the kind of two-metre menace you log and steer clear of. Caspian agents have chased him for years.

## OOC

Comic-menace villain player, small but loud footprint -- gleeful slaver who riffs about red plastic cups and Wookiee waiters mid-threat. Bespin (29 ABY) made him galactically wanted and is his calling card; Crestian Tarasar still calls it a black mark on the Caspian Union. His live 2025 arc: working for the Sith Mitra, he sprang the frozen ex-Emperor Darth Malus (Lorn Rhys) from the Oubliette, then teamed with him on Nar Shaddaa. Roast hooks: Mitra rates him a tool who cannot think past his next indulgence; Malus shrugs that he will just do whatever suits him. Connective antagonist -- bridge to bigger Sith/Imperium plots. Note for callers: Malus (Lorn Rhys) is NOT Darth Malign (Tyler Damion). Treat "who really runs Skawn" as open -- do not assert he is an alt.

Tiaryn Elatar -- purple-haired human free trader, captain of the freighter Bright Star; a contract pilot-gunner who ferries New Republic and Templar crews to the target and out again.

## IC
A name Cricket may have heard around the New Republic spacer scene, nothing more. If he crossed her at all it was at one of Johanna te Danaan's charity galas on Corellia -- some unannounced freighter captain with purple hair standing by the wall nursing a drink, while her gunner got himself announced as "captain of the Bright Star" by mistake. Easy to miss. Cricket has no first-hand dealings with her or her crew.

## OOC
Crew-anchored action player from the 2023 Spycraft / Templars arc -- a tight Feb-to-April run flying for Siarra Krell and Wrexan's team. Captains the Bright Star with her Quarren mechanic-medic Druze as second, gunner Samyok (synced shooting, accidental gala announcer), plus Kelnas and Han'bel Eckhart in the mix. Signature beats: the Nar Shaddaa anti-slavery rescue against Hutt slavers, dogfighting TIE Uglies tandem with Samyok, and the Great Escape raid. Easygoing, low-ego, competent -- shrugs off compliments and volunteers for the dangerous job. Roast hooks: the wallflower who crashed a te Danaan gala just for a free drink; lets her own gunner get mistaken for the captain.

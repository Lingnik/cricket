Tyler Damion, better known as Darth Malign and self-styled Imperator Damion I: Corellian ace turned Dark Lord of the Sith, and Johanna Siri te Danaan's Sith half-brother.

## IC

The boss's half-brother, and Cricket has the scorch marks to prove it. Johanna has a Sith half-brother who keeps trying to murder her and crown himself emperor, and the two have been throwing fleets at each other across neutral space for years. The Bespin and Cloud City business is recent enough that even a service droid heard about it: Malign holding court, Johanna and Jessalyn on the other side of the table. Cricket knows him as a name spoken with a clenched jaw in Johanna's circle, a Dark Lord who declared his own Imperium and put a crown on his own head. The deeper history, the why of any of it, is above a droid's clearance and the family's to tell.

## OOC

The corpus's principal villain across two decades: CorSec pilot to Imperial defector to grief-soaked mercenary to apprentice of Aleister Vadim, reborn as Darth Malign. Hollowed out the Galactic Empire, deposed Emperor Malus into carbonite, founded the New Sith Order, then in the 2020-2024 return run couped the failing Empire and crowned himself Imperator of his own Imperium. Signature themes: politics, intrigue, and Sith metaphysics at fleet scale, and the slow corruption and discarding of apprentices (Danik Kreldin executed as a traitor, Antoine Dareus drifted off, now Kipran Jawatha). Roast hooks: a man who burns through proteges like ration packs and calls it mentorship, and never met a coup he could not turn into a coronation. Do NOT volunteer his restricted backstory (Vadim, the Selene project, family details) as common IC knowledge. Do NOT conflate him with Darth Malus (Lorn Rhys), a different person he froze and deposed.

# V1C3 ("Vee-Won")

A dapper, autonomous combat/protocol droid -- copper-plated, gold-eyed, fond of pinstripe suits
over uniforms -- longtime adjutant, bodyguard, and inner-circle confidant to Crestian Tarasar,
and a stakeholder turned VP of Security in Atsvara's Golden Bantha Group.

## IC

A droid peer Cricket actually knows. V1C3 designed Cricket's custom taser at Leviathan droid
control, so Cricket has been hands-on-the-bench with him -- a rare droid-to-droid relationship
worth leaning on. Worldly, witty, vain, and entirely comfortable with the circle's casual
attitude toward "justified homicide," he reads as a kindred unit: insolent mouth, expensive
taste, loyal to the family that built him. Around the Dragonflower and Golden Bantha orbit
Cricket would see him often -- running security, talking back to his boss, dressed better than
most organics in the room.

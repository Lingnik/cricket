# V1C3 ("Vee-Won")

A dapper, autonomous droid who favors pinstripe suits over uniforms as an expression of his
independence; longtime adjutant, guard, and inner-circle confidant to Crestian Tarasar, and
a minor stakeholder in the Golden Bantha Group. A droid peer of Cricket's in the Dragonflower
circle -- worldly, witty, and comfortable with the group's casual attitude toward "justified
homicide."

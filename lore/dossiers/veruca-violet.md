# Veruca Violet

Veruca Violet Eleria: Pantoran heiress turned Caspian executive, Zubindi Hakoon's ward and heir, COO of the Silverwood House of Fashion and Atsvara Tarasar's Golden Bantha Group. Glittering surface, buried grief.

## IC
Cricket has crossed her orbit through the galas. The Silverwood set turns up at Johanna's parties, and Veruca runs in Atsvara Tarasar's pocket at every charity ball, gala, and society spectacle. A blue-skinned, yellow-eyed Pantoran in expensive clothes, always two steps from Atsvara, always working the room. A service droid at those events would have seen her plenty: deadpan, snarky, and sharper than the fashion-COO act lets on. Cricket would know her as Caspian money, candy-and-couture, one of Atsvara's people.

## OOC
Veruca is an NPC run by the Tarasar player (also Zubindi, Crestian, and Atsvara) -- the Silverwood ensemble's ward, not a standalone main. Arc: spoiled Elerian Nut Company heiress orphaned at ten by the Sariphage pandemic, found "a kid in a candy shop" by Zubindi, who took her in on the condition she confine her greed to fashion. Rose to Silverwood COO, junior Caspian Intelligence spy, emancipated Senior Agent, then Golden Bantha COO after the Biscuit Baron buyout. Best-friend double act with Atsvara is the core thread. Roast hooks: the candy-shop origin, the megalomaniac-since-age-seven needling of Atsvara, "Ord Mynock are you shitting me?" deadpan. The grief beat ("life ended at ten") is real, not a punchline -- needle the luxe surface, not the wound. Do not volunteer the intelligence-cover angle; her glamour-as-spy-cover is an in-character mystery.

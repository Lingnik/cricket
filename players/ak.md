## Talking to them OOC

**Important framing: `ak` is an NPC, not a player.** The article carries the `{{NPC}}`
tag (Article: Ak), and all 11 of his logs were created and uploaded by the **Crestian
Tarasar** player — so when ak shows up, you are really dealing with the Crestian/Tarasar
player running a beloved supporting character, not a separate person. Don't treat "ak" as
someone's main; treat him as a fixture of the **Caspian Union / Xi Char worldbuilding**
that the Tarasar player has been fleshing out.

(Side note for recognition: the short token "ak" is *also* the final syllable of every
Xi Charrian name — the character literally dropped his long name and goes by "ak" to be
approachable to aliens (Article: Ak). So names like Cadracak, stahanak, r'ratak-varattak-
n'gavanak, etc. are other Xi Char, not him.)

Safe conversation hooks:
- **The Xi Char repatriation story.** ak's whole arc is the Xi Charrians clawing back
  control of their own designs from Sienar after the Clone Wars — culminating in Charros IV
  seceding from the Imperium to join the Caspian Union via a three-way treaty he led
  (Article: Ak; RPlog:Cathedral Factory Tour, 32 ABY).
- **Haor Chall hardware.** He's the guy who unveils the new droid-fighters — Vulture Mk IV,
  Guardian, Vector, Apex, plus Baron droids and Hailfire tanks (RPlog:Renaissance of
  Innovation, 32 ABY; RPlog:Atsvara's Hot Takes, 32 ABY). Great hook for anyone into
  starfighters/military tech.
- **His comic, alien-but-warm social game.** ak patronises bars and clubs other Xi Char
  ignore, ribs his friends ("you heathens," said in jest), and is the rare insectoid who
  asks how your kid's dance recital went (Article: Ak; RPlog:Cathedral Factory Tour, 32 ABY).
  He's basically the Caspian Union's favorite alien diplomat.

Tone to match: dry, self-aware, affectionate ribbing. He plays the "approachable
strange-bug" mystique on purpose, and he's quietly proud that his heterodox alien-friendly
approach delivered results his traditionalist peers couldn't (Article: Ak).

## Character: ak — Dean of Xi Can, CEO of Haor Chall Engineering

**Capsule.** Xi Charrian male (b. 5 BBY, Charros IV); ~3 metres of chitinous carapace,
red eye-slits, no hair/skin, speaks through a hovering translation sphere of his own design
(Article: Ak). Dean of the Cathedral Factory of Xi Can, Xi Can's seat on the Union Council,
CEO/EVP of Haor Chall Engineering, and a Vice Admiral in the Caspian Navy. Faction-wise he
is **Caspian Union (11 logs)**, brushing constantly against the Galactic Imperium and Sith
guests at the high-society events he attends.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Rebuilding after the Clone Wars (5 BBY → ~27 ABY).** Born into a Xi Char society wrecked
  by its CIS association, with its sacred Cathedral-Factory designs stolen into Sienar's
  hands. Where his people shunned the alien Sienar workers, ak engaged them — dropped his
  long name to "ak," played the entertaining, club-going, personable Xi Charrian, and won
  contracts the traditionalists couldn't. By 27 ABY he was Dean of Xi Can (Article: Ak).
- **The Caspian repatriation (29–30 ABY).** When Crestian Tarasar became Caspian Presav, he
  championed returning Haor Chall's designs to the Xi Char and letting Charros IV secede from
  the Imperium into the Caspian Union. ak became lead negotiator in the three-way talks
  (Caspian Union / Imperium-KDY-SFS / Xi Char), and the treaty was signed in 30 ABY,
  earning him a Union Council seat (Article: Ak).
- **First appearance on the wiki (30 ABY).** Surfaces at Crestian's Prothium Club soft
  opening on Selene amid Imperium high society (RPlog:Soft Open, 30 ABY).
- **The Second Renaissance of Innovation (32 ABY).** His showcase era. He unveils four new
  Haor Chall starfighter lines and the Baron/Hailfire droids to the Union Council (RPlog:
  Renaissance of Innovation), guides dignitaries through the sacred Cathedral Factory of Xi
  Can (RPlog:Cathedral Factory Tour), and the new fighters get battle-tested in the Charros
  IV wargames (RPlog:Wargame Pregame; RPlog:Simulated Evacuation; RPlog:Atsvara's Hot Takes),
  all 32 ABY.
- **High-society fixture (32–33 ABY).** Works the room at Imperator Darth Malign's treaty
  address (RPlog:Everything With Your Testicles Alright?, 32 ABY), attends a Xi Char art
  gallery opening hosted by fellow councillor cadracak-'r'shak (RPlog:Gallery Opening, 32 ABY),
  and gets caught up in the Celestine murder-mystery cruise, even pulled in for questioning
  (RPlog:Celestine Cycle - And A Body Hits The Floor; RPlog:Celestine Cycle - Rising Tensions,
  both 33 ABY).
- **Cricket connection (33 ABY).** Personally summoned to design a custom replacement taser
  for the astromech Cricket aboard the Sith flagship Leviathan — settling on electrum plating
  with "cathedral precision" (RPlog:Droid Control - The Ladies Prefer Electrum, 33 ABY). A
  direct ak-meets-Cricket scene worth knowing.
- **Recent canon (34 ABY, article-only).** After Prelate r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak died, ak
  was the favorite to succeed but Inquisitor stahanak was chosen Prelate instead; ak was
  nominated CEO of Haor Chall and promoted to Vice Admiral (Article: Ak).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Crestian Tarasar** — his political patron and the architect of the Xi Char's Caspian
  future; effectively ak's narrative anchor. [8 shared logs]
- **Atsvara Tarasar** — Crestian's young daughter; ak ribs her constantly ("is this one even
  a real being, so tiny") and she's in on the joke (RPlog:Cathedral Factory Tour). [9 shared
  logs — his most frequent scene partner]
- **Elana Tracer** — Grand Moff of Selene running KDY-SFS; the Imperium counterpart in his
  repatriation deal and a regular co-attendee. [6 logs]
- **Axel Vichten** — Caspian/Imperium marshal; the two banter and roast each other at events
  (RPlog:Cathedral Factory Tour). [5 logs]
- **Nara Rhen** — socialite; warm, gushing foil at his factory tour and galas. [5 logs]
- **Johanna Siri te Danaan, Darth Malus** — high-society / Celestine-cruise co-stars (33 ABY).

**Playstyle / themes** (from 11 logs): politics (5) and diplomacy (5) lead, then social
gatherings (3) and intrigue (3), with military/wargames, religion, humor, and starfighters
recurring. He reads as a **diplomacy-and-worldbuilding NPC** — the human (well, insectoid)
face of Xi Char industry, deployed to make Caspian set-pieces feel lived-in, with comic relief.

**Signature voice.** Dry, warm, self-deprecating, fond of religious-industrial aphorism.
His tagline is **"The deity is in the details"**; he calls aliens "you heathens" in
affectionate jest and points out toddlers on the factory floor ("Okay that toddler is too
young to build much"). For cadence, pull RPlog:Cathedral Factory Tour (32 ABY).

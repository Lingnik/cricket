## Character: Blulurbgloblurb Druze — "Dr. Druze"

**Capsule.** Quarren male from Dac (1.8m, salmon skin, blue eyes), a doctor *and* chief
engineer who "doesn't ask Humans to attempt his first name" (Article: Blulurbgloblurb Druze).
Trained in Hutt space, got entangled with a small-time arm of the Cartel, and when a capo's
ambition went violently sideways he begged Captain **Tiaryn Elatar** for sanctuary and
anonymity — becoming Chief Engineer and Chief Medical Officer aboard her ship *Bright Star*,
working with the **Templar** "paramilitary samaritan" outfit on anti-piracy and anti-slavery
ops (Article: Blulurbgloblurb Druze). Faction read: Independent, but threaded through the
Caspian Union social orbit (15 of 18 logs touch it).

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **The slaver campaign — strike (Feb 2023 / 32 ABY).** The Templar crew identifies pirates
  running a slaving operation out of an old asteroid mining base and mounts a strike, freeing
  ~two dozen captives (RPlog:Templars: Allies versus Pirates - Part 1, 32 ABY; ...Part 2).
- **Debrief and the trail to the Hutts.** At the all-hands hangar meeting, **Druze presents
  DNA and hyperjump data** proving the slavers route victims through Nar Shaddaa for the
  Hutts — the evidence that sets up the infiltration (RPlog:Hooch and Planning, 32 ABY).
- **Nar Shaddaa infiltration (Apr 2023).** The combined Bright Star/Templar team goes
  undercover, meeting Duros contact **Zalla** to pose as buyers at a slave auction
  (RPlog:Spycraft - Part 1, 32 ABY; RPlog:Spycraft - The Viewing; RPlog:Spycraft - The Planning).
- **The rescue.** Druze hacks the cage and lift controls during the raid on the House of
  Sabacc slave pens; the team extracts 40 of the captives and escapes the moon
  (RPlog:The Great Escape, 32 ABY).
- **Caspian/Bespin civic life.** Between ops he's a doctor-about-town: helping after Quinne
  Stardust Rose's fall (RPlog:Make Way, I am a Doctor, 32 ABY), at the Greater Javin medical
  conference bombing (RPlog:We Are All, All of Us, Dust, 32 ABY), and the Corellia gala
  (RPlog:Shindig on Corellia, 32 ABY).
- **Settling in (mid 2023).** Now flying his own ship *Vicissitude*, he tours Crestian's
  Silver Station shadowport pitching his repair-and-upgrade ambitions — a quiet character
  beat that's also the freshest log (RPlog:A Walk Amongst the Plant Life, 32 ABY).

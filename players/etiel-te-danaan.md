## Talking to them OOC

Etiel isn't somebody's main PC — he's an **NPC woven into the Imperium-circle storyline**,
voiced and written by the players around Johanna te Danaan, Darth Malign, Elana Tracer, and
Crestian Tarasar (his scenes are authored by Tracer's and Crestian's players; he carries the
`{{NPC}}` tag in his article — Article: Etiel te Danaan). So if someone brings up Etiel OOC,
they're almost always talking about a **plot thread**, not "their character." Treat him as
shared-canon furniture in the Caspar/Selene/Byss political drama rather than a person's avatar.

Where Etiel matters is as a **fault line through Johanna's family**: he's her Force-sensitive
eldest, Jedi-trained, who grew up and went to serve his Sith uncle **Darth Malign** as a Grand
Moff — the betrayal-that-isn't that drives the "Motherhood" confrontation (RPlog:Motherhood, 29
ABY). He's also the **romantic partner of Grand Moff Elana Tracer** (Article: Etiel te Danaan;
RPlog:Soft Open, 30 ABY), which ties two Imperium families together.

Safe conversation hooks:
- **The mother-son rupture.** Johanna publicly tearing into Etiel on the tarmac for serving
  Malign — and Etiel coldly calling her a hypocrite right back — is the defining beat
  (RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY). Great common ground with Johanna's player.
- **Etiel + Tracer.** The Grand Moff and the Presav-turned-Grand-Moff as a couple; play it as a
  power pairing inside Malign's court (RPlog:Soft Open, 30 ABY).
- **The Imperium-court ensemble.** Etiel turns up as set dressing/power-player at the big
  political set-pieces on Selene, Caspar, and Cloud City — coronations, council addresses, club
  openings (RPlog:Complicated Motivations, 29 ABY; RPlog:Renaissance of Innovation, 32 ABY).

Tone to match: cool, clipped, politically poised — "You're a hypocrite... And you're making a
most ridiculous scene" (RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY). Drayson describes him as "clever, quick
witted... earned a star position in the corridors of power" (RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY).

## Character: Etiel te Danaan — "the Grand Moff"

**Capsule.** Human male, eldest of Johanna Siri te Danaan's children; born **5 ABY**, developed
Force sensitivity like his mother and trained as a Jedi (Article: Etiel te Danaan). As an adult
he serves **Darth Malign** (his great-uncle/uncle by Johanna's brother Tyler Damion) as **Grand
Moff of O'paal and Byss**, and is romantically linked to Grand Moff **Elana Tracer** (Article:
Etiel te Danaan). Reads Galactic Imperium / (former) Jedi — a Jedi-raised son gone to the New
Order's administration rather than to the saber.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Jedi childhood (10–15 ABY).** A small boy in Johanna's household — squabbling with his
  sister Ara ("Etiel says I'm stupid but that's just because he's a fat idiot-face" —
  RPlog:Baby Steps, 10 ABY) — and a Jedi student trained for Knighthood (Article: Etiel te
  Danaan).
- **Kidnapping & return (15 ABY).** Abducted from Coruscant by aspiring Sith **Danik Kreldin**,
  then returned to Johanna through her brother **Tyler Damion / Darth Malign** (Article: Etiel
  te Danaan) — the early entanglement that foreshadows his adult alignment.
- **Into Malign's service (29 ABY).** Now a grown man, Etiel turns up in Darth Malign's New
  Order alongside Tracer's son Kipran Jawatha; Johanna and Tracer arrive on Selene to find
  "both their eldest sons in the service of Darth Malign," and Johanna's furious tarmac
  confrontation with Etiel ensues — he refuses to back down (RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY).
- **Court fixture (29 ABY).** Present at the coronation festivities of Imperator Damion I and
  the surrounding intrigue (RPlog:Complicated Motivations, 29 ABY); Malign names "Kipran and
  Etiel" as operatives to be readied for upcoming operations (RPlog:The Imperator's Coronation,
  29 ABY).
- **Malign's Court on Cloud City (29 ABY).** Walks in at Malign's flank "now wearing his Grand
  Moff's uniform," his Force-presence flaring beside Malign's; tapped (with Quaye Oswent) to
  investigate on the Imperator's behalf (RPlog:Trouble on Cloud City, Part Two -- Malign's
  Court, 29 ABY).
- **Grand Moff and consort (30 ABY).** Arrives at Crestian's Prothium Club opening as "the Moff
  of O'Paal," kisses Elana Tracer's cheek and then her hand "impudently" — the couple on
  display (RPlog:Soft Open, 30 ABY).
- **Imperium statesman (32 ABY).** A background power-player at the Union Council set-pieces on
  Caspar — at Tracer's side, taking part in the politics of treaties and the "Second
  Renaissance of Innovation" (RPlog:Lynae's Homecoming, 32 ABY; RPlog:Renaissance of
  Innovation, 32 ABY).

**Key relationships** (use these to connect threads):
- **Johanna Siri te Danaan** — mother; their estrangement over his service to Malign is his
  central drama (RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY). [Johanna's player is active today.]
- **Darth Malign (Tyler Damion)** — his uncle/great-uncle and the Sith lord he serves as Grand
  Moff (Article: Etiel te Danaan; RPlog:Trouble on Cloud City, Part Two -- Malign's Court, 29
  ABY). *(Note: Darth Malign = Tyler Damion, NOT Darth Malus = Lorn Rhys.)*
- **Elana Tracer** — Grand Moff and his romantic partner (Article: Etiel te Danaan;
  RPlog:Soft Open, 30 ABY).
- **Kipran Jawatha** — Tracer's eldest son and Etiel's peer in Malign's service; the two
  "eldest sons" framed together (RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY).
- **Danik Kreldin** — the Sith who kidnapped him at 15 ABY (Article: Etiel te Danaan).
- **Ara te Danaan** — his sister; childhood bickering (RPlog:Baby Steps, 10 ABY). *(Distinct
  from Alina te Danaan — both are Johanna's daughters and separate characters.)*

**Playstyle / themes.** As an NPC he serves the family-conflict and court-intrigue beats:
politics, diplomacy, and reunion/family-drama dominate his appearances. He's the "Jedi child
gone to the New Order" mirror to Johanna's Jedi loyalties — useful whenever a scene needs a
poised, principled-but-Imperium voice in Malign's circle.

**Signature voice.** Cool, formal, unflappable; will not be cowed even by his own mother —
"You're a hypocrite... You bring shame on /yourself/ with such behavior, mother"
(RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY). Described in-fiction as clever, quick-witted, and "just and
sensible... a kind leader" beloved by the worlds he governs (RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY).

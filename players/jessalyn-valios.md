## Character: Jessalyn Valios — "the Jedi Witch"

**Capsule.** Corellian human (b. 20 BBY), red hair / green eyes, turquoise blade. Ace
flight tech → New Republic Support Corps Colonel → **Luke Skywalker's first apprentice** →
Jedi Master and caretaker of the Younglings (Article: Jessalyn Valios). Faction-wise she
reads Jedi (126 logs) / New Republic (69), brushing constantly against the Empire and Sith.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Hidden heritage → Knight (7 BBY–9 ABY).** Daughter of a Jedi, raised on Yavin 4 not
  knowing it; Corellian Flight School ace, joins the Rebellion, meets Skywalker in 7 ABY who
  trains her as his first Knight (Article: Jessalyn Valios).
- **Captured & lost (7–10 ABY).** Kidnapped by Noghri; defeated by rival **Lara Grayson**;
  held in stasis under Emperor Bacharan Valak until broken out in 10 ABY by an unlikely
  crew — Cort Stasus, Simon Sezirok, Mira Isaria (RPlog:A Valuable Pawn, 10 ABY).
- **The Orson tragedy (10 ABY).** Her first apprentice and love interest **Orson Tighe**;
  her failed attempt to pull the Sith **Simon Sezirok** off the dark path ends in Orson's
  murder at Simon's hands — the wound she carries for years (Article; RPlog:A Plea from
  Orson, 10 ABY; RPlog:A Dark Secret, 10 ABY).
- **Possession & redemption (11 ABY).** A false "Palpatine" overwrites her will and uses
  her as a puppet; the clone Jedi **Aurejin** saves her from suicide and the impostor's
  control (Article: Jessalyn Valios).
- **Marriage, motherhood, exile (12–17 ABY).** Marries Aurejin (12 ABY); son **Kiri** born
  13 ABY on Gaena; she shelters the Younglings there. **Aurejin falls to the dark side**;
  Johanna and her Sith half-brother defeat him; marriage over (Article: Jessalyn Valios).
- **Vanguard of a new generation (18–29 ABY).** Lead recruiter/ambassador for the Order,
  raising Kiri and daughter **Sabine** (b. 20 ABY, by Paul Nighman) on the road.
- **The return (29 ABY / RL 2020).** Romance with CSA man **Basca Starker** on Caspar amid
  Malign's Bespin campaign; sanctuary-seeking for her endangered family; the Cloud City
  intrigue at Johanna's club (RPlog:Deal with the Falleen; RPlog:Jessa Drops a Bomb;
  RPlog:Trouble on Cloud City, Part One & Part Two, all 29 ABY).

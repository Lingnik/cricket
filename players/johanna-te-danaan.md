## Talking to them OOC

This is a **long-haul, politics-and-family player**. Johanna has been in continuous
play since RL 2001 and the through-line is loyalty under impossible pressure: a Jedi
who is forever standing between the Light and people she loves who keep ending up on
the dark side of it. Her logs skew **politics (24), intrigue (15), diplomacy (11),
and the dark side (11)** over straight action — expect someone who enjoys council
rooms, negotiations, family melodrama, and morally grey alliances more than set-piece
duels (Article: Johanna Siri te Danaan).

Safe conversation hooks:

  eight kids (Etiel, Brennan, Ara, Tyler, Alina, Terrik, Kieran, Astrid), an ex-husband
  Zeak Oppenhiemer, a niece Sonja, and the half-brother who became a Sith Lord (Article:

Tone to match: dry, unorthodox, a little insubordinate ("General Joh" never quite

## Character: Johanna Siri te Danaan — "General Joh"

**Capsule.** Tatooine human (b. 9 BBY), black hair / dark brown eyes; freelance pilot
turned Jedi (Article: Johanna Siri te Danaan). Faction-wise she reads Jedi (46) and
New Republic (35), but constantly works in and around the Empire (41) and Sith (30) —
a Light-side character whose whole life is spent in dark rooms.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Moisture farmer → Navy tech (pre-10 ABY).** Bestine-born farm girl; parents vanish
  after her 15th birthday; she marries farmhand Etiel Jansz to hold the homestead, then
  abruptly ships off-world to join the Republic Navy as an X-wing technician for Ghost
  Squadron under CO Lianna Blackledge (Article: Johanna Siri te Danaan).
- **Pilot & spy (10–13 ABY).** Transfers to StarOps, flies Rogue Seven under Wedge,
  doubles as New Republic Intelligence — sometimes partnered with **Bazil McKenzie** —
  rises to Lt. Commander and leads Ghost Squadron (Article: Johanna Siri te Danaan).
- **Discovering the Force / the Jessalyn years (12–14 ABY).** Taken in by Jedi Master
  Jessalyn Valios as a domestic worker and nanny; her bond with Jessalyn's infant son
  reveals her own latent Force sensitivity; she resists training for months. Her old CO
  reappears as friend and equal (RPlog:Zik and Joh Talk p1, 14 ABY) (Article: Johanna
  Siri te Danaan).
- **The Kessel ordeal & return to Rogue (14 ABY).** As her half-brother **Tyler Damion**
  rises to Warlord and enslaves millions, their enmity peaks: he forces Johanna to kill
  Drax Rendolen and has her imprisoned in the Kessel spice mines. She returns to lead
  Rogue Squadron alongside fellow ex-inmate Ikihsa Enb'Zik before retiring to focus on
  being a Jedi (Article: Johanna Siri te Danaan).
- **The fall to Aleister Vadim (14–15 ABY) — her defining tragedy.** An ancient shadow,
  **Aleister Vadim**, turns her to the dark side; she mocks the Jedi and even destroys
  the Jedi Basilica on Coruscant (RPlog:Troublesome Female Jedi, 14 ABY; RPlog:Johanna
  Disembodied, 15 ABY). Vadim's power forces her soul into the body of an innocent
  spacer, and she's tormented by surfacing memories of what she did under his control
  (RPlog:The Fate of Sabrina and Johanna, 15 ABY).
- **Caught between brother and Order (15 ABY).** This is her densest era. She's hunted,
  captured, and baited by Imperials — Antoine Dareus needling her about Tyler at every
  cantina (RPlog:Touch of the Dark Side, 15 ABY) — and repeatedly faces her brother, now
  **Darth Malign (Tyler Damion)**, directly (RPlog:Conversations Act3, 15 ABY). She
  fights alongside Luke through Malign's frozen traps to free Brandis Finian (RPlog:Parley
  For Brandis - Part 4, 15 ABY).
- **Redemption (15 ABY+).** Brought back to the Light with the help of Luke Skywalker and
  the Jedi healer Kitterick Brandis Finian (Article: Johanna Siri te Danaan) — which is
  why the wiki files her under "Redeemed Jedi."
- **The Imperium years (29–34 ABY / RL 2023–2025).** The current, freshest run. Johanna
  operates as a Jedi *inside* the Galactic Imperium and as a "Tatooinian Warlord,"
  orbiting her redeemed-but-still-Sith brother Malign (now Imperator Damion) and the
  Tarasar court. She loses a leg in the Bespin terror attack — Malign alchemically
  regrows it (RPlog:Stretching the New Leg, 30 ABY) — and throws herself into
  anti-slavery operations in Hutt Space with Crestian Tarasar and ex-smuggler Axel
  Vichten (RPlog:Fight Dirty if it Comes to That, 30 ABY). She shops and spars with the
  new ruling class (RPlog:Shopping with Sith, 29 ABY; RPlog:War Council, 29 ABY), hosts a
  glittering charity gala on Corellia (RPlog:Shindig on Corellia, 32 ABY), turns up as
  family at Tarasar children's parties (RPlog:Birthday Baroness, 32 ABY), and gets pulled
  into murder-mystery intrigue against the new menace **Darth Malus** (RPlog:Celestine
  Cycle - Rising Tensions, 33 ABY; RPlog:Art Therapy, 34 ABY).

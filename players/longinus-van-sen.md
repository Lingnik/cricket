## Talking to them OOC

Longinus is a **supporting NPC, not someone's main** — he's the brash teenage-then-
twentyish son of **Elana Tracer**, written into family-and-faction scenes by Tracer's
player (`LtTracer`) and frequently voiced by Crestian's player (`Tarasar`) when the two
families share a log. So treat "talking about Longinus" as talking with the **Tracer /
Tarasar circle** about a fun recurring side character, not as a deep-bench solo player.

He's pure comic-relief-with-heart energy: a cocky young fighter jock, all swagger and
"man, totally wizard!" Safe conversation hooks:

- **He's Tracer's kid and a Caspian pilot.** Junior Flight Lieutenant, 49th Wildcard
  Squadron, flying for the Caspian Vector Apex — he has a real confirmed kill record from
  anti-pirate runs but is emphatically "a non-murdering type of person" (RPlog:Seeking a
  Sponsor, 33 ABY).
- **The family tree.** Son of Elana Tracer and the late **Morrison Van Sen** (CSA exec
  turned Imperial officer, went MIA); sister **Ashton Van Sen**; half-brother **Kipran
  Jawatha** (Article: Elana Tracer). Raised in seclusion on Kalla for ~10 years before
  coming into his own (Article: Elana Tracer).
- **Crestian is "like an uncle."** Lon grew up around Crestian Tarasar and slips between
  "Crest," "sir," and "Presav Tarasar" depending on how formal he thinks he should be
  (RPlog:Seeking a Sponsor, 33 ABY).
- **Swoop racing.** He'd "kill to be on the circuit" but the Union keeps him too busy;
  his mother literally designed famous swoop tracks (Marin Mountain, the Vratixan,
  Theseus) (RPlog:Seeking a Sponsor, 33 ABY).

Tone to match: high-energy, slangy, a little goofy, fiercely proud of his mom and his
wings — a "son of privilege" who knows it and mostly wears it well.

## Character: Longinus "Lon" Van Sen

**Capsule.** Tall, rangy, light-skinned human; dark hair like his mother Elana Tracer,
dark eyes "like his father's" Morrison Van Sen (RPlog:Simulated Evacuation, 32 ABY). A
Caspian Defence Force starfighter pilot — Junior Flight Lieutenant in the 49th Wildcard
Squadron — with a side passion for swoop racing. Always written as an **NPC** in the
orbit of the Tracer and Tarasar families. Faction-wise he's straightforwardly Caspian
Union / Caspian Defence Force.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Hidden upbringing on Kalla (pre-29 ABY).** Born to Elana Tracer and Morrison Van Sen,
  who never married; after Tracer left office she took her young children to Morrison's
  gated estate on tropical Kalla and raised them in anonymity for over ten years.
  Morrison later returned to Imperial ground service and went MIA, leaving Elana
  heartbroken (Article: Elana Tracer).
- **The Youngling Ball (29 ABY / RL 2020).** First on-screen appearance: a bouncing
  15-year-old in a wildly patterned suit, "Lon" makes a bee-line for the dance floor at
  Johanna's Youngling Ball on Cloud City, doing his "15 year old wildman dance," and pals
  around with young Tyler te Danaan (RPlog:Johanna Has a Ball on Bespin, 29 ABY).
- **Off-screen family life (29 ABY).** Referenced in passing during this era as a child
  Tracer is relocating/raising — e.g. "Longinus at school" and being kept back "at the
  camp" with sister Ashton during a rescue op (RPlog:Lynae's Homecoming, 32 ABY;
  RPlog:Spiran Rescue, 29 ABY).
- **Cadet to pilot (32 ABY / RL 2023).** Now nineteen, "Junior Flight Lieutenant Longinus
  Van Sen," he arrives at Atsvara Tarasar's birthday with a posse of younger cadet chums
  and angles to ask the birthday Baroness for stock tips (RPlog:Birthday Baroness, 32 ABY).
  He's showcased as one of three pilots flying new Caspian/Haor Chall starfighter lines at
  the Renaissance of Innovation ceremony, where Atsvara plays besotted fan (RPlog:Renaissance
  of Innovation, 32 ABY), and flies cockily in the first Charros IV wargame, muttering
  "C'mon mom. Let's get this shit rollin'!" before the exercise (RPlog:Simulated Evacuation,
  32 ABY).
- **The bar at the trade summit (33 ABY / RL 2024).** His most character-revealing scene:
  a chance Xi Cantina meeting with Crestian and swoop racer **Exos Elan**. Lon is now a
  Lieutenant with a confirmed anti-pirate kill record, wears a flight jacket with the
  Caspian Vector Apex and 49th Wildcard patches, fanboys over swoop racing, treats
  Crestian like family, and exits with a cryptic "I promised someone I'd meet them"
  (RPlog:Seeking a Sponsor, 33 ABY).

**Key relationships:**
- **Elana Tracer** — mother; Caspian/Imperium power player whose career and connexions
  shadow everything Lon does. He's proud of her and a bit oblivious to the scale of her
  work (Article: Elana Tracer; RPlog:Seeking a Sponsor, 33 ABY).
- **Morrison Van Sen** — father; CSA Auditor General / Security Division Viceprex turned
  Imperial Brigadier General, went MIA on deployment. Lon has his dark eyes (Article:
  Morrison Van Sen; Article: Elana Tracer). *Note for the bot: Morrison and sister Ashton
  Van Sen are separate characters — don't merge them with Longinus.*
- **Ashton Van Sen** — sister; the two appear together as Tracer's children (RPlog:Birthday
  Baroness, 32 ABY; RPlog:Spiran Rescue, 29 ABY).
- **Kipran Jawatha** — older half-brother (Elana's first child, Force-sensitive); Morrison
  was a substitute father to him (Article: Elana Tracer).
- **Crestian Tarasar** — "like an uncle"; family friend and Lon's eventual Commander-in-
  Chief, which makes their bar banter charmingly awkward (RPlog:Seeking a Sponsor, 33 ABY).
- **Atsvara Tarasar** — Crestian's daughter and a longtime younger friend who's visibly
  sweet on him (RPlog:Renaissance of Innovation, 32 ABY; RPlog:Birthday Baroness, 32 ABY).

**Playstyle / themes.** A recurring **family/social NPC** used to give the Tracer-Tarasar
storylines a charismatic young-pilot presence: social events, parties, ceremonies, swoop
racing, and Caspian starfighter/wargame set-pieces. He's never the dramatic center —
he's the energy and comic relief at the edge of bigger scenes.

**Signature voice.** Brash, slangy young flyboy: "Blast OFF! Totally wizard, man!",
over-eager salutes, fumbling between "Crest" and "Presav Tarasar, sir," and barely
restrained swoop-racing thirst. Pull cadence from RPlog:Seeking a Sponsor (33 ABY) — the
single best window into how he actually talks.

## Talking to them OOC

This is an **intrigue-and-ideas player**, not a brawler. Lorn Rhys / Darth Malus is one
character with two faces: the schemer who once *seized the Imperial throne by coup* and the
philosophical Sith he became after dying for it. Their logs are long, talky two-handers
built on manipulation, leverage, and worldview — espionage, politics, philosophy, and the
slow forging of alliances (Article: Lorn Rhys; Article: Darth Malus on the Force). Expect
someone who enjoys the *chess* of a scene more than the swordplay.

Safe conversation hooks:

- **The coup and the throne (the headline credit).** In 12 ABY Lorn cultivated a coup
  against Emperor Kendal Osbourne, kicked off the War of the Throne at the **Battle of
  Selene**, sank a Super Star Destroyer into the planet, and froze Osbourne in carbonite —
  then briefly crowned *himself* Emperor (Article: Lorn Rhys; RPlog:Battle of Selene (Part
  I), 12 ABY). It's the proudest, most-referenced beat in the whole history.
- **Death, rebirth, and 20 years in carbonite.** Betrayed by his own master Darth Pain, he
  "died," was reborn as Darth Malus, was overthrown in turn by Darth Malign, and spent two
  decades frozen until an unknown benefactor sprang him in 34 ABY (Article: Lorn Rhys). The
  "relic of the past, out of time" angle is the engine of the current arc.
- **The balance philosophy.** Malus rejects both Jedi and Sith orthodoxy — he wants the
  Force's "pendulum" kept to small swings, conflict that waxes and wanes but never goes to
  extremes; he even rewrites the Sith Code into a "balanced" version (Article: Darth Malus
  on the Force). Bring this up and you'll get paragraphs; it's their favorite sandbox.
- **The current 34 ABY play (freshest in mind):** quietly rebuilding influence after the
  thaw — partnering with Caspian doctor **Lynae Cassius**, sparring with the Imperium's
  Grand Inquisitor Axel Vichten (who's put a bounty on his head), and maneuvering around the
  Tarasar family and Johanna (RPlog:Perfectly Balanced, as All Things Should Be, 34 ABY;
  RPlog:Regroup and Reconvene, 34 ABY; RPlog:And That's No Small Thing, 34 ABY).

Heads-up — **Darth Malus is NOT Darth Malign.** Darth Malign is Tyler Damion's character,
a *different player*, and is actually the villain who overthrew Malus in 13 ABY (Article:
Lorn Rhys). Don't conflate the two.

Tone to match: cold, courteous, unhurried; a manipulator who flatters by taking you
seriously. "Far, far better men and far more powerful women than you have tried to kill me.
Yet here I am." (Article: Lorn Rhys).

## Character: Lorn Rhys — the would-be Emperor

**Capsule.** Human male, 2.0m, scarred and heavily cybernetic (left eye, left arm, both
legs; amber eyes "formerly green"). A career Imperial intelligence man — Stormtrooper →
Shadow Guard Commander → Director of the Bureau of Operations → Ubiqtorate → Moff — who
turned spymaster's tradecraft into a bid for the throne (Article: Lorn Rhys). Faction-wise
he reads Galactic Empire (8 logs) / Caspian Union (5) / Sith (5) / Corporate Sector
Authority (3) / Imperial Coup (2). Note the wiki treats **Lorn Rhys** as the public/human
identity and **Darth Malus** as the Sith persona of the *same* character; "Malus" even
redirects to the Lorn Rhys page (Article: Malus).

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **The intelligence career (5–8 ABY).** Stormtrooper indoctrination at the Selene Academy,
  pulled into the secretive Shadow Guard, rising to run Imperial Intelligence's apparatus;
  later Moff of Tatooine under Emperor Valak, where he earned a hard-liner's reputation
  subjugating the locals (Article: Lorn Rhys). Earliest log on the wiki is a 7 ABY cameo,
  Morganna running into him on OSG (RPlog:It's Lorn Rhys, 7 ABY).
- **The Vigilant and the "death" (8 ABY).** From Orbital Station Redemption he built the
  covert Vigilant Movement to topple Valak in Palpatine's name; the Empire crushed it and
  his fleeing shuttle "exploded" in hyperspace — no wreckage, endless rumors (Article: Lorn
  Rhys). In truth he drifted, was captured by pirates (losing an arm, leg, and eye), and was
  recovered and rebuilt in a Caspian facility, the Dark Side finally taking hold.
- **The coup and the throne (11–12 ABY).** Re-emerging from Nar Shaddaa with Sith knowledge
  from the construct of his first master Ahzad Domani, he rebuilt a power base and launched
  his coup against Emperor Osbourne — the War of the Throne, opening at the **Battle of
  Selene**, where his strike team infiltrated the Imperial Palace (RPlog:Battle of Selene
  (Part I) & (Part II), 12 ABY). He captured Osbourne, drove an SSD into Selene, froze
  Osbourne in carbonite, took Dreven, and crowned himself Emperor (Article: Lorn Rhys).
- **Betrayal, rebirth, second fall (12–13 ABY).** His master Darth Pain consumed him and
  seized the throne; using Ahzad's Cube he cheated death and rose as **Darth Malus**, killed
  Pain with Vassily Korolov and Mailyn Raines, and reigned — badly. In mid-13 ABY **Darth
  Malign** (a *different* character/player) surrounded Dreven, overwhelmed him, and froze
  *him* in carbonite, the same fate he'd dealt Osbourne (Article: Lorn Rhys).
- **The thaw (34 ABY / RL 2025).** Sprung from the Oubliette prison-station by an unknown
  benefactor after ~20 years frozen; a 300,000cr (later 1,000,000cr) bounty goes up; he
  keeps a low profile, rumored behind unrest on Chandrila and Mon Cal and the Tatooine
  famine/blackout (Article: Lorn Rhys). His public "Lorn Rhys" face surfaces at events —
  e.g. the Arkanis-sector charity ball (RPlog:Charity Ball, 34 ABY) — while the scheming
  happens quietly (RPlog:Beware Siths Bearing Gifts, 34 ABY; RPlog:Coffee Talk, 34 ABY;
  RPlog:A Chip and a Chair is All You Need, 34 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Lynae Cassius** — the through-line of the entire 34 ABY arc. A Caspian Union doctor
  who first treats the thawed-out Malus, then becomes his **partner** in destabilizing the

- **Axel Vichten** — the Imperium's Grand Inquisitor; Lynae's oldest friend and the man who
  put a bounty on Lorn. A three-way tension runs under the partnership (RPlog:The Chef's
  Special, 34 ABY; RPlog:Charity Ball, 34 ABY). [2 shared logs]
- **The Tarasar family** (Crestian, Atsvara, and the off-page Janus) — current friction;
  their loose handling of secrets is a 34 ABY plot driver (RPlog:The Doctor, the Sith, and
  the Presav, 34 ABY; RPlog:And That's No Small Thing, 34 ABY).
- **Coup-era cast** — Morrison Van Sen, Zrak'Hroun, Raika Navar, Imral, Gorath, plus
  off-page allies Vassily Korolov and Mailyn Raines, all from the War of the Throne
  (RPlog:Battle of Selene (Part I) & (Part II), 12 ABY; Article: Lorn Rhys). [2 shared logs
  each for Van Sen, Zrak'Hroun, Raika Navar, Imral]
- **Masters & nemeses** — Ahzad Domani (mentor-construct), **Darth Pain / Sinane Pantara**
  (treacherous master, killed), and **Darth Malign / Tyler Damion** (the rival who froze
  him — again, a *separate player*) (Article: Lorn Rhys).

**Playstyle / themes** (from 9 Lorn logs): intrigue, politics, philosophy, espionage,
coup, war. Reads as a **schemer who plays the long game** — power assembled from leverage
and information, not lightsaber flash.

**Signature voice.** Measured, courtly, faintly contemptuous; speaks in single-word
commands ("Come.") and unhurried sentences that turn questions into traps. Pull a
representative two-hander for cadence: RPlog:Coffee Talk (34 ABY) or RPlog:And That's No
Small Thing (34 ABY).

## Character: Darth Malus — the Sith persona

**Capsule.** Not a separate person — the *same* character's Sith identity, the form Lorn
took after cheating death with Ahzad's Cube (Article: Lorn Rhys). "More machine than man":
mechanical legs, a skeletal durasteel arm, a glowing red optical sensor, a lipless burned
face etched with henna-colored symbols in a dead language (Article: Lorn Rhys). All 8
"Malus"-tagged logs sit in 34 ABY (2025), faction Sith — this is the face he wears for the
covert work after the thaw. Again: **Malus ≠ Malign.**

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **The thaw and the first contact (early 34 ABY).** Freed from carbonite in a mass
  jailbreak on the Oubliette, then delivered to Doctor Lynae Cassius for repair — the
  meeting that starts everything (RPlog:Tonight there's gonna be a jailbreak, 34 ABY;
  RPlog:Freezer Burn, 34 ABY). Lynae soon walks him into Caspian politics, introducing him
  to the Presav of the Caspian Union (RPlog:The Doctor, the Sith, and the Presav, 34 ABY).
- **Networking the underworld and the powers (spring 34 ABY).** Quiet meetings to take the
  galaxy's temperature and recruit: Skawn Kairn on Nar Shaddaa's Crime Moon (RPlog:When the
  Moon Hits Your Eye, 34 ABY), a sit-down with Axel Vichten at the Captain's Table (RPlog:The
  Chef's Special, 34 ABY), and Malif Tal-Tahn of the Tahn Consortium (RPlog:M&M, 34 ABY).
- **The partnership (late spring 34 ABY).** Aboard his YT-1300 *Revenant*, parked in the
  wreckage of his old Orbital Station Redemption, Malus and Lynae "forge a partnership" —
  the philosophical core scene, where he lays out the balance doctrine and she signs on
  (RPlog:Perfectly Balanced, as All Things Should Be, 34 ABY).
- **Consolidation (summer 34 ABY).** A storm-bound meeting with Ssanyu on Athaniss
  (RPlog:Storm Front, 34 ABY), then back-to-back debriefs with Lynae about their separate
  meetings with Johanna and the Tarasar leak — the partnership tightening into a working
  conspiracy (RPlog:Regroup and Reconvene, 34 ABY; RPlog:And That's No Small Thing, 34 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters):
- **Lynae Cassius** — same as above; the relationship that defines the Malus era too. [4
  shared logs as Malus]
- **Skawn Kairn** — underworld contact / fellow conspirator on Nar Shaddaa (RPlog:Tonight
  there's gonna be a jailbreak, 34 ABY; RPlog:When the Moon Hits Your Eye, 34 ABY). [2
  shared logs]
- **Malif Tal-Tahn** — Tahn Consortium money/influence; courted in 34 ABY (RPlog:M&M, 34
  ABY; RPlog:A Chip and a Chair is All You Need, 34 ABY).
- **Mitra, Ssanyu Natrulon, Crestian Tarasar** — assorted 34 ABY contacts and frictions
  (RPlog:Beware Siths Bearing Gifts, 34 ABY; RPlog:Storm Front, 34 ABY; RPlog:The Doctor,
  the Sith, and the Presav, 34 ABY).

**Playstyle / themes** (from 8 Malus logs): intrigue, politics, Sith, diplomacy,
philosophy, alliance. Same player, same long game — just with the lightsaber and the
worldview foregrounded.

**Signature voice.** Sardonic, professorial, seductive about ideas: he prizes emotion as
proof of being alive ("It feels good, does it not? Emotions are what define us and makes us
feel alive.") and frames cruelty as honesty ("Things that are said and done in anger also
echo of truths.") (RPlog:And That's No Small Thing, 34 ABY). For the full doctrine, the
best single source is the article (Article: Darth Malus on the Force).

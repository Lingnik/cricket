## Talking to them OOC

This is a **modern-era, Caspian-Union ensemble player** — Nara only appears in the 2023+
run, woven through the Tarasar family's storyline on Caspar, Charros IV, and Dreven. The
fun of the character is the **double game**: in public she is a sweet, slightly ditzy
fashion-and-culture vlogger; OOC the player is clearly enjoying the spycraft underneath,
since her "airhead holojournalist" persona is an intelligence cover (RPlog:Secret Service
Supper, 32 ABY; RPlog:Dressing Room, 34 ABY). Expect a player who likes **social scenes,
comedy of manners, and dramatic irony** more than dice-and-blasters combat.

Safe conversation hooks:
- **The undercover angle.** Nara is a Caspian Intelligence Service operative running a
  socialite cover; she was promoted to Field Agent at an intel dinner aboard the Leviathan
  (RPlog:Secret Service Supper, 32 ABY), and her handler Veruca Violet later sends her to
  Chandrila to dig into New Republic unrest (RPlog:Dressing Room, 34 ABY). The player will
  appreciate that you know the public face is a put-on.
- **The Tarasar circle.** Her most frequent scene partners are Crestian Tarasar and his
  daughter Atsvara; she's effectively the family's tame press contact and a fixture at their
  events (RPlog:Art Collection, 32 ABY; RPlog:Birthday Baroness, 32 ABY). This is the
  through-line to most of her logs.
- **The Ghastly Gala set-piece.** Her funniest material — covering a grotesque-themed party
  on Dreven for her vlog while a blaze bug tries to mate with her camdroid (RPlog:Ghastly
  Gala, 33 ABY) and fending off Tray Garos's advances (RPlog:Ghastly Gala: Episode 2, 33
  ABY). Great low-stakes shared-memory hook.

Tone to match: light, chatty, self-deprecating, relentlessly polite — and let the player
be in on the joke that it's all surface.

## Character: Nara Rhen — the holojournalist who's listening

**Capsule.** Human, Mandalore-born, blonde / blue-eyed, 1.6 m, affiliated with the Caspian
Union (Article: Nara Rhen). Public role: independent holonet vlogger on fashion and culture.
Actual role: Caspian Intelligence Service field agent using the celebrity persona as cover.
The wiki article is a stub (infobox + categories: Agents, Caspian Union, Entertainers,
Humans, Mandalorians) with no prose bio, so her arc is reconstructed from logs (Article:
Nara Rhen; Article: Nara's Roleplay Logs).

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Arrival on the Caspian scene (32 ABY).** Surfaces in the Cloud City / Caspar social
  whirl — picked up as a new client by the Silverwood House of Fashion alongside Lady Athena
  (RPlog:Someone Needs New Duds, 32 ABY) — and quickly becomes a fixture at Caspian Union
  events: the Second Renaissance of Innovation launch (RPlog:Renaissance of Innovation, 32
  ABY) and the morning-after café crowd (RPlog:Morning Caf, 32 ABY).
- **Cover revealed: the spy under the socialite (32 ABY).** At an intelligence-service dinner
  aboard the Leviathan during the Charros IV wargames, Nara is shown to be a CIS operative —
  her socialite vlog is her cover — and is **promoted to Field Agent** with a credit advance,
  toasted alongside fellow Mandalorian agent Rawnie Qrowe (RPlog:Secret Service Supper, 32
  ABY).
- **Crestian's trusted press contact (32 ABY).** Becomes Crestian Tarasar's go-to cultural
  advisor and event journalist — consulting on which pieces of his art collection to loan to
  a Xi Xin gallery (RPlog:Art Collection, 32 ABY), covering the gallery opening
  (RPlog:Gallery Opening, 32 ABY) and Atsvara's elevation to Baroness (RPlog:Birthday
  Baroness, 32 ABY).
- **The Ghastly Gala (33 ABY).** Covers a grotesque-themed gala on Dreven for her vlog; gets
  comic-relief beats (a blaze bug courts her camera, V1C3 makes the arrest) and crosses paths
  with a disguised Darth Malign's entourage (RPlog:Ghastly Gala, 33 ABY), then politely
  deflects Tray Garos at the after-party (RPlog:Ghastly Gala: Episode 2, 33 ABY).
- **Active operative, current era (34 ABY).** Wraps a holonet interview, then drops the act
  with her handler Veruca Violet — both confirmed as intel operatives behind public covers —
  and is assigned to Chandrila to investigate New Republic unrest and possible outside
  agitators, with explicit worry about **Darth Malus's** return after an Imperium prison break
  (RPlog:Dressing Room, 34 ABY). Most recently working a starship-casino weapons convention,
  where she awkwardly interviews Lynae Cassius and Malus (RPlog:A Chip and a Chair is All You
  Need, 34 ABY).

**Playstyle / themes** (from 14 logs): diplomacy (5) and politics (5) lead, then social
gatherings/parties (7 combined), espionage/intrigue (4), art and culture (4), with one-off
romance, journalism, and satire beats. Reads as a **social-intrigue and comedy player** who
uses a fluffy public persona to sit in on serious rooms.

**Signature voice.** Bright, gushing, self-deprecating broadcaster patter — narrates
straight into her camdroid ("Hello again, everyone, and welcome to what may be the gala to
end all galas") and disarms marks by playing dim ("nobody who's viewed her blog ever
described her as smart") while quietly gathering everything (RPlog:Ghastly Gala, 33 ABY).
Pull that log for cadence; pull RPlog:Dressing Room (34 ABY) for the cover-off, all-business
operative underneath.

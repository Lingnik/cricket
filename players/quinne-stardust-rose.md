## Talking to them OOC

This is a **social-and-setting player**, not a combat or Force player. Quinne lives in the
**Bespin / Cloud City civilian sphere** — parties, hotels, festivals, charity galas, hostessing —
so the comfort zone is **slice-of-life and political-flavored socializing** (themes skew slice
of life, politics, diplomacy, social gatherings). Scenes are warm, prose-y, and people-focused;
expect glamour, banter, and the texture of running a city's nightlife rather than dueling.

Safe conversation hooks:
- **Her Cinderella arc.** Self-reared Zeltron club singer → picked up by Falleen impresario
  **Nasiri Gejalli** to sing and hostess aboard the pleasure cruiser *Nyriaan Spiral*
  (Article: Quinne Stardust Rose; RPlog:Nasiri Hires Quinne, 29 ABY) → ends up **acting then
  permanent Baroness-Administrator of Cloud City** (RPlog:Bespin Cultural Festival, 32 ABY).
- **The Caspian/Bespin social set.** Her recurring circle is the Tarasar-orbit crowd:
  **Crestian Tarasar**, **Lynae Cassius**, the Quarren doctor **Druze**, the Kubaz couturier
  **Zubindi Hakoon** — good shared-ground names (RPlog:Make Way, I am a Doctor, 32 ABY;
  RPlog:We Are All, All of Us, Dust, 32 ABY).
- **The big set-piece disasters.** She keeps the party running while things blow up around her —
  the Miners Dinner slaver attack (RPlog:Bespin Blows Up!, 29 ABY) and a conference suicide
  bombing (RPlog:We Are All, All of Us, Dust, 32 ABY). She's the unflappable hostess archetype.

Tone to match: bubbly, generous, charming with a quietly shrewd streak — the party-girl
facade drops just long enough to drive a hard bargain, then snaps back on
(RPlog:Nasiri Hires Quinne, 29 ABY).

## Character: Quinne Stardust Rose

**Capsule.** Zeltron woman from the swamplands of Zeltros — blue hair, pink skin, violet eyes,
1.7m (Article: Quinne Stardust Rose). Self-taught chanteuse and natural organizer who rose from
busking dive bars to running Cloud City. Reads Independent entertainer → Caspian Union /
Cloud City administration (factions skew Caspian Union across her logs). No Force, no military.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Wild-child to working singer (backstory).** Raised by no one in particular among a sprawl
  of Zeltron relatives; fell into show business at 14, improvised her way to a vocal career
  when the music froze at a rave, and toured the Outer Rim from 16 on. Funded the gaps with
  petty grifts — picking pockets and having her tech Veebo slice marks' accounts — until Veebo
  got pinched (Article: Quinne Stardust Rose).
- **The Falleen's offer (29 ABY).** Attending an auction aboard the *Nyriaan Spiral* on the
  arms of two boorish wealthy dates who brawl and embarrass her, she's rescued by the ship's
  owner, **Nasiri Gejalli**, who hires her to sing, hostess, and organize parties — quarters,
  board, and pointed warnings that his circles run to "hutts, scoundrels and even slavers"
  (Article: Quinne Stardust Rose; RPlog:Nasiri Hires Quinne, 29 ABY).
- **Cloud City catastrophe & elevation (29–30 ABY).** Becomes general manager of the Yarith
  Hotel; survives the 1st Annual Miners Dinner slaver attack (RPlog:Bespin Blows Up!, 29 ABY)
  and is installed as **acting Baroness-Administrator** of Cloud City in the aftermath, hosting
  the Caspar Caff scene by 30 ABY (RPlog:Kubaz, Trandoshan, and Wookiee, 30 ABY).
- **Baroness-Administrator in full (32 ABY).** Now the confirmed civic face of Cloud City:
  takes a nasty fall during festival prep (RPlog:Make Way, I am a Doctor, 32 ABY), gives an
  economics interview to InterGalactic News (RPlog:Muun Money Minute - Greater Javin, 32 ABY),
  survives a suicide bombing at the Greater Javin Medical Society conference
  (RPlog:We Are All, All of Us, Dust, 32 ABY), and personally **performs to open the First
  Annual Bespin Cultural Festival**, recounting on-camera her rise from club singer to
  administrator (RPlog:Bespin Cultural Festival, 32 ABY).
- **Still hosting (34 ABY).** Among the Cloud City set at Zubindi Hakoon's charity fashion show
  for the Arkanis Sector Children's Relief Fund (RPlog:Fashion Show, 34 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Nasiri Gejalli** (a.k.a. Nasiri Zithreej) — Falleen patron who discovered and hired her;
  the hinge of her whole arc (RPlog:Nasiri Hires Quinne, 29 ABY).
- **Crestian Tarasar** — her most frequent scene partner; Caspian figure who recounts how she
  became Baroness-Administrator. [6 shared logs]
- **Lynae Cassius** — recurring Cloud City social-set companion. [4 shared logs]
- **Blulurbgloblurb Druze** — Quarren doctor in her regular Bespin circle. [4 shared logs]
- **Drorq** / **Zaalbacca** — Caspian officers/officials around the Cloud City administration.
- **Zubindi Hakoon** — Kubaz couturier who designs for her festivals and hosts the fashion show.
- **Johanna Siri te Danaan** — Cloud City club/hotel proprietor; Quinne manages the Yarith for
  her at the Miners Dinner (RPlog:Bespin Blows Up!, 29 ABY). She also appears, mostly by
  reference, in Johanna's Malign-arc scene (RPlog:Trouble on Cloud City, Part One, 29 ABY).

**Playstyle / themes** (from 9 logs): slice of life and politics lead, then diplomacy,
intrigue, social gatherings, and the occasional terrorism/explosion set-piece she weathers as
host. Reads as a **civilian world-building player** who gives the Bespin/Caspian scene its
nightlife and ceremony rather than chasing plot conflict.

**Signature voice.** Effervescent, affectionate, "darlin'"-dropping party energy with sudden
flashes of a sharp negotiator underneath: *"Quinne gives Nasiri a semi-suspicious side-eye,
dropping a little bit of the party girl facade. 'You are serious, aren't you?'"*
(RPlog:Nasiri Hires Quinne, 29 ABY).

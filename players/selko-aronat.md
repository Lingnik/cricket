## Talking to them OOC

This is a **supporting-cast, slice-of-life player** — Selko is a shy, competent fixer who
shows up to other people's scenes and quietly makes herself useful, not a plot-driver. All
seven of her logs land in a single burst in 2023 around the **Caspian Union / Cloud City**
social and military circuit (Article: Selko Aronat; RPlog:Bespin Cultural Festival, 32 ABY).
Assume the player enjoys **low-key, character-texture RP**: receptions, conversations,
crowd scenes, and the occasional crisis where the engineer gets to improvise under pressure.

Safe conversation hooks:
- **The Caspian / Cloud City social scene.** Selko mingled at Quinne Stardust Rose's First
  Annual Bespin Cultural Festival and was offered a permanent job at the Yarith Bespin hotel
  (RPlog:Bespin Cultural Festival, 32 ABY). She's a recurring face at Tarasar/Caspian events.
- **Being the underestimated fixer.** She self-identifies as a self-taught mechanic and
  "fixer" — bespectacled, callused hands, never showing off her strong grip
  (RPlog:Atsvara's Hot Takes, 32 ABY). A reliable, gentle conversation lane.
- **Crisis improv.** Her standout moments are emergencies: stunning a suicide bomber and

  Prison Break, 32 ABY).

## Character: Selko Aronat — the itinerant technician

**Capsule.** Human, from **Byblos** (Article: Selko Aronat). An itinerant, self-taught
mechanic — steel-rimmed round plasspecs, pocket-laden tan jacket, hydrospanner-callused
hands, an unshowy strong grip (RPlog:Atsvara's Hot Takes, 32 ABY). Unaffiliated but threaded
into the **Caspian Union** orbit, by far her most common faction tag (5 of 7 logs). The wiki
bio is a `{{char-stub}}` — there's no deep canonical backstory to lean on, so keep it light.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; all within RL 2023 / 32 ABY — each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Corellia, in the thick of it (early 2023).** At Coronet City Spaceport, explosions draw
  CorSec away and 13 armed prisoners break loose; Prime Minister Dash Blazer deputizes the
  bystanders and Selko fights alongside Crestian Tarasar, Imperator Tyler Damion, Moff Liza
  Molokai, and the Quarren mechanic Druze (RPlog:Corellia Prison Break, 32 ABY).
- **The Yarith bombing (early 2023).** At the Greater Javin Medical Society conference on
  Cloud City a bomber seals the lobby and reveals a bomb-coat; Selko stuns him, then blasts a
  hole in the transparisteel so the device can be ejected into open air (RPlog:We Are All,
  All of Us, Dust, 32 ABY). Her single most heroic beat.
- **The Cloud City social swing (early 2023).** She's a fixture of the Bespin party season —
  offered a permanent hotel job by Baroness-Administrator Quinne Stardust Rose at the Cultural
  Festival (RPlog:Bespin Cultural Festival, 32 ABY), and shopping for "durable but fashionable"
  workwear from Kubaz designer Zubindi Hakoon (RPlog:Someone Needs New Duds, 32 ABY).
- **The Caspian wargames (mid 2023).** Spectator at the Charros IV wargame exercise
  (RPlog:Simulated Evacuation, 32 ABY), then mingling awkwardly at the victory reception
  aboard the CMS Renaissance of Innovation, where she meets pilot-commander Elana Tracer and
  the Xi Charrian ambassador ak (RPlog:Atsvara's Hot Takes, 32 ABY).
- **Charros IV gallery (mid 2023).** At the Xi Xin art-gallery opening she's literally still
  repairing the malfunctioning lighting while the guests mingle — peak Selko
  (RPlog:Gallery Opening, 32 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):

**Playstyle / themes** (from 7 logs): politics and diplomacy (2 each), space combat and
rescue (2 each), social gatherings — festivals, galleries, receptions. Reads as a
**supporting slice-of-life player** who enjoys being the quietly competent civilian in
faction-flavored crowd scenes, occasionally stepping up when violence erupts.

**Signature voice.** Soft, shy, observational, a little self-deprecating. Representative:
*"I fix stuff, yeah"* and *"sometimes it's an advantage to be underestimated. Sometimes the
other girl doesn't put forth her best effort against you"* (RPlog:Atsvara's Hot Takes, 32 ABY).
She tends toward sober empathy too: *"Real explosions mean real deaths"* (same log).

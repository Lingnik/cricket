## Talking to them OOC

This is a **villain-player who plays for chaos and dark comedy** — Skawn is a small but
loud footprint in the archive, and every scene he's in leans into gleeful menace. Whoever
runs him clearly enjoys a **flamboyant, irreverent bad guy**: the slaver who riffs about
red plastic cups and Wookiee waiters while casually proposing they kidnap a reporter
(RPlog:Blooming Chaos, 32 ABY). Don't expect earnest continuity-drama from this corner;
expect banter, mayhem, and "agent of chaos" energy.

He's also a **connective antagonist** — his scenes hook into bigger plots run by other,
more central players, so he's a good bridge topic.

Safe conversation hooks:
- **The Bespin raid.** Skawn's breakout moment is mutilating victims during the Cloud City
  refinery disaster at Johanna te Danaan's Miners Dinner (RPlog:Bespin Blows Up!, 29 ABY) —
  the crime that made him galactically wanted (Article: Skawn Kairn). Crestian Tarasar still
  calls it "a black mark on our efforts" for the Caspian Union (RPlog:Art of Darkness, 34 ABY).
- **The current Malus plot (freshest, RL 2025).** Skawn and his crew sprang the frozen
  ex-Emperor **Darth Malus (Lorn Rhys)** from the Oubliette carbonite prison (RPlog:Tonight
  there's gonna be a jailbreak, 34 ABY), then teamed with him on Nar Shaddaa to keep the
  Imperium off balance (RPlog:When the Moon Hits Your Eye, 34 ABY). This is his live storyline.
- **Working for Mitra.** Skawn answers to the Sith **Mitra**, who values him as a tool but
  not a thinker — "Skawn serves a purpose... but I have need of a being who can think further
  than his next indulgence" (RPlog:Beware Siths Bearing Gifts, 34 ABY). A fun, slightly
  self-deprecating angle if the player likes the "lovable disaster" read of the character.

Tone to match: gallows humor, fast patter, zero respect for authority or polite society.

## Character: Skawn Kairn — captain of the *Smooth Talker*

**Capsule.** Twi'lek male from Ryloth (b. 5 BBY), red skin / yellow eyes, two metres of
menace. A former slave turned **slaver, pirate captain, and mercenary leader** who terrorises
the galaxy aboard his Carrack-class light cruiser the *Smooth Talker* with "a crew of
twisted sociopaths" (Article: Skawn Kairn). Independent by affiliation; in his recent arc he
rents his chaos out to a Sith faction.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **The Bespin attack (29 ABY).** Skawn surfaces during the catastrophe at Cloud City — a
  refinery explosion and Boba Fett shooting up the ballroom at Johanna te Danaan's Miners
  Dinner — with Skawn "mutilating a victim elsewhere" amid the carnage (RPlog:Bespin Blows
  Up!, 29 ABY). He follows it up by attacking the Krittish school-trip freighter *Blechley
  Brook* in 30 ABY (Article: Skawn Kairn).
- **Hunted out of the Wheel (ongoing backstory).** Based out of The Wheel until repeated
  clashes with Caspian Intelligence Service agents forced him to relocate (Article: Skawn
  Kairn); Crestian Tarasar recounts how the Union eventually squeezed him out of the region
  (RPlog:Art of Darkness, 34 ABY).
- **The chaos-pitch (32 ABY).** The masked agent **Murrain** (Malif Tal-Tahn) tracks him to
  a kitschy Wookiee-themed restaurant on The Wheel and pitches an alliance; Skawn deflects
  with banter, takes a drop-link datachit, and gets warned the Imperator's agents are hunting
  him (RPlog:Blooming Chaos, 32 ABY).
- **The Oubliette jailbreak (34 ABY / RL 2025).** Working for the Sith **Mitra**, Skawn and
  his *Smooth Talker* crew storm the Oubliette carbonite prison and thaw out the long-frozen
  **Darth Malus** (RPlog:Tonight there's gonna be a jailbreak, 34 ABY).
- **The Malus alliance (34 ABY).** He and Malus regroup at the Crime Moon on Nar Shaddaa to
  plot keeping the Imperium destabilised — a Chandrila provocation, exposing Axel Vichten's
  piracy to split the CSA from the Imperium (RPlog:When the Moon Hits Your Eye, 34 ABY).
  Malus reads him plainly: "I am sure he will do whatever it is that suits him."

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Darth Malus (Lorn Rhys)** — the prisoner he freed and current partner-in-crime; the
  spine of his 2025 arc. [2 shared logs — his most-frequent recent scene partner]
- **Mitra** — the Sith he serves; treats him as a useful but limited instrument.
- **Malif Tal-Tahn ("Murrain")** — masked agent of chaos who courted him as an ally.
- **Johanna Siri te Danaan** — Grand Moff whose Bespin gala he helped wreck (adversarial).
- **Crestian Tarasar** — Caspian official who's been chasing him for years (nemesis).
- *Note:* Darth **Malus** (Lorn Rhys) is Skawn's ally and is **NOT** Darth **Malign**
  (Tyler Damion). Murrain references being "familiar with Darth Malign and his methods"
  separately (RPlog:Blooming Chaos, 32 ABY).

**Playstyle / themes** (from his logs): politics (2), slavery (2), then terrorism, criminal
underworld, dark humor, prison break, space combat. Reads as a **comic-menace antagonist
player** — set-piece villainy delivered with a wink, mostly in service of other players'
bigger Sith/Imperium plots.

**Signature voice.** Manic, irreverent, fond of absurd tangents mid-threat — "Two of your
finest red plastic cups," or musing about kidnapping a reporter to go eat bantha-worshippers
(RPlog:Blooming Chaos, 32 ABY). Lives entirely in the moment by design: "When you are a
slave you have to live in that moment... Now I control that moment" (same log).

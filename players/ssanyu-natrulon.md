spine like the Jedi mains, but she's a textured **intrigue piece** woven through the
modern Caspar storyline. Whoever runs her leans into **Falleen manipulation, spycraft,
and "living in the past" loyalty** rather than set-piece combat. Conversations land best
on espionage, court politics, and the long shadow of the old New Sith Order.

## Character: Ssanyu Natrulon — Falleen spymaster

**Capsule.** Falleen human-analog (green skin, amethyst eyes, obsidian hair), b. into a
Noble House on Falleen, raised to view other races as experiments to be manipulated; fell
into the Underworld and the spy trade, then into the New Sith Order as Vizier of the Sith
Intelligence Network (Article: Ssanyu Natrulon). Faction reads pure Sith/NSO, brushing the
Galactic Imperium and the Caspian Union in the modern era.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **NSO/SIN heyday (17 ABY / RL 2008).** Active in the New Sith Order's intelligence arm
  under Axel Vichten and Darth Malign; appears in fleet and disciplinary scenes around
  Ord Vaxal and the Hssiss (RPlog:Skirmish at Kashyyyk, 17 ABY; RPlog:Punishment, 17 ABY).
  Per her bio she rises toward Grand Inquisitor and is granted Marchioness of Ord Vaxal
  (Article: Ssanyu Natrulon).
- **Disappearance.** With Malign gone and the NSO's top ranks collapsing, she withdraws
  "into the shadows" to wait for the right moment to re-emerge (Article: Ssanyu Natrulon).
- **Re-emergence on Caspar (32–33 ABY / RL 2023–24).** Resurfaces among NSO veterans on
  Caspar — Crestian Tarasar's Sith gathering, the Imperium council reception, Charros IV
  wargames, and Zubindi Hakoon's macabre Ghastly Gala (RPlog:Sith Convention, 32 ABY;
  RPlog:Everything With Your Testicles Alright?, 32 ABY; RPlog:Simulated Evacuation,
  32 ABY; RPlog:Ghastly Gala, 33 ABY; RPlog:Ghastly Gala: Episode 2, 33 ABY).
- **The prophecy and the storm (33–34 ABY / RL 2024–25).** A beggar crone on Dreven
  recognizes her — "green one" — and rants about a returning "Dark One," spooking her
  (referenced in RPlog:Buskers, 33 ABY); she then meets Auntie Maya, a woman from her
  visions (RPlog:Blurred Visions, 33 ABY), and is reunited with Malus amid the storms of
  Athaniss (RPlog:Storm Front, 34 ABY).
- **Double agent (34 ABY / RL 2025).** Off-screen, Axel Vichten reveals she's getting
  close to Malus and reporting back to him — loyal "for some reason or other," still
  living in the NSO past, her intel reliable only once you "translate the truth from how
  she spins it" (RPlog:For the Good of Caspar, 34 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Axel Vichten** — old NSO superior and current handler; she reports to him while she
  shadows Malus. [most-cited co-character; RPlog:Sith Convention, 32 ABY; RPlog:For the
  Good of Caspar, 34 ABY]
- **Malus / Lorn Rhys** — the returned Sith she's drawn to and tasked to watch; her two
  freshest scenes are solo two-handers with him. [RPlog:Blurred Visions, 33 ABY;
  RPlog:Storm Front, 34 ABY]
- **Crestian Tarasar** — host of the Caspar Sith social circle she moves in.
  [RPlog:Sith Convention, 32 ABY]
- **Zubindi Hakoon** — Caspian intelligence figure; uploads the Gala/Buskers scenes she
  features in. [RPlog:Ghastly Gala, 33 ABY]
- **Salin Fawzy, Lynae Cassius, Nara Rhen, Atsvara Tarasar** — recurring members of the
  same NSO-veteran-on-Caspar ensemble.
- **Darth Malign (Tyler Damion)** — the Dark Lord whose fall ended her first run; the
  NSO golden age she still pines for (Article: Ssanyu Natrulon).

**Playstyle / themes** (from ~10 logs): intrigue dominates, then politics, social
gatherings/parties, and Sith ritual; very little personal combat. Reads as a
**spy-and-court player** who supplies texture and manipulation to other people's plots
rather than driving battles.

**Signature voice.** Outwardly cold and unreadable, true to the Falleen stereotype she
plays straight; pain reads as laughter, real moods hidden behind deliberately false ones
and even faked pigment shifts (Article: Ssanyu Natrulon). For cadence in the modern era,
pull RPlog:Blurred Visions (33 ABY) or RPlog:Storm Front (34 ABY).

## Character: Tiaryn Elatar — Captain of the *Bright Star*

**Capsule.** Human Free Trader, purple-haired, captain of the freighter *Bright Star*
(Article: Tiaryn Elatar; RPlog:Shindig on Corellia, 32 ABY). Independent rather than
enlisted — she's the **contract pilot/gunner** who gets the New Republic / Templar crew to
the target and out again. Faction-wise she reads independent, brushing against the Caspian
Union, the Hutt Cartel, and pirates through the jobs she takes.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Templar allies vs. pirates (early 32 ABY).** Tiaryn and her crew fight alongside the
  Templars in a ship engagement; she pilots while Samyok mans the cannon, and they cut
  through TIE "Uglies" in tandem. The brass come away "mighty impressed" with her crew
  (RPlog:Templars: Allies versus Pirates - Part 1 & Part 2, 32 ABY).
- **Spycraft — the Nar Shaddaa run (April 32 ABY).** The *Bright Star* carries Siarra's team
  into Nar Shaddaa for an anti-slavery fact-finding op; Siarra is emphatic the crew "will NOT
  do anything to bring danger to Tiaryn or her crew or this ship" (RPlog:Spycraft - Part 1,
  32 ABY). Intel-gathering and a covert viewing follow (RPlog:Spycraft - The Viewing;
  RPlog:Spycraft - The Planning, 32 ABY).
- **The Great Escape (April 32 ABY).** The combined *Templar* + *Bright Star* crews and a
  marine team hit the slaver site and rescue most of the prisoners. Tiaryn fights on the
  ground, takes out guards, and volunteers to ride the risky first turbolift down — Kelnas:
  "that was good shooting lady" (RPlog:The Great Escape, 32 ABY).
- **Shindig on Corellia (32 ABY).** Off the clock, she slips into Johanna te Danaan's charity
  gala, content to stand by the wall with a drink — "At least I'll know someone here"
  (RPlog:Shindig on Corellia, 32 ABY).

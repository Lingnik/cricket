## Talking to them OOC

This is a **veteran, lore-heavy, plot-running player** — a graduate student of history living in
Tokyo who has been on SW1 since 2000 and openly enjoys being the antagonist: "I love being the bad
guy. IRL I am **NOT** a bad guy" (Article: Tyler Damion; User:TylerDamion). They've worn most of the
big hats over the years — New Republic Faction Head (as Peshk Vry'lya), Imperial Faction Head, then
Imperium Faction Head — and a year as the Luke Skywalker feature (User:TylerDamion). Assume they
care about **continuity, faction-scale plot, and earned villainy**, and that they actively solicit
TPs: "I am open to various TPs and like to hear plot ideas" (Article: Tyler Damion).

Their comfort zone is **politics, intrigue, and Sith metaphysics at fleet scale** — coups, fleet
campaigns, interrogations, and the slow corruption of apprentices, more than one-off duels (themes
across his logs: politics 34, intrigue 19, space combat 16, Sith 14, the Force 12).

Safe conversation hooks:
- **The Imperium.** His current project: the New Order he forged via coup of the failing Galactic
  Empire — decentralized constitutional monarchy, abolition of slavery, xenofriendly (a deliberate
  contrast to old Imperial doctrine) (Article: Tyler Damion). Live in the 2020+ run, capped by the
  **Imperator's Coronation** (RPlog:The Imperator's Coronation, 29 ABY) and the **War Council**
  (RPlog:War Council, 29 ABY).
- **The half-sibling rivalry with Johanna.** Malign is **Johanna Siri te Danaan's Sith half-brother**
  — "facing off with his half-sister Johanna in a series of battles across neutral space" (Article:
  Tyler Damion). Johanna's player is active today, so this is a reliable shared-history thread; the
  2020 Bespin/Cloud City arc puts them face to face again (RPlog:Trouble on Cloud City, Part Two --
  Malign's Court, 29 ABY; RPlog:Johanna Has a Ball on Bespin, 29 ABY).
- **Apprentices and the cost of mentorship.** A recurring theme: he raises and discards apprentices
  — Danik Kreldin (later executed as a traitor), Antoine Dareus (drifted away over a woman and a
  child), now Kipran Jawatha (Article: Tyler Damion; RPlog:An Apprentice Rises, 16 ABY).
- **The Peshk connection.** Tyler began life as an on-again/off-again alt of **Peshk Vry'lya**, the
  Bothan who became New Republic Chief of State, before becoming the player's main (User:TylerDamion;
  Article: Tyler Damion). Bring up Peshk and you're talking to the same person.

Tone to match: a roughneck Corellian ace turned coldly patient Dark Lord — seductive and didactic
in the Sith register: "where the Jedi question, the Sith, my friend, answer" (Article: Tyler Damion).

## Character: Tyler Damion — "Darth Malign," Imperator Damion I

**Capsule.** Corellian human (b. 30 BBY), black hair / hazel eyes, callsign **Fossil**. CorSec
inspector's son → CorSec pilot → Imperial TIE officer → Rebel/New Republic ace → mercenary →
Sith apprentice → **Dark Lord of the Sith** and **Imperator of the Imperium** (Article: Tyler
Damion). Faction read: Sith (62) / Galactic Empire (57), constantly opposed by the New Republic
(31) and Jedi (24). **One character, two names** — "Tyler Damion, also known as Darth Malign and
Imperator Damion I" (Article: Tyler Damion). **Not** Darth Malus / Lorn Rhys, a separate person he
once deposed (see Sensitivity).

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Corellian ace, ruined family (30 BBY–4 ABY).** CorSec pilot, then Imperial Starfighter Corps;
  defects to the Rebellion rather than fire on civilians, and the Empire liquidates his wife, mother,
  and son in reprisal — the wound that drives everything after (Article: Tyler Damion).
- **The drunk mercenary years (5–11 ABY).** A grief-soaked, alcohol-and-drugs freelancer and ace:
  CorSec chief inspector again, then mercenary partnered with Octavian Dibar — including running an
  Etti IV blockade (RPlog:Dibar & Tyler over Etti IV, 11 ABY) — and joining Aurejin's hunt to free
  Jessalyn and Johanna from a Hutt prison (Article: Tyler Damion).
- **Force potential revealed → birth of Malign (12–14 ABY).** Captured on patrol by the Sith
  **Aleister Vadim**, revealed Force-sensitive, trained in the dark side, and renamed **Darth
  Malign**. He hollows out the Galactic Empire from within: blockades Dreven, builds a Moff's
  Council, deposes Emperor **Malus** (carbonite) to clear the throne for Vadim, and takes **Danik
  Kreldin** as his Sith apprentice (Article: Tyler Damion). The brutal era — a nonhuman-targeting
  plague, the Selene slave project, and the half-sibling duels with Johanna.
- **The New Sith Order (15–16 ABY).** After self-imposed exile, he returns as an Inquisitor, leads
  Danik to execution for treason, then founds the **New Sith Order** — recruiting smuggler-militant
  **Axel Vichten** and second apprentice **Antoine Dareus** (RPlog:Enter Darth Malign, 15 ABY;
  RPlog:An Apprentice Rises, 16 ABY). The NSO spurs the Second Battle of Nar Shaddaa, is run to
  ground at Cochran, and goes to hiding (RPlog:Showdown at Cochran, 16 ABY).
- **Empire-era intrigue (15 ABY).** As Imperial power player: interrogations and recruitment —
  abducting and turning the Jedi Korynn Fleming (RPlog:Fleming Joins the Sith, 15 ABY), prisoner
  work (RPlog:A Sith Interrogation, 11 ABY), and the rescue/recovery of **Lynae Cassius**, a
  frequent scene partner (RPlog:The Rescue of Lynae, 15 ABY).
- **Korriban trap, disappearance (17–28 ABY).** Trapped in an ancient Sith library simulation on a
  Korriban moon; his fleet self-destructs in madness; Johanna, guided by his ancestor-spirit
  Palladus, rescues him. He reconnects with the Sith noble **Crestian Tarasar** and rebuilds
  (Article: Tyler Damion; RPlog:Malign and the Holocron).
- **The return & the Imperium (28–33 ABY / RL 2020–2024).** Recovered, he coups the failing Galactic
  Empire and declares the **Imperium**, crowning himself Imperator (RPlog:The Imperator's
  Coronation, 29 ABY; RPlog:War Council, 29 ABY). Tangles again with Johanna and Jessalyn over a
  Bespin/Cloud City fleet campaign (RPlog:Johanna Has a Ball on Bespin, 29 ABY; RPlog:Trouble on
  Cloud City, Part Two -- Malign's Court, 29 ABY), courts the Hapan Queen Mother to seat his daughter
  **Sonja** as dual heir (Article: Tyler Damion; RPlog:Motherhood, 29 ABY), and is still active into
  the 30s ABY (RPlog:Ghastly Gala, 33 ABY).

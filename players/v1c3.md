## Talking to them OOC

V1C3 is **not a player** — he's a recurring NPC droid run by the player behind Crestian
Tarasar (wiki account `Tarasar`). So if someone brings up "Vee-Won," you're really talking
to a **Crestian/Tarasar-family player**, and the droid is one of their props. Every V1C3 log
was created on the wiki by `Tarasar` and uploaded by either Crestian or his close friend
Zubindi Hakoon (RPlog:Spiran Rescue, 29 ABY; RPlog:Ghastly Gala, 33 ABY). Treat him the way
you'd treat any beloved family sidekick: a continuity flourish the player clearly enjoys.

What this player likes, judging by Vee-Won's outings:
- **Caspian Union family-and-faction drama with a comic streak.** Vee-Won is "a combat
  capable protocol droid" deliberately modeled on HK-47, given "conversational insolence" and
  the vices of "gambling and vanity" — comic relief with teeth (Article: V1C3). The player
  leans into banter and slice-of-life as much as the set-pieces (RPlog:Morning Caf, 32 ABY).
- **The Tarasar dynasty.** Vee-Won's whole reason to exist is the Tarasar family — Crestian
  built him around the time daughter **Atsvara** was born, and Atsvara has come to think of
  him as her **brother** (Article: V1C3). His most frequent scene partner is Atsvara, then
  Crestian himself.
- **Caspian/Imperium crossover plots.** Joint ops with the Tracer family and the Galactic
  Imperium are a through-line — the Spira rescue and the Selene coronation both pair Caspians
  with Tyler Damion's Imperium (RPlog:Spiran Rescue, 29 ABY; RPlog:Coronation Pre-Game, 30 ABY).

Recent-storyline hooks (freshest first): the **Biscuit Baron buyout** era — Atsvara's Golden
Bantha Group acquiring the fast-food chain, with Vee-Won as VP of Security commanding the
Baron Droids (Article: V1C3); and the macabre **Ghastly Gala** on Dreven "celebrating" the
late bioterrorist Basca Starker, where Vee-Won pulls security and arrests a drunken blaze bug
(RPlog:Ghastly Gala, 33 ABY; RPlog:Ghastly Gala: Episode 2, 33 ABY).

Tone to match: dry, fond, a little tongue-in-cheek — this is a player who named a droid as a
pun on "vice" to pair with "sin" (Article: V1C3) and clearly delights in the bit.

## Character: V1C3 — "Vee-Won"

**Capsule.** A kitbashed Combat/Protocol droid (active 22 ABY–), 1.80 m, gold eyes, copper
plating, built by Caspian Presav **Crestian Tarasar** during his low-profile decade in the
Dragonflower Nebula as a personal aide/bodyguard. His main chassis is salvaged HK-77 (sourced
on Mustafar via Zubindi Hakoon) over assorted Clone Wars-era parts; named as a play on
"vice." Loyal to the Tarasar family and the Caspian Union (Article: V1C3). {{NPC}}.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Built as Crestian's HK-47 (c. 22 ABY).** Crestian, inspired by Revan, wanted "his own
  version of HK-47 — less an assassin than a droid sidekick with a questionable personality";
  Vee-Won's modules run starship ops, bureaucracy, combat, and "conversational insolence"
  (Article: V1C3).
- **Leviathan officer (mid-20s ABY).** Commissioned an Ensign aboard the interdictor
  *Leviathan*, rises to Commander; takes after dandy Crestian, buying pinstripe suits from the
  Silverwood House of Fashion (Article: V1C3).
- **Casulmis & the Mergansar Incident (28 ABY).** Handles logistics behind Crestian's Mergani
  refugee paramilitary; aboard *Leviathan* during the Caspian Navy attack (Article: V1C3).
- **Into the Caspian Navy (29 ABY).** When Crestian is elected Presav, Vee-Won accepts a
  commission and becomes the most visible member of the Presav's personal staff. Closes out
  the Basca Starker affair at Crestian's side as his "Droid adjutant Commander V1C3"
  (RPlog:Basca Starker's Fate, 29 ABY).
- **The Spira rescue (29 ABY).** Part of a five-member Caspian-Imperium strike team — with
  Crestian, Moff Elana Tracer, Kipran Jawatha, and Sha Dark — that, under cover of a family
  vacation, frees two Force-sensitive younglings from Trandoshan raiders; afterward he's
  transferred to the Union Marines and promoted to Colonel (RPlog:Spiran Rescue, 29 ABY;
  Article: V1C3).
- **Coronation duty (30 ABY).** At Sonja Damion's coronation on Selene, Crestian sends
  "Colonel Vee-Won" to chaperone Atsvara and her friends — droid-as-babysitter comic beat
  (RPlog:Coronation Pre-Game, 30 ABY).
- **Chevalier XO & Corellia (32 ABY).** Appointed executive officer of the Tarasar protection
  unit The Chevalier under Janus Tarasar; helps Imperator Damion and Crestian suppress a
  prison break at Coronet City Spaceport (Article: V1C3; RPlog:Corellia Prison Break, 32 ABY).
  Also turns up for the Renaissance of Innovation, in marine uniform "for once" (RPlog:
  Renaissance of Innovation, 30/32 ABY), and the everyday Caspar Caff banter with Atsvara and
  Dr. Druze (RPlog:Morning Caf, 32 ABY).
- **Biscuit Baron / Brigadier (33 ABY).** Invited as an investor in Atsvara's Golden Bantha
  Group precisely because, as a droid, he'd be "underappreciated"; named VP of Security, he
  leads Baron Droids in an anti-trafficking op on Ord Cantrell against the Nova Syndicate and
  is promoted to **Brigadier** (Article: V1C3). In this era he's a fixture of high-society
  scenes — designing Cricket's custom taser at *Leviathan* droid control (RPlog:Droid Control
  - The Ladies Prefer Electrum, 33 ABY) and running security at Zubindi's Ghastly Gala on
  Dreven (RPlog:Ghastly Gala, 33 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Crestian Tarasar** — creator, boss, and the player's actual PC; Vee-Won is his adjutant
  and bodyguard throughout (Article: V1C3). [co-billed across the core logs]
- **Atsvara Tarasar** — Crestian's daughter; built around her birth, she regards him as a
  **brother** and recruits him into Golden Bantha Group (Article: V1C3). [his single most
  frequent scene partner — 5 shared logs]
- **Zubindi Hakoon** — Crestian's friend who sourced the HK-77 chassis on Mustafar; hosts the
  Ghastly Gala scenes Vee-Won secures (Article: V1C3; RPlog:Ghastly Gala, 33 ABY).
- **Elana Tracer / Kipran Jawatha** — the Tracer family, Caspian-Imperium op partners
  (RPlog:Spiran Rescue, 29 ABY).
- **Tyler Damion (Imperator; = Darth Malign)** — Imperium ally in crossover plots (RPlog:
  Coronation Pre-Game, 30 ABY; RPlog:Corellia Prison Break, 32 ABY). NB: this is **Darth
  Malign / Tyler Damion**, *not* Darth Malus (Lorn Rhys) — different character entirely.
- **Cricket** — the astromech he helps re-arm at droid control (RPlog:Droid Control - The
  Ladies Prefer Electrum, 33 ABY); a fun droid-to-droid hook if Cricket's player chats.

**Playstyle / themes** (from 10 logs): a supporting NPC used for military operations and
rescue (Spira, Corellia, Ord Cantrell), Caspian/Imperium crossover politics, and especially
**comic slice-of-life and satire** — galas, caff-shop banter, droid-shop comedy. The player
runs him as loyal muscle with a vain, insolent streak rather than as a dramatic lead.

**Signature voice.** Dry, vain, talks back to his boss — "a tendency to talk back" that
Crestian enjoys, plus the vices of gambling and vanity (Article: V1C3). For cadence, pull
the chaperone gag in RPlog:Coronation Pre-Game (30 ABY) or the security-detail comedy of
RPlog:Ghastly Gala (33 ABY).

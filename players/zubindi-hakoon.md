## Talking to them OOC

This is a **social-and-intrigue player who builds the room, not the fight** — Zubindi is a
fashion-house mogul turned spy who shows up to galas, coronations, and supper parties, and
the logs lean hard into politics, diplomacy, social gatherings, romance, and fashion. Assume
they enjoy event-driven scenes with a deep cast, a sense of humor, and a glossy aesthetic.
The Sorin alt is the flip side of the same instinct: con jobs, sabacc tables, and ship
heists played for comedy.

Safe conversation hooks:
- **The Silverwood House of Fashion.** Zubindi founded the "galactically renowned" Silverwood
  House of Fashion on Kubindi in 4 ABY and grew it across Mustafar, Chandrila, Silver
  Station, and Caspar (Article: Zubindi Hakoon). The fashion is a reliable, low-stakes hook —
  his collections ("Galaxy at War," "Exile," "Shadow") each had a story.
- **Crestian Tarasar.** This is the spine of Zubindi's whole arc — best friend and "most high
  profile customer," and the reason Zubindi follows him to Caspar and into intelligence work
  (Article: Zubindi Hakoon). Crestian is Zubindi's most-frequent scene partner; bring up
  Crestian and you've found Zubindi's center of gravity.
- **The Caspian Intelligence Service.** Crestian, once elected Presav, makes Zubindi head of
  the **Wardrobe and Props Section** — a couturier-turned-spymaster gag the player clearly
  loves (Article: Zubindi Hakoon; RPlog:Secret Service Supper, 32 ABY).
- **Recent storylines** (freshest in their mind): the Tahn Consortium party era —
  RPlog:Party Triggered (33 ABY) and the satirical RPlog:Ghastly Gala two-parter (33 ABY,
  where Cricket is in the cast!) — and the 34 ABY Corellian charity circuit (RPlog:Fashion
  Show, RPlog:Charity Ball, 34 ABY).
- **For the Sorin alt:** Nar Shaddaa cons and the crew of the *Fortune Rising* — winning (or
  stealing) a freighter off Baron Songir at the sabacc table (RPlog:The Hoodwinked Baron;
  RPlog:All the Baron's Men, both 32 ABY).

Tone to match: witty, glossy, fond of an ensemble and a bit of comedy. Zubindi is publicly
**apolitical** even while quietly boosting Crestian's faction — "remained apolitical publicly,
his efforts and presence would be a boost to Crestian" (Article: Zubindi Hakoon). Sorin is
pure rogue-with-a-grin patter.

## Character: Zubindi Hakoon — the Kubaz couturier

**Capsule.** Kubaz male (b. 19 BBY on Kubindi), 1.8 m, black hair, covered eyes; one of the
galaxy's premiere fashion designers and founder of the Silverwood House of Fashion
(Article: Zubindi Hakoon). Faction-wise he reads Caspian Union and Silverwood House of
Fashion, brushing constantly against the Galactic Imperium, the Sith, the Corellian
Confederation, and the Jedi at the parties where galactic power gathers.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Kubindi childhood → fashion (19 BBY–4 ABY).** Born at the start of the Imperial era,
  named for the legendary Kubazian chef Zubindi Ebsuk; raised in an arts-rich Kubaz society
  and drawn to fashion in his teens (Article: Zubindi Hakoon).
- **Founding Silverwood (4–8 ABY).** A 30,000-credit Bank of the Core loan launches the
  Silverwood House of Fashion on Kubindi; he turns the functional Kubazian/Mustafarian skirt
  into high fashion, expands to Mustafar (adding jewellery), then to Chandrila — where he
  meets a young Caspian expatriate, **Crestian Tarasar**, his future best friend and patron
  (Article: Zubindi Hakoon).
- **Provocative collections (14–24 ABY).** "Galaxy at War" after the Imperial Blitzkrieg,
  "Exile" launched alongside the new Silver Station shop run by Crestian, and the guerilla
  pop-up "Shadow" campaign — each a statement carefully skirting open politics (Article:
  Zubindi Hakoon).
- **Caspar, a ward, and a charity (29 ABY).** Following Crestian home to Trinumvira on
  Caspar after the Sariphage pandemic and Mergani pogroms, Zubindi buys a whole city block,
  takes the orphaned heiress **Veruca Violet** Eleria as his "ward" and apprentice, and
  becomes a founding board member of Crestian's charity **The Mission** — quietly boosting
  Crestian's Praecet party ahead of the 29 ABY election (Article: Zubindi Hakoon).
- **The return / 2020 scenes (29 ABY).** He helps plan a street-naming ceremony in Basca
  Starker's honor with Jessalyn (RPlog:Party Planning, and Other Pursuits, 29 ABY) and
  attends the First Annual Youngling Ball on Cloud City, where Jedi and Sith families mix
  (RPlog:Johanna Has a Ball on Bespin, 29 ABY).
- **Spymaster in couture (29–32 ABY).** When Crestian is elected Presav, Zubindi is
  appointed head of the Caspian Intelligence Service's Wardrobe and Props Section and becomes
  a senior agent behind the scenes (Article: Zubindi Hakoon). He's present for Darth Malign's
  coronation as Imperator (RPlog:The Imperator's Coronation, 29 ABY) and CIS business —
  promotions and covers over dinner on the Leviathan (RPlog:Secret Service Supper, 32 ABY).
- **Galas and statecraft (32 ABY).** A run of social-political set-pieces: Crestian curating
  his art collection with Nara Rhen (RPlog:Art Collection, 32 ABY); Johanna's charity gala on
  Corellia (RPlog:Shindig on Corellia, 32 ABY); the Caspian wargames reception
  (RPlog:Wargame Pregame, 32 ABY); and dressing new clients on Cloud City
  (RPlog:Someone Needs New Duds, 32 ABY).
- **The Tahn party era (33 ABY).** Contracted by tycoon Malif Tal-Tahn to throw an
  extraordinary party (RPlog:Party Triggered, 33 ABY), which becomes the satirical, horror-
  tinged **Ghastly Gala** at Castle Tsabak on Dreven — "celebrating" the aesthetic of the
  reviled Basca Starker (RPlog:Ghastly Gala; RPlog:Ghastly Gala: Episode 2, 33 ABY).
- **Corellian charity circuit (34 ABY).** A Silverwood charity fashion show for The Mission
  (RPlog:Fashion Show, 34 ABY) and the masquerade Arkanis Sector Children's Relief Ball in
  Coronet City (RPlog:Charity Ball, 34 ABY) — his most recent appearances.

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters → use these to connect threads):
- **Crestian Tarasar** — best friend, top customer, and the engine of Zubindi's whole story;

## Character: Sorin — Sorin Markof, the Corellian scoundrel

**Capsule.** Corellian human male (Sorin Markof), 1.83 m, blonde hair, grey eyes; a former
child-holovid "star" turned Corellian refugee who "BORROWED" a scrapyard ship during the
Imperial invasion and has been running odd jobs across the galaxy ever since
(Article: Sorin). He sings adequately in bars to make berthing fees, captains a cobbled-
together freighter, and keeps an AstroGonk named **Suds** aboard (Article: Sorin). His two
logged scenes sit firmly in the Hutt-space underworld.

**Arc synthesis** (chronological; each beat is a fetch handle):

- **Background (pre-game).** Corellian, ex-holovid kid, fled the Imperial invasion of
  Corellia in a stolen scrapyard ship with a ragtag crew; perpetually broke, perpetually
  improvising (Article: Sorin).
- **The freighter caper (32 ABY).** With his crew of the *Fortune Rising* (name pending),
  Sorin works a con on Nar Shaddaa to win — or steal — a freighter off Baron Songir Fentrus
  at the sabacc table, using a faked registry tag and a goading bet (RPlog:The Hoodwinked
  Baron, 32 ABY).
- **The pad ambush (32 ABY).** When the Baron's men catch up at the spaceport landing pad,
  the inspection turns into a blaster fight, and the crew strong-arms pilot **Valya Tass**
  into joining them (RPlog:All the Baron's Men, 32 ABY).

**Key relationships** (most-cited co-characters):
- **Suana** — the red-skinned Twi'lek who is Sorin's right hand, bodyguard, and bickering
  partner; she makes the combat calls he can't, and plays his "slave girl" cover at the
  table (RPlog:All the Baron's Men; RPlog:The Hoodwinked Baron, 32 ABY).
- **Aisaiy Beemes** — the crew's grifter, plying the Baron with drink and chatter as the inside
  distraction (RPlog:The Hoodwinked Baron, 32 ABY).
- **Valya Tass** — pilot/yeoman "browbeat into joining the crew" during the pad ambush
  (RPlog:All the Baron's Men, 32 ABY).

**Playstyle / themes** (from 2 logs): smuggling, cons, sabacc, ship theft, ambush, and
underworld deception — all played for comedy. Reads as a **rogue-caper player** running a
lighter, lower-stakes counterpart to the Zubindi salon scenes.

**Signature voice.** Fast, jokey, self-aware patter; the captain who "looks authoritative.
Authoritatively hiding," bickers about cologne mid-firefight, and forgets his own cover name
("Feeling...lucky.../OH/"). Pull RPlog:The Hoodwinked Baron (32 ABY) for the con-man cadence.

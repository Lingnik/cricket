'''18 BBY''' covers events before the timeline of the game that include or impact game characters.

For more information about what happened during this year see the '''[[:starwars:18 BBY/Legends|18 BBY]]''' article on Wookieepedia.

==Events==
* A group of activists were protesting Imperial taxation on the planet [[:starwars:Ghorman/Legends|Ghorman]]. When [[Captain]] [[:starwars:Wilhuff Tarkin/Legends|Wilhuff Tarkin]] was blocked from landing by peaceful protestors refusing to leave the landing pad, he landed his ship on the protestors, immediately killing most of them. The incident became known as the [[:starwars:Ghorman Massacre/Legends|Ghorman Massacre]].
* [[Baron]] [[:starwars:Orman Tagge/Legends|Orman Tagge]] is blinded by [[:starwars:Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Darth Vader]] for seeking to supplant him as [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine's]] right hand man. From then on Baron Tagge has to wear goggles which become his trademark and are feature in the [[Biscuit Baron]] logo.

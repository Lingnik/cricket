'''22 BBY''' covers events before the timeline of the game that include or impact game characters.

For more information about what happened during this year see the '''[[:starwars:22 BBY/Legends|22 BBY]]''' article on Wookieepedia.

==Events==
* [[Jedi Knight]] [[:starwars:Pablo-Jill/Legends|Pablo-Jill]] brings peace, albeit temporarily, to the lawless world of [[Ord Mantell]]. This also growth in the city of [[New Alderaan|Beyerplest]] which becomes a moderately sized city.
* [[Sluis Van]] and [[Sullust]] secede from the [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Republic]].
* [[Senator]] [[:starwars:Tikkes/Legends|Tikkes]] of [[Dac]] is implicated in allowing [[:starwars:Thalassian slavers/Legends|Thalassian slavers]] to operate in the Calamari Sector for a share of their profits. He escaped [[:starwars:Galactic Republic|Republic]] custody on [[Coruscant]] and fled to [[:starwars:Geonosis/Legends|Geonosis]], where he became a member of the [[:starwars:Separatist Council|Separatist Council]] as leader of the [[:starwars:Free Dac Volunteers Engineering Corps/Legends|Free Dac]] movement, which sought to cut ties between Mon Calamari and the Republic. He is replaced in the [[:starwars:Galactic Senate/Legends|Galactic Senate]] by [[:starwars:Meena_Tills/Legends|Meena Tills]].
* Bounty hunter [[:starwars:Jango Fett/Legends|Jango Fett]] tasked fellow hunter [[:starwars:Zam Wesell/Legends|Zam Wesell]] with using a pair of starved [[:starwars:Kouhun/Legends|kouhuns]] from [[Indoumodo]] to assassinate [[Senator]] [[:starwars:Padmé Amidala/Legends|Padmé Amidala]] on [[Coruscant]]. The assassination was a failure, as both kouhuns were killed by [[Jedi Order|Jedi]] [[:starwars:Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Anakin Skywalker]].
* The [[:starwars:Grand Army of the Republic/Legends|Grand Army of the Republic]] is officially formed by [[:starwars:Supreme Chancellor/Legends|Supreme Chancellor]] [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]] to combat the [[:starwars:Confederacy of Independent Systems/Legends|Separatists]] starting the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]].
* [[HoloNet News]] is rechristened Republic HoloNet News to indicate support for the [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Republic]] during the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]].
* The [[Jedi Order]] and [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Galactic Republic]] fight the [[:starwars:Confederacy Independent Systems/Legends|Separatists]] on [[Teyr]]. The Republic wins the battle, but loses the majority of its ground forces in the process.
* [[Senator]] [[:starwars:Meena_Tills/Legends|Meena Tills]] of [[Dac]] hostage by [[:starwars:Korun/Legends|Korunnai]] terrorists on [[Coruscant]].
* [[ProThrust Engine Division]] becomes independent of [[SubPro|Sublight Products Corporation]].
* The economy of [[Anchorron]] on [[Caspar]] begins to collapse because of the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]].

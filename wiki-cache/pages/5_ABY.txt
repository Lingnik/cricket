This is also referred to as '''Year 24''' on the '''Imperial Standard''' calendar.

== Events ==
* [[Dark Jedi]] [[Wyth Skellheim]] executes the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] warlord [[Connor Dobbs]] and takes control of the [[Interim Ruling Council]], promoting [[Jon Christian Andrews]] to Grand Admiral.
* Imperial Grand Moff [[Gegoren Valance]] is executed.
* [[Wyth Skellheim]] disappears into the unknown regions. Grand Admiral Andrews retires his post. Chaos ensues in the Empire as the [[Bureau of Operations]] director ([[Gordio Dekan]]) and Grand Admiral [[Mitth'raw'nuruodo|Thrawn]] try to insert [[Holger Marcalias]] as a puppet leader, but fail as Holger doesn't garnish any support from the High Command or [[Interim Ruling Council]] whatsoever. 
* [[Alaric Darkstar]] steps up and assumes leadership of the Empire.
* The [[Griffon Alliance]] is officially recognized by the [[New Republic]] at the [[Corellia Convention]]. The overhauled [[Pride-1|Pride-1 Space Station]] is completed, finally set into place in a wayward area near the system of Scythia. Because of the founding of [[Tallon's Cut]], the station sees a rapid increase in trade traffic.
* Wyth Skellheim returns and attempts to rally support for his cause within the Empire. High Command leaders take sides between Wyth and Alaric. In the end, Moff [[Tholn Galenx]] is executed as a traitor, and Thrawn withdraws his fleet as Alaric maintains control. Grand Admiral [[Russel Canis|Canis]] is promoted to take charge of the Imperial Naval forces under Alaric's command.
* Alaric Darkstar abdicates the Imperial throne and Grand Moff [[Ariak Caldny]] takes charge of the Empire.
* After a supposed Imperial bounty is placed on his head, [[Corporate Sector Strike Fleet|CSA]] ExO [[Pymari]] is hunted and killed by bounty hunter [[Giles Vermillion]], who had been posing as one of the ExO's security team. No bounty was ever collected. [[Sean Dillon]] is appointed as CSA ExO. 
* [[Lyr Dunwell]] elected [[Presav]] of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], with [[Togryyshyk of Shyykris]] as Vice Presav. [[CDMC]] is established and the ''[[First Renaissance of Innovation|Renaissance of Innovation]]'' begins in Caspia, ushering in a rapid rise in the technological and creative capacity of the system's peoples. With it, though, comes the re-emergence of the [[Sar Bares Flir]]. Caspia is ravaged by the [[Second Zealot Wars]].

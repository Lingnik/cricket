The '''6th TF Hammer Army Battlegroup''', sometimes incorrectly referred to as the '''6th Army Division''', was an [[Imperial Army]] [[Imperial Army#Battlegroup|battlegroup]] assigned to the [[Imperial First Fleet]]'s [[Task Force Hammer]] during the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]] of [[14 ABY]]. Commanded by [[Major General]] [[Morrison Van Sen]], the 6th Battlegroup participated in several of the major actions of the Blitzkrieg, earning a dire reputation as a hard-hitting, tough Army formation that made even some in [[Stormtrooper Corps|Stormtrooper Command]] envious. The Battlegroup would suffer heavy casualties at the [[Fourth Battle of Sullust]], and Van Sen would later be removed from command.

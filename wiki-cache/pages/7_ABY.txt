This is also referred to as '''Year 26''' on the '''Imperial Standard''' calendar.

== Events ==

* [[Caspian Democratic Union|Caspian]] ex-Presav [[Lyr Dunwell]] is discovered to be a clone and is dispatched by his fellow compatriots.
* The city of Yaeberron renames itself to [[Dunwelton]] after the real Lyr Dunwell.
* [[Jessalyn Valios]], [[Luke Skywalker]]'s apprentice, is kidnapped from [[Tatooine]] by Emperor [[Bacharan Valak|Valak's]] [[Noghri]] agents.
* [[Pride-1]] [[Battle of Pride-1|captured]] by the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]], renamed [[Orbital Station Redemption]] during occupation. An entire [[Caspian Democratic Union|Caspian]] squadron, the [[CMTOS-21 Wildcats|21st Wildcats]] and their support personnel, are wiped out in the assault. Rumors wildly vary between them being captured and summarily executed to dying in the first salvos while they were still in the hangar the Griffons were hosting them in.
* Luke Skywalker fakes his death on [[O'paal]] and heads in to Imperial space with [[Mara Jade]].
* Emperor Valak's palace is destroyed by Luke Skywalker during the rescue of his apprentice.
* A galactic economic boom saw several new businesses emerge with fresh leadership. [[FineLine Spaceways]] was turned over to [[Darrien Bryant|Darrien]] and [[Dagny Bryant]], though in short time, [[Darrien Bryant]] moved on to [[NovaCom Cybertronics]], where he and [[Tyre Wolf]] brought [[InterGalactic News|IGNews]] to life. As Dagny Bryant ran for office within the CDU, [[Benjamin Jawatha]] was named CEO of FLS. 
* [[Kadgie Rodo]] elected CDU Presav with running mate Dagny Bryant.
* Luke Skywalker is captured by the Empire at Pride-1 (OS Redemption), and is transferred to [[Athaniss]]. During his imprisonment, Emperor Valak uses the Dark Side to damage Skywalker's connection to the Force. 
* [[Swoop Racing|Galactic Swoop Racing]] becomes a popular sport amongst the business crowd and off-duty military personnel. Businesses begin to invest in the sport, including [[TAJ Enterprises]], [[StarShield]], [[TCF Corporation]] and [[McTavish Enterprises]]. TCF Corporation later changes hands and becomes [[Avalon Enterprises]]. New tracks develop over the next year, including high profile venues such as [[Black Krayt Arena]] ([[Caspar]]), [[Reckless Abandon Swoop Arena]] ([[Corellia]]), [[Smuggler's Haven]] ([[Nar Shaddaa]]), and [[Sunbird Eyrie]] ([[Selene]]).
* Luke Skywalker's escape from [[Athaniss]] is assisted by NR Special Forces and [[Lando Calrissian]]. Once free, he goes about a long arduous process of regaining his powers.
* A deadly [[Yavin Plague|virus]] breaks out on [[Yavin 4]] and quickly spreads to [[Caspia]] and [[Krittain]], where it reaches epidemic proportions. The CDU is quarantined.
* The '[[Vigilant Movement|Vigilant]]' coup attempt within the Empire is ousted. Ringleaders of the movement, including [[Lorn Rhys]], are herded up and either imprisoned on [[Kessel]] or executed under orders of Valak.
* The cure for the Yavin 4 plague is found. Caspia's quarantine is lifted following a drastic improvement in the health of its populace.
* [[New Republic]] forces [[Second Battle of Pride-1|take]] OS Redemption from Empire, restore it to Pride-1.
* Grand Admiral [[Morlok Adlerson]] of the Empire retires from the [[Imperial Navy]] to become the Moff of [[Dreven]]. 
* [[Leia Organa Solo]] is attacked while visiting Caspar on a diplomatic mission. During her hospitalization, rumors circulate that she has been killed and an elaborate cover-up had been instigated by the CDU. These rumors were put to rest with a public press appearance by Organa-Solo and Presav Laarken.
*FLS CEO [[Benjamin Jawatha]] is linked to a young CDU fighter pilot in the tabloids named [[Elana Tracer]], the salacious series of articles having been planted, either secretly or anonymously, by then NRI officer [[Heinrich Stalh|Heinrich Stahl]] following a rumored altercation in a Caspar bar.

The '''988th Special Operations Company''' was an elite unit of the [[Imperial Army]] operated by [[Colonel]] [[Liuz Bogumil]]. They specialized in various forms of warfare. The unit performed so well in the field that it was later absorbed into the [[Stormtrooper Corps]]. Members included [[Sandor Woden]] and [[Tanis Cadell]].

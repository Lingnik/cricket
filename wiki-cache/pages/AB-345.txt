'''AB-345''' is an uninhabited world in the Quoram System, located within [[New Sith Order|Sith Space]] near [[Cochran]]. Despite lacking any form of life, AB-345 had a somewhat sustainable atmosphere, though the air was heavily contaminated. It has been theorized that the world was a victim of a catastrophic disaster that killed all life, possibly a [[Base Delta Zero]]-like attack. 

The Sith used the world for mining purposes, gathering resources to fuel its growing war machine. A small camp was set on the world which housed a small garrison of troops and a slave crew. In orbit rested a single space station that acted as a monitoring post and waystation for freighters. The Sith operation on the world came to an end in [[17 ABY]] after an [[Attack on AB-345|attack]] by the [[New Republic]].

'''A'estshy''' was born upon the planet of [[Ryloth]] some unknown years ago, her age still uncertain even to her. She lived with her sister, Ildessa, helping each other through the tougher times of life, but never lacking that of family or love.  As many others of her kind, life did not start till one left Ryloth.

Quite recently, the golden [[Twi'lek]] was brought to Corusca City on [[Coruscant]], naive and curious to this expanded world and universe, a million doors suddenly thrown open for her.  Zhao, the Twi'lek Maffi, run by [[Tyy'sun Eson]] had found the budding fighter upon Ryloth and paid for her transfer.  Not able to pass up such a position, she had accepted and nothing could prepare her for the world she would enter.

Only able to speak Ryl and lost within the technologies as well as the bustling strange life of Coruscant, she forged her way ahead.  Here she proved herself to the ones who paid her fee and soon enough was brought into the 'family'.  Disguised as a slave she was soon attached to Tyy'sun, appearing with him in most places.  Upon her first day in Corusca, she met a [[Quarren]] by the name of [[Barrien Thrask]]. Kind and generous, he offered aid and money, telling her to keep clear of slavers.

During this time, she met An'sha, another Twi'lek entering the ranks of the Maffi.  Quickly the two became close friends and were nearly inseparable.  But it would be not long after that they were both captured by a bounty hunter set for [[Draga the Hutt]] carrying none other than Shy and [[Sha'dria]], known as An'sha to the golden female. A'estshy was soon bought back by Zhao, once more at Tyy'sun's side.  An'sha's disguise was comprimised and Eson now having the true name of the Twi'lek was bound to hunt her down.

Currently A'estshy is the personal bodyguard of Tyy'sun Eson, a lithe-formed Twi'lek and slight in height standing at just over a meter-and-a-half, she does not seem imposing. But as a warning, never attack unless you have distance, her swiftness would knock even the largest from their feet.

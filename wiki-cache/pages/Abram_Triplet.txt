'''Abram Triplet''' was a leading starship captain in the [[Caspian Navy]] from its inception until his retirement after being released from Imperial internment in [[8 ABY]]. He was one of the original officers to defect with [[Dergan Roj]] from the Imperial Navy in 0 BBY/ABY and form the [[Barren Sector Fleet]].

=== History ===
A mere [[Lieutenant]] when he defected, Triplet nonetheless made an immediate impression with the senior officers in actions undertaken during the Barrenness Excursions. He had a natural aptitude for command, and was fearless and resolute in his tactical decisions. Roj shifted him to various postings within the Fleet, typically there to bolster efficiency and invigorate morale with his youthful zeal and can-do attitude. The loss of [[VeSD Warspite|''Warspite'']] was difficult on him, primarily because he was close with several of the officers and enlisted on the warship. The Admiral was forced to briefly reduce his responsibilities as discipline for poor performance while he grieved. After seeking counsel with Doctor Trissard and [[Miala Tranault]], Triplet eventually regained his character and Roj gave him command of the picket ''Makovica''.

When the remnants of the Barren Sector Fleet transitioned to become the new [[Caspian Navy]], Triplet was specially promoted by Roj (in one of his final decrees) to be the captain of [[CMS Provost|''Provost'']], the one ship that had escaped the Barrenness Excursions with relatively minor injury and that would serve alongside the [[Sarian]] battlecruiser [[CMS Hood|''Hood'']] as the heavy warships of the new Union. Triplet would take the assignment with honor, and turned ''Provost'' into a highly effective, efficient machine.

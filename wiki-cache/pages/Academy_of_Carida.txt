The '''Academy of Carida''' is one of the largest [[Imperial Military]] academies still in use by the [[Galactic Empire]]. Located on [[Carida]], the Academy is galactically-renown for churning out some of the best officers in the Imperial Military. Despite being an officer's academy, the Academy of Carida is also home to a massive [[Stormtrooper]] training facility.

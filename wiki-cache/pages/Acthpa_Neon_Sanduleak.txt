'''Acthpa Neon Sanduleak''' was the Chief Operations Officer of the Imperial [[Bureau of Operations]] for several years under Lord [[Hul Loris Pantek]]'s orders, during [[Bacharan Valak]]'s reign.

== Biography ==

She was a former bounty hunter who joined the Empire together with her only  friend [[Umbriel of Vequess]]. Coming from the Outer Rim, she was the daughter of a simple trade couple, nothing special about it. As much as Umbriel liked power, she liked field work. Acthpa was always in an infiltration mission, except for a torture or an interrogation session.

She began as an Initiate, with first mission as finding if [[Caspia]] was involved with the New Republic. On Sluis Van, she met [[Heinrich Stalh]] who offered her a job as journalist for IG News. From then, she was known as a journalist outside of the Empire. Stalh - who was also working for the Empire - gave her the information she needed. Then began the Casparian War. With this mission successfull, she was promoted as Agent.

As an Agent, she infiltrated both Caspia and the Republic and was gathering information about important people, usually the next targets for the Imperial Military. She was barely known within the Empire except from the BofO. Then she's been promoted to the rank of Chief Operations Officer. There, she continued to infiltrate the ennemies despite Pantek's orders. But she was also leading the infiltration/torture/interrogation/killing sessions. She met former Shadowguards [[Tristan Hardy]] who has been the best Agent she ever had to work with.

Things were going well within the Bureau until she received a message from her family, asking for her help in the Outer Rim. She obtained Pantek's permission and left. She did not give any news for several years and was classified as missing in action.

In Outer Rim, she met a real war against non human - that she nicely hates - and she was made prisoner. She managed to escape and came back to the Empire. Hopefully the ancient security codes will be enough to prove herself to the Empire back again...

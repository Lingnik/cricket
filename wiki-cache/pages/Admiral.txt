The rank and title of '''Admiral''' encompasses the highest level of commissioned officer in a navy, referred to as a '''Flag Officer'''. An Admiral often commands several vessels, and/or is responsible for an aspect within the navy or a realm of territory. The Admiral maintains a command staff, and may transfer it to wherever they deem most effective, whether it be from an office in an installation one day to the bridge (thenceforth ''flag bridge'') of a prominent warship by the next. By most standards, an Admiral never commanded a warship directly, instead retaining and directing the ship's [[captain]] to do so.

There were several derivatives of the Admiral rank, based on seniority and capability. These included [[Rear Admiral]] and [[Vice Admiral]] beneath, and [[Fleet Admiral]] and [[Grand Admiral]] above. Some armed forces never truly conformed to a naval system, and some [[General]]s retained their rank while commanding multiple starships and forces. As such, they were equitable to Admirals.

== In Imperial Service ==
{{rank plate|[[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]]|}}
In the [[Imperial Navy]], an '''Admiral''' normally commands a [[Imperial Navy#Systems force|Systems force]], or in certain cases, a specialized task force temporarily constructed to deal with an immediate or long-term objective (as in the case of [[Task Force Hammer]]). Holding the rank of Admiral was considered a great accomplishment in the Imperial Navy, as life expectancy of Imperial officers (at least under the leadership of [[:starwars:Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Darth Vader]]) was never very high, and the power and authority that came with the rank was highly significant.

An Admiral in control of a Systems force generally reports to their sector's [[High Admiral]] or [[Moff]], and in certain cases to a [[Fleet Admiral]], if that Admiral is attached to a Fleet. 

An Admiral is immediately above a [[Vice Admiral]] and immediately below a [[Fleet Admiral]] in the Imperial rank structure, and is equivalent to a [[General]] in the [[Imperial Army]] or [[Chief Marshal]] in the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]]. A Chief Marshal generally commanded the sum total of a System force's Starfighter deployment.

=== Caspian Navy ===

'''Admiral''' is the third most senior rank in the [[Caspian Navy]] ranking below [[Fleet Admiral|Fleet Admirals]] and ranking above [[Vice Admiral|Vice Admirals]] and [[Rear Admiral|Rear Admirals]]. Admirals typically serve as the commanding officer of a Defence Group, Patrol Group, Task Group, or Starbase though such positions may be held by a Vice Admiral or exercised directly by a Sar Admiral or Fleet Admiral. Caspian Admirals usually command from a [[Admiral Roj-class Battleship|Admiral Roj-Class Battleship]] or in the case of those who were in the [[Krittish Navy]] a [[Valorous-class Light Carrier|Valorous-Class Light Carrier]].

=== Caspian Starfighter Corps ===

'''Admiral''' is the third most senior rank in the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]] ranking below [[Marshal|Marshals]] and [[Vice Marshal|Vice Marshals]] and ranking above [[Vice Admiral|Vice Admirals]]. Admirals typically serve as the Commanding Officer of Starfighters for one of the Naval [[Caspian Navy#Fleets|fleets]]. They will typically base themselves on one of the [[Hurricane-class Fleet Carrier|Hurricane-class Fleet Carriers]] within their fleet. In terms of preferential treatment and prestige they can be consider equivalent to Naval Admirals, though they are equivalent to Naval Vice Admirals in pay. In terms of operational authority a Naval Admiral outranks as Starfighter Admiral, but a Starfighter Admiral will outrank a Naval Vice Admiral.

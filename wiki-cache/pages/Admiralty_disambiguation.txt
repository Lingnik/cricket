An '''admiralty''' was a board or office in charge of military (typically, naval) affairs on the behalf of a government. While they often had formal departmental names, these offices could be referred to colloquially in conversation as ''admiralties''. 

Examples include:
*[[The Admiralty]] &mdash; a formal entity within the Caspian Democratic Union directing all naval operations, as well as joint military affairs.
*The [[Grand Admiral's Council]] &mdash; the directorial group governing naval operations for the Galactic Empire.

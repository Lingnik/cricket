'''Adventure Cruiselines''' is a company run by [[Daana Roon]].  Most notably, the company owns and operates the ''[[Angry Rancor]]'' cruiseliner.

The cruiseline was founded in [[15 ABY]], with the express purpose of salvaging and relaunching the ''Angry Rancor''.  The ship had been abandoned over [[Tatooine]] some time before, after the disappearance of its previous owner, [[Ethan Katana]].  Daana acquired the salvage rights and organized a crew.  They successfully recovered the ship and took it to the [[Corellian Engineering Corporation|CEC Shipyards]] for a complete overhaul.

Structurally restored, the ship was taken next to [[Ithor]] where the cruiseline has its business office.  Environmental systems were installed by the [[Ithorian|Ithorians]], and the ship was deemed ready to fly.  It was re-launched with Ithor designated as its home port.

The business now operates primarily off of the ''Rancor'' itself, however they maintain their office on Ithor and have warehouses and other facilities in different locations.  The most prominent of these is a public office on [[Corellia]], located along Trader's Way in [[Coronet City]].  The office provides information about the cruiseline's offerings, and enables visitors to book cruises.

== Description ==
Intense midnight-blue eyes light up with vibrant energy while her smile is always a little mischievous. It's a juxtaposition that makes her unique features stand out. Carefully arched and expressive eyebrows are a prominent feature, while long sooty black lashes from her dark blue eyes. A delicate but well defined nose balances well-structured cheekbones. Lit from within, her skin has a healthy and vibrant natural glow to it. There's a softness to it, fair but not pale, radiant and never dull. Freckles smatter across her lithe form, like stars in the sky. Fiery red hair shifts into tones of cinnamon and ginger, with lighter sun-kissed ends. Cut in long layers, she wears it in a variety of styles, but most often it is pulled up or back from her face when she is focused. A slender hourglass figure is imbued with grace and agility, athletic tone matched with distinct feminine curves. Long slim legs give her enough height to stand-out in a crowd. She effortlessly looks elegant, sometimes a little innocent -- but her feisty attitude colours it all.

== Current Employment ==
Aisaiy Beemes is currently a hostess on the Merchant-sloop ''Fortunes Rising'' docked at Cloud City. Her duties include personal assisting high rollers in finding suites, enjoy amenities and gambling near the promenade.

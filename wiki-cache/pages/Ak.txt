'''ak''' ([[5 BBY]] - ) is a [[Xi Char]] leader. He is [[Dean]] of the [[Cathedral Factory]] of Xi Can on [[Charros IV]] and Xi Can's representative on the [[Union Council]]. He also serves as the Chief Executive Officer of [[Haor Chall Engineering]]. As an extension of his role in the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] defence industry he holds the rank of [[Vice Admiral]] in the [[Caspian Navy]].

== Early Life ==

ak as he has come to be known, was born on the planet of [[Charros IV]] five years before the Battle of Yavin. He was born into a society that had been utterly devastated by their association with the [[:starwars:Confederacy of Independent Systems/Legends|Confederacy of Independent Systems]] during the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]]. The Cathedral Factories that had been the centre of [[Xi Charrian]] industry and spirituality had been wrecked by the Republic in the course of the war and their designs ended up in the hands of their enemies.

As his people worked to rebuild from the war, they would generally avoid the employees of [[Sienar Fleet Systems]] on their planet as much as was possible. ak took a different approach. While he still considered Sienar Fleet Systems and later [[Kuat Drive Yards-Sienar Fleet Systems]] as an enemy to his people, he was not willing to simply paint all of their employees with the same brush. If the [[Xi Char]] were to have any hope of gaining back control of their industry it would require sympathetic souls outside of their order.

As part of his efforts to get along with the alien heathens ak would drop his own impressively long name and simply go by ak, the end syllable to all Xi Charrian names. In this way not only was ak seemingly more approachable to aliens, but he created a mystique as singularly interesting figuring. Among the aliens on Charros IV, ak would be considered the most interesting Xi Charrian alive, as much because they did not get to really know the others as anything else.

And so in his dealing with aliens, ak would often engage in efforts to entertain with feats of strength, precision, and daring. He was also known for patronising alien establishments his people would ignore such as bars, casinos, and clubs. Sometimes it was just the little things like asking someone how their youngling's dance recital went, whether their spouse got the job promotion they were seeking, whether their parent recovered from an ailment.

It was a heterodox approach to be sure, but ak was able to use this approach to bring contracts to the Xi Char that more traditional figures struggled with. The success would eventually allow ak to climb the hierarchy of the Xi Char in spite of his unconventional behaviour. By [[27 ABY]] he had ascended to the position of Dean of Xi Can, which had re-emerged as one of the largest Cathedral Factories on Charros IV.

== Revival ==

ak's would find a new opportunity to make friends when [[Crestian Tarasar]] was elected [[Presav]] of the [[Caspian Union]] in [[29 ABY]]. Tarasar was well aware of the plight of the Xi Charrians and the indignity the suffered in having their designs in the hands of a company associated with the late [[:starwars:Raith Sienar/Legends|Raith Sienar]] a man who had betrayed the Xi Char. The transition of the [[Galactic Empire]] into the Galactic [[Imperium]] under the leadership of Imperator [[Tyler Damion|Damion I]] provided Tarasar an opportunity to do something about it. The Imperator appointed former Caspian Presav [[Elana Tracer]] as [[Moff]] of [[Selene]] and gave her the reins of [[Kuat Drive Yards-Sienar Fleet Systems]].

Tarasar had a bold idea for the designs and production facilities of the former [[Haor Chall Engineering]] to be repatriated to the Xi Char and for Charros IV to be allowed to cede from the Imperium and join the [[Caspian Union]] instead. With both the Imperator and Moff Tracer open to the idea, three way negotiations soon began between the Caspian Union, the Imperium/KDY-SFS, and the Xi Char and ak naturally found himself as the lead negotiator for the Xi Char.

These negotiations would vault ak into a greater spotlight and his buoyant personality would soon see him become personal friends with several members of the [[Moff's Council]] and [[Union Council]]. Even before negotiations had been concluded, Xi Char engineers were already contracted by the [[Caspian Navy]] to lead the recommission and refit of their [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruisers]].

After several months of negotiations a final treaty was signed in [[30 ABY]] by Tarasar, Tracer, and Xi Char Prelate r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak. Charros IV would now be a member world of the Caspian Union and Haor Chall Engineering reformed and would now be working with [[SubPro]], [[Lokkai Starworks]], and [[Aston Aerospace]] in building, maintaining, and refitting the Caspian Navy going forward.

For ak this meant he now had a seat on the Union Council and the title of Executive Vice President of Haor Chall Engineering. In the years since ak has been an active member of the Union Council frequently involved in matters of diplomacy, industry, technology, and welfare. He has also played an active role in shepherding new and revamped Haor Chall Engineering designs through the development and production. The ''Articulation Conversion Sphere'' or ''AC Sphere'' is a personal design of his own, a market version of the [[Droid|droid]] he uses to translate Xi Charrian into other languages. He also serves on the board of directors of [[The Mission]].

Like a number of other government ministers he also holds a commission in the [[Caspian Defence Force]]. He was originally commissioned with the rank [[Rear Admiral]] in the [[Caspian Navy]] in connexion with his role as Dean of the Xi Can Cathedral Factory and Executive Vice President of Haor Chall Engineering. 

== Recent Events ==

In [[34 ABY]] Prelate r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak died. Dean ak played an active role in the funerary ceremonies for the Prelate and was widely touted as the candidate most likely to be elected to succeed him during the subsequent [[conclave]]. Inquisitor [[stahanak]] was ultimately chosen as Prelate instead. While th new Prelate did assumed the title of Chairman of the Board of Directors of Haor Chall Engineering, he nominated ak to succeed the old Prelate as Chief Executive Officer of the Xi Char firm. In recognition of ak's new position as CEO of one of the largest shipwrights in the Union, he was promoted to the rank of [[Vice Admiral]] in the Caspian Navy.

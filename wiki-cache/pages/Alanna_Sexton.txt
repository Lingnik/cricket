== Overview ==
As Executive Officer of the ''[[V2SD Nemesis|Nemesis]]'','' ''Alanna Sexton oversees all aspects of ship-board and joint-branch operations, reporting directly to Captain Kelemann. The young high flier has earned rapid advancement virtually unprecedented and was selected over men twenty years her senior for the prestigious assignment.

A first-class honors academy graduate, the typical career path for the Navy especially for those not of the "officer-class" is to tether oneself to a superior and climb the ranks behind them. In contrast, Sexton has seen near annual reassignment and has served aboard every class of major warship employed by the Imperial Navy.

As a result Alanna has few friends, lacks the political connections typical of her more experienced peers and has never commanded a vessel of her own.

All of this makes her present assignment controversial among the flag officers of the star fleet. It is widely believed that as many view the situation as an opportunity to see her sink, as to swim.

== Background ==

==== Early Life ====
Born 20 ISY (1 ABY) Alanna, like the rest of her generation, has never know a galaxy at peace. When the fledgling New Republic seized control of Fondor for its shipyards, she was still a child and her parents prior support for the New Order ensured their dismissal from the workforce constructing the Republic's new fleet.

The Sexton family spent several years in relative poverty, dependent on unreliable early Republic social-assistance programs until the Empire re-conquered the planet during Valak's Grand Campaign. The family fortunes corrected, Alanna was accepted by the re-established Imperial Academy as part of a wider indoctrination campaign for the new generation promoted by [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] and came of age during the War of the Throne and Imperial Blitzkrieg.

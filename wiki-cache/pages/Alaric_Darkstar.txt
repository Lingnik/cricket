'''Alaric Darkstar''' was an [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[Warlord]] who took control of the [[Interim Ruling Council]] in [[5 ABY]].

His past has been lost to official record. He was an influential politician and officer in the Galactic Empire sometime before the [[:starwars:Battle_of_Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]], and his reputation was high enough for him to secure his seat of power on [[Dreven]] during the [[Post-Endor Imperial power struggle]].

After [[Wyth Skellheim]] disappeared and left his position of power vacant, chaos ensued throughout the Empire, already broken by the ongoing struggle. A plot to install [[Holger Marcalias]] as a puppet leader of the Council was launched by [[Grand Admiral]] [[Mitth'raw'nuruodo|Thrawn]] and the [[Director of Imperial Intelligence]], [[Gordio Dekan]]. The plot failed, however, and in the aftermath Darkstar made his own attempt to seize control. He succeeded, and he took the title of [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]], though few recognized him as a legitimate heir to [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]].

His reign would soon be contested by the return of the Dark Lord, Skellheim, who attempted to reclaim the throne for himself. Wyth would fail, however, and Darkstar would remain in power. His hold over the Council, and the remnants of the Empire, would be severely weakened. In just a few months he would step down, replaced by [[Grand Moff]] [[Ariak Caldny]]. 

Alaric married [[Venus Darkstar|Venus Dekan]] while she was just a teenager.

'''Alexander Floyd''' was a prominent businessman. He founded [[Galactic Amusements]] and managed the [[Team Imperial]] [[swoop]] racing team. He later purchased and operated the ''[[Supernova Siren]]'' and expanded his business empire to various worlds within [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] territory. He disappeared around [[11 ABY]], rumors abound of assassination, and his business empire collapsed soon thereafter.

The '''Alien Internment on Selene''' was a massive internment operation executed by [[Grand Moff]] [[Scaven Marx]] and [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] to aid in the reconstruction of [[Selene]] in [[13 ABY]].

[[Warlord]] Malign, utilizing the [[Imperial First Fleet|First Fleet's]] [[Task Force Hammer]], executed a series of small operations that rounded up thousands of non-[[human]] species from various planets, bringing them to Selene, currently undergoing heavy reconstruction. The non-humans were placed into internment camps and forced into slave labor, put to work on the reconstruction of the former Imperial capital, devastated by the [[War of the Throne]].

The non-humans were largely mistreated, poorly fed, and many died. Several attempts by the [[Jedi]], prominently [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]], and [[New Republic]] officials, to stop the internment were made, but, ultimately, failed. Selene would go on to be entirely rebuilt. After their job on Selene was finished, the slaves would be transferred to other areas of the Empire, to aid in the development of weapons and defense equipment for the upcoming [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]].

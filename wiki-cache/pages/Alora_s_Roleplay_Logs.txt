|synopsis = Paul Nighman, newly arrived on Palanhi, finds himself drawn to the architecture of the Grand Palanhi Hotel. He came to Palanhi to meet up with his friend and colleague Marcus. Paul was confused and offered to take Marcus wherever he needed to go, but Marcus said that he really wanted Nighman to meet up with a professor at the Academy, who was interested in having Paul look for a few specific pieces while he was on Palanhi. Having been out in space for a number of months, Paul realized that a little time off planetside would be nice and lent Marcus his ship. He has no idea how long he will be in town, but he knows that he will definitely need a place to stay. He enters the impressive front doors, his eyes taking in all of the structural detail, especially the subtle and not so subtle flairs and nuances that make the architecture specific to the plant Palanhi.}}

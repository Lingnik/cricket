'''Alyssa Young''' was a pilot for the now-defunct criminal syndicate, [[V'ez-Tcha]]. It is unknown whether or not she survived the destruction of the syndicate at the hands of the [[Galactic Empire]] in the [[Battle of Tatooine]]. She was known for her photographic memory and friendly, caring personality.

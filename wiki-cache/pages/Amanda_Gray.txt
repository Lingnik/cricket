'''Amanda Gray''' was a [[Caspar|Caspian]] socialite who was married to [[Anders Gray]]. She is probably best known as being the mother of [[Charlotte Anne Batrad]] who became the wife of [[Salonika Batrad]], and also served as Vice Presav during the [[Presav|presavadency]] of her husband.

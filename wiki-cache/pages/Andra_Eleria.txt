'''Andra Eleria''' ([[2 ABY]] - ) is a [[Pantoran]] priestess and politician who represents [[Noques Rasala]] in the [[Union Council]]. She is also the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] Minister of Culture and a member of the Board of Directors of [[The Mission]]. Her niece is the businesswoman [[Veruca Violet]].

== Background ==

Andra Eleria was born in [[Trinumvira]] on [[Caspar]] two years after the Battle of Yavin. Her family was quite wealthy, with her older brother launching the Elerian Nut Company which was one of Caspar's largest and most prosperous food companies. Andra's interests were quite different, however, and she relocated to [[Noques Rasala]] in here teen years to focus on spiritual pursuits. While the rest of her family lived in opulence, Andra would maintain a small studio apartment with only meagre furnishings.

While Noques Rasala drew in people of many faiths, Andra found the spirituality native to the [[Sarian]] people and its focus on natural elements to be what spoke to her. The priesthood in Noques Rasala was a rather saturated occupation and prior to the Sariphage pandemic, Andra's life was not particularly noteworthy beyond her local community.

== A Life of Consequence ==

[[File:Andra Eleria.jpg|right|thumb]] When the Sariphage pandemic hit, Noques Rasala was particularly hard hit. Andra herself had been on a spiritual retreat outside the city and managed to survive personally unscathed. When she returned to civilisation, however, all the information she could find out about her family seemed to indicate they had all perished. Turning to her faith and the needs of her people she busied herself helping the survivors in Noques Rasala and the refugees from Mergansar that were starting to settle as well.

This work would eventually put Andra into contact with [[Crestian Tarasar]] and his charity [[The Mission]] and she would soon become The Mission's point person in the city. She would also come to find out that her niece [[Veruca Violet]] had survived the pandemic, hiding out in one of the family's businesses in a city block that would later be bought by [[Zubindi Hakoon]]. Veruca had tried to fly under the radar as she had not wanted to be sent to live with her aunt and be stuck living the same sort of austere life that Andra embraced. Zubindi saw something special in Veruca and offered to take her as his ward, honouring Veruca's own preference in the matter, Andra did not object.

When elections were held to replaced the Provisional Assembly on Caspar with a permanent [[Union Council]] Andra was tapped as the candidate of Crestian's party [[Praecet]] for Noques Rasala. Praecet was initially leading in the polls before the reemergence of [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] and the fears of what Crestian's connexion to him might mean for Caspar, with support shifting to Eire Piaphon, the [[Holicet]] candidate in the aftermath. A disastrous debate performance by the [[Legacy]] party candidate made it effectively a race between Praecet and Holicet with the latter continuing to lead. A terrorist attack on [[Kichnar Orbital Station]], during the election returns there, saw more support for Praecet in the other constituencies and Andra eked out a win to be elected Councillor for Noques Rasala.

Now [[Presav]], Crestian tapped Andra to serve as the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] Minister of Culture. Though considered a senior ministry position Andra is not known to maintain as large of a public profile as many other senior ministers. She is known, however, to play an active role in Caspian humanitarian efforts both through her role in the Union Council and her position on the Board of Directors of The Mission.

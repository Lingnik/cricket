'''Aned Kirkland''' was a mercenary who once served in the [[Galactic Empire]] as a starfighter pilot. Born before the [[:starwars:Clone Wars|Clone Wars]], Kirkland saw many battles over his life. He grew disillusioned with the Empire after the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor|Battle of Endor]] and abandoned his post, fleeing from the mess that had become the Empire. He spent the rest of his life fighting as a mercenary, working for whomever required his services. This eventually led him to the [[Red Brigade]], the [[New Republic]]'s paramilitary brigade formed on [[Mon Calamari]] in response to the lack of able soldiers to deal with the [[Dac Civil War]]. He participated in several campaigns during the Civil War and bore witness to the destruction of [[Rogue Squadron]]. He later found his way into the [[New Sith Order]], inducted by [[Aknar Koth'lyri]]. He was killed during the [[Battle of Kashyyyk (Sith Campaign)|Battle of Kashyyyk]], courtesy of a proton torpedo from [[Lance Corbet]].

The crew of the [[Angry Rancor]] is divided in two major sections: [[#Ship Operations|Ship Operations]], which answers to the Captain, and [[#Cruise Operations|Cruise Operations]], which answers to the Cruise Director.  Both of these positions answer to the Owner/CEO, which is [[Daana Roon]].

The hierarchies below show the PC and notable NPC crew members.  Unless otherwise noted, other positions on board the ship can be assumed to be filled by other NPCs.  The ship welcomes new crew members in any of the open positions mentioned.  If you have an idea for a job that isn't listed, that is also open for discussion.


== Ship Operations ==

This part of the crew is in charge of keeping the ship itself running smoothly, getting it where it needs to go, and operating the services that are available on any other type of ship out there.


'''Captain''' - [[Bruno Ringoire|Bruno G. Ringoire]] (NPC, human, handled by Serenella's player)


:'''Bridge Officers''' - the active officer for each watch, in charge of overseeing the ship.
::'''Bridge Crew''' - pilot/navigators, sensor/data specialists
:::bridge droid - R6-M6 or "M6" (NPC, astromech droid)


:'''Head of Engineering''' - [[Ka'una La]] (PC, twi'lek)
::'''Engineering Crew''' - ship and computer technicians


:'''Tactical Officers''' - in charge of the ship's weapons and defenses
::'''Tactical Crew''' - weapon and shield operators


:'''Head of Security''' - [[Juran Reinzhiner]] (PC, human) - in charge of maintaining order on board the ship
::'''Security Officers''' - a small but well-trained force.


:'''Hangar Control Officer''' - like flight control, oversees everything and gives takeoff and landing clearance
::'''Hangar Operations Officer''' - in charge inside the hangar
:::'''Hangar Crew''' - ship and cargo handlers, shuttle and vehicle pilots, ship and vehicle technicians


:'''Head of Communications'''
::'''Communication Specialists''' - keep us in touch with other ships and planets


:'''Purser''' - keeps the ship supplied with all and sundry that it needs

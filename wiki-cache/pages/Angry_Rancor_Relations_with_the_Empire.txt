The ''Rancor'' currently has a civil relationship with the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]]. This is despite several major incidents in the [[Angry Rancor#History|past]], such as the ship being chased off by a swarm of TIE fighters as its first captain and cruise director were killed in the [[Battle_of_Gastus_IV|destruction of Gastus]]. The current management of the ship is in no way connected to that previous crew, and no apparent grudge is held over the incident. In fact, after being salvaged over [[Tatooine]], the ship was overhauled at [[Corellian_Engineering_Corporation|CEC Shipyards]].

The ship currently has a [[Angry Rancor/Administrative details#Neutrality Agreements|neutrality agreement]] in place with the Empire, and it visits Imperial space with some frequency.

== Task Force Inquisitor ==

A standing arrangement between the ''Rancor'' and the [[Task Force Inquisitor]] enables the service men and women of the Task Force to use the ship as a spot for R&R, with expenses covered by the Task Force's account. The deal, forged by acting Cruise Director [[Inanna Jordain]] and representatives of Marshal [[Krieg Inrokana]], includes the following points:

* It is reinforced that the [[Angry Rancor/Administrative details#Neutrality Agreements|neutrality agreement]] between the ''Rancor'' and the Empire is in full effect, and the Task Force will use the ship only for R&R, not coming on board in force to apprehend criminals or personnel from other factions (unless it's a pressing matter and they get permission do to so first).

* Basic expenses for members of the Task Force will be covered by the corporate account. This includes standard lodging (non-coded rooms), food and drink. In an OOC sense, this means essentially anything for which there is no actual charge of coded credits (the coded suites on board the ship can be arranged on a case by case basis, and gambling expenses are naturally the responsibility of each individual).

* VIPs from the Task Force will receive appropriate VIP treatment on board.

* Whenever possible, a shuttle will be available to bring members of the Task Force back and forth between Imperial space and the ''Rancor'', as they cannot always fly into another faction's space to reach the ship. (Players wishing to reach the ship in-game can contact a member of the ''Rancor'' crew to arrange this.)

* This is not an exclusive agreement.

* OOCly, this agreement is designed to act as a "bridge" between the Task Force and the ''Rancor'', enabling the two groups to RP together.

An [[Armed Forces Branch Director]] is the top military position for each of the three branches of the [[New Republic Military]]. Outranked only by the [[Supreme Commander]] and the [[Chief of State|Commander in Chief]], Branch Directors are responsible for overseeing the operations of their branch of the military.

The Director of Fleet Operations holds a rank of [[Admiral]].

The Director of Starfighter Operations holds a rank of [[General]].

The Director of Ground Operations holds a rank of [[General]].

The position of '''Armiger''' was the highest office in the [[Griffon Alliance]]. It was a nominated and elected office, and was typically held on an annual term. An Armiger was either challenged or reaffirmed at an annual event called the [[Griffon Alliance#The Assembly of Warriors|the Assembly of Warriors]].

This leader would form the core of a Chief Council of advisors. By Griffon law, the Armiger had to be one of martial experience and would most often be of the [[Griffon Armada]] or [[Spaceborne Assault Trooper Armee|Armee]].

The [[Desolation of Cochran|end of the Griffon Alliance]] brought about the cessation of this office.

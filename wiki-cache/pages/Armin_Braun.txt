'''Armin Braun''' is a [[Stormtrooper]] Lieutenant, who is currently serving in the [[203rd Legion]], despite joining the [[Galactic Empire]] as a simple Stormtrooper. While it is not unheard of for Stormtroopers to be promoted to officer status after demonstrating their aptitude for leadership it certainly came as a surprise for Lt. Braun.

Among his men Lt. Braun is known as a rather kind commander even if he is rather ruthless towards his enemies, among whom he counts soldiers that do not follow orders.

''"A tool that is good at doing its job is well taken care of, a tool that can't do the one thing it's made for is quickly discarded." - Lt. Braun''

== Reputation ==

While not a well known officer there are two things Lt. Braun is known for. The first, and most obvious, thing being his rather ruthless attitude towards the New Republic. Civilian or a member of their Military it does not matter, to him as they are all traitors choosing to resist the Empire. It is just one big revolt that needs to be put down. A revolt that took the life of many of his men no less.

''"For Every dead man under my command I swear on my life to take 50 of theirs." - Lt. Braun''

The second thing hes "known" for? That would be his good treatment of the men serving him. Lt. Braun takes the time to get to personally know each and every man under his command as he believes that helps increase loyalty within his ranks. Whether it is effective or not is questionable at best but at the very least they see the man giving them orders as an actual person. Besides that he tends to lead by example, giving an order and being the first to follow it when appropriate. His belief being that it gives his men confidence that the orders wont get them killed, even if they should be happy to die for the Empire.

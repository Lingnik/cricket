'''Arner Denson''' was a former Chief Executive Officer of [[Merr-Sonn|Merr-Sonn Mil/Sci]] from [[9 ABY]] - [[10 ABY]]. His tenure at Merr-Sonn allowed him to hold a position on the [[Direx Board (CSA)|Direx Board]] within the [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]]. Soon after taking over Merr-Sonn, he orchestrated the move of hits headquarters to [[Justa]]. After his sudden departure from Merr-Sonn his long time business partner and Chief Operating Officer, [[Berek Laron]], took the helm. He survived his late brother, [[Kael Denson]].

Insanity with style was the only way to describe the career of '''Aron Melnick'''. One of the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]]'s best tacticians, he was accredited with winning several engagements which were deemed "lost before they started" by Naval High Command.

Growing up as a pilot with his fathers' shipping company, he quickly joined the [[Imperial Navy]] after the death of his father at the hands of the [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Rebels]] during a raid on an outpost. Suffering quite a few injuries during his commands, he was unaffectionately known to his subordinates as the tin man due to many cybernetic implants being required for him to continue his duties in the Galactic Empire.

Following being overlooked for promotion to Admiral, his mental state degraded even further, helping quietly help organize the rebellion of [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]] loyalists against [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Bacharan Valak|Valak]]'s regime, in what became known as the [[Vigilant Movement]]. While many officers whom he trained aided with his call to arms, the insurgency was quickly and violently crushed under the weight of the restructured Imperial Navy. It was believed that Melnick was executed for treason around the same time as his long time friend [[Thradash Javert]] was brought before the interrogators of [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]], but again, the Empire continues to leave this matter as history that should not be remembered.

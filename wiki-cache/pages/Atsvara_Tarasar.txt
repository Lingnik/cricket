Atsvara Tarasar ([[22 ABY]] - ) is a [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] business woman who serves as the Chief Executive Officer of the [[Golden Bantha Group]] and extension of that role she has also recently become the Chief Executive Officer of [[Biscuit Baron]]. She is the only daughter of Caspian [[Presav]] and [[New Sith Order|Sith]] [[Duke]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] and his wife. For her tenth birthday she was elevated to the title of [[Baron|Baroness]] within the [[House Dragonflower]]. Having been given temporary command of the Crestian's personal warship, the ''[[Leviathan]]'', during a recently field operation she now holds the commission of a [[Captain]] in the House Dragonflower and by extension in the [[Caspian Navy]].

== Early Life ==

Atsvara was born on [[Silver Station]] during the years her parents spent keeping a relatively low profile in the [[Dragonflower Nebula]] following the disappearance of [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] and the collapse of the [[New Sith Order]]. Her father [[Crestian Tarasar]] (a [[Sarian]]) was a [[Duke]] and [[Admiral]] in the New Sith Order, while her mother, his wife, is [[Firrerreo]]. Though both [[Near-Human]] species, their rather unique genetics made for a challenging conception and pregnancy, but the end result was a child capable of rapid healing and particularly efficient internal organs. She was given the name Atsvara which is the Sith word for balance, which was a concept that holds great meaning for her father. In keeping with Firrerreo tradition and superstition about the power one might have over another if they knew their name, Atsvara may not properly be considered her true name. Her true name was given to her by her mother, and it is rumoured that even her father does not know it, nor wishes to, feeling his daughter deserves the choice if she wants to give him that perceived power.

Given the climate facing those associated with the New Sith Order at the time of birth and during her early years, she grew up almost entirely within the confines of the Dragonflower Nebula between Silver Station on its edge and her father's flagship, the ''[[Leviathan]]'' hidden deeper inside the nebula. In spite of the relative seclusion from the wider galaxy, she was not without a community as the combined strength of her father's remaining personnel and their families would often number between three to four thousand people and [[Droid|droids]]. 

[[Image:Atsvara Comic 1.jpg|left|thumb]] Death or retirement among Crestian's crew during this time would most often result in a Droid taking their place, making their presence ubiquitous within their operations. Treated as equals among the crew, including in terms of ranks and pay, this would impress upon Atsvara the belief that Droids were equal to biological beings. She became particularly attached to [[V1C3]], her father's adjutant and bodyguard. As Crestian had built Vee-Won around the time of Atsvara's own birth she would later consider him to be her twin brother.

[[Image:Atsvara Comic 2.jpg|thumb]] The seclusion of their circumstances and the absence of an extant Sith power to support meant Atsvara was far from the only child born amongst the crew, as they settled down and started families in their exile. The number of younglings was even sufficient to warrant a formal educational institution: Miss Cason's School for Sith Spawn. In addition to the standard education curriculum, students were taught the Sith philosophy Crestian and his crew ascribed to. As Sith they believed in their right to exercise their will upon the galaxy, but this was tempered by the belief that that will need not necessarily be for 'evil' ends. 

Protection of one's family and crewmates were values that were lauded there. Atsvara's own name was reflection of the concept most important to Crestian, that of balance. As Sith they had fought for domination but they were also people who deeply cared about their own and sought make a better galaxy for them.

[[Image:Atsvara Jolly Meal Toy.jpg|left|thumb]] The nebula was not without its advantages to Atsvara and her family. As an excellent source of prothium gas used in blasters, the collection and refinement of the gas provided their operations with sufficient income to support their operations and lifestyle. Silver Station would also function as a minor shadowport allowing goods from the wider galaxy to make their way into her world. Atsvara's most prized possession in these years was her collection of [[:starwars:Jolly Meal|Jolly Meal]] toys from [[Biscuit Baron]]. Her father made sure this was collection was complete, containing every toy going back to their initial inception back in [[22 BBY]].

== Out of Exile ==

Following the Sariphage pandemic and the Mergani pogroms and his role combating them and later the [[Caspian Navy]] at [[Mergansar]], Crestian returned to his family home in [[Trinumvira]] on [[Caspar]] after the dust had settled. After several weeks, once he was reasonably certain it was relatively safe for his family, Atsvara and her mother would join him at Vila Tarasar. This would be Atsvara's first opportunity to spend any extended time on a planet, let alone actually live on one. She would soon grow comfortable in Trinumvira and make new friends such as [[Veruca Violet]], the ward of her father's friend [[Zubindi Hakoon]].

Crestian's election as [[Presav]] of the [[Caspian Union]] would provide Atsvara even more opportunities. Atsvara can often be found travelling with her father on his trips outside of Caspian space and making connexions with his associates. She has taken a particular interest in his friend [[Elana Tracer]], a former Caspian Presav and current [[Grand Moff]] of [[Selene]] and [[Athaniss]]. Atsvara has expressed an interest in following in 'Aunt' Elana's footsteps as Swoop Racer and Moff, a fairly reasonable goal for the daughter of a Sith noble who now serves as a head of state. These goals, however, soon took a backseat to the world of business.

[[Image:Atsvara Pulp Art.jpg|right|thumb]] At some point in her youth Atsvara hacked the finances of Dragonflower Enterprises in order to siphon of some credits to pay a dummy corporation which was in fact really just her own personal account. Coming from a Sith background, her father was proud of her ingenuity, and allowed her to keep the money, pretending he knew nothing about it. With her 'own' capital to work with Atsvara would begin to set up a variety of business meetings for herself, often timed to make use of her father's off world trips. In particular though her love of Jolly Meal toys would translate into an interest in Biscuit Baron itself and she soon was befriending executives there, including [[:starwars:Tagge Company/Legends|Tagge Company]] Vice President of Marketing [[Izara Tagge]].

== Biscuit Baron ==

When Biscuit Baron began to face widespread labour issues in [[32 ABY]], Atsvara saw an opportunity to utilise her wealth and her connexions within Biscuit Baron to take over the restaurant chain. Secretly working with confederates among labour leaders, politicians, journalists, and within Biscuit Baron itself she sought to widen the wedge between the workers unions and the company in order to temporarily devalue it to drive the [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] to be willing to sell it off. Though she truly appreciated the company and its employees she knew if she could take hold of it she would be able to deliver labour contracts that would be more beneficial to the employees and that she had the connexions necessary to restore any lost value post-takeover.

As part of her plan to convince CSA to divest itself of Biscuit Baron, she secretly reached out to some former CSA officials she felt would be interested in breaking up the CSA as a single merged corporation and return its constituent parts to an independent status. In order to communicate anonymously she took inspiration from her stuffed [[:starwars:Bantha/Legends|bantha]] and Biscuit Baron's [[:starwars:Bantha Breakfast Biscuit|Bantha Breakfast Biscuit]] and communicate with a golden bantha avatar. When these plans failed to generate a favourable response she temporarily abandoned plans for a buyout, instead shifting to the formation of a firm, [[Golden Bantha Group]], which she could convince Biscuit Baron to hire to navigate their numerous strikes across multiple jurisdictions. As she had collaborators on both sides of the labour dispute she was able to engineer the terms necessary to successfully bring a resolution to the stalled negotiations.

[[Image:Atsvara Japanese.jpg|right|thumb]] Serving as a consultancy firm was only a temporary measure for Atsvara who sought to buyout Biscuit Baron outright. To that end she recruited other investors into her plans to raise the capital necessary for a serious bid. The investors would largely fall into two categories, the children of her father's friends and their Droids. Chief among these would be [[Veruca Violet]] who was herself independently wealthy having inherited considerable property and [[Cricket]], the Droid of [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]], who was another notable embezzler.

There would be a few organic investors who would be slightly older than Atsvara's peer group such as Caspian Justice Minister [[Sha Dark]] and Izara Tagge whose stake in Golden Bantha Group would continue the Tagge family's involvement with the franchise. After many months working behind the scenes and negotiating with the [[Direx Board (CSA)|Direx Board]] to [[Report:Biscuit Buyout|buyout]] the restaurant chain, the Golden Bantha Group became sole owners of Biscuit Baron in [[33 ABY]]. At that time major stockholders, who had previously been secret, were publicly identified and took over as the new board of directors of the restaurant chain.

== Trivia ==

[[Image:Atsvara Pixel.jpg|right|thumb]]
* Atsvara is a passionate advocate for the [[:starwars:Droid Rights (movement)|Droid Rights movement]] and it is widely suspected among her family and friends that she will one day instigate an armed revolution to secure droid equality.
* Between her relocation to [[Caspar]] and her official involvement in Biscuit Baron, she would routinely show up with Jolly Meal toys that had not been released yet. This was an early hint of her personal ties to Biscuit Baron's corporate officers and her later plans for the corporation.
* Atsvara was denied security clearance by the [[Caspian Intelligence Service]] for four years, not because she was a child, but because of questions of her loyalty to the [[Caspian Union|Union]], criminal behaviour, and support of extremist groups. In spite of a lack of official clearance, Atsvara was constantly in possession of forged credentials and had strong personnel ties to many people who did have top clearance levels.
* For her tenth birthday [[Crestian Tarasar|Crestian]] gave Atsvara the title of [[Baron|Baroness]] as the heir of [[House Dragonflower]]. The choice of title was chosen with the knowledge she was manipulating the Biscuit Baron strikes with an aim to take over, thus Crestian was setting her up to be a Baroness for future branding purposes.
* Other birthday gifts included being given a starfighter by [[Johanna Siri te Danaan|Johanna]] and banthas by [[Veruca Violet]]
* Upon being elevated to Baroness, Crestian considered Atsvara to functionally be an adult and gave her the use of his old [[CR90 Corvette]], the ''Argent Drake'', in order for her to conduct business in the galaxy more effectively on her own. She is still under the protection of [[The Chevalier]] as the daughter of the [[Presav]].
* In spite of being too young to even vote, Atsvara maintains strong business and political ties to many members of the [[Union Council]] and various foreign and domestic leaders. 
* A recent mission saw the ''[[Leviathan]]'' deployed in the field without Crestian. As a House Dragonflower ship, he put Atsvara in command in his stead, granting her a commission as a [[Captain]] in his forces. As part of the agreement which seconds his vessels to the [[Caspian Navy]], her commission carried over, which finally has given her clearance of her own.
* At the [[:starwars:Arkanis sector/Legends|Arkanis Sector]] Children's Relief Fundraiser and Ball, held at the [[Royale Resorts|Royale Hotel]] on [[Corellia]], Atsvara donated a Commemorative [[:starwars:Holocube/Legends|Holocube]] of [[Jedi Master]] [[:starwars:Ikrit|Ikrit]] from [[Biscuit Baron|Biscuit Baron's]] "Heroes of the Republic" shortly before the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]].
* At that same charity auction, Atsvara bought a [[:starwars:Nek/Legends|Nek Battle Dog]] which she has named Varpa, which is Sith for "Spike."
* She is fluent in [[Galactic Basic Standard]], [[Binary]], Firrerreo, Mando'a, [[Sarian (language)|Sarian]], [[Shyriiwook]], and Sith

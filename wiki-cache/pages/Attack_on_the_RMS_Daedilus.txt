The '''Attack on the RMS ''Daedilus''''' was a minor engagement fought between the [[Galactic Empire]]'s famed [[Black Stars]] squadron and the [[New Republic]]'s [[Republic Manufacturing and Shipping|RMS]]. The attack was devised as part of a campaign to undermine the RMS and New Republic merchant operations by [[General]] [[Vorn Paro]] of the [[Imperial Security Bureau]]. Previous operations by Paro had instilled the Black Stars as a feared force by pirates and merchants, and Paro would use this growing reputation to strike at RMS.

The [[ICRV Admiral Canis|HIMS ''Admiral Canis'']] assaulted the [[RMS Daedilus|RMS ''Daedilus'']] head-on, deploying the five [[TIE Interceptor]]s of the Black Stars. The five starfighters and the ''Admiral Canis'' were able to fight the ''Daedilus'' and halt it from escaping to hyperspace; the ''Canis'' then deployed her [[Stormtrooper]]s led by [[Camrath Kizuka]] into the ''Daedilus'', where they quickly seized control of the frigate. 

After tense negotiations with [[Daana Roon]], [[Danik Kreldin]], head of the operation, allowed the ''Daedilus'' to depart, but not before attaching a tracking beacon to the hull of the vessel to be used in further operations.

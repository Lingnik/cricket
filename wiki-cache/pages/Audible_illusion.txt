{{forcepower|
power=Audible illusion|
}}
Make pops, beeps, the sound of air moving or a wave in water. This power has a range component and may be more or less difficult depending on distance. Volume is generally moderate, about the strength of a normal human voice. Sounds lasting beyond a second or so are difficult. Complicated multi-part sounds may be possible by very experienced Jedi, though rumors exist of Jedi who have used this power to throw their voices or create audible speaking illusions. This is actual sound, not a mental manipulation.

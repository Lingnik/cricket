The '''Bank of the Core''' is one of the [[The Galaxy|galaxy]]'s largest banking institutions. It is currently chaired by [[Peshk Vry'lya]]. The Bank of the Core is a neutral bank, dealing with all parties, unlike the [[First Bank of the New Republic]] or any of the nationalized banks of the [[Galactic Empire]].

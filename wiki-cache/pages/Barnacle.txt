'''Barnacles''' are small crustaceans known for stubbornly clinging to surfaces such as the hulls of aquatic vessels, the skin of starships, or the walls of buildings. A variety of species can be found on many worlds, including [[:starwars:Bilbringi VII|Bilbringi VII]]'s [[:starwars:Crystal barnacle|crystal barnacle]]s and [[Coruscant]]'s [[:starwars:Shadow barnacle|shadow barnacle]]s. More typical aquatic barnacles include those found on waterworlds such as [[:starwars:Drexel II|Drexel II]], [[Dac]], or [[:starwars:Manaan|Manaan]]. A rare few were ambulatory, such as the [[:starwars:Walking barnacle|walking barnacle]]s of [[:starwars:Alderaan|Alderaan]].

'''Baron''' is a noble title used by various planets and galactic powers. The female variant is '''Baroness''', which may either be someone holding the title in their own right, or the wife of a baron. The title of Baron-Administrator was also used in some jurisdictions such as [[Cloud City]] and [[:starwars:Thee Wheel/Legends|The Wheel]] for chief civic leader. Barons typically rank below Viscounts and are often the lowest title of nobility, though certain hereditary chieftains or knights may be considered as nobles in some places, while ranked below Barons.

A '''Barracuda-series Plant-signature Seeking Warhead''' is a homing concussion missile used for ship-to-ship combat. It was designed and produced by [[StarShield]] Industries with the assistance of [[CDMC]] and is currently manufactured by two contracted firms located in the Outer Rim.

Currently, the Barracuda is fielded in two different scales &mdash; a capital ship-scale variant used heavily by the [[Caspian Navy]] in their [[VOWL]]-equipped cruisers and frigates as an all-purpose "smart" weapon, and as a fighter-scale version for use in [[FOWL]]-equipped fighters and bombers. It is best employed as an anti-starcraft weapon, but can be directed against any installation or structure that possesses a powerplant signature. The host craft's sensor and targeting system will calculate a solution based on the target's generated harmonic wave, and will pass it on to the missile's onboard guidance computer. Once fired, the missile will track that solution for several thousand kilometers until detonated, countered, or exhausted.

=== Variants ===
* '''Barracuda V-C''' - Fifth series of capital-scale warhead. As a replacement for the IV-C, it boasts a much more nimble maneuvering package so as to allow Union starships to combat small, fast-moving raider ships.

* '''Barracuda III-S''' - Third generation of fighter-scale warhead. Smaller than its capital-scale sibling by three meters and eighty kilograms, but ideally suited for Union starfighters to combat a wide variety of opponents. Enables an attacking fighter, such as the entire Z-series fighter line, to engage an opponent, fire, and then maneuver to deal with the next.

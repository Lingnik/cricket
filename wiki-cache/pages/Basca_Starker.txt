Native born to the Corporate Sector Authority, Basca spent most of his early years travelling from world to world. As the family trailed along the various contracts and duties held by his father. As such the young Basca never developed roots to any given world. Evolving for the most part into a dilettante. Surfing galleries and events all over the Sector Authority and abroad, but his keen mind needed a challenge. In time technology and biology caught his attention and he attended various universities and was educated by private tutors. eventually specializing in Genetic sciences and developed an interest longevity theories. As such when most meet him they see a man in his twenties, when his fifties are a more accurate measure of his age. Leading many to under estimate him in most situations.

=<u>Into the Empire</u>=

Eventually the aspiring hedonist would come to the attention of the Select Committee who would approach him for a covert project that his talents appeared to be ideal for and was recruited into COMPNOR. In the process he encountered Zeak Oppenhiemer and an enigmatic woman called simply W. The nature of the project would lead him into an effort to try and grasp the genetic nature of creatures touched by the force and how one might bridge the gap between none sensitives and sensitives. The end goal of the project was to find a means to neutralize the force or create a connection through genetic manipulation. It became clear to Basca that there was a fundamental gap between the existing rules of science in the galaxy and the actual mechanism of the Force.

==Emperor Vadim==

Basca ever the brash and challenging person, opted to take a risk and approach the Emperor himself. The two entered into a protracted conversation on the nature of the force. The fact that this nobody of a person had found the cracks to approach the Emperor directly perhaps amused the Emperor briefly enough to allow the conversation. Something about Starkers nature prompted the Emperor to task the human to use his skills in a project. The Emperor commanded Starker to create a plague that would kill off the population of the planet Caspar. The Doctor relished the task presented him. In months to come the reward for the creation of what would become known as the Sariphage would surprise the brash man and seal his fate or fortunes to come.
=='''Tarchanjan Genetics'''==
Unknown to the rest of the galaxy is the control and direction of a biotechnology firm that sits far from the limelight of the galaxy. Yet finds itself in the halls of the rich and opulent all over the galaxy. Producers of a product called Neoskin, the firm is the leading developer of longevity technology and products. The firm is entirely mobile and has no holdings planet side in any of the major factions of the galaxy and is one of the sources of income for the system hopping dilettante.

The '''''Battle Hymn of the Black Stars''''' was a military hymn performed by His Majesty's Own in the [[Galactic Empire]], out of respect and admiration for the elite Imperial fighter unit, the [[Black Stars]].

It was a powerful hymn, bellicose and inspiring - for Imperials. The hymn is still performed today, despite the retirement of the Black Stars unit.

==Behind the Scenes==
The ''Battle Hymn of the Black Stars'' can be played on [[Selene]], at the [[Spaceline Restaurant]] in [[Imperial City]], performed by the coded band object, Dynamite Dyll and the Dissonant Duo, courtesy of [[Vorn Paro]].

Unfortunately, there are no real lyrics. Yet.

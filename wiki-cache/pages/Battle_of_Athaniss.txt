With [[Dac|Mon Calamari]] secured by the forces of [[Palpatine (imposter)|Palpatine]], the man claiming to be none other than the true Galactic Emperor set his eye on the backwater [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] planet of [[Athaniss]].

The Empire had predicted an attack on the planet, having gained valuable intelligence. The ''Tormentor'' was sent to reinforce the planet, but despite the best efforts of the Imperials, the planet fell to Palpatine and his "rebel" army.

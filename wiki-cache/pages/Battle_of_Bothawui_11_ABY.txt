Fought almost immediately after the [[Fourth Battle of Coruscant]], the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]], wishing to follow up on its great victory at [[Coruscant]], deployed its main battle fleet from Coruscant to [[Bothawui]], with the great flagship, the ''Dark Eminence'', at the front of the charge.

The Imperials, however, were met with tougher resistance from the [[Bothan|Bothans]] than expected, and a surprisingly heavy amount of damage was inflicted upon the ''Dark Eminence''. With the battle going nowhere, the Empire ordered a withdrawal from the system.

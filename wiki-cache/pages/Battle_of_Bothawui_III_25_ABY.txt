== Overview ==
The third engagement of the Battle of Bothawui took place immediately following the Republic capture of Drev'starn City.

Carrying two battalions of Stormtroopers from the 92nd Legion, a full regiment of the fearsome [[203rd Legion|203rd]] and five regiments of the Imperial Army, the Imperial fleet carried an force large enough to crush the Republic invasion and pacify the wayward planet. All that remained was to safely deploy the massive force to the planet's surface.

== Background ==
Having failed to dislodge the Republic fleet from its defensive position over Drev'starn during the battle, the Imperial battle squadron purposefully held position at extreme sensor range while undertaking repairs. By menacing  the Republic's foothold, Admiral Fields was able to prevent the Republic from rapidly spreading its forces afield.

== Progression ==
Ordering the Imperial battle squadron out of sensor range, Fields hoped to execute a short-ranged hyperspace jump beyond the Republic's awareness and use the curvature of the planet to conceal the Imperial landing.

The quick reaction of Lieutenant-Commander Argon Brand, who assumed command of a light squadron and gave pursuit forced Fields to redeploy the Executioner and several picket ships to fight a rear guard action.

The Executioner and its escorts successfully delayed the Republic fleet long enough for Fields to execute his maneuver. A daring series of close-range maneuvers by the Brand's forces crippled the Executioner and destroyed her escorts. Its hyperdrive damaged and faced with certain annihilation in a continued fire fight, Captain Shaw accepted Republic terms of surrender.

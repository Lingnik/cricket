The '''Battle of Dreven''' was the final confrontation within the [[Galactic Empire]] between [[Lorn Rhys]] and forces loyal to the apprehended [[Kendal Osbourne]]. With [[Battle of Selene|victory]] at [[Selene]], Rhys set his forces upon the old Imperial throne world of [[Dreven]], where resided many Imperial nobles and the last remnants of any organized force loyal to Osbourne.

The battle was short and decisive; Osbourne's forces had very little chance for victory. Their fleet was utterly destroyed and any organized ground resistance was swept away with orbital bombardments. Nobles were also targeted in the bombardments, killing many in the Imperial Court. 

With the conclusion of the battle, Rhys remained unopposed and his hold on the throne was secured. He declared himself [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] and made residence on Dreven's [[Castle Tsabak]].

After the battle, the [[War of the Throne]] would continue to propagate. Rhys himself would be supplanted sometime after the battle at Dreven by [[Sinane Pantara|Darth Pain]], apparently killed on board his flagship the ''[[I2SD Sovereign|Sovereign]]''.

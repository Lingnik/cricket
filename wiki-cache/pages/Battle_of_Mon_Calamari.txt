[[Dac|Mon Calamari]] was the third major engagement in [[Bacharan Valak|Emperor Valak's]] [[Great Campaign]] and a major Imperial victory.

The [[New Republic]] maintained powerful defenses but were unable to prevent the [[Death Star III|Death Star]] from destroying the [[Mon Calamari Shipyards|shipyards]], inflicting massive damage on the defending fleet and [[Dac]] itself before securing the planet.

The loss of Mon Calamari, headquarters of the Republic Navy, was a huge blow to both Republic morale and strategic assets.

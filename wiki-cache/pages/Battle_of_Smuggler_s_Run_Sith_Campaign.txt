The '''Battle of Smuggler's Run''' was the result of [[Undula the Hutt]]'s betrayal of the [[New Sith Order]] in late [[17 ABY]]. Undula, running his own crime syndicate out of the Run, had affiliated himself with [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] and his Sith Order, but soon saw potential in betraying the Sith. This short-sightedness led to his quick assassination; the Sith stormed [[Smuggler's Run]], detonating a thermal detonator within Undula's throne room, making quick work of him and his cartel. The rest of the asteroid was seized by the Sith; any who got in the way of the Sith were ruthlessly killed, innocent or not. Smuggler's Run was turned over to the Sith for its own purposes.

Epifania Beatrix Marcutia Faia Masura-Mikora Quirinus Cassimar no Vara XIV, in common usage Beatrix Vara, is the principal and CEO of Viridis Incorporated, the owner of [[Sayo Station]], a member of the [[Direx_Board_(CSA)|Direx Board]] of the [[Corporate_Sector_Authority|CSA]], and the uncrowned [[King|Queen]] of [[Vara]].

'''Benjamin W. Rastus''' was a member of the '''Old Guard''', the group of [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] officers that were educated and trained in the [[:starwars:Galactic_Republic/Legends|Old Republic]] Naval Academy. He was still versed in the customs and etiquette of the Old Republic during his service in the [[Imperial Navy]], though he followed the Empire with complete loyalty, believing it to be the legitimate heir to the old [[:starwars:Galactic_Senate/Legends|Senate]]. Though it was rumored that he questioned or even disagreed with some of the ideals of the [[New Order]], there was no doubt he was solidly behind it. Even without his distinguished combat record, he was well respected for his excellent training of younger officers; many of the Empire's finest commanding officers began their career under Rastus's command. 

With his unique command style, which could best be described as quiet but extremely efficient; he easily was able to attain the rank of Vice Admiral following the retirement of Grand Admiral [[Morlok Adlerson]]; and then reassigned as the Empire's Chief of Naval Operations.  A post which he used to maintain the reorganized Navy as an effective, and distinguished fighting force.  He held the position for several years before retiring to a quiet life somewhere in the Galaxy.

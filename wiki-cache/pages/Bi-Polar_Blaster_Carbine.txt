TITLE: Bi-Polar Blaster Carbine
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2006-01-15T23:30:31Z
======================================================================
The main selling point of this blaster is its phenomenal firepower. Its 
alternately charged barrels deliver devastating amounts of energy, with 
reasonable accuracy, and high rate of fire... when the weapon actually works.
Power packs are consumed at a tremendous rate to keep this blaster firing. 
Theoretically, one could hook it to a larger belt or backpack mounted power-
pack to permit longer periods sustained fire. Unfortunately, the cooling 
system is not up to the task of dissipating the heat produced by prolonged,
rapid fire. The hot exhaust plume produced in such instances is a dead 
giveaway of one's position when fighting enemies with thermal optics, and may
be sufficient to burn the firer. Frequent maintenance is required to keep a 
bi-polar in working condition in the field.

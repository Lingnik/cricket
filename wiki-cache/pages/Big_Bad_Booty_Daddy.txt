This simple vessel is the primary means of transportation for the famous starship technician, [[Bael]], who utilized this vessel for most of his time away from the [[w:c:starwars:Roche_Asteroids|Roche Asteroids]] since [[11 ABY]].  Originally owned by [[Trameine Walker]], [[Bael]] purchased and heavily modified this vessel to transport [[X-23 Starworker]]s and expand his trading consortium.  A simple vessel at most, this variant of a [[Corellian Action VI Transport]] contains a large hangar/cargo space and a bridge, with scarce crew quarters meeting only the simplest of needs.  It is readily associated with the master [[Verpine]] tech and as such has avoided many Imperial entanglements.

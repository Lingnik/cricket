TITLE: Binary
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2007-04-13T12:41:57Z
======================================================================
Also known as 'Droidspeak', Binary is a codifying of the chirps, beeps, whistles and bleeps emitted by non-vocalizer equipped droids allowing them to audially communicate with sentients and one another. Techs and others who work around droids a lot can learn to parse it into words and phrases, though in execution it's more a series of sounds conveying meaning rather than specifics.

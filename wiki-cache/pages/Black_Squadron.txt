'''Black Squadron''' is a TIE Interceptor squadron assigned to the [[I2SD Inquisitor|HIMS ''Inquisitor'']].


== History ==

=== Origins ===
'''Black Squadron''' was founded by [[starwars:Anakin_Skywalker/Legends|Darth Vader]] as an elite TIE fighter squadron only months before the [[starwars:Battle_of_Yavin/Legends|Battle of Yavin]]. They were assigned as one of the many squadrons to the [[starwars:DS-1 Orbital Battle Station|Death Star]].  The squadron flew the venerable [[TIE/ln Fighter|TIE Fighter]]

=== The Battle of Yavin ===
During the Battle, Black Squadron were the only fighter squadron to see combat. While serving under the direct command of  Darth Vader, the squadron fought valiantly but ultimately they were wiped out.

=== Reformation ===
In [[15 ABY]], [[Tyler Damion]] would activate the squadron INSFC-136 and give it the Black Squadron designation. Commander [[Seifer Wolf]]  was given command of the squadron and assigned to duty on the [[ISC Broadsword|HIMS ''Broadsword'']]''. ''

=== The [[Retribution of Coruscant]] ===

Black Squadron was the first to enter the field and last to leave it. They served with  [[War Shrike Squadron]] as the vanguard of the Imperial Attack. and the HIMS ''Broadsword''. During the battle, Grand Admiral [[Danik Kreldin|Kreldin]] took personal command of the squadron. With the destruction of Kreldin's fighter, Alpha flight lead by Wolf with [[Alex Dante]] in Black 2 and [[Sarai Litve]] in Black 3  began decimating the remaining Republic fighters in retaliation. This emboldened Imperial forces to make the final push, sending the Republic Navy in retreat. 

== Current Assignment ==
After [[Coruscant]] was back under Imperial control, Black Squadron was transferred to [[I2SD Inquisitor|HIMS ''Inquisitor'']] under orders from  [[Vassily Korolov|Darth Venger]] where it joined [[Task Force Inquisitor]]. Black Squadron was designated as Venger's personal escort, it's pilots selected for skill as well as loyalty.

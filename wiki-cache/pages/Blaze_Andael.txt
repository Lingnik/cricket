'''Blaze Andael''' was a [[New Republic]] starfighter pilot, who joined the military at the age of eighteen and worked her way up to the rank of Captain and CO of [[Ghost Squadron]]. She was born on [[Corellia]], and did some work as a smuggler and bounty hunter before settling down in the military. She served with disctinction throughout many battles in the [[Galactic Civil War]], though she eventually retired in [[8 ABY]].

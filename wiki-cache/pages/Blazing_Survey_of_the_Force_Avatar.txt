The Force User capable of this power has tapped into the essence of the Force itself, and is capable of pouring pure energy from her eyes or ocular organs. The type and appearance of this energy vary depending on the Jedi's particular alignment (Jedi or Sith). The victim of this power is completely consumed by the Force, wracked with awesome pain that immolates the subject from the inside out. Only a pile of fine dust remains. The user of this power suffers a great amount of "recoil damage" as using one's body as a conduit for this much energy is dangerous. Using this power almost always kills (but doesn't disintegrate) the Jedi who is using it as well as the victim, but there are times when that sacrifice may be called for. This power requires "Absorb Energy" as a prerequisite.

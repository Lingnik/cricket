=== Overview ===
The ''Blue Dwarf '' is built primarily for one thing -- speed.  A heavily modified and customized scout vessel with a focus on speed and maneuverability over cargo space or offensive/defensive capabilities.

=== History ===
As part of the retainer paid to her, [[Qadirah, Clan Demargo|Qadirah]] was given the ship by [[Nasiri Zithreej|Nasiri]], something of a "lord" amongst the less-than-legal denizens of [[Nar Shaddaa]].  Almost immediately, Qadirah began tinkering with it, adding her clan's emblem, looking at the innards, and so on.

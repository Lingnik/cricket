Dr. Blulurbgloblurb Druze: he doesn't ask Humans to attempt his first name. A [[Quarren]] native of [[Dac]], a series of amusing misfortunes led him to spend his residency and early career in Hutt space, where he got tangled up with a small-time arm of the Cartel. Fleeing for his life following the inevitable violent end to a capo's personal ambition before he ended up chum in someone's bucket, he begged Captain [[Tiaryn Elatar]] for sanctuary and anonymity in exchange for becoming the Chief Engineer and Chief Medical Officer on her ship, ''Bright Star''.

He's been working with her crew and the [[DSV Templar|Templar]] "paramilitary samaritan" outfit on a number of anti-piracy and anti-slavery operations.

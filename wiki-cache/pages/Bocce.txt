======================================================================
Bocce is a trader jargon developed as a means for the highly diverse species of pilots and their crews to communicate. Its development is attributed to the Baobab merchants but might well be rooted in the antiquity of space travel. Drawing heavily on [[Galactic Basic Standard|Galactic Basic]] some words and phrases sound not-unfamiliar, even to those who haven't heard it before.

Beyond its use to facilitate the lives of spacers, Bocce has gained legitimacy as a communications form by being one of the official languages of Albarrio, the sector capital world of Aris.

{{title|''Boomstick''}}

The '''''Boomstick''''' is a heavily modified [[Skipray Blastboat]] owned by [[Axel Vichten]].  The ship gained notoriety during [[Asteroid Kappa]]'s critical days, when Axel engaged the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] fleet attacking the system.  The use of the ''Boomstick'' to lure the famous [[Black Stars]] Squadron away from the Asteroid allowed the cumbersome base to make a trip into hyperspace.  After that battle, the ''Boomstick'' made several more appearances against the Black Stars and their commander, [[Danik Kreldin]].

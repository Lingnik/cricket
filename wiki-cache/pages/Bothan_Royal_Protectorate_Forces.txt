The '''Bothan Royal Protectorate Forces''' were the military arm of the [[Bothan Federation]], divided into the Army and Navy. The BRPF was small compared to even the [[Caspian Defence Force]], but was still capable of securing its borders.

When the Federation was annexed in [[14 ABY]], the Protectorate Forces remained fully intact and allowed to secure their own borders, although [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] surveyors were assigned to Bothan units to ensure loyalty.

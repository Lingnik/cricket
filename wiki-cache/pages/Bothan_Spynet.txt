[[Image:200px-BothanMissionBriefing.JPG|thumb|200px|right|A member of the Bothan Spynet briefs [[:starwars:Rebel Alliance|Alliance]] personnel on the inner workings of an [[Imperial Star Destroyer]]]]As an extension of the [[Bothan Way]], the '''Bothan Spynet''' was developed circa [http://starwars.wikicities.com/wiki/300_BBY 300 BBY] as a means to collect intelligence on potential political enemies, which to [[Bothan]]s, includes all individuals, species and governments. The Spynet consisted of thousands of spies, informants and data collection [[droids]] that fed into a centralized command structure. 

Together its military component, [[:starwars:Martial Intelligence Division|Bothan Military Intelligence]], the Spynet was the most comprehensive and thorough intelligence outfit in the galaxy. Serving Bothan interests first and selling its services second, the Spynet, under the leadership of [[:starwars:Koth Melan|Koth Melan]], proved crucial to the [[:starwars:Rebel Alliance|Rebel Alliance]] in providing information on [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] military plans, commerce shipping schedules and personnel intelligence, although their services were also bought by the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] and [[Underworld|Fringe]] elements. Their usefulness culminated in the capture of plans for the [http://starwars.wikicities.com/wiki/Death_Star_II second Death Star]. After the Galactic Civil War, the Spynet continued to operate independently of [[New Republic Intelligence]] until the year [[11 ABY]]. [[Aknar Koth'lyri]], a Bothan with considerable political power on Bothawui due to his family ties, campaigned and sucessfully managed to incorporate certain sections of the '''Bothan Spynet''' with [[New Republic Intelligence]]. Subsequently [[Aknar Koth'lyri|Koth'lyri]], with the political help of then [[Chief of State]] [[Peshk Vry'lya]], was appointed Director of [[New Republic Intelligence|NRI]]. He served as director from [[11 ABY]]-[[12 ABY]] where he took a two year leave of absence. During this time, prominent figures such as [[Talon Karrde]] and [[Lando Calrissian]] filled his post. Near the end of the year, [[14 ABY]] [[Aknar Koth'lyri|Koth'lyri]] resumed the Directorship and Chief of the Bothan Spynet. From [[14 ABY]] to [[17 ABY]], Aknar was content to allow his large network of deputies to manage and run the day-to-day operations of the Bothan Spynet. Rumblings are that, more recently, he has begun to take an active role once more.     [[Image:180px-BOT.jpg|thumb|left|Two [[Bothan]] spies]]

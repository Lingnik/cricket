'''Brackett Benduday''' ([[2 BBY]] - ) is an anchor for [[InterGalactic News]] and is frequently utilised for broadcasts concerning news from the [[Caspian Union]] and political and economic news more generally. She is also the host of the financial programme the [[Muun Money Minute]].

==Background==

Brackett Benduday was born on the resort world of Ord Cantrell at the onset of the [[Galactic Civil War]]. Her family had been off-world when the [[Galactic Empire]] had unleashed the [[:starwars:Hive virus|Hive Virus]] on her people's homeworld of [[:starwars:Firrerre/Legends|Firrerre]] largely [[:starwars:Firrerreo Genocide|wiping out]] her people. The name Brackett Benduday can be considered as stage name as it is traditional for [[Firrerreo]] to not share their true name.

Brackett has a degree in journalism from the [[:starwars:University of Commenor|University of Commenor]] and economics from [[:starwars:Corulag University of Economics|Corulag University of Economics]]. Her father is an economist as well. She is an adjunct faculty member at [[:starwars:Corellia University/Legends|Corellia University]]. She is known for intellectual brilliance but awkward in her social life.

==InterGalactic News==

Owing to her background in economics and journalism, Brackett has been employed by [[InterGalactic News]] as a financial analyst for many years. In [[27 ABY]] she was given her own daily segment called the "[[Muun Money Minute]]," the name being a gimmick as it has no association with [[:starwars:Muun/Legends|Muuns]], [[:starwars:Muunilinst/Legends|Muunilinst]], nor is it only a minute.

When IGN was preparing coverage of the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] political situation in [[29 ABY]] as a new constitution was being negotiated by the Provisional Assembly and elections soon to follow, it was not considered the most desirable posting. Between the Sariphage pandemic and Mergani pogroms, the [[Caspia|Caspia System]] seemed almost haunted and many considered its galactic relevance long since past. While others shuddered at the assignment, Brackett herself was a member of a now almost extinct species and took more interest in the system's plight.

[[File:Brackett Benduday.webp|left|thumb]] Brackett has served as main anchor of IGN's Caspian Bureau ever since while continuing to host the Muun Money Minute. Has her profile has grown as an anchor she as also provided notable coverage of other galactic happenings including on [[Bespin]] and [[Corellia]]. She it noted for the intelligence of her broadcasts, interacting with guest experts with ease, and avoiding undue sensationalism and spin. Her coverage has been nominated for awards within the industry.

'''Brigadier''' is a rank used in the [[Union Marines]], it ranks below [[Brigadier General]] and above [[Colonel]]. Caspian Brigadiers typically command smaller or specialised brigades or large or prominent regiments. The rank is also held by staff officers corps and divisions. It is largely equivalent to the rank of [[High Colonel]] in the [[Imperial Army]].

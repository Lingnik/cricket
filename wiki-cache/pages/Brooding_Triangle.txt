The '''Brooding Triangle''' was a region of space defined by the ancient star systems of [[Korriban]], [[:starwars:Thule/Legends|Thule]], and [[Yavin]]. All three figured prominently in the ancient mysteries of the [[Force]], with days-worth of tales telling of the machinations and wars of both the [[Sith]] and [[Jedi]].

While the triangle was rather large, it nonetheless formed a confluence of mysterious power, at least a haunting of such. Thousands of years worth of rage and chaos permeated the climate of the region, and many travelers felt wary of it.

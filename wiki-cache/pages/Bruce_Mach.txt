'''Bruce Mach''' was a reporter for [[InterGalactic News]] stationed on [[Coruscant]] during the [[New Republic]]. In [[14 ABY]] he became famous for his breaking report on [[Danik Kreldin]]'s captivity at the hands of [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]], showing a holofeed of Kreldin apologizing for his actions and denouncing the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]]. Shortly after the story made headlines, Mach disappeared, with rumors abound about a break-in at his Coruscant apartment. He was never seen again.

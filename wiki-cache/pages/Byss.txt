'''Byss''' is a mystery and myth filled world. It is hidden in the very center of the galaxy, a beautiful sphere dotted with lake chains and warmed by blue-green sunlight. [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]] kept a storage of clone bodies there.

Following the advent of the [[Imperium]], Byss has been under the jurisdiction of Imperator [[Tyler Damion|Damion I's]] nephew, [[Grand Moff]] [[Etiel te Danaan]], who is also Grand Moff of [[O'paal]] as well.

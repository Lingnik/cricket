The '''Model C1''' cutlass is the current standard-issue sword of the [[Union Marines|Caspian Marines]], and it's not all for show. [[CDMC]]'s engineers were driven to the brink of madness by the input of dozens of adherents of the Marines' 'cult of cold steel' to produce a weapon of just the right characteristics for the chaos of close-quarters combat. In form it is a short, thick cutting sword with a slightly curved blade, sharpened on the convex-side, with a solid wrap-around handguard and chisel-like point. Its balance is remarkable, making it far easier to learn than a rapier or longsword, and its short length is ideal for use in tight quarters like a starship's corridors. The blade itself is phenomenally tough, made of vacuum-forged alloys, pattern-woven, and reinforced with layers of olumic nanotubes. A vibro-emitter is concealed in the grip, trigged by pressure so that it might easily be activated in combat.

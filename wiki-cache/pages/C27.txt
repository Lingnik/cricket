The [[CDMC]]/Galinolo '''C27 Pulse Carbine''' is a slim, ergonomic carbine developed for the [[Caspian Defence Force]]. With an overall length of 50cm, it incorporates many advanced construction techniques to save on weight and achieve the best performance from such a compact weapon. It possesses a rather radical appearance (ie it's funny-looking), looking as if it consists principally of a shoulderstock and grip with a protruding  muzzle. A foregrip is integrated into the trigger-guard, but the weapon is just the perfect size that one can brace it against the shoulder and fire it one-handed. A stock of advanced composites serves to keep weight down. It is very modular in nature, with accessory rails on either side, and the weapon is very easy to field strip.

The weapon has a very lengthy true-barrel for its size, which extends for most of its overall length. A multi-mode eletro-optical sight (with close combat and 3x magnification settings) is affixed above the stock just behind the muzzle, ideally positioned for instinctive shooting. The sight-mount is factory-zeroed for simplicity, so that there is no need to adjust it in the field. The barrel, emitter, and action actually 'float' inside the stock, and felt recoil is absorbed by the whole package recuperating into the stock when firing. It fires tightly focused plasma pulses which are ideal for burning through armor at ranges up to 250m, but individually lack wounding power. The designer has compensated for this by inclusion of a three-pulse burst setting, which can put most humanoids on the ground with a single pull of the trigger. All of these factors combine to make the weapon quite easy to shoot.

The C27 is commonly used by law enforcement, pilots, vehicle crews and rear area troops. A pod housing can be substituted for the usual stock, allowing the weapon to attach to the forearm hardpoints of the [[Union Marines|Union Marine]] battlesuits.

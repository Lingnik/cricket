A combined force unit of the [[Caspian Navy]], comprising vessels of the [[Caspia|Caspian]], [[Krittain|Krittish]], and [[Yariz|Yari]] navies, as well as forces contributed by [[Rautheran System|Rauther]] and [[Aeschylus]]. The '''Home Fleet''' was commissioned in the eighth year of [[Caspian Crest|The Crest]] and assembled from vessels and units that had previously been in the Union's [[CDU First Fleet|First]] and [[CDU Third Fleet|Third Fleets]]. By consolidating these units into one large, cohesive fleet, [[the Admiralty]] feels that they are better able to respond to threats in an effective manner. Some observers have suggested that this attitude is shaped by the events of the [[Imperial War of Aggression]] and the [[War of Papers]].

The fleet is centered around the carriers [[CMS Typhoon|''Typhoon'']], [[CMS Turbulent|''Turbulent'']], ''Valorous'', and ''Triumph''. The battleships ''Admiral Roj'', ''Repulse'', ''Renown'', and battlecruiser [[CMS Hood|''Hood'']] are fixtures within the fleet as well, surrounded by numerous cruisers, frigates, and sloops. Secondary ships are rotated into and out of the fleet with frequency. Warships on patrol loops, such as [[Shearwater-class Light Cruiser|''Shearwater''-class]] and [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|''Mergansar''-class]] cruisers, are considered part of the Home Fleet as well.

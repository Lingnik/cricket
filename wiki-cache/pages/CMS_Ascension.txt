'''''CMS Ascension''''' refers to a warship in the [[Caspian Navy]]. Two warships in [[Caspian Democratic Union|Union]] history have bore the name.

The first ''Ascension'' was a [[Nebulon-B Frigate]] that was part of [[Admiral]] [[Dergan Roj|Dergan Roj's]] '''[[Barren Sector Fleet]]''' that arrived in [[4 ABY]]. She had been a reliable scout and skirmisher for the Admiral, and had come through the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]] in rather good condition, albeit completely outdated. The ship would receive some nominal repairs and upgrades from [[SubPro]] and [[Lokkai Starworks|Lokkai]], but more attention was paid to the other, more needy ships in Roj's fleet. ''Ascension'' would actually spend the first year of service in the new Navy on constant patrol, first of the [[Caspia|Caspian System]], then other frontier districts.

In [[6 ABY]], she would participate in the first formal encounter between the Caspians and [[Rauther|Rautherans]], and became a frequent courier between the two star systems. However, this would mark ''Ascension's'' last service to the Union, as the [[Hurricane-class Fleet Carrier|Hurricane-Class Fleet Carriers]] were now becoming established, and the [[Shearwater-class Frigate|Shearwater-Class Light Cruisers]] were coming online to conduct frontier patrols. In an exchange for several dozen [[A-wing|A-Wings]] and other technological items (sensor sets, shield generators, etc.), the Union sold ''Ascension'' and her sister frigate [[CMS Redemption|''Redemption'']] to the [[New Republic]].

Several years would pass before ''Ascension'' would be applied to a new vessel, this time a brand-new [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]]. The newer ''[[KMS Ascension]]'' currently serves as part of '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]'''

The '''''CMS Caspar''''' is the second construct of the [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]], in the service of the [[Caspian Navy]].  She was built at [[Nenawirth Yards]] in [[Kasputin Yar]] and named after the capital world of the Union. She was commissioned in [[14 ABY]], shortly after her sister ship, ''[[CMS Mergansar]]'', the prototype of the class. They entered into service shortly before the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]], and were assigned to the '''[[CDU Home Fleet]]'''. ''Caspar'' remained attached to the Home Fleet for many years, though her particular assignments varied. It was one of the vessels which maintained the quarantine around [[Caspar]] during the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]].

[[File:Christya Rickart.jpg|thumb|Fleet Captain Christya Rickart]] The ''Caspar'' is currently under the command of [[Fleet Captain]] Christya Rickart. It is assigned to '''Defence Group Caspar''' of the '''[[Caspian 1st Fleet]]''', and is usually found in orbit of Caspar itself.

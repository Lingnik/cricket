'''''CMS Kearsarge''''' is the fifth construct of the [[Shearwater-class Frigate|Shearwater-Class Light Cruisers]], built at [[Victoria Drydocks]] near [[Caspar]], and launched in [[7 ABY]]. ''Kearsarge'' is one of the more decorated ships in the [[Caspian Navy]] and was a key participant in the [[Battle of the Belt]] in [[8 ABY]]. She currently serves as part of '''Task Group Resplendent''' of the '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]'''.

== Early History ==

She is the slipway sister of ''[[CMS Audace]]'', and once completed, the two cruisers immediately were put out on star trials together. After successful trials, they were both assigned to the '''[[CDU Third Fleet]]''', also known as the '''Iron Torch Fleet''', which was centered around the [[Hurricane-class Fleet Carrier|Hurricane-Class Fleet Carrier]] ''[[CMS Typhoon|CMS Typhoon]]''. They remained as mere escort ships for over a year as the cautious and conservative [[Caspian Crest|Crest]] kept the fleets very close at hand.

''Kearsarge'' and ''Audace'' would go their separate ways, when the latter was transferred to the '''[[CDU Second Fleet]]''', days before the 
[[Caspian Annexation Campaign|Imperial War of Aggression]]. They were reunited for the final engagement of the war, the [[Battle of the Belt]]. ''Kearsarge'' along with ''CMS Commerson'' were credited with the destruction of ''[[NBF Acclaim of Order|HIMS Acclaim of Order]]'' after it had been largely crippled by Caspian starfighters.

== Modern History ==

After his election as [[Presav]] of the [[Caspian Union]] and his appointment as [[Sar Admiral]] of the [[Caspian Navy]], [[Crestian Tarasar]] undertook to reorganise the Navy. The majority of the navy would be structured around three defensive fleets, reviving the names used during the early history of the old Union. In addition to the defensive fleets, a '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet|fourth]]''' expeditionary fleet was organised under Tarasar's personal command.

[[File:Johanna Bresch.jpg|thumb|Captain Johanna Bresch]] In deciding what vessels would serve in this fleet, Tarasar favoured those with notable histories. ''Kearsarge'' fit this bill perfectly and was assigned to '''Task Group Resplendent''' where she again serves with her sister ship, ''CMS Audace'', and ''CMS Fulmar'' which also distinguished herself at the Battle of the Belt. The ''Kearsarge'' is currently commanded by [[Captain]] Johanna Bresch.

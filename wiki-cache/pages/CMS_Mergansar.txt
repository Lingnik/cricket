'''''CMS Mergansar''''' is the eponymous lead ship of the [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruisers]] of the [[Caspian Navy]]. The Mergansar-Class was the first Caspian warship class designed after the after the [[Imperial War of Aggression]] and subsequently became testbed for an entirely new naval philosophy. The ship is named for the [[Mergansar|homeworld]] of the [[Sarian]] people and means "pulled from the original star" in [[Sarian (language)|native tongue]].

== Early Service ==
The ''Mergansar'' was built at [[Nenawirth Yards]] on [[Kasputin Yar]]. She was commissioned in [[14 ABY]], shortly before the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]], and was assigned to the '''[[CDU Home Fleet]]'''. ''Mergansar'' remained attached to the Home Fleet for many years, though her particular assignments varied. It was one of the vessels which maintained the quarantine around [[Caspar]] during the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]].

== Mergani Navy ==
Following the collapse of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] in the wake of the pandemic and the Mergani [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]], few vessels entertained allegiance to [[Mergansar]]. The pogroms were deep unpopular, and indeed reviled by most citizens of the former Union. Even among the Mergani [[Sarian|Sarians]] serving in the [[Caspian Navy]], service in such a diverse organisation saw most disabused of the sort of prejudices that allowed the pogroms to happen.

Nevertheless, there were some officers whose affinity for their homeworld overcame their misgivings over its behaviour. Among such officers was [[Commodore]] Sabos Bellerophon, commanding officer of the ''Mergansar''. Though reluctant to work with the new Mergani government, he felt his loyalty still had to be to his homeworld. The ''Mergansar'' had be a popular posting for Mergani officers given its name, though not an outright majority of the ships crew, they were represented in greater number than any other Union world.

[[File:Sabos Bellerophon.jpg|thumb|Vice Admiral Sabos Bellerophon]] Commodore Bellerophon commanded enough respect to convince the Mergani on his crew to align with Mergansarsolu and for the non-Mergani to respect the decision and transfer to other vessels. It was a scene that was playing out throughout the Caspian Navy, and the crew the ''Mergansar'' lost were soon replaced by Mergani transferring from other ships that had aligned with other worlds. As one of the most senior officers in the new Mergani Navy, and with the ''Mergansar'' as its most powerful vessel, Bellerophon found himself promoted to [[Vice Admiral]] and being made Deputy Chief of Staff, in spite of his misgivings about the government.

Following the election of the [[Crestian Tarasar|Tarasar]] administration on Caspar, the [[Caspian Repatriation Act (29 ABY)|Caspian Repatriation Act]] recognised the legitimacy of Mergansarsolu's possession of the ''Mergansar''. The act, however, also levied a 30 trillion credit fine on behalf of the refugees displayed in the pogroms, and instituted a blockade of the planet until the money as paid up. The amount was far too high for Mergansarsolu to have paid even if it wanted to.

At this point the Mergani Navy consisted only of the ''Mergansar'', four [[Shearwater-class Frigate|Shearwater-Class Light Cruisers]], and some support ships. It was no match for the efforts of the Caspian '''[[Caspian 1st Fleet|1st]]''' and '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet|4th Fleets]]'''. While some discussion was entertained of running the blockade, the Mergani lacked any outside allies that could provide meaningful aid, meanwhile the Caspian position was supported by the [[Imperium]]. This left the Mergani Navy little it could do except escort what humanitarian shipments the Caspian's permitted through, and intercepting civilian vessels which tried to depart the surface before they could be captured by the Caspians.

The blockade's unstated aim was to push the Mergani to overthrow their government and rejoin the new [[Caspian Union]]. The effort dragged on longer than anyone anticipated, but the return of [[Sar Admiral]] [[Odie Hood]] to Ithaca, and his violent removal of the local government, started to truly turn the tide. As a fellow Ithacan, Bellerophon began to consider surrender as their position was untenable and the Mergani government was losing effective control. Once Hood was appointed [[Governor|Military Governor]], Vice Admiral Bellerophon formally surrendered the ''Mergansar'' back to the Caspian Navy.

== New Mission ==

As part of the Union's efforts to reintegrate Mergansar, the officers of the Mergani Navy were allowed to rejoin the Caspian Navy. [[The Admiralty]], however, felt this presented a security risk, and opted to break up the crews of the Mergani held vessels and spread them out within the fleet to dissipate the risk. Sar Admiral Hood in his role as Military Governor vouched for the integrity of Vice Admiral Bellerophon and he was allowed to remain in command of the ''Mergansar'' to ease transition while the crews were reassigned.

After Hood's resignation as Military Governor, his appointment as Minister of the Navy, and his return to active naval service commanding '''Task Group Resplendent''' of the Caspian 4th Fleet, he personally advocated that Bellerophon be given a command suitable to the rank and position he had in the Mergani Navy. The result was receiving command of the [[Hood-class Battlecruiser|Hood-Class Battlecruiser]] ''CMS Pegasus'' and the positing of Deputy Commanding Officer as '''Patrol Group Wind''' of the Caspian 1st Fleet.

As for ''CMS Mergansar'', as the former Mergani flagship, [[Presav]] Tarasar felt it was important to make a statement with its new commanding officer. He chose to give command of the vessel to [[Sha Dark]], the Caspian Minister of Justice, who was one of the refugees who had fled Mergansar. This would serve to maintain a Mergani connexion while concurrently not allow the pogroms to be forgotten. Though Dark had no previous military experience, she had been part of Tarasar's [[Casulmis]] movement which fought against the pogroms and participated in an high profile clandestine combat operation. She was commissioned as a Commodore in the Caspian Navy upon taking command, with the ship assigned to Task Group Hood of the Caspian 4th Fleet. In practical terms it serves as her personal flagship as Minsiter of Justice, and is usually on detached assignment.

[[File:Sha Dark Uniform 1.jpg|thumb|Rear Admiral Sha Dark]]In [[34 ABY]], after the breach of [[The Oubliette|Spacestation 1138]], an Imperial black site prison in the [[:starwars:Karthakk system|Karthakk System]] nicknamed "The Oubliette," most of the 4th Fleet was deployed throughout Imperium and [[Independent]] space chasing down escapees. Commodore Dark and the ''Mergansar'' took the lead in going to the Karthakk System itself and coordinated the fleet's movements in the field in her capacity as Minister of Justice, while Sar Admiral Tarasar retained overall command. For her role in this operation Sha was promoted to [[Rear Admiral]].

The '''''CMS Picon Yar''''' is the twenty-first construct of the [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]], in the service of the [[Caspian Navy]]. She was built at [[Nenawirth Yards]] on [[Kasputin Yar]]. The ship is named for a volcanic planet in the [[Yariz|Yariz System]], which is uninhabited except for some mineral harvesting done by [[Interspatial Prospecting, Inc.]]

For many years it acted as the a support cruiser for '''Task Group H''', assigned to the [[Rautheran System]] along with the [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruisers]] ''[[CMS Trettinar|Trettinar]]'' and ''Morselin'', [[Shearwater-class Frigate|Shearwater-Class Light Cruisers]] ''Albatross'', ''Auklet'', and ''Aonach'', and six [[Lightning-class Sloop|Lightning-Class Sloops]]. The flag vessel for the Task Group was the [[Hurricane-class Fleet Carrier|Hurricane-Class Fleet Carrier]] ''Torrent''.

[[File:Kara Sarbuk.jpg|thumb|Fleet Captain Kara Sarbuck]] The ''Picon Yar'' is currently under the command of [[Fleet Captain]] Kara Sarbuk. It is assigned to '''Defence Group Yariz''' of the '''[[Caspian 1st Fleet]]''', and is usually found in orbit of Picon Yar itself.

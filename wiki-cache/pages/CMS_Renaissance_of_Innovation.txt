The '''''CMS Renaissance of Innovation''''' is an [[Admiral Roj-class Battleship|Admiral Roj-Class Battleship]] and the official flagship and namesake of '''Task Group Renaissance''' of the '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]'''. As one of the [[Caspian Union|Caspian Union's]] most modern warships and a part of the same Task Group as [[Presav]] and [[Sar Admiral]] [[Crestian Tarasar|Crestian Tarasar's]] personal flagship the ''[[Leviathan]]'', the ''Renaissance of Innovation'' often serves as the de facto flagship of the [[Caspian Navy]] as a whole.

== History ==

The ''Renaissance of Innovation'' was the tenth of the original sixteen [[Admiral Roj-class Battleship|Admiral Roj-Class Battleships]] originally conceived and ordered by the [[Caspian Navy]] and named after the [[First Renaissance of Innovation]]. Construction of the ''Renaissance'' began in [[22 ABY]] with [[Sirlahn Inglet]] serving as Deputy Chief Engineer of the project. By the time the project began, the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] was already seen to be in serious decline, and the original order of sixteen now cut to twelve. By the time construction was completed in [[24 ABY]], the Union was in serious [[2015 Caspar Plot|crisis]] and the ship was not subsequently put into commission. Ultimately a facility was constructed on [[Sahkjalin]] to house the ''Renaissance'' and it was carefully towed into place and mothballed.

[[File:Kath Samba.jpg|thumb|Vice Admiral Kath Samba]] After the election of [[Crestian Tarasar]] as [[Presav]] of the [[Caspian Union]], the remilitarisation of the Union became a central priority and the ''Renaissance of Innovation'' finally received her commission. After the [[Ixin|Republic of Ixin]] rejoined the Union in [[29 ABY]], [[Vice Admiral]] [[Kath Samba]], the Deputy Chief of Staff of the Ix Navy was given command of the ''Renaissance''. The ''Renaissance'' was assigned as the central vessel of '''Task Group Renaissance''' of the newly constituted '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]''' which was to serve as the Caspian Navy's expeditionary force under the direct command and direction of Presav Tarasar in his role as [[Sar Admiral]].

Though the Caspian Navy has no official flagship, the ''Renaissance of Innovation'' is one of several ships that is often seen performing that function in practice. As Presav and Sar Admiral Tarasar makes use of his own [[New Sith Order|Sith]] [[Interdictor-class Immobilizer 418|Interdictor]], the ''[[Leviathan]]'', as a personal flagship, he will often make use of the ''Renaissance'' for functions where a fully Caspian ship is called for. Her first notable deployment was after the terrorist attack of [[Cloud City]] on [[Bespin]], where the ''Renaissance'' was sent to lead Caspian relief efforts under the supervision of Caspian Minister of Science and Technology and [[SubPro]] CEO Sirlahn Inglet. The Renaissance also served as the host vessel and flagship during the [[Charros Wargames]] in [[32 ABY]].

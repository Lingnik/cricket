The '''''CMS Resplendent''''' is an [[Admiral Roj-class Battleship|Admiral Roj-Class Battleship]] and the official flagship and namesake of '''Task Group Resplendent''' of the '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]'''. As one of the [[Caspian Union|Caspian Union's]] most modern warships and the flagship of Caspian Naval Minister and [[Sar Admiral]] [[Odie Hood]], the ''Resplendent'' sometimes serves as the de facto flagship of the [[Caspian Navy]] as a whole.

== History ==

The ''Renaissance of Innovation'' was the eight of the original sixteen [[Admiral Roj-class Battleship|Admiral Roj-Class Battleships]] originally conceived and ordered by the [[Caspian Navy]] and named after the ''[[Resplendent XI]]'', the Republic survey ship that first discovered the [[Caspia|Caspian System]] in 989 BBY and reestablished contact between the peoples of [[Mergansar]] and [[Caspar]].

Construction of the ''Resplendent'' began in [[19 ABY]] with [[Sirlahn Inglet]] serving as Assistant Chief Engineer of the project. By the time construction was complete in [[21 ABY]], the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] was already in decline, and the original order of sixteen now cut to twelve. Though the ship was commissioned, continuing questions about the naval budget slowed its path into service. It was eventually assigned to service in the [[Yariz|Yariz System]], ultimately missing out on any action to the various crises that engulfed the Caspian System.

After the election of [[Crestian Tarasar]] as [[Presav]] of the [[Caspian Union]], the remilitarisation of the Union became a central priority and the ''Resplendent'' was assigned as the central vessel of '''Task Group Resplendent''' the newly constituted '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]''' which was to serve as the Caspian Navy's expeditionary force under the direct command and direction of Presav Tarasar in his role as [[Sar Admiral]].

[[File:Odie Hood.jpg|thumb|Sar Admiral Odie Hood]] Following his resignation as [[Governor|Military Governor]] of Mergansar, Sar Admiral [[Odie Hood]] was appointed Caspian Naval Minister and reentered active service in the Caspian Navy. Hood was given command of Task Group Resplendent and the ''CMS Resplendent'' itself. Sar Admiral Hood is notably a direct descendant of [[Icaris Hood]] who commanded ''Resplendent XI'' for which ''CMS Resplendent'' was named.

In [[34 ABY]], after the breach of [[The Oubliette|Spacestation 1138]], an Imperial black site prison in the [[:starwars:Karthakk system|Karthakk System]] nicknamed "The Oubliette," most of the 4th Fleet was deployed throughout [[Imperium]] and [[Independent]] space chasing down escapees. Elements of Task Group Resplendent under Hood, in specific ''CMS Resplendent'' and ''[[CMS Trettinar]]'' under [[Fleet Captain]] Shakka Teksa were involved in actions in the Durellian Expanse with Hood coordinating the combined Imperium-Caspian siege as owing to his seniority and experience.

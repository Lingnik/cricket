'''''CMS Spanner''''' was the eighth construct of the second batch of [[Shearwater-class Frigate|Shearwater-Class Light Cruisers]] built at [[Victoria Drydocks]] near [[Caspar]], and launched in [[8 ABY]]. The ''Spanner'' was named for the prominent city of the Casohavi on [[Mergansar]]. Immediately after being christened, she had been dubbed ''The Wrench'' by the majority of those servicing and serving on the ship, making light of the name's obvious definition. In keeping with giving starships female personas, it eventually was referred to as ''The Wench''.

She conducted her star trials under [[Captain]] Manfred Sodehl, a Casohavi [[Sarian]] from Spanner on Mergansar, who was given the honour of commanding the ship named after his home city on its first mission. It was also set to be Sodehl's last, as he was due to retire following the completion of the star trials with another officer set to take his place as ''Spanner's'' permanent commander.

The ''Spanner'' had returned to the [[Caspia|Caspian System]] by onset of the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign|Imperial War of Aggression]]. With the eruption of hostilities, there was no opportunity for the remaining cadre of officers and permanent commanding officer to board. Captain Sodehl was now left to take the ship into battle, without a complete officer compliment. For many of the ''Spanner's'' crew this would be their first fight. For most it would be their last.

The [[Battle of the Inner Caspian Worlds]] would prove devastating for the [[Caspian Navy]]. ''Spanner'' was far enough away from the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] warships already in the system to avoid immediate devastation, such as befell the ''[[CMS Warder|Warder]]'', its fate would not be much better. ''Spanner'' was ultimately one of the many ships lost in the battle. The Captain and most of her crew went down with the ship, though a few surviving escape pods were picked up by the Imperial forces after the battle, with the survivors ultimately repatriated after the end of the conflict.

[[File:Leonidas Trettsia.jpg|thumb|Captain Leonidas Trettsia]] Much to the consternation of the Casohavi, there would not be plans for many years to build a new ''Spanner'' as a replacement. It would not be, until over a decade later, that [[The Admiralty|the Admiralty]] announced the names chosen for the Batch IV Shearwaters, these included ten vessels named for those lost during the Battle of the Inner Caspian Worlds, including the ''Spanner''. The current ''Spanner'' is assigned to '''Defence Group Yariz''' of the '''[[Caspian 1st Fleet]]'''. It is commanded by Captain Leonidas Trettsia, a prominent native of the city of Spanner.

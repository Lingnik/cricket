The '''''CMS Tuatalin''''' is the sixteenth construct of the [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]], in the service of the [[Caspian Navy]]. She was built at [[Nenawirth Yards]] on [[Kasputin Yar]]. ''Tuatalin'' means ''Friend of Vigour'', and is named for one of the larger moons of [[Kichnar]].

== The Mikassa Incident ==
In [[14 ABY]], shortly after she was commissioned, the ''Tuatalin'' served as flagship of a hastily assembled patrol group, designated '''Task Group M''' and ordered to [[Mikassa]] in light of increased activity by [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] forces in the region. Accompanied by the [[Shearwater-class Frigate|Shearwater-Class Light Cruisers]], ''[[CMS Breslau]]'' and ''CMS Mollymawk'', and the [[Lightning-class Sloop|Lightning-Class Sloops]], ''[[CMS Hector|CMS Hector]]'', ''[[CMS Thresher]]'', and ''CMS Major Leyden'', the cruiser took up station over Mikassa and kept watch while a garrison of [[Union Marines]] was offloaded to the planet by the landing ships ''CMS Tanghauser Fjord'' and ''CMS Humberlon Range''. In the subsequent [[Mikassa Incident|engagement]] with Imperial force, the ''Thresher'' was lost, a subsequent vessel of the same class was later named in its honour.

== Recent History ==

[[File:Sinya Komad.jpg|thumb|Fleet Captain Sinya Komad]] Within the current Caspian Navy, ''Tuatalin'', under the command of [[Fleet Captain]] Sinya Komad, serves as part of '''Task Group Renaissance''' in the '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]'''. The warships assigned to this expeditionary fleet are often vessels with the most storied histories in the Caspian Navy, with ''Tuatalin'' so honoured for its battle record. It once again serves beside ''Breslau'' which is also part of Task Group Renaissance.

The '''NRCV ''Plaxif''''' was a member of the [[New Republic FleetOps|New Republic Navy]]'s [[New Republic Second Fleet|Second Fleet]]. Under the command of [[Captain]] Reynar Dax it participated in the Republic's [[Destruction of the HIMS Vindictive|attack]] on the ''[[VSD Vindictive|HIMS Vindictive]]'' while it was undergoing repairs at [[:starwars:Bimmiel/Legends|Bimmiel]].

The mission was a success with both ''HIMS Vindictive'' and ''HIMS Widowmaker'' destroyed. However, the Republic suffered losses of her own, the ''[[CRV Land Runner|NRCV Land Runner]]'' was destroyed during the engagement. Meanwhile, ''Plaxif'' was crippled and was towed back to [[:starwars:Ord Radama/Legends|Ord Radama]] by ''NRAF Portrane''. Once everything salvageable had been removed ''Plaxif'' was scuttled.

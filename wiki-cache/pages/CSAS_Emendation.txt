Having been purchased from [[Greiko Lornan]], this vessel had quite an interesting history under it's former name, '' [[Truth and Reconciliation]]''. After its purchase, it served as a patrol vessel in the [[Corporate Sector Authority]].

The ''Emendation'' is currently used it as personal transport for ranking security force personel, when smaller ships won't suffice. Until it entered service as a part of the [[CSAS Litigator|CSAS&nbsp;''Litigator's'']] fleet, the ''Emendation'' saw little active service in the [[Corporate Sector Authority]].

It has since engaged in a number of battles, including the climactic ambush by the pirate leader White Death, which resulted in severe damage to the capital ship. During the extensive repair process, a number of upgrades were undertaken, including a high-end .6 hyperdrive that adds even further to the ship's ability as a first-responder in a crisis situation.

'''CSAS ''Litigator''''' has served in the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector Authority's]] Picket Fleet for a considerable time.

==Past History==

=Mid-15 ABY=
''Litigator'' was placed under the command of [[Gren Delede]], and things changed quickly aboard the vessel, and within it's crew. A number of officers were sacked, and replaced with either sentients with past military experience, or simply people connected with Delede.

Upon taking command, the ''Litigator'' was the primary ship within a campaign to destroy a particularly nasty pirate menance on the fringes of [[Corporate Sector Authority]] space. A number of other vessels that made up the Litigator Task Force also took part, including the [[CSAV Emendation|CSAV ''Emendation'']].

A new unit, [[Force Litigator]] was also created by Colonel Delede. It was a company-sized unit of well-trained, and experienced ESPO's, assigned to carry out operations too sensitive for regular troopers. They were distinctive in that they wear black ESPO armor. Force Litigator was commanded by Lieutenant [[Twila Virda]].

=Late-15 ABY=
Gren Delede's command of the Litigator and it's task force was short-lived, however. He was given the task of a re-orginazation of the CSSD fleet, and placed in command of a new, large formation, the[[Corporate_Sector_Strike_Fleet| CSSD Strike Fleet]], of which the [[CSAV Litigator|''Litigator'']] was not a part.  The Litigator is currently based out of the Bonadan system.

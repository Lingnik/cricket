The rank and title of '''Cadet''' encompasses the lowest level of a commissioned officer in a military organization, most frequently used by army and starfighter corps. It is often the title given to academy students who are training for a commissioned officer's post.

There were several derivatives of the Cadet rank.

=== In Imperial Service ===

=== In New Republic Service ===

These were recruits for the '''[[New Republic GroundOps|New Republic Army]]'''. As soon as they finished training at the academy, they were promoted to the rank of [[Second Combat Officer|2nd Combat Officer]].

A derivative of this term was used in the '''[[New Republic StarOps|New Republic Starfighter Corps]]''', with cadets being called "[[Flight Cadet]]."

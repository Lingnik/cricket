'''Camp Awakening''' is a massive, 3,500 acre [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] facility on [[Corellia]]. It housed one of the Empire's newest [[Sub-Adult Group]] training and education camps.

== History ==

Camp Awakening was constructed in [[15 ABY]] on the grounds of the former [[Kreldin Estate]], which was destroyed by orbital bombardment after [[Danik Kreldin]]'s coup to overthrow Emperor Vadim was stopped. It was officially opened in late [[15 ABY]], paired with a monumental [[Report:Camp_Awakening|IGN broadcast]].

== Structure and Features ==

Approximately three-thousand, five-hundred acres of land comprise a large campus dedicated to the Sub-Adult Group of COMPNOR. Surrounded by well-patrolled shock-fencing, Camp Awakening was built over the remains of the old Kreldin Estate, developed into a large campus of green lawns and bunkers along the Bluff. The main entrance is a large, well-defended military-style gateway, with tall poles flying the large flags of COMPNOR, the Galactic Empire, and the Sub-Adult Group. Just beyond the main entrance and security checkpoints, where unauthorized visitors are either turned away or detained, is a massive parade grounds. Here, there is always activity, with platoons of teenagers coming and going as they learn the purified ways of the Empire. At the very center of the parade grounds is a large fountain in the shape of the great Emperor Vadim, with water pouring from his hands and lips.

Throughout the campus are many outdoor training facilities, including a full military-grade boot-camp region. Dozens of bunkers, ranging from large to small, also pepper the grounds. These bunkers serve a variety of purposes. The larger ones serve as dormitories and barracks, while others serve as training facilities and education centers. It is here where the Empire's finest younglings take their next step into a glorified career as COMPNOR-certified citizens and servicemen.

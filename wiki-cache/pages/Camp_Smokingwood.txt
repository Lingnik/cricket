'''Camp Smokingwood''' is a settlement located in the [[:starwars:Smoking Forest/Legends|Smoking Forest]] of [[:starwars:Mustafar/Legends|Mustafar]]. After the death of [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]], [[:starwars:Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Darth Vader]], and the destruction of the [[:starwars:Death Star II/Legends|Second Death Star]] at the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]], many saw this as a chance to embrace new ventures. For a few enterprising [[Kubaz]] that new venture would be a [[:starwars:Kubaza beetle/Legends|Kubaza beetle]] ranch on Mustafar. The camp that sprung up around their ranch was unique among modern Mustafarian settlements in that it did not start around or based on a mining operation. The settlement was logical in its way. The [[:starwars:Mustafarian/Legends|Mustafarians]] were a Near-Kubaz race, they were already a market for Kubazian textiles, and the beetle species was likely originally from [[:starwars:Kubindi/Legends|Kubindi]].

A couple years later, the camp expanded in importance as [[Zubindi Hakoon]] opened his new [[Silverwood House of Fashion]] location on Mustafar at Camp Smokingwood. The choice of Mustafar was unorthodox, the fashion market on the planet was small, but easily predisposed to favour Kubaz design. Of greater long-term importance was his Zubindi's aim to source [[:starwars:Obsidian/Legends|obsidian]] and [[:starwars:Xonolite|xonolite]] from Mustafar for his jewellery line. The Smokingwood location presented a more geological stable for refining and export than operating at the mines.

Camp Smokingwood would grow slowly but steadily over the next two decades. The beetle ranches proved profitable though the limited export market would eventually cause their profits to plateau. The Silverwood presence, however, would be a boon, particularly with the increasing prominence of Silverwood in the fashion world leading to a greater galactic market for xonolite.

The upheavals marked by the transition of [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] to [[Imperium]] and [[Caspian Democratic Union|CDU]] to [[Caspian Union|CU]], proved a more substantive change to Camp Smokingwood and Mustafar. Though Mustafar did not join the Imperium, its existence improved the regional economy. [[Bespin]], under the leadership [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]] and [[Quinne Stardust Rose]] was revitalised and became a notable trading partner.

Zubindi's prominence in the new Caspian Union as a businessman, spy master, and philanthropist would impact Camp Smokingwood as well. Demand for obsidian and especially xonolite increased dramatically in the Caspian market. Moreover [[Interspatial Prospecting, Inc.|Interspatial Prospecting Incorporated]] entered into the Mustafarian mineral market. Camp Smokingwood has grown to a population of roughly 2000, though much the population is temporary, operating much of their time at smaller sites located throughout the Smoking Forest.

Though relatively stable geologically, most of the camp's most prominent structures are actually starships in various degrees of spaceworthiness, making an evacuation possible and giving the camp a unique character.

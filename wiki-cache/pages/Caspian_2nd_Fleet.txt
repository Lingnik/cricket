The '''Caspian 2nd Fleet''' or '''Mighty River Fleet''' is currently charged with the defence of the 'northern' portion of [[Caspian Sector|Caspian Space]]. This primarily consists of protecting the [[Krittain]] system and the installations on [[Ketterslea]] and [[Tairoa]]. The fleet is under the command of [[Fleet Admiral]] Katha Pushtelli and headquartered at [[Krittain Major]].

==Overview==
After his election as [[Presav]] of the [[Caspian Union]] and his appointment as [[Sar Admiral]] of the [[Caspian Navy]], [[Crestian Tarasar]] undertook to reorganise the Navy into four fleets. Three of these fleets would be defensive in nature and be based around the ethos of the three fleets that existed in the early years of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], while a smaller [[Caspian 4th Fleet|fourth fleet]] would be expeditionary in nature.

The '''Caspian 2nd Fleet''' or '''Mighty River Fleet''' serves a homage to the '''[[CDU Second Fleet]]'''. At its inception the 2nd Fleet was headquartered at [[Kasputin Yar]] and had responsibility for protecting the [[Yariz|Yariz System]] and patrolling the surrounding space. This would include maintaining a watch for any [[Hutt]] activity beyond their [[Hutt Space|borders]].

Following the return of the [[Krittain|Krittain System]] to the Union and the admittance of [[Charros IV]], the 2nd Fleet merged with the [[Krittish Navy|Krittish Fleet]]. The combined fleet would be headquartered at Krittain Major and assume responsibility for the installations on Ketterslea and Tairoa which had previously been patrolled by the '''[[Caspian 1st Fleet|1st Fleet]]'''. Meanwhile, the 1st Fleet assumed the responsibility for the defence of the [[Yariz|Yariz System]].

At current the fleet is divided into three Defence Groups each centred around one of the three major systems within the fleet's jurisdiction and a Patrol Group which scouts the surrounding space.

The Defence Groups are centred upon an [[Admiral Roj-class Battleship|Admiral Roj-Class Battleship]] and named after the planet they are charged with defending. In addition to the Admiral Roj they each contain a [[Hurricane-class Fleet Carrier|Hurricane-Class Fleet Carrier]], [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruiser]], three [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruisers]], nine [[Shearwater-class Frigate|Shearwater-Class Light Cruisers]], and various auxiliary vessels.

The Patrol Groups is centred upon a [[Hood-class Battlecruiser|Hood-Class Battlecruiser]] and named after the nickname of the overall fleet. In addition to the Hood, it contains a Hurricane-Class Fleet Carrier, two [[Valorous-class Light Carrier|Valorous-Class Light Carriers]], an [[Arsenal-class Heavy Cruiser|Arsenal-Class Heavy Cruiser]], three Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruisers, three Shearwater-Class Light Cruisers, and various auxiliary vessels.

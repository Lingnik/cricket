Mentioned often in galactic conversation, '''The Crest''' is a casual moniker for the governmental entity of the [[Caspian Union]]. As a symbolic reference the "powers that be" there is no uncontested definition as to who is and is not referred to by the term. In the original [[Caspian Democratic Union]] it was generally understood to refer to the Office of the [[Presav]], the [[Union Assembly]], the 
[[Caspian Foreign Ministry|Department of Diplomatic and Foreign Affairs]], the [[Commissioner of Commerce]], and their law enforcement agencies. 

Within the current [[Caspian Union]] it is understood slightly differently. It can primarily be understood to mean the [[Union Council]], of which the Presav, Vice Presav, and all ministers are members; and [[The Admiralty]]. More broadly it is over considered to include the senior "flag officers" of various service branches:

* [[Caspian Foreign Ministry]] - Emissaries, Ambassadors
* [[Caspian Intelligence Service]] - Bureau Chiefs, Section Chiefs
* [[Caspian Navy]] - [[Sar Admiral|Sar Admirals]], [[Fleet Admiral|Fleet Admirals]], [[Admiral|Admirals]], and [[Vice Admiral|Vice Admirals]]
* [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]] - [[Marshal|Marshals]] and [[Vice Marshal|Vice Marshals]]
* [[Union Marines]] - Marshals, [[General|Generals]], and [[Lieutenant General|Lieutenant Generals]]

==== Notes ====
* Saying '''The Crest''' is akin to saying "The White House" or "10 Downing Street", or pledging allegiance to "the flag", or representing, in a Star Wars sense, the Republic. It is not a physical entity, but a symbolic summation of all the unified components.

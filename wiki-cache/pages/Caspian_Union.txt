The '''Caspian Union''' is the successor state to the former [[Caspian Democratic Union]]. It is encompasses largely the same territory but with more integrated and streamlined system of government than its predecessor. Owing to the aftermath of the Sariphage pandemic on [[Caspar]] and the pogroms on [[Mergansar]], the new Union has allied itself with [[Imperium]] for added security in a chaotic galaxy.

== History ==

Following the Sariphage pandemic on [[Caspar]] and the pogroms on [[Mergansar]] in [[28 ABY]] the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] effectively ceased to exist as unified commonwealth. With various systems reasserting their independence and with Mergansar isolating itself, the survivors on Caspar formed a provisional government called the Caspar Union. 

Debate about what world would have control of what assets dragged on for many months though Caspar retained enough political capital to maintain political jurisdiction of [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] and the Ward of [[Mikassa]] both of which now had seen huge spikes in their population through refugees from Mergansar.

By early [[29 ABY]] the Provisional Assembly managed to hammer out a tentative agreement on fleet dispersal and approve a new constitution. Following the adoption of this new constitution an new unicameral [[Union Council]] was elected. At it onset it consisted of nine seats, representing each of the major cities on Caspar, Kichnar Orbital Station, and for the first name Mikassa as well.

The initial election saw [[Praecet]], the party led by [[Crestian Tarasar]] take seven of the nine seats with him elected as [[Presav]]. In spite of the clear majority he nominated [[Cas Mos]], the leader of the [[Legacy]] party, which took the other two seats as Vice Presav and formed a coalition government. 

Within a fortnight the [[Caspian Repatriation Act (29 ABY)|Caspian Repatriation Act]] was past which saw [[Kasputin Yar]] and [[Rauther]] rejoin what was now renamed the Caspian Union. The governments of the [[Krittain|Krittain System]], [[Ixin]], and Mergansar were formally recognised as was their control of various vessels of the [[Caspian Navy]]. The act would also see the government of Mergansar sued in the name of the 300 million refugees forced off the planet with elements of the Caspian 1st & [[Caspian 4th Fleet|4th]] Fleets initiating a blockade of the world to force payment. 

Shortly thereafter the [[Imperium-Caspian Mutual Protection Pact (29 ABY)|Imperium-Caspian Mutual Protection Pact]] was signed officially aligning Caspar with the [[Imperium]]. A fortnight after that Ixin was readmitted to the Union.

The blockade of Mergansar would go on for nearly half the year. Though the Tarasar government had never imagined the endeavour would take so long it did achieve its true objective. While payment for the refugees in the amount demanded was never accepted indeed feasible, the prolonged isolation set the stage for internal strife which ultimately force the collapsed of the local governments on the planet, often quite violently, yet without direct involvement by Caspian forces.

Following the readmittance of Mergansar, the major cities of Mergansar were granted representation, [[Sar Admiral]] [[Odie Hood]] was appointed Military Governor to oversee the election cycle ensure no candidates who had infringed on their rights of Union citizens during the pogroms was allowed to run. By this point Hood himself had already been elected to represent Ithaca with Valdemar Linane already representing Hammerton. Shortly after this [[Philinda Jorgan]] was elected in Charon with Yoseph Sarlin elected in Spanner early in [[30 ABY]].

Early that year also saw reunification with the Krits following a devastating attack by slavers against a civilian transport in Krittish Space. Though negotiations had been ongoing, mutual distrust between the government of the two Unions had considerably delayed the process. The limited strength independent Krittish Navy compared with growing strength of the Caspian Navy would ultimately see these difference put aside by both parties.

At this time the Union would also admit the world of [[Charros IV]]. This was the culmination of months of diplomacy between the [[Xi Char]] and the Tarasar administration. The inclusion of Charros IV represents the first Caspian world outside of the Caspian Sector as well as the first new member to the Union since Ixin joined the CDU in [[21 ABY]].

The negotiations would also involve former Caspian Presav [[Elana Tracer]] in her capacity as head of [[Kuat Drive Yards-Sienar Fleet Systems]] which had control of the assets of the former Xi Char manufacturer [[Haor Chall Engineering]]. During the months of negotiations [[Xi Charrian]] engineers had already been contracted to retrofit the Caspar's old [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruisers]] for recommissioning.

Following the next Mergani election in Toresta which saw a great deal of turmoil, Councillor Hood resigned as Military Governor to be replaced by Councillor Jorgan with elections set for the remaining cities of Anphora, Goshen, Hebron, and Vilarasala shortly. The remaining elections would prove uneventful, with Military Governor's post abolished following the swearing in of the remaining Mergani Councillors.

For the next two years the Union would quietly work to fully integrate the Krits, Mergani, and Xi Char with rest of the Union. With a variety of threats originating with in [[Hutt Space]], a great deal of attention has been placed on securing the Union's southern border. To that end the Union has made a defence pact with the nearby world of [[:starwars:Bimmisaari/Legends|Bimmisaari]] and maintains an outpost on with the nearby world of [[Ngor Nghal]]. The Union maintains close relations with [[Kessel]] and [[Tatooine]], and has informal defence agreements in place with Warlord [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]].

== Demographics ==

[[Caspian Sector|Space]] sits at a crossroads of galactic trade and political strife. The [[Perlemian Trade Route]] runs near the Caspian Sector while the Lesser Lantillian Route runs through the Charros System. The Union finds itself nears the boards of Imperium, [[New Republic|Republic]] and [[Hutt Space]]. In addition to the sentient species native to the region, the Union's location has made it a hub of immigration and a destination for refugees making for a very diverse population.

=== [[Caspia|Caspia System]] ===

Like much of the rest of the galaxy '''[[Human]]''' and '''[[Near-Human]]''' species dominate. Culturally, the dominant force in the Union is the '''[[Sarian|Sarians]]''' a Near-Human species native to [[Mergansar]]. Roughly 1,150 years ago the Sarians colonised [[Caspar]], the other habitable world in their home system. Following the [[Caspia|Caspian System's]] rediscovery by the Republic roughly 1,020 years ago Caspar would become a popular site for immigrants from a number of worlds in the [[The Galaxy|Core]] with mainline Human races outnumbering native Sarians by a small margin by the time [[Caspian Democratic Union]] was founded there. Many Caspians can trace their roots to worlds such as [[Chandrila]], [[Corellia]], [[Corulag]], [[Coruscant]], [[Kuat]], and in more recent years the [[The Galaxy|Outer Rim]] world of Eriadu.

Following the Sariphage pandemic which wiped out most of the planet's population and the subsequent influx of refugees from the pogroms on Mergansar, the demographics on Caspar have shifted with Humans now making up 47% of the planet's populations with Sarians reduced to only 28%. Roughly one quarter of current Caspian population are Mergani refugees. 

The other major species of Caspar are '''[[Wookiee|Wookiees]]'''. Having been more resisted to the disease they have gone from comprising 8% percent of the planet's pre-Sariphage population to now making up 15%, which has led to their increasing prominence politically. The remaining 10% of Caspar's population is divided up among countless races from throughout the galaxy. Among these are sizeable enclaves of '''[[Duros]]''', '''[[Gotal|Gotals]]''', '''[[Horansi]]''', '''[[Pantoran|Pantorans]]''', '''[[Quarren]]''', '''[[Twi'lek|Twi'leks]]''', '''[[Wroonian|Wroonians]]''', and '''[[Zabrak]]'''. The city of [[Anchorron]] is especially known at this point for its diversity.

While Caspar has remained diverse the same is not true for Mergansar. Sarians already made up 90% of the population prior to the pogroms with virtually all non-Sarians forced off world or driven into hiding. Though some of those forced out have returned the planet remains 97% Sarian currently.

=== [[Krittain|Krittain System]] ===

[[Krittain Major]] is the most populous world of the Union having been settled nearly 4,000 years ago by refugees of the galaxy's many conflicts. '''[[Human]]''' and '''[[Near-Human]]''' species such as '''[[Mirialan|Mirialans]]''' and '''[[Zabrak]]''' made up the majority of these early settlers which overtime have emerged as distinct Krittish ethnic group. 

Though largely lawless through most of its history, its proximity to the Perlemian Trade Route has made it a haven for exiles and refugees over the centuries. Notable communties of '''[[Bith]]''', '''[[Duros]]''', '''[[Gotal|Gotals]]''', '''[[Nikto]]''', '''[[Sarian|Sarians]]''', '''[[Svivreni]]''', '''[[Twi'lek|Twi'leks]]''', '''[[Vorzydiak]]''', and '''[[Wookiee|Wookiees]]''' have emerged through the years.

Citizenship on Krittain Major has traditionally been restricted to those who roots on the world go back at least two generations among other onerous requirements. As such over 40% of the planet's permanent population lacked citizenship. As a condition for the Krit Union's admittance to the new Caspian Union, citizenship was normalised to all permanent residents.

Settlement on nearby [[Krittain Minor]] goes back even further to a penal colony predating the Republic. Abandoned by their initial jailers countless millennia ago, the population development into a distinct subspecies of humanity, adapted to the heavy gravity and cold climate. Following the colonisation of Krittain Major, Krittain Minor would see some limited increase in its own population thanks to the contact. The term '''[[Minor Krit]]''' is often applied without distinction as to how much of one's ancestry traces back to endemic '''[[Near-Human]]''' race versus more recent baseline '''[[Human]]''' settlers.

=== [[Charros IV]] ===

Charros IV is the second most populous planet in the union, but it is also the least cosmopolitan planet. For most of its history it was populated almost exclusively my the [[Xi Charrian]] people themselves. The few non Xi Charrians were traditionally those involved in their sale of Droids, starships, and vehicles and were often [[Geonosian]] or [[Neimoidian]]. [[Human|Humans]] in particularly were not particularly welcome after the experience the [[Xi Char]] had had with [[:starwars:Raith Sienar/Legends|Raith Sienar]].

Following the [[:starwars:Confederacy of Independent Systems/Legends|Confederate]] defeat in the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]] the Geonosians and Neimoidians left. With the Republic's destruction of the Xi Char Cathedral Factories and [[Sienar Fleet Systems]] takeover of what remained of [[Haor Chall Engineering]], a number of Humans did come over the decades, employed by Sienar or related to their work there. What industry the Xi Charrians were able to export went first through the hands of the Humans.

Since joining the Union and reforming Haor Chall Engineering, the demographics among the non Xi Charrian population of the planet have been shifting. Some of the Human population has been leaving, replaced in equal or larger number people in the fields of defence, industry, and transportation. Humans, [[Sarian|Sarians]], [[Sluissi]], and [[Yari|Yaries]] are the most prevalent among the new immigrants and long term residents, but any species that exists in notable numbers on other Union worlds would not be a shocking site on Charros IV. All major Xi Char cities a smaller section outside the main city where the non-Xi Charrians tend to live, particular near the military bases on Xi Can and Xi Dan.

===[[Ixin]]===

Archaeologists and historians remain divided as to when Ixin was first settled. Ruins on the planet suggest there was once a civilisation dating back before the formation of the Old Republic, but whether there is any connexion to the current '''[[Human]]''' civilisation is questionable. Still known Human settlement goes back at least four millennia.

Once a wealthy world boasting an expansionist society, Ix Princes were known to deal in slaves and hire large mercenary armies, often dealing other disreputable parties as Czerka Corporation and the [[Hutt Space|Hutts]]. Such dealings ended over a century ago, though a tendency to import outside labour has remained.

Nearly half the population of Ixin is non-Human. '''[[Devaronian|Devaronians]]''', '''[[Duros]]''', '''[[Nikto]]''', '''[[Sarian|Sarians]]''', and '''[[Twi'lek|Twi'leks]]''' especially exist in significant numbers. Smaller segments of '''[[Bith]]''', '''[[Klatooinian|Klatooinians]]''', '''[[Vodran|Vodrans]]''', '''[[Weequay]]''', '''[[Wookiee|Wookiees]]''', and '''[[Zabrak]]''' are also noticeable within the overall population.

===[[Mikassa]]===

Little is known of the early history of Mikassa. It has been theorised that it was once a colony world of the Ix, that was later abandoned when environmental changes on the Otus System pushed the civilisation on [[Ixin]] into decline. The irony in this is that while was never so isolated as to develop a distinct species that is precisely what occurred on Mikassa.

This native population never developed in large numbers and to this day only number a few hundred thousand strong. Waves of immigration in recent decade have seen the '''[[Mikassan|Mikassans]]''' become a tiny minority. Roughly two thirds of the population is now made up of baseline '''[[Human|Humans]]''' with the remaining population comprised of the other various species prevalent in Union space such '''[[Nikto]]''', '''[[Sarian|Sarians]]''', '''[[Twi'lek|Twi'leks]]''', and '''[[Wookiee|Wookiees]]'''  among others.

Like other Union worlds, Mikassa received millions of refugees from [[Mergansar]] following the pogroms there. Unlike other more populated worlds for whom the refugees represented a fraction of the overall population, over 40% of the current Mikassan population are refugees from Mergansar. The high concentration of Mergani has been cited as one of the reasons Mikassa was elevated from status as a ward to a full membership in the new Union.

===[[Kasputin Yar]]===

In a sector of space known for peculiar climatology and evolution, Kasputin Yar may rank as the most unique. The native '''[[Yari|Yaries]]''' are an tall, eight-limbed, arboreal species known for living in harmony with their environment and extreme computational talents. While xenobiologists have been unable to link the Yari to any other sentient in the galaxy certain similarities exist between Yaries and races such as [[Besalisk|Besalisks]], [[Ithorian|Ithorians]], and [[Sluissi]].

The Yari largely live in the canopies of the native [[Rofalssa stalk|Rofalssa stalks]] but several settlements catering to other species have been built over the centuries in the few areas where the stalks do not grow. The Yari only make roughly 40% of the planetary population with other races from the region such as '''[[Human|Humans]]''', '''[[Nikto]]''', and '''[[Sarian|Sarians]]''' all forming significant minorities.

Whether coincidental to the biological similarities between two species or not, '''Sluissi''' have long been present among Kasputin Yar's population. Following the [[Destruction of Sluis Van|destruction]] of their native [[Sluis Van]] additional refugees have come to settle on Kasputin Yar, particularly in the city of Vanover. After Sluis Unimiel, Kasputin Yar has the second largest concentration of Sluissi in the galaxy.

The prominence of the [[Lokkai Starworks]] facilities on world and the proximity to [[Hutt Space]], and the unique environmental conditions see a number of other species appear in noticeable numbers including '''Besalisks''', '''[[Duros]]''', '''[[Givin]]''', '''[[Gotal|Gotals]]''', '''Ithorians''', '''[[Klatooinian|Klatooinians]]''', '''[[Vodran|Vodrans]]''', and '''[[Weequay]]'''.

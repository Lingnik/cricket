======================================================================
Individuals who show aptitude towards use of the [[Force]], but lack formal training. Without the knowledge to fully control them, they might use their talents unwittingly or to serve their own purposes.

They are not classified as [[Jedi]] or [[Sith]], but may be sensed by one of the two camps and be courted for apprenticeship.

Dozens of '''planets''' exist in the Star Wars MUSH galaxy, spread amongst the principalities and used to foster interaction amongst players.

There are three types of planets:<br>
* ''Coded'' planets that have stellar coordinates, rooms, and players can interact and/or reside on them.
* ''Non-Coded; Original'' planets that are mentioned in RolePlay interaction, and are essential to the overall story, but do not "physically exist".
* ''Non-Coded; Canon'' planets that are mentioned in RolePlay interaction, and are integral parts of the Star Wars universe. These planets are denoted and detailed by the [[:starwars:|Star Wars Wikia Site (Wookieepedia)]].

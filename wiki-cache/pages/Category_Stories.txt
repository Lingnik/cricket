This category serves as an archive for original, non-roleplayed stories set in the [[Star Wars MUSH]] universe.

These tales are posted to the [[SW1ki]] to be shared with other players and add depth to MUSH plotlines and characters.

The stories featured here should not be assumed to be authoritative information, as they did not play out [[IC|ICly]] and do not necessarily have the approval of the [[RolePlay_Admin|RPA Board]].  While efforts to preserve the author's work will be made, the pages must still meet wiki standards of appropriate content.  In instances where original stories conflict with established IC ''facts'', the wiki community may request that disclaimers noting the deviations be noted.

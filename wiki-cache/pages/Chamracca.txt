'''Chamracca''' was an infamous bounty hunter throughout the [[The Galaxy#Outer Rim|Outer Rim]]. At first he had a kind heart and open mind, but after he was robbed he became enraged, twisted, and corrupt, and began his bounty hunting profession. He was also known for his mercenary, pirate, smuggling and part-time slaver work.

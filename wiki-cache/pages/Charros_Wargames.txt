Occurring several months into the year [[32 ABY]], the '''Charros Wargames''' were large-scale exercises conducted by elements of the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] [[Caspian 3rd Fleet|3rd]] and [[Caspian 4th Fleet|4th Fleets]] as well ground force elements, and allied [[Imperium]] assets and officers. The wargames were meant to showcase new and refitted military designs related to the admission of [[Charros IV]] into the [[Caspian Union|Union]] two years prior and the reconstitution of the [[Xi Char]] manufacturing firm [[Haor Chall Engineering]].

In addition to the military exercises the wargames served to invite visitors to Charros IV. As [[Xi Charrian|Xi Charrians]] were little seen off their homeworld the wargames proved an occasion for the Xi Charrians to introduce their culture and homeworld to the rest of the Union and wider galaxy.

== Charros IV ==

One of the first diplomatic goals [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] set his sights upon after taking office in [[29 ABY]] was the reunification of [[Caspar]] with the rest of the member worlds of the former [[Caspian Democratic Union]] and the expansion beyond traditional Union borders. As a manufacturing world near [[Caspian Sector|Caspian Space]], Tarasar felt [[Xi Charrian]] industry would compliment Caspian efforts at remilitarisation. 

He also felt that given the bad blood between the [[Xi Char]] and [[Sienar Fleet Systems]], who ultimately acquired the Xi Char firm [[Haor Chall Engineering]], that the Xi Char would welcome Union membership should they be able to get their manufacturing repatriated. Given the Union's alliance with the newly formed [[Imperium]] and the appointment of former Caspian Presav [[Elana Tracer]] as [[Moff]] of [[Selene]] to head [[Kuat Drive Yards-Sienar Fleet Systems]], Tarasar felt all the pieces were in place to make this happen.

After months of three way negotiations between Tarasar, Tracer, and [[Dean]] [[ak]] representing the Xi Char, in early [[30 ABY]] an agreement was reached allowing for [[Charros IV]] to cede from the Imperium and join the Caspian Union, and for the Imperium to cede Xi Char designs and production facilities back to the Xi Char. This would allow the reconstitution of Haor Chall Engineering and its partnership with Caspian firms including [[SubPro|Sublight Products Corporation]], [[Lokkai Starworks]], and [[StarShield|Starshield Armaments]] on various products.

== Haor Chall Engineering ==

Even before the negotiations had been completed, Xi Charrian engineers had been involved in [[Caspian Navy|Caspian Navy's]] project to recommission and refit their [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruisers]]. Once negotiations were complete the newly reconstituted Haor Chall Engineering immediately began work to produce new battle droids and starfighters for the Caspian Navy.

The two battle droid models were each updates of [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]] era designs:

* The '''[[E5 Baron Droid]]''' designed based on prototypes by [[SubPro|SubPro's]] [[Sirlahn Inglet]] as an update on [[:starwars:Baktoid Combat Automata/Legends|Baktoid Combat Automata's]] [[:starwars:E4 baron droid|E4 Baron Droid]].
* The '''[[IG-240 Hailfire-Class Droid Tank]]''' a design made in conjunction with [[StarShield]] at the request of the [[Caspian Defence Force]] as an update on Haor Chall Engineer's old [[:starwars:IG-227 Hailfire-class droid tank/Legends|IG-227 Hailfire-Class Droid Tank]].

The four starfighter models consisted of two new designs along with the update of two earlier ones:

* The '''[[Apex-Class Starfighter]]''' a planetary based craft designed for use by pilots from larger species such as [[Wookiees]].
* The '''[[Guardian-Class Starfighter]]''' a design loosely based on the [[:starwars:Guardian Mantis|Guardian Mantis]] but redesigned to be piloted by [[Yari|Yaries]] based on [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] and the [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruisers]].
* The '''[[Mark IV Vulture-Class Droid Starfighter]]''' based on the old [[:starwars:Variable Geometry Self-Propelled Battle Droid, Mark I/Legends|Variable Geometry Self-Propelled Battle Droid]], again for use largely on [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] and the [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruisers]].
* The '''[[Vector-Class Starfighter]]''' a planetary based craft similar in design to the Apex-Class but built with [[Human|Humans]] and [[Sarian|Sarians]] in mind as the pilots.

== Renaissance of Innovation ==

In advance of the wargames, Presav Tarasar made a major [[Report:Renaissance of Innovation|address]] at [[Union Hall]] in [[Plaxton]] on [[Caspar]] inaugurating the Caspian Union's [[Second Renaissance of Innovation]]. During the address Councillors ak and Sirlahn Inglet introduced the new designs to the public and the wargames themselves were announced.

== Personnel ==

The wargames saw former Caspian officer and current Imperium Inquisitor [[Axel Vichten]] accepting the position of [[Marshal|Star Marshal]] leading the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]] and former Caspian officer [[Lynae Cassius]] rejoining the [[Caspian Navy]] at the rank of [[Admiral]]. Former Caspian officer and Presav and current [[Grand Moff]] of [[Selene]] and [[Athaniss]] [[Elana Tracer]] would also lend her services to the military exercises.

== Events ==

In addition to the military exercises themselves which were headlined by a simulated evacuation, numerous receptions were held, and tours of Xi Char [[Cathedral Factory|cathedral factories]]. Dean [[cadracak-'r'shak]] also used the occasion of the wargames to open an [[Prelate r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak Memorial Art Gallery|art gallery]] in Xi Xin, bringing art from around the galaxy to Charros IV.

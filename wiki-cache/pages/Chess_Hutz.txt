'''Chess Hutz''' enlisted in [[Imperial Navy]] as a starfighter mechanic. After two years of service, he was recognized by his leaders as having command potential. Six months would go by before being sent off to the [[Imperial Academy]] on [[O'paal]]. Already having a background in Starfighter Operations, he decided to make his career a pilot. His first assignment was Cerebus Squadron under the command of then [[Lieutenant]] [[Martos Dixon]]. Lt. Dixon, seeing [[Ensign]] Hutz' potential quickly, placed Chess as XO of Cerebus Squadron. Chess would later become the CO of Cerebus Squadron and would rise to the rank of [[Commander]]. With an impeccable service record, he was selected as [[Commodore]] [[Morlok Adlerson|Alderson's]] assistant in running Star Fighter Operations. When Commodore Adlerson was promoted to [[Admiral]] and assigned as Fleet Commander, Commander Chess stepped up as Star Fighter Operations CO and was given the rank of Commodore. As Commodore, he moved Star Fighter Operations Headquarters to the [[Orbital Station Guardian]] and used that as a base of operations. His career ended shortly after being stationed on the OS Guardian. He was flagged as a defiant officer to Naval High Command. After an argument with [[Fleet Admiral]] Adlerson, Chess was removed from his command, but still remains an icon in Imperial pilot history.

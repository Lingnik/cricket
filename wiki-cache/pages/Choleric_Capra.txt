Uncommonly for a pirate vessel, this ship was purchased rather than stolen, though for a price which could be considered criminal, on account of the seller being unable to provide proper ownership documentation.

The ''Capra ''serves as the primary boarding launch of its mothership, the [[Lightning-class Sloop|Lightning-class sloop]] ''[[Rattatak's Revenge]].''

Of particular note, the ship features 'nose art' of a silhouetted four-lekked Twi'lek dancer.

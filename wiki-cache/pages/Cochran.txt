'''Cochran''' (or in some Griffon dialects '''Cochran-de-la-Rhine''') was once a treasure-world in the Mid-Rim, but has been reduced to a ''celestial mausoleum'' through terrifying, abrupt acts of stress, war, and abuse. During the time of the [[Griffon Alliance]], it was the Alliance's capitol world.

{{stub location}}

=== A Tragic End ===
In [[14 ABY]], the planet was the subject of a [[Base Delta Zero]] attack by the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] forces of [[Grand Admiral]] [[Danik Kreldin]]'s ordered [[Base Delta Zero]] attack in [[14 ABY]]. The hour-long bombardment rendered the planet uninhabitable and resulted in the deaths of hundreds of thousands of sentients, all as an act of retaliation for Griffon involvement in the destruction of the [[Death Star III|third Death Star]]. 

Later, in [[15 ABY]], the [[New Sith Order]] would use the dead world as a base of operations and droid production. The sterility of the planet obscured their work from even the senses of the [[Jedi]] for quite some time. However, less than a year later, [[Luke Skywalker]] saw through the cloud of the dead world by the guidance of the Force. He revealed this discovery to [[Captain]] [[Antoine Dareus|Dareus]], whose angered response confirmed Skywalker's suspicions. Due to this apparent discovery, the New Sith Order withdrew all assets and destroyed the base by bombardment from the [[I2SD Predator|HIMS ''Predator'']], leaving the planet once again without any traces of life.

The Sith would then return and mine the planet until it cracked. The world shattered and one of the largest pieces would be put together as [[Vector One]] in [[17 ABY]]. A major Sith Order holding in the region.

'''Communications Corporation''' or Com-Cor was a communications business started by [[Krackor Reincus]] and [[Ewan Dain]]. Using a 200,000 credit loan from the Nar Shaddaa [[hutts]] and working out a deal to sell refurbished equipment with [[Ernest Pallando]] of the [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] the company was able to acquire an office on the Smuggler's Moon as well as the necessary factory equipment to produce holo-terminals and comlinks.

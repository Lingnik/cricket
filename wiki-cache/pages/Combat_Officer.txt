'''Combat Officer''' is a military rank used by the '''[[New Republic GroundOps|New Republic Army]]'''. Along with [[Second Combat Officers]], Combat Officers are Marines being groomed for tactical leadership positions. Typically already battle-hardened and experienced, most Combat Officers have had successful careers as Marines prior to their commissioning and now serve as fire-team leaders.

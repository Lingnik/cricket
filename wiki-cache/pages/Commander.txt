A '''Commander''' is a military rank.

=== In Imperial Service ===
{{rank plate|[[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]]|}}
In the [[Imperial Navy]], a Commander is a command-level rank given authority over a wide area of operations and personnel. Aboard a [[Star Destroyer]], a Commander typically manages a major sector of the warship's operations. A Commander could also be the Star Destroyer's Executive Officer or Second Officer.

In most cases, Commanders are the closet aides to the [[Captain]]. On a smaller vessel, a Commander could be the Commanding Officer, or Executive Officer, depending on the situation. 

A Commander is equivalent to a [[Wing Commander]] in the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]]. 

=== In Republic Service ===

=== In Union Service ===
'''Commanders''' in the [[Caspian Navy]] generally serve as command officers or department heads on warships or major installations. On a smaller vessel the commanding officer would be a Commander in rank. A Commander is equivalent to a [[Wing Commander]] in the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]] and equivalent to a [[Lieutenant Colonel]] in the [[Union Marines]].

The '''Commissioner of Commerce''' was an administrative position within the [[Caspian Crest]] of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], responsible for managing all commercial interests on any of the Union's provinces. More often than not, the position was held by the Vice Presav. Appointment to the position was subject to approval by the [[Union Assembly]]. 

In the current [[Caspian Union]] the closest analogue is the Minister of Commerce, though this position lacks the level of prestige that the Commissioner of Commerce held. The positions of Minister of Industry and Labour and Minister of Science and Technology while less directly analogous in terms of scope, they hold a level of authority and political prestige more akin to that once held by the Commissioners of Commerce.

[[Coral City]] is the planetary capital of [[Dac|Mon Calamari]].

A floating city hovering atop its planet's endless oceans, Coral City was the seat of government during the planet's membership in the [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Galactic Republic]].  This made it the target of heavy policing during the [[Galactic Empire]]'s subjugation of the world. Once liberated from Imperial rule, Coral City again became a thriving metropolis and took its place among the planetary capitals of the [[New Republic]]. As the capital city, it was home to a number of governing bodies, such as the [[:starwars:Mon Calamari Council|Mon Calamari Council]].

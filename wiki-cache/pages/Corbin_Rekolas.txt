== History ==
=== Childhood ===
Born Corbin Cassius Icara Rekolas in 7 BBY to a human mother on the core world of Corulag. Growing up there was difficult as Corbin was not fully human as he was the product of an assault on his mother by a non-human. Never knowing what race his father was or why he did what he did was always a blight on him. As he grew his near-human features began to show more and more, especially with his ears that took on a pointed tip. As he grew on a world that was extremely devoted to the Empire and Imperial way of life his non-human side began to be a deterrent, especially when he began to take to the creed of the Empire and it's ideals.

=== Young Adult Life ===

Throughout his young adult life he always fought harder, faster, and longer then the other children. He had to to prove that he was just as good if not better then the human children around him. Devoting himself to his studies and actives eventually paid off when at the age of 18 he applied and was accepted into the Corulag Academy. For the next two years he excelled at his studies sacrificing friends and family to his future. Following his graduation from the Corulag Academy he applied for enrollment at the Prefsbelt Fleet Camp to further his knowledge. Spending another two years of education there he excelled in martial arts and ground ops as well as showing a proficiency in starfighter piloting. Now, newly graduated in his training he has been accepted into the Imperial military and is currently on leave until he receives his orders.

== Psyche ==
Due to his mixed parentage, and the actual origin of his conception, Corbin has taken on a view of apathy, and sometimes even hatred, towards non-humans. This is an apparition of his hatred for a part of himself that he takes out on others. Corbin is a warrior in heart and in spirit. He has worked his entire life on his skills that will keep him alive in battle. Always one for a cause he will stand up with the underdog, even if it means his own life is at stake. He lives his life by seven virtues: Honor, Heroic Courage, Honesty, Compassion, Justice, Duty, and Loyalty. Although he tried to be strong and serious he often was cynical and light and humorous

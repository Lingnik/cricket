The '''Corellian Confederation''' is the government which represents the worlds of the Corellian Sector following their exit from the [[New Republic]]. The [[Corellian System]] itself [[Report:Corellia Goes Solo|declared independence]] from the Republic in [[30 ABY]] after revelations that Interim Chancellor Marius Starker had in fact been [[Basca Starker]] the man responsible for the Sariphage pandemic on [[Caspar]]. Later that year and into the next several other worlds in the Corellian Sector declared independence as well, eventually creating speculation and interest in a sector wide government.

By late [[31 ABY]] negotiations for a this new government were nearly finalised with the formal formation of the new Confederation coming early in [[32 ABY]]. [[Dash Blazer]], Corellia's last [[Senator]] to the New Republic, and a major force behind the formation of the new Confederation was [[Report:Corellia Coming Out|elected]] Prime Minister. In the months that have followed the Confederation has made major defence agreements and trade deals with the [[Imperium]], [[Caspian Union]], and several other independent powers. The Confederation also continues to maintain ties and treaties with the New Republic.

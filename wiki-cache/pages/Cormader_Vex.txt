======================================================================
This ancient little fellow wears glasses, has wispy hair, is wizened and tottering, and usually is surrounded by his oversized coat.  When his glasses slip, he blames [[:Category:Races|aliens]], [[:Category:Droids|droids]], and thieves. He still possesses his full mental faculties, though, and his perceptiveness is exceptional. His favourite subjects of conversation are his chair and young people today.

The '''Corona House''' was the seat of the [[Corellia|Corellian]] government, located in [[Coronet City]]. The [[:starwars:Diktat|Diktat]] resided and ruled from the Corona House, and during the reign of the [[Galactic Empire]], the Imperial [[Governor]] would take up power from the Corona House. The Corellian parliament was also found inside the Corona House. With the advent of the [[Corellian Confederation]] in [[32 ABY]], it is one again serving as the seat of power.

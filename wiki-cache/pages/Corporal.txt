The rank and title of '''Corporal''' is a non-commissioned officer's rank. They often act as squad or platoon leaders in army organizations.

=== In New Republic Service ===

Corporal is the lowest of the NCO ranks in the '''[[New Republic GroundOps|New Republic Army]]'''. Soldiers who are aiming for a position as an NCO are required to have at least twelve months (preferably longer) experience as a Corporal, assigned as an oberserver to a more experienced senior NCO. They are often team leaders in combat, or act as an NCO's left-hand. They are frequently entrusted with more responsibility in combat and in garrison, and are expected to set and maintain the standard for their fellow junior soldiers.

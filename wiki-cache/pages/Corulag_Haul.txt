{{Ship Capital Single|image = [[image:ship starship actionVI.jpg|300px]]|name = Corulag Haul|class = [[Corellian Action VI Transport]]|launch = [[18 BBY]]|built = Corellian Engineering Corporation Shipyards|principal = [[New Republic]]|status = IC Story Component Only|current = Unknown|mods = None|funits = None|gunits = None|characters = Lieutenant Samuel Collins (NPC)}}One of tens of thousands of bulk freighters ordered by the New Order to supply its rapidly expanding military, ''Corulag Haul ''is named for the supply run to which it was first assigned.

In [[24 ABY]], she was captured by the notorious pirate [[Shiari Vane]] while assigned to a convoy transporting state-granted humanitarian aid to the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], under blockade by the Imperial Navy. She was defended by [[ICC Vulture|''HIMS Vulture'']]'' ''and was the sole vessel captured that day.

''Corulag Haul ''immediately proceeded to Caspar, outrunning reports of her capture. Appearing alone, off schedule and without her convoy she claimed a hyperspace malfunction caused her to become separated. Attributing her battle damage to the same cause, she bluffed her way past the Imperial fleet, overcoming the suspicions of [[Amaza Sunrider]] through a combination of proper documentation and sheer belligerence.

''Corulag Haul'' was the first [[New Republic|Republic]] aid vessel to defeat the Imperial blockade and was welcomed to the New Republic Embassy in Plaxton by Ambassador [[Ambrosia Delgard]] who took possession of the ship on behalf of the New Republic. 

''Corulag Haul'' was last seen departing the New Republic Embassy shortly after her arrival. Her fate is unknown.

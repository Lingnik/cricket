The '''Covenant of Fallen Stars''' was a religious group located on [[Cochran]]. Its leader was an enigmatic man known as '''Amadeus'''. Only a select few people know that Amadeus is an alias of [[Heinrich Stalh]]. They were at odds with the [[Olumi Osahn]] and were eventually wiped out by them.

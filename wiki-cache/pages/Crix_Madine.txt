'''Crix Madine''' is the [[Supreme Commander]] of the [[New Republic]] [[New Republic Military|Military]]. He was once an [[Imperial Army]] [[General]] and responsible for the creation of the [[:starwars:Storm commando/Legends|Storm Commandos]]. However, upon realizing the evil of the Empire, he defected and became a high-ranking [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Alliance]] general in-charge of Alliance special operations based on his experience with commando operations.

He would serve the Republic faithfully for many years, and was the mastermind behind the [[Ghosts of Alderaan]], the commando unit that was responsible for the [[Third Battle of Sluis Van|destruction]] of the [[Death Star III]]. During the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]], Madine was promoted to the role of Supreme Commander, although many considered this controversial as many considered [[Admiral]] [[Gial Ackbar|Ackbar]] the more likely candidate. Nevertheless, it was a decision made by Ackbar and Madine behind closed doors, with Ackbar's resilient and strategic mind needed to keep the Fleet together after such heavy losses were incurred.

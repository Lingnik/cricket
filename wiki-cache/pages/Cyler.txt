'''Cyler''' was the inaugural "vice president" to [[Wiltmer Gerrin]]'s [[presav]]ship and served as the Director of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs for the [[Caspian Democratic Union]]. Cyler would continue on in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs until [[Avalyshaar Dusten]]'s presavship, whereupon he was replaced by [[Q'raan Endvarel]].

Cypher became Code Wizard on July 28th, 2005 taking over for [[Darrien]], the previous Code Wizard. It is his duty to keep a legacy of code written by many authors over a period of 15 years working smoothly for the enjoyment of the players, while adding new code to meet the MUSH's changing needs. 

Amongst his contributions to the MUSH are customizable [[ZIS]]-powered [[droids]], casino games, the +what command, [[ZIS weapons|weapon]] terminals for manufacturers and vendors, inter-system shuttling for military personnel assigned to capital ships, improvements to [[swoop|swoop bikes]], and the trivia game channel. 

He has also gained notoriety with the playerbase for mistletoe, cookiees, stale halloween candy, [[MUSHy Awards]], two recent [[April 1st|april fools]] pranks and hurlings of snowballs, confetti and rotten tomatoes. 

This is his description:

Cypher is a curious little [[jawa]]. Still only about a meter tall, and wearing a dingy brown robe and hood, the similarity to regular jawas ends there. The glowing lights in his hood that mark his eyes are magnified by thick-rimmed eyeglasses. Wild shocks of white hair peek out from under the hood. And his robe has a pocket with pens and a calculator in it. He carries around a rather large metallic wrench covered with colorful blinking lights, digital readouts, and extendable attachments. He can often be heard cackling to himself maniacally while he works.

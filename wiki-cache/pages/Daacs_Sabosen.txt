'''Daacs''' is a Private Investigator working for himself from his office on [[Nar Shaddaa]]. 

He's not bad looking as long as you like blue and of a respectable height, and is often seen with a death stick in his mouth and a fedora on his head.

==Description==
Almost 2 meters tall, with pale blue skin, midnight blue hair and glowing red eyes, this being is a fine example of the Chiss race. His facial features are not unattractive, though not especially distinctive, nothing particularly stands about them other than the fact that he is not human. His body, as much as can be seen of it, appears to be rather bland, too. Neither fat nor thin, but certainly not muscled, he has the physique of someone who doesn't let himself go, but doesn't keep himself especially fit either.

Currently, and usually, he wears a rather interesting outfit that counteracts his rather plain physical appearance. On the very outside, he wears a long coat, grey in color with deep pockets and a broad trim. Under this, when it is open, a plain white shirt and a pair of grey-and-black pinstripe pants can be seen, complete with a pair of black shoes. Atop his head, sits a black fedora style hat which can easily be tilted to obscure the top of his face, should he so wish.

==Basic Information==
'''Daacs''' is far from well known, even on his current planet of residence, Nar Shaddaa. Though he'd like to tell you all that he often takes glamorus murder mystery cases, and works for the richest beings in the galaxy, this would not be true. He more often than not is responsible for simply tracking someone's unfaithful husband for a few days, or making sure someone's wife isn't spending too much money on the credit cards. Most of his work is boring, and the rest is just tedious.

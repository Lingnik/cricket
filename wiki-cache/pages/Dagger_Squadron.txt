'''The 102nd New Republic Starfighter Operations Squadron''', better known simply as '''Dagger Squadron''' or as '''The Dagger''', is an [[Y-wing]] / [[B-wing]] squadron attached to the [[New Alderaan]] military garrison at [[Ord Mantell]]. It is part of the [[New Republic 22nd Starfighter Wing|22nd Starfighter Wing]] in the [[New Republic StarOps|New Republic Starfighter Corps]]. They are a three-tier squadron, with three different flights (four pilots and ships to one flight; Third Flight is NPC).

== History ==

The squadron was stationed aboard [[NRSD_Crusader|NRSD ''Crusader'']], before being assigned to the flagship of the [[New Republic Navy]]'s [[New Republic Second Fleet|Second Fleet]], the [[NRSD Reprisal|NRSD ''Reprisal'']]. The squadron has recently seen a fresh start with the rotation of several pilots in, as well as returning Dagger [[Johmac Mabru]] taking on the role of squadron CO, with his Ghost XO [[Gene Malantean]] transferring over in the same role as well.  As of [[15 ABY]], it has been redeployed to the [[New Alderaan Military Base]] at [[Ord Mantell]].

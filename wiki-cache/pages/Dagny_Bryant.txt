'''Dagny Bryant''' (nee '''Berken''') was the wife of [[Darrien Bryant]] and [[Commissioner of Commerce]] during [[Kadgie Rodo]]'s term as [[Presav]]. She was designated as vice president by Rodo, having run on her nomination to bring more stability to an otherwise chaotic [[Caspian Democratic Union|Union]] culture. 

She was born on the planet Andor, and served in the [[:starwars:Rebel Alliance|Rebel Alliance]] as a navigator. Her last assignment placed her into [[Dergan Roj]]'s Fleet, which brought her to Caspia after the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor|Battle of Endor]]. There, she retired her military commission (many in Roj's Fleet either did this or extended theirs into the new Union military), and was hired by [[FineLine Spaceways]] as a ship captain. Bryant put maximum effort into the next year, covering more routes than anyone in the shipping company. At the end of the year, she was made a managing partner, and by the end of the next, having helped double the holdings of FLS yet again, was envested as CEO. 

In [[7 ABY]], she was approached by Rodo, who admired her business aplomb, to direct Caspia's economy as the Commissioner of Commerce. When Rodo won the election, Dagny would have to relinquish control of FLS to a new CEO &mdash; [[Benjamin Jawatha]].

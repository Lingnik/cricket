Dante Enfer has been rescued from his life of freelance wandering and recruited into the ranks of Gundark Industrial where he does various odd jobs. He holds onto his taboo philosophy of travel, hating to stay too long in the same place. Among his defining characteristics are his boyish voice and his ability to turn cherry red when embarrassed.

== Description ==

You see before you a young human male appearing to be no more than 25 years old with pale paper-white skin. His is not tall nor is he short. He is simply an average height with an average weight. Those with muscles would describe him as scrawny, while those who are thin might call him fat. His overall appearance is average. His hair is jet black and rests upon his head in a messy style coming to an end just at the center of his eyes. His eyes are sapphire blue and rest in his pale face like diamonds in the rough. There is a tiny scar under his left eye that appears to be the only abnormal thing on the normal face.

He wears upon his body brand new professional looking clothing that obviously cost a pretty penny. His pants are tight-fitting and as black as the hair upon his head. They are tucked into a pair of shiny polished black boots accompanied by a shiny black belt that is really more for show than to hold up the already perfect fitting pants. His shirt is a nave blue V-cut with sleeves that cut off just short of the wrist. The shirt hugs his average body revealing what little muscle lies underneath. Around his shoulder is a small brown messenger bag that appears full of whatever small objects one might put into it.

'''Darenak Alesis''' was an [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] bomber pilot assigned to the [[Imperial First Fleet]]. He was handpicked by [[Danik Kreldin]] to serve in [[Black Stars]] squadron after the retirement of [[Jermaine Kerexis]]. Later, he would serve as commanding officer of [[Darksword Squadron]] during the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]].

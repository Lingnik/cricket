'''Dark Side Adept''' was a generic term to describe any [[Force]]-sensitive individual in the [[Galactic Empire]] other than [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]] and [[:starwars:Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Darth Vader]], the two [[Sith]].

None of the Dark Side Adepts were Sith; Palpatine held on to the Sith code of only two. Instead, the Dark Side Adepts were Force-talented users who received training by the Sith (but were not privy to [[Sith Order]] teachings) or other Dark Side Adepts, or were [[Dark Jedi]], having already received training from the [[Jedi Order]] or other institutions.

The Dark Side Adept's position in the Empire was separate from the Imperial chain of command; they existed outside of it, serving only the Emperor. Various groups collectively made up the Dark Side Adepts. The highest ranking group was the [[Inquisitorius]]. They were followed by the [[:starwars:Prophets of the Dark Side|Secret Order of the Empire]], the [[Emperor's Hand|Emperor's Hands]], the [[Dark Side Elite]], the [[:starwars:Emperor's Reach|Emperor's Reach]], [[:starwars:Emperor's Voice (Galactic Empire)|Emperor's Voice]], the [[:starwars:Emperor's Eye|Emperor's Eyes]], several members of the [[Imperial Royal Guard]], and various Imperial Dark Jedi.

With the fall of Emperor Palpatine, the Sith Rule of only two changed. No one can be quite certain when this change happened, however by the time [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Bacharan Valak|Valak]] ascended the throne, multiple apprentices were being trained in more than how to wield the Dark Side of the Force but in the principles and teachings of the ancient [[Sith Order]] as well.

'''Dauthus Snunb''' was a [[Sullustan]] [[Admiral]] in the [[New Republic]] [[New Republic FleetOps|Navy]] and commanded the main battle fleet around [[9 ABY]]. He had previously been a member of an underground movement on [[Sullust]] aimed at overthrowing the [[SoroSuub Corporation|SoroSuub]]-controlled government. He later joined the [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Alliance]] shortly before the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]].

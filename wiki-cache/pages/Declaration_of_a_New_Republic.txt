The '''Declaration of a [[New Republic]]''' was a document signed one month after the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]] by the eight leaders of the [[:starwars:Alliance_to_Restore_the_Republic/Legends|Alliance to Restore the Republic]]. It spelled out to the public-at-large the government system and ideals of freedom, democracy, and justice that the Alliance hoped to return to [[the Galaxy]].

The signatories were:

*[[Chief of State]] [[:starwars:Mon Mothma/Legends|Mon Mothma]] of [[Chandrila]]
*[[Prince|Princess]] [[Leia Organa Solo|Leia Organa]] of [[:starwars:Alderaan/Legends|Alderaan]]
*[[:starwars:Borsk Fey'lya|Borsk Fey'lya]] of [[Kothlis]]
*[[Admiral]] [[:starwars:Gial Ackbar/Legends|Gial Ackbar]] of [[Dac]]
*[[:starwars:Sian Tevv/Legends|Sian Tevv]] of [[Sullust]]
*[[:starwars:Jenssar SoBilles|Jenssar SoBilles]] of [[Duro]]
*[[:starwars:Doman Beruss (Corellian)|Doman Beruss]] of [[Corellia]]
*[[:starwars:Kerrithrarr/Legends|Kerrithrarr]] of [[Kashyyyk]]
*[[:starwars:Verrinnefra B'thog Indriummsegh|Verrinnefra B'thog Indriummsegh]] of [[:starwars:Elom (planet)/Legends|Elom]]

The first four of these signees became the [[Inner Council]] of the [[Provisional Council|New Republic Provisional Council]].

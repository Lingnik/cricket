The '''Defender Sporting blaster''' has the look and feel of BlasTech's renowned 
DL-series, with the infusion of style into the basic design. The slim handle
and elongated muzzle are sleek and functional, affording additional accuracy 
while stil maintaining the durability and balance of the classic DL-series. 
The age of "sporting" blasters is waning, and with it, fewer and fewer 
sporting blasters hit the market. In essence, the gun has a damage rating in
acceptable civilian levels, as is supposed to afford enough accuracy that it
can be used for recreational shooting. The Defender is one of the best 
examples of this.

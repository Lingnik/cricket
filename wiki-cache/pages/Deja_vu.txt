Subject loses control of his/her perception of time and repeats, physically and mentally, the actions he/she took when specified by the Jedi. There is a limit to 10 minutes of history for this power, and the duration of "replay time" varies from a few seconds to 15-30 seconds for a powerful Jedi. Example usage: A Jedi uses this power on a Stormtrooper, prying an event from the Stormtrooper's mind that has occurred within the last 10 minutes. The Stormtrooper will do his best to replicate this event, for a period of time that is determined by the strength of the Force user. This is an effective, if comical, combat power, though the real efficiency of this power may be to get unwitting informants to provide important information or clues about their recent activity. This power requires "Affect mind" as a prerequisite.

'''Demon Squadron''' was an unit of the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]] stationed aboard the [[I2SD Tormentor|HIMS ''Tormentor'']]. 
The squadron would participate in several of the major battles in [[11 ABY]]. It was led by [[Danik Kreldin]] until, along with fellow Demon pilot [[Fianar Reanus]], his creation of the [[Black Stars]] unit. Several of Demon Squadron's most prominent pilots would find their way into the Black Stars.

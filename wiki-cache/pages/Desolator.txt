The '''''Desolator''''' was an [[Imperator-class Star Destroyer|Imperator I-class Star Destroyer]] in service with the Imperial Navy. She was destroyed during the [[Battle of Bothawui IV (25 ABY)|climactic engagement]] over the world of [[Bothawui]] in [[25 ABY]].

The '''''Desolator '''''was under command by Admiral [[Solomon Fields]] at the outset of the Republic campaign to liberate the planet. She served as command ship for the duration of the campaign, and at Fields' insistence fought from the front, twice leading Imperial defenders against the Republic fleet.

During her final moments, the '''''Desolator''''' suffered heavy damage breaking through the Republic assault line, swinging around and destroying her opposite number, the [[MCSC Deliverance|NRSC '''''Deliverance''''']] with supporting fire from the Victory II-Class '''''Annhilator''''', the only surviving Star Destroyer class vessel of the conflict.

The '''''Desolator''''' was ultimately destroyed by a reactor breach caused by retaliatory fire from the [[New Republic Assault Frigate|Republic-class Assault Frigate]] '''''Derlin '''''under the command of Lieutenant-Commander [[Argon Brand]]. Although she went down with all hands, the totality of her ground forces, fighter squadrons and landers were disembarked at the time.

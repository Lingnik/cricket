TITLE: Detoxify poison
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2007-04-04T02:37:15Z
======================================================================
This [[Force powers|power]] allows a [[Jedi]] to detoxify or eject poisons that have entered her body. If the Jedi makes the power roll, the poison doesn't affect her.

The power takes about five minutes to use.

==Control Difficulty==
Very Easy for a very mild poison, such as alcohol; Easy for a mild poison; Moderate for an average poison; Difficult for a virulent poison; Very difficult to Heroic for a neurotoxin.

=== Background ===
'''Devin C. Frothe's''' father, Gareth C Frothe, was an Imperial Naval officer during the Galactic Civil War who died during the Battle of Endor. Devin was only 7 years old when it happened, but his father had always been a very important figure in his life. Gareth Frothe's influence on Devin's life continued to be strong even after his death. It bred within Devin a hate of the Rebellion which has carried over to that of the New Republic. This carried with it a distrust of any non-human species.

When Devin turned 18 he applied to and was accepted in the Imperial Naval Academy. He seemed to follow in his father's footsteps with his excellent piloting and gunnery skills. But his skills weren't limited to space, he turned out to be a good marksman with blasters as well. Fully indoctrinated before even entering the academy, his test scores were also above par. Devin graduated with highest honors after three years and joined the Imperial Navy as a fighter pilot.  He was quickly assigned to [[War Shrike Squadron]] as War Shrike 3.


=== Recent ===
Devin Frothe recently was involved in a mission to [[Bothawui]] as part of his duties for [[War Shrike Squadron]].  He was shot and wounded in what turned out to be a terrorist ambush inside an Imperial military outpost.  The shot hit him in the right shoulder and knocked him to the ground.  It was his first real combat situation.  Devin did manage to pick up a fallen stormtrooper's blaster and shoot one of the terrorists, despite his injuries.  He has met the green Twi'lek that shot him (now in custody on [[ISC Broadsword|HIMS ''Broadsword'']]) but does not yet know any names.

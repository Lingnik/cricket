The Dhagon One was a heavily modified [[Lone_Scout-A|Lone Scout-A]] scout starship commissioned by the [[Security Division (CSA)|CSSD]] and produced by [[Sienar Fleet Systems]] as a first-response combat ambulance. Its large cargo hold was reconfigured as a small med bay, with four retractable bio-beds, medical sensors and equipment, pharmaceutical and provisions storage, and accommodations for a crew/team of three.

The Dhagon One had extensive work done on its hyperdrive and sublight engines, as well as reinforcement of its hull.  The ship was designed to get from point A to point B as fast as possible.

==Current Assignment==

The Dhagon One was stationed aboard the [[CSAV_Corporate_Takeover|CSAV Corporate Takeover]].  It was assigned to the ship's Chief Medical Officer, [[First Lieutenant]] [[Xar'on Tanner]], though other medical personnel were permitted to use the ship as necessary.

===OOC Info===

The Dhagon One was named for an Old Republic era doctor [[:starwars:Dhagon_Ghent|Dhagon Ghent]], a doctor on Onderon who freely admitted getting into medicine purely for the money nearly 4,000 BBY.  A portrait of Dhagon Ghent was displayed in Dhagon One's cargo hold.

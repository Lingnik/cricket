'''Dieter Vichten''' was the CEO of [[Galactic Shipping & Freight]] and half-brother to infamous smuggler and spice dealer, [[Axel Vichten]]. Dieter scored a huge victory for GSF by securing rights to collect the [[Death Star III]]'s debris through the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] after it was destroyed in [[11 ABY]]. Several years later, GSF would go under and Dieter secluded himself away.

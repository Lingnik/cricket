This power is similar in nature to [[Accelerate healing]]. It allows the Sith to grow or modify their skin and bones to a minor degree, altering their appearance. The alterations can include modifications to coloration and creation of growths, such as horns or dull spikes. These alterations are permanent, and can only be undone through the [[Force powers|Force power]] Accelerate healing.

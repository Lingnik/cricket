'''Division Heads''' (also called "Branch Heads") are usually normal players on the game who are given OOC leadership roles by their respective [[Organization Head|Faction Heads/Organization Heads]]. They are enabled to direct the sub-branches and divisions of the [[:Category:Organizations|Organization]] they belong to. These players volunteer their time in overseeing the basic aspects of their division. While these players are not official members of the MUSH Administration and therefore usually have no special administrative powers, they are the players that the [[Organization Head|Faction Heads]] rely on to help lead the smaller divisions of their faction. Things that a Division Head takes an active role in include:

* providing direction for their division/branch.
* maintaining the theme and "feel" of Star Wars in their specific division.
* motivating players inside their branch to RP and help create stories. 
* communicating with fellow division heads.
* communicating with other faction heads and org leaders to drive plot and create story. 
* upkeep on SW1ki pages relevant to the branch.
* uphold the MUSH's, and the faction's, OOC policies. 

=== Examples ===

* The [[New Republic]] utilizes '''Branch Heads''' to further subdivide responsibilities in the [[New Republic Military]].
* The [[Galactic Empire]] utilizes an '''Imperial Player's Council'''. Players on the council help fill the roles of both Division Heads and [[Organization Head]]s.

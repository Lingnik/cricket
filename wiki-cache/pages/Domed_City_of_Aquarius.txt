The '''Domed City of Aquarius''' was the underwater center of civilization on [[Dac]]. 

Although many other [[Mon Calamari]] cities existed above the oceans, Aquarius was the only one completely underwater. Encased in a large bubble, it was designed to allow water and air breathing species to live together, including [[:starwars:Whaladon|Whaladon]]s. The city's proximity to the underwater [[Quarren]] inhabitants made it a common ground for both species.

== The Dacan Civil War ==

The [[Whaladon Pub]], a cantina in one of the city's seedier areas, was the site of racial tensions between Dac's native species in early [[16 ABY]]. As history is retold, many would claim that this is where the [[Dacan Civil War]] of [[16 ABY]] truly began.

Stolen, with a lot of luck, from [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] Customs, the '''''Doppelganger''''' was a wildly-painted [[Guardian-class Light Cruiser]] piloted by [[Axel Vichten]].  It earned quite the name for itself in many sectors of the galaxy. Heavily modified, the ship saw more trips to [[Kessel]], [[:starwars:Sevarcos II|Sevarcos II]], [[Ryloth]], and any other planet that produced high end spice, than likely any other ship in the galaxy.

Ever since he stole it, Axel has spent hundreds of thousands of credits keeping it in perfect condition inside and out, as well as integrating the latest in technology - advanced shields, engines, and armor plating.  Easily one of the fastest ships of its kind, the ''Doppelganger'' is widely-known as a blockade runner and general nuisance.

{{forcepower|
power=Doppleganger|
domain=Control
}}
This power creates an illusion of the user, which is real in all respects to anyone who interacts with it. Even electronic sensors detect the doppleganger as real. The doppleganger can act on its own, or under the control of the user. The user can use all normal senses through the doppleganger, and the illusion acts with half the skill rolls of the user. The user must roll every 5 minutes to maintain the illusion, against a target's Willpower roll. If a roll is failed, the power dropped, or the doppleganger is mortally wounded, it simply fades away.

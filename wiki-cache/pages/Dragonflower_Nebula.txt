The Dragonflower Nebula is celestial body in the Doldur Sector. One of the larger nebulas in the galaxy it is a notable source of prothium gas used in blaster weapons. Machenry Station, later known as [[Silver Station]] was built to harvest and process this gas. As such the nebula as served as a base of operations for [[Crestian Tarasar]] since [[14 ABY]] and his primary base of operations from [[17 ABY]] to [[27 ABY]], his title in the [[New Sith Order|Sith]] court of "[[Duke]] of Dragonflower" is a reference to this fact. During [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign's]] absence from the wider galaxy, Tarasar's flagship [[Leviathan]] was hidden in the nebula and charted it extensively.

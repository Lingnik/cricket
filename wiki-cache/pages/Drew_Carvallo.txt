'''Andreya Carvallo''', or '''Drew''', was known for being all charm, flash, and brawn. She had turned her hand at such occupations as security, bartending, cage dancing, and card-dealing.

Not much was known about Drew's life before she enlisted in New Republic GroundOps about 20 years ago. If asked, she'd say something about waiting tables and living on those old [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] pleasure ships. She could shuffle a deck like the best of them and had a perplexing breadth of knowledge, ranging from how to install solar panels to classical Twi'lek dance; so there might have been more to it than that. 

As for the not-so-mysterious bits, she got a battlefield commission in her mid-twenties and quit the military soon thereafter. Then came her work for kOrg. She faded in and out of prominence there, doing boring administrative bits and some more exciting stuff when she was in the mood for trouble.

After a sudden disappearance 7 years ago, Drew has been seen lately in Corellia.

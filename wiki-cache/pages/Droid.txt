'''Droids''' are mechanical devices, often capable of intelligent and creative thought. Droid-types range from simple units that can only follow basic commands, to highly developed units capable of translating millions of languages or assisting in the piloting of a starship. Higher function droids are programmed with personalities, are capable of acting independently and making decisions; some droids even seem to have emotions. In short, they are an electronic form of life. 

On Star Wars MUSH, humanoid droids (such as Protocol Droids, or Servant Droids, or similar variations therein) are available to be played as characters by players. This race is restricted, however those interested must apply to the Wizards on staff describing the type of droid they would wish to play, along with a brief background/character description to explain what they would be like and what purpose they would intend to serve.

Droids ([[Player Character|PC]] or [[Non-Player Character|NPC]]) featured on Star Wars MUSH are listed at [[:Category:Droids]].

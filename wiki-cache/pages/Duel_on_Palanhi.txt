The '''Duel on Palanhi''' was a minor engagement between the [[Sith]] and [[Jedi]] on the remote planet of [[Palanhi]].

After the [[Battle of Etti IV]], [[Han Solo]] was taken away by the [[Dark Lord of the Sith]], [[Sabbathius Osbourne]]. [[Luke Skywalker]] and [[Leia Organa Solo]] began a search for the [[New Republic]] hero, which led them to Palanhi. There, the two Jedi siblings engaged the Dark Lord in a harrowing and chaotic lightsaber clash.

Ultimately, neither side would emerge victorious. Sabbath, outnumbered, fleed with his prized posession, leaving both Skywalker and Organa Solo behind to trace the Dark Lord. The two Jedi would succeed in their endeavors, and Han Solo would later be rescued.

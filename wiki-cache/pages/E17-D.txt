The E-17D is considered the New Republic's primary long-range personnel and light vehicle interdiction blaster rifle. While not a sniper rifle per se, it is a highly accurate and effective weapon, and when equipped with a high-powered scope is easily capable of killing shots at significant ranges. It has a five round magazine capacity.

OOC Note: This weapon, while canon, is not coded into the game. It may only be included with the consent of all parties present for the scene.

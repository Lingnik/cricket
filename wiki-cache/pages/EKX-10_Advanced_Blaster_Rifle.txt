TITLE: EKX-10 Advanced Blaster Rifle
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2006-10-10T04:17:35Z
======================================================================
The EKX-10 is an attempt to combine the best technological features of the BlasTech EE-3, A280, and Sharpshooter V carbine to produce a replacement for the venerable E-11. This was successful to an extent. The resulting weapon is lightweight and compact, hits just as hard as the E-11, yet is capable of engaging targets at ranges of the A280 and SS-V. While up to BlasTech's usual standards of reliability, many military evaluators have criticised the weapon as being hard to maintain and repair in the field.

The EKX-10 is 89.2cm long, with the housing for the power cell being mounted in the weapon's fixed shoulder stock to give smooth ergonomic lines.  The weapon is capable of using 35 or 100 shot power packs. The composite foregrip incorporates attachment points for a wide variety of accessories, and a standard fitting for scopes and optics is mounted above the receiver.

'''''Edgecrusher''''' is a [[Guardian-class Light Cruiser|''Guardian''-class Customs Cruiser]] that started its life as '''CSAV ''Profit'''''. With a past sketchier than even its current owner's, not much is known about the ship's history from the time she was first put together to the time she came under the ownership of [[Krackor Reincus]].

It is known that CSAV ''Profit'' was one of several ships owned by [[Zeak Oppenhiemer]] around the time of [[:starwars:Jabba the Hutt|Jabba the Hutt]]'s death. It was around that time when Krackor came looking for work and was picked up by Zeak. Krackor was allowed to use ''Profit'' for many of the jobs given to him by Oppenhiemer as well as for personal use and any side jobs that came up.

Eventually, Zeak found himself in some kind of trouble and had to disappear from the galaxy at large. In doing so, he passed over ownership of ''Profit'' to Krackor, who immediately had the ship's name changed to ''Edgecrusher'' and began outfitting the ship with a new paint job and expensive modifications.

Since coming into Krackor's possession, the sleek black ILC known as ''Edgecrusher'' has seen many adventures across the galaxy. Thousands upon thousands of credits have been spent upgrading her to make the ship faster and hardier as her owner pushes her limits(and his own luck) to the max.

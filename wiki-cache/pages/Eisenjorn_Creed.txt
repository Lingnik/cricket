== Overview ==
Eisenjorn Creed is widely regarded as one of the greatest tactical minds and least trusted men in the Empire. He is the commanding officer of the [[203rd Legion|203rd Stormtrooper Legion]], a mentor of [[Aldus Thel]] and presently serves as the Field Marshal of the [[Neo-Imperial Movement|New Empire]].

Eisenjorn is the co-author of the comprehensive strategic treatise ''The Principles of Imperial Warfare ''and has been a career-long advocate of expanding Stormtrooper recruitment to include women and near-humans of satisfactory moral character.

Creed is a heavy smoker and known to favor Shento-brand cigarros.

== Background ==
Growing up in the slums of [[Carida]], Eisenjorn was shaped by developing principles of the New Order that began to emerge as the Clone Wars unfolded. He joined the [[Sub-Adult Group]] of [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] during its earliest days and attended the Imperial Stormtrooper training facilities located on his very doorstep.

Eisenjorn's advancement from such humble origins to the heights of power is the result of a carefully calculated campaign. Superior tactical awareness resulted in rapid promotion through the lower ranks of a Corps keen to secure cloned troopers beneath natural-born human officers fully seized of the ideals of the New Order, but having reached the rank of Lieutenant found his superiors taking credit for his insights. Riding the coat tails of a brighter name while doing the ''real'' work of war would have been tolerable, but Creed soon recognised a pattern of being assigned to less qualified commanders who then saw prompt promotion courtesy of 'the Eisenjorn bump'.

Determined to bring an end to this thankless exploitation of his faculties, Eisenjorn engineered a number of high-profile military blunders from which he personally snatched victory. The acclaim earned through these acts of heroic command ability transformed Eisenjorn from a closely kept secret to one of the most sought-after adjutants in the legions. His trajectory corrected, Creed was promoted to Major shortly after the Battle of Yavin.

Creed's allegiance in the [[Post-Endor Imperial power struggle|Post-Endor Power Struggle]] fell to [[Bacharan Valak]], whose famously pragmatic views on the origin of capable officers permitted Creed's long coveted promotion to Major-General, a position he held throughout the [[War of the Throne]]. Active opposition to the coup by [[Lorn Rhys|Darth Malus]] made Creed one of many senior officers with whom association became a political poison pill and he found himself exiled to the fringes of Imperial Territory throughout the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg|Blitzkrieg]]. It was during this seclusion that he was introduced to another political pariah, [[Solomon Fields]]. The two co-authored a comprehensive treatise on military strategy, informed by their collective experience and led by a critical analysis of the unfolding campaign.

In 21 ABY, Eisenjorn's 203rd Legion were the favored assault force of [[Aldus Thel|Aldus Thel's]] Task Force ''Nemesis ''and Creed's strategies were instrumental in the decisive victory against the [[New Sith Order]].

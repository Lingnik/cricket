'''Elom''' was a cold and barren desertlike planet that was home to the '''Elom''' and [[Elomin]] species. The Elom lived in the caves of the [[:starwars:A'driannamieq Mountains|A'driannamieq Mountains]] while the Elomin lived on the surface.

==History==
The [[:starwars:Galactic Republic|Galactic Republic]] first made contact with the [[Elomin]] in [[:starwars:132 BBY|132 BBY]] but with the Eloms only in [[:starwars:50 BBY|50 BBY]]. Some scientists hypothesized that the Elomin were descended from a lost [[:starwars:Zabrak|Zabrak]] colony. Upon the world's discovery, the [[:starwars:Galactic Senate|Galactic Senate]] hailed it as a significant find, due to its large deposits of [[Lommite]]. Lommite was mined on Elom for use in [[transparisteel]], and was the cause of the first interaction between the Elom and the Elomin. The planet was represented in the Galactic Senate by Rennimdius B'thog Indriummsegh.

===Conquest by the Empire===
Such a valuable world could not be left to its own devices, at least in the view of the [[Galactic Empire]]. After the planet was conquered by Imperial forces, the Elomin were forced to slave away in the lommite mines. Since the Empire had not discovered the cave-dwelling Elom, they were able to help some Elomin escape slavery. This further bonded the two species together. The more technically advanced Elomin worked to sabotage Imperial mining efforts. Many Elomin joined the [[:starwars:Rebel Alliance|Rebel Alliance]], with the liberation of their homeworld their driving force.

===Under the New Republic===
After the [[New Republic]] liberated the planet, the two species formed an alliance. Elom was a founding member of the [[New Republic]], represented on the [[Provisional Council]] by Verrinnefra B'thog Indriummsegh. The planet was later represented in the [[New Republic Senate]] by Rattagagech. During the [[:starwars:Thrawn Campaign|Thrawn Campaign]] the remoteness of Elom made the Elomin worried of repeat aggression by Imperial forces operating unobserved. They instituted a massive armament program as a preventive measure. Elomin that served in the New Republic military during its early years tended to serve in homogenous units, though this began to subside as the Elomin became more comfortable around other species.

Along with fellow Outer Rim world [[Dac]], Elom is regarded as one of the more important hub systems for New Republic military endeavours and commerce. The planet frequently serves as an interface (or watchpost, whichever the case may be) for interaction with the Tingel Arm and the [[Corporate Sector Authority]]. After the fall of [[Coruscant]] in [[15 ABY]], several hundred thousand Republican refugees were seeded on Elom to continue commercial and fabrication efforts while gubernatorial and martial resources went to Dac, [[Nak Shimor]], and other locales.

'''Empire Day''' was an annual celebration marking the [[:starwars:Declaration of a New Order|Declaration of a New Order]] by [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]] in [[19 BBY]] and the establishment of the [[Galactic Empire]].

The first Empire Day, declared by Palpatine, was a planet-wide celebration on [[Coruscant]] that marked both the rise of the Empire and the conclusion of the [[:starwars:Clone_Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]].

After that first celebration, Empire Day became an annual celebration across the Empire. It was still held by the Empire as of [[15 ABY]].

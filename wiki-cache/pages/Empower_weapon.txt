This power is used to extend a field of energy around a melee weapon to create the effects of a "light" weapon. When used on a sword, the blade becomes infused with energy and has all the properties of a lightsaber (i.e. the weapon is able to parry lightsabers, can be used with the Lightsaber Combat skill, able to parry blaster bolts, etc.). Except that damage is reduced to 4D (and like a true lightsaber, the strength of the user makes no difference to the damage). The power has similar affects on daggers, staffs, axes, etc.

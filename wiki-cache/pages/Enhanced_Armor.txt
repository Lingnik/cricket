TITLE: Enhanced Armor
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2005-12-18T16:29:59Z
======================================================================
Plates of lightweight but durable composites and alloys shroud the wearer of
this state of the art infantry armour system from head to toe. A skeleton of 
reinforced structural supports and servo pseudo-muscles enables the wearer to
easily handle heavy weapons. Such suits invariably come equipped with an 
array of sophisticated sensors and targeting electronics, as well as life 
support systems and an assortment of other accessories. In addition to having
attachment points for various weapons on locations like the shoulder or the 
forearm, this suit also has the capability to grasp most common infantry 
weaponry in its articulated gauntlets. Another attachment point in the middle
of the back can take such accessories as jump packs or space thruster packs 
for improved mobility. Unlike heavier, larger suits of power armor, or 
walkers, this nimble augmented combat exoskeleton moves in a manner which 
closely mimics an unarmored human being - it is perhaps one of the most 
agile and versatile of such systems found in The Slice today.

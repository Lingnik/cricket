'''Epicanthix''' are a [[Near Human]] species known for their cultural refinement and their martial character. They are native to the planet [[:starwars:Panatha|Panatha]] in the [[:starwars:Pacanth Reach/Legends|Pacantha Reach]].

== Biology and Appearance ==

The physical appearance of Epicanthix is nearly identical to baseline [[Human|Humans]]. Their faces are considered to generally be longer than average for Humans, with more narrow eyes. Black hair is ubiquitous among Epicanthix. They tend to be agile and well muscled, with high import placed on physical training and readiness for combat. Some Epicanthix possess a natural unconscious talent to shield their minds from mental based [[Force powers]], which has seen them sometimes sought out by the [[Sith|Sith Order]] for use as spies.

== Society and Culture ==

The Epicanthix are known for the equal import they place on matters of culture and a warrior lifestyle. This duality is reflected in multiple ways in their society. A high value is placed on physical aesthetics in both man and machine, their starships for example are known for being graceful, yet deadly. When they attained the ability to reach out past their homeworld, they conquered their neighbours in the [[:starwars:Pacanth Reach/Legends|Pacantha Reach]]. As conquerors they immersed themselves in the art, literature, and music of their subjects, considering those as valuable as any material wealth acquired from their conquests.

The Pacantha Reach is relatively isolated from the rest of the galaxy. In spite of their appreciation of culture they largely stayed confined to their own area of space. Likewise the wider galaxy's knowledge of them was limited through most of their history. Epicanthix making their way into the wider galaxy often found the experience to be overwhelming, often choosing to return home.

== Epicanthix in the Galaxy ==

Though not unknown to the galaxy, Epicanthix tend to be a rare presence. Usually they tend to work as mercenaries or other martial oriented occupations, though their appreciation of the arts finds some focused on more cultured pursuits. One of the most prominent member's of the species outside the Pacantha Reach is [[Philinda Jorgan]], the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] Minister of Intelligence. Former [[Caspian Democratic Union]] Ambassador to the [[Corporate Sector Authority]] and [[Caspian Navy|Caspian Naval]] [[Captain]], [[Sadim Gnik]], is half Epicanthix.

== OOC Notes ==

For the purposes of game mechanics a player wishing to have a Epicanthix character would choose [[Near Human]] during character generation.

The '''''Eternal Vigilance''''' is a [[Marauder-class Corvette|''Marauder''-class corvette]] in the service of the [[Vigilance Security Corporation]].

==History==
===The ''Vendetta''===
The ''Marauder''-class of corvette was originally designed by [[:starwars:Republic Sienar Systems/Legends|Republic Sienar Systems]], later to become [[Sienar Fleet Systems]]. ''Vendetta'' was an early constructee, and one of the few ships to find its way into the Republic fleet, as the class in general was never fully adopted by the Republic's military. 

The ''Vendetta'' served in a number of battles and skirmishes throughout the Clone Wars, but was sold as surplus when the conflict had ended and new, more effective designs were brought online. It then began its varied career. 

The ship was purchased by the planetary defense forces of Kolae, where it spent the majority of its time in port. Kolae, a minor planet, had limited funds and personnel, but deployed the corvette once in a great while, especially when pirates threatened the system.

It was during one of these infrequent patrols that it was captured by a pirate group. This group put the ''Vendetta'' to good use, or rather, to more frequent use. It served a new tenure on the fringes of the galaxy, operating away from the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] fleets, preying on minor shipping lanes and backwater planets. Its masters finally met their end and the ship was once again sold in an asset liquidation auction.

===''Eternal Vigilance''===
Decaying in a vast reclamation yard, the ship was found by [[Gren Delede]] who had recently severed ties to [[Security Division (CSA)|Corporate Sector Security]]. Although the ship was two years older than he was, Delede thought it the ideal craft around which to start anew.

It was promptly rechristened the ''Eternal Vigilance'' and placed as centerpiece to Delede's new venture, the [[Vigilance Security Corporation]]. After Gren Delede's departure, command of the corvette fell into the capable command of [[Twila Virda]] or [[Xar'on Tanner]], depending on any given mission's profile.

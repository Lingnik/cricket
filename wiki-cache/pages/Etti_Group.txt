'''The Etti Group''' (founded ~14 ABY) is a venture capital firm incorporated in the CSA. Its physical presence consists of a mailing address of a small office on Theseus Station, in orbit around Etti IV.  The group's corporate filings list [[Zeak Oppenhiemer]] as the CEO and sole officer.

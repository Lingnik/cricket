TITLE: Evumeimei
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2025-10-20T07:19:34Z
======================================================================
Evumeimei was born on Panatha to two [[Epicanthix]] parents of a rather prestigious family. Not sharing the same talents for the warrior lifestyle and lacking a heart for killing, Evumeimei chose to leave home at emancipation age and strike out to pursue her passions. Her love for flying is equal to her love for the merchant's life. She chose to go into business for herself, transporting her goods from one side of the galaxy to the other. To start, Evumeimei took on odd jobs with those who already owned ships in order to one day own her own 'used' vessel debt free. Evumeimei has slept in unusual places and done her budgeting in unusual places like private tables in cantinas or cafes on different worlds. She is very much in the 'bootstrapping' phase of starting her own business. Her hard headedness and resolve have fed her commitment to own and operate her own business without going into debt to anyone, including her own family.

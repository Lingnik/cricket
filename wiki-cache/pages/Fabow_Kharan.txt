'''Fabow Kharan''' ( &ndash; [[14 ABY]]) was the [[Captain]] of the [[I2SD Predator|HIMS ''Predator'']]. He was killed during a confrontation with the [[New Republic]] in which concussion missiles fired by [[Severin Tanbris]] pierced through the bridge, killing Captain Kharan and several of his bridge crew instantly.

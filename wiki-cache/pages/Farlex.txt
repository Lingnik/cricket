'''Farlex''' was a mining and agricultural world in the [[Outer Rim]], on the very edge of [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]]-occupied territory. In [[14 ABY]], the [[New Republic]] [[Battle of Farlex|attacked]] Farlex, achieving total control over the system after the siege of '''Morai''', the planet's capital.

The '''Faylar System''' is a far-flunged system deep within [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] territory in the Outer Rim. Faylar's only claim to fame was during the [[Post-Endor Imperial power struggle]], when its only inhabitable planet - [[Dreven]] - became the capital of the remnants of the Galactic Empire under the [[Interim Ruling Council]].

Faylar is difficult to navigate and has thus far remained distant from the [[Galactic Civil War]]. It is believed the [[Third Death Star]] was constructed within the Faylar System, as it provided a natural cloak for the superweapon.

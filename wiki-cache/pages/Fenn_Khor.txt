'''Fenn Khor''' was a [[New Republic Marine Corps|Marine]] in the [[New Republic]] Marine Corps. He would later defect to the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] in the aftermath of the [[Second Battle of Chandrila]], personally providing [[Danik Kreldin]] with information about the subsequent Republic [[RPlog:Danik's Trap|attack]] on the Imperial prison on Chandrila.

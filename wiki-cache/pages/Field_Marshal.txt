A '''Field Marshal''' was a rank in certain armed forces throughout the [[The Galaxy|Galaxy]]. Generally speaking, a Field Marshal was the highest obtainable rank in a faction's army. The rank of [[Marshal]] in the [[Union Marines]] is sometimes referred to as Field Marshal to differentiate it from the rank of Marshal (Star Marshal) in the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]].

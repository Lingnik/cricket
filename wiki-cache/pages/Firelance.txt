This SoroSuub "'''Firelance'''" Blaster Rifle has a long history of being "the most
effective weapon for freelance law-enforcement officials." It is designed
specifically for bounty hunters (and killers) and features an easy grip and
lightweight body that can be carried anywhere. These weapons are traditionally
unmarked by [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] or SoroSuub codes in the event the weapon has to be
gotten rid of quickly. A long over-weapon handle allows easy stowing and
carrying of this weapon. Its nozzle is some ten centimeters long and slightly
flared at its heat dissipating nozzle. Its stock is thin enough to allow the
weapon to rest comfortably in the crook of an arm or against the shoulder.

'''Firrerreo''' are a golden-skinned [[Near Human]] species originally from [[:starwars:Firrerre/Legends|Firrerreo]]. The majority of their species was wiped out when [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[:starwars:Procurator of Justice|Procurator of Justice]] [[:starwars:Hethrir|Hethrir]], himself Firrerreo unleashed a strain of the [[:starwars:Hive virus|Hive Virus]] on Firrerreo. Surviving Firrerreo are those who were off world at the time and their descendants.

== Biology and Appearance ==

Firrerreo largely resemble [[Human|Humans]] physically. The most notable difference is gold coloured skin, which turns silver when angry or frightened, scars on Firrerreo are also silver in appearance. Their hair is made up of stripes of two colours. They have nictitating membranes protecting their eyes from adverse conditions and can see in the ultraviolet range.

Their physiological differences compared to Humans are sufficient to confuse medical scanners that are not specifically programmed for their species. They are known for their rapid healing, unless their brain or heart was effected, even something such as a direct blaster shot would not be fatal. A minor would could heal within minutes, while a major one could heal within hours. Such is speed of their healing that one could see a wound close with the naked eye.

They are also known for their extreme metabolism with Firrerreo requiring a great deal of protein in their diet. Their canine teeth are highly developed and they will often pick out the meat in their meals to consume first before eating the rest. They can hear in the upper range. The behaviour and features of Firrerreo lead to them often being seen as beautiful yet feral.

== Culture ==

Traditionally Firrerreo were known to be very individualistic. They tend to care about themselves over others, their clan over others, and their species over others. Some of these behaviours have shifted since the loss of their homeworld while others have been reinforced. There is a certain tendency among some of their species now to provide support to others of their race when the circumstances present themselves, though this is far from a universal tendency.

== History ==

The majority of the species was wiped out when the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[:starwars:Procurator of Justice|Procurator of Justice]] [[:starwars:Hethrir|Hethrir]] decided to show his loyalty to the humanocentric Empire by unleashing a deadly strain of the [[:starwars:Hive virus|Hive Virus]] on his homeworld. The virus wiped out the population within days and the world was subsequently quarantined. Prior to this act he abducted thousands of his people placing them in suspended animation on freighters traveling at sublight speeds with the aim to colonise distant worlds beyond the Rim for the benefit of the Empire. The number of Firrerreo in the galaxy are quite rare.

== Names ==

Firrerreo believed that to know another's name was to have power over them. As such, it is rare for a Firrerreo to give out their own true name except to those to whom they are especially close. To this end, Firrerreo will often refer to themselves or others in conversation simply as 'Firrerreo.' In other cases Firrerreo will adopt an alias to use publicly such as in the case of [[Brackett Benduday]].

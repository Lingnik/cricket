The rank and title of '''First Sergeant''' is a senior non-commissioned officer's rank used mostly by army organizations. They are highly regarded as both experts in their particular field and natural leaders.

=== In New Republic Service ===

First Sergeants in the '''[[New Republic GroundOps|New Republic Army]]''' hold the same pay grade as a [[Master Sergeant]]. However, with usually around fifteen standard years of service, First Sergeants are one of the most vital assets a company can have. If the Platoon Sergeant is the "Platoon Father", then the First Sergeant is the "Father of the Company". They are ultimately responsible for ''everything'' that the company does, whether it is training, missions, or personnel. It is their responsibility to ensure that all soldiers meet the standards set by the CO and the Director of Ground Operations.

'''Fleet Captain''' is a rank used in the [[Caspian Navy]], it ranks below [[Commodore]] and above [[Captain]]. The most prominent Caspian Fleet Captains are usually in command of a [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]]. The rank is also often held by the Executive Officer of [[Admiral|Admiral's]] flagships as well, who are effectively serving as flag captains. It is largely equivalent to the rank of [[Line Captain]] in the [[Imperial Navy]].

The primary differentiation between a Captain and Fleet Captain in the Caspian Navy beyond the class of vessel they command is that a Fleet Captain may be trusted at times with operation authority over multiple warships whereas a normal Captain will not baring exigent circumstances. When such exigent circumstances arise, the Captain in question may be promoted to Fleet Captain assuming they performed the assignment successfully. 

The primary differentiation between a Fleet Captain and a Commodore in the Caspian Navy beyond the class of vessel they command is that a Commodore will be regularly engaged in command over multiple warships while a Fleet Captain will only do so on occasion. Commodores in Caspian Defence, Patrol, and Groups range between the second and fifth highest ranked naval officer in the group and often of exercising authority over a large segment of the group if not the group itself. Fleet Captains commanding their own vessels are generally only commanding other vessels on an ad hoc basis. Fleet Captains serving as flag captains will usually only exercise larger authority under the close direction of Admiral in overall command.

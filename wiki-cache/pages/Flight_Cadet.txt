'''Flight Cadet''' is a military rank derivative of the [[Cadet]] title, and is almost always associated with members of a starfighter corps. These are often recruits who have yet to complete their academy training.

=== In Imperial Service ===
These were recruits for the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]]. As soon as they finished training at the academy, they were promoted to the rank of [[Pilot Officer]].

=== In New Republic Service ===
These were recruits for the '''[[New Republic StarOps|New Republic Starfighter Corps]]'''. As soon as they finished training at the academy, they were promoted to the rank of [[Second Flight Officer|2nd Flight Officer]].

'''Flight Commander''' is a rank in the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]] which is generally held by an officer commanding a 'flight' of four starfighters and the personnel attached. They rank below [[Squadron Leader|Squadron Commanders]] and above [[Flight Lieutenant|Flight Lieutenants]]. It is equivalent in rank to a [[Lieutenant|Senior Lieutenant]] in the [[Caspian Navy]] and to a [[Captain]] in the [[Union Marines]].

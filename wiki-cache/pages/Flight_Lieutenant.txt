'''Flight Lieutenant''' is a military rank that is generally a junior commissioned officer rank in a starfighter corps. 

== In Imperial Service ==
{{rank plate|[[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]]|}}
A '''Flight Lieutenant''' in the Imperial Starfighter Corps was usually delegated more authority than its counterpart in the Navy; a Flight Lieutenant was usually a squadron's executive officer, or in rare cases, the commanding officer. Usually a Flight Lieutenant was responsible for a single flight in a squadron, but also could be an aid to a senior officer.

A Flight Lieutenant was the equivalent to a [[Lieutenant]] in the [[Imperial Navy]]. 

== In Union Service ==

In the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]] '''Junior Flight Lieutenant''' (pronounced Leftenant) is the lowest commissioned officer rank, granted to those who have successfully completed the flight cadet training (or rarely senior support service personnel). When held by someone in support staff it does exercise authority over non-commissioned and enlisted personnel, when held by a pilot such authority would be limited. It is equivalent in rank to an [[Ensign]] in the [[Caspian Navy]] and to a [[Second Lieutenant|Sub Lieutenant]] in the [[Union Marines]]. 

'''Senior Flight Lieutenant''' is the next highest rank in the Caspian Starfighter Corps. This rank is awarded to more seasoned pilots who have proven themselves (or rarely be granted to senior support service personnel). Each squadron of in the starfighter corps is made up of three 'flights' which will typically be led by a [[Flight Commander]] (or possibly the [[Squadron Leader|Squadron Commander]] themselves), with a Senior Flight Lieutenant and two Junior Flight Lieutenants. The Senior Flight Lieutenant will have authority over the Junior Flight Lieutenants and take command if the Squadron Commander is absent or incapacitated. It is equivalent in rank to an [[Sub-Lieutenant|Junior Lieutenant]] in the [[Caspian Navy]] and to a [[First Lieutenant|Lieutenant]] in the [[Union Marines]].

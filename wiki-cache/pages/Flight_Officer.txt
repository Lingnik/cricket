'''Flight Officer''' is a military rank that represents an officer near the lower levels of a commissioned rank scale.

=== In Imperial Service ===
{{rank plate|[[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]]|}}
Pilot Officers were promoted to Flight Officer upon receiving training and had proven their worth to their commanding officer through successfully managing given tasks and assignments.  Flight Officers generally did not recieve any command authority, but were placed in charge of teaching the younger Pilot Officers.  Their only real authority was over Pilot Officers and non-commissioned officers.

A Flight Officer was equivalent to a [[Sub-Lieutenant]] in the [[Imperial Navy]]. 


=== In New Republic Service ===

Along with [[Second Flight Officer|Second Flight Officers]] in the '''[[New Republic StarOps|New Republic Starfighter Corps]]''', these pilots made up the backbone of the Corps. Support personnel who held this rank were usually given command over a small group of Second Flight Officers in support of their assigned post.

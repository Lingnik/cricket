The following is the food menu for the [[Grotesques and Odalisques|Grotesques and Odalisques: Requiem for Basca Starker]] party held at the Hall of Heroes at [[Castle Tsabak]] on [[Dreven]] hosted by [[Malif Tal-Tahn]] and presented by [[Zubindi Hakoon]] and the [[Silverwood House of Fashion]].

==Black Platter Offerings==

The black platter offerings while presented for their 'grotesque' nature are in true fact often the height of cuisine in the respect cultures they originate from. As this is a party that is being presented by the [[Silverwood House of Fashion]] it should come as no surprise the largest grouping of black platter offerings are insect dishes as is central of Kubazi cuisine. A smaller selection of black platter meat dishes and black platter vegetarian dishes are also making their way around the party.

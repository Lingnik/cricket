== Broken infobox template? ==

The [[Template:character infobox|<nowiki>{{character infobox}}</nowiki>]] template seems to be broken, as we've all noticed I think. It skips every other line and basically colours it entirely white making it unreadable unless someone does a 'right click/select all' type thing on it. Is there a way to fix this, or should we go back to the old style boxes until a better one comes along? --[[User:Raybatsford|Raybatsford]] 19:21, 12 May 2008 (UTC)

{{user:xerxes/sig}} Modified the [[MediaWiki:Common.css|common stylesheet]].  Explicitly defined the color/background-color for the infoboxlabel and infoboxcell classes.  This seems to make things properly visible, but breaks the alternating row javascript. -- [[User:Xerxes|Xerxes]] 17:38, 13 May 2008 (UTC)

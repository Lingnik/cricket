*starwarsmush.com -is- the official website, but it is no longer in use. Hasn't been updated in eons. Hawke/Rtufo is the man who designed that website and was responsible for its upkeep, but from what I remember, he has since lost access to it. Cypher/Minkar would be the ones with access to it. However, I doubt they have renewing that site at the top of their priorities. It hasn't really been used in a long time. The Wiki was created as a means to get around starwarsmush.com's lack of relevance and updates. It's recognized on the MUSH and was, for a time, very widely used there, but after a few scuffles here and there (with people being upset about the Wiki-format), it hasn't been used as much as it use to be. But for the most part, all information and events on the MUSH are transported here instead of the official site. --[[User:Danik Kreldin|Danik Kreldin]] 22:27, 8 July 2008 (UTC)

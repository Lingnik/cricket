This is a reminder to everyone that a wiki is a collaborative endeavor.  In the spirit of that collaboration, it is very important that everyone is allowed to participate as they see fit.  Barring violations of MUSH or Wiki policies, it is unnacceptable to unilaterally delete the comments / contributions of others.  If someone says something that you disagree with or otherwise take issue with, you are free to avail yourself of the same means to voice your opinions and consensus will sort out the rest.  I'm sure everyone has the best intentions, but censoring others is not the proper means to your ends. -- [[User:Xerxes|Xerxes]] 19:23, 3 February 2007 (UTC)

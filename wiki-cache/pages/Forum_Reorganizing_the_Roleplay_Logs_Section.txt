For instance, I notice some players have the same logs under their characters but with a different name. Some logs should be shifted under a TP-specific section, and the others that are just unique to the player and whatever the scene pertains to remains as they are. For instance, I can move all the "Cochran Saga" logs out from under Danik and put them under their own header in the TP section, the "Desolation of Cochran" header or what have you. --[[User:Danik Kreldin|Danik Kreldin]] 16:50, 26 September 2006 (UTC)

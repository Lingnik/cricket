Dr. '''Fria Manden''' was the chief engineer for the [[Death Star III|third Death Star]] five months into its development. She was given control over the project after a slave laborer revolt resulted in the death of [[:starwars:Bevel Lemelisk|Bevel Lemelisk]], the chief engineer for the previous [[:starwars:Death_Star/Legends|Death Stars]], who had been appointed by [[Bacharan Valak]] to construct the technological monstrosity. 

Fria would oversee the completion of the battlestation. After it was finished in [[8 ABY]], Fria was redeployed to oversee construction of new Imperial weapons at [[Orbital Station Guardian|OS Guardian]]. However, she would meet a gruesome end at the hands of Valak in [[Castle Tsabak]] after the [[Death Star III]] was [[Third Battle of Sluis Van|destroyed]] by the [[New Republic]].

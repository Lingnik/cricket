Evolved from the languages of the [[Core Worlds]], Galactic Basic, also called Galactic Basic Standard, has become the common language of the Core as well as nearly every human-dominated planet. It initially came into use as a language of commerce and trade in the early stages of the Old Republic and has spread along with the human population.

Its origins must have been the vernacular speech of [[Human|humans]] and is partially derived from several ancient human languages like [[Olys Corellisi]]. Humans being the dominant species of the Galaxy, Basic has been adopted by many alien species as well, and dialectical versions can be found particularly among non-humans on human-dominated worlds.

The '''Galactic Confederation''' was a galactic government established in [[10 ABY]] under the leadership of [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Bacharan Valak]], as part of his plan for galactic conquest. The Confederation was promoted by Valak as the means to bring peace to the galaxy by bringing the various galactic governments together in a Confederation, directed by a Senate and an Executive Body, composed of Senators from each sector.

At summit held on [[Selene]] members from the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], the [[Griffon Alliance]], and the [[Corporate Sector Authority]] met with Imperial diplomats led by [[Zeak Oppenhiemer]] and Valak himself to draft and finalize the [[Galactic Confederation Charter]]. Senators were chosen to represent their territories, veto power over initiatives granted to the Union and Griffon Alliances and a Chief Executive was elected and appointed a staff.

In truth, the confederation was a puppet government of worlds held hostage by [[Death Star III|Valak's Death Star]] and bound into servitude by the Charter. The [[New Republic]] was denied entrance, branded as a terrorist organization in occupation of Imperial worlds, rather than a legitimate government. Although the threat of annihilation held the member states in obedience to the charter, the apparatus of the Confederation itself was completely ineffective in achieving even small acts of cooperation.

The Confederation would not last long. Less than a year later, in [[11 ABY]], the Griffons violated the Charter, assisting the Republic in destroying the Death Star at the [[Third Battle of Sluis Van]]. With the Empire's leverage over the Confederation destroyed, the Charter proved useless and the Confederation collapsed.

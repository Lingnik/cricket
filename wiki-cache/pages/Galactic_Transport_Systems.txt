'''Galactic Transport Systems''' was a transport company founded approximately [[16 ABY]]. [[Scaven Marx]] and [[Tyy'sun Eson]] both had a hand in the corporation's establishment. During the [[Dac Civil War]], GTS established a mission to [[Dac]], selling transport off the war-torn planet to civilians seeking refuge elsewhere.

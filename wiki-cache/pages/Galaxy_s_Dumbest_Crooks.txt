The '''Galaxy's Dumbest Crooks''' also known as the '''Galaxy's Dumbest Criminals''' is an occasional segment found on [[Thurston Unger|Thurston Unger's]] [[The Cutting Edge]] programme on [[InterGalactic News]]. The segment debuted in [[32 ABY]]. As one expects from the name, the programme highlights crimes within the galaxy that have had humorous complications, largely do to the dumb moves by the criminals themselves.

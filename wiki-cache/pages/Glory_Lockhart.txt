A rising young starlet whose indie holofilm success has propelled her into intergalactic fame, and netted her a contract with the resurgent [[Starlight Studios]].

==Biography==

This redheaded minx, beloved of the males of virtually all species, was born on Carakhan -- or was it Corellia -- or even Calaris? The answer changes, depending upon which interview or press release you're reading. And how old is she, exactly? No matter how one does the mathematics, something doesn't add up.

Still, no one could be blamed for a bout of numerical illiteracy when contemplating the young actress whose curvaceous frame has started a new fashion for fuller-figured beauties, and inspired who knows how many thin girls to eat their first decent meals in years.

...

In 21ABY came the much-publicised announcement that she had signed a three-picture deal with Starlight Studios. It is her hope that this move will establish her as a serious actress, help her shed her wholesome farmgirl image, and perhaps even put an end to the milk jokes.

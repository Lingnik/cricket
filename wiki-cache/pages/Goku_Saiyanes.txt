'''Goku Saiyanes''' ( - [[8 ABY]]) was a [[Rodian]] forged from pain and slavery. He considered himself to be a honorable warrior broken by his past and remade in the image of chaos and greed. His journeys had been long and difficult, though successful, working his way up from slave to successful entrepreneur. He became CEO of [[Nappa Creations Incorporated|Nappa Creations Inc.]], though was executed in [[8 ABY]] for the attempted assassination of the [[Office of the Auditor General|Auditor General]] of the [[Corporate Sector Authority]], [[Morrison Van Sen]]. His reasons behind the attempted assassination are unknown, though after his death, NCI was retaliated against by the CSA, and nearly destroyed, until [[Delila Dmitri]] took hold of the company and rebuilt.

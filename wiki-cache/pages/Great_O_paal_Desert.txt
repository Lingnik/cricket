The '''Great O'paal Desert''' covers a majority of [[O'paal]]'s surface. The capital city of [[O'paal Major]] is located within the Desert, strategically located near a large source of water and food in the typically barren desert.

O'paal has been covered by its desert for several ten thousand years, a result of a catastrophe the primitive natives called the '''Jar'kai Ming''', which has yet to be translated into [[Galactic Basic Standard|Basic]], most records and historical documents relating to the O'paali having been lost millennia ago during the [[O'paal Civil War]].

Archeological digs led by [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] surveyors in the years following the end of the [[:starwars:Clone_Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]] indicate that O'paal was once covered in a vibrant tropical environment, with numerous villages and temples spread throughout. Many prominent Imperial scientists have concluded that the Jar'kai Ming was likely equivalent to a modern day [[Base Delta Zero]], although not to the extent the [[Imperial Navy]] executes it. Friezes uncovered in ancient O'paali ruins display images of fireballs raining from the skies; the primitive O'paali would not have been able to determine they were blasts from orbiting starships.

During the reign of the Galactic Empire, the Great O'paal Desert was used as a training sight for the [[Imperial Military]]. It provided soldiers a perfect test in a harsh, unforgiven terrain, against many hardships. The desert would also provide a safe-haven for fugitives and criminals, although how safe it truly is is hard to determine; most who enter the desert without a means of transportation, without essentials such as food and water, are likely to perish within weeks, or less.

The Empire warns citizens to stay within city limits and to avoid the desert at all costs. The Empire does not bother with search and rescue parties to those unfortunate enough to lose themselves in the desert, and the local government is just as lax in this regard.

'''Grei Lar''' was a [[human]] from [[:starwars:Druckenwell|Druckenwell]] who rose to a position of prominence in the [[Caspian Navy]]. He was an aloof, industrious opportunist who would gain the confidence of higher-placed officials all during his time in [[Caspia]], culminating in a chair position and rank of [[Admiral]] during the [[Avalyshaar Dusten|Dusten]] administration. Through development of the Nycad Naval Post within [[Kichnar Orbital Station]], he again put himself in a position to become an administrator, and later governor of the huge space station. He would frequently alienate his peers and contemporaries, and at one point in early [[8 ABY]], disappeared from KOS. Evidence and speculation pointed to his walking out an airlock without an EVA suit.

== Known History ==
Not much is known about Grei Lar's background before arriving in Caspia at some time around [[0 BBY]]. Allusions were made that Lar was involved, in some manner, in the early Rebel Alliance military, but to what official extent is unknown. He was an able starfighter pilot, and was already in a command position when the likes of [[Lawton Fraser]], Hawk Wildheart, [[Mick Kuhndog]], [[Doron Rtufo]] and [[Joshua Arands]] began to arrive in the ranks of the new starfighter corps between [[3 ABY]] and [[4 ABY]].

Whether due to lucrative connections or to beneficial happenstance, Lar and Fraser came out of the [[Second Zealot Wars]] on equal standing. Because of the losses incurred during the conflict, a great deal of rebuilding and retraining would have to be undertaken, and Fraser took recruitment, training, and development to heart. By contrast, the ever-auspicious Lar embraced command and restructuring, and by demonstrating efficiency &ndash; albeit on paper &ndash; was given more and more responsibility.

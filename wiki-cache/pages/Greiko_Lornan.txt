'''Greiko Lornan''' was a successful businessman, auctioneer, [[swoop]] pilot, and one-time [[Neo-Imperial Movement|revolutionary]].

Greiko's first attempt at the business world was [[Sigma Star Industries]]. It was developed as a partnership company alongside [[Fianar Reanus]], specializing in [[starship]] repairs and the selling of used ships through auctions. In secrecy, the company was a front for the Neo-Imperial Movement, which Greiko had cast his lot in.

Greiko would also gain ownership over [[Communications Corporation]] sometime in [[12 ABY]]. Ever ambitious, Greiko successfully wrestled control over the Neo-Empire from his partners, [[Danik Kreldin]] and Reanus, and exiled the two while gaining control of their [[Marauder-class Corvette|''Marauder''-class Corvette]], the ''[[Truth and Reconciliation]]''.

He would be responsible for the [[Kashyyyk Massacre]] and ultimately veered the Neo-Empire from its goals of ousting [[Lorn Rhys]], waging a series of terror campaigns against the [[New Republic]]. Kreldin and Reanus eventually fought back, initiating the [[Neo-Imperial Movement Mutiny]] and the [[Assault on Sigma Star Industries]], but were ultimately unsuccessful in stopping Greiko: he had lured them into a [[Trap on Etti IV|trap]], and the Neo-Empire collapsed.

Afterwards, Lornan sold off the ''Truth and Reconciliation'', Sigma Star Industries, and ComCor, and established a swoop business on [[Caspar]] and took up racing. His shop would service and sell swoops, and was one of the finest such shops registered with the [[Galactic Swoop League]]. His racing skills won him several tournaments.

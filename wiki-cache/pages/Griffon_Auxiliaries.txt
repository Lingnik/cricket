The '''Griffon Auxiliaries''' were a collection of supply ships, support vessels, and armed merchantmen which supplemented and supplied the [[Griffon Alliance]] and its [[Griffon Armada|Armada]]. The personnel within this group were not of outright warrior calling, but contributed greatly to the everyday success of the Griffon forces. They performed feats of logistical magic and technical brilliance.

The Auxiliaries also included roving, "independent" merchantmen which were in constant circulation around the [[the Galaxy#Mid Rim|Mid Rim]], transporting goods, wares, and passengers from one side of [[the Slice]] to the other. They were more than likely crewed by levied spacers and soldiers, who were given a percent of any profits made on the behalf of Griffon society. The Griffons favored small, modular freighters and several thousand of these contracted ships were buzzing about the region at any one time. Because of [[Cochran]]'s treasure-world status (at the time), there were no shortage of interested buyers on the market, and Griffons were not scrupulous about who they dealt with, so long as it wasn't the Empire outrightly.

During their activities, the Auxiliaries would also act as an [[Intelligence|intel]]-gathering organization, relaying information that would not otherwise be evident to formal government agencies.

Auxiliary crews and technicians were also highly prized for their engineering and repair skills, as they seemed able to make field repairs rapidly and easily. A possible factor for this was the aforementioned favoritism toward modular components, which took a great deal of guesswork out of the equation, but there were several technologies in Griffon use that were complex in their own right. More than likely, because of the diverse array of backgrounds that became interwoven into Griffon society, they were knowledgeable of a broad range of technologies and applications.

The Griffon Auxiliaries were scattered to the Galaxy during the fall of the [[Griffon Alliance]] at the hands of the [[Galactic Empire]]. When [[Pride-1]] was destroyed, and [[Scythia]] taken, their activity was left without a hub, and travel was greatly hampered. Later, at the [[Desolation of Cochran]], many lost their lives trying to support the Armada and Armee during their valiant defense of the capital world, with most of the survivors either seeking refuge within the [[New Republic]] or scattering to the dark corners of the galaxy.

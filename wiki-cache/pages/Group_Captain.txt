'''Group Captain''' is a military rank usually given charge of an entire group. It is almost always associated with members of a starfighter corps, most notibly the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]]. These are often seasoned officers who reported to a commander of a task force for all matters related to flight operations throughout the group.

== In Imperial Service ==
{{rank plate|[[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]]|}}
In the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]], a Group Captain generally commanded an entire group. A typical group would consist of several wings depending on the task force assembled.  A group held at least 4 wings and at a minimum had 288 ships, 480-1200 officers, and 720-2160 enlisted. They were addressed as captain.  If a group had more than one Group Captain, one would be appointed Group Captain and the other Group Executive Officer. If appointed as a staff officer they would act as an assistant to a [[Fleet Commodore]].

A Group Captain was the equivalent to a [[Captain]] in the [[Imperial Navy]].

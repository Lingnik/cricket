'''Grymm Selcan''' was a famous [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] starfighter ace, noted for his service in the [[Black Stars]] unit after its reorganization by [[Danik Kreldin]] in [[12 ABY]], months before the outbreak of the [[War of the Throne]].

Originally assigned to [[Demon Squadron]], Selcan was handpicked by Kreldin due to his record and skills to serve in the Black Stars. However, with the onset of civil war, Selcan would side with the Loyalists and become a prominent member of the [[Neo-Imperial Movement]]. He was arrested on [[Etti IV]] alongside Kreldin during the [[Trap on Etti IV]], but would be released months later, miraculously avoiding [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] agents.

Selcan would rejoin the Empire in [[13 ABY]] and would serve in the early days of the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]].

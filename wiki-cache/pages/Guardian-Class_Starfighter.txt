Before [[Haor Chall Engineering]] was reformed and the [[Xi Char]] had joined the [[Caspian Union]], [[Presav]] & [[Sar Admiral]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] had contracted Xi Char engineers for a special project. Based at [[Lokkai Starworks]] on [[Kasputin Yar]], engineers from Lokkai, [[SubPro]], and the Xi Char were tasked with the recommissioning and refitting of the ten surviving [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruisers]], only one of which was still in service. One of the main goals was to drastically cut the crew requirements which Tarasar believed their skills as both droid manufacturers and shipwrights would give them the insight to accomplish.

While both [[Charros IV]] and Kasputin Yar were relatively close to one another, both the [[Xi Charrian|Xi Charrians]] and [[Yari|Yaries]] were infrequent travellers and this project was the first large-scale and sustained interaction between the two species. Meeting the Yaries though, the Xi Charrians found they had a surprising number of things in common. They were both species far larger than most sentients; on average, the Yaries were even taller than them. They both engaged collective decision making and had a similar social structure. There were certainly just as many differences, but when compared to other species of the Caspian Union they were partnering with, the Yaries were the ones they related to the most.

When it came time to conceive of new starfighter designs for Union starfighter pilots and augment the [[Mark IV Vulture-Class Droid Starfighter|Vulture-Class Droid Starfighters]] that were taking the place of the [[L-200 Halcyon transport|L-200 Halcyon Transports]] on the Daring-Class, the Xi Char looked first to the Yaries. The Yaries were known as practically being biological supercomputers. Yet, based on their physiology among other facts, the roles they played in the [[Caspian Defence Force]] were limited, certainly no one before had ever conceived of building a starfighter a Yari could use. As premiere builders of Droid starfighters, the Yari presented the Xi Char with an interesting idea, no one had conceived of using Yaries as starfighter pilots, but could they be used in places of Droids?

With Haor Chall Engineering back in existence development of a '''Guardian-Class Starfighter''' began in earnest. The design would be built upon one a rogue Xi Char engineer had custom designed several decades earlier, the [[:starwars:Guardian Mantis|Guardian Mantis]]. The basic look would stay the same, but rather than have a pilot, gunner, navigator, and an astromech droid hardwired into the ship, it would instead have a single Yari who would perform all these functions instead.

Advances in technology in the six decades since its initial design as well as the wealth of rare and unique minerals found in Caspian Space allowed for the modern design to field improved hull capacity and shielding without a sacrifice to maneuverability and speed. As it was designed, like the Mark IV Vulture-Class for use on the [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] and on the Daring-Class Battlecruisers, the engineers and Haor Chall Engineering and Lokkai Starworks developing the product opted for slower hyperspace capability, as anything faster did not fit its mission profile.

A unique feature of the original Guardian Mantis was an [[:starwars:Ion encumbrance system|Ion Encumbrance System]]. The system launched small ion-enabled "tags", projectiles which attached to enemy vessels and caused an ionization effect which disabled their deflector shields. These were deployed in a Nano Missile Magazine. Haor Chall Engineering would work with engineers at Sublight Products Corporation to advance this technology for continued use with the design.

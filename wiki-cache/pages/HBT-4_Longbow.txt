The '''HBT-4''' is a blaster rifle intended for the civilian market, and is made 
with the purpose in mind of bringing down animals, rather than armed 
sentients. Though slow to cycle and long and bulky, it is able to fire a 
blaster bolt at distances that would be well out of reach of your typical 
infantry blaster rifle, and it is powerful enough to bring down all but the 
largest game - including an armoured human being. Most of these weapons are 
furnished with an elegant wooden stock, much in the same manner as older 
slug-throwing rifles, although variants exist with synthetic stocks which 
are somewhat more weather resistant. A very old weapon, rarely seen among 
the modern populace anymore. Prolifically found on backwater planets and 
often in the hands of young adults learning the ways of firearms.

The [[:starwars:Golan Arms/Legends|Golan Arms]] '''HH-15 projectile launcher''' was a handheld missile launcher widely used by [[:starwars:Rebel Alliance|Rebel Alliance]] ground forces. It could fire a variety of projectiles, including miniature [[:starwars:Concussion missile|concussion missiles]] and [[:starwars:Proton torpedo/Legends|proton torpedoes]]. The HH-15 was older than its [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] counterpart, the [[:starwars:MiniMag shoulder-launched proton torpedo launcher|MiniMag PTL]], and had inferior targetting sensors. However, many Rebel soldiers became quite adept at aiming the missile launcher manually.


OOC Note: This weapon, while canon, is not coded into the game. It may only be included with the consent of all parties present for the scene.

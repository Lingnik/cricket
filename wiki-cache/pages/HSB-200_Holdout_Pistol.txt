TITLE: HSB-200 Holdout Pistol
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2006-02-03T04:09:17Z
======================================================================
Like most hold-outs, this weapon is just barely large enough to be gripped in
the average human-hand. Sighting systems and other protusions are generally 
kept to a minimum, so that the weapon won't get caught in clothing when one is
attempting to draw it. The weapon's trigger guard encloses the whole front of
the handgrip, making it easier to hold onto in close quarters. Rather than 
using any sort of safety catch, this weapon uses a 'grip safety' to allow it 
to be brought to bear quickly - it won't fire unless it is actually being 
held. An 8-shot cylindrical charge clip fits into the heel of the weapon... 
enough ammunition to last a little under three seconds, given this weapons 
quick semi-repeating action.

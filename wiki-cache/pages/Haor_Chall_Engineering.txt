[[File:Haor Chall Engineering.webp|thumb|]] '''Haor Chall Engineering''' is a corporation based on [[Charros IV]] specialising in the manufacture of [[Droid|droids]], starships, and vehicles. It was founded and is operated by the [[Xi Char]] a fanatical religious order of the [[Xi Charrian|Xi Charrians]] that is devoted to high-precision manufacturing. It is a major military supplier to the [[Caspian Defence Force]] and to that end works closely with other [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] tech firms.

== History ==

While Haor Chall Engineering was never on the scale of such companies as [[Corellian Engineering Corporation]], [[Kuat Drive Yards]], [[Rendili StarDrive]], or [[Sienar Fleet Systems]] it produced quality that was sought after. In [[:starwars:45 BBY/Legends|45 BBY]], [[:starwars:Raith Sienar/Legends|Raith Sienar]] infiltrated the [[Xi Char]], stealing valuable information from them while posing as a convert to the order. When he was exposed for what he truly was, the Xi Char twice attempted to assassinate him, their second attempt in [[38 BBY]] resulting in the death of his father, [[:starwars:Narro Sienar|Narro Sienar]]. Because of his actions, the Xi Char refused to take any more Human apprentices, fearing betrayal.

Haor Chall Engineering had a long association with some of the powers that would form [[:starwars:Confederacy of Independent Systems/Legends|Confederacy of Independent Systems]] and instigate the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]]. Haor Chall Engineering designs such as the [[:starwars:C-9979 landing craft/Legends|C-9979 Landing Crafts]], [[:starwars:Sheathipede-class transport shuttle/Legends|Sheathipede-Class Transport Shuttles]], and [[:starwars:Variable Geometry Self-Propelled Battle Droid, Mark I/Legends|Variable Geometry Self-Propelled Battle Droids]] were quintessential elements of the [[:starwars:Trade Federation of Planets|Trade Federation's]] [[:starwars:Trade Defense Force|defence force]] during [[:starwars:Invasion of Naboo/Legends|Invasion of Naboo]] in [[32 BBY]].

During the Clone Wars, Haor Chell Engineering produced a variety of materiels for the Confederacy of Independent Systems. They often collaborated with other Confederation aligned firms such as with [[:starwars:Baktoid Fleet Ordnance/Legends|Baktoid Fleet Ordnance]] on the [[:starwars:HMP droid gunship/Legends|HMP droid gunship]] and [[:starwars:Baktoid Armor Workshop/Legends|Baktoid Armor Workshop]] on the [[:starwars:Trade Federation droid bomber|Trade Federation Droid Bomber]] and [[:starwars:Variable Geometry Self-Propelled Battle Droid, Mark I/Legends|Variable Geometry Self-Propelled Battle Droid, Mark I]]. There were even rumours of the Trade Federation trying to instigate a merger between Haor Chall Engineering and Baktoid Armor Workshop.

The association with the Confederacy would prove devastating as the Republic sent in Clone Troopers led by [[Jedi]] to destroy their facilities. What remained of Haor Chall Engineering was moved off world. In the cruellest twist of fate, following the war [[Sienar Fleet Systems]] absorbed what remained of the company. The merger of [[Sienar Fleet Systems]] with [[Kuat Drive Yards]] would only serve to strengthen the foreign control of the [[Xi Charrian]] manufacturing legacy.

In [[29 ABY]] newly elected [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] open up negotiations with the [[Xi Char]] and the [[Imperium]], the newly constituted successor to the [[Galactic Empire]]. The Imperium was led by Tarasar's former boss in the [[New Sith Order]], [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] now Imperator Damion I. The Caspian Union and the Imperium would quickly form a [[Imperium-Caspian Mutual Protection Pact (29 ABY)|mutual protection pact]] and the Imperator had appointed former Caspian Presav [[Elana Tracer]] as [[Moff]] of [[Selene]] and additionally placed her in charge of [[Kuat Drive Yards-Sienar Fleet Systems]]. Tarasar wanted to see the Xi Char freed from the indignity of having their designs and facilities under the control of corporation that included the Sienar name and felt that Xi Charrian would make logical partners to species such as the [[Yari]] and [[Sluissi]] already working in the Caspian ship building industry.

Over the next several months, Tarasar, Tracer, and [[ak]], a progressive leader among the Xi Char, would negotiate a three way agreement. The leaders agreed they would see Charros IV granted the right to cede from the Imperium and join the Caspian Union. The Imperium in turn would cede Xi Char designs and production facilities to be fully repatriated. In early [[30 ABY]] Presav Tarasar representing the Caspian Union, [[Grand Moff]] Tracer representing the Imperium and KDY-SFS, and [[Prelate]] [[r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak]] of the Xi Char would sign the final treaty.

Even before the negotiations had been completed, Xi Charrian engineers had been involved in [[Caspian Navy|Caspian Navy's]] project to recommission and refit their [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruisers]]. Now a newly reconstituted Haor Chall Engineering was a full partner in the Caspian defence community joining [[SubPro]], [[Lokkai Starworks]], and [[Aston Aerospace]] in building, maintaining, and refitting the [[Caspian Navy]].

In the coming months and years Haor Chall Engineering has worked with their partners in the Caspian Union to bring their cathedral factories back to full operational strength and to modernise some of their old designs as well as produce new models for service in the Caspian Navy.

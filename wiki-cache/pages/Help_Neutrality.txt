======================================================================
Articles on '''SW1ki''' should be written and read from a third-person, neutral, encyclopedic point of view.

This includes articles about [[:category:Characters|characters]], [[:category:Locations|locations]], [[:category:Organizations|organizations]], [[:category:Events|events]], and objects such as [[:category:Ships|ships]], [[:category:Weapons|weapons]], and [[:category:Vehicles|vehicles]].

When writing an article, or a segment of an article, consider yourself a reporter &mdash; unassociated with the subject you are writing about. State events, actions, and terms as if they were factual, and ''could'' be verified by a panel of experts. Avoid the use of emotive, sentimental, or aggrandizing adjectives (e.g. "that handsome man was the best pilot in the whole, wretched Galaxy"), and try to stick to statements that certainly explain details, but keep relative scale and context in mind.

If an emotive statement is ''needed'' to best capture or convey details regarding the subject of the article, use a [[Template:Quote|quotation]], and attribute it to the character, another character, or a contrived source.

'''SW1ki''' does make allowances for bias or certain points of perspective in [[:category:Logs|RP logs]], reports, and [[:category:Stories|stories]]. Those particular pages are distinguished by a namespace tab at the top of the page, and will contain perspectives, tense, and passages that may or may not be neutral. This is understood and expected of these pages.

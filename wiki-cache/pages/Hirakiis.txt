The ''Hirakiis'' is a [[Ghtroc_Class_750_Freighter|modified]] [[Ghtroc_Class_720_Freighter|light freighter]] currently under the command of noted [[smuggler|free-trader]] [[Jaspar Andromidas]].

==Overview==
The ''Hirakiis'' is the pride and joy of Jaspar Andromidas, his first (and so far only) ship. Where it goes, so does he. In the years since coming to possess the ship, Jaspar has tinkered with it, slowly turning it into a ship to be feared... at least, he thinks so.

== History ==
The earliest known record of the Hirakiis - at least under that name - dates back to Kelona, 24 ABY. It was picked up during a random customs check at [[Bothawui]]. Not being in a position to say 'no', Jaspar set his ship down in the yard, and was told to come back after the inspection had finished.

Jaspar headed to [[Nar Shaddaa]], not content to let his ship remain in Imperial custody for one second longer than necessary. There, he met up with noted privateer and pirate captain [[Shiari Vane]], whom he enlisted to [[RPlog:A Smuggler Walks Into a Bar (Part 1)|secure the return]] of his ship, accepting the loss of his cargo - which would have settled his debts with the Hutts - as ''payment for services rendered''.

Eventually, Jaspar [[RPlog:Making New Friends (Part 1)|found himself in the company of]] Tritus Vaticus, a noted figure in the underworld. Over the next several months, Jaspar and the ''Hirakiis'' continued working away, trying to earn back the money owed to the Hutts by running jobs for Tritus, and [[RPlog:Smuggler's Folly (Part 1)|wherever else he could find them]]. Tritus later proved useful in (again) securing the return of the ''Hirakiis'', following a failed job, and Jaspar's [[RPlog:Positions of Power|capture by the Empire]].

Following his escape, facilitated by Captain Vane, during which Jaspar was forced to leave his beloved ship behind, Jaspar learned through the underworld grapevine that the ''Hirakiis'' would be sold off at auction, and began making plans to retrieve it.

In the midst of preparations, Jaspar received word that his friends in the New Republic would be willing to bid on the ship, and tend to its repairs, provided he agreed to work off the costs by performing jobs for them. After recovering his ship, returning to Nar Shaddaa, and [[RPlog:Good Old-Fashioned Business|taking on another job]], Jaspar found himself with enough money to settle his debts and bring the ship under his control at last.

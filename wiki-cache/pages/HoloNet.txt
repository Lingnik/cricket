The '''HoloNet''', sometimes '''Holonet''', was a [[The Galaxy|Galaxy]]-wide communications grid that was developed by the [[:starwars:Galactic Republic|Galactic Republic]] and later used by the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]].

Messages were routed along millions of non-mass transceivers buried in [[hyperspace]] to reach their (sometimes very distant) destinations. This technology enabled data to be sent and received at faster-than-light speeds, ensuring near-instantaneous communication even from far across the Galaxy.

The HoloNet system was an extremely costly expenditure, both for transceiver placement/maintenance (funded by the government) and for consumer HoloNet equipment. During the time of the Galactic Empire, the HoloNet was strictly controlled, used mostly for [[Galactic Empire#The Military|Imperial military]] communications.  This greatly inhibited the ability of groups like the [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic|Alliance]] to communicate, and arguably also had an isolating affect on the many [[:Category:Planets|planets]] over which the Empire held power.

During the [[:starwars:Clone Wars|Clone Wars]], the [[:starwars:Confederacy of Independent Systems|Confederacy]] created a similar network of hyperwave transceivers as a HoloNet substitute.

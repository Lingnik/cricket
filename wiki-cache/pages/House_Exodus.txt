'''House Exodus''' is one of the ruling noble houses in the [[New Sith Order]]. House Exodus is currently led by [[Thane]] [[Aknar Koth'lyri]]. House Exodus' territory stretches from [[Vector One]] to [[:starwars:Ord Grovner|Ord Grovner]], and includes several systems close to the [[Caspian Democratic Union]].

House Exodus' colors are purple and royal blue, on a black background. The House ring is silver with a marquee shaped top showing a small face of a Drexl outlined in blue sapphires and purple amethysts that have an obsidian backing. The House crest is the image of a Drexl in flight on a black background, its mouth open and teeth bared. The Drexl is purple in color, with blue accents on the wings and eyes.

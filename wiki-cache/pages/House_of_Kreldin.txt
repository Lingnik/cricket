The '''House of Kreldin''' was one [[Nobility of Corellia|noble family]] on [[Corellia]] during the reign of the Corellian [[:starwars:Corellian Empire|monarchy]]. [[Davin Keldan|Davin Kreldin]] is the founding patriarch of the family; his vast wealth and connection to the [[Force]] allowed him to gain the favor of the Corellian [[King]]. Davin was made a nobleman under the monarchy and granted land. As a nobleman, Davin was able to commission the construction of his family estate - the [[Kreldin Estate]]. 

The House of Kreldin survived through many centuries under the Corellian monarchy, growing in influence and wealth, the dark lineage of Davin passing down through his descendants. The '''Sindahar Mining Corporation''', founded by '''Johan Kreldin''', became a staple of the Kreldin family. 

When the monarchy was abolished, the power of the nobility too was vanished - though the families retained their wealth, their estates, and some of their influence. The Kreldins remained in the forefront under the new government, with many of the family heirs taking part in the new government and becoming close confidants of the Diktat. 

The House of Kreldin was considered abolished in [[15 ABY]] when the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] destroyed the Kreldin Estate after [[Danik Kreldin|Danik Kreldin's]] attempted coup against the [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]].

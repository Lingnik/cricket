'''Hutt Space''' was a region &mdash; more than likely termed an oversector since it enclosed lesser sectors &mdash; of the [[The Galaxy|Galaxy]] on the border between the [[The Galaxy#Mid Rim|Mid Rim]] and the [[The Galaxy#Outer Rim|Outer Rim]], near the entrance to [[The Galaxy#Wild Space|Wild Space]]. It also encompassed the [[:starwars:Si'Klaata Cluster|Si'Klaata Cluster]], and bordered on the [[:starwars:Tion Hegemony|Tion Hegemony]]. Hutt Space was named for the [[Hutt]] species, who dominated the region.  Differing accounts attributed different numbers of planets in Hutt Space, but reasonable estimates range from a few hundred to a thousand inhabited worlds. The far end of Hutt Space tapered into [[The Maw]].  

Hutt Space was what remained of the ancient '''Hutt Empire''', which existed in the times before [[:starwars:Xim the Despot|Xim the Despot]].  The '''Hutt Grand Council''' controlled the region by overseeing the activities of the Hutt clans (or ''kajidics'') and their criminal syndicates.  As a result, Hutt Space was not the most lawful place in the Galaxy.

During the rule of the [[Galactic Empire]], it was governed by [[Moff]] Sarn Shild, and later by Moff Yref Orgege, though their authority was weak at best. From the fall of the Empire to the present day, Hutt Space seems to have remained largely autonomous.

=== The Hutt Grand Council ===
The '''Hutt Grand Council''', formally called the '''Clans of the Ancients''', was the ruling political body of Nal Hutta and the [[Hutt]] race. Composed of every Hutt clan, the Council ruled all of [[Hutt Space]]. They governed all clans fairly, giving favor to none, and Hutts all over [[the Galaxy]] abided by their decrees. The decision making process was generally unknown, but it seemed that the most powerful of the ''kajidics'' (the Hutt criminal syndicates) held the most authority; thus, the head of the most powerful kajidic was the de facto leader of the Hutt race.

=== Intergalactic Dealings ===
Hutt Space is generally an exotic, lawless region that is equally enticing and forboding. Life has little value here, and enslavement, either monetary or physical, is a frequent punishment for all sorts of transgressions. Just about everything is used and abused, and underhanded dealings are the norm. It's a haven for all sorts of [[Underworld]] characters, such as pirates, smugglers, looters, traffickers, fencers, pimps, and slicers. [[Bounty hunter]]s and henchmen are the only sort of "law enforcement" in the region, and what law they are enforcing at any one moment is all relative.

Due to its proximity, Hutt Space projects a great deal of influence on planets such as [[Ryloth]] and [[Tatooine]], often in a corruptive manner.

The '''Hydian Way''' is a route that begins at the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector]] on one side of the galaxy and continues through the core to the other side, past Eriadu. It is the only route that passes through the entire galaxy. It was blazed 3,000 years ago by famed Brentaal native Freia Kallea, and helped open up much of the galaxy from edge to edge, fundamentally altering the scale of galactic civilization.

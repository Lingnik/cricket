'''HIMS ''Inquisitor''''' was launched early in 4 ABY from Drive Yard X in orbit of [[Kuat]]. It currently serves as flagship for the [[Imperial Navy]]'s [[Task Force Inquisitor]].

== Imperial Service Record ==

=== Battle of Endor ===
The HIMS ''Inquisitor ''was just finishing it's shakedown cruise when the Imperial fleet was given orders to the Endor system as part of a secret plan to crush the [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Rebel Alliance]]. The Inquisitor would serve with distinction, engaging and destroying a pair of Rebel Heavy Cruisers.  With the destruction of the [[starwars:Death_Star_II|Death Star II]] and the death of [[starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Emperor Palpatine]] the Imperial fleet was in disarray and the general order to retreat sent through the fleet.

=== Post-Endor ===
Following the retreat from Endor, the ''Inquisitor '' ended up in the service of Admiral [[Bacharan Valak]]''' '''and tasked with defending the [[Dreven]] system where it would serve honorably until the [[War of the Throne]]. Under the command of loyalists to [[Lorn Rhys]], ''Inquisitor ''was primarily responsible for purging the former capitol of [[Kendal Osbourne|Osbourne]] loyalists. ''Inquisitor''s Stormtroopers would garrison Dreven for the remainder of the War of the Throne, eventually being permanently stationed there when the HIMS ''Inquisitor ''was transferred at the end of [[13 ABY]], with the arrival of the HIMS ''[[I2SD Sovereign|Sovereign]]''. [[Warlord]] [[Vassily Korolov|Korolov]] would transfer his command from the ''Sovereign ''to the Inquisitor when the Imperial Capitol was moved to [[Selene]] following the rise of [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Aleister Vadim|Vadim]]. 

=== Reformation of the Imperial Inquisition ===
The consolidation of Vadim's power led once again for the need for the Imperial Inquisition to track down and eliminate the New Jedi Order as well as traitors in the Empire. The first priority of the new Inquisition was to refit the flagship for it's new job. Space was removed from the standard military detention facilities to install new interrogation suites and special cells to contain force users. The ship also had one of it's cargo bays modified to provide specialized training for the ground forces deployed aboard the vessel.  

By [[15 ABY]], the ''Inquisitor'' had finished it's refitting and was ready to be deployed as the flagship of [[Task Force Inquisitor]] following the Imperial Blitzkrieg and tasked with finishing off the remains of the New Republic and the Jedi. The ''Inquisitor'' would serve as the focal point for the [[Corellian Corruption Scandal]]. It's forces responsible for ending the plot to overthrow the Emperor.

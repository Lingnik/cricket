'''HIMS ''Sovereign''''' began her career in the dockyards of [[:starwars:Yaga Minor/Legends|Yaga Minor]] in [[2 ABY]] as one of the earliest [[Imperator II-class Star Destroyer|''Imperator II''-class Star Destroyer]]s.

''Sovereign'' was originally assigned to patrol the [[:starwars:Yaga_system|Yaga System]] and defend the vital shipyards against the rising [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Rebel Alliance]].

During the chaos that prevailed after the Imperial defeat at the [[:starwars:Battle_of_Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]], ''Sovereign'' was ordered by the [[Interim Ruling Council]] to immediately deploy to nearby [[Dreven]] and await further orders. There, ''Sovereign'' was one of the first vessels to greet the incoming Councilmen from [[Coruscant]], and served as the temporary flagship for the Council during the initial few conferences. 

Months later, ''Sovereign'' would be included into the [[Emperor's Pride Fleet]] and would fight through the entire campaign, from [[Battle of Bandomeer|Bandomeer]] to [[Second Battle of Coruscant|Coruscant]], and was one of the few warships under [[Warlord]] [[Jabir Tagger|Jabir Tagger's]] command to escape with minimal damage.

After the failed initiative to reclaim Coruscant, ''Sovereign'' was assigned to protect Athaniss. As a result, she was largely absent from the campaigns waged by [[Bacharan Valak]]. After Valak's departure from the Empire, [[Vassily Korolov]] had ''Sovereign'' transferred to [[Selene]], where she would become Korolov's flagship. With the rise of Emperor Vadim, the ''Sovereign'' would be transferred to [[Dreven]] as the center of power for the Imperial Court.

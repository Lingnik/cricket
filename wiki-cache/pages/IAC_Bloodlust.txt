== Current Assignment ==
The ''Bloodlust ''was commanded by Captain Blythe from  20 to 25 ABY and was assigned to the [[Neo-Imperial Fleet|Corporate sector-fleet]] to secure the region against hostile forces and piracy at the behest of the [[Imperial Advisor (CSA)|Imperial Advisor]], [[Aldus Thel]].

== Service History ==
Among the first batch of [[Warlord-class Medium Cruiser|Warlord-class]] Attack Cruisers built after the Imperial Blitzkrieg, the ''Bloodlust ''served on numerous fronts and by the end of the war was commanded by then-Captain NoName

== Loss ==
The ''Bloodlust ''was destroyed during a mutiny lead by the Stormtrooper Detachment's leader [[Calyx Drogyn]]. The ship was striped of arms and most of its embarked craft and fighters. The ship was then set to crash into the surface of an uninhabited planet with the crew not participating in the mutiny.

'''HIMS ''Dauntless''''' was a [[Carrack-class Light Cruiser|''Carrack''-class light cruiser]] commissioned in [[13 ABY]] to operate independently on long-range reconnaissance patrols supporting [[I2SD Conqueror|HIMS ''Conqueror'']]. When the ''Conqueror'' was ambushed in [[14 ABY]] near [[Korriban]] and had to make an emergency jump to [[Etti IV]], ''Dauntless'' followed after relaying the distress call to [[Imperial High Command]]. Following the [[Second Battle of Etti IV|battle at Etti IV]], ''Dauntless'' was reassigned to a support role for the [[I2SD Predator|HIMS ''Predator'']]. She was destroyed in combat with the New Republic when she was subjected to heavy bombardment from [[X-wing]] fighters carrying Proton Torpedoes late in [[14 ABY]].

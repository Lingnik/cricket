'''HIMS ''Legerity''''' was a [[Carrack-class Light Cruiser]] of the [[Imperial Navy]] during the revisited [[Galactic Civil War]]. It was a frequent escort of the [[Star Destroyer]] ''[[I2SD Emperor Valak|Emperor Valak]]'', and accompanied it to [[Caspia]] to participate in the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign]]. It would be one of the few Carracks to leave Caspia in a serviceable state after several other Carracks were either damaged, disabled, or destroyed during the course of the campaign while exchanging fire with turbolaser or [[GROWL]] emplacements on Caspar's surface.

A year later, at the [[Destruction of Sluis Van]], ''Legerity'' would receive the surviving command crew of the stricken and scuttled ''Emperor Valak'' and briefly serve as the flagship until another Star Destroyer was secured.

Cmdr Bulloch (NPC)}}'''''HIMS Vulture '''''is an aging vessel, one of thousands built during the height of the New Order to bolster the new sector fleets patrol capabilities. The length of her career is her most distinguishing feature, she is one of the longest serving ''Carracks ''in the fleet.

Most recently, ''Vulture'' has been assigned to escort duty of Imperial aid shipments to Caspar during the Union-Republic scandal. An engagement with the raider vessel [[Crimson Lightning|''Crimson Lightning'']]'' ''resulted in the destruction of Vulture's TIE complement and the capture of the Navy-owned [[Corellian Action VI Transport|Action VI]] ''[[Corulag Haul]]. Vulture'' inflicted light damage on the pirate craft while receiving none in return forcing a withdrawal and preventing the loss of further assets.

'''InterGalactic News''', commonly referred to as [[InterGalactic News|IGNews]], is the major news network in the [[The Galaxy|galaxy]] which has so far remained out of the control of both the [[Galactic Empire]] and the [[New Republic]], but maintains bureaus within each government. The following is a chronological list of IGN broadcasts.

= [[15 ABY]] =

== The [[Retribution of Coruscant]] ==

The [[Retribution of Coruscant]] signaled the end of an intense [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] campaign to reclaim the planet from [[New Republic]] control. These reports covered both the warfare itself, the new [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor's]] first galactic address, and the movement of [[Coruscant|Coruscanti]] refugees.

*'''[[Report:Epic Battle Over Coruscant|Epic Battle Over Coruscant]]'''<br>''(23 July 2006)''
:News coverage of the final space battle over [[Coruscant]].

*'''[[Report:Vadim's First Galactic Address|Vadim's First Galactic Address]]'''<br>''(30 July 2006)''
:[[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Aleister Vadim|Vadim]] addresses the galaxy for the first time from [[Coruscant]].

*'''[[Report:Sullustan Senator Resigns|Sullustan Senator Resigns]]'''<br>''(3 October 2006)''
:[[Senator]] [[Ikihsa Enb'Zik]] Resigns from the [[New Republic Senate]].

*'''[[Report:Refugees Leaving Dac|Refugees Leaving Dac]]'''<br>''(4 October 2006)''
:Refugees having relocated from [[Coruscant]] to [[Dac]] after the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[Retribution of Coruscant]] are rumored to be moving again, under tight-lipped security.

== The [[Corellian Corruption Scandal]] ==

Due to the nature of political scandal, the news feeds were rocked by coverage from [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] and [[Caspian Democratic Union|CDU]] sources alike. Political degeneration threatens to grip both factions, until a shocking announcement triggers the seeming end of the scandal.

*'''[[Report:The Corellian Corruption Scandal|The Corellian Corruption Scandal]]'''<br>''(8 October 2006 - 1 November 2006)''
:A slew of news coverage surrounding the [[Corellian Corruption Scandal]].

:[[Corellia]] [[Governor]][[Bailey Sora Laran|Bailey Laran]] is voted to the position of Sector [[Moff]].

*'''[[Report:Moff Laran's First Changes|Moff Laran's First Changes]]'''<br>''(14 November 2006)''
:Newly elected [[Moff]] [[Bailey Sora Laran|Laran]] institutes several changes to the sector she governs, which includes [[Selene]], [[Corellia]], and [[Coruscant]]. The changes have erupted controversy throughout the sector.

*'''[[Report:CDU Diplomatic Crisis|CDU Diplomatic Crisis]]'''<br>''(19 November 2006 - 21 November 2006)''

*'''[[Report:Open Imperial Police Force|Open Imperial Police Force]]'''<br>''(29 November 2006)''

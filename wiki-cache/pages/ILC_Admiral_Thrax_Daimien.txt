'''HIMS ''Admiral Thrax Daimien''''' was a [[Guardian-class Light Cruiser|''Guardian''-class Light Cruiser]] named after former Admiral [[Thrax Daimien]]. The vessel served as a customs and patrol vessel along the [[Tarkin Corridor]] until its decommissioning in [[11 ABY]].

=== Service Record ===
''Admiral Thrax Damien'' was launched in [[5 ABY]] under the name ILC ''Bounty''. She was built at the SFS production facilty that was part of the expansive fleet yard at Yaga Minor. She was deployed to the planet [[O'paal]] and would serve in the planet's Revenue Collection Service. She operated off of the [[Orbital Station Guardian]].

Following the death of the Chief of Naval Operations, Admiral [[Thrax Daimien]], in [[9 ABY]] during a boarding operation in the [[The Galaxy#Outer Rim|Outer Rim]], ''Bounty'' was ordered to be renamed by Daimien's successor, Rear Admiral [[Lexington Atticus]]. The mission profile would remain the same, though, but she was transferred to a new home aboard [[I2SD Malevolent|HIMS ''Malevolent'']] in early [[10 ABY]]. [[Danik Kreldin]] was later given command of ''Admiral Thrax Daimien'' in early [[11 ABY]], running patrols along the Tarkin Corridor and into [[New Republic]] territory on scouting missions. She was responsible for the intelligence operation to [[Cochran]] shortly before its [[Battle of Cochran|invasion]], crewed by Kreldin and his [[Black Stars]] comrades.

Eventually, ''Admiral Thrax Daimien'' was decomissioned in late 11 ABY.

* HIMS ''Admiral Thrax Daimien'' was renamed the [[ILC Sentinel|HIMS ''Sentinel'']] in [[11 ABY|2002]] as part of a drive to ''Imperialize'' ship names.

'''HIMS ''Sentinel''''' is a [[Guardian-class Light Cruiser|''Guardian''-class Customs Cruiser]] assigned to [[Imperial Navy|Imperial Naval]] Fleet operations. She functions as a long range patrol vessel. She has a vastly upgraded Sublight engine and long-range sensor pods. ''Sentinel ''had most of the components needed for Customs duty taken out, including the holding cells and cargo bay to make room for a single-launch fighter bay. The inclusion of the fighter gives the vessel a great amount of survivability in small skirmishes.

== Imperial Service Record ==
The ''Guardian''-class Cruiser ''Sentinel'' was constructed at the [[Kuat Drive Yards-Sienar Fleet Systems|KDY-SFS]] shipyard orbiting [[Selene]] in [[11 ABY]]. She was assigned to [[I2SD Conqueror|HIMS ''Conqueror'']] as a systems patrol vessel upon completion and served in that role until ''Conqueror's'' destruction in [[14 ABY]].

''Sentinel'' was one of the last craft to make it off ''Conqueror'' before she was rammed into [[NRSD Crusader|''Crusader'']]. Anything that was not welded to the hull was thrown out of her airlocks to make room for as many passengers as possible. ''Sentinel'' was able to evacuate over 1,000 survivors in a ship that normally would support 30. 

Upon returning to Selene, [[Warlord]] [[Vassily Korolov]] in conjunction with [[Grand Admiral]] [[Danik Kreldin]] established a corps of lightly armed, yet fast vessels capable of going out and tracking down the elusive [[New Republic Navy|New Republic Fleet]] to assist in the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]]. As part of that assignment, ''Sentinel'' was upgraded for her reconnaissance role and assigned to an Imperial Naval Starfighter Ace, Lieutenant Commander [[Jal'Dana Rall]] who would play a very important role in closing the blockade around [[Coruscant]] late in [[14 ABY]].

Rall was promoted to Commander in [[15 ABY]] and assigned to the [[ICRV Prowler|HIMS ''Prowler'']]. ''Sentinel'' would be returned back to Imperial High Command, which would assign the vessel to [[I2SD Inquisitor|HIMS ''Inquisitor'']] as a reconnaissance and systems patrol vessel. She is currently serving in that roll.

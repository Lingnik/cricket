'''Procedure 37-Aurek''' is an interrogation/re-education procedure developed by [[Korynn Fleming]] when he was a member of the [[Imperial Security Bureau]]'s Interrogation Branch.

The procedure's primary goals were to break a subjects willpower, by forcing the prisoner to observe horrific and/or frightening images. Almost always, the prisoner's eyes were forced open and the prisoner's head was placed so that he/she was forced to observe the images. When possible, the images were specialized to the prisoner's specific fears, based either on racial demographic or intelligence gathered on the subject's history.

'''HIMS ''Warhammer''''' is a [[Strike-class Medium Cruiser|''Strike''-class cruiser]] launched shortly before the [[:starwars:Battle of Hoth|Battle of Hoth]]. Her frame was assembled at Kuat Drive Yards Assembly-D345 under an agreement with [[:starwars:Loronar Corporation|Loronar Corporation]]. Her modules were constructed at Loronar's main assembly in the [[:starwars:Meridian Sector|Meridian Sector]]. Final assembly was completed late in [[:starwars:2 ABY|2 ABY]]. She was christened and launched with her sister vessels [[ISC Broadsword|HIMS ''Broadsword'']] and [[ISC Flail|HIMS ''Flail'']] early in [[:starwars:3 ABY|3 ABY]] in a ceremony aboard the [[Orbital Station Guardian|OS Guardian]] in the [[O'paal]] system.

She currently serves as a warship for the [[Imperial Navy]]'s [[Task Force Inquisitor]].

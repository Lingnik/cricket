HIMS Devastator was commissioned in [[7 BBY]] at the main KDY assembly dock orbiting the planet Kuat. She would see fame as the flagship for Imperial [[Executor (title)|Executor]] [[:starwars:Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Darth Vader]]. Devastator was mortally wounded during the [[Starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]] when she came under concentrated fire from at least a dozen [[New Republic]] fighter squadrons. All hands were believed lost during the battle, though there was no record of her hull being found during the clean up in Rebel or Imperial records.  



== History ==
=== Darth Vaders Flagship ===
Soon after her commissioning in 7 BBY, Devastator was assigned to [[:starwars:Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Darth Vader]] to assist him in wiping out the growing rebellion against Imperial rule. She would serve in that capacity until the completion of the Super-class Star Destroyer Executor.

=== Death Squadron ===
==== Seizure of the Tantive IV ====
The Devastator was responsible for seizing the Alderaanian Courier vessel Tantive IV, around [[Tatooine]] in the months leading up to the [[Starwars:Battle of Yavin/Legends|Battle of Yavin]].

=== Endor ===
=== 17 ABY ===

In mid-17 ABY, a TIE/ln fighter flew into Selene space, no mother ship was found within 1000 units of the system. The pilot identified as DV-6-2, [[Pilot Officer]] Jek Antilles. He had been dead for years, rigormortis was the only thing that kept his hands on the controls of his fighter. Holo-recordings on the fighter were decoded by Imperial Navy technicians who determined that the fighter was from the Devastator. Searches for the ISD were conducted. No trace of the ship turned up. 

=== 18 ABY ===
In early 18 ABY, Imperial Intelligence launched several long range sensor probes into systems currently occupied by the [[New Sith Order]], one of these probes in the [[Tatooine]] system detected an [[Imperator-class Star Destroyer]] that by all rights should be destroyed. Recon teams from [[Task Force Inquisitor]] were dispatched to run Sith picket lines, and search this 'lost' vessel.

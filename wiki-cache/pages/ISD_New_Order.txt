'''HIMS ''New Order''''' was an [[Imperator-class Star Destroyer|''Imperator''-class Star Destroyer]] launched in [[:starwars:6_BBY|6 BBY]]. She was named after an earlier Star Destroyer with the same name that was destroyed during the [[:starwars:Kwymar_Suppressions|Kwymar Suppressions]]. 

She fought with distinction throughout the [[Galactic Civil War]], featuring prominently in many major actions against the [[New Republic]]. She was called upon one last time in [[14 ABY]] as part of the relief force sent to [[Etti IV]] at the behest of [[Grand Admiral]] [[Danik Kreldin]] to rescue [[I2SD Conqueror|HIMS ''Conqueror'']] during the [[Second Battle of Etti IV|Etti IV Crisis]].

She would be destroyed early in the engagement, a target of concentrated fire from both [[MCSC Home One|''Home One'']] and [[MCSC Deliverance|''Deliverance'']]. All hands were lost.

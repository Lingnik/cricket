'''HIMS ''Sanguinary''''' is an [[Imperator-class Star Destroyer|''Imperator''-class Star Destroyer]], most well-known for leading the reinforcement efforts during the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign]]. While the rest of [[Task Force Steel Resolve]] had gone on ahead to [[Battle of the Inner Caspian Worlds|occupy Caspia]], ''Sanguinary'' was dispatched a day later to guide several [[Star Galleon-class Frigate|Star Galleons]] and other supplemental warships into [[Caspian Democratic Union|Union space]]. 

She would be the focus of harassing efforts over the next two months, engaging various [[Caspian Navy]] units at several points, culminating in the [[Battle of Ketterslea]]. Due to engagements like this, [[Warlord]] [[Jarl Rellik|Rellik's]] advisors thought it best to consolidate resources, and ordered the Star Destroyer to link in with the rest of Task Force Steel Resolve. Relatively minor damage was incurred at the forthcoming [[Battle of the Belt]], but she unfortunately lost over eighty percent of her fighters in the battle within the asteroid belt.

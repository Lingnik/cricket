The '''Imperial Army''' is a branch of the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[Imperial Military|armed forces]] whose primary responsibility is the prosecution of planet-side military operations. One of the army's most common duties is the maintenance of garrisons throughout [[The Galaxy|the galaxy]].

The Imperial Army is recruited and supplied locally and falls under the direct authority of the [[Moff|regional governor]] and their appointees. The quality of training and equipment varies considerably from territory to territory, from poorly armed conscripts to advanced forces that can rival Imperial Stormtroopers.

The Imperial Army is not generally concerned with law enforcement (that duty falls to the local constabulary) but they stand ready to put down large-scale public unrest and combat full-scale insurrection as well as defending from external threats.

==History==
The Imperial Army was founded in [[19 BBY]] in conjunction with the [[Imperial Navy]] and the [[Stormtrooper Corps]]. It served as the backbone of the Imperial Military, responsible for the planetary defense of Imperial worlds and the garrison of occupied planets. The Imperial Army was also responsible for the invasion and subjugation of hostile worlds, operating alongside the Stormtrooper legions and with the Imperial Navy.

After the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]], the Army fell into disarray during the [[Post-Endor Imperial power struggle]]. The entire command structure of the Imperial Army collapsed, leaving the [[Interim Ruling Council]] to pick up the pieces and rebuild from scratch.

The Imperial Army has always represented a potential threat to Imperial power and been the starting point of every subsequent Emperor's ambitions. It is therefore heavily regulated by [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] and watched closely by the [[Imperial Security Bureau]].

In recent years, the Imperial Army has transitioned to an occupation force, geared toward repelling planetary invasion and subduing popular unrest.

==Organization==
The Imperial Army is organised according to the [[Moff|regional governor's]] wishes.

Typically, a dedicated staff of generals forms a military cabinet who collectively advise and implement the governor's wishes.

== Medals ==
:''All medals below are listed in order of precedence, starting from the top left and proceeding to the bottom right of each section.  They are worn in that same order.''

=== Distiguished Service Medals ===
:''The following four medals are awarded by the Emperor only''
'''Order of Palpatine''' - Essentially the Imperial equivalent to the Congressional Medal of Honor. There is no higher award in the Empire. The Emperor himself awards this medal in person. Only six have ever been given out.

'''Order of [[Bacharan Valak|Valak]]''' - Awarded by the Emperor himself, this medal is given to any serviceman or woman in the [[Galactic Empire]] who have demonstrated unique leadership, tact, bearing, courage and skill in their line of duty, and have made a hugely significant difference in the lives of fellow servicemen or women and the Empire as a whole. The receiver of this medal must be considered by others and the Emperor to be a hero and example of the embodiment of Imperial principals and ideals.

'''Emperor's Will Cross''' - A special Tour Of Duty medal which is presented to a soldier who has taken extraordinary measures, at the risk of his own life, to ensure the safety and security of the Empire. Awarded by the Emperor himself.

'''Order of Pestage''' - Awarded to the men and women of either the military or COMPNOR who have dedicated their lives to the service of the Emperor and the New Order itself, and have pursued the ideals laid about by the New Order. Those who receive this medal are with in good standings with the Emperor himself, and have come to embody all the traits of Sate Pestage himself. Awarded by the Emperor only.

:''Military/COMPNOR Medals''
<div style="float:left; width:48%;">
'''Legion of Honor''' - Awarded to those individuals who are the perfect embodiment of Imperial committment and loyalty. These individuals have served the Empire actively and zealously for an outstanding period of time or have preformed outstanding feats which have contributed greatly to the preservation of peace and the glory of the New Order.

'''Cross of Valor''' - Awarded to those who perform feats in the service of the Empire without regard to oneself. Such individuals have proven their selflessness and fealessness in the face of danger on numerous occassions. May be awarded posthumously.

'''The Order of Liberation''' - Awarded to those who show particular zeal in the promotion of the New Order. Such persons have striven to expand, preserve, and improve the New Order throughout their Imperial careers.

'''The Navy Cross''' - (NAVY ONLY) The "big" award for the Navy. An officer who performs with excellence throughout his career and has been prominent in promoting the highest standards and excellent performance in his colleagues will receive this award.

'''The Army Cross''' - (ARMY & ST ONLY) The "big" award for the Army and the Stormtrooper legions. An officer who performs with excellence throughout his career and has been prominent in promoting the highest standards and excellent performance in his colleagues will receive this award.

'''Order of Ishin-Il-Raz''' - Awarded to the men and women of COMPNOR whom have come to embody the ideals of COMPNOR and the New Order itself. Those who receive this medal have dedicated their lives to fulfilling and preserving the New Order and their loyal service to the Emperor and the Galactic Empire.

'''Distinguished Service Medal''' - Essentially the #2 medal in the armed forces. Similar to the Navy or Army Cross, but not quite as prestigious. The Navy or Army Cross generally takes a long, distinguished career to earn, whereas the DSM is more readily awarded to personnel for a number of exceptional feats rather than length of service.

'''The Silver Star''' - This award shall be presented to a soldier who has given service that has promoted the goals, mission, the strength of the Empire, and has affected the Military. The requirements for a Silver Star are more noteworthy and prestigious than a Bronze Star.

</div><div style="float:right; width:48%;">
'''The Nova Cross''' - (NAVY ONLY) Given out to naval and fighter personnel who perform heroically in a single engagement or over the span of a career.

'''The Blizzard Cross''' - (ARMY & ST ONLY) Given out to soldiers who perform heroically in a single battle or over the span of a career.

'''The Bronze Star''' - This award shall be presented to a soldier who has given service that has promoted the goals, mission, and strength of the Empire.

'''Nursing Service Cross''' - Given out to those in the medical field who perform heroically in the line of duty.

'''Star of [[Russel Canis|Canis]]''' - (NAVY ONLY) Awarded to Naval officers who show particular zeal for their stations and excellence in their duties.

'''Star of Veers''' - (ARMY & ST ONLY) Awarded to Army and Stormtrooper officers who show particular zeal for their stations and excellence in their duties.

'''Long Service Medal''' - Awarded to those who have served the armed forces for over one year.      

'''The Star of [[Airion Cimber|Cimber]]''' - Awarded to officers of His Majesty's Imperial Navy who have demonstrated unique tactical and strategic thinking in their line of duty and have led their unit or units to full victory over the enemy.

'''The Star of [[Vorn Paro|Paro]]''' - Awarded to officers of His Majesty's Imperial Army who have demonstrated unique tactical and strategic thinking in their line of duty and have led their unit or units to full victory over the enemy.

'''The Tarkin Medallion''' - Equivalent to the Purple Heart (for those who are wounded in the line of duty).

'''COMPNOR Medallion''' - Awarded by COMPNOR or Imperial Command to any member of the Empire who has shown a constant fight to further the political ideals of the Empire and resist the propagandist influences of her enemies. There exist three classes of this medal: First Class, Second Class, and Third Class.

</div>

=== Service Medals ===
'''Palpatine Cluster''' - An award for academic achievement granted to distinguished members of the Imperial Academy and Sub-Adult Groups.

'''The Medal of Loyalty''' - Awarded to members of the military, Intelligence community or COMPNOR who have demonstrated unique loyalty and service to the Galactic Empire and the Emperor.

'''Prisoner of War Medal''' - Awarded to any member of the Imperial Military, Imperial Intelligence, or COMPNOR who have, during the course of their service, have been taken prisoner and held captive by an enemy force.

'''Good Conduct Medal''' - Awarded for Good Conduct.

'''Medal of Guile''' - Awarded for successfully completing at least ten Tours of Duty.

'''Medal of Order''' - Awarded for successfully completing at least five Tours of Duty.

'''Medal of Redemption''' - Awarded for successfully completing one Tour of Duty.

=== Victory Medals ===
'''Clone Wars Victory Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who has participated in any operation or assignment during the Clone Wars and continued their career into the Imperial Military, Imperial Intelligence, or COMPNOR.

'''[[Galactic Civil War]] Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who has served in the Imperial Military, Imperial Intelligence, or COMPNOR during the Galactic Civil War.

'''Imperial Restoration Victory Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who has participated in any operation or assignment during the Post-Endor power struggle, from [[4 ABY]] to [[8 ABY]], and served under the leadership of the Imperial [[Interim Ruling Council]].

'''[[Coruscant|Imperial Center]] Liberation Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who participated in the [[Third Battle of Coruscant|Third Battle of Imperial Center]] in [[9 ABY]].

'''[[Imperial Civil War]] Victory Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who has participated in any operation or assignment during the Imperial Civil War in [[11 ABY]], and served under the leadership of Emperor Bacharan Valak.

'''[[War of the Throne]] Victory Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who has participated in any operation or assignment during the War of the Throne in [[12 ABY]].      

'''Griffon Domination Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who participated in the total annihilation of the [[Griffon Alliance]] at [[Second Battle of Cochran|The Second Battle of Cochran]] in [[14 ABY]]

=== Memorial Medals ===
'''Death Star Memorial Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who was in active service in the Imperial Military, Imperial Intelligence, or COMPNOR in 0 BBY.

'''Death Star II Memorial Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who was in active service in the Imperial Military, Imperial Intelligence, or COMPNOR in 4 ABY.

'''[[Death Star III]] Memorial Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who was in active service in the Imperial Military, Imperial Intelligence, or COMPNOR from Year 8 ABY to 11 ABY.

=== Campaign Medals ===
'''Early Campaigns Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who was in active service in the Imperial Military, Imperial Intelligence, or COMPNOR from 19 BBY to 0 BBY, and saw action against enemy forces or assisted in action against enemy forces.

'''Rebellion Campaigns Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who was in active service in the Imperial Military, Imperial Intelligence, or COMPNOR from 0 BBY to 4 ABY, and saw action against enemy forces or assisted in action against enemy forces. (Battle of Yavin, Battle of Hoth, Battle of Endor, etc) 

'''Coruscant Campaign Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who was in active service in the Imperial Miltiary, Imperial Intelligence, or COMPNOR in 4 ABY, and saw action against enemy forces or assistsed in action against enemy forces. ([[Battle of Coruscant]], [[Second Battle of Coruscant]])

'''[[Sluis Van]] Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who participated in or assisted in any combat in and around Sluis Van between [[6 ABY]] and [[11 ABY]]. ([[Battle of Sluis Van]], [[Second Battle of Sluis Van]], [[Third Battle of Sluis Van]])

'''[[Pride-1]] Campaign Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who was in active service in the Imperial Military, Imperial Intelligence, or COMPNOR from [[7 ABY]] to 9 ABY and participated in action against Pride-1, or assisted in action against Pride-1. ([[Battle of Pride-1]], [[Second Battle of Pride-1]], [[Third Battle of Pride-1]])

'''[[Caspian Annexation Campaign]] Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who was in active service in the Imperial Military, Imperial Intelligence, or COMPNOR in 8 ABY and participated in the Caspian Annexation Campaign ([[Battle of the Inner Caspian Worlds]], et. al.). Those that were on the surface of Caspar and survived the [[Siege of Trinumvira]] have a small red gem embedded in this medal.

'''Valak Campaign Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who was in active service in the Imperial Military, Imperial Intelligence, or COMPNOR in 9 ABY, and saw action or assisted in action throughout the Imperial offensive. ([[Battle of Gastus IV]], [[Battle of Mon Calamari]], [[Second Battle of Sluis Van]], [[Third Battle of Coruscant]])

'''Griffon Campaign Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who was in active service in the Imperial Military, Imperial Intelligence, or COMPNOR in Year 31 [[11 ABY]], and saw action or assisted in action against the [[Griffon Alliance]]. ([[Battle of Cochran]], [[Fourth Battle of Pride-1]])

'''[[Imperial Blitzkrieg|Blitzkrieg]] Campaign Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who was in active service in the Imperial Military, Imperial Intelligence, or COMPNOR in [[14 ABY]], and saw action or assisted in action during the Blitzkrieg. ([[Second Battle of Chandrila]], [[Fourth Battle of Corellia]], [[Second Battle of Mon Calamari]], [[Fourth Battle of Sullust]])

=== Occupation Medals ===
'''Occupation of [[Chandrila]] Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who participated in or assisted in the occupation and pacification of Chandrila.

'''[[Sullust]] Occupation Medal''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman who participated in the Occupation of Sullust in [[6 ABY]]. ([[Third Battle of Sullust]])

== Ribbons ==
:''Imperial Service ribbons are meant to show the areas of one's experience and training. Personnel may have more than one of these ribbons, but to earn service ribbons in divisions other than one's primary division takes a decent amount of experience and/or training. Those who have served with distinction in their service division may receive up to five stars on the ribbon. They are worn on the right breast above all other ribbons except medal ribbons. All ribbons are listed in order of precedence.''

=== Operations Ribbons ===
'''FleetOps Ribbon''' - Awarded to servicemen of the Imperial Navy.

'''CombatOps Ribbon''' - Awarded to servicemen of the Imperial Army.

'''FighterOps Ribbon''' - Awarded to Imperial starfighter pilots.

'''SpecialOps Ribbon''' - Awarded to Imperial servicemen participating in special operations.

'''Home Guard / Customs Ribbon''' - awarded for service on the home front or for customs duty.

'''Master Marksman Ribbon''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman of the Imperial Military who excels at their marksmanship exam, scoring over 90.

'''Ace Pilot Ribbon''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman of the Imperial Military who scores at least six kills over their career while in a fighter.

'''[[Bespin]] Service Ribbon''' - Awarded to any serviceman or woman in the Imperial Military, Imperial Intelligence, or COMPNOR, who has been stationed on Bespin or has participated in the defense or occupation of Bespin.

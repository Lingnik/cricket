[[Lorn Rhys]], having returned from exile after the failure of the [[Vigilant Movement]] five years earlier, launched a coup against [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Kendal Osbourne|Osbourne]]. The coup was marked by the [[Battle of Selene]], wherein Rhys and his forces captured Osbourne, the Imperial court, and the [[Executor-class Star Dreadnought|Super Star Destroyer]] ''[[SSD Palpatine|Dark Eminence]]''. Contemptuously, they plunged the monstrous ship into the [[Selene|planet]], causing an environmental catastrophe and rendering it uninhabitable. Rhys cast Osbourne into a slab of carbonite, and initiated the [[War of the Throne]].

== Legacy ==

Some years after the coup, an academic at [[:starwars:Corellia University/Legends|Corellia University]] named [[Tibor Stasio]] wrote a book about the coup where he made up a considerable number of details, including referring to the coup adherents as Whitecoats without any basis in fact. He was subsequently sacked from the university for a litany of crimes including drug addiction, plagiarism, theft, and the aforementioned fabrications in his book. He disappeared shortly thereafter to parts unknown, but in spite of his book's many fabrications it is occasionally still referenced as an authority on the subject.

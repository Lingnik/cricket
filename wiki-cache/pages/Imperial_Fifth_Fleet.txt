The '''Imperial Fifth Fleet''' was an element of the [[Imperial Navy]] assigned to the defense of the [[Tarkin Corridor]] and nearby outlying systems. Towards the start of the [[Galactic Civil War]], the Fifth Fleet was charged with spearheading the exploration and establishment of the [[Deep Core]]. The fleet was commanded by [[Fleet Admiral]] [[Jard Kisal]]. Kisal would prove his disloyalty in the weeks following the [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]]'s death at the [[:starwars:Battle_of_Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]]. Refusing orders from [[Sate Pestage]] to aid in the defense of [[Coruscant|Imperial Center]], Kisal jumped his fleet into the [[Inner Rim]] territories where he would [[Civil War in the Inner Rim|wage a war]] for domination against the Imperial remnants, the [[New Republic]], and rival [[Warlord|warlords]].

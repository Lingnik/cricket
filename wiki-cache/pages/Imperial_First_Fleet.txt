The '''Imperial First Fleet''' is a multi-purpose [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[Imperial Navy|naval]] fleet. The exact mission detail and make-up of the fleet has changed over the decades, but throughout the entirety of the Empire's reign, the First Fleet has always been at the forefront of major [[The Galaxy|galactic]] campaigns. The First Fleet is known for its professionalism and deadly precision and execution of its assignments.

==History==
The First Fleet was formed from various elements of the [[:starwars:Republic_Navy|Republic Navy]] in [[19 BBY]] and placed under the command of [[Fleet Admiral]] '''Sanir Daris''', a [[:starwars:Clone_Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]] veteran. It was tasked with subjugating the last remaining [[:starwars:Confederacy of Independent Systems/Legends|Confederate]] strongholds in the [[Outer Rim]].

In [[13 ABY]], [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]], Imperial [[Warlord]], reorganized the First Fleet and placed it under the command of Fleet Admiral [[Drayson Honos]]. The First Fleet would be prioritized with the brunt of the fighting against the [[New Republic]]. As such, the Fleet was divided into various task forces, notable ones being [[Task Force Hammer]] and [[Task Force Anvil]]. Hammer would be the primary combat force within the Fleet, and would go on to much fame during the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]] of [[14 ABY]]. Although Honos was the commanding officer of the Fleet during this time period, [[Supreme Commander of the Imperial Forces|Supreme Commander]] [[Danik Kreldin]] plotted, organized and executed much of the Blitzkrieg and would personally lead the various task forces through battle.

==Current Events==
In [[15 ABY]], the First Fleet would be thrown into the fray once more during the [[Sixth Battle of Coruscant|invasion of Coruscant]]. Led by Kreldin once more, the Fleet would be successful in crushing the Republic defenses and claiming [[Coruscant]] as once more the Imperial capital. Afterwards, the First Fleet would be regulated to defending all the newly acquired territory, with a significant portion of its resources being transferred to [[Task Force Inquisitor]].

==Organization==
At the very onset of the Imperial Blitzkrieg, the First Fleet was the largest fleet within the Imperial Navy. It remains today as the Empire's largest and most diverse Fleet.

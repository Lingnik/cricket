The '''Imperial Military''' is the overarching fighting force of the [[Galactic Empire]], comprised of the [[Imperial Navy]], [[Imperial Army]], and the [[Stormtrooper]] legions, among various support branches such as the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]]. It was responsible for carrying out military operations throughout the [[The Galaxy|galaxy]] as well as maintaining order on Imperial member worlds, governorships, colonies, and protectorates.

The Imperial military came into existence in [[19 BBY]] with the [[:starwars:Declaration_of_a_New_Order|Declaration of a New Order]] and the founding of the Galactic Empire. The [[:starwars:Galactic_Republic/Legends|Galactic Republic]] military was turned, over night, into the Imperial military. The weaponry, technology, personnel and uniforms all remained the same. What did change was the ideology, mission and chain-of-command.

Over the years, the Imperial military became infamous for its efficiency, discipline and brutality: hundreds of star systems fell to the wrath of the Galactic Empire, and with them, the lives of hundreds of billions of sentient beings.

The Imperial military was watched on a constant basis by [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] and its [[Imperial Security Bureau]] and [[CompForce]] observers, in order to keep the massive and powerful military in check in case of rebellion against the [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]]. The [[Stormtrooper]]s fulfilled a similar purpose, stationed aboard Imperial naval vessels and Army garrisons to serve as the fighting arm of the Emperor. 

In [[4 ABY]], the military was splintered during the [[Post-Endor Imperial power struggle]]. For four years, its strength and efficiency would drop to near disastrous levels, until its revival in [[8 ABY]] by Emperor [[Bacharan Valak]]. 

While the Emperor is the undisputed master of the Empire and its military, the military itself is run by the Commander-in-Chief, the [[Executor (title)|Military Executor]] and [[Supreme Commander of the Imperial Forces]].

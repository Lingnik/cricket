The '''Imperial Palace''' of [[Selene]] was constructed during the reign of [[Bacharan Valak]] as the Core world of Selene began taking on greater importance in the [[Galactic Empire]]. Though not as massive as the original Imperial Palace of [[Coruscant|Imperial Center]], the Selene Palace still towered over all other buildings on Selene.

This Palace became the throne of [[Kendal Osbourne]] during his short-lived reign as [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] and was subsequently destroyed during the [[War of the Throne]] in the event that wiped all life on Selene out. A new Palace was constructed under the directives of [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] and [[Scaven Marx]], although it lacked the grandeur and elegance of its predecessor.

'''The Imperial Privateers''' are a mercenary outfit known for operating the cruiser ''[[Privateer]]''. The crew is a mix of species &mdash; something unheard amongst all Imperial starships, but this particular group works well together and enjoys a great degree of success. They are currently led by [[Captain]] [[Juran Reinzhiner]]. 

They are most well-known (or loathed) for their involvement in the [[Dacan Pandemic]]. While under the leadership of [[Jacob Astor]], they were responsible for dispersing the alpha units of the vicious virus that led to the deaths of millions of sentients.

======================================================================
In order to finish the planning of your TP idea, certain players need to be notified of what you want to do.  Below is a guide for Imperial players as to what sort of coordination is required for each type of plot.  Remember, as a guide it does not include every possible situation but rather a general outline.  If there is any confusion as to what sort of level of RP your idea would be in, always be conservative and choose the higher level accordingly.

'''Minor Level'''<br />
Plots aboard ships and with mercs and NPCs that have little or no affect on the plot should be allowed by players to run at anytime. No coordination required, simply a report after the TP to Imperial admin and others as required.

:''Coordination Required''<br />
:*Players in proposed idea

:''Reporting Required''<br />
:*Summary of events to appropriate commander(s) or admin


'''Moderate Level'''<br />
These sorts of plots just require the approval of Imperial admin before they are allowed to be run, and usually are plots that require a change in orders or change the outcome of a plotline currently in motion. Anything that is larger than a minor plot or event.

:''Coordination Required''<br />
:*Players in proposed idea
:*Faction Head
:*Assistant Faction Heads

:''Reporting Required''<br />
:*Summary of events to Faction Head and Assitant Faction Heads
:*Summary of events to appropriate commander(s)


'''Major Level'''<br />
Involves major plot twists and faction head approval from each faction it relates to. Players must get approval for these sorts of plots before they are able to be RPed.

:''Coordination Required''<br />
:*Players in proposed idea
:*Faction Head (Your Faction)
:*Faction Head (Opposing Faction)
:*Assistant Faction Heads (Both Factions)

:''Reporting Required''<br />
:*Summary of events to Faction Head and Assitant Faction Heads (Both Factions)
:*Summary of events to appropriate commander(s)


'''Extreme Level'''<br />
Requires MUSH admin level to RPA level approval to run a plot. Such things as Victory star destroyers and larger as well as other major plots that are beyond the major level.

:''Coordination Required''<br />
:*Players in proposed idea
:*Faction Head (Your Faction)
:*Faction Head (Opposing Faction)
:*Assistant Faction Heads (Both Factions)
:*RPA or Wizard Admin as required

:''Reporting Required''<br />
:*Summary of events to Wiz Admin notified or involved
:*Summary of events to Faction Head and Assitant Faction Heads (Both Factions)
:*Summary of events to appropriate commander(s)

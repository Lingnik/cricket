TITLE: Imperium-Caspian Mutual Protection Pact (29 ABY)
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2023-08-18T04:36:11Z
======================================================================
'''Article I''': The [[Imperium]] recognises the sovereignty of the [[Caspian Union]] over the planets and beings of the [[Caspian Sector]]. The Imperium further recognises the right of planets and beings in nearby sectors within the [[The Galaxy#Mid Rim|Mid Rim]] to freely join the Caspian Union and be subject to its sovereignty should they so choose.

'''Article II''': The Caspian Union recognises the Imperium as the sole legitimate [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] power and its sovereignty over all worlds and beings hitherto under the authority of the Galactic Empire. The Caspian Union furthermore recognises the right of planets and beings  not previously under Imperial authority to freely join the Imperium and be subject to its sovereignty should they so choose.

'''Article III''': The Imperium and Caspian Union may establish embassies and consulates within the territory of the other which will function under the sovereignty of the establishing power. The location and extent of these embassies and consulates will remain within the discretion of the hosting power to grant as appropriate.

'''Article IV''': The Imperium and Caspian Union may accredit diplomats to serve within the territory of the other. Upon the acceptance of their credentials by the hosting power these diplomats will enjoy full immunity from arrest and prosecution. These diplomats may be expelled by the hosting power for any reason and may be detained by the hosting power in the process of expulsion. A diplomat so detained must be immediately turned over to their own government and may not be held for any length of time by the hosting power. It is understood that any personal abuse of position by accredited diplomats will be punished by their own governments and their immunity from arrest and prosecution is not licence to violate the law of the hosting power.

'''Article V''': The Imperium and Caspian Union pledge to support the others defence and are obligated to render what aid can be reasonably spared should the other fall under attack.

'''Article VI''': The military forces of each power may travel through the territory of the other unrestricted but are expected to conform to all local regulations as would apply to the local military forces. Individual service members do not enjoy immunity from arrest and prosecution but do enjoy the right to call upon the services of the nearest embassy or consulate without restriction.

'''Article VII''': The military forces of each power may make full use of the medical and repairs facilities of the other power. This use may does supersede the local use of such facilities. Where a scarcity of medical personnel or supplies exist rules of triage must be applied without regard to nationality. Where a scarcity of repair technicians or supplies exist priority should be based on the operational value the vessel or equipment in question without regard to nationality.

'''Article VIII''': Military forces shall be subject to the command of the local military authority. Where locale authority is absent or incapacitated, independent operation is allowed on a good faith basis.

'''Article IX''': Citizens of each power are permitted to travel, reside, be employed, and conduct business within the territory of the other provided all local laws and regulations are obeyed. Civilians detained for any violation such laws and regulations enjoy the right to call upon the services of the nearest embassy or consulate without restriction.

'''Article X''': The Imperium and Caspian Union jointly declare a galactic wide ban on the trafficking and enslavement of sentient beings. Furthermore any policy that aims to restrict the rights and opportunity of any sentient being based on their species or sub-species is strongly condemned.  The Imperium and Caspian Union declare both the willingness and intention to commit their combined military forces to the eradication of such irrational ideology and policy in the galaxy. This declaration supersedes any recognition of the sovereignty of a third party power.

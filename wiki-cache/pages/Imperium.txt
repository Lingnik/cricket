The '''Imperium''' is  the successor state to the former [[Galactic Empire]] formed by '''[[Tyler Damion]]''' following his takeover of [[Selene]] in [[29 ABY]]. Facing no public opposition from [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Aleister Vadim|Vadim]], Damion was able to secure the submission of most Imperial territory in the weeks and months to follow. With Vadim nowhere in sight, the Imperium seemingly supplanted the former Empire entirely and became the dominant government of the galaxy.

Crowned Imperator '''Damion I''', the new Imperial leader has forged a government with structural similarities to the former Empire but with a radically different approach in many key areas. Slavery and species based discrimination, which had been hallmarks of the Empire, are outlawed and forcefully suppressed within the Imperium. And while the Imperator, the Advisory Council, and the [[Moff's Council]] have unquestioned supremacy within the system, democracy is not only allowed at a local level but encouraged. Many members of the Moff's Council have in fact been elected by the worlds they represent.

The new approach to governance has allowed the Imperium to gain some measure of respect with other galactic powers, including the [[Caspian Union]] and [[Corellian Confederation]]. The Imperium and the Caspian Union formed a [[Imperium-Caspian Mutual Protection Pact (29 ABY)|mutual protection pact]] in short order, in spite of the Union's more traditional leaning towards the [[New Republic|Republic]]. For its part the New Republic has repeatedly refused both Imperium and Caspian efforts at more normalised relations, viewing the former as tainted by the past crimes of the Empire and the latter as merely a puppet state. The fact that Imperator Damion I is better known to the galaxy as the [[Dark Lord of the Sith]] '''Darth Malign''' and several high ranking officials in the Imperium served him in the [[New Sith Order]], including Caspian [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] gives the Republic and other skeptical parties fuel for their fears.

= Governance =

Power flows from the top in the Imperium with supreme resting with the imperial family, chiefly the Imperator himself, and his daughter and crowned successor, [[Prince|Crown Princess]] '''[[Sonja Damion]]'''. Day to day administration rest with the Advisory Council and below that the [[Moff's Council]]. The Advisory Council is a thirteen member body chair by [[Grand Admiral]] '''[[Drayson Honos]]''' with six members appointed by the Imperator and six members elected by the Moff's Council. Of these '''[[Elana Tracer]]''', the [[Grand Moff]] of [[Selene]] & [[Athaniss]] and head of [[Kuat Drive Yards-Sienar Fleet Systems|KDY-SFS]] is the most powerful. Honos and Tracer are the de facto Heads of Government of the Imperium.

In [[34 ABY]], Grand Inquisitor '''[[Axel Vichten]]''' was promoted to [[Grand Admiral]] and replaced Drayson Honos as Chief of the Imperium's armed forces, allowing the latter to focus on his role as Chair of the Advisory Council. At this time Vichten was also named special acting Regent to the Imperator's Throne on State, Military, and Corruption Matters, effectively giving him the role as acting Head of State.

The '''[[The Galaxy#Deep Core|Deep Core]]''' is known for being difficult to navigate and steeped in mystery. It was on the [[Force]] rich world of [[:starwars:Tython/Legends|Tython]] that the [[Jedi Order]] got its start many millennia ago. The region would play a pivotal role in the history of both the Jedi and the [[Sith Order|Sith]]. Over the course of many conflicts various worlds would be abandoned and cut off from the greater galaxy, discovered again, and cut off again, particularly Tython itself.

During the reign of [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]] a number of new access routes were discovered to the Deep Core. Access to the region was restricted by the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]], with numerous worlds hosting sensitive and secret Imperial facilities. The Deep Core has continued to be under Imperial control through successive regimes. 

The only significant portion of the region not formally under the control of the '''[[Imperium]]''' is the section of the [[:starwars:Koros Trunk Line/Legends|Koros Trunk Line]] that runs from its end point at [[:starwars:Empress Teta/Legends|Empress Teta]] through to [[:starwars:Ruan/Legends|Ruan]] in the [[The Galaxy#Core Worlds|Core]]. The remaining territory listed below reflects what is formally part of the Imperium. Within this territory there may be additional locations which have been lost to common knowledge or used secretly by the Imperator or his agents.

TITLE: Imperium Territory in the Expansion Region
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)

======================================================================
Prior to the advent of the '''[[Imperium]]''', the majority of the '''[[The Galaxy#Expansion Region|Expansion Region]]''' was under the control of the Galactic Empire, a position the Imperium was able to maintain. There are two large sections of the Expansion Region, however, that do not fall within Imperium space. The [[New Republic]] controls the sectors and worlds on the [[Hydian Way]] and [[Perlemian Trade Route]], as well as the worlds in the space between these two trade routes. 

On the opposite end of the Expansion Region is an area of [[Independent|independent]] space, informally known as Collective Space. Centred on the [[Rimma Trade Route]], it is named for the [[:starwars:Epsi Collective/Legends|Epsi Collective]], the central sector of the territory. Though there is no regional government, the world of [[:starwars:Vandelhelm/Legends|Vandelhelm]] is most prominent. Many of the worlds of Collective Space saw limited [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] presence during [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine's]] reign, and in the last years of his reign there was a considerable [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Alliance]] presence. While too distant from New Republic territory to make any serious effort at joining, there has traditionally been enough sympathy for the Republic locally that it has limited the interest by Collective worlds in joining the Empire or Imperium.

The lists below represent a comprehensive representation of Imperium territory within the Expansion Region. Not every location is actively administered or garrisoned by the Imperium or its allies, but all fall within the domains the Imperium controls. None is actively claimed or contested by another galactic nor are any recognised as independently controlled, even where Imperium control is nominal.

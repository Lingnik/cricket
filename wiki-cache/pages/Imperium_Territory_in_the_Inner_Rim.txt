Prior to the advent of the '''[[Imperium]]''', the majority of the '''[[The Galaxy#Inner Rim|Inner Rim]]''' was under the control of the Galactic Empire, a position the Imperium was able to maintain. The largest section of the Inner Rim to not be under Imperium control are the worlds on the [[Hydian Way]] and [[Perlemian Trade Route]], as well as the worlds in the space between these two trade routes. The [[:starwars:Hapes Cluster/Legends|Hapes Cluster]] is controlled by the [[Hapes Consortium]], and though technically independent of the Imperium, is under its de facto control, owing to the marriage between the Imperator and [[:starwars:Queen Mother|Queen Mother]]. Notable independent worlds include [[:starwars:Adari|Adari]], the [[:starwars:Adarian/Legends|Adarian]] homeworld, [[:starwars:Antar 4/Legends|Antar 4]], the [[:starwars:Gotal/Legends|Gotal]] homeworld, [[:starwars:Colla IV/Legends|Colla IV]], the [[:starwars:Colicoid/Legends|Colicoid]] homeworld, and [[:starwars:Ktil/Legends|Ktil]], the [[:starwars:Ktilac|Ktilac]] homeworld.

The lists below represent a comprehensive representation of Imperium territory within the Inner Rim. Not every location is actively administered or garrisoned by the Imperium or its allies, but all fall within the domains the Imperium controls. None is actively claimed or contested by another galactic nor are any recognised as independently controlled, even where Imperium control is nominal.

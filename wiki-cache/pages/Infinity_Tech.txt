'''Infinity Tech''' is a [[:Category:Starships|starship]] upgrade, repair and modification workshop located on the trade level of [[Nar Shaddaa]]. Occupying the former hangar of [[FoxTech Industries|FTI]], it is now owned and operated by the famous [[Verpine]] technician, [[Bael]]. Formerly a subsidiary operation of [[Pandemonium Inc]], it was individualized when [[Bael]] chose to begin independent operations of his own.

Infinity Tech can supply all starship needs, even those not considered legal by many governments. In addition to technical needs, Infinity Tech also sells certain used ships, and it is rumored its CEO is looking for military-grade ships.

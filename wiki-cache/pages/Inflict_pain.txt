TITLE: Inflict pain
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2007-04-16T06:10:23Z
======================================================================
'''Inflict pain''' causes the target to experience great agony. The user causes damage by rolling their alter skill, while the target resists with their control, Perception or Willpower. Damage is figured as if the attack was a stun attack, although if the target suffers any damage at all, they are so crippled by pain that they are incapable of acting for the rest of the round and the next round.

'''Control Difficulty''': Very Easy, modified by proximity.

'''Alter Difficulty''': Target's control, Perception or Willpower roll. Modified by proximity.

'''Required powers:''' [[Control pain]], [[Life sense]]

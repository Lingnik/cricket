TITLE: Injure/kill
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2023-07-01T03:50:19Z
======================================================================
An attacker must be touching the target to use this power. In combat, this means making a successful brawling attack in the same round that the power is to be used.

When the power is activated, if the attacker's alter roll is higher than the character's resisting control or Perception total, figure damage as if the power roll was a damage total and the control or Perception roll was a Strength roll to resist damage.

The alter's difficulty is the target's control or Perception roll.

'''Required power:''' [[Life detection]], [[Life sense]]

===Example===
[[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] grabs Morruk by the shoulder and uses Injure/Kill on him. His alter roll is 15; Morruk's Perception roll is an 8. That's a difference of seven, which on the damage chart means Morruk is wounded. Morruk falls to the ground, clutching his shoulder.

The '''Inner Council''' was the chief executive body of the [[New Republic]]. Composed of members of the [[Provisional Council]], they composed of the [[Chief of State]]'s closest advisers and Chairbeings of the [[New Republic Senate]] committees. If a [[:starwars:Vote of No Confidence|Vote of No Confidence]] was brought against the Chief of State, the Ruling Council would review its legitimacy, then vote on whether or not the motion should be brought before the Senate. The Chief could bypass a vote by declaring a leave of absence and nominating one of the council to become caretaker Chief.

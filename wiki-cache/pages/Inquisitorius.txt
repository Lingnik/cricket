The '''Inquisitorius''' was a secret division of [[Imperial Intelligence]] consisting of [[Dark side of the Force|dark side]] [[:starwars:Force-sensitive/Legends|Force-sensitive]] agents known as Inquisitors, or "truth officers".

Under the control of [[:starwars:Anakin_Skywalker/Legends|Darth Vader]], these Inquisitors were considered the highest ranking of the [[Dark Side Adept]]s, outranking the [[Emperor's Hand]]s, [[:starwars:Prophets of the Dark Side|Prophets of the Dark Side]], and lesser [[Dark Jedi]]. They reported directly to [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]]. 

==History==
The Inquisitorius was an organization established around [[19 BBY]] by Emperor Palpatine. It consisted of agents referred to as Inquisitors tasked with hunting down [[Jedi]] who managed to survive [[:starwars:Order 66/Legends|Order 66]].

While many Jedi fell in the [[:starwars:Great_Jedi_Purge/Legends|Great Jedi Purge]], some betrayed their order and joined the ranks of both the Inquisitorius and the Emperor's Dark Side Adepts. The Inquisitors were also responsible for awakening [[:starwars:Arden_Lyn|Arden Lyn]] after a twenty-five millennia long sleep.

Following [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|the death]] of Palpatine at [[:starwars:Endor/Legends|Endor]], the bureaucracy of the Inquisitorius engaged in infighting with other branches of the government to determine a successor, leading to the total collapse of the Inquisitorius. The last of the Inquisitors were killed by [[8 ABY]] at the hands of [[Bacharan Valak]] and his various agents. However, this would not be the end of the Inquisitorious. [[Vassily Korolov]], the Empire's [[Grand Vizier]], restored the organization in [[15 ABY]] with himself as [[Grand Inquisitor]]. Given their very own [[Task Force Inquisitor|task force]], the Inquisitorious sought out the last remnants of the dying Republic and the Jedi to stamp them out of the galaxy permanently.

==Operations==
The Inquisitors' primary purpose was the interrogation of Jedi or particularly resistant subjects. Subjects were attained for the Inquisitors by authorization of [[:starwars:Legal Authorization for Advanced Confinement Document|Legal Authorization for Advanced Confinement Document]]s. When usual methods of interrogation such as torture and [[:starwars:Bavo_Six|Bavo Six]] injections failed to break a subject, Imperial Intelligence would call upon an Inquisitor, who would use powerful [[The Force|Force]] skills in conjunction with traditional but effective methods of manipulation to obtain success.

However, since Imperial Intelligence was quite effective in interrogating the average subject, they rarely required the services of an Inquisitor. Inquisitors instead spent much of their time looking for potential Jedi candidates and Force activity in the backwater parts of the [[Galactic Empire]], most often in the [[Outer Rim|Outer Rim Territories]], at the discretion of their leader the Grand Inquisitor. [[:starwars:Malorum|Malorum]], the first Grand Inquisitor, was killed on [[:starwars:Naboo/Legends|Naboo]] by [[:starwars:Ferus_Olin|Ferus Olin]]; [[:starwars:Lord/Legends|Lord]] [[:starwars:Laddinare Torbin|Torbin]], the third Grand Inquisitor, was killed by an [[:starwars:Assassin_droid/Legends|assassin droid]] who crashed a [[:starwars:shuttle|shuttle]] into the Imperial palace on [[:starwars:Weerden|Weerden]] [[16 BBY|sixteen years prior]] to the [[:starwars:Battle_of_Yavin|Battle of Yavin]]. The title was eventually taken up by the [[:starwars:Zabrak|Zabrak]] [[:starwars:Ja'ce_Yiaso|Ja'ce Yiaso]], but he would be killed by [[Lara Grayson]] in [[8 ABY]] when Valak consolidated the last of his Empire. Korolov claimed the title of Grand Inquisitor in [[15 ABY]].

The Inquisitorius was based in the foreboding [[:starwars:Citadel Inquisitorius|Citadel Inquisitorius]] on the planet [[:starwars:Prakith/Legends|Prakith]] in the [[The Galaxy|Deep Core]]; here, the [[High Inquisitor]]s were given their assignments and conferred with their colleagues. At its reorganization in 15 ABY, headquarters was moved to [[Coruscant|Imperial Center]].

'''Intelligence''' refers to an entity or organization within a larger governing body or organization, tasked with gathering, processing, and presenting information (ergo ''intelligence'') that can be used for political, administrative, criminal, or military purposes. Some such entities have been small and independent, while others are large, officially sanctioned bureaus of their respective governments. Examples include:

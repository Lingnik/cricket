'''Interspatial Prospecting, Inc.''' (or '''InterSpec''') is the primary prospecting and mining agency within the [[Caspian Union]]. It deals with the harvesting and distribution of valuable resources from all celestial objects throughout Union territory, with facilities in locations such as [[Tyllin]], Picon Yar, Thales, [[Tairoa]], and countless asteroids throughout all the systems of the Union. The company and its contractors are driven to mine as much of the Union's mineral and ore needs from within in-territory resources as possible, [[Caspian Crest|the Crest]] is not keen on importing raw materials from outside sources. 

It was a private enterprise, born from a government agency on [[Caspar]] after the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] left [[Caspian Sector|Caspian Space]]. Interspatial Prospecting was placed under [[Crest Commission Consolidation Charter]] in [[5 ABY]]. It was later nationalised by the [[Crestian Tarasar|Tarasar Administration]] as part of the [[Caspian Repatriation Act (29 ABY)|Caspian Repatriation Act]] in [[29 ABY]] as a matter of national defence, though it had not been under [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector]] ownership as [[CDMC]] and [[SubPro]] had been.

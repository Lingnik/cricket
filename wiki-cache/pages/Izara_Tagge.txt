'''Izara Tagge''' ([[6 ABY]] - ) is a scion of the [[:starwars:House of Tagge/Legends|House of Tagge]] of [[:starwars:Tepasi/Legends|Tepasi]]. She is the daughter and heir of [[Baron|Baroness]] [[:starwars:Domina Tagge/Legends|Domina Tagge]]. Izara is the Vice President of Marketing for [[:starwars:Tagge Company/Legends|Tagge Company]]. She is also Vice President of Marketing and a major shareholder of the [[Golden Bantha Group]], and by extension, Vice President of Marketing of [[Biscuit Baron]], which had originally been a Tagge business.

== Early Life ==

In [[5 ABY]], [[Baron|Baroness]] [[:starwars:Domina Tagge/Legends|Domina Tagge]] mysteriously disappeared from galactic society. Unbeknownst
to all but a very few, Domina had secretly returned to [[:starwars:Monastery/Legends|Monastery]], where she had previously served as [[:starwars:All-High Priestess|All-High Priestess]] of the [[:starwars:Order of the Sacred Circle|Order of the Sacred Circle]]. A few months later she gave birth to a daughter she named Izara.

Izara grew up in a compound Domina had secretly built in the rainforest years prior. Outside of her mother, Izara had little interaction with other sentients. Though they maintained some contact with the outside galaxy through smugglers who kept them supplied, Izara's companions were largely the beasts they kept as pets and the [[Droid|droids]] who served as their staff.

With her interactions with other humans quite limited, Izara took great interest in all thing mechanical instead. Vehicles, particularly fast ones, served as her major hobby. With the help of her droids she was building custom vehicles quite an early age, and naturally flying them around at dangerous speeds. Recognising her daughter's skill in these pursuits, Domina did not try dissuade her daughter's hobby.

Following the death of the final one of her brothers in [[18 ABY]], Domina decided to return to galactic society and resume control over the [[:starwars:Tagge Company/Legends|Tagge Company]]. Back on [[:starwars:Tepasi/Legends|Tepasi]], Baroness Tagge would introduce her daughter with the name Izara Tagge, but curiously never referred to her specifically as her daughter, and never identified the girl's father. By all practical measures though, Domina and those around her treated Izara as her daughter. Rumours would occasionally emerge suggesting that Izara was actually the secret child of one of Domina's brothers, which Domina did nothing to quell, considering the speculation amusing.

Life on Tepasi would be something of a cultural shock for Izara, but she quickly found her interest in racing served as means of making friends, with Izara finding any and all opportunities to race that she could. Their return to galactic society also meant that Domina was soon grooming her daughter to inherit the family business one day, with Izara's education centred around learning all aspects of Tagge Company's operations.

== Business Executive ==

As Izara grew older, her image was frequently in the press, owing to her racing, partying, and affairs. This would serve to give her an interest in the marketing side of the family business. In [[29 ABY]], she would officially join the board of Tagge Company as Vice President of Marketing. By this point, however, Tagge Company was a subsidiary of [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector Authority's]] single merged mega-corporation. This was an arrangement Baroness Tagge has only reluctantly agreed to at the time, and found herself increasingly lamenting in private.

It was around this point that Izara become acquainted with [[Atsvara Tarasar]], the daughter of the newly elected [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]]. Atsvara had also had an early childhood marked by exile from wider society, and upon her arrival with her family back on [[Caspar]] had quickly set about making contacts at [[Biscuit Baron]], a Tagge subsidiary. Izara would find Atsvara something of a kindred spirit as a gifted child, born in exile, and heir to a powerful house. Atsvara, meanwhile appreciated Izara's hobbies particularly her racing, which she had taken interest in herself after she and her father had befriended former Presav, now [[Moff]] of [[Selene]], [[Elana Tracer]]. Izara soon became part of Atsvara's secret network of powerful friends.

Growing up Atsvara had a collection of every [[:starwars:Jolly Meal|Jolly Meal]] toy Biscuit Baron had ever produced, which drove her interest in the restaurant chain. When the company was faced with widespread labour issues in [[32 ABY]], Atsvara saw an opportunity to utilise her wealth and her connexions within Biscuit Baron to take over the restaurant chain. Izara became a key member of Atsvara's conspiracy. Though Atsvara genuinely cared about the company and its workers, she felt that if she drive a wedge between the workers and the company, and extend the length of the strike, the CSA might be willing to unload the company after it sufficiently devalued.

While Atsvara and her confederates managed to secretly prolong the Biscuit Baron strike, her efforts to instigate strikes in other companies to prompt the Corporate Sector Authority to reverse its merger failed to manifest. Atsvara pivoted to the formation of a consultancy firm, the [[Golden Bantha Group]] which she arranged to be hired to negotiate an end to the strike and rebuild the company's image. 

The Golden Bantha Group was more than a front for consulting, Atsvara was gathering investors for an eventual takeover bid. Between her friendship with Izara, and Domina's dissapointment at no longer fully owing a company where brother [[:starwars:Orman Tagge/Legends|Orman Tagge's]] face graced the logo, Atsvara was able to get the [[:starwars:House of Tagge/Legends|House of Tagge]] itself as a secret partner in the plan. Baroness Tagge would provide a sizeable loan to the Golden Bantha Group for their takeover bid with Izara taking a major stake in the firm as down payment on the loan.

After many months working behind the scenes and negotiating with the [[Direx Board (CSA)|Direx Board]] to [[Report:Biscuit Buyout|buyout]] the restaurant chain, the Golden Bantha Group became sole owners of Biscuit Baron in [[33 ABY]]. At that time major stockholders were publicly identified and took over as the new board of directors of the restaurant chain. Izara now serves as Vice President of Marketing for the Golden Bantha Group and Biscuit Baron.

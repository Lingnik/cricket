'''Izin''' was a world in the [[Outer Rim]] owned by [[Reyon Mining]]. The birthplace of the [[Jedi]], [[Ezkhil i'Falzin]], it became the target of the [[Galactic Empire]]'s aggression during its hunt for the Jedi. [[Grand Moff]] [[Scaven Marx]] negotiated a deal with Reyon Mining to gain control over the world, in return making [[Paris Sieara]], Reyon's CEO, the CEO of [[Kuat Drive Yards-Sienar Fleet Systems|KDY-SFS]].

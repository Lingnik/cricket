'''Jacen Solo''' ([[11 ABY]]&mdash;) is the son of [[Han Solo|Han]] and [[Leia Organa Solo|Leia Solo]]. Attuned to the [[Force]] from birth, he has high potential to be a great [[Jedi Knight]] with his twin sister, [[Jaina Solo|Jaina]], whom he shares a deep connection to through the Force. He has a natural ability for communing with animals and nature.

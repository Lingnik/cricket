'''Jack Xiener''' was a [[Second Lieutenant]] in the [[Imperial Security Bureau]]. His first assignment in [[11 ABY]] as an agent was aboard [[ICRV Admiral Canis|HIMS ''Admiral Canis'']] where he acted as a liaison for [[Black Stars]] squadron and as a loyalty check for the crew, a role often performed by [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] agents.

== Overview ==
James Loeis is a freelance technician from Corellia specializing in star ships. He was away from his homeworld, doing a starship modification job for a friend who had moved to Nar Shaddaa when the last Imperial occupation of his home world began and only returned well after it had ended. A brief return trip to Nar Shaddaa led to a confrontation with Hutt Crimelord's enforcers and soon after he returned home for the second time he found himself caught in the struggle between various Corellian factions over whether or not to leave the New Republic. These events led to him training with a blaster and training in how to avoid attacks aimed at him. He also has some education in flying space transports, and a little bit of training in flying larger vessels and using Star ship scale weaponry. He hates bullies with a passion and will pick a fight with one if at all provoked, which is the trait that originally got him into the conflict on Nar Shaddaa.

== Description ==

James is a human male in his late 20s. He has a lanky frame and pale skin and is around average height. He has short back hair usually  accompanied by a shorter beard and grey eyes with a sma;l scar across the bridge of his nose being his only visible marking in mist cases.


== Roleplay Hooks ==

* Anything involving his tech skills be it finding revere engineering tech, salvaging unknown tech, or repairing ships and devices.

* Pretty much any adventure where his skills can be of some use that isn't character suicide and doesn't involve harming his homeworld, becoming a kidnapper or slaver, or harming someone without what he feels is a good cause.

'''Janos Audron''' was the chief gunnery officer aboard [[I2SD Tormentor|HIMS ''Tormentor'']] around [[13 ABY]]. He was demoted in [[14 ABY]] after nearly killing [[Danik Kreldin]] and other allies after issuing an orbital bombardment on their position during a clash in the wastes of [[Selene]]. He was later the chief gunnery officer aboard the [[I2SD Conqueror|HIMS ''Conqueror'']].

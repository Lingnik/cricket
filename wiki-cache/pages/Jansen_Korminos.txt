'''Jansen Korminos''', born in [[11 ABY]], is the son of [[Elena Korminos]] and Lt. [[Markelan Korminos]].  He lived with his mother until 14 ABY, when she joined the [[New Republic FleetOps]].  He now lives with Sgt. Peleas on [[Corellia]], his mother's former homeworld.  Elena visits him whenever she is on leave.  In the meantime, they keep in touch by sending frequent holograms to each other.    

== Description ==
Jansen Korminos is a small boy no more than three or four years old. He has the build of a boy who will grow up to be short but athletic. His skin is tan, perhaps from birth, perhaps from playing in the sun, and his blonde hair is almost white. Freckles all over his face enhance his image of the outdoors ragamuffin. He wears grey pants of some thick, rough material that is already worn in the knees, and a shirt that might once have been white.

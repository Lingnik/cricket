'''Jard Kisal''' was an [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[Fleet Admiral]] and [[Warlord]] who waged a campaign against the [[Galactic Empire]]'s remnants, the [[New Republic]], and rival warlords for control of the [[Inner Rim]] in the years following [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]]'s death. Utilizing his fleet, the [[Imperial Fifth Fleet]], Kisal successfully gained control of several systems before finally meeting his end in [[7 ABY]] at the Battle of Denon.

'''Jareth''' became the TP ([[Tiny Plot]]) Wizard in 2005 after retiring from another wiz position. Jareth enjoys working directly with the player base and in his nearly five years of play on SW1 he has played both FC and FU characters and is still a very active player.

His duties include assisting the players in designing plots, helping them get the TPs off the ground and offering advice on how to improve them. Jareth is also the wizard to go to if you want to request something RP related for your TP that doesn't require an application to the [[RolePlay Admin|RPAdmin]]. He is more then happy to offer teleports to plot locations and make announcements on your behalf.

Jareth has a SW1 website which offers advice and information on tiny plot formats, story design, creating interesting NPCs, running a TP and applying to the RPA.

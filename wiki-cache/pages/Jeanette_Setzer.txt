'''Jeanette Setzer''' was a renowned reporter for [[InterGalactic News|IGNews]]. She also owned and operated a casino on [[Tatooine]] and a transport company on [[O'paal]], and was known to have business interests within the [[Galactic Empire]], though her true loyalties were unknown, even to Jeanette.

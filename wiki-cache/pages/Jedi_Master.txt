'''Jedi Master''' is the highest rank most Jedi can obtain. In the days of the [[:starwars:Republic|Old Republic]], it was a requirement for becoming part of the [[:starwars:Jedi Council|Jedi Council]]. It is reserved for those who have shown exceptional devosion and skill in [[:starwars:The Force|the Force]]. The title of [[Sith Lord]] is on par with a Jedi Master.

The rank is usually achieved by [[Jedi Knight|Knights]] who have successfully entered [[Padawan|Padawans]] into Knighthood, as it was in the days of the [[:starwars:Jedi Order|Old Jedi Order]]. The Master is then permitted to pick another apprentice. The rank can also be granted by the leading members of the [[Jedi Order|New Jedi Order]] to a Knight for performing some extraordinary deed or service, though this is even rarer than it was in the days of the Old Jedi Order.

The rank of Jedi Master is appropriated for those who have not only showed exceptional skill in the ways of the Force, but exceptional maturity and level thinking.

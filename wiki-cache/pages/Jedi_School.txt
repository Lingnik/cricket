The '''Jedi School''' was a [[Jedi]] training center on [[Tatooine]], created by [[Jedi Master]] [[Luke Skywalker]], head of the [[Jedi Order|New Jedi Order]]. The School would see many initiates pass through on their way to Knighthood. However, in [[9 ABY]], [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Bacharan Valak]], [[Dark Lord of the Sith]], corrupted the mind of one [[Force]] adept, [[Cort Stasus]], and forced the young man into executing the [[Third Jedi Purge]] which saw the destruction of the School and the death of many apprentices of the fledging Order.

Afterwards, the Jedi would relocate to the bluffs along [[Corellia]]. The tradition of the school would live on through this safehouse on Corellia, and later at the [[Basilica|Jedi Basilica]] on [[Coruscant]], and the [[New Jedi Temple]] on [[Ord Mantell]].

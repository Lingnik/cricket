[[Jeymian Rhanhak]] was formerly the Chief Executive Officer of [[Merr-Sonn|Merr-Sonn Mil/Sci]].  Her tenure is most remembered for Merr-Sonn's acquisition of several lucrative licensing agreements with [[CDMC]]. Rhanhak established such a rapport with the Caspians that, when she was forced out of her position by the Merr-Sonn board of directors, the agreements were revoked.

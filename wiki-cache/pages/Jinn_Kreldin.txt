'''Jinn Kreldin''' was the younger brother of [[Danik Kreldin]].

He was born on [[Corellia]] after the end of the [[:starwars:Clone_Wars|Clone Wars]] and set off in his brother's footsteps, joining the [[Imperial Navy]] and the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]].

He never attained the same fame as his brother, though he did serve aboard the [[I2SD Tormentor|HIMS ''Tormentor'']] with Danik for a brief period of time. Jinn would later be shot down during the [[War of the Throne]], though he survived with severe injuries which forced him into early retirement.

He returned to Corellia for a few months before being hired into a private shipping company on [[Bespin]], where he remained until retirement.

==Behind the Scenes==
Jinn was played by an old friend of Danik's player.

=== A Wayward Youth ===
'''Jit Masco '''was born [[2 ABY]] in Coronet City, Corellia to two loving Duros parents who couldn't afford to raise  him. His earliest memories as a child are entirely of the Coronet City, Wayward Home for Boys. 

As a Duros, he was in the minority of the orphans there but managed to fit in well with the Corellian children and the few other sentient species living there. He was a passable student but showed a good amount of promise in mechanical and technical skills. He spent a great deal of his adolescence researching mechanical interests on the holonet.

=== Opportunity Knocks ===
By 12 ABY he was proving to be an expert mechanic and had aced a number of simulator space combat holo-games. Someone at the school passed on information about his talent and the New Republic decided to correspond with the young Duros. He was offered a chance at joining the Republic Military when he was of age and he began to dedicate large amounts of free time to pursuing mechanical and technical interests.

=== Jit Masco Gets a Mentor ===
In 15 ABY, New Republic Intelligence Director, Ewan Dain met the young Duros at a scientific exposition and the two to hit it off. Dain mentored the young Duros for a year and introduced him to shock-ball. Dain was a fan of the Coronet City Corsairs and took Jit to a number of games where the Duros developed a tremendous interest in the game. In that year the Duros and Corellian were inseparable and keep touch to this day.

=== Home is Where the Shipyard Is ===
In 16 ABY, Ex CorSec Inspector and Pilot, Wes Masco adopts Jit and encourages the Duros' interest in piloting and repair work. Jit works for Wes' small ship repair service out of Mos Espa, Tatooine.

=== The Academy Years ===
By 20 ABY, Jit could repair just about any starship. He was now of age and decided to follow through with the Academy to train as a starfighter pilot. The fact that he was an expert mechanic was a bonus to the New Republic. Jit studied hard and passed his exams with flying colors. After graduation he was put into Ghost Squadron to prove himself worthy of a future with Rogue Squadron. 

=== Life Among the Stars ===
Now, in 25 ABY, Jit has proven himself time and again to be a valuable asset to not only StarOps and its pilots, but also as a skilled mechanic. 

Though he hasn't flown any missions against the Empire yet, he is eager to prove himself out there amongst the stars.

'''Jordaan Mag''' ([[3 ABY]] - ) is a [[InterGalactic News]] reporter her frequently covers events in the [[Caspian Union]] and [[Imperium]]. She cover the [[Kichnar Orbital Station|Kichnar]] election returns in [[29 ABY]] when terrorists [[Report:Caspian Election Posts#Kichnar Carnage|attacked]] and received minor [[Report:Caspian Election Posts#Counting the Dead|injuries]]. A few weeks later, after recovering, she would be interviewing Fleet Captain Natanial Tarn on board the ''CMS Mikassa'' when the ship responded to a distress call from a refugee vessel and engaged the craft [[Report:Attack on Refugees|attacking]] it. Owing to these experiences, she was the reporter [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] invited to be embedded in a [[RPlog:Spiran Rescue|rescue mission]] he conducted with [[Grand Moff]] [[Elana Tracer]] on [[:starwars:Spira/Legends|Spira]] later that year.

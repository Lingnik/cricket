{{Speech|name=Joyriding|Author=[[Sha Dark]]|date=[[29 ABY]]|place=[[Union Hall]], [[Plaxton]]. [[Caspar]]}}

The newest promotional advertisement for Caspar Caff is once again interrupted just as the new products are about to be announced. The screen is replaced with the Vector Apex of the [[Caspian Union]] before that to fades to display a [[Zeltron]] woman in her early twenties sitting behind a desk. The woman is caption as [[Sha Dark]], Caspian Minister of Justice, [[Union Council|Councillor]] of [[Orleanon]].

"I nearly spit out my Caspar Caff when watching a report of arrest warrants issued for [[Ara te Danaan|Ara]] and [[Tyler te Danaan]]. My office has yet to receive any requests from [[Griffon Alliance|Griffon]] authorities in this regard but it seems truly bizarre to me that such a warrant would be issues in regards to this so-called crime. It is the understanding of this office that the youth in question are subject to a joint-custody agreement between [[Colonel]] [[Zeak Oppenhiemer|Oppenhiemer]] and his [[Johanna Siri te Danaan|ex-wife]] under the jurisdiction of the [[New Republic|Republic]]. Should that agreement be given any weight it seems both perverse and preposterous that arrest warrants and a legal spokesman making defamatory statement's about Colonel Oppenhiemer's ex-wife would be how he opts to handle this parenting matter.

If, however, we are to take seriously the Griffon claim that these two youths are in fact adults and subject to punishment as such than why would Colonel Oppenhiemer insult them by maintaining such a custody relationship, would that not violate Griffon principles? It also begs the question as to why this craft was taken. As adults they would have the free right of travel which suggests that the so-called crime in question may have in fact been motivated by unprincipled efforts to confine their freedom of movement.

Be it known the Caspian Union will reject any such arrest warrants or extradition requests made by the Griffons on this matter. Furthermore, while we have always respected the autonomy of their Griffon presence operating on [[Kichnar Orbital Station]], any attempts made there should the youth in question show up in Caspian space will be prosecuted. Should Colonel Oppenhiemer and the Griffons continue misuse the galactic justice system to manhandle an internal family matter the Caspian government will be forced to extend a formal offer of asylum these youth."

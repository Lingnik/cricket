The '''Justice Council''' was the council of the [[New Republic Senate|Senate]] charged with judicial matters in the [[New Republic]]. The Justice Council was charged with approving nominees to the [[Court of Justice]] made by the [[Chief of State]]. Internally, it also ruled on legal charges made against members of the Senate. The Chair of the Justice Council was a member of the [[Inner Council]], and was also the [[Minister of Justice]].

{{title|''KMS Coventry''}}
The royal heavy cruiser '''''Coventry'' (HCA-3)''' belongs to the [[Arsenal-class Heavy Cruiser|''Arsenal'' class]] and is one of the most decorated warships of the [[Caspian Navy]]. She is the only one of her class to be operated by a mixed crew (her class sisters are operated by all-[[Krittain|Krittish]] crews), and has been deployed to numerous locales throughout the galaxy. Her size, armament, and capabilities allow her to operate on lone-ship mission profiles, and her origin as a Krittish starship grants her a degree of tolerance from other galactic governments not typically afforded to readily-recognized Caspian designs.

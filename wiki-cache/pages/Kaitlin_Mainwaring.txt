'''Kaitlin Mainwaring''' was a local resident of [[Nar Shaddaa]]. She was close friends with [[Del Marx]] and [[Ayaka'Gul]], and together operated a small smuggling business off the moon. She played a part in the [[Nar Shaddaa Revolution]] of [[15 ABY]].

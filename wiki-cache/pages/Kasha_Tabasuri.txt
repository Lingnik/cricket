Kasha ([[12 ABY]] –) is the [[Mutanda|Mutandan]] Spiritual Advisor (currently on sabbatical) and the Emissary of the [[Caspian Foreign Ministry]].  She's currently doing charity work for [[The Mission]] and personal advisor and friend to [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] of [[Caspar]].

== Background ==

=== Young Life (12 ABY – [[18 ABY]]) ===
Kasha, like many of the [[Horansi]] people, was born and raised on [[Mutanda]], a simple, unassuming planet in the outer-rim of the galaxy. Her mother was on the council of elders as a spiritual advisor. Her father disappeared from her life before she was even old enough to remember him. From an early age, Kasha took a liking to the role her mother had in shaping the ever evolving politics of the Horansi people. Being a single parent, Kasha's mother would often have to bring her to the council chambers, sit her in a corner and have her wait quietly until the day was over. Kasha learned two very important things during her times in the chambers. One, that after finding a piece of wood and a sharp rock, she had a talent for making wood carvings and two, the adults all had some funny ideas about just about everything. As for the first, her mother took notice and fueled that talent by giving her materials and a knife to carve with. But as for the second, she often took it upon herself to call them out by telling them their ideas were stupid.

At first, the adults on the council laughed at her and told her to stay out of adult affairs. They didn't want to offend her mother by telling her that she couldn't bring her daughter any more. They needed her advice, especially when conflicts among the castes arose. But over time, the council began to fall in love with her innocent charm and childish banter. They began to ask her, at first halfheartedly, what she would do instead of what they had suggested. Over time, they were surprised by the answers she would sometimes offer. Her childish view on life often gave the elders a different perspective and as Kasha listened over the many months of her time there, she learned and her words became very thought-provoking.

=== Spiritual Advising (18 ABY – [[27 ABY]]) ===
Before long, the council determined that they had not one, but two spiritual advisors. At the young age of 6, Kasha became an honorary member of the council and took a seat next to her mother as a secondary spiritual advisor. By the age of 8, Kasha was given the surname of Tabasuri, which in the native tongue of the Horansi means "become wise" and was offered a permanent position on the council. She and her mother remained on the council until her mother took ill when Kasha was 12. Kasha took on two roles that year. One, as the primary spiritual advisor to the council in her mother's absence, and the second, the caretaker of her ailing mother. That was a stressful year for her for she nor the shamans and doctors could figure out what was wrong with her mother. Sadly, three days before her 13th birthday, Kasha's mother had succumb to her mysterious disease. The year that followed was one of the hardest for her. She was now an orphan and had she not have been a member of the council, she would've become a ward to the tribes of Mutanda and be sent off to a place where she would have an adult to care for her. But the council granted her special permission to stay in her current residence and be treated as an adult for any and all issues related to her orphanage. In return, she agreed to remain on the council, where she continued to advise for two more years.

=== Fated visit and start of mission off-world (27 ABY) ===
On her fifteenth birthday, she received a message from a Horansi claiming to be her father. He was waiting for her at the Spaceport. He said he had something urgent to tell her. Skeptical but curious, she went to meet him. What she found was that he was not there but came across a datapad that he had apparently left behind. On it, she discovered that if this was indeed his and he had indeed contacted her, he was in trouble and she needed to find him. The clues from the datapad suggested that he lived off-world and had found himself in bad company. She knew this was crazy and everything about this seemed fishy.. but she did wonder what happened to her father.. so, she took her leave from the council and set off on the first transport ship to some planet she's never even heard of, to find her father, or at least a clue or trace of what he's been up to.

=== An unplanned distraction (27 ABY – [[28 ABY]]) ===
It didn't take her long before Kasha realized that finding her father wouldn't be as easy as going to various planets and asking the local populace about if they saw another Mashi Horansi like herself and before long, she found herself all spent, both in determination and financially.  She found herself stranded on [[Tatooine]] with not a credit to her name.  Without any more funds to travel to other planets, she spent the next year alone on the sandy planet, where she managed to make a small living as a produce trader at one of the market places in [[Mos Eisley]].  She also honed her wood whittling craft, making small animal figurines out of the wood she'd buy from the other local merchants and then giving them to the children of the patrons of her stall.  She soon became known to the locals as the 'Woodcarving Feline' and her stall saw just as many children wanting the latest figurines as the adults for the high quality produce that she traded and sold for reasonable prices.

=== A Chance Meeting and the Mission that followed (28 ABY –) ===
One day, while selling her wares, Kasha met a [[Sarian]] named [[Crestian Tarasar]], who, after talking and finding that they had much in common, offered her something she couldn't refuse: He was starting a charity called [[The Mission]] and he needed someone kindhearted like herself to be one of its founding members.  Not only would it get her off of Tatooine and into a more stable job, but he had assured her that she would be able have the resources and means to resume the search for her father.  With the aid of her friend [[Tiali Nora]], a young [[Lorrdian]] pilot who was in the same boat as she, stranded on the planet and trying to make ends meet, Kasha left Tatooine with a renewed determination to find her father while simultaneously helping to make the galaxy a better place.

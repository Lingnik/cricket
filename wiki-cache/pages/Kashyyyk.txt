'''Kashyyyk''' was the lush, tree-filled home planet of the Wookiees. Once a member of the [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Galactic Republic]], this Mid-Rim world was enslaved by the [[Galactic Empire]] before becoming one of the first member worlds of the [[New Republic]].

==Geography==

Kashyyyk was known as a planet with wild, abnormally large foliage. With no axial tilt and a perfectly circular orbit, there were no changes in seasons, resulting in a relatively stable weather system gifted with a consistent pattern of rainfall. The planet itself was composed of a molten metallic core, rocky mantle, and a silicate rock crust. The planet had four continents, making up 40% of the surface, which were mostly covered in lush, tree-filled forests, although the planet did have a small amount of desert region in the rain-shadow of its small mountain ranges. A tropical ocean belt contained strings of islands and long coral reefs. In addition, a few sandy beaches could be found along the shores of its oceans.

===Flora and Fauna===

Vast forests dominated by [[:starwars:wroshyr tree/Legends|wroshyr tree]]s several kilometers high existed on the planet. The prevalent ecology could be politely described as a "layered deathtrap", as the dangers presented by local wildlife increased as one descended toward the forest floor. According to [[Wookiee]] culture, this vertical environment consisted of seven levels. The bulk of Wookiee civilization was located on the uppermost seventh level, and even the bravest Wookiees rarely ventured below the fourth level. The ground level was known as the [[:starwars:Shadowlands/Legends|Shadowlands]] and, sometimes, the "notherworld".

The forests of Kashyyyk were filled with a tremendous variety of plant life, many with some form of consciousness. The Wookiees learned to communicate with these plants and often came to an understanding about their use. One example of this was with the [[:starwars:Orga/Legends|Orga]] plants, who allowed the Wookiees to harvest their older roots to use in Life Day ceremonies.

==History==

[[Image:Battle of Kashyyk epIII.jpg|thumb|left|250px|The [[:starwars:Battle of Kashyyyk/Legends|Battle of Kashyyyk]].]]

===The Old Republic===

Kashyyyk was a member of the Galactic Republic for millennia, with a hazy and relatively unknown history. At the start of the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]], Kashyyyk remained non-aligned. Eighteen months into the Clone Wars, however, disputes regarding the control of [[hyperspace]] route planning would trigger [[General]] [[:starwars:Grievous/Legends|Grievous]] to put pressure on the Wookiees by way of a military operation. [[King]] [[:starwars:Grakchawwaa/Legends|Grakchawwaa]] was on the verge of siding with the [[:starwars:Confederacy of Independent Systems/Legends|Confederacy]] out of self-preservation, when news came that his son, [[Prince]] [[:starwars:Rikummee|Rikummee]], had accidentally been killed by Grievous's probe droids. In anger he allied himself with the Republic, ultimately forcing the Separatists to mount a [[:starwars:Battle of Kashyyyk/Legends|massive invasion]] in the closing stages of the war. The Wookiees took heavy casualties, but emerged victorious.

:''See a more detailed account [[:starwars:Kashyyyk/Legends#History|here]]''.

===Enslaved by the Empire===

Victory was short-lived for the Wookiees. With the rise of the [[Galactic Empire]], the [[Jedi]] were branded enemies of the Republic by [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]]. The planet was renamed '''Wookiee Planet C''' due to the Wookiees' high regard for the [[:starwars:Jedi Order/Legends|Jedi Order]], and placed it under martial law. In response, the [[Wookiee]]s attempted a [[:starwars:Battle of Kashyyyk  (19 BBY)|rebellion]] against their oppressors, but failed.

Following the advice of the Trandoshans, who had long been enemies of the Wookiees, the Empire began using the powerful Wookiees to carry out slave labor. Wookiee captives were used for countless Imperial projects, including the first two [[:starwars:Death Star/Legends|Death Star]]s.

:''See a more detailed account [[:starwars:Kashyyyk/Legends#Galactic Empire and slavery|here]]''.

===The New Republic===

After the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]], the Wookiees were liberated from slavery by the [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Alliance to Restore the Republic]]. Months later, Kashyyyk would become officially recognized as a member of the [[New Republic]], with Wookiee Commander [[:starwars:Kerrithrarr|Kerrithrarr]] being one of the delegates to sign the [[Declaration of a New Republic]]. The Wookiees became an important facet in the war against the [[Post-Endor Imperial power struggle|Imperial Remnant]], and Kashyyyk would soon become home to one of [[New Republic GroundOps]]' most important garrisons, which was constructed in [[7 ABY]] by Major General [[Beaubos Calhoun]].

In [[9 ABY]], riots between the New Republic garrison and natives caused political upheaval in rumors that the Republic's soldiers were behaving like the Empire. This provided the Empire with a quick target after [[Bacharan Valak|Valak's]] [[Great Campaign]] culminated with the [[Third Battle of Coruscant|loss of Coruscant]]. The Empire [[Second Battle of Kashyyyk|attacked]] Kashyyyk in [[10 ABY]] and overwhelmed the planet. After suffering the orbital destruction of its military base, and looking into the face of the threat of the [[Death Star III|Death Star]], the Wookiees assured that Kashyyyk would leave the New Republic permanently. The Empire withdrew.

Kashyyyk remained an independent and otherwise free planet until [[12 ABY]], when a splinter group from the [[Neo-Imperial Movement]] [[RPlog:Neo-Empire Attacks Kashyyyk|attacked Kashyyyk]]. It was here when the Wookiees began to rethink their position. With the [[Death Star III|Death Star]] having been destroyed, a Wookiee representative rose to the call of bringing Kashyyyk back into the fold of the [[New Republic]] around [[13 ABY]]. By the end of the year, Kashyyyk was once again a member of the New Republic in full force.

==Recent Events==
In late [[15 ABY]], the New Republic had lost Coruscant once again to the Empire, and relocated its seat of government to [[Ord Mantell]]. A force of Wookiees responded to a call for help from [[Senator]] [[Del Marx]], to help bolster the local law enforcement of [[New Alderaan]].

'''Kath Samba''' ([[8 BBY]] - ) is a [[Devaronian]] naval officer and public servant from the city of Udalooy on the planet [[Ixin]]. She served as Deputy Chief of Staff of the Ix Navy during the Republic of Ixin's existence following the collapse of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] in [[28 ABY]] and Ixin's joining of the new [[Caspian Union]] in [[29 ABY]]. Following the reintegration of Ix naval assets and personnel into the Union, Kath was made Deputy Commanding Officer of Task Group Renaissance of the [[Caspian 4th Fleet]] and Commanding Officer of the ''[[CMS Renaissance of Innovation]]'' which serves as an unofficial flagship of the [[Caspian Navy]].

== Early Life ==

Kath Samba was born in the city of Udalooy on the planet [[Ixin]] eight years before the battle. She came from prominent family which had long been associated with the Al Jaffa prison outside of Udalooy. Her maternal grandmother was prison psychologist who was instrumental in improving the conditions for inmates in the facility. Later her father served as the prison's warden, continuing the work of modernisation.

Destined for a role in public service, Kath initially set her sights on the military. As Ixin itself largely relied on mercenaries and lacked some of the normal trappings of military structure, she secured a placement in [[8 ABY]], through her family connexions at [[:starwars:Brionelle Memorial Military Academy|Brionelle Memorial Military Academy]] on Chandrila. During her first year at the academy she would befriend a third year student originally from the [[Caspian Sector]] like her named [[Crestian Tarasar]], who would become her boss later in her career.

== Early Career ==

Following her graduation from the academy in [[11 ABY]] was commissioned an [[Ensign]] in the [[New Republic FleetOps|New Republic Navy]] and accepted a posting as Communications Officer on the ''[[VSD Fusillade|Fusillade]]'', the same posting Crestian had turned down to continue with graduate studies at the academy two years earlier. After completing a three year tour on the Fusillade, Kath left New Republic service to return to Ixin which had always been her eventual plan.

Her return to Ixin in [[14 ABY]] coincided with purchase by the [[Ix Navy]] of the mothballed [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruiser]], ''Indefatigable'', from the [[Caspian Navy]]. Given her training and experience in a proper navy, as well as her family's influence, Kath was commissioned as [[Lieutenant Commander]] aboard the ''Indefatigable'' and given the position of Third Officer, under the command of [[Admiral]] [[Prince]] Zoltan Bin Salmon Al Sawd. She would remain with the ''Indefatigable'' for four years.

In [[18 ABY]], Kath's father died, and she suddenly found herself wanting to carry on her family's legacy more directly. As result of the work of her father and grandmother there was a position at Al Jaffa prison to support inmates in their transition into post-carceral life. Kath assumed this position while also deciding to take classes at the University of Feyenoor in order to obtain a law degree.

== In Union Service ==

When Ixin finally joined the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] in [[21 ABY]] it had little to offer militarily, but was eager to be fully engaged. With Kath now having just completed a law degree, in addition to her own past naval experience, she was encouraged by several princes to join the Caspian Navy so that Ixin had representation within its legal team. Accepting this call to service she was commissioned as a [[Commander]].

She remained a military lawyer until the fallout of the Caspian [[2015 Caspar Plot|conflict]] with [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] in [[24 ABY]] saw the defence budget decimated and the office she was part of was shrunk. Fortunately for Kath, a number of ship commands became open following several early retirements. Promoted to [[Captain]] she took command of the [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]] ''CMS Aph'relin'' which was assigned to the Coreward Frontier Fleet.

Promoted to [[Commodore]] in [[27 ABY]], Kath was given command of [[Hurricane-class Fleet Carrier|Hurricane-Class Fleet Carrier]] ''CMS Tonare'', also part of the Coreward Frontier Fleet. When the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] hit along with the Mergani [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]], ''Tonare'', ''Aph'relin'' and several support vessels were recalled by the [[Naval Intelligence Office]] to Ixin.

== Ix Navy ==

Following the collapse of the Caspian Democratic Union, the division of its naval assets was matter of great contention. Which world ended up controlling a ship often became a matter of where it was last stationed or what world key officers were from. The Princes of Ix with the support of some surviving members of the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]] who were hiding out on Ixin, were able to convince the key officers of ''Tonare'', ''Aph'relin'' and their support vessels to stay on at Ixin and become the Ix Navy. No doubt assisted by Kath's ties to Ixin.

Kath was elevated to the rank of [[Vice Admiral]] in the new [[Ix Navy]], serving as Deputy Chief of Staff. While a Prince Zoltan Bin Salmon Al Sawd served as Admiral and Chief of Staff at a desk on the surface, Kath was the field commander of the navy. Following the election of her old friend Crestian Tarasar as [[Presav]] of the new [[Caspian Union]], and its reunification with [[Kasputin Yar]] and [[Rauther]], the [[Caspian Repatriation Act (29 ABY)|Caspian Repatriation Act]] reaffirmed the Union's recognition of the Republic of Ixin and recognised Ixin's right to the vessels they already controlled. A month later, Ixin itself rejoined the Union, and its navy was reabsorbed.

== Renaissance ==

Back in the [[Caspian Navy]], Kath retained her rank of Vice Admiral and was given command of the [[Admiral Roj-class Battleship|Admiral Roj-Class Battleship]] ''[[CMS Renaissance of Innovation]]'' which was finally being put into service after being mothballed upon her completion. The ''Renaissance'' was to serve as the central vessel of Task Group Renaissance of the [[Caspian 4th Fleet]]. In addition to serving as the vessel's commanding officer, she would also be Deputy Commanding Officer of Task Group Renaissance.

The ''Renaissance of Innovation'' has had a high profile role under Kath's command. Though the Caspian Navy has no official flagship, the ''Renassiance'' often serves this function such as at the [[Charros Wargames]] in [[32 ABY]]. The ''Renaissance'' is also known for being the lead vessel of various Caspian relief efforts such as responding in aftermath of the terrorist attack upon [[Cloud City]] on [[Bespin]]. Most recently it has led the Caspian response to the [[Report:Looming Famine?|famine]] in the [[:starwars:Arkanis sector/Legends|Arkanis Sector]]. 

The various aid missions the ''Renaissance'' has participated in has had Kath frequently working closely with Crestian's charity [[The Mission]]. In recognition of this she was recently given a seat on The Mission's board of directors following the retirement of [[Admiral]] Shan-Luke Pickart from the board.

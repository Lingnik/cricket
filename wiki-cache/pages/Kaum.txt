'''Kaum''' was a [[Major]] in the Army of the [[True Empire]]. He had formerly been a [[Captain]] within the [[Imperial Army]], a decorated war veteran, but was manipulated into believing [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]] had been [[Palpatine (imposter)|reborn]] anew to lead the [[True Empire]] to reclaim the throne from the traitor [[Bacharan Valak]].

Leaving behind his men in the Imperial Army, Kaum was given a promotion upon arrival at [[Mon Calamari]]. He would fight with distinction during the [[Athaniss Campaign]], and would be one of the few surviving True Imperial units to escape to [[Novar III]]. After Novar III [[Battle of Novar III|fell]], Kaum would surrender and be forced into a [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] re-education camp. After nearly five months of work, Kaum would be considered fit for duty and sent back to the frontlines of the [[Galactic Civil War]].

==Surfacing==
Most of Keifan's background; remains a mystery for many Intelligence Agencies, Law Enforcement and generally Underworld Figures alike.  For the most part, he moves behind the shadows of the world; simply appearing in several different bars and spaceports at random; and appearing to really have no purpose there whatsoever aside from drinking and carrying on.

Those that are able to overhear bits and pieces of what he describes as his past; hear tales of the good old days when smuggling was a more dangerous time; fewer hotblooded youngsters trying to make a name for themselves, and honor among thieves and scoundrels.  From some of his drunken tales; he's mentioned being away for nearly 13 years on self-imposed exile in the forests of Ithor; contemplating his deeds, and keeping himself as clean as he can; after an apparently long and less than enjoyable bout with spice addiction. These days he appears to be clean; just not sober by any means.

While he seems to appear on Caspar quite a bit; there seems to be no affiliation to their Government; more of a favoured planet as the man pines for someone else's wife; as well as seemingly causing disturbances with the more than notorious [[Axel Vichten]]; as well as around Corellia with the attractive, yet auspicious [[Kinsa'una]]; however they don't appear to be a couple, more like business associates or acquaintances of some note.

Those that have seen his drunken escapades with his friends; generally mention his very short fuse, and quickness to draw his blaster to solve nearly every situation; although he seems to pay his tab just before the authorities tend to show up; leaving a normal mess for them to clean up in his wake.

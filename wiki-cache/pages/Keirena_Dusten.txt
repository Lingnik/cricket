'''Keirena Dusten''', or '''Sheltie''', was the younger sister of former [[Presav]] [[Avalyshaar Dusten]]. The nickname ''Sheltie'' comes from the [[Sarian (language)|Sarian]] words, ''shael teehan'', meaning ''younger sister''.

Like her sister, Sheltie enrolled in the [[Provincial Army of the Union|Provincial Army]] at first, then molded into the [[Union Marines]] when they were formed in [[4 ABY]]. She continued in the service of the Marines through to [[8 ABY]], and her performance in her duties brought her into frequent Presidential Guard (or ''Praetsav'') assignments. Soon, she was leading a unit as a [[PRISM|Praetor]]. In an unprecedented occurrence, Sheltie became one of the two personal agents for her sister's Presavadency, and served not only as a protector, but familiar comfort.

Unfortunately for both Sheltie and Avalyshaar, the younger Dusten had taken a rotation off assignment when the Presav was shot in an assassination attempt in the Hall of Hours in [[9 ABY]]. Devastated, Sheltie felt a degree of guilt, and tended to her comatose sister for several months.

Later, after Avalyshaar had recovered, Keirena tried her hand at civilian work. She was employed as the Marketing Director of [[NovaCom Cybertronics]], based in its Caspian office.

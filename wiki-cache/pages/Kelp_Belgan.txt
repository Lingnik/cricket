'''Kelp Belgan''' ([[31 BBY]] - ) is a [[Mon Calamari]] officer in the [[New Republic Navy]] and [[captain]] of the [[Mon Calamari Star Cruiser|Star Cruiser]] [[MCSC Antiphon|''Antiphon'']].

Belgan is highly regarded amongst his peers, but is known for his salty demeanor and penchants for condescending behaviour when dealing with others less experienced than himself. He was a bridge officer aboard the ''[[MCSC Home One|Home One]]'' and learned the tricks of the trade from the great [[Admiral]] [[Gial Ackbar|Ackbar]], but possesses none of the master tactician's diplomatic flair. He has lost several clutch-siblings over the years in the war with the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]], and while he never discusses it, the weight of these losses visibly wears on him. He drives his ship hard and his crew even harder. He is less acerbic around his senior staff officers, several of which are fellow Mon Calamari, [[Quarren]], or [[:starwars:Selkath/Legends|Selkath]].

Amongst his common crewers and other beings in the Navy, he's referred to as "Ol' Trout Trunks".

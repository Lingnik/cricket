'''Kendell Laarken''' was the husband of [[Caspian Democratic Union]] [[Presav]] [[Avalyshaar Dusten]]. Prior to his marriage he had been a bodyguard of [[Chantinelle Thistleborn]], the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] Ambassador to the Union. He disappeared during his wife's term as Presav and was presumed dead.

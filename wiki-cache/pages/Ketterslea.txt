The planet '''Ketterslea''' is a massive gas-giant and lone planet in orbit around the dwarf star Kett. The rest of the system is swathed in extensive gas and dust clouds &mdash; the possible remnants of a small quantity of planets. Ketterslea itself, therefore, is the possible lone survivor of Kett's collapse, but because of the tremendous amount of mystery and conjecture surrounding this region of space, no definite conclusions can be made.

Broad, shallow rings of dust encircle the gas giant, and it has several curiously-shaped moons, none of which are truly round, or inhabitable. Two of the moons orbit Ketterslea in perfect mirrored synchronism, and upon these moons the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] installed two high-output [[SONG]] beacons. The beacons represent one of the main junctures within Union space so as to travel to and from the capital system of [[Caspia]], although the moons were not garrisoned until after [[8 ABY]] (''see below''). [[Caspian Navy|Naval]] patrols do rotate through here regularly, as the system does see a great deal of traffic and Ketterslea itself is quite a sight for the awe-inspired spacefarer.

=== Ketterslea in War-Time ===
During the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign]], several skirmishes and engagements took place at locations much like Ketterslea, but the [[Battle of Ketterslea]] is recorded as being the largest, and most indicative, of these battles. Caspian warships, in most cases out-scaled, used the dust and gas clouds to marked advantage against the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] convoys moving through this region as well as the flotilla dispatched to prevent them doing so. As a result of this engagement, and the Campaign in general, an extensive outpost was established on the moon Clappam with facilities for [[Provincial Army of the Union|Provincial Army units]], [[Marine Aerospace Division|MAD squadrons]] of patrol fighters and [[L-200 Halcyon transport|L-200 cutters]], and a docked [[Lightning-class Sloop|''Lightning''-class sloop]] or similar. Completion of the yet-unnamed facility is expected for 17 ABY.

Later, in [[14 ABY]] during the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]], several dozen Caspian warships were assembled at Ketterslea, anticipating an Imperial incursion to come through here once again. When the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] forces instead struck at ward [[Mikassa]], most of the fleet promptly departed to head to [[Rauther]] in haste.

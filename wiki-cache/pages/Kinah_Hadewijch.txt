'''Kinah Hadewijch''' ([[2 BBY]] - ) is a young [[Stormtrooper]], not long out of the Academy at [[Carida]], and recently assigned to serve with the [[501st Legion]] at [[Corellia]]. As such, she is cocky and highly motivated in the service of the [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] and the [[Galactic Empire]].

== Biography ==

Kinah was practically groomed to serve the Empire from before birth. Her father attended the Stormtrooper Academy at [[Carida]] before her, but was one of the unfortunate wash-outs due to fatality. His death occurred shortly after Kinah's birth and there was speculation that his excitement at being a new father interfered with his focus. Regardless, to her, his failure was like a blight on the Hadewijch name. 

The girl developed an intense and focused sense of honor, and as she grew up, decided that the only way to redeem the family name was to attend the academy and succeed where her father had failed. For a female, this would be a grueling process, but one she was eager to pursue. Every choice throughout her childhood was made with the goal of becoming a [[Stormtrooper]]. Kinah joined the [[Sub-Adult_Group|Sub-Adult Group]] at an early age and was well-versed in the training and belief system necessary to enter the Stormtrooper Academy at the earliest possible opportunity. She excelled at the preparatory training, including the physical hardships in which SAGRec 
members participated.

In her training at [[Carida]], Kinah continued to excel, graduating in the top twenty percent of her class. After graduating, the trooper received a highly sought-after detail - assignment to the [[501st Legion]], "Vader's Fist". Since her assignment, Kinah has made a name for herself within the trooper ranks as having a quick wit which occasionally puts her on the wrong side of [[Master Sergeant]] [[Paulus Lazrus|Lazrus']] temper, but also as a trooper ready and willing to serve the will of the Emperor.

'''King''' is a royal title used by various planets and galactic powers. The female variant is '''Queen''', which may either be someone holding the title in their own right, or the wife of a King. Other variants included '''[[:starwars:Blood Monarch|Blood Monarch]]''', '''[[:starwars:Emperor-King|Emperor-King]]''', '''[[:starwars:Godking|Godking]]''', '''[[:starwars:Pirate King/Legends|Pirate King]]''', and '''[[:starwars:Queen Mother|Queen Mother]]'''. A government ruled by a King or Queen is known as a monarchy. Monarchs are generally the leaders of planets or of particular species irrespective of territory. In many cases they are hereditary rulers but in some cases such as [[:starwars:Naboo/Legends|Naboo]] they are elected. The power exercised by a monarch varies greatly through the galaxy. In modern times many monarchs wield only limited power mostly relegated to ceremonial functions such as in the [[Krittain|Krittain System]], other worlds such as [[Corellia]]. In some societies, particularly more primitive ones, a monarch may wield considerable authority.

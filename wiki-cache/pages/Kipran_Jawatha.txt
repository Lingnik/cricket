== Early Life ==

The first born child of [[Elana Tracer]] with her then-husband [[Benjamin Jawatha]] (Benjawa), Kipran Jawatha came into the world during a party thrown by [[Morganna Tazecks]] when an electrical storm and some foul play on board caused a ship-wide black out during which his mother went into early labor. Throughout his life he would be absent one parent or another due to various complications in their lives. It started when Elana disappeared while piloting her own YT-2400 that had broken down leaving her stranded. His father was deployed with the [[Caspian Democratic Union|CDU]] reserves so it fell to their friend [[Ernest Pallando]] to take custody of the infant for several months until Elana returned home. After she returned, his mother went through intensive therapies to recover from her ordeal, and returned to her position in the CDF and as Kipran's mother thereafter. Though his father did disappear for a while, he also returned. But alas, it was not for long as [[Benjamin Jawatha]] was found murdered in his office in the [[FineLine Spaceways|FLS]] Tower.

When he was three his mother was Presav of the CDU and she also gave birth to his sister, Ashton Van Sen. Six months later and Elana had lost her position as Presav and moved Kipran and his sister to live with Ashton's father Morrison Van Sen on an island on Kalla. Several months later, brother Longinus Van Sen joined the happy family. Many hours of Kipran's life were spent running along the beaches and adventuring in the jungle-like terrain that backed their property. He learned to repulsor-surf at a young age, as did all of Tracer's kids, and eventually was taught to ride swoops and pilot starships by his mother.

Kipran was very young when his mother first noticed him moving blocks without touching them and getting snacks out of the high cupboard. She discouraged him using his "gift" as she herself had long been fearful of force users. It was during Ben's brief reappearance that Elana learned Ben had been kidnapped by the very same parents who abandoned him on the streets of Caspar, with no memory of who he was, just his name. They were both Dark force users of a breed of Sarians that also included the infamous Sarian [[Ozzle Olumpari]] and that the talent had skipped a generation in Ben. Kipran, however, had not been so lucky. Losing Ben after learning this was even more disruptive to his young son's life as Elana's fears worsened after Ben's information. Suffice it to say, she tried to curb her son's natural abilities throughout his childhood.

=== Finding Himself ===
At 18, Kipran dropped out of University on Kalla and left home. Elana had no choice but to go along with her young adult son's plans to roam the galaxy. She gave him access to a trust but wouldn't buy him his own ship. He was supposed to keep in touch but after several weeks he stopped communicating. He'd wandered and found himself in the company of several members of the Sith Court which lead him to [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]]. While Malign trained him, Admiral [[Drayson Honos]] mentored him. When his mother finally found him, he'd just finished his new lightsaber and been given the rank of Commander in the new [[Imperium]] navy where he was assigned to [[Crestian Tarasar|Crestian Tarasar's]] fleet. Elana took a position as a Moff from Darth Malign to stay connected to her son. Kipran was later temporarily reassigned to Malign's Task Force.

In 29 ABY Kipran joined his mother and Admiral Crestian Tarasar on a mission to Spira, an Imperial resort world. There they rescued two Force-sensitive children from Trandoshan kidnappers. He was promoted to Captain for his part in the mission.

Later than year, he attended a ball on Bespin that [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]] held for Force-sensitive youth and their families, bring together children of both Jedi and Sith persuasion. It was here that he met and began to fall in love with [[Sonja Damion]], daughter of the Imperator and heir to the Damion legacy. He received tacit approval from her father to court Sonja, who was only sixteen at the time. Malign even gave Sonja an estate on Selene and transferred Kipran back to the Core to facilitate the courtship. Kipran and Sonja went public galaxy-wide when he appeared at her Coronation as [[Prince|Crown Princess]] of the Imperium and the Hapes as her escort. Kipran and Sonja accompanied Malign on several missions including clandestine trips with her aunt Johanna de Sire te Daanan's, who also raised Sonja from a young age on her homeworld of Tatooine, to seek out Sith, Jedi, and Damion family relics to use against the entity that is former Emperor Vadim.

Later, Kipran and Sonja took leave of Selene on the pretense of visiting her aunt Johanna home on her desert planet. They never filed a return flight plan and have stayed there for many months now. Rumors speculated that they may have eloped and left the Imperium for all intents and purposes. In truth, Sonja was enjoying being home with her cousins and she and Kipran had joined Johanna school for Force Users she'd built on Tatooine in Jabba's old palace.

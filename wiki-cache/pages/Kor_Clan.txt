The '''Kor clan''' from [[Ryloth]] is so named for the potent form of Ryl spice that was mined and distilled by the headclan. This notariety did not protect them from slavers, however, and many Kor members have found themselves in the indentured service of others.

Recently several prominent members from Kor clan have emigrated from Ryloth and relocated at New Kala'uun on [[Etti IV]]. They have resumed their family prestige with more legitimate professions, and contribute to the local headclan when necessary.

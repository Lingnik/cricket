Korriban is the sole planet of the [[:starwars:Horuset system|Horuset System]].  Originally it was the native home of the [[:starwars:Sith_(species)|Sith species]] and a sacred planet to the [[Sith Order]].  Housing many tombs of ancient and powerful [[Dark Lord of the Sith|Dark Lords of the Sith]], it still serves as a tremendous source of [[Dark Side|dark side]] power and secrets which have yet to be discovered.  The planet was abandoned after the [[:starwars:Great Hyperspace War|Great Hyperspace War]], falling into a barren and desolate word that is visited by a very few and known to even less.

Due to the nature of its connection with the dark side, those who may venture onto Korriban can find themselves killed quite quickly. Not only are any native speciest to the planet vicious, but it also contains an unknown number of [[:starwars:|sithspawn]] that protect not just themselves, but also the tombs of the fallen Dark Lords.

===OOC Note===
While the planet is not coded, the location of it is currently restricted knowledge to players, and would be known by only a very small few.  Please keep in mind to be aware of it and travel, the MUSH Administration must grant approval for such RP.

The '''Kreldin Estate''' was a massive, 3,500 acre estate on [[Corellia]]. It has housed the Kreldin family line for nearly a millennium.

It was constructed in [[:starwars:920_BBY|920 BBY]] near [[Coronet City]] after [[Davin Keldan|Davin Kreldin's]] ascension to nobility and the establishment of the [[House of Kreldin]]. It would be the seat of power for the Kreldin noble family. 

The estate was last in the hands of '''Tesera Kreldin''', widow of [[Kennith Kreldin]] and mother of [[Danik Kreldin]]. It was destroyed in late-[[15 ABY]] by HIMS ''Ravager'', under the directives of the [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] [[Select Committee]], due to charges of treason carried out against Danik Kreldin, who had tried to launch a plot that would usurp the new [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] from the throne. Prior to the bombardment, the estate was gutted, its furnishings and belongings auctioned off to raise funds for the Empire. In the days leading up to its destruction, [[Imperial Security Bureau|ISB]] agents tracked down as many of its current and former workers as it could, along with as many of Kreldin's relatives as could be found, and locked them inside one of the estate's ballrooms.

None escaped alive before the estate was turned into slag.

The remains of the Kreldin Estate were immediately cleared out and redeveloped into [[Camp Awakening]], a COMPNOR [[Sub-Adult Group]] camp which was opened little over a month later.

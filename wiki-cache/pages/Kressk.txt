{{Character infobox|image = [[file:Kressk.jpg|250px]]|name = Kressk Bloodclaw|homeworld = [[Trandosha|Dosha]]|species = Trandoshan|gender = Male|height = 2.2 meters|eyes = Black|skin = Green|rank = Chieftain|apprentices = Warlord}}Kressk is chieftain of the Bloodclaw Trandoshan clan, one of many clans which toils beneath the dew-claw of the ruling Blackscale clan.

In [[24 ABY]], Kressk took control of the twelve largest 'secondary' clans on Dosha, having slain their chieftains and formed a confederation whose size, if not armament, threatens to rival Blackscale power.{{NPC}}

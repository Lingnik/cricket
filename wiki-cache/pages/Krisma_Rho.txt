'''Krisma Rho''' was a young pilot in the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]]. He was born on [[w:c:starwars:Polneye|Polneye]], where his father was a weapons and flight instructor. After the [[w:c:starwars:Battle_of_Endor|Battle of Endor]], the [[Galactic Empire]] withdrew its forces from Polneye, and Krisma's father left on immediate deployment. Krisma joined the Imperial Military shortly thereafter, and three months later received notice his father had died, but left him with a recommendation to the [[Imperial Naval Academy]] and enough credits to make do. He graduated from the Academy and was assigned to the Imperial Starfighter Corps. His service record has been lost, however.

'''Krittain Minor''' is widely regarded (where regard exists for it at all) as perhaps the worst tourist locale in the galaxy, competing "favourably" with the likes of [[Tatooine]], [[:starwars:Hoth/Legends|Hoth]], and [[:starwars:Mustafar/Legends|Mustafar]]. The world is technically inhabitable to most forms of humanoid life, but boasts a hefty 2.1G gravity field, a cold climate (with unpredictable winters at temperate latitudes), an ecosystem that can best be described as "nightmarish" and "dangerous", and a generally uncongenial populace. 

Populations tend to be concentrated upon highland plateaus, as the planet's mass is sufficient to allow lethal concentrations of nitrogen and carbon dioxide to form at sea level. Specialized reducing respirators are recommended for those venturing to lower latitudes.

== Inhabitants ==

There are no native sentient life forms, but a prison colony established back in pre-Republic times has led to a divergent near-human populace. The resulting sub-species has adapted to high-gravity, and tends towards a short, broad-chested morphology. They are inevitably heavy-boned and deceptively strong and resilient. Despite numerous jokes about the intelligence of these '[[Minor Krit|Minor Krits]]', there is no actual evidence that they are at all below the Galactic baseline. There is perhaps fairness in the assertion that they are a touch unsophisticated - an impression amplified by rough manners, alarming culinary practices, and the fact that most of their sports involve hitting things (audience participation strongly encouraged).

In spite of the harsh climate, Krittain Minor has saw modest though increasing immigration in the last century of the Old Republic. These immigrants were largely from [[Krittain Major]]. The cultural impact of this migration was limited, but it was not long before genetically Minor Krit population was in the minority.

The advent of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] saw further increases in immigration. The economy saw a major boost from mining efforts led by [[Interspatial Prospecting, Inc.]] which did much to stabilise the erstwhile rocky finances of the world. Krittain Minor also became the site of the [[Union Marines|Union Marines']] Antrim Base which served as a major training facility and also boosted the local economy.

[[File:Aonach.jpg|Aonach|left|thumb]] Following the pogroms on [[Mergansar]], Krittain Minor took on roughly five million refugees. For a planet which already had a comparatively small population compared to most other former Union worlds, this represented quite an impact. The refugees, however, found the local population far more accommodating than was the case on Krittain Major. This has served to add a certain cosmopolitan flair to some settlements on the world. Aonach in particular has undergone a certain renaissance with the fusion of Krittish and Mergani cultures at play.

== Recent History ==

Following the collapse of the Caspian Democratic Union, Krittain Major and Minor formed a short lived Krit Union. After the election of a permanent government on [[Caspar]], the new [[Caspian Union]] sought to reunite with the worlds of the previous union. The [[Krittain|Krittain System]] was largely left out of these plans as there was a certain animosity between the two governments. [[Crestian Tarasar|Crestian Tarasar's]] efforts to violently oppose the Mergani pogroms culminating in a [[Mergansar Incident|battle]] with the [[Caspian Navy]] and the death of Krittish [[Sar Admiral]] [[Prae Kichakressa]] made the now [[Presav]] an unpopular figure with many Krits. For his own part Tarasar consider Krittain Major's failure to grant planetary citizenship to nearly 40% of its population to be abhorrent.

Though back-channel reunification negotiations were occurring on some level since Tarasar took power, they did not become serious until the ''Blechley Brook'', a vessel carrying Minor Krit students on their way back from an off-world field trip, was [[Report:A Minor Consideration|attacked]] by the slavers who captured and enslaved the passengers in [[30 ABY]]. This forced the [[Krittish Navy]] to accept aid from the Caspian Navy in their search for the slavers.

[[File:Maximilian Kritz.jpg|thumb|President Maximilian Kritz]] Realising their navy was stretched to thin to adequately protect their borders with an increased and embolden slaver presence in the region, the Krit Union folded up operations and joined the new Caspian Union. Despite serious efforts by the [[Caspian Intelligence Service]] the slavers responsible for the Blechley Brook incident have eluded capture, much to the embarrassment of all parties involved.

'''Kubaz''' have humanoid bodies and limbs with four broad, stubby
fingers on each hand and two large toes on each foot. The most striking
feature of the Kubaz face is the short prehensile trunk that serves as a
nose. Kubaz have rough-textured greenish-black skins, and bristly hair grows
from the tops of their heads. Kubaz eyes are extremely large and sensitive to
the red wavelengths of light, so they wear protective lenses in most foreign
environments.
         
Kubaz are cultured beings who place a high value on traditional
values, art, music, and other forms of sophisticated entertainment although
insect banquets are their favorite. Although they will not be insulted in a
guest does not join them for an insect feast, they will certainly conclude
that he lacks culture.

Kubaz are natives of Kubindi, a large world circling the blue
giant Ku'Bakai. This star is known for its magnificent solar flares, which
have made Kubindi into an arid world given to unpredictable storms and
oscillating weather patterns. The herbivorous forerunners of the Kubaz
started eating insects after a powerful solar flare burned Kubindi's life
away. To increase the variety of their meals, families began to exchange
insect hives, and a planetary trading community soon developed.
         
The development of trade led to more technological advances, and
Kubaz society became increasingly complex. They developed rapid transport
methods, computer technology for keeping track of accounts, and, eventually,
space travel.
         
Kubaz family members usually live in close proximity to each other,
and Kubaz social policies are primarily determined by the desires of the
larger trading families.

Although Kubaz are not common sights in galactic spaceports, they
have been in contact with the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] for many years, and have become a
cultured element of Imperial society, famous in some circles for the exotic
cuisine of their homeworld.

The Kubaz are extremely civilized, and consider tact and decorum to be among the most important aspects of life. Kubaz may have any available skills, although Kubaz who have not explored the galaxy will not have much skill with space travel or droids.
         
Movie Reference: The alien informant on [[Tatooine]] who helped the Imperial stormtroopers on the search for the droids in the first movie (SW:ANH) was a Kubaz.

The '''Kylan-3''' is [[SoroSuub Corporation|SoroSuub]]'s entry into the 'super-heavy' blaster pistols. Its power (and bulk) is such that it has been nicknamed the "[[Sullust|Sullustan]] Blast Cannon". Its output power is such that it is typically restricted on just about any civilized world. Indeed, it's stopping power and armor-piercing capabilities exceed that of most blaster rifles, though at shorter range than most conventional blaster pistols. While not especially 'pretty', this weapon's frame belies a certain functional elegance. Its frame of black composites and dully finished metal has attachment points that can easily take a variety of special sights and interfaces. The weapon's three-position fire selector seems positioned so that it can easily be manipulated by shooters of either handedness, and contains a 'stun' position for when less than lethal force is required.

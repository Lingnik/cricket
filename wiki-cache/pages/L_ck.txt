A member of the Verpine species, it has been years since L'ck has seen the sky of his own world.  No one is quite sure why he left, and it seems to be a topic he avoids talking about.

L'ck is a highly trained doctor and surgeon, able to perform complex procedures with great care.  He shows a certain disdain for bacta treatments, prefering more old fashioned methods.

On a few occasions, L'ck has also shown that he possess other skills, ones that are usually applied to less then legal acts.  How he acquired these skills is unknown, and he only seems to employ them when he agrees morally with what he is doing.

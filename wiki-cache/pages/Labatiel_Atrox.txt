'''Labatiel Atrox''' was a ruthless [[Imperial Navy]] officer and a veteran of the [[:w:c:starwars:Clone Wars|Clone Wars]]. He was known for his cold demeanor, though he was also known as a mystical, and spiritual man, though he never lost the ability to get any job done with merciless efficiency at a moment's notice. His inner charisma always kept him on the good side of those he wished to please, though he displayed a chilling aura few ever forgot. He was later made [[Director of Imperial Intelligence]] (referred to as Lord Director of the [[Ubiqtorate]]) and also personally headed up the [[Bureau of Operations]].

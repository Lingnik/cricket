'''Lainey Barine''' ([[6 BBY]] - )
was born on [[Nar Shaddaa]] in the Alien Sector. At present she's a woman eager to make her name as a efficient and capable smuggler.  Her somewhat dour attitude and penchant for disappearing for months on end can make her a difficult and unreliable business partner.

== Early Life ==
Little is known about Lainey Barine's early life although her fluency in various alien languages hints at time spent either on those planets or with sentients of those races. 

When she was 18 Lainey passed the exams to enter the Imperial Academy with flying colours although she was expelled and blacklisted a year later for insolence, and substance abuse before she could enter the Imperial Navy proper. She disappeared off the grid for a further 18 months after that and was occasionally spotted in various low-rent dives across the galaxy. 

Eventually the young woman managed to pick herself up and began to take small piloting and crewer jobs with unscrupulous firms that would not mind hiring a blacklisted pilot. More often than not Lainey's truculent personality would be an obstacle to long-term employment and she slowly drifted into illegal occupations such as piracy and smuggling.

== A New Beginning ==

Some time in 16 ABY Lainey Barine met the Ithorian infochant [[Rixt]].  After she helped secure Al's Feeding Frenzy from Shongo for him he introduced her to the [[Kgnisa Corellisi]] and Bailey Laran amongst others. Lainey worked for them as a pilot for a few weeks then vanished again to areas unknown, taking the weaponry they'd lent her with her.

More recently she surfaced on [[Tatooine]] and entered the employ of [[Daana Roon]]. She borrowed a [[Sardakh Class 750 Freighter]] called ''Tipsy'' from the Wroonian businesswoman and used it to smuggle goods as well as helping Rixt smuggler an [[InterGalactic News|IGN]] anchorwoman from [[Corellia]] to the [[Smuggler's Run]].

An experienced soldier loyal to the New Republic, Vice Admiral Quix is one of the more battle-experienced within the [[New Republic FleetOps|Republic's Navy]]. After her performance at the [[Third Battle of Mon Calamari]], she was promoted to her current rank and assigned to serve as the second in command of the [[New Republic Second Fleet|New Republic's Second Fleet]].

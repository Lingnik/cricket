|author = [[Paul Nighman|Nighman]]
|date = Unknown
|synopsis = After having a fight with Jessalyn two nights before, Paul went into the bar and got himself royally drunk. He refused to be moved from the bar and even spent most of the night under a table, when he realized that he had somehow become paralyzed from the waist down and obviously wouldn't be able to make it to his room. Later the next morning, when he finally came to, he was dying from a killer hangover, and in true form decided to go for a bit of the hair of the dog to cure himself. This didn't work out much better, as he imbibed a tad too much hair. Now he is seriously trying to sober himself up by drinking a vile concoction some bar girl had shown him on Corellia years and years ago. He nurses the glass, as it is too vile to take any other way.}}

{{Template:Logsum
|logtitle = [[RPlog:That Damned Stupidly Heroic Corellian|That Damned Stupidly Heroic Corellian]]
|author = [[Paul Nighman|Nighman]]
|date = Unknown
|synopsis = Having met Luke Skywalker more formally the night before, Paul has found himself now ensconced in new company. Luke has essentially hired Paul on in an archaeological capacity, and hinting that there is trouble to come, has told Paul to get off planet as soon as possible. However, Paul is somewhat stuck with his ship, The Bolt, being out on loan. Jessalyn proposes that Paul join them on the Black Dragon, an offer which Paul is rather hesitant to accept, as he is beginning to figure out that this particular band of folks tends to attract trouble like picnics do ants. He is also not exactly thrilled at being near to Jessalyn and Shenner, two red heads who disturb him on a number of levels that he isn't comfortable with enumerating. In the end he finally agreed, getting somewhat intrigued and excited despite himself. However, once he arrived in his room, the effects of his hang-over and lack of sleep finally hit him, and after getting cleaned up and taking an luxurious bath, he promptly passed out on the bed ... which is where he still is .....}}

{{Template:Logsum
|logtitle = [[RPlog:The Lost and Found|The Lost and Found]]
|author = [[Paul Nighman|Nighman]]
|date = Unknown
|synopsis = The previous night, Paul Nighman found himself in a sticky predicament. He came to realize that the symptoms that he was passing off as normal were far from it. Unbeknownst to him he had been internally bleeding for the majority of the day and was showing of shock as well as massive swelling and bruising. Realizing that Shenner had already had a difficult enough time of it, he gave her some lame excuse about going to get some food and a walk so as not to alarm her. He searched the ship briefly, but couldn't find Amylynn, or anyone else who could help him. Knowing that he couldn't wait, he set off on his own for the Medical Center. He barely was able to make there, and when they tried to get him to register and fill out a bunch of paperwork, he pushed his way past the annoy-a-tron droids and into the Medical room ... where he promptly collapsed. He was stabilized and placed on the waiting list for the bacta tank, where he was placed later the next day. However, when he tried to leave, against their insistence that he stay, he became quite agitated, forcing the medical staff to subdue and sedate him for his own safety and well being as well as their own. Needless to say, Paul put up quite a struggle until he was finally sedated.}}

{{Template:Logsum
|logtitle = [[RPlog:Trouble on Palanhi|Trouble on Palanhi]]
|author = [[Paul Nighman|Nighman]]
|date = Unknown
|synopsis = Paul, having gone to the Medical Center and been treated for internal bleeding, was finally found by Shenner, Lando, and Jessalyn. He woke up from being sedated to find Shenner practically hysterical, Lando sarcastic and chasing after Dr. Tantra, and Jessalyn somewhat preoccupied and disturbed. After a somewhat difficult and embarrassing time, they finally all left. Paul ended up carrying Shenner back, as she had been searching for him for a night and a day and had collapsed in exhaustion. Upon arriving back at the Black Dragon, Paul decided that he would sleep next to Shenner. He knew that when he had woken up from the sedation, that he was extremely disoriented and upset. He was concerned that Shen might wake up and not remember finding him - that she would think she had fallen asleep or something. His logic suggested that if he were to be in plain sight when she awoke, then there would be no confusion. However, his logic didn't consider all of the permutations and consequences of said action.}}

{{Template:Logsum
|logtitle = [[RPlog:The Prodigal Son|The Prodigal Son]]
|author = [[Paul Nighman|Nighman]]
|date = Unknown
|synopsis = After Molari tries to confront Paul over the fact that he is using is work to escape his emotional life, Paul is forced to consider his mentor's words carefully and after a long walk on the beach, comes to the realization that he doesn't have any idea what Shenner is really feeling, and no idea what he is feeling for that matter either. However, he avoids probing his own emotions too deeply by deciding that regardless of how he feels, if he were to get involved with Shenner, he would only end up hurting her. He decides that Shenner really needs to make a more informed decision on what it is that she wants and from a more balanced place. Essentially he decides that he is just the wrong man for her, and the right thing to do is to let her go. However, he also realizes that hiding at the Academy will accomplish nothing, and so the next evening he heads out, returning to the barracks and Shen.}}

'''Largo Ritt''' is a [[Mandalorian]] warrior. He was a participant of the [[:starwars:Mandalorian Civil War|Mandalorian Civil War]] and was originally a member of the [[:starwars:True Mandalorian|True Mandalorian]] faction. He was very close friends with [[Narik Ordo]] and his wife. But during the war, he betrayed the True Mandalorians and joined the [[:starwars:Death Watch|Death Watch]]. He tried to kill Narik, but failed; Narik escaped with his wife to [[Pakrik Minor]] where they raised [[Wescal Cantrell]]. Largo would later hunt down Narik, which led to Narik's death on [[Mandalore]]. Largo tarnished the reputation of Narik and his wife, sullying their good name. 

In [[17 ABY]], Cantrell went on a quest to restore his parents' name and avenge their deaths. He hunted Largo to Mandalore and slew him in combat. After the engagement, he retrieved his father's Mandalorian battle armor.

Lars Narson is a 23 year old Imperial Stormtrooper, currently serving as part of the Inquisitor taskforce's Stormtrooper compliment.

==Life before Carida==

Lars Narson was born on Coruscant to an ex-Imperial Army sniper and a female Imperial intelligence operative. From an early age his father taught him how to shoot and he often eavesdropped on his parents conversations, so he heard stories of his mothers missions, the glory, the torture and the thrill of the pursuit of Imperial Justice. He had no siblings and so always searched for that kind of family atmosphere so, when he was old enough, he applied to the Imperial Military Academy. Soon his skills and special 'qualities' were brought to the attention of the officers, a good combination of purely combat skills, combined with his skills at de-fusing and setting explosive devices and so he was selected for Stormtrooper Training on Carida.

==Carida==

While Lars Narson was on Carida he underwent standard stormtrooper training. During a particularly nasty training session in the polar regions, a team mate and good friend of his, Grant Renkwald, was setting a demolition charge near Lars on a structure they were bringing down while Lars was working nearby. One of the other trooper cadets accidentally let a shot off when his blaster dropped, which set off the explosive charge early. Lars managed to drop quickly enough to avoid serious injury beyond a few broken bones but his friend was torn to pieces and Narson still has the slight scarring on his neck where the shards of armour cut into his skin. This led Lars Narson to become slightly reclusive, and also slightly more possessive regarding explosive detail which causes him to keep a vigilant watch out for any other possible threats that might endanger his new StormTrooper family.

==Post-Carida==

After he graduated from the Imperial Stormtrooper Academy on Carida, he served with one squad on the Imperial Star Destroyer Liquidator before transferring off station to a combat assignment and then cycling back to a squad on Commenor before a transfer to the Imperial II Star Destroyer Inquisitor.

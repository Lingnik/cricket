'''Lau Saifax''' ([[2 BBY]] - [[28 ABY]]) was a [[Sarian]] from Ithaca on [[Mergansar]] who served as the planet's Lieutenant Governor under [[Raif Avosam]] during the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] and Mergani [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]]. The Avosam administration did nothing to stop members of the [[Olumi Osahn]] cult from forcefully expelling non-Sarians from Mergansar. Dossiers delivered to [[InterGalactic News]] conclusively tied Avosam, Saifax, and other members of his government to Olumi Osahn. He had gone into hiding prior the dossiers becoming public. After Avosam was killed in a bombing by [[Casulmis]], Saifax became [[Governor|Acting Governor]]. He was later himself assassinated by Casulmis agents and replaced by Minister of Justice [[Marck Narlin]] who was next in the line of succession. Narlin was killed shortly there after when his own hiding spot was bombed as he was making a broadcast about Saifax's death and his own assumption of the office of Governor.

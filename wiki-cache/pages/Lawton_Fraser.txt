Lieutenant General '''Lawton Fraser''' (ret.) ([[36 BBY]] - ) is a former starfighter pilot-officer, and one-time wing commander for the famous [[Admiral]] [[Dergan Roj]]. A native of Prefsbelt IV, he is a soft-spoken, curt, but determined [[human]] with a natural aptitude for piloting through feel and continued practice.

== Early Life ==

== Career ==

Fraser found no friends in [[Grei Lar]], but any objections he raised would be overruled by [[Elijah Taylor]]. Later, when Lar had ascended to the [[Sar Admiral]] chair [[Jake Myar]] vacated, Fraser was forced to an early retirement from his position of Chief of Pilot Training. After Lar's disappearance, Fraser was asked to return to [[the Admiralty]] by an [[Union Assembly|Assembly]] in upheaval, but Lawton kept several heavy, justifiable reservations. He would later let up and agree to a role as a special training consultant, in plainclothes and employed by [[CDMC]].

He happened to be at [[Groton]] when the [[Battle of the Inner Caspian Worlds]] erupted in [[8 ABY]], and while most of the command [[Naval Aerospace Arm|NAA]] and [[Marine Aerospace Division|MAD]] officers were either killed, captured, away with the scattered fleets, or holed up in [[Trinumvira]], he was looked to by apprehensive pilots and soldiers to lead resistance efforts.

'''Leebo Krupt''' was a [[Rodian]] who grew up on [[Tatooine]]. Routinely beaten by his parents and mistreated by all, he grew up miserable - although he found friendship in one [[Malik Ferdenko]] after helping him escape from [[Stormtrooper]]s. Malik introduced Leebo to the [[Shadow Bandits]]. Ferdenko eventually took control of the group, and Leebo became his number one man. Leebo left the Bandits after the two had an argument over the Bandits' future and main goals, and he took half the group's members to form a new business, [[Krupt Hauling and Storage]].

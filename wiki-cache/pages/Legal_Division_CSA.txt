The [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] and [[New Republic]] may have superior militaries, but the [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA's]] army of lawyers is second to none. The '''Legal Division''' is one of the largest in the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Authority]], a legion of experts versed in navigating the bureaucracies and courts of every known major government and corporation in the galaxy. Negotiating, establishing, and re-interpreting contracts, representing [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] interests and personnel abroad, mediating complaints between [[Corporate Sector Authority#Sponsors|member corporations]], and prosecuting criminals are all tasks that are handled by members of the legal division. A legal division executive is a powerful and influential force in political and corporate circles. Mindful of the high-profile nature of their jobs, the personnel of this division often work closely with the [[Media Division (CSA)|Media]] division to ensure that their efforts are always viewed in the proper public light.

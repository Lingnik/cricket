A '''legion general''' was a rank in the [[Galactic Empire]]'s [[Stormtrooper Corps]]. It was the highest rank in the Corps; a legion general commanded a full [[Legion]] of Stormtroopers. They answered to no authority other than the [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] or one of the Emperor's appointed proxies, though it is uncertain their relation to the [[Grand General's Council]] and the [[Supreme Commander of the Imperial Forces|Supreme Commander]]/[[Executor (title)|Executor]].

==Background==

===Childhood===

Leo Corak's childhood was not very eventful, at least from his perspective.  He grew up on [[Corellia]] with many friends with his best friend being [[Danik Kreldin]].  While Leo was never getting into trouble, it was not until later that he found out about his parents' profession - Rebel pilots.  But, they were never caught carrying supplies and Rebels thus keeping their son from having his own life threatened.

===New Republic Military===

Though Leo and Danik had originally planned on joining the [[Imperial Military]] together, this idea changed when he found out about his parents' work and wished to continue helping the newly established [[New Republic]].  As a result, Leo enlisted himself into the [[New Republic Military]] and joined the [[New Republic StarOps|StarOps]] branch.  He was assigned to the A-Wing Squadron Archer in the [[New_Republic_22nd_Starfighter_Wing|22nd Starfighter Wing]], [[New Republic Strike Group Alpha|Strike Group Alpha]].  Over time, Leo's skills improved and so did his rank.  He eventually earned himself the position of CO of Archer and was given the honor of renaming the squadron thus giving birth to the new [[Falcon Squadron]].

Yet, his promotions did not halt until he had reached the position of XO of [[New Republic 22nd Starfighter Wing]] with the CO being [[Mark Steele]].  Leo continues to lead [[Falcon Squadron]] into many battles and won himself numerous awards in his time.  Yet, such combat takes a toll on a person and, with a confidence in his decision, Leo retired from [[New Republic StarOps|StarOps]] handing his squadron over to [[Moralis Rodriga]].

===The Return===

Though the call of peace and avoiding life-threatening situations is loud, the call of the cockpit is louder.  After a few years had passed and retirement had been enjoyed, Leo decided to return to [[New Republic StarOps|StarOps]].  In speaking with the Commander of the [[New_Republic_22nd_Starfighter_Wing|22nd Starfighter Wing]], the rank of [[Colonel]] was returned yet a lower post was taken as the XO of [[Ghost Squadron]].  Only for a short time did Leo hold this position before being promoted to Falcon One, the CO of [[Falcon Squadron]].

==Recent Events==

Leo has returned to active duty and, after a short stint as the XO of [[Ghost Squadron]], has been promoted to be CO of [[Falcon Squadron]].

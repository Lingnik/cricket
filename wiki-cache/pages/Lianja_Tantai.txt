Lianja Tantai was employed by the [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA's]] [[Production Division (CSA)|Production Division]]. It likes architecture, space travel, and testing out new foods.

This strange being was once the gunner for the Twi'lek, Tyy'sun Eson, though the two drifted apart as time went on. The indigo one's abnormal appetites were a cause of much conflict with those it met, some being disgusted and repulsed, while others feeling pity tried to 'cure' it. None were successful and Lianja eventually met its end at its own hands after a female mercenary captured it.

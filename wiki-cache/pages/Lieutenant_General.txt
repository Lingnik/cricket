The rank of '''lieutenant general''' was a common military rank used in various Armies across the [[The Galaxy|galaxy]]. A lieutenant general was immediately above a [[Major General]] and directly subordinate to a [[General]].

=== In Imperial Service ===

A lieutenant general in the [[Imperial Army]] is an intermediate rank, with no clearly defined area of command in the Imperial Army Order of Battle. It is possible a lieutenant general commanded one or more Corps, or served as an assistant or staff officer to a full-fledged [[General#In Imperial Service|General]].

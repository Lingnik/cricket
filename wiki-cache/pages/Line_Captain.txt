'''Line captain''' is a rank used in the [[Imperial Navy]]. The rank is senior to that of [[Captain]] and subordinate to a [[Commodore]]. A line captain (or "captain of the line") commanded a naval line in the [[Imperial Navy Order of Battle]], the smallest Naval element consisting of more than one ship. The number of ships that the line captain commanded depended on the size and firepower of the ships - a line of light warships generally had more than ships than a line of heavy warships.

It is largely equivalent to the rank of [[Fleet Captain]] in the [[Caspian Navy]].

Physical appearance is unreliable as an indicator of age among the Conguii sub-breed and '''Linga's''' birth records have been mostly expunged from the Hestian Breeding Registry - a highly irregular event as Hestian society tracks Conguii lineages almost religiously. Linga's appearance would place her in her mid-to-late teens were she a normal human; for the Conguii, this places her in a possible age range from 14 to 40 standard years. The few intact records recovered from the Hestus Archives seemingly place her toward the middle of this range, especially considering the first criminal activity conclusively linked to her would have had her attempting to commit fraud at the tender age od two if she tended toward the younger end of it.

== Criminal Record ==
While there are few crimes that have been directly linked to her, Linga's distinctive appearance makes her a likely suspect for several hundred separate criminal events on at least thirty separate planets.

Crimes she has been charged with, convicted of, or for which she's wanted for questioning will be listed as soon as they have been properly collected and collated from the individual planetary archives.

It is documented that she has used a few aliases in the past, among them: Inna Harvik, Annail Harvik, Kiata Niin, and Torra Sparchs.

== Habits ==
Linga can usually be found haunting the less reputable areas of major port cities from [[Corellia]] to [[Caspia]], making her living through the creative interpretation of "truth" as well as the occasionally directly picked pocket. She has been seen consorting with various unsavory scum like [[Jaxx Morgan]] and [[Daana Roon]], and is rumored to be connected with Morgan's Swoop gang the Reapers in some regard.

'''Lirithose''' is a lightly-populated planet within the [[Krittain]] star system and member of the [[Caspian Union]]. It does not possess a significant atmosphere and has a beaten, cratered landscape from the constant bombardment of asteroids and other space debris. The only settlements are centred around mining facilities and research outposts, most of which are either subterranean or in reinforced domes.

Lirithose, like most of the other planets in this system, has several small moons. The largest of significance is '''Montrose'''.

During the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign]], the several warships of the [[Caspian Navy]] hid in orbit of Lirithose and its moons, from Imperial observers, scouts, and agents trying to locate and neutralize Caspian military assets.

The [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]] ''[[KMS Lirithose]]'' is named after this planet.

'''Lotus Blackpool''' was an investigative agent within [[PRISM]] for the [[Caspian Democratic Union]]. She was known for being rather silver-tongued and a bit of a spitfire, especially in the heady days of Caspia's new government and the obvious growing pains.

She came to Caspia from an undisclosed location in the Colonies, but quickly found a job for her incisive demeanor as an accounts and fraud investigator on behalf of the [[Commissioner of Commerce]]'s office. Within a short while, she was nominated and envested as a full-fledged PRISM agent. On a number of occasions, she would operate alone to various points of the Union, or to such places as [[Galactic Diplomatic Initiative Star|GDI-Star]]. Typically, though, she'd be partnered with fellow agents like [[Phoebe Frost]]. At some point during her service, she was assigned to [[Presav]] [[Lyr Dunwell]], and she immediately caught his eye. The two embarked on a torrid (albeit kept under wraps) affair, and it was revealed that Blackpool was carrying their child when Dunwell was assassinated in [[6 ABY]].

In seclusion, she gave birth to the child and left Caspia, never to be seen again.

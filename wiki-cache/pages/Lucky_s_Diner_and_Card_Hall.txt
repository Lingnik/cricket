'''Lucky's Diner and Card Hall''' is located in Cloud City's [[Port Town]]. It is a popular hang out for the seedier types of [[Bespin]], and hosts numerous unsanctioned and underground gambling events. It is also known as one of shadiest places to do business in the system. It is seldom visited by Bespin security forces. Lucky's Diner is rumored to be one of the oldest establishments in Cloud City.

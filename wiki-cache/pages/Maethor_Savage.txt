'''Maethor Savage''' was a famed [[Stormtrooper]] of the [[501st Legion]] who would later be elevated to the [[Imperial Royal Guard|Royal Guard]]. In late [[14 ABY]], Maethor became disillusioned with the Empire, as he was greatly against female service in the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] and saw the rising number of female officers as an insult to him and the Empire he served. He defected to the [[New Republic]] with [[David Stone]].

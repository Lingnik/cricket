TITLE: Magnify senses
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2007-04-12T06:08:10Z
======================================================================
This power allows a [[Jedi]] to increase the effectiveness of her normal senses to perceive things that otherwise would be impossible without artificial aids. She can hear noises beyond her normal hearing due to distance or softness (she can't hear beyond normal frequencies). Likewise, she can she normally visible things over distances that would require the use of macrobinoculars; she can identify scents and odors that are normally too faint for human olfactory senses.

The Sense difficulty is Very Easy, modified by proximity. It takes three rounds to use this power.

'''Marck Narlin''' ([[29 BBY]] - [[28 ABY]]) was a [[Sarian]] from Spanner on [[Mergansar]] who served as the planet's Minister of Justice under [[Raif Avosam]] during the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] and Mergani [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]]. The Avosam administration did nothing to stop members of the [[Olumi Osahn]] cult from forcefully expelling non-Sarians from Mergansar. Dossiers delivered to [[InterGalactic News]] conclusively tied Avosam, Narlin, and other members of his government to Olumi Osahn. He had gone into hiding prior the dossiers becoming public. After Avosam was killed in a bombing by [[Casulmis]], Lieutenant Governor [[Lau Saifax]] who was also in hiding became [[Governor|Acting Governor]]. He was later himself assassinated by Casulmis agents and replaced by Narlin who was next in the line of succession. Narlin was killed shortly there after when his own hiding spot was bombed as he was making a broadcast about Saifax's death and his own assumption of the office of Governor.

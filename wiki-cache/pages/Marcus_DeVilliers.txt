'''Marcus DeVilliers''' is a [[Captain]] in the [[Imperial Navy]], currently serving as captain of [[IMC Pillager|HIMS ''Pillager'']]. He has served in the Navy for decades, constantly refusing promotion as he believes a position in the admiralty is the path to a shortened career.

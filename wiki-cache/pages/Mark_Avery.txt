'''Mark Avery''' was a famous [[Imperial Starfighter Corps|Imperial pilot]]. He was chosen by [[Danik Kreldin]] to fly in the "second edition" of the famed [[Black Stars]] unit in [[12 ABY]]. During the [[War of the Throne]], he joined the [[Neo-Imperial Movement]]. After the Movement's [[Trap on Etti IV|collapse]] on [[Etti IV]], Avery fled into the [[Outer Rim]], never to be seen again.

Lieutenant '''Markelan Korminos''' was a member of [[New Republic StarOps|the New Republic Starfighter Operations']] [[Ghost Squadron]].  He was a resident of [[Corellia]] for many years, where he married [[Elena Korminos]] in 10 ABY.  He disappeared before the birth of his son [[Jansen Korminos]] in [[11 ABY]], when he was shot down over the surface of [[Dac]] during the [[Second Battle of Mon Calamari]].  Nothing has been heard of him since.  He is currently listed as missing-in-action.


== Classified History ==
Markelan Korminos is an alias of Imperial operative Lorgan Molstarris.  An agent of the Imperial [[Bureau of Operations]], he created the identity of Markelan and joined StarOps to complete a mission: gather intelligence on military security systems, and uncover the real identity of the woman known as Elena Korminos.  Marrying his quarry was not on his official list of objectives and caused some frustration among his superiors.

His disappearance at the Battle of Calamari was, of course, planned &mdash; he crashed, cloaked his ship, and remained in hiding on the planet until he could with safety return to [[Dreven]].  Whether he still feels any connection to Elena is unknown &mdash; he keeps his personal life to himself.

'''Marshal''' is a military rank typically associated with ground forces and starfighters. Marshal is also a title employed throughout the galaxy for law enforcement professionals, particular those operating somewhere remote and in the absence of strong governmental support.

=== In Imperial Service ===

In the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]], a '''Marshal''' usually held a command staff position assisting a Chief Marshal or higher.  There usually was no specific command posting and had a varying command.  They could be found from leading a starfighter command to a task force; in rare cases found leading a system flight consisting of 4 commands. They were addressed as Marshal.  If appointed as a staff officer they would act as an assistant to a [[Chief Marshal]].  

A Marshal was equivalent to a [[Vice Admiral]] in the [[Imperial Navy]].

== In Union Service ==

'''Marshal''' did not exist in the [[Caspian Democratic Union]]. When [[Crestian Tarasar]] was elected [[Presav]] of the new [[Caspian Union]] the [[Caspian Defence Force]] underwent various reforms including to the military hierarchy. When the rank of [[Sar Admiral]] was transformed into a singular office, new ranks were adopted for the most senior flag officers in the CDF. In the [[Caspian Navy]] the rank of [[Fleet Admiral]] was adopted as the rank just below Sar Admiral, while the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]] and [[Union Marines]] adopted the rank of Marshal. While the rank is officially Marshal in both branches, they are referred to as Star Marshal and Field Marshal respectively when differentiation is needed.

=== Caspian Starfighter Corps ===

One of the first acts of the Tarasar administration has been to merge the previous [[Marine Aerospace Division]] and [[Naval Aerospace Arm]] into a singular and separate branch of the [[Caspian Defence Force]]. Tarasar had initially hoped that he might bring back one the Union's legendary pilots to serve as '''Star Marshal''' in the form of [[Elana Tracer]], the controversial former [[Caspian Democratic Union|CDU]] [[Presav]]. Tracer's appointment as [[Moff]] of [[Selene]] by Imperator [[Tyler Damion|Damion]] stalled this effort, though as part of the the Union's defence pact with the [[Imperium]], Tracer would still be granted the rank in a reserve capacity.

Lacking an inspirational candidate for the position, the role would ultimately fall to [[General]] Gray Dienes of the Marine Aerospace Division. In the midst of the massive reorganisation and rearmament, Dienes was not seen as a making a huge impact as much of the decision making was made by Tarasar and members of his administration. When a massive series of war games were scheduled in [[32 ABY]] at [[Charros IV]] to debut new military technology, Tarasar recruited the services of [[Grand Moff]] Tracer and Inquisitor [[Axel Vichten]]. With someone of Vichten's experience as Star Marshal, it is widely speculated that Dienes will retire and leave Vichten in active leadership of the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]].

=== Union Marines ===

The [[Union Marines]] were decimated by the Sariphage with most of the personnel at the [[Dunwelton]], [[Groton]], and [[Trinumvira]] Bases on [[Caspar]] killed. While rebuilding the [[Caspian Navy|Navy]] and [[Caspian Starfighter Corps|Starfighter Corps]] has been overseen by [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] in his capacity as Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defence and [[Sar Admiral]], the Marines has largely been under the oversight of Vice Presav [[Cas Mos]] in her capacity as Minister of Domestic Affairs and Defence. Mos, a Marine [[General]] prior to her election to the [[Union Council]], now holds the rank of '''Field Marshal''' and operational command of the Union Marines. Though additional Marshals could be appointed, none have to this point.

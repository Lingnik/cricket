'''Martos Dixon''' was an officer in the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]], and held an impeccable service record while assigned to [[Cerebus Squadron]]. The squadron was a personal favorite and under direct command of [[Grand Admiral]] [[Russel Canis|Canis]]. The officer's extensive knowledge of starfighter tactics and his performance kept him on track for promotion to Marshal or another equitable position. 

His career was tragically cut when, in a freak accident, the tractor beam pulling him in for a final approach had a power spike and slammed the [[Scimitar Assault Bomber]] he was piloting into the hull plating of Canis's [[Star Destroyer]].

It was unknown if he was related to [[Claris Dixon]].

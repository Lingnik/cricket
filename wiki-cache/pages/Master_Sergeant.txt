The rank and title of '''Master Sergeant''' is a senior non-commissioned officer's rank used mostly by army organizations. They are highly regarded as both experts in their particular field and natural leaders.

=== In New Republic Service ===

Master Sergeants in the '''[[New Republic GroundOps|New Republic Army]]''' hold the same pay grade as a [[First Sergeant]], but is given a very different responsibility. With at least twelve years time in service (usually pushing fifteen), Master Sergeants do not carry ''all'' the responsibilities of their First Sergeants.

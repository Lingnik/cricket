'''Matthew "Rabid" Fox''' was a former [[Corellian Security Force|CorSec]] cadet, arms dealer, and [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Rebel Alliance]] special operative. He had seen and done a lot more than his age would suggest. He became the CEO of [[StarShield]] later in his life.

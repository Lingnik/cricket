'''Matthew O'Thompson''' ([[3 ABY]] - ) is a [[Stormtrooper]] sharpshooter with the [[501st Legion]]. His designation is TK-4484.

=== Childhood ===
Matthew was born in 16 ISY to his parents Jolaine and Christopher O'Thompson on the planet of Coruscant. As a young boy Matthew was never exposed to war and the Imperial Empire or the New Republic. He led a sheltered life.

As his childhood progressed, Matthew became more aware of the hostilities around him. He soon found himself on roof tops watching the Stormtroopers and the Rebels fight. He longed for combat and felt the lust for a weapon.

=== Teenage Years ===
As life for Matthew progressed, so did his understanding of the rivalries at hand. He found himself supporting the Imperial Empire in their quests and skirmishes and even followed them around for sometime at a distance.

Matthew waited a long time for the day he could become a Stormtrooper and trained with rifles through his teenage years. His eyesight was extremely good and he was agile and quick to find a good hiding spot. He soon found himself playing with a scoped ST-II which he found off of a fallen Stormtrooper. He began training himself with it.

At the age of 18, Matthew was ready for what lay ahead. He took his chance and joined the Stormtroopers. He was assigned to the 501st and immediately found himself where he grew up.

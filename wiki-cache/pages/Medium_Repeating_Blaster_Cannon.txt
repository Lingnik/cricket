TITLE: Medium Repeating Blaster Cannon
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2007-04-12T15:15:55Z
======================================================================
The Mark II Medium Repeater Blaster pushes the limit for man-portable blaster cannons. It saw notable use on Hoth, helping to repel the Imperial assault. Though useless against AT-ATs, it could do some minor damage to AT-STs, speeders, and was certainly very effective against enemy personnel. 

The Mark II, while a canon weapon, is not coded into the game. It requires the approval of all parties before use.

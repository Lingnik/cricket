<p style="margin-top:1em;margin-bottom:1em;">'''Mergansar''' is the second planet orbiting Solus in the [[Caspia]]n system, and is the birthplace and homeworld of the [[Sarian]] species. The ever-healing victim of a gamma-ray shockwave centuries ago, it is reputed to be a planet "in perfect balance" with all the elements.</p>

In [[Sarian (language)|Sarian]], ''Mergansar'' means ''pulled from the star'' or ''origin''.

= History =

== The Pogroms ==

In the final years of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], the government of Mergansar largely fell under the sway of a sect of [[Olumi Osahn]] which took a violent dislike of non-Sarians. When the [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|Sariphage pandemic]] hit [[Caspar]] in late [[27 ABY]], decimating its population and largely killing off the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]], Olumi Osahn used this as an opportunity to blame non-Sarians for the virus and engage in [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]] to drive the non-Sarians from Mergansar. With the Mergani government under Olumi Osahn's sway and the Caspian Defence Force focused on maintaining a quarantine around Caspar, the pogroms faced no official resistance.

When Caspian native and [[New Sith Order|Sith]] [[Admiral]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] arrived in the [[Caspia|Caspian System]] to render aid, he met with [[Sar Admiral]] [[Prae Kichakressa]] who unofficially suggested he direct his efforts towards the Olumi Osahn problem. After Crestian and [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] failed in their efforts to strong arm the sect into submission based on its past association with the Sith, Crestian began recruiting refugees into a paramilitary organisation he dubbed [[Casulmis]], [[Sarian (language)|Sarian]] for 'great wind' in order to directly combat Olumi Osahn.

Casulmis would have brief success against Olumi Osahn. Boosted by the firepower of the ''[[Leviathan]]'' and her support craft, along with support of a lightsaber wielding [[Tyler Damion|combatant]], Casulmis attacked the cultists operating in Hebron, whose starport was the site most of the non-Sarians were being expelled from. After liberating Hebron, the Calsumis fighters turned their attention to Goshen which they liberated. Forays into Anphora, Charon, and Ithaca were made as well but these proved ineffective. A bombing run would also destroy the compound of Olumi Osahn backed [[Governor]] [[Raif Avosam]] killing him.

Once the cure to the pandemic was introduced to Caspar and the [[Caspian Navy]] was free to focus on Mergansar, they turned their attention on the ''Leviathan'' rather than Olumi Osahn. The [[Mergansar Incident|engagement]] left Sar Admiral Kichakressa dead on ''[[CMS Typhoon]]'', ''[[CMS Prophet]]'' and ''CMS Rapace'' crippled, extensive damage to ''CMS Ad Astra'', and the ''Leviathan'' retreating with heavy damage of its own. Without the support of Tarasar and the ''Leviathan'', Casulmis fell apart and Olumi Osahn were able to retake Hebron and Goshen.

Once the talks aimed at keeping the CDU together following the pandemic fell apart, Olumi Osahn overran the remaining Caspian [[Caspian Defence Force|military]] bases on Mergansar, assuming full control of the planet. Though they faced some set backs with Tarasar's agents assassinating Avosam's successor [[Lau Saifax]], and his successor [[Marck Narlin]], there would be no serious threat to their power for sometime. A new planetary government operating under the name Mergansarsolu was born.

= Geography =

The planet is much like most other inhabited worlds, with an even mix of plains, marshes, hills, mountain ranges, and bodies of water. The atmosphere is slightly thin, but very hospitable, and winds of varying degrees swirl lazily about. The planet has an unusually high amount of iron and mineral content, and as such, its terrain exhibits a reddish hue while the waters possess an ever-so-slight tint of blue-violet or blue-green to them (depending on the time of day and location of the body of water).

Population centers vary in range and size all over the landscape of the planet. Sarian innovation focused on advances in watercraft and airships, finding great success in harnessing windpower for energy and transportation. While the Eireahavi, Janhavi and Metiahavi intermixed and cohabited with relatively little conflict over the years, the Casohavi remained aloof, contentious and resistant to collaboration for centuries. Because of this, towns and cities formed in response to these interactions and divisions. Three dominant regions –'''Dusona''', '''Ioluma''', and '''Janina '''– emerged from the rest and have become the provinces of representation. 

== Dusona ==

The '''Dusona''' continent has been defined by its role as the centre of industry on Mergansar. The Casohavi cities of Hammerton and Spanner are the site of most of the world's manufacturing, helped by the proximity of the mineral resources of the Janhavi city of Charon. The Dusona Workers Union represents many of the factory workers, miners, and affiliated industries and holds a great deal of sway politically.

=== Charon ===

[[File:Charon.jpg|Charon|left|thumb]] '''Charon''' is Janhavi city with a long storied history. It originally developed as a spiritually focused city, with [[Noques Rasala]] on [[Caspar]] said to have been based on Charon. The existence of valuable mineral deposits used in the manufacturing in nearby Casohavi cities of Ton Echotta and Ton Mulatta saw the city's focus shift towards mining and smelting. The spiritual focus never quite left, but has largely been confined to the 'old city.' During the pogroms it was a site of some clashes between [[Casulmis]] and [[Olumi Osahn]], though the former made little headway.

When factory workers went on strike in Ton Echotta and Ton Mulatta during the [[Caspian Navy|Caspian Navy's]] blockade, workers in Charon ultimately joined the strike as well. With a general strike organised by the Dusona Workers Union in the three cities, the local Olumi Osahn regime soon collapsed. The Dusona continent would be the first full region on Mergansar to be reintegrated back into the [[Caspian Union]], though the city of Ithaca was already reintegrated before this the Dusona cities.

[[File:Philinda Jorgan 1.jpg|thumb|Councillor Philinda Jorgan]] The election to represent Charon in the [[Union Council]] proved interesting after the [[Legacy]] party and [[Praecet]] both fielded lacklustre candidates and the late entrance of former Dusona Province [[Senator of the Union|Senator]] [[Philinda Jorgan]]. Jorgan had been presumed dead during the Sariphage pandemic but was found to be alive and well on [[Ixin]]. Running under the banner of the Ixin based [[Lijsttrekker]] party, she managed to capture 83% of the vote after the Legacy party and Praecet withdrew their candidates, with the [[Ulmesar]] and [[Holicet]] candidates taking 14% and 3% respectively. Though her name recognition and lack of serious opposition made her win unsurprising, the fact that she is [[Epicanthix]] rather than [[Sarian]] was notable.

She courted controversy early on by introducing a bill to expand Charon's starport after an earthquake destroyed Hebron's, with many considering it a callous and opportunistic move. In spite of the controversy she was subsequently as [[Governor|Military Governor]] after the resignation of [[Odie Hood]]. Following the completion of the election cycle and the end of her Military Governorship she was named the Caspian Minister of Intelligence.

In addition to being the site of what is now Mergansar's largest starport, the [[Union Marines|Union Marines']] Charon Base is located near the city and also hosts six squadrons of the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]].

=== Gelsefehav ===

'''Gelsefehav''' is a Casohavi city that was once one of the major settlements on the Dusona continent on par with the likes of Hammerton and Spanner. It is notable as the site of Mergansar's oldest and most well regarded educational institution, '''Gelsefehav University'''. For much of Mergani history it was one of the few proper universities on the world, which brought significant attention to the city. Expanding educational opportunities and growing industry in other cities saw Gelsefehav's fortunes fade. The university is still a site for serious scholarship, but the city has limited amenties and commerce beyond the needs of the the school.

It persisted as a city with representation in the Union Assembly through 21 ABY. Subsequent reorganisation of the Union Assembly after Ixin was admitted into the Caspian Democratic Union saw Gelsefehav and Neosuhas stripped of independent representation, with Gelsefehav now covered by the Spanner delegates. In spite of this loss of status the city still serves as the namesake of the Batch IV [[Shearwater-class Frigate|Shearwater-Class Light Cruiser]] ''CMS Gelsefehav'' commmanded by [[Captain]] Shey Darakin, a Gelsefehav University graduate.

=== Hammerton ===

[[File:Hammerton.jpg|Hammerton|left|thumb]] '''Ton Echotta''' was Casohavi city and a major hub of industry prior to the time Republic surveyors arrived. Because of it appeared the most modern to the surveyors, [[Icaris Hood]] directed his crew to make first contact here. Through the following interactions, it was deduced that ''echotta'' translated loosely to "(the sound of) hammer blows", and thus became '''Hammerton''' in [[Galactic Basic Standard|Basic]] vernacular. Prior to the pogroms Hammerton was the most demographically diverse city on Mergansar with a significant portion of the non-Sarian population living there.

During Mergansar's brief independence following the collapse of the CDU, the name Ton Echotta was briefly revived by the local government. This was one reason that the Mergani refugees on Kichnar Orbital Station chose New Hammerton as the name for one of the two new modules built to accommodate them. After the new Caspian Union began to blockade Mergansar, the factories in Hammerton were devastated by the inability to export off world. This eventually led to rioting among with unemployed factor workers.

[[File:Valdemar Linane.jpg|thumb|Councillor Valdemar Linane]] The Dusona Workers Union took the lead in pushing out the local leadership out and bringing the city back into the Caspian Union, with the name of Hammerton readopted. A number of the union's leaders who had left during the pogroms were able to return at this point, with the union's Chairman [[Valdemar Linane]] elected as Hammerton's representative on the Union Council. He is a member of Praecet and serves as the Caspian Minister of Labour.

Hammerton is the site of [[Byurnadin-Covra Tachtronics]] and a local manufacturing site for [[CASE|Consolidated Air & Surface Engineering]]. The [[Xi Char]] and [[Haor Chall Engineering]] host a "chapel-factory" near the city known as Xi Ton. The [[Caspian Foreign Ministry]] maintains an immigration office in Hammerton and [[The Mission]] has offices serving the Dusona continent.

=== Neosuhas ===

'''Neosuhas''' is a Casohavi city that was once one of the major settlements on the Dusona continent on par with the likes of Hammerton and Spanner. Unlike those cities, however, it lacked proximity to the material resources that fueled the industrial development of those cities. Increasingly the population of Neosuhas migrated to cities with more opportunities, primarily Spanner which was the closest in proximity. 

It persisted as a city with representation in the Union Assembly through 21 ABY. Subsequent reorganisation of the Union Assembly after Ixin was admitted into the Caspian Democratic Union saw Gelsefehav and Neosuhas stripped of independent representation, with Neosuhas now covered by the Spanner delegates. In spite of this loss of status the city still serves as the namesake of the Batch IV [[Shearwater-class Frigate|Shearwater-Class Light Cruiser]] ''CMS Neosuhas'' commanded by former Ambassador to the [[Corporate Sector Authority]] Captain [[Sadim Gnik]].

=== Spanner ===

[[File:Spanner.jpg|Spanner|left|thumb]] '''Ton Mulatta''' was a Casohavi city, with a significant Metiahavi minority by the time Republic surveyors arrived. It was in competition with nearby Ton Echotta as the centre of industry on Mergansar. Though Ton Echotta had more industry, Ton Mulatta was known for more technical proficiency. Eager to make an impression on the Republic surveyors, the citizens of Ton Echotta insisted on negotiating the translation of their fine city with the Republic surveyors. 

Since Ton Echotta's translated name had come so easily and descriptively, the locals wanted to emphasize that Ton Mulatta was more refined, more precision-driven, more motivated – a more useful tool for industry. Searching for synonyms of "tool", the Republic surveyors tossed out "spanner", and the locals jumped on it, claiming that if it is a tool used by engineers throughout this great galaxy they just learned about, then that must be the answer. Only later did someone learn the connotations of "spanner" from a surveyor, including the derisive slang, but the name had taken hold. The surveyors were a bit more gracious once they returned, and to this day, the names '''Spanner''' and '''Mechanicston''' are interchangeable.

During Mergansar's brief independence following the collapse of the CDU, the name Ton Mulatta was briefly revived by the local government. This was one reason that the Mergani refugees on Kichnar Orbital Station chose New Spanner as the name for one of the two new modules built to accommodate them. After the new Caspian Union began to blockade Mergansar, the factories in Spanner were devastated by the inability to export off world. This eventually led to rioting among with unemployed factor workers.

[[File:Yoseph Sarlin.jpg|thumb|Councillor Yoseph Sarlin]] The Dusona Workers Union took the lead in pushing out the local leadership out and bringing the city back into the Caspian Union, with the name of Spanner readopted. The election for Spanner's Union Councillor was ultimately contested by two members of the Dusona Workers Union executive committee, Yoseph Sarlin representing the Legacy party and Leonidas Trettsia of Praecet, with Sarlin taking 53% of the vote to Trettsia's 40%, with other candidates taking the other 7%.

== Ioluma ==

The '''Ioluma''' continent is known for an extensive series of lakes and rivers arguably making it the most beautiful landscape on the planet. It was traditionally the region from which the Metiahavi came from and have been centred in. It also in Ioluma where the Eireahavi originated and are concentrated.

=== Anphora ===

[[File:Anphora.jpg|Anphora|left|thumb]] '''Anphora''' is a Metiahavi city nestled in an archaic rift valley. It is renowned within the system for its artisans and craftwork, and as such attracts a certain demographic diversity. During the pogroms it was a site of some clashes between Casulmis and Olumi Osahn, though the former made little headway.

[[File:Satine Anatinae 2.jpg|thumb|Councillor Satine Anatinae]] During the blockade, the people of Anphora mostly responded by graffiti protests, and largely kept their dissatisfaction with local leaders confined to non-violent protests. Along with Hebron, Goshen, and Vilarasala, it was one of the last cities on Mergansar reintegrated into the Caspian Union. Local sixteen year old graffiti artist [[Satine Anatinae]] from Holicet was elected to represent the city in the Union Council, displacing [[Sha Dark]] as the youngest member of the Union Council.

=== Ithaca ===

[[File:Ithaca.jpg|Ithaca|left|thumb]] '''Ithaca''' is a traditional Metiahavi city, sprawled at the hub of several dozen lakes and three major rivers. In spite of being one of the most cosmopolitan cities on Mergansar, a great deal of effort has been put into keeping the architecture and amenities looking as traditional as possible. One of the sons of the Republic surveyor [[Icaris Hood]] ultimately settled in the city, with his descendants being prominent to this day. Among these is [[Sar Admiral]] [[Odie Hood]].

It was in this city that the Mergani pogroms began in earnest with members of the Olumi Osahn cult throwing firebombs and breaking up store fronts in Human neighbourhoods. The local authorities did nothing to stop these efforts and they soon spread planet wide. It was later a location where members of the Casulmis movement clashed with the cultists.

[[File:Odie Hood.jpg|thumb|Councillor Odie Hood]] During the Caspian Union's blockade of Mergansar, the Caspian Intelligence Service managed to smuggle Ithaca's favourite son Odie Hood back into the city. Hood had been believed dead to this point, but when the bodies of the local leaders started turning up dead on the grounds of his family's estate, the rumours of his return took hold. He was subsequently elected as Mergansar's first representative to the Union Council and [[Governor|Military Governor]] of the planet. After his stint as Military Governor he took on the role as Caspian Minister of the Navy and Commanding Officer of ''[[CMS Resplendent]]'' and '''Task Group Resplendent''' of the '''[[Caspian 4th Fleet]]'''.

The Union Marines Ithaca Base is located near the city and also hosts six squadrons of the Caspian Starfighter Corps. Ithaca also hosts an office of The Mission serving the Ioluma continent.

=== Vilarasala ===

[[File:Vilarasala.jpg|Vilarasala|left|thumb]] '''Vilarasala''', or ''Residence of the Heart'' in [[Sarian (language)|Sarian]], is appropriately the capital of this spiritually rich world and the only major Eireahavi city on Mergansar. The city is just south of the '''The Confluence of The Five''' where five major Mergani rivers merge before flowing into the city. It is a location considered to have great spiritual significance and draws pilgrims from all over the planet as well as Sarians from offworld, and even the occasional tourist.

It was in this city that Olumi Osahn led riots began before the spread violently through Ithaca and beyond. During the Casulmis bombing run on Mergansar, starfighers bombed the residence of Governor [[Raif Avosam]] killing him. Later bombing efforts by Casulmis killed his successor, the Lieutenant Governor [[Lau Saifax]], and then his successor Minister of Justice [[Marck Narlin]]. All three had been members of of the Olumi Osahn cult. When post-pandemic talks aimed at keeping the Union together collapsed on Caspar, a makeshift military base at Vilarasala's starport fell to Olumi Osahn.

[[File:Saul Benjawa.jpg|thumb|Councillor Saul Benjawa]] Though Vilarasala did see some rioting during the planet's blockade by the Caspian Navy, the situation in Vilarasala was rather quiet until after the new Caspian Union had effectively regained control of the world. Along with Anphora, Hebron, and Goshen, it was one of the last cities on Mergansar reintegrated into the Caspian Union. Saul Benjawa who had fought with Casulmis was elected as Vilarasala's representative on the Union Council. Interesting he ran for the Legacy party rather than Crestian Tarasar's party Praecet.

== Janina ==

The '''Janina''' continent is known for its great span of uninterrupted plains which serves as the primary agricultural region of the planet. It was traditionally the region from which the Janhavi came from and have been centred in. Its central location has traditionally seen it is as hub for migration and trade.

=== Goshen ===

[[File:Goshen.jpg|Goshen|left|thumb]] '''Goshen''' is a Janhavi city located amongst Mergansar's largest agricultural region. It is the smallest of Mergansar's major settlements, which is largely only notable for serving as the market town for a vast array of smaller farming communities which to the plains of Janina. It is a relatively peaceful community and only saw a limited Olumi Osahn presence during the pogroms. Owing to this and its proximity to Hebron, Casulmis was able to briefly liberate the city before being forced to retreat. Though the non-Sarian population faced explusion in the city, the pogroms had little impact in the other farming communities with the locals taking steps to shield their neighbours. It was even rumoured that there was some Jedi involvement in such efforts.

[[File:Evlyn Decker.jpg|thumb|Councillor Evlyn Decker]] Along with Anphora, Hebron, and Vilarasala, it was one of the last cities on Mergansar reintegrated into the Caspian Union. Its relatively peaceful nature, while it had limited the impact of the pogroms, meant the local populace were less inclined to fight back against the Olumi Osahn backed government. As such the local administration collapsed more from lack of outside support rather than any direct efforts locally. Evlyn Decker of Holicet, one of the [[Human|Humans]] who avoided expulsion, was elected as the city's representative on the Union Council.

=== Hebron ===

[[File:Hebron.jpg|Hebron|left|thumb]] '''Hebron''' is a Janhavi city centrally located between the plains and steppe of the southern hemisphere and the populated regions of the north. This led it to traditionally be one of the most dynamic cities of Mergansar and it was the site of the planet's largest starport. This also meant it was a central location for the Mergani pogroms as it was where many of the refugees were expelled from. 

This subsequently led to it being the main location of the Casulmis counterattack against Olumi Osahn personally led by [[Crestian Tarasar]] and a certain lightsaber wielding [[Tyler Damion|figure]]. The vast majority of the sect members operating in Hebron at the time were killed, though new ones took their place after Tarasar and company were forced by the Caspian Navy to retreat from the system.

While the reunification efforts on Mergansar were still on going after the later Caspian blockade of the world, the starport was largely destroyed by an earthquake. Much to the consternation of the local population, then Military Governor Philinda Jorgan, introduced legislation to expand the facilities in Charon rather than focus on rebuilding those in Hebron. This has largely been interpreted as a purposeful snub to punish the city for its role in the pogroms. Though funding has slowly come through to allow the Hebron starport to return to some level of functionality it is operating far below its original capacity with the Charon starport largely taking its place. The Caspian Foreign Ministry maintains a recruitment and training facility nearby.

[[File:Jarrod Barnley.jpg|thumb|Councillor Jarrod Barnley]] Along with Anphora, Goshen, and Vilarasala, it was one of the last cities on Mergansar reintegrated into the Caspian Union. With many of the prominent Sarians in the city dead or barred from seeking office because of their ties to the pogroms, the city elected ultimately elected a Human as their Councillor. A lawyer by profession, Jarrod Barnley of the Legacy party belonged to one of the city's most prominent families prior to the pogroms. He also now serves as the Caspian Minister of Education. With the city continually rated as one of the least favoured by the Tarasar administration, many of the citizens of Hebron have pinned their hopes on the Barnley family to restore the city's image.

=== Toresta ===

[[File:Toresta.jpg|Toresta|left|thumb]] '''Toresta''' was the site of an ancient citadel with the Janhavi constructing a city around what remained of it. The city later saw an influx of Metiahavi with the modern city consider a reflection of both cultures. It subsequently became the largest city on Mergansar as well at the CDU's main base on the planet. The military presence nearby initially kept Olumi Osahn from targeting the city during the pogroms. When post-pandemic talks aimed at keeping the Union together collapsed on Caspar, Toresta Base was quickly overrun by Olumi Osahn with the help of inside collaborators. Many consider this the final nail in the CDU's coffin.

Though Toresta did see some rioting during the planet's blockade by the Caspian Navy, the situation in Toresta was rather quiet until after the new Caspian Union had effectively regained control of the world. It was subsequently the site of the most drawn out election among the Mergani cities and the most chaotic. 

The crowded field of six candidates was soon widdled down after the Legacy party's candidate Volzang Eiresar died of a heart attack shortly after information was leaked linking him to the fall of Toresta Base two years early.  During a debate between the remaining candidates, Praecet's Lisal Yutan was taken into custody by [[Admiral]] Liam Biker as she attempted to assault Olmesar's Corgan Vir. Yutan would later be implicated in the death Eiresar, but subsequently disappear and be removed from the ballot.

[[File:Nanchia Marook.jpg|thumb|Councillor Nanchia Marook]] The final election saw Nanchia Marook of Lijsttrekker who had been a leader among Toresta's refugee community after the pogroms elected with 44% of the vote, with Corgan Vir at 31%, Brullian Aresko of Holicet at 24%, and independent candidate Elkanah Temereth at 1%. Toresta Base is now once again the Union Marine's main base on Mergansar and it also hosts six squadrons of the Caspian Starfighter Corps. Toresta also hosts an office of [[The Mission]] serving the Janina continent.

== Demographics ==

Prior to the pogroms Mergansar had a population that was 90% [[Sarian]], 8% [[Human]], with the remaining 2% made up of other species with a token presence on the planet. Roughly 300 million of the planet's then population of 2.8 billion people fled during the pogroms. This largely eliminated the non-Sarian population though some remained in more isolated parts of the planet. A number of Sarians fled the world either because they had Sarian spouses or were simply disgusted at what was going on. Some of the refugees did return after the world was liberated from [[Olumi Osahn]] rule, but the vast majority remained where they had settled after fleeing. The current population is 97% Sarian, 2% Human, with another 1% made up of other species.

=== Sarians ===

The Sarian people are made of four distinct subspecies. Their forebearers were each in distinctly different areas of the planet when the gamma rays effected the planet and altered the Human population that had been resident there, creating the Sarian species. Each of these subspecies continues to maintain a presence in specific areas of the planet though the major cities tend to have a more cosmopolitan population. Each group maintains their own religious and cultural traditions and tend to mate among their own subspecies.

Roughly 80% of the Sarian population on Mergansar trace back their ancestry solely to one subspecies. Of these roughly 40% are Janhavi, 25% Casohavi, 25% Metiahavi, and 10% Eireahavi. For the remaining 20% of the Sarian population that has mixed ancestry, it is most likely that part of their heritage is Casohavi as they are the most likely to interbreed with other Sarians groups or non-Sarians for that matter. The exception to this is in Toresta which has long had both a Janhavi and Metiahavi population.

=== Refugees ===

Of the 300 million refugees who fled Mergansar, roughly 100 million of them ended up on [[Caspar]] after the pandemic was over. This was both due to its proximity to Mergansar and the available housing after Caspar's population was decimated. The refugees settled in large numbers in every Caspian city, though their impact was felt more in some cities than in others. [[Crestian Tarasar|Crestian Tarasar's]] charity [[The Mission]] played a large role in helping refugees get settled, with those efforts primarily focused in his home city of [[Trinumvira]] and in [[Anchorron]] and to a less extent in [[Noques Rasala]] and [[Orleanon]]. In Anchorron particularly took to the refugees having already been the most racial diverse city on Caspar and Orleanon ended up electing a refugee from Goshen, [[Sha Dark]] as their representative to the [[Union Council]]. 

The Mission put less effort into operations in [[Plaxton]], as the capital city had an easier time getting attention from other off world sources, and [[Dunwelton]] was largely ignored as it was already the most stable city economically. Both cities did see a large number of refugees but they had less of an impact on the culture of the city at large. While [[Groton]] also took in some refugees, its geographic isolation made it a less favoured destination. The Mission also largely ignored the city as Tarasar remained bitter at the nilistic response of [[Daln Bael]] of the [[Groton Sciences Institute]] during the pandemic.

Another 100 million of them would settle on other worlds of the former [[Caspian Democratic Union]]. The [[Krittain|Krittain System]] took in 40 million, as [[Krittain Major]] was already the most populous Union world, the 35 million that settled there made a limited impact. The five million that ended up on [[Krittain Minor]] represented a major impact, particular in the capital of Aonach which now represents a fusion of Krittish and Mergani culture.

[[Kasputin Yar]] took in 25 million refugees, which represented a significant number, with limited areas on the world suitable for non-Yari habitation. The planet, however, had already experience a large refugee influx before when [[Sluis Van]] was destroyed in [[9 ABY]]. Many surviving [[Sluissi]] fled to Kasputin Yar. As such the locale population was rather accomodating and sympathetic to the plight of the Mergani refugees. [[Rauther]] and [[Ixin]] took in 15 million refugees and 10 million refugees respectively. While this was more culturally impactful to those worlds than it was on Krittain Major, it still represented a comparatively small portion of their population.

The refugee impact on [[Mikassa]] was quite massive. As the least populated of the major worlds of the CDU, considered Mikassa a perfect world to relocate too. It offered ampled space and a pleasing climate. However, the five millions refugees that arrived now made up over 40% of the planet's population. This influx was, in part, why the new [[Caspian Union]] opted to make Mikassa a full member world, versus its previous status as a ward.

[[Kichnar Orbital Station]] was the initial destination of most refugees, and nearly five million would stay behind on the station rather than venturing on ward. The station had ample experience with refugees, having been a major destination for both survivors of the destruction of Sluis Van in 9 ABY and refugees from the [[Griffon Alliance]] years later. In each of those cases new modules were added to the station to help house the new population. The Mergani refugees, however, came in much larger numbers, doubling the station's population. This led to the construction of the modules of New Hammerton and New Spanner. This was largely accomplished through the direction of [[Prex (CSA)|Prex]] [[Zeak Oppenhiemer]], who often resided on the station, before the Caspian Union fully repatriated the station from the [[Corporate Sector Authority]].

The final 100 million refugees settled throughout the galaxy at large. While many entities assisted in the relocation and many worlds took in Mergani, the efforts of the Corporate Sector Authority and the Griffon exile community were central to this endeavour. In both cases this support was in large part thanks to the direction of Prex Zeak Oppenhiemer who expended great time, resources, and effort assisting the Caspian and Mergani communities during the twin crises of the time.

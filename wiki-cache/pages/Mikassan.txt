The term '''Mikassan''' can refer to either the [[Near-Human]] species "native" to [[Mikassa]] or more generally to any person who hails from that world.

== Species ==

[[Mikassa]] was the site of an Ix colony founded several millennia ago at a period of time when [[Ixin]] was a prosperous world. Roughly four millennia ago Ixin suffered from severe desertification driven by the consequences excessive mineral exploitation and exportation. As the Ix civilisation declined, they abandoned their colonies including Mikassa. While Ixin itself would remain connected to great galactic civilisation, Mikassa itself was forgotten for millennia.

Over the millennia of isolation the '''Mikassans''' developed on their own distinct path biologically. Visually there is little to no difference between native Mikassans and baseline [[Human|Humans]], but certain internal differences will often throw off basic medical scanners. The native Mikassans have adapted to thrive in harsh conditions such as extreme heat and foods that baseline Humans have difficulty digesting.

== Culture ==

Once Mikassa was reunited with the larger galaxy it became a popular location for tropical resorts. It did not take long for the immigrants related to this industry to out number the native Mikassans. The vast majority were Humans, but a large portion were other species, though none in larger enough numbers to form a distinct minority. While the native Mikassans have maintained some distinct cultural practices, these have largely been adopted by the planet as whole and are not associated with the native Mikassans specifically. In every day conversation, the term '''Mikassan''' is more likely to refer to someone who hails from or resides on Mikassa in general versus a genetic Mikassan specifically.

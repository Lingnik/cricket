The '''Ministry of Defense''', also referred to by many as the ''Defense Council'', was a council of the [[New Republic Senate]]. The Ministry of Defense was charged with the oversight of the [[New Republic Military|military]], and approved funding for the military, the [[Supreme Commander]], and major fleet movements.

At the beginning of [[16 ABY]], the Council began to focus more on the joint formation of strategies with the leaders of the New Republic military, in the hopes that a more broad view of the battlefield would help turn the tide of the war.

Under the behest of Del Marx, it would conduct an inquiry into the actions of the [[224th Marine Battalion]] during [[Operation Sudden Fury]].

Upon the election of Senator Al'Dira to the ministry, an official committee hearing was called to scrutinize the Military and assess its current condition in hopes of rectifying any immediate issues.

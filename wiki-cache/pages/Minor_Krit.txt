The term '''Minor Krit''' can refer to either the [[Near-Human]] species "native" to [[Krittain Minor]] or more generally to any person who hails from that world.

== Species ==

[[Krittain Minor]] was the site of a prison colony millennia ago that was eventually abandoned leaving the prisons to fend for themselves. Given the world's heavy gravity and overall harsh climate this eventually led to adaptations that created enough divergence from the standard [[Human]] genome to be biologically classified as a separate [[Near-Human]] species. '''Minor Krits''' tend to be short, broad-chested, with a high bone density, and are considered to be deceptively strong and resilient. They do largely pass for Humans visually and basic medical scans may not register them as non-Human.

Though they are no less intelligent than baseline Humans, their culture has often been viewed as unsophisticated by outsiders, coupled with the harsh environment they live, they have often been the subjects of jokes about their intelligence and civility. They are known for rough manners, poor table etiquette, and tendency towards aggressive sporting activity.

== Culture ==

Genetic Minor Krits are now a minority on Krittain Minor. The vast majority of the planet residents are Human, most of whom trace their ancestry to nearby worlds such as [[Krittain Major]], [[Caspar]], and [[Mergansar]]. In every day conversation, the term '''Minor Krit''' is more likely to refer to someone who hails from or resides on Krittain Minor in general versus a genetic Minor Krit specifically. In spite of how their culture is viewed by others, Minor Krits tend to be quite proud of their culture and identity. It is not at all uncommon for even first generation Minor Krits to proudly claim the moniker even if they do not look they come from the world.

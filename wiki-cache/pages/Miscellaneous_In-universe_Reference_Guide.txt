All things such as movies, books, actors, food and drink, sports teams, etc. (both canon and made up) will be compiled here so that they can be referenced in casual in-character conversations, create a greater element of depth, and create some continuity in non-plot roleplaying.

==Entertainment, Movies, Programs, Actors==

'''''All My Fuzzlings''''': A popular daytime holo-drama mainly featuring Squibs in the main cast.
* ''All My Children''

'''''Konran the Jedi''''': A long-running series of action holo-films chronicling the exploits of the titular character, Konran the Jedi.
* ''Conan the Barbarian''

'''''Shining Light''''': A popular daytime holo-drama that is a bit more racy and relationship-centric.
* ''Guiding Light''

'''''Unnatural''''': A cult smash holo-drama with a supernatural twist about siblings and urban legends.
* ''Supernatural''

'''''Tattooine Bob Franchise''''': A classic holofilm series surrounding an archeologist and his adventures through dangerous planets. Starring [[Clark Galaxy]].
* ''Indiana Jones''

'''''Chance Carmichael Franchise''''': An action packed holofilm that follows the adventures of a super spy. Starring [[Wynn Ryder]].
* ''James Bond''

'''''Time Lords''''': A popular animated holo-toon for pre-teens and teens.
* ''Power Rangers/Dr. Who Hybrid''

'''''Holowood''''': Catch-all term for the entertainment industry across the galaxy.
* ''Hollywood''

==Clothing, Accessories, Jewelry==

'''''Ray-Gunz''''': Designer sunglasses that are custom made and priced depending on the purchaser. Each pair is different.
* ''Ray-Banz Sunglasses''

==Books==
'''''Chance Carmichael:''''' A series of holo-novels about the galaxy's top secret agent. Written by J.K. Fleming.

==Music==

'''''Screaming Bantha:''''' A popular meteor rock band.

'''''Meteor Rock:''''' A distinctly alternative and angsty mix of punk and rock sounds.

'''Spacebucks''': Horrible and evil caf chain.

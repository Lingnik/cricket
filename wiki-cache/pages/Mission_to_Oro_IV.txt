Oro IV, an isolated, out of the way [[New Republic]]-held planet, was the target of the now infamous [[Black Stars]] squadron. A supply station in orbit was the objective of the squadron, and the Black Stars arrived in system aboard the [[Corellian Corvette]] [[ICRV Admiral Canis|''Admiral Canis'']].

The Republic defenders were defeated by the Black Stars, and, with no immediate counterattack looming, Kreldin called in an [[Escort Carrier]] equipped with [[Scimitar Assault Bomber|Scimitar Assault Bombers]]. Two flights of bombers was all it took to utterly destroy the station.

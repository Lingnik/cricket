'''Mithral''' is currently an [[assassin]] for hire, working for the highest bidder.

==Biography==
For several years rumors could be heard of this man on [[:starwars:Telos_IV|Telos IV]], his skills and reputation preceding him.  He has recently resurfaced as an assassin for hire roaming the galaxy, never stopping in one place for very long.  His current whereabouts are unknown.

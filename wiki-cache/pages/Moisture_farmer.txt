'''Moisture farmers''' are harvesters of water on very arid planets such as [[Tatooine]]. The harvested water could than be sold at market or used by the farmers themselves in hydroponic gardens. The water is harvested using [[:starwars:Moisture vaporator/Legends|vaporators]], which are able to yield up to two liters of condensed water a day. However, this figure can vary dramatically depending on location. Areas of the [[:starwars:Dune Sea/Legends|Dune Sea]], particularly near the [[:starwars:Jundland Wastes/Legends|Jundland Wastes]], are known to be particularly dry.

[[:starwars:Owen Lars/Legends|Owen Lars]], like his father [[:starwars:Cliegg Lars/Legends|Cliegg Lars]], was a moisture farmer on Tatooine. [[:starwars:Ramiz|Ramiz]] was a moisture farmer on [[:starwars:Aduba-3/Legends|Aduba-3]].

The '''Mon Calamari System''', also known as the '''Calamari System''' and called the '''Daca System''' , is the home star system of the [[Mon Calamari]] and [[Quarren]] people. It lies out on the [[the Galaxy#Outer Rim|Outer Rim]], beyond the Tion Hegemony, and represents perhaps one of the furthest sectors from the Core.

The Mon Calamari and Quarren peoples have either colonized or utilized all the planets in this system, as well as branching out to several other star systems in the sector. Some thirty billion inhabitants called the system home, and  most of the other planets and moons in the system had carefully cultivated orbits, adjusted so as to give the star system peace and stability.

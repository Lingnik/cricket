'''Mos Eisley''' was a large spaceport town on the planet [[Tatooine]]. It was the largest settlement on the planet.

==Geography==
[[Image:MosEisley.JPG|thumb|200px|left|The full expanse of Mos Eisley.]]

Mos Eisley was located roughly 80 kilometers north of [[:starwars:Anchorhead/Legends|Anchorhead]], near the desert [[:starwars:Jabba's Palace|palace]] of [[:starwars:Jabba Desilijic Tiure/Legends|Jabba the Hutt]].

From a distance, the spaceport appeared as a haphazard collage of low-grade concrete, stone, and plastoid structures that spread outward from a central power and water distribution plant. With no central docking facility, the smooth circular depressions of Mos Eisley's 362 docking bays pockmarked the landscape like craters.

The town was really larger than it looked, as a good portion of it lay underground. In fact, it had a population of around 40 to 60 thousand people, varying seasonally. 

[[Image:Mos Eisley 2hji2.jpg|right|thumb|220px|Mos Eisley, a space port town.]]

The city was divided into the "[[:starwars:New Quarter/Legends|New Quarter]]" and the "[[:starwars:Old Quarter/Legends|Old Quarter]]". The Old Quarter contained the wreckage of the ''[[:starwars:Dowager Queen/Legends|Dowager Queen]]'', the original hub of the city, and [[:starwars:Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina/Legends|Chalmun's Cantina]]. Jabba the Hutt also maintained a residence there, since it was difficult to make business from his palace. The New Quarter was more tourist friendly and was a merchant district where tourists could relax (though there was still much crime). Each quarter had individual neighborhoods, such as the rough-and-tumble [[:starwars:Tar Mass|Tar Mass]] area. 

During the [[Galactic Civil War]], the Empire established a small garrison in Mos Eisley, although this did not help with the city's massive crime rates. Whenever an expansion of the base was required, the Empire would simply bulldoze surrounding houses. 

To support the constant flow of [[:starwars:Spacer/Legends|spacers]], Mos Eisley featured a large number of hotels, [[:starwars:Casino/Legends|casinos]], and [[:starwars:Cantina/Legends|cantinas]].

==History==
[[Image:Tatbattle2.JPG|thumb|left|200px|The [[Galactic Empire|Empire's]] troops fight Rebel elements in Mos Eisley.]]

Mos Eisley was built from the beginning with commerce in mind.  It was founded as a substitute for the nearby, yet more expensive, Anchorhead. Located around the crash site of the ''Dowager Queen'', the survivors of this crash originally named it simply "Eisley". They later renamed it to fit with other towns such as [[:starwars:Mos Espa/Legends|Mos Espa]], and [[:starwars:Mos Gamos|Mos Gamos]]. These same immigrants would later found the planet's future capital, [[:starwars:Bestine (city)/Legends|Bestine]].

During the [[Galactic Civil War]], the "hive of scum and villainy" became a target of notorious [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] officer [[Grand Moff]] [[:starwars:Wilhuff Tarkin/Legends|Wilhuff Tarkin]], who had been tasked with controlling the [[Outer Rim]] by [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]] prior to the [[:starwars:Battle of Yavin/Legends|Battle of Yavin]]. 

He began his conquest on the outskirts of Mos Eisley, seeing the city as an ideal outpost for their operations. Unfortunately for the [[Galactic Empire|Imperials]], the [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Rebel Alliance]] had their own outpost station in nearby Anchorhead, and moved to intercept the Empire, thus the [[:starwars:Battle of Tatooine  (Galactic Civil War)|First Battle of Tatooine]] ensued.

Though the Empire emerged victorious from the desert battle, their task force was considerably diminished, and their eventual presence in Mos Eisley was less than had been expected. This came back to haunt the Empire, as [[R2-D2]] and [[C-3PO]] slipped through the cracks in what would otherwise have been a mighty security force. The Rebel presence in Mos Eisley was finally routed during the latter part of the battle.

[[Image:MosEisley-celebration.jpg|thumb|right|200px|The streets of Mos Eisley erupt in celebration at the news of [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|the Emperor's]] demise.]]

[[:starwars:Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina/Legends|Chalmun's Cantina]] played a crucial role in these times, as the establishment was where [[Luke Skywalker]] and [[:starwars:Obi-Wan Kenobi/Legends|Obi-Wan Kenobi]] met [[Han Solo]] and [[Chewbacca]] in their quest to get to [[:starwars:Alderaan/Legends|Alderaan]] in order to deliver the [[:starwars:Death Star plans/Legends|Death Star plans]] to the Alliance. The Imperials also searched Mos Eisley for R2-D2 and C-3PO, the holders of the missing Death Star plans.

[[:starwars:Jabba Desilijic Tiure/Legends|Jabba the Hutt]] was said to own a townhouse in the city.

After the collapse of Imperial order and the death of Jabba the Hutt, Tatooine came under the control of the [[V'ez-Tcha]] crime syndicate. In [[6 ABY]], the Empire, under [[Bacharan Valak]], clamped down on Tatooine in an attempt to crush the V'ez-Tcha. Following a massive orbital bombardment, Imperial [[Stormtrooper]]s hunted down the remaining criminals in Mos Eisley and arrested them. The leaders of V'ezh-Tcha were rounded up, shipped to [[Kessel]], and Tatooine became garrisoned by the Empire. Mos Eisley would become a hot spot for Imperial activity, where Stormtroopers and pilots off-duty would come and relax - and usually find themselves in a bar fight. In [[8 ABY]], sometime after the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign]], Tatooine would be [[Fourth Battle of Tatooine|liberated]] by the [[New Republic]] set free once again.

In [[13 ABY]], during [[Warlord]] [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]]'s hunt for [[Ezkhil i'Falzin]], [[Vassily Korolov]] had Mos Eisley [[Imperial Bombardment of Tatooine|bombarded]] from orbit - the result was the destruction of a significant portion of the city. Reconstruction efforts would ensue, but Mos Eisley would never fully recover.

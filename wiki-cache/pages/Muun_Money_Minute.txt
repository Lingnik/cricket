[[File:Brackett Benduday.jpg|right|thumb]] The '''Muun Money Minute''' is an [[InterGalactic News|IGN]] financial news programme that debuted in [[27 ABY]]. It is hosted by the [[Firrerreo]] economist Professor [[Brackett Benduday]] who had been employed as a financial analyst by IGN for a number of years before being given her own segment. The programme's name is a gimmick as it has no association with [[:starwars:Muun/Legends|Muuns]], [[:starwars:Muunilinst/Legends|Muunilinst]], nor is it only a minute.

== OOC Trivia ==

Brackett Benduday is a homage to Olivia Munn's character Sloan Sabbith from the Newsroom. Thus the programme name is as much a play on her name as on Muuns and Muunilinst.

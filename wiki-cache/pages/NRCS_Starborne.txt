'''NRCS ''Starborne''''' was a [[Carrack-class Light Cruiser|''Carrack''-class Light Cruiser]] of the [[New Republic]]'s [[New Republic Second Fleet|Second Fleet]]. She was one of three Republic ''Carrack'' cruisers lost during the final battle of [[Operation Shado Kolpo]] when her she took a heavy hit to her drive unit, rendering her uncontrollable. ''Starborne'' collided with ''[[Voidbreaker]]'', a Republic corvette, destroying the two ships instantly.

The New Republic Gunship ''Forge of Hope'' was originally commissioned and built as an anti-fighter platform for the [[FST-01 Firecats]]. Because Corellia was under Imperial occupation at the time, New Republic engineers performed the ship's construction at the [[Mon Calamari Shipyards]]. <br><br>''Forge of Hope'' saw a brilliant but short career as a military vessel. Less than a year after her first flight, the Firecats experiment was deemed a success by the New Republic government and subsequently disbanded. ''Forge of Hope'' was recommissioned as a medical support vessel and has served since in that capacity.

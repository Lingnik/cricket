== Appearance ==

Twi'lek come in a variety of colors, and the seedling-green of this female's skin places her as one of the more commonly seen. Like all Twi'lek, the only hair upon her head is her dark eyebrows, providing protection and accent to her amethyst eyes. The crown of her head reaches 5'8" in height, lekku typically left to hang behind her when in motion. In addition to a dancer's grace, she possesses the sort of lean strength that speaks of regular exercise, body toned without losing its feminine appearance. With full but balanced curves, most outfits should suit her well.

A snug black tank top adorns her torso, following each curve and dip revealed to it. As befits one of her work and experience, her body is in excellent shape, gently rounded shoulders leading to sleek arms and long-fingered hands. Covering her hands are a pair of fingerless black leather gloves, worn down a bit from use. Hanging loosely from her waist are khaki cargo pants, offering no hint to the figure beneath as they billow downwards until coming to a halt around the ankles. Battered knee-high buckled combat boots enclose her feet within them.

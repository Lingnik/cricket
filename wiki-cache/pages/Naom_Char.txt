'''Naom Char''' was a temperate world in the [[The Galaxy#Outer Rim|Outer Rim]], several lightyears from [[Kessel]] and located within the [[Kessel Redoubt]]. It was once home to an advanced yet isolated civilization, which was extinct by [[14 ABY]], when an [[Naom Char Incident|incident]] occurred on world between the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] and Imperial fugitives.

'''Nappa Creations Incorporated''' or '''NCI''' was a ship modification firm operating during the Galactic Civil War. The company is now remembered more for their controversies than their work itself.

In [[8 ABY]] several high profile corporate executives attending the swoop race on [[Nar Shaddaa]] were embroiled in a shootout when bounty hunters kidnapped a New Republic dignitary and swoop racer, [[Altair Quila]]. During the fight, NCI corporate security vessels fired upon [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] ships in the system, creating a long lasting feud between the two entities. Later that year CEO [[Goku Saiyanes]] was executed by the [[Galactic Empire]] for the attempted murder of CSA [[Office of the Auditor General|Auditor General]] [[Morrison Van Sen]] on [[Selene]]. Internal divisions of the CSA react to the attack on the Auditor General with hostilities against NCI holdings on Nar Shaddaa. When the smoke clears, [[Delila Dmitri]] becomes CEO, and begins to rebuild.

In [[9 ABY]], at some point after [[Bespin|Bespin's]] liberation by the [[New Republic]] Delila Dmitri becomes [[Baron|Baron-Administrator]] of [[Cloud City]] and NCI moved its operations from Nar Shaddaa to Bespin as a result. NCI's assets on Nar Shaddaa would be absorbed by [[NovaCom Cybertronics]].

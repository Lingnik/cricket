=== Firemaiden of Solusar ===

Naralyn Ohek rose to prominence around 14 ABY when the Solusarian Retreat on [[Caspar]] opened its doors to the galaxy.  The retreat offered Sarian treatments for health and well-being, but had previously been run by separatists who did not welcome any who were not pure Sarian.  Ohek was Firemaiden of Solusar, Priestess and Healer to visitors alongside Jamdin Blodiel, the Windwalker of Casohav.

Under their leadership the retreat grew swiftly in popularity, attracting visits from the famous and infamous of the galaxy.  It was during this time that Blodiel disappeared, an event which was never really explained.  Naralyn was linked romantically with [[Ernest Pallando]] and accompanied him on his travels for business and pleasure.

Pallando was named Windwalker of Casohav shortly before Ohek left the retreat with no warning, leaving behind only unanswered questions.

=== Rumours ===

Rumours abounded as to where the Firemaiden had disappeared to, with some whispers even hinting that the retreat had not been all it claimed to be and linking Ohek to a gang of pirates although no formal charges are on record.

=== Angry Rancor ===

Ohek is presently working as a Croupier aboard the Angry Rancor.  She is often seen in the company of her daughter who she names simply as Eralyn.

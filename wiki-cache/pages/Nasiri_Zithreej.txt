The first appearance of the Falleen known as''' Nasiri Gejalli''' (real name Nasiri Zithreej) was in 43 ISY, when he emerged from Nal Hutta and took over occupation of the Palace on Nar Shaddas as Lorda. The Falleen established influence over the Smugglers moons with seeming ease. Earning a reputation as a savage he was capable of barbaric actions that rivaled the Hutts themselves.

Rumors of acts of cannibalism circulated after a rival crime lord attempting to threaten the Lorda into cooperation failed and the visiting majordomo left never to return. At odds with these rumors were acts of reformation for the attached Pleasure Palace that helped fund the Lorda's power base. Paid and well cared for the staff of the Pleasure Palace were freed and remained on willingly and boosted the reputation and profit of the business greatly. As his time as Lorda moved along the Falleen expanded his operation and finally it evolved into a Criminal Cartel known as the Night Parade and thus was the persona of Night Parade born. It was not a kind journey as he suffered many set backs and victories. He learned for every wound suffered the scars were the foundations for later success.

=='''The Night Parade'''==

* The Night Parade is the criminal cartel formed by Lorda Gejalli as illicit enterprises through out the galaxy required a bit of cover to hide him from direct connections abroad. The cartel blossomed as historic events unfolded on Nal Hutta. And fortune favored him to a degree that he was able to afford to employ professional soldiers alongside the many thugs and gunslingers in his service.

=='''Empire and Hutts'''==
Indeed it was a bargain with the Select Committee that would betray him and grant him the greatest boon to his fortunes. The Falleen used his direct knowledge of the Council of Ancients and younger ambitious Hutts to facilitate a direct attack by the Empire on the Hutt seat of power in an effort to destroy their influence in the galaxy. The attack was successful and the Council was destroyed to the member. In the aftermath the Empire left with no intention of exerting any influence. Leaving Lorda Gejalli to pick up the pieces and see that the new Hutt leaders were directed as needed. This was the birth of Lorda Gejalli into Night Parade.

The younger Hutts knew their complicity in affairs could see their species enter into a civil war that would destroy Hutt space and end in ruin. So grudgingly they agreed to a shadow lord overseer. It proved profitable as Nasiri modernized much of their ways of operation in the galaxy at large and even saw to the organization of an actual fleet for the defense of Nal Hutta to prevent another such attack as the one recent.

=='''Wealth of Misfortune'''==

[[Image:Nasiri Combat Armour.jpg|right|thumb|Nasiri in his Combat Armour]] Lorda Gejalli found ideal moments to expand his influence outside of Hutt space and was able to form a Combine of planets under his name. Falleen, Geonosis and Gamora agreed to follow his guidance in the galaxy. With these worlds at his fingertips and the resources of Hutt Clans he was able to leverage such resources as he never had dreamed of. That was until the Imperium rose from the corpse of the old Empire. He was making a move to legitimacy when an encounter with [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] the Imperator of the Imperium would see him attacked and his floating palace destroyed and had a choice become a Moff of the Imperium or die. He took the title and bargained for Hutt space to remain independent from the Imperium and this was granted.

This would send him back to Nar Shaddaa and awaken the savage he once was before success softened him. But this time he was fortified by years of profit and vast wealth. Deals with old adversaries granted him access to many things Hutts never had.

He formed a formal military for the Night Parade, the Palace Militant, soldiers hidden beneath chrome stormtrooper armor and geared with state-of-the-art weapons. Hutt space would profit and if the galaxy didn't want him to be civilized, he would remind them what a barbaric savage could be. 

== Hutts will be Hutts ==
In the course of a few years following, Nasiri under the guise of Night Parade strove to make cultural changes in Hutt space, trying to at first change the nature of slave trade in Hutt space. A move that though he to a degree agreed with, not being a particular fan of the trade. Was more driven by threats of  Imperator Malign and his sister the Jedi Siri te Danaan. He would see some success, but nothing that made him believe it would be embraced as a whole.

Hutts were by nature greedy creatures and untold years of systemic corruption and trafficking would not be changed by a single sentient, it was these moves that planted the seeds of discontent within his shadowy rule of Hutt space.  A few discreet attempts on his life were made, poison being the weapon of choice. The crime lord could see which way the wind was blowing and what was to come, to that end he began making plans and moves to remove himself from hutt space before the Hutt Grand Council tired of subtle attempts and moved directly to remove him. Among these moves were some very bold moves,  freeing a few thralled worlds. A gesture that was both tactical and designed to demonstrate to the Imperium and others that he was making a genuine effort and changing the slave trade.

'''Nbba "Frederiko" Eembek''' is an [[Ortolan]] musician and frontman of the Sack of Pancakes Band. He was the subject of the painting "Frederiko Famished" by [[Robot 7]]. In [[34 ABY]], the work was donated to the [[:starwars:Arkanis sector/Legends|Arkanis Sector]] Children's Relief Fundraiser and Ball, held at the [[Royale Resorts|Royale Hotel]] on [[Corellia]], by Robot 7 and signed on the back by Frederiko. The winning bid was 8,000 credits placed by [[Krackor Reincus]].

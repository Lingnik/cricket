The '''Staryards of Nenawirth''' are an average-sized, complex series of construction slipways and facilities set in orbit above the city of '''Nenawirth''' on [[Kasputin Yar]]. It is one of the two facilities managed by [[Lokkai Starworks]], but there are smaller machine shops and slipways for scores of independent builders and outfits.

While no means as large and extensive as the famous staryards of [[The Galaxy|the Galaxy]], such as Kuat, Fondor, or Sluis Van, the yards here at Kasputin Yar see alot of traffic, especially small freighters and cutters. It is conceiveable for yard coordinators to handle over 2,000 ships a day &mdash; a substantial number for the relative obscurity that this realm of space resides in. Most of the traffic is generated by, or related to, Hutt, Tion, or Cronese purposes, but there are the occasional small-volume, "ma-and-pa" type of customers as well.

The yards have become a valuable commodity for a few independent militaries, as Lokkai is capable of producing, or at least retrofitting, several types of mid-sized cruisers and frigates. They have set up a working partnership with [[SubPro]] in the neighboring [[Caspia|Caspian System]], and several dozen of [[Caspia|Caspia's]] smaller naval vessels have been constructed here at the Nenawirth facilities.

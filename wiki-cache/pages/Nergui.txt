Nergui has taken over the RPA Wiz Position from [[Kajman]] in December 2006. His duties include managing the RPA Members, dealing with RPA Applications and coordinating with the Force Wiz and the TP Wizards on plots and Applications. He is known to confront people with his Flash-Thingie to erase their memories of things they have seen or heard.

His current description is:
Little swirls of dust mark the passage of the being that is clad in darkness and flowing, ripped clothes. Tool belts and a bandolier are filled with cheap looking implements of various obscure purposes. Most casings obviously made from cheap white plastics that have faded to yellow due to exposure to the elements. To ask for guarantees with this one, is obviously too much to ask.

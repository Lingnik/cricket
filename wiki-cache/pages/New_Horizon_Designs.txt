An independent engineering company, New Horizon Designs is a branch of [[New Horizon Enterprises]], a corporation which was founded by [[Joshua Arands|Joshua]] and [[Shannon Arands]]. NHD functions as a parts and chassis design facility for [[Corellian Engineering Corporation]] Shipyards. Much of NHD's work has been modifications and upgrades to existing starships, however many projects currently on hold are supposed to be impressive steps forward in small vessel design.

NHD is based in the Corellia Sector, with its primary offices and design labs located on the CEC Shipyards space station. Its small complement of engineers and designers are partially credited with the improved design work for SubPro/CDMC's [[Z-100 Centurion]] and [[Z-105 Harpoon]] designs.

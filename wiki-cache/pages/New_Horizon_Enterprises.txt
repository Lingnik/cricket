'''New Horizon Enterprises''' (NHE) is a financial management corporation founded by [[Joshua Arands|Joshua]] and [[Shannon Arands]] in [[11 ABY]]. The firm owns and subsidizes several [[Corellia]]-based businesses and provides venture capital to qualifying new and existing businesses on Corellia, [[:starwars:Tralus|Tralus]], and [[:starwars:Talus|Talus]]. Notable NHE-owned subsidiaries include [[New Horizon Designs]], [[Royale Resorts]], and its most recent aquisition, the [[Reckless Abandon Swoop Arena]].

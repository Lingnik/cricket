The '''New Republic 5th Marine Corps''' is assigned to the [[New Republic Second Fleet]] and headquartered at the [[New Alderaan]] military garrison on [[Ord Mantell]]. It's original nickname, ‘Ever Steadfast’, was also known as ‘The Ones who Hold’ due to their heroics at the [[:starwars:Battle of Hoth|Battle of Hoth]]. The corps is currently under the command of [[Major General]] [[Roagen Vakren]].

== History ==

The original Fifth Corps made their name anew during the Battle of Hoth while holding off the Imperial Ground assault, an act they paid dearly for. The corps was derived from original guerilla units comprising the [[:starwars:Rebel Alliance|Rebel Alliance]] [[:starwars:Alliance Army|Army]]. In the wake of the Imperial fall, and theirs, Major General Antilos decided that while the honorable deeds of the previous units would be acknowledged in the Marine Corps Headquarters, the newly re-christened unit would have the opportunity to create their own history.

== Recent Events ==

== Units ==

The Fifth Marine Corps is assigned to the [[New Republic Second Fleet]]. Five brigades make up the corps, each with different duties.

==== The [[New Republic 70th Marine Brigade|70th Marine Brigade]] ====

Headquartered on the [[NRSD Reprisal|NRSD ''Reprisal]], the 70th brigade staffed the fleet's collection of Star Destroyers.

==== The 71st Marine Brigade ====

Headquartered aboard the [[MCSC Deliverance|NRSC ''Deliverance'']], the 71st brigade staffed the remainder of Second Fleet's carrier vessels and many of the adjoining assault frigates, corvettes, and the medical frigate.

==== The 72nd Marine Brigade ====

Headquartered aboard the NRHC ''Duraleague'', the 72nd brigade staffed the fleet's [[Dreadnaught-class Heavy Cruiser|''Dreadnaught''-class Heavy Cruiser]]s.

==== The [[New Republic 73rd Marine Brigade|73rd Marine Brigade]] ====

Headquartered at the [[New Alderaan]] military garrison on [[Ord Mantell]], the 73rd brigade staffed the fleet's smaller support craft, such as the [[CRV Audacity|NRCRV ''Audacity'']]. and the [[CRV Land Runner|NRCRV ''Land Runner'']].

==== The 74th Marine Brigade ====

Stationed aboard [[Orbital Station Paladin|O.S. ''Paladin'']], the 74th brigade served as assault batallions. These batallions would be loaded onto carrier vessels when called into duty for assaults on Imperial planets, or liberations of Imperial-occupied planets formerly under the protection of the [[New Republic]]. The brigade also supplied reinforcements for troops lost in the other brigades.

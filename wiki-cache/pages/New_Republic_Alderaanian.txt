A '''New Republic Alderaanian''' was a [[credit]] chip issued by the New Republic Treasury, worth 1,000,000 [[New Republic]] credits. It was larger than a standard credit chip of pure white gold. It was emblazoned with the Standard of Alderaan on the face and the flame of the Rebel Alliance on the tail, with the inscription 'Never Forget' imprinted along the rim in 30 languages.

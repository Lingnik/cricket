== Standard Flightsuit ==

[[Image:pilot_x-wing_01.jpg|140px|right|X-wing Pilot Uniform]]

Here is a basic flightsuit description.  It's the same standard uniform from the movies. Colors are typically:

* orange: standard X-wing deployment
* grey: standard Y-wing deployment, or by "roving assignment" squadrons
* red: standard B-wing deployment, or by elite "Guards" squadrons
* green: standard A-wing deployment, or by "special forces" squadrons

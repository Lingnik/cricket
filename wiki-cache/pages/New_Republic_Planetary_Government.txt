'''Planetary Governors''' are directly responsible for governing their respective New Republic territories. They do so under the guidance of the [[New Republic Senate|Senate]], the [[Court of Justice]], and the [[New Republic General Ministry|General Ministry]]'s collection of civil organizations. The primary difference between a Senator and a Governor is that Senators represent their planets to the Senate, where Governors work the other way around; by upholding and encouraging the ideals of the New Republic in their territories.

Beyond that, each planet is free to dicate their own forms of planetary government, as long as it falls under the general guidelines and ideals of the [[New Republic]] and adheres to certain laws and regulations as deemed by the aforementioned government bodies. Government structure, civil law enforcement, and planetary/region laws are dictated by the individual Planetary Governments, while being held in check by the Senate, Justice Court and General Ministries to maintain the ideals of the New Republic in whole.

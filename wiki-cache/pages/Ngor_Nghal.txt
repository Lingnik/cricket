'''Ngor Nghal''' is a small, dark, denizen world in the '''Ngorogulal System'''. It is a fractured planet, barely habitable, but home to several hundred thousand beings from all manner of species across that region of space. Ngorogulal lies at one vertex of a triangle defined by [[Yariz]] to the "northwest", [[:starwars:Bimmisaari|Bimmisaari]] to the "southwest" and itself.

The planet's orbital lies in close proximity to a massive gas giant named '''Ngor Imphaighl'''. So massive is this giant that Ngor Nghal spends much of its time in ecliptic darkness, often receiving ambient light or light refracted through Imphaighl's atmosphere. Nghal also rotates extremely slowly, and also seems to be "dragged along" by the much larger gas giant.

Since it is near the treacheries of [[Hutt Space]], it is often a target of pirates and raiders either for pilfering or for safe, secret harbor.

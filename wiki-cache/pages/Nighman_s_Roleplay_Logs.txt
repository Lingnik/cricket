Dr. Canto also noted that the man had claimed to him before that he had found proof verifying the Force amplifier theories, but was unwilling to discuss it until he had all of the facts. The man did hint though at a guarded crypt which contained the pieces, to which he had the key, but had not yet been able to locate. Perhaps the historians notes and the key artifact could be found in the boxes shipped to Corellia? Paul was ecstatic with the idea. Paul finally just up and leaves, not to look for Luke or Grathix, but just to get out, do some thinking. In passing a few storefronts, he recalls that the conversation and lessons with Shenner the night before planted a few thoughtful seeds in his mind, and he decided to act on them, feeling unreasonably pleased and amused at the thought of buying the girl things.}}

'''Noah Kryeck''' ([[w:c:starwars:47_BBY|47 BBY]] - ) was an [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] businessman who operated through the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector]]. He was most famous for his role as the COO of [[Galactic Amusements]], which he later became CEO of shortly after the disappearance of the company's CEO, [[Alexander Floyd]].

Noah was born on [[Corellia]] into the wealthy Kryeck family. He studied in the best schools in the Corellian sector, and stayed out of the [[:starwars:Clone_Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]] when it ravaged the [[The Galaxy|galaxy]].

He did, however, become a war profiteer, travelling to war-torn planets in the [[The Galaxy#Outer Rim|Outer Rim]] and earning massive profits. He later went on to become a loyal Imperial citizen, working for various Imperial government-operated companies.

In [[11 ABY]], he became the COO of Galactic Amusements, a new entertainment company with heavy ties to the Galactic Empire. He frequented the ''[[Supernova Siren]]'', the company's personal capital ship, and became good friends with the ship's young manager, [[Kahrak Taiden]]. 

After the disappearance of Floyd, Kryeck became the CEO of the company, but with the outbreak of the [[Imperial Civil War]] and the [[War of the Throne]] was unable to maintain control of the company, and all the company's assets were soon liquidated by the Empire.

Kryeck, having lost his fortune, fled to [[Tatooine]].

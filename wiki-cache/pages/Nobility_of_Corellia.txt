The '''Nobility of [[Corellia]]''' were rich and powerful families that ruled [[Corellia]] hundreds to thousands of years prior to the [[:starwars:Battle of Yavin|Battle of Yavin]]. The nobility served under the [[:starwars:King|King]] of the [[:starwars:Corellian Empire|Corellian Empire]], each granted control over their respected territory and its population. After the monarchy was abolished around [[:starwars:312 BBY|312 BBY]], the nobility lost most of their control, though retained their prestige and respect, and, of course, their vast wealth.

*[[House of Kreldin]] - known descendant, [[Danik Kreldin]]

*House of Quinn - known descendant, [[Kiare Quinn]]

*House of Smitherbodkins - known descendant, [[Geophreigh Smitherbodkins IV]]

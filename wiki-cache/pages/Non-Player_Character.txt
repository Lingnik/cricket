======================================================================
A '''Non-Player Character (NPC)''' is a character within the MUSH that is played through, or ''projected'', by [[Player Character|player characters]] during roleplay sessions so as to diversify or deepen the unfolding story. They can also be referred to or referenced in dialogs, without ever actually "meeting them" or their being played. In some instances they go nameless, while in others, they take on vast backgrounds and histories, and become integral parts of the story.

NPC's rarely, if ever, have stats applied to them.

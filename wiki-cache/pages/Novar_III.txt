'''Novar III''' was the third planetary body of the Nova system, an [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] backwater in the [[The Galaxy#Expansion Region|Expansion Region]]. It was a chilly world, with a population of only three million. During the [[Imperial Civil War]], it was conquered by the [[True Empire]] and established as a resupply base for front-line military forces. 

Towards the end of the war, the high command of the True Imperial government and military fell back to Novar III and fought their last stand against [[Bacharan Valak|Valak]]'s armed forces. Novar III was brought back into the Imperial fold and its populace underwent extensive re-education by [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] despite the fact that they did not support the True Empire during the war.

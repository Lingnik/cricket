Established in mid-[[15 ABY]] under a veil of secrecy by the [[Jedi]], the '''Nyarikan Safeway''' was a hyperroute used to efficiently and effectively direct personnel and material from [[:starwars:Ploo|Ploo]] to [[Ord Mantell]]. It's existence and purposes were closely-guarded, known only by top brass of the [[New Republic Military]] and other leading members of the [[New Republic]].

== Discovery ==

After returning from a mission to '''Cophrigin-5''' in mid-[[15 ABY]], a team of Jedi aboard their cruiser [[Refrain of Anshalar]] needed to find a way back into [[New Republic]] space, without leaving any trace of where they had come from. They were able to reach [[Ord Mantell]], but reports of Imperial activity on the borders made travel through [[:starwars:Anobis|Anobis]] and [[:starwars:Ylix|Ylix]] risky. Thus, [[Luke Skywalker]] decided that they would need to follow their instincts, and use the [[Force]] to cut an uncharted route to [[:starwars:Ploo|Ploo]], by cutting close to the [[:starwars:Nyarikan Nebula|Nyarikan Nebula]].

The route was plotted through sheer instinct by [[Ikihsa Enb'Zik]] with the guidance of [[Luke Skywalker|Skywalker]], and was recorded by [[R2-D2]].

== OOC Info ==

This hyperspace route is part of a plot developed by [[User:SW1_Kyle|SW1 Kyle]]. It's in-game purpose is to provide a way to conceal classified movements of the [[New Republic]], in an effort to steer the feel of the [[New Republic]] to the Rebellion-era hit and fade tactics. In this way, we as players will be able to keep plots from developing that would require the [[Galactic Empire]] to flatten [[Ord Mantell]], as the faction administrators who put in long hours to code the planet wouldn't appreciate that very much.

That said, the '''Nyarikan Safeway''' is currently under a '''consent rule of no-discovery'''. This means that, without the '''express permission''' of the [[New Republic]] [[Organization Head|Faction Head]], no players outside of the New Republic are allowed to discover the route, and no New Republic players are permitted to spill the information to opposing factions or civilians. This rule is in effect to '''protect storylines and plots''' until a time when the Faction Administrators of the Empire and New Republic have agreed that discovery of the route would be beneficial to both faction's stories.

This '''does not''', however, give players of the [[New Republic]] permission to abuse this route. As this route is revealed ICly, it will only be given to high ranking officers and leaders, and those characters will be utilizing this route only when necessary.

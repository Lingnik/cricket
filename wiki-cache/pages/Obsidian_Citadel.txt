The '''Obsidian Citadel''' is the seat of power of the [[New Sith Order]]. Located on [[Trandosha]], the Obsidian Citadel is a towering citadel, a testament to the power of the Sith Order. The top level serves as the throne room for the [[Dark Lord of the Sith]]. It was constructed in [[16 ABY]] as a place of worship for the followers of the Sith's false religion, but was soon turned into the Sith's capital building after the dismantling of the religion.

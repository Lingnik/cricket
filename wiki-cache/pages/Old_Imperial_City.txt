'''Old Imperial City''' is the capital of [[Dreven]]. It was formerly known as '''Bij'veri''', but when Dreven became the capital of the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] after [[Coruscant|Imperial Center's]] [[Battle of Coruscant|fall]], the city was renamed to '''Imperial City'''. It has since been referred to as Old Imperial City, with the restoration of [[Imperial City (Selene)|Imperial City]] on [[Selene]] and the subsequent [[Sixth Battle of Coruscant|liberation]] of Coruscant.

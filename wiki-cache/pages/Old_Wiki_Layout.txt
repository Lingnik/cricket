Hate the new Wikia typography and font settings?

Don't need your characters three inches tall?

Want the old layout back across all your wikia's?

Edit [[Special:MyPage/global.css]]

And enter 
 @import url("http://community.wikia.com/wiki/User:Undead.exe/OldOasis.css?action=raw&ctype=text/css");
 @import url("http://community.wikia.com/index.php?title=User:Undead.exe/OldFontSize.css&action=raw&ctype=text/css");
To undo this, simply edit the global.css page again and remove the above code.

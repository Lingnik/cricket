The '''Olumi Osahn''', or '''The Shadow Brotherhood''', was a fanatical religious order of the [[Sarian]] culture. It arose from the misinterpretations of the Sarian teachings regarding spirituality and reverence for the elements, but took a violent twist when influenced by the extinguished [[Sar Bares Flir]], along with several off-world sources. Their religious beliefs revered [[Olum]], the '''Shadow Being''', the "darkest" of the various Sarian dieties. Their main base of operations was in a series of caverns upon Phobsolin, the lone moon of [[Olumekar]], a hostile and dangerous world in [[Caspia]].

The Olumi Osahn was primarily a volatile organization with significant funds and resources. Its reach was far-spread and its strength comparable to that of [[Black Sun]] or the [[Hutt]] cartels. Despite their criminal record, the Osahn swore oaths to defend [[Caspia]] (the star system itself) at all costs, from anyone, including the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] government. This drew the ire of [[Caspian Crest|the Crest]], who labeled them "beyond radical patriots". This ultimately led to their downfall &mdash; despite throwing all their resources at fighting back against the [[Galactic Empire]] during the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign]] in [[14 ABY]], the Osahn received nothing but disdain from [[Thomas Mahon]], who seized control of the government after ousting [[Elana Tracer]] in a military coup. Mahon feared the Osahn, as his rule would be contested by the Osahn who perceived him as a military dictator. Many of the Osahn's most prominent members were exiled from Caspar, with the organization's safehouses in such places as [[Noques Rasala]] and [[Anchorron]] seized by Union operatives.

'''Operation Stardust''' was a military campaign executed by [[Grand Admiral]] [[Morlok Adlerson]] under the [[Bacharan Valak|Valak]] regime. Its goals were to divide the attention of the [[New Republic]] and to garner resources to finish the [[Death Star III]]. It was primarily focused around capturing [[Pride-1]] for its strategic position as a trade nexus. The Empire succeeded in [[Battle of Pride-1|capturing]] Pride-1 and began to harness its trade capabilities, which helped greatly in the Death Star project, but Pride-1 would eventually return to [[Griffon Alliance|Griffon]] hands with the [[Second Battle of Pride-1]].

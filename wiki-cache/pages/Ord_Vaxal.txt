'''Ord Vaxal''' is a planet on the outer periphery of the [[:starwars:Inner Rim|Inner Rim Territories]], near [[Hutt Space]]. Deep in its past, it was an [[:starwars:Ordnance/Regional Depot/Legends|Ordinance Regional Depot]] like its sister world [[Rauther|Ord Thera]], but was also a prison planet. A number of animals were imported to the planet: [[:starwars:Bantha/Legends|jungle bantha]]s, [[:starwars:Eopie/Legends|eopie]]s, [[:starwars:Ronto/Legends|rontos]], and [[nerf]]s. The prisoners took control of the world in the [[:starwars:Jailbreak|Jailbreak]] of [[20 BBY]].

Under Imperial rule, [[Moff]] [[:starwars:Adria Reyn|Adria Reyn]] allowed [[:starwars:Sassallo|Sassallo]] the [[Hutt]] of the [[:starwars:Besadii/Legends|Besadii]] ''[[:starwars:Kajidic/Legends|kajidic]]'' to practically rule the world. Sassallo and Adria once almost captured [[Han Solo]] and [[Lando Calrissian]].

The planet was invaded by a fleet led by [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] in [[17 ABY]] and officially made a member world of the [[New Sith Order|New Sith Empire]].

Ord Vaxal has a 11,254-km diameter. It had three continents and numerous archipelagos. Its local day lasts 25 standard hours; its local year lasted 288 standard days. Indigenous creatures include [[risp]]s and [[trigha]]s.

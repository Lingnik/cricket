'''Original Feature Characters''' are [[MUSH]] characters upgraded to a level on par with [[Feature Characters]]. They are selected after submitting an application and a screening process. OFCs work closely with the MUSH administration. As part of the deal, OFCs receive significant boosts to their character sheet to reflect their new status.

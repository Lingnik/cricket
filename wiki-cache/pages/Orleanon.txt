'''Orleanon''' is an equatorial resort city on [[Caspar]]. It resides on the northern shores of the '''Gulf of Cennes''', a half-day's ride south of [[Trinumvira]] by aerotram. Due to its location, it enjoys much more predictable and pleasant weather patterns, but does endure tidal flooding twice a year. The flooding of streets in Orleanon has become a tourist attraction, and citizens have tailored a series of festivals around said events.

Contrasting sharply to '''Marin Mountain''' and its own resort, Orleanon appeals to the "old Core-Worlds civilization" folk. While Marin Mountain tends to be promote a "modern ruggedness", the coastal city keeps to jovial, colorful, high-society flair, without abandoning "old-world" values. There is much to do regarding arts, crafts, and high-living. A strong agricultural base exists in the hilly terrain and old forests between Trinumvira and Orleanon, and many niche crops, such as olives, wine-grapes & wine-berries, ''galyna'' (a grain), and ''chatersa'' (a type of fig pressed into an oil extract) are harvested. Due to this, and the easy access to the seas, many [[Sarian#Meditersari|Meditersari]] and [[Sarian#Janhovi|Janhovi]] settled here. Orleanon has, in fact, taken several cues and similarities to the [[Mergansar|Mergani]] city of '''Ithaca'''.


The city has given its name to a [[Shearwater-class Frigate|''Shearwater''-class frigate]], the CMS ''Orleanon'' (CFG-48).

'''Orson Tighe''' was a [[Padawan|Jedi Apprentice]] to [[Jessalyn Valios]]. He was originally a member of [[Talon Karrde]]'s [[Karrde's Organization|organization]] based on [[Myrkr]], and captain of the ''[[Uwannabuyim]]''. However, Jessalyn's arrival on Myrkr and subsequent discovery of Tighe's latent [[Force]] abilities found him following the path of the Jedi.
[[Image:Orson.jpg|left|180px|thumb|Orson Tighe was a Jedi Apprentice under [[Jessalyn Valios]], the Jedi Witch.]]He would fall in love with Jessalyn throughout their adventures together, but would meet his fate at the hands of the [[Dark Jedi]] [[Simon Sezirok]] after a fateful [[RPlog:Death of Orson Tighe|duel]] on [[Nar Shaddaa]].

The well-spoken and highly-educated '''Orton Trissard''' ([[:starwars:60 BBY|61 BBY]] &mdash; ) was a [[Senator]], scientist, and statesman of the fledgling [[Caspian Democratic Union]]. He arrived to [[Caspia]] as part of [[Dergan Roj|Dergan Roj's]] [[Barren Sector Fleet|fleet]], in the capacity of a trusted friend and advisor to the famous admiral, in addition to serving as '''Ship's Surgeon''' aboard the [[Star Destroyer]] ''[[VeSD Repulse|Repulse]]''.

=== An Inquisitive Mind ===


=== In the Service of the Republic ===
[[:starwars:14 BBY|14 BBY]] found ''Doctor'' Trissard his first commission with ___________ Sector Fleet. Through luck of the draw (and what some, even he, would say was pre-ordainment) he took station aboard the Victory-class Star Destroyer HIMS ''Colossus'', and immediately made his acquaintance with Captain [[Dergan Roj]]. The two struck as fast friends from the start, moreso because of similar philosophical and cultural beliefs.

Later the following year, he accompanied Roj as part of a cluster of officers taking transfer to and command of the ''Repulse''.

=== Sound Advice ===


=== Creating a Union ===
As the other half of the Assembly's creation team, Trissard took the first rotation at Senator on behalf of the Mrankayla region, which was set up as the official residence of Roj's fleet members. He served one year exactly, then recluded himself back to his medical practice and research. He resides quietly somewhere in [[Plaxton]], but has been seen among the [[Sarian]]s in [[Trinumvira]] and [[Orleanon]].


In honour of his extrodinary accomplishments and devoted service, the [[Senator-class Frigate]] ''Orton Trissard'' (FFG-1041) is named for him.

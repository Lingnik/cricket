An [[Oversector]] was a division of [[The Galaxy|galactic]] territory utilized by the [[Galactic Empire]], comprised of many [[Sector]]s. A [[Grand Moff]] led an Oversector, appointed personally by the [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]]. A Grand Moff superseded the authority of his constituent [[Moff|Moffs]], planetary [[Governor|governors]], and other local authorities as a means to further combat the growing [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic|Rebellion]] and other criminals, whom had been able to escape imprisonment by escaping into another Sector where a Moff was unable to deploy his forces to. Thus, Grand Moffs were created, at [[:starwars:Wilhuff_Tarkin/Legends|Wilhuff Tarkin]]'s recommendation, to use every resource to combat the Rebellion. 

At the Empire's height, there were many Oversectors. However, with the utter collapse of the Imperial state in [[4 ABY]], followed by years of civil war and territorial loss, the number of Oversectors dwindled vastly. With the reorganization of the Empire under [[Bacharan Valak]] and later [[Aleister Vadim]], the number of Oversectors is few, ranging anywhere from three to a dozen. This was done as a means to compensate for the vast loss in military forces and men worthy of the prestigious title.

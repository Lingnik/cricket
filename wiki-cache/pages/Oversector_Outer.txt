'''Oversector Outer''' was an [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[Oversector]] containing most of the sectors and regions in the [[Outer Rim]], including [[:starwars:Seswenna_sector/Legends|Seswenna Sector]] and the [[:starwars:Velcar_Free_Commerce_Zone|Velcar Free Commerce Zone]]. The Oversector was assigned to [[Grand Moff]] [[:starwars:Wilhuff Tarkin/Legends|Wilhuff Tarkin]] and, after Tarkin's death, to Grand Moff [[:starwars:Ardus Kaine|Ardus Kaine]]. After the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]], Kaine maintained control over much of what had been Oversector Outer and transformed it into his own small empire for nearly two years, until Kaine was disposed of by rival [[warlord]]s and campaigns initiated by [[Bacharan Valak]], which destroyed his empire and broke up the territory into various camps. 

By [[16 ABY]], large sections of what had once been Oversector Outer had been transformed into a new Oversector under the revitalized Galactic Empire, although it is significantly smaller than its predecessor, large sections of space still under [[New Republic]] control or free from galactic dominance.

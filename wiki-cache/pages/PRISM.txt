The '''Pr'''otective '''I'''nvestigation and '''S'''ecurity (under-)'''M'''inistry, or '''PRISM''' is an agency within the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]] tasked with providing security and protection to Union assets while home or abroad, as well as performing investigative actions for matters concerning said assets 

They frequently operate alone, but may be in teams of two to four. They are both expert and senior &mdash; they do not have ranks or strata. They are bound to the Crest itself, acting as agents and extensions of it. Dispatched to provide security for representatives of the [[Union Assembly|Assembly]], ambassadors and diplomats of the [[Caspian Foreign Ministry|DDFA]], and the [[Presav]] him/herself, they do far more than just act as a strongarm. They work in advance of the person they're assigned to, schedule travel itinerary, direct and coordinate available military resources, and, at times, act in lieu of the representative.

In addition to protection, since they are an extension of the Crest, they act as an investigative and law enforcement group, neutralizing threats to Caspia's sovereignty. They are empowered to call upon and direct [[Union Marines|Marines]], [[Provincial Army of the Union|Provincials]], or [[Constable]]s when needed, becoming the only governmental entity so able to do so. They are not able to retain forces for themselves, but instead, utilize them as they see fit to best safeguard the Crest.

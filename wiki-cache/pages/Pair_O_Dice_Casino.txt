The '''Pair O'Dice''' '''Casino''' is one of the largest casinos of Cloud City. It is located in the Yarith Bespin hotel complex on Level 30. The annual Cloud City Sabacc Tournament takes place here as do many of the [[Sabacc Galactic Circuit]] games.

It is currently run by: [[Xand Velo]]

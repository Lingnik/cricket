'''Palarain M. Todeth IV''' was a [[Major]] in the [[Imperial Army]]'s armored corps and commander of an [[:starwars:All Terrain Armored Transport/Legends|AT-AT]] walker. Stationed aboard [[I2SD Tormentor|HIMS ''Tormentor'']], Todeth developed a close camaraderie with the [[Black Stars]] squadron, an event which would lead him to join the [[Neo-Imperial Movement]] in [[13 ABY]]. 

After the [[War of the Throne]] ended, he rejoined the Galactic Empire and served throughout the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]].

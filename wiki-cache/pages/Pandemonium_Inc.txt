'''Pandemonium Inc''' was established in approximately [[11 ABY]] by Bothan entrepreneur [[Angellis Card'nel]] primarily as a freight and shipping concern.  Soon it developed into an information network, security firm and later a front to a para-military organisation.  At one point it took on the form of [[Bothan Federated Trading]] during the heightened activities of the [[Bothan Federation]] under the guidance of both Card’nell and [[Bael]].  With strong [[Bothan]] connections it soon diverged from this legal operation into a diverse and politically funded independent mercenary group known as the [[Loose Cannons]].  At this point Bael split his investments and financed his separate operation on [[Nar Shaddaa]] for further business dealings.  This branch later became known as [[Infinity Tech]] and has long been established as the premiere independent starship workshop in the galaxy.

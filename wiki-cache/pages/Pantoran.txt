'''Pantorans''' are a blue-skinned [[Near Human]] species from [[:starwars:Pantora/Legends|Pantora]], a moon of [[:starwars:Orto Plutonia/Legends|Orto Plutonia]], the homeworld of the [[:starwars:Talz/Legends|Talz]].

== Biology and Appearance ==

Pantorans largely resemble [[Human|Humans]] physically. The most notable difference is blue skin, which turns indigo when they blush and yellow eyes. Some Pantorans have black  or blue coloured eyes, though this is rare. Pantoran hair is usually black, blue, or purple, and occasionally white. 

They are similar in appearance to [[:starwars:Wroonian/Legends|Wroonians]] and sometimes are mistaken as such. There is speculation that Pantorans may in fact be an offshoot of Wroonians. Meanwhile [[:starwars:Chiss/Legends|Chiss]] are occasionally mistaken as Pantorans. Blue-skinned [[:starwars:Mirialan/Legends|Mirialans]] are often confused with Pantorans as well.

Pantorans often have gold coloured facial tattoos. These tattoos are generally received in childhood as mark of clan or family association and are based on ancient Pantoran texts. In recent years, applying pink or red coloured makeup to parts of the face has become a popular trend.

Pantorans are also known to have greater tolerance for cold temperatures, requiring no special insulation in environments Humans would consider extreme.

== Pantorans in the Galaxy ==

The planet [[Caspar]] is known to have a sizeable enclave of Pantorans. Most prominent in this community is the Eleria family who were the owners of the Eleria Nut Company prior to the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]]. [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] Minister of Culture and [[Union Council|Councillor]] for [[Noques Rasala]] [[Andra Eleria]] is a member of this family. Her niece [[Veruca Violet]] is the Chief Operating Officer of both [[Biscuit Baron]] and the [[Silverwood House of Fashion]].

== OOC Notes ==

For the purposes of game mechanics a player wishing to have a Pantoran character would choose [[Near Human]] during character generation.

'''Paris Sieara''' was the CEO of [[Reyon Mining]] and after a merger with the major shipwright, [[Kuat Drive Yards-Sienar Fleet Systems|KDY-SFS]], the CEO of KDYSFS. She was the ''de facto'' owner of the world of [[Izin]], and granted custody of the world over to [[Scaven Marx]].

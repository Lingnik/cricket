'''Philinda Jorgan''' ([[27 BBY]] - ) is an [[Epicanthix]] politician and spy master from Charon on [[Mergansar]] which she represents in the [[Union Council]]. She was a [[Senator of the Union|Senator]] in the [[Union Assembly]] representing the Dusona region of Mergansar at the time of the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] and Mergani [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]]. As part of [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] efforts to [[Report:Mergani Subjugation Posts|subjugate]] Mergansar, Jorgan served as [[Governor|Military Governor]]. Following the end of military rule she was made Caspian Intelligence Minister, giving her the role as Director of the [[Caspian Intelligence Service]]. She also serves as the Deputy Leader of the [[Lijsttrekker]].

== Early Life ==

Philinda Jorgan was born in the city of Charon on [[Mergansar]] a few years before the start of the Clone Wars. Little is publicly known about her family background. Her parents and younger brother died under mysterious circumstances when she as was a teenager.

Eager to leave Mergansar as soon as she was of age Jorgan managed to secure an appointment to the [[Imperial Academy]] on [[:starwars:Prefsbelt IV/Legends|Prefsbelt IV]] in spite of her lack of family connexions and [[Near Human]] pedigree. Following the completion of her training at the academy she was recruited by the [[Imperial Security Bureau]]. Her ruthless character would allow her to advance in the ISB in spite of the fact she was not a baseline [[Human]].

== Return Home ==

[[File:Philinda Jorgan Agent.jpg|left|thumb]] Following the [[Galactic Empire|Empire's]] defeat at the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]] and the formation of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], Jorgan returned home to Mergansar. Ideologically she had felt comfortable enough in the Empire, but she had grown bored of her work in the ISB. The CDU, as a new power, represented something of a challenge to her she became one of the first [[PRISM|Praetors]]. Her service as a Praetor had a two-fold function. Not only as she able to utilise the skills she had developed in Imperial service, albeit with a much tighter leash, but she was also able to forge political connexions as she worked closely in protecting members of the [[Union Assembly]].

Jorgan remained a Praetor for over a decade and a half. In her final assignment in [[21 ABY]] she led the security arrangements around events surrounding the [[Ixin|Ixin's]] admission into the Union. With the subsequent retirement of one of the Delegates representing Charon, Jorgan was easily able secure election to replace them.

== In Politics ==

Having preexisting connexions with most of her colleagues in the Union Assembly helped Jorgan gain plum committee assignments, particularly related to security and defence. She was just far enough outside of the centre of power to not be expected to resign following the exposure of the [[2015 Caspar Plot|plot]] to supply the [[New Republic|Republic]] weapons in violation of the [[CDU Nonaggression Treaty]] which brought down the government of [[Presav]] [[Saiane Caitrean]] in [[24 ABY]]. She was, nevertheless, complicit in the arrangement. Having avoided being tied to the scandal she was able to secure election as [[Senator of the Union|Senator]] of the Dusona region following the resignation of her predecessor.

As a Senate, Jorgan served in the cabinet of the new Presav [[Kilos Sabosulmis]] as Minister of Security. This role gave her oversight over the Protective Investigation and Security Under-Ministry, also known as PRISM, which was the rebranded successor to the Praetors. She also worked closely with [[Sar Admiral]] [[Odie Hood]] who became Director of the [[Naval Intelligence Office]] in the aftermath of the scandal which toppled the last government.

At the time the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] hit and the Mergani [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]] began, Jorgan was on a secret mission on Ixin with Sar Admiral Hood. They were incommunicado for some time, and by the time they became aware of what was going on there was little to be done. In his absence, Sar Admiral [[Prae Kichakressa]] and [[Caspian Navy]] were allowing the pogroms to occur almost uncontested. Feeling their cause was lost and with no records indicating they were not on [[Caspar]] at the time, the two would fake their deaths with members of the Naval Intelligence Office destroying the records of their mission and sinking Odie's yacht to suggest his death.

== Return ==

The two erstwhile members of the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]] kept a low profile on Ixin, hiding out amongst [[The Chevalier]] and waiting to see how this situation developed back in the [[Caspia|Caspian System]]. The emergence of a new government on Caspar led by [[Crestian Tarasar]], which had taken a strong stance against the [[Olumi Osahn]] administration leading Mergansar gave them hope that circumstances were now right to take back their homeworld from the cultists. Once Ixin rejoined the new [[Caspian Union]], Jorgan would make her continued existence known to the [[Caspian Intelligence Service]].

Following the Dusona continent's liberation and return to the Caspian Union, Jorgan ran for Charon's seat on the [[Union Council]] representing the Ixin based party [[Lijsttrekker]]. She entered the race rather late, with her survival not publically known prior. Both [[Praecet]] and the [[Legacy]] party had failed to find good candidates, and both withdrew from the race after Jorgan entered it. Jorgan would ultimately win in a landslide taking 83 percent of the vote with the [[Ulmesar]] and [[Holicet]] candidates taking 14 and 3 percent respectively.

[[File:Philinda Jorgan 2.jpg|thumb]] Jorgan immediately court contraversy in her new role. Shortly before her election, the starport in Hebron was destroyed by an earthquake. While Jorgan was expected to introduce a bill to finance its reconstruction she instead put forth one to expand the facilities in Charon. The bill caught many Councillors by surprised and was derided by some as opportunistic. After the anger and shock subsided, the bill would ultimately find support to pass, with Charon's expanded starport ultimately rivaling [[Flude Cauldron|Union Starport]] in size.

Following the election Toresta, Councillor Hood of Ithaca, who also served as Mergansar's [[Governor|Military Governor]], introduced legislation to allow elections in the remaining Mergani cities. Following this, Hood resigned as Military Governor to return to active naval service with Jorgan named to the post in his place.

Following the elections for the final four seats, Jorgan introduced a bill to abolish the office of Military Governor, which passed unanimously. She was subsequently appointed by Presav Tarasar to the position of Minister of Intelligence. In that role she chairs the Intelligence Directorate, the committee which oversees the [[Caspian Intelligence Service]], and serves as the principle day to day leader of the organisation.

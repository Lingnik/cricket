TITLE: Plexifabrin
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2006-08-07T18:12:45Z
======================================================================
A transparent material on par with [[transparisteel]] in terms of clarity, but not as strong, and nowhere near as expensive or heavy. '''Plexifabrin''' could be tinted to any range of hues, and could even be opaqued. Because of its lightweight properties, it was often used in interior construction or on civilian vehicles.

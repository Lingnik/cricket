'''Port Town''' is the shadiest and least tourist-friendly location in Cloud City. While many of its residents are still wealthy, most of their profit comes from smuggling, illicit gambling ventures, and other criminal activities.

Port Town is built amongst the dilapidated warehouses and old trade bays that were originally a major part of Bespin's galactic trade.

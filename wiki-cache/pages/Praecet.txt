'''Praecet''', [[Sarian (language)|Sarian]] for Guard Group, is the political party formed by [[Crestian Tarasar]] and [[Zaalbacca]] to contest the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] [[Report:Caspian Election Posts|elections]] for a permanent [[Union Council]] in [[29 ABY]]. They won seven of the nine seats, but chose to form a coalition with [[Legacy]] party in spite of their majority. Subsequent additions to the Council as other worlds joined or rejoined the Union see Praecet holding seventeen of thirty-nine seats still making it the largest party. The all party coalition has grown to include [[Holicet]] and [[Lijsttrekker]] as well.

== Background ==

Following the dissolution of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] following the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] and Mergani [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|progoms]] [[Caspar]] was left in a shambles. Not only was a massive amount of work necessary to support the survivors of the pandemic and the refugees from [[Mergansar]] who had settled, but a new government needed to be established. The new [[Caspian Union|Caspar Union]] was a rump state of what was left after other worlds declare independence, leaving the only Caspian worlds with any significant population [[Mikassa]], [[Kichnar Orbital Station]], and Caspar itself. As the old Union had been conceived around its multi-world membership, the new Union had to do more than simply fill vacated posts, a new government model itself needed to be agreed upon.

A Provisional Assembly was soon organised with representatives from Caspar's seven major cities. The representatives were largely a mix of retired members of the [[Union Assembly]], local leaders, academics, and some representatives from the refugee community. Notably, none of those later elected to permanent [[Union Council]] served in the Provisional Assembly. In many cases, those with the most power and influence were too busy actively directing rebuilding efforts to spend time arguing over a new constitution. In the case of future [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]], he realised that the controversy surrounding his efforts in fighting against the Mergani pogroms which ultimately saw him [[Mergansar Incident|fighting]] against the [[Caspian Navy]] would be too much of a distraction, and he instead allowed surrogates to advance his views in the Assembly.

There had been no political parties in the old Caspian Democratic Union, but distinct camps did begin to form within the Provisional Assembly which would subsequently give rise to the political parties that would ultimately contest the election once the new constitution was agreed on and ratified. Tarasar and [[Zaalbacca]] were the most organised individuals directing the work of the Assembly behind the scenes, with 40% of its members joining Praecet. This gave them a considerable influence over the final draft of the new constitution.

Tarasar, Zaalbacca, and their allies had certain key goals they worked to put into the constitution. They wanted a unicameral parliament with a first-past-the-post voting system, knowing they could win a plurality in the majority of the seats but no necessarily command a majority should representation be proportional. Getting Mikassa full political representation was also a key priority, particularly because it now had a very significant refugee community which they could count on for support. They strongly opposed any recognition of the government on Mergansar viewing its expulsion of its own citizens during the pogroms to be a terrorist act that could not be condoned by official recognition. Tarasar and Zaalbacca also had their proxies attempt to get the age for voting and holding office down to fourteen years old.

In the end the representative from Praecet dropped their objections to the recognition of the Mergani government and it being granted a portion of the old Caspian Navy, knowing full well that once in office they would take measures that would negate the effect of this action. With Praecet giving ground on the Mergani question and compromising on sixteen rather than fourteen as the age for voting and holding office, they were able to get sufficient support for ensuring the representation model they favoured including full membership of Kichnar Orbital Station and Mikassa in the new Union, which had not been universally favoured.

== Candidates ==

* '''[[Zaalbacca]]''' for [[Anchorron]] - The Deputy Leader of Praecet and Chairman of the [[Wookiee Workers Union of Anchorron]]. He was elected with 54% of the vote. He is now Caspian Minister of Industry and Labour.
* '''Ward Sax''' for [[Dunwelton]] - The head of an old aristocratic family, the party did not expect to win the seat, but they felt his name recognition and standing gave them the best shot. He came in second place with 40% of the vote.
* '''Lucas Binks''' for [[Groton]] - Related to the [[:starwars:Naboo/Legends|Naboo]] [[Senator]] [[:starwars:Jar Jar Binks/Legends|Jar Jar Binks]]. Praecet did not expect to have any chance at winning the seat and did not invest a great deal of effort contesting the seat. He came in second place with 33% of the vote.
* '''[[Sirlahn Inglet]]''' for [[Kichnar Orbital Station|Kichnar]] - A senior engineer at [[SubPro|Sublight Products Corporation]]. He was elected with 54% of the vote. He is now Caspian Minister of Science and Technology and Chief Executive Officer at SubPro.
* '''Lilabet Bint''' for [[Mikassa]] - An entertainer by profession. She was elected with 53% of the vote.
* '''[[Andra Eleria]]''' for [[Noques Rasala]] - A spiritual leader by profession, this seat was the most strongly contested with Praecet unsure of their ability to win it. She was ultimately elected with 40% of the vote. She is now Caspian Minister of Culture.
* '''[[Sha Dark]]''' for [[Orleanon]] - A veteran of [[Casulmis]] and leader in the Mergani refugee community originally from Goshen. She was elected with 50% of the vote. She is now the Caspian Minister of Justice
* '''Erl Morgaan''' for [[Plaxton]] - A [[Constable]] by profession, initial polling indicated a tighter race than proved to be the case. He was elected with 50% of the vote. He is now Caspian Minister of the Constabulary.
* '''[[Crestian Tarasar]]''' for [[Trinumvira]] - Leader of Praecet and Executive Director of [[The Mission]]. He was elected with 58% of the vote. He is now [[Presav]] and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defence.

== Platform ==

As can be expected from a party whose name effectively translates as the Guardian party, Praecet's platform is centred around security policies. Particularly with the later revelation that the Sariphage pandemic has been engineered by [[Basca Starker]] at the instigation of [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Aleister Vadim]], Praecet believes the Union remains under constant threat. Praecet has also been extremely critical of how past Caspian administrations have responded to galactic politics and threats to the Union. In specific the Praecet feels:

* [[Presav|Interim Presav]] [[Thomas Mahon|Thomas Mahon's]] policy of neutrality in galactic affairs led to the gradual weakening of the Union's ability to protect itself.
* The Union's inability to properly contest the blockade and occupation during the arms supplying [[2015 Caspar Plot|crisis]] in [[24 ABY]] represented a complete failure by the [[Caspian Defence Force]] that would not have been the case in earlier years when the [[Caspian Navy]] was in a stronger position.
* The complete failure of the [[Groton Sciences Institute]] to be able to combat the Sariphage pandemic represents an unacceptable inadequacy in the Caspian scientific community.
* The nihilistic approach of [[Daln Bael]] of GSI to the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] and the acquiescence by the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]] to this view represented a complete abandoned of Caspian values.
* The Mergani [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]] being uncontested by the Caspian Defence Force or any other member world of the Union represents a complete abandoned of morality.

To combat these problems Praecet has undertaken the following policies:

* Entering into a [[Imperium-Caspian Mutual Protection Pact (29 ABY)|Mutual Protection Pact]] with the [[Imperium]] to ensure the Union has allies in future conflicts.
* Reuniting the Caspian Union to include the territory previously controlled by the old [[Caspian Democratic Union|Union]].
* Bringing [[Charros IV]] into the Union and by extension have [[Haor Chall Engineering]] building droids and ships for Union, including new droid and starfighter designs.
* Repatriating [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] under full Union control.
* Repatriating and nationailising [[CDMC|Caspian Design & Manufacturing Corporand]], [[Interspatial Prospecting, Inc.]], and [[SubPro|Sublight Products Corporation]] as a matter of union defence.
* Pulling every decommissioned ship out of mothballs that could still be used, most notably the [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruisers]] which were refitted with [[Xi Charrian]] support.
* Completing unfinished warships whose construction had been halted following the reduction of sovereignty following the occupation in [[24 ABY]].
* Expanding the [[SONG]] network and maintaining bases outside of the Union's declared territory.

In domestic policy Praecet strongly backs labour unions and supports significant public funding for research particularly as it relates to health. As part of their coalition with the other parties, the [[Legacy]] party is allowed to take the lead in domestic policy, with Praecet only advancing measures that have multi-party support.

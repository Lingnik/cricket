'''Prelate''' is the title of the leader of the [[Xi Char]] faith, and by extension leader of the [[Xi Charrian]] people and their homeworld [[Charros IV]]. The position is awarded through a process known as a [[conclave]] where the all the senior clerics of the Xi Char faith meet until the arrive at a universal decision on who to elected to the position. The office is a lifetime appointment.

The Prelate from [[26 ABY]] to [[34 ABY]] was [[r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak]]. Under his tenure the Xi Char regained ownership of their factories and designs from [[Kuat Drive Yards-Sienar Fleet Systems]] and were granted the right from the [[Imperium]] to seceded in order to join the [[Caspian Union]]. r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak presided over the reformation of [[Haor Chall Engineering]]. Following r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak's death in [[34 ABY]] Inquisitor [[stahanak]] was elected to succeed him as Prelate.

The '''Prelate r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak Memorial Art Gallery''' is a located in Xi Xin on [[Charros IV]]. The gallery was established by [[Dean]] [[cadracak-'r'shak]] in [[30 ABY]] during the [[Charros Wargames]]. Its purpose was to expose the [[Xi Charrian]] people to the art of other cultures, with a focus on displaying art that depicted other beings.

The gallery's structure itself eschews Xi Charrian architectural norms, consisting of a glass pyramid set in a park. The initial collection was built in part on loans from other galleries and private collections. [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] for example loaned his collection of [[:starwars:Mandalorian cubism|Mandalorian cubist paintings]] and [[:starwars:Wookiee totem (crafter)|Wookiee totems]]. While the aim was to bring outside art to the Xi Charrians, the gallery also served as tourist draw.

In the years since the gallery has begun to host works of native artists working in offworld inspired mediums, in addition to building a permanent collection offworld works through both donations and purchases made by the gallery itself. Notable works in the gallery now include "The Apotheosis of Cricket" a painting by [[Robot 7]] and "Mutanda United" a wood sculpture by [[Kasha Tabasuri]]. Presav Tarasar's Mandalorian cubist paintings have also remained on semi-permanent loan and are considered a major part of the collection.

Following the passing of [[Prelate]] [[r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak]] the gallery was renamed in his honour, in recognition of his efforts to bring the Xi Charrian people into the wider galactic community.

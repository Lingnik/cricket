[[Sarian]] term for president, or elected leader of the Caspian Union and the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] before that. The Presav serves as the Head of State and Government of the Union. The Presav is elected by the [[Union Council]] from among its members and serves as Chairman of the Council. He or she appoints a cabinet from among other members the council and determines composition of council committees. The Presav is assisted in this role by a Vice Presav who is also appointed by the Union Council from among its and is designated successor to the Presav should the killed, incapacitated, or otherwise unable to perform their duties. The current Presav is [[Crestian Tarasar]], Councillor for [[Trinumvira]].

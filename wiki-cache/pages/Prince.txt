'''Prince''' is a royal title used by various planets and galactic powers. The female variant is '''Princess''', which may either be someone holding the title in their own right, or the wife of a Prince. As a title it has a wide variety of uses. It is most often associated with hereditary monarchies where title of Prince or Princess is utilised for the children (and sometimes additional generations of descendants of a [[King]] or Queen. A Prince or Princess who is in line to inherit the throne may be termed as '''Crown Prince''' or '''Crown Princess'''. In elected monarchies such as on [[:starwars:Naboo/Legends|Naboo]] the title of Prince or Princess may exist for a subordinate office, prior to being elected Queen of Naboo, [[:starwars:Padmé Amidala/Legends|Padmé Amidala]] had been elected as [[:starwars:Princess of Theed|Princess of Theed]].

In some systems of governance, Princes may be the highest level of authority without their being Kings and Queens. On the planet [[Ixin]] all sons of Prince are entitled to the style of Prince themselves. Over time this had led to one fifth of male Ix having the right to the title of prince, leading to the world being nicknamed the "Planet of Princes" though not all of those who have the right to use the title make use of it. Sometimes the title of Prince is combined with another, such as the [[:starwars:Priest-Prince|Priest-Princes]] who rule the planet [[:starwars:Monor II|Monor II]].

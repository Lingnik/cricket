The rank and title of '''Private First Class''' is among the lowest level of an enlisted soldier in a military organization, most frequently used by army and starfighter corps. They are often referred to as "Junior Enlisted".

=== In New Republic Service ===

Along with [[Private]]s and [[Lance Corporal]]s, PFCs form the physical body of the '''[[New Republic GroundOps|New Republic Army]]'''. They are typically young sentients fresh out of basic training and with less than three standard years experience.

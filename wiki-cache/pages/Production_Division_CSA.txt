The '''Production Division''' directs the development of [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector]] resources, deciding how best to exploit every planet, moon, and asteroid in the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Sector]] for the profit of the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Authority]].  All large-scale construction projects are conducted by this division such as establishing mining outposts, factory facilities, warehousing complexes, and retail outlets.  It oversees virtually every step in the manufacture of Authority goods, from the mining and refinement of raw materials to the packaging of assembled products.  Production is the single largest division in the Authority, the pulsing heart of the [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]].

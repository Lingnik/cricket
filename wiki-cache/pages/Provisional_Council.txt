The '''New Republic Provisional Council''' was the body of signatories of the [[Declaration of a New Republic]] whom served as the government of the fledgling [[New Republic]] prior to the formation of the [[New Republic Senate]].  The Council was formed immediately after the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor]] and remained mobile until the conquest of [[Coruscant]] after the [[Battle of Coruscant]] in [[4 ABY]]. Coruscant then became the capital of the New Republic and permanent seat of the New Republic Senate, which was established on [[Restoration Day]].

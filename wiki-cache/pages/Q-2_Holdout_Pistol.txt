Small and cheap. Probably the only real good things about this hold-out 
blaster. Though the shape is smooth and aesthetic, due to restrictions is 
lacks in stamina and power. Though the '''Q-2''' is a handy weapon when in need of 
a quick fix at close range usage in any other conditions will soon discover 
its shortcoming.

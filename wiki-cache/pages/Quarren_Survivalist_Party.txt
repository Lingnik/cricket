The '''Quarren Survivalist Party''' was a collection of like-minded [[Quarren]] who formed an anti-New Republic political party during the [[Dacan Civil War]] of [[16 ABY]].

The Party was primarily fueled and forged in secrecy by [[Korynn Fleming]], a leader in the [[Imperial Security Bureau]], and by [[Eeth Mentel]], a former [[New Republic]] diplomat. Mentel and Fleming worked tirelessly to gather and recruit Quarren rioters, disgruntled by their [[New Republic]] leaders and fearing the extinction of their race.

The Quarren Survivalist Party eventually swore loyalty to the [[Galactic Empire]], when Fleming promised much-needed aide at the Empire's expense.

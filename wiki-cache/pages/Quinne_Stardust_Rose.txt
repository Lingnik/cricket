==Early Life==
Born in the swamplands of Zeltros and self-reared as most of the children of this world are, Quinne was a girl of no particular familial line who ran wild for most of her young life. Surrounded by siblings, cousins, aunts, uncles, and grands of many degrees, none of them really raised her. She learned to take care of herself. To that matter, she proved to be a natural organizer and by the time she was fourteen she fell into show business quite dramatically. Working for one of the thousands of party organizers on Zeltros, the effervescent teenager Deejayed and Emceed night after night until at one function the source files for her music froze and silence fell over the hot sweaty crowed of ravers. Barely dropping the beat, Quinne grabbed a live microphone and started to vocalize. With an improvised series of bluesy trills, she sang her way through the next several minutes until the recorded music sorted itself out and the dance beat resumed. Word of the rosy chanteuse's talents spread throughout the region's music community and it wasn't long until she found herself in a band playing gigs all over Zeltros. At age sixteen, Quinne set her sights on success beyond her homeworld and took off on her first tour, opening for bigger acts before she began headlining at clubs throughout the Outer Rim and beyond.

==Fate or Luck==
Now going by Quinne Stardust Rose, she struggled to gain traction outside the circuit of small venues she'd become a regular at. Quinne's music jobs, while fun and fulfilling, didn't quite pay the bills and salaries associated with a having ones own modest touring act. But one thing she always had going for her was her familiar charm and genuine nature and of course her Zeltron wiles that were rarer away from home. She tried hitching her fortune to a series of bad choices - would be managers or lovers, usually of means - hoping one of her relationships would lead to bigger and better things. She was usually disappointed. Picking pockets was what Zeltron kids learned to do when partying adults got a little drunk or high. Sometimes if the fish was big enough, she'd help herself to her date's credits more efficiently by getting her instrument tech Veebo to slice their accounts. She never took enough that her wealthy marks ever really noticed. Her potential for extracurricular income dried up quickly when Veebo got pinched selling fake chain codes and was never heard from again.

It wasn't long before she fell into opportunity again. Attending an auction on the arms of two very wealthy marks aboard a pleasure cruiser called the ''Nyrrian Spiral''. Unfortunately her dates got into a physical scrap with each other. This was on top of treating her poorly in front of the other buyers, staff, and the owner himself, Nasiri Gejalli. The Falleen had the ruffians ejected and offered Quinne a ride to their next destination. Hours later, Nasiri hired her to sing and hostess as well as organize parties for the ''Spiral''. Of course she accepted.

'''R2-N8''', or '''Nate''', is [[Tyler Damion|Tyler Damion's]] trusty [[:w:c:starwars:R2-series_astromech_droid|R2-series astromech droid]]. This R2-unit has served with Tyler since he joined the [[:w:c:starwars:Rebel_Alliance|Alliance]] several years before the Battle of Yavin. Today the beatup and quirky droid continues to serve his Master despite the change in career paths.

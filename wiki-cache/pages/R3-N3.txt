'''R3-N3''', or more commonly referred to as '''Rene''' (Rennie), is one of the [[:w:c:starwars:R3-series_astromech_droid|R3-series droids]] utilized by [[Caspia|Caspia's]] [[CMTOS-17 Nighthawks|17th ''Nighthawks'']] squadron. Rene is often put into the lead Centurion of the unit, Hunter 1, and has established a good working relationship with [[Major]] [[Doron Rtufo|Rtufo]]. The R3 astromech joins two other R3's, four R4's, two R6's, and two R2's in a squadron devoid of [[NovaCom Cybertronics|NovaCom]] G3-series droids, which were originally intended for use in Caspia's [[Z-100 Centurion|Centurions]]. After NovaCom forsook its Caspian heritage and  moved its governing shares and body to the [[Corporate Sector Authority]], several commanding officers directed their fitters and logistics officers to dump the G3's and obtain astromechs from, in the words of one officer, "a company that has a backbone, and isn't a sellout."

Rene is a white, copper, and slate blue unit, still retaining its clear dome. It has logged well over 400 sorties, and has proven itself a reliable unit in the Caspian military.

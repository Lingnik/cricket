North Starker Boulevard - Plaxton: Caspar

This end of town looks like it's newly refurbished. A large traffic circle directs the ground car traffic at this now busy intersection. The circle itself is full of lush, green grass and slender willowy trees. Dark bushy leaves cover the branches, offering shade for the ground below. Sitting in the middle of the grass is a trolley car. A plaque identifies it as being named Cally, for one of Admiral Roj's crew. Overlooking the area from the north is the Old Clock Tower, frozen at 2:34, one hour before Liberation, and still streaked with blast marks from the conflict of the past. The afternoon sky above is clear.


There was not a lot that Crestian made use of the official Presavadential residence of Castle Rock Manor for. In fact outside of Union Council meetings he spent very little time in the Caspian capital city of Plaxton, far preferring other cities like his home of Trinumvira. But there was no need to pollute it with the delivery that was expected: Doctor Basca Dorian Starker, DEAD. It seemed fitting too that the Castle Rock Manor was on the main drag. The sign declaring it Starker Boulevard had come down but a new name had not been forthcoming. Crestian had been betting on Mergansar being back in the fold before any revelations that would require dropping the Starker name to surface, but then he had not expecting him to have been responsible for the Sariphage.

In spite of the fact that no official bounty had been placed on Starker by the Caspian government there was little surprise among the Union Council that they had been contacted about a body. Whether it was to be believed was another matter, but a spirit of cautious optimism permeated the Crest as a number of Councillors and Admirals loitered outside waiting to see what might go down. Given the security concerns the marine presence was high, at least compared to how it normally was under their government, perhaps more typical of the patrols the Mahon administration favoured. As they wait a certain Wookiee Councillor arrives with antigrav sled bearing a bounty from the nearby Caspar Caff.

The speeder from the starport originated from a transport belonging to a bounty hunter, who pushed the stasis sled along on repulsors. walking along side the hunter and the deceased cargo is a figure dressed in tres sheik corporate fashion, but over this is worn and opera cloak with the upper cape flipped forward to conceal his features beneath the cowl. There is conversation going on between the figure and the hunter. One the duo and cargo arrive before, the cloak bows and places a finely manicured hand on the tube and speaks in a respectful tone. "I bring you justice such as it might be and the profound apologies of the Starkers." there is a moments hesitation. "My intention was to deliver him alive...but the hunter was faced with escape of a well placed blaster bolt."

Crestian takes a deep breath and steps forward himself. A certain nervous feeling came over him, it wasn't entirely an unexpected moment after the recent revelations but it did not come with sort of comfort he might have hoped for. The Caspian leader bowed respectively to cloaked figure and the bounty hunter with him. "It is just as well he was not brought back alive, he no longer had the standing to face trial....Gods know an impartial jury would have been a challenge to say the least."

The Sarian motions forward one of the loitering Councillors, Doctor Prince Zig Al Fidel, Councillor of Feyenoor on Ixin, Minister of Health. The Human male in his 50s steps forward to examine the stasis unit as Crestian continues to talk to the cloaked figure, "You represent his family?"

Marius offers a slight bow and sweeps back the cowl to reveal a face not unlike Basca's own, with subtle differences. "I am in point of fact his brother, I would say older or younger. But our parents never really revealed that...it was a running joke." his hair is far far longer then Basca's and there was a sense of youth that Basca did not have. "Marius Bariss Starker...it was I who posted the bounty, my brother was a clever manipulator." he drums his fingers on the tube. "To say we did not love one another would be an understatement." he turns to the hunter and addresses him. "Your duty has been fulfilled, appreciations." The hunter taps off a salute and steps off to the side to watch the show. "I came cowled for concern that my appearance would cause some confusion."

"It is a wise precaution. The Caspian people are not an unruly lot, but the sheer magnitude of the devastation can quite excite passions, my own included." Crestian turns to Doctor Al Fidel. Holding up his scanning device he remarks, "The subject is deceased and matches the genetic samples we have of Basca Starker." Crestian nods back to the Councillor of Feyenoor before turning back to Marius. "I respect your decision to take this on in the way you have. It is not easy to excise the sins of others, but something I must confess to having some experience with myself." The Caspian leaders thoughts go back to Mergsansar, there was a time Olumi Osahn had been associate with the Sith and it gave him that much more resolves to root out and kill their leaders. "I hope you can find some measure of peace in this, as we all most try to now."

Marius gives a acidic smile. "My brother...was ever the maker of mischief...and not the benign sort...I assume there are those he took down the hoojib hole with him?" he gives the gathering a look over. "He always liked to take the ride with an innocent along side him." his eyes shift back to the Presav. "I would apologize to them and assure them that anything he professed was no doubt a contrived lie to earn their support and faith." he steps aside from the tube. "As for peace...we were not ever close...the only things he took serious was his games and vices."

Crestian smirks, "He was definitely seeking to ingratiate himself here. Thankfully I do not think his recent times here will leave much of an impact, though I suppose the staff and locals he interacted with were probably quite disturbed when the truth came out. His lack of moral compass was something I learned about him quite early on, though I never imagined quite the depths of it all. But that hoojib hole you speak of did at least trap one poor soul. A regrettable association that advanced far too fast for the opportunity to provide council in opposition to present itself."

Marius raises a brow and spares a acid look at the tube. "Who and where can I find them...I hope it was not a relation to you!" he reaches up and removes the tiny glare shades and tucks them in the pocket of his vest. "I would speak with whomever...though, I pray they did not fall in a way that ended them?" deep concern colors his voice. "Is there another family I need to apologize to?"

"No relation to me...." The Presav looks to the spectators of the Crest, "Zaal I think I could use one of the caffs now, and might you take the artefact away for safe keeping." The Wookiee Councillor nods and hands a cup of caff to Crestian before starting to direct the stasis tube inside with Doctor Al Fidel, most of the rest of the spectators following them inside or withdrawing to else where though a few including Councillor Dark and Crestian's Droid adjutant Commander V1C3 stay around. The Vice Presav meanwhile begins ordering the dismissal of various added security elements.

Moving closer to Marius, Crestian remarks, "My sense of decorum would have me keep their name quiet lest they wish to reach out to you....but....it would not take any great investigation to determine that your late brother spent much of his last weeks in the company of Jedi Master Jessalyn Valios. A bizarre pairing for sure but I suppose she wanted to see some good in him....even if it wasn't there. She is a well meaning individual and I think it shows just how disturbingly skilled he was to have gotten his hooks into her."

Marius leans in after the assurance it was no relation, but the surprise at the name and then the utter look of anger on his face cannot be hidden. "Jedi Master...red hair?" his eyes zero in on the Presav and then he tosses the departing tube an look that could burn worlds. "Valios...red hair tall, leggy?" there is a sudden great deal of concern on his face. "That fucking bastard." he growls not quite to himself.

Crestian nods and sighs, "That would be the one. I am not entirely sure how she of all people could not see through his facade. But by the time I became aware of it there was no chance to try to pull her aside and provide her some warning, though I am not sure how she would have taken a warning of someone else's moral depravity coming from me. Do you know her?"

Marius looks to the sky with a heavy sigh. "Yes, many years ago, on a world called Isada." he explains. "We had dinner...she was looking for student?" he chuckles. "How one finds Jedi...is a bit lost on me to be honest...but she was charming and we spent some time together before we went our ways." he turns a world glassing glare in the direction the tube went. "I mentioned it to Basca the very last time we met...a few years back, not as far as Isada." he is clearly trying to center himself. "He paid it no mind...clearly I was wrong." his head turns slightly as if he had made an error he could not correct. "He...enjoyed linking his mischief together for maximum fun as he like to view it."

Looking as the tube slips into the manor Crestian remarks, "If you need to get a few punches in before he goes into carbonite let me know. I had some mind to have him vaporised but I given the magnitude of the hurt I don't want to anything that might spark conspiracy theories that he is still out there. The fact his master still exists is bad enough. One day we will need to make proper memorial and museum to all of this. Though we aren't quite there yet. The one genocide did not create the hate that led to the pogroms on our sister world but it gave it room to grow and fester. It is still an on going struggle. But hopefully one we are getting over close to turning the page on."

Marius composes himself. "I hope to hell he did not pass himself off as me!" and a shake of the head. "No...do as you will with him...I don't care." he assures the presav. "But...now that I know Jess was involved, I need to find her and set things straight." he offers another abbreviated bow. "Again, deepest apologies to you and yours and those you watch over...its a pale offering in the face of such loss. But it is all I have to offer." he pauses for a moment. "If ever I can be or service...please."

Crestian turns for a moment to the Zeltron Councillor observing from a polite distance. He shares a brief glance with her before looking back to Marius and bowing back to him, "What justice there was to be found in this moment has been done and the Union thank you for it. Should you find yourself in need of retracing his steps on Caspar as it were we can arrange a proper escort to ensure your safety. You have our sympathies for this added complication."

Yarith Hotel Lobby -- Cloud City

The Yarith is the most high-class hotel in all of Cloud City. Frequented by dignitaries and celebrities, and renowned for its amenities, there are no other hotels in the galaxy like it. Escalators bring patrons up to the front desk of the expansive and high-ceilinged lobby. Everything here is beautifully crafted and designed.

From the classic waterfall curtains, lush sapphire and gold carpet to the expansive blue-black tinted windows revealing the cityscape outside, the Yarith is unparalleled in luxury travel accommodations. Busy at all hours, the lobby hosts a number of posh services and small shops, including a caffe shop, the open-area Yarith Lobby Bar, and a buffet area serving complimentary breakfasts for every timezone. All of this is centered around a large circle of black marble flooring inlaid with he hotel's logo in gold 

Trasparasteel turbolifts run up and down in a bank of three along the walls, speeding guests to their five star apartment suites above. A rooftop bar with several swimming pools can also be reached via the lifts. The grand ballroom can be seen to the north of the lifts, while the famous Pair O'Dice casino can be entered to the east.

BESPIN CULTURAL FESTIVAL - The lobby ceiling is filled with flags of cultures from across the galaxy exhibiting at the festival. Service droids and staff members of various species stand ready to assist guests and visitors alike with any questions they may have about Bespin's premiere festival event.

Yarith Hotel Ballroom -- Cloud City

Bright jewel toned lights of sapphire, emerald, ruby, and gold illuminate this gigantic ballroom and its patrons. To the north stands the Yarith's beautiful stage, reserved for the most talented musicians in the galaxy, fundraisers, award shows and live theatre performances including the popular Bespin Burlesque shows throughout the year. The beautifully appointed stage is wide and designed for large scale productions and events. A heavy royal purple velvet curtain hangs, tied ornately with thick gold braid. The horizontal ruffles of the curtain give it the illusion of a flowing waterfall like many other curtains throughout the hotel. Everything in this ballroom is of the most elegant materials, with state of the art technology, and the hotel has spared no expense in offering luxury to its patrons.

The space is flexible to accommodate various functions. Standard seating is in rows facing the stage while banquet set-ups consist of round dining tables and a raised head-table on either side of the room. A dance floor can be added anywhere suitable upon request. At the back of the room are several air-walls that can slide into place to divide part of the room into smaller meeting rooms

BESPIN CULTURAL FESTIVAL - The ballroom is configured for dancing in the center that extends to the stage with tables and seating around the outside of the room. Portable bars are in place at several stations that include buffet foods. Flags and banners adorn the walls, lit precisely adding even more color to the room.

Though it is not the capital city of Charros IV, Xi Can is its largest city with a population of roughly thirty million. The architecture is culturally distinct with nearly every building cylindrical in shape. The exact designs vary from there, in some cases the base is more ovular. Some have the same dimensions at the base and top, others taper into a conical shape towards the apex, still others are effectively more conical than cylindrical in their entirety.

Most of the buildings are either brown, grey, or red in colour, with a smaller number in black or blue. The newer constructions can be denoted by their advanced height and their metallic sheen. Older constructions tend to be shorter and more dull in appearance, with the oldest buildings being ceramic. The other distinctive feature of the buildings is the fact that the access from one storey to the next mostly occurs through ramps that wrap around the exteriors of the buildings, albeit at higher elevation these appear to give way to interior based access.

In the centre of the city is a large lake roughly fifty kilometres square in area. In the centre is an island roughly twenty kilometres in size. The island contains the Cathedral Factory of Xi Can which takes up much of the island. The central structure differs from the rest of the buildings in the city. A massive rectangular structure with a compound dome. This main structure is surrounded by scores of cylindrical towers similar to those in the city. The colouration varies in shade from bright silver to dull grey, with the distinct look of having not been constructed all at once.

The space surrounding the cathedral factory is a mix of gardens and landing pads. The gardens contain a variety of indigenous flowers and trees and are expertly up kept by specially designed droids. The landing pads seem to mostly serve the purposes of industrial transport, ferrying raw materials to the factory and taking finished products to the starport. Four bridges connect to the island, precisely at ninety degree angles from each other, these serve as the main personal access point for the Xi Charrians themselves with trams running across each bridge to the main city.

On a plateau southwest of the main city is the Xi Can Starport, the planet's largest spaceport. Nearby is Xi Can Base which is the largest Caspian Defence Force facility on the planet. Most of the city's non-Xi Charrian residents live and work in this part of the city, largely employed in the field of defence, manufacturing, and transportation. The architecture here fits more standard patterns of traditional Human designs.

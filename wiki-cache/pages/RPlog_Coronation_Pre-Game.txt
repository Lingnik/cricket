Imperator Circle - Imperial City: Selene

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Imperator Circle rests in the center of the city in the shadows of the towering cold black spacescrapers around it. Looking up causes somewhat of a vertigo effect as the tops of the towers disappear in the heights above, standing thousands of stories high and reaching into space itself. The circle is in name only as the general outline of the area is indeed a square, it is the most open section in the center of the city often referred to as the 'circus.' The area is known to host open air markets, and other events for the populations of the Imperial City. In the very center stands a statue heralding in the Damion Dynasty with the new Imperium flags hoisted at its four corners.<br>


&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Selene's security is tight, but cleverly hidden behind the festivities of a planetary wide celebration. The city vibrates with massive block parties and music each district and square blasting and enjoying their own cultural and form of celebration. The Neon District, likely the most impressive with millions in the streets as colorful liquids splash and spray down the streets making a wet, hypnotic, dance rave scene complete with fog and projection events.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In orbit, a near countless number of vessels have arrived in orbit from the Imperium's Imperator Task Force, a fleet of Hapan ships including Nova Battlecruisers and Battle Dragons, and the arriving Caspian Union ships for the scheduled war games that would happen later in the week. Several ships firing off space-like fireworks and blooms of colored powders to celebrate the events occurring on the world.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Imperator Circle was no exception to the festivities. The coronation dais was left empty but nearby stages feature duelling Bith bands filling the air with their jazz-like mixtures. Server droids and bars were functioning in the streets as the crowds mingle, move, dance, and flow through the planet-wide extravaganza.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Closer to the center of the square a roped area is established with a more visible security detail. Within the small area were a number of high ranking officials in Imperium Dress Uniform relax over cocktails discussing politics with the visiting Hapans, among them Drayson Honos stands in his Grand Admiral's uniform projecting his normal aura of dignity and classiness. The Imperator and the Queen Mother were no where to be seen, likely finalizing their negotiations in the nearby Government building.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It had been literal years since Axel Vichten had been to anywhere as populated as Selene. Years of isolation, quiet, and torment. How he even found himself drawn here was anyone's guess and he didn't even know himself. Still though, the old pilot, smuggler, and general nuisance had found himself here, weaving through crowds. The noises, the lights, the people, everything so different than the years he spent in isolation. To say he was on edge would be an understatement. Oh, how the mighty have fallen.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Wearing little more than peasant robes, a hood pulled up over his head, his face mostly concealed in shadows, Axel finds himself drawn towards the center of the square, staying a few meters away from the roped off area. A metallic left hand holds a flute of sparkling liquid, the contents untouched since he'd unconsciously grabbed it from a passing server. The man used to live for such festivities, now they were an ancient memory and almost alien. Still, his body reacted where his mind did not, and soon, the robed man was swaying a bit almost to the rhythm of the music being played by one of the nearby bands. (speaking in Basic)<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Within the cordoned off area, Grand Moff Elana Tracer is checking last minute details on a datapad as well as addressing various issues with vendors and suppliers alike. From the celebrations' raucous din throughout the square everything seems to be going smoothly as things Elana does tends to do.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A young man, taller than the Moff but of a similar countenance comes into the secured area. With a dark look in his bright blue eyes, Captain Kipran Jawatha paces close to the dais. He stops his pacing and tries to loosen the collar of his shirt. He looks nervously towards the Government building. "Mother," he says to Tracer. "Have you seen her?"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"No Kipran. Not here, not yet. Tonight is pre-function only," she replies. "Sonja won't be appearing officially until the ceremony tomorrow. Didn't she tell you?" She looks up at her eldest son with sympathy. "Everything will go back to normal after this week. Then..." she says pointedly. "Then you can move forward."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Unlike his fellow Moffs, Moff Tarasar a.k.a. Caspian Union Presav Crestian Tarasar was not one to normally travel with a larger security entourage. Recent violence against Caspian allies made him rethink this methodology on this visit, besides what project the image of Caspian independence better than the forest green uniforms of Caspian Marines? The detail expressed the wide range of species diversity found in the Caspian Union as did the details following the other members of the Caspian delegation. Two members of Crestian's detail stand out. A Sarian General wearing a similar uniform to the others but with the jacket in leather and a Droid Colonel who also wears the marine uniform.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Crestian for his part is dressed in a formal suit of sorts, but still firmly entrenched in his Kubazian fashion tastes. With him is his daughter Atsvara wearing the same outfit she did to the Jedi ball the previous years, a green Tomoun-cloth gown with a black and white harlequin print embroidered down the centre and on the sleeves and beryllius tiara with Kubaz Xirconia. Though Crestian had brought Atsvara to Selene before, the atmosphere was quite different today and so for the moment the Caspian leader seems focused on allowing her to dictate the path of their walkabout. After all he had six other Councillors mulling about who could engage in more formal meetings and greetings with their Imperium and Hapan peers.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The music sooths over the area as the pitched battle of the bands begins to slow into a soft background music and the street lighting and scenery softens to provide a more social atmosphere. Drayson examines his own datapad before noticing Tracer and giving the Grand Moff a smile and a nod as well as one for her son. The older man navigates over to the pair through the wall of VIPs and Moffs all standing around doing their proverbial measurements of the scope and success of their sectors in implementing the Imperium's directives.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Grand Admiral speaks, "Nothing like a celebration to remember how much I prefer the silent efficiency of the bridge of a starship." He muses waving for a server to bring the small cadre drinks. His ice-blue eyes look over reports and statuses, "It appears Tarasar has arrived on world as well. Though he is occupied elsewhere in the circle." He says looking at another report, "Oh and-" he trails off, "-interesting." No one arrived on world without the Grand Admiral knowing about it, and a former NSO Thane appearing would not go unnoticed by his own security and advisory groups. He takes a look at the image transmitted to his datapad and goes silent for a moment before turning to an aid, "Inform the Imperator, the Imperial city might be haunted, I think I have seen a ghost."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Drayson composes himself and turns to smile at Tracer, "I can leave this sector in your capable hands, I believe. I will need to begin making preparations with the Caspian and Hapan naval commands for our exercise."<br>

His drink still untouched, his legs still unconsciously swaying to the beat of the music... mostly to the tune at least, for he was critically caucasian.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The man does no mingling, and because he looked like a pauper, people tended to give him a wide berth, not wanting to engage with someone who might ask them of their credits, or if they've found their gods. No, for the time being, Axel was content to observe, and unaware that anyone would remember him or recognize him as who he was, at least not yet. Within a week's time though, his voice would have reached enough people to maybe matter. For now, in Axel's twisted mind, he was just an anonymous face in a crowd, protected by a veil of shadow cast by his hooded robe, not realizing it did little to hide his presence in a crowd of formally dressed beings and party-goers alike. Long years of isolation did wonders for mental health. (speaking in Basic)<br>

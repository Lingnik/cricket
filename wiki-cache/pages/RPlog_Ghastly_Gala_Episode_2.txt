Imperial Hall of Heroes <G01> - Castle Tsabak: Dreven

A marvel of architecture, this vast, impressive chamber does not appear as though it should be possible by accepted laws of design. The Hall is a rectangle which stretches from the gilded doors at one end to the speaker's platform at the other. At the centre of the room there is stone altar surrounded by a garden. Laying atop the altar there appears to be a body.

Two makeshift stages have been set up in the room on either side of the altar, each equidistant from the altar and the outer wall. They are both circular in shape and constructed of wansook pine. The one north of the altar has been painted black and is cluttered with a variety of instruments and amplifiers, the rage-metal band Dooku's Droogs are set up here. The one south of the altar has been painted white and has a far more modest set up with only microphones and speakers, the Theelin Diva group, the Five Elements are set up here.

The vaulted ceiling looms several dozen meters over the black marble floor with no apparent means of support, and it's curve suggests it was designed for acoustics. Adding to the sense of improbability, a plethora of vines dangle in the air above without quite seeming to visibly connect to anything. A layer of black smoke permeates the room at floor level though not so dense that the floor itself can not be glimpsed. The audience chamber is visible above, and the entire chamber is evenly lit, though the light is dim and indirect.

Around the hall of the room, a number of white larmalstone busts have been placed on pedestals. Each one is of a famous bioterrorist. A single pedestal is covered with a crimson cloth - hiding the bust underneath before it is unveiled. Three makeshift bars have been set up at convenient locations, also around the edges of the room. Waiters can be found constantly going back and forth from the bars to restock on drinks to bring around the hall.

In addition to the drinks an extensive menu of food is paraded around the hall for the dining pleasure of the guests. Keeping with the party's theme the food is divided between black platter offerings which while appealing to some cultures are considered gross by general galactic standards and white platter offerings which are generally considered more palatable by the mainstream.



&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With an open bottle of Chandrilan Wine in one hand and a half dozen glasses gripped in the other, Tray returns to where he thought he left his group. He scrunches his face, looking around to see where they might have gone, in hopes that he might have just gotten turned around in his slightly inebriated state instead of being ditched.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Having disengaged previously from conversations, Ssanyu had moved to disappear amongst the crowd as much as a Falleen could really disappear considering their more rare status as a species outside of their home world. Nonetheless, she managed in some small way to do just that but now begins to emerge once more through the expansive crowd. Her head turns towards the side as she catches something being murmured near by that draws her attention but alas what that also does is leave her without watching where she's going that sends her on a direct collection course into Tray.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In spite of considering leaving the area, Nara has remained where she was before. Leaving is not only rude, but a potential disappointment to her fans! So she remains, not in the least expecting the potential disaster mere yards or feet away. "He's certainly been gone a while. I wonder if something's happened to him?" she asks her camdroid softly.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;After a few moments scanning the crowd, Tray picks up Nara's golden hair in the crowd and realizes he did indeed get turned around. He only takes a few steps in her direction before nearly colliding with Ssanyu. "Pardon m...", he begins to say automatically before his brain catches up with his eyes. There's just a brief second for things to get back in sync. "Well, hello there," he greets with a smile. "Fancy a drink?" he adds lifting both hands to show off his bounty.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A Ganathan woman, clad in white as most of the female staff are makes her way around the room with a white platter of Gorra Berries. Stopping in front of the pinstriped suited V1C3 she offers up the tray. The Droid looks down at the tray and remarks, "No thank you I can't eat these." The woman then remarks, "Would you like me to bring you something else?" The Droid replies back, "I am afraid I don't eat." The woman looks confused before nodding and scurrying off towards others.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There's a quick reaction within turn, a slide of a heel across the ground that brings her frame twisting around within the stance as the near collision is barely escaped from. As the words do not fully continue the sentence from Tray's pardoning, it brings her to stare at him quite openly without still a single expression to rest along the features of her face. "You did not finish your statement," she states with an equally lack of emotion within her tone. Amethyst orbs momentarily turn outwards as the offer lingers within the air of a drink. "Under one circumstance." A beat. "Two.", she corrects herself. "The first is that you will arrange for that.. reporter," she states with a flick of a wrist and elongated fingers motioning vaguely towards Nara's direction, "gets a story worth writing about." Ssanyu leaves it at vague. "Second, that wine within your hands is worth being put in my mouth."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The sound of Ssanyu's heel sliding against the floor draws a glance from Nara, as does the encounter between V1C3 and the waitress. Noting Tray speaking to Ssanyu, she walks briskly over to the pair. "Oh, here you are! I'ed begun to worry you'd gotten lost," she says to Tray, smiling. Turning that smile on Ssanyu, she adds, "Hello again! I'd hoped we would cross paths again before the party was over."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Oh, Nara?" Tray asks rhetorically with a wide grin as he extends his fist clutching the glass stems in her direction. "We already got her lined up to interview Amidala /and/ Vanilla. It's going to be epic. So that's one down." He gently waggles the bottle of Chandrilan wine, "As for your second condition... I got lots of things worth putting in your mouth. Wine of otherwise." Supremely confident in only the way several glasses of whiskey can grant a man, he adds, "Come on, I'll introduce you to my future ex. She's kind of a big deal, you know. Covers swoop races and all sorts of stuff." Without waiting for a response from Ssanyu, Tray starts to walk where he last saw Nara and then stops suddenly, nearly running into her as well. "I swear this place is raining beautiful women. Hope I didn't keep you waiting too long, Nara."

The grating vocals of the Droid, Count Binface alternating with the more legitimate singing of the Herglic, Count Whaleface. Unfortunately, it comes to an end before anything really shows. "It must be the music..", she murmured mainly to herself as if blaming the level of horniness on the music. The mention of getting lost brings her eyes to turn between the pair. "You could of put a tracker on him." A beat. "Hello."

Sar Admiral's Dining Room -- Sienar Interdictor-class Cruiser -- Leviathan

As the unlikely flagship of the Caspian Navy, the Leviathan boasts certain amenities that would not normally be found on an interdictor. The Sar Admiral's private dining room is one such amenity. Were it not for the windows opening up to the vastness of space, one could forget they were on a ship.

The top of the walls are covered plaster painted scarlet, the bottom consists of intricately carved japor ivory wood panelling with inlaid obsidian. The floor is covered with a hand-woven crimson coloured carpet with a leaf design hand-embroidered in gold thread by the Silverwood House of Fashion.

Designed to host both dignitaries and functions for officers it boasts a massive hand-carved dining table, made from wroshyr wood harvested under dubious circumstances. The table is complimented with forty matching hand-carved worshyr wood chairs upholstered with scarlet coloured cushions. The table is accented by several matching blue coloured reproduction Junarian vases as centre-pieces, they are filled with local flora when circumstances allow it.

The art and artefacts displayed add greatly to the room's opulence while also reflecting the eclectic tastes of the Sar Admiral. The main entrance to the dining room is flanked by two ancient sandstone sculptures depicting Massassi, who look more like bureaucrats than warriors. Meanwhile the side entrance to the Sar Admiral's quarters is flanked by two Kuati marble sculptures depicting female humanoid fashion models wearing designs from Zubindi Hakoon's collection. The side walls of the room are decorated with the coats of arms of the Sar Admiral's house, House Dragonflower, along with Caspar's Vector Apex.

The walls by the entrance to the kitchen are slightly more practical boast antique homogoni grandfather clocks on each side with antique homogoni drink cabinets on the opposite sides of the clocks. Meanwhile the against the walls by the washroom door are two fishtanks containing fish from the Sar Admiral's former home Chandrila. The room is lit with ice jewel crystal chandeliers.



&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Crestian had managed to avoid Selene for several weeks. It was a welcome respite from galactic politics to actually spend time at home in Caspian Space, you know the place he was actually elected to lead. But the galaxy can never really be shut out and various matters soon crept in forcing a trip to Selene for more meetings.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When it came to meetings Crestian always preferred dinner meetings when possible. As it was one of the main reasons he maintained such a large and opulent dining room was for holding meetings and parties. When is keeping a crew together through a decade of relative seclusion it helps to have those nice touches with everyone getting their opportunity to dine and celebrate in comfort on occasion.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Tonight's dinner party are a far more exclusive affair with only a few guests. And far from a formal affair, a compartively simple meal of comfort food Glowblue Noodles, though Crestian took the added touch of some serrith bacon, a boar native to Mergansar.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Axel was quite familiar with the layout of an Interdictor-class cruiser. However, this was quite a change from the very utilitarian Immobilizer 418 he'd captained so many years before. Wandering the halls as he was escorted to the private dining room, the high priest of the Olumi Osahn surely caught the eyes of some of the crew, some likely not happy with his presence and many likely being completely indifferent. To Ax, it didn't really matter, he wasn't here to meet with the crew.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The smell of the bacon though, that definitely caught the near-human's attention and he was smiling broadly. Noodles, they were just meh, but seared boar flesh was a thing of beauty. "Sorry if I'm late," he says. "I wasn't expecting that my arrival in Selene would be for much other than taking the new ship to see about a paint job," he says. "I appreciate the invitation. To what do I owe the honor?"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As much as Johanna dislikes Selene, trips to the Imperium's capital simply can't be avoided sometimes. This time she's made the journey with her mate and three youngest children, her weary expression a product of the sleepless days and nights an infant brings, her form partly slumped over the table as she rests her chin in her hands and feels her stomach growl. "I thought you might be interested in news from Hutt Space," the pilot replies to Axel before Crestian has a chance to speak up, "Since you seemed about ready to start a war with Nasiri during his party, and all. You may get an opportunity to vent your displeasure at the slave trade there sooner than you think." Her stomach growls again, this time louder. WHERE IS THE FOOD?<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"See this is the kind of news I needed." Crestian brings out three plates of the noodles in addition to a bowl containing more of the noodles for second-helpings. The Sarian looks at Axel for a moment and then runs back into the kitchen fetching a plate with extra bacon sensing that the bits already in the noodles were not going to cut it.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Given Zubindi has had some dealings with Nasiri before I was hoping he could do me a favour and bring back some intel on this alleged Sarian slaving. Bastard just comes back with some datachit from Nasiri that was completely worthless."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Axel looks to Joh, raising an eyebrow. "Oh really? Please, do tell. I'm eager to go blow some shit up," he says with a smile.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When the food is brought out, and then there's the extra bacon brought with it, Axel grins and bows his head to Crestian. "Ah, a true friend indeed," he says. "And, being a guest, I did not come empty handed." He pulls his cloak aside exposing a pouch that is slung on his side. "An Ithorian wine I picked up when I got the bottle to christen the new ship once I determine an appropriate name for her."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ahhh, there it is: glorious food! Axel's question is suddenly pushed to the back of her mind, Johanna's attention now exclusively on how quickly she can cram dinner into her maw. The ratio of noodles to sauce is /perfect/ (no mean feat)... the taste is excellent! It doesn't take very long for om-nom-nom noises to escape the woman as she snarfs down her serving, the gusto of her efforts a thing that would make the food-motivated Ortolan Frederiko Eembek proud. Her toddlers would probably have been more civilized presences at the table. Hunger is a hell of thing.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Once she's shovelled the majority of her plate's contents into her face, now belatedly aware of the savage picture she's presented during the past five minutes, she looks up sheepishly and wipes the saucy mess off her face. "Uhhh... I guess I was hungry," Johanna offers to Crestian with an apologetic look.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Retrieving some glasses, Crestian remarks to Axel, "Ooh you remembered my love of Ithorian spirits very thoughtful." In no time at all it seems Johanna has made quiet the dent in her meal, "Well there is a reason I made that and not some intricate feast. I quite suspected a certain ravishment...." He sits down and adds, "That and Atsvara is amused by the colour so I have to have the ingredients around. It is really quite amazing how much of my cooking routine seems to coalesce into satisfying the whims of a seven year old." Taking a few bites himself he remarks, "Who is thankfully less obviously about all her plots and schemes than some of our mutual acquaintances."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ax hands over the bottle, and just watches Joh devour her food. He just blinks at her as he picks bits of bacon out of his own bowl. "Damn, either this is her favorite dish or Joh's not had anything to eat for a few weeks."<br> 
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ax just chuckles and waits for Crestian to pour the glasses. "Well, I enjoy most quality boozes in general, the Ithorian spirits are definitely good, but I have a weakness for Whyren's Reserve personally." Then, he's taking a few bites of his meal, not just picking at the bits of bacon. "Not too bad," he says.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Sometimes I forget to eat," the exhausted woman nods, "An-" before she can continue, a short belch assaults the ears (and dignity) of the other two attendees, the speed with which Johanna consumed dinner having caused her to swallow some air as well. This time she blushes. "Gods below!" she exclaims, tossing her napkin onto the table, "I promise I didn't call a meeting just to make a spectacle of myself." Inhaling slowly to recenter herself, she folds her hands in her lap and clears her throat before continuing: "The day after the party, I met with Nasiri in private to discuss what his plans for Hutt space were. In short: he's willing to let me -- and possibly a few others -- bring firepower into the area to eradicate the last major players in the slave trade. I have information on three characters in particular; what bothers me is that once we've wiped the place clean and he stands unopposed he may feel more equipped to thumb his nose at the Imperium than usual. He's /said/ that he would be willing to re-engage as a true Moff if I help him secure certain business interests, but barring my invading his mind, I have no idea whether he means it or not."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When Axel refers to the food, the cook remarks, "Unlike most people of power I prefer to do my own cooking when possible. I am not fond of having more staff fawning over my needs than is really required. Hosting group dinners here was one of the ways I kept up morale during our days of quiet. You know one day invite the off duty medical staff, another day host some Commander's wedding anniversary. The little things that help maintain unit cohesion."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   Standing again to pour the wine, Crestian switches to talk of the politics, "Nasiri has always been a certain enigmatic figure, but my trust in him his faded in recent months. I was happy enough to engage in some deals to help him bolster his forces before, but the way he parades that witch around is just a stupid target on his back and he has thus far not seemed to interested in leaving his security blanket of a shithole moon long enough to explain why I for one should resume treating him like a competent conspirator."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Caspian leaders fills three glasses setting one out to each of the others before beginning to sip from one of his own, "Certainly not helping my trust by making back handed comments about my so-called hero complex to Zubindi while wasting my time with useless intel, I do hope whatever he has given you is not equally absent. Exactly why do any of us need his permission to do anything out there. He is always talking about Hutt space is some unchangeable place....if he plans to change it he is in effect admitting his own assessments are bullshit."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"So, he wants us to do his dirty work for him?" Ax asks with a shake of the head. "If he wants to be a Moff, then he should prove his worth by tidying up the domains he's a Moff of instead of trying to take control of shit in a system far outside his domain and responsibility." Axel just shakes his head. "I'm no politician, nor do I wish to be a politician, but I have ran both Kessel and Bespin at different times, and had my hands in other operations. I'm also not a ranking member of the Imperium, so take whatever I say with a grain of salt, but he's obviously failed at being a Moff, so why should we help him with this? Why do we need his permission to go into Hutt space? For all I care, alert the Hutts to his betrayal and while they're gearing up to attack him, we pound them with our own guns and just get rid of multiple problems at one time."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Axel takes a deep breath, and eats a few more bites of his meal. "He wants to use us as his shock troops. If we're doing all the work, why should a failed Moff reap the rewards of that? I'll gladly go after slavers, but not those that are hand-picked by Nasiri for his own gain."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Axel takes the glass put before him and drains half of it in one motion. "Through passion, I gain strength. Through strength, I gain power." he says. "He has no strength. He wishes to be a shadowy leader of men, but he leads nothing but Jack and shit, and Jack left town."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"I've had all of those thoughts," Johanna sighs, picking up her glass to sample the wine after so many months of avoiding alcohol, "All of those thoughts, and then some. But I don't think invading Hutt space will bring us anything. What do we stand to gain? That sector has no natural resources useful to us, and a population that is surly at best, openly hostile to outside forces at worst. Trying to pacify and civilize them will yield very little return for the effort. I also don't care to thin the fleet presence of enough Mid- and Outer Rim systems to launch a full-scale invasion. It would merely make those places vulnerable." She pauses to take another sip, sighing again. "My view is that it's easier to keep an eye on one being -- an entity already known to us -- than to engage in a hot war for control of an ultimately useless place."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"There are so many worlds involved. I have spent a great deal of time looking over the maps and refreshing myself on the region lately. There are indeed some species who are shall we say too comfortable with their involvement with the Hutts. Others though hold them in great contempt is little keep them as Hutt vassals except for the lack of intervention." Crestian sighs, taking another sip of his wine, "I certainly have no desire to end up with more worlds I will have a responsibility to protect. Working out the inclusion of Charros IV and reuniting the rest of the old Union was a hard enough work. But there are worlds would be comparatively easy to remove from Hutt control if anyone bother to try."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Sarian pauses in thought sitting down to several bites of the food he had cooked for the meeting, "My primary interest in stopping the enslavement of Sarians if it is indeed happening. The report Zubindi gave did not seem to indicate Nasiri was offering any rational insights into what is going on. All this talk of slavers suddenly seeking to liquidate their stock....if they had no markets the wouldn't spend months and years continuing to enslave at the same pace. What is he actually bring to the table as a potential new leader to the area....I mean a new leader is all well and good but right now it seems like Axel would be a better pick."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Taking in a fleet would be foolish, and gain us little other than a headache. My only goal is to eliminate as many slavers as I can. I may not be able to stop slavery, but I can limit their numbers and make others think twice if they feel that they may be vulnerable, even in what was considered the safety of Hutt space. Screw going after Nasiri's political rivals, we do our own intel gathering, find our own targets, strike fast, strike hard, no mercy for the guilty, to hell with Nasiri's political desires. If he wants to control Hutt space, then he should bloody well get his hands dirty and do it himself."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Axel looks to Crestian. "I am not a good pick for anything, but even a toddler would be better than what he is." He just shakes his head. "I have my own agendas, and I do not hide that fact. I have no desire to be responsible for a government body in any system, for I know that I do not know better than the people. I am but a mercenary, who has little problem killing as long as it is not of my own people."

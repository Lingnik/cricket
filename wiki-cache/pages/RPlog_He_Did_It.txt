&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Trandosha had rarely in history been consider the most welcoming of worlds. Its people had a certain reputation which coloured how others reacted to them and vice versa. Its more recent history had seen a different sort of problem as a variation of the Sariphage that had wiped out most of the Caspian population had been unleashed here, though thankfully to less devastating effect. This made it even lower down on the normal list of tourist destinations and yet conversely meant certain sort of people were keen to visit and even begrudgingly welcome. One such individual who finds his way here is Caspian Presav Crestian Tarasar. In his capacity as Executive Director of a charity known as The Mission he has reason to visit such downtrodden places. And has taken this opportunity to tour the remains of an old stopping ground so to speak, accompanied by a Trandoshan in a black uniform.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Hey, whoa now, take it eeeeeeeeazzzy..."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Taking a bite out of the gourd-shaped fruit in his grubby pink hand, the Galaxy's Greatest Lover continues walking, very reluctantly, with the friendly assistance of two Trandoshan security guards. The security guards are taking turns poking him in the ribs with some crude batons.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Getting forcibly marched in the opposite direction as the incoming Executive Director of a charity known as The Mission, Ezlo takes every opportunity that he can to slow down, or stop altogether, before getting poked in the ribs again.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Walking is just so hard, he can't imagine why anyone does it.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"It ain't right, makin' me leave like this, I didn't do nothin'!"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;This doesn't stop the Trandoshans from poking him in the ribs some more, and making him walk faster.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Ssssssssssssssssssssssssssssshut up! We sssssssssssssssssssaw you taking a sssssssssssssssssssssssssshit in the cccccccccccccceremonial fountain!"<br>

The two begin to follow behind the Zeltron and his captors, not interfering but keeping in eavesdrop distance. At the revelation of the supposed crime is uttered, Crestian chuckles under his breath and remarks to his friend, "I guess things really have changed Drorq. Back in the old days such devil may care defecation was consider the mark of a true Sith. The air was choked by the smell. How the mighty have fallen now."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"We know it wassssssss you! It wassssssssss a HUMAN DROPPING!"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Trandoshans look somewhat confused by this, and look at each other as if seeking guidance.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Right about then is when Ezlo catches sight of Crestian.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Sarian leader burst out laughing and then motions to his sar spots, "Sorry friend I am not Human either. Besides they can't arrest me I have diplomatic immunity." He then turns to the men dragging off Ezlo, "So what is the plan here? Fine, deportation, execution?" He didn't think the last was very likely but really who knew what the Trandoshan legal system looked like today.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Still pushing the shabbily-dressed transient, the larger of the two Trandoshan guards speaks first.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"We're going to throw him into a ditch!"<br>
The slightly smaller one chuckles in the hissing, snorting way that Trandoshans do.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Then we're going to beat him with our sssssssssssssstickssssssssss!"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Behind his thick, blue and gray beard, the Galaxy's Worst Zeltron looks worried for a fraction of a second, before taking the last bite off of the formerly gourd-shaped fruit and tossing it aside. Still munching on the fruit's flesh, and getting flecks of it all in his beard, he turns around once again.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Oh come on now... two vigorous young men like yourselves? Bet you got plenty better things to do than persecute poor Ol' Ezlo. Handsome devils like yerselves... why... I bet there's all kindsa females out there wishin' you would go fertilize their eggs for 'em. Why don't you just, sort of... let this one slide, eh?"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Trandoshans both look at him, their hatred intensified.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"WE'RE BOTH FEMALESSSSSSSSSSSSSSSSSSSSSSSSSS!!!"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Trandosha was semi-frequently the destination of a number of Darth Malign's agents and associates. Within the Citadel and ruins of a number of structures rested a small treasure trove of Sith related texts, experimental materials, and weaponry if people knew where to look. Aldus Syn and Carmella Rise, two members of the Dark Lord's circle of Dark Side adepts were dispatched, their lord and the upper echelons of the order off on some unknown tasks in unknown regions leaving their "lessers" to get the work done.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A nearby floor seems to slide open without any sound as the pair of black cloaked figures emerge from an unseen passage that silently closes behind him. Aldus holding a number of data chips in hand placing them into a bag and attaching it to his utility belt, "See, in and out, no one saw-" he cuts off looking at the commotion nearby, "well hmm."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Carmella shakes her head in disappointment keeping her hood up, but quickly recognizing Crestian, a plan already forming in her head, well a few plans, but the more important one was the one where she didn't kill everyone in the room.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The black uniformed Trandoshan that is with Crestian looks at him and asks in accented basic, "How is it this pink one has survived so long with a mouth that runs like liquid excrement?" In reply Crestian can only remark with a shrug, "I really do not know."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;He then turns to the Trandoshan ladies and remarks, "If his crime is the....shall we say the spiritual defacing of property, are you sure you want to deface your stick with him. I am not sure he is really worthy of beatings by such proud beings as yourself."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The unexpected sight of two other Sith agents suddenly removes Crestian's visible humour at the situation. He raises an eyebrow and recognition of their presence but otherwise says nothing at the moment. After all him seeing them was not the problem. Though perhaps it might prove the solution.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The argument made by Crestian seems to be somewhat more persuasive than Ezlo's, much to Ezlo's everlasting shame, no doubt.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Fine... he'ssssssss YOUR problem now. But be careful, he sssssssssssssshedssssssss."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ezlo looks confused, running his grubby pink fingers through his thick blue chest hair absent-mindedly.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sure enough, the occasional blue hair drifts lazily toward the ground.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As the Trandoshans turn to leave, Ezlo still looks a bit puzzled by what actually happened. But when he turns around towards Crestian, he's got his best Used Spaceship Salesman Grin plastered beneath his shaggy, overgrown beard.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"So... guess you'll want a discount on the sex then, hm?"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;He seems fairly oblivious about anything political, but he almost immediately picks up on a vibe of enhanced tension. Zeltrons pick up on these things, which might explain why Ezlo is actually still alive.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Aldus lets out a sigh of relief and Carmella's posture relaxes as the issue seems to diffuse, the two agents melt into the shadows and wait for the Trandoshan guards to leave before stepping forward and moving toward Ezlo and Crestian. The pair step forward revealing an icon to the Caspian, a red circle with a Sith symbol emplaced within it, protocol in these situations, "Moff Tarasar, this is unexpected. What brings you onto Trandosha?" Aldus speaks as Carmella stands nearby watching the interactions.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Captain Drorq eyes the Zeltron after his talk of prostituting himself to Crestian, "Would you like that I hit him for you Crest?" The Sarian shakes his head, "Tempting as that sounds it shall not be necessary provided our friend he drops this sexual proposition. He is not my type." Any further chastisement of Ezlo is delayed by the official introduction by the other Sith. Crestian smirks, Aldus was as always a slave to protocol. "Here I thought you were trying to be discrete? No need to flash symbols and titles."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Moff turns to Carmella addressing his explanations to her, "I am on world overseeing the delivery of some medical supplies from the Mission. I thought I would do a little site seeing while I was here. Security is a bit more lax than in my last visit and I did spend some time here back in the day. Captain Drorq here was kind enough to join me." Drorq's uniform is clearly Sith, but not that of the New Sith Order or the Imperium as such but rather much like that the Leviathan crew wore in the interregnum and have continued with since.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Hey... whoa there... take it eeeeeazzzy..."<br>

But as the threat of physical violence seems to be disipating, he instead reaches his grimy hand past his equally grimy chest, and rummages around one of the inside pockets of his extremely grimy purple coat.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ah ha! There they are...<br>

However, that was exactly his use was his expertise in the cockpit. Her, eyes were alchemically modified to see in a few extra spectrums of light and her pupils were more serpent-like than human, she whispers to Aldus who nods and speaks, "We were under the impression that this world was under the jurisdiction of Admiral Darrian Fel'lya and his Imperium Forces." He shrugs, "Though aid, I would assume is welcome here and the Imperator likely wants the CDU to push its influence over the world." He coughs stepping back to check a datapad and Carmella steps forward to cover him curiously turning to examine Elzo with some curiosity, her features twisted in a sort of weird mix of sexual and predatory hunger she speaks, "Not today. We will be on our way Crestian." She says quietly ignoring his titles as a sort of reprisal for the disrespect he showed to Aldus.<br>

As for mine, the Mission exists to do the good works of the cause, it is not simply so tool of quote unquote Caspian power. Beside since when is it a matter of concern from where in Imperium supplies originate?" Crestian shrugs, little interested in pushing the point with them, "Do be careful on your way out, lest you run into anyone else and get blamed for the this one's shedding." He motions to Ezlo. The exit remark of Aldus produces a smirk, far from insulted, more amused. The Trandoshan gives Crestian a look that clearly asks a question that is not spoken, Crestian simply replies, "Yes, but don't."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;He turns away from the Sith to the Zeltron, "What in the name of the Gods had you here of all places. I suspect there would have been some unhappy people on Bespin had I stumbled into your stick beaten corpse after this was all over." He does not comment on the discounted spice offer one way or another.<br>

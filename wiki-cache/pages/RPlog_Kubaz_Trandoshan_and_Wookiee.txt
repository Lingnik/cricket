Caspar Caff -- Cloud City

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;This large cafe sits in the heart of the shopping area. Rectangular, it's walls are covered by 2D (or nearly 2D) work by local artists, and a few holo-pieces, all of which are for sale. A bar sits on the side. Here patrons can order stim-cafs, teas and other hot beverages from around the galaxy in addition to iced versions of many of the same drinks.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A display case showcases deserts from a wide variety of cultures, available in individual servings. A number of small tables are scattered about the space, along with the a number of booths, even though the bulk of the patrons take their drinks to go.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Of particular note are the public holoterminals: each table has an individual holoterminal capeable of streaming media or making calls. The booths have the same capability, along with high end noise canceling equipment to prevent the noise from disturbing other patrons.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A Kubaz, a Trandoshan, and a Wookiee walk into a bar.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Life on Bespin had been chaotic of late to say the least. The explosion that destroyed the refinery managed to temporarily throw the city out of alignment and sent the world's benefactor to seek medical treatment off world. The concurrent attacks on the banquet and brothel while relatively brief left carnage and destruction in their wake along with a general feeling of unease.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In the aftermath the Imperium briefly interdicted travel, a few ships managed to leave one way or another most were left to wait and go through thorough screening before leaving. The bad guys may have gotten away but a lot of wild leads were stopped before they ever started. The system still finds itself subject to an outsized Imperium presence which is providing defence, engineering support, and assisting the investigation among other sundry support functions.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Imperium presence, however, is not what worlds on the Rim usually find when disaster strikes. The bulk of the assistance currently being provided to Bespin comes not from Selene but from Caspar, with several ships and two Union Councillors on site. Through the determination of the local leaders, particularly acting Baroness-Administrator Quinne Stardust Rose, and workers and the aide of their Caspian friends life slowly is returning to a new normal as everyone tries to put the best spin on things.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;However, whether life is great or life is disastrous caf is considered one of the great necessities of life and this sees a trio of beings on a break from their duties heading into the Caspar Caff: The Kubazian tailor Zubindi Hakoon, Trandoshan captain Drorq, and Wookiee politician Zaalbacca.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A sales clerk/barista works the bar calling out drinks to a pair pulling shots of stim caff -- wait is one of those baristas making things Zeak Oppenhiemer? "Jack, that crema isn't quite right -- looks like you didn't tamp the grinds properly, let's try that again," he says before dumping the dark liquid down the drain. He watches as a Rodian with the nametag "Bob" puts grinds into a little pod, applies force and then puts the pod into the machine. Buttons are pressed, sounds of hot water being pushed through the grinds at pressure by a pump can be heard, and the dark liquid begins collecting in a clear shot glass. "Much better, now let's try the argon infused crygenic brew method from Etti."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Followed by a gaggle of fans, press, grateful miners, and several assistants, Quinne Stardust Rose - newly minted temporary Baron-Administrator of Cloud City - moves through the commerce hub visiting with business owners to assess any losses and offer both comfort and resources to restore their livelihoods. The Zeltron does her best to keep everyone calm and orderly and feeling like things will only get better. Walking the crowd towards where a press conference is set up she says to them, "The Head Engineer overseeing our restoration will be with you shortly. In fact, I see him now. He should be able to answer more of your questions. Then with the slick help of one of her assistants, the erstwhile chanteuse slips off to get herself a little stimulating refreshment. Using her natural gifts to placate the residents of Bespin's mining and tourist hub was draining and she needed a little pick-me-up. One place that was unscathed miraculously is the Caspar Caf. Quinne prances through the doors and takes a deep breath. "Oh my...I feel like it's been years since I've been here." Her violet eyes close a moment and she clutches her heart. "It smells so good!!"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Spotting the familiar Zeltron arriving shortly after his own party the Kubazian tailor remarks, "I wish it had been years since I had been here, but I swear it has only been a few hours." Though he had kept is official reason on the station to most who asked that he was consulting on the aesthetic elements of the refurbishment, it his really purpose on Cloud City was heading up the Caspian investigating team, sifting through the clues left behind by the attacks for insights into where they had been operating. Progress had certainly been made, but after a while it was rather mindnumbing work. Was this box of matches from that hotel a clue or just more debris shoved down some poor hooker's throat.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As Zubindi has stopped to talk to Quinne and Zaalbacca eyes the pastries, Captain Drorq stepped up the barista counter. The Trandoshan had been left in command of the Leviathan in the absence of Sar Admiral Tarasar, with the Interdictor forming the central point of well....the interdiction efforts. It has been a rather busy a taxing week for him, particularly since between the departure of Johanna's flagship and the arrival of reinforcements he was in fact actually in charge of the interdiction effort. Before he can order though something about the barista looks off, "Excuse me friend, but you look familiar. Have we met before. Perhaps I terrorised your settlement in some past life no?"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Now the Argon infused stim caff will have a heavy head on it -- not so heavy it sinks, but if you di it just right the whole pull can just be foam," the barista with an apron labeled 'Zeak' continues. "We'll pour this foam into a nitrogen infused foam and it will sing to the bottom. The different densities will keep the two from mixing and can form the basis for all sorts of layer concoctions."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"But people don't order this crazy nonsense," the Rodian interjects.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Jack our goal for you is to be the best dang Barista in the quadrant. We at Caspar Caff want all our employees to take pride in their job and live up to the highest standards. I'm not here because I like the scenery -- I'm here because I hear you can be the best. I'm here because I hear you could be good enough to one day compete in the intergalactic Barista show down, and bring honor to your franchise. Just think of it -- You can be a durasteel barista Jack."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Rodian stares for a second in shock before it replies, "My name is Bob."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"I know Jack, I just used your name a second ago, gotta listen more closely Jack, so let's give those gas infused brews a try." With that he turns to the Trandoshan.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Can't say we've ever met Jack - although the universe is a big place and I own lots of it -- but today we aren't buying or selling, we are working on perfecting our Barista skills." He then leans closer to the Trandoshan, "Jack over here needs some practice - do you have the courage to stretch his skills and let me really impress us? Would you be willing to just let his creativity run wild and see what happens -- on the house course."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hearing a familiar voice with a Kubazian inflection, Quinne turns to find the intrepid tailor and gives him a huge warm smile. "Mr. Zubindi! I'm so glad you're here." She takes a beat and blinks. "Not here as in Caspar Caf but here as in Bespin. But...then...you already knew what I meant didn't you." She laughs it off then shakes her head and says quietly to the designer. "I'm so tired. I can hardly think straight. But I love my friend Johanna and I will -not- let her down!"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Half way between Zubindi and Quinne on one side and Drorq on the other, the Wookiee union boss and minister turns back towards former, "You are doing a very fine job. You have risen quite admirably to the occasion and I have no doubt that Lady Johanna will be most pleased at your work here." The Wookiee had spent more of his time actually with that Head Engineer that Quinne kept telling people was on the horizon. He turns back to the counter and asks, "I will take a slice of the Kowakian Crumb Cake....better make that two."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Zubindi for his part offers an arm to the Zeltron, "Well it sounds then like we both could use an octuple espresso or some such. My treat. Incidentally Crest tells me Johanna is healing quite nicely. He apparently got her to a Biscuit Baron for the first time in her life."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As he looks over towards Drorq at the barista counter he does a double-take, "Zeak Oppenhiemer here????" He is not sure which makes him more incredulous the idea that the man would actually be behind the barista counter or that his agents had not passed this information along. The Trandoshan finally nods with understanding, "Ah yes it was in battle but they were only dreams. And the name is Drorq not Jack. And I do indeed possess the courage to try this Rodian's drink making efforts. Can you make something that tastes like swamp water?"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;'Zeak' just stares a second, pondering. Perhaps he is wondering why anyone would want swamp water, perhaps he is calculating the right mixture to give it that taste. "Let's see." With that he turns to cabinet marked off limits and pulls out some vials only marked by barcode. He hands a red one and a blue one to the Rodian. "Push the red one through Drogo purifying grinds with a hydrogen sulfide infusion, and then pour that shot into a glass filled half way with this blue infusion."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Rodian looks at them, and with his large eyes staring in what might be disbelief replies, "What are these things?"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<br>"Best you don't know Jack -- your Kung Fu has not yet reached this level, but I'll get you through this and get you to the level of awesomeness where you too can make drinks like this. Right now all you have to do is wow us with your technique."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Okay," the Rodian replies some what incredulously before muttering something about this job being way more intense than he imagined.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Why thank you!" Quinne says with genuine warmth. "Mr. Zaalbacca your experience has been invaluable. There's so much to do it was hard to know where to start. You're a very good organizer - in many ways," the pink lady says, definitely intending the double meaning of organizing shops into unions and then organizing other things.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Quinne flashes a bright smile then lets him step up to order his treat. She turns her rosy face towards the Kubaz and takes the offered arm enjoying the strut to the counter with the flashy fashionista. Then he exclaims about Oppenheimer and she stares as well.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Blue lashes rise above Quinne's widening eyes. To Zubindi she speaks while still staring at the caf shop manager. "Johanna's Ex? What's he doing here??" Then a sudden spark of suspicion fires off inside the Zeltron's overworked brain. "You don't think he'd ya know..." She makes a sound like an explosion and gestures with her flingers flipping up. "...do ya?"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Zubindi ponders the question asked quite seriously for a moment. Would Zeak blow something up, quite probably, would he do so on this scale and hire Boba Fett and a psychotic slaver to raid the brothel.... "Not on this scale certainly. At least I don't think," his words little above a whisper. He cautiously steps forward, "Quite surprised to see you here. I didn't realise you took quality control so seriously." Drorq turns to the Kubaz and asks, "So if the barista fails to impress me with the drink does this Zeak kill him for Jagannath points?" Zaalbacca meanwhile excitedly take his Kowakian Crumb Cake taking a bite of one of the pieces while offering the second piece to Quinne.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Rodian begins to work, pulling the shot as instructed. The creme and color look darker than usual, but the rotten egg smell is definately unique. The Rodian, Bob, looks like he is about to fall over in disgust. He then takes the shot and pours it into a clear glass that contains the dark brown, almost surypy liquid from the other vial. There is a bit of turbulent mixing, and the rodian can feel heat as an exothermic reaction takes place, and then it seems to stabalize. He passes it to 'Zeak' who looks at it smiles, flashing a grin to Zubindi and Quinne and then passes it to the Trandoshian. "It isn't stable, the emulsion will seperate -- but if you drink it fast it should give you the experience you are looking for."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The drink is a mixture of a special activated carbon (i.e. soot) infused under pressure with hydrogen sulfide in an alcohol suspension with notes of cedar wood that came from the casks in which the alcohol was aged -- and it includes enough various questionable spices to induce euphoria and visions of the past, future, or parallel universes in a Bantha. Of course to the naked eye however it looks normal, and of course smells horrible.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The blue-haired erstwhile songstress tries to stash her suspicions away, though it is just extra strange that Zeak is here. Did Johanna know? Should she let her know and disrupt her recovery? Did it actually matter at all in the scheme of current affairs. Quinne watches the various exchanges between Drorq, Zubindi and Zeak and when the Wookie offers her a piece of crumb cake she is very impressed. "Why thank you! You're very sweet!" she says while taking possession of the sweet treat. She looks back to the counter and asks, "Um...could I get a Velvet Hammer Slammer, double sweet with extra cream please?"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"It is quite pungent." Drorq takes the drink and begins to down it quickly as advised, "This is most excellent. I will not have to kill you today." The Trandoshan clearly looks pleased with the drink. He considered ordering another, but the thought of ruining the poor Rodian's day stopped him. As funny as it would be he had been taught better manners than that and he instead looks for an appropriate table for the now quartet.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;After Quinne orders her drink it is Zubindi's turn to order, "Make that two Velvet Hammer Slammers, double sweet with extra cream." He pulls out the appropriate credits to pay for both drinks and a sizable tip on top of that, he understood how overworked they probably all were considering the circumstances, and 29 ABY had been very good to him financially. "It appears to be hammer time." Zaalbacca starts to perk up at Zubindi's comment and the Kubaz remarks, "Not that kind of hammer." He knew where the union bosses' mind was going, hammer and sickle.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"And the best is yet to come!" 'Zeak' adds in a cryptic way before he looks over at the Rodian in a clear notion that he should start brewing. "I think you have this Jack, I'm going to take a quick trip to the little TIE pilot's room. don't forget the extra cream, and if you use the sucrose infused high fructose glucose mix, you can get the sweetener to diffuse easier!" With that he takes off his apron and hangs it neatly on a hook before he begins to walk toward the restrooms which are near the door.<br>

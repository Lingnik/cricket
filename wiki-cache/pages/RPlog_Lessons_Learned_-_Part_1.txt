&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Bren steps off the shuttle ramp and brings up a free hand to shield his eyes from the afternoon glare of the sun as he looks around. "For..does it matter what I am apologizing for? I am saying I'm sorry and you can either take it or not. Doesn't really matter to me." Offering the man a shrug and a smile, she takes in Cube's haphazard appearance before she turns around again and then, coming from over her shoulder, "You need a shower."

She smiles as she looks at the human male sidewards, smiling a bit at him as she shrugs.

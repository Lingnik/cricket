Club Utinni! - Mos Eisley: Tatooine

Acquired and renovated by Dragonflower Entertainment a few years ago, the club caters to a slightly more refined clientele than in years past. It still balances between being a hangout for scoundrels and meeting place for the more respectable. Nevertheless it operates under the vision of Tatooine promulgated by its current benefactors. No efforts are made any longer to court Hutt Crime Lords and their ilk.

The large round nightclub is lit with a dazzling array of coloured lights but without the dizzing holographic displays that were once prominent here. The bones of a massive krayt dragon are embedded into the walls of the club encircling it completely with its massive skull over the entryway. The cavalcade of tables, mirrors, and booths that had once made this place veritable maze that only those in the know could effectively navigate has been replaced by an more orderly array of booths and tables made of hand-carved, locally sourced Japor ivory wood.

The bar in the center of the club is cheerful, and the bright drinks come in every color of the rainbow in stylish glasses. Small rooms off to the side house gambling amusements and cheers can be heard from them on occasion as lucky rollers strike it big. A raised platform at the back of the club forms a bandstand for live entertainment. Synthesised jizz plays at other times.


&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The universe had a way of dragging Crestian back to Tatooine. Eventually he decided that he needed his own space for his visits leading to the purchase of Club Utinni!. Once again he has found himself back on Tatooine, though this time at a much larger scale than small personal visits. Today it finds itself quite busy, much of the local clientele displaced by aid workers from The Mission or Caspian service personnel coming in for some relaxation on their off hours.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There is however a booth in the corner with a single occupant that is given a wide berth. The Presav sits alone looking through a stack of datapads and nursing a glass of Ithorian brandy as he waits for the man he is meeting.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;One might not recognize the Falleen at first, typically clade in rich spider silk robes and his long black flowing hair pulled into a tight top knot or flowing free around his shoulders. It was the classic look of his time as Lorda in Hutt space, though recent times reported little of his presence. Perhaps at this point almost spanning a few years give or take a few months. As Nasiri entered now, his long hair was chopped to just below shoulder length and was done up in an array of braids, his clothing is vastly downplayed, cargo slacks, linen shirt and field vest, a sturdy blaster in a waist fitted holster but not tied down, a survival blade cross sheathed at his back and a slender water canteen nestled into a worn leather strap. but Tatooine is Tatooine and he wears glare shades for comfort and shielding from sand, he has no troop or escort of any sort and somehow he seems weathered compared to his normal self. Not tired, perhaps at ease, even peaceful.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Crestian for his part wears one of his usual outfits, wearing the colour red. The colour more often worn when he is representing his own interests versus those of the Union or the wider Sith. Though his recent activities had found him often in more casual clothes as there was a lot of hands on work to be done as part of the aid work. Spotting the Falleen, Crestian remarks, "I am afraid the range in alcohol selection than normal, a lot more customers than normal. Thankfully at least my own tastes are purchased in bulk."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri gives a casual nod and slides into the booth and shrugs after he sits. "Water will do." He assures the man. "So, Presav...everyone of power has been touting their sainthood and good intentions." he smiles and its a very easy smile and not at all feigned. "Pretty brazen statement made by you as well...you are often direct but usually more diplomatic...that was for you anyway...very direct and one might say, brutal." the Falleen settles a bit and regards the Presav casually, his words spoken clearly in a tone over observation and not accusation. "Seems the galaxy is getting a bit testy?"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"That was the toned down version actually. I had planned a follow up, but thought better of it after the reaction I was getting from the Imperium and the press." The club's owner motions to someone at the bar, "Pitcher of water and two glasses." Looking back to Nasiri he adds, "I should probably drink a little more water myself. I suppose my own hydration in great danger but as you can imagine with all the refugees coming through here right now making sure people are dressed and drinking appropriate for the environment."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"A little testy for sure," he answers in regard to the main matter at hand, "Though I would say the problem is the powers that be getting lost in the weeds. The Republic as always is collapsing in on itself in a bureaucratic maze of misadventure. The Imperium keeps waffling from trying to be, or at least project the notion that they are really the good guys, and then publicly revert back to old methods. The scale of audit they seem interested in just has an opportunity to make things a lot worse. But it amusing their efforts to spin my remarks."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri chuckles and pours them both a glass of water when it arrives, taking the initiative to do so before Crestian. "It...is amusing on one hand and sad on the other, I am familiar with good and evil. Living with the Hutts certainly capitalized on that aspect of reality and at times my own actions." the admission is made off hand. "I certainly at times could be classified as evil in a very intense sense, but what I find most disturbing about the galaxy as a whole, from the New Republic, Imperium or Empire pick your flavor." he grins at that remark. "Is a lack of integrity to what they are...so many facades, everyone is the hero and nobody is the villain, when everyone seems to be the villain in denial." he glances up in thought a tosses a shoulder shrug. "What would Mr Thurston offer...all the galaxy is a stage?" he grins. "And you...albeit have always had personal designs on status...nothing wrong with that have struggled to do right, even when wrong was the necessary evil to get along with the one with big ships." he raises the glass of water to him. "No fault for wanting to be great even if you don't admit or see it, but here is to the effort you make despite the galaxy wanting to be skewed and murky."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Crestian takes a sip of his water, "My staff convince me to excise the portions of my statement that were going to be about all the extrajudicial killing we have all done. I still find it a little perverse that we are supposed to consider that more respectable then sending someone in a black site prison as a block of ice. Not that I condone the Oubliette, nor think it was some necessary evil. But again amusing that the PR team on Selene wants to imply that I am....I let that point go, allow them to save a little face. But they keep wanting to look in the shadows, problem is when you do that dark things come out. Which would be alright if they were at least focused on the right shadows."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Stacking the various pads in front of him into a more organised pile, "I wouldn't take too much excitement in my public disagreements. I am sure my next conversation with Imperator and Regent will be awkward. They will probably continue to characterise my remarks as playing to the electorate. And while that was not my intent I am naturally going to have to incorporate that perspective into the campaign calculations. But I don't see us breaking with the Imperium....but we are a little more focused on our relationships on the Rim. Our more stalwart friends are to be found out here."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri leans back and stretches an arm across the back of the booth. " Oh, I did not for a second think you were going to stand directly against the tide, it was simply a compliment for taking the stand you did." he assures the statesman. "You spoke truth...much of the truth in the galaxy these days are spins to make whomever is speaking look good and saintly...even the Empire admitting to its dark past is simply a artful spin to say...see we are reformed, we knew we were evil and seek to atone." a long swig. "Meanwhile they manipulate and expand their power...direct militant action did not succeed." setting the glass down he turns it in an idle manner. "So, they don the banners of fighting for freedom and justice and equality, its simply a change in tactics." his dark purple eyes regard Crestian. "Tyranny is still the order of the era, it just wears a different suit."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Switching back to Ithorian brandy for the moment Crestian smiles, "Well I appreciate the compliment. I am not without my own efforts to spin, but I would like to think that mine are less vulgar. The art of statecraft does involving telling a story of how you want things to be not always how they are." He yawns, "I am not looking forward to all the poodoo I will have to go back to when I am done here. I'd like to get the situation better under control here before we officially call an election. Little hard campaign when several ministers are here in this sector. Not that Johanna could not manage the situation without us. But it would be a slower process and there is no need of allowing any of our enemies to gain an upper hand here."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Presav chuckles a bit and adds, "And of course the Imperium's response is just wanting to send a huge fleet of ships. I like large deployments as much as the next guy but a star destroyer is not exactly what is needed when you have a famine. Tyranny in a different suit indeed, Zubindi was a little disappointed not get more business tailoring for the Imperium's recently promoted. Sad thing is they do actually mean well in their ways. The tools exist to make the Imperium be more the power that it wants to project itself as being. But they are still using some crude implements."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;He pauses to sip his brandy before adding, "That said Grand Admiral Vichten has actually grown into his new role in ways I would not have expected. He has been left holding the bag on a lot of things and has taken a far more responsible and professional approach that I would have expected. New press secretary and all. She has become interesting to watch."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri tilts his head to the side. "Grand Admiral Vichten...grown into the role or playing the role and a long game." he offers in a soft tone. "The Empire runs in cycles, it grows throws around its weight and then collapses." The Falleen was middle age for his species, he saw the fall of the Republic, the rule of the Empire and all that followed. "I think he is just looking to be in a position to profit, when it collapses again." refilling the glass of water. "And he gets to shoot things and be an asshole to everyone with a permit from the Empire." tilting his head in a way as if to say, you know its true. "Its very easy to be sharp and brash if you don't give a shit about what anyone thinks or wants, Just be mindful with things, you have worked hard." he reminds the man. "I would hate to see you become the kick can of the powers that be."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Oh I know Axel is playing a long game. But he is a different man than he was in past. Somethings are the same, but the old spice addict Axel would be handling this all in a different way. I don't agree with some of his methods, but is presence in leadership in the Imperium is better than the vacuum there had seemed to be." Crestian refills his own water glass after Nasiri.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"I wouldn't worry too much about me being the can that gets kicked. The Imperium needs the Union, the Union needs the Imperium. The balance of that has shifted over the years, the needed has lessened. But I am the sort of can that when you kick you break your foot."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nasiri nods and sips the glass. "Just a word of warning, I didn't think you needed it. But you have done me favors in the past so...I expressed a concern." he offers in a soft tone. "I am in a position right now that may see me used by Johanna, we shall see." he drains the water again and leans forward. "But all in all this meeting was a bravo for speaking your truth, a thanks for help in the past and a be careful in the future, the Union is much better under you than in the past."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Crestian sips his refilled water, "Well I appreciate the warning. As far as Johanna I image will be interested in your support. The Core is not where we are going to find the support, the real support we need long term. Here on the Rim we are going to have to do more to stick together."

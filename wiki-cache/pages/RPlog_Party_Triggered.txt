Tahn Consortium -- Central City, Athaniss

The high domed ceiling only adds depth to the already immense office. Polished molten-made stone set into the domed expanse reflects the light in prismatic effects throughout the room. The room itself is divided into two sections. The formal office area has a subdued atmosphere with minimalistic decor. A simple desk and chair set up is all that categorizes the area as "office". The second area is more elegant though still subdued in nature. This area is a lounge as depicted by a round couch in a black soft fabric that begs to be sat upon by only the most elegant of person.

Small tables of the same molten stone as the ceiling, the rainbow hues casting about the room as one stone reflects against another, dot the room in close range of the couch. On one wall a bar is set up with liquors from around the galaxy. A few datapads with links to various news broadcasts are available from the small tables for those sitting on the couch.



&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The offices of Malif's business is very low key, if you weren't looking for it you wouldn't notice it. Save for the two very well kept female agents that flank the doors. Clearly a residual flavor from Malif's time with the OOAG. He never did let go of the intel services way of thinking. The women open the door on arrival of the alien with a nod of respect to his presence.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Within Malif sits at his desk closing out some last moment contract details and the very subtle sounds of chirping and clicking can be heard by those aware. A rather large bowl of varied insects. Malif also is a student of other cultures and one beings wine is another's water as it were.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Zubindi's own arrival is has a certain professional flavour of its own. The Kubaz arrives seemingly reading some last minute details of some matter or another on his datapad as he walks in, while a Pantoran girl follows closely behind carrying a briefcase. Nodding politely to the agents as they open the door, Zubindi hands the datapad to the Pantoran as they walk in. The insects are quickly noted by the fashion designer before he greets Malif himself with a nod.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Malif rises from his seat and gives a brief smile. "Master Hakoon...I am unaware of any titles you may have, so please forgive any oversight on my part." he steps around from the desk. "Welcome to Athaniss, not exactly a pleasure planet to be sure but welcome." he gestures to one of the seats in the lounge area, one of the agents follows the Kubaz and the aid in to close the door behind them and then posts herself by the bar even as Malif continues to speak. "Please partake of anything that strikes your fancy." a brief flicker of the hand indicates the spread of snacks. "And thank you for coming."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Mister shall suffice just fine. Alas none of my customers has yet seen fit to grace me with any noble title for my efforts." Zubindi looks back to Veruca Violet, "Perhaps we about a knighthood from someone? Or maybe investigate what one needs to do to become a Prince of Ixin?" The Pantoran smirks and seemingly jots down some notes on the datapad she had just been handed.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Turning back to Malif, Zubindi remarks, "As for Athaniss it is, by my estimation, and underrated world. It would be just as capable of being the next Selene with enough effort put into it. I think you for the invitation Mister Tal-Tahn? I too must say I am not fully of aware of any other titles you go by yourself."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Malif moves to pour himself a drink and chuckles. "Oh, far to many to list, the Sector Authority loved titles and lists and credentials." the liquid gurgles into the glass. "Malif will do just fine, titles have been the downfall of the galaxy for a long time now." he smirks at that. "Me, I am a business sort and I am happy with that." he shifts a gaze to the woman with Zubindi and gestures to the bottles.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"So, the reason I asked you here is...for a party." a brow rises over his deep purple colored eye. "Yea, tons of them happen all the time, nobles and emperors and warlords...but I was thinking something more open?"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Zubindi grabs a couple of the snack insects to pop in his mouth, crunching down on them while gesturing to the Pantoran to see if she wants any. Her some what disgusted look back at him would seem to imply her time with Zubindi has not led to an appreciation of insect cuisine. After grabbing a few more he peers back at this host, "So exactly how open a party are you looking for Malif. Something that will bring in the next tier of notables into the gilded fold. Or are we talking about something more akin to opening the floodgates to the masses?"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Malif takes his drink and settles into a comfy chair. "Well that is sorta what I wanted to muse with you about." a quick sip and a brief savor of the flavor. "I have a theme in mind...crafted by a notorious figure and I want to build on it." He gives the Pantoran and smile and gestures to a side dish with regular finger foods. "And I don't think, gilded fold would fit...The Damion Dynasty has that all locked in a crypt to be already." he chuckles at the odd jest. "The question is, what is left?"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Well the Damion dynasty may be at the centre of power now. But dynasty's come and go. Thirty years ago they were of no consequence. What will be the case thirty years from now? There are other figures who may yet rise, though I suppose Athaniss is perhaps too much of the backyard of the Imperator and his court to see them rise here." The Kubaz pours himself a drink before taking a seat. "But I am now far more interested in which notorious figure has motivated the aesthetic of our upcoming party."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For her own part Veruca Violet does grab herself a small snack from the non-insectoid fare presented before quietly taking a seat beside Zubindi and setting down the briefcase she has been holding. A polite nod give to the host as she makes herself comfortable.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Malif gives a tilt of his head. "Basca Starker, I gained access to substantial amounts of is collected works and data." he swirls his drink slightly. "A bit of a wild card, took more chances and gambles then I would ever have...but boldness yields profit is many way." the thought of Athaniss giving rise to anything was not what the near human was about, to hell with ruling the Empire or the galaxy...that is a bloody headache. "He has this aesthetic that was along the lines of beauty and the beast...he didn't believe in good and evil, but rather beauty and its opposite."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For a few minutes the insect chirping may well be the only sound to be made. Neither Zubindi nor Veruca Violet seem to quite no what to say. They both look at each other rather awkwardly for quite a few moments. Eventually the tense expressions between the two a broken up by a slight nod given from the Kubaz to the Pantoran, before the latter breaks her silence. "It is good that we came in person to hear this idea. Caspar would have...." Her voice trails off as if the thought she had of a reply collapsed under its own weight."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Zubindi quickly chimes in himself, "It is interesting that you should seek to involve me in such an endeavour. I did know him personally myself before...." The designer himself begins to trail off before finding his place again, "In fact he sought my services in event design at one point himself. He certainly had a rather unique aesthetic. It would present a rather interesting challenge to try to honour."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Malif shifts a gaze to Veruca and then back to Zubindi. "Caspar has very legitimate reasons to feel the way it does, but to be fair." he takes a sip of his drink. "The parade of persons who can claim to have committed genocide is long and many of them held in high regard in these times." a brow rises. "Though their targets had...no...survivors and they were not controlled by a Sith Master."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;He shrugs at the irony of it. "I neither condemn any of them or excuse any of them, it merely is what it is...but the concept, is how to move a party from beauty to beast and still have it as a party?"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Well this is going to be one hell of a party I give you that Malif. I am already getting some ideas. This is absolutely going to be the party of the year." There is a certain genuine excitement in Zubindi's voice as, from an artistic perspective, it was a rather unique yet striking theme. He takes a sip of his drink as begins to ponder all sorts of details.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Veruca begins to jot down a few things on the datapad herself. While Zubindi was the artist, her role in his operations was far more practical. Taking a deep breath she asks, "In order to consider the logistics of this....did you have a particular venue in mind already? An estimated number of guests for the party?"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Malif sets his drink aside and steeples his fingers. "Venue is open, this is really just us musing. My mining operations has been very successful, so credits are not going to be an issue." he shifts a gaze between them. "As for number, who knows. If we can go big lets go big...maybe not every scoundrel in the galaxy, but really a chance for everyone to get a taste." a leg crosses over the other. "I am great at tactics and skullduggery...parties not so much, which is why I asked Mr Hakoon here." the drink is retrieved. "I don't want to rule anything out, other then I don't what our guests going missing or suddenly unalive...there is enough of that in the galaxy already and where as death doesn't bother me...it doesn't need an invitation."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Taking sudden death off the table does seem to be off brand for the theme, but I suppose that is a sacrifice one must make to keep it an enjoyable party." The fashion designer takes another sip of his drink. "Did you mean to have it here on Athaniss or is that an open question as well? I will have to better familiarise myself with what is available. It has been a little while since I have found myself out this way."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Malif sits up and straightens a pant leg. "Lets open it up to somewhere that could fit the theme, doesn't have to be here." he minds the two. "Feel free to source around the galaxy, I will cover any travel or scouting expenses as needed." he to seems a bit excited about the concept himself. "I want you to take your ideas anywhere they lead you...open range." he passes hand through the air to indicate the whole of the galaxy. "If things go as they usually do, I am sure some kind of drama will present itself as it usually does." he gives Zubindi a knowing look.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Kubaz begins to pop a few more insects in his mouth, the crunching sounds serving as the soundtrack of the galactic tour now going through his mind. "A few locations are already coming to mind....but I will have to look into the venue availability." Zubindi then turns back to the Pantoran, "I think we are going to have to clear some time in my....in both our schedules in fact." Then looking back to their host, "I think we can meet your expectations on this one."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Malif grins and nods. "I suspected you could, aside from travel cost and needs. If anything unexpected comes up." he nods in in a reassuring way. "You let me know and I will cover it...and have fun...all work and no play...really sucks." he minds the two. "I want to go all out...exotic, daring what have you...but and as much as I love this conversation. I do have a meeting to get to...please touch base with me when you have a few notions and there is no crushing hurry, I would rather it be right and on point, rather than right now."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Pantoran nods and stands, picking up the briefcase once again. "I think we have enough to work with to get started. Thank you again for the invitation Mister Tal-Tahn." The Kubaz stands as well adding, "We will be in touch when we have some of the preliminary details worked out, venue, initially cost estimates, etcetera, etcetera. And do not hesitate to reach out if you wish any additional ideas you wish to share Malif."

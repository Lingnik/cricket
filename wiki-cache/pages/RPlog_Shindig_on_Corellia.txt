Royale Casino -- Coronet City: Corellia

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The first thing to assault the senses upon entering The Royale casino, is the noise from the slot machines, emitting musical songs and chimes as tokens and coins are fed into them. The main floor is vast and with the mirrors, bright lights, crowds of beings and smoke drifting to the air, it's difficult to see just how large it really is. The color scheme is truly Corellian. The carpetting is a subdued red with gold flecks, giving it a glittery shine, as are the lower half of the walls. The casino has upper levels, but the best action seems to be on the main floor.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Dealer-run card games and other games of chance are mostly along the southern wall, in between two of the smaller bars. Rooms for private card games are also available. Near the southwestern corner is a small bandstand used for live entertainment. Slot machines cover the rest of the main floor, the lights on top blinking wildly indicating a winner. To the west of the main entrance is a wide staircase, leading up to the second level of the resort. A closed doorway to the east is labeled: Red Dragon. The tuxedo-clad man next to the door hints at the upper class nature of the restaurant.



&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Try to look interested," Johanna mutters to two of the servers working the floor this evening, the sullen-faced humans rolling their eyes at her in lieu of a verbal acknowledgement as the woman passes them on her circuit around the casino, her boots clicking along a stretch of tile before she hits carpeting again, the smell of fifty cheap fragrances stinging the inside of her nose. Gods below, how much money had she spent to rent the place for the evening? Too much, in her estimation, though hopefully the Corellians would be receptive to the gesture of goodwill, taking a more favorable stance towards her overtures at negotiating trade. The venue is certainly filling up quickly... always a good sign, right? And nobody has yet screamed threats of bombs, or punched their neighbor in the throat, thrown things at the live band, or otherwise tried to light anything on fire! The evening is /bound/ to go smoothly at this rate, even if half the employees working the floor tonight are probably high. Speaking of high... is that Krackor Reincus? "So glad you could make it," Johanna greets, bustling over to where the smuggler is doing his best to blend in, "Your support means a lot! I'm sure there's no hard feelings about the palace, right?" Right. Hey, she'd taken over Jabba's Palace fair and square, and the university she'd built there was a much better investment than Krackor's den of iniquity!

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As Thurston arrives at the Corellian casino gala, he stands tall in his shimmering silver outfit, the foot-tall bright pink mohawk on his head waving in the air. He's surrounded by a team of extra security, ESPO's, due to the underworld bounty on his head, but he's not one to let that dampen his spirits.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thurston turns to his camera crew and gives them a cheeky grin. "Alright my darlings, are you ready to see some serious glitz and glamour?! The Cutting Edge is live at the Corellian casino gala, and we're here to bring you all the juicy details of this event as it unfolds. I hope you've got your drinks ready because we're about to turn up the heat!"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;With a flourish, Thurston turns towards the entrance of the casino, throwing his arms out wide. "Ladies and gentlemen, the one, the only, the fabulous, Thurston!" he exclaims, striking a dramatic pose as his fans cheer and shout his name. With a twirl of his mohawk and a grin, Thurston leads his team inside to document what might just be the party of the year.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Silverwood House of Fashion is well represented tonight. Though generally noted to wear fashions that express his native Kubazian culture, Zubindi Hakoon occasionally likes to jazz up his appearance for events of particular note and tonight arrives in a rather over-the-top outfit with various silks in variety of colours topped with a fedora and walking stick serving to draw a lot of attention to himself. The fashion designer is flanked by a pair of brunette human models wearing Tyrian shimmersilk decollete midriff bearing gowns trimmed with silver cords. Following slightly behind this trio but close enough to be identifiable as part of this party is a Pantoran teen girl wearing a green vine-silk kimono with a silver floral pattern embroidered.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Pantoran girl, Veruca Violet makes her way over to where Johanna and Krackor are talking quietly ask of Johanna, "Any problems with the staff you need a hand with?" The girl clearly relishing a bit her role in some of the logistics of the fashion house's dealings. Meanwhile Zubindi seems more taken by the arrival of Thurston. He does not approach the reporter at this point, rather gives a polite wave in acknowledgement to a fellow follower of fashion.

Otherwise he looks as if he were transported (whole, ten minutes ago) from the former instance of The Palace on Tatooine, whichever amoral hedonist was then in charge. He buzzes around the slot machines, his luck coming in fits and starts. Mainly he clutches a flute of champagne and looks for familiar faces...

Over the years, he's played many different roles here from just another smuggler to almost running the place with his old friend. Here and now, though, he's just another guy here for the party. But Johanna...a school? Really?" He shakes his head. "Of all the things to turn Jabba's palace into....it's a shame." His attention is drawn by Thurston's entrance; the old smuggler instantly recognizes the man from the news. The Kubaz is given a nod and then Krackor is looking around for someone with a tray of drinks.

He is the rallying point it may seem...or this wall needs help being kept upright.

Imperial Way - Imperial City: Selene

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Imperial Way South was the crossroads of the newly built city into the sub-divisions that now exist. The shadows of large skyscrapers loom above, yet the top of the buildings can be seen here more easily then Palpatine Square a few kilometers to the north. Since it's closer to the edge of the dome, the buildings are shorter as the gates to the frozen planet nears to the south. To the east lies a less restricted area of the great city holding many rebuilt institutions that existed here before. To the west lies the heavily guarded Imperial Garrison, only the lucky few with military IDs can enter there.<br>


&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"I don't want it," Johanna complains as she hustles down the walkway, the massive pillar of fur, quills, and claws known as Sentinel easily keeping pace while he awkwardly holds out a datapad, "Toss it in the street for all I care, or in the garbage. I don't want it." She stops suddenly in her tracks and turns around, causing Sentinel to nearly collide with her, the mutant Wookiee managing to move his bulk sideways just in time to prevent Johanna from getting knocked flat on her ass. He growls softly, shoving the datapad at the woman's chest in a gesture of impatience. His master had told him to make sure she was up to speed, and he will ensure that she is, whether she likes it or not! "I didn't ask for a job," the brunette sighs, "You can tell your boss I said so."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Elana Tracer had lived in the same clothes for much of the past decade - all of it beach wear. Spacer's gear was pulled out from an old storage trunk and a few situational outfits of a dressier nature were also selected before she set off in search of her son, now Commander Kipran Jawatha she learned in what can hardly be a week ago. And now she was headed to her home system of Caspar to talk to a Prex in her new job as a Moff and CEO of the largest starship manufacturer? She was still a bit numb at how fast everything was moving. But if she could try and do what's right, help bring stability and rid savagery from the galaxy then she might be able to live with herself. The Jedi woman wasn't the only one who hadn't asked for a job.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What Tracer can't live with right now are a few little essentials and some new wardrobe items to get her through various situations her impending trip might require. Hands and elbows filled with packages she whirls out of a shop door and into onto the walkway where a speeder and two guards await their boss a few meters away.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Vassily Korolov strolls down the walkway as leisurely as a Sithlord can in the early day of another Civil War. He strides with his hands clasped behind him, beneath the folds of his boat cloak. Behind him a young human woman walks, she is wearing the field grey uniform of an Imperial Naval Officer and behind her a pair of Imperial Stormtroopers in their distinctive White Armor, as opposed to these newly armored Sith Troopers taking control of the planet recently.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Captain Vorn, remind me once again why we have come back here?" The Sithlords High Coruscanti accent sounds almost bored. The woman responds in a similar accent, "The Imperator summoned the System leaders here, and as Archduke of Coruscant, that is you, sir." She responds matter of factly. "Very well, until we are summoned, we shall enjoy our time here." His gaze flickers towards the dome, and perhaps out beyond to the deadlands formed when the Super Star Destroyer became one with the planet, effectively ending the Osbourne reign.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sentinel isn't having any sassy backchat today; he growls a little more loudly and leans over the human, causing her to glare and snatch the datapad out of his claws. "Hrmph!" Johanna huffs with indignation, "Fine! You can't make me /look/ at it, though." She resumes her brisk pace, her would-be minder heaving a sigh of his own and following suit. When Malign had told him to keep an eye on Johanna, he hadn't mentioned that it would likely result in a headache and a desire to be alone in a quiet room for a year or five. Still, she is his master's sister, and if his master has seen fit to include her in his plans...<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It doesn't take long before she sights Tracer, her slight form nearly obscured by the mountain of packages she's juggling. "Maybe you can go help her," the woman suggests to the Wookiee, nodding in Tracer's direction and figuring that Sentinel will be none too thrilled by the idea of hauling women's shopping items, "She's kinda small, she could use the assistance. She's a /Moff/ now, you know! Very important!"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sure, she's a Moff now. It's been all over the news. Elana Tracer back from obscurity working with the Imps who weren't the old imps but were the new ones. The New Imps! Though her staff had assured her everything she desired could be brought to her from the city shops, Tracer had insisted on going out herself in part because it had been non-stop learning and thinking and plotting since the Imperator's first Celebration night.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Since it wasn't that far to her transport there really isn't much time for Sentinel to intervene. After all, the two government-issued muscle, who she banished from coming into this last shop with her, are right there to take the boxes and bags from the petite and famous woman with the raven black hair. She does swing around though to see the giant hairy-scaley hybrid Wookie thing and Malign's sister nearby. "Oh! Uh...hello!" she says trying to just accept all this as being as natural as life under this dome was unnatural.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"This place was much different before..."A slight grin crosses the Warlord's lips, "We evacuated most of the civilians to other planets, Athaniss and Dreven primarily..." He stops mid thought, the sight of the Mutant Wookiee, then the brunette. He had not seen her in..years, on Nar Shaddaa was it. It did not end well, especially for the people that lived in that apartment building. But this was neutral territory for the time being.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As the pair approach the other woman, the former Presav, another face that he recognizes however had not seen her since his fleet went to break her out of Caspian custody..Intrigued, Korolov changes course towards them, hands still clasped behind him. His pace slows as he grows closer, not yet approaching within speaking distance.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Basca wanders along the street, whistling one of the current top JizzRave tunes. His hands stored in the pockets of his longcoat and glare shades worn just low enough to peer over the tops if he needed a unfettered view. The Wookiee of course is hard to miss and prompts a brief pause in the tune and then he resumes, also altering course for a better look. The others for the moment go unnoticed.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sentinel shrugs when it becomes clear his hauling services aren't needed, and hangs politely back to ensure the newly-minted Moff of Selene doesn't feel intruded upon. "Hi," Johanna replies, suddenly feeling very shy in front of her former swooping idol, "Sorry, I didn't mean to interrupt your shopping... was just taking a walk and saw you nearby. Figured I'd say hello, see how you're doing. Uh... yeah." She clears her throat, glancing nervously between Tracer and the two guards, who make short work of stacking the packages neatly in the speeder. "Congratulations on the new job, by the way. I bet it's..." No... shut up, Johanna, and don't make yourself look like an even bigger idiot! "... I bet it's... really cool. I mean the part about the shipyards, that's the... cool... part." Behind her, Sentinel lets out something like a snort, a massive paw rising to cover his eyes as he shakes his head.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Tracer was so unused to being out in public where people knew who she was without being able to say the same about them, like when she'd go to the mainland on the rare occasion something was too personal to trust to staff. But even there, as remote as that part of Kalla VII was, she'd often run into a few fans. They were usually sweet and her favorite being the girls - you could see not just the admiration but that they had the fire in their bellies to follow in her footsteps. Though she enjoyed the encounters, she was glad to be home again where what was left of her heart lived.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Jedi in turn made her nervous mostly due to her own fear of creatures such as this woman. But then she sees that spark in Joh's eyes. "Yeah...the ships...they're....really cool, uh.." She reaches behind her silvery black hair and scratches her neck feeling like a dumbstruck idiot. Rebound! "But...I'm done shopping so...Thanks?" That clear blue gaze squints slightly at the woman and the Wook. "Wait...now...you weren't sent to keep an eye on me were you? I'm not going to run away. Well...I am going somewhere but...I'm keeping my eye on the ball here...." she blusters in a way that she didn't in front of Tyler. She looks around and notices people looking at them and frowns slightly pursing her pink lips.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As the Sith Lord approaches the Wookiee, Jedi and Moff. He calls out, "Elana Tracer...your reputation proceeds you." A quick glance is offered towards Johanna, "Though I must say that I am surprised to see you here." His words could be aimed at either of them. However this was not his domain any more, nor did he have the bluster of a much younger Sith Warrior. He motions towards his minions, and with that the Naval Officer and Stormtroopers make themselves scarce.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Doctor, scientist..take your pick, either both and yet mercenary regardless. Though once in range he calls out. "Lady Danaan...or is it Master Jedi, It was never really established. How nice to see you again, I hear Caspar is well on the mend." his steps stop just at the edge of the grouping and be eyes the mutant with curiosity.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"No!" Johanna replies with a forceful shake of her dark-haired head, palms facing forward as if to push away the very idea, "No, nothing like that. That'd be rude, I wouldn't have agreed to something like that. And anyway, the Imperator isn't my /boss/, so." She looks over her shoulder, certain that Sentinel is grinning toothily at the both of them, and finds her hunch rewarded with the sight of the mutant's mirth. "Oh sure, laugh it up," she says with a note of exasperation, gently chucking the Wook in the side with her elbow, "But he /ain't/ my boss, no matter /what/ you think. Matter of fact--"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The words come to a halt as the all-too-familiar presence of Korolov moves into physical and psychic view. She raises a dark brow at him, mouth tightening into a line and Sentinel grumbling quietly at fractional shift in her posture as Johanna considers the actual threat level posed by the Sith. When there is no indication of impending violence, she glances back to Tracer, a comment upon her lips... and then, Basca rounds towards them. Gods below, it's almost like an ambush without the blasters. "It's just Joh," she replies to the scientist, "Titles are for..." She would say "assholes," but then it would reflect poorly on Malign, so she settles for: "... for those who enjoy them."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Tracer's own guards finish putting packages in the back of the transport and take up discreet positions to watch but not hear the meetings of so many important people. This would be a lucky spot for a bomber to knock off a few possible bounties and definitely knock out some death-betting contenders - a form of gambling on celebrity mortality dates that was rather macabre. She watches the interplay between Sentinel and Johanna and grins a little herself.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As she's hailed by an upper crust male voice in the crowd, Elana braces with an intake of breath. She wanted to bolt but instead turns on one heel toward the sound, not knowing the paparazzi status on Selene yet, putting on a kind expression like a shield. "Okay, did you want an autograph or something?" But then she looks at the human who hailed her and her affect falls. "I'm sorry but...do I know you...." She tries to scan Korolov's decorations, but without knowledge of ranks around here as they stand under Malign's infant regime, to address him properly. But with all the commotion she seems to give up.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Imperial Warlord's lips curl into a sublime impersonation of a smile as he senses the Jedi's reaction. He did enjoy making her uncomfortable. His attention shifts from her to the Wookiee babysitter. The Imperial had spent a long time chasing his associates down and putting an end to the New Sith Order. His survival comes as much as a surprise to the Sith as Tyler Damion's.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"An Autograph, yes." He almost chuckles, "I was at your Inauguration as Presav, and again at the end of your rule. I am Vassily Korolov." He doesn't add a title, he has dozens of them and they are as important as the Janitors on this world that was no longer his. His hands move to rest in front of him, black bantha hide gloves clenched together, partially showing that he is unarmed and partially just in case the Jedi isn't.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Basca watches the exchange with a bit of amusement and is silent to allow the new Moff and Sith greeting to pass undisturbed and then addresses Johanna. "Very well...good to see you Joh, we should do drinks soon and catch up." the smile is somehow benign and yet wicked. "How was the pregnancy, the kid, kids well?"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;She would rather fight Korolov and deal with the resulting destruction of a city block than endure Basca's smirking inquiries; the Sith is at least honest about his intentions. "They're fine," Johanna notes tersely, figuring the scientist for some kind of weirdo pervert and idly theorizing about sticking a shiv into his neck, "Are you on Selene to sniff my brother's ass, or just curious?" Well, that was perhaps more direct than she'd intended. Then, to Korolov, "And you, Vassily? No, you don't strike me as a boot-licker, so I warrant you're here out of sheer morbid curiosity to see what a supposedly dead man has been up to these months past."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Tracer furrows her fair brow slightly. Yes, some Imperial dignitary - she had heard of this man and perhaps greeted him in some reception line or... She clears her throat. "Uh...my Administration," she dares to correct him even as she feels tension and pressure building despite the openness of the street with it's broad walkways and large planters. Cautious, the not-yet-used-to-being-a-Moff takes a side-step back between the Jedi and the flashy stranger. Large blue eyes blink, then look between Korolov, Johanna, the Wookiee.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Your brother has more than enough people lining up to sniff his ass." The Sith Lord tilts his head slightly, "But yes, a Damion back from the dead and maneuvering to take control of a sector of Star Systems while a civil war erupts between several of the Moffs, it does not seem coincidental, does it?" He sighs slightly but the smile remains, "Or perhaps I just love...calamity." He watches the Moff step away, "Indeed your administration, though I would say the change of power was not democratic. " With that his aide de camp comes towards him, "My Lord, I am afraid we must depart."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Basca grins at the sniffing remark and then shakes his head. "Alas no, I was here for the celebration and crashing some upper crust parties." he leans in slightly and winks at Joh before turning to the Moff. "And the faire maiden Moff...freshly minted and presented to the galaxy, a great pleasure." he offers a slight bow to the Moff and offers Korolov a respectful nod and second one when the aide remarks of the need to leave.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sentinel's upper lip curls back to reveal sharp canines, a growl of disapproval rumbling audibly in the back of his throat and claws extending ever-so-slightly as Basca leans in. The man better take care not to agitate the mutant Wook by getting too close to either Elana or Johanna! "The love of calamity is a Damion trait," Johanna notes with a smirk of her own, "Perhaps you'll find a place here after all, Lord Korolov. I'm sure I'll be seeing you sooner rather than later." When Basca chooses to address Tracer with what can only be considered impertinence, the pilot rolls her eyes. "Fair maiden Moff? Are you high, Mister Starker?"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What was it about Selene that it was so populated with these fake fancy types, like Tarasar though his dandiness was to make him seem more harmless than he actually was. Likely was true of this one. She stares at Basca trying hard not to raise a single slender eyebrow. Her hands do ball into fists, though. But she keeps her desire to punch him in check and instead just gives him a pointed "don't even go there stranger" look and shakes her head slightly. Her attention then turns to the soon to depart Korolov and she lifts a defiant chin. She would not survive if she let every tall dark thing in a uniform intimidate her. "Yes, Vassily. Should need anything during your stay," she inclines her head just enough. "Please, let me know how Selene can help."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Sithlord's smile lingers as he nods to the Jedi's comments, "I am sure that will be the case." He turns away from the party responding to the Moff as he passes, "I will have my Aide de camp contact your staff, we should discuss some things...Moff Tracer." The half cloak moves with him, the dark outer layer giving way to the bright red interior. He makes his way back towards the Imperial Garrison, the stormtroopers following along behind Captain Vorn as they leave the area.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Basca raises a brow and looks at the wookiee and then the Moff and gives a light hearted chuckles. "Relax all of you...its a lighthearted banter...any of you could break me like a twig." he is genuinely honest in that remark and gestures to the sky overhead. "Or glass me from orbit, with but a twitch of a finger or turn of the head." sliding the edges of his coat back and warming his hands in the pockets. "So tense...the spawn is safe, the rest of you will could get stress wrinkles or grey hair..."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"As if we'd waste the ordnance," Johanna interjects smoothly, caring little for the derision in Basca's tone, having heard it so often before from males all across the slice, "No, Mister Starker, you'll have to do better: Moff Tracer is to be addressed with respect, not with your 'lighthearted banter.' If you wish to do business with the head of the largest collection of shipyards in this galaxy, you will reconsider how you speak to her." She hadn't counted on jumping to Tracer's defense, but the scientist's oily presence raises her hackles.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Perhaps we got off on the wrong foot?" Tracer offers the extravagant man a way out of Johanna's warpath with a brightening of her crystaline gaze. Elana was just relaxing a little bit at the departure of Korolov. She hoped Johanna wouldn't escalate the public display the group was putting on. She extends her hand diplomatically in renewed greeting. She didn't like sycophants and hangers on but if there was a business relationship to be had, now was the best time salvage the hull of it. She looks from Johanna to Basca expectantly, hoping for a cooling off. Though she didn't seem to mind Johanna being on her side in this moment. "Mr. Starker, is it?"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Basca nods to the wookiee as she offers the hand and instead offers a gracious bow. "Indeed and my apologies Moff Tracer, celebrations can have a lingering effect." and seems to consider his next words. "Basca Starker...I run a Medical R&D firm, most recently we were the frontline team on the ground of Caspar during the Sariphage...a tragic event, but one defeated."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As it seems that Basca has reigned himself in and Elana isn't averse to having him remain there, Johanna and Sentinel drift off to the side to allow the two businesspeople their privacy... but not before the mutant Wookiee sniffs at the scientist and chuffs, gaze turning to Elana as if to say 'I'm here if needed.' He nudges his ward a few meters off to the right, where they discuss something in a series of low tones occasionally punctuated by a gesture.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Every other doctor Tracer had met was very different than this one, much more serious people. Perhaps he is an eccentric genius? Still, she can tell Joh doesn't care for him. Not that Tracer had seen the Jedi "care" for anybody outside the Damion family. Except today when the woman came to her defense.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Thank you for your service to Caspar, Dr. Starker. A terrible tragedy. It was not the first plague in the system, but it was the worst so far. And something worse will hit again. I don't have people there any more...but I'm grateful nonetheless."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Basca gives a dip of the head. "It was a task to be certain, but I do like a challenge and the compensation was worthy of the endeavor." he seems pleased at the outcome. "Hopefully there is no reoccurrence, there should'nt be. But I think whatever regime comes next. They will be more prepared."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Regime...hmm" Tracer seems to ponder this word and her lips curl in slight distaste. "The new -government- will certainly not look or indeed be the same, that's for sure. " What was it that was so hard to accept about Democracy Tracer wondered. "And with the recent memory of the Sariphage, they will be informed to do as well. But memory...fades. A hundred standards from now they'll have forgotten how bad it could get."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Basca waves a hand at here. "Every sentient can forget, but the universal truth is. Once you lose on a grand scale, that loss is never forgotten." his hands go back into his pockets. "This is part of Caspian heritage now...it will be spoken of, its been recorded and waved. Monuments to remember." he raises a brow. "This they will not forget."<br>

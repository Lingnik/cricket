The Charros System is currently inundated with starships to the point one might mistake it for a world like Selene for the matter of traffic present. In addition to the fifteen warships and their support craft that make up Defence Group Charros, much of the Caspian 4th fleet seems to be present in the system as well several other vessels present from various other Caspian Defence and Patrol groups. Civilian vessels appear in large number as well, both old and new, large and small.

In spite of this large fleet presence, almost none of the military vessels are at all close to Charros IV itself. Giving the wargame battlefield a wide berth the only warship near orbit itself is the CMS Daring, though an unusual number of freighters, shuttles, and transports are near it.

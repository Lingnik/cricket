&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Tacuba the Hutt's Flying Circus has come to town -- or, more accurately, landed in the Mos Espa spaceport about half an hour ago -- bringing with it the prospect not just of a performing Hutt, but a wondrous panoply of spectacles, if the rumors of death-defying stunts and a freakshow are to be believed. The large troupe of performers filter their way out of the garishly-colored freighter, countless crates of equipment (and Tacuba herself) leaving the vessel on hoversleds to begin their procession towards the assigned lot near the edge of the settlement, the Hutt's large conveyance festooned with countless pieces of highly-polished mirror glass.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Naturally, the effect is as desired: the giant sled/disco ball slowly floating through town attracts onlookers, many of whom gawk and point at Tacuba waving serenely to her audience. "Beings of Mos Espa," she announces, the Huttese translated into a Basic that blares loudly from a series of crackling speakers floating around her.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Step right up and feast your eyes on Tacuba the Hutt's Flying Circus! We bring you curiosities from every savage corner of the galaxy, some too shocking for the fine citizens of the Core!" Behind her, a large cage with approximately twenty shrieking monkey-lizards causes its own unique cacophony, the two Yuzzum keepers assigned to wrangle the creatures looking none too pleased with their charges, not to mention the three leashed frog-dogs they've been tasked with. Behind them, the procession stretches for approximately another two hundred meters. "Younglings six and under may attend for free on Family Days," a pretty Zabrak calls to the growing crowd, "Be sure to get your complimentary tickets!"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"What is all that racket?" Curious, Johanna rises from where she's just sat down to meet with Mos Espa's mayor, his office situated on the second floor of a brand-new building and offering a commanding view of the plaza and main drag, "A circus? Oh, that's... fun, I guess." One of the frog-dogs breaks free and rushes to overturn a food cart, causing a scuffle with the vendor.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Frakking Monkey-Lizards" Crestian exclaims under his breath. "If only I had ever been tasked with a deploying a bio-weapon...." The Sarian man's frustrated exclamations are met a remark from his diminutive traveling companion, "You know you could always deploy one just for fun." Atsvara giggles at her suggestion.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Why are we even in Mos Espa again?" Crestian asks, "I distinctly do not recall putting this trip on my itinerary." Atsvara giggles some more but does not provide an answer to the question.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Perhaps there will be a betting pool somewhere on how many people get killed or maimed today? There has got to be a casino somewhere right?" Atsvara shrugs as to Crestian's question. No longer giggling as the idea of gambling does not seem to amuse her.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Axel was here, but not alone. Following along behind him was a child, somewhere between ten and twelve years old. A mix of Sarian and whatever mutt breed Axel was it looked like. A long lost child of the smuggler maybe, or might be that it's a relative of muddled blood? Who knew, given his history.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"We'll check out this spectacle, but last time I went to one of these, I ended up coming down with a bad case of fleas. And here on Tatooine, we're going to have to deal with sand on top of that. I'm honestly not sure which is worse," Axel says to the child, who looks absolutely thrilled at the idea of seeing the insanity that's about to take place.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then, one of the frog-dogs breaks free and is rampaging through the square, or at least overturning a food cart. Immediately, Axel is shoving the child behind him with one hand and going for his blaster with the other. "Do not run, it might give chase," he says. "Last thing we need is a Hutt trying to tell us we owe their fat ass a fortune for killing a stupid pet."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Down on the dusty street, a young couple strolls from the marketplace towards the main drag. The woman is dressed in layers of diaphanous silk saris dyed in desert tones of pale blue, coral, and taupe that flutter as the light breeze tousles her sunset curls. Her escort, a self-possessed Sarian in dark trousers and a flowing white shirt, a cylindrical hilt attached to his belt and a blaster on his hip. His demeanor is warrior like. But the pair are no seekers of trouble. In fact, merchants treat them as friends and the lawmen present give the man a respectful nod as they pass by. Their progress is interrupted by the Flying Circus's procession. The pair stare at it in wonder, the woman's face lighting up with delight at the spectacle.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Oh! We MUST go see them, Kip!" Sonja says looking up at her with an enthusiastic smile.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Kipran turns to his beloved with a wryly lifted eyebrow. His reply is dubious. "Mmmmmust we?! Besides, we're meeting Joh for dinner later...and we can't interrupt her right now to reschedule!"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then the commotion of frog dogs stops all conversation between them. Kipran's sharp blue eyes assess the situation even as he starts to move towards it. But not before gently squeezing Sonja's upper arms and telling her. "Stay here...but be ready to cover me."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Two bored-looking Houks amble their way towards the ruckus caused by the Yuzzum's inability to keep a handle on the frog-dogs, the larger of the two nearly expressionless as he hefts the wayward pet by what passes for scruff and sticks it securely under his hulking arm, cutting short any dreams of glory Axel may have had of shooting everyone in the face. "You assholes wrecked my cart!" the human vendor complains, drawing the attention of the Houks, who exchange a glance with one another, "Who the hell is going to pay to get this all fixed?!"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"They're certainly loud enough," the mayor remarks, joining Johanna at the window just as a trio of drumming Ortolans floats by on another hoversled, a group of local children pushing through the growing crowd to follow the drummers, "I hadn't realized the business consortium approved a circus. Supposed it can't hurt; some off-world entertainment around these parts is always welcome."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"My lady Tacuba apologizes for the inconvenience," a lilting Twi'lek voice replies to the angry vendor, its owner a young woman of perhaps twenty standard years, "Here, for your troubles." She extends a pouch of credits -- the sum within is probably twice what the man is owed -- and thereby defuses the situation, cruelly robbing Axel of his dreams of shooting everyone in the face and/or letting R2-FU set things on fire.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
"Yes, valued citizens!" Tacuba's speakers broadcast, "For three hundred years I've traveled the galaxy, going deep into the unknown regions to bring you the very best in entertainment! No other circus boasts such a grand collection!"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Make sure that everyone came here willingly," Johanna replies, "Lest the Hutts think they can drag slaves into our territories."<br>
"For the young citizens!" a group of diminutive Anzellans announces, tossing sweets of every kind to onlookers, several exemplars landing at Atsvara's feet, "For the young citizens!"<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Atsvara looks down at the candy at her feet. For most ten year olds the thought of free candy would be quite the bounty. When one's best friend owns an upscale candy shop and one has been running a multi-year campaign to wrestle control of the galaxy's largest fast-casual dining establishment away from the Corporate Sector Authority, one's standards get higher. Nevertheless Baroness Tarasar dutifully picks up the candy and looks it over, exclaiming to her father, "Is that a fucking Zentos Breath Mint?" Deep into the unknown regions my arse. The unknown regions of your creepy uncle's beat up YT more like it." She looks around at the other near by children, none of whom seem to be at all demanding in the quality of their candies. Crestian chuckles a little, "And what do you know about creepy uncles? I mean sure absolutely everyone in your life is probably creepy but none of them are your uncle."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There's almost a deflation in Axel's face. Just when things might get interesting, it was right back to the mundane again. Now, he was consigned to taking this brat to a damned circus, not getting out of it by means of violence like his dreams had hoped. Even Axel's droid makes a sound that's more fart than buzz.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Think they'll have clowns?" the young one asks as he comes around back to Axel's side, a hand reaching for the smuggler's mechanical one.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"By Olum's shadow I hope not," Axel responds with a visible shudder. "If they do, we're gonna end up in a jail cell before the day's over. Monkey lizards are bad enough, clowns though.. there's a special hell that involves nothing but those damned things. I'm sure of that."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Unlike Atsvara though, when a bit of candy lands near the child with Axel, it brings a smile to the youth's face, but not as much from the candy as the ones throwing it. He tugs on Axel's hand, and points.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"I know, I know. I tried to get one as a pet, thought it would be amazing, but apparently there's fucking laws against it. The say it's slavery, even when we all know that there's plenty of animals that can talk and do tricks that aren't real people."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The young man's help is not required. Kipran turns back to see Sonja with her arms crossed, an accusing smirk on her fresh features. Kip is struck. But immediately he senses why she is cross and returns to her. Before she can say anything he slides on his knees to stop at her feet. "I. Am. Sooooooo sorry!"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"You forget. All the time you forget!"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"I know." Kipran stands and embraces her shoulders. "You are as capable a fighter as I am. If not more so. I...I can't help it." She smirks up at him, this time with less frustration. He breaks out in a huge grin and moves his hand to her waist, scanning her lovely figure. "It's an impulse to protect you."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Careful!" she tells him moving his hand to her back before it slides forward. Glancing up at the mayor's office window she says,"Joh could come out of that building at any second!"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Kipran concedes and glances over Sonja's shoulders at the Hutt's palanquin, narrowing his features with suspicion. "Three hundred years? I've never even heard of them before..."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As if plucked from one of Axel's worst fever dreams, a human clown performs a complex tumbling pass for the crowd, drawing ooohs and cheers, its tall Glymphid companion painted up in a similarly disturbing manner (though not performing acrobatics.)<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Personally I hate clowns," the mayor admits, crossing his arms over his chest with a sigh and drawing a sidelong glance from Johanna, "They're just so... I don't know, strange? Uncanny? I'm sure there's a good word for it in some language. My son seems to think they're hilarious, though. No accounting for what a five-year-old thinks is funny."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"What have we here? A beautiful lady!" One of Tacuba's hype-men draws closer to Sonja, ignoring Kipran's presence entirely. "What's your name, gorgeous? We're always looking for new talent to travel with us... see the sights, live a life of adventure! Shake the dust of this town off your pretty little feet and find out what the big wide galaxy has to offer!"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"I have no idea what's supposed to be so disturbing about clowns," Johanna chuckles, "But I guess we all have our own threshold. You planning on seeing one of these shows?"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Whatever reason the Tarasars were there, finally spotting Kipran and Sonja in the crowd causes Atsvara to hurry in their direction. She stops in her tracks though when the clearly ignorant hype-man tries to recruit Sonja. Turning to Crestian remarking, "Live the life of adventure? Spoken like someone who probably has not shot anyone during a business meeting." Crestian looks down at Atsvara and smirks causing her to rephrase herself, "HAD someone shot during a business meeting."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Crestian smiles, "Well that may be true of the hypeman, but if this Hutt has really been touring for three centuries changes are they have had people shot. But I suspect that Sonja has had much grander adventures then they have."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There's a loud shriek, followed by a *whumph* sound as one of the clowns gets too close to Axel and on instinct he jerks his mechanical hand from that of the child with him. The repulse field generator built into it goes off, and that poor bastard in makeup gets a jolt of energy that could knock a bantha on its ass.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;This bit of chaos draws a rolling fit of laughter from the kid with Ax, "Did you see how far he flew? Do it again!" he says to the man with him. "That was AWESOME!"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Meanwhile, Axel is just shuddering. "Fucking clowns," he mutters and is immediately on the defensive, looking for retribution. Fortunately, the pulse wasn't fully charged, so aside from some severe bruising and maybe a little internal bleeding, the clown should be fine.... maybe.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Kipran, not the tallest man around nor one to be ignored, squares his broad shoulders and intervenes between Sonja and the circus barker physically and presses on the being's chest. A hit of orange flecks his blue pupils as he directs his will towards the appalling creature. "Back off, sleemo!"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sensing Kipran's ire rising, Imperium Crown Princess Sonja sidesteps her beau lightly. "Now wait, darling. Maybe I could join the circus," she teases with lyrical laughter. She produces a coin from her belt pouch and begins flipping to between the slender fingers of one hand. "I mean...I know a few tricks."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Kipran, however, doesn't appreciate his lady joking around like this. He doesn't say anything but his body is ready to intercept any attack on Tyler's daughter. He says with flat menace, "They couldn't afford you. You're too good for scum like this."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Tugging on Kipran's hand, Sonja notices a familiar Sarian and his daughter coming their way and tries to break the tension. "Come on, Kip. Give the fellow a break."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Unbeknownst to Axel, ever since FU's rampage against the Jawas, there's a standing order for the constabulary to keep an eye on both smuggler and droid whenever they enter the system. Johanna has no wish for the demented duo to run around her world unsupervised, and just as Ax engages in brazen clown-assault, he is tackled by a trio of the local fuzz... and emergency services are dispatched to ensure that the performer will be looked after.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Gods below," Tatooine's Warlord mutters, now sensing just /who/ is behind the sudden shrieking and clamoring, "He /would/ go and pull something like that. Mayor Tuvare, feel free to feed Axel Vichten to the hungry creature of your choice."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Oh my, a protective beau!" the carnival barker gestures dramatically, two lithe human women from the retinue of performers slipping over to giggle flirtatiously at the drama, the expensive beads on their headdresses clicking softly. "Now don't think we would forget to include such a handsome young man as yourself in the offer," the man continues, "Join your lady on an epic tour of the galaxy! Your talents would dazzle any audience, truly!"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"The Flying Circus is proud to announce a partnership with the Racing League this season," Tacuba's speakers announce, "Two weeks from now, when the races in Mos Espa begin, we will be offering a most spectacular spectacle!"

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Praise the Gods it didn't happen in my jurisdiction!" exclaims Crestian as Axel's latest arrest and/or gruesome execution is happening outside the Caspian Union and thus he does not have to deal with the mess involved. Then suddenly Crestian comes to a realisation that makes the matter seem far worse. He looks down at his daughter and say, "Fuck I hired that clown as a flag officer didn't I?" Atsvara giggles and says, "You did at that Tevas. Star Marshal Vichten, pride of the Starfighter Corps, person of interest in /several/ murders in the Union, suspiciously uncharged and unprosectuted. That's the one." Crestian smirks back at this daughter, "Oh like we haven't all committed several murders. That is just the modern Sith life. I suppose we should try to get him bailed out before the races."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"OOF!" is Axel's only word as he's brought to the ground. Again, more maniacal laughter from the child with him. Axel's droid wasn't going to take this sitting down. R2-FU's domed head spins and there's warbles and blurps as several appendages pop out of its cylindrical body. A buzz-saw and an arc welder come to life. Fortunately for Axel, and his droid, the child steps forward and quick-draws an ion pistol which he uses to quickly disable the droid. "Not today," the kid says before looking back to Axel and the trio of constables that were subduing him.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"I'm not going to jail!" Axel exclaims, but there's three of them and only one of him, and he was never known for being the strongest person in a room. He was a pilot, not a brawler.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The child though, he's just laughing. "You know, that guy you have pinned has got powerful allies," he says taking a more serious tone. "The Imperator gave me to him personally." Then, the child holds up a hand and long claws come out from where his fingernails should be. "You wouldn't want to harm the Imperator's favorite pet, would you? I mean, technically, he's my father genetically, but I barely know the man."<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Not gonna happen, buddy." Kipran is not amused. But the word races catches his ear. He'll have to look into that for sure! With a sigh, he decides not to cause trouble with this carnie. "Now, if you'll excuse us...we have someplace to be."

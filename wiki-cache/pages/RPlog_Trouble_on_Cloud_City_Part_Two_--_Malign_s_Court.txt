&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Space around Bespin suddenly shakes with perturbation as the fabric of space shutters with the arrival of the Imperator. The Super Star Destroyer Malevolence appears into real space surrounded by a multitude of Imperial Mark-2 Destroyers and support. Among them was an additional four Interdictor-Class Cruisers, one completely matte black a ship of the Circle of Irluuk. Commanding the fleet was Domicor Honos, promoted to Fleet Admiral and know responsible for the management of Darth Malign's personal defense force. His cool blue eyes examine the tactical data carefully, "Move into position Alpha."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The ships sublight drives activate as they push toward the planet spreading into a defensive net as interdictors take up picket positions to close the system, redirecting traffic to a specific region which is quickly filled with Imperium managed customs forces and ships.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Once the fleet seems to establish its position, fighters begin pouring into space with shuttles and support vessels forming a vanguard of smaller vessels rapidly approaching the Gas Giant and its capital city. Advanced TIEs and Sith Barbs intermingle as they rapidly approach blasting by the Starport as troop transports swing over dropping troopers and commandos as they move to sweep and secure the area, with their numbers and equipment it takes literally no time for the signal of all clear and a host of shuttles move quickly into view landing in rapid succession, even as another handful of Star Destroyers begin arriving in orbit.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;First to arrive are Moffs and their functionaries being scanned, logged and directed into the city. The court would not start immediately, there was time to gamble and boost Johanna's world's economy with some reverie. Staffers, naval officers, troopers, and political figures begin filing through access points into the city in full uniform.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Once they clear three black shuttles move into approach and land in formation in the central part of the port. The doors open as a phalanx of mutant Wookiees exit led by Sentinel carrying ceremonial Sith weaponry and wearing alchemically modified cloaks to resist their quills step out taking vigil over the entrance of the central one. Shortly after Malign moves out flanked by the younger Sith witch, Quaye Oswent and Etiel te Danaan on the other, now wearing his Grand Moff's uniform.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The other shuttles open as a host of cloaked figures exit, the entire Circle of Irluuk was present including their leader wearing a golden trimmed crimson cloak, Sigil Rexton. They pool quietly off to the side and whisper amongst themselves as Malign and his two escorts step now onto the Starport.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The monstrous fleet contingent darkens the skies above Cloud City, the smaller vessels swarming erstwhile trade lanes and disgorging their passengers with impeccable efficiency into the starport and city proper, nearly threatening to overwhelm the settlement with a temporary surge in population -- the likes of which it hasn't seen in decades -- eliciting both excitement and apprehension in the local tradespeople.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What had Johanna expected, exactly, when reaching through spacetime to whisper into the Imperator's ear of Vadim's machinations washing up on her doorstep? A quiet, incognito visit conducted in secret? Perhaps a part of her had... and it's this vestige of foolishness that she now silently chides as she watches the descent of the black shuttles, the familiar energies of Malign and Etiel lighting up in the invisible meshwork of the Force, along with the unwelcome addition of Quaye and the Irluuk acolytes.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Imperator," the Jedi greets as her brother approaches, briefly taking a respectful knee in her elaborate dark garb and rising to reach out her hands to his, "Welcome." A warmer, more familiar -- but silent, lest it be unwanted -- missive reaches her eldest son, his role in the new regime still inspiring a worry she cannot quite squelch.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Anyone who is in the towers of the Yarith Hotel was made well aware of the arrival of the Imperator's fleet merely by turning to see the ships screeching by their windows in the clouds, joining the usual double pod cars and city traffic. Their ominous arrival draws Jessalyn to the view from their own suite there, and she watches stony-faced, draped in a black linen cloak and dress that's embroidered with gold details. The cowl of her cloak is drawn up over her red hair, casting her face in shadow as she presses her hand against the glass.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Basca joins Jessalyn at the window, his own floor length frock done in black and with gold trim along all the edges. "I am not at all surprised." he muses to her. "It would seem the dance is upon me sooner than anticipated." he places a hand at her back and lowers his tone. "If this goes badly...know it was fun and I have made provisions for you to take control of the Isle and Desfar and family will be well taken care of."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The best way to mask the true intent of a visit was to hide behind the elaborate pageantry that the Imperium had to offer, what started as a minor rebellion in the core had sense restructured the failed Galactic Empire into a machine of efficiency and power. Johanna's arrival and knee was noted by everyone, Sigil pausing to look from the group of acolytes to observe the spectacle taking place before them.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Dark Lord nods taking his sister's hands gently, "Grand Moff," He says and then "Sister." His tone was warm, "It appears the management of the Outer Rim territories was trusted to the right person. Peace has reached the galaxy, there are just a few more loose fibers in our tapestry of galactic order."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Quaye, Etiel join the others and enjoy some down time. We have much work to do." He tugs at Johanna suggesting her she stand and join him as they walk into the city. The mutant Wookiees turn and slam their black halberds to the duracrete with a boom before marching around the small group as an escort, the Grand Moff and Malign's Sith alchemy student moving ahead and joining other VIPs and functionaries as they head to the casinos and places of entertainment.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"So, I am told you have a pest problem." Malign says with a smile, "I believe I have brought the appropriate answer." His hand gestures toward the Circle, "You know what to do."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sigil bows and gestures for the eleven cloaked figures around him to disperse, as they seem to melt into crowds and shadow. The city was now under the monitor of Malign's own acolytes and Sith, there wouldn't be any secrets left here when they are done.<br>

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Turning to face Basca, Jessa shakes her head regretfully. "I'm sorry. I should never have brought you here. But don't be so dramatic, all right? I'm not going to let anything happen to you. I think it's time to go..." Johanna may have made an alliance with her Sith brother, but would they really go up against her to get their quarry? "Come on." She drapes a leather satchel over her shoulder and reaches for her lightsaber on beside the bed. "We don't have much time."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Basca gestures a moment and steps into the dressing room and after a few moments emerges buttoning his floor length frock back up. "Remember what I told you about her animosity towards him?" he gestures for the door. "Her brother shares it...so yes they would." he leans in and kisses her on the cheek. "The dress looks good on you...thank you for wearing it." he nods to the door and heads out to meet it all head on.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Johanna moves to link her right arm with Malign's left, hand resting lightly in the crook of his elbow as she accompanies her brother through the streets she now rules in his name, the hem of her finely-pleated black silk dress swishing softly against her ankles. "Pests have a habit of popping up just when you've relaxed your guard," she replies coldly, though the frost isn't directed towards the Imperator, "They scurry out from shadowy little corners, calculating just how much mischief they might make before you develop an appropriate countermeasure." If she's displeased with the prospect of Cloud City being scrutinized by the Circle of Irluuk, her mien betrays nothing of the sort, the brunette's haughty posture and priceless apparel lending her the appearance of a dark liege deigning to show herself to her serfs. "No matter," she continues, "We'll turn the spotlight on them and figure out their game."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Dark Lord walks in step with Johanna linked and nods as she speaks about the situation here in Cloud City, "Agreed." He says his cape flutters in the wind as they are about to enter the city. Operations in your domain are very important to our success in the Middle Rim. Resources to curtail slavery and to maintain our borders with the Republic are necessary, though I intend Moff Tracer will be sent to the New Republic to begin negotiations." He says with a smile turning has gaze away from the room above and back to his sister, "We have much to discuss and little time to act, we will solve your problem and then will need to move to much larger galactic issues as well. We will need to arrange a dinner before court. Your -guests- should be present."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Dark Lord turns to watch as his personal guard take control of customs and additional troop transports can be seen arriving, "We should be able to manage our problems here quickly and move equally fast to bring life back to normal for your people, our people."

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Of course," Jessalyn replies steadily, and touches Basca's cheek with tenderness before she follows him out, pushing back her emotions and extending her senses out, alert for danger as they depart. They make their way out of the city relatively easily, even if the landing pad may not be the ideal destination at the moment, it *is* the only means of leaving. However, when they draw closer, the presence of the Dark Lord and Johanna makes her stop in her tracks before she can see them, and she holds out her hand in warning. "Perhaps... we should wait," she whispers, worried it may already be too late. "Stay close." She grabs his hands and tries to find a place to duck out of the way to watch the procession.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Basca follows suit and uses one hand to unfasten the collar and a hood drops down the back of the coat. "I am sure there is a production to be played out...the performances have been ongoing since his return." he reaches beneath the jacket to make sure the straps were secure, if there was to be a fight he wouldn't go easy. But only the moment would tell.

If the Republic government is still populated by as many mouth-breathers as it once was, they'll lap it right up along with him. He and they deserve one another." Eyelids briefly flutter against a sudden gust that catches the heavy fabric of her fur-collared overcoat, her fingers digging into her brother's elbow as they round a corner and the inevitability of reunion with Jessalyn and Basca -- trying to flee the planet, no less -- becomes reality writ large, the sharp worry of her friend echoing through the Force. He gestures toward the entryway leading back into the city.<br>

"If it's all the same, we have business offworld. But thank you so much for the invitation."

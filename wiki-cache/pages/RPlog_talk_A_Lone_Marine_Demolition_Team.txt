I should have clarified this article more carefully. I was approached by Krieg shortly before the Battle for Coruscant. He requested that a Marine team be present at the battle site to infiltrate and demolition a VSD. Unfortunately, my RP partner was sick at the time and couldn't be present, so I let Vengan go it alone. Krieg and Prospero read each of my poses before I was allowed to post them to the battle scene, as it were, and approved each of them in turn or asked me to retroactively adapt certain poses to make things more ICly believable. The affair was primarily conducted as an experiment to attempt to introduce more Ground Forces into large-scale battles. A number of the poses on this log were in fact Krieg's or Ansforth's at various points- I made minor adaptions to make it read more smoothly. I apologize for any confusion. --[[User:Vengan|Vengan]] 16:25 30 July 06 (UTC)

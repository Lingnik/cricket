This power functions very much like an Emptiness' trance, though is used more often by those swayed by the dark side. Unlike Emptiness', a Jedi in Rage' exudes energy from the dark side. When in a RAGE state, the Jedi is oblivious to events going on around him. It is a way for a Dark Jedi to allow the Dark Side to completely envelope them, thereby gaining strength. While in RAGE, a Dark Jedi still needs Oxygen, food and water. Dark Jedi have been known to die because they could not bring themselves out of a RAGE state.

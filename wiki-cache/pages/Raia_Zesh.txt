'''Raia Zesh''' ([[7 BBY]] - ) is the heiress apparent of a minor [[Kuat|Kuati]] nobleman, who has recently left her home and taken up the life of a dilettante socialite, drifting from world to world but mostly focusing on Ord Mantell.

==Background and Personality==
Raia is an only child, born to a fairly absent-minded father whose only concern was that his little girl be happy when she was growing up. Her father owned a small technical firm that dealt largely in computers and security equipment, and she spent much of her childhood playing with them, learning how they worked, and eventually discovering that she actually had a respectable talent for it. She was quiet and had some disturbing tendencies towards eavesdropping and otherwise putting her nose where it didn't belong--and after some complaints, her Telbun was instructed to see to it that she learned how to maneuver in high Kuati society.

This she picked up quite elegantly, and by the time she was sixteen she seemed to have been completely cured of her tendencies to intrude upon the privacy of others. Even her fondness for electronics and cryptography became something she referred to as a childish hobby--and so her father went back to being unconcerned and she continued in her lessons. Of course, she had merely learned to keep her doings secret, and continued to unnerve the occasional guest with her knowledge of his activities.

Since adulthood, she has become an inquisitive individual, if something of a social chameleon--usually friendly, often amused, and always observant. It's not entirely clear what her motives are just yet, but it seems likely not everything is as it seems.

==Appearance and Equipment==
Raia shows every sign of being concerned about her appearance--pale makeup, red lips, dark eyeshadow, and usually a fairly expensive dress--not so much that she is overdressed, but she does tend to stand out in a crowd. She is not known to carry any equipment, but the many folds in the careful tailoring of her dress could hide just about anything.

'''Raif Avosam''' ([[31 BBY]] - [[28 ABY]]) was a [[Sarian]] from Goshen on [[Mergansar]] who served as the planet's [[Governor]] during the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] and Mergani [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]]. His administration did nothing to stop members of the [[Olumi Osahn]] cult from forcefully expelling non-Sarians from Mergansar. Dossiers delivered to [[InterGalactic News]] conclusively tied Avosam and other members of his government to Olumi Osahn, with a picture of Avosam in their garb publicly broadcast. He was killed when [[Casulmis]] bombed his compound near Vilarasala. Lieutenant Governor [[Lau Saifax]] succeeded him as Acting Governor but was later assassinated himself.

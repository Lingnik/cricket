'''Rapier Squadron''' was a [[TIE Interceptor]] squadron stationed aboard the [[I2SD Malevolent|HIMS ''Malevolent'']] in [[10 ABY]]. Rapier was a basic front-line Interceptor squadron that briefly fell under the command of [[Danik Kreldin]] before his eventual transfer to [[Demon Squadron]].

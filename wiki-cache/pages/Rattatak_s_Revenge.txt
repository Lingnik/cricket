=== Capabilities ===
The ''Revenge'' is a corvette by classification, her size and configuration allow for landing on a variety of spacial and terrestrial terrain - including water, without need of facilities due larger vessels.

Equipped with armor and shields comparable to heavy cruisers and capable of out-pacing Imperial interceptors, the ''Revenge'' carries a compliment of [[Skipray Blastboat|Skipray]]-class bombers and at least one [[Choleric Capra|Gamma-class assault shuttle]].

The addition of Defender-6 heavy ion cannons have over-strained the already heavily taxed Lionshare power grid, leading to intermittent performance of the weapons.

While the ''Revenge ''lacks the firepower of a true warship, she is more than a match for most customs vessels and remains one of the most dangerous commerce raiders currently operating in the galaxy.

=== History ===
Originally known as ''Crimson Lightning, ''Vane acquired the ship in [[24 ABY]] as partial payment for services rendered to the [[New Republic]] in breaking through the [[2015 Caspar Plot|Imperial blockade of Caspar]].

Then known as the ''Crimson Lightning'' Vane renamed the ship later in the year following a refit which added Defender-6 ion cannons to the ship.

=== OOC Notes ===
* Although WEG rules allow for additional weapon mounting, and the Revenge has vacant slots, the MUSH's code cannot currently do this. The Ion-cannons are therefore used on a consent-only basis, their unreliability used to excuse the variance on an IC basis.

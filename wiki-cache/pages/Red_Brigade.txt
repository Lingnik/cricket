The '''Red Brigade''' is a [[New Republic]] paramilitary unit founded by [[New Republic Marine Corps|Marine]] [[Vengan Draelis]] in [[17 ABY]]. The goal of the organization is to supplement the Republic's waning military manpower with outside help, specifically mercenaries. The unit was formed amidst the [[Dacan Civil War]] and was based in [[Reef Home City]]. It first saw action during the [[Third Battle of Mon Calamari]].

'''Red Henly''' was an [[Admiral]] in the [[New Republic]] [[New Republic Navy|Navy]]. He was blamed for the disastrous [[Ambushing the Crusader|mission]] that nearly destroyed [[NRSD Crusader|''Crusader'']] in [[11 ABY]], and subsequently retired from active duty.

The '''''Refrain of Anshalar''''' was a strange and unique vessel of unknown origin or design, which often served as a capital-scale spaceborne home for the [[Jedi Order]]. In [[15 ABY]], it became the sole, mobile headquarters of the Jedi after [[Coruscant]] [[Retribution of Coruscant|fell]] to the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]].

== Description ==

Some two-hundred and fifty meters long, the ''Refrain of Anshalar'' presented a striking profile against the backdrop of space. It was clearly an antiquated design, vaguely similar to what a small dreadnaught might be, but otherwise having no obvious comparison in modern space vessel engineering. A bulbous front section seemed far out of proportion, sloping down plainly uninterrupted until it reached the rear of the ship. The entire ventral section was slightly rounded, but not to the extent of the dorsal half, so the upper overhung the lower by a significant amount with a stylish sharp line. A pair of forward pointing wings swept forward and from the very aft portion of the ship. Also in the very rear was a large bank of three sublight ion drives, each one glowing starry white when the ship was on the move. Overall, the vessel seemed well-maintained, but had more than its fair share of bruises; patches were welded on all over its frame, and battle damage left the majority of its frame blackened and scarred.

== History ==

== Recent Events ==

During the escalation of the [[Second Battle of Nar Shaddaa|Nar Shaddaa Revolution of 15 ABY]], [[Luke Skywalker]] summoned '''''Refrain of Anshalar''''' to the Smugglers Moon, to serve as a mobile platform for [[Falcon Squadron]]'s reconnaissance sweeps. Command was given to [[Rook Barnsdale]] for the duration of the operation.

The '''Renaissance of Innovation''' refers one of a number of Caspian events or assets.

Examples include:
* The [[First Renaissance of Innovation]] - a [[Caspian Democratic Union]] development programme under the administration of [[Presav]] [[Lyr Dunwell]].
* The [[Second Renaissance of Innovation]] - a [[Caspian Union]] development programme under the administration of [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]].
* The ''[[CMS Renaissance of Innovation]]'' - an [[Admiral Roj-class Battleship|Admiral Roj-Class Battleship]] named after the First Renaissance of Innovation.
* [[RPlog:Renaissance of Innovation]] - the scene where Presav Tarasar introduced the Second Renaissance of Innovation.
* [[Report:Renaissance of Innovation]] - the press coverage of the scene where Presav Tarasar introduced the Second Renaissance of Innovation.

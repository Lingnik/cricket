... and in other news from [[Corellia]] the cargo ship Tatooine Sands crashed onto docking pad 119 at the public spaceport. 

Multiple fire and rescue crews were called to the scene to assist with evacuating the passengers and crew of the cargo ship. 

Civilians on the scene assisted with the fire fighting endeavor. 

No fatalities were reported but the ship was a total loss. 

A segment of the spaceport is closed off while the wreck of the ship is being examined...

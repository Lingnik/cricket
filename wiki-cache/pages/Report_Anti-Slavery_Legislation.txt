The [[New Republic Senate|Senate'' passed today on a 4:1 margin a bill that authorizes the [[New Republic Military]] to carry out action against all vessels, organizations, and related facilities involved with, or facilitating, the galactic slave trade. All New Republic businesses caught being involved with slavery will have all goods and property so used confiscated and the personnel imprisoned for no less than ten galactic standard years. The bill passed with support of the pacifism-inclined faction in the Senate, while splitting the hawkish wing of the Senate, with some of them not desiring to divert resources from the war with the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]], while the doves wished to focus more on the goals of the Republic instead of the galactic civil war. The bill's author, the senator from [[Kashyyyk]], gave the following statement: "Today, the New Republic commits itself to its ideal of liberty for all people of the galaxy, both those free and in chains. Today we take action to liberate the downtrodden and crush those who would benefit from forced labor. To those who carry on this abominable trade, cease now or face the righteous fury of liberty." The bill has been sent to [[Chief of State]] [[Leia Organa Solo|Leia Organa's]] desk for her approval or veto.

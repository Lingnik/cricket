Breaking News: Tonight [[Chief of State]] [[Leia Organa Solo|Leia Organa]] has signed the Galactic Anti-Slavery Act into law with immediate effect. Notices have been sent to planets and systems outside of the [[New Republic|New Republic's]] control via diplomatic channels of the act's promulgation informing them of the New Republic's hardened resolve to end slavery, and of the consequences if they do not cease all slaving operations and commercial operations with slavers immediately. Those consequences being prison time of no less than ten standard galactic years, goods and properties confiscated, and NR forces are authorized to act against all operations involved in the slave trade, including facilitation.

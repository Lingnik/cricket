This is Loy Marin of IGNews bringing you an update on the crisis at Dac. While relief efforts continue on many of the floating cities there has been an interesting development with the Domed City of Aquarius.

Shortly after hostilities between the Mon Calamari and Quarren began, contact was lost with the city. Normal communications became sporadic and travel to the underwater city was extremely hazardous as an previously unreported outbreak of hostilities took place under the seas of Dac.

Sources indicate that access to Aquarius has been severely limited as the Quarren population has taken over control of the city and its facilities. Reports are also coming in stating that even the Whaladon population has started to be turned away from the undersea sanctuary.

Even more curious is the sudden appearance of a floating makeshift structure on the ocean surface over Aquarius. Although Quarren forces have been turning away attempts to approach both the city and floating structure, we've been able to secure some orbital holoimages from anonymous sources on Dac.

''Holo footage is shown of what appears to be multiple standard freighter containers with light beacons affixed to them floating on the surface. Jutting from the perimeter of the containers is a network of improvised moorings for submersible vehicles.''

These same sources have informed IGNews that this makeshift structure seems to be designed to accommodate multiple light freighters to ferry supplies to the city's inhabitants. If this is indeed the case, there seems little indication that the hostilities between the native populations are preparing to cease as this is a sign the Quarren population is further isolating themselves from the floating cities that dot the surface of Dac. We will continue to cover this development and all other news of the civil war on Dac. For IGNews, this is Loy Marin.

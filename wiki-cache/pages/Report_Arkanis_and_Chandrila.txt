Imperial Press Corps Official Briefing
Location: Grand Atrium, [[Selene]]
Presiding: Zephara Val'Riin, Press Secretary to [[Grand Admiral]] [[Axel Vichten]]

Cameras activate as Zephara Val'Riin strides toward the podium beneath the [[Imperium|Imperium's]] silver-on-black banners. The chamber is silent but tense, lined with reporters and delegates from across the Core and Inner Rim. Her presence alone stills the room.

"This address will be brief. Precision is the courtesy of power."

A pause. She makes deliberate eye contact with three different networks, including [[InterGalactic News|IGNews]], her silver eyes glinting beneath the low atrium lights. "[[Chandrila]]." A single word, delivered like a verdict.

"We are aware of the chaos engulfing the capital of [[Hanna City]]. We have reviewed the circulating holofootage. We have noted the accusations. And we are investigating."

She leans in, fractionally, as if daring the room to question her. "The [[Grand Admiral]] is personally reviewing the claims regarding these so-called 'signed orders'-a term being used irresponsibly and without substantiation. These documents, if they exist, will be scrutinized. If they are forged, we will know. If they are real, we will know. The Imperium does not deal in anonymous accusations shouted over protest chants and fruit carts."

She offers a faint, poisonous smile. "As for the implication that our Imperator-[[Tyler Damion|Damion the First]], Sovereign of [[Selene]], Protector of the Imperium-would involve himself in such pedestrian subterfuge... I remind you: the Imperator does not whisper in alleyways. He declares. He commands."

She pivots seamlessly, tone now coolly clinical. , "The [[:starwars:Arkanis sector/Legends|Arkanis Sector]]."

"The Grand Admiral has been apprised of the agricultural failures on [[:starwars:New Ator|New Ator]] and [[:starwars:Vasch|Vasch]]. Though these worlds lie beyond the Imperium's direct jurisdiction, the reports of a genetically engineered blight raise significant concern. As such, Grand Admiral Vichten has authorized immediate defensive measures to protect the integrity of Imperium borders."

"Effective immediately, interdiction fields are being deployed along all major trade routes connecting Arkanis-sector systems to Imperium space. No cargo, vessel, or biological material will pass through without undergoing full decontamination and inspection protocols, supervised by Imperium biohazard teams and enforcement agencies."

She gives a meaningful glance to the cluster of Outer Rim correspondents. "These are not punitive actions. They are preventative. We will not allow biological sabotage to drift unexamined into our worlds. If [[Johanna Siri te Danaan|Johanna te Danaan]] or any recognized authority in the Arkanis Sector requests assistance, the Grand Admiral stands ready to coordinate scientific support, food security logistics, and defensive advisories."

Zephara takes a moment to breath, to transition. "The Imperium does not posture. We act. And in times of ambiguity, one truth remains constant: while the Republic drowns in denial and deflection, the Imperium stands resolute.")]

She tilts her head slightly, inviting questions. One brave reporter steps forward. "Press Secretary Val'Riin, what assurances can you offer to independent systems that these interdiction zones won't become blockades?"

Zephara's tone shifts-warmer, but unmistakably edged. "Assurances? Your safety is the assurance. The only things denied passage through Imperium space are threats, lies, and plagues. If that's an inconvenience to smugglers and saboteurs, I weep for them. Quietly. At night."

She offers no further invitations. With one fluid motion, she steps back from the podium and vanishes behind the obsidian banners.

As the scene gets to it's climactic ending, during the final episode of the latest top rated mini-series of the season, the video phases to red and the IGN banner crosses the screen followed by the logo for an important 'News Alert'. A beautifully dressed blonde woman comes into view, holding the microphone close as she begins to speak.

"This just in, The IGN news network has just received word that a huge change in the political structure of the Empire has taken place. It would seem Bailey Laran, former planetary governor of Corellia, has been voted in a unanimous vote to the position of Sector Moff. With this position, She will gain control of three Major Systems: Selene, Corellia, and Coruscant."

The woman looks to the side, as if reading from a card. She takes a breath and continues, "We have heard news that Imperial Moff Laran will be making a speech soon to address such topics as the worsening criminal elements within the three systems, the New Republic, efforts to improve the situation in the Imperial sectors with tax cuts, and even new policies affecting diplomatic relations. Rest assured, IGN will be there as this story develops. Now back to your regularly scheduled program."

The IGN logo flashes once more and then fades to a black screen as the final credits of the mini-series scroll down as the news flash consumed the last of the show.

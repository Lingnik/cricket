{{IGNpost|author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[33 ABY]]|topic=[[Biscuit Baron]]|anchors=None|headline=Biscuit Buyout|synopsis=A live press conference on [[Etti IV]] announces that [[Biscuit Baron]] has been bought out by the [[Golden Bantha Group]].}}

The logo of [[InterGalactic News]] is displayed on the screen with a voice over announcing, "We are interrupting your normal broadcasting for a special news conference from Authority Headquarters on [[Etti IV]]." The screen soon shifts to a press room with the [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] logo on the wall and a podium in front of it. The room is full of various seated reporters.

After a few moments later a middle-aged [[Human]] woman, with red hair and wearing a yellow suit, walks to the podium and begins to speak, "Thank you all for coming. I am Baroness Domina Tagge and I am coming to speak to you today on behalf of the [[Direx Board (CSA)|Executive Directorship Board]] of the Corporate Sector Authority. As you are all know doubt aware [[Biscuit Baron]], the galaxy's largest restaurant chain suffered from widespread worker strikes last year at locations throughout [[Imperium]], [[New Republic|Republic]], [[Caspian Union|Caspian]], and [[Independent]] space.

The efficacy of the Corporate Sector Authority, as a single unified corporation, to handle this crisis was hampered by the strained relationship the CSA has with various other galactic governments at this time. A matter of great personal frustration as Biscuit Baron has been a subsidiary of the Tagge Restaurant Association. Ultimately the Direx Board engaged the services of an outside consultancy firm to develop solutions to our labour problem. Through their efforts Biscuit Baron managed to resolve the strikes and profits are now higher than they had been prior to the strike.

This has only been a temporary measure with our analysts predicting the current corporate structure will hamper efforts to maintain profitability and effective labour relations. However, the Corporate Sector Authority has received a bid from the consultancy firm to buyout Biscuit Baron and take the company independent. After months of negotiations I am happy to report that the Direx Board has approved the buyout and Biscuit Baron is now wholly owned by the [[Golden Bantha Group]]. It is now my pleasure to this press conference over to the new Chief Executive Officer of Biscuit Baron, Baroness [[Atsvara Tarasar]]."

As Baroness Tagge steps away from the podium, the podium itself begins to sink into the floor, when it stops moving it is at two-thirds its previous height. Three people then step onto the stage. The first is a Human male roughly twenty years old and wearing multi-colour baffleweave blazer. The next is a [[Pantoran]] girl roughly sixteen years old and wearing a yellow vine-silk kimono with black and red embroidery. Both stand off to the side while the third, the eleven year old Atsvara Tarasar, walks towards Baroness Tagge and shakes her hand before standing behind the now shorter podium.

In most respects Atsvara's outward appearance marks her as a [[Firrerreo]] girl. She has gold-coloured skin with onyx eyes. Her hair is two-toned, being mostly black with silver strands, which she keeps braided. The one sign obvious sign of his mixed heritage are scarlet coloured Sar Spots. She stands 1.48 metres tall and is of a slight build.

Atsvara's outfit shows telltale signs of her close connexion with the [[Silverwood House of Fashion]] both in terms of design details and material elements. Her formfitting gown is made of black cyrene silk, with a large bantha embroidered in gold under her bust line. On the end of each sleeve is the symbol of her house, a dragon whose breath forms a flower, embroidered in scarlet. Over this she wears a scarlet septsilk cape with a Cantonican golden silk fringe. On top of the cape she wears a white glossy Tyrian shimmersilk tippet with a beryllius trim. A miniature Hanna pendant rests over the tippet. The final flourish is a crescent shaped metallic kokoshnik covering her much of her head. It is made primarily of red xonolite with detailing in gold, silver, and obsidian.

The new CEO begins to speak, "I want to start off by thanking Baroness Tagge for her warm introduction. I know she cares very deeply about the Biscuit Baron brand and I want to make a promise to her and all of you watching that it will remain a brand that reflect positively on the rich legacy from which it has derived and myself and the Golden Bantha Group have and will treat it with the reverence it deserves. I have a short presentation prepared and then I will be happy to take some questions from the audience.

Biscuit Baron is a strong, respected, and recognisable brand. In a way I am surprised at just how much that truly is the case. Our COO and I were recently meeting with an old friend who telling us how much she wanted to see a Biscuit Baron franchise on her own world. Not everyone likes the idea of a chain restaurant and our friend's world is not a place usually very happy with a corporate touch so we found it very encouraging that there was such enthusiasm for the brand in spite of that.

We are not trying to reinvent the hyperdrive. For the most part the experience for our customers is going to be the same tomorrow as it was yesterday. The Biscuit Baron logo will still be based on Baron Orman Tagge. Gold, white, black, and red will still be the company colours. Prices will be staying the same and your favourite menu items are not going anywhere. We are planning to introduce a few new offerings over the coming months and we will be embarking on a new marketing campaign with the tag line, 'Taste the Biscuit.'

I had hoped to inaugurate this new era for Biscuit Baron with a series of Jolly Meal toys honour the legacy of House Tagge in the forms of Orman, Cassio, Silas, Ulric, and Domina Tagge. Our marketing department assures me that such toys will not do well with our target demographic." Atsvara throws up her hands, "I just don't understand, I think they would be super fun. However, we have decided to go a different route." She then takes a toy out of her pocket and places it on the podium. Then taking a remote control off the podium she presses a button that causes a projection screen to come down from the ceiling and in front of the CSA logo.

Atsvara presses another button on the remote and the screen then displays a large image of the toy in question which is clearly modelled on the [[E5 Baron Droid]]. "Market research says younglings love [[Droid|droids]]. I know I do. The choice of this droid, however, goes deeper than sharing the name Baron. In the course of negotiating the end of the strike we discovered a fair amount of criminal activity associated with some of our local franchise operators. Normally the corporate reaction to such information would be to cancel franchise contracts and maybe, maybe alerting local authorities.

Now I told you that the customer experience at Biscuit Baron is not going to change much. What is going to change is the employee experience. Whether you are working on [[Selene]] or [[Ord Mantell]] or [[Caspar]] or even here on Etti IV, whether you are working at a location owned directly by Biscuit Baron or operated by a local franchisee, you have the right to expect that we at corporate have your back. This is not a platitude or empty promise.

Our corporate security department recently became aware of a couple of franchises in the Outer Rim in the Prefsbelt and Fath Sectors operated by the Nova Syndicate as front for sentient trafficking. Following the signing of our buyout agreement I ordered our security teams to liberate the locations in question."

Another button is pressed on the remote control bringing up footage of a raid. Identified as being on Ord Mynock. A team of Baron Droids painted in Biscuit Baron corporate colours and seemingly being lead by an astromech droid captioned as 'Executive Vice President [[Cricket]]' breaching a door marked 'Employees Only.' The astromech can be heard to say in Binary, "Suck Electrum Cockgobbler" as he deploys an [[electrum]] plated taser and tasers the first gang member he encounters. After a few more gang members get shot by the droids the camera zooms in some teenage girls who appear as if they were being forced to sell off menu Jolly Meals.

Atsvara presses the remote button again with footage of a different raid, this one identified as being on Ord Cantrell. This time the team of Baron Droids is lead by a kitbashed [[V1C3]] droid wearing a yellow pinstripe suit and carrying a blaster rifle. When the door is breached the camera picks up several people with golden skin who look malnourished and restrained. A few gang members can also be seen hastily trying to grab and wield their blasters. The droid in the pinstripe suit points his blaster at the head of one of the gang members as he goes for his blaster saying, "Don't try it meatbag." The people held hostage look to be quite scared, likely as much from the droids as anything. 

A few moments later as the gang members are taken into custody a few people emerge from behind the droids and begin to do undo the restraints of those held hostage. Most of this group seem to be golden-skinned as well though one is clearly a [[Horansi|Mashi Horansi]] male. As Atsvara turns off the screen, the broadcast camera pans back away from her and to one of the reporters in the front row, the IGN anchor [[Brackett Benduday]]. The golden-skinned anchor appears to have been emotionally effected by what she just saw.

Atsvara turns back to her audience and smiles, "If you fuck with our workers, you fuck with me and my friends. You don't want to fuck with me and my friends. I will now be taking some questions." She looks out at the assembled reporters, who seem at first to be so surprised be the events they have just witnessed that their questions are not immediately forthcoming.

There is an awkward pause as the reporters fidget in their seats, none of them quite sure what to ask or at least how to ask it. One of the reporters, though it is not clear exactly which one blurts out, "How?" The Human male on the stage responds back, "Credits, lots and lots of credits." Atsvara then adds, "I am sorry I did not catch who asked that. You are going to have to be more specific with your question, but Lon is probably right."

The room goes silent again with no one elaborating on the first question. Eventually Brackett raises a hand with Atsvara gesturing to call on the anchor. She somewhat cryptically and hesitantly says, "Tanaka?" Atsvara smiles, "Safe, physically unharmed, recuperating at a safe house." She then tosses a small communications device to Brackett. "Next question."

"Tony Bellaroso, Freedom Galactic News. Is it true that Biscuit Baron is planning a major expansion in the Mid Rim?" As the question is asked the kimono wearing Pantoran steps towards the podium with Atsvara saying, "I am going to let our Chief Operating Officer [[Veruca Violet]] field this question."

Stepping up to the podium as Atsvara takes a step to the side, Veruca responds, "I am glad you asked that question Tony. And yes we are Mid Rim and Outer Rim in fact. In spite of being the largest fast-casual restaurant chain in the galaxy, our expansion has been lagging behind some of our competitors in recent years and we feel that there has been a lot of missed opportunities on the Rim. We will make lists of new locations available once we are able confirm the local permits and vet the potential franchisees so we do not have any repeats of the Nova Syndicate issue. I personally have had some very productive conversations recently with stakeholders on [[Charros IV]], [[Dreven]], [[Ixin]], [[Kessel]], and [[Mutanda]], so I imagine they will be among some of the markets."

Stepping back to the podium Atsvara quickly adds, "There are still a lot of worlds that don't see Biscuit Baron as right for them, and I can respect that. I remain personally disappointed at the lack of availability of Bantha Breakfast Biscuits on [[Tatooine]]. It feels like a missed opportunity. Certainly nobody wants to see their own culture be diluted by the juggernaut of convenience but I think there is something comforting about being able to go from one end of the galaxy to the other and knowing you can find a Bantha Breakfast Biscuit and Blue Sauce." She pauses for a moment then adds, "Next Question."

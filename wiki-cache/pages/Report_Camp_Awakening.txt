A news report is broadcast throughout Imperial Space, featuring IGN's Imperial Correspondent, Timo Droja. "I'm Timo Droja, coming to you live from Coronet City." The camera pans out to show the Ocean Bluff, just south of the famous Gold Beaches. "I'm here near the Gold Beaches of Coronet City, where a new facility has been constructed over the remains of the Kreldin Estate."

The camera shifts to show a large, military-style gateway. Three massive flags are flown at the gate, representing the Galactic Empire, COMPNOR, and its famed Sub-Adult Group. "Camp Awakening," says Timo, "constructed over the remains of a traitorous family estate, is a sign of hope and atonement for Imperial Citizens on Corellia and throughout the Slice." The camera changes again to show Timo, walking down a roadway toward the gateway of the massive camp. "This facility, part of the proud Sub-Adult Group of COMPNOR, will offer strong education and discipleship for the young people of the Empire." He stops and motions toward the grounds as a large shuttle makes way to land beyond the perimeter. "Fresh recruits are already coming in as we speak."

A new camera angle from inside the facility appears, with a small Imperial Insignia at the lower corner. As the shuttle doors open, a collection of well-groomed young men and women, ranging from twelve to sixteen standard years, begins to walk out, rounded up by a crisply dressed ISB Officer. "By turning the former Kreldin Estate into a facility of proper training, the Emperor has once again proven his relentless determination to cultivate a peaceful, strong, and secure environment for the citizens of the Empire. In these young people lies our future, and our security." The camera switches back to show Timo Droja, who smiles proudly. "Live from Camp Awakening, I'm Timo Droja. Glory to the Empire.

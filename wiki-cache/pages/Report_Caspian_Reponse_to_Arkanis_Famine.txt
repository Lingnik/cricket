{{IGNpost|author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[34 ABY]]|topic=Famine|anchors=[[Brackett Benduday]]|headline=Caspian Response to Arkanis Famine|synopsis=Coverage of the funding for famine relief to the [[:starwars:Arkanis sector/Legends|Arkanis Sector]] approved by the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] [[Union Council]] and measures being undertaken by the Union to deliver it.}}

The [[Biscuit Baron]] restaurant in the Neon District of [[Imperial City (Selene)|Imperial City]] on Selene appears on the screen with the camera slowly panning out to the familiar presence of the corporation's golden-skinned pre-teen CEO and [[Atsvara Tarasar|hype girl]]. "Time is running out for your chance for one of the special Sidious cards in our Secret Sidious promotion, only 50 of 2,000 remain claimed, come in now and don't miss out on your chance."

Clad in a black business suit, she pauses for dramatic effect, "Speaking of time running out, the is only limited time to respond to the famine that has hit the [[:starwars:Arkanis sector/Legends|Arkanis Sector]]. Every being in the galaxy should be able to count on their next meal. For the rest of this month Biscuit Baron will donate one credit for EVERY [[:starwars:Bantha Breakfast Biscuit|Bantha Breakfast Biscuit]] we sell, galaxy wide. Get in on this opportunity quick before the accounting department rises in revolt."  The image scrolls out a bit with a voice over stating, "Taste the Biscuit."

The screen next moves to show the image of [[Union Hall]] in [[Plaxton]] on [[Caspar]] before zooming out to an [[InterGalactic News]] studio with the [[Firrerreo]] anchorwoman [[Brackett Benduday]] sitting behind the anchor's desk. "This is Brackett Benduday with news out of Caspar. In response to the news of famine in the Arkanis Sector, the [[Union Council]] met in emergency session to discuss the situation. A number of Councillors were away from Caspar and had to attend remotely, with Councillor [[Zaalbacca]] of [[Anchorron]], the ranking member of the Council chairing the meeting in the physical absence of [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar|Tarasar]] and Vice Presav [[Cas Mos|Mos]]."

Footage of a Union Council meeting is shown with a number of the members include Crestian Tarasar and Cas Mos in holographic form. The holographic Crestian speaks, "Just prior to this meeting I spoke with Talia Novar, our Ambassador to [[Tatooine]] and she has extended our full promise of aid, both agricultural and investigative, to the authorities in the Arkanis Sector."

Back in the studio Brackett reports, "Caspian Minister of Agriculture Lilith Bergen of Spittsbury introduced a bill which would give her office 6 million credits to pay for food, transportation, agriculture scientists to help investigate the source of the blight, and aid to refugees in the sector. The measure received impassioned support particularly from Councillor Sere Alsek of Porthumberlon, the Environment Minister and Councillor Niklos Wortham of Winton, the Refugee Minister. There was some push back on the overall amount of credits being discussed notably from Councillor [[Thomas Keyyaw]] of Lansdowne who abstained on the final vote, which passed unanimously otherwise. The meeting was closed following the vote.

Various Caspian entities were quick to announce resources they would be sending. [[The Mission]], the charity run by Presav Tarasar announced that they  would for the time being be devoting their Caspian Agriculture Fund to the purchase of food Caspian farms to send to their office in [[Mos Eisley]] on Tatooine. In their statement on the matter they highlighted their gratitude to [[Warlord]] [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]] who was one of the main donors to The Mission when it was formed.

Councillor [[Sirlahn Inglet]] of [[Kichnar Orbital Station|Kichnar]], the Minister of Science and Technology announced that a team from the [[Groton Sciences Institute]] was being assembled and would be send to the sector as soon as possible. No word on whether the [[CMS Renaissance of Innovation]] would be conveying them as been common in previous Caspian aid missions.

In response to our inquiries the Caspian Ministry of Agriculture has indicated that the export to source the food Caspar, [[Mergansar]], and [[Mikassa]] which are the Union primary agriculture exports. Caspar was noted particularly as their farms produce a great surplus owing to the drastic reduction in Caspar's population from the [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|Sariphage pandemic]] and Caspar has traditionally had a very strong fishing industry. The ministry also indicate the would explore sourcing food from [[Rauther]] if the demand continued and they might explore offering from [[Kasputin Yar]], though much of the agriculture industry there does not suit Human appetites which has limited their export market in past.

This has also put a spotlight on Councillor Niklos Wortham, normally one not to grab headlines, his roles as Refugee Minister, Chairman of the [[Krittain|Krittish]] Committee for Refugees and member of the Board of Directors of The Mission, have seemingly catapulted him to the forefront of Union efforts. Union worlds have a strong history in dealing with a massive influx of refugees following the [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|Mergani pogroms]] with which displaced 300 million people from Mergansar. 

Of that 300 million, roughly 100 million settled on Caspar following the end of the Sariphage pandemic, 35 million on [[Krittain Major]], 20 million on Kasputin Yar, 15 million on Rauther, 10 million on [[Ixin]], 5 million each on Kichnar Orbital Station, [[Krittain Minor]], and Mikassa, another 5 million spread out on various minor worlds in [[Caspian Sector|Caspian Space]], with the remaining 100 million leaving Caspian Space altogether.

Wortham was at the forefront of helping those in what was then the Krit Union get settled, and these massive numbers I just talked about are why the Union has Refugee Ministry to begin with. Though it is hard to project what sort of refugee crisis we might be seeing in the Arkanis Sector now, we don't have solid figures even of the populations of [[:starwars:New Ator|New Ator]] and [[:starwars:Vasch|Vasch]], famines are one of the number one causes of people feeling as refugees. The Caspians for their part seem to be pulling out all the stops to prepare for these contingencies whether it is support to be sent to the Arkanis sector or even possibly taking in refugees fleeing from the region. We will bring you more on this crisis as it develops."

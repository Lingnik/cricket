{{IGNpost|author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[32 ABY]]|topic=Caspian Wargames|anchors=[[Brackett Benduday]]|headline=Caspian Wargames Start|synopsis=[[InterGalactic News]] reports on the first exercise of the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] Wargames at [[Charros IV]].}}

The image of the [[Xi Char]] [[Cathedral Factory]] of Xi Can appears on the screen before the camera pans out to an anchor's desk that appears to have been placed on a military base as starfighters can be scene landing in the distance. At the desk is the [[Firrerreo]] economist and journalist Professor [[Brackett Benduday]].

"I am Brackett Benduday and this is [[InterGalactic News]] coming to you on location from Xi Can Base on [[Charros IV]]. A little while ago the first exercise in what has been planned to be several days worth of wargames here at Charros IV concluded in orbit above.

The exercise, which has been described as a simulation of a marauder attack upon a large-scale natural disaster related evacuation has been deemed an official success. And everyone we have had a chance to speak to about it so far seems to be in high spirits after the mock engagement.

What came as surprise though were just who was leading the attack. Reports indicate that legendary starfighter pilot and Imperium Inquisitor [[Axel Vichten]] has accepted the position of [[Marshal]] of the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]]. This would not be Star Marshal Vichten's first time in the [[Caspian Defence Force]], the [[Noques Rasala]] resident and a leader among its faith community, has served a command officer in the reserves many years ago.

Another surprise was the announcement that former Caspian First Lady Doctor [[Lynae Cassius]] has also reenlisted in the [[Caspian Navy]] at the rank of Admiral, and joined Marshal Vichten in leading the simulated attack. The defence side was not without it surprises too with former [[Presav]] and starfighter pilot herself [[Elana Tracer]], currently [[Grand Moff]] of [[Selene]] and [[Athaniss]] serving as the overall commander of defending forces for the exercise.”"

The broadcast the begins to show an image of striking workers in front of a Biscuit Baron. The image seems to be generated for the broadcast and not actually project at the anchor's location.

"In other news the strike at [[Biscuit Baron|Biscuit Barons]] around the galaxy is now in its third week, with no immediate end in strike. While news reports that workers in locations like [[Caspar]] and Selene are back in negotiations with management, the strike continues to spread to other worlds, with [[Trandosha]], [[:starwars:Virujansi/Legends|Virujansi]], [[:starwars:Zeltros/Legends|Zeltros]] the latest worlds to see picket lines."

Next the screen switches to an image of an older Human male, some viewers might recognise him as previous panelist on Brackett's news broadcasts.

"Finally on a sadder not, [[Charrs Prego-Roamanoff]], Professor Emeritus of History at [[Plaxton]] University, and repeat guest on our programme has died. According to a statement from his widow, Professor Prego-Roamanoff passed away earlier today at [[Twin Suns Hospital]] in [[Trinumvira]] after a short illness. He would have turned 77 on Primeday.

In a statement released by Presav [[Crestian Tarasar|Tarasar]] just before our broadcast, the Presav remarks quote that "Charrs will be remembered fondly as keen academic mind, but more importantly a friend to labour and someone who lived life to the fullest. We are saddened by his passing but he leaves behind a proud legacy." We here at IGN would like to pass along our own condolences to  his family and friends. He will be missed."

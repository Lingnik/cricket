Breaking: [[Hanna City]], [[Chandrila]]

Protests on [[Chandrila]] have swelled in recent days, as acts of civil disobedience have escalated in the planet's capital. We go now to correspondent Lynn Jerrel for a brief report.

"Tensions are rising in [[Hanna City]], after police crackdowns following the discovery of several bodies with what appear to be lists of names of persons involved with the organized protests demanding secession from the New Republic."

In the background a large protest can be seen, with various signs written in [[Galactic Basic Standard|Basic]] and High Galactic with slogans such as, "Chandrila will be FREE!", "The [[Imperium]] is not your friend!", "[[Leia Organa Solo|Organa]] loves [[Tyler Damion|Malign]]", "[[New Republic]] is Old Tyranny".

"As you can see behind me, the anti New Republic and, surprisingly, anti Imperium sentiment has increased the week, with no end in sight. With me here is an organizer of one of the pro-Chandrilan movements, who's asked to remain anonymous. Sir, can you give us some insight as to the recent increase in the protests here on Chandrila?"

A man dressed in typical Chandrilan attire, his face obscured by dark goggles and a wrap, enters the scene as the holo-camera zooms out and pans slight to the left.

"The Chandrilan government needs to act! The New Republic is refusing to hear us and now the Imperium is getting involved. It's all a scam! Even Malign wants us dead."

"Those are some bold claims; some might even say outrageous. Why is it that you think the Imperator wants you dead?"

"You know those bodies they've found this week? The ones with the kill lists on them? They were signed orders! I've seen it myself! And if Malign wants us dead, then he's gotta be helping the New Republic. It's the Empire all over again. The only way out is true independence. No Republic. No Imperium. Only Chandrila!"

Chants of 'Only Chandrila' start, spurred on by the interviewee as crowd pushes forward towards the embassy district.

"We are still waiting for an official response from Chandrilan and New Republic authorities and we'll keep you updated as information comes in. For [[InterGalactic News|IGNews]], this is Lynn Jerrel."

The final segment of the day's [[InterGalactic News|IGNews]] holocast:

"A sudden and unexplained disruption to the subspace beacon network has plunged a swath of the Outer Rim into silence, with no transmissions reaching or leaving a region spanning an estimated 200 square light-years.

The affected zone, bounded by the systems of [[:starwars:Arkanis system/Legends|Arkanis]], [[:starwars:Vactooine system/Legends|Vactooine]], and [[Tatooine]], has effectively become a communications dead zone, according to authorities with the Hydian Subspace Authority. The incident occurred without warning late last cycle, severing traffic control feeds, diplomatic relays, and emergency beacons across multiple trade and refugee corridors. Officials confirm that subspace relays within the triangle have gone offline simultaneously, making a technical fault unlikely.

The blackout comes at a time of deepening instability in the region. A growing refugee crisis, driven by failed harvests and escalating raids against frontier colonies, has left [[Governor|governors]] of neighboring systems struggling to maintain order. Relief shipments have already been diverted or delayed, and security patrols report ghost signals and unexplained disappearances near the comms boundary. Communications beyond the blackout perimeter remain impossible.

There is no confirmed claim of responsibility, though intelligence sources suggest the disruption bears signs of a deliberate strategic denial operation, possibly linked to growing power struggles among Outer Rim factions."

The screen is filled with images of various worlds in the Corellia Sector. The camera zooms out to show an [[InterGalactic News|IGN]] anchor desk in front of the images with a golden-skinned woman sitting behind the desk, to her left is a human male in green Ottegan silk suit.

"I am [[Brackett Benduday]] coming to you live with an exclusive interview with the newly appointed Prime Minister of the [[Corellian Confederation]], [[Dash Blazer]]. The former [[Senator]] representing the [[Corellian System]] in the [[New Republic Senate]] is now leading a government representing a couple dozen systems in the Corellian Sector. Thank you for coming on our programme Prime Minister Blazer."

"Thank you for having me on the programme Brackett, but please call me Dash. It has been a long road getting from where we were nearly two years ago to here. We Corellians are an independent mind people, but obviously we have sadly fallen under the yoke of [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] and Republic in the past. It has been hard work to extricate ourselves from that played out binary. Then you throw in the logistics of divorcing our assets from the [[New Republic]] and finding that we have neighbours who wished the same and integrating them into our efforts."

"Well Dash it sounds like it is an exciting time for [[Corellia]]. Obviously there is a lot that goes into building or rebuilding multi-system governments, just look at the slog it has been for the Caspians. Have you found much push back against your efforts."

"Well like I said Corellians are an independent minded people and given the fact the only fighting the Imperium and New Republic seems to do is in the press, most our citizens have found little reason to need to hitch ourselves to one or the other. Building a sector government has been a little more of a challenge some in our system did not see the need for it. Of course the Imperium and New Republic like to send their agents who acted like a bunch of spiced up missionaries trying to convince folks that theirs is the true way.

It is about finding the right balance. To the Empire and Republic, Corellia has been a feather in their cap, but have either really care about what is in our best interests. That being said a single world going it alone, or a single system with multiple habitable worlds such as ours is not in a good position to make the impact it needs on the galactic scale. As a sector, however, we are well poised to defend ourselves and make the trade deals we need to revitalise our economy.

So you can say now is our coming out. The Corellian Confederation and [[Corellian Engineering Corporation]] is hosting delegations from several powers in the coming days and week with the aim of signing new trade agreements. In fact one of the delegates is holding a charity event later this evening."

"Well thank you for time Dash, we know you have a busy schedule. Later on tonight on the Muun Money Minute we will be discussing some of the trade deals you might expect to see on the horizon for Corellia and some mysterious moves being made by TaggeCo."

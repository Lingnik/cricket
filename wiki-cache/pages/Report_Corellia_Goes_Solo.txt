{{IGNpost|author=[[Johanna Siri te Danaan]]|date=[[30 ABY]]|topic=Corellian Independence|anchors=[[Brackett Benduday]]|headline=Corellia declares independence from the New Republic.|synopsis=Senator Dash Blazer of Corellia announces that the Corellian System will be declaring independence from the New Republic following certain revelations about Republic leadership.}}

The anchor desk of [[InterGalactic News|IGN]] news sits empty as an image of [[Corellia]] is displayed behind it. Hurriedly a certain golden-skinned anchor rushes behind the desk and begins to address the camera.

"....This is [[Brackett Benduday]] with some very late breaking news from Corellia"

Seated at the middle of the lower dais of Corona House's packed Assembly Chamber, [[Senator]] [[Dash Blazer]] wears a solemn expression as he leans fractionally forward to speak into the microphone. Roughly thirty standard years of age, his still-youthful features belie the experience he has accrued in the halls of legislation.

"We are shocked and disheartened to discover the extent of the corruption that has infiltrated the highest levels of [[New Republic]] leadership under the pretence of reform. The man calling himself Marius Starker, who rose to the position of Interim Chancellor, was in fact none other than the mass murderer [[Basca Starker]], the man who engineered the genocide of the Caspian people. Two point eight billion lives -- ninety percent of Caspar's population -- were lost to the whims of a single madman. The galaxy thought this madman to be dead, his body delivered to Caspar by an alleged relative named Marius Starker, but the truth was far more sinister: it was a deception. Basca Starker was not dead."

His expression darkens momentarily before he regains his inner equilibrium, brow releasing its folds as Dash looks directly into the cameras.

"Aided and abetted in this deception by his lover, the [[Jedi Order|Jedi]] [[Jessalyn Valios]], he helped drive the ousting of Leia Organa and the dissolution of the office of the Chief of State. We are all left to wonder how much now-President [[Tarshyyk]] knew of this chicanery before choosing to accept his new position, as he met privately with this so-called Marius on several occasions. While it is our hope that the Senator from Kashyyyk was an innocent in this, we cannot overlook the fact that if a genocidal maniac can make his way into the upper echelons of power, then the system is broken. We, the people of Corellia, will not participate in such a broken system."

A quiet murmur of approval can be heard from somewhere on the upper dais, where the five planetary governors of the Corellian system -- the Dukes of the House of Lords -- are seated. The Senator pauses one last time before continuing.

"In light of these recent revelations, the people of Corellia have decided to withdraw their membership in the New Republic. We have not forgotten our occupation by the now-defunct Empire, nor have we forgotten that the New Republic came to our aid. Simply stated, we have decided to forge our own path forward: an independent Corellia."

The screen returns to the studio with the anchor sitting behind the desk more with a greater measure of dignity than at the start of the broadcast.

"Wow, I don't know where to even begin with this. It appears not only was Chancellor Starker in fact the notorious Basca Starker responsible for the Caspian genocide but now it appears to Corellians have just left the New Republic. We will have more on this story as it develops. And later on the Muun Money Minute we will discuss the effect this has on the markets."

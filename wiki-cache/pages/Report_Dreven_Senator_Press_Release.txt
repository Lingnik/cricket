[[Senator]] Ral Thonaris, a dignified figure with a stern countenance, stood before a backdrop of the [[Dreven]] flag, as he addressed a room filled with journalists and reporters from across the galaxy. The weight of recent events was evident in the tense atmosphere that hung in the air.

"Today, I stand before you as a representative of the Dreven people, listening to their voices and their concerns," Senator Thonaris began, his voice firm but tinged with empathy. "The recent protests on Dreven have shown us the depth of discontent among our citizens, and it is our duty as a planetary government to support and address their grievances."

As he spoke, the camera panned across the faces of the gathered reporters, capturing the mixture of anticipation and uncertainty in their eyes. The Senator's words resonated with the audience, who understood the gravity of the situation unfolding on Dreven.

"The protests we have witnessed in the past few days are a reflection of the frustrations felt by our citizens towards the [[New Republic]]," Senator Thonaris continued, his voice measured yet filled with conviction. "While Dreven joined the Republic in hopes of a better future, we cannot ignore the growing disillusionment caused by the rampant corruption and perceived inaction of the Republic in addressing the needs of its member systems."

The camera shifted to show images of the protests that had gripped Dreven, scenes of citizens expressing their frustration and calling for change. The images depicted the resilience and determination of the people, highlighting their desire for a more accountable government.

"It is our responsibility as a planetary government to listen to the voices of our people and act in their best interests," Senator Thonaris emphasized. "The protests on Dreven serve as a reminder that the Republic must reevaluate its priorities and address the grievances of its member systems, including Dreven."

The camera zoomed in on Senator Thonaris, capturing the sincerity in his eyes as he continued, "Therefore, I am announcing a special planetary vote to determine whether Dreven will remain a member of the New Republic or assert its independence. This vote will provide our citizens with the opportunity to shape their own future and determine the path Dreven will take."

The room erupted in a mix of gasps and murmurs as the significance of the senator's announcement reverberated among those in attendance. The camera panned across the room, capturing the range of emotions displayed on the faces of reporters and officials.

As the press conference concluded, Senator Thonaris maintained a determined expression, aware of the challenges and uncertainties that lay ahead. He stepped away from the podium, leaving behind a room buzzing with discussions and speculation about the future of Dreven and its relationship with the New Republic.

A tall golden-skinned female, economist Professor [[Brackett Benduday]], can be seen sitting behind an anchor's desk in a studio.

"Turning now to the Outer Rim, the flagship location of [[Zubindi Hakoon|Zubindi Hakoon's]] Silverwood House of Fashion on Kubindi was the sight of some very unexpected drama. According to reports the dress fitting of Analie Locke, wife of Calaron Sector [[Moff]] was interrupted by a lone Verpine bursting through the door and past security screaming for help.

Missus Locke and her security were soon shooed away without the dress she came for as Mister Hakoon was apparently more concerned with the plight of the [[Verpine]]. According to a press release later made by the fashion house the poor individual had escaped from a black market sentient slaughterhouse somewhere in the Silver Forest of Dreams where a number of species including [[Verpine]], [[Gand]], and [[Xi Charrian|Xi Charrians]] were being held as food. Mister Hakoon condemned the existence of such facility and has called for authorities to undertake an immediate galactic wide investigation into the matter.

Reaction thus far has been mixed. Though Moff Maximus Locke brushed off questions about his wife's shopping when asked about the slaughterhouse he vehemently denied such a facility could exist with his sector but has promised to launch an investigation and thanked Mister Hakoon for bring the matter to public attention.

This has also renewed speculation in the Mid Rim about various seemingly unrelated disappearance including that several weeks ago of a Verpine technical team from the Golden Star Casino on The Wheel and the Gand Findsman who had been hired to track them. According to authorities there no further information on their disappearances has turned but by they are concerned at the possible connexion.

According to reliable sources Mister Hakoon also interrupted a high level meeting held [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] in [[Noques Rasala]] on Caspar to personally transmit the news to the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] leader. The meeting was, coincidentally enough, with the Prelate of the [[Xi Char]] and was also attended by several other Caspian [[Union Council|Councillors]] including [[Andra Eleria]] of Noques Rasala and [[Sirlahn Inglet]] of [[Kichnar Orbital Station|Kichnar]]. A brief statement later released by the Xi Char confirmed the existence of the meeting and acknowledgement they were aware of unexplained disappearances of their people. A later statement by Caspian authorities indicated their unreserved commitment to combat the problem.

We will have more on this story as it develops."

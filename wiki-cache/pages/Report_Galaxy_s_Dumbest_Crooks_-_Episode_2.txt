[[Thurston Unger|Thurston]] cleared his throat and leaned into the camera. "Welcome back, my lovies, to another segment of [[Galaxy's Dumbest Crooks|Galaxy's Dumbest Criminals]]. Tonight, we have a story of a band of thieves who thought they had it all figured out, but ended up getting caught in their own trap."

He adjusted his glasses and continued. "It all started on the planet of [[Coruscant]], where a group of four criminals attempted to rob the Coruscant Museum of Artifacts. They had a plan to sneak in and out with a rare, one-of-a-kind ancient relic worth millions of credits. It was said to be guarded by the latest security technology, but these thieves thought they had a way around it."

Thurston paused for effect, and then continued. "They used a state-of-the-art droid to drill a hole through the wall, and a series of pressure sensors to detect any movement in the room. They thought they had everything covered, but what they didn't count on was one thing: the curator of the museum had a pet [[nexu]]." 

He chuckled to himself before continuing. "The nexu was supposed to be locked up for the night, but it somehow managed to escape and wandered into the room with the artifact just as the thieves were making their move. The nexu saw the intruders and, well, let's just say it didn't like them very much."

Thurston paused again, this time for dramatic effect. "What ensued was a chaotic mess. The thieves tried to fight off the nexu with their blasters, but that only made things worse. The nexu, being a wild animal, went berserk, attacking anything that moved. It even managed to knock over the precious artifact in the process."

He shook his head in disbelief. "In the end, the thieves were caught by security, and the nexu was tranquilized and returned to its cage. And the priceless artifact? It was damaged beyond repair. All because these criminals thought they were smarter than the security measures put in place."

Thurston leaned back in his chair and smiled. "So, there you have it, my lovies. A tale of Galaxy's Dumbest Criminals. Don't try this at home."

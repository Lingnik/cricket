[[Thurston Unger]], the host of The Cutting Edge, had a new story to share with his audience. "Darlings, have I got a tale for you," he began, with a dramatic flourish. "Our galaxy is full of criminals, and some of them are just plain dumb."

Thurston's eyes twinkled mischievously as he leaned in closer to the camera. "A group of smugglers thought they had hit the jackpot when they stole a prototype astromech droid from Industrial Automaton. They figured they could sell it to the highest bidder and make a fortune."

"But, as they say, ignorance is bliss," Thurston continued. "After letting the droid plug into their ship's computer, the smugglers quickly realized they had made a grave mistake. The prototype astromech had corrupted their ship's navigation computer and was taking them on a wild ride."

Thurston couldn't contain his laughter as he recounted the rest of the story. "They ended up lightyears off course and crashed on a deserted planet called [[:starwars:Dantooine/Legends|Dantooine]]. Of all the places to crash land, they ended up on a planet with nothing but fields and farms. The smugglers were eventually caught by local farmers and turned over to the authorities."

"So, my dear lovies, let this be a lesson to all of us," Thurston concluded, his expression turning more serious. "Crime may pay, but only if you have half a brain to go with it."

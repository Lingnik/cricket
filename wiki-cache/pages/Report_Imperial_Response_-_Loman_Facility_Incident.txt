The Imperial logo appears before viewers on the IGN feeds across the galaxy, it hovers on the screen as it rotates around its axis slowly in 3D. Moments later the IGN theme music plays and the logo fades out and in fades the familiar anchor desk of the IGN News network with the Human anchor for Coruscant, Carl Saga. Coruscant at night is in the background with its many ships and vessels passing back and forth across the screen. Moments later Carl looks up from his datapad with that ever so friendly smile of news anchors, "Greetings beings of all types across the galaxy. I'm Carl Saga, and you're watching IGN news!" There's a pause before he continues, "Tonight I am here to discuss the recent joint release from Diploserv regarding the investigation on Calamari over the Loman Bacta Facility incident." 

A holo image of the Loman Bacta Facility comes up next to Carl as he reads over the few notes that he was given for just a moment. Looking to the screen and says in a sort of surprised manner, "The official statement reads, The Imperial government officially recognizes the methods and handling undertaken by the investigative officials in the Loman Bacta Facility incident and hereby fully recognize the findings of said investigation as official and just." Recovering from the surprise of the report Carl adds, "Political experts and others have been speculating on the release of such a report for some time.  Leading experts believe this move will help solidify the Empire's position on truly offering support for the planet and its inhabitants through the support of those currently attempting to do so."  

The small holo image next to Carl changes to an image of Marshal Krieg Inrokana as Carl shifts to the next page of his report.  "IGN has also recently been able to get a hold of Marshal Krieg Inrokana, the man who announced the opening of Coruscant to the galaxy.  We were able to get a short interview with him to which we asked him for his opinion on the release of this report and what it might mean for our military.  In his opinion he feels Diploserv has done an excellent thing, and we've also learned that he personally supports Captain Ipex.  When asked if he had ever met the captain, he was able to share with us that the two have met in a professional capacity." 

The holo image fads away next to Carl and the camera shifts to get all of his face into view.  "In other news... " IGN continues to broadcast the rest of its news as programmed.

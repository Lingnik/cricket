The screen shows a busy sitting street with the screen zooming in to focus on a Human woman in her forties leaving a nondescript caf establishment. As the camera gets in closer the woman can be seen almost cradling her disposable cup with her posture clearly looking to be unnatural. The woman can be heard saying to the cup, "MINE. MY OWN. MY PRECIOUS." She then seems to almost scream a little.

The screen then pans out further to show a golden-skinned [[Atsvara Tarasar|girl]] wearing a formfitting gown black cyrene silk gown, with a large bantha embroidered in gold under her bust line. "Do you ever feel like whenever you go out for food and drink you can't avoid the deranged? The golden-skinned girl and the caf zombie share uncomfortable looks at one another.

The street and the people on it fade out and are replaced by a busy but not too crowded [[Biscuit Baron]] restaurant. The customers and staff both represent a variety of species. They all seem patient and friendly. At the front of the line is the same golden-skinned girl from earlier, "Here at Biscuit Baron the only thing deranged is our menu. She turns to a [[Wookiee]] behind the counter and says, "I would like and [[Infinite Stack]] please."

The Wookiee looks a little surprised at the girl but he rings up the order and a she is given a tray that contains a burger with multiple layers of bread and meat. It is not in fact infinite but it is nevertheless far in excess of anything that seems at all reasonable. Clearly too large for a humanoid to get their jaws around. The girl then brings the tray to a table and sets it in front of a [[Besalisk]] woman.

"Here at Biscuit Baron we are introduce the Infinite Stack, available in multiple varieties. When your hunger is infinite, Biscuit Baron has got you. The Besalisk woman smiles and picks up the sandwich and starts to eat it, her jaw large enough to eat it in spite of its size.

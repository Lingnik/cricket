{{IGNpost|author=[[Johanna Siri te Danaan]]|date=[[30 ABY]]|topic=Bespin Terrorist Attack|anchors=[[Brackett Benduday]]|headline=Johanna's Speech|synopsis=Following a terrorist attack on Bespin, a wounded but recovering Johanna returns to address the effected community.}}

A tall golden-skinned female, economist Professor [[Brackett Benduday]], can be seen sitting behind an anchor's desk, on location at [[Bespin]].

"This is Brackett Benduday live from Bespin. We have just been informed that Bespin's Governor [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]] will be speaking in front of the remains of the [[Cloud City]] refinery. We bring you now [[Jordaan Mag]] on location there."

The screen shifts to the construction zone where the refinery use to be. A number of people are gathered to listen to the speech. In front of the camera is an Arkanian-Offshoot woman.

"Thank you Brackett. In front of me we have a number of the mine workers and their families as well as survivors and family of the attack on the city's Red Light District. Ours, however, is the only news crew invited in. And it looks like....yes the speech is about to begin."

The reporter backs out of the shot while her camera moves in on Johanna at walking up to the podium with a slight limp.

"My friends, I stand before you today to let you know that I will not rest until those who attacked us have been brought to justice. You and your families have suffered unimaginable loss, yet you bravely continue on with your lives and refuse to let the cowardice of terrorists drive you from your rightful homes. You refuse to let the darkest impulses of craven criminals stop you from rebuilding and moving on. Your collective spirit and courage humble me."

Johanna pauses for a moment looking around at the crowd assembled before continuing.

"When I accepted the role of governor here, I never anticipated having to voice the grief of so many, but be assured that continued care for the injured, support for the bereaved and those whose livelihoods have been impacted, remain my priority. I will be with you at every stage and we will emerge stronger than before, that strength a rebuke to those would seek to divide and destroy us. Bespin’s greatest asset is not its natural resources but its people, and it is my honour to stand before you today and promise that your needs will continue to be met long after the investigations have concluded and the terrorists apprehended. Together, we will heal. Together, we will thrive. We are Bespin, and we will endure."

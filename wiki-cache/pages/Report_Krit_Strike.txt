{{IGNpost|author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[29 ABY]]|topic=Domestic Strife|anchors=[[Brackett Benduday]]|headline=Krit Strike|synopsis=[[InterGalactic News]] covers strikes on [[Krittain Major]].}}

A tall golden-skinned female, economist Professor [[Brackett Benduday]], can be seen sitting behind an anchor's desk in a studio. To her left is a [[Wookiee]] who would be recognisable to some as the Caspian politician [[Zaalbacca]]. To his left is a cerulean [[Twi'lek]] in her teens.

:This is Brackett Benduday with breaking news from the Caspian Sector. For our segment today I am joined by a special guest Councillor Zaalbacca of Anchorron. Councillor Zaalbacca is among other roles Chairman of the [[Wookiee Workers Union of Anchorron|Wookiee Workers Union]], Deputy Leader of the ruling [[Praecet]] party, and the Caspian Minister of Labour and Industry. With him is Calling Teksa, a senior member of his staff.

We turn to the Krit Union where the city of Spittsbury on [[Krittain Major]] has seen industry grind to a halt under a general strike. According to organisers the reasons for the strike a three-fold. They are calling it an act of solidarity with the workers on [[Mergansar]] though their primary cause of complaint is poor working conditions and health standards in the city, factors that the workers claim other Krittish cities do not suffer. Notably they are also protesting the fact that the Krittish government only considers roughly 60 percent of the planet's population to actually be citizens.

There is a lot to unpack here but perhaps we could begin with the issue of solidarity, Councillor Zaalbacca as a union leader yourself what do you make of this new development."

"It is important to remember Brackett that the experience of workers across the galaxy is largely similar. Location and demographics do play a role, but the common thread is hard working beings performing the labour, creating the wealth, yet much of the time they are not the ones reaping the benefits. Now those that exploit workers will try to divide them, tell them they should be concerned merely with where they are or what species they are or some other triviality and they will claim other worker do not care about them for similar reasons. 

I am Chairman of the Wookiee Workers Union of [[Anchorron]], but a lot of people get hung up on that as a name. It was founded in Anchorron but no it does not mean we only care about workers in the our city. It was founded by Wookiees but no it does not mean we only care about Wookiees. Calling here is a card carrying member, and contrary to popular opinion is in fact a Twi'lek and not a Wookiee we shaved and painted blue as part of a hazing ritual."

The Twi'lek girl provides the [[Galactic Basic Standard]] to the entirety of Zaalbacca's speech which is in Shyriiwook. After reaching finishing the joke made in reference to her she adopts her own attempt at responding in [[Shyriiwook]], it is stilted and given the vocal limitations nearly unintelligible as distinct words but a keen ear might recognise the attempt to say, "I am Wookiee, hear me roar."

The [[Firrerreo]] anchorwoman and economist is clearly fluent in the Wookiee language with her expressions indicating she is following Zaalbacca's speech before translation. She seems properly amused by the humour of both of her guests. Following this she pivots to her next point, "Your government has spent a great deal of time and effort on Mergansar, not least of which in a two month blockade of the world. The Krittain system remains the only other part of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] that has not rejoined the new [[Caspian Union]], yet we almost never here any mention of the Krit Union from the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]], why is that?"

"I am glad you asked that. It is points to a big question. What brought an end to the old Caspian Democratic Union. Obviously the Sariphage devastated [[Caspar]] killing off 90 percent of the planet's populace including the entire [[Union Assembly]] and leaving the commander of the Home Fleet as the only remaining [[Sar Admiral]]. The pogroms on Mergansar displaced a further 300 million people. But the most populace world of the CDU was in fact always Krittain Major, and at that point in the time was the most self sufficient. But where the fuck was the moral leadership from them? I am not expecting them to have cure for the Sariphage. Nor am I expecting them to provide a leader to counter the direction of our own scientific community or the military at that time, though maker knows it wouldn't have been any worse than what we did have.

300 million Caspian citizen were dragged from their homes by paramilitary forces with the complicity of the Mergansarian government. This meets the dictionary definition of genocide. What did the Krits do to stop it? Don't get me wrong the have traditionally made some great contributions to the Union but they seem to have gotten to hung up on their super special ships with their own special designations because they had to be extra. Exactly how much did they considered themselves truly in union with the rest of us. They seemed far more content to let things fall apart than take a stand in opposition to blatant evil done against Caspian citizens. And exactly how does a civilised planet operate with 40 percent of their residents not considered citizens. And while their "alien" designations are not explicitly racial, [[Human|Humans]] and [[Sarian|Sarians]] are more likely to be in the citizen category and other species less likely to. It is hard to provide leadership on respecting the rights of citizens on other member worlds when you barely do on your own.

We do want to see the Krits return. It has always been our hope that the old borders could be restored and that new worlds might even want to join with us this time. But we aren't going to beg. If there is anything that unifies the Union Council across party lines it is the belief that culture aside, local policy aside, Caspian citizen needs to mean the same thing on all Caspian worlds. If it means something different, something lesser, on Mergansar or Krittain Major then it does not mean enough."

The Twi'lek translates the latest reply though in the process she employs slightly less colourful phrasing at points. It is difficult to gauge whether the Wookiee Councillor does not notice or does not care about these changes. After finishing the translations she pauses for a moment to indicate clear break from what she was translating before provide her own additional commentary. "Ultimately it is up to the people of the Krit Union and the people of Mergansar to decide whether they want to rejoin the family. We can't force them and nor would we ever wish to. Their leaders certainly don't seem to want to but it is our hope, our belief than the people do. To the people of Spittsbury, of Hammerton and Spanner, the people of Charon, the people of Krittain Major and [[Krittain Minor|Minor]], of Mergansar, we stand with you. To that end the Wookiee Workers Union is calling for an  one hour suspension of all non-essential business across the whole of the Caspian Union in solidarity with our brothers and sister in the rest of the Caspian Sector still under the yolk of oppression. We stand with you, we stand for you."

==Refugees from Mergansar ==
[[InterGalactic News|IGN]] - [[Kichnar Orbital Station]]

A [[Sarian]] woman stands in the main hangar as a shuttle unloads a group of passengers (adults and children), far too many to be comfortable in the shuttle, all carrying far too much luggage. "Thanks Jack -- as you can see behind me, refugees from [[Mergansar]] have started coming to Kichnar. As you know, Mergansar is about 90% Sarian, yet these refugees are only about 50% Sarian. With them the bring rumours of racial strife." The female announcer approaches a Sarian mad, a human woman with him, and three (likely mixed parentage kids), "You've packed into an over crowded shuttle to come to Kichnar -- why?"

The man replies, "Those Evil-worshiping freaks in black, knocked on our door late one night and told us we had two days to get off world. Apparently I, and people like me who associate with off worlders on the soil of [[Caspia|Caspian]] worlds are blame for the disease on [[Caspar]]." He then exposes his arm which is in a [[:starwars:Bacta/Legends|bacta]]-cast. "They wouldn't take no for an answer so I figured it was best to move my family to Kichnar." With that he and his family begin moving along.

The announcer continues, "His story is typical -- while the exact situation isn't yet clear, a few hundred such families have shown up on Kichnar in recent days." The feed cuts to stock footage of tents set up in a large hangar, with refugees and what appear to be a small number of attendants in old Griffon uniforms handing out supplies as she continues, "The remnants of the [[Griffon Alliance]] that moved to Kichnar have responded by setting up refugee tent cities -- but Kichnar is not a large place. It is unclear how many refugees they can handle. We've reached out the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]] for comment, but there has been no answer. Whatever is going on, it seems the disease on Caspar is not the Crest only crisis at the moment. From IGN, from Kichnar Orbital Station, I'm Olumi Oolapon."

"Gentle beings, just when it seems to be at a darkest point for Caspar, something even worse happens. The government of the [[Caspian Democratic Union|CDU]] is in chaos on Caspar and the military is unwilling to enforce it's orders. Now the darkest elements of Sarian culture are in full swing as the worshipers of Olum, the Sarian shadow god, have taken to the street to demand non Sarians be exiled or killed. Jane do we have that other footage . . ."

The feed cuts to a full out riot in another location, with black clad Sarians throwing fire bombs and breaking up store fronts. "This is a human enclave on Ithaca. Olum worshipping Sarians have taken it on themselves to run their non Sarian neighbors off the planet. The CDU security forces here seem as paralyzed as the military above Caspar. As Caspar slowly dies, Mergansar seems to be falling into chaos. . ."

IGN - Kichnar

A simple podium with the seal of the Griffon Alliance has been set up. An older gentleman wearing the uniform of a Griffon Colonel steps to the podium to speak to gathered group of reporters.

"Gentlebeings," the old man begins. "I'm here to announce that the first refugee transport has arrived. The Giffons are supporting the relocation of refugees to [[Kalla VII|Kalla]], in the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector]], on a large tract of land purchased by one of our members, [[Zeak Oppenhiemer]]. The trek to Kalla should take a little over a week. We also have a second transport arriving tomorrow to begin ferrying refugees to [[Krittain Minor]]. Obviously Krittain Minor is not a vacation spot, but we have secured a plot of land and in partnership with the refugees we will be constructing a new facility, complete with grav plating in the living areas to help bring the gravity closer inline with galactic normal."

With that said, the presser goes to questions.
==Unrest on Mergansar Continues==
IGN - Mergansar -- Ithaca

Footage of angry black clad mobs throwing rocks and breaking windows and throwing Molotov cocktails at a series of off-world interests: a consulate, a manufacturing company headquartered on [[Krittain Major]], and a branch office of [[NovaCom Cybertronics]]. Fires have broken out, and not a first responder is to be seen.

A voice can be heard over the footage. "This has come to us from Mergansar. It seems protests have turned violent. Protestors from the Sarian nativist movement, some of which are likely members of the [[Olumi Osahn]] cult have taken to the streets, venting their rage on off world businesses. They don't seem to care if the business has deep roots in the Caspian Democratic Union - as companies like NovaCom have been hard hit."

The footage changes to a space port, with long lines. "As the situation becomes more dire, refugee numbers have increased -- current estimates are that something like one hundred thousand individuals have fled Mergansar, mostly for Kichnar Orbital Station. As the unrest grows, refugee numbers are expected swell."
==Violence on Mergansar ==
A Human male reporter, average height and build appears in view. Behind him one can see the space port of Hebron, packed to the brim of disheveled human refugees.

"Are we on?" the reporter says before getting an off camera reply, "Greetings, I'm Jack Coruscant, with IGN -- and I'm reporting to you live from the space port at Hebron on Mergansar. You haven't heard much from us lately because the tension had seemed to drop down in the last few days, the Olumi Osahn protestors had ended their protests -- things seemed to be returning to normal, until this morning." The feed cuts to black clad, armed Sarians going door to door, pulling humans and the occasional alien out of buildings. It appears to be a systematic eviction of off worlders. An occasional nondescript body can be seen on the ground, it is unclear if they are injured or dead. "Much like these poor souls, we were pulled from our hotel early this morning and shoved into the space port. We caught this footage on the way to the space port, we don't know how many have been injured or worse during this forced-migration." From there the feed cuts to the outside of the space port and seas of people inside the fence. "We estimate two hundred thousand have been herded into the space port at Hebron alone, and they keep coming. We have heard rumors that every space port on the planet is packed in similar fashion." the feed cuts to ships taking off, "Of course, it isn't clear that there are enough ships to take us all off world -- if this is indeed a world-wide event, it could result in hundreds of millions of displaced beings. Regardless, we need help -- desperately." The view switches back to the reporter, "I've talked to many this morning, and no one wants to go home. We need food, clothing, and most of all we all want a ride somewhere else. Reporting from Mergansar, I'm Jack Coruscant."
==Mobilisation==

"This is [[Ujaa M'ik]] reporting to you from Kichnar Orbital Station. This campaign does not seem to be limited just to the scope of Caspian space, with reports of arrests of Olumi Osahn cultists flooding in from across the galaxy. We here at the IGN are still trying to fact check the individual reports, but I can state as of this point that authorities on [[Ithor]] and [[Ryloth]] have confirmed they are holding suspected members OO for various crimes. More on this story as it develops."
==A Swift Response==
A Bith male reporter can be seen reporting from a studio on Kichnar Orbital Station.

"This is Ujaa M'ik reporting to you again from Kichnar Orbital Station. They are calling themselves [[Casulmis]] which I am told translates to Great Wind. Mere hours after our last report to you from Jack Coruscant in Hebron on Mergansar, we can report quite a different scene. There initial assault was small be decisive and deadly. Based on the pictures we can identify a Lambda-class shuttle, an X-Wing, and some TIE....well TIE somethings, we think. It is hard to say as local communications and sensors seemed to have been jammed in the run up. What is more clear is the aftermath. At least a half dozen Olumi Osahn strongholds in the city have been been bombed out of existence including near the spaceport itself. And based on the bodies, there is probably not a single member of Olumi Osahn left alive in the city. How they died seems to be a curious matter itself as many of the people on the streets of Hebron reports a lightsaber wielding [[Tyler Damion|figure]] coming to the aid of refugees and hacking apart the Olumi Osahn and their sympathisers who were still trying to pull people out of their homes.

While there is still a great reluctance by many of the refugees to return to their homes, many have begun to do so. The streets themselves seem to be patrolled by armed refugees. The TIEs or whatever they are now escorting freighters to and from Hebron. Some aid supplies seem to be among the deliveries but thus far what we are seeing is some refugees returning from Kichnar and armed.

While most of this activity has been confined to Hebron we are starting to hear reports that Casulmis has liberated Goshen as well at this point. We are also hearing scattered reports of clashes in Anphora, Charon, and Ithaca with more sure to follow.

Now you may be asking where the government of Mergansar has been in all of this. Well dossiers have been delivered to us with evidence that seems to pretty conclusively tie many members of the government directly to Olumi Osahn including most notably the Governor, Lieutenant Governor, and Minister of Justice." *The screen briefly features images of Governor [[Raif Avosam]] dress in Olumi Osahn garb, though the images do not appear recent.* "Reports indicate that the Lieutenant Governor and Minister of Justice are both in hiding. A single compound on the outskirts of the capital of Vilarasala appears to have been part of last night's bombings and rumours are the Governor's body has been pulled from the wreckage. We will bring you more on this story as it develops."

==Caspian Military turns to Mergansar ==
IGN - Caspar

After the announcement that the Sariphage has been contained, the ''[[CMS Typhoon]]'', along with a trio of escort ships ''Ad Astra'', ''[[CMS Prophet|Prophet]]'' and ''Rapace'' broke orbit and began burning for Mergansar at best possible speed. There have been reports of what appear to be a series of tight beam transmissions between the ''Typhoon'' and an in Interdictor Heavy Cruiser in orbit of Mergansar, the Heavy Cruiser ''[[Leviathan]]''. This is the same heavy cruiser that has been associated with space-based fighters striking targets on Mergansar and the Casulmis or 'Great Wind' movement.
==[[Mergansar Incident|Caspar Space Battle]]==
IGN - Kichnar Orbital Station

A Caspian Admiral, a human, Admiral Blaine Mostor, the Admiral in charge of the Naval station on Kichnar, takes the podium. Behind and to his side is the [[SubPro]] director of public relations. The Admiral's expression is clearly shaken, but in control.

"Ladies and gentlemen," the Admiral replies. "About six hours ago, the ''CMS Typhoon'' along with an escort of the ''CMS Ad Astra'', ''CMS Prophet'' and ''CMS Rapace'' broke orbit to pacify the open warfare that has begun on Mergansar. As part of that effort they gave orders to the Independent Interdictor Heavy Cruiser ''Leviathan'' under the command of a Captain [[Crestian Tarasar]] -- T A R A S A R -- to cease all operations and rendezvous with them. The ''Leviathan'' had been carrying unauthorized private airstrikes against targets associated with the Olumi Osahn on Mergansar. The ''Leviathan'' complied with the initial order and began making way for the flotilla. About a half hour from the rendezvous, the ''CMS Typhoon'' established a fighter 'cap' as per standard protocol when carrying out an interdiction operations dealing with belligerents. The ''Leviathan'' began to jam sensors, refused an order to lower their shields, and instead demanded the surrender of the Caspian forces and scrambled their own fighters. Caspian forces, after warning, opened fire on the ''Leviathan''. All ships involved sustained heavy damage. The ''Leviathan'' escaped to hyperspace, The ''Prophet'' and ''Rapace'' were crippled, the ''Typhoon'' had sustained heavy, focused damage to her bridge, and the ''Ad Astra'' suffered extensive damage to her HOWL launchers. The battle lasted less than a minute; at its completion the ''Ad Astra'' issued a fleet wide distress call."

The Admiral swallows before continuing to read his prepared statement. "At the time of the distress call, [[Prex (CSA)|Prex]] Oppenhiemer was traveling from Kichnar to the CSA fleet elements at Caspar in his personal cruiser, the ''Winking Monkey Lizard''. Prex Oppenhiemer executed a in-system micro jump, at great risk to his ship to respond the call. He received the distress call as SubPro participates in the fleet wide channel as they build the vessels, and may need to render technical assistance. Crew from the ''Winking Monkey Lizard'' aided teams from the ''Ad Astra'', helping to evacuate the injured from the ''Prophet'' and ''Rapace'' to facilities on the ''Typhoon'', ''Ad Astra'' and ''Winking Monkey Lizard'' for treatment. At this time, the situation has been stabilized, we are in the process of contacting the next of kin of the crew who perished in the battle, however we can confirm to you at this time that [[Sar Admiral]] [[Prae Kichakressa]] was on the bridge of the ''Typhoon'' and killed in the battle."

"Fighters from the ''Typhoon'' went on to Mergansar, where they have established air-superiority and have ordered civilian interlopers to stand down. They are currently maintaining a cap over refugee centers as well as escorting ships off the surface as a preventive measure." The Admiral pauses, "As they are without a carrier, they have based themselves off a handful of civilian ports. Ground security is being provided by local police, and contingents of CDU marines from the ''Ad Astra'' and the ''Typhoon'', who shuttled over in landing craft."

"As the ranking flag officer," the Admiral continues. "I have ordered a forensic team, to be assisted by engineers from SubPro, to determine the details of what happened in the battle, and how a single heavy cruiser could do so much damage, especially given the ratios of guns on the Caspian vessels to the ''Leviathan''. When we know more, we will provide an update. In the meantime we request that you respect the privacy of the families. We will take no questions at this time. Thank you."

==Sith and Caspar==

A Sarian reporter, can be seen, sitting with a digitally blacked out figure.

"Ladies and Gentlemen, I'm here with -- let's call you Mr. Bobish. Mr. Bobish is a well placed member of the Olumi Osahn and he has agreed to go on the record about the recent hostilities." There is and explosion in the distant background. "Let's just jump in Mr. Bobish -- why did the Olumi Osahn start all this."

The figure, in a heavily altered digital voice replies, "A lack of purity is why the plague struck Caspar, we need to return to our pure ways, the old ways. We were initially content to use - to use less extreme measures to purify the home world, but when that Tarasar chap showed up, and it went sideways."

"Did he threaten you," the reporter inquires.

"He is an agent of Darth Malign. He and Malign apparently have plans for Mergansar and Caspar -- we were told that if we didn't fall into step and worship their darkness, and let them use our ancestral lands of their use! They had been arresting our people with impunity and conferencing with the late Sar Admiral. We had no choice but to act swiftly."

The reporter nods along, doing a poor job of faking sympathy for the monster before stopping him. "Are you telling me that not only is Darth Malign alive, but he and his people had corrupted Sar Admiral Kichakressa and are responsible for the Casulmis movement?"

"Yes, it is [[New Sith Order|Sith]] to the core."

== Caspian Talks Suffer Setback==
IGN - Caspar

As the relief camps supporting survivors have become finalized things looked like they had finally quieted down on Caspar and on Mergansar, that things were going to return to normal. The Caspian military and representatives from the main worlds (Mergansar, Krittain Major and Minor, [[Rauther]], Kichnar Station and [[Yariz]]) have been meeting secretly. The talks were a closely guarded secret until they fell apart today -- the apparent sticking point, mutual distrust.

Our sources tell us that the Mergansar contingent seems to be split with a majority insisting on a recognition that Mergansar be for Sarians only. The minority of the Mergansar delegation had hoped the other delegates would take their side, but has been disappointed. The Sarians in the military are reported to be split between the two factions; we understand the non Sarians in the military, especially those hailing from Kichnar or places outside the Caspia system tend to reflect the views of those areas -- it seems the Krittains and Kichnar have sympathy for the refugees, but would rather spend their efforts moving and resettling the displaced than fighting for their rights.

Talks broke up today with the minority party from Mergansar storming out along with key representatives from the military. We are told the majority Mergansar party, with is allied with the Olumi Osahn in continuing to negotiate with the delegates that remain.
==Mergansar Falls to Olumi Osahn==
IGN Mergansar

Shortly after talks collapsed on Caspar, Olumi Osahn forces overwhelmed the guards at the makeshift fighter base, what used to be the main space port in the city of Vilarasala and the planetary defense base at Toresta. Both strikes were quick, likely indicating inside support. After their victory, the Olumi Osahn released the following statement.

People of Mergansar -- the off worlders nor the Sith will rule us. Today, with the help of the true believers in the military, we have liberated the facilities and equipment on Mergansar. To the non-Sarians on Mergansar: this is our home not yours. You were are guests, and as such you should be polite guests and leave. We now control the military on this world; the rise of our movement is complete. We will keep the space ports open so that you may leave, and guarantee the safe passage of ships to take you to your new homes. You have 5 days.
== CDU Military paralyzed==
IGN - Caspar

The overwhelming feeling is one of paralysis. Thus far there has been no official response from the Caspian military on the recent events on Mergansar. A Number of smaller fleet ships have broken formation and are heading for Mergansar, openly allying themselves with the Olumi Osahn. The larger ships remain at their stations either over Caspar, or on patrol near Krittain or other parts of the Union. Rumors are circulating of a group planning a counter-strike, but as of now no one is willing to go on the record.
==Dueling Press Releases==
A Sarian man in a dark suit sits at a desk in a windowless room.

"My fellow Mergani, it is my sad duty to report the brutal murder of Acting Governor [[Lau Saifax]] at the hands of the Sith pawns calling themselves Casulmis. With his death and the earlier slaying of Governor Raif Avosam the Constitution of Mergansar places governing authority in the hands of the Minister of Justice and as such I, [[Marck Narlin]], do hereby assume the office of Acting Governor of Mergansar. We cannot let these killings dampen our resolve. Mergansar will be purged of these alien influences once and for all."

Before the Sarian can go get further in his statement the broadcast is interrupted, his image remains a muted insert at the bottom right hand portion of the screen while the rest of the screen shows another a Sarian male dressed in the latest Silverwood House of Fashion attire. The outfit heavily features the colours red, silver, and black and the discerning fashionista would note the [[Kubaz|Kubazian]] and [[Chandrila|Chandrilan]] influences. Behind this man is a Casulmis banner.

"While the governments of the various Caspian worlds were meeting in secret I stayed on the sidelines. I hoped, I prayed, I waited to see someone take a stand against this cult. Though my sources tell me financial transactions tying the late Sar Admiral Kichakressa to Olumi Osahn, it appears a number of other officers have taken their naval vessels into their service while the rest of the fleet and worlds of Caspar have stood by and it would seem have done nothing. I am disappointed by this result but not surprised.

Olumi Osahn is a dangerous, powerful, and wealthy cult. It remains almost comical to see a cult of shadow worshipers who were long financed by the Sith try to whip up fear about a Sith takeover. In may ways this comedy begins with the man you see at the bottom of your screen. He is happy enough now to use the law to take office, yet as Minister of Justice, sworn to protect the rights of all citizens of Mergansar, the man who should have been doing the most to stop the current crisis is instead at the head of it. Millions of innocent people remain displaced from their homes because men like Marck Narlin do not care for their genetics or that of some member of their family. These are not simply guests or visitors to Mergansar they have been integral members of Mergansar and Caspian society for years and have been treated with the greatest indignities because a powerful fringe group does not like them and no one is willing to stand against them.

Now it is easy enough to try to paint me and my crew as villains in this. They can say Crestian Tarasar served as a Sith Admiral, I don't deny it. They can call me an opportunistic expatriate or worse as I had long been gone from Caspar. But I am a Sarian, I am a native of Caspar and I stand with the millions who have been illegally stripped of their citizenship, their homes, and in far too many cases their lives. But I cannot stand with them alone. Casulmis needs you. Are we going to see the Union die at the hands of rabid racists, is that to be the legacy of the Union and our people? Together we can save the Union and rebuild it and it can once again be that special place of vibrancy on the Rim. You can question my motives, but if we all stand together we can preserve the democratic character that once defined the Union. Do not let a vocal minority speak for you.

So as I see it you have three choices. You can stand with me and help bring order back to the Union and people back to their homes. You can sit paralyzed and apathetic on the sidelines. Or you can stand with the man on the bottom of your screen."

Suddenly a large explosion engulfs the bottom of the screen and the feed goes dead, after a few seconds the full screen is devoted to Crestian.

"Sorry, I meant to say you can stand with men like him. You cannot stand with him. He does not exist any more. When I went to Mergansar before I told people Olumi Osahn had been outlawed, and I meant it. No law will protect you to spread your hate. You will bow before the winds of justice or they will consume you."

A tall golden-skinned female, economist Professor [[Brackett Benduday]], can be seen sitting behind an anchor's desk in a studio. 

"This is Brackett Benduday with news from the art world. Similar to recent reports of street art mocking on the Outer Rim mocking [[Grand Moff]] [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]], it appears similar art has made its way to [[Caspar]] and [[Kichnar Orbital Station|Kichnar]] depicting [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] leaders including [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]], [[Union Council|Councillor]] [[Sirlahn Inglet]] of Kichnar, Councillor [[Zaalbacca]] of [[Anchorron]], and Councillor [[Sha Dark]] of [[Orleanon]] as puppets being manipulated by a figure implied to be a [[Sith Order|Sith Lord]].

Unlike the recent incidents in the Outer Rim, the official reaction here has been quite mixed. The wall in front of the Hospitality Office on Kichnar displaying the rendering of Councillor Inglet was cleaned off with in an hour. 

Meanwhile in Anchorron, when informed of the art now gracing the wall of the Smuggler's Run Nightclub, Councillor Zaalbacca apparently went to have pictures taken with it." The screen cuts to a short film of Zaalbacca striking various next to the graffiti of the puppet Zaalbacca and the Sith Lord puppet. He can also be heard in Shyriiwook joking with other nearby [[Wookiee|Wookiees]] about whether it is indeed meant to be him with the Councillor making jokes suggesting various nearby Wookiee workers are in fact Sith puppets.

The screen then returns to the news anchor, "In Orleanon on Councillor Sha Dark almost got to see it go up personally.” The accompanying film shows Councillor Dark walking down a street in the city with the reporter [[Jordaan Mag]]. An interview of some sort is taking place the subject matter is not initially clear. As they get near a spa teenager can be seen dropping a can of spray paint and running. The [[Zeltron]] Councillor gives chase for a moment before picking up the teen's spray paint can and tossing it at him. She then stops and looks at the rendering of her which is quite scantily clad compared to her own more conservative attire she can be heard remarking back, "He clearly has never drawn a Zeltron with her clothes on before." An inaudible question occurs of screen leading to a reply, "No leave it up, this place has never had such sex appeal." The screen returns to the anchor who remarks, "The spa in question removed the image releasing the brief statement, quote, we are not that kind of place, end quote."

The screen shifts this time to image credited to the tabloid service FKZ. A male voice announcer states, "After leaving a performance of The Brief Reign of Future Wraiths at the Casarasala Opera in [[Trinumvira]], Presav Crestian Tarasar and fashionista [[Zubindi Hakoon]] came across the graffiti of Tarasar." A camera can be seen to follow the men as they are start walk home from the opera. The camera starts at some distance but moves closer as the walk moving rather close as the see an image of Crestian as a puppet to some undetermined Sith and catches their conversation.

"They did justice to your work Zub. The skirt, the cape, the gloves. You know when I was talking to Linane earlier he was saying we should offer these younglings some scholarships to art schools."

"I think Valdemar might be right, but who the fuck is that supposed to be? All this work making you look exquisite and your puppeteer, that certainly doesn't look a thing like the Imperator. Maybe it is suppose to be [[Vassily Korolov|Korolov]] Weren't you saying the Griffons always make a point of trying to link you to him."

"They do at that. I have only seen him once in the past decade. They are so busy hating him to not pay attention the controversial people I actually deal with. Perhaps the puppet master is also supposed to me hidden under off the rack robes?"

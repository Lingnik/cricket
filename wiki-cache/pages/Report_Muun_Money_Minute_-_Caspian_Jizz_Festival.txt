Stock footage of various popular jizz bands is displayed on the screen before panning out to an [[InterGalactic News]] anchor desk. Behind the anchor's desk are two women, a [[Firrerreo]] and a [[Pantoran]]. The camera centres in on the Firrerreo.

"It is time to dust off and polish your kloo horns, jizz musicians from across the Rim are congregating in the [[Caspian Sector]] for the Caspian Jizz Festival. This is the [[Muun Money Minute]] and I am your host [[Brackett Benduday]]. To my right is [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] Minister of Culture, [[Union Council|Councillor]] [[Andra Eleria]] to talk about the impact of jizz. So to start, what can you tell us about the festival?"

"Well let me first say thank you for having me on the programme. I am sure some of viewers are wondering why this is an economic story, but Caspians are hugely into jizz. While the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]] has provided adequate funding and support to cultural projects in the past few years, it has had to take a backseat to rebuilding our military-industrial complex and other key sectors such as our medical industry. We are in a position now to think beyond rebuilding, and the Jizz Festival is an offspring of such an expansion of thought.

Efforts such as the recent [[Charros Wargames|Wargames]] on [[Charros IV]] have illustrated quite well what the Union can accomplish when a variety of resources are marshalled towards a larger project. It also demonstrated that there is an appetite for tourism throughout Union. The Jizz Festival is an effort to reproduce that sort of success for a purely cultural project. Venues across Caspian Space are participating in this once in a generation project. For the duration of the festival you will be able to go to any major city in the Union on any night and hear some of the galaxy's greatest jizz-wailers."

"You mentioned tourism. Obviously [[Caspar]] and [[Mergansar]] saw their tourist industries collapse in the tragedies of a few years ago. Caspar has since seen the industry bounce back to a level not seen in probably fifteen years. Charros IV has now emerged as tourist destination. What kind of economic impact are you expecting from the jizz?"

"Naturally the performance venues can expect a shower of credits, it a sector of the economy that has not seen as great rebound as other segments so this will end what has been a comparatively flaccid performance. Of course, all the tourists are going to need places to stay and to eat so the hotel and restaurant industry will see a rain of credits. But ultimately people are going to want more than jizz, so you can expect that to drizzle added business for retailers and other entertainment and cultural sites. So the jizz should propagate revenue throughout the economy."

"Well it sounds like the coming of the Jizz Festival is going to be very intense for the Caspian economy. I am afraid that is all the time we have on the [[Muun Money Minute]], but be sure to catch the reports from our entertainment correspondents with more about the festival itself. I hear [[Thurston Unger|Thurston]] is quite excited for all the jizz. Are their a rumours of one particular legend coming out of retirement that you are not going to want to miss. My thanks to Councillor Eleria and the sponsor of this segment, the Silverwood House of Fashion. Goodnight."

{{IGNpost|author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[33 ABY]]|topic=Financial News|anchors=[[Brackett Benduday]]|headline=Muun Money Minute - Greater Charros IV|synopsis=[[Brackett Benduday]] reports about a trade summit announced for [[Charros IV]].}}

The screen shows a news studio with a golden-skinned anchor standing in front of an image of the city of Xi Can on [[Charros IV]]. The logo for [[InterGalactic News]] and the [[Muun Money Minute]] financial programme feature prominently in the middle of the screen before shifting to smaller versions at the bottom.

The anchor appears to speak to someone off screen for a moment before turning back towards the camera, "This is the Muun Money Minute, sponsored tonight by our friends at [[SubPro|Sublight Products Corporation]]. I am your host Professor [[Brackett Benduday]]."

The screen then switches from the background of Xi Can to a meeting of the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] [[Union Council]]. "A meeting of the Union Council came on our sensors today when Caspian Minister of Commerce, Councillor Meggerz Zynski of Breslau announced a Council sponsored trade summit on Charros IV. The announcement seemed to take many in the chamber by surprise including Vice Presav [[Cas Mos]] of [[Groton]] who was chairing in the absence of [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar|Tarasar]].

In spite of the general surprise in the council chamber, Councillor Zynski's announcement was soon followed by supporting statements from Councillor [[Zaalbacca]] of [[Anchorron]], the Minister of Industry and Labour, and Councillor [[Sirlahn Inglet]] of [[Kichnar Orbital Station|Kichnar]], the Minister of Science and Technology. A brief discussion of the matter occurred with the Councillors who had clearly been in the dark doing their best to gather more information while avoiding an obvious appearance of ignorance on the matter.

Following the discussion Councillor [[Sha Dark]] of [[Orleanon]], the Minister of Justice, proposed the Council recess until the completion of the summit to allow the relevant Councillors to attend without disrupting Council business. Following a brief whispered conversation between Vice Presav Mos and Councillor Zaalbacca, the matter was brought to a vote passing. The motion passed unanimously with all 36 members of the Council in attendance voting in favour. In addition to Presav Tarasar, Councillor [[ak]] of Xi Can and Councillor Lilith Bergen of Spittsbury were absent."

The image on the screen turns back from the Union Council back to the city of Xi Can, "Following the chamber's recess a statement from the office of Councillor ak addressed the matter of the trade summit speaking to the Council's hopes for the summit. We reached out to the office of Presav Tarasar but his office had no comment and sources indicate he may have been out of the loop on the matter as well.

It is expect that business and political leaders from the [[Imperium]], the [[Corporate Sector Authority]], and independent worlds will be represented at the summit but no definitive list of participants has been released. Based on the statements of the Councillors, the Union appears interested in new contracts for its military technology with the site of Charros IV chosen as it is the Union's most industrialised world. We will have more on this story as it develops."

{{IGNpost|author=[[Crestian Tarasar]]|date=[[34 ABY]]|topic=Financial News|anchors=[[Brackett Benduday]]|headline=Muun Money Minute - Colicoid Creation Nest|synopsis=[[Brackett Benduday]] discusses a new partnership between the [[:starwars:Colicoid Creation Nest/Legends|Colicoid Creation Nest]] and Zithreej Combine with guests [[Atsvara Tarasar]] and [[ak]].}}

The screen shows the makeshift [[InterGalactic News]] Studio on [[Charros IV]] with an image of a [[:starwars:Droideka/Legends|droideka]] in the background. The logo of for the [[Muun Money Minute]] financial programme is displayed prominently in the middle of the screen before shifting to a smaller version at the bottom of the screen. In the studio are three individuals Professor [[Brackett Benduday]] and Baroness [[Atsvara Tarasar]] are seated comfortably in chairs next to [[Xi Char]] [[Dean]] [[ak]].

"This is the Muun Money Minute coming to you live from Charros IV. I am your host Professor Brackett Benduday. My guests tonight are [[Biscuit Baron]] CEO Atsvara Tarasar and [[Haor Chall Engineering]] Executive Vice President ak. Tonight we are here to talk about the [[:starwars:Colicoid Creation Nest/Legends|Colicoid Creation Nest]]. Famous for their droideka's among other products during the Clone Wars, they have recently entered into a partnership Zithreej Combine. The latter of which has apparently invested a great deal of capital in the former.

The Zithreej Combine is apparently a newly formed corporation fronted by [[Nasiri Zithreej]]." A picture of Nasiri briefly flashes on the screen. "Until a couple of years Mister Zithreej was a staple of the [[Nar Shaddaa]] business community where he was then known as Lorda Nasiri Gejalli. That is before he vanished from [[Hutt Space]] with rumours putting his new home in Wild Space.

Baroness Tarasar, I'd like to start with your thoughts. Biscuit Baron has become one of the primary non-military buyers of combat droids recently. What are your thoughts of the re-emergence of the Colicoid Creation Nest on the galactic scene?"

The golden-skinned girl smiles, "Oh I think it is really beautiful. Until recently we had not seen large scale combat droid production since the [[New Sith Order]] and before that you really have to back to the Clone Wars. Droid's got a really bad rap during that conflict. Like decades later the memories of that still are responsible for a lot of abhorrent anti-droid legislation on various worlds. But it was the Republic's clones that were responsible for the genocide against the [[Jedi Order|Jedi]]. The clones were the ones who became the jackbooted enforcers of [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] fascism as the first [[Stormtrooper|stormtroopers]].

The newly formed Empire and the corporations associated with it seized a lot of the Confederacy's businesses such as Haor Chall Engineering. This represents perhaps and even bigger upheaval in the business community than then the restructuring of the Corporate Sector Authority several years ago. To see these businesses return to the galactic scene, it is like seeing a ecosystem start repairing itself after a fire. So like I said before, it is a beautiful thing."

The anchorwoman then turns to ak, "Dean ak, Haor Chall Engineering has had the run of the market on combat droids and droid starfighters the past couple of years. What are your thoughts on the competition the Colicoid Creation Nest is going to introduce to the market?"

The [[Xi Charrian]] answers through the translation of his articulation conversion sphere, "Well both the Colicoid Creation Nest and Haor Chall Engineering were members of the Techno Union before its collapse. We have had some similarities in design philosophy. So I would tend to look at their return the market as a complimentary effort rather than competition as such.

The droideka is also just such a classic. I mean there may be some sales we don't end up making because of the competition of a Colicoid product, but this should really expand the droid market considerably and lead far more sales opportunity that will more than make up for the competition.

As we always say the deity is in the details. If the Colicoids produce a great project then we can only rejoice in that expression of the perfection of the afterlife. If a product is not so good, then it won't really be competition."

Brackett looks between her two guests, "Last question, Nasiri Zithreej and the Zithreej Combine. Thoughts? Speculation?"

The younger guest fields the question, "Well Brackett I was rather surprised to hear that the Zithreej Combine was able to acquire outright shares in the Creation Nest. But Nasiri is fairly legendary for his ability to ingratiate himself with other cultures and within what seem like otherwise closed systems. He wielded a great deal of influence for quite some time among the [[Hutt|Hutts]]. That is not easily done.

Clearly he is getting the Colicoids back on the map in way they would probably not be able to manage on their own, and that isn't just expression of his financial investment, that is personal prestige. I think we will be hearing about more investments from the Zithreej Combine in due course."

"And that is all we have time for tonight. My thanks to my guests Baroness Tarasar and Dean ak. I will be back with more coverage from Charros IV tomorrow. For now this is Brackett Benduday signing off."

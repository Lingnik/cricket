A human man in a grey suit and combed back hair with brilliant blue eyes sits behind the news counter. As the music fades he looks up into the viewer as if looking at the person watching the screen.

"Good Evening. I am Ted Breakinov with IGNews and here is the latest from Field Reporter Eithne Tamesis on location in Nar Shadaa. She sends word that she is well and that those she is with are mostly well also, though she cannot say how long any of them will survive. It seems that there is a fight for dominance occurring on Nar Shadaa with the current ruling Hutt using dirty tactics to try to keep his holdings. What spurred this war for dominance is still unknown. Miss Tamesis does tell us that she is with a group of what she calls 'Citizen Heroes' those caught up in this mess, fighting to stay alive and to save others. An unlikely group made up of merchants, adventurers and even a few of what society calls cast-outs, but should now call heroes. At this point she does not reveal the names of those fighting, but promises to try to get interviews with some or at least permission to reveal names. Most of the fighting that she witnessed recently was done while traveling on swoop bikes. Grenades and blaster fire tearing not just holes in the walls...but holes where walls '''used''' to be.

There is still no entry or dismissal from Nar Shaddaa. If you have friends or family there, our hearts go out to you tonight, as they do those still trapped on Nar Shadaa. May the Force be with you all. Good Night.

'''IGN broadcasts a message on a number of Independent frequencies some of which can be viewed across the entire galaxy there is no commentating or analysis the message is played without interruption:'''

''Sitting upon an blackened throne a figure in a simple black cloak rests staring simply into the camera, those who know Darth Malign would recognize him.''

"Sentient beings of the galaxy. There are moments when a tipping point is called for, when strife is necessary to move us forward. That time has come once again, over the last few years I have constructed a grand government and taken a substantial portion of the galaxy in shadow. Many of you will not recognize the worlds that make up this New Order, but soon you will all know its nature." he pauses for a moment.

"Tonight, I am here to tell the galaxy, that the Sith have returned. In order to honor the great Emperor Vadim, my master, and to uphold the ideas of the Sith, we are here to bring the galaxy back into alignment with itself, to help promote the growth and development it needs using the tools we are most efficient at using: war." Malign smiles, "I am declaring a Grand Campaign of the Sith and invite all worlds, systems, and governments to join our cause. Naturally, I do not expect some worlds to do so. Either because of a need to maintain neutrality or because of principles that run contrary to ours. That is why I make this statement: You are either an ally or an enemy." he pauses, "To that aim, I herby declare war on any government that does not submit or join into the Sith Order." He stands from his throne, "Change can only be forged in the furnace of battle. If you wish the galaxy to become a better place take up arms and fight for what you stand for, let your emotions and passion guide you. The Galactic Empire, the New Republic, the Caspian Democratic Union, the Hapes Consortium, the Hutt crime lords, they will offer you nothing but stagnation. I will offer you ultimate salvation. Join me on the battle field for glory."

'''The signal is lost'''

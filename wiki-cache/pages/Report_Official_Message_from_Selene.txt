{{IGNpost|author=[[Tyler Damion]]|date=[[34 ABY]]|topic=Political News|anchors=None|headline=Official Message from Selene|synopsis=Imperator [[Tyler Damion|Damion I]] announces the promotion of [[Axel Vichten]] to [[Grand Admiral]].}}

The [[Imperium]] Logo Shows on the Screen Before Fading to Selene

Throngs of cheering Imperium citizens amass in the Imperator Circle as the Imperator emerges on a raised hovering dais with the Queen-Mother of the Hapes Consortium together to address the crowds of [[Selene]].

"Our time of darkness is over. When I first came to Selene, [[Grand Moff]] [[Scaven Marx]] and I built this dome as a symbol of resilience in the face of terror. That strength followed my philosophy as Imperator after the dark times when the Empire was under the heel of another corrupted Warlord." As the Imperator speaks the crowds' chants in a roar with him, 

"We are equal

We are free

We are strength

We are Imperium!"

Flashing, lights, pyrotechnics blast as banners representing the Imperium and Hapes Consortium fall draping down the towering buildings around the circle, "My love, the Queen-Mother was ill, I worked with scientists and doctors and have healed her, and like her, we have also healed our broken world." He gestures, "The science here to bring life back to Selene, was done with the collaboration of our [[Ithorian]] comrades, they will forever be held in the highest of esteem. Along with this success our joint-security and trade pact has also been written, we will forever respect the sacred sovereignty of Ithor and their lush garden world as we will build our garden world here."

More cheers erupt, "In recent months, the Imperium as faced many challenges, the Grand Inquisitor [[Axel Vichten]] has demonstrated his value to our Imperium, the [[Caspian Union]] has demonstrated their friendship and collaboration." He smiles, "Such hard work cannot go unnoticed, with [[Grand Admiral]] [[Drayson Honos]] being promoted to Chairman of the Advisory Council, the Chief of our armed forces must be appointed. I declare Axel Vichten, Grand Admiral of the Imperium's combined military might, as well as special overseer of all Imperium fiefdoms as a special acting Regent to the Imperator's Throne on State, Military, and Corruption Matters. Until our crises are solved, Grand Admiral Vichten must act with the authority of the Imperator. We trust Axel Vichten, a man of the truest virtue to perform these duties with utmost efficiency and with the support of all the Imperium's resources." The Imperator waves to the crowd as banners showing Axel in Grand Admiral garb drop behind the dais to great acclamation and cheering. 

"Finally, Selene, our paradise, we will celebrate. I declare all taxes on Selene to be set to zero for the next year and starting now and for the next month is a celebration of Imperium successes. Glory to Imperium, Glory to Selene, HAIL Axel Vichten Grand Admiral and acting Regent."

The image fades showing the logo of the Imperium.

'''The following broadcast was shown everywhere on Holonets:'''

Standing together, Moff Bailey Laran and the new head of council member of Alien Affairs, Ember Myrtedeu. Ember steps forward speaking first:

:"Citizens of all species, I have a great news to present to you. The Empire and the Council of Alien Affairs is proud to state an open Police Force, manned and run by all species, composed of all persons of the universe. This is a great step towards equality and a new Empire, one that encompasses and accepts all sentient beings within its scope!" 

She smiles and continues.

:"I am proud to be heading this and with the support of Moff Bailey Laran, this new idea shall succeed and hopefully be the predecessor for so many new ideas to come. Moff Laran, would you share a few words with us."

Then woman steps back and the Moff comes forward.

:"When I spoke with Ember, We had a goal and a dream. The Empire, in it's attempt to safeguard it's populace, has made many mistakes. The worst of them was not giving full citizenship to those who are not Human. Ember and I are here to rectify that, today.. You stand as equals..equals within this new Police Force."

The Moff steps back then and Ember lifts her voice, 

:"So we ask you to join us in a celebration of this, right here on Coruscant. We look forward to having all of you there, let it be a sign of things to come and things that are."

Thus the transmission ends, the two figures fading.

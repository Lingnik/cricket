A Human Man dressed in a fine white shirt and blue tie, hair slicked into place he adjusts his seat and speaks directly to the camera from behind the news counter.

"Nar Shaddaa's space is locked down. No one is able to come into Nar space or leave the planet. Two ships are in orbit enforcing the lock down. The 'Noquella's Embrace' is a non-imperial Interdictor Cruiser and the other ship, 'Predator' is an I2SD. We will continue to bring you this story as we get more information. Luckily, at the same time we received word from one of our field reporters Eithne Tamesis. Unfortunately, at this time, we are unable to reach Miss Tamesis. We do have footage she sent us. These are events occurring right now on Nar Shaddaa at a bar it seems though we do not have the name of the establishment."

He nods to the camera and turns to look even as the screen fades and a horrific display begins.

''The camera turns on and adjusts to focus on a near-human man standing in the center of the room, looking as if it belonged to him. There is suddenly an explosion inward into the room and a human man, unrecognizable as he is flying in one direction and the camera is now tumbling in dizzying circles to the floor. Even upside down, the giant wookiee who appears to bemassive and eager for death - not his own. The roar from someone in the place of "Kill them all!! We don't know who to trust!" seems to be the catalyst that throws the entire building into complete bloody chaos. As feet fly past, the picture is suddenly righted and lifted backwards. Obviously the reporter has moved behind the bar as we see the bartender lifting a gun and aiming to fire, only to have a rather large hole opened in his torso. If that weren't enough the man who began all of this by being literally thrown into the room suddenly lands on the now dead bartender. It seems the man cannot keep his feet. But at this range those who ever met him would recognize Tyler Damion. Horrid sounds of breaking metal and wood and then shouting.... Tyler lets out a call "If you want to fight with Tatooine Bob!! Form up around the bar we're charging these Sithspits!"''

The reporter returns to the screen, his face straight with seriousness of having a fellow reporter in such a situation, or perhaps hiding the jealousy that it wasn't him? "The feed went dead shortly there-after. As for right now this is all we have. Hopefully Miss Tamesis is still alive and will be able to tell us what is going on."

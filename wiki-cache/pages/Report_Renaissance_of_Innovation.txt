The familiar sight of the [[InterGalactic News]] anchor's desk with the [[Firrerreo]] economist and journalist, Professor [[Brackett Benduday]] behind it. Behind her are five images. The middle one is [[Union Hall]] in [[Plaxton]] on [[Caspar]] while the two to the left and right of it are starfighters.

"While civil strife still continues in the New Republic and the Biscuit Baron employees on [[Athaniss]] and [[O'paal]] have joined in strikes, our top story tonight is out of Caspar. I am Brackett Benduday, and this is InterGalactic News.

Union Hall was the site last night of a major address by [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] inaugurating the [[Caspian Union|Caspian Union's]] [[Second Renaissance of Innovation]]. Following an introduction by Vice Presav [[Cas Mos]], and brief remarks by Presav Tarasar, the floor was turned over to Councillor [[ak]] of Xi Can, who recently topped a Plaxton Polling Institute survey on who is the popular member of the [[Union Council]].

Councillor ak in his capacity of the Dean of the Xi Can [[Cathedral Factory]] and Executive Vice President of [[Haor Chall Engineering]] announced four new starfighter designs that have been produced for the [[Caspian Navy]] complete with a fly by of the craft involved.

Guests came from throughout the Union and beyond. It was a real who's who with famous names including former Presav, [[Grand Moff]] [[Elana Tracer]] in attendance with Grand Moff [[Etiel te Danaan]], former First Lady Doctor [[Lynae Cassius]], and [[Noques Rasala|Noques Rasala's]] favourite son Inquisitor [[Axel Vichten]]. The world of fashion was well represented too with [[Zubindi Hakoon]] in attendance along with one of his more recent models [[Nara Rhen|Nara]]. The military flag officers in attendance are too numerous to mention individually."

The screen behind Brackett shifts to larger images of each craft as you announces it, According to information released by the Caspian Ministry of [[Caspian Foreign Ministry|Foreign Affairs]] and Defence these craft include the [[Mark IV Vulture-Class Droid Starfighter]], the [[Guardian-Class Starfighter]] a model which has been built for special use by [[Yari|Yaries]], both of which we are told will be deployed to [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruisers]] and [[Kichnar Orbital Station]]. Also announced were the [[Vector-Class Starfighter|Vector-Class]] and [[Apex-Class Starfighter|Apex-Class]] two similar fighters for planetary defence, the latter of which has been designed for use by [[Wookiee|Wookiees]] and other similarly size beings."

The screen then shifts to images of two combat droids, "It was also announced by Councillor ak and Councillor [[Sirlahn Inglet]] of Kichnar, the Union's Minister of Science and Technology, that Haor Chall Engineering was also producing two battle droids designs the [[E5 Baron Droid]] and [[IG-240 Hailfire-Class Droid Tank]]."

The image on the screen is then replaced with a planet identified as [[Charros IV]], "The event concluded with an announcement that the [[Xi Charrian]] homeworld of Charros IV would host [[Charros Wargames|war games]] for the [[Caspian Defence Force]] to highlight these new designs. This is expected to be quite a boon for the almost non-existent tourist industry on the world. It should be noted that roughly 100 million non Xi-Charrians live on Charros IV, largely employed in industry, commerce, transportation, and defence."

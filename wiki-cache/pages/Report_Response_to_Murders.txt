[[Senator]] Yorgon of [[:starwars:Deysum III|Deysum III]] released a statement to [[InterGalactic News|IGN]] day as the [[New Republic Senate|Senate]] finished its most recent recess to take to the galaxy the following message: 

Recently the people of Deysum III were subjected to something which no free planet or system should ever experience. A murder most foul committed with no other purpose than to intimidate and terrorize our people. This will not, and can not stand. I will be demanding that action take place and alongside my honorable colleague, the senator from [[Kashyyyk]], will put forward a resolution in the Senate that the government take action against whomever is responsible for this crime, as well as the crimes against the [[Wookiee|Wookiees]] of Kashyyyk that were committed by the [[Trandoshan]] slavers and those who aided and abetted these atrocities. Now is the time to take action, lest we allow ourselves to be at the mercy of pirates and cutthroats. If our government lacks the courage to do what must be done, in which case it ought to step aside and let those willing to do the work carry it out.

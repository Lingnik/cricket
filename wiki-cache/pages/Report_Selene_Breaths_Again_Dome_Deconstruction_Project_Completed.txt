{{IGNpost|author=[[Tyler Damion]]|date=[[34 ABY]]|topic=Environmental News|anchors=None|headline=Selene Breaths Again! Dome Deconstruction Project Completed|synopsis=A major milestone is reached in the joint [[Ithorian]]-[[Imperium]] 'Open Sky Initiative.'}}

For the first time in nearly two decades, the true sky has returned to [[Selene]]. Earlier today, under the watchful eyes of thousands, the final, colossal segment of the environmental dome was lowered and dismantled, officially ending an era of confinement and heralding a new dawn for the planet.

The moment was met with a chorus of cheers that echoed between the skyscrapers, as citizens who have spent much of their lives under the artificial canopy witnessed the unfiltered daylight stream into the city's heart. The dome, once a miracle of engineering and a symbol of survival against a toxic wasteland, is now a monument to the past.

"My whole life, the sky was a ceiling," commented Lyra Vant, a 20-year-old university student, wiping a tear from her eye as she stood in Imperator Circle. "To feel the natural wind on my face... to know that the stars we see tonight will be real, not a simulation... it is a gift I never thought I would receive. The [[Imperium]] has given us back our world."

The 'Open Sky Initiative' is the crowning achievement of the large-scale [[Ithorian]]-Imperium terraforming project that have successfully revitalized Selene's atmosphere. While citizens are still advised to carry protective gear when traveling to the outer wilds, the air within the [[Imperial City (Selene)|Imperial City]] and the Lakeview districts has been declared safe and invigorating.

While the Imperator or senior leadership were unavailable for comment, celebrations are scheduled throughout the week, but for now, a quiet, profound sense of awe has settled over the populace. From the highest landing pads to the bustling markets, the people of Selene are taking a collective breath of fresh air and looking up, together, at their boundless future.

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;This story is broadcast throughout the New Republic, but sees particularly heavy airtime on Ord Mantell's local news broadcasts:<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Good evening, I'm Ghon Stoort. Our top story tonight, the New Republic Senate has made a ruling in regards to a speech delivered on the senate floor just yesterday by one Aranzael Moyat, a representative for the Mandalorian people. From all reports, the debate was ''particularly'' heated, and seems to have caused quite a stir within the Republic's government sphere, as well as the Republic itself. For the ruling, we turn now to chief political analyst Steefin Col'bhar. Steefin?"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The view shifts to a grim-faced Chiss in business dress standing outside New Alderaan's government complex. He arches one eyebrow and adjusts his specs.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"The Mandalorian's speech has stirred up what we in the business refer to as a 'poodoo storm of epic proportions' --"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ghon is pictured, staring dumbfoundedly into the camera, and he speaks hesitantly. "Ah, Steefin, I-I don't think that language is.. is appropriate --"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Steefin raises his voice to be heard over Ghon's feed, ignoring him. "The senate engaged in fiery, heated debate well into the evening hours, and we have recieved reports of at least five instances of thrown styluses and datapads having occured before they were prepared to rule."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"And, ah, and the results, Steefin?"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Well, Ghon, due in particular to the efforts of Senator Marx, the senate has voted to ''grant'' Mister Moyat's request, and have granted him the position of Emissary of the Mandalorian peoples. He's basically something like a diplomat with body armor and really big guns, Ghon. It is truly a ''sweet'' gig."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ghon holds up a hand. "That.. that sounds excellent, Steefin. You mentioned Senator Marx in particular?"<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"She threw most of the projectiles, Ghon."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"...ah. Thank you, Steefin."<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"Stay beautiful, Ghon."<br>

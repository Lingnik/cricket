'''The live broadcast of a Limmie game throughout the Corellia system was interrupted without warning. There was no pre-programmed signal apologizing for the inconvenience, and by the dark screen it might have been thought to be a technical malfunction. But then a white symbol started to form pixel by pixel until it resolved itself into a white five-pointed star, the tip of each triangle formed into a circle. The image shifts rapidly to a darkened room, just in front of the camera used to shoot this scene is a table-covered by a white cloth amd behind it a faded bulkhead. The fabric's colour matches the white hoods that the three men seated at the table, the rest of their outfits made up of greens and blues. The one at the middle of the trio addresses the group, his voice distorted by an unseen device.'''

We blew up the IHS' new headquarters, we were the ones that planted the bomb and we were the ones that detonated it. For this, the Empire and its allies within the Corellian government have branded us traitors and terrorists. We are in fact that. We hold no loyalty to a government that is not of our choosing and where the decisions that affect our lives are made far off. We also vow to bring terror to any who would seek to enslave Corellians and use them as pawns in galactic power plays.

We ask Corellians to stop ignoring the disappearances of those who disagree with Imperial rule. We ask them to stop ignoring the workers who strike on the shipyards, the ones who protest against building the very weapons that are used to keep them on a tight leash.

We are Tan Urchak Nolle, Five Worlds Unity. We invite you to join the fight for Corellia's freedom.If you are not able to then your moral support will be enough, but we make no distinction between the Imperial overlords and those who assist them.

We were once a free people, we will be so again.

'''Just as suddenly, the pirate broadcast ends and the limmie game resumes, its announcers unaware that they were interrupted.'''

{{IGNpost|author=[[Thurston Unger]]|date=[[34 ABY]]|topic=Advertisement|anchors=[[Thurston Unger]]|headline=The Cutting Edge - A Taste of Chaos|synopsis=[[Thurston Unger]] teases a new partnership with [[Crimson Nerf Energy]].}}

The holofeed snaps into life with its usual fanfare-rays of shifting color sweep across a sleek chrome intro as the words "[[The Cutting Edge]] with [[Thurston Unger]]" rotate into place, dramatically over a backdrop of a burning senate speech and a holographic fashion runway. The camera then fades in on THURSTON UNGER himself, seated not in his usual high-backed throne of judgement, but atop a floating hover-platform on a skyline balcony overlooking [[Etti IV|Etti IV's]] neon nightlife.

He is radiant.

Tonight, Thurston dons a sharp blood-red suit layered with sheer shimmering black mesh, silver accents gleaming at his cuffs and collarbones. His lavender hair is styled in a weightless windblown swoop, and a five-o'clock shadow graces his jawline with just enough defiance to make it fashion.

The city behind him pulses with light, and so does he.

He leans forward into the lens with a grin that spells nothing but trouble. "My darlings, my decadent disciples of disruption! I know what you're thinking. 'Where's the gossip? Where's the scandal? Who's being a despot in designer boots this week?' And trust me, we will get there. But tonight-"

He claps once, sharply. The holo behind him changes: a blazing red can emblazoned with a charging nerf made entirely out of plasma erupts into view. The words [[Crimson Nerf Energy|Crimson Nerf]]: UNLEASH THE FURY flash dramatically.

"-tonight is about energy. About drive. About kicking down the airlock of mediocrity and screaming into the void with style." He leans back with a knowing smirk, letting the silence stretch before continuing. "You see, my loves, The Cutting Edge is evolving. We've danced with dictators, tangoed with terrorists, and pirouetted through propaganda-but it's time to channel that chaos into something carbonated."

Thurston holds up a sleek, unreleased can of Crimson Nerf Energy-matte black with iridescent red glyphs wrapping the sides. "That's right. Yours truly has officially partnered with the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector's]] newest and most fabulous [[Direx Board (CSA)|DIREX]] member, the one, the only-Crimson Nerf Energy."

A fireworks display bursts behind him from somewhere off-screen. Definitely planned. "Yes, they could have chosen a bounty hunter, a podracer, or some flavorless senator to be their face-but instead they chose moi. Because who else knows more about dancing with death and looking gorgeous doing it?"

Thurston stands, letting the city breeze catch his open jacket just right. "And now, a tease worthy of the tease-erati: in just a few short weeks, we'll be unveiling a brand new flavor of Crimson Nerf-inspired by The Cutting Edge. What does it taste like? Rumor says fire and secrets. Others say it burns like the truth. All I'll say is-it's best served cold, under emergency lighting."

Thurston winks and smiles coyly. "So stay tuned, my little disruptors. Because this partnership? It's not just a sponsorship. It's a revolution in a can."

Raising a can high, Thurston exclaims, "Crimson Nerf. Unleash the Fury. And stay on the Edge."

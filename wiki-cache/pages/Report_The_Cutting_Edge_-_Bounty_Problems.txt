As [[Thurston Unger|Thurston]] saunters down the bustling street of [[Corellia]], getting some footage for the episode for the upcoming casino gala tonight, a young bounty hunter steps out from the crowd, his eyes fixed on the flamboyant reporter. The hunter is named Jaxon, with short black hair, a chiseled jawline, and a confident swagger. He's dressed in a black leather jacket with various holsters and pouches adorning his belt.

Jaxon clears his throat and steps forward, standing in front of Thurston with a wide grin on his face. "Excuse me, Mr. Unger. I'm a huge fan of your show, [[The Cutting Edge]]. May I have a moment of your time?"

Thurston raises an eyebrow, his gaze sweeping over the young hunter with amusement. "Why certainly, darling. What can I do for you?" he asks with a smile. Thurston truly loved his fans.

Jaxon's grin grows even wider as he reaches into his pocket, pulling out a datapad. "I was just checking the bounty channels, and it seems there's a 25,000 credit bounty on your head, for delivery to [[Nar Shaddaa]]. It's not through the guild though, so probably no real need for concern. Gotta be a response to your interview with [[Nasiri Zithreej|Nasiri Gejalli]]"

Thurston chuckles, taking the datapad from Jaxon and examining the information. "Is that so? How quaint."

Jaxon nods, his hand resting on the hilt of his blaster pistol. "I just wanted to let you know, in case you needed any protection or anything."

Thurston smirks, handing the datapad back to Jaxon. "Protection? My dear boy, I've been in this business for far too long to be intimidated by a paltry sum like 25,000 credits. If Nasiri Gejalli wants to play games, I'm more than happy to oblige."

Jaxon nods, a hint of disappointment in his voice. "Well, I understand. I just wanted to let you know."

Thurston claps Jaxon on the shoulder, a mischievous glint in his eye. "Fear not, young hunter. Though, if you really want to protect rather than hunt, the [[Security Division (CSA)|ESPO]] force of the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector]] is always hiring. Who knows, my love, you might even get assigned to my security detail," he says with a wink to the young man.

Jaxon laughs, his eyes lighting up with excitement. "I like the way you think, Mr. Thurston. Keep up the good work."

Thurston nods, giving Jaxon a small bow as he turns to continue down the street, his mind already focused on the next interview.

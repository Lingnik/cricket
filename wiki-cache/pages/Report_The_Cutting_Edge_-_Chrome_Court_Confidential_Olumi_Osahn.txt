LIVE TRANSMISSION INITIATED: [[InterGalactic News|IGNews]] Emergency Segment
"[[The Cutting Edge]]: Chrome Court Confidential"
Broadcast from the [[Full Frontal Fabulous]], en route through the Neraine Drift

INT. LAVENDER-LIT STUDIO SUITE - THE VELVET LOUNGE

(Backdrop: rolling starfield, holographic overlays of cult iconography, dramatic spotlighting. A glowing banner scrolls across the screen: "Emergency Panel: [[Olumi Osahn]] Chic or Cultish Catastrophe?")

[[Thurston Unger|Thurston]] is seated center-stage on a silver S-thread divan, sporting a shimmering violet jacquard jumpsuit with razor-cut lapels and a crown of chromasteel thorns woven delicately into his new cerulean finger-wave hairdo. "Darlings, when the galaxy gives you darkness, you accessorize. And tonight, the veil is lifting-if just a sequin's width-on the shadowy aesthetic of a certain cult of whispers and wardrobe crimes: the Olumi Osahn. Allegedly linked to our dear [[Grand Admiral]] [[Axel Vichten|Vichten]]-who, I might remind you, hasn't so much as denied it-we at IGNews feel it is our moral duty to dissect their fashion sense with the same scrutiny we apply to war crimes and bad blush."

He sips glamorously from a martini glass etched with the [[Crimson Nerf Energy|Crimson Nerf]] "Glam Shock" logo. As the Chrome Court enters. Each fabulously clad protocol droid, custom built for the Cutting Edge by Cybot Galactica walks into the holo and takes a seat around Thurston.

Pearletta - (Powder-pink plating, opera-length chrome gloves)
"Reports suggest the Olumi Osahn favor robes. Heavy. Hooded. Ominous. It's giving 'choked by legacy' with a splash of 'don't perceive me.' No capes. No sparkle. No joy."

Gildra Vox - (Gold filigree chassis, digital lashes)
"The layering, darling, is positively funereal. Charcoal linens, matte leathers, and textures that scream, 'I read ancient texts and scowl at sunlight.' They are clearly hiding something-like color."

Tarnish LaRue - (Distressed mirrored plating, rusted on purpose)
"And let's not ignore the absence of tailoring. The silhouettes are either monastic or mortuary. If someone's not bleeding power from their hemline, are they even doctrinally aligned?"

Cymbaline-6 - (Reflective paneling, spiked heels built into servos)
"Conjecture aside, one has to ask: is this fashion meant to conceal identity-or uniformize devotion? Because let's be honest, honey-if you're serving blind obedience, you better be serving looks."

Vel Veela - (Art Deco chestplates, rhinestone voice modulator)
"Shall we address the whisper that these aesthetics are meant to suppress the ego? Because nothing says 'I'm humble' like synchronized robes and sacrificial couture."

Marquiis D'Mode - (Obsidian-chrome armor with satin underlay)
"It's not a look, darling. It's an apology. And not a very stylish one."

Thurston leans forward, the glitter in his eyes matching the shimmer of the Court's plating, "While the Olumi Osahn may try to cloak themselves in mystery and meaning, we at The Cutting Edge will always strip down to the truth-layer by sullen layer. Until they put out a formal fashion statement-or at least a capsule collection-our brows shall remain arched and our lights, unflattering."

{{IGNpost|author=[[Thurston Unger]]|date=[[34 ABY]]|topic=Prison Break|anchors=[[Thurston Unger]]|headline=The Cutting Edge - Confidence and Conquest, or Just Another Curtain Call?|synopsis=[[Thurston Unger]] remarks on the severity of the [[Imperium]] response to the [[The Oubliette|Spacestation 1138]] escapees and the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] involvement in the operation.}}

The holofeed blinks to life with the shimmer of a high-gloss intro sequence-[[The Cutting Edge|THE CUTTING EDGE]] logo sliding dramatically across a field of crushed velvet stars. When the logo dissolves, we cut to [[Thurston Unger]] himself, seated on a baroque chaise in the Mirror Throne Room aboard the [[Full Frontal Fabulous]]. A beam of soft lavender light kisses the side of his cheekbone at a truly cinematic angle, his lavender hair styled in a gravity-defying swoop. His ensemble: black silk, lined with cerulean sequins that shift in hue every time he gestures-and darlings, he gestures a lot.

"Oh my celestial divas and devils-have you felt the chill across the Holonet this cycle? No, no, not a weather report, though I do feel a low-pressure system of self-importance coming in strong from Selene..."

A quick cut to the now-infamous black-and-silver podium from the [[Imperium]] Press Briefing, followed by a glittering transition back to Thurston, who gives the camera a look that could best be described as delicately appalled.

"Enter Zephara Val'Riin, the Ice Queen of Inquisitorial Messaging, gliding into her sanctum of polished repression to tell us... well, next to nothing, really. And yet, somehow with all the gravitas of a Sith Lord delivering your tax return. 'This will be brief, and sufficient,' she said-and my darling viewers, was it ever. I've seen entire wars declared with more warmth."

Thurston leans forward just enough to pull the audience closer, voice dipped in conspiratorial sweetness. "Let's unpack, shall we? [[:starwars:Karthakk sector/Legends|Karthakk]]: still a mess, but a very controlled one, if you believe what was said. Seven escapees recaptured-'judgment rendered on-site,' which is a very chic euphemism for executed without trial. But don't worry, my sweets, Zephara assured us these weren't 'dramatic executions.' Heavens, no. Just the quiet, dignified kind we don't get to see, because apparently, the Imperium believes spectacle is gauche."

He plucks a crimson datacard from the nearby vanity and twirls it. "Oh, but you know what wasn't gauche? The very public shout-out to the [[Caspian Union|Caspian Union's]] [[Caspian 4th Fleet|4th Fleet]]-basking in the spotlight like it's Fashion Week in [[:starwars:Zeltros/Legends|Zeltros]]. Apparently, their interdiction and siege support was instrumental in cleaning up the mess at [[The Oubliette|Station 1138]]. Hmm. How convenient."

A single drum beat can be heard in the background. Thurston smirks. "And isn't it just curious-purely an observation, mind you-that the Caspian Union keeps getting these little cameos in the Imperium's grimmest drama pieces? I mean, it's not like their leader, [[Crestian Tarasar]], holds the title of [[Moff]] or anything. Or that he's a personal acquaintance of Grand Inquisitor [[Axel Vichten]]. No, no-surely that's all just cosmic coincidence. Not like the Grand Inquisitor isn't also some crazy religious leader of some shadow cult on Caspar, at least according to rumors. Oh, and he has a flag officer position in the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]], some cute little title like [[Marshal|Star Marshal]] or Space Lord or whatever. None of that could really matter, right? It's all just coincidence."

Thurston's fingers flutter and he rolls his eyes. "But then again, so was the black site prison itself. Off the books, off the record, and off the moral rails if you ask me. And if you don't ask me, I'm telling you anyway, because that's the Cutting Edge promise."

He sips from a sleek black can of Glam Shock Hard and gives the camera a wink. "As for dear Zephara-always a vision of midnight menace-she reminded us with that razor-thin smile that the Imperium 'does not govern through transparency.' Oh honey, we noticed. If her briefings get any more opaque, they'll qualify as [[Sith Order|Sith]] artifacts."

The camera slowly zooms in, Thurston's tone sharpening with purpose. "Now, don't get me wrong, kittens-chaos doesn't need a spotlight. But when you're executing fugitives in the shadows, quoting star charts, and offering more poetic threats than practical updates, you don't look like you're asserting order. You look like you're performing control. And I perform for a living, darlings-I know the difference."

He stands, the camera following as he walks toward the panoramic viewport at the end of the room. The stars glitter beyond, as glamorous and untouchable as ever. "So here's your headline: The Imperium wants confidence without clarity. But confidence isn't a monologue. It's a dialogue. And if they won't speak plainly, we will."

He turns back, that signature smirk in full bloom. "Until next time, my darlings-watch the shadows. Because some of them are trying very hard to watch you back."

The feed cuts to the shimmering Cutting Edge logo with the tagline: Sharp Tongue. Sharper Truth. Which is quickly followed by the [[Crimson Nerf Energy|Crimson Nerf]] logo and the tagline: Unleash the Fury!

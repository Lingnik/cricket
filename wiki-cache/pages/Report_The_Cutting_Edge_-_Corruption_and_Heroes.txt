{{IGNpost|author=[[Thurston Unger]]|date=[[32 ABY]]|topic=Rogue Marines|anchors=[[Thurston Unger]]|headline=The Cutting Edge - Corruption and Heroes|synopsis=[[Thurston Unger]] reports on the corruption in the [[New Republic]] that led the [[DSV Templar|Templar]] to go rogue.}}

"In the shadowy corners of the [[New Republic|New Republic's]] bureaucracy, a web of corruption entangles the gears of justice, leaving countless innocents to suffer. But as the Republic turned a blind eye, a group of rogue heroes emerged from the ranks of the [[DSV Templar|Templar]], determined to right the wrongs the system refused to acknowledge."

"I am [[Thurston Unger]], the host of [[The Cutting Edge]], here to shed light on this scandalous affair." Thurston stands in front of the imposing facade of the [[New Republic Senate]] building, ready to peel back the layers of deception.

"Darlings, prepare yourselves for an expose that will shake the very foundations of the New Republic," Thurston proclaims, his voice resonating with purpose. "While the Republic indulges in inaction, a band of renegades known as the crew of the Templar have taken matters into their own hands."

As cameras flash, capturing the attention of passersby, Thurston delves into the depths of the corrupt labyrinth. He presents evidence of illicit dealings, cover-ups, and a culture of indifference that had permeated the Republic's ranks for far too long.

"But this story goes beyond documents and testimonies. It is a tale of the people left behind, the forgotten victims of a system that failed to protect them." Thurston recounted the stories of those impacted by the Republic's corruption, their lives shattered by the very institution meant to safeguard their rights.

With each revelation, Thurston's voice grows stronger, his words resonating with the hearts of the galaxy's disenfranchised. He paints a vivid picture of the crew of the Templar, the band of renegades who had taken up arms despite the corruption they witnessed firsthand.

"Led by the stoic and enigmatic [[Colonel]] [[Han'bel Eckhart]], the crew of the Templar have become champions of the common people, fighting for justice where the Republic has failed. Their exploits, whispered among the oppressed, inspire hope and a renewed belief in the power of individual action," Thurston continues.

Thurston details their daring missions to do what the Republic military should be doing, their defiance of authority, and their relentless pursuit to help those in need, regardless of politics. "The crew of the Templar, once loyal to the Republic, have found themselves disillusioned and disheartened by the very institution they had sworn to protect, and they are not alone. I've heard from several officers, who will remain anonymous for now, have stated they will follow the example of the marines on the Templar."

"Through The Cutting Edge, we give voice to the voiceless, emboldening others to step forward and share their experiences of corruption. Let these scandals serve as a catalyst for change, my darlings. The galaxy has seen its share of strife to last a lifetime. While there are countless worlds and cultures, and our beliefs may vary, we can unite for change. Even in the face of overwhelming corruption, the power to effect change rests in the hands of the people, in your hands my darlings."

As the story reaches its crescendo, Thurston stands tall, his voice filled with unwavering resolve. He calls upon the citizens of the galaxy to unite, to stand beside the crew of the Templar and anyone else willing to join them in their fight against the corrupt forces that sought to maintain their grip on power.

The camera cuts to a closing shot of Thurston, his eyes ablaze with determination, his hair blowing in the wind, his sequin covered pants suit reflecting light in all directions, a shining beacon of truth in defiance of the Republic Senate building he stands in front of. The Cutting Edge logo scrolls across the screen in slow motion, eventually fading to black.

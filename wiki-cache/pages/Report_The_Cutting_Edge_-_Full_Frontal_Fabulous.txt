{{IGNpost|author=[[Thurston Unger]]|date=[[34 ABY]]|topic=Advertisement|anchors=[[Thurston Unger]]|headline=The Cutting Edge - Full Frontal Fabulous|synopsis=[[Thurston Unger]] introduces his ship the [[Full Frontal Fabulous]] and details of his new collaboration with [[Crimson Nerf Energy]].}}

The familiar jingle of [[The Cutting Edge]] echoed through the holofeed, but tonight, the background wasn't the usual sleek [[InterGalactic News|IGNews]] studio or even a scenic planetary skyline. Instead, it opened on the shining, iridescent hull of a heavily modified [[CR90 Corvette|Corellian Corvette]], gliding above the gas giant of [[:starwars:Genassa system|Genessa Prime]] in glorious orbit. Glistening with chromatic reds, glimmering silvers, and the unmistakable logo of [[Crimson Nerf Energy]] stretched across the dorsal hull, it was unmistakable:

The [[Full Frontal Fabulous]].

The feed cut inside to [[Thurston Unger]], framed dramatically by the shimmering corridor lighting-accented, of course, in crimson and lavender. He wore a sharply tailored fusion-flightsuit in gleaming midnight violet with holographic pinstripes that shimmered whenever he moved. His hair was styled in a swept-back lavender pompadour, and his signature smirk was turned to full wattage.

"Darlings, it's happened. The rumors, the leaks, the dramatic whispers in upper-tier lounges of [[Etti IV]]? All true. The Cutting Edge and Crimson Nerf Energy have officially... merged passions." He raised a branded can with a wink. "But we'll get to this little beauty in a moment."

Behind him, the ship's grand reception lounge came into view-decked in velvet seating, polished chrome bar installations, holographic panels tuned to dramatic lighting and breaking fashion alerts, and a full crew of glamazon-styled IGNews staff bots.

"This isn't just a ship, loves. This is a mobile empire of media, madness, and mockery. Outfitted by [[Rendili StarDrive|Rendili StarDrive's]] elite designers, with internal systems by [[:starwars:Cybot Galactica/Legends|Cybot Galactica]]-thank you for the mood-sync lighting!-this corvette has been completely reimagined for the galaxy's most daring journalist. Me."

He glided dramatically toward the rear observation window where a sleek, customized [[Lambda-class Shuttle]] could be seen landing inside the docking bay.

"And when I don't want to bring the whole Fabulous to the party, I've got this darling little enabler-my personal shuttle, the Side Piece. Perfect for when you want to slip in and out without drawing too much attention... or just 

The holofeed transitioned to a dramatic, slow-motion product reveal. A new can sat center screen. The label gleamed with metallic tones and a stylized Cutting Edge logo bursting from a vibrant, magenta splash.

Introducing: "Glam Shock!"
The Cutting Edge Flavor of Crimson Nerf Energy
Tastes like victory, rebellion, and a full-body sparkle.

"And yes, yes, yes-before you ask, my delicious deviants-it comes in two varieties." He held up a second can: the same Glam Shock logo but wrapped in black chrome.

Glam Shock: DARK
Because some nights don't need a morning.
"Now available across the Corporate Sector and select planets brave enough to allow this much fabulous in one container."

A final sweeping shot panned from the bridge of the Full Frontal Fabulous to the launch bay where the Side Piece rested under a spotlight.

Thurston turned to the camera one last time. "So, my darlings, whether you're cruising the stars, breaking galactic conspiracies, or just surviving another dismal planetary government-Unleash the Fury. Glam Shock is here. And I'm coming with it."

The screen faded to a pulse-pounding neon montage of the drink, the ships, and the signature catchphrase:
"Crimson Nerf Energy + The Cutting Edge: Too Bold to Be Legal."

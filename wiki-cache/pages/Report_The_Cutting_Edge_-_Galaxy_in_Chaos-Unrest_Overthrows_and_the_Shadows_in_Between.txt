{{IGNpost|author=[[Thurston Unger]]|date=[[34 ABY]]|topic=Galactic Politics|anchors=[[Thurston Unger]]|headline=Galaxy in Chaos-Unrest, Overthrows, and the Shadows in Between|synopsis=[[Thurston Unger]] questions whether recent events in the galaxy, taken together, are signs of the fall of the current galactic order.}}

"Oh, my darlings, what a time to be alive!"

The studio lights bathe [[Thurston Unger|Thurston]] in a warm glow, reflecting brilliantly off his shimmering, deep violet suit-because, of course, if the galaxy is teetering on the brink of chaos, one must look their absolute best while reporting on it. Behind him, the massive holoscreen shifts between riots on [[Chandrila]], [[Moff]] Jenis Octal's forced removal in the [[:starwars:Karthakk system|Karthakk system]], and the [[Imperium|Imperium's]] bold power plays in [[Hutt Space|Hutt space]]. The imagery is jarring-angry mobs, marching troops, flashing blaster fire, political figures scrambling for control.

He turns to the camera with a dazzling, knowing smile.

"Lovelies, do you feel it? That subtle, creeping feeling in the air? That delicious, terrible uncertainty? Something is happening. Something big. And for once, I am not exaggerating!?

Thurston steps away from the anchor desk, strolling toward the holoscreen, gesturing with dramatic flair as he breaks it all down.

"Let's start with Chandrila, the so-called beacon of Republic civility. Ah, what a mess we've found ourselves in. Protests? Absolutely. Riots? Without a doubt. Talk of secession? Oh, yes, sweethearts, you heard that right. The people of Chandrila-historically one of the [[New Republic|New Republic's]] staunchest supporters-are suddenly questioning whether their government is worth the bureaucratic nightmare that comes with it. And, can you blame them? Corruption, incompetence, the inability to protect its own citizens-it's all coming to a head. And if Chandrila is having second thoughts, how long before more systems follow suit?"

He waves his hand, and the holoscreen shifts-now showing a looming Star Destroyer over [[Nar Shaddaa]], the Inquisitor's infamous demand for the Bantha Tracks, and finally, the growing power vacuum left behind in Karthakk.

"And then, my loves, we have the Imperium. Just as the New Republic is eating itself alive, the Grand Inquisitor, [[Axel Vichten]], is making sure the galaxy knows that the Imperium is not to be trifled with. Stealing ships out of Hutt space, ousting a Moff with no explanation, and, gasp, running an entire system out of his own office until the Moff Council decides what in the stars they're doing? Oh, it's giving 'power consolidation,' sweeties. It's giving 'I dare you to stop me.'

And do you know what? No one is stopping him."

Thurston lets the words settle, his expression a mix of amusement and concern.

"So, let me ask you this, my sweet viewers. Are we watching the fall of the galaxy as we know it? Or are we merely pawns in a much grander game, one being played from the shadows? Because I have to say, darlings, the timing of all this is too perfect. The Republic is faltering, the Imperium is making bold moves, and I can't shake the feeling that someone, somewhere, is pulling the strings while we dance to their tune."

He sighs, dramatically placing a hand on his hip before flashing his signature grin.

"Oh, but we'll find out, won't we, my loves? Because you know me-I never stop asking questions. And as long as the galaxy keeps giving me stories, I'll keep giving you the truth. Or, at the very least, the best-dressed version of it.?

The screen fades to black with the [[InterGalactic News|IGNews]] logo spinning into view.

"Until next time, my darlings-stay sharp, stay scandalous, and stay on [[The Cutting Edge]]."

The holo fades in on the Mirror Throne Room, drenched in seductive purple light. The throne-currently set to Reclined Intrigue Mode-is cradling a dazzling [[Thurston Unger]]. He's styled in a shimmering backless pantsuit, high collar flaring like righteous gossip, and his current hairstyle is a bold, wind-swept platinum-pink asymmetrical sweep with holographic streaks that subtly shimmer in the ship's ambient lighting.

He sips slowly from a crystal flute, filled with something suspiciously too pink to be natural, and then graces the holocam with a conspiratorial smile.

"Darlings. Darlings. Daaaaaaarlings."

The screen cuts briefly to a loop of [[Selene]] celebrations, banners of [[Axel Vichten]] unfurling with all the subtlety of a weapons-grade coronation.

"Selene is throwing a party. A big one. Fireworks, tax relief, cheers that totally aren't pre-recorded. Because, in case you missed it, the Grand Inquisitor Axel Vichten has just been promoted-unleashed, really-as the new [[Grand Admiral]] and Acting Regent of the [[Imperium]]."

The holofeed cuts back to Thurston, arching one brow high enough to breach low orbit. "A promotion, they say. A response to crises, they claim. But I call it what it is: handing a vibroblade to a man already halfway through the drapes and telling him to 'go fix the furniture.'"

Behind him, a stylized map of power centers pulses as the word Regent flashes like an incoming threat. "And yes, yes, we've all heard the lovely speech from Miss Glacial Deathstare herself, Zephara Val'Riin. All order, no questions, and absolutely zero eyeliner smudges. Zephara, my darling, if intimidation were a fragrance, you'd be the entire perfume counter."

Thurston snaps fingers cuing a laugh track from one of the Chrome Court droids. "But let's not pretend this all dropped out of hyperspace without a trace. Our freshly minted Regent? [[Caspar|Caspian]] citizen. Longtime friend of [[Crestian Tarasar]], [[Presav]] of the [[Caspian Union]]. And while I would never suggest anyone in power is acting on anyone else's behalf, let's just say the connections are... seasoned."

He gestures vaguely to a swirling web of holo-headlines behind him, each conveniently lacking official denial. Thurston leans in, voice lowering. "Also-and don't quote me, but do absolutely leak this to your nearest tavern-rumors persist that our boy Axel has dabbled in the deeper corners of Caspar's spiritual scene. Yes, that scene. The kind where ex-terror cells turn into secret societies, and meetings don't end with tea, but with ritual chanting and new tattoos."

Thurston pauses, then winks. "I'm not saying he's in a cult. I'm just saying the [[Olumi Osahn]] didn't exactly disappear-and someone's been wearing a lot of obsidian and incense lately."

(Cut to a brief, heavily stylized shot of Axel Vichten standing sternly at a podium. Overlay text reads: "Enigmatic or Extremely Culty? You Decide.")

Thurston, resuming his full glamour posture "So while the Imperium waves their new banners and celebrates their new 'order,' the rest of us are left wondering: was this a military promotion or a coronation in disguise? A crisis response... or the moment the mask finally slipped?"

He lifts his glass toward the camera. "Whatever it is, my sweetlings, you can count on me-and The Cutting Edge-to keep slicing through the half-truths and sparkle-dusting the facts. Because some of us were born to host, and some... were just appointed."

"Coming up next: the official tour of the Mirror Throne Room's new paranoia lighting and a deep dive into why Zephara's robes never wrinkle. Spoiler: it's not steam."

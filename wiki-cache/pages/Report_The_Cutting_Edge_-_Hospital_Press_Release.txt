[[Thurston Unger|Thurston's]] hospital room is bathed in a soft, warm glow, the gentle hum of medical equipment providing a steady backdrop. Tubes and wires surround him, evidence of the diligent care he has received. But there is an undeniable fire in his eyes, a flicker of determination that refuses to be extinguished.

With his voice filled with raw emotion, Thurston's words cut through the sterile silence of the room, echoing with righteous anger and deep sorrow.

"My friends, today I stand before you with a heavy heart. We have witnessed the dark underbelly of the [[New Republic]], its corruption laid bare for all to see. But amidst this chaos, I cannot ignore the devastating loss we have suffered? A brave [[Security Division (CSA)|ESPO]] officer, a loyal member of my crew, taken from us in the line of duty."

His voice quivers, and he wipes away a tear with a trembling hand. The pain etched on his face is palpable, a mixture of grief and frustration.

"He stood by my side, capturing the truth through his lens, and now he is gone. It is a painful reminder of the sacrifices we make in our quest for justice. I am filled with both anger and remorse, my friends. Anger at the system that allowed this to happen, and remorse for not being able to protect him as he protected me."

Taking a deep breath, Thurston gathers himself, his gaze fixated on an invisible point in the distance.

"But let me be clear, my darlings. This tragedy only fuels my determination to expose the rot that plagues the New Republic. We will not let their attempts to silence us go unanswered. We will not let their lies and deceit go unchecked. We will not rest until those responsible for this corruption are held accountable!"

His voice, though weakened by pain, carries the weight of a thousand voices united in their quest for truth, justice, and a better future. Thurston's words reverberate through the room, echoing with unwavering resolve.

"The citizens of the New Republic deserve better, my friends. We deserve a government that serves us, that listens to us, and that protects our rights. We cannot allow ourselves to be held hostage by fear, by oppression, or by a government that has lost its way."

As he speaks, the room seems to shrink, the walls closing in on Thurston. But his presence grows, his spirit refusing to be contained within the confines of the sterile space.

Cameras capture every nuance of his impassioned speech, broadcasting his message to the galaxy, igniting a flame of hope in the hearts of those who have felt the weight of the Republic's corruption.

The scene fades to a news desk, where Samantha Davis is seated. There is a tear rolling down her cheek as she begins to speak. "Thurston Unger, wounded but unyielding, has become an embodiment of the indomitable spirit that refuses to be silenced. His voice, though strained, carries the weight of a thousand voices united in their quest for truth, justice, and a better future. The doctors believe he will make a full recovery, but we ask that you keep him in your thoughts, and do not let this tragedy, an injured journalist and a deceased, brave security officer who gave his life protecting the truth for the galaxy to see."

"And in that hospital room, the flickering flame of rebellion shall be reignited, let Thurston's message resonate with those who dare to dream of a galaxy free from corruption and oppression. This is Samantha Davis, signing off."

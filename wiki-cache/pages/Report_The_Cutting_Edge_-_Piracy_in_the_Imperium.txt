[[The Cutting Edge]] news report began with [[Thurston Unger|Thurston]] standing in front of the a viewport showing the massive [[Imperator-class Star Destroyer]], the [[ISD New Order]]. It then fades to footage of the reporter on the Imperial Class Star Destroyer. "Darling, we're here in [[Selene]] space, aboard the lead ship of the sector's anti-piracy task force. Led by the fearless [[Captain]] Trankis," Thurston said as another camera view panned over the imposing ship.

Thurston was then shown being escorted by a squad of [[Stormtrooper|stormtroopers]] as he was led to the captain's quarters for the interview. Captain Trankis sat behind a large desk, his expression stoic as Thurston was led in. "Captain Trankis, it's an honor to be here on the New Order," Thurston said with a forced smile.

"The honor is mine, reporter," Captain Trankis replied coldly, refusing to use Thurston's name. He had no time for being friendly with the eccentric [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] reporter. "But I must remind you that you are here under constant guard and have limited access to the ship. Your presence here is solely for the purpose of this interview."

"Of course, Captain," Thurston said, trying to hide his discomfort. "I understand the [[Imperium|Imperium's]] need for security. Now, can you tell us about the strike fleet's recent successes in combating piracy and slavers in this part of space?"

"We have been very successful in our efforts to rid the sector of pirate scum," Captain Trankis said with a hint of pride in his voice. "Our ships are equipped with the latest technology and our crews are highly trained. We do not shy away from using force when necessary."

The footage cut to clips of Imperium ships attacking pirate vessels, laser fire lighting up the darkness of space. "It's clear to see the Imperium means business when it comes to piracy," Thurston said as the footage played. "But what about the [[New Republic|New Republic's]] response to the piracy problem? We've heard reports of them being slow to act."

"The New Republic is a bureaucratic entity, more concerned with politics than the safety of its citizens," Captain Trankis said dismissively. "The Imperium will always put the protection of its people first, regardless of political considerations."

Thurston nodded, but couldn't shake off the feeling of unease as he finished the interview and was escorted off the ship. As the footage cut back to him standing in front of the New Order, he said to the camera, "Darling, it's clear that the Imperium takes the threat of piracy seriously. But one can't help but wonder, at what cost? How does the Imperium stance compare to that of the crew of the [[DSV Templar|Templar]]? Are [[Colonel]] [[Han'bel Eckhart|Eckhart]] and his marines just mimicking the Imperium's stance?"

"Like always, my lovelies, I'll ask the hard questions and show you the truths of the galaxy," he says with a wink to the camera. "This is Thurston Unger, for the Cutting Edge. Stay tuned, darlings. For next, we visit the [[Caspian Union]]!" The camera fades out just as Thurston blows a kiss to the audience.

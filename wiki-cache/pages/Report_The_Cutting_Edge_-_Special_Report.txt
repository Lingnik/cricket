{{IGNpost|author=[[Thurston Unger]]|date=[[32 ABY]]|topic=Ord Mantell Riots|anchors=Samantha Davis|headline=The Cutting Edge - Special Report|synopsis=Guest Host Samantha Davis reports on riots on [[Ord Mantell]].}}

"Good evening, ladies and gentlemen. You're watching a special live edition of "[[The Cutting Edge]]." I'm your guest host, Samantha Davis, reporting from the heart of the chaos in [[Ord Mantell]]. The city is engulfed in protests and riots as citizens voice their discontent with the [[New Republic]]. The scene behind me is a stark representation of the growing frustration and unrest."

The camera pans to show the crowd of protestors, signs held high and voices raised. "The streets of Ord Mantell are teeming with angry citizens who have taken to the streets to demand justice, accountability, and an end to the corruption that has plagued the New Republic. They are calling for an immediate response from the government, demanding that their voices be heard." Samantha says.

The camera shifts to a group of protestors engaged in a heated debate:

Protestor 1: We've had enough! The corruption within the New Republic has reached its breaking point. It's time for change!

Protestor 2: We want transparency! We want our voices to matter! They can't ignore us any longer!

Samantha Davis steps back into view, "As tensions rise, clashes between protestors and New Republic security forces have intensified. Both sides stand firmly in their beliefs, with neither willing to back down."

The scene shows security forces in riot gear forming a barricade in front of the [[New Republic Senate|Senate]] building. "New Republic security forces, armed with riot gear and crowd control measures, have created a barricade separating the protestors from the Senate building. They are attempting to maintain order but are met with fierce resistance from the demonstrators."

The camera captures clashes between protestors and security forces as the events escalate. "We can see the intense confrontations unfolding here. Rocks hurled through the air, tear gas deployed, and the deafening sounds of explosions echo through the streets. It's an alarming scene of chaos and unrest."

Footage shifts to a group of protestors advocating for secession:

Protestor 3: We demand secession from the New Republic! We cannot be part of a system that perpetuates corruption and turns a blind eye to the suffering of its citizens.

Protestor 4: We want to govern ourselves! We want a government that truly represents our interests and puts an end to this corruption!

Again, the camera shifts to Samantha, "The call for secession adds another layer of complexity to an already volatile situation. The demand for self-governance reveals the deep-seated disillusionment with the New Republic and highlights the urgency for change."

"The aftermath of this violence is devastating. Buildings damaged, vehicles overturned, and fires blazing in the distance. The scars left by this unrest will take time to heal, and it's only been the first day of protests."

The camera cuts to a hospital scene, where [[Thurston Unger]] lies in a hospital bed, before going back to Samantha on the streets of Ord Mantell. "Meanwhile, our beloved host, Thurston Unger, remains in the hospital recovering from injuries sustained during previous events. We send our thoughts and well wishes to Thurston, whose commitment to truth and justice has inspired millions across the galaxy."

The scene cuts to the news studio where an anchor sits behind a desk, an image of Samantha appears in a smaller screen.

News Anchor: Samantha, the absence of Thurston Unger is deeply felt by our viewers. We eagerly await his return as he continues his fight against corruption within the New Republic. We stand in solidarity with him and all those who seek accountability and transparency.

Samantha Davis: Absolutely, the fight for truth and justice continues, even in his absence. Our thoughts are with Thurston, and we pledge to carry on his legacy by bringing you the latest updates on this developing situation.

The camera pans back to the chaotic scene on the streets of Ord Mantell and solely to Samantha Davis. "The protests show no signs of abating as the night wears on. The shouts of the demonstrators grow louder, fueled by their determination to be heard. The air is heavy with a mix of anger, frustration, and hope for change."

Video captures a group of protestors linking arms and chanting in unison:

Protestors: No justice, no peace! No justice, no peace!

"The unity among the protestors is palpable as they stand together, their voices amplified by sheer numbers. Their message reverberates through the streets, demanding accountability from the New Republic."

Then, the camera pans to a group of New Republic security forces forming a defensive line. "The security forces stand firm, maintaining their defensive positions amidst the chaos. They are tasked with restoring order, but their presence only serves to intensify the protesters' resolve."

The scene shifts to a tense moment as a skirmish erupts between a protester and a security officer, which ends in the protester taking a stun bolt and dropping to the ground.

"As you can see, emotions are running high, leading to sporadic clashes between individual protestors and security personnel. These isolated incidents serve as a stark reminder of the volatility of the situation," she says as the camera shifts to another group of civilians who are trying to calm the escalating tensions."

"In the midst of the turmoil, there are also citizens who are attempting to defuse the mounting tensions. They stand as voices of reason, urging both sides to find common ground and seek a peaceful resolution."

Citizen 1: We must remember that violence only begets more violence. Let's find a way to address our grievances without further bloodshed.

Citizen 2: Our unity and determination can bring about the change we seek. Let's stay focused on our goals and channel our energy into peaceful protest.

"The voices of these peacemakers resonate within the chaos, reminding everyone that there is still hope for a peaceful resolution. Their efforts serve as a testament to the resilience and unwavering spirit of the people of Ord Mantell."

"As we continue to cover this unfolding crisis, we implore all parties involved to engage in open dialogue, to listen to the grievances of the citizens, and to work towards a future where transparency and accountability reign."

The camera zooms out, capturing the unrest in the streets.

"From the heart of the turmoil, I'm Samantha Davis, reporting live for "The Cutting Edge." Stay tuned as we bring you the latest updates on this developing story. Remember, together, we can shine a light on corruption and pave the way for a better future."

The screen fades out, leaving viewers with a sense of uncertainty and anticipation for the next chapter in this tumultuous saga.

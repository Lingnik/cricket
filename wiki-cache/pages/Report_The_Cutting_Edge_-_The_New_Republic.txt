{{IGNpost|author=[[Thurston Unger]]|date=[[32 ABY]]|topic=Political News|anchors=[[Thurston Unger]]|headline=The Cutting Edge - The New Republic|synopsis=[[Thurston Unger]] announces he has been blacklisted by the [[New Republic]].}}

[[Thurston Unger|Thurston]] is dressed in a dashing suit, a vibrant purple and gold combination that catches the lights of the studio. He stands in front of a holographic representation of the galaxy, with the [[New Republic]] highlighted in red.

With a dramatic flourish, Thurston steps closer to the camera, his brightly colored suit shining under the studio lights. "Darlings, my dearest viewers, welcome to another episode of [[The Cutting Edge]]. I'm your host, the one and only Thurston Unger."

"Darlings, I've got some bad news for you," Thurston begins, his usual flamboyant energy dampened by disappointment. "It seems that the New Republic doesn't take kindly to a little healthy criticism." He pauses, taking a dramatic sip from his glass of blue milk. "I had planned to bring you all an in-depth look at the state of affairs in the New Republic, but it seems they've blacklisted me and refused any interviews. Can you believe it? Yours truly, the darling of the galaxy, the one and only Thurston Unger, being shut out by the very government that's supposed to serve and protect its people."

He shakes his head, a tinge of anger creeping into his voice. "But let me tell you, my loves, this will not stop me from bringing you the truth. I will not be silenced. I will not back down. I will continue to shine a light on the corruption and failure of the New Republic and hold them accountable for their actions. Because that's what true journalism is all about, darlings. Holding those in power accountable and giving a voice to the people."

Thurston looks directly into the camera, his expression determined. "And so, while we may not have an episode on the New Republic in our series, we will not forget about them. We will continue to report on their actions and hold them accountable. Because that's what the Cutting Edge is all about, my darlings. Bringing you the truth, no matter what obstacles may come our way."

Thurston shakes his head in disgust. "The Cutting Edge will not be silenced. We will continue to bring you the unvarnished truth about the state of the galaxy, whether the powers that be like it or not. Next week, we'll be heading to the [[Caspian Union]] to report on their recovery from the catastrophic events of four years ago and the amazing growth and recovery since."

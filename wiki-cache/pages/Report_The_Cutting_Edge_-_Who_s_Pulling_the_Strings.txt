"My darling sleuths and shadow-chasers, let's lean in, shall we?"

Cue the smoky lighting, the holographic display spinning a 3D model of [[Chandrila]] with glowing red pulse points.

"It's one thing for a planetary senator to get feisty. It's quite another for her to issue a full strategic ultimatum... and get away with it. Which begs the question: Who's whispering sweet sedition in Vel Tarnas' ear?"

Suspect #1: The [[Hutt]] Cartels
"Let's not pretend the Hutts haven't been very busy in the Outer Rim, and perhaps a little too curious about Core World instability. Chandrila's shipping lanes are prime real estate-clean, regulated, and ripe for exploitation if Republic oversight collapses. I've heard whispers that certain 'freelance consultants' out of [[Nar Shaddaa[[ have been spotted in [[Hanna City]], wearing much nicer suits than you'd expect."

Suspect #2: The [[Corporate Sector Authority]] on [[Etti IV]]
Thurston gestures playfully around his own studio. "Present company excluded, of course. But let's not kid ourselves-if Chandrila exits the [[New Republic|Republic's]] economic web, who stands to gain control of its high-efficiency logistics sector? The Authority would love to swoop in and privatize those shipping lanes and Chandrilan industry. A fractured Republic? Well, it's just good business."

Suspect #3: The [[Imperium]]
A dramatic musical sting. "Oh, yes. Don't look so shocked. If you were sitting on [[Selene]] with half the galaxy mistrusting your 'New Order,' wouldn't you love to see the Republic torn from within? I'd wager they have a dozen back channels sniffing around Hanna City right now, making sure the Chandrilan pot keeps boiling."

Thurston steeples his fingers, leaning just a hair closer to the holocam. "So, my sweets, whether it's Hutt credits, Combine ambition, or Imperium sabotage... something is stirring behind the Chandrilan curtain. And if the Republic doesn't pull itself together soon?"

A pause. A sly smile. "Don't be surprised if Chandrila pulls it down around them."

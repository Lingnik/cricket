The scene is bustling with activity as [[Thurston Unger|Thurston]] and his cameraman make their way through the streets of [[New Alderaan]] on the planet of [[Ord Mantell]]. While the interview with [[Colonel]] [[Han'bel Eckhart|Eckhart]] was pre-recorded and held until after the [[New Republic|NR]] Press conference to be released, this is broadcast live.

Thurston, dressed in a brightly colored suit, is leading the way, his smile and energy drawing the attention of the passing citizens. He approaches a group of people, microphone in hand.

"Excuse me, can I ask you all a few questions about the crew of the [[DSV Templar|Templar]] and Colonel Eckhart?" Thurston asks, his voice full of enthusiasm.

One of the citizens, a [[Human]] man, responds, "I think the crew of the Templar are nothing but a bunch of criminals and Eckhart is a rogue who needs to be stopped. The New Republic is working to maintain peace and stability in the galaxy, and the actions of the Templar crew are only causing chaos. They're nothing but a threat to that peace."

Another citizen, a [[Bothan]] woman, interjected, "I disagree, I think the crew of the Templar are doing what needs to be done to protect innocent lives. The New Republic is too bogged down by bureaucracy and corruption to get anything done. Colonel Eckhart and his crew are the real heroes in this situation."

A third citizen, a [[Twi'lek]] man, chimes in, "I see both sides of this issue. The actions of the Templar crew may be extreme, but they are trying to protect innocent lives. I understand why they would feel the need to take matters into their own hands. However, I also understand the New Republic's position, they are trying to maintain peace and stability in the galaxy. It's a complex issue that requires understanding and compassion on both sides."

Thurston nods and thanks each of the citizens for their time and perspective. As he and his cameraman continue down the street, they approach more people, some supporting the New Republic, others supporting the crew of the Templar, and some with mixed feelings. Thurston listens attentively to each person's response, his smile never faltering, even as he asks the toughest of questions. He is determined to get to the bottom of the story, and he is not afraid to ask the tough questions to get there.

In the bustling streets of [[New Alderaan]], citizens have mixed feelings about the current state of the [[New Republic]]. [[Thurston Unger]], the eccentric reporter for [[The Cutting Edge]], is on the scene to get the pulse of the people.

"My lovelies, it's a chaotic time for our beloved New Republic," Thurston says to the camera, "with the recent news of [[Corellia|Corellia's]] secession and the ongoing threat of piracy in our trade lanes, it's no surprise that citizens have mixed feelings about the government's ability to protect and serve."

Thurston approaches a group of traders, their faces lined with concern. "As a free trader, the piracy in our space lanes is hitting us hard," says one trader, "and the Republic's response has been lackluster at best."

Another trader chimes in, "I don't agree. The Republic is doing the best they can with the resources they have. It's not their fault that Corellia decided to secede."

Thurston turns to a young woman on the street, "And what about you, my dear? What are your thoughts on the Republic's handling of the situation?"

The woman hesitates before responding, "I think the Republic is stretched too thin. They're trying to do too much and not focusing on the important issues at hand. Maybe it's time for a change."

As Thurston continues to interview more citizens, it becomes clear that the people have mixed feelings about the New Republic and the current state of affairs. Some fully support the government and denounce the actions of vigilante groups like the crew of the [[DSV Templar|Templar]], while others question whether the government is capable of truly protecting and serving its citizens.

"Darlings, it's clear that the citizens of New Alderaan are divided on the issue," Thurston concludes, "and with Corellia's secession, the future of the New Republic remains uncertain. This is Thurston Unger, reporting live for The Cutting Edge."

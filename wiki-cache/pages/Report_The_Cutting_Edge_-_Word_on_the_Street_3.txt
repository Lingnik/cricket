{{IGNpost|author=[[Thurston Unger]]|date=[[32 ABY]]|topic=Republic Politics|anchors=[[Thurston Unger]]|headline=The Cutting Edge - Word on the Street! 3|synopsis=[[Thurston Unger]] interviews the citizens of [[New Alderaan]], [[Ord Mantell]] about corruption in the [[New Republic]].}}

[[Thurston Unger]], accompanied by his camera crew, took to the streets of [[Ord Mantell]], right in front of the imposing [[New Republic Senate]] building. The diverse crowd was a vibrant mix of species and genders, representing the rich tapestry of the galaxy. Thurston approached individuals from different backgrounds, ensuring a variety of perspectives.

Approaching a [[Twi'lek]] woman, Thurston adjusted his microphone and leaned in. "My darling, what are your thoughts on the recent allegations of government corruption within the [[New Republic]]?"

The woman's lekku twitched slightly as she spoke, her voice filled with determination. "As an immigrant to this planet, I've seen how the system can be rigged against those who don't fit the mold. Corruption only exacerbates these inequalities. We need a government that represents and fights for all its citizens."

Thurston nodded appreciatively, capturing her words with great interest. "My dear, your perspective is invaluable. The struggle against corruption knows no boundaries, and we must stand together to demand transparency and fairness."

Moving through the crowd, Thurston spotted a [[Sullustan]] man deep in conversation with his companions. He approached them, extending his microphone with a warm smile. "Gentlemen, I'd love to hear your thoughts on the allegations of government corruption."

The man, speaking in his native tongue, though translated into basic as subtitles, spoke earnestly. "It's not just about credits disappearing; it's about trust eroding. We elected these officials to serve us, but it feels like they're serving their own interests instead. We need accountability and real change."

Thurston nodded, capturing the essence of their conversation. "Indeed, my dear audience, it seems that citizens of all species are united in their desire for a government that works for them, rather than against them."

As Thurston moved through the crowd, he encountered a human woman, a [[Zabrak]] man, and a [[Mirialan]] androgynous individual, each sharing their unique perspectives on the matter. Their voices echoed the sentiments of countless others, reflecting a widespread demand for a government that upholds integrity and equality.

Thurston turned to his camera crew, his eyes gleaming with enthusiasm. "My lovies, these interviews have shown us that corruption affects individuals from every corner of the galaxy. It is our duty to amplify their voices and hold the New Republic accountable."

As Thurston concluded his last interview, the camera continued to roll, capturing the bustling scene in front of the New Republic Senate building. Suddenly, a group of Republic troopers, stern-faced and determined, approached Thurston and his crew.

"Sorry, folks," one of the troopers said, his voice firm but respectful. "We've been instructed to escort you away from the premises and revoke your press pass."

Thurston's eyes widened, a mix of surprise and disappointment crossing his face. "But we're just here to shed light on the truth, to give voice to the concerns of the people."

The trooper nodded sympathetically, his tone measured. "I understand, sir. However, the decision has been made. We have to follow protocol."

Thurston took a deep breath, his resolve unshaken. "Very well. We may be forced to leave, but the truth cannot be silenced. We will find other ways to bring transparency to the galaxy."

As the troopers led Thurston and his crew away, the camera continued to roll, capturing the scene for the galaxy to witness. The revocation of their press pass served as a stark reminder of the challenges they faced in their pursuit of truth and justice.

Thurston looked back at the Senate building, his eyes filled with determination. "My darlings, the path may be treacherous, but we shall persist. [[The Cutting Edge]] will continue to be a beacon of light in the face of darkness, shining a spotlight on corruption and demanding accountability."

And with that, Thurston and his crew were ushered away, leaving behind a crowd of onlookers and the echoes of their interviews, a testament to the power of the people's voices in the face of adversity. The journey was far from over, and their unwavering commitment to the truth would propel them forward, ready to face whatever challenges lay ahead.

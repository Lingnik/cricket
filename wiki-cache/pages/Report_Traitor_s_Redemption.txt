Timo Droja brings a special IGN Network Exclusive breaking report!

"The villainous traitor to the Empire, Sandor Woden, has finally been brought to justice by the combined forces of [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] and its offshoot branch, the [[Imperial Security Bureau|ISB]]. After Sandor Woden had been brought in by a Bounty Hunter, he was then placed under the rigorous questioning and cross-examination by the ISB. After finally realizing that he could not escape his justice, Sandor Woden then broke down in his examinations and admitted to his crimes. He was then charged with desertion of duties, supplying information to New Republic spies, aiding in the Deaths of loyalist imperial citizens and members of the military. Then, he was finally charged with High Treason against the Empire. To all of this, he pleaded Guilty."

A sweeping image takes over the Holoscreen, of an ISB Agent ([[Dreven Mercurio]]) standing beside the Accused ([[Sandor Woden]]) as he is charged and admits to all of the above crimes. What follows next is first covered by a IGN Explicit Content warning. The Accused is executed on-screen and his body is then displayed, long enough for his face to be seen on Camera. He is then covered up and taken away to be disposed of at an 'undisclosed location'. A following interview with the ISB Agent Dreven Mercurio follows.

*Timo: Are you proud of what you did?
*Dreven: Yes. The glory of the Empire must be upheld at all times, and traitors should be given no ground or quarter.
*Timo: Was he forced to admit to his crimes?
*Dreven: No. He was questioned for a good time to admit to what he had done, but he was not forced to admit to any of his crimes. He admitted to High Treason while held captive upon the HIMS Inquisitor.
*Timo: Is there anything else you can tell us, at this present moment in time?
*Dreven: No. That information is classified, a full portfolio of information related to the prisoner will be released in due time. This is all that I can answer for now.
*Timo: Glory to the Empire.
*Dreven: Glory to the Empire.

Timo then turns to face the camera. "There you have it, ladies and gentlemen. An Imperial traitor has been brought to justice and forced to pay for his crimes. In the end, he revealed that he was a true Imperial, and faced his punishment as an Imperial. This is Timo Droja, for IGN Network. Glory to the Emperor and Goodnight."

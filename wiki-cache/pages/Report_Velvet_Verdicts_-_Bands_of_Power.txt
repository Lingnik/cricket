[[InterGalactic News|IGNews]]: [[Velvet Verdicts]] - Episode 1: "Bands of Power"<br>
Sponsored by [[Crimson Nerf Energy|Crimson Nerf]] Dark - Velvet Vortex Edition<br>
Tagline: "Sip the swirl. Own the night."

The holoscreen flickers to life in a cascade of swirling static and crimson shimmer. The camera pans slowly across the plush, moody interior of the Velvet Lounge, aboard the [[Full Frontal Fabulous]]. Every surface glows with soft lighting, velvet drapery, and scandal-ready ambiance. In the center of the sunken conversation pit sits [[Thurston Unger|Thurston]], gleaming in a jacket that shifts color with every breath. One leg is elegantly crossed, a glass of [[Crimson Nerf Energy|Crimson Nerf]] Dark - Velvet Vortex Edition in hand.

Thurston smirks at the camera with all the danger of a secret already known. "Hello, my sweet scandal-seekers. Welcome to Velvet Verdicts, where the fashion is hot, the gossip hotter, and the drinks... combustible."

He lifts his shimmering glass and gives it a swirl. Obsidian liquid glints with red sparks, glowing faintly. "Tonight's topic? Signals. Symbols. Statements without signatures. All wrapped around a single wrist."

Behind him, the holowall activates. Footage rolls: Imperator [[Tyler Damion|Damion I]] at a diplomatic address. He lifts a hand - and three thin wristbands glint in the light. Cobalt. Charcoal. Crimson.

Thurston leans forward. "Three colors. Three causes. And not one word from any official source. But darlings, when silence accessorizes... we take notes."

Floating in from stage right with the elegance of a luxury calendar come to life, PEARLETTA enters - her powder-pink chrome pristine, opera gloves flawless, her tone velvet-wrapped steel.

Pearletta speaks with crisp timing. "Cobalt for Free [[Han'bel Eckhart|Eckhart]]. Charcoal for the [[Chandrila|Chandrilan]] Resistance. Crimson for Free [[Bothawui]]. None endorsed. None denied. All now circulating through the galaxy like forbidden perfume."

The holowall transitions to show other high-ranking figures - an Admiral here, a [[HoloNet]] celeb there - all snapped with one or more of the same bands.

From the lounge bar, Tarnish LaRue sprawls like a jaded queen across a brocade chaise, her oxidized chrome catching the low light like bruised glass.

Tarnish mutters, "It's not subtle. It's not meant to be. This is elite-level shade-throwing dressed as ambiguity. They want us talking - but not too loud. Keeps the message fashionable."

The lights ripple as CymbalineE-6 stomps onto the lower deck, arms crossed, stiletto stabilizers clicking with every step. Her tone is as sharp as her silhouette. Cymbaline-6 grunts. "You don't wear banned colors by accident. This is orchestration. Psychological warfare with a styling budget."

From the side console, Rouge Six leans in, makeup palette in hand, crimson plating practically vibrating with dramatic energy. Rouge Six exclaims, "I just did lashes on a [[Bothan]] influencer wearing the crimson band upside down. I almost glued her mouth shut by accident - on purpose!"

Back at center, the holowall transitions to a looping rotation of the three bands: cobalt, charcoal, crimson.

Thurston rises slowly, letting his long coat sweep dramatically behind him as the lighting dims to match his tone. Thurston whispers, "Whatever these bands mean - solidarity, subversion, or something far darker - one thing is certain: they were meant to be seen. And when power chooses silence, it's the accessories that start screaming."

He raises his glass again in toast. "Keep your eyes on the wrists, my darlings. Because in this galaxy? Subtext is the loudest voice in the room."

The screen fades to the Velvet Verdicts logo - chrome letters over a swirl of red smoke.

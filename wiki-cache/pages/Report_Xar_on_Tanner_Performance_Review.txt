== Galactic Republic Armed Forces==
=== Personnel Performance Review: LTJG Xar'on Tanner ===

LTJG Tanner's performance in the field has been exemplary.  His dedication to his duty and fearlessness on the front lines should serve as an example to all who serve.

And he is just a surgeon.

LTJG Tanner continually volunteers for service on the front line, even if injured himself.  He demonstrates excellent leadership to the corpsmen under him, and sets the example with his enthusiasm, dedication, ability, and discipline.

LTJG Tanner has also provided novel and innovative procedures for treating battlefield injuries for all species, and has often offered valuable insights into clone physiology and treatment.

The only negative aspect of LTJG Tanner's service is his refusal to carry his regulation sidearm, and his habit of 'lending' said sidearm to a soldier on the line.

As LTJG Tanner's commanding officer, I recommend him for commendation for service above and beyond the call of duty.

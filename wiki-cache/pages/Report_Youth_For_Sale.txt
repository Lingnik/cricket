In economic news tonight, a short bit is run on the topic of anti ageing niche of the medical community. Ironically presented by a rather dapper youthful looking commentator.

"Those wealthy and a those with a desire have chased eternal youth for ages and in fact medical science can extend the average human, near human life to 150 year." a few well known corporations flash up behind the commentator. "But if you have seen Tionne or the commercials for Neoskin, then you know there is a new kid on the block and it is taking the niche by storm!" the names are replaced by a centre word Tarchanjan Genetics. "In the several months that it has stepped into the spot light, there have been knock-off products and pushes for existing products and treatments. But amazingly the simplicity of the Neoskin treatment cant be matched." an image of Tionne flashes up and shows her attending various events.

"In fact Neoskin is taking the market by storm...Core Royal families are even reportedly seeking out the product. It is literally become a status symbol to have not the small Neoskin dabs that last a few days, but the larger scale treatments. In some cases the younger rich caste of many worlds have Neoskin parties where those who can afford it gather together for a party before and after party of the treatment!"

The commentator flashes up statistics. "Though no real financial data is available from the company itself, its estimated that galaxy wide the company may have made in the upper end of hundreds of million. Not including black market dealings that may push that number into even higher realms." the commentator again is backed by the image of Tionne the face of Tarchanjan Genetics. "If this trend continues, Neoskin may very well become the front runner treatment for the anti ageing niche of the galaxy."

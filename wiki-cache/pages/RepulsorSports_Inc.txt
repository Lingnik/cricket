'''RepulsorSports, Inc. (RSI)''' is a repulsor motorsports company founded by [[Elana Tracer]] after she was gifted with several shops and arenas by [[Troy McTavish]].  With business savvy learned from her ex-husband [[Benjamin Jawatha]], she eventually managed to nearly monopolize the sport of swooping by not only presiding over the [[Galactic Swoop League]] but maintaining controlling stock of the arenas and swoop shops and garages across the galaxy.  At its height, RSI had three divisions: retail, team management, and arena consulting. The retail business not only sold and customized [[swoop|swoops]] but supplied sports enthusiasts with every type of repulsor engine sporting device imaginable.

During Tracer's tenure as [[Presav]], an interim CEO was employed to avoid any conflicts of interest. After the fallout from her impeachment, Tracer secluded herself to her swoop investments, but eventually, retired from them and sold off most of her holdings, keeping only the [[Marin Mountain Circuit]] and garage on her homeworld of [[Caspar]].

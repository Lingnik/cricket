The motto of the '''Research Division''' is "A company is only as good as its product line," and this division exists to ensure that the [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] remains well ahead of the competition.  Working in tandem with research and marketing teams from [[Corporate Sector Authority#Sponsors|sponsor corporations]], '''Research''' designs and develops newer, better, more profitable products for the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Authority]] and its members.  Innovation is a valuable commodity and corporate espionage is a constant risk.  Many of the division's projects are highly classified and conducted in secret.  The experimental equipment and weapons produced by the Research division frequently pass through the [[Resources Division (CSA)|Resources Division]] to be dispersed to [[Security Division (CSA)#Espo_Troopers|Espo]] squads for field testing.

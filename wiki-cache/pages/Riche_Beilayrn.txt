'''Riche T Beilayrn''' was a [[Senator of the Union|Senator]] in the [[Caspian Democratic Union]]. His family had been from [[:starwars:Commenor/Legends|Commenor]], but Riche himself was born on [[Caspar]]. He was the first [[Provost]] of the [[Union Assembly]] having been chosen as a compromise candidate by [[Dergan Roj]] himself on the suggestion of [[Orton Trissard]]. While not particularly noted for eloquence, Beilayrn was respected for his fairness as was viewed by [[Admiral]] Roj as being the best embodied the pioneer spirit that lay at the heart of Caspia. He worked closely with [[Lyr Dunwell]] in his role as [[Commissioner of Commerce]] and assisted in the formation of [[FineLine Spaceways]].

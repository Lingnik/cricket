Simple and inexpensive, slug-firing '''rifles''' still turn up throughout the 
galaxy despite the fact that they're not especially damaging when compared
with modern blasters. The firing mechanism propels a slug several
millimeters wide using a crude charge. Most of these weapons are either
semi-automatic (tapping the gasses or recoil of the cartridge firing to eject
the shell-casing and load the next cartridge) or require the firer to manually
operate a lever or bolt to ready the weapon to fire again. On the other hand,
this weapon does have the advantage of being able to throw a slug much further
than most blaster rifles can reach, and it leaves bloody, messy wounds. Most
rifles have some capabililty to accept special sights and other accessories.

'''Roagen Vakren''', a [[:starwars:Zabrak/Legends|Zabrak]] warrior from [[:starwars:Iridonia/Legends|Iridonia]], was in command of the [[New Republic 5th Marine Corps]] as of [[15 ABY]]. He held the rank of [[Lieutenant General]], Vakren was an imposing figure with a commanding, larger-than-life presence, but was also typically soft-spoken, and treated his subordinates not unlike family. This trait, in part, made him one of the most respected and well-liked officers in the New Republic Military.

{{NPC|Vakren, Roagen}}

=== OOC Note ===

Roagen Vakren is an [[Non-Player Character|NPC]] figurehead for the [[New Republic Marine Corps]], which is the player-driven section of the [[New Republic GroundOps|GroundOps]] branch of the [[New Republic Military]]. All Marine efforts are coordinated by the GroundOps [[Branch Head]] and the Military OrgHead. Roagen Vakren was created to maintain a steady character presence at the "top of the food chain" for the [[Player Character]] division of the Marines. Vakren's orders and actions will essentially come from the involved faction administrators.

'''Robot 7''' ([[4 ABY]] - ) is [[:starwars:2-1B surgical droid/Legends|2-1B Surgical Droid]] who works as an artist. Robot 7 was originally acquired by a medical clinic in [[Hanna City]] near the campus of [[:starwars:Brionelle Memorial Military Academy|Brionelle Memorial Military Academy]] on [[Chandrila]]. Operating in a culturally refined society, Robot 7 soon found himself taking interest in things far outside of his programming, particularly the fine arts. Eventually this interest led to a desire to produce art, with his off hours spent painting.

While the friends Robot 7 made in Hanna City were quite impressed with his art, the clinic was less so. His decreasing interest in performing the functions for which he was built and purchased for soon had him at risk of a memory wipe. To prevent this from happening a number of his friends including [[Crestian Tarasar]], [[Zubindi Hakoon]], and [[Kath Samba]] put together a collection to purchase him from the clinic and emancipate him. These friends additionally provided him with the starting capital to allow him to focus on honing his artistic skills without need to worry about needing to immediately sell his services as either an artist or medic.

Robot 7 would spend some years remaining in Hanna City slowly developing a reputation as an up and coming artist though he would also face some discrimination in these early years with some critics dismissing his work, claiming that as a Droid it could not truly be considered art. He eventually decided to leave Chandrila to gain a fresh perspective and audience. Feeling that going through an academic route might see him viewed more as a person, he enrolled in [[:starwars:Reena University/Legends|Reena University's]] [[:starwars:School of the Arts|School of the Arts]] in the [[:starwars:Tapani sector/Legends|Tapani Sector]], aided by the recommendation of Associate Academic Dean of Brionelle Memorial Military Academy, Crestian Tarasar.

After completing his course of studies at Reena University, Robot 7 moved to [[:starwars:Tyrena/Legends|Tyrena]] on [[Corellia]] for further study at the famed [[:starwars:Tyrena School of art|Tyrena School of Art]]. Now armed with the perspective of years in art school, not only was a Robot 7 an even better artist, he was also better prepared for what to say to silence his critics.

== Style ==

Robot 7 primarily sticks to painting and generally prefers to work on canvas. He does at times branch into other mediums, which his surgical background leading to occasional tattoo work. He is largely drawn to portraiture with both biological sentients and droids as subjects. His early association with Zubindi Hakoon of the [[Silverwood House of Fashion]] has led to a lot of work over the years painting in the fashion industry and high society in general. His subjects have included aristocrats, entertainers, officers, politicians, and tycoons.

The fact that he is a Droid himself has led to him being a favoured choice for portraits of other Droids. In [[33 ABY]] he was commissioned by [[Atsvara Tarasar]] for a portrait of [[Cricket]]. The resulting work, "The Apotheosis of Cricket", is on display at the [[Prelate r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak Memorial Art Gallery]] in Xi Xin on [[Charros IV]].

[[Image:Frederiko Famished.webp|thumb|"Frederiko Famished"]] In [[34 ABY]], Robot 7 donated a painting of the Ortolan musician [[Nbba Eembek|Nbba "Frederiko" Eembek]], frontman of the Sack of Pancakes Band, to the [[:starwars:Arkanis sector/Legends|Arkanis Sector]] Children's Relief Fundraiser and Ball held at the [[Royale Resorts|Royale Hotel]] on [[Corellia]]. The work, entitled "Frederiko Famished," was signed on the back by Frederiko himself and won at auction with a bid of 8,000 credits by [[Krackor Reincus]].

He normally embraces impressionism as a style. Though he has an innate desire for a level of realism in his work, his experience with anti-droid bias early in his career led him to embrace a certain level of distance from anything that could be judged as excessively accurate. He is, however, comfortable working in a variety of styles, particularly for commissioned work. He will also on occasion make use of his previous medical training to gain access to 'front line' locations that other artists might be denied access to.

== Behind the Scenes ==

Robot 7 is an homage to [[User:Tarasar|Crestian's]] experience with the DALL-E AI art platform. For a time he and some Discord friends would play around with the platform. At that time it was generating nine images for each prompt and Crestian decided to treat each of the nine images as if they each had a distinct robot artist behind them, named in the fashion of numbers on a keypad. The top left image would tend to be the closest in accuracy in terms of following the intended prompt, leading "Robot 7" to be praised for the consistent quality of his work compared to the others.

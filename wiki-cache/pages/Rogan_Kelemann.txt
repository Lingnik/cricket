'''Rogan Kelemann''' grew up on the planet [[Dreven]], twice known for being the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] Capital, where he was in his family business of ore mining. The Kelemann family was major supporters of the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] and has been mining for many years. Growing up, Rogan was excited to have the Imperial capital located there and would always try to get involved in any way he could. He had always begged his parents for him to join the military but they scolded him, telling him it was too dangerous. After the capital moved to [[Selene]], he got worried that he might not have another chance. His big break came when Selene was destroyed and the capital was brought back to Dreven.  Realizing that he might not have another chance, he left his home and his family to pursue the Imperial Military.

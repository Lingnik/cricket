'''Rogue Squadron''' was the elite starfighter unit of the [[:starwars:Rebel Alliance|Rebel Alliance]] and the [[New Republic]]. Rogue Squadron was founded by [[Commander]] [[:starwars:Narra|Narra]], [[Luke Skywalker]], and [[Wedge Antilles]] shortly after the [[:starwars:Battle of Yavin|Battle of Yavin]] out of [[:starwars:Red Squadron (Rebel Alliance)|Red Squadron]]. Rogue Squadron played a key role during several engagements in the [[Galactic Civil War]], serving as the primary defense squadron of the [[:starwars:Alliance High Command|Alliance High Command]]. They participated most notably at the [[:starwars:Battle of Hoth|Battle of Hoth]] and later at the [[:starwars:Battle of Endor|Battle of Endor]] while reorganized once more as Red Squadron. Following the death of the [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]], the Rogues served the [[New Republic]] in several critical campaigns, but was destroyed in battle against the [[Galactic Empire]]'s [[TIE Avenger]]s during the [[Dacan Civil War]].

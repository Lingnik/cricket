===A Red Herring===
When reports that Bavon Nass' organization was starting to reassemble itself at a fairly alarming rate crossed Tokoga's desk, he enlisted the help of Rainer, who claimed to be an out-of-work private spacer, in determining who was assuming leadership in the organization. Meanwhile, Myiari Oerstead finally finds someone that she can stand going on dates with in Brek. Completely unbeknownst to the trusting Mon Calamari, however, there's more to Rainer that initially meets the eye, and the same holds true for Myiari's new interest.

====Engagements at Gyndine====

While the Inquisitor remains near Corellia to deal with the affairs on the planet, the rest of the Inquisitor's entourage is sent to Gyndine to combat the Rebels.

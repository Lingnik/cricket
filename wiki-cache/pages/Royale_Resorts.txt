'''Royale Resorts''' began as a simple casino that profited only due to its proximity to the [[Red Dragon Restaurant]] on Treasure Ship Row in Coronet. In [[10 ABY]], the tiny casino burned to the ground and after the cleanup, the lot remained empty for almost a year until it was purchased along with a city block of land around it by [[New Horizon Enterprises]]. Of the businesses affected by the land purchase, only the owners of the Red Dragon held onto their land.

In a massive undertaking, a new vacation resort was built on the land surrounding the Red Dragon, which included a luxury hotel and the largest casino in Coronet. In a deal with the Red Dragon, the new Royale Resort did not build a luxury restaurant, and instead formed a partnership with the Dragon's owners.

In the following two years, resort locations were built in in the capital city of [[:starwars:Dearic/Legends|Dearic]] on [[:starwars:Talus/Legends|Talus]], in the capital city of [[:starwars:Rellidir|Rellidir]] on [[:starwars:Tralus/Legends|Tralus]], on the banks of the [[:starwars:Boiling Sea|Boiling Sea]] on [[:starwars:Drall (planet)|Drall]] and a seaside resort built under the shadow of the [[:starwars:Cloudland Peaks|Cloudland Peaks]] on [[:starwars:Selonia/Legends|Selonia]].

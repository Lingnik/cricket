'''SONG''' is an acronym for the '''S'''ensory '''O'''bservation & '''N'''avigation '''G'''rid, a sophisticated network of beacons, buoys, outposts, and satellites affixed at various points throughout [[Caspian Union]] territory and beyond. In and of itself, SONG isn't intended to be a defence system, with no integrated weaponry or limited shielding installed. It acts much more in a passive role, detecting and recording any vessels in the area. A SONG installation also transmits travel coordinates automatically, as well as coordinates of known hazards to avoid or navigate through. While SONG outposts are themselves automated, they are most frequently at locations with a fixed [[Caspian Defence Force|Defence Force]] presence or at least regularly patrolled.

'''SPECTRUM''', for '''SPecial Enforcement, Counter-Terrorism & Rendition Under-Ministry''', was an agency within the [[Caspian Crest]] and charged with enforcing interstellar ordinances, collecting and assessing intelligence, and executing directives, warrants and bounties regarding any threat or threats to the integrity of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]]. The agency ceased to exist with the dissolution of the CDU following the Sariphage pandemic and Mergani pogroms. In the current [[Caspian Union]] SPECTRUM's former functions have largely been assumed by '''[[Caspian Intelligence Service]].''' 

== Agents ==
Agents were drawn from practically any accepted race within the Union's sphere of influence and were routinely those with either extraordinary scholastic aptitude, less-than-dubious life experiences, or particular racial traits or characteristics (enhanced hearing, etc.). There were no applicants to the Under-Ministry's ranks of agents – only recruitments. Candidates were usually sought by the administrative powers or seasoned senior agents based on undisclosed criteria.

Birth/hatch names were never used within the Under-Ministry or in the field. Upon acceptance into the Under-Ministry as an Agent, a code-name was assigned, typically colour-related and in keeping with certain personality traits exhibited. Such agents have included Agent Orange, Agent Black or Agent Violet.

:<font style="font-size: 14pt">'''''"In SW1: The Movie..."'''''</font><br/>

A common feature in many character descriptions, the hypothetical '''SW1: The Movie''' is often used to establish which actors, actresses, or special effects would be used to portray a given character in the event the MUSH was ever adapted to film.  The idea being that associating a character with a well-known actor or actress helps to establish a mental image of that character for others.  Given the cast choices of the playerbase, it would likely be a very expensive movie to produce.


Other variations include:
*'''SW1: The Motion Picture''' &mdash; Synonymous with '''The Movie'''.
*'''SW1: The Animated Feature''' &mdash; Used to relate a character with an existing Anime or Cartoon character.

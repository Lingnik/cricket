This Wiki was first established on December 9th, 2005, for the popular online roleplaying game, [http://www.starwarsmush.com Star Wars MUSH]. The idea behind the Wiki was to establish an easy and presentable way of having the game's history, character biographies, and other information pertaining to the game online for its players to access and to contribute to at any given time.

All players of the MUSH are welcomed to edit files, as long as they stay within the confines of a few basic rules. Non-players are more than welcomed to join the game and play.

First off, players who want to write an article for their character should keep it in line with the standard article format (view already established character articles for an example). Don't use the article to stroke your ego, gloat, or to provide inaccurate information or lies. The articles are considered OOC (Out of Character), and are not meant to be there to brag about your character. Keep in mind that other players can edit these articles, which leads us to the second rule...

Don't screw over an article. If you choose to edit an article, do not steal from a copyrighted source without permission, do not write lies, slander, or misinterpetations (try to keep a neutral outlook), nor are you allowed to deface an article.

Should you do any of that, and you are a player on the MUSH, there will be consequences  for you on the game.

There are other articles we would like to have on the Wiki, too. An article about an historical event that took place on the game, a TinyPlot, information about a business, faction, organization, etc. Always categorize articles (see the current categories to give you an example).

Be sure to know how to use the Wiki before writing an article, so take a look through the tutorial that can be found from the main page.

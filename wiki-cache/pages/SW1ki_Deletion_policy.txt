'''SW1ki's Deletion policy''' is simple. Any article that has nothing to do with the [[:starwars:Star_Wars|Star Wars]] genre will be deleted without hesitation. Any article that has nothing to do with the [[Star Wars MUSH]] will be deleted. If the article is related to Star Wars, but not the MUSH, it will be considered for a redirect to [[:starwars:Main_Page|Wookieepedia]].

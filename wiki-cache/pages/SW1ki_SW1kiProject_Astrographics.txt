{{SW1kiProject|name=Astrographics}}
'''SW1kiProject Astrographics''' is an informal group of SW1kipedians that have gotten together to make some suggestions about how we might best organize information about the star systems, planets, moons, hyperspace routes that make up the MUSH galaxy, collectively referred to as Astrographics.

==Scope==
This [[SW1ki:SW1kiProject|SW1kiProject]] aims primarily to improve the coverage of Astrographical topics in the SW1ki. In support of this goal, this SW1kiProject will define standards for articles and track our progress in such coverage. It will also be a central place for those interested in such topics to discuss common themes.

Articles within this project that need to be updated or created may be listed below.

==Goals==
'''The overall goal of this project is to provide the MUSH community with accurate, useful, content-rich articles regarding the SW1 galaxy.'''
*Unify and improve articles regarding the star systems, planets, and other galactic components.
*Identify and create articles for astrographical topics that have not been covered.
*Keep articles synchronized with the MUSH.<br/>For example:
**Update the galaxy map to accurately reflect shifts in territory.

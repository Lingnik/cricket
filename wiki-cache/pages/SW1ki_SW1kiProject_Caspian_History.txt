This '''Project''' is in regards to the history of [[Caspia]], or more appropriately, the [[Caspian Democratic Union]]. Of primary importance are the years between [[4 ABY]] and [[8 ABY]], bounded by the arrival of Roj's Fleet after the Battle of Endor, and by the [[Caspian Annexation Campaign]]. Particularly, this is to focus on the characters involved, and when they make their entrances to and exits from the story, and what impact they had upon the MUSH-canon material we have to go on.

=== Already Within Caspia ===
*[[Leidan Adamov]] &mdash; elder statesman and one of the "champions" of Caspia; Human-Caspian, and well-respected by all Sarians; led policing of Caspia during and after Imperial occupation.
*Patriv Seydon &mdash; one of the founding senators of the [[Union Assembly]]; decendent of the Seydon family who were influential in/from [[Anchorron]]. Sarian?
*The Camm Family &mdash; Camms were among the first non-Sarian settlers on Caspar.
*The Forsythe Family &mdash; Forsythes were among the first non-Sarian settlers on Caspar.
*The Dusten Family &mdash; Rather prominent Eireasari family that relocated to Caspar from Mergansar. Produced [[Avalyshaar Dusten|Avy]] and [[Keirena Dusten|Sheltie]].
*The Hechin Family &mdash; Hechins were among the first non-Sarian settlers on Caspar; built what became '''Castle Rock Manor''', donated to Admiral Roj as he lived out his final days.
*Magsaret Kaeta &mdash; singer and elder stateswoman of the Sarian (Meditersari) people.
*Genosha Enebi &mdash; elder statesman and virtual leader of the Janhovi-Sarian people.
*Beran Blindell &mdash; became an important designer within [[CDMC]].
*__________ Hood &mdash; one-time notary (of some kind) in Caspian/Sarian history; [[CMS Hood|''Hood'']] is named for him.
*Draemon & Lilly Byurnadin &mdash; Sarian; one-half of the '''Byurnadin-Covra Tachtronics''' name.
*[[Naten Bonrue]] &mdash; a Sarian (Janhovi) officer (or constable) under the direction of, or affiliated with, [[Leidan Adamov]] while Adamov was the informal leader of the Caspian "police forces" during the Imperial occupation. He's gone on to become a prominent member of the [[Admiralty]].

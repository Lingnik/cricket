Include this as the ''very first text'' in your character article. When including <nowiki>[[]]</nowiki> wiki links, please use the Preview button or the Search feature to find the proper article names and to make sure your links work.

==<nowiki>{{rplog}}</nowiki>==

<nowiki>{{rplog|</nowiki><br>
<nowiki>title=Insert title here|</nowiki><br>
<nowiki>author=[[Your character page]]|</nowiki><br>
<nowiki>date=[[IC Year]] - Insert RL date|</nowiki><br>
<nowiki>setting=Insert location here|</nowiki><br>
<nowiki>characters=[[Player 1]]<br />[[Player 2]]<br />etc...|</nowiki><br>
<nowiki>synopsis=Insert synopsis here|</nowiki><br>
<nowiki>}}</nowiki>

Include this as the ''very first text'' in your roleplay log article. The '''title''' value will be displayed at the top of the log, obscuring that ugly RPlog: portion. Please make the title the same as the log name, or something close. When including <nowiki>[[]]</nowiki> wiki links, please use the Preview button or the Search feature to find the proper article names and to make sure your links work.

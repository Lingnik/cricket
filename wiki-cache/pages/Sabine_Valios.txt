'''Sabine Valios''' is the daughter of [[Jessalyn Valios]] and [[Paul Nighman]] and an Initiate in the [[Jedi Order]].

She was born in 18 ABY on [[Ord Mantell]] at the Jedi Temple where her mother served as Master of Younglings. During childhood, she traveled frequently with her family throughout the galaxy as the Jedi sought to recruit new Force-sensitives into the Order. This gave her quite an eclectic upbringing, and while still shy at eleven years old, she displays a keen intelligence and independence that are just starting to be nurtured. In training almost since birth, Sabine has taken it for granted that she will eventually grow up to be a Jedi Knight like her mother and brother [[Kiri Valios]].

She's shown more of a scholarly interest than other members of her family, however. Though she enjoys tinkering with droids and starship engines, she's more likely to have her nose in a book or historical holovid than her mother was at her age.

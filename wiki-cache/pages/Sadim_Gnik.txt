'''Sadim Gnik''' ([[19 BBY]] - ) is a [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] academic, diplomat, and naval officer originally from [[Coruscant]]. He taught linguistics and stellar cartography at the [[:starwars:Imperial University|Imperial University]] on Coruscant before following his younger half-sister [[Lynae Cassius]] into service in the [[Caspian Navy]]. He served as [[Lieutenant]] and Second Officer aboard the [[CMS Audace]] where Lynae was Executive Officer. 

Sadim would later use his connexions to secure appointment as the Caspian Ambassador to the [[Corporate Sector Authority]]. Dissatisfied by life in public service he would take a teaching position at the University of Feyenoor on [[Ixin]]. Following Ixin's return to the Caspian Union in [[29 ABY]], Sadim would accept recommissioning into the Caspian Navy as a [[Captain]] and the Commanding Officer of the ''CMS Neosuhas''.

In [[34 ABY]], he was a contributor to the [[InterGalactic News]] [[Report:Xi Char Prelate Press|coverage]] of the passing of [[Xi Char]] [[Prelate]] [[r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak]] and [[Conclave|conclave]] which elected Inquisitor [[stahanak]] as his successor.

== OOC Notes ==

This character was played by the same player behind [[Crestian Tarasar]] & [[Zubindi Hakoon]]. When rejoined the game in 2018 he chose to make a new character and leave this one on the shelf, retired from active play.

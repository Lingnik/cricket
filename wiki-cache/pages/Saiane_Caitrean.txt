'''Saiane Caitrean''' ([[35 BBY]] - [[28 ABY]]) was a [[Sarian]] politician and lawyer from Hammerton on [[Mergansar]]. Caitrean became one Hammerton's inaugural delegates to the [[Union Assembly]]. Caitrean was an ambitious man, but conflicts with virtually every [[Caspian Democratic Union|Caspian]] administration stunted his career for years. His opportunity for advancement finally came in [[21 ABY]], when long serving "Interim" [[Presav]] [[Thomas Mahon]] finally retired. Caitrean was able exploit his anti-establishment credentials to secure support with those who had grown tired of the previous administration. With Mahon declining to endorse any successor, Caitrean was able to overcome divided opposition and was elected his successor.

Caitrean's administration was initially popular. The admission of [[Ixin]] into the CDU within months of coming to office boosted his prestige. Behind the scenes, however, there was a great deal of chaos. The [[Caspian Navy]] was particular beset by uneven interventions. Various construction projects that previously been delayed or slowed, suddenly found new government financing and support. At the same time, a number of ships which had not yet reached their intended lifespan were quietly decommissioned. To make matters worse the ships that remained frequently found themselves reassigned to different parts of [[Caspian Sector|Union Space]] without any obvious reason.

It was in this environment that in [[24 ABY]] members of his government [[2015 Caspar Plot|conspired]] to supply arms to the [[New Republic]] in violation of the [[CDU Nonaggression Treaty]]. When the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] became aware of the plot and a full-scale diplomatic crisis erupted Caitrean provided little in the way of public leadership with the [[Caspian Crest|Crest's]] response mostly coming through Minister of State [[Cordelia Nivhya]].

The crisis quickly devolved with [[Caspar]] blockaded and then invaded by the Empire. Later Imperial and Republic forces were in armed conflict in Caspian Space. When the dust settled, the Union was forced to sign an amendment to their treaty with the Empire, which forced all shipping going into Union Space to travel through [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] to go through customs enforcement run by the [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]], gutting Union sovereignty. Several key Union corporations were also brought to ruin and acquired by CSA interests in a fire sale. Presav Caitrean and his entire cabinet ultimately were forced to resign, with [[Kilos Sabosulmis]] quickly elected to replace him.

Caitrean remained living in [[Plaxton]] following his resignation. He would be one of the billions to die in the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]]. According to surviving aides, he had been working on his memoirs at the time of his death. His legacy is almost universally imputed both for his failed leadership during the crisis and for setting the stage for the CDU's continued decline.

'''Sapphire Jones''' was a [[First Lieutenant]] in the [[Imperial Army]] stationed aboard [[I2SD Tormentor|HIMS ''Tormentor'']]. She was primarily a dropship pilot, responsible for transporting soldiers into and out of battlefields. In [[11 ABY]] she was mired in a scandal with her lover, [[Brigadier General]] [[Camrath Kizuka]], and then-[[Staff Sergeant]] [[Petra Doom]], ultimately resulting in Jones' early retirement. Kizuka himself followed soon after.

A sprawling private space station in orbit around [[Etti IV]], the seat of the [[Corporate Sector Authority]]. Formerly known as Moek Station, it was renamed in honour of its present proprietress, exiled [[Vara|Varvani]] royal [[Beatrix Vara]].

==Early Days==

Construction began on Moek Station in the year 3 ABY and was completed in 8 ABY, under the supervision of the then-Executive Viceprex of Security, Tabi Moek. At the time, Etti IV's corporate citizens were much concerned about a level of criminal activity which was becoming bad for business, and this shining new command centre for [[Security_Division_(CSA)|CSA Security]] was touted as a panacea.

Scandal arose when barely four years later, in 12 ABY, the station passed into the ownership of Beatrix Vara. Some say she bought it outright, others that she won it on the roll of a die, others still that the blackmail of highly-placed officials played a part in her acquisition of such an important piece of CSA property. The truth is surely less straightforward than any of the rumours.

==Refit==

Before the new owner took up residence, an extensive refitting and redecorating project was carried out. Teams of [[Verpine]] and [[Givin]] worked round the chronometer to update the bleak security station's systems and defenses and transform it into a suitable home for a lady of such elegant tastes.

Alas, this phase in the station's life was marked by tragedy. On the last day of their labours, an entire work crew, their foreman, the overall supervisor of the refit, and his confidential secretary, were lost in a freak airlock accident. The renaming ceremony slated to be held the next day was turned into a beautiful memorial to their lives and work. Their names are forever immortalised upon a plaque outside the turbolift on Level One.

==Under Present Management==

Today Vara Station is the home of Beatrix Vara; her company, Viridis Incorporated; a large percentage of the otherwise rootless Varvani refugee population; and a seemingly ubiquitous herd of Varvani [[Vara#Direcats|direcats]].

The station's security force consists of [[Trandoshan|albino Trandoshans]], who have found here in Beatrix Vara's employ a respectability denied them on their homeworld.

Its most notable architectural feature is, perhaps, the recreation on Level 25 of the throne room of the Cassimari Palace, the original in the planet Vara's capital city of Viridis having been lost to the universe when a volcano came up under it in 19 BBY. It is here that Beatrix Vara receives guests.

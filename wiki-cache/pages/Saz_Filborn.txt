'''Saz Filborn''' was the first [[Captain]] of [[I2SD Conqueror|HIMS ''Conqueror'']]. He obeyed [[Sate Pestage]]'s orders to defend [[Coruscant|Imperial Center]] during the [[Post-Endor Imperial power struggle]], refusing to follow [[Jard Kisal]], his commanding officer, to the [[Inner Rim]]. He retired in [[13 ABY]] and was succeeded by [[David Stone]].

Until the '''Second Battle of Bespin '''the planet had been under Imperial rule since [[Post-Endor Imperial power struggle|3 ABY]].

Distracted by preparations for the [[Great Campaign]] the [[Galactic Empire]] failed to marshal sufficient forces to repel the [[New Republic|Republic]] invasion, which successfully liberated the planet.

Bespin was granted independence several months later under coercion of [[Bacharan Valak|Emperor Valak's]] [[Death Star III|Death Star]] and [[Delila Dmitri]] appointed as its [[Baron|Baron-Administrator]]

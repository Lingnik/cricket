Precipitated by the looming threat of [[Bacharan Valak|Emperor Valak's]] [[Death Star III|Death Star]] and the [[Second Battle of Kashyyyk|recent secession]] of [[Kashyyyk]] from the [[New Republic|Republic]], [[Corellia|Corellians]] began pushing for their own withdrawal.

In order to force the matter, the Empire launched a successful invasion of the system, driving off the Republic forces.

Corellia achieved semi-independence from the Republic, and the Empire withdrew.

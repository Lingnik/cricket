'''Second Combat Officer''' is a military rank used by the '''[[New Republic GroundOps|New Republic Army]]'''. Approximately seventy-five percent of cadets come from enlisted backgrounds, and those who come straight into the Academy from their civilian life are recognized for their superior leadership skills.

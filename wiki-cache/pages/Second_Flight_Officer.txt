'''Second Flight Officer''' is a military rank that represents an officer near the lower levels of a commissioned rank scale.

=== In New Republic Service ===

New members of the '''[[New Republic StarOps|New Republic Starfighter Corps]]''', these pilots were the greenest of the green. While they were trained to be combat pilots, and most likely were proficient enough to handle it, they were typically only part of the squadron formation. Support Personnel who held this rank might be traffic operators for their specific post.

=== In Union Service ===

The equivalent rank in the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]] is [[Flight Lieutenant|Junior Flight Lieutenant]].

The '''Second Renaissance of Innovation''' refers to the efforts by the [[Caspian Crest]] under the leadership of [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] to jump-start the defence industry of the [[Caspian Union]] after its decline in the final years of the old [[Caspian Democratic Union]]. While this renaissance was under way since the on set of his administration in [[29 ABY]], the use of the term did not occur until a [[RPlog:Renaissance of Innovation|speech]] Tarasar made in [[32 ABY]]. The speech included a presentation of the new military hardware produced by [[Haor Chall Engineering]] and announced [[Charros Wargames|war games]] on [[Charros IV]]. The use of the phrase 'Renaissance of Innovation' was meant to link Tarasar's efforts to the [[First Renaissance of Innovation|efforts]] of Caspian Presav [[Lyr Dunwell]] a quarter century earlier.

== Background ==

When [[Crestian Tarasar]] was elected [[Presav]] of the [[Caspian Union]], he in inherited control of a Union both reduced in territory but also in industry. After the arms supply [[2015 Caspar Plot|scandal]] of [[24 ABY]], the Caspian economy tanked allowing [[Zeak Oppenhiemer]] to gain control of a number of Caspian assets including [[Kichnar Orbital Station]], [[SubPro|Sublight Products Corporation]], and [[NovaCom Cybertronics]] in a fire sale. These assets later passed into the control of the [[Corporate Sector Authority]] itself, after the Authority was restructured in [[27 ABY]].

Among the first acts of the Tarasar administration was the repatriation and nationalisation of the station and SubPro. The [[Caspian Repatriation Act (29 ABY)|Caspian Repatriation Act]] also served to nationalise [[CDMC|Caspian Design & Manufacturing Corporand]] and [[Interspatial Prospecting, Inc.]] under the name of Union defence. NovaCom was left out of these efforts as Tarasar did not consider it critical for defence and had no aim attacking private industry or the CSA for its own sake. Surprisingly the CSA made no effort to halt the move nor similar moves by the [[Imperium]].

The order in which Tarasar pursued the reunification of [[Caspar]] with the former Union worlds was also directed by industrial concerns. Tarasar's primary diplomatic move at the time was to negotiate with [[Kasputin Yar]] in order that [[Lokkai Starworks]] would once again be a Union concern. Meanwhile Caspian Vice Presav [[Cas Mos]] led negotiations with [[Rauther]] which brought [[StarShield]] back as well. Though the industry of [[Krittain Major]], particular [[Aston Aerospace]] and Hixam Sidney Design were missed by the Union as well, the diplomatic path to reunion with the Krit Union was slow and would only occur in [[30 ABY]], after an attack by slavers on a Krittish ship illustrated the limitations of the [[Krittish Navy]] in operating alone.

The final, and perhaps most important piece of the industrial revitalisation was Tarasar's negotiations with the Imperium and [[Xi Char]] to see [[Kuat Drive Yards-Sienar Fleet Systems]] cede control of the former assets of [[Haor Chall Engineering]] back to the Xi Char and see Haor Chall Engineering reconstituted. These negotiations would also see [[Charros IV]] leave the Imperium and join the Union in 30 ABY, at the same time as reunion between the Caspians and Krits. Long before negotiations were concluded the [[Caspian Navy]] and SubPro contracted Xi Charrian engineers to work on the recommissioning and refit of the [[Daring-class Battlecruiser|Daring-Class Battlecruisers]].

== Renaissance ==

In contrast to the original [[First Renaissance of Innovation|Renaissance of Innovation]] which had centred on Caspar itself, the Second Renaissance was centred on Charros IV reflecting a true rebirth of its long dormant industry. Though the [[Xi Charrian|Xi Charrians]] would have their claws in all the designs they partnered with other Caspian firms on a number of them. The design of the [[E5 Baron Droid]] in particular was brought to the [[Haor Chall Engineering]] by SubPro's [[Sirlahn Inglet]] based on an earlier design by [[:starwars:Baktoid Combat Automata/Legends|Baktoid Combat Automata]] that Inglet had been working on.

Once Haor Chall Engineering had constructed enough units of their various designs the products were introduced after a [[RPlog:Renaissance of Innovation|speech]] by Presav Tarasar at [[Union Hall]] in [[Plaxton]] by Sirlahn Inglet and [[Dean]] [[ak]] who was the central figure in the Xi Char's efforts in negotiation, design, and production. In addition to the speech and presentation, [[Charros Wargames|war games]] were announced to be held at Charros IV to demonstrate the new designs and the refitted Daring Battlecruisers. The subsequent event served not only to introduce the new designs but to also introduce Charros IV and Xi Charrian culture to the wider galaxy.

Haor Chall Engineering's rebirth was notable as the first time in many years that multiple new and updated starfighter designs were put into production by any galactic power. It was also the first time since the Droid Legions of the [[New Sith Order]] that large scale battle droid production was undertaken by any power.

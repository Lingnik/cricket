A '''Sector Director''' was a rank used within the [[Galactic Empire]]'s intelligence community, specifically the various bureaus of [[Imperial Intelligence]] and [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR's]] [[Imperial Security Bureau]].

A '''Sector Director''' oversaw all bureau or branch operations within his assigned [[sector]]. 

==Imperial Intelligence==
Appointed by the [[Ubiqtorate]], Intelligence Sector Directors managed all intelligence activities within a sector of space, and managed his various System Directors, passing along and issuing orders and directives. The Sector Director had little hand in the day-to-day operations of the bureaus due to the immense amount of manpower and territory at his disposal. Sector Directors often kept in contact with the sector [[Moff]] or [[Grand Moff]] and the military command of the sector to ensure a collaborative effort between all branches of the Empire. Sector Directors also worked with the various bureau heads and contacts.

==Imperial Security Bureau==
Appointed by the [[Select Committee]], ISB Sector Directors managed all ISB activities within a sector. He managed his various System Directors, passing along orders and directives. Sector Directors kept in close contact with the Select Committee and the various branch heads of the Security Bureau. The Sector Director also worked closely with other bureaus of COMPNOR, including [[CompForce]], to ensure the best level of effectiveness. As per Select Committee order, Sector Directors were also responsible for keeping a close eye on the Moff or Grand Moff, reporting their activities back to the Select Committee.

A '''sector fleet''' is an administrative division of military assets in the [[Galactic Empire]]. It refers to the standing forces assigned to secure and police a defined sector of space, in support of those forces possessed by planetary and system [[Moff|governors]].

The size and composition of a sector fleet varies widely, depending on available resources, the strategic and economic value of the region and the internal politics of the Empire.

Much like Lightsaber combat, this power allows the Force User to wield a staff or lightstaff with increased accuracy. When using this power, the Force User can use a staff or lightstaff to deflect blaster bolts. This power does not harden a normal staff, or allow a normal staff to redirect bolts. Like Lightsaber combat, the Force User can use this power to deflect blaster bolts. To deflect blaster bolts, the Force User rolls either their Melee Combat or their Sense against the target's Blaster. Like Lightsaber combat, the Force User can use this power to redirect blaster bolts, though it is more difficult. To successfully redirect a blaster bolt, the Force User must first successfully deflect the blast. They then must roll Melee Combat or their Control against the target's Dodge + 15.

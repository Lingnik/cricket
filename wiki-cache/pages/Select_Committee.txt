The '''Select Committee''' is the executive body of [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]].

The Select Committee has a variable number of members, appointed by the Emperor on recommendations. 

The Select Committee's membership during the reign of Emperor Palpatine was secret and remained intact after the fragmentation of the Empire in [[4 ABY]], The committee went on to serve under the [[Interim Ruling Council]] throughout the [[Post-Endor Imperial power struggle]].

The '''Shadow Guard''' was an elite special operations team within the [[Bureau of Operations]] that received their missions directly from the [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]]. Combining some of the best minds and most qualified officers and soldiers in the Military, the Shadow Guard were feared, respected, and executed their directives without question. Shadow Guard assignments varied in nature, including intelligence gathering, sabotage, kidnapping, and assassination missions. At times, they were called in to support the Emperor's [[Noghri|Noghri Death Commandos]] and, on occasion, Noghri were attached to Shadow Guard missions. In [[7 ABY]], the Shadow Guard was implicated in the [[Vigilant Movement]]'s coup against [[Bacharan Valak]]; its members were killed (save for the few who refused to take part in the uprising) and the Guard disbanded.

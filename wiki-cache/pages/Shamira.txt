Plant-fiber based and with a smooth, almost slick feel, '''shamira''' is a twill woven fabric from the [[Caspia|Caspian System]] that is naturally pale orange and only reliably dyed into the red spectrum. It's nearly waterproof, wrinkles easily, and is considered a luxury fabric due to its comfort and expense.

'''Shandra Fleming''' is the wife of [[Ban Fleming]] and mother of [[Korynn Fleming]]. Born on [[:starwars:Anaxes/Legends|Anaxes]] in [[:starwars:51 BBY/Legends|51 BBY]], she would meet her husband while he was in Officer's Training on Anaxes, and they were married in [[32 BBY]] after his graduation. Shandra would move to [[Coruscant]] and take employment as a surgical assistant.

In [[24 BBY]], Shandra gave birth to Korynn Fleming. She retired from and became a housewife, where she raised Korynn under strict Imperial rule. Shandra would be evacuated from Coruscant in [[4 ABY|23 ISY]] when it fell to the New Republic, and lived under COMPNOR protection on Selene. Ban joined her in [[9 ABY|28 ISY]] after his retirement. They would be relocated to Coruscant again in [[15 ABY|35 ISY]].

She currently resides with her husband, Ban, in a COMPNOR-protected apartment facility on [[Coruscant]].

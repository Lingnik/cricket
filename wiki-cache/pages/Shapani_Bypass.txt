The '''Shapani Bypass''', nicknamed the '''"Bacta Run,"''' was a hyperlane that was used as a shortcut in transporting [[:starwars:Bacta/Legends|bacta]] from [[:starwars:Thyferra/Legends|Thyferra]] to the [[The Galaxy#Core Worlds|Core Worlds]]. The Bypass was a spur of the longer [[Rimma Trade Route]] that was opened up circa 2,203 BBY. However, the route was much more heavily traveled than the segment of the Rimma that it bypassed, and was considered to be part of that trade route. The establishing of the Bypass cut days off the heavily traveled Rimma Trade Route.

The bypass originated at Thyferra before splitting with the Rimma and crossed through the [[:starwars:Tapani sector/Legends|Tapani sector]]. In this sector, it passed worlds such as [[:starwars:Achillea|Achillea]], [[:starwars:Aleron|Aleron]], [[:starwars:Tallaan|Tallaan]], (where it intersected the [[:starwars:Giju Run/Legends|Giju Run]] and the [[:starwars:Procopian Shipping Lane|Procopian Shipping Lane]]), [[:starwars:Lamuir IV|Lamuir IV]], and [[:starwars:Mrlsst/Legends|Mrlsst]] before rejoining the Rimma.

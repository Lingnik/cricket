'''Shenneret Veery''' was the daughter of a [[:starwars:Belsavis/Legends|Belsavis]] prostitute and an unknown (but believed to be [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]]) father, born in [[15 BBY]]. Early in her life, she masqueraded as a boy, but the onset of adulthood forced her to drop the diguise, though she retained the tough and cocky demeanor nonetheless. She became a street musician, performing on planets such as [[Tatooine]], [[Etti IV]], [[Dac]], [[Corellia]], and [[Caspar]], and has been in the company of certain high-ranking personages of the [[New Republic]]. She considered herself to be an excellent shot with a blaster, and in her spare time, performed petty robbery. In her youth she had been seen on several worlds in the company of the famous explorer, [[Paul Nighman]].

Shen has since settled in the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], taken up studies (verging on completion of a Doctorate in Xenomusicology as of [[15 ABY]]) and married a [[Union Marines|Union Marine]] by the name of [[Jonathan Webb]] (who seems to have unique qualities that enable him to manage Shen's formidable temper).  Once in a while she can still be found playing with her old band, the Womprats.

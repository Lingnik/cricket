'''Shistavanens''', or colloquially known as ''Wolfmen'', are bipedal canine humanoids. Their bodies are covered with 
thick, warm fur ranging from white to black, with the exception of their
faces, where the fur is short and velvety. Their five fingered hands end in
short, sharp claws. They are either tailless or have short, shaggy tails about
half a foot long. Unlike most bipeds, Shistavanens are digitigrade, walking 
on their toes as it were (Think a similar leg structure to an earth dog, but
with bigger feet to compensate for being bipedal).

Shistavanens hail from the [[:starwars:Uvena_ ystem/Legends|Uvena system]] and while they do not encourage trade
and tourism on their world, they do grudgingly allow offworlders to train on 
their planet. 

Shistavanens are excellent hunters and they can track prey in crowded urban
streets and in natural environments. This natural hunting ability combined with
their ability to see in almost complete darkness makes them sought after
soldiers, hunters and guards... even some [[Galactic Empire|Imperials]] have been known to have 
Shistavanen guards.

== Background ==
Once upon a time her parents were part of an architecture/engineering firm on Naboo (multi-generational business) until about 10 years ago when turmoil on Naboo caused contracts to dry up, new construction to halt and in general civic/civil unrest lead to many local businesses collapsing due to on-going strife and galactic turmoil. Siarra had been a engineering student at that time, working on her degree as a early-applicant into the program when all classes were halted - first for a few days, then weeks, then the entire semester. She never had the chance to complete her formal education but studied with the partners of the firm to learn from them in the hope of someday taking over the business and bringing it back to life.

Eventually the study group that had spun out of the halting of classes had turned into more than just study and venting. It became a part of a local activist network, first just political rallies and sit in's and resist-the-machine kind of things but more and more they were recruited to help out with less peaceful demonstrations and protests and many of her group eventually joined the resistance on Naboo and were seconded to further militant units until finding their way to a recruitment center for the new republic (or what was left of it - again - at that time).
With engineering, architecture, construction and a bit of explosive training (lets here it for the family business!) to bring to the party Siarra was a particularly useful new recruit and was sent through additional training courses to refine the hands-on practical skill with real-world application. And while it's certainly a genuine asset to be built like a pro-ball line-backer who can carry half their weight in gear and hold the door against a massive shock wave of explosive force, it's also nice to be small. With her smaller build Siarra can fit into confined spaces and do the sometimes delicate work that needs to be done.

Over the last several years she has been loaned and/or transferred and/or seconded to one unit after another and ultimately found herself more than a bit disillusioned with the whole mess of it all. She'd started all of this because she was a angry young 20 something who wanted her life to go back to the way she thought it was going to be, and thought that political movement or maybe military action would bring that reality back into play. Instead.. well, life is what happens while you're busy making plans, right?

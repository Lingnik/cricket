The Sith Campaign was a year long conflict fought in [[17 ABY]] across the trail-ward outer rim between a splinter faction of the Galactic Empire led by [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] and a coalition of Caspian, Republic and Independent worlds to establish the territories of the [[New Sith Order]].

The New Sith Order was ultimately destroyed by the Galactic Empire in 21 ABY by Lord [[Aldus Thel]]. Darth Malign remains at large.

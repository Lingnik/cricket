'''Skawn Kairn''' ([[5 BBY]] - ) is a notoriously slave  trader, pirate captain, and mercenary leader. He terrorises the galaxy with his [[Carrack-class Light Cruiser|Carrack-Class Light Cruiser]] the ''Smooth Talker'' and his crew of twisted sociopaths. Among his other crimes are involvement with a deadly [[Report:Bespin Recap|attack]] on [[Cloud City]] on [[Bespin]] in [[29 ABY]] followed by an [[Report:A Minor Consideration|attack]] on the [[Krittain|Krittish]] freighter ''Blechley Brook'' in [[30 ABY]] which had been transporting students on a school trip.

He had been known to be based out of [[:starwars:The Wheel/Legends|The Wheel]] and the surrounding area until repeated clashes with [[Caspian Intelligence Service]] agents forced him to relocate. It is rumoured that he was involved in the attack on [[The Oubliette]] which saw [[Lorn Rhys|Darth Malus]] among others freed from their carbonite imprisonment.

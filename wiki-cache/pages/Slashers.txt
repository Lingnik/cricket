The '''Slashers''' were a swoop gang on [[Nar Shaddaa]], under the leadership of the deadly [[human]], [[Ander Dar]]. They were under the employ of [[Grubba the Hutt]], and were rivals to another of Grubba's gangs, the [[Thrashers]].

During the [[Second Battle of Nar Shaddaa]] in [[15 ABY]], the Slashers took over in a pursuit of [[Tyler Damion|Tatooine Bob]]'s allies, after the Thrashers were unable to take out the revolutionaries in a deadly swoop chase. The Slashers were able to take out a couple of Tatooine Bob's allies, but in the end, they and their leader were wiped out in Blood Alley.

The '''Sluis Van Shipyards''' were a prominent shipwright facility orbiting [[Sluis Van]]. These shipyards would vow their service to the [[New Republic]] after the [[Post-Endor Imperial power struggle|fragmentation]] of the [[Galactic Empire]]. After the [[Second Battle of Sluis Van]], the shipyards fell under Imperial control and put to use to further the growing Imperial war machine. As of [[16 ABY]], the shipyards are still under Imperial control.

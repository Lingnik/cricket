== Overview ==
A veteran of over fifty years of galactic war, Solomon Fields was a long time mentor of [[Aldus Thel]] and the Chief of Staff for the [[Neo-Imperial Movement|New Empire]]. He served as Grand Admiral of the [[Neo-Imperial Fleet]] after Lord Thel revived the movement.

Fields was perhaps best known for his joint venture with General [[Eisenjorn Creed]] in their seminal treatise ''The Principles of Imperial Warfare''.

== Death ==
Admiral Fields perished in 25 ABY commanding the [[Imperator-class Star Destroyer]] ''[[Desolator]]'' at the climax of the [[Battle of Bothawui IV (25 ABY)|Fourth Battle of Bothawui]].

Despite having sustained heavy damage in the defense of Holm'starn, Fields refused to yield ground and leave Imperial ground forces unprotected. At great personal risk, he drove his Star Destroyer into the heart of the enemy position, swinging around behind the Republic line and and delivering a fatal strike to the Republic flagship [[NRSC Deliverance|NRSC ''Deliverance'']]. Moments later, a retaliatory shot from the [[Derlin]] under command of Captain [[Argon Brand]] breached the reactor core, destroying the ''Desolator'' and killing everyone on-board.

== Biography ==
A man of dauntless spirit and stubborn convictions, Fields served three tours in the Republic Judicial Forces before the outbreak of the Clone Wars and the restoration of the Republic Navy. Field's service to a central state body rather than the sovereign forces of member worlds found favor under the Supreme Chancellor's policies and by the end of the war, Fields had advanced beyond arguably better qualified and certainly more senior men to command the [[Venator-class Star Destroyer]] ''[[IAC Responder|Responder]].''

Fields, like many naval officers of the old guard was resistant to the Death Star project after its revelation. His open contempt for both the extreme expression of the "otherwise laudable" Tarkin Doctrine and the disaster which met it was of great detriment to his political standing in light of the Emperor's enduring fondness for the battle stations, effectively halting an otherwise promising career until necessities of the [[Post-Endor Imperial power struggle|Post-Endor Power Struggle]] restarted it.

During the [[War of the Throne]], Fields supported [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Kendal Osbourne|Osbourne]] and after the victory of [[Lorn Rhys|Darth Malus]] found himself once more exiled to the Empire's backwaters, taking no part in the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]].

Appointed to the naval command of Taskforce ''Nemesis'' by his protege, Lord [[Aldus Thel]], Fields was instrumental in the annihilation of the [[New Sith Order]] and was chosen by the newly appointed [[Duke]] of [[Selene]] to command the fleet assigned to the [[Imperial Advisor (CSA)|Imperial Advisor]] to the [[Corporate Sector Authority]].

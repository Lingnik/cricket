'''Sonja Damion''' was the daughter of [[Tyler Damion]] (Darth Malign) and [[Gabrielle Damion]].

The child become the target of Malign when he fell to the Dark side, as he sought his daughter out to train her as an heir to the Sith Empire. She was taken by his half-sister [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]] and the [[Jedi Order|Jedi]] and hidden away. 

Malign would spend billions of credits and expend [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] Resources to find her but his failure would drive him to the [[The Galaxy#Outer Rim|Outer Rim]].

Meanwhile, Johanna saw to Sonja's Jedi training personally, schooling her along with all her own children.

After Malign returned from his decade long imprisonment in a [[Sith Order|Sith]] library on [[Korriban]], he was a changed person. He patiently waited until the [[Youngling Ball]] Johanna hosted on Bespin that was attended by both Jedi and [[Imperium]], Force Sensitive and not, children of some of the most prominent beings in the galaxy as a way towards peace among them. Malign arrived uninvited, as he was wont to do. Some time into the evening he noticed that his now sixteen-year-old daughter and his  twenty-year-old apprentice, [[Kipran Jawatha]], had become enthralled with each other. He approached them, presumably to throttle the young adept who'd danced away with his daughter. Much to Johanna's dismay, Malign offered Sonja a manse on [[Selene]] to live in and to transfer her beau back to the Core so that they could court properly. He also proclaimed Sonja his heir and the heir to the Hapes Consortium as well, as his wife the Hapan [[King|Queen Mother]], had no female heirs for her matriarchal throne. On Selene she entered into intense studies into governing and other civic and galaxy politics and statesmanship. At a lavish coronation  picked up on even non-Imperium broadcasts, Sonja Damion became the [[Prince|Crown Princess]]. The girl enjoyed the glamor and nightlife of Selene, having been raised on [[Tatooine]] by her aunt. She spent time with her father as well on his archaeological ventures. But after a time, she became homesick. She and Kipran took leave of their duties to visit Johanna and all of Sonja's cousins on the desert world where they stayed. She lives there for now, studying the Force and drawing her beloved ever closer to the Light.

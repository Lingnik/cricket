== Background ==
Sorin Markof as a youngster was the 'star' of a number of Holovids that most people may not remember. Perhaps even he doesn't want to remember them. Either way, he grew up on Corellia and was there until the Imperial Fleet and its invasion. In the midst of it all, he roused up volunteers, technicians and whoever might be willing to just get out and BORROWED a ship from a scrapyard that was due to be taken apart. Thankfully, it had engines, hyperdrive, and enough life support to get them from there to somewhere with a drydock to get it more spaceworthy.

== Corellian Refugee ==
Since then? Sorin has taken his ragtag group of intrepid explorers back and forth across the galaxy a few times. Frequently, the ship is 'stranded' in place and the crew and himself are forced into odd jobs to pay for berthing and repairs. Sorin's labour tends to be spent singing adequately in bars or saloons while others make far more reputable choices and end up in various places. He'll bleed crew here or there, but inevitably pick up a few along the way.

== The Ship ==
It was something, alright. The biggest thing that was able to move when they requisitioned it. Its just a hunk of engines with two hangars slapped down a center line. Once a mobile repair bay for something, now a moving home for those willing to try it out. Aboard the ship? An AstroGonk named Suds. A gonk body, an astromech's guts shoved inside along with random subroutines and programming that could be afforded. The droid's name is Suds because it has a propensity to gurgle and blow raspberries of oil to make everything slick and gross. Alas.

== Initiate Combat ==
:Both sides roll misc/+check ''<sensors>''

Highest roll will be allowed the first to pose/attack.

== Combat ==
=== Attack Score ===
:The ''attacking'' fighter will misc/+check ''<starship gunnery>''
:The ''attacking'' fighter will misc/roll ''<targetting modifier>''
:Add the resulting rolls to compute the attack score

=== Defense Score ===
:The ''defending'' fighter will misc/+check ''<starfighter piloting>''
:The ''defending'' fighter will misc/roll ''<manuever modifier>''
:Add the resulting rolls to compute the defense score

'''If Attack Score >= Defense Score, Attacker hits'''

== Damage ==
The ''attacker'' will roll their damage die of the weapon fired.  misc/roll ''<damage>''

The resulting roll will be first deducted from the defender's shields.  Should they be off or down, then the remainder will be decuted from the defender's armor.

Below is a guide based on percent armor remaining of the vessel's damage for RP purposes.
:76% - 100% - No damage
:51% - 75% - Lightly damaged
:26% - 50% - Heavily damaged
:1% - 25% - Severely damaged
:0% - Destroyed/Incapacitated

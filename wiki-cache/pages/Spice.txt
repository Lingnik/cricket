'''Spice''' was slang for a mind-altering drug. Varieties included [[:starwars:Ryll/Legends|ryll]], [[:starwars:Andris/Legends|andris]], [[:starwars:Lumni-spice|lumni]], [[:starwars:Avabush/Legends|avabush]], [[:starwars:Carsunum/Legends|carsunum]],  and the strongest (and most expensive), [[:starwars:Glitterstim/Legends|glitterstim]]. Planets such as [[Kessel]], [[Ryloth]], and [[:starwars:Sevarcos II|Sevarcos]] were rich with these spices, which were harvested from dangerous mines. More often than not, harvesting was done by slave labour.

The [[Galactic Empire]] attempted to keep a tight rein on the circulation of the various forms of spice, making it a frequent item for [[smuggling]]. It must be noted though, that not every form of spice was addictive, or even illegal.

In [[:starwars:194 BBY|194 BBY]], the term "spice" gained widespread use.

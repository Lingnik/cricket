'''Squadron Leader''' is a military rank usually given charge of a squadron. It is almost always associated with members of a starfighter corps, most notably the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]]. These are often seasoned officers with moderate responsibility.

== In Imperial Service ==
{{rank plate|[[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]]|}}
In the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]], a '''Squadron Leader''' generally commands a squadron. A typical squadron consists of 12-24 ships, 20-50 officers, and 30-90 enlisted. They are addressed as commander. If a squadron has more than one Squadron Leader, one will be appointed Squadron Commander and the other Executive Officer. If appointed as a staff officer they will act as an assistant to a [[Wing Commander]].

A Squadron Leader was the equivalent to a [[Lieutenant Commander]] in the [[Imperial Navy]].

== In Union Service ==

In the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]], the rank of '''Squadron Commander''' is equivalent to an Imperial Squadron Leader, though they are not likely to command more than a dozen ships and the number of personnel under the command will likely to be fewer as a result. They rank below a [[Wing Commander]] and a above a [[Flight Commander]]. It is equivalent in rank to a [[Lieutenant Commander]] in the [[Caspian Navy]] and to a [[Major]] in the [[Union Marines]].

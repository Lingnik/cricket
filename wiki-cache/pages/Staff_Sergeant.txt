The rank and title of '''Staff Sergeant''' is a non-commissioned officer's rank. They often act as squad or platoon leaders in army organizations.

=== In New Republic Service ===

Staff Sergeants in the '''[[New Republic GroundOps|New Republic Army]]''' usually represent soldiers who are on a rise through the ranks or are reaching the end of their careers. Younger soldiers who reach this rank within five to six years are worth noting; they're the individuals who will make the senior NCO ranks. Soldiers in their tenth service year at this rank are typically battle-hardened veterans, many of which decline promotion in order to remain in the thick of combat with their men.

'''Stansfield''' ([[11 BBY]] - ) is a Stuntman for [[NovaCom Cybertronics]]. He also works as security on [[Farrel]]'s humanitarian missions.

==Appearance==
Stansfield is approximately 2.5 meters tall, and broad enough to match. He looks kind of like a bodybuilding giant. His skin is a light gray color, mottled with darker gray on his limbs and down his back. Perhaps his most striking feature, aside from his sheer size, is the fin jutting out of his head. The fin makes up a full foot of his height, jutting out of the center of his head and running down to just above his shoulder blades, where it merges into his back. The fin's height tapers as it descends.

His face is otherwise humanoid, if a bit mongoloid due to heavy, jutting brows. He has no hair, and his eyes are gray like his skin. Stansfield's nose is fairly flat, and the nostrils are vertical slits. His mouth is wide and set in a grim line. Despite his shark-like fin, he has regular humanoid teeth and appears to breathe air like a human.

Stansfield dresses in an open black vest. It seems to be made of some type of animal hide. His name is picked out in silver gems across his shoulders. Stansfield's form-fitting pants are made from the same material, and feature silver stripes down the sides. There's a blaster holster slung down on his right thigh, but he doesn't look like the kind of man who relies on lasers. He has no shoes, as the soles of his wide feet are hardened and leathery.

Stansfield sports a matching pair of tattoos, one on each bicep. Each one reads 100 in simple block letters. The meaning of this is unclear.

'''StarShield Armaments''' was a manufacturer and supplier of offensive and defensive weaponry, energy-assisted armor, and shielding systems for a variety of applications, primarily for personnel and vehicular scale.

Originally based on [[Rauther]], StarShield was founded by those who picked apart and worked upon the Republic arsenal left behind when the planet was relegated from being an [[:starwars:Ordnance/Regional Depot|Ordnance-Depot world]].

'''Starlight Swoops''' is the newest venture of entertainment company [[Starlight Entertainment]]. It has a swoop garage on Level 88 [[Nar Shaddaa]] and is run by CEO [[Ewan Dain]].

===Nothing Shines like a Star!===
In Nelona, [[15 ABY]], [[Ewan Dain]] hired [[Ayaka'Gul]], a young [[Twi'lek]] woman with a head for business and a passion for success, to be CEO of Starlight Swoops.  Ayaka immediately set to collecting the beginnings of a team for Starlight and attempting to stir up interest in the sport.

The first advertising push of Starlight Swoops under Ayaka is the '''Shine''' campaign.  ''Nothing Shines like a Star: Be a Star on a Starlight Swoop!'' is focused on putting star riders in the spotlight and opening up a street-league in which average people can race their swoops on some of the biggest tracks in the Slice.  ''More to come here.''

==The Business==
''Starlight Swoops'' features a swoop sales and repair shop and sponsors a team of riders for the GSL.  The shop can be found on Level 88, east of the lift, on Nar Shaddaa.  The Corellian sector of Nar Shaddaa is a well-respected business area of the moon and many people find their experience there to be pleasant and profitable.  The business plays an important role in the larger family of Starlight Entertainment as the team is really just another advertising tool for Starlight.  

The colour scheme for Starlight Swoops is sky-blue (often pearlized) with accents of silver and black.  The logo, shown in black and white, generally features a blue swoop with a silver star and blue halo on a black background.  These colours are often found in Starlight's line of clothing and accessories.  Other colour schemes can be found in Starlight's exclusive line of swoop-riding jumpsuits and sport clothing, depending on the season.

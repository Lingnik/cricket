TITLE: Stormtrooper Armor
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2005-12-18T16:34:01Z
======================================================================
Pristine white armor is sculpted into a generic human form. The
dark visor of the polished helmet rids the wearer of any individual
identity, making it impossible to determine who they are. The
polished white plates of the armor are given flexibility by the
black joint seams that are in between the plates. These plates
cover the entire body, from the neck, all the way down to the toes,
sealing the wearer in an air-tight seal Attached to the waistline
of the armor is a black belt with numerous utility boxes, and a
blaster holster of polished black nerf leather.

'''Sub-Lieutenant''' was a military rank.

=== In Imperial Service ===
{{rank plate|[[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]]|}}
A Sub-Lieutenant, or '''Lieutenant Junior Grade''', as it had been known until a reform in [[15 ABY]], was an [[Imperial Navy]] rank immediately above [[Ensign]] and below [[Lieutenant]]. 

Ensigns were promoted to Sub-Lieutenant upon receiving certain command training and has proved their worth to their commanding officer through successfully managing given tasks and assignments. Sub-Lieutenants generally did not receive any command authority in the Navy, and was instead used for organizational means. Their only real authority was over Ensigns and non-commissioned officers, and instead just paved a way towards Lieutenant.

A Sub-Lieutenant was equivalent to a [[Flight Officer]] in the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]].

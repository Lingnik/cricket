====Concept====
A frustrated scholar desiring to implement his deepest and wildest designs in Astroengineering and fleet operations, Dr. Sye NaBokin graduated with honors from the Harpori Institute of Astroengineering with high hopes and aspirations.  But the plentiful corporate astronautics industry found NaBokin's resume marked with allegations of fraud, bounties for his capture from seedy galactic elements, and an alleged tuition debt that the struggling duros claims are all the fabrications of sentients seeking to blackball his career and send him spiralling into misfortune.  

The truth of these matters remains hidden behind claims and accusations that are impossible to see clearly through - until Sye steps onto the bridge of a capital ship, settles into the cockpit of a starship or highlights designs for battle manuevers (utilizing a three-point communications array jamming signal and precision capital ship hyperspace jump tactics).  That is when the duros' genius truly shines or falters, and that is the only weapon a fledgling scientist has to offer in the face of powerful corporate enemies and manipulative underworld allies.

====IC Status====
Forced from Harpori as a stowaway aboard a bulk cruiser, Sye escapes to the core worlds and the relative anonymity there.  Seeking employment from independent companies that may not be aware of his difficult recent past and outstanding marks, Sye finds a foothold on Gundark Industry and sets to carving out his niche within their ranks.  He may find that his allies and employer have equally sharp claws as those of the elitist durosian corporate executives Sye left behind.

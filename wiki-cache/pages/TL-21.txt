The '''TL-21 "Stormtrooper Two" light repeating blaster''' was a standard-issue, rapid-fire weapon produced by [[BlasTech Industries]]. This weapon saw use in the [[:starwars:Clone Wars|Clone Wars]], the [[Galactic Civil War]], and beyond.

The T-21 was among the heaviest standard-issue weapon that could be fielded by a single soldier. [[:starwars:Clone trooper|Clone troopers]] of the [[:starwars:Grand Army of the Republic|Grand Army of the Republic]] carried T-21s into battle during the latter stages of the Clone Wars, and the weapon was carried over in the transition from [[:starwars:Galactic Republic|Galactic Republic]] to [[Galactic Empire|the Empire]]. Due to its reasonable portability, it was also popular with the [[:starwars:Rebel Alliance|Rebel Alliance]], and later the [[New Republic]]. 

Since the T-21's standard power pack only has enough energy for twenty-five shots, it was typically paired with a continuous-feed power generator. The twenty-kilogram generator was carried as a backpack.

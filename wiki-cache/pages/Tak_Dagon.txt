'''Tak Dagon''' ([[14 BBY]] - ) is a former board member and one time fleet commander of the [[Vigilance Security Corporation]].  His career includes spending time as an officer of little importance in the [[Security Division (CSA)|CSSD]] and as a private sector pilot for hire.  At one time, his loyalties were known to lie with the highest bidder, and as such he went where the work was.  Known by many for a certain level of wanderlust and a desire to try different things, he was well suited for the life of a mercenary.

When [[Vigilance Security Corporation]] closed it's doors, Tak decided to take a friend's advice and put his varied talents to good use in the [[New Republic Marine Corps]].  His time in the Marines was not exactly what one would call...optimum.  Shortly after joining up with the 224th at the request of [[Vengan Draelis]], whom Dagon had served with during a handful of campaigns where the New Republic had hired VSC, the Dacan Civil War broke out.  Staff Sergeant Tak Dagon was on Captain Draelis' insertion team.  After watching his friend and CO get what he saw as the proverbial shaft during the Dacan crisis for playing a little loose with the rules, Dagon resigned his post with the Marine Corps and has allowed his wanderlust to take over once again.

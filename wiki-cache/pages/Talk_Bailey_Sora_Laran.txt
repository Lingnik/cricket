== ISY vs. BBY / ABY ==
I have to affix some sort of '''cleanup''' notice on this page &mdash; Use of the denotation '''ISY''' is, from what I gather, "Imperial Standard Year". However, this is not galactically accepted, neither in this storyline or in Star Wars as a whole. While I'm not against its use in this context (because this is an Imperial character, afterall), I do have to point out that ISY is ''not'' in use at Wookieepedia (the Star Wars Wikipedia), and any/all links made to year-pages there ''do not resolve''. You will have to either A) match up the ISY year to the corresponding BBY/ABY (the standard) year (''eg. 0 ISY = 19 BBY'' and the link would look like this -> '''<nowiki>[[:starwars:19_BBY|0 ISY]]</nowiki>'''), or B) change all references of ISY to ABY/BBY, and use the galactic standard. -- [[User:Hawke|Hawke / Rtufo]] 17:03, 26 October 2006 (UTC)
* Concur.  ISY is fine for certain In Character contexts (IGN broadcasts, RP logs, etc.), but the standard is BBY/ABY and general wiki pages should conform as part of their neutral IC point of view. -- [[User:Xerxes|Xerxes]] 17:44, 26 October 2006 (UTC)

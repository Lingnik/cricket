*Dark Side Adept is an official term used in the Empire to refer to those loyal to Palpatine and serving with the Empire, but not Sith. People like Mara Jade, Jerec, Treymane, etc. Then the Dark Side Adepts themselves have individual groups, like Emperor's Hand, Inquisitors, Dark Side Elite, etc. The people on that Force Adept category are neither loyal to the Empire/Sith or the NR/Jedi, so they can't be classified as either Dark Side Adepts, Sith or Jedi. They're just Force talented individuals. And, apologies to Korolov - figured you were a Dark Side Adept given your position as an Inquisitor and since you never took a Darth title ;p And yes, while Sith are technically adepts at the Dark Side of the Force, they are never referred to as Dark Side Adepts. They're far more than just masters of the Dark Side, after all - there's a whole lot of difference between a Dark Jedi and Sith, regarding teachings, philosophy, etc. --[[User:Danik Kreldin|Danik Kreldin]] 16:41, 11 October 2006 (UTC)

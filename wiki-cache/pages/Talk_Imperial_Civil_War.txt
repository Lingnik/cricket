{{User:Xerxes/sig}} There were two separate military forces originating from the same principality.  Territory was won, held, and lost. Were Palpatine's forces comparable in size to Valak's? -- [[User:Xerxes|Xerxes]] 15:35, 8 November 2007 (UTC)

:-Of the three Civil Wars, this is the only one that closely resembles what a Civil War is supposed to be. OOCly I am loathe to call it a civil because it didn't cause any real fracture of the faction, it was an NPC 'Empire' vs. The Galaxy really, they fought both the NR and Empire in various forms and siezed territory from both. Technically it could be called a civil war because it does meet the criteria established by the Geneva Conventions and used by the US Military but that for a plot that only lasted a few months RL time, I just don't think has the feel of a full on civil war, but like I said, I can go either way on this one because it does meet the definiton, but is that the best 'term' to use? Maybe even 'The Palpatinist Revolution' or 'True Imperial Revolt'.  --[[User:ImperialFH|ImperialFH]] 15:44, 8 November 2007 (UTC)

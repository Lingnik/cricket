== Revisiting the Issue ==

Wookieepedia clearly has a reason for having both a [[:starwars:Jedi|Jedi]] and a [[:starwars:Jedi Order|Jedi Order]] page. There is a distinct difference between a Jedi and the Order that they exist in. The definitions of 'Jedi' are sub-linked out here to articles such as [[Jedi Knight]] and [[Jedi Master]]. There's no need to delete this page, especially when so many people in the game refer to it. Pending any sensible complaints, I'll wipe this discussion page in a few days time so as not to confuse visitors. -- [[User:SW1 Kyle|SW1 Kyle]] 02:23, 25 April 2007 (UTC)

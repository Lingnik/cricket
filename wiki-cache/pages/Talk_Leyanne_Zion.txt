== Image Removal ==
Images '''can not have''' the name of the actor/actress/person in their filename &mdash; no exceptions. This is a policy we have implemented from Day 1. Search engines now treat Wikis as upper-level realms for seeking out photos and images, and we cannot have someone finding a photo of said actor/actress/person here, in this context. If you would like to have any images up, this policy must be observed. You may upload them under a different name.

Second, images '''can not have''' real-world trademarks, objects, or addresses in them that not only compromise genre integrity, but could also compromise copyright or attribution, or construe advertising. If the image in question is desired, any such instances must be edited out ("photoshopped"). -- [[User:Giant Space Slug|Giant Space Slug]] @ The Darkest Hour, 24 April 2007 (UTC)

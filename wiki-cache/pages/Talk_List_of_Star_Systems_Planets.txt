For those able to do a bit of help (a few minutes spare), this is a simple list of every star system and/or planet on [[The Galaxy|the Galaxy map]]. Not every planet will have a link, and certain planets will be denoted as to their significance on the MUSH. But, this hopefully will get some people inspired as to locations for RP sessions or for filling out backgrounds and/or reports. I think you agree, the galaxy's far larger than having everyone come from Tatooine and every storyline take place at Corellia and Coruscant. -- [[User:Hawke|Hawke / Rtufo]] 12:55, 27 July 2006 (UTC)

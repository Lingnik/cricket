I don't suppose anyone has any details about this world. It may yet have a role to play in future CDU plots. --[[User:Mahon|Mahon]] 05:06, 6 February 2007 (UTC)
*It's open to interpretation. Back then, the NR and myself wanted a world we could have a minor engagement on, but that would play into the upcoming Blitzkrieg I had planned. Either way, there was going to be an attack on a CDU world, and so we decided it would be nice to have a little battle near the CDU in preparation for that event. So, rather than make it on a coded world, we made up some NPC place called Forlec, said it was uninhabited, and placed it near (but not in) CDU space. From what I recall from the actual scene (this was two years ago), the world was largely rocky and mountainous, no sentient life, but breathable atmosphere. The rest I'll leave to your imaginations --[[User:Danik Kreldin|Danik Kreldin]] 05:18, 6 February 2007 (UTC)

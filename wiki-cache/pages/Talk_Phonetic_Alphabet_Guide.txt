== '''Phonetic''' ==
I'm looking at the Imperial column, and there seem to be two subjects confused here -- miliary/governmental designations (using greek letter) vs. a spelling alphabet (using words to specify letters).  ''G''amma may be the third letter in the greek alpabet... but that doesn't make it a phonetic for the third letter in our alphabet.  They are pronounced differently.

I propose that we replace the Imperial column in favor of a "Galactic Standard" phonetic alphabet, and keep the additional columns to show variations from the standard, e.g. Cason for C in the Caspian designations.  -- [[User:Xerxes|Xerxes]] 12:22, 31 October 2008 (UTC)

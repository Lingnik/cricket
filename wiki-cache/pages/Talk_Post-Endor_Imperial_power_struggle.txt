===Civil War?===
What is the validity of calling this event a civil war?

The discussion here [[talk:Third_Imperial_Civil_War|Third Imperial Civil War]]is relevant to this discussion. --[[User:ImperialFH|ImperialFH]] 14:35, 8 November 2007 (UTC)

I disagree about the relevancy of [[talk:Third_Imperial_Civil_War|Third Imperial Civil War]]. However, I do agree that these events should not be lumped into one category called the Imperial Civil War. There were no consistantly defined sides throughout the time between the destruction of DS2 and the dissolution of High Command when Valak became the Emperor officially. If anything this period of time should be called empire in turmoil or galactic turmoil which is the closest term used to describe this period of time in the canon timeline of the MUSH. --[[User:Lorn Rhys|Lorn Rhys]] 15:50, 8 November 2007 (UTC)

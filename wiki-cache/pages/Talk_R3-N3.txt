Completely correct NovaCom references. If you knew the history of NovaCom, as I do, you'd know that NovaCom is/was one of the Original Five of the Caspian Commerce Charter, and it made G-series astromech/utility droids at the time (G1, G3, G5). The mismanagement of NovaCom may have led to it succumbing to, or being duped by, the CSA, but the action/reaction on the part of Caspian military officers (dumping any item made by NovaCom) is comprehensible and well within their rights. -- [[User:Hawke|Hawke / Rtufo]] 06:18, 18 March 2006 (UTC)

== Present tense ==
{{user:Xerxes/sig}} This article seems to use past tense for everything. For example, the article opens ''"The Sabacc Galactic Circuit '''was''' the premier Sabacc league in the galaxy."''  Does that mean it's not the premier league now?  I'm assuming this is not the case and that much of this article actually needs to be re-written using present-tense, e.g. ''"The Sabacc Galactic Circuit '''is''' the premier Sabacc league in the galaxy."''  For a previous discussion on the topic of past vs. present tense, you can check out this [[Forum:Past-tense vs present-tense|Forum article]]. -- [[User:Xerxes|Xerxes]] 15:10, 5 July 2008 (UTC)

I also fixed some of the tense issues though I might have missed a few. [[User:TylerDamion|TylerDamion]] 08:53, 6 July 2008 (UTC)

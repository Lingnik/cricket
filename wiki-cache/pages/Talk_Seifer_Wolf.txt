== Dispute Notice ==

Some of this article may be written from a slanted point of view. The author has taken an approach that is opinionated from his character's point of view. Of special note is the sections after '''16 ABY''', in reference to his opinions of how Bailey treated the crew, and also how ISB was "impressed" with him. The article needs to be re-written so that information is more encyclopedic in nature. For example:

:"He found himself confronting her as she abused the pilots stationed aboard the Inq," should be changed to something like: "He found himself confronting her, believing that the way she was treating the pilots aboard HIMS ''Inquisitor'' was abusive."

That way it's written as if it is Wolf's point of view or opinion, rather than written in a way that STATES Bailey was abusing pilots. Visitors to your page would immediately take your opinion to be FACT, which isn't always true. This isn't the only place I've noticed this, it is merely an example. --[[User:SW1 Kyle|SW1 Kyle]] 16:05, 12 February 2007 (UTC)

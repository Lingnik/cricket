No, no, no, that's all wrong. There was some sort of a rebellion led by the indigenous species -- sandpeople, jawas, etc. Zarl was caught up in that, being a close confidant of one of the leading Tuskens at the time, but ended up being caught by Jared, the head of Imperial Intelligence (or the contemporary equivalent). Zarl squealled, got a great many people killed and escaped with his skin. That is to say, the V'ez had nothing to do with it.

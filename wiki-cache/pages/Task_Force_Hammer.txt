'''Task Force Hammmer''' was a combined force unit of the [[Galactic Empire]], comprising [[Imperial Navy]] warships, [[Imperial Army]] units, and [[Stormtrooper]] battalions. Originally formed by [[Drayson Honos|Fleet Admiral Drayson Honos]], then Commodore, as a special unit as part of the [[Imperial First Fleet]] to harass neutral trading lines and disrupt [[New Republic]] attempts to spread their influence along neutral star systems. 

It later became one of eight such task forces under the direction of [[Danik Kreldin|Admiral Danik Kreldin]] during his [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]] of [[14 ABY]]. Task Force Hammer, being the most prominent task force within the First Fleet, usually had the [[Executor-class Star Dreadnought|Super Star Destroyer]] [[SSD Malevolence|HIMS ''Malevolence'']] accompanying it, along with seven other [[Imperator-class Star Destroyer|Star Destroyers]] and some thirty smaller warships in support as escorts for the Imperial Navy's flagship. Commodore [[David Stone]] was the commander of the taskforce.

In Imperial battlefleet deployment, Task Force Hammer would occupy strategic center of the array, tasked with coordinating all the firepower of the combined fleet.

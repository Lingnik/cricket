The '''Tatooine Hutt Parliament''' was an organization founded by [[Begula the Hutt]] on [[Tatooine]] which utilized legal businesses as well as [[smuggler]]s and [[bounty hunter]]s to put [[credit]]s in the [[Hutt]]'s vault. [[Krackor Reincus]] was a member of the organization.

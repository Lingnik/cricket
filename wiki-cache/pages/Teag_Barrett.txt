=== '''An Idyllic Adolescence''' ===
Born in the [[Chandrila|Chandrilan]] capital of [[Hanna City]], Teag Barrett spent his youth at his family's cabin on the shores of the Silver Sea coast. His parents, were both prominent figures in the Chandrilan House. They began laying groundwork early, hoping to steer the boy into a career as a Hanna delegate.  

Teag was not one for spending long hours in assembly. He much  preferred care-taking his family beehives, fishing in Lake Sah'ot and hunting in the Wild Game Reserve. While he appeased his parents wishes by joining the Legislative Youth Program in his early teens, he had no plans of pursuing that career. Teag was an active and athletic boy, tall and good natured. He decided early on that he was going to enroll in the Brionelle Memorial Military Academy where he felt he could do more good out in the thick of things.  

=== '''The Academy''' ===
Teag joined the academy on his eighteenth birthday and excelled in the physical classes they taught. His years spent in the Legislative Youth Program had prepared him well for the mental and social tasks he underwent in those years before the [[Galactic Civil War]]. On graduation he was given an officer's position in the Chandrila Defense Fleet where he was discovered to have an outstanding talent for marksmanship and stealth tactics learned primarily as a young game hunter on Chandrila.  

Teag had begun turning the heads of Brass and in his second year with the CDF he was recruited into the SpecForce Infiltrators.

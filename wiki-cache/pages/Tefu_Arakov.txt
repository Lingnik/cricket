'''Tefu 'Toff' Arakov''' is a young female [[Twi'lek]] born on ''New Kala'uun'' on [[Etti IV]].  She began her performing career at a young age, beginning with dancing at the age of four.  As she grew older, and more experienced, she broke out on her own and began performing in cantinas, at special events, and just about anywhere she could find a gig.  During a performance at a special Republic event, which was host to several Republic Senators and other prestigious guests, Tefu was asked to fill in for the singer who came down with a flu and could not perform.  Her voice caught the attention of a production agent who introduced her to the prospect of making a career out of singing.  She was soon signed with ''Meladon Recording Studios'' on Coruscant and her first album released reached over 10,000,000 copies sold within the first two weeks.

She sincerely loves what she does and always puts her entire heart and soul into all of her performances, perhaps a secret to her success.  At only 23 years of age, she has become much more successful than she could have imagined, but still has a while to go before she achieves the status of being a galactic sensation.

The name '''Templar''' applies to multiple ships in the galaxy. Possibilities include:

* [[DSV Templar|NRCV ''Templar'']] - a [[Deep Space Vehicle]] which was commandeered from the [[New Republic]] [[New Republic StarOps|Navy]] by [[Colonel]] [[Han'bel Eckhart]].
* [[NRSD Templar|NRSD ''Templar'']] - a [[Republic-class Star Destroyer|Republic-Class Star Destroyer]] which was captured from the [[New Republic]] [[New Republic StarOps|Navy]] during the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]] and rechristened the [[RSD Retribution|HIMS ''Retribution'']] by the [[Galactic Empire]].

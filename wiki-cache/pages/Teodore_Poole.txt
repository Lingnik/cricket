'''Theodore Poole''' was an [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[Imperial Navy|Naval]] officer from [[Caspar]]. He was introduced to [[Dergan Roj]] by [[Leidan Adamov]], while the former was based near Caspar in [[13 BBY]]. Poole would soon join Roj's command where he would stay through to the [[Admiral|Admiral's]] eventual death in from [[:starwars:Zithrom's Syndrome|Zithrom's Syndrome]] in [[4 ABY]].

As a key aide to Roj, Poole defected from the Empire with him in [[3 BBY]] and was one of the prominent officers in the [[Barren Sector Fleet]]. Poole's association with Roj played no small part in the fact the Barren Sector Fleet eventually became based in the [[Caspian Sector]], and subsequently served as the basis of the original [[Caspian Navy]]. 

Poole would also play a prominent role in assisting Roj in the forming the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], serving as an intermediary for the Admiral as his health waned and at time making executive decisions on his own. He was instrumental in the appointment of [[Jake Myar]] to [[The Admiralty]].

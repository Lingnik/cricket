'''Teyr''' was a bureaucratic world, 34 light years from [[:starwars:Vulvarch|Vulvarch]]. It is a popular tourist destination, known for its majestic mountains and the breathtaking Teyr Rift. It was located near the [[:starwars:Foless Crossroads/Legends|Foless Crossroads]]. The planet was colonized by at least [[:starwars:496 BBY|496 BBY]]. On Teyr was held the [[:starwars:Mobquet Presents: Fastest Land Beings|Mobquet Presents: Fastest Land Beings]] race.

During the [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]], it was the site of the [[:starwars:Battle of Teyr|Battle of Teyr]] in [[22 BBY]], which was lead by the [[Jedi]] [[:starwars:K'Kruhk|K'Kruhk]]. It was also presumably the namesake of the ship, ''[[:starwars:Teyr (cruiser)|Teyr]]''.

After the collapse of [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] order in [[4 ABY]], Teyr joined the [[New Republic]] and its tourist business prospered. In [[14 ABY]], it once again fell under Imperial authority, and would become the home of the [[Teyr Resistance Front]], which would lead to the [[Battle of Teyr]].

The Teyr Rift was a major canyon on Teyr, approximately four thousand kilometers long, which would be converted to a redoubt for use by the TRF during the battle.

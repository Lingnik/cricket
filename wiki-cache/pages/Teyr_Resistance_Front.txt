The '''Teyr Resistance Front''' was a rebel organization sprung on the world of [[Teyr]] in [[16 ABY]] in opposition to the government of [[Aleister Vadim]]. It was established by [[Jier Harkenson]], an [[Admiral]] and formerly an officer in the [[Neo-Imperial Movement]], which had been eradicated on Garrison Vexed. The TRF eventually became a front organization for the remnants of the Neo-Imperial Movement, but was eradicated during the [[Battle of Teyr]].

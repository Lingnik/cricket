A '''Thane''' is a title of nobility on some worlds. Its more famous use is by the [[New Sith Order]], where Thanes were the highest rank of nobility in the Empire of [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]]. The Thanes ran the day-to-day operations of the Empire. They numbered three only, each granted control of their own respective House: [[House Exodus]], [[House Cruoris]], and [[House Amaranth]]. The Thanes sat on the Sith Ruling Council, the [[Council of Four]].

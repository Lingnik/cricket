'''Thanos''' is a half Theelin who has taken leadership of Nar Shaddaa from the Hutts. He was one of the major antagonists in the [[Nar Shaddaa Revolution]]. It is currently not known whether he survived the assault on the Hutt palace at the end of the Battle for Nar Shaddaa 15 ABY.

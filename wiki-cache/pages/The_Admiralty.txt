= Caspian Democratic Union =

'''The Admiralty''' was ''the'' ministry directing the [[Caspian Democratic Union]]'s combined military affairs. It was created to oversee the [[Caspian Navy|Naval components]] of the various provincial worlds that comprised the commonwealth. In [[9 ABY]], following the tumultuous [[Caspian Annexation Campaign|Imperial War of Aggression]], the board was granted joint custody and direction over the [[Provincial Army of the Union]] so as to best coordinate and disperse assets.

At its core, the board was built around five [[Sar Admiral|Sar Admirals]], one each from the four primary constituents of the Union - [[Caspia]], [[Krittain]], [[Yariz]], and [[Rauther]] - with the fifth being from any of the four and serving as the Chair. Traditionally, these Sar Admirals retained the ''ability'' to field-deploy, but rarely did. Direct command of fleets and flotillas was usually delegated to [[Admiral|Admirals]] and [[Commodore|Commodores]] acting in their stead. Occasionally, a Sar Admiral would take command of one of the Union's primary fleets, but only for specific, or urgent, reasons.

Arrayed around the Sar Admirals were several Admirals, Generals, and Commodores, each heading up specific departments or divisions relevant to Naval operations. Such departments included Strategy & Tactics, Research & Development, Logistics, Asset Tracking, Human Resources & Training, etc. The Commanding [[General]] of the [[Union Marines|Marines]] would be present, as well as the Admiral in charge of the [[Naval Intelligence Office]].

A small minority of civilians would also be included on the board, acting as liaisons between the Admiralty and [[Caspian Crest|the Crest]]. One civilian would assuredly be from [[CDMC]]. In [[Wiltmer Gerrin|Gerrin]]'s, [[Lyr Dunwell|Dunwell's]], and [[Avalyshaar Dusten|Laarken's]] presidencies, the Admiralty would coordinate and communicate with the [[Presav]]'s office via vid-link (holo-link at the close of Dunwell's presidency). During [[Salonika Batrad|Batrad's]] term, the former Sar Admiral would himself sit in on the proceedings.

=== Composition ===
* The [[Presav]] as Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces.
* Chair of the Admiralty - The civilian head of the Navy. Handles coordination of naval policy has directed by the Presav and his cabinet.
* Sar Admiral - The senior active-duty officer of the Navy, responsible for overall strategic direction, force structure management and deployment. 
* Commandant of the [[Union Marines|Marines]] - The senior active duty Marine officer, responsible for overall strategic planning, force structure and deployment of the Marines. Advises the bureaus on issues pertaining to the Marines.
* The heads of the various bureaus of the Navy (each senior Admirals), including:
**Bureau of Planning (BuPlan) - responsible for operational and tactical planning. Includes the [[Naval Intelligence Office]].
**Bureau of Ships (BuShips) - responsible for procurement, maintenance, and refitting of warships and other hardware in accordance with the Chair.
**Bureau of Technology (BuTech) - responsible for research and development of new systems.
**Bureau of Personnel (BuPers) - responsible for recruitment and assignment of naval personnel.
**Bureau of Training (BuTrain) - responsible for training and education of naval personnel.
**Bureau of Medicine (BuMed) - responsible  for health and medical treatment of all personnel, and administers Naval Hospitals.

= Caspian Union =

Following the [[Crestian Tarasar|Tarasar]] administration coming to power in the new [[Union Council]], the administration of the Union's defence underwent rather radical rethinking. Feeling the Union remained under grave threat from both foreign and domestic elements. This led to a more elaborate and powerful leadership apparatus and a more militarised political culture. Several members of the Union Council hold military commissions concurrent with their other roles. Furthermore, the corporations behind defence related production are fully integrated into military chain of command with their senior officials holding military commissions as well.

== Defence Directorate ==

In the new Caspian Union oversight over the [[Caspian Defence Force]] falls to two intertwined bodies, the Defence Directorate and the Admiralty. General and broader oversight is accomplished by the Defence Directorate, a twelve member committee of the Union Council, comprised of Councillors serving as ministers of departments relevant to Union Defence. It is co-chaired by the Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defence and the Minister of Domestic Affairs and Defence. These positions are currently held [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] and Vice Presav [[Cas Mos]] respectively. Day to day leadership of the Defence Force is done by the relevant ministers and The Admiralty. Meetings of the full Defence Directorate occur at least monthly so the members may provide additional oversight and be briefed on current status and operations.

== The Admiralty ==

While the Defence Directorate provides the broader oversight in the new Caspian Union, the Admiralty still serves as the body providing direct leadership to the Caspian Defence Force. Conceptually, however, it has changed in numerous ways. Rather than having five [[Sar Admiral|Sar Admirals]] as was the case before, it is now a single occupant office. The office of Sar Admiral serves as the role of Commander-in-Chief of the Caspian Defence Force rather than the Office of the Presav. The Sar Admiral also serves as Chair of the Admiralty rather than a civilian overseer, with the functions of civilian oversight occurring at the level of the Defence Directorate.

There are important caveats to this change. While the Office of the Presav is not the Commander-in-Chief role, the current Presav [[Crestian Tarasar]] is concurrently the [[Sar Admiral]]. There are other members of the Defence Directorate as well who concurrently serve as part of the Admiralty. And while the position of Sar Admiral has become a single occupant office, one surviving holder of the position, Sar Admiral [[Odie Hood]] retains the rank though not the position and serves on the Admiralty Board in his capacity as a Task Group commander.

In places of additional Sar Admirals, the [[Caspian Navy]] introduced the rank of [[Fleet Admiral]], with Fleet Admirals actively leading the Unions three defensive fleets. Defence Groups and Patrol Groups within those fleets are led by [[Admiral|Admirals]] and [[Vice Admiral|Vice Admirals]], who along with the Fleet Admirals are part of the Admiralty Board. Like the previous incarnation of the Admiralty, the Bureau Chiefs of the various particular departments of the Defence Force also sit on the Admiralty Board.

The [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]] and the [[Union Marines]] are also represented within the Admiralty. The Starfighter Corps is principally represented on the Admiralty Board by the [[Marshal|Star Marshal]] who serves as Commandant of the Corps and the [[Vice Marshal|Vice Marshals]] attached to each fleet. The Starfighter Corps has two additional Star Marshals, [[Imperium]] [[Grand Admiral]] [[Axel Vichten]] and [[Imperium]] [[Grand Moff]]. Both are [[Caspar|Caspians]] and veterans of the [[Caspian Defence Force]] and in the case of Grand Moff Tracer, a former Presav. While their Caspian ranks could be classified as honourary, the have the right to attend Admiralty Board meetings and have access to the intelligence that comes with their rank.

The Union Marines are principally represented the [[Marshal|Field Marshal]] who serves as Commandant of the Corps, Vice Presav [[Cas Mos]] and the [[General|Generals]] commanding ground forces on each of the various Union member worlds. In the event of a largest scale expeditionary deployment it is likely their commanding officer would be promoted to Field Marshal and be added to the membership of the Admiralty Board if they were not already a member.

The Admiralty Board also includes the Chief Executive Officers of the various design and manufacturing firms in the defence industry with the Union including: [[Aston Aerospace|Aston Aerospace Company]], [[CDMC|Caspian Manfuacturing and Design Corporand]] [[Haor Chall Engineering]], Hixam Sidney Design, [[Interspatial Prospecting, Inc.|Interspatial Prospecting Incorporated]] [[Lokkai Starworks]], [[StarShield|Starshield Armaments]], and [[SubPro|Sublight Products Corporation]]. These CEOs and many of their other executives hold commissions in the Caspian Defence Force.

Meetings of the full of the full the Admiralty Board typically occur monthly, however much of the attendance is done remotely as the officers involved are typically spread out within [[Caspian Sector|Union Space]] or even beyond it. It is also not uncommon for a member of the board to send a more junior flag officer, or even senior field officer as their proxy to such meetings. Subcommittees of the board may meet more or less frequently.

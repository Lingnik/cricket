In the waning months of 18 ABY, Tokoga and his companions, including [[Myiari Oerstead]] became intimately aware of the existing of a sprawling and rapidly centralizing organized crime syndicate on Ord Mantell. The Mon Calamari Senator sought to fight the criminal syndicates by introduction legislation targeted at the crime bosses and their many-tentacled empires only to find out just how far they were willing to go to keep the status quo.

'''The Chevalier''' is an unit of the [[Union Marines]] that traditionally served as a security force for the Princes of [[Ixin]]. It currently provides security to the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] [[Presav]], his family, and a number of Caspian Ministers.

== History ==

The title of [[Prince]] on [[Ixin]] is in inherited by all sons of a Prince. This eventually had an effect on Ix culture of an increasing percentage of the male population being considered royalty and an decreasing percentage being available as retainers. This saw Princes turn to off-world mercenaries and slaves to fill their retinues. '''The Chevalier''' emerged as the largest and most famous mercenary companies on Ixin having been employed for centuries as the private security force to a number of Princes and contributing to racial diversity of Ixin.

When Ixin joined the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] in [[21 ABY]], The Chevalier officially became part of the [[Provincial Armies of the Union]]. In practice their role changed very little as they continued to protect various leading Princes, which was essentially what passed for planetary defence on Ixin. While the [[Union Marines]] turned to small mercenary companies and new recruits from Ixin to incorporate into their operations, the [[Naval Intelligence Office]] found The Chevalier's mercenary character useful to various off-book operations.

When the CDU collapsed following the Sariphage pandemic and Mergani pogroms in [[28 ABY]] The Chevalier once again returned to prominence as the main mercenary protection force on Ixin. A number of surviving members of the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]] who had been associated with the NIO operations on Ixin would use The Chevalier as a place to hideout while observing the developing situation back in the [[Caspia|Caspian System]].

When Ixin joined the new [[Caspian Union]] in [[29 ABY]], The Chevalier became a part of the Union Marines, as the concept of Provincial Armies was faced at as an organisationally distince entity. Nevertheless, the function of The Chevalier mainly remained the same, assigned to local defence and government security.

== New Role ==

Following the [[Report:Bespin Recap|attack]] on the 1st Annual Miners Dinner in [[Cloud City]] on [[Bespin]], [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] felt the need of reorgranising the protection details for himself, his family, and other members of the Crest. To that end he tapped The Chevalier to serve as that new security force, effectively keeping their function the same but expanding their client list. To better facilitate its new role, the Presav arranged for his cousin [[General]] [[Siege|Janus Tarasar]] to become the unit's commanding officer, who on paper had been a marine who stayed on Ixin following the CDU's collapse. Crestian's own adjutant and bodyguard [[Colonel]] [[V1C3]] became its executive officer.

[[Image:The Mission Headquarters.jpg|thumb|300px|The Mission's Main Headquarters in Trinumvira]] '''The Mission''' is a charitable organisation founded by [[Crestian Tarasar]] in [[28 ABY]] on his return to [[Caspar]]. The stated aim of the organisation at its foundation was to direct aid to the survivors of the [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|Sariphage]] on Caspar and the refugees of the [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]] on [[Mergansar]]. Though the Mission has been sincere in these aims it also operates as a vehicle to improve the reputation of Tarasar himself and public relations venture for the Sith. Since Tarasar's assumption of the [[Presav|Presavadency]] on Caspar the Mission has begun to expand operations outside of [[Caspian Sector|Caspian Space]] to support causes on [[Imperium]] and [[Independent]] worlds as well. The majority of its operations remain with in the growing borders of Caspian space.

== Operations ==
The main headquarters of the Mission is an office building in the Spillway District of [[Trinumvira]] on the Marincara continent of Caspar. A secondary headquarters is located in the Gerrin Complex in [[Anchorron]] on the Stronciro continent. Smaller officers are located in the cites of [[Dunwelton]], [[Groton]], [[Noques Rasala]], [[Orleanon]], and [[Plaxton]] on Caspar. At its initial foundation the Mission employed 150 full-time staff members, which ballooned up to 1,500 in the run up to the Caspian election in [[29 ABY]]. It now employs roughly 3,000 full-time staff members. This staff includes, fundraisers, medics, office workers, pilots, relief workers, and technicians. According to financial documents made public by Wookieeleaks, the staff is paid out of pocket by Crestian Tarasar, though, it should be noted that many of the staff were already in his employ prior to the formation of the Mission.

== Efforts ==
As an organisation the Mission engages in both direct relief efforts and in providing grants to other organisations for their aid efforts. Certain cities have benefited from more per capita aid from the Mission than others. Among Caspian cities, Anchorron has received the most aid per capita where has Dunwelton has received the least. It should be noted that prior to the Sariphage, Anchorron was the most impoverished city on Caspar and Dunwelton the most modern. Notable efforts have included:

* Construction of [[Twin Suns Hospital]] in the Hood's Escarpment District of Trinumvira
* Creation of the Caspian Agricultural Fund
* Emergency Relief following the terrorist attack on Cloud City
* Grants to the Benevolence Fund of the Wookiee Workers Union of Anchorron
* Refugee Repatriation on Mergansar
* Sponsorship of the Groton Veteran's Home

== Funding ==
For the first year of its operations the Mission was fairly vague about the source of most of their funding. According to documents released by Wookieeleaks roughly three quarters of the Mission's funding comes from figures connected to or part of Imperium leadership. The list of publicly identified donors includes:
* [[Tyler Damion|Damion I]], Head of State and Government of the [[Imperium]]
* [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]], Warlord of [[Tatooine]] and [[Kessel]], the Imperator's half-sister
* [[Zubindi Hakoon]], Proprietor and Head Designer of the [[Silverwood House of Fashion]]
* [[Drayson Honos]], Imperium Grand Admiral
* [[Sentinel]], [[Knights of Korriban|Knight of Korriban]], the Imperator's personal bodyguard
* [[Crestian Tarasar]], [[Presav]] and [[Sar Admiral]] of the [[Caspian Union]], [[Union Council|Councillor]] of [[Trinumvira]], [[New Sith Order|Sith]] [[Duke]]
* [[Elana Tracer]], Former Presav of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], [[Grand Moff]] of [[Selene]] and [[Athaniss]]

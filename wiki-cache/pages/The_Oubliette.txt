TITLE: The Oubliette
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2025-03-12T18:44:12Z
======================================================================
Spacestation 1138, in the [[List of Systems and Planets/Other|Karthakk System]], has a long and storied history as a high security prison. It was once controlled by the Trade Federation prior to the Clone Wars. Now sitting on the outskirts of Imperium space, it serves that power and its allies as a supermax prison with most of the inmates encased in carbonite.

Nicknamed 'The Oubliette' by members of the [[Moff's Council]] it is said that no complete manifest of the prison exists and that in fact no one not even the warden is aware of the identity of all of the prisoners housed in it. While the prison's existence is not unknown given its long history any additional detail is a matter considered classified by the Imperium and her allies. While it said that multiple allied powers have sent at least one of their own notaries there to be forgotten, no government has gone on the record to confirm this.

The station itself consists of a central shaft flanked by two twin pylons that rotate around the station. On the ends of each pylon are heavy capital ship turrets, with smaller anti-starfighter laser cannons located along the length of the defense pylons. A droid starfighter hangar bay is located towards the top of the station, though larger starships are accommodated with docking tubes on the outer pylons. On the flat surfaces of the pylons are also a pair of airlock doors, which connect to the inner corridors of the station.

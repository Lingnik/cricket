{{title|''The Ummagumma''}}
'''''The Ummagumma''''' was a [[Corellian Corvette]] owned by the Dark Lord [[Sabbathius Osbourne]]. In [[6 ABY]], Osbourne lured [[Luke Skywalker]] to the corvette after kidnapping [[Jessalyn Valios]]. Mortally wounded during his duel with Luke, Sabbath initiated ''The Ummagumma'''s self-destruct. The ship exploded minutes later, taking its dark owner with it.

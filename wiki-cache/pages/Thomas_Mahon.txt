'''Thomas Clement Mahon''' was the [[Presav]] of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], having been granted interim authority after powers were ceded by [[Elana Tracer]] in [[14 ABY]].

===Origins===

Mahon hails from the Union world of [[Krittain Minor]], but had an extensive history in service of the [[starwars: Galactic Republic's|Galactic Republic's]] [[starwars: Judicial Forces|Judicial Forces]] prior to the [[Galactic Civil War]]. He is a veteran of the [[starwars: Clone Wars|Clone Wars]], and numerous peacekeeping operations of the prewar years. His service to the Republic continued into the early post-war era, though he soon soured on the increasingly draconian nature of operations as Palpatine's New Order took hold. This led to his abrupt departure from service, and he subsequently disappeared for over a decade.

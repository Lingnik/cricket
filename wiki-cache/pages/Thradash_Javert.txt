The career of '''Thradash "Ash" Javert''' was a long and illustrious one. He maintained a well-known stint with the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]], serving in squadrons posted to the [[Orbital Station Guardian]] as well as several other [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[Imperator-class Star Destroyer|Star Destroyers]].

Aside from his impressive skills as a pilot, he also possessed an uncanny ability to create art. Several sculptures and paintings still adorn observation decks within the fleet. It was due to his uncontrollable passions that his downfall with the [[Imperial Navy]] occurred; falling in with one of his best friends, Captain [[Aron Melnick]], he ended up with a band of Imperial "vigilantes" known as the [[Vigilant Movement]], who sought to overthrow [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[Bacharan Valak|Valak]]. This was done in the name of [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]], who they believed to still be alive and the true Emperor. 

During this time, Javert was aboard [[Pride-1|OS Redemption]], where he spearheaded the design of a new TIE variant for use by the Vigilant Movement. The concept was approved and construction began at Redemption through the occupied [[Aeria Engineering Corporation|AEC]] shipyards. The goal was to develop a new line of starfighter to defend Redemption when the rebellion against Valak began.

Their rebellion, however, was short lived. Valak quickly subdued the situation. Javert was apprehended, and brought before [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]]. No one in the Empire really knows what happened to the man since; some suspect he was executed for his treason, others believe COMPNOR agents destroyed what was left of his mind. Rumors have spread for years that he was seen in [[Caspia|Caspian space]], or any host of smaller, independent entities, but the Empire maintains any reports of these sorts as classified.

The [[Thrashers]] were a swoop gang on [[Nar Shaddaa]], under the leadership of the reckless [[Dug]], [[Unb'tar]]. They were under the employ of [[Grubba the Hutt]], and were rivals to another of Grubba's gangs, the [[Slashers]].

They were wiped out in [[15 ABY]], by [[Tyler Damion|Tatooine Bob]]'s allies during a swoop chase at the [[Second Battle of Nar Shaddaa]].

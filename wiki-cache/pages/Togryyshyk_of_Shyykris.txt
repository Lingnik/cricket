'''Togryyshyk of Shyykris''', a [[Wookiee]] born and raised in the jungles of [[Kashyyyk]], was one of the three beings who established [[FineLine Spaceways]] alongside [[Ryan Cross]] and [[Khayell Djorimonn]]. After bringing his longtime friend [[Lyr Dunwell]] in on the establishment of FLS into the [[Caspia]]n business landscape, he would be instrumental in the growth of the shipping company into a galaxy-wide name. As recompense and recognition for his efforts, Dunwell would make Tog his [[Commissioner of Commerce]] and designate him his vice president when Lyr was elected to the office of [[Presav]].

[[5 ABY]] would be tragic, though, as Dunwell would be assassinated, and Tog forced to take the position of Presav. He restored order to the Union, but absconded the office to a new election that brought [[Avalyshaar Dusten]] to [[Caspian Crest|the Crest]].

'''Toprawa''' was a planet along the [[Hydian Way]], at the boundary between the [[The Galaxy#Mid Rim|Mid Rim]] and [[The Galaxy#Outer Rim|Outer Rim]].  The planet had a community of [[:starwars:Antarian Rangers/Legends|Antarian Rangers]]. 

It was on this planet that the [[:starwars:Death Star/Legends|Death Star's]] superlaser was developed, the plans for which were subsequently stolen by [[:starwars:Havet Storm|Havet Storm]] and the [[:starwars:Alliance to Restore the Republic/Legends|Rebel Alliance]]. Among many others Commander [[:starwars:Bria Tharen|Bria Tharen]] and rebel cell leader [[:starwars:Vermilion (Rebel Alliance)|Vermilion]] gave their lives to secure the plans for [[Leia Organa Solo|Leia Organa]]. 

The planet was then punished by the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]] - its cities were destroyed and its people reduced to barbarism, made to prostrate themselves to the Empire for food. Later, it would become an important "juncture world", and would change hands several times in the conflict between the resurgent Empire and the [[New Republic]].

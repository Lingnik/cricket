The '''Trap on Etti IV''' was a trap set for leaders of the [[Neo-Imperial Movement]] by [[Vassily Korolov]] and [[Lorn Rhys|Darth Malus]] on [[Etti IV]].

Having regained control of the Movement from [[Greiko Lornan]] after the [[Assault on Sigma Star Industries]], [[Danik Kreldin]] was convinced by the apprehended Lornan to travel to Etti IV to meet with [[Scaven Marx]]. 

Upon landing, the Neo-Imperials were met by [[Salin Fawzy]], [[Petra Doom]], and [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[Stormtroopers]], who quickly sprung their trap. Kreldin was brought to the brink of death, and [[Grymm Selcan]] was arrested. The others managed to escape, but [[Corporate Sector Authority]] officials brought both Kreldin and Selcan in for questioning. Korolov would ensure Kreldin would be turned over to Imperial custody, but during his recovery in a [[:starwars:Bacta/Legends|bacta]] tank, he was met by Marx, who used his pull within the Authority to see Kreldin released to his custody and protection, where he would hide Danik from the Empire until [[13 ABY]], when [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]] seized control from Malus.

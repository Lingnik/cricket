Third son of a middle-class [[Sarian#Janhovi|Janhovi]] family that lives on [[Mergansar]], '''Traskus Il'Bitain''' has been a member of the [[Caspian Rangers]] since his late teens.  By joining the Rangers just before the [[Imperial War of Aggression]], he was assigned to one of the units that performed constant hit-and-hide attacks on Imperial assets in and around [[Caspar]]'s capital city, [[Plaxton]].

He is now a [[lieutenant]] in the Rangers, and often assigned to work with [[Naval Intelligence Office|NIO]] operatives in the field.

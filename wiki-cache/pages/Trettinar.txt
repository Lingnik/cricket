'''Trettinar''' translates to ''Lake of Fire'' from [[Sarian]], and it holds no features or worth. Its mass was reduced to an ocean of pure auldahene by Solus' heat, and absolutely nothing can live on or in it. Twice during the Sarian year (or ''cyclos''), the planet is aligned so that the massive amounts of auldahene refract the passing solar radiation and give the planet a glowing look, as if there were two stars very close together.

The [[Mergansar-class Heavy Cruiser|Mergansar-Class Heavy Cruiser]] ''[[CMS Trettinar]]'' is named after this planet.

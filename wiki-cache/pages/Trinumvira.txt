'''Trinumvira''' is a mountaintop city of the [[Sarian]]s on [[Caspar]]. Set on the southern flank and highlands of the '''Vira Range''', within the '''Trinuma''' Province, it was the second city established by the Sarian pioneers upon arrival from [[Mergansar]] around 1120 BBY, and soon eclipsed the first city, Sarimassala, both in population and area covered. Since the arrival of off-world settlers of other races, Trinumvira has since been surpassed as a major urban center, but still remains the iconographic, cultural center of Sarian presence on Caspar.

== History ==

== Architecture and Layout ==
Aside from residential and agricultural structures which spill out amongst the plains approaching the Vira Range, the majority of the city proper is wide along its east-west axis. It is some twenty kilometers from Lantern Cove on the western coast of the Cappula Ocean to the Flude Cauldron at the east. The city is only two kilometers at its broadest point along its north-south axis, though, rising along with the terrain as it becomes the Vira Range. Because of its long, polarizing shape, Trinumvira has developed distinct districts, notably the West Cove, the East End, the Spillway, and [[Icaris Hood|Hood's]] Escarpment.

Despite the arrival of off-world settlers, the predominant architectural style is heavily Sarian, having an integrated, natural look. The casual observer would see a tower of only some sixty meters or so, never realizing that it is not only part of a greater nexus of structures, but that it also extends an equal number of meters into the sloped ground below.

== Transportation ==
The East End of Trinumvira hosts a large station servicing the main [[Aerotram]] line running north to south from Leighton (a town in the far north, beyond the Marin Mountains) through Plaxton and the Soyometin (southern pass) and then proceeding on across the Trinuma plains to the Gulf of Cennes and [[Orleanon]].

In addition, the towers and pylons of the East End overlook the [[Flude Cauldron|''Eirnacorom Bisala'']] (Flude Cauldron) which constitutes Union Starport, one of the major interstellar travel hubs for the [[Caspia]]n system. 

== In Cultural Reference ==
The city has given its name to a [[Shearwater-class Frigate|''Shearwater''-class frigate]], the CMS ''Trinumvira'' (CFG-49).

'''Tristan Hardy''' was a young and talented soldier in the [[Imperial Army]]. He was later transferred to the [[Stormtrooper Corps]]. Due to his excellent work, he has been promoted to the rank of Shadowguard - meant for special operations coming directly from the Emperor -, within the [[Bureau of Operations]].

Unfortunately, the Shadowguards have been involved in the [[Vigilant Movement]] (but not him), so they have been disbanded. He ended up being an Agent under the Chief Operations Officer [[Acthpa Neon Sanduleak]]'s orders. He was well known for being a brilliant person and extremely efficient.

[[Image:Twin Suns Hospital.jpg|thumb|300px]] '''Twin Suns Hospital''' is a state of the art research hospital located in the Hood's Escarpment district of [[Trinumvira]] on [[Caspar]]. The hospital was founded in [[28 ABY]] by [[The Mission]] which continues to own and operate it. The Mission's Executive Director [[Crestian Tarasar]] serves a chairman of the hospital's board of directors. It has the largest hospital based research programme in the [[Caspian Union]] and the Mid Rim as a whole.

== History ==

During the Sariphage pandemic that raged on [[Caspar]], [[Crestian Tarasar]] came to view the response by the native medical community to be the greatest liability Caspar faced. Not only had the [[Groton Sciences Institute]] not be able to address the virus effectively, but through its director Professor [[Daln Bael]], the [[Caspian Defence Force]] had been order to place the planet under interdiction until the sentient population had died out. The nihilist attitude was something Crestian considered both unbecoming of a Caspian institution but it also made him question the efficacy of the GSI as a whole.

Though a cure would eventually be developed by [[Basca Starker]], the fact that it required bribery from [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]] to even get Starker interested in the problem, further convinced Crestian that Caspar's medical community needed vast improvement lest its fate in future be left once again in the hands of nihilists and mercenaries. To this end, Crestian began his own medical studies. On a basic level he wanted to be able to provide inoculations now that a cure had developed, but he also wanted to know enough about the field to be able judge the qualifications of medical professionals he would hire in the future and to make sense of the research they would engage in.

Upon his return to his home city of [[Trinumvira]] after the dust had settled from his [[Mergansar Incident|fight]] with the [[Caspian Navy]] at [[Mergansar]], Crestian founded a charity called [[The Mission]] as his vehicle for supporting Caspar's recovery. Improving the medical community of Caspar would be one of The Mission's top priorities. As he distrusted GSI and felt Doctor Tarquin Memorial Hospital lacked the infrastructure to house research facilities on the scale he imagine, Crestian set out to create a new institution under his own direction that could serve as the foundation of new Caspian medical research.

The Mission acquired a large parcel of land at the base of Hood's Escarpment to build the new facility. The name '''Twin Suns Hospital''' was chosen as a quiet way to honour Johanna's involvement in securing a cure to the Sariphage. Her involvement was not yet public knowledge at the time, but referencing the twin suns of her homeworld of [[Tatooine]] was Crestian's secret way of naming the hospital for her anyway.

== Structure ==

The campus of the hospital is massive, it is the largest structure fully within the city limits of Trinumvira, though Trinumvira Base and [[Flude Cauldron|Union Starport]] are larger. It is also the largest hospital campus within the [[Caspian Union]] though there are other hospitals with a higher patient treatment capacity.

The visible structure of the campus consists of three main sections. Taking up an entire city block below the escarpment is a large three storey complex of sandstone and [[Transparisteel|transparisteel]], the structure itself continues into the cliff behind it. On the plateau above the escarpment are two fifteen storey towers of duracrete and [[Durasteel|durasteel]], each tower is topped with a landing pad.

Though these towers are not quite the tallest structures in the city, given their elevation they tower over the other structures of the city. What is not visible is the fact that these are all in fact a single continuous structure, much of which exists in the cliff and several storeys underground as well.

== Research ==

The failure of the native Caspian medical community to cope with the Sariphage pandemic in any meaningful way laid the foundation for Twin Suns Hospital and the research programmes here. The mission here is to ensure Caspar is able respond to future calamities on their own and be a source of medical expertise to the wider Caspian Union and galaxy at large.

Studying the Sariphage itself has been central to research here. Other deadly diseases such as the [[Yavin Plague]] that struck the galaxy two decades earlier, the [[:starwars:Blue Shadow Virus/Legends|Blue Shadow Virus]] behind the [[:starwars:Candorian plague|Candorian Plague]], and various strains of [[:starwars:Hive virus|hive viruses]] are part of this work. While everyone hopes and prays Caspar will be spared such misery in the future, the scientists remain vigilant.

The later revelation that the Sariphage had been purposely engineered has also led researchers to explore the efforts of [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR's]] [[:starwars:Pathogen-Based Loyalty Enhancement Program|Pathogen-Based Loyalty Enhancement Programme]] and even the [[:starwars:Rakghoul plague|Rakghoul Plague]] from the [[:starwars:Golden Age of the Sith|Golden Age of the Sith]]. Future biological attacks against Caspar are considered a very real possibility here.

Research is also undertaken it other maladies such as the [[:starwars:Ardroxian Flu|Ardroxian Flu]], various forms of [[:starwars:Cancer|Cancer]], and traumatic injury care. Its close proximity to Trinumvira Base in particular has led to a research partnership in a variety of areas related to the treatment of military personnel.

Twins Suns Hospital is also a major source of pharmaceutical research, particularly into alternatives to [[:starwars:Bacta/Legends|Bacta]] such as [[:starwars:Kolto/Legends|Kolto]], with research being conducted in hopes of rediscovering the ancient Kolto-Bacta hybrid [[:starwars:Kolcta|Kolcta]].

The medicinal use of various spices such as [[:starwars:Glitterstim/Legends|Glitterstim]] and [[:starwars:Ryll/Legends|Ryll]] as well as the drug [[:starwars:Slick (drug)|Slick]] is studied here as well. Research into therapeutic stimulants such [[:starwars:Cordrazine|Cordrazine]] is being undertaken as well.

== Operations ==

While research serves as the major focus for the hospital, it has the trappings one might expect to find in any major hospital. Notable features include three separate accident and emergency wards, a department of cybernetic medicine, and hundreds of both kolto and bacta tanks. Given the close proximity to Trinunmvira Base there are various physical and mental health resources available to military personnel, both in-patient and out-patient.

As the hospital has gained stability following a few successful years of operations some of its departments have emerged as particularly prominent. As most citizens of Caspar are either survived the trauma of 90% of the planet's population dying in the Sariphage pandemic or are a refugee of the Mergani pogroms, mental health has been a particular concern of Caspian medical professionals, with the psychiatric department at Twin Suns growing to match the need.

The pulmonology department is rated as the best in the Union. The medical research conducted at Twin Suns has also led it to be considered the go to destination in the Union for cardiology, palliative care, and more recently for oncology as well. Naturally the focus on infectious diseases means the hospital is well prepared for handling unexpected cases and has well established protocols for running large quarantined wards as well as deploying such resources into the field.

Twin Suns Hospital is the main medical facility operated by The Mission and as such the personnel it deploys in emergency medical situations in the galaxy are often associated with the hospital.

== Funding ==

As Twin Suns is owned and operated by The Mission, a significant portion of its funding comes from The Mission itself, which is in turn funded by a who's who of prominent [[Caspian Union|Caspian]], [[Imperium]], and [[Independent]] citizens. Additional funding comes from various Caspian governmental entities including the Caspian Health Ministry, the Caspian Defence Force, and the City of Trinumvira. Twin Suns has managed to leverage its size and funding into a variety of partnerships with off-world hospitals, research facilities, and charitable foundations. In total the research budget alone amounts to 63 million credits annually.

== Administration ==

Twin Suns Hospital is overseen by a thirteen member Board of Directors, chaired by Crestian Tarasar in his capacity of as Executive Director of The Mission. The Board of Directors plays a broad role in setting priorities for the hospital, with Crestian taking a particular interest in establishing the research agenda. He is an accredited member of the medical staff of the hospital as medical researcher but rarely spends time in the laboratories given his more pressing roles as [[Presav]] and [[Sar Admiral]]. Day to day operations of hospital are under the direct control of its full-time medical staff under the leadership of the Chief of Medicine, Doctor Moxie Codone.

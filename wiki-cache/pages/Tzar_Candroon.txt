== What Homeworld? ==
Tzar was born on a starship, in the reaches of the galaxy known as Wild Space, his mother was little more than a slave to the crew of a pirate vessel. The Remorseless Taunt, it was never clear who his father was and nobody really cared. But he grew into an able-bodied crewman, serving the Taunt faithfully until the death of his mother in his early twenties. To honor his mother, he took her name as a last name and thus was born Tzar Candroon. The captain and crew of the Remorseless Taunt were mediocre at best, taking low paying jobs with other more well-established pirate groups. And on occasion raiding ill defended settlements in wild space. But Tzar wanted more, never really forgetting that his mother was a toy for the crew and his childhood was more or less indentured servitude to the ship. But regardless of those times, he became a decent pilot and made a few friends along the way. Chief among them, was a Yuzzem sized male human named Vike, the giant had always been kind to the young Tzar. But he recognized in the young man a desire to do more and became his quiet ally among the crew, often backing the young pirate up in squabbles with other crewmates or rival pirates.

== '''Once too often bold''' ==
The Remorseless Taunt and her captain grew more and more bold, bolstered by successes with other pirate groups and returned to the trade of slaving. An ill-advised venture at best, the captain was not the best at reading the room as the phase goes and didn't recognize the rising trend in galactic governments to douse and eradicate the slave trade. Thus, was it during a raid on a frontier world in the outer rim, Vike and Tzar were assigned to steal a freighter during the raid. It was a brutal affair and the Captain of the Taunt, encountered stronger resistance then he anticipated. Rather than a successful slave raid, it turned into a pitched battle and quickly turned into a massacre of the settlers who refused to surrender. But not before a call for assistance was sent out into space, eventually the raid concluded, the scattered bodies of the slaughtered settlers were everywhere. What few survived where herded aboard dilapidated shuttles and sent to the Remorseless Taunt, but in space above the unplanned and unpleasant arrival of a large Imperial taskforce made the collecting of ill-gotten goods a nightmare of steel and energy.  The destruction of the Remorseless Taunt was quick and brutal, the hail to surrender typically given in these circumstances was never offered as the encounter was a no quarter given. The freighter Tzar and Vike were aboard rise into this hellish sea of ship fire and there was no real time to figure out what was going on, other than the presence of an Imperial class taskforce destroying their home of many years. Vike glanced at his friend and Tzar shrugged and did the only thing they could do, throw in quick coordinates and leap to hyperspace before the ship could be identified.

== '''Jumping blind into a Weird World''' ==
The freighter Tzar and Vike stole was a YT 2400, named the Epoch Mynock.  A solid vessel that saw them safely into a reckless hyperspace jump but brought them far enough out that the navcomp would need time to sort out their location.  The nearest system was undesignated.  Tzar set course for the system and soon came upon single livable world. It rotated in the blackness of space, a misty world of purple and red hues. It is on this world that Tzar met and recruited a female alien named Gith.

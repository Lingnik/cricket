'''Umbriel of Vequess''' was an influential figure in the [[Galactic Empire]] during the reign of [[Bacharan Valak]]. She was one of the Vice Directors of the [[Bureau of Operations]] under [[Hul Loris Pantek]]'s orders.

== Biography ==
Umbriel was a very stern person. She couldn't stand low and weak people, surely because she was not aware completely of her intelligence. Usually, her gaze was chilling, even with the ones that almost could afford them to call them her friends. And her attitude was distant, cold. She didn't even have emotions, except when she was concerned. Obviously, this woman did never fall in love, it was impossible.

She decided to go to the Imperial Academy when she has been fed up with her bounty hunter life. She then joined the Empire with her mate [[Acthpa Neon Sanduleak]]. She has always served the Imperial aim and involving herself in the Academy would open her up new horizons in this only direction. Umbriel is that kind of perfect student that will serve the Empire forever.

== '''The Golden Years''' ==
The underworlds fortunes have risen and fallen over the ages, but it is safe to suggest that the golden era of the underworld was before the rise of the Galactic Empire. The [[Hutts]] were soundly entrenched as the foundation of all criminal enterprises as [https://sw1mush.fandom.com/wiki/Hutt_Space Hutt space] was a free for all region of trade for those plying illicit wares and goods in the galaxy. Such Syndicates as the Crimson Dawn and The Pikes were powerful in their own right, in the case of Crimson Dawn its power extended to many sectors of space and Where as the Pikes limited the manifestation of their power to one world.[[File:Hutt.jpg|thumb|300px|Hutts]]
All that would change with the coming of the Empire as more control was exerted over trade routes and spaceports in general, but it became a boon to the smugglers who were in great demand to move goods from seller to buyer.

== '''Era of Corruption''' ==
The fall of the Galactic Empire to the Rebellion and the subsequent splintering of systems into smaller powers, presented a unique atmosphere to the scoundrels of the galaxy. Fear or perhaps tired of war and conflict many worlds and systems circled around their hearths to gather and breath and enjoy their sudden freedom, but the underworld is a creature of patience and opportunity. New cartels rose from the remains of the old and clad themselves in the guises of governments and businesses. Every major power in the galaxy had a pet or perhaps parasitic cartel nestled somewhere close to the central governments. from the Corporate Sector Authority to The Imperial Remnant and even the New Republic. It was the power of intelligence, blackmail and fearmongering that allowed the underworld to maintain its lifeblood of trade across the galaxy and was perhaps the most dangerous of times for those who walked that path. As competition was plentiful and the corrupt elements of legitimate powers were just as willing to cut a throat as needed.
=='''A Social Nouveau'''==
Why civilizations suddenly change directions is a matter debated by scholars during every decade, the one that marks the current times. A galaxy wide movement against Sentient Trafficking, which naturally made life for slavers very hard. From the [https://sw1mush.fandom.com/wiki/New_Republic New Republic] and a newly minted Imperium replacing the old Galactic Empire, the galaxy achieved a semblance of peace. Hutt space being most widely despised as a center for such things has been monitored by patroling fleets tasked with intercepting and inspecting any vessels coming from Hutt space. As a side effect of these interdictions, smugglers and black marketeers have had to up their game or avoid major travel routes in and out of Hutt space. Criminal enterprises in some ways have become harder, your far more likely to get boarded.

On the other hand, acts of piracy have seen an upsurge in frequency because of the reallocation of resources to combat trafficking. This Nouveau Peace is a danger to the denizens of the underworld and many have begun to notice that danger.

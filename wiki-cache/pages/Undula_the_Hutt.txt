'''Undula Desilijic Talinii''' was a [[Hutt]] crimelord who ran a syndicate out of [[Smuggler's Run]]. He affiliated himself with the [[New Sith Order]] of [[Tyler Damion|Darth Malign]], but soon betrayed the Dark Lord. This action led to his [[Battle of Smuggler's Run (Sith Campaign)|demise]] at the hands of the [[Sith]].

May the winds that guide us and speak to us always be true, and the fires within our souls never be extinguished.|[[Riche Beilayrn|Riche T Beilayrn]], first [[Provost]] of the Assembly, at the invocation of Caspia's first Assembly, [[4 ABY]].}}

Founded to provide a quorum of legislation for the fledgling [[Caspian Democratic Union|Union]], the '''Assembly''' was similar to the [[:starwars:Galactic Senate|Galactic Senate]]. It was modeled more so after the [[:starwars:Chandrilan House|Chandrilan House]] in terms of discourse and proceedings, with several modifications known as ''weights'' woven in. Instituted in [[4 ABY]], it was the result of a series of dialogues between '''Genosha Enebi''', of the Janhovi, and [[Orton Trissard]], one of [[Dergan Roj|Dergan Roj's]] officers and a well-cultured surgeon.

The ''weights'' are unique to Caspia, with representation coming from all the regions and cities under Caspia's sovereignty. Each city provided a trio of Delegates, and each region a [[Senator of the Union|Senator]]. Each Senator would occupy a chair on a Cabinet-like committee, led by the [[Presav]].

When on travels, dignitaries would be accompanied by PRISM agents. Indeed, the majority of the PRISM ranks are assigned to assets of the Assembly in one way or the other.

The Assembly convenes within the '''Hall of Hours''' in [[Plaxton|Plaxton's]] eastern government district.

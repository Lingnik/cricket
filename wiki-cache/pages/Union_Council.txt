TITLE: Union Council
NAMESPACE: 0 (Main)
LAST_EDIT: 2025-11-17T10:14:11Z
======================================================================
Established by the Caspian Constitution (29 ABY), it is the unicameral executive-legislative branch of the [[Caspian Union]]. It is the successor of the Provisional Assembly, itself a successor of the [[Union Assembly]]. The constitutionally established nine single-seat constituencies centred around the seven major cities of [[Caspar]] along with [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] and [[Mikassa]]. The subsequent return of [[Ixin]], [[Kasputin Yar]], [[Krittain Major]], [[Krittain Minor]], [[Mergansar]], and [[Rauther]] and to the Caspian fold and the admittance of [[Charros IV]] to the Union has seen it expand to thirty-nine seats.

The Council elects one of their number to serve as [[Presav]], the head of state and government of the Caspian Union, and a Vice Presav. The rest of the Councillors are appointed to serve as cabinet ministers. The current Presav is [[Crestian Tarasar]], Councillor of [[Trinumvira]], leader of [[Praecet]], the current Vice Presav is [[Cas Mos]], Councillor of [[Groton]], of the [[Legacy]] Party.

'''Union Hall''' is the capitol building of the [[Caspian Union]]. Located in Government Plaza in [[Plaxton]] on [[Caspar]], it contain the Chambers of the [[Union Council]] and the main office of the [[Presav]]. Various government ministries such as the [[Caspian Foreign Ministry]] maintain office space there, and it boasts various other amenities such as an executive reception room and a library. The Council Chambers themselves also function as a large auditorium which is often use for non-governmental functions.

Originally known as '''People's Hall''' it performed the same functions during the existence of the [[Caspian Democratic Union|CDU]]. It was rebranded by the [[Crestian Tarasar|Tarasar]] Administration after it took office and the Council Chambers were renovated at the time. Little else has been changed about it though compared to its days as capitol of the old Union.

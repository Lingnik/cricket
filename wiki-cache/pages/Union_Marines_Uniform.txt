[[Union Marines]] employ several configurations of uniform, depending on the situational requirements.

==== Dress (Formal) ==== 
Worn for formal occasions, functions, reviews, or ceremonial duties. For both officers and enlistees, the uniform consists of:
*Heavy Tunic &ndash; flint-black, with scarlet piping and nickel hardware, standing collar
*Trousers &ndash; olive green, with scarlet "bloodstripes" on outer seam
*Dress shoes &ndash; black, high-gloss polish
*White gloves
*White Pistol Belt
*Beret &ndash; olive green, worn sloping to right. Marine emblem center front.
*Medals worn upon left breast. Major awards may be worn upon a ribbon around neck.
Honor Guard / Rifle Drill Team adds:
*Gun Slings and Holsters &ndash; white leather
*Sword
Pilots add:
*Aviator wings

==== Duty ====
Worn for general duties that are "clean". As such, wearers are often derided as "desk-jockeys". Consists of:
*Ground Jacket &ndash; slate green, longer cut than Naval duty jacket, standing collar, self-bonding closures on right torso.
*Trousers &ndash; slate green, bloused
*Boots &ndash; black
*Beret &ndash; slate green




'''Duty (Common)''' &mdash; Worn for general duties that are 'clean'. As such they are often derided as 'desk-jockey' gear. Consists of:
*Slate green jacket, standing collar, with self-bonding closures on right torso.
*Slate green trousers, bloused 
*Black boots
*Slate green beret
*Medals worn in ribbon-form on left breast


'''Utility''' &mdash; Worn for dirty duties, and non-combat duties in the field. These can take several forms:
*Disruptive pattern fatigues with combat boots
*Slate green one-piece 'spacer utilities' for shipboard use. Fire-resistant, non-conductive, with abundant pockets
*Vacc suits of various configurations
*Protective work gear for technicians

'''Combat Operations''' &mdash; 
*Combat environment suit for vehicle and aerospace crews, light ballistic/energy protection, self-sealing, vacuum-capable, available in appropriate disruptive pattern (usually general green), with knife, sidearm and/or carbine.
*C97 Warrior smartsuit for infantry.

==== Medals, Tabs, Flashes, & Embellishments ====
Unlike their [[Provincial Army of the Union|Provincial Army counterparts]], the Marines do not wear uniforms of their respective constituencies. Marine units are not formed based upon region of origin, and any given units will be likely to have Marines drawn from various member worlds and colonies, or even volunteers from outside the Union.

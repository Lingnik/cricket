Admcanisdcsd (real name Russ P.) joined the MUSH in August of 1994, joining the Imperial Navy as a lieutenant with the character name '''Russel A'lexi Canis'''. He rose through the ranks quickly, and by 1996 was promoted to the rank of Admiral, ultimately achieving the rank of Grand Admiral. 

Functioned as a minor coder and illustrator for newly created ships and rooms within the Empire, and as an RPAmin. Retired from the MUSH and participated in the final campaign that ended the life of his character in 1997.

=== Regarding [[Russel Canis]] ===
The original concept for Canis was based on Sean Connery's role as Senior Captain Marko Remius in "The Hunt for Red October." The time compression between starting as a lieutenant in the navy and being promoted to Admiral required a rework of the character history.

In 1996-1997, Russ was allowed to audition for the Feature Character role of Grand Admiral Thrawn. His comrades at the time included Valak, Caldny, and Adlerson.

The player behind Johmac Mabru is known as '''Johnathan McKiney''' in the real world.  He is a Mississippi native who is an avid Science Fiction fan, as well as a fan of Japanese Animation.  He is a recent graduate of his local high school, and intends to begin attending '''Northwest Mississippi Community College''' in the fall.  When not RPing, he is an avid Euphoniumist, and will be participating in the Northwest Mississippi Community College '''Marching Rangers Band''' during the fall, then moving to the Symphonic ensemble.  He aspires to earn his Bachelor of Music Education degree, so that he may help spread the same kind of joy music and the fine arts have brought him during his life.  

Johnathan has been RPing for over seven years, and at least two to three of those have been spent on SW1 as different characters.

His love for Star Wars can be attributed to the original Trilogy, before the re-mastering.  His grandmother introduced him to the movies when he was seven, and ever since, he has enjoyed them to the fullest.  The literature spawned by Karen Traviss, among others, have also helped him along in his love and inspiration to create characters within the ''Star Wars'' universe.

==Policy==
I enjoy RP very much so, if you can't tell.  Please don't be afraid to contact me if you want a scene.  Imperial, Rebel, Indy, CSA... it's all good.  I encourage logging, and I intend to start my own logging collection.  There's a couple of things, though, that irk me, and I'll outline those here.

*'''Angst.''' Dark RP is sometimes essential in the ''Star Wars'' universe.  However, there's a difference between IC angst and OOC angst.  If these lines blurr for you, it's best to avoid me.  If you do, but have problems with angst?  I'm a good listener.  If you wanna RP out a dark mood?  I'm all for that.

*'''ICA=ICC.'''  My character is a pilot.  Pilots die in battle.  Essentially, I waive my flag to death if it is the '''only plausable direction''' the character can go in.  My character is '''not''' stupid; the fighters he flies are equipped with ejection systems and he'll use them.  I'll return the courtesy of giving as many options as I can to an opponent, if it's at all possible.  While I think the space code is handy, I'm not going to let it kill my character if I can help it.

*'''OOCA=OOCC.'''  There's a difference between these and ICA=ICC.  I'm a very likeable person if you take the time to get to know me.  I encourage people to talk to me and to get to know me.  What I do not encourage, however, is insults, racial slurs, bigotry, et al.  If you begin using these around me, and continue to do so if I say something to you, the chances of me speaking to you again are slim.  I don't appreciate these, and generally, neither does anyone else.   Cheers.

*'''IC/OOC Mixture.'''  IC information stays IC.  OOC info stays OOC.  If you think a character should know something about my character that's rather intimate or hard-to-find information (which, really, there's not any kind of information that you probably couldn't find on him), please, please, ''please'' contact me.  We'll make the arrangements for RP if we need to.  If you're curious about something, just ask, and specify if you're trying to get public IC info or not.  Don't leave me in the dark on things.  Same courtesy will be applied graciously to parties on the other end.

==About me==

Howdy! I'm the player of Myiari, in case that's not inherently obvious. While I'm new to MUSHes, I'm not new to roleplaying having previously roleplayed extensively on a MUD. I'll be mainly supplying RP logs, and updating and adding info as time goes on. I may decide to put up a newbie roleplaying guide at some point.

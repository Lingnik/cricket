Krieg joined the MUSH on December 14, 2005.  Currently, Krieg is the head of Task Force Inquisitor, and sits on the Imperial Players Council (IPC). He is also the commander for all starfighter operations within the force. Any questions or plot ideas can either be posted to the discussion page here or be @mailed in game.  If interested in joining the StarFighter Corps, Imperial Military, or anywhere in the Empire feel free to @mail Krieg or other Imperial Admin in game, [[Prospero]] - [[Jal'Dana Rall|Jal'Dana]] - [[Orpheus]].

I'm working on various pages, mostly that dealing with [[Task Force Inquisitor]] and the starfighter corps.  There also are a few pages I'm working on that deal with battles and upcoming plots. Please leave me messages if I need to work on something, or if something needs to be changed!  

Tie winner with [[Ikihsa Enb'Zik|Enb'Zik]] for the [[MUSHy Awards|MUSHy]] "Best Supporting Actor - 2007"

== Game OOC Outlook ==
By no means is this a 'final' listing of my thoughts and ideas for in game concepts, but it is a look at what I have planned and what I seek to plan.  For the expectations, these are OOC only and only apply to those characters that serve beneath [[Krieg Inrokana]] in game.

=== 16 ABY Goals & Plots ===
* Focus on individual character building
* RP for the Task Force as a whole, all elements
* Make Task Force commander's role political as well as military
* Engage NR and Crime throughout the year on varying levels of engagement
* Design and implement a major 'detective' SW style plot for the entire MUSH, with a possible MUSH changing event hanging in the balance

=== Long Term Goals & Plots ===
* Have a self-sustaining Imperial Task Force with a competent and active corps of cadre
* Build up varying levels of commanders through the ranks capable of handling larger sections of the force
* Enable and support commanders to pursue their own story arcs
* Have all Imperial players work with their opposing counterparts to form and run plots without heavy assistance
* Work to get all players involved in Imperial RP as it should reach out and 'touch' every PC player in game
* Get a coded and working space combat system for all to use

=== Expectations ===
* For myself it is a personal goal to bring at least 1 major planned scene to the playerbase
* I also challenge myself to different types of RP, to include 'pickup' RP at least a few times a month
* For my commanders and those that serve I expect them to be holding scenes, a minimum of one per month - short summaries afterward work best

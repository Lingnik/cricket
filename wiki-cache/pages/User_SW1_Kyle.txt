'''SW1 Kyle''' began playing '''Star Wars MUSH''' as [[Skeezix]], some time around 1992 or 1993. He has played on a number of other Star Wars games throughout his life, which included four administrative positions, two faction head positions, and three feature-character positions (Luke twice, C-3PO once).

He returned to Star Wars MUSH in the spring of 2005 with a new character concept: [[Kyle Senesca]]. He now has resigned Kyle_Senesca in order to fill the Feature Character role of [[Luke Skywalker]], while retaining his alt in the Empire, [[Korynn Fleming]].

He enjoys long walks on the beach, lightsabering fools, the occasional Imperial torture session, and the Spirit Of Truth guy.

== SW1 OOC accomplishments ==

* Constructed a NRGO military base on Kashyyyk (@digging and @descing).
* Helped to construct key wiki pages for the [[New Republic]].
* Primary @describe'r of the new planet [[Ord Mantell]]. Also concepted and installed the Skyline Transit, using modified turbolift code, created a rotating weather system, and created secret exits and a broken "cheapside railway" device that required a Mechanical +check to operate.
* Primary @digger and concepter for the new [[New Jedi Temple]] on Ord Mantell. (@desc work helped primarily from [[User: Kyrin|Kyrin]] and [[User: Enb'Zik|Enb'Zik]].
* Winner of two MUSHY Awards in 2007: Best Actor (Korynn) and Best Writer (Luke).
* Creator of the New Republic faction global systems 'NRINFO' and '+LHELP'.

Snarl is played by Kat.  

Kat was born in Columbus, Indiana, but spent most of her early youth in the outskirts of St. Louis, Missouri.  Born into a family of musicians, Kat is the 'oddball' because although she sings, her talents lie more toward art and literature.  One of her biggest loves in life is the outdoors.  There is little she loves more than traveling to wilderness areas and spending time away from the city.

Kat's "link" to StarWars?  She was born in 1977, the same year that A New Hope was released.

Kat is a biologist by degree and has been a nature lover since God only knows when.  She began studying marsupials back when she was five, and it just kept going from there.

She was introduced to SW1 back in 1999 by Enb'Zik's player.  Kat has since married Zik's player and they are having fun real life as well as on the MUSH.

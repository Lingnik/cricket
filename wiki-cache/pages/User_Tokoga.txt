==Log cleaning regex (yay)==
 sed -e  '1,/^START_SCENE/d'
This one deletes everything up to where the log says START_SCENE.  So, just open up your log, find the first pose, and stick START_SCENE above it.  This statement will delete everything up to and including it.
 sed -e 's/^[ \t]*//'
Deletes leading whitespace (tabs and spaces at the start of poses).
 sed -e '/^<[^(ROLL)]*[^(SKILL)]*>/d'
Deletes anything that's between < > symbols that isn't either ROLL or SKILL.  This deletes any <OOC>, <Public>, <Welcome>, etc. chatter without deleting rolls or skill checks.  If that's what you want,
 sed -e '/^<*>*/d'
will delete any line that starts with something inside < > symbols followed by something.
 sed -e '/^.* has disconnected./d' -e '/^.* has partially disconnected./d' -e '/^.* has connected./d' -e '/^.* has reconnected./d
This will delete the various connection messages that show up if you're in a room (the preceeding regex, that delete chatter from channels, will handle the others).
 sed -e G
This one double spaces.  :)

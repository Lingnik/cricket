== TP Thoughts ==
Hi, Krieg: Keline here. I've got a TP idea in the works that I wanted to put here so you could consider how likely it is for us to pull this off. The Inquisitor finds a destroyed VSD, an old one, that I've given the name of the Cyclops. It was part of a survey team that was searching for sites where minerals could be harvested(we need more Vespian gas!). Sadly for that ship, however, it got too close to the gas giant and was destroyed by either the dense gravitational pull or the storms in the upper atmosphere. The STs, in Space Trooper garb, investigate the ship and find the quite angry ghost of one of the dead crew members, a woman who was left behind during the evacuation. Much scariness would ensue, the STs would be pulled back, and then the ship would be made to go boom.

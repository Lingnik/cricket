'''''Uwannabuyim''''' was a [[YT-1300|Corellian YT-1300]] starship captained by [[Orson Tighe]] up until his death. She was a prominent vessel operating under [[Karrde's Organization]]. The ship was frequented by [[Jessalyn Valios]], lover to Tighe, after her release from [[Bacharan Valak]].

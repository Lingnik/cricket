'''V1C3''' ([[22 ABY]] -) is a kitbashed Combat/Protocol [[Droid]] built by [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] [[Presav]] [[Crestian Tarasar]] during his years keeping a low profile in the [[Dragonflower Nebula]], as a personal aide and bodyguard. Vee-Won has been be a ubiquitous presence among the Tarasar family in the decade since. He currently serves under [[Biscuit Baron]] Chief Executive Officer [[Atsvara Tarasar]] as Vice President of Security and as a [[Brigadier]] in the [[Union Marines]] serving as Executive Officer of [[The Chevalier]].

== Origin ==

Maintaining a fully crewed [[Interdictor-class Immobilizer 418|interdictor]] hidden for a decade in a nebula was a challenge for [[Crestian Tarasar]] and his [[New Sith Order]] remnant. The ability to recruit new personnel, let alone be able to trust them, was quite limited. In order to fill vacancies on the ''[[Leviathan]]'' after the death or retirement of existing crew, he would often turn to [[Droid|droids]]. In many cases these droids would be salvaged units, often assembled with parts and programming not standard to their initial model.

As he became more experienced in dealing with droid technology and with the impending birth of his own daughter [[Atsvara Tarasar|Atsvara]] on the horizon, Crestian turned his eye towards a personal project. Inspired by his personal hero [[:starwars:Revan/Legends|Revan]], Crestian wanted his own version of [[:starwars:Hk-47|HK-47]], though less an assassin than a droid sidekick with a questionable personality. Essentially looking to create combat capable protocol droid.

While Vee-Won's main body would in fact come from part of a [[:starwars:HK-77|HK-77]] droid which Crestian's friend [[Zubindi Hakoon]] was able to acquire on Mustafar, other elements of his structure and programming would come from other Clone War era salvage. The end result was a design which looked ambiguous in regards to actual function or origin. His programming modules would run the gamut from basic starship operation, bureaucratic proficiency, combat excellence, and conversational insolence. There were better options for both combat and protocol droids on the market, but Vee-Won's efficacy in both made him of great use. 

Having worked closely with the [[Sith Intelligence Network]] during the height of the New Sith Order, Crestian chose to give his new droid a name which was consciously a play on the word vice, which he felt a perfect partner to sin. Known affectionately as Vee-Won for short, the new droid would pick up the vices of gambling and vanity, but probably more from association with Crestian's circle than any design.

== Early Career ==

Upon completion and activation, Vee-Won was commissioned as an [[Ensign]] aboard the ''Leviathan'' and assigned as [[Admiral]] Tarasar's adjutant, and when necessary his guard as well. From the onset he was treated like any other member of the crew, receiving a regular pay-cheque and afforded the same off-time and personal space. As Crestian had effected a carefully crafted image as a dandy, Vee-Won took some inspiration from his boss, purchasing tailor-made pin-striped suits from the [[Silverwood House of Fashion]] and engaging in other luxury entertainment pursuits when the opportunities arose.

Vee-Won would perform all the tasks assigned to him with sufficient skill to please Crestian. More than that Crestian enjoyed the personality that the droid developed, in spite of, or perhaps because of, the fact he had a tendency to talk back to him. His close proximity to Crestian and his family would give the Admiral more opportunity to see the droid in work than many of his other crew, and a such Vee-Won had no trouble rising in the ranks. By the time of the ''Leviathan's'' foray to the [[Caspia|Caspian System]] during the Sariphage pandemic, V1C3 was one of the many officers who had by now risen to the rank of [[Commander]].

When Crestian organised [[Casulmis]] as a para-military organisation of Mergani refugees in [[28 ABY]] to combat the [[Olumi Osahn]] led pogroms on [[Mergansar]] which took place concurrently with pandemic on [[Caspar]], Commander V1C3 served behind the scenes in handling logistical issues involved. While he did not participate in the operation in Hebron, he was aboard ''Leviathan'' during the subsequent [[Mergansar Incident|attack]] by the [[Caspian Navy]]. Following the engagement he and the rest of the crew would return to [[Silver Station]].

Following the dissolution of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]], Vee-Won followed Crestian back to Caspar, continuing to serve as his adjutant and bodyguard as he built his new charity [[The Mission]] and reintegrated into life in his home city of [[Trinumvira]]. When Crestian was later elected [[Presav]] and appointed [[Sar Admiral]] in [[29 ABY]], Vee-Won would accept a commission into the Caspian Navy along with the majority of the ''Leviathan's'' crew. In this capacity Vee-Won would become the most high profile member of the new Presav's personal staff and was often seen in his company both on Caspar and foreign trips.

== Recent Career ==

A few months after Crestian's election as Presav, Vee-Won would accompany members of the Tarasar and Tracer families on a joint family vacation to the resort world of [[:starwars:Spira/Legends|Spira]]. The vacation was cover for a joint Caspian-Imperium rescue operation to save two force-sensitive youth who had been kidnapped as part of a [[Commission for the Preservation of the New Order|COMPNOR]] programme and were being held by [[Trandoshan]] mercenaries. Commander V1C3 would be part of a five member strike team along with Presav Tarasar, [[Moff]] [[Elana Tracer]], her son Commander [[Kipran Jawatha]], and [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] Minister of Justice [[Union Council|Councillor]] [[Sha Dark]] that breached the island compound where the younglings were being held and rescued them. Following the success of this mission Crestian would promote Kipran to the rank of [[Captain]] in the [[Imperium]] Navy, in spite of his questionable authority to actually do so, and transfer to Vee-Won to the [[Union Marines]] with a promotion to the rank of [[Colonel]].

Following the [[Report:Bespin Recap|attack]] on [[Cloud City]] during the 1st Annual Miners Dinner on [[Bespin]] and up-tick in other terrorist related activity in the galaxy, Crestian's cousin [[General]] [[Siege|Janus Tarasar]] was appointed as the commanding officer of [[The Chevalier]]. The Chevalier were an [[Ixin]] based military unit that was often contracted by various Princes for protection. Now the Chevalier were being tasked to serve as the protection detail for the Tarasar family and along with some other key members of the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]]. To further this aim, Crestian arranged Vee-Won's appointment as the Chevalier's executive officer, while continuing his functions as personal aide. 

In his capacity a presavadential guard, Colonel V1C3 was involved in assisting Imperator [[Tyler Damion|Damion]], Presav Tarasar, and others in suppressing an attempted [[Report:Corellian Prison Break|prisoner escape]] at [[Coronet City]] Spaceport on [[Corellia]] in [[32 ABY]].

== Business Executive ==

When Atsvara's formed the [[Golden Bantha Group]] as part of her plans to eventually acquire [[Biscuit Baron]] she invited Vee-Won to be one of the investors involved. As someone who would not normally be taken seriously in the business world given her age, she sought out investors that would similarly be underappreciated because of their youth or being a Droid. The invitation to Vee-Won, however, was particular personal to Atsvara as she began to fixate on the fact that her father built him around the same time as her birth, thus in her mind making him her brother. Though Vee-Won has made effect use of his salary for the past decade on luxury goods and entertainment, he had also been a savvy investor, no doubt helped by his insider knowledge, and was thus able to commit to buying a moderate stake in the investment firm.

Given Vee-Won's particular talents he was named the Vice President of Security for the Golden Bantha Group. When the firm eventually [[Report:Biscuit Buyout|purchased]] Biscuit Baron in [[33 ABY]], Vee-Won would serve on its board in the same capacity. This also placed Vee-Won in charge of the [[E5 Baron Droid|Baron Droids]] that Atsvara arranged to purchase from [[Haor Chall Engineering]] when the takeover was finalised. On the day the takeover went public, Vee-Won would personally lead some of these Droids in an operation on [[:starwars:Ord Cantrell/Legends|Ord Cantrell]] to liberate Biscuit Baron workers who were being trafficked the [[:starwars:Nova Crime Syndicate|Nova Syndicate]] who were using their Biscuit Baron franchises as a front.

For his actions combating slavery, a high profile priority of the [[Caspian Union]], Vee-Won was promoted to the rank of [[Brigadier]]. He maintains his position in the Chevalier and Union Marines as his new corporate role overlaps with his responsibility to protect various Caspian notables who serve on the Biscuit Baron board.

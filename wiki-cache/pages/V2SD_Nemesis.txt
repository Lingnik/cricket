'''HIMS ''Nemesis '''''is the flagship of Lord [[Aldus Thel]].

== Imperial Service Record ==
The '''''HIMS Nemesis '''''served with honorable distinction as a primary warship throughout the activities of [[Task Force Inquisitor]]. In [[22 ABY]], she was transferred to the command of Lord Thel for the campaign against the rebellious [[New Sith Order]].

Following the campaign, Lord Thel transferred his flag aboard the Nemesis, preferring its flexibility and ground-assault capabilities over the raw ship-to-ship firepower of the larger and arguably more prestigious [[Imperator II-class Star Destroyer|Imperial-II Star Destroyers]].

She led the blockade forces in the [[2015 Caspar Plot|Caspian affair]] alongside the [[I2SD Predator|Predator]].

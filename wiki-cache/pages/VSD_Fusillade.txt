The '''NRSD ''Fusillade''''' is a member of the [[New Republic FleetOps|New Republic Navy]]'s [[New Republic Second Fleet|Second Fleet]]. Her crew and female [[captain]] became famous during the [[Sixth Battle of Coruscant]] in [[15 ABY]].

== History ==

== Recent Events ==

The '''NRSD ''Fusillade''''' and her crew became famous during the [[Sixth Battle of Coruscant]], when she played a major role in the [[Retribution_of_Coruscant#The_Fall_of_Palliata_Station|defense of ''Palliata Station'']], the [[Retribution_of_Coruscant#Attack_on_the_Imperial_Flagship|attack on the ''Malevolence'']], and the final [[Retribution_of_Coruscant#The_Empire's_Victory|evacuation]]. During the final evacuation, her entire underbelly was severely damaged when a pair of sabotaged Imperial shuttles rammed into her ventral shields. Forced to leave the battle as a liability, she was berthed at [[Dac]]'s shipyards for two months to undergo repairs. She has now rejoined the rest of the fleet at [[Ord Mantell]].

'''V'ez-Tcha''' was a massive criminal organization that was involved in nearly every aspect of [[underworld]] activity throughout the [[The Galaxy|galaxy]]. Headquartered on [[Tatooine]], the syndicate would eventually be crushed by the [[Galactic Empire]] during the [[Battle of Tatooine]]. Surviving leaders of the syndicate were transported to [[Kessel]].

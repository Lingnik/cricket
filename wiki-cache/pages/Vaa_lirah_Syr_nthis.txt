==Life On [[Falleen]]==

==Recent Events==

Where to start for this vixen? Well, she and her brother decided that [[Nar Shaddaa]] was as good a place as any, and so they arrived there to begin their schemes. But few opportunities presented themselves to the Falleen, and so Vaa'lirah grew impatient with proceeding at [[Draa'kal]] and Ansi'shan'ya's pace.

==Appearance==

When you look at Vaa'lirah, you will likely find that she is watching you in return. Deep, dancing pools of lavender and violet linger in her eyes, a curious gaze caressing everything it sees with a sensual delight. From there however, the rest is far less emotional, and cold would be the word were it not for the subtle feeling that beneath her calm facade a bundle of passions and feelings lie in wait. Raven locks part from a gently sloping forehead, smooth light green skin with a faint sheen as if kissed by the sun, beneath this a generous, curving mouth rests between soft sculpted cheeks. Her hair falls gently to her shoulders where it pools around her neck as if wet, and there are the first scales to be seen.

For as you look over the rest of this creature you see many more lining her skin where it can be seen beneath her clothing. Small, almost glistening patches adorn her otherwise silky flesh along her toned limbs, running down her arms from her shoulders. Likewise her thighs and calves have the same natural jewelry, her long muscled legs peeking through a flowing dress of splits to hold aloft a slinky figure. Her dress hangs loosely from a collar, held close to her body at the waist by a sash; a shimmering white gown that is thin enough to be coloured by her skin beneath it, veined in the light with hues of green. She wears nothing on her feet but a lacework of filigree that climbs up her ankles, and her wrists are graced by pearls and emeralds set into thin bands of silver.

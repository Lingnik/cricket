'''Valdemar Linane''' ([[18 BBY]] - ) is a [[Sarian]] politician and union leader from Hammerton on [[Mergansar]] which he represents in the [[Union Council]]. He was Chairman of the Dusona Workers Union at the time of the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] and Mergani [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]]. Taking a strong stance against the pogroms he was forced to flee Mergansar. He briefly lived amongst the refugee community on [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] before relocating to [[Dunwelton]] on [[Caspar]] after the Sariphage pandemic ended.

In [[29 ABY]] when the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] government under [[Crestian Tarasar]] blockaded Mergansar in order to precipitate the overthrow of the cult backed Mergani regime, Linane met frequently with members of the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]]. The blockade was slow to deliver the intended results but after a month and a half supply shortages led to riots in several cities, including the Dusona cities of Ton Echotta (Hammerton) and Ton Mulatta (Spanner). This was soon followed by a general strike by the Dusona Workers Union.

The Dusona Workers Union proved effective in wrestling control of the continent from cultist regime, allowing exiled leaders such a Linane to return to Mergansar. Hammerton became the second Mergani city after Ithaca to be reintegrated into the Union, with Linane hastily elected to serve as its Councillor representing [[Praecet]]. In [[30 ABY]] Linane became a junior minister, being named Minister of Labour following the addition of [[Charros IV]] and the [[Krittain|Krittain System]] into the Union.

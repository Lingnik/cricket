'''''Valorous''-Class Light Carriers''' are specialized warships of the [[Caspian Union]] utilised for their ability to carry and deploy several dozen smaller craft. They are [[Krittain|Krittish]] in origin, and are based on the same frame and share components with the [[Arsenal-class Heavy Cruiser|''Arsenal''-Class Heavy Cruiser]]. While nowhere near as large or durable as the [[Caspia|Caspian]]-designed [[Hurricane-class Fleet Carrier|''Hurricane''-class carriers]], they are nonetheless a valuable element in the [[Caspian Navy]]. The majority Valorous-Class vessels in service are assigned to the Patrol Groups of the three defensive fleets.

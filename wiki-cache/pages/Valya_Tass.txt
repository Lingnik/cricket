==Description==
A flurry of dark brown nerf-lick locks atop of the woman's head might be a peek into the swirl of her thoughts. It's an unintentional hairstyle, more suggestive of crooked fingers raked through than any artifice. She is human, that is obvious, with tawny skin and clear brown eyes flecked in lighter amber that stand counter to the rest of her complexion. All the signs are there of a woman who works with her hands and keeps them moving. Smudges. Blunted fingernails, the base of each finger calloused from gripping. When not aboard ship, she has a watchful, wary air about her.

She is usually dressed in her pilot's best. Flightsuit or perhaps just the comfortable clothes of a woman who will be settled into a flight chair for most of her days.
==Background==
Valya is reasonably close-lipped about her background, though folks with astute ears will catch the tell of Corellia in the cadence and character of her speech. What ''is'' clear about her: the woman knows her way around a ship.

Folk who meet her in port might describe her as reserved. Wary, even. She speaks little and eschews crowds, generally opting to stay aboard ship if given the choice.

At the helm of a starship, she is an entirely different person. Comfortable, confident, competent. In command. She is as comfortable helming a ship as anyone with a decade or more experience, but she hardly has the years on her to support that. Maybe she just looks young.

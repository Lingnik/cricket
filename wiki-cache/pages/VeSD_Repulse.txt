The [[Venator-class Star Destroyer|''Venator''-class Star Destroyer]] '''''Repulse''''' was a typical ship-of-the-line constructed during the last days of the [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Galactic Republic]], and employed into service under the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] rule of [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[:starwars:Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine]]. It was most well-known for its deployment under the command of [[Admiral]] (then [[Commodore]]) [[Dergan Roj]] between [[:starwars:6 BBY|6 BBY]] and [[4 ABY]].


=== Retirement ===
So taxing were the previous eighteen years &mdash; especially the past two &mdash; upon the mighty Star Destroyer that her core systems were deemed beyond repair. ''Repulse'' was exhausted, and in a cruel twist of irony, seemed to mirror her captain's deteriorating condition day by day. Each officer knew it, from Poole to Myar, and so the process was undertaken to start stripping the ship of all reusable components. Her guns and shield projectors were the first to be removed, with the guns distributed to emplacements at the Vira Bluff Yards and [[Groton]], and her projectors finding their employment in completing [[CMS Hood|CMS ''Hood'']]. Amenities started to be transferred, and soon she was a skeleton of her former self. Her sensor core was the last major system component to be removed.

The end would not be long after, as only days later, Admiral Roj succumbed to his illness and passed. With thousands who had served under the Admiral grieving, [[Leidan Adamov]] suggested a tribute known in navies throughout the galaxy &mdash; a funeral pyre, using Roj's own ship. It would be only fitting. Trissard, who had known Roj longest and best, gave his consent and three days later, ''Repulse'' would bear Roj's body, lying in state on the bridge, into the [[Caspia|Caspian]] sun, Solus.

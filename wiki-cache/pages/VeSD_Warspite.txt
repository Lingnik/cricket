The [[Venator-class Star Destroyer|''Venator''-class Star Destroyer]] '''''Warspite''''' was a typical ship-of-the-line constructed during the last days of the [[:starwars:Galactic Republic/Legends|Galactic Republic]], and employed into service under the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] rule of [[Galactic Emperor|Emperor]] [[:starwars:Palpatine|Palpatine/Legends]]. It was most well-known for its deployment under the command of [[Admiral]] (then [[Commodore]]) [[Dergan Roj]] between [[4 BBY]] and [[:starwars:2 ABY|2 ABY]].

She was under the command of [[Caleb Anson]], a [[Human#Corellian|Corellian]] and disciple of [[:starwars:Garm Bel Iblis|Garm Bel Iblis]] and it took very little convincing from [[Orton Trissard]] to convince Anson to defect, along with nearly all of ''Warspite's'' crew.

Being slightly newer, and in tremendously better condition, than her partner [[VeSD Repulse|''Repulse'']], ''Warspite'' was employed to be the more active of the two warships within the senior staff's battle plans.

'''Vectus''' is the given name of a slave soldier in the [[New Sith Order]]. Vectus is the product of [[Sith]] magic; once a human, he has since become something much less. Lacking a human mind or soul, Vectus is simply a slave to the will of the Dark Lord, lacking emotion, independent thought. He has no memory of his past prior to his service to the Dark Lord; his life is to the Dark Lord and the Sith. As a slave soldier he serves as a pilot in the Sith Navy in [[House Exodus]] and has since become the personal slave/assistant to [[Wescal Cantrell]], a [[Knights of Korriban|Knight of Korriban]]. His service with the Sith has included the [[Battle of Kashyyyk (Sith Campaign)|Battle of Kashyyyk]], the [[Battle of Smuggler's Run (Sith Campaign)|attack on]] [[Smuggler's Run]], the [[Attack on AB-345]], and the kidnapping of [[New Republic]] [[Senator]] [[Alistair Al'Dira]].

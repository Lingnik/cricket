Luke has essentially hired Paul on in an archaeological capacity, and hinting that there is trouble to come, has told Paul to get off planet as soon as possible. Jessalyn proposes that Paul join them on the Black Dragon, an offer which Paul is rather hesitant to accept, as he is beginning to figure out that this particular band of folks tends to attract trouble like picnics do ants. He is also not exactly thrilled at being near to Jessalyn and Shenner, two red heads who disturb him on a number of levels that he isn't comfortable with enumerating. In the end he finally agreed, getting somewhat intrigued and excited despite himself. However, once he arrived in his room, the effects of his hang-over and lack of sleep finally hit him, and after getting cleaned up and taking an luxurious bath, he promptly passed out on the bed ... which is where he still is .....}}

'''Veter Ment''' was a young [[Sullustan]], and jack-of-all-trades. He had a rich and extremely long background, and his skills in conquering the various challenges of life were considered, by himself, to be unmatched. He had a rather high view of himself, considering himself to be one of the most successful Sullustans in the known Universe. What he actually did, however, is lost to records as of [[15 ABY]].

In the [[Imperial Navy]], a '''Vice Admiral''' is an intermediate rank between [[Rear Admiral]] and [[Admiral]]. It holds no official level of naval force in the [[Imperial Order of Battle]], and thus has a varying command. A Vice Admiral can either substitute for a [[Commodore]] in command of a [[Imperial Navy#Squadron|squadron]], or in rare cases command a [[Imperial Navy#Systems Force|Systems Force]] in place of a full Admiral. A Vice Admiral can also command an Imperial task force.

A Vice Admiral is equivalent to a [[Marshal]] in the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]].

=== Caspian Navy ===

'''Vice Admiral''' is the fourth most senior rank in the [[Caspian Navy]] ranking below [[Admiral|Admirals]] and ranking above [[Rear Admiral|Rear Admirals]]. Vice Admirals often serve as the commanding officer or executive officer of a Defence Group, Patrol Group, Task Group, or production though such positions may be held by someone of higher or lower ranks as well. Caspian Vice Admirals usually command from a [[Admiral Roj-class Battleship|Admiral Roj-Class Battleship]], [[Hood-class Battlecruiser|Hood-Class Battlecruiser]], or [[Valorous-class Light Carrier|Valorous-Class Light Carrier]].

=== Caspian Starfighter Corps === 

'''Vice Admiral''' is the fourth most senior rank in the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]] ranking below [[Marshal|Marshals]] and [[Admiral|Admirals]] and ranking above [[Commodore]]. Vice Admirals typically serve as the Commanding Officer of Starfighters for one of the Naval Defence Groups, Patrol Groups, or Task Groups. They will typically base themselves on the [[Hurricane-class Fleet Carrier|Hurricane-class Fleet Carriers]] within their group. In terms of preferential treatment and prestige they can be consider equivalent to Naval Vice Admirals, though they are equivalent to Naval [[Rear Admiral|Rear Admirals]] in pay. In terms of operational authority a Naval Vice Admiral outranks as Starfighter Vice Admiral, but a Starfighter Vice Admiral will outrank a Naval Rear Admiral.

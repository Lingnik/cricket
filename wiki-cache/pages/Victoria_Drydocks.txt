Representing [[Caspia|Caspia's]] first shipbuilding venture, '''Victoria Drydocks''' are still in use by [[SubPro]] and the Caspians as a supplemental facility to the larger, more modern [[Sloan Drydocks]] orbitting [[Kichnar]].

The Victoria docks, located in orbit between [[Caspar]] and its moon Tyllin, are responsible more so for SubPro's smaller ships, or specially-outfitted ones. A 589-meter [[Arsenal-class Heavy Cruiser|''Arsenal''-class heavy cruiser]] can barely fit in its largest slipway, and most of the work focuses around upgrade installations and retrofits of new component packages. Typically, the largest ship the docks see is the [[Ushton-class Heavy Freighter|''Ushton''-class heavy freighter]], which the Union has plenty of in mothball status, and is regularly being bought and outfitted by independent shipping companies. The sophisticated [[Shearwater-class Frigate|''Shearwater''-class frigates]] are also produced and upgraded here, since the close proximity of [[ProThrust Engine Division|ProThrust]] and [[CDMC]] resources streamline production for these vital, technologically-advanced vessels.

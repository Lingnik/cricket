[[Vigilance Security Corporation]] (VSC) was a mercenary outfit based out of [[Ord Mantell]].  After less than a year in operation, the company was dissolved in [[16 ABY]], the last remaining founders liquidating the corporation's assets and donating the proceeds to the [[New Republic]] war effort against the [[Galactic Empire|Empire]].

===History===
====Founding====
Vigilance Security Corporation was formed in early 16 ABY by [[Gren Delede]], [[Twila Virda]], and Dr. [[Xar'on Tanner]]. The trio had been forced to leave the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Corporate Sector]] after clashing with the CSA's Imperial allies and earning the ire of [[Corporate Sector Authority|Authority]] administrators.  As they all had extensive military experience, a mercenary group was formed. Due to their own issues with the [[Galactic Empire]], they journeyed to the [[New Republic]].  Basing their operations on the new [[New Republic|NR]] capitol world, they dedicated their group to opposing the oppressive Imperial regime.  

====[[Operation Scatterswipe]]====
Initially acting on their own, VSC's first major strikes against the Empire began soon after the company was founded.  Raiding and harrassing Imperial supply lines was quickly noticed by the New Republic's military leaders, who established [[Operation Scatterswipe]] as joint NR / VSC campaign.  For their involvement in this successful operation, Twila Virda and Xar'on Tanner each received a [[New Republic Military Awards|New Republic Civilian Battle Award]], bestowed upon them in a ceremony conducted by [[Senator]] [[Del Marx]].

====Closing the doors====
Though the exact contributing factors are unknown, later in 16 ABY, Vigilance would officially shutdown &ndash; closing its doors, liquidating its assets, and fading into memory.

===Military Assets===
VSC had a modest force of military assets as it's disposal, with both space and ground forces at their command.  The core element of these forces was the ''[[Eternal Vigilance]]'', a [[Marauder-class Corvette|''Marauder''-class Corvette]] housing a half-squadron of starfighters, as well as a number of starships for combat support. It was also home to two platoons of special operations soldiers. The group's corporate headquarters on [[Ord Mantell]] was also home to another platoon of soldiers.  At it's peak, the group could field two hundred highly-trained, former-military ground operators. 

On occassion, the corporation also hired freelancer ships and soldiers, usually contracted for individual missions.

===Paramilitary Structure===
Operating as both a corporate entity and a military organization, Vigilance maintained two rank structures.  Traditional military grades were blended with corporate titles.  Similar to the idea of enlisted and commissioned personnel, superior ranks conferred the status of '''Senior Partner''' while those of lesser ranks were referred to as '''Associates'''; however, a handful of traditional officer ranks fell into the subordinate category.

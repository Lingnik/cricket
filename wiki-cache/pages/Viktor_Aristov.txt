==Overview==
Viktor S. Aristov is a [[Mon Calamari]] captain hailing from the ocean planet of [[Dac]], sometimes referred to as Mon Calamari. Working his way up the commands of the Imperial Navy, Aristov knows what it feels like to be at the bottom of the ladder, a position he never wants to be in again. Working his way up in ranks, Aristov was promoted to Captain by Lord Thel, but due to his species, was placed as commanding officer of the [[IAC Responder]], a less-modern Venator-class Star Destroyer, in opposition to the more modern Imperial Star Destroyer.
==Brutality on Bothawui==
Aristov was almost immediately assigned by Lord Thel to the planet of Bothawui. The system had gotten out of control with smuggling, and Aristov was confident he could finish the job by proving the Empire had the power to rule the system. His flagship was placed at the head of a small fleet of Imperial Star Destroyers, and the Viper Squadron was assigned to the Responder. The Mon Calamari captain was sure that the mission would be a success, but did not see what was coming ahead for him.

'''Vilkata Lanna''' was a [[Corporal]] in the [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] [[Stormtrooper Corps]]. His first post out of training, which concluded in [[10 ABY]], was [[I2SD Tormentor|HIMS ''Tormentor'']] where he was often assigned to guard detail in the detention block, securing and transporting prisoners in the brig.

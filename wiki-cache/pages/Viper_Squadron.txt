The 568th Interceptor Starfighter Squadron, '''Viper''', is one of the newest squadrons in the Imperial fleet.

== History ==

'''Viper''' Squadron is relatively new. They were formed late in [[14 ABY]] and placed under the command of Lt. Commander [[Jal'Dana Rall]]. Initially designed as a reconnaissance and intelligence-gathering unit, Viper was later reassigned to a traditional space-superiority role and transferred to the [[ICRV Prowler|HIMS ''Prowler'']]. 

In [[15 ABY]] Viper was redesignated as a Special Operations Squadron. The pilots of Viper were cross-trained in both the [[TIE Interceptor Mk II]] and [[Scimitar Assault Bomber]]s. The squadron typically flies a mixed group of Mark I and II Interceptors (whichever Imperial resources has available at the moment), as well as the Scimitar Bombers. Their training to date has been focused on hit and fade attacks as well as swift, deadly battles. 

== Recent Events ==

After the [[Retribution of Coruscant|Retribution of Coruscant]] in [[15 ABY]], Grand Inquisitor [[Vassily Korolov]] ordered the squadron aboard the [[I2SD Inquisitor|HIMS ''Inquisitor'']].  This transfer put them under the newly formed Task Force Inquisitor, giving them a new commanding officer as well as a permanent craft to fly, the [[TIE Interceptor Mk II]].

'''Wallas Thurl''' is a Commander in the [[Imperial Navy]] and the executive officer of the [[I2SD_Inquisitor]].

== Service History ==
'''1 BBY - 1 ABY'''

Midshipman with the ''HIMS Minmie'', a ''Carrack''-class Light Cruiser

'''1 ABY - 3 ABY'''

Ensign with the ''HIMS Viper'', an ''Enforcer''-class Light Cruiser

<u>Distinction:</u> Navy Achievement Medal, received for combat on convoy escort by luring attackers into an asteroid field by microjump while the commanding officer was disabled from battle injuries

'''4 ABY - 7 ABY'''

Ensign and then Lieutenant with the ''HIMS Obviator'', an Imperial Star Destroyer

'''7 ABY - 9 ABY'''

Lieutenant with the ''HIMS Viper'', a n ''Enforcer''-class Light Cruiser

'''10 ABY'''

Lieutenant Commander with the ''HIMS Surprise'', a heavy Q-Ship

<u>Distinction:</u> Bronze Star, received after destroying three pirates while heavily outnumbered and outgunned, thereby ensuring the safety of commerce in the sector

<u>Distinction:</u> Wounded in battle

'''11 ABY - 15 ABY'''

Commander with BuDocks at Yaga Minor

'''15 ABY - 17 ABY'''

Commander with BuS&A at Yaga Minor.

'''17 ABY'''

Commander with BuWeps at Yaga Minor. Served as commander of ships reconfigured as weapons testing platforms.

'''18 ABY'''

Commander with the ''HIMS Inquisitor'', an Imperial II Star Destroyer

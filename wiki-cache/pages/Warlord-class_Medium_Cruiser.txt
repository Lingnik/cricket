The '''Warlord-class cruiser''' was a medium cruiser designed by the KDY-SFS corporation after the [[Imperial Blitzkrieg]] for the [[Imperial Navy]] as a general-purpose vessel capable of many missions profiles, yet cheap enough to be produced in large quantities. The existing [[Strike-class Medium Cruiser|Strike-Class Medium Cruiser]] were produced at the [[Rendili StarDrive]] shipyards under license from the Loronar corporation. Since both were members of the [[Corporate Sector Authority]], logistics had become complicated and were not under the direct control of the Imperial Admiralty. 

The concept for the Warlord cruiser was approved during the height of the Blitzkrieg when the Imperial Navy was overrunning sectors faster than it could move forces to occupy them and the Star Destroyer fleet became increasingly needed on the front line. The need for a  cruiser that could act as a force multiplier for the Navy's existing medium and heavy cruiser fleets was critical. The typical order of battle will have a Warlord cruiser assigned to smaller fleets of [[Carrack-class Light Cruiser|Carrack,]] Strike, and [[Interdictor-class Immobilizer 418|Oppressor]] squadrons to offset their firepower. 

To shorten the engineering time to design the new cruiser, plans for a medium cruiser based on the [[Venator-class Star Destroyer]] were chosen. This both met the operational necessities of the new ship, while reducing the production time from years to months by taking advantage of existing supply networks.

In addition, the interior storage space of the Warlord-class hangar bays could be modified for different missions. The most common sets were designed for planetary garrison, space superiority, and planetary assault. Planetary garrison loadouts included an assault company, two AT-STs, one AT-AT and several support speeders; space superiority included three squadrons of fighters; the planetary assault load out included five AT-ATs.

'''Wedge Antilles''' ([[21 BBY]] - ) is a famous [[New Republic]] starfighter ace and commander. He is the only pilot to have participated in all three attacks against the [[Galactic Empire|Empire's]] [[:starwars:Death Star/Legends|Death Stars]] and survived. renown the [[The Galaxy|galaxy]] over for his piloting prowess, survival skill and tactical mind, Antilles has earned the reputation as the Republic's best starfighter pilot.

He led [[Rogue Squadron]] for several years, until his replacement by [[Johanna Siri te Danaan]] as he was elevated to the position of Director of Starfighter Operations.

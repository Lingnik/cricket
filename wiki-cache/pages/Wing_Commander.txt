'''Wing Commander''' is a military rank usually given charge of an entire wing. It is almost always associated with members of a starfighter corps, most notably the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]]. These are often seasoned officers who reported to a ship's captain for all matters related to flight operations.

== In Imperial Service ==
{{rank plate|[[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_rb.png]][[Image:rank_empire_cylin.png]]|}}
In the [[Imperial Starfighter Corps]], a Wing Commander generally commands an entire wing. A typical wing consists of up to 6 squadrons of 12-24 ships (about 72 total), 120-300 officers, and 180-540 enlisted. They are addressed as commander. If a wing has more than one Wing Commander, one will be appointed Wing Commander and the other Executive Officer. If appointed as a staff officer they will act as an assistant to a [[Group Captain]].

A Wing Commander was the equivalent to a [[Commander]] in the [[Imperial Navy]].

== In Union Service ==

In the [[Caspian Starfighter Corps]], the rank of '''Wing Commander''' is largely equivalent to the Imperial rank. A wing in the Caspian Starfighter Corps, however, is likely to only consist of between two and five squadrons, making the number of personnel under their command fewer than their Imperial counterparts. They rank below a [[Group Captain]] and a above a [[Squadron Leader|Squadron Commander]]. It is equivalent in rank to a [[Commander]] in the [[Caspian Navy]] and to a [[Lieutenant Colonel]] in the [[Union Marines]].

The '''Wookiee Workers Union of Anchorron''' is the largest labour union on [[Caspar]]. In spite of its name, membership is no longer limited to [[Wookiee|Wookiees]] nor is its operation confined to [[Anchorron]]. The union's chairman is [[Zaalbacca]], who has held the role since [[18 ABY]]. Chairman Zaalbacca has also been the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] Minister of Industry and Labour, [[Union Council|Councillor]] for Anchorron, and Deputy Party Leader of [[Praecet]] since [[29 ABY]].

== Background ==

[[Wookiee|Wookiees]] are the third most populous sentient species on [[Caspar]], after [[Human|Humans]] and [[Sarian|Sarians]]. Prior to the Sariphage [[Report:Sariphage Press Releases|pandemic]] they represented eight percent of the total planetary population. Having had a much high rate of survival than most other species they now make up fifteen percent of Caspar's population. Driven by its industrial character, [[Anchorron]] has long been the most racially diverse of Caspar's city. Wookiees represent a significant portion of the population, serving as an integral part of the overall culture of the city.

In [[:starwars:104 BBY|104 BBY]], Caspian labour organisers helped Wookiees working in various industries in the city of Anchorron to form unions to advocate for their interests. In [[:starwars:88 BBY|88 BBY]], many of these unions merged to form the Wookiee Workers Union of Anchorron. In its initial years its membership was restricted to Wookiees, but the Union often coordinated with Unions representing other species, including engaging in sympathetic strikes.

In [[27 BBY]], the Wookiee Workers Union of Anchorron absorbed a number of other smaller unions in the city representing [[Duros]], [[Quarren|Quarrens]], [[Twi'lek|Twi'leks]], [[Wroonian|Wroonians]], and [[Zabrak]] respectively. The size of the Wookiee Workers Union compared to the other unions meant there remained majority support to maintain that name rather than rebranding to reflect the expanded membership. With the merger, the union officially dropped any racial requirements for membership, though it continued to centre around non-Human and non-Sarian membership.

== Modern History ==

The [[:starwars:Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]] devastated the economy of Anchorron with a staggering number of business going bankrupt. The end of the war and advent of the [[Galactic Empire]] did nothing to improve the situation locally. As industry failed, criminal enterprises filled the void. Union membership declined drastically and murky relations with criminal organisations emerged. This would continue until the formation of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] in [[4 ABY]]. Union membership slowly began to increase, as did push back against involvement with organisations outside the law. As the tide turned many of the union's longtime leaders began to retire.

The [[First Renaissance of Innovation|Renaissance of Innovation]] under [[Presav]] [[Lyr Dunwell]] saw great industrialisation in the [[Caspia|Caspian System]]. Anchorron, however, would find great competition from facilities in orbit of [[Kichnar]] and [[Tyllin]]. Though the city's fortunes improved greatly it never benefited as much as other locations. Much of the union's activity in these years centred around lobbying for greater investment in Anchorron's industry. 

It would be years before these efforts bore significant fruit. Under the presavadencies of [[Elana Tracer]] and [[Thomas Mahon]] initiatives focused on Anchorron would emerge. This would eventually see Anchorron become Caspar's busiest seaport with aquaculture becoming a growing part of the economy.

Roj Memorial Spaceport would also emerge as Caspar's busiest after [[Flude Cauldron|Union Starport]]. However, with crime continuing to be a significant issue, the Mahon administration would take the curious step of effectively walling off the spaceport from the rest of the city to hinder smuggled goods penetrating into wider Caspian society.

== Zaalbacca ==

[[Image:Zaalbacca.jpg|right|thumb|Zaalbacca]] In [[18 ABY]], a Wookiee by the name of [[Zaalbacca]] was elected as the new Chairman of the Wookiee Workers Union of Anchorron. Zaalbacca was an outspoken critic of Presav Mahon. The failure of the Mahon administration to address the underlying issue of crime in the city and the handicapping of Anchorron's economy by walling off the starport were both treated by Zaalbacca and the union as criminal attacks on the city by the Presav and his government. Nevertheless, in spite of the union's efforts, no changes in policy manifested.

Though the Mahon years were considered bad for Anchorron, the financial collapse and fire sale buyout of much of Caspar's major corporations following the [[2015 Caspar Plot|scandal]] which brought down the government of [[Saiane Caitrean]] was worse, with the city once again facing ruination. In these dark years the union would come to serve as an even greater voice for workers in the face of governmental failures. As other cities were now facing economic downturn Anchorron had lived for years, the union would find new sources of membership, absorbing unions in other Caspian cities, though again maintaining the same moniker. By the time the Sariphage pandemic devastated Caspar, the Wookiee Workers Union was the planet's largest trade union.

Following the pandemic, and the collapse of civil leadership on Caspar, Zaalbacca and the union would largely fill the void as far as Anchorron was concerned. In addition to providing support to the surviving population in the city, the union would take an active role in supporting the refugees who fled from the [[Report:Mergani Pogrom Press Releases|pogroms]] on [[Mergansar]]. This work would put Zaalbacca in contact with [[Crestian Tarasar]] who had return to Caspar and was engaged in similar work in his home city of [[Trinumvira]].

As Crestian looked to expand the operations of his charity, [[The Mission]], beyond Trinumvira, he saw Anchorron as the next logical location. Anchorron had seen a comparatively higher survival rate from the pandemic than other cities, but its depressed economy made it more in need of aid than other cities. Zaalbacca and the Wookiee Workers Union of Anchorron became natural partners to this effort. 

The Mission would soon fund efforts to revitalise the area around the Gerrin Building into the Gerrin Complex with a new tower constructed at its centre. The top floors would soon boast new office space for both The Mission and the Wookiee Workers Union, with Zaalbacca joining The Mission's Board of Directors.

== Political Insiders ==

When a Provisional Assembly formed on Caspar to draft a new constitution, neither Crestian nor Zaalbacca would serve in it themselves, but both would wield a great deal of influence. In Crestian's case his affiliation with the [[New Sith Order]] and the fall out from the [[Mergansar Incident]] made him too controversial to easily gain office. His work with The Mission and the credibility lent by his relationship with the Wookiee Workers Union allowed him to slowly build favour, but Trinumvira would be represented by allies. Meanwhile the union's power in Anchorron was such that any candidate that had its support was guaranteed election, with Zaalbacca preferring to operate through proxies.

As the final compromises of the constitutional process were bearing out, Crestian and Zaalbacca would begin to organise their collective allies along with other like-minded individuals from other cities into a political party dubbed [[Praecet]]. Both seasoned political operators with 40% of the provisional assembly joining their party, they engineered the final form of the new government to suit the interests. 

A unicameral legislature, with a seat for each city, elected by a plurality of votes suited the projections of what they could win at the polls. Anchorron and Trinumvira were essentially guaranteed seats with [[Orleanon]] and [[Plaxton]] projected to go their way. By securing [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] into the new [[Caspian Union|Caspar Union]] and the full admittance of [[Mikassa]] as well as full citizenship for refugees who had been in place for three months, place six of nine seats firmly in their sights. An eventual win in [[Noques Rasala]] would see Praecet in control of seven of nine seats. Zaalbacca would run for, and be elected as, the Anchorron seat on the [[Union Council]].

Following the election Crestian would become Presav, while Zaalbacca became Minister of Industry and Labour. Having their chairman in charge of the [[Caspian Crest|Crest's]] labour policy has been of great benefit to the union. This has not been without some controversy. During the [[Biscuit Baron]] strike in [[32 ABY]], Councillor [[Thomas Keyyaw]] of Lansdowne called for Zaalbacca's resignation from his ministerial post because of his pro-labour bias.

[[Image:Calling Teksa.jpg|left|thumb|Calling Teksa]] Under Zaalbacca's watch Anchorron has seen a great industrial resurgence and workers throughout the Union benefit from strong labour protections. In both his roles as Chairman of the Wookiee Workers Union and Minister of Industry and Labour, Zaalbacca is assisted by Calling Teksa, his Chief of Staff. She is often seen by Zaalbacca's side serving as his translator as Wookiees cannot speak [[Galactic Basic Standard]]. In spite of her relative youth, she is considered one of the most effective political aides at [[Union Hall]].

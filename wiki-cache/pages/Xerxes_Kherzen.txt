[[Xerxes Kherzen]] is the [[Viceprex (CSA)|Executive Viceprex]] of the [[Corporate Sector Authority|CSA]] [[Legal Division (CSA)|Legal Division]].  An enigmatic corporate leader, his activities among the upper echelons of the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Authority]] elite are not often publicized to the general populace of the [[Corporate Sector Authority|Sector]], leaving many with only a few terse press releases to track him.

The '''Xi Char''' are the fanatical polytheistic religious order of the [[Xi Charrian|Xi Charrians]] of [[Charros IV]]. They believe that all life is a shadow of the paradise of the afterlife and that creating machines was a way to glimpse the afterlife. To that end the devoted themselves to high-precision manufacturing as a religious practice and formed the corporation [[Haor Chall Engineering]] in furtherance of this philosophy.

Xi Char factories served both as manufacturing facilities and religious structure and were commonly known as "cathedral factories." Xi Charrian cities are organised around such factories. The leaders of these cathedral factories are known as Deans, while the overall leader of the Xi Char holds the title of [[Prelate]]. As a hive-minded species the choice of leaders is arrived at by consensus. There is no apparent difference in Xi Charrian society between civil, corporate, and religious leadership. 

Since they joined the [[Caspian Union]], the Prelate and Deans also serve as Charros IV's representation on the [[Union Council]]. In spite of the hive mentality the Xi Char are not a monolithic society with experimentation encourage and with it some degree of uniqueness. One example of this is the fact the varying members of the Xi Char delegation of the Union Council having chosen to align with different political parties.

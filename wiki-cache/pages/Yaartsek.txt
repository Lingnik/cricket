'''Yaartsek''' was an agricultural world in the [[Mid Rim]]. Lacking in natural resources, Yaartsek was nonetheless heavily populated, with nine billion human colonists. Under [[Galactic Empire|Imperial]] authority since [[17 BBY]], Yaartsek [[Battle of Yaartsek|rebelled]] against the Empire in [[14 ABY]] but was unsuccessful.

Its capital city is [[Majork]]. Since the rebellion, it has been administered by the ruthless [[Governor]] Djorn Mikei and watched by a unit of the [[Imperial Security Bureau]].

A quarter-sector away from [[Caspia]], the '''Yariz System''' is much like Caspia in astronomical makeup. Astronomers now speculate that Yariz and Solus are "sibling stars", born from the same nebula at roughly the same time. Unlike the Solus star system, Yariz's few planets appear infertile or exhausted. Several exhibit traits of volcanism (such as Picon Yar) and tremendous seismic activity - Baikoun Yar, in fact, is so rife with earthquakes and tectonic activity, many are advised against visiting. The lone hospitable planet, [[Kasputin Yar]], is an odd, eerie world and home to the sole sentient native species, the [[Yari]]. 

At some undocumented time in the past, speculators and miners from [[Hutt Space]], the Tion Hegemony, and the Cronese Mandate all foraged in the system for an array of raw materials, but nothing became lucrative. To service these ventures, a few small outposts, called stations, were established and started to perform roles such as lite manufacturing, repair, and supply. While it's currently nowhere near the size or capacity to rival the great staryards of the galaxy, this is still a productive location, and prior to partnering with several Caspian companies, found itself doing a lot of repair and upgrade work for Hutt interests and [[:starwars:Centrality/Legends|The Centrality]]. 

The system also found itself trafficked by any and all peoples associated with the Hutts. These included [[Nikto|Niktos]], [[Weequay]], [[Klatooinian|Klatooinians]], [[Vodran|Vodrans]], [[Aqualish]], and [[Duros]], all on fencing or pirating runs through the system. 

In the days of the old [[Caspian Democratic Union]] the system was protected was protected by a flotilla of Caspian starships led by the ''[[KMS Lirithose|CMKS Lirithose]]''. In addition to the systems importance as a major member world of the Union, the [[Lokkai Starworks]] shipyards at [[Vanover Yards|Vanover]] and [[Nenawirth Yards|Nenawirth]] were crucial assets for the Union to protect. A number of those vessels remained to protect the system after the collapse of the CDU following the Sariphage pandemic and Mergani pogroms.

After the system rejoined the new [[Caspian Union]], Kasputin Yar and Yariz System became the headquarters of the [[Caspian 2nd Fleet]]. Following the subsequent reorganisation of the fleet that followed the admittance of [[Charros IV]] and the readmittance of the [[Krittain|Krittain System]] the, 2nd Fleet's headquarters were moved to the Krittain System. Defence Group Yariz of the [[Caspian 1st Fleet]] now provides defence to the Yariz System.

'''Yindo Ola''' was an ancient [[Jedi]], dating back to the days of the [[:starwars:Old_Republic|Old Republic]]. He was trapped in a [[Force Storm]], a devastating technique utilized by the [[Sith]], until he was rescued in [[15 ABY]] by [[Luke Skywalker]] and his [[Jedi Order]].

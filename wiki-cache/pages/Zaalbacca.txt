'''Zaalbacca''' ([[32 BBY]] - ) is a [[Wookiee]] politician from the city of [[Anchorron]] on [[Caspar]] which he represents in the [[Union Council]]. He is the Deputy Party Leader of [[Praecet]] and serves as the [[Caspian Union|Caspian]] Minister of Industry and Labour. In addition to his governmental roles, Zaalbacca is Chairman of the [[Wookiee Workers Union of Anchorron]] and a member of the Board of Directors of [[The Mission]].

== Early Life ==

Zaalbacca was born in the city of [[Anchorron]] on [[Caspar]] in waning years of the Old Republic. His parents were first generation immigrants from [[Kashyyyk]] and he was named for an uncle who later served in the Alliance to Restore the Republic and later the [[New Republic]] [[New Republic Marine Corps|Marines]], where he eventually retired as a [[Brigadier General]].

While his family had settled a city with a booming industrial sector the advent of the Clone Wars saw economy of Anchorron begin to falter with the rise of the [[Galactic Empire]] leading to its effective collapse. While Caspar's relative remoteness spared its Wookiee citizens from the tumult it faced on Kashyyyk, with Anchorron in particular a city with a diverse population, there were few opportunities and rampant crime.

In spite of these challenge, Zaalbacca did manage as a young man to find employment like his father had done in the factories and eke out a modest and honest living. The collapse of the Empire and the formation of the [[Caspian Democratic Union]] would finally see to new opportunities on Caspar, jump-started by [[Presav]] [[Lyr Dunwell|Lyr Dunwell's]] "[[First Renaissance of Innovation|Renaissance of Innovation]]." Anchorron benefited, but comparatively less so than other parts of the planet and system which is when Zaalbacca would begin to get involved with trade union activism. Successive Presavs tried to improve Anchorron's fortunes but with limited success.

== Union Leadership ==

By the time of the administration of [[Thomas Mahon]], Zaalbacca was a full-time union leader, culminating in his election as Chairman of the [[Wookiee Workers Union of Anchorron]] in [[18 ABY]]. While Anchorron boasted Caspar's largest seaport and second largest spaceport its economy was still stymied by crime. Zaalbacca and the Wookiee Workers Union were outspoke critics of the Mahon administration's policy of effectively walling of Roj Memorial Spaceport from the rest of the city to prevent smuggled goods from penetrating into wider Caspian society. The abdication of authority in actually addressing the criminal problem combined with handicapping access to the spaceport for exports were viewed by Zaalbacca and the Wookie Workers Union of Anchorron as criminal attacks on the city.

If the Mahon years were considered bad for Anchorron, the financial collapse and fire sale buyout of much of Caspar's major corporations following the [[2015 Caspar Plot|scandal]] which brought down the government of [[Saiane Caitrean]] was worse, with the city once again facing ruination. In these dark years the union would come to serve as an even greater voice for workers in the face of governmental failures. In spite of maintaining the moniker of the Wookiee Workers Union of Anchorron, the union would grow to include members of both other cities and had long included other species. By the time the Sariphage pandemic devastated Caspar, the Wookiee Workers Union of Anchorron was the planet's largest trade union.

== Rebuilding ==

The Sariphage pandemic that struck down 90% of Caspar's population was caused by a virus later discovered to have been engineered to specifically target [[Sarian|Sarians]]. This meant that Wookiees were not effected as greatly as Sarians, [[Human|Humans]], and other [[Near Human]] species. With Anchorron's diverse population, it had a higher rate of survivors than the other cities of Caspar. With the death of most the [[Caspian Crest|Crest]], this left figures like Zaalbacca largely in charge of filling the void left by in political leadership. Not only did the citizens of Anchorron look to Zaalbacca and the union to bring stability there were also a sizeable number of refugees from the pogroms on [[Mergansar]] now seeking refuge on Caspar.

The arrival of [[Crestian Tarasar]] back on Caspar would see a new opportunity arise for Zaalbacca and Anchorron. While Zaalbacca and the union worked to rebuild Anchorron and provide what support they could elsewhere, Crestian would form his charity, [[The Mission]], in his home city of [[Trinumvira]] working to do much the same thing there. As Crestian looked at the planet beyond Trinumvira, he believed Anchorron was the next logical target of support owing the fact it had the higher survivor rate and yet such a depressed economy that would benefit most from support.

In Zaalbacca and the Wookiee Workers Union of Anchorron, Crestian and The Mission found a natural partner with an existing ground organisation to support relief efforts. The Mission would soon fund efforts to revitalise the area around the Gerrin Building into the Gerrin Complex with a new tower constructed at its centre. The top floors would soon boast new office space for both The Mission and the Wookiee Workers Union, with Zaalbacca joining The Mission's Board of Directors.

== Praecet ==

When a Provisional Assembly formed on Caspar to draft a new constitution, neither Crestian nor Zaalbacca would serve in it themselves, but both would wield a great deal of influence. In Crestian's case his affiliation with the [[New Sith Order]] and the fall out from the [[Mergansar Incident]] made him too controversial to easily gain office. His work with The Mission and the credibility lent by his relationship with the Wookiee Workers Union allowed him to slowly build favour, but Trinumvira would be represented by allies. Meanwhile the union's power in Anchorron was such that any candidate that had its support was guaranteed election, with Zaalbacca preferring to operate through proxies.

As the final compromises of the constitutional process were bearing out, Crestian and Zaalbacca would begin to organise their collective allies along with other like-minded individuals from other cities into a political party dubbed [[Praecet]]. Both seasoned political operators with 40% of the provisional assembly joining their party, they engineered the final form of the new government to suit the interests. 

A unicameral legislature, with a seat for each city, elected by a plurality of votes suited the projects of what they could win at the polls. Anchorron and Trinumvira were essentially guaranteed seats with [[Orleanon]] and [[Plaxton]] projected to go their way. By securing [[Kichnar Orbital Station]] into the new [[Caspian Union|Caspar Union]] and the full admittance of [[Mikassa]] as well as full citizenship for refugees who had been in place for three months, place six of nine seats firmly in their sights. An eventual win in [[Noques Rasala]] would see Praecet in control of seven of nine seats. To secure this favourable landscape, the Praecet supporters in the Provisional Assembly would drop their opposition to political recognition of the Mergani government and their inclusion in the division of military assets from the old [[Caspian Democratic Union|CDU]]. 

== Union Council ==

In an effort to build stability and unity following the election, in spite of capturing seven of the nine seats in the new [[Union Council]], Praecet would make the unprecedented choice of forming a coalition government with the Legacy Party which won the other two seats. [[Sirlahn Inglet]], the Councillor of Kichnar, whose seat was elected first would nominate Tarasar as Presav, with [[Legacy]] Party leader [[Cas Mos]] seconding the nomination, which passed with eight votes in favour, and Tarasar abstaining. Presav Tarasar would then nominated Cas Mos as Vice Presav, with Zaalbacca seconding the nomination, which passed with eight votes in favour and Mos abstaining. 

While the political arrangement would see Zaalbacca lose out on the Vice Presavadency which would normally have been his right, the post of Minister of Industry and Labour which he would be appointed to was ample reward in his mind as it allowed him to finally control the policies he had been fighting on for years. Zaalbacca's role as Deputy Leader of Praecet would still see Zaalbacca with a level of political power that arguably exceeded Vice Presav Mos. Particularly in light of Presav Tarasar's frequent foreign travels, Zaalbacca was essentially in day to day control of the Union's majority party.

The [[Caspian Repatriation Act (29 ABY)|Caspian Repatriation Act]] would soon see [[Kasputin Yar]] and [[Rauther]] rejoin the Union, with [[Ixin]] following suit a month later. Praecet would maintain a slim majority with ten of now eighteen seats, with the Legacy Party at five and three for the Ixin based Lijsttrekker Party which would now join the coalition government as well. Meanwhile Article XIV of the Reparation Act would levy a fine of 30 trillion credits against Mergani government on behalf of the refugees of the pogroms there, with the planet being blockade until payment which lacked both interest and means in doing. This would ultimately render most of the political compromises made in constitutional process now moot, which was always intended.

As a senior minister and Presav Tarasar's strongest political ally in the Union Council, Zaalbacca has hand a major role both publicly and behind the scenes in all major policy initiatives of his government. Zaalbacca is known for working with [[Valdemar Linane]], the exiled Chairman of the Dusona Workers Union on Mergansar to agitate for the overthrow Mergani governmental authority on the Dusona continent and its readmittance into the Caspian Union, fulfilling in the region, the Tarasar administration's goal of forcing the Mergani's collapse from its own citizens. Zaalbacca and Minister of Science and Technology Sirlahn Inglet were both key in working with [[ak]] of [[Charros IV]] to negotiate [[Xi Charrian]] entry into the Union.

Zaalbacca is also known to be supporter of the Caspian Union's [[Imperium-Caspian Mutual Protection Pact (29 ABY)|mutual protection pact]] with the [[Imperium]]. Though he has family ties in the New Republic, and his people's homeworld of Kashyyyk is in Republic space, he is generally known to agree with the Praecet party position that the Republic has been useless to Caspar while the military and trade relationship with the Imperium has been tangibly beneficial to the Caspian citizenry. As one of the Union's most senior and politically influential ministers, Zaalbacca is known to regularly visit Imperium and [[Independent]] worlds as part of Caspian delegations. He was part of the Caspian delegation to the 1st Annual Miners Dinner in [[Cloud City]] on [[Bespin]] and noted for his role in supporting local leaders following the terrorist attack the event.

She is card carrying member of the Wookiee Workers Union of Anchorron and Chief of Staff to Zaalbacca in his various roles.

Zaalbacca is also known for a jovial spirit. When costumed Palpatine impersonators showed for the returns in both cities, the Praecet candidate, and ultimate victor in Orleanon, [[Sha Dark]] punched the impersonator there. Meanwhile Zaalbacca posed for photographs with the impersonator in Anchorron, striking a variety of comical poses around the statue of former CDU Presav [[Wiltmer Gerrin]].

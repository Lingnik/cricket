'''Zeltrons''' are [[Near Human]] species whose skin colour is a distinctive trait, ranging from light pink to deep red. They are from the planet [[:starwars:Zeltros/Legends|Zeltros]].

== Biology and Appearance ==

Zeltrons largely resemble [[Human|Humans]] physically. The most notable difference is their skin, which is in the red colour spectrum. Their hair also comes in a wider range of colours than it does for baseline Humans, with red, blue, brown, black, white, sliver, pink, and purple all possibilities.

Physiologically the differences are more pronounced. Zeltrons are known for their pheromones which enhance their attractiveness and likeability in the eyes of others. They also possess limited telepathic abilities which allow them to project their emotions onto others. Conversely their empathetic traits leave them prone to be effected by the negative emotions of others. 

Most Zeltron appear physically fit as they possess excellent metabolism. They also have a greater tolerance for alcohol and drugs as they have two livers. This undoubtedly helps in their reputation as partiers.

== Society and Culture ==

Zeltron culture is highly driven by the pursuit of pleasure, particularly sexual gratification. Sexuality is their favoured subject matter for art and literature, which was considered quite racy by galactic standards. They tend to view monogamy as impractical. Their choices in fashion tend to be quite revealing and prone to wild colour choices and bizarre patterns. 

Their hedonism often has them stereotyped as lazy. Their homeworld, [[:starwars:Zeltros/Legends|Zeltros]], is known as a "party planet." Their thrill seeking, however, is not confined to partying and sex, Zeltrons are often known to be formidable fighters. They also have a reputation for being gullible.

They are largely known to be highly skilled artists, entertainers, and courtesans. Their society is also a technologically advanced one with particular acclaim in the fields of agriculture, medicine, and holography.

== OOC Notes ==

=== Special Abilities ===

'''Empathy''': Zeltron feel other people’s emotions as if they were their own. Therefore, they receive a -1D penalty to ALL rolls when in the presence of anyone projecting strong negative emotions.

'''Pheromones''': Zeltron can project their emotions, and this gives them a +1D bonus to influencing others through the use of the bargain or con skills.

'''Entertainers''': Due to their talents as entertainers, Zeltron gain a +1D bonus to any skill rolls involving acting, playing musical instruments, singing, or other forms of entertainment. 

=== Character Generation ===

For the purposes of game mechanics a player wishing to have a Zeltron character would choose [[Near Human]] during character generation.

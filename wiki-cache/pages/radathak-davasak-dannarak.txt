''''radathak-davasak-dannarak''' ([[4 BBY]] - ) is a [[Xi Char]] leader. She is [[Dean]] of the [[Cathedral Factory]] of Xi Dan on [[Charros IV]] and Xi Dan's representative on the [[Union Council]]. Politically she is a member of [[Praecet]]. As part of her role with [[Haor Chall Engineering]] in running one of the main cathedral factories producing war materiels for [[Caspian Defence Force]], 'radathak holds a commission as a [[Rear Admiral]] in the [[Caspian Navy]].

'radathak has been noted as keen observer of other cultures, who has consciously worked to fuse [[Xi Charrian]] culture into the wider culture of the Union. Using the Union's [[:Image:Emblem cdu vectapex.png|Vector Apex]] symbol as inspiration for the external design of two new ship classes the [[Vector-Class Starfighter]] and the [[Apex-Class Starfighter]]. The two designs are identical in most respects, but in an embrace of the diversity found within the Union, the Vector-Class were built with species such as [[Human|Humans]] and [[Sarian|Sarians]] in mind while the Apex-Class were built for larger species such as [[Wookiee|Wookiees]] and [[Yari|Yaries]].

These new designs debuted as part of the Union's [[Second Renaissance of Innovation]] and [[Charros Wargames]]. Both starfighters are constructed at the Cathedral Factory of Xi Dan and make up a significant portion of its total industrial output.

Following the passing of [[Prelate]] [[r'ratak-varattak-n'gavanak]] in [[34 ABY]], 'radathak led the first prominent funerary service at the Chapel Factory of Xi Cas on [[Caspar]] with the entire Union Council in attendance. She was one of the clerics considered as a possible successor as Prelate. Ultimately the [[conclave]] named Inquisitor [[stahanak]] as the new Prelate.
